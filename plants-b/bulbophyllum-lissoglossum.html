Bulbophyllum lissoglossum

Bulbophyllum lissoglossum, commonly known as the velvet-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features long, narrow leaves and bears striking, showy flowers with a velvety texture on the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
