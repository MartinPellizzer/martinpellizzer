Besleria elongata

Besleria elongata, commonly known as the elongated besleria, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its leaves are usually elongated and have a glossy appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
