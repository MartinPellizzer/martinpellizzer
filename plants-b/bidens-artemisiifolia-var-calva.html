Bidens artemisiifolia var. calva

Bidens artemisiifolia var. calva, commonly known as common ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from late summer to early fall. Its seeds are encased in a pappus, which aids in wind dispersal.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
