Beyeria physaphylla

Beyeria physaphylla, commonly known as the sandstone lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers with a distinctive, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
