Bulbophyllum lobbii var. siamense

Bulbophyllum lobbii var. siamense, commonly known as the Siam Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a epiphytic plant, often found growing on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features elongated pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its inflorescence consists of multiple small, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white and pink.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
