Burmannia lutescens

Burmannia lutescens, commonly known as the golden burmannia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burmanniaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble small suns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Burmannia lutescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
