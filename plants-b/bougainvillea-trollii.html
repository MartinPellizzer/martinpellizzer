Bougainvillea trollii

Bougainvillea trollii, commonly known as the pink-flowered bougainvillea, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, papery bracts that surround small, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
