Bulbophyllum planiplexum

Bulbophyllum planiplexum, commonly known as the flat-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its flat, broad leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding environment.

Bulbophyllum planiplexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
