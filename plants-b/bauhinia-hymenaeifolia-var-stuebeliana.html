Bauhinia hymenaeifolia var. stuebeliana

Bauhinia hymenaeifolia var. stuebeliana, commonly known as the Stuebel's bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are composed of numerous small leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern. The plant produces showy, papilionaceous flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
