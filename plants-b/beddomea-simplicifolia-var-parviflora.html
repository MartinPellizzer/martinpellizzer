Beddomea simplicifolia var. parviflora

Beddomea simplicifolia var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered beddomea, is a flowering plant native to the western coast of South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, clustered inflorescences. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and the flowers are typically small, pale blue to white in color.
