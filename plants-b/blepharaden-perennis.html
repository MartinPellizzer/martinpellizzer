Blepharaden perennis

Blepharaden perennis, commonly known as the everlasting flower, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant produces numerous small, tubular florets surrounded by a ring of petal-like bracts. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions.

Due to its long-lasting blooms, it is often used in floral arrangements and gardens.
