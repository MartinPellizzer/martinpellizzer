Boraginella Aucheri

Boraginella aucheri is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It belongs to the Boraginaceae family, known for its unique blue or violet flowers. This species thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in alpine environments. Its simple structure and charming blooms make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners. Despite its modest size, Boraginella aucheri holds significant ecological value in its native habitat.
