Buddleja macrostachya

Buddleja macrostachya, commonly known as the giant butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that produce abundant nectar-rich flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and often have a slight pubescence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its long blooming period attracts a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees.
