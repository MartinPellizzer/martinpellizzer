Bignonia egensis

Bignonia egensis, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is characterized by its long, climbing stems and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
