Bulbophyllum yunnanense

Bulbophyllum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying environmental conditions.

The flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with complex patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.
