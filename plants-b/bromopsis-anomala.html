Bromopsis anomala

Bromopsis anomala, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Bromopsis anomala is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
