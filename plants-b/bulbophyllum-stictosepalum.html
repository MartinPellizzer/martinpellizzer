Bulbophyllum stictosepalum

Bulbophyllum stictosepalum, commonly known as the Stictosepalum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features elongated, strap-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in inflorescences that can produce numerous blooms.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
