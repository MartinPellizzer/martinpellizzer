Bricchettia

Bricchettia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. Bricchettia species typically have thick, waxy leaves that help them retain moisture in their native arid environments. They are often cultivated as ornamental plants due to their attractive foliage and blooms.

The genus includes several species, with Bricchettia nana being one of the most popular for indoor gardening.
