Bunium paucifolium var. junceum

Bunium paucifolium var. junceum, commonly known as slender fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its aromatic seeds are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The species is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in temperate climates.
