Bulbostylis clarkeana

Bulbostylis clarkeana, commonly known as the Clarke's bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale green flowers. Bulbostylis clarkeana is typically found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
