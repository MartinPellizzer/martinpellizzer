Bidens goiana

Bidens goiana, commonly known as Brazilian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear leaves. The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Bidens goiana is known for its adaptability and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
