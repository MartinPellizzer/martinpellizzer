Babingtonia tozerensis

Babingtonia tozerensis, commonly known as the Tozer's babingtonia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

Babingtonia tozerensis is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
