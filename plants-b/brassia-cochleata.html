Brassia cochleata

Brassia cochleata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a velvety texture. Brassia cochleata thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
