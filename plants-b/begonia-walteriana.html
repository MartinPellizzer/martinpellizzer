Begonia walteriana

Begonia walteriana, commonly known as Walter's begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
