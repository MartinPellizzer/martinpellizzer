Begonia × frigida

Begonia × frigida, commonly known as the frost begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of horticultural characteristics.

It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in various colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a bedding plant or in hanging baskets.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic value, Begonia × frigida is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color to shaded areas.
