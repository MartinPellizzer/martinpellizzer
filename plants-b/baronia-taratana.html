Baronia taratana

Baronia taratana, commonly known as the Sierra starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in alpine and subalpine environments. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, often covered in a fine, silvery bloom.
