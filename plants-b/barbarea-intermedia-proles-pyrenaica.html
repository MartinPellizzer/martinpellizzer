Barbarea intermedia proles pyrenaica

Barbarea intermedia proles pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean winter cress, is a perennial herb native to the Pyrenees region.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring.

The seeds are enclosed in a capsule that splits open to release the tiny, winged seeds.
