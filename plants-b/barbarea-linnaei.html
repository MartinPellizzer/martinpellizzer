Barbarea linnaei

Barbarea linnaei, commonly known as Linnaeus' honesty, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by spherical, white seed pods. Its leaves are lobed and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Barbarea linnaei is often found in woodland edges and meadows, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
