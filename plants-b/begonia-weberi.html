Begonia weberi

Begonia weberi, commonly known as the Weber's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often appearing in spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia weberi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
