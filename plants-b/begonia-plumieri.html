Begonia plumieri

Begonia plumieri, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to purple. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia plumieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and compact growth habit.
