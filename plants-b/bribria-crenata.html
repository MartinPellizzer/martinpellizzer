Bribria crenata

Bribria crenata, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Bribria crenata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
