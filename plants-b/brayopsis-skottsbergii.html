Brayopsis skottsbergii

Brayopsis skottsbergii, commonly known as the African rock fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. It thrives in well-drained environments and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.

Brayopsis skottsbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
