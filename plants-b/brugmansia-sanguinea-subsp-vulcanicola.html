Brugmansia sanguinea subsp. vulcanicola

Brugmansia sanguinea subsp.

vulcanicola, commonly known as the Red Trumpet Tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America. It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color.

The plant produces yellow, fleshy berries that contain toxic seeds, making it important to handle with care. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its bark is smooth and grayish.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
