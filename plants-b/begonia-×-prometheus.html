Begonia × prometheus

Begonia × prometheus, commonly known as the Prometheus begonia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia erythrophylla.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a favored plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
