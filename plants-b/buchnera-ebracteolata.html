Buchnera ebracteolata

Buchnera ebracteolata, commonly known as the slender leaf balm, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. Buchnera ebracteolata belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
