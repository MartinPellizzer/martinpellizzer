Brassia panamensis

Brassia panamensis, commonly known as the Panama orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Brassia panamensis thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
