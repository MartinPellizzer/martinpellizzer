Basistemon pulchellus

Basistemon pulchellus, commonly known as the pretty bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Basistemon pulchellus is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, well-drained soils.
