Bulbophyllum oligochaete

Bulbophyllum oligochaete, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, elongated pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
