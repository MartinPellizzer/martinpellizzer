Bermudiana quadrangulata

Bermudiana quadrangulata, commonly known as the Bermuda grass, is a perennial grass species native to the warm temperate regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, flat blades and a robust root system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant is known for its ability to form dense turf, making it a popular choice for lawns and pastures. It has a distinctive four-sided stem, which gives it its scientific name.

Bermudiana quadrangulata is also notable for its rapid growth and high tolerance to drought and grazing.
