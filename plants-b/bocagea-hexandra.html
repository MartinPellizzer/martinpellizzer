Bocagea hexandra

Bocagea hexandra, commonly known as the Florida beauty or Florida bocagea, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and dry woodlands.

Bocagea hexandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal habitats.
