Begonia balmisiana var. mitellifolia

Begonia balmisiana var. mitellifolia, commonly known as the Mitellifolia Begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns of red, purple, and green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
