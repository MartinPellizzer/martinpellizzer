Berchemia trichoclada var. leioclada

Berchemia trichoclada var. leioclada, commonly known as the smooth berchemia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.
