Brunia pillansii

Brunia pillansii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that attract pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for both landscape and container planting.
