Bertiera procumbens

Bertiera procumbens, commonly known as the creeping bertiera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub or perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 30 cm. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Bertiera procumbens is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
