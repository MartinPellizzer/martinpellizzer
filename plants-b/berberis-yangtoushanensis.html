Berberis yangtoushanensis

Berberis yangtoushanensis, commonly known as the Yangtoushan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Yangtze River region.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
