Bulbophyllum dempoense

Bulbophyllum dempoense, commonly known as the Dempo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its survival in seasonal environments. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of white, green, or yellow.

It thrives in humid, shaded conditions and is often found in forest understories.
