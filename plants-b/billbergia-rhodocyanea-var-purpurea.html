Billbergia rhodocyanea var. purpurea

Billbergia rhodocyanea var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It features vibrant, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a unique water-holding structure. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, often in shades of red and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
