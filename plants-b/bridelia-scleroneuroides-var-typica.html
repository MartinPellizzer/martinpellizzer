Bridelia scleroneuroides var. typica

Bridelia scleroneuroides var. typica, commonly known as the African sandmat, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
