Biscutella lima

Biscutella lima, commonly known as the Lima biscuit plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Biscutella lima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
