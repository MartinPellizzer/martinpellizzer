Breweria abscissa

Breweria abscissa, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, and its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Breweria abscissa is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
