Bixa Orellana F. Leiocarpa

Bixa orellana f. leiocarpa is a lesser-known variant of the annatto plant known for its unique characteristics. This cultivar is prized for its smooth, pale yellow seeds that are used in traditional medicine and food coloring. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well. The plant's leaves are edible and have been used in various cuisines across South America. Despite its benefits, it remains underappreciated compared to its more popular relatives.
