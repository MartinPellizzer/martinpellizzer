Bredia glabra

Bredia glabra, commonly known as the smooth bredia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often displaying white or pale pink hues.

Bredia glabra is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional remedies.
