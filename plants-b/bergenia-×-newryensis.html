Bergenia × newryensis

Bergenia × newryensis, commonly known as Newry Bergenia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the British Isles and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that forms a low-growing mat. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in terminal racemes. Bergenia × newryensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its hardiness and ornamental value contribute to its cultivation in temperate regions.
