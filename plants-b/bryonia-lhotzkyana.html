It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its trailing stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits. Its leaves and fruits contain toxic compounds, including alkaloids, which can be harmful to humans and animals.
