Begonia baikoides

Begonia baikoides, commonly known as the Baikoides begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a waxy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia baikoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
