Berberis lijiangensis

Berberis lijiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a shrub native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
