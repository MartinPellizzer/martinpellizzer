Babiana stellata

Babiana stellata, commonly known as the star lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright red or orange bulbs that store nutrients for the next growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Babiana stellata is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
