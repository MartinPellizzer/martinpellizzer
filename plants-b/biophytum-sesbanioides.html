Biophytum sesbanioides

Biophytum sesbanioides, commonly known as the silverleaf sesban, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a sprawling growth habit with succulent, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy, silver coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, bean-like seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
