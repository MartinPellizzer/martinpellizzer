Banisteria spixiana

Banisteria spixiana, commonly known as the purple passion vine, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which bloom in clusters and attract a variety of pollinators. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found growing along trees and shrubs. Its rapid growth habit makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.

Banisteria spixiana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
