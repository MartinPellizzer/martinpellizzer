Bulbophyllum nasica

Bulbophyllum nasica, commonly known as the Nasica's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often epiphytic on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually fleshy and dark green, adapted for water storage.

The flowers, which can be white or pale yellow, are often adorned with intricate patterns and are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
