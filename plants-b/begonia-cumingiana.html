Begonia cumingiana

Begonia cumingiana, commonly known as the Cuming's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
