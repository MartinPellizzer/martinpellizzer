Brachystelma comptum

Brachystelma comptum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed stonecrop, is a member of the Crassulaceae family.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region. The plant features short, thick stems and fleshy, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

Brachystelma comptum is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for succulent enthusiasts.
