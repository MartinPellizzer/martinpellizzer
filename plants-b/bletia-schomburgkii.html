Bletia schomburgkii

Bletia schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's bletia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, with flowers arranged in a raceme along the stem. Its leaves are elliptical, arranged in a rosette, and are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Bletia schomburgkii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
