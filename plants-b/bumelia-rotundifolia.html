Bumelia rotundifolia

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and oval-shaped leaves with rounded tips. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in catkins, and it is often found in moist, wooded areas.
