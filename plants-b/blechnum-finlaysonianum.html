Blechnum finlaysonianum

Blechnum finlaysonianum, commonly known as the Finlayson's holly fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its leathery, evergreen fronds that are typically dark green in color. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that allow it to spread and form dense colonies. Its fronds are usually pinnately compound, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
