Bondtia polyclada

Bondtia polyclada, commonly known as the spiral tree, is a unique tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of branches and leaves, which gives it a visually striking appearance. The plant has a woody stem that grows in a helical pattern, creating a natural spiral effect. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a dark green color.

Bondtia polyclada is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unusual and decorative form.
