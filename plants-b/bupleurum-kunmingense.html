Bupleurum kunmingense

Bupleurum kunmingense, commonly known as the Yunnan angelica, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lanceolate leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are white to pale yellow, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Bupleurum kunmingense is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
