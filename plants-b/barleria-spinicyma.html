Barleria spinicyma

Barleria spinicyma, commonly known as the spiked barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria spinicyma is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
