Biophytum insigne

Biophytum insigne, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that features vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant has a branching stem covered in soft, hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems, creating a dramatic visual display.

Biophytum insigne thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
