Brossaea Caespitosa

Brossaea caespitosa is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes and coastal scrub habitats. Known for its small, clustered flowers, it blooms in late spring and early summer. This plant is critically endangered due to habitat loss and urban development. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
