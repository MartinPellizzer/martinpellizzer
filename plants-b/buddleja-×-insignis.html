Buddleja × insignis

Buddleja × insignis, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, slender, gray-green leaves and its clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range from white to pink to purple. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Buddleja × insignis is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
