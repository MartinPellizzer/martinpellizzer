Botrycarpum

It is characterized by its distinctive, five-lobed, star-shaped fruit that turns yellow when ripe. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers with five petals. Botrycarpum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking addition to tropical gardens.
