Bulbine dewetii

Bulbine dewetii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem and produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has fleshy, rhombic leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Bulbine dewetii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
