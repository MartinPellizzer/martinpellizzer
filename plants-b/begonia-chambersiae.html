Begonia chambersiae

Begonia chambersiae, commonly known as the chamber's begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a dense, bushy growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in both gardens and as a houseplant.
