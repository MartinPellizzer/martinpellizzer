Begonia putaoensis

Begonia putaoensis, commonly known as the Putao begonia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Myanmar.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a deep green color and a waxy texture, while its flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
