Bulbophyllum sapphirinum

Bulbophyllum sapphirinum, commonly known as the Sapphire Lipstick Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lip-like petals that are often a vivid pink or purple, resembling a lipstick. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
