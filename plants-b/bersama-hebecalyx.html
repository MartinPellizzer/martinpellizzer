Bersama hebecalyx

Bersama hebecalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved berberis, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. Its roots contain alkaloids, which have been used in traditional medicine.

The species is characterized by its dense growth habit and adaptability to various soil types.
