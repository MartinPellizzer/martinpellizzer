Bactris inermis var. tenuissima

Bactris inermis var. tenuissima, commonly known as the slender palm or tenuissima palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to southern Mexico and parts of Central America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its slender, unbranched stem and pinnate leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. Its inflorescence produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, fleshy fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and potential fruit yield.
