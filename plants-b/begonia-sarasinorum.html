Begonia sarasinorum

Begonia sarasinorum, commonly known as the Sarasin's begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia sarasinorum is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
