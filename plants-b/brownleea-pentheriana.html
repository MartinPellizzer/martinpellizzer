Brownleea pentheriana

Brownleea pentheriana, commonly known as the Cape Town rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to brownish in color, with a unique, spurred shape.

Brownleea pentheriana is often found in coastal and mountainous areas, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
