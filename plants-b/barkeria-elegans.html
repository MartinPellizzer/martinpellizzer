Barkeria elegans

Barkeria elegans, commonly known as the elegant barkleaf, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring. Barkeria elegans thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
