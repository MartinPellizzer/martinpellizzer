Brownea multijuga

Brownea multijuga, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves and a woody stem, making it suitable for ornamental purposes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brownea multijuga is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant floral display.
