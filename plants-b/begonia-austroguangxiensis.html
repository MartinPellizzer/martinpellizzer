Begonia austroguangxiensis

Begonia austroguangxiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi begonia, is a species native to southern China, particularly Guangxi Province.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its attractive, lobed leaves and showy, often red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests and rocky areas. Its foliage is often variegated, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
