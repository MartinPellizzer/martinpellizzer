Besleria peruviana var. dissimilis

Besleria peruviana var. dissimilis, commonly known as the Peruvian flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the Andes.

It features vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red and orange, with a distinctive, elongated shape. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in arid conditions.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.
