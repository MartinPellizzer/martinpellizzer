Bulbophyllum praestans

Bulbophyllum praestans, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a distinctive red lip. It has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

Its unique floral morphology makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
