Bistorta plumosa

Bistorta plumosa, commonly known as the feathery bistort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant is characterized by its upright, grass-like stems and striking, feathery plumes that appear in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Bistorta plumosa thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
