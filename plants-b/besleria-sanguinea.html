Besleria sanguinea

Besleria sanguinea, commonly known as the blood orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Besleria in the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a glossy surface.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
