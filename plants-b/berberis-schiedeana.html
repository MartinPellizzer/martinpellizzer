Berberis schiedeana

Berberis schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiedea barbed spurge, is a shrub native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
