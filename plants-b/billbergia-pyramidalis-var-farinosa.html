Billbergia pyramidalis var. farinosa

Billbergia pyramidalis var. farinosa, commonly known as the farinosa billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that form a rosette, creating a vase-like structure that holds water. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding a dramatic visual element. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a unique texture.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indirect light conditions.
