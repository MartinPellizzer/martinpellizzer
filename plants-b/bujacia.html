Bujacia

Bujacia, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its milky sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation. The plant typically has smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. Bujacia is often found in dry, rocky areas and is adapted to arid conditions.
