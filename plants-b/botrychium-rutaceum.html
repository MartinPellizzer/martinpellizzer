Botrychium rutaceum

It is characterized by its distinctive morphology, featuring a fleshy, club-shaped rhizome and a pair of fiddlehead-like fronds. Botrychium rutaceum is a perennial plant that plays a role in forest ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for microorganisms.
