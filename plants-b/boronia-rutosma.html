Boronia rutosma

Boronia rutosma, commonly known as the pink boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in heathlands and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Boronia rutosma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
