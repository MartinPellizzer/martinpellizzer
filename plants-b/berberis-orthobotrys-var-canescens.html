Berberis orthobotrys var. canescens

Berberis orthobotrys var. canescens, commonly known as the silver barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves that are often covered in a silvery-gray pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
