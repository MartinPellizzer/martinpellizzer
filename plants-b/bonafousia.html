Bonafousia

Bonafousia, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that appear in spring, resembling small, drooping raindrops. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade.
