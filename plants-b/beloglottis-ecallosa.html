Beloglottis ecallosa

Beloglottis ecallosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and South America. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal spikes.

Beloglottis ecallosa is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
