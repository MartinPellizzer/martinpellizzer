Beckera polystachya var. schimperi

Beckera polystachya var. schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky areas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
