Besleria tuberculata

Besleria tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate gesneriad, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, tuberculate leaves and vibrant, spurred flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
