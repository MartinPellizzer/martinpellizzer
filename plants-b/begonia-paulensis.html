Begonia paulensis

Begonia paulensis, commonly known as the Brazilian begonia, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage is often adorned with silver or white markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Begonia paulensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among gardeners for its beauty and ease of care.
