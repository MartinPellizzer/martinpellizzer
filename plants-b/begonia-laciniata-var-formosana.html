Begonia laciniata var. formosana

Begonia laciniata var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan lace begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Taiwan.

It features delicate, lobed leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is often cultivated as a rare and sought-after houseplant.
