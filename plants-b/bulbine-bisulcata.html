Bulbine bisulcata

Bulbine bisulcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating called farina. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and can store water in its leaves.

Bulbine bisulcata is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
