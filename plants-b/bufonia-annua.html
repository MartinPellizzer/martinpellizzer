Bufonia annua

Bufonia annua, commonly known as the annual bufonia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Bufonia annua is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
