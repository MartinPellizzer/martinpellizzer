Baeckea gunniana

Baeckea gunniana, commonly known as the pink baeckea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge, and the plant is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Baeckea gunniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
