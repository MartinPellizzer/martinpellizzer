Brassica monensis

Brassica monensis, commonly known as the Indian mustard, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round pods that contain numerous seeds, which are used in various culinary and medicinal applications. Brassica monensis is also valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of vitamins and minerals.

Its cultivation is important in both traditional and modern agricultural systems.
