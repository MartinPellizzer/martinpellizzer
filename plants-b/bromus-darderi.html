Bromus darderi

Bromus darderi, commonly known as the Dardanian brome, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces spikelets that are often clustered at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, which helps reduce water loss in dry climates.

Bromus darderi is valued for its role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
