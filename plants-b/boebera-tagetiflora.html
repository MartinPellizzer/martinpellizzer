Boebera tagetiflora

Boebera tagetiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, linear leaves along its stem.

Boebera tagetiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
