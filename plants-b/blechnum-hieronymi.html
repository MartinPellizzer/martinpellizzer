Blechnum hieronymi

Blechnum hieronymi, commonly known as the New Zealand maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce upright, lance-shaped leaves that are often bisected.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, finely divided structure, making it a popular ornamental plant.
