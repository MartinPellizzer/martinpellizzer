Begonia suffruticosa var. gueinziana

Begonia suffruticosa var. gueinziana, commonly known as the Gueinziana Begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-draining soil.

The Gueinziana Begonia is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
