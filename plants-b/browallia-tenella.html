Browallia tenella

Browallia tenella, commonly known as the dwarf browallia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a short-lived perennial that typically grows to a height of about 15 centimeters. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
