Buxus chaoanensis

Buxus chaoanensis, commonly known as the Chaoan boxwood, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact foliage consisting of small, shiny, dark green leaves. The plant typically reaches a height of about 1 meter and has a rounded, bushy shape. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a foundation plant.

Buxus chaoanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays due to its hardy nature.
