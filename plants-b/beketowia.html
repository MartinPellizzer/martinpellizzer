Beketowia

Beketowia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers typically bloom during the late spring and early summer months.

Beketowia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its elegant appearance and subtle fragrance.
