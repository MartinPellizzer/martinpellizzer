Begonia solimutata

Begonia solimutata, commonly known as the Solimutata Begonia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from red to orange in color, often with intricate patterns. The leaves are usually heart-shaped and can be quite large, adding to the plant's ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among gardeners for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
