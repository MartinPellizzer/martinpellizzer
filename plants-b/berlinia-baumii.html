Berlinia baumii

Berlinia baumii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptic or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and yellowish-green, arranged in panicles, and it produces woody pods containing flat, lens-shaped seeds.

The tree is adapted to dry conditions and plays an important role in its native ecosystems.
