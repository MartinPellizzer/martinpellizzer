Bulbophyllum gehrtii

Bulbophyllum gehrtii, commonly known as the Geert's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Bulbophyllum gehrtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
