Bryodes minutissima

Bryodes minutissima, commonly known as the minute moss, is a small, cryptogenic moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its diminutive stature, with plants typically less than 2 centimeters in height. Bryodes minutissima is notable for its ability to survive in extreme conditions, making it a resilient component of microhabitats.
