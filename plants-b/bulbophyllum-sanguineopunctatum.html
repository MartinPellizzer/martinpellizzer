Bulbophyllum sanguineopunctatum

Bulbophyllum sanguineopunctatum, commonly known as the red-spotted orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and dense, velvety foliage. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with petals that range in color from white to pink, often with red or purple markings. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
