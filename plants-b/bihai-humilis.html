Bihai humilis

Bihai humilis, commonly known as the dwarf banana plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and elongated, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by berries containing seeds. While not typically cultivated for fruit, it is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in tropical climates.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
