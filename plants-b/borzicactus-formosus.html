Borzicactus formosus

Borzicactus formosus, commonly known as the beautiful borzicactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered with spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for desert gardens and rockeries.
