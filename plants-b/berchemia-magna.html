Berchemia magna

Berchemia magna, commonly known as the giant berchemia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
