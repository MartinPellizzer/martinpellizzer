Bulbostylis cardiocarpoides

Bulbostylis cardiocarpoides, commonly known as the heart-shaped bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Bulbostylis cardiocarpoides is often found in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along stream banks.
