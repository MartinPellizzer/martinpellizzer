Bladhia humilis

Bladhia humilis, commonly known as the small bladhia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Bladhia humilis is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.
