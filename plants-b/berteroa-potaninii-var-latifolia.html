Berteroa potaninii var. latifolia

Berteroa potaninii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf berteroa, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, woolly, gray-green foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
