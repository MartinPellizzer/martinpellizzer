Begonia lambirensis

Begonia lambirensis, commonly known as the Lambirensis begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of tubular or funnel-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia lambirensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
