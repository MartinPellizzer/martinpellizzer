Bidens musoziana

Bidens musoziana, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in full sun. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts, and it produces small, bead-like seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Bidens musoziana is known for its hardy nature and is often used in traditional medicine.
