Brackenridgea nitida subsp. australiana

Brackenridgea nitida subsp.

australiana, commonly known as the slender brackenridgea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
