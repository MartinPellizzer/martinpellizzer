Bulbostylis squarrosa

Bulbostylis squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture that gives it a distinctive appearance.

Bulbostylis squarrosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
