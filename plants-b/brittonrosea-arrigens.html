Brittonrosea arrigens

Brittonrosea arrigens, commonly known as the Sierra rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California. This species is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations.

It is a perennial shrub that plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators.
