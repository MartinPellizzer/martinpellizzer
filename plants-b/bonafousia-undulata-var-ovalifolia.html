Bonafousia undulata var. ovalifolia

Bonafousia undulata var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the wavy leaf bonafousia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its undulating, wavy-edged leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are ovate in shape, which distinguishes it from other varieties of the same species.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
