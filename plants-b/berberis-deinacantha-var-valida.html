Berberis deinacantha var. valida

Berberis deinacantha var. valida, commonly known as the yellow barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas and parts of southern Asia.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it both visually striking and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
