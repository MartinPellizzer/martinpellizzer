Blumea Hieraciifolia Var. Flexuosa

Blumea hieraciifolia var. flexuosa is a unique variant of the Blumea genus known for its delicate, fern-like foliage. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and gracefully arranged, giving it a soft, cascading appearance. Often cultivated as a houseplant, it is prized for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
