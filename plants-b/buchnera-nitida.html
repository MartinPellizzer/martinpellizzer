Buchnera nitida

Buchnera nitida, commonly known as the slender false heath, is a low-growing shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Buchnera nitida is often found in sandy or well-drained soils in coastal areas and dunes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and garden cultivation in suitable climates.
