Bernardia dichotoma var. dichotoma

Bernardia dichotoma var. dichotoma, commonly known as the red-flowered bernardia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and clusters of small, bright red flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
