Baphia compacta

Baphia compacta, commonly known as the African dwarf lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Baphia compacta prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds.
