Bouchea hanningtonii

Bouchea hanningtonii, commonly known as the Hannington's bouchea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive white or yellow center. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Bouchea hanningtonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
