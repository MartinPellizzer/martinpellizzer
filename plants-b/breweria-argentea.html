Breweria argentea

Breweria argentea, commonly known as silverleaf breweria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with similar climates.
