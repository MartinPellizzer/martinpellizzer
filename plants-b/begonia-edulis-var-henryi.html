Begonia edulis var. henryi

Begonia edulis var. henryi, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with silver markings, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
