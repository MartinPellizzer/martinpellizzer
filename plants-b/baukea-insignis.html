Baukea insignis

Baukea insignis, commonly known as the mountain heather, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of pink or white flowers in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded location.

Baukea insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
