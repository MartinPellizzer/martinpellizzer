Bulbophyllum santosii

Bulbophyllum santosii, commonly known as the Santos' orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and vibrant colors.

Bulbophyllum santosii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
