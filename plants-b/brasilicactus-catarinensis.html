Brasilicactus catarinensis

Brasilicactus catarinensis, commonly known as the Santa Catarina cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered with spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
