Bidens acuticaulis var. filirostris

Bidens acuticaulis var. filirostris, commonly known as the slender tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets. It is typically found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and woodland edges.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a nectar source for pollinators like bees and butterflies.
