Besleria ignea var. semiannularis

Besleria ignea var. semiannularis, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
