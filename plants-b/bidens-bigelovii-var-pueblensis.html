Bidens bigelovii var. pueblensis

Bidens bigelovii var. pueblensis, commonly known as the Pueblan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with prominent central disks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
