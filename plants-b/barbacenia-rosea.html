Barbacenia rosea

Barbacenia rosea, commonly known as the pink flame plant, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has velvety, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Barbacenia rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
