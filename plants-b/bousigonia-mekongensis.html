Bousigonia mekongensis

Bousigonia mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong bousigonia, is a rare aquatic plant native to the Mekong River basin.

It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
