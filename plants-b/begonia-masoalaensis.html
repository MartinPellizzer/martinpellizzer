Begonia masoalaensis

Begonia masoalaensis, commonly known as the Masoala begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and unique features, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
