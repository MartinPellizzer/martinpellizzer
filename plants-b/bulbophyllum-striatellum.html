Bulbophyllum striatellum

Bulbophyllum striatellum, commonly known as the streaked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bulbophyllum striatellum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
