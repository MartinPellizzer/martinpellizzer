Blumea hieraciifolia var. hieraciifolia

Blumea hieraciifolia var. hieraciifolia, commonly known as the Hieracium leaf blumea, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that resemble those of the dandelion. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family.

This variant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
