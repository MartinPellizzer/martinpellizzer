Beaucarnea hartwegiana

Beaucarnea hartwegiana, commonly known as the Hartweg's elephant tree, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, twisted, tree-like trunk and sparse, fleshy foliage. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Beaucarnea hartwegiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
