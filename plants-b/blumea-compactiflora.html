Blumea compactiflora

Blumea compactiflora, commonly known as the compact-flowered blue mist shrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters, giving it a mist-like appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental value.
