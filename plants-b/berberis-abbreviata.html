Berberis abbreviata

Berberis abbreviata, commonly known as the Pacific prickly barberry, is a shrub native to western North America.

It features small, spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers in the spring. The plant is characterized by its red, berry-like fruits that mature in the fall. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Berberis abbreviata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
