Barbacenia splendens

Barbacenia splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Barbacenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with erect stems and opposite leaves. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences, often displaying a striking combination of colors.

Barbacenia splendens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
