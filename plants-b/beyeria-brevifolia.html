Beyeria brevifolia

Beyeria brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved beyeria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Beyeria brevifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
