Begonia standleyi

Begonia standleyi, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow eye. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia standleyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
