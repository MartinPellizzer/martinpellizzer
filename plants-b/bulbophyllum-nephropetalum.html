Bulbophyllum nephropetalum

Bulbophyllum nephropetalum, commonly known as the kidney petal orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a unique kidney-shaped lip that is often brightly colored. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
