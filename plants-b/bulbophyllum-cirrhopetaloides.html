Bulbophyllum cirrhopetaloides

Bulbophyllum cirrhopetaloides, commonly known as the feathered lip orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves and striking, lance-shaped flowers with a distinctive feather-like lip. The plant produces multiple inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant blooms throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
