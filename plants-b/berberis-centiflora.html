Berberis centiflora

Berberis centiflora, commonly known as the thousand-flower barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Berberis centiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
