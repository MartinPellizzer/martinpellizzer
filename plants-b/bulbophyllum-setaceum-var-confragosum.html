Bulbophyllum setaceum var. confragosum

Bulbophyllum setaceum var. confragosum, commonly known as the fragrant spike orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and pendant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive scent, attracting specific pollinators.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and fragrance.
