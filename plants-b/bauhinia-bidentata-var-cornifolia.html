Bauhinia bidentata var. cornifolia

Bauhinia bidentata var. cornifolia, commonly known as the horned bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 5 meters in height. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while its branches are often thorny.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
