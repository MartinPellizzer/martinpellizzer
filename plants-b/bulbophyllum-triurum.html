Bulbophyllum triurum

Bulbophyllum triurum, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in the ecological diversity of tropical rainforests.
