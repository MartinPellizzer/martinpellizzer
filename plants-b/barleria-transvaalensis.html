Barleria transvaalensis

Barleria transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
