Bulbophyllum perrieri

Bulbophyllum perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with purple or red markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
