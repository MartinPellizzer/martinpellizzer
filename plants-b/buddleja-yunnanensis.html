Buddleja yunnanensis

Buddleja yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is known for its ability to attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees.

Buddleja yunnanensis is relatively low maintenance and suitable for gardens in USDA hardiness zones 6 to 9.
