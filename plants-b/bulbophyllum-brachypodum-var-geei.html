Bulbophyllum brachypodum var. geei

Bulbophyllum brachypodum var. geei, commonly known as the Gee's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, elongated blooms in shades of white, yellow, or orange. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the foliage and flower spikes.

This variety is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively manageable care requirements.
