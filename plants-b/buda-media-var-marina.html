Buda media var. marina

Buda media var. marina, commonly known as the sea buda, is a variant of the Buda media species native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its salt-tolerant foliage, which allows it to thrive in saline environments. The plant typically features broad, waxy leaves that can range in color from silver to deep green. It is often found growing along shorelines and in brackish water areas.

Its adaptability makes it a valuable plant for coastal landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
