Begonia palmata var. difformis

Begonia palmata var. difformis, commonly known as the variegated begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive variegation of white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
