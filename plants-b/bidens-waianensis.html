Bidens waianensis

Bidens waianensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its yellow flowers bloom in clusters and are followed by distinctive bracts.

It plays an important role in the local ecosystem and has been used traditionally by Native Hawaiians for various purposes.
