Brachystegia reticulata

Brachystegia reticulata, commonly known as the African brachystegia, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical woodlands of central and eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, often thorny foliage and a broad, spreading crown. The tree has smooth, grey bark that becomes fissured with age, and its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.

This species plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
