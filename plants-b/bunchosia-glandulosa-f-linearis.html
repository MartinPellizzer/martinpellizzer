Bunchosia glandulosa f. linearis

Bunchosia glandulosa f. linearis, commonly known as the linear-leaved bunchosia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in glandular hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
