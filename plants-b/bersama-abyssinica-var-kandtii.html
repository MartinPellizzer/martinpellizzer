Bersama abyssinica var. kandtii

Bersama abyssinica var. kandtii, commonly known as the Abyssinian Bersama, is a shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, temperate climate. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
