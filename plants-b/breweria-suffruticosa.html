Breweria suffruticosa

Breweria suffruticosa, commonly known as the silverleaf breweria, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
