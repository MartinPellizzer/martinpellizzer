Bulbophyllum tenuislinguae

Bulbophyllum tenuislinguae, commonly known as the narrow-tongue orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are often clustered and adapted for nutrient absorption in its natural habitat.

The orchid is prized for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
