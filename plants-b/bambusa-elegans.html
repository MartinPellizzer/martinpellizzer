Bambusa elegans

Bambusa elegans, commonly known as the elegant bamboo, is a tall, slender grass native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has a strong, fibrous stem that is used in construction and crafts. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Bambusa elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and sustainable utility in landscaping and traditional industries.
