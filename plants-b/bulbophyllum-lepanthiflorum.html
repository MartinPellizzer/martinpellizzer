Bulbophyllum lepanthiflorum

Bulbophyllum lepanthiflorum, commonly known as the Lepanthiflorum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique floral structure.
