Batatas cavanillesii

Batatas cavanillesii, commonly known as the yellow batata or sweet potato, is a tuberous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its swollen, underground storage roots. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers. Its tubers are rich in carbohydrates and have a sweet, moist texture.

It is cultivated for both ornamental and edible purposes in various regions.
