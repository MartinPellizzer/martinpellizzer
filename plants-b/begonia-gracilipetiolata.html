Begonia gracilipetiolata

Begonia gracilipetiolata, commonly known as the slender petiole begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that have slender petioles, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia gracilipetiolata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
