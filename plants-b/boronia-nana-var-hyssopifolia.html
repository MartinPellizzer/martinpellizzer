Boronia nana var. hyssopifolia

Boronia nana var. hyssopifolia, commonly known as the dwarf boronia, is a small shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The leaves are narrow and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
