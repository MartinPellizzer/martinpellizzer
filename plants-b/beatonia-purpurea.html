Beatonia purpurea

Beatonia purpurea, commonly known as the purple beatonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Beatonia purpurea blooms in summer, producing clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
