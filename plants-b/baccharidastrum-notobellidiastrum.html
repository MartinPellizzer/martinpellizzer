Baccharidastrum notobellidiastrum

Baccharidastrum notobellidiastrum, commonly known as the coastal baccharis, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting various pollinators. It is adapted to sandy soils and salt spray, making it a hardy species in coastal environments.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
