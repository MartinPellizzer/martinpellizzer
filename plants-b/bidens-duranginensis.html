Bidens duranginensis

Bidens duranginensis, commonly known as the Durango ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and rocky areas. It produces small yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets, and its leaves are usually hairy and oval-shaped.

Bidens duranginensis is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
