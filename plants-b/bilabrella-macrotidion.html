Bilabrella macrotidion

Bilabrella macrotidion, commonly known as the giant bilabrella, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually bright yellow or orange, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
