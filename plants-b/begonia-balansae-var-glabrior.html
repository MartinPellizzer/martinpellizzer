Begonia balansae var. glabrior

Begonia balansae var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved begonia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, often displaying a mix of green and silver hues. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
