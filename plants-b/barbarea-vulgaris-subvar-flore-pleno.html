Barbarea vulgaris subvar. flore-pleno

Barbarea vulgaris subvar. flore-pleno, commonly known as the full-flowered winter cress, is a cultivated variant of the common winter cress.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features lobed leaves and produces small, edible seeds. It is often grown as a winter annual in temperate climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental and edible gardens.
