Bromus natalensis var. lasiophilus

Bromus natalensis var. lasiophilus, commonly known as Natal bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The leaves are typically narrow and hairy, with a bluish-green coloration. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spike that bears small, two-rowed spikelets.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
