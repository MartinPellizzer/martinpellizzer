Bulbophyllum concinnum

Bulbophyllum concinnum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Bulbophyllum concinnum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
