Begonia × cheimantha

Begonia × cheimantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a hybrid species native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to deep purple. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
