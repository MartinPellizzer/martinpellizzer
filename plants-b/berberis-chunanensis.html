Berberis chunanensis

Berberis chunanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in gardens and parks.
