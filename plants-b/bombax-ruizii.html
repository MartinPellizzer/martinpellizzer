Bombax ruizii

Bombax ruizii, commonly known as the pink silk-cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds enclosed in a woody capsule. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth surface.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
