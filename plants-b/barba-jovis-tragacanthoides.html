Barba-jovis tragacanthoides

Barba-jovis tragacanthoides, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. Its latex sap is milky and has been traditionally used for medicinal purposes.

The plant is also valued for its role in traditional herbal medicine and as a source of natural gum.
