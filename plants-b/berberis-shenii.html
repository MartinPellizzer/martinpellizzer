Berberis shenii

Berberis shenii, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often bitter. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Berberis shenii thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
