Bulbophyllum rupicola

Bulbophyllum rupicola, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates and is adapted to dry, shaded environments. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, helping it store water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum rupicola is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
