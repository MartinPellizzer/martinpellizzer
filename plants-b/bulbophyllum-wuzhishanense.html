Bulbophyllum wuzhishanense

Bulbophyllum wuzhishanense, commonly known as the Wuzhishan orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white and pink, displaying a complex arrangement of petals and sepals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
