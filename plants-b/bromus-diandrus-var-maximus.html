Bromus diandrus var. maximus

Bromus diandrus var. maximus, commonly known as the giant brome, is a tall grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces dense clusters of spikelets at the ends of its stems. It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and adaptability.
