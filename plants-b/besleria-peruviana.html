Besleria peruviana

Besleria peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian flame violet, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in humid, warm environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Besleria peruviana requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish.
