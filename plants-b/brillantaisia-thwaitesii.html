Brillantaisia thwaitesii

Brillantaisia thwaitesii, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to New Zealand.

Brillantaisia thwaitesii plays an important ecological role in New Zealand's native forests, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
