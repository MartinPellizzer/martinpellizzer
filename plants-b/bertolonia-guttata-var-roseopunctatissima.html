Bertolonia guttata var. roseopunctatissima

Bertolonia guttata var. roseopunctatissima, commonly known as the spotted bertolonia, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are prominently dotted with red and purple spots. The plant produces showy, pink to lavender flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
