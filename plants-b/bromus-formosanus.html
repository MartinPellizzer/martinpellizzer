Bromus formosanus

Bromus formosanus, commonly known as the Formosa brome, is a grass species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish spikelets. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a rough, hairy texture.

Bromus formosanus is adapted to temperate climates and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
