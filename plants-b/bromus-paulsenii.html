Bromus paulsenii

Bromus paulsenii, commonly known as Paulsen's brome, is a species of grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Bromus paulsenii is often found in arid or semi-arid regions and is adapted to survive in low-nutrient environments.
