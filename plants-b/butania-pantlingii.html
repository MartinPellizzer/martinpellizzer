Butania pantlingii

Butania pantlingii, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Butania pantlingii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
