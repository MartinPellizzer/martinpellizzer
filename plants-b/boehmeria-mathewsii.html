Boehmeria mathewsii

Boehmeria mathewsii, commonly known as the Mathews' hemp.

It is a flowering plant species belonging to the nettle family, Urticaceae. Native to Southeast Asia, it is characterized by its fibrous stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is known for its strong, durable fibers, which have been traditionally used in textile production.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
