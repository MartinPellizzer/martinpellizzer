Bromus mollis var. latiglumis

Bromus mollis var. latiglumis, commonly known as the slender brome, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces spikelets that are often clustered at the base of the plant. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in dense clusters along the stems.
