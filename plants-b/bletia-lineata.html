Bletia lineata

Bletia lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with narrow, linear leaves that are often slightly curled. The plant produces pendant, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive white lip, typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Bletia lineata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
