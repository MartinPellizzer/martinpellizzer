Baliospermum sinuatum

Baliospermum sinuatum, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, showy flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white. Its seeds are enclosed in capsule-like fruits that split open when mature.

Baliospermum sinuatum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
