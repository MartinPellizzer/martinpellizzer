Buxus madagascarica subsp. sambiranensis

Buxus madagascarica subsp.

sambiranensis, commonly known as the Sambiranensis Buxus, is a rare evergreen shrub native to Madagascar. It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
