Bletilla florida

Bletilla florida, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a fan-like pattern, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Bletilla florida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and woodland settings.
