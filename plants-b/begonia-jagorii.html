Begonia jagorii

Begonia jagorii, commonly known as the jagorii begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that range from red to pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture, and its stems are often trailing or upright depending on the growing conditions. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The botanical interest of Begonia jagorii lies in its unique floral structure and adaptability to various environments.
