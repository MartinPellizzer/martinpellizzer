Begonia megalantha

Begonia megalantha, commonly known as the giant begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
