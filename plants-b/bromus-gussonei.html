Bromus gussonei

Bromus gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's brome, is a flowering plant species native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and typically grows in temperate climates. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight bluish-green hue.

Bromus gussonei is generally found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
