Berchemia racemosa var. magna

Berchemia racemosa var. magna, commonly known as the large-flowered berchemia, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is notable for its robust growth and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
