Brachycorythis truncatolabellata

Brachycorythis truncatolabellata, commonly known as the dwarf shield orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, shield-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a unique truncate labellum, which is a specialized structure involved in pollination. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
