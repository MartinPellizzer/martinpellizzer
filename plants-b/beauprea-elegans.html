Beauprea elegans

Beauprea elegans, commonly known as the elegant beauprea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
