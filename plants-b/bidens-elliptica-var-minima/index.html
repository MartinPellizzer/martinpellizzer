Bidens Elliptica Var. Minima

Bidens elliptica var. minima is a small, delicate flowering plant native to parts of South America. It is known for its tiny, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. This variant of Bidens elliptica is particularly prized for its compact size and ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a standout addition to any garden setting.
