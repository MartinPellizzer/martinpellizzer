Bulbophyllum hovarum

Bulbophyllum hovarum, commonly known as the Hovar's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, which help it survive in its tropical environment.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.
