Brassica chinensis var. musifolia

Brassica chinensis var. musifolia, commonly known as Chinese mustard or Chinese kale, is a leafy vegetable native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants like cabbage and broccoli. The plant features thick, dark green leaves with a slightly bitter taste, often used in Asian cuisine. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various growing conditions.
