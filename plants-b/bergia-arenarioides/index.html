Bergia Arenarioides

Bergia arenarioides is a hardy, flowering plant native to the rocky coastal regions of southern Africa. It thrives in harsh, saline environments and is often found growing on sandy or gravelly soils. This small shrub is known for its delicate, blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its ability to withstand strong winds and salt spray makes it a resilient addition to coastal landscapes. Bergia arenarioides is a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
