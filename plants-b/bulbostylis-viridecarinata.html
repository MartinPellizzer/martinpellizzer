Bulbostylis viridecarinata

Bulbostylis viridecarinata, commonly known as the green-ribbed bulbostylis, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance.

This species is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
