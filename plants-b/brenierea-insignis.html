Brenierea insignis

Brenierea insignis, commonly known as the velvetleaf brenierea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
