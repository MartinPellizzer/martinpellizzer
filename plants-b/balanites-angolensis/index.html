Balanites Angolensis

Balanites angolensis is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa. It is known for its thick, waxy leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces edible fruits that are used by both humans and animals in times of scarcity. Its ability to survive in harsh environments makes it an important part of local ecosystems. Balanites angolensis is also valued for its medicinal properties and traditional uses across various cultures.
