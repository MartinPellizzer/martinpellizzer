Bridelia abyssinica var. densiflora

Bridelia abyssinica var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered bridelia, is a shrub or small tree native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, typically in well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
