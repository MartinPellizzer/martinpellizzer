Bactris aubletiana, commonly known as the pink palm, is a species of palm native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, round fruits that are about the size of a ping-pong ball. Bactris aubletiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
