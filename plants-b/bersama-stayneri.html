Bersama stayneri

Bersama stayneri, commonly known as the white-flowered stinkwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its bark is thick and fissured, often exuding a milky sap when cut.

Bersama stayneri is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
