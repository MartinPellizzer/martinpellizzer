Babiana cuneifolia

Babiana cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed babiana, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and showy, cup-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing vibrant yellow or orange petals with a contrasting darker center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Babiana cuneifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
