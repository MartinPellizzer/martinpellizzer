Bidens izabalensis

Bidens izabalensis, commonly known as the Izabal ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as riverbanks and wetlands. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Bidens izabalensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
