Barleria stenophylla

Barleria stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, tubular flowers are usually bright red or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
