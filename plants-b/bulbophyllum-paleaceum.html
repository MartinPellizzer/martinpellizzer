Bulbophyllum paleaceum

Bulbophyllum paleaceum, commonly known as the paleaceum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces clusters of small, pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant has fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
