Begonia eiromischa

Begonia eiromischa, commonly known as the purple-veined begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep purple veins against a lush green background. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
