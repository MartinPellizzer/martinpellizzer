Blumenbachia pterosperma

Blumenbachia pterosperma, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a visually stunning display. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Blumenbachia pterosperma is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
