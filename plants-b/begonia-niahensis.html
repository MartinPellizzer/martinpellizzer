Begonia niahensis

Begonia niahensis, commonly known as the Niah begonia, is a rare and endemic species native to Borneo.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the rainforest understory. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and a velvety texture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
