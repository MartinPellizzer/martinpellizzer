Boronia triphylla var. flore-pleno

Boronia triphylla var. flore-pleno, commonly known as the full-flowered boronia, is a compact shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features dense clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in winter and spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and compact growth habit.
