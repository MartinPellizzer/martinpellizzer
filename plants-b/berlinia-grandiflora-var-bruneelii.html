Berlinia grandiflora var. bruneelii

Berlinia grandiflora var. bruneelii, commonly known as the Bruneel's Berlinia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically white or pink in color. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, and its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple leaflets.
