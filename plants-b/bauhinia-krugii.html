Bauhinia krugii

Bauhinia krugii, commonly known as the Krug's bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or open woodland areas.
