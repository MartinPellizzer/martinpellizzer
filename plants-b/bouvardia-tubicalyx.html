Bouvardia tubicalyx

Bouvardia tubicalyx, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and slightly hairy.

Bouvardia tubicalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
