Buceras polyceratia

Buceras polyceratia, commonly known as the horned milkweed, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, branched stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Buceras polyceratia thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
