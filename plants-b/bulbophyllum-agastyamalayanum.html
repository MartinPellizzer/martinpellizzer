Bulbophyllum agastyamalayanum

Bulbophyllum agastyamalayanum, commonly known as the Agastyamalaya Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, intricate flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, which is essential for survival in its native habitat.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
