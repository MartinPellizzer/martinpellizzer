Brossaea strigosa

Brossaea strigosa, commonly known as the hairy brossaea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
