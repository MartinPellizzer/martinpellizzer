Bidens pinnatipartita

Bidens pinnatipartita, commonly known as the slender tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, pinnately lobed leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. Its showy yellow flowers, with prominent white ray florets, bloom in late summer and early fall.

Bidens pinnatipartita is typically found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and along streambanks.
