Begonia × hybrida

Begonia × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its diverse foliage and vibrant flowers.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular or showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Begonia × hybrida is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as houseplants.

Its botanical versatility makes it a favored choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
