Bridelia tomentosa var. glabrata

Bridelia tomentosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a capsule containing several small, round seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
