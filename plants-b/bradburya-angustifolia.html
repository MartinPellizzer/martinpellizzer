Bradburya angustifolia

Bradburya angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bradburya, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires a cool, humid environment to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and is often found in specialized botanical gardens.
