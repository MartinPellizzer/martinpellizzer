Botrychium Obliquum F. Dissectum

Botrychium obliquum f. dissectum is a unique fern species known for its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble lace. This plant is part of the Botrychiaceae family and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts. The "dissectum" form refers to its highly subdivided leaves, which set it apart from other varieties. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure and rare plant in the wild.
