Berberis jiangxiensis var. pulchella

Berberis jiangxiensis var. pulchella, commonly known as the Jiangxi barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically the Jiangxi province.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for garden landscapes in temperate climates.
