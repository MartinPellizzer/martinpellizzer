Bulbophyllum katherinae

Bulbophyllum katherinae, commonly known as the Katherina's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
