Brunfelsia Uniflora Var. Pubescens

Brunfelsia uniflora var. pubescens is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers that change color as they bloom. Native to South America, this species thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its unique feature is the gradual shift in petal color, starting with white and progressing to pink and finally red. The plant's fuzzy texture and compact growth make it a popular choice for gardeners and collectors. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and light conditions to flourish.
