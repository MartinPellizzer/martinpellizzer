Bipinnula plumosa var. philipporum

Bipinnula plumosa var. philipporum, commonly known as the Philippine feather fern, is a delicate and ornamental fern species native to the Philippines.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a soft, lacy appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish. Its unique morphology includes bipinnate leaves that are both aesthetically pleasing and structurally intricate.

This variety is valued for its ability to add a touch of elegance to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
