Begonia nemoralis

Begonia nemoralis, commonly known as the shade begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to shaded gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in less sunny conditions.
