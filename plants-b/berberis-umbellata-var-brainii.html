Berberis umbellata var. brainii

Berberis umbellata var. brainii, commonly known as the Brain's barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, which are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in umbels, followed by red or orange berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This variety is well-suited to rocky or well-drained soils and can thrive in full sun.
