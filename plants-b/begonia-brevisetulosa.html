Begonia brevisetulosa

Begonia brevisetulosa, commonly known as the short-hairy begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dense, short hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
