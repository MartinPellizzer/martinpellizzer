Brachystele pedicellata

Brachystele pedicellata, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its short, stout trunk and a dense crown of feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically broad and leathery, contributing to its ornamental value.

Brachystele pedicellata thrives in humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.
