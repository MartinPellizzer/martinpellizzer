Beaucarnea inermis

Beaucarnea inermis, commonly known as the ponytail palm, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its swollen, bottle-shaped stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant has a rosette of long, fibrous leaves that resemble a ponytail, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters at the top of the stem.

Beaucarnea inermis is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.
