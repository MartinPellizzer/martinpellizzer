Bergenia × Media

Bergenia × media is a hybrid plant known for its hardy nature and attractive foliage. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and is often used in woodland gardens. This plant features broad, waxy leaves that provide year-round interest. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, add a splash of color to the landscape. Bergenia × media is a versatile choice for gardeners looking to add both beauty and resilience to their outdoor spaces.
