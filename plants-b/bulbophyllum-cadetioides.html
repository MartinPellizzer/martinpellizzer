Bulbophyllum cadetioides

Bulbophyllum cadetioides, commonly known as the Cadet's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
