Bulbophyllum confertum

Bulbophyllum confertum, commonly known as the clustered lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and often covered with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Bulbophyllum confertum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
