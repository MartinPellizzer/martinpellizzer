Bulbostylis yalingensis

Bulbostylis yalingensis, commonly known as the Yaling sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in wetland or marshy environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
