Baeckea cochinchinensis

Baeckea cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese baeckea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.
