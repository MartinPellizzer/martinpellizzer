Boerhavia cortensis

Boerhavia cortensis, commonly known as the creeping boerhavia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herb with fleshy, succulent stems and small, white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Boerhavia cortensis is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it both a resilient weed and a potential ornamental plant.
