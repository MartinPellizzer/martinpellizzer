Bulbophyllum lineare

Bulbophyllum lineare, commonly known as the linear orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants rather than in soil. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
