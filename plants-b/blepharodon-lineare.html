Blepharodon lineare

Blepharodon lineare, commonly known as the linear leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and delicate, pale greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it can receive filtered sunlight. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the moist soil, and it thrives in areas with high humidity and moderate temperatures.
