Baphia micrantha

Baphia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered baphia, is a member of the Leguminosae family.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

Baphia micrantha is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
