Barleria delagoensis

Barleria delagoensis, commonly known as the African shield flower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, shield-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and grows in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Barleria delagoensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
