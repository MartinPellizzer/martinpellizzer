Bakerisideroxylon djalonense

Bakerisideroxylon djalonense, commonly known as the West African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of West Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has a straight, cylindrical trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy upper surface.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.
