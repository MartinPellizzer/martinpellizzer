Brillantaisia

Brillantaisia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous coat. Brillantaisia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its foliage is dense and provides good shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
