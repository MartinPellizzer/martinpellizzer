Bertiera arctistipula

Bertiera arctistipula, commonly known as the woolly bertiera, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a leathery texture.

Bertiera arctistipula is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
