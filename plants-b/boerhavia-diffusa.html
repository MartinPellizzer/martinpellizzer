Boerhavia diffusa

Boerhavia diffusa, commonly known as spreading hogweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a sprawling annual herb with erect stems that often branch out near the base. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture. Boerhavia diffusa has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

It is often found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas where it can spread rapidly.
