Brosimum krukovii

Brosimum krukovii, commonly known as the Krukovii rubber tree, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a large, fast-growing tree that can reach heights of up to 25 meters. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its latex, similar to that of the rubber tree, has been historically used for various purposes.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in tropical rainforests.
