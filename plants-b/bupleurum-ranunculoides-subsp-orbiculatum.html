Bupleurum ranunculoides subsp. orbiculatum

Bupleurum ranunculoides subsp.

orbiculatum, commonly known as the round-leaved bellflower, is a member of the Apiaceae family. It is characterized by its rounded, orbicular leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
