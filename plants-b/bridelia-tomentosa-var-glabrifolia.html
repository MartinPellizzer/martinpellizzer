Bridelia tomentosa var. glabrifolia

Bridelia tomentosa var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
