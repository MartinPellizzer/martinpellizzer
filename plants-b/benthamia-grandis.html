Benthamia grandis

Benthamia grandis, commonly known as the large benthamia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its deep root system. Due to its unique adaptations, it is of interest to botanists studying xerophytic plants.
