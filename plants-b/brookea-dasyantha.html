Brookea dasyantha

Brookea dasyantha, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, vivid red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dramatic and eye-catching appearance. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves and produces woody, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Brookea dasyantha is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
