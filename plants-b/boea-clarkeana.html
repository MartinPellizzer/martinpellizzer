Boea clarkeana

Boea clarkeana, commonly known as the silver leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-blue hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and hanging baskets.
