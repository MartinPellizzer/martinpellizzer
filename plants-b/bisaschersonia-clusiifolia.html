Bisaschersonia clusiifolia

Bisaschersonia clusiifolia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in pendulous clusters, resembling a shower of golden droplets. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape with a smooth surface. It belongs to the family Clusiaceae, which includes other ornamental and economically important plants.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
