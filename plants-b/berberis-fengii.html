Berberis fengii

Berberis fengii, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in the spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it both visually striking and ecologically beneficial.
