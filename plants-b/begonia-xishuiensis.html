Begonia xishuiensis

Begonia xishuiensis, commonly known as the Xishui begonia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or pink in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
