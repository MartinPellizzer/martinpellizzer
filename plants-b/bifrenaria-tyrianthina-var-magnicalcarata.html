Bifrenaria tyrianthina var. magnicalcarata

Bifrenaria tyrianthina var. magnicalcarata, commonly known as the giant calcarate bifrenaria, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display vibrant red and orange hues. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, textured surface. Its stems are often woody and can grow quite tall, making it a notable ornamental species.

This variety is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments.
