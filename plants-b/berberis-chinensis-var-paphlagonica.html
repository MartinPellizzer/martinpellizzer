Berberis chinensis var. paphlagonica

Berberis chinensis var. paphlagonica, commonly known as the Paphlagonian barberry, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features thorny stems and small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Berberidaceae family.
