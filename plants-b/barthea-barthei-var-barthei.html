Barthea barthei var. barthei

Barthea barthei var. barthei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.
