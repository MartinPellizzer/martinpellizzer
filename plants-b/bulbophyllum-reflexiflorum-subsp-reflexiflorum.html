Bulbophyllum reflexiflorum subsp. reflexiflorum

Bulbophyllum reflexiflorum subsp.

reflexiflorum, commonly known as the reflexiflorum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its rhizome system allows it to store nutrients and water, aiding survival in its natural habitat.

This orchid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
