Bidens aequisquama var. guanajuatensis

Bidens aequisquama var. guanajuatensis, commonly known as the Guanajuato ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It features small, yellow flowers with prominent central disk and surrounding ray florets.

Its foliage is usually narrow and lance-shaped, with hairy surfaces that help reduce water loss in arid environments.
