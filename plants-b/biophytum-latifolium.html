Biophytum latifolium

Biophytum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf biophytum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored, leaf-like bracts that are typically more showy than the small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Biophytum latifolium is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
