Bulbophyllum nitens var. pulverulentum

Bulbophyllum nitens var. pulverulentum, commonly known as the dust-veined orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, dark green leaves. The plant produces intricate inflorescences with small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers that have a unique, velvety texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts, though it is not widely commercially available.
