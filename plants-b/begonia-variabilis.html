Begonia variabilis

Begonia variabilis, commonly known as the variable begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish-green hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Begonia variabilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
