Barlia

Barlia, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, barlia thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

It is often used in herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
