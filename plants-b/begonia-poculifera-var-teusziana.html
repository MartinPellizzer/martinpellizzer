Begonia poculifera var. teusziana

Begonia poculifera var. teusziana, commonly known as the Teuszian begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, adding visual interest. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
