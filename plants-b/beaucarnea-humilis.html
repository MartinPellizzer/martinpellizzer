Beaucarnea humilis

Beaucarnea humilis, commonly known as the ponytail palm, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a swollen, tree-like stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant has a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that give it a unique appearance. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring.

Its slow growth rate and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
