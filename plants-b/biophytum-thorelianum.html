Biophytum thorelianum

Biophytum thorelianum, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling miniature flames. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
