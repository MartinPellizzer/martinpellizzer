Brizopyrum acutiflorum var. capillare

Brizopyrum acutiflorum var. capillare, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and cylindrical flower spikes that bear small, scale-like flowers. This variety is typically found in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread vegetatively and form dense stands.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
