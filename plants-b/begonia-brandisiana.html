Begonia brandisiana

Begonia brandisiana, commonly known as the Singapore begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia brandisiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
