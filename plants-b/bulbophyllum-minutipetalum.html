Bulbophyllum minutipetalum

Bulbophyllum minutipetalum, commonly known as the miniature-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, contributing to its epiphytic growth habit.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its unique floral structure and ornamental value.
