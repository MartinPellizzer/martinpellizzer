Brachyscome tasmanica

Brachyscome tasmanica, commonly known as the Tasmanian daisy, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Brachyscome tasmanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
