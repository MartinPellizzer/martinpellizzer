Bambusa boniopsis

Bambusa boniopsis, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to China.

It features narrow, bright yellow-green leaves that give it a striking appearance. The plant has culms that are slender and often have a reddish tint, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Bambusa boniopsis is popular in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
