Bouvardia longiflora var. latifolia

Bouvardia longiflora var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf bouvardia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant, pink or white flowers. The leaves are broad and oval, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This plant is often used in floral arrangements due to its delicate blooms and long-lasting nature.
