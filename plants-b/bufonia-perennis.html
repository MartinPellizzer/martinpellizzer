Bufonia perennis

Bufonia perennis, commonly known as the everlasting flower, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its unique ability to retain its color and form for extended periods has earned it its common name.

Bufonia perennis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
