Berberis pingjiangensis

Berberis pingjiangensis, commonly known as the Pingjiang barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, glossy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
