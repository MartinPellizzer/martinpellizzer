Brunia speciosa

Brunia speciosa, commonly known as the showy brunia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Brunia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
