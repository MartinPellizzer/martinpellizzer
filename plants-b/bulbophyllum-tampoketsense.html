Bulbophyllum tampoketsense

Bulbophyllum tampoketsense, commonly known as the Tampoketsense orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
