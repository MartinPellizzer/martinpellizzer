Boronia thujona

Boronia thujona, commonly known as lemon balm, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.
