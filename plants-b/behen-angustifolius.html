Behen angustifolius

Behen angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaf behen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Behen angustifolius is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
