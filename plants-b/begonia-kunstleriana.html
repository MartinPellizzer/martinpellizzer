Begonia kunstleriana

Begonia kunstleriana, commonly known as the Kunstler's begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and a distinctive, waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia kunstleriana is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
