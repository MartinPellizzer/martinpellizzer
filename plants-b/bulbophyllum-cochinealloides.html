Bulbophyllum cochinealloides

Bulbophyllum cochinealloides, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often feature a deep red lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
