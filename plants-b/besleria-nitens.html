Besleria nitens

Besleria nitens, commonly known as the silver lace vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, spurred flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture. This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
