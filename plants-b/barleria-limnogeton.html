Barleria limnogeton

Barleria limnogeton, commonly known as the water barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes at the ends of the branches.

Barleria limnogeton is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
