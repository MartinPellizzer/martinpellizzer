Bonyunia pulchra

Bonyunia pulchra, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fibrous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Bonyunia pulchra is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
