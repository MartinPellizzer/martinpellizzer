Blumea cafra

Blumea cafra, commonly known as African chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Blumea cafra is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
