Bufonia ephedrina subsp. ephedrina

Bufonia ephedrina subsp.

ephedrina, commonly known as ephedra, is a shrub-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. It is characterized by its jointed stems and small, scale-like leaves.

The plant produces greenish-yellow flowers and red or purple berries. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal water.

Botanically, it belongs to the Ephedraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties.
