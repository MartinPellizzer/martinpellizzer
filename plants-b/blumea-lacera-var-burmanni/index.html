Blumea Lacera Var. Burmanni

Blumea lacera var. burmanni is a lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family that thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and delicate yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. Found primarily in Southeast Asia, it often grows in moist, shaded environments like forest undergrowth. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Its ecological role and potential medicinal uses make it an intriguing subject for further study.
