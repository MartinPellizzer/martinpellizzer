Bowlesia elegans

Bowlesia elegans, commonly known as the elegant bowlesia, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Bowlesia elegans is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
