Begonia geniculata

Begonia geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
