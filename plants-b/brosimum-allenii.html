Brosimum allenii

Brosimum allenii, commonly known as the Allen's balsa, is a deciduous tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its light, porous wood, which is among the lightest in the world. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with smooth margins. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by elongated, fibrous fruits.

Brosimum allenii plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
