Begonia paganuccii

Begonia paganuccii, commonly known as the Paganucci begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with a mix of green, red, and purple tones. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
