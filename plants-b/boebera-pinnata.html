Boebera pinnata

Boebera pinnata, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct, aromatic scent. Boebera pinnata thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
