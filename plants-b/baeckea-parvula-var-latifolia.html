Baeckea parvula var. latifolia

Baeckea parvula var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf baeckea, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically broader than those of the typical variety, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal and sandy soils.

This variety is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
