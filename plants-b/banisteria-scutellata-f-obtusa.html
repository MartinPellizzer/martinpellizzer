Banisteria scutellata f. obtusa

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
