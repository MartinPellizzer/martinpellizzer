Bluffia

Bluffia, commonly known as the yellow daisy or *Bluffia coccinea*, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Bluffia typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
