Bulliarda pedunculata

Bulliarda pedunculata, commonly known as the clustered bulliard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its showy yellow flowers appear in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Bulliarda pedunculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact growth and vibrant blooms.
