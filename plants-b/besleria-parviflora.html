Besleria parviflora

Besleria parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered besleria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture.
