Briza lilloi

Briza lilloi, commonly known as Lillo's grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Briza lilloi is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
