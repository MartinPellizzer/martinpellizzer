Begonia balansana var. rubropilosa

Begonia balansana var. rubropilosa, commonly known as the red-haired begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a distinctive red or orange hairy texture along the edges. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
