Bulga Chamaecistus

Bulga chamaecistus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is known for its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. This plant thrives in rocky soils and requires a cool, dry climate to flourish. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique species.
