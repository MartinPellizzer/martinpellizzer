Besleria crenata

Besleria crenata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow and orange flowers. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit and possesses lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag or wavy margin. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Besleria crenata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both greenhouse and outdoor settings.
