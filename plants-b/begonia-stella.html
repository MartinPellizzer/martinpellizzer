Begonia stella

Begonia stella, commonly known as the star begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and shaded outdoor spaces.

Begonia stella is also valued for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
