Bidens schaffneri

Bidens schaffneri, commonly known as Schaffner's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are usually serrated and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
