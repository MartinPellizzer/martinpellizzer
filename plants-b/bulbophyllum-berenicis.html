Bulbophyllum berenicis

Bulbophyllum berenicis, commonly known as the Berenicis orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in forested areas with high humidity. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile soils. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
