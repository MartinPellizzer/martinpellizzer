Briquetia denudata

Briquetia denudata, commonly known as the naked-stemmed bignonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, with yellow petals that attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Briquetia denudata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
