Begonia robustior

Begonia robustior, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often a deep green or reddish hue. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia robustior is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
