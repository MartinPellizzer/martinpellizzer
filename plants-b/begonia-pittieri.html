Begonia pittieri

Begonia pittieri, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, meaning it thrives in humid environments by growing on other plants. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Begonia pittieri is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
