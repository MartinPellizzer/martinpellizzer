Barringtonia oblongifolia

Barringtonia oblongifolia, commonly known as the large-leaf banyan, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, showy white flowers that bloom at night. The tree produces a unique, spherical fruit that splits open to release its seeds. It is often found in coastal areas and thrives in humid, warm climates.

The plant has a fibrous root system and can grow quite large, making it a prominent feature in its natural habitat.
