Boltonia indica

Boltonia indica, commonly known as the Indian tarragon, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, branched stems covered in soft, gray-green foliage. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flower heads that resemble daisies, blooming from late summer to early autumn. Boltonia indica thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
