Bulbostylis oritrephes subsp.

oritrephes, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. It typically grows in wet, marshy habitats such as swamps and seasonally flooded areas.

The plant produces slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It has a short, rhizomatous root system that helps it thrive in waterlogged soils.
