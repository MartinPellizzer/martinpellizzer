Boerhavia glabrata

Boerhavia glabrata, commonly known as smooth blite, is a low-growing annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its stems are often prostrate or sprawling, adapting well to rocky or disturbed soils.

Boerhavia glabrata is often used in traditional medicine and as a leafy vegetable in some cuisines.
