Bothriochloa yunnanensis

Bothriochloa yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Botanically, it is classified within the Poaceae family, which includes grasses and cereal crops.
