Bolusia ervoides

Bolusia ervoides, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming during the summer months. Bolusia ervoides thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
