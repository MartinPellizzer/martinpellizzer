Begonia modestiflora var. sootepensis

Begonia modestiflora var. sootepensis, commonly known as the Sootep begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
