Bouvardia triphylla var. splendens

Bouvardia triphylla var. splendens, commonly known as the splendens bouvardia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has soft, velvety leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Bouvardia triphylla var. splendens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance and long-lasting blooms.
