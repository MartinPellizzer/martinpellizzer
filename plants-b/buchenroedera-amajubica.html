Buchenroedera amajubica

Buchenroedera amajubica, commonly known as the amajubica orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and yellow. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually linear and grass-like, adapting to its arid habitat.

Buchenroedera amajubica thrives in well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
