Begonia tabonensis

Begonia tabonensis, commonly known as the Philippine begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep red or purple in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
