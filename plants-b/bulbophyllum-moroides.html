Bulbophyllum moroides

Bulbophyllum moroides, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking yellow and green floral structure, which resembles a lady's slipper. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a pouch-like lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
