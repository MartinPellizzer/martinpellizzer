Begonia fraseri

Begonia fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in horticulture but is also considered a rare and endangered species in its natural habitat.
