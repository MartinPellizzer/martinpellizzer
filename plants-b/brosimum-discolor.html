Brosimum discolor

Brosimum discolor, commonly known as the white mulberry, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a dense canopy and smooth, grayish bark. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a distinctive white or pale underside. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.
