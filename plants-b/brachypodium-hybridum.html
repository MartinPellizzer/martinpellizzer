Brachypodium hybridum

Brachypodium hybridum, commonly known as false brome, is a grass species native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, blue-green leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It is widely used in ecological restoration projects due to its rapid growth and ability to stabilize soil.

Additionally, Brachypodium hybridum is a model organism in plant genetics research.
