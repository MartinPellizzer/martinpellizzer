Brachiaria regularis var. nidulans

Brachiaria regularis var. nidulans, commonly known as the soft brome, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Brachiaria regularis var. nidulans is valued for its use in forage and erosion control.
