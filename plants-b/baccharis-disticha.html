Baccharis disticha

Baccharis disticha, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Baccharis disticha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
