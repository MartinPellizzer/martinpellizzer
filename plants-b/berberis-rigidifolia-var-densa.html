Berberis rigidifolia var. densa

Berberis rigidifolia var. densa, commonly known as the dense barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, glossy green leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
