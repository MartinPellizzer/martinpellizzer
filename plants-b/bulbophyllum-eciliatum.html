Bulbophyllum eciliatum

Bulbophyllum eciliatum, commonly known as the Eciliatum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant appearance make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
