Begonia triflora var. caloskiadia

Begonia triflora var. caloskiadia, commonly known as the caloskiadia begonia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with wavy margins and a deep green color, often adorned with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming from spring to autumn. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or container cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and delicate, long-lasting blooms.
