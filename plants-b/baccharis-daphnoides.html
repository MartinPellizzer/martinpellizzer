Baccharis daphnoides

Baccharis daphnoides, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in heads. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its aromatic leaves and adaptability make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
