Bulbophyllum perakense

Bulbophyllum perakense, commonly known as the Perak orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

The flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.
