Bupleurum malconense

Bupleurum malconense, commonly known as the Malconese angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

Bupleurum malconense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
