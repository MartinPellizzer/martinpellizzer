Balaka spectabilis

Balaka spectabilis, commonly known as the showy balaka, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Balaka spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual display.
