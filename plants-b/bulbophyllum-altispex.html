Bulbophyllum altispex

Bulbophyllum altispex, commonly known as the high-branching orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence and striking floral structure. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum altispex is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
