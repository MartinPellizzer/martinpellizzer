Barleria stelligera

Barleria stelligera, commonly known as the star barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Barleria stelligera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
