Buddleja × weyeriana

Buddleja × weyeriana, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, drooping panicles of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract a variety of pollinators. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and has dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

The botanical features of Buddleja × weyeriana make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping due to its showy blooms and hardy nature.
