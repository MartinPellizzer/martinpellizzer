Balania harlandii

Balania harlandii, commonly known as the Harland's kaffir lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Balania harlandii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in container gardens.
