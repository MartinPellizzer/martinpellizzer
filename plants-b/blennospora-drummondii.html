Blennospora drummondii

Blennospora drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's blennospora, is a species of grass native to North America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically thrives in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often subtended by a leafy bract.

Blennospora drummondii is notable for its small, wind-pollinated flowers and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
