Begonia lynchiana

Begonia lynchiana, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, silver-veined leaves that give it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
