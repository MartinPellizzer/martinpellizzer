Begonia espiritosantensis

Begonia espiritosantensis, commonly known as the Espirito Santo begonia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial herb, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a waxy surface, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
