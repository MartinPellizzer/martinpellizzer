Boeica ornithocephalantha

Boeica ornithocephalantha, commonly known as the feather-headed orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Boeica ornithocephalantha thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or in dense undergrowth.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
