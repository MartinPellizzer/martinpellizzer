Brachypodium tenue

Brachypodium tenue, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with slender, wiry stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces panicles of tiny, spikelet-like flowers, which are typically green to reddish in color. Brachypodium tenue is often found in disturbed soils and is used in ecological restoration projects.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil conditions make it an important plant in grassland ecosystems.
