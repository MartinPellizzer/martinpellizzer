Begonia sutherlandii var. rubrifolia

Begonia sutherlandii var. rubrifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its green stems and leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among collectors for its unique leaf coloration and ornamental value.
