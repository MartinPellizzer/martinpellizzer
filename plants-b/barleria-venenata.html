Barleria venenata

Barleria venenata, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria venenata is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
