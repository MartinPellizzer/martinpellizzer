Blumea virens

Blumea virens, commonly known as the green blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, making it an attractive ornamental plant.
