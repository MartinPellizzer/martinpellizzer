Bulbophyllum barbellatum

Bulbophyllum barbellatum, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to store water.

Bulbophyllum barbellatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
