Bulbophyllum ligulatum

Bulbophyllum ligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces small, linear leaves. The plant typically blooms with pale yellow to greenish flowers that are arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
