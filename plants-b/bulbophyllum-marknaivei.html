Bulbophyllum marknaivei

Bulbophyllum marknaivei, commonly known as the Marknaive's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique inflorescence and intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in protected areas and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
