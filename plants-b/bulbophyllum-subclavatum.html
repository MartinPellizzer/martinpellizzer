Bulbophyllum subclavatum

Bulbophyllum subclavatum, commonly known as the narrow-clawed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, narrow pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Bulbophyllum subclavatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable growing environment.
