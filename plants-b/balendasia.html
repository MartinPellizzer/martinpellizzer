Balendasia

Balendasia, commonly known as the "devil's backbone," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa and are characterized by their striking, often brightly colored flowers. They are often grown as ornamental plants due to their vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
