Bidens hintonii

Bidens hintonii, commonly known as Hinton's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets, typical of the genus Bidens. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies.
