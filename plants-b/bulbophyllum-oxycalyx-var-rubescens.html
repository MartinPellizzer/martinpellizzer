Bulbophyllum oxycalyx var. rubescens

Bulbophyllum oxycalyx var. rubescens, commonly known as the Red-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid that typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic plant, often found in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water and nutrients. Its flowers are striking, with bright red petals and sepals, and a yellow lip, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

It requires careful attention to light, temperature, and humidity to thrive.
