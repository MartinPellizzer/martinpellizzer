Biarum Tenuifolium Var. Gramineum

Biarum tenuifolium var. gramineum is a rare and delicate plant known for its striking, bell-shaped flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This variant is prized by collectors for its unique foliage and subtle coloration. Its growth is slow, requiring careful attention to soil and light conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate successfully.
