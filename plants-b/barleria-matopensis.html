Barleria matopensis

Barleria matopensis, commonly known as the Natal barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to violet. Barleria matopensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
