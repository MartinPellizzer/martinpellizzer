Barleria holstii

Barleria holstii, commonly known as the silverleaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
