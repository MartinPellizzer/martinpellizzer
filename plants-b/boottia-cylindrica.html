Boottia cylindrica

Boottia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical boottia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Boottia cylindrica is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
