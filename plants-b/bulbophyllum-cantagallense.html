Bulbophyllum cantagallense

Bulbophyllum cantagallense, commonly known as the Cantagallense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence and a variety of flower colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has a complex root system that helps it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.
