Brugmansia × amesiana

Brugmansia × amesiana, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a hybrid species within the genus Brugmansia, native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be white, yellow, or pink, depending on the cultivar. The plant is a fast-growing shrub or small tree with smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators like bees and moths.
