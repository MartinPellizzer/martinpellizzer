Bulbophyllum fasciatum

Bulbophyllum fasciatum, commonly known as the striped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive striped pattern on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum fasciatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
