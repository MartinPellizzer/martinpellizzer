Berberis canadensis

Berberis canadensis, commonly known as Canadian barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves that are typically dark green and oval in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control.
