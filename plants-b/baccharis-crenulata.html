Baccharis crenulata

Baccharis crenulata, commonly known as the silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Baccharis crenulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
