Bauhinia ridleyi

Bauhinia ridleyi, commonly known as the red bauhinia, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Bauhinia ridleyi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
