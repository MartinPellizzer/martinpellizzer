Boehmeria ingjiangensis

Boehmeria ingjiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan nettle or Yunnan flax, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of nettles. The plant is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its fibers are used in traditional crafts and have potential applications in textile production.
