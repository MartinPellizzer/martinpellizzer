Bulbophyllum careyanum var. ochraceum

Bulbophyllum careyanum var. ochraceum, commonly known as the Orange-stemmed Pterostigma, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dark green, pleated leaves and striking orange-red pseudobulbs. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
