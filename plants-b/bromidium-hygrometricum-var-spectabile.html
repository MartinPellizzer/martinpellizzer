Bromidium hygrometricum var. spectabile

Bromidium hygrometricum var. spectabile, commonly known as the showy bromidium, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family native to South America.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in rocky or forested areas. The plant has rosette-shaped leaves that collect moisture, aiding in its survival in arid conditions.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
