Boronia rozefeldsii

Boronia rozefeldsii, commonly known as the Rozefelds boronia, is a flowering plant native to western Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant grows as a shrub, often reaching a height of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Boronia rozefeldsii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and sandy soils.
