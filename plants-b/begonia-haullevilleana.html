Begonia haullevilleana

Begonia haullevilleana, commonly known as the Haulleville begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or purple hue. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Begonia haullevilleana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
