Baccharis tripterix

Baccharis tripterix, commonly known as green baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in spherical heads. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Baccharis tripterix is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
