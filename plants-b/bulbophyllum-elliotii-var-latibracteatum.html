Bulbophyllum elliotii var. latibracteatum

Bulbophyllum elliotii var. latibracteatum, commonly known as the Broad Bract Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered with a layer of brownish bark, while the leaves are narrow and grass-like.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
