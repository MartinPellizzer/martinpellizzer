Begonia payung

Begonia payung, commonly known as the umbrella begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, shield-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and indoor spaces.
