Bulbophyllum digoelense

Bulbophyllum digoelense, commonly known as the Digoel orchid, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its tropical habitat.

Bulbophyllum digoelense is typically found in shaded, humid environments within lowland forests.
