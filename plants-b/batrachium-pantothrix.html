Batrachium pantothrix

Batrachium pantothrix, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
