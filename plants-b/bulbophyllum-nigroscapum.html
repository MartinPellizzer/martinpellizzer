Bulbophyllum nigroscapum

Bulbophyllum nigroscapum, commonly known as the black-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black stems and its clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bulbophyllum nigroscapum is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
