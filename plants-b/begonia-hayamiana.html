Begonia hayamiana

Begonia hayamiana, commonly known as the Japanese begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Japan.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a striking red or orange coloration. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a visually appealing foliage plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its popularity in both indoor and outdoor settings.
