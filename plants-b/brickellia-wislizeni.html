Brickellia wislizeni

Brickellia wislizeni, commonly known as the golden brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in the spring, which are clustered in showy, rounded inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance.

Brickellia wislizeni is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
