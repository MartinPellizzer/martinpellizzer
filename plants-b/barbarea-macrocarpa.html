Barbarea macrocarpa

Barbarea macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional herbal remedies.
