Babiana cinnamomea

Babiana cinnamomea, commonly known as the golden babiana, is a South African flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem with dense clusters of tubular flowers. Native to the grasslands of South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
