Bulbostylis conifera

Bulbostylis conifera, commonly known as the coniferous bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a tendency to curl at the edges.

Bulbostylis conifera is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
