Brossaea purpurascens

Brossaea purpurascens, commonly known as the purple brossaea, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, composite flower heads. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by gardeners interested in native plant species.
