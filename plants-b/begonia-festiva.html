Begonia festiva

Begonia festiva, commonly known as the festival begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a prominent midrib. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia festiva is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and foliage.
