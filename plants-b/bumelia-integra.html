Bumelia integra

Bumelia integra, commonly known as the smooth bumelia, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes plants known for their unique floral structures. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and dark green on top. Its flowers are small and clustered in greenish-yellow cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Bumelia integra is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full shade.
