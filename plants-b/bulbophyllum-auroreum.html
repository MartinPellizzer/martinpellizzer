Bulbophyllum auroreum

Bulbophyllum auroreum, commonly known as the golden bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with numerous bulb-like structures that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
