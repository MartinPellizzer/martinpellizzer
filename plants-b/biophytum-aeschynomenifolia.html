Biophytum aeschynomenifolia

Biophytum aeschynomenifolia, commonly known as the African violet or velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale purple, often clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
