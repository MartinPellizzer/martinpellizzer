Baeckea grandiflora

Baeckea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Baeckea grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
