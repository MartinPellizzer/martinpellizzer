Begonia hoegeana

Begonia hoegeana, commonly known as the African begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners seeking a unique and attractive foliage plant.
