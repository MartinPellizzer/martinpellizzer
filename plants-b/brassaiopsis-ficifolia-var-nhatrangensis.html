Brassaiopsis ficifolia var. nhatrangensis

Brassaiopsis ficifolia var. nhatrangensis, commonly known as the Nha Trang fig-leaf plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable environments.
