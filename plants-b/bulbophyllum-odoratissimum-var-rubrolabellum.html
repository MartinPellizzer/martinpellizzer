Bulbophyllum odoratissimum var. rubrolabellum

Bulbophyllum odoratissimum var. rubrolabellum, commonly known as the Red-lipped Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red lip and yellow-green petals, which make it visually distinctive. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small but numerous, often blooming in clusters during the wet season.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
