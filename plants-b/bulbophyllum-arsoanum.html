Bulbophyllum arsoanum

Bulbophyllum arsoanum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, red and orange inflorescences that resemble flames, making it a visually dramatic plant. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its epiphytic growth habit, often found on trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are typically short-lived but highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts around the world.
