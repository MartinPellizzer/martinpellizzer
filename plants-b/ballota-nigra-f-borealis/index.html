Ballota Nigra F. Borealis

Ballota nigra f. borealis is a lesser-known variant of the common black ballota, native to northern regions with cooler climates. This plant exhibits distinct morphological features that set it apart from its more widespread relatives. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. Despite its limited distribution, it plays a vital role in local ecosystems. Its unique characteristics make it an intriguing subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
