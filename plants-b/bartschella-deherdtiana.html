Bartschella deherdtiana

Bartschella deherdtiana, commonly known as the Cape fynbos, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the region's Mediterranean climate. It has a compact growth habit and produces yellow to orange flowers.

Bartschella deherdtiana is often found in rocky soils and is an important component of the local fynbos vegetation.
