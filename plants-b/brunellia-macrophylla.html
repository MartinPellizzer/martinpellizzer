Brunellia macrophylla

Brunellia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved urn tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, urn-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine and are also valued for their ornamental qualities.
