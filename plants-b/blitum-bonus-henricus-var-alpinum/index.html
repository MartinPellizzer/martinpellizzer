Blitum Bonus-Henricus Var. Alpinum

Blitum bonus-henricus var. alpinum is a rare and striking plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers that bloom in late summer. This variety thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous environments. Its unique coloration and hardy nature make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
