Brachypodium schrenkianum

Brachypodium schrenkianum, commonly known as Schrenk's bromegrass, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and rocky soils.

Due to its hardiness, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
