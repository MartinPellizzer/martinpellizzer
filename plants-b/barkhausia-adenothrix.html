Barkhausia adenothrix

Barkhausia adenothrix, commonly known as the Cape York gum tree, is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to South Africa.

It has a smooth, greyish-brown bark that becomes fissured with age, providing a distinctive appearance. The leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture with a glossy upper surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the winter months.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for coastal and sandy soils.
