Barringtonia acutangula subsp. acutangula

Barringtonia acutangula subsp.

acutangula, commonly known as the Madagascar lantern tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar. It is a large, evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, lantern-like inflorescences that are yellow-green in color and emit a strong, sweet fragrance.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological importance.
