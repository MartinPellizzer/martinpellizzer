Banisteria pubipetala var. virgultosa

Banisteria pubipetala var. virgultosa, commonly known as the red-veined spider plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify indoor air.
