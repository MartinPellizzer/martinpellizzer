Blechnum occidentale f. bifidum

Blechnum occidentale f. bifidum, commonly known as the bifid maidenhair fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is native to regions of Central and South America. The fronds are typically bi-lobed, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil.

This fern is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
