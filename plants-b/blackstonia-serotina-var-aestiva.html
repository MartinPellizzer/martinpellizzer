Blackstonia serotina var. aestiva

Blackstonia serotina var. aestiva, commonly known as the late-flowering blackstonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Polemoniacaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer.
