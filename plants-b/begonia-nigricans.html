Begonia nigricans

Begonia nigricans, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
