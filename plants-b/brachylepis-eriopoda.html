Brachylepis eriopoda

Brachylepis eriopoda, commonly known as the woolly bristlegrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Brachylepis eriopoda is a perennial herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
