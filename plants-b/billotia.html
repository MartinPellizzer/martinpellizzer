Billotia

Billotia, commonly known as the "buttercup plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that often resemble those of the buttercup, with five petals and yellow or white coloration. They play a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in their native habitats.
