Baccharis Tridentata F. Integrifolia

Baccharis tridentata f. integrifolia is a unique variant of the shrub known as the brittle bluestem. This plant is native to the western United States and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are broader and smoother than those of the typical Baccharis tridentata. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its ecological benefits. It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
