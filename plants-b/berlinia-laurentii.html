Berlinia laurentii

Berlinia laurentii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to central and eastern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The tree has compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated pods.

Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought.
