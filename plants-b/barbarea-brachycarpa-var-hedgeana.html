Barbarea brachycarpa var. hedgeana

Barbarea brachycarpa var. hedgeana, commonly known as hedge garlic, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, with a distinctive yellow-orange flower cluster resembling a garlic bulb. The plant produces small, yellow, cruciform flowers that bloom in early spring. Its seeds are enclosed in a three-lobed capsule, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
