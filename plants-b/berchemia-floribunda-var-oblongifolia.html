Berchemia floribunda var. oblongifolia

Berchemia floribunda var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the African bellvista, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bright yellow, spurred flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with oval to oblong leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid conditions.
