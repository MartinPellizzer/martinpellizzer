Bulbophyllum meson

Bulbophyllum meson, commonly known as the Meson orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth during dry periods. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
