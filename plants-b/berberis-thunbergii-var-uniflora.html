Berberis thunbergii var. uniflora

Berberis thunbergii var. uniflora, commonly known as Japanese barberry, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and ecological impact.
