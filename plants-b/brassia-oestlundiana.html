Brassia oestlundiana

Brassia oestlundiana, commonly known as the Oestlund's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, showy flowers with vibrant colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Brassia oestlundiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
