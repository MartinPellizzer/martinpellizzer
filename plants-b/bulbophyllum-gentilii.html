Bulbophyllum gentilii

Bulbophyllum gentilii, commonly known as the Gentil's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance and delicate blooms.
