Barleria velutina

Barleria velutina, commonly known as velvet barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety hair, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from white to pink.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
