Begonia truncatiloba

Begonia truncatiloba, commonly known as the Brazilian begonia, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin. The plant produces showy, pale pink to white flowers with five petals. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Begonia truncatiloba is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
