Begonia lucidissima

Begonia lucidissima, commonly known as the shining begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia lucidissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
