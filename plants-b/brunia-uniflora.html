Brunia uniflora

Brunia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower brunia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Brunia uniflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive floral display.
