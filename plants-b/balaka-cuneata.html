Balaka cuneata

Balaka cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed balaka, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Balaka cuneata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
