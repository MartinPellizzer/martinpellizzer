Beilschmiedia roxburghiana f. brevipetiolata

Beilschmiedia roxburghiana f. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, characterized by aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with short petioles, distinguishing it from the typical form of B. roxburghiana. Its leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and have a distinct aromatic scent.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
