Beaumontia khasiana

Beaumontia khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi beaumontia, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern region of India.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a smooth surface.

Beaumontia khasiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
