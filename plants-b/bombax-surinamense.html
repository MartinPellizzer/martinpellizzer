Bombax surinamense

Bombax surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam cotton tree, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces fluffy, cotton-like seed fibers, which are used in traditional crafts and textiles. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree's rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular species in horticulture.
