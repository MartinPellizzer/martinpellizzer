Bourreria rowellii

Bourreria rowellii, commonly known as the desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in dry conditions.
