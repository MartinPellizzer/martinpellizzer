Baphia orbiculata

Baphia orbiculata, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves and showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Baphia orbiculata also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
