Beccariella chartacea

Beccariella chartacea, commonly known as the paper birch, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are typically white or pale green on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright red or orange berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Beccariella chartacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
