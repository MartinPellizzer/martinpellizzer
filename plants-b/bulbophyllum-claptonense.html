Bulbophyllum claptonense

Bulbophyllum claptonense, commonly known as the Clapton's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful inflorescences. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually fragrant and can bloom multiple times a year under proper care.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
