Bidens urceolata var. urceolata

Bidens urceolata var. urceolata, commonly known as the tickseed sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with yellow flowers that bloom from summer to fall. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and covered in fine white hairs. It produces small, spiky fruits called pappus, which aid in wind dispersal.

This species is often found in open habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils.
