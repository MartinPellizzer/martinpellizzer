Begonia julaihiana

Begonia julaihiana, commonly known as the Julaihiana begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia julaihiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
