Beaumontia campanulata

Beaumontia campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower beaumontia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

Beaumontia campanulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
