Begonia jackiana

Begonia jackiana, commonly known as the Jack's begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
