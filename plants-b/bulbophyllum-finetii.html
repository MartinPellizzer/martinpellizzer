Bulbophyllum finetii

Bulbophyllum finetii, commonly known as the Finet's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structures. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them.

Bulbophyllum finetii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
