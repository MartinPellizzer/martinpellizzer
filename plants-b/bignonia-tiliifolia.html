Bignonia tiliifolia

Bignonia tiliifolia, commonly known as the sweetflag or American sweetflag, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the spring. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
