Bromopsis koeieana

Bromopsis koeieana, commonly known as the Koeie's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the grass family.

Bromopsis koeieana is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ornamental landscaping.
