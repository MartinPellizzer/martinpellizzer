Begonia thelmae

Begonia thelmae, commonly known as the Thelma begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia thelmae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
