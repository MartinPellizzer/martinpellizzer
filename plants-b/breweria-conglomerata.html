Breweria conglomerata

Breweria conglomerata, commonly known as the clustered brewer's grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It is adapted to arid environments and can withstand periods of drought.

Breweria conglomerata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
