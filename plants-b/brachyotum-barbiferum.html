Brachyotum barbiferum

Brachyotum barbiferum, commonly known as the hairy leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white, woolly hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often borne on short, erect stems. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in arid or semi-arid environments.

Brachyotum barbiferum is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water availability.
