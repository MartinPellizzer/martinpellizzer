Bufonia stricta var. hymettia

Bufonia stricta var. hymettia, commonly known as the Hymettian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight hairy texture.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its historical use in traditional medicine.
