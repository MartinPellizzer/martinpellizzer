Bomarea ovata var. cavanillesiana

Bomarea ovata var. cavanillesiana, commonly known as the Cavanilles' bomarea, is a striking flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often climbing or trailing with tendrils. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptical, and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in botanical gardens.
