Bulbophyllum gabunense

Bulbophyllum gabunense, commonly known as the Gabon orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or green with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
