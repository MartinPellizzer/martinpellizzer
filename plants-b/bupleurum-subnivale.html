Bupleurum subnivale

Bupleurum subnivale, commonly known as the snowdrop bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. The plant features slender, wiry stems that bear small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Bupleurum subnivale is adapted to cold climates and is often found in open, sunny habitats at high elevations.
