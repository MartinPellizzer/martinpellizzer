Begonia arnottiana

Begonia arnottiana, commonly known as the Arnold's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia arnottiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
