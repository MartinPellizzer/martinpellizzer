Begonia borneensis

Begonia borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean begonia, is a tropical plant native to the island of Borneo.

It is characterized by its attractive, often vibrant foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or ovate, with a glossy surface and deep green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
