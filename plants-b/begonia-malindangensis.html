Begonia malindangensis

Begonia malindangensis, commonly known as the Malindang begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in montane forests. Its foliage is often marked with intricate patterns and colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
