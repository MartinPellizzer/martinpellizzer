Breweria burchellii

Breweria burchellii, commonly known as the silver leaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features hairy, silver-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. It is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats.

Breweria burchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
