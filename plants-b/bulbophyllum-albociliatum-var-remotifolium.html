Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. remotifolium

Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. remotifolium, commonly known as the remote-leaved white-ciliate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, white flowers that have a distinctive white ciliate margin. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
