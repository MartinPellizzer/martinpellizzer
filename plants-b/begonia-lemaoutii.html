Begonia lemaoutii

Begonia lemaoutii, commonly known as the Lemaout's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia lemaoutii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
