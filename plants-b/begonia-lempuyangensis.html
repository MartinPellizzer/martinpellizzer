Begonia lempuyangensis

Begonia lempuyangensis, commonly known as the Lempuyang begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia lempuyangensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
