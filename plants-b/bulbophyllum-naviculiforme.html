Bulbophyllum naviculiforme

Bulbophyllum naviculiforme, commonly known as the boat-shaped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its unique, boat-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces linear, grass-like leaves and bears small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
