Burragea fruticulosa var. frutescens

Burragea fruticulosa var. frutescens, commonly known as the broad-leaved burragea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, pale green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and inland heathlands.
