Brachystelma shirense

Brachystelma shirense, commonly known as the Shiren's stone orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It features a compact, leafless stem that produces clusters of tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, adding a delicate floral display to its rocky habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil, which is characteristic of its arid environment.

Brachystelma shirense is often found growing on rocky outcrops, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
