Buddleja obtusifolia

Buddleja obtusifolia, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Buddleja obtusifolia is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
