Bulbophyllum miniatum

Bulbophyllum miniatum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
