Barkhausia grandiflora

Barkhausia grandiflora, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy species. Its unique morphology includes long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Barkhausia grandiflora is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
