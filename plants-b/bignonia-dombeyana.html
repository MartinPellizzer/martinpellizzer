Bignonia dombeyana

Bignonia dombeyana, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous climbing vine characterized by its bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
