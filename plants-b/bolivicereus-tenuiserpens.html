Bolivicereus tenuiserpens

Bolivicereus tenuiserpens, commonly known as the slender serpentine cactus, is a species native to Bolivia and northern Argentina.

It is a low-growing, sprawling cactus with cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
