Begonia × spinksii

Begonia × spinksii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pendula, combining traits from both parents. The plant typically produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting veins. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, Begonia × spinksii is widely used in gardens and floral arrangements.
