Bulbophyllum retusiusculum var. tigridum

Bulbophyllum retusiusculum var. tigridum, commonly known as the Tiger Orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tiger-striped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant produces long, slender pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
