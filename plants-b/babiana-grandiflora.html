Babiana grandiflora

Babiana grandiflora, commonly known as the giant babiana, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces large, clustered inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
