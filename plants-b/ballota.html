Ballota

Ballota, commonly known as Spanish borage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features hairy, branched stems and produces blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive, somewhat bitter taste.

Ballota is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
