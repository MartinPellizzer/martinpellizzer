Bichea flavovelutina

Bichea flavovelutina, commonly known as the yellow velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers, which are covered in a velvety texture. The plant has fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid habitat. It grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches.

Bichea flavovelutina is known for its unique floral structure and is a subject of interest in botanical research.
