Brunia albiflora

Brunia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered brunia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, dark green leaves and produces clusters of delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant is part of the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Brunia albiflora is often used in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
