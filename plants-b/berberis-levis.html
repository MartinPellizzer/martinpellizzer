Berberis levis

Berberis levis, commonly known as the smooth barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Berberis levis is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
