Bletia purpurata

Bletia purpurata, commonly known as the purple bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of purple and pink, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bletia purpurata prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shade gardens and greenhouse cultivation.
