Bauhinia splendens var. latifolia

Bauhinia splendens var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, papery flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, often with a contrasting white or yellow base. The plant features bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
