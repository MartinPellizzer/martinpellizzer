Biswarea tonglensis

Biswarea tonglensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lily, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows at elevations between 2,000 and 3,500 meters, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine.

Biswarea tonglensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.
