Briquetina affinis

Briquetina affinis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care.

Briquetina affinis is often grown as a houseplant and is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
