Bontia

Bontia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and similar plants. Bontia species are characterized by their showy, daisy-like flowers with colorful ray florets surrounding a central disk. The plants typically have a low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves.

They are often used in ornamental gardening for their vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil conditions.
