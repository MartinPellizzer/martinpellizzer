Bulbophyllum unguilabium

Bulbophyllum unguilabium, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red lips on the petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, blooming in clusters during the wet season.

The genus Bulbophyllum is known for its diverse and often ornamental species, many of which are popular in horticulture.
