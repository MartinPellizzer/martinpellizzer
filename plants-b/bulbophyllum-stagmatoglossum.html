Bulbophyllum stagmatoglossum

Bulbophyllum stagmatoglossum, commonly known as the Stag's Tongue Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, often with a distinctive, elongated lip. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water and nutrients.

It is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
