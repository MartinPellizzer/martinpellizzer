Begonia guangdongensis

Begonia guangdongensis, commonly known as the Guangdong begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
