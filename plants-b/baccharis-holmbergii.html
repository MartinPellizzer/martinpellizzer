Baccharis holmbergii

Baccharis holmbergii, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are opposite and often have a fuzzy or hairy texture.

Baccharis holmbergii is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
