Bursera fagaroides f. elliptica

Bursera fagaroides f. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic bursera, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes frankincense and myrrh trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

The bark and leaves of this plant are often used in traditional medicine for their aromatic and medicinal properties.
