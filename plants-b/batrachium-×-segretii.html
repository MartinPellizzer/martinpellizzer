Batrachium × segretii

Batrachium × segretii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring. Batrachium × segretii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
