Bulbophyllum rhynchoglossum

Bulbophyllum rhynchoglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a pseudobulb and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
