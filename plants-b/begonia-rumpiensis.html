Begonia rumpiensis

Begonia rumpiensis, commonly known as the Rumpiense Begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy texture. It thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color and texture to shaded garden spaces.
