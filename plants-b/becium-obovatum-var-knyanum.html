Becium obovatum var. knyanum

Becium obovatum var. knyanum, commonly known as the Knyanum becium, is a variant of the becium plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, obovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
