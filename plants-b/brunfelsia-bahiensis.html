Brunfelsia bahiensis

Brunfelsia bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshade plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep blue. It produces berries that change color as they mature, starting green and turning red or purple.

Brunfelsia bahiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
