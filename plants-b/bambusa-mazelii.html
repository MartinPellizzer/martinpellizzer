Bambusa mazelii

Bambusa mazelii, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking golden-yellow culms that contrast beautifully with its dark green leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for smaller garden spaces or container cultivation. Its foliage is lance-shaped and provides a lush, ornamental appearance throughout the growing season.

Bambusa mazelii is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
