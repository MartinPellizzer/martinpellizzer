Batatas heterophylla

Batatas heterophylla, commonly known as the sweet potato vine, is a member of the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant foliage. Batatas heterophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
