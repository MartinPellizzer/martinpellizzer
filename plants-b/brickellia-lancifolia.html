Brickellia lancifolia

Brickellia lancifolia, commonly known as slender brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy yellow blooms. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Brickellia lancifolia is often found in disturbed areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities in native plant gardens.
