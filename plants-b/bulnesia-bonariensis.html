Bulnesia bonariensis

Bulnesia bonariensis, commonly known as the Argentine bulletwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
