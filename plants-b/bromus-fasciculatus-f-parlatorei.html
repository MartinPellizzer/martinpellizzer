Bromus fasciculatus f. parlatorei

Bromus fasciculatus f. parlatorei, commonly known as the Parlatore's brome, is a variant of the slender brome grass.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, elongated leaves. The inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spikelet that is often slightly compressed. This subspecies is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and environmental conditions.
