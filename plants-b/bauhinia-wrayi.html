Bauhinia wrayi

Bauhinia wrayi, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in vibrant orange or red hues. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and are pollinated by insects.

Bauhinia wrayi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
