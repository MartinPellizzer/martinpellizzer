Begonia josephi var. typica

Begonia josephi var. typica, commonly known as the Joseph's begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant green coloration. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among collectors and gardeners seeking unique foliage plants.
