Berkheya maritima

Berkheya maritima, commonly known as the coastal berkheya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in spring. The plant thrives in sandy, coastal environments and is adapted to salt spray and well-drained soils. It is often found in dune habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Berkheya maritima is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
