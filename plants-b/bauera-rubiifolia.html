Bauera rubiifolia

Bauera rubiifolia, commonly known as the red-leaved bauera, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple foliage, which contrasts vividly with its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Bauera rubiifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
