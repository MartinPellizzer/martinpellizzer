Brachystephanus africanus var. recurvatus

Brachystephanus africanus var. recurvatus, commonly known as the African dwarf fern, is a small, terrestrial fern native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that help it spread along the forest floor. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a slightly curled appearance, giving it a unique aesthetic.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland and shrubland habitats.
