Brachyscome lissocarpa

Brachyscome lissocarpa, commonly known as the silverflower, is a low-growing, perennial plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the cooler months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.
