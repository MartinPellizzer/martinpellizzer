Baphia mildbraedii

Baphia mildbraedii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
