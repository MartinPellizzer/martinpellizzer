Brachycyrtis macrantha

Brachycyrtis macrantha, commonly known as the broad-leaved daisy or mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Brachycyrtis macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
