Boerhavia plumbaginea var. socotrana

Boerhavia plumbaginea var. socotrana, commonly known as Socotra's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces striking blue or purple flowers with five petals. The plant has slender stems and small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to the harsh climate of its native habitat.

Its unique floral structure and coloration make it a notable species in the flora of Socotra.
