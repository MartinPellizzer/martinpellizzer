Babingtonia fascifolia

Babingtonia fascifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed babingtonia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tasmania.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as heathlands and rocky areas. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, with a distinctive blue-green coloration.

Babingtonia fascifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal flowering.
