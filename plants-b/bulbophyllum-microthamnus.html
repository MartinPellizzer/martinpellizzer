Bulbophyllum microthamnus

Bulbophyllum microthamnus, commonly known as the small thorned orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, often in humid tropical environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of numerous tiny flowers clustered along a stem.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires specific care to thrive in cultivation.
