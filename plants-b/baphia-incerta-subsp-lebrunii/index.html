Baphia Incerta Subsp. Lebrunii

Baphia incerta subsp. lebrunii is a lesser-known plant species native to the tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the Leguminosae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities and ecological importance. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and vibrant floral displays. Despite its botanical significance, it remains under-researched and often overlooked in horticultural circles. Understanding its biology and habitat can contribute to conservation efforts and botanical diversity studies.
