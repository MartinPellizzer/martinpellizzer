Brachystelma micranthum

Brachystelma micranthum, commonly known as the dwarf starling orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant features compact, succulent-like stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it is adapted to survive in arid or semi-arid conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
