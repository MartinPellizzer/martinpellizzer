Belamcanda chinensis var. curtata

Belamcanda chinensis var. curtata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yellow flag, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its sword-shaped leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands.
