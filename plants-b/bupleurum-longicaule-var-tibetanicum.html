Bupleurum longicaule var. tibetanicum

Bupleurum longicaule var. tibetanicum, commonly known as Tibetan angelica, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Tibet.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 3,000 and 5,000 meters. The flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, appear in clusters and are followed by small, dry fruits.
