Bulbophyllum elisae

Bulbophyllum elisae, commonly known as the Elisae's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms that are clustered in inflorescences. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the plant's foliage and flowers.

Bulbophyllum elisae prefers bright, indirect light and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
