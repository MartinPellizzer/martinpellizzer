Bulbophyllum transarisanense

Bulbophyllum transarisanense, commonly known as the Transarisan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
