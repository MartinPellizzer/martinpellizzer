Baeometra breyniana

Baeometra breyniana, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are small and scale-like, adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry environments.

Baeometra breyniana is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to xerophytic conditions.
