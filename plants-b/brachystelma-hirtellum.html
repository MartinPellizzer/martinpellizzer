Brachystelma hirtellum

Brachystelma hirtellum, commonly known as the woolly stone orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
