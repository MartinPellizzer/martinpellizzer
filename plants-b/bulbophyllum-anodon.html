Bulbophyllum anodon

Bulbophyllum anodon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces striking, elongated flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. Its bulb-like structures, which store nutrients, are a key feature of its morphology.

Bulbophyllum anodon is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
