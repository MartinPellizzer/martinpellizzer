Barleria jasminiflora

Barleria jasminiflora, commonly known as the jasmine barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers resembling jasmine. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its sweet scent and vibrant floral display.
