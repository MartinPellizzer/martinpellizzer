Baissea minutiflora

Baissea minutiflora, commonly known as the tiny-flowered baissea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a tendency to form dense mats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
