Blastemanthus densiflorus

Blastemanthus densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered blastemanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
