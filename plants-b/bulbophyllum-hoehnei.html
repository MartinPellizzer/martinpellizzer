Bulbophyllum hoehnei

Bulbophyllum hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that are often pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal conditions.

Bulbophyllum hoehnei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
