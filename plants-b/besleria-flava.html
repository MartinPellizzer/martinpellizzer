Besleria flava

Besleria flava, commonly known as the golden besleria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Besleria flava is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
