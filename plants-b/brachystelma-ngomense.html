Brachystelma ngomense

Brachystelma ngomense, commonly known as the Ngomene stone orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and has adapted to arid environments with its succulent-like leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive structure that aids in pollination by insects.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
