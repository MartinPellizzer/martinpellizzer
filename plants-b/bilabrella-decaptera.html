Bilabrella decaptera

Bilabrella decaptera, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white and green, adding to its ornamental value.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex inflorescence and specialized pollination mechanisms.
