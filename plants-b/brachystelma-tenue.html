Brachystelma tenue

Brachystelma tenue, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, fleshy leaves that help store water. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pink to purple flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its adaptability and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid gardens.
