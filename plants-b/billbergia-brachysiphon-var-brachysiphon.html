Billbergia brachysiphon var. brachysiphon

Billbergia brachysiphon var. brachysiphon, commonly known as the short-siphon billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that form a central water-holding cup. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom sequentially. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
