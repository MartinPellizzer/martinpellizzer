Bulbophyllum uniflorum var. pluriflorum

Bulbophyllum uniflorum var. pluriflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces multiple small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
