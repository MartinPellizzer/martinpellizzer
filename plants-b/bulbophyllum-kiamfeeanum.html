Bulbophyllum kiamfeeanum

Bulbophyllum kiamfeeanum, commonly known as the Kiamfeean orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in conditions with seasonal dry periods.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.
