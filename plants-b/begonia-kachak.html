Begonia kachak

Begonia kachak, commonly known as the Kachak begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to purplish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia kachak is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
