Bulbophyllum kusukusense

Bulbophyllum kusukusense, commonly known as the Kusukusen orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts.
