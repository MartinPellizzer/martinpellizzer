Begonia sagaingensis

Begonia sagaingensis, commonly known as the Sagaing begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Myanmar.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its botanical features include opposite leaves and showy inflorescences that attract pollinators.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
