Bactris elegans

Bactris elegans, commonly known as the elegant palm, is a species of palm native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to 5 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, round fruits with a sweet, fibrous pulp. Bactris elegans thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in wetland or swampy areas.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and for the nutritional value of its fruits.
