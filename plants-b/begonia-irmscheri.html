Begonia irmscheri

Begonia irmscheri, commonly known as the Irmscher's begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant patterns of red, green, and white. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage and attractive flowers, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
