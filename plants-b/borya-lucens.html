Borya lucens

Borya lucens, commonly known as the shining borya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the plant is often found in alpine meadows.

Borya lucens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
