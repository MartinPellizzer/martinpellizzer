Brachyris euthamiae

Brachyris euthamiae, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has silver-gray foliage with dense, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss. It produces small, white to pinkish flowers in late spring to early summer.

Brachyris euthamiae is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low moisture conditions.
