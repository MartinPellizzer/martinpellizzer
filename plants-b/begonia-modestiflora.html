Begonia modestiflora

Begonia modestiflora, commonly known as the dwarf begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in container gardening.

Begonia modestiflora is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
