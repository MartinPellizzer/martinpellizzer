Bulbostylis trabeculata

Bulbostylis trabeculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a species of grass found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, spikelet-like flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often in wetlands or along water margins.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
