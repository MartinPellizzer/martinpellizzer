Barbacenia nana

Barbacenia nana, commonly known as the dwarf barbacenia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators. Barbacenia nana prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for container gardening and woodland settings.
