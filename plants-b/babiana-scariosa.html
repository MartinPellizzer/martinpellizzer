Babiana scariosa

Babiana scariosa, commonly known as the fireball lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spherical orange-red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow stamens with prominent anthers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Babiana scariosa is popular in gardens for its vibrant display and is relatively easy to cultivate.
