Bromus madritensis var. delilei

Bromus madritensis var. delilei, commonly known as the Delile's brome, is a grass species native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, fields, and gravelly soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while the flowers are usually pale green to brown in color.

This variety is often found in Mediterranean climates and is adapted to dry, temperate conditions.
