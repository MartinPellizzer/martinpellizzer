Bridelia harmandii

Bridelia harmandii, commonly known as the African starfruit tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fleshy, star-shaped fruits that are edible.
