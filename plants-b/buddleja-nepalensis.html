Buddleja nepalensis

Buddleja nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features slender, arching branches and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Buddleja nepalensis is also noted for its adaptability and resilience to various environmental conditions.
