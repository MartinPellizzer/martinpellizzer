Begonia malmquistiana

Begonia malmquistiana, commonly known as the Malmquist begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish or purple underside. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect light to prevent leaf scorch.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouse cultivation.
