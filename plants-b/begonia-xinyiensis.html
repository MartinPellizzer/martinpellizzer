Begonia xinyiensis

Begonia xinyiensis, commonly known as the Xinyi begonia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can vary in color from green to purple, depending on the variety. Its flowers are usually bright red or orange, with five petals and a distinctive spurred shape.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and blooms.
