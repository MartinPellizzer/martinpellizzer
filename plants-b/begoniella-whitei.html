Begoniella whitei

Begoniella whitei, commonly known as the White's begoniella, is a unique species within the Begoniaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, Begoniella whitei is considered a rare and sought-after plant among horticulturists.
