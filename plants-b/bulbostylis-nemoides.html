Bulbostylis nemoides

Bulbostylis nemoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters at the base of the plant.
