Banisteria fulgens

Banisteria fulgens, commonly known as the flame bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is known for its showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Banisteria fulgens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
