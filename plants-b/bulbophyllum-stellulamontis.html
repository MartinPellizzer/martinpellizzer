Bulbophyllum stellulamontis

Bulbophyllum stellulamontis, commonly known as the star-like bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
