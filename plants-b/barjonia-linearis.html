Barjonia linearis

Barjonia linearis, commonly known as the linear barjonia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and it blooms from spring to early summer.

Barjonia linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
