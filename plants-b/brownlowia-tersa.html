Brownlowia tersa

Brownlowia tersa, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brownlowia tersa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
