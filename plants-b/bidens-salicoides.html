Bidens salicoides

Bidens salicoides, commonly known as the willowleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, willow-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disk. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along stream banks.

Bidens salicoides is known for its ability to tolerate waterlogged soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
