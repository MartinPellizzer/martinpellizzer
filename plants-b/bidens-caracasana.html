Bidens caracasana

Bidens caracasana, commonly known as the scarlet globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers with prominent black centers, and its leaves are usually lobed and hairy. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats such as roadsides and open fields.

Bidens caracasana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability.
