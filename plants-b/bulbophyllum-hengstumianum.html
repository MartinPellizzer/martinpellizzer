Bulbophyllum hengstumianum

Bulbophyllum hengstumianum, commonly known as the Hengstumian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
