Bignonia craterophora var. acutifolia

Bignonia craterophora var. acutifolia, commonly known as the sharpleaf bignonia, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the Caribbean.

It is a member of the Bignoniaceae family, which includes many ornamental and woody plants. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets and produces showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
