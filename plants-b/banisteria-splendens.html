Banisteria splendens

Banisteria splendens, commonly known as the flame bean, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom along its slender stems. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Banisteria splendens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
