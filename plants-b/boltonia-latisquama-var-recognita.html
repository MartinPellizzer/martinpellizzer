Boltonia latisquama var. recognita

Boltonia latisquama var. recognita, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Boltonia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy, daisy-like flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, producing white to pale pink ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets.

Boltonia latisquama var. recognita is often found in moist, open woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
