Begonia fonsecae

Begonia fonsecae, commonly known as the Fonseca begonia, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia fonsecae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
