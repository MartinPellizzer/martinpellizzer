Bulbophyllum stolleanum

Bulbophyllum stolleanum, commonly known as the Stolleanum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water and nutrients for periods of drought.

The orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
