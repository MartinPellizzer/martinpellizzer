Brachystegia trijuga

Brachystegia trijuga, commonly known as the African teak, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Bombacaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open woodlands.
