Bulbophyllum oligoblepharon

Bulbophyllum oligoblepharon, commonly known as the few-eyed bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of numerous small, rounded pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
