Bactris circularis

Bactris circularis, commonly known as the pink pineapple, is a species of palm native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pinkish-red fruit, which is typically harvested when fully mature. The plant has a robust trunk and produces leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its fruit contains a high concentration of nutrients and is valued for its unique flavor.

Bactris circularis is also appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated in various tropical regions.
