Bouchea spathulata var. longiflora

Bouchea spathulata var. longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered bouchea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features showy, clustered inflorescences of bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are dark green and slightly serrated. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Bouchea spathulata var. longiflora is well-suited for rock gardens, borders, or as a specimen plant in arid landscapes.
