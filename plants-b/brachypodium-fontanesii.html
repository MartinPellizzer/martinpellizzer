Brachypodium fontanesii

Brachypodium fontanesii, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
