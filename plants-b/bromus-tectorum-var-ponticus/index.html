Bromus Tectorum Var. Ponticus

Tall brome, scientifically known as Bromus tectorum var. ponticus, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in dry grasslands and disturbed areas. Due to its aggressive growth, it can outcompete native vegetation and is considered an invasive species in many regions. Its adaptability and rapid reproduction make it a significant ecological concern.
