Bulbophyllum aureobrunneum

Bulbophyllum aureobrunneum, commonly known as the golden-brown orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, yellowish-brown flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
