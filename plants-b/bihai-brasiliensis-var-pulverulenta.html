Bihai brasiliensis var. pulverulenta

Bihai brasiliensis var. pulverulenta, commonly known as the Powdered Bihai, is a rare and striking variant of the Bihai plant.

It is characterized by its unique, powdery white coating on the leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Cannabaceae family and is native to certain regions of South America. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
