Brachycorythis hispidula

Brachycorythis hispidula, commonly known as the hairy short-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a short, stocky stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are hairy, giving it the common name, and it blooms during the late summer and early autumn.

This orchid is known for its resilience in arid environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
