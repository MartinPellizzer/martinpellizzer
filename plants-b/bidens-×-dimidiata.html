Bidens × dimidiata

Bidens × dimidiata, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features upright stems covered in fine, sticky hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets and a central disk, blooming from late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Bidens × dimidiata is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
