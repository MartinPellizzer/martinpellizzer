Berberis cliffortioides

Berberis cliffortioides, commonly known as the cliffortioides barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red berries in the fall. The plant has thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant seasonal changes and ability to attract wildlife.
