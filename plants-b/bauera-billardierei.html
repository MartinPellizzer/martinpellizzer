Bauera billardierei

Bauera billardierei, commonly known as the bluebells, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense, low-growing mat. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

Bauera billardierei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
