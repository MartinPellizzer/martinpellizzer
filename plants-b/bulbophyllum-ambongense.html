Bulbophyllum ambongense

Bulbophyllum ambongense, commonly known as the Ambongense orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a waxy texture, which helps in moisture retention.

Bulbophyllum ambongense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
