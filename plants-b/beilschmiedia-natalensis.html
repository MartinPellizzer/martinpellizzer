Beilschmiedia natalensis

Beilschmiedia natalensis, commonly known as Natal wax tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
