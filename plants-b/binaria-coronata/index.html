Binaria Coronata

Coral tree, scientifically known as Binaria coronata, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is renowned for its vibrant, flame-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm seasons. This tree can grow up to 10 meters tall, making it a prominent feature in both natural and cultivated landscapes. Its unique appearance has made it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and urban landscaping. Despite its beauty, the Coral tree requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
