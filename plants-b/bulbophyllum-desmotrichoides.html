Bulbophyllum desmotrichoides

Bulbophyllum desmotrichoides, commonly known as the hairy-stalked orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a rhizome with numerous bulb-like structures that store nutrients and water. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
