Besleria cuneata

Besleria cuneata, commonly known as the purple orchid or the violet orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Besleria cuneata is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
