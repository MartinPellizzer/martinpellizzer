Bulbophyllum dayanum

Bulbophyllum dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of yellow, green, and red hues. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall.

Bulbophyllum dayanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
