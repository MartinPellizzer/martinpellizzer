Brownleea macroceras

Brownleea macroceras, commonly known as the giant sand mat, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain water in its harsh environment. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in sandy soils and access deep moisture sources.

Brownleea macroceras plays a vital role in stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
