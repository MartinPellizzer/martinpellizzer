Beaucarnea tuberculata

Beaucarnea tuberculata, commonly known as the ponytail palm, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a swollen, bottle-shaped stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant has a rosette of fleshy, light green leaves that resemble a ponytail, giving it its common name. It produces yellowish-white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
