Bromus gaudinii

Bromus gaudinii, commonly known as the Gaudin's brome, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Bromus gaudinii is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
