Bilabrella falcicornis

Bilabrella falcicornis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bilabrella, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its growth habit is often prostrate or creeping, allowing it to spread across the ground.

The plant produces small, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, facilitating wind dispersal.
