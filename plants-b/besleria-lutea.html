Besleria lutea

Besleria lutea, commonly known as the golden besleria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow bracts that enclose the inflorescence, giving it a vibrant and showy appearance. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a heart-shaped base.

Besleria lutea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching floral display.
