Brachiaria plantaginea

Brachiaria plantaginea, commonly known as flatleaf grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, narrow leaves and tufted growth habit. This plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Brachiaria plantaginea is known for its adaptability and is sometimes used in forage and erosion control.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
