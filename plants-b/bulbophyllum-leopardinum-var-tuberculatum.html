Bulbophyllum leopardinum var. tuberculatum

Bulbophyllum leopardinum var. tuberculatum, commonly known as the Leopard Orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, leopard-patterned pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of colorful flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The variety tuberculatum is distinguished by its distinctive tuberculate (knob-like) structures on the pseudobulbs.
