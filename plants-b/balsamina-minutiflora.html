Balsamina minutiflora

Balsamina minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf balsam, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Balsamina minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
