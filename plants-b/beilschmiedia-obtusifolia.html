Beilschmiedia obtusifolia

Beilschmiedia obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange-red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense tropical forests.

Beilschmiedia obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
