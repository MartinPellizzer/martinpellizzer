Balkana

Balkana, commonly known as the Balkan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
