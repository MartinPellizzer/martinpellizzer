Basanacantha altiscandens

Basanacantha altiscandens, commonly known as the tall spurge.

It is a perennial herb native to arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features upright stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, covered in soft, woolly hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a bluish-green hue.

It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, followed by bead-like seed capsules that disperse easily in the wind.
