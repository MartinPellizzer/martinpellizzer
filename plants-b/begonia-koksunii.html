Begonia koksunii

Begonia koksunii, commonly known as the Koksunii begonia, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deeply lobed, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun, depending on the climate.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
