Boldu

Boldu, commonly known as *Plectranthus barbatus*, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and basil. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Boldu is often cultivated for its fragrant leaves, which are used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
