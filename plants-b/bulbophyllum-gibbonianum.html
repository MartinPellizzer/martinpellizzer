Bulbophyllum gibbonianum

Bulbophyllum gibbonianum, commonly known as the Gibbonian Pterostigma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with contrasting markings. It is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and requires high humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

The genus Bulbophyllum is known for its diverse array of species, many of which are prized for their ornamental value.
