Biota delicatissima

Biota delicatissima, commonly known as the delicate beauty, is a rare and elusive plant species native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that shimmer with a faint bioluminescent glow under moonlight. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pale pink and white, which bloom only during the night. Its roots are highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it an indicator species for ecological health.

Due to its rarity and unique botanical features, Biota delicatissima is considered a subject of scientific interest and conservation concern.
