Begonia sageaensis

Begonia sageaensis, commonly known as the Sagesaense Begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range from dark green to reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
