Billardiera drummondiana

Billardiera drummondiana, commonly known as the slender flame vine, is a climbing shrub native to southern Australia.

It features slender, twining stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a red or orange throat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
