Breweria africana

Breweria africana, commonly known as the African brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Breweria africana is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
