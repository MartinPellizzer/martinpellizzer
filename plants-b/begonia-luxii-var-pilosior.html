Begonia luxii var. pilosior

Begonia luxii var. pilosior, commonly known as the velvety begonia, is a striking hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
