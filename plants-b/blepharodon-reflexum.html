Blepharodon reflexum

Blepharodon reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, reflexed petals that give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually violet or blue in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Blepharodon reflexum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
