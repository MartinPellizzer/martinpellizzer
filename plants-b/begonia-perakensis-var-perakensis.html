Begonia perakensis var. perakensis

Begonia perakensis var. perakensis, commonly known as the Perak begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive tubular shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
