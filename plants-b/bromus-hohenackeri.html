Bromus hohenackeri

Bromus hohenackeri, commonly known as Hohenacker's brome, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikelets that are often clustered at the ends of its stems. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.

Bromus hohenackeri is often found in association with other grasses and forage plants in its native range.
