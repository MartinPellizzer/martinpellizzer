Bowiea myriacantha

Bowiea myriacantha, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, paddle-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, typically blooming at night. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release bright red seeds.

Bowiea myriacantha is adapted to arid environments and can store water in its thick, fleshy leaves.
