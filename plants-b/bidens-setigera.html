Bidens setigera

Bidens setigera, commonly known as the zigzag groundcherry, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Bidens setigera is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
