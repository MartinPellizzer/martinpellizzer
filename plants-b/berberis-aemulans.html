Berberis aemulans

Berberis aemulans, commonly known as the Mexican barberry, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features thorny stems and small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often sour. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
