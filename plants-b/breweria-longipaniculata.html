Breweria longipaniculata

Breweria longipaniculata, commonly known as the long panicle breweria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, elongated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It is native to parts of South America and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Breweria longipaniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
