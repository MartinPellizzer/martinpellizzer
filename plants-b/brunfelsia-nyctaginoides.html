Brunfelsia nyctaginoides

Brunfelsia nyctaginoides, commonly known as the nightshade bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often opening in the evening. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is also known for its fragrant blossoms and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
