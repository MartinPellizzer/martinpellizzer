Bulbophyllum afzelii var. microdoron

Bulbophyllum afzelii var. microdoron, commonly known as the miniature bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
