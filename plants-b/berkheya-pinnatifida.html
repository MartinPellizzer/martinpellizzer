Berkheya pinnatifida

Berkheya pinnatifida, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Berkheya pinnatifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
