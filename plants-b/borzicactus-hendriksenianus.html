Borzicactus hendriksenianus

Borzicactus hendriksenianus, commonly known as the Hendriksen's borzicactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its otherwise muted appearance.
