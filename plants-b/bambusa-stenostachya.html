Bambusa stenostachya

Bambusa stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed bamboo, is a species of grass in the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like China, India, and Indonesia. This bamboo is characterized by its slender, often hollow stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and is also used in traditional construction and crafts.
