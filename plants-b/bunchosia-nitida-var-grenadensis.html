Bunchosia nitida var. grenadensis

Bunchosia nitida var. grenadensis, commonly known as the Grenada hibiscus, is a shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly Grenada.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its lanceolate, hairy leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
