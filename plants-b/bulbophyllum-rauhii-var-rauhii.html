Bulbophyllum rauhii var. rauhii

Bulbophyllum rauhii var. rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful inflorescences. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually borne on erect, branching stems and can last for several weeks.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
