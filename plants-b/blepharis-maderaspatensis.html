Blepharis maderaspatensis

Blepharis maderaspatensis, commonly known as the Madras grass or Indian grass, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
