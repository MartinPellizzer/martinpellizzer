Berberis philippinensis

Berberis philippinensis, commonly known as Philippine barberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often sour. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
