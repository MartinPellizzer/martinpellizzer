Borinda fungosa

Borinda fungosa, commonly known as the fungal borinda, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant's most distinctive feature is its association with fungi, as it forms a mycorrhizal relationship with certain soil fungi, enhancing its nutrient absorption. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Borinda fungosa is often found in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests.
