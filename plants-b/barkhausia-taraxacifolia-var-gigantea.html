Barkhausia taraxacifolia var. gigantea

Barkhausia taraxacifolia var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant dandelion, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, bright yellow composite flowers and deeply lobed, dandelion-like leaves. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners.

However, it is considered invasive in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively.
