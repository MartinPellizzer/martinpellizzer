Bunchosia plowmanii

Bunchosia plowmanii, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in humid, mountainous environments and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Bunchosia plowmanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
