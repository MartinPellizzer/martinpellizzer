Brunfelsia hopeana var. macrocalyx

Brunfelsia hopeana var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant Brunfelsia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
