Bulbophyllum scariosum

Bulbophyllum scariosum, commonly known as the scarious bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and narrow, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
