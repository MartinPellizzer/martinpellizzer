Bulbostylis capillaris subsp. capitata

Bulbostylis capillaris subsp.

capitata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, often inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a dense, compact growth habit.
