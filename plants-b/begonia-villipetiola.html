Begonia villipetiola

Begonia villipetiola, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
