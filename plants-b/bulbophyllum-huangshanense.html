Bulbophyllum huangshanense

Bulbophyllum huangshanense, commonly known as the Huangshan orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers with a complex morphology, often featuring a spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments on mountainous cliffs and trees.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
