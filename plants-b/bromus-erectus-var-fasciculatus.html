Bromus erectus var. fasciculatus

Bromus erectus var. fasciculatus, commonly known as the clustered brome, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces spikelets that are arranged in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Bromus erectus var. fasciculatus is often found in temperate climates and is adapted to a range of soil types.
