Begonia quadrialata var. speciosa

Begonia quadrialata var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with red, orange, or yellow markings. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
