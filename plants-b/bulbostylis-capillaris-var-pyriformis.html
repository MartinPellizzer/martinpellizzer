Bulbostylis capillaris var. pyriformis

Bulbostylis capillaris var. pyriformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically at the base of the plant. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a relatively uncommon and specialized species within its native range.
