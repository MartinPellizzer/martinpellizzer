Bulbophyllum macrostachyum

Bulbophyllum macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular among horticultural enthusiasts.
