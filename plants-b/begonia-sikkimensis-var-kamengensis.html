Begonia sikkimensis var. kamengensis

Begonia sikkimensis var. kamengensis, commonly known as the Kameng begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that range from green to dark purple in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
