Billardiera bignoniacea

Billardiera bignoniacea, commonly known as the sweet pea vine, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Billardiera bignoniacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
