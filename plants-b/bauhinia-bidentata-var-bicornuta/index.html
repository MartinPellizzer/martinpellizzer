Bauhinia Bidentata Var. Bicornuta

Bauhinia bidentata var. bicornuta is a striking legume native to parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique, bipartite leaves and vibrant pink flowers, this plant is a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils. The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native environments.
