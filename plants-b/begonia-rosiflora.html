Begonia rosiflora

Begonia rosiflora, commonly known as the roseleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, rose-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia rosiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
