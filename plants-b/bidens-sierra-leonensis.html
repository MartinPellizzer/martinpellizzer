Bidens sierra-leonensis

Bidens sierra-leonensis, commonly known as Sierra Leone ironweed, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Bidens sierra-leonensis is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as riverbanks and woodland edges.
