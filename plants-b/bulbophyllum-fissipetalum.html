Bulbophyllum fissipetalum

Bulbophyllum fissipetalum, commonly known as the Split-Petalled Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique floral morphology and ability to bloom multiple times a year make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The species is also noted for its relatively easy care requirements, making it suitable for both novice and experienced growers.
