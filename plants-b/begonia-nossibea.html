Begonia nossibea

Begonia nossibea, commonly known as the Nossi Island begonia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep green or reddish-brown in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming during the dry season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
