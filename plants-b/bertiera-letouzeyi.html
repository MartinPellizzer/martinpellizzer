Bertiera letouzeyi

Bertiera letouzeyi, commonly known as the African sandbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, oval-shaped leaves. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Bertiera letouzeyi plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and animals.
