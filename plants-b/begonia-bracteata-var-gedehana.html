Begonia bracteata var. gedehana

Begonia bracteata var. gedehana, commonly known as the Gedehana begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among horticulturists for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
