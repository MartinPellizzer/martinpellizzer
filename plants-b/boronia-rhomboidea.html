Boronia rhomboidea

Boronia rhomboidea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf boronia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces fragrant, white or cream-colored flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Boronia rhomboidea is often found in heathlands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
