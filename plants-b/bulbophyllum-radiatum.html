Bulbophyllum radiatum

Bulbophyllum radiatum, commonly known as the Radiant Lipstick Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble lipstick, with a bright red or orange hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are borne on slender stems and are often fragrant, attracting various pollinators.

Bulbophyllum radiatum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
