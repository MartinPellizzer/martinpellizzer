Bromus japonicus var. rapaicsii

Bromus japonicus var. rapaicsii, commonly known as Japanese brome, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often found in disturbed areas, meadows, and along roadsides.
