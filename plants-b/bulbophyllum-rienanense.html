Bulbophyllum rienanense

Bulbophyllum rienanense, commonly known as the Rienanese Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth and distinctive, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal or dry environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
