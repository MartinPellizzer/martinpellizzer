Berberis taoensis

Berberis taoensis, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

The berries are edible but should be consumed in moderation.
