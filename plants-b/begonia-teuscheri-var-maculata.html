Begonia teuscheri var. maculata

Begonia teuscheri var. maculata, commonly known as the spotted begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red and yellow spots, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in both home and garden settings.
