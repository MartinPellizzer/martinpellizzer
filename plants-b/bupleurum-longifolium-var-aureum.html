Bupleurum longifolium var. aureum

Bupleurum longifolium var. aureum, commonly known as golden thread, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in racemes. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The varietal name 'aureum' refers to the distinctive golden hue of its leaves.
