Bletia uniflora

Bletia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower bletia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its flowers are small and white or pale green.

Bletia uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
