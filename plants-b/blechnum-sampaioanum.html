Blechnum sampaioanum

Blechnum sampaioanum, commonly known as the Sampaio's holly fern, is a species of fern native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a dense arrangement of small, lanceolate pinnae.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
