Buena magnifolia

Buena magnifolia, commonly known as the splendid beauty tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value, Buena magnifolia is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
