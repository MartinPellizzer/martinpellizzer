Blumea obliqua var. aurita

Blumea obliqua var. aurita, commonly known as the golden blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
