Briza media var. pallescens

Briza media var. pallescens, commonly known as the silver false oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, silver-gray blades that are often lined with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its inflorescence consists of a loose, panicle-like cluster of small, pale green to brownish flowers.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate, grass-like foliage and ability to tolerate a variety of environmental conditions.
