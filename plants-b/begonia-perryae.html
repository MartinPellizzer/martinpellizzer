Begonia perryae

Begonia perryae, commonly known as Perry's begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This begonia is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to flourish in a variety of indoor and outdoor settings.
