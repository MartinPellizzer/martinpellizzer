Bidens × multiceps

Bidens × multiceps, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has finely divided, serrated leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in disturbed areas.

Bidens × multiceps is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
