Barosma lanceolata

Barosma lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barosma, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Barosma lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
