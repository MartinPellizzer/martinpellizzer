Biophytum heinrichsae

Biophytum heinrichsae, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, silver-white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a succulent, fleshy stem and leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
