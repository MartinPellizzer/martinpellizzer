Begonia × rex-discolor

Begonia × rex-discolor, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a hybrid plant prized for its striking, velvety foliage.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with deep lobes and a rich, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically small and tubular, appearing in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
