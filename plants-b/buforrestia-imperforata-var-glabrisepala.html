Buforrestia imperforata var. glabrisepala

Buforrestia imperforata var. glabrisepala, commonly known as the smooth-sepal buforrestia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface. The varietal name "glabrisepala" refers to its smooth, hairless sepals, which distinguish it from other varieties.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
