Begonia plagioneura

Begonia plagioneura, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the margins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in both its foliage and its delicate floral display.
