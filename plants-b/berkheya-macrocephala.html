Berkheya macrocephala

Berkheya macrocephala, commonly known as the giant yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by white ray florets. The plant has a robust, upright stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
