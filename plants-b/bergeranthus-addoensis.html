Bergeranthus addoensis

Bergeranthus addoensis, commonly known as the Addo daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in the Addo Elephant National Park, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

Bergeranthus addoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
