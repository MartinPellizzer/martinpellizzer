Brachypodium macropodum

Brachypodium macropodum, commonly known as giant false brome, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and rough texture.

It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
