Bulbophyllum papulipetalum

Bulbophyllum papulipetalum, commonly known as the papery-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, papery-like petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and often clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
