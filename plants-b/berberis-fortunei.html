Berberis fortunei

Berberis fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thick, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Berberis fortunei prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
