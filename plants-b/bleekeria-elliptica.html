Bleekeria elliptica

Bleekeria elliptica, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The leaves are elliptical and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow.

The plant is valued for its unique appearance and ability to survive in harsh conditions.
