Bignonia chinensis

Bignonia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese crossvine, is a climbing plant native to China.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its long, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color. These flowers bloom in the summer and are followed by elongated, cylindrical seed pods.

Bignonia chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
