Begonia bonus-henricus

Begonia bonus-henricus, commonly known as the Christmas Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative displays.
