Bulbophyllum macphersonii var. macphersonii

Bulbophyllum macphersonii var. macphersonii, commonly known as the Macpherson's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile conditions. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate markings, and it blooms during the rainy season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
