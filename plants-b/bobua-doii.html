Bobua doii

Bobua doii, commonly known as the golden bell tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling small bells. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Bobua doii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
