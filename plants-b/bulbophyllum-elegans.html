Bulbophyllum elegans

Bulbophyllum elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers with a complex, often fragrant structure. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in survival in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum elegans is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
