Bulbophyllum macrocoleum

Bulbophyllum macrocoleum, commonly known as the large-stalked orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
