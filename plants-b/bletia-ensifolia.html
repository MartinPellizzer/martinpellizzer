Bletia ensifolia

Bletia ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf bletia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually appearing in shades of white, pink, or purple.

Bletia ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
