Bulbophyllum contortum

Bulbophyllum contortum, commonly known as the twisted bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, twisted pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
