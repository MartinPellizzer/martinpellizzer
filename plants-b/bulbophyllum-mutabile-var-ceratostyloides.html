Bulbophyllum mutabile var. ceratostyloides

Bulbophyllum mutabile var. ceratostyloides, commonly known as the horned-stem orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive horn-like spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
