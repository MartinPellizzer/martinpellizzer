Biscutella auriculata var. balearica

Biscutella auriculata var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic biscuit plant, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought conditions.
