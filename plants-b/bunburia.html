Bunburia

Bunburia, commonly known as the desert cactus or the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and showy yellow or orange flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems.
