Billbergia baraquiniana

Billbergia baraquiniana, commonly known as the Baraquiniana billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. Its inflorescence consists of a cluster of vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Billbergia baraquiniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
