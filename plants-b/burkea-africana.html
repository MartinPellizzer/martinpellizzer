Burkea africana

Burkea africana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree has a straight trunk with a broad, rounded crown and produces woody pods that contain seeds. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Burkea africana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
