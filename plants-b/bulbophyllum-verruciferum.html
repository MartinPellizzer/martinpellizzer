Bulbophyllum verruciferum

Bulbophyllum verruciferum, commonly known as the疣茎万代兰, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white, yellow, or green.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
