Bulbostylis diffusa

Bulbostylis diffusa, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
