Billbergia distachya var. straussiana

Billbergia distachya var. straussiana, commonly known as the Strauss's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, strap-like leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
