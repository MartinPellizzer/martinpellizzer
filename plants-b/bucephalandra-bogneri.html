Bucephalandra bogneri

Bucephalandra bogneri, commonly known as the "Bogner's Bucephalandra," is a species of aquatic plant native to the clear, slow-moving waters of Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft, slightly acidic water with a stable temperature range. Bucephalandra bogneri is often cultivated in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
