Berberis potaninii

Berberis potaninii, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often bitter. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties, as it contains berberine, a compound with various health benefits.
