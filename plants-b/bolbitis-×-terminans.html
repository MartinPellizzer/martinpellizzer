Bolbitis × terminans

Bolbitis × terminans, commonly known as the hybrid shield fern, is a rare and intriguing plant resulting from the cross between Bolbitis heterophylla and Bolbitis capensis.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, which features a mix of different leaf shapes and sizes, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in the understorey of forests. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance, which contributes to its common name.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Bolbitis × terminans is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
