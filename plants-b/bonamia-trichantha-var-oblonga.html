Bonamia trichantha var. oblonga

Bonamia trichantha var. oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bonamia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological restoration projects.
