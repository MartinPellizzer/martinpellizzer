Bulbophyllum micholitzianum

Bulbophyllum micholitzianum, commonly known as the Micholitz's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
