Begonia leipingensis

Begonia leipingensis, commonly known as the Leiping begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
