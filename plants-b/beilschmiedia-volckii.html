Beilschmiedia volckii

Beilschmiedia volckii, commonly known as the Volck's beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
