Barleria gidoleensis

Barleria gidoleensis, commonly known as the Gidolensis false pennyroyal, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
