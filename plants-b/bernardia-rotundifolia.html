Bernardia rotundifolia

Bernardia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf bernardia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, rounded leaves that are typically clustered at the ends of its branches. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
