Besleria anomala

Besleria anomala, commonly known as the red-flowered gesneriad, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial belonging to the Gesneriaceae family, known for its striking, showy flowers. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy in appearance. Its flowers are typically orange-red with a white or yellow throat, and they bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.
