Bletilla striata var. gebina

Bletilla striata var. gebina, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant's foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch to woodland gardens.
