Begonia chenii

Begonia chenii, commonly known as the Chen's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia chenii is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
