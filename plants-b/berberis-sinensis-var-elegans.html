Berberis sinensis var. elegans

Berberis sinensis var. elegans, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it can be invasive in some regions and may pose a risk to native plant species.
