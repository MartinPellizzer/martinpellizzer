Bhidea borii

Bhidea borii, commonly known as the mountain saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Central Asia.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Bhidea borii is often found in mountain meadows and is a member of the Polygonaceae family.
