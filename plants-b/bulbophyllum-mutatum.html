Bulbophyllum mutatum

Bulbophyllum mutatum, commonly known as the yellow-tipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
