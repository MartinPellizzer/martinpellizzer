Boscia paolii

Boscia paolii, commonly known as the Namib Desert boscia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Namibia and Angola.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and smooth, greyish bark that provides protection against extreme temperatures. The plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments by storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. Its leaves are often reduced to small spines, minimizing water loss through transpiration.

Boscia paolii plays a vital role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
