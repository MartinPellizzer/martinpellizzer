Begonia sleumeri

Begonia sleumeri, commonly known as the Sleumer's begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, Begonia sleumeri is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts worldwide.
