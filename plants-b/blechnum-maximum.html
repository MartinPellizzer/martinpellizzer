Blechnum maximum

Blechnum maximum, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or forested substrates.

The fronds are compound, with a distinctive shield-like appearance, and are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value.
