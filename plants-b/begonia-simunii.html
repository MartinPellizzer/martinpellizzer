Begonia simunii

Begonia simunii, commonly known as the Simunii begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia simunii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
