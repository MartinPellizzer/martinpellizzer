Billotia flexuosa var. angustifolia

Billotia flexuosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed billotia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas.
