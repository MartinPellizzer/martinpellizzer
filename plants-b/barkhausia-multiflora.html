Barkhausia multiflora

Barkhausia multiflora, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss.

Barkhausia multiflora is a perennial species that produces multiple flower stems from a single plant, making it a visually appealing addition to gardens.
