Boehmeria hirta

Boehmeria hirta, commonly known as China grass, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the荨麻科 (Urticaceae) family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small greenish-white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its fibrous stems, which are used in the production of textiles and paper. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it both useful and sometimes invasive in certain regions.
