Basselinia moorei

Basselinia moorei, commonly known as the Moore's grass, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers.

Basselinia moorei is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
