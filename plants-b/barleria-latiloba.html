Barleria latiloba

Barleria latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaved barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and range in color from pink to purple.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.
