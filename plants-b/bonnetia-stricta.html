Bonnetia stricta

Bonnetia stricta, commonly known as the silver bonnet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered with fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Bonnetia stricta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
