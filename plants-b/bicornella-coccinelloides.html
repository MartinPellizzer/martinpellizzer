Bicornella coccinelloides

Bicornella coccinelloides, commonly known as the scarlet lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its striking, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in a loose raceme. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers have a distinctive shape with two prominent lobes, giving it its scientific name.

Bicornella coccinelloides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
