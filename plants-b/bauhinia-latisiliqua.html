Bauhinia latisiliqua

Bauhinia latisiliqua, commonly known as the broadleaf bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Bauhinia latisiliqua is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
