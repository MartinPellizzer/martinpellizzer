Bactris humilis

Bactris humilis, commonly known as the dwarf palm or small palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. Bactris humilis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid lowland areas.
