Bulbophyllum intertextum

Bulbophyllum intertextum, commonly known as the wrinkled lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, wrinkled petals and a distinctive, recurved lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with a contrasting red or purple lip.

Bulbophyllum intertextum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
