Bulbophyllum trongquyetii

Bulbophyllum trongquyetii, commonly known as the Vietnamese ghost orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, delicate floral structure. The plant produces small, pale green or white flowers that are often difficult to spot amidst its foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
