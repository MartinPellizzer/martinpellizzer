Bulbophyllum platyrhachis

Bulbophyllum platyrhachis, commonly known as the broad-ridge orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and clusters of dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
