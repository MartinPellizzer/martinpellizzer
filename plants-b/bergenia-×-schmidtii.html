Bergenia × schmidtii

Bergenia × schmidtii, commonly known as the hybrid bergenia, is a perennial evergreen plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. The plant features thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. It produces clusters of pink or red flowers in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Bergenia × schmidtii is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded, moist conditions.
