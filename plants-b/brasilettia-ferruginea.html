Brasilettia ferruginea

Brasilettia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty Brazil nut tree, is a large evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-barked trunk and large, compound leaves. The plant plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
