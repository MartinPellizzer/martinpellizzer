Begonia brevicyma

Begonia brevicyma, commonly known as the dwarf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used in container gardening and as a ground cover.
