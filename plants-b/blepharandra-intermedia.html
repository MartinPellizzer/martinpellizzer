Blepharandra intermedia

Blepharandra intermedia, commonly known as the silver sword plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
