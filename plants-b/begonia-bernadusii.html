Begonia bernadusii

Begonia bernadusii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a trailing plant, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.

Begonia bernadusii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
