Begonia uvana

Begonia uvana, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and unique floral display.

Begonia uvana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
