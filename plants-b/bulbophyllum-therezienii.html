Bulbophyllum therezienii

Bulbophyllum therezienii, commonly known as the Theresienii Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall.

Bulbophyllum therezienii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
