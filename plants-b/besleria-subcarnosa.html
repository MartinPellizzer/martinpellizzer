Besleria subcarnosa

Besleria subcarnosa, commonly known as the pink flame orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pendulous, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
