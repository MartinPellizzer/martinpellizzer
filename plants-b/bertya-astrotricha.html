Bertya astrotricha

Bertya astrotricha, commonly known as the starry bertya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Bertya astrotricha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
