Brickellia longifolia var. multiflora

Brickellia longifolia var. multiflora, commonly known as the showy brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and ecologically valuable addition to diverse plant communities.
