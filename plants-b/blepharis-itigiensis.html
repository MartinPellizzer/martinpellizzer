Blepharis itigiensis

Blepharis itigiensis, commonly known as the silverleaf blepharis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and hairy foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
