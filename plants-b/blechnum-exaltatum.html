Blechnum exaltatum

Blechnum exaltatum, commonly known as the tall holly fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green fronds with a distinctive, serrated margin. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Blechnum exaltatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
