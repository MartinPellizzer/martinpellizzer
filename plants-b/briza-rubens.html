Briza rubens

Briza rubens, commonly known as red feathergrass, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, pinkish-red plumes that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden settings.
