Bihai pendula

Bihai pendula, commonly known as the hanging hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular in ornamental gardening for its dramatic, cascading appearance.
