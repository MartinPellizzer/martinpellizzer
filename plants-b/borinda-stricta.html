Borinda stricta

Borinda stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf borinda, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Borinda stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage and blooms.
