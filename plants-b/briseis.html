Briseis

Briseis, commonly known as the white veldtgrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Briseis is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
