Barbacenia longiscapa

Barbacenia longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed barbacenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
