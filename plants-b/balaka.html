Balaka

Balaka, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, tubular leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Balaka is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for indoor cultivation and decorative arrangements.
