Begonia itatiaiensis

Begonia itatiaiensis, commonly known as the Itatiaia begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting yellow markings. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
