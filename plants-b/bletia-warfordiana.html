Bletia warfordiana

Bletia warfordiana, commonly known as the Warford's bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical forests of Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often near streams or in the undergrowth of dense vegetation. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bletia warfordiana is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to certain cultivated conditions.
