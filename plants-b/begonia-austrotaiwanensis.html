Begonia austrotaiwanensis

Begonia austrotaiwanensis, commonly known as the southern Taiwan begonia, is a species native to the island of Taiwan.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom during the summer months.

This plant thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and prefers moist, well-drained soil.
