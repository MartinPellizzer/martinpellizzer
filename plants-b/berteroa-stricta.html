Berteroa stricta

Berteroa stricta, commonly known as the rigid berteroa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features woody stems and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss.

Berteroa stricta is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in regions with limited rainfall.
