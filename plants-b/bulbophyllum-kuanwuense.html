Bulbophyllum kuanwuense

Bulbophyllum kuanwuense, commonly known as the Kuanwu orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are linear and often covered with a waxy coating, which helps in reducing water loss.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
