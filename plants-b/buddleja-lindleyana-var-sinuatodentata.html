Buddleja lindleyana var. sinuatodentata

Buddleja lindleyana var. sinuatodentata, commonly known as the sinuate-lobed buddleia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, panicle-like clusters of pale lavender to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are ovate with a distinctive sinuate-dentate margin, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
