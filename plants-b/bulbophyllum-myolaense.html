Bulbophyllum myolaense

Bulbophyllum myolaense, commonly known as the Myola orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
