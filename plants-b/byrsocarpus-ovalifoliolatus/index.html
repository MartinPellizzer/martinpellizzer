Byrsocarpus Ovalifoliolatus

Byrsocarpus ovalifoliolatus is a rare and unique plant species native to the deserts of the southwestern United States. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. This plant thrives in harsh, arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptations to extreme conditions. Its flowers are typically pale green or yellow, blooming in late spring or early summer. Despite its resilience, it remains a vulnerable species due to habitat loss and climate change.
