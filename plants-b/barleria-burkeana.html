Barleria burkeana

Barleria burkeana, commonly known as the Cape barleria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Barleria burkeana thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.
