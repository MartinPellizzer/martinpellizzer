Bulbisperma ovigera

Bulbisperma ovigera, commonly known as the African bulbous grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant features a bulbous base that stores nutrients, allowing it to survive dry periods. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture.

Bulbisperma ovigera is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
