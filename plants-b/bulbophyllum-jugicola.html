Bulbophyllum jugicola

Bulbophyllum jugicola, commonly known as the Hainan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
