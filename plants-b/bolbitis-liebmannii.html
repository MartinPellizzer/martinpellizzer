Bolbitis liebmannii

Bolbitis liebmannii, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique fern species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its distinctive, velvety, dark green fronds. The plant lacks true leaves and instead produces a single, broad, undulating frond that resembles a fern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Bolbitis liebmannii is notable for its ability to survive in arid conditions, making it an interesting subject for botanical study.
