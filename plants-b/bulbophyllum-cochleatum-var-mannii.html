Bulbophyllum cochleatum var. mannii

Bulbophyllum cochleatum var. mannii, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
