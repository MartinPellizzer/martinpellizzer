Biscutella mollis

Biscutella mollis, commonly known as the silky biscuit flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Biscutella mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
