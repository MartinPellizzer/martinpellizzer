Boltonia latisquama var. decurrens

Boltonia latisquama var. decurrens, commonly known as the narrowleaf Boltonia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine, hairy texture. The plant produces large, showy composite flowers that are typically white to pale pink in color. Its leaves are lanceolate, narrowing toward the tip, and are arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
