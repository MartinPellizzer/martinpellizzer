Bulbophyllum minahassae

Bulbophyllum minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in a spike. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
