Bulbophyllum scaberulum var. fuerstenbergianum

Bulbophyllum scaberulum var. fuerstenbergianum, commonly known as the Fuerstenberg's Bulbophyllum, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and strikingly patterned leaves that are often a deep green with contrasting veins. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with intricate, spurred petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
