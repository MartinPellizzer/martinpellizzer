Ballota mollissima

Ballota mollissima, commonly known as velvet ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Ballota mollissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
