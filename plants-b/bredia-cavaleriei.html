Bredia cavaleriei

Bredia cavaleriei, commonly known as the red-flowered bredia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a glossy surface that reflects light.

Bredia cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
