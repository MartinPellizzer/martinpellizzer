Bromus virens

Bromus virens, commonly known as green brome, is a perennial grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Bromus virens is often found in disturbed areas, meadows, and along roadsides.
