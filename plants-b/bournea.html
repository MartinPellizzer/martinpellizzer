Bournea

Bournea, commonly known as the golden trumpet or yellow trumpet, is a tropical plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that resemble trumpets, which bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bournea is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
