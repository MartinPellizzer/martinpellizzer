Babiana occidentalis

Babiana occidentalis, commonly known as the western rain lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. Babiana occidentalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
