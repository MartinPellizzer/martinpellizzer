Barleria prionitis subsp. angustissima

Barleria prionitis subsp.

angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf barleria, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils.

The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It is often found in coastal dunes and is adapted to saline and well-drained conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
