Benstonea verruculosa

Benstonea verruculosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
