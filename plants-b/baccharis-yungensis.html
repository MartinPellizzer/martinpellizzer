Baccharis yungensis

Baccharis yungensis, commonly known as Yungense Baccharis, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, specifically found in Argentina and Chile. This shrub or small tree typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
