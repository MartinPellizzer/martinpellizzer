Begonia bekopakensis

Begonia bekopakensis, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal begonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, red, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It is relatively easy to cultivate indoors or in shaded garden settings.
