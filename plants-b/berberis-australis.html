Berberis australis

Berberis australis, commonly known as the golden gooseberry, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features bright yellow flowers and red, berry-like fruits that are edible. The plant has spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
