Bidens chaetodonta

Bidens chaetodonta, commonly known as the hairy beggar's ticks, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features small, yellow flowers with prominent ray florets and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its hairy stems and leaves contribute to its common name, while its seeds are adapted for attachment to animals or clothing.

Bidens chaetodonta is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
