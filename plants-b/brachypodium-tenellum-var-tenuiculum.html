Brachypodium tenellum var. tenuiculum

Brachypodium tenellum var. tenuiculum, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its small, spike-like clusters of flowers are usually greenish or brownish in color.

Brachypodium tenellum var. tenuiculum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
