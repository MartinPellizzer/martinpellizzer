Begonia guixiensis

Begonia guixiensis, commonly known as the Guizhou begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and the flowers can be red, orange, or yellow.

Begonia guixiensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
