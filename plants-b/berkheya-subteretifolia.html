Berkheya subteretifolia

Berkheya subteretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Berkheya subteretifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
