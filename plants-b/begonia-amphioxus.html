Begonia amphioxus

Begonia amphioxus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.

Begonia amphioxus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
