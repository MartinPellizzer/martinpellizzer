Baccharis nemorosa

Baccharis nemorosa, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Baccharis nemorosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
