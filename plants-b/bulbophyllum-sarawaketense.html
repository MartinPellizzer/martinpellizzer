Bulbophyllum sarawaketense

Bulbophyllum sarawaketense, commonly known as the Sarawak Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Borneo.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within lowland forests. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are usually pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
