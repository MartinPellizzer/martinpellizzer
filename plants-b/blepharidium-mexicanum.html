Blepharidium mexicanum

Blepharidium mexicanum, commonly known as the Mexican eyelash cactus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spineless, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
