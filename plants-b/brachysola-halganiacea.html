Brachysola halganiacea

Brachysola halganiacea, commonly known as the coastal brachysola, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to withstand salt spray and arid conditions.

Brachysola halganiacea plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for local wildlife.
