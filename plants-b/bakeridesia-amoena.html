Bakeridesia amoena

Bakeridesia amoena, commonly known as the pretty orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Bakeridesia amoena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
