Betonica psilostachys

Betonica psilostachys, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in whorled clusters along the stem, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Betonica psilostachys is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and tonic properties.
