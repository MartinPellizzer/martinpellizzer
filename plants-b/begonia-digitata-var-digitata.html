Begonia digitata var. digitata

Begonia digitata var. digitata, commonly known as the pink begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
