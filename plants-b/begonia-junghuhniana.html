Begonia junghuhniana

Begonia junghuhniana, commonly known as the Junghuhn's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a glossy surface. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses as a decorative plant.
