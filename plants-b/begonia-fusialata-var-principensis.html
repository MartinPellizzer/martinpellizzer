Begonia fusialata var. principensis

Begonia fusialata var. principensis, commonly known as the Principense Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
