Beaufortia decussata

Beaufortia decussata, commonly known as the Honeycomb Bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern regions of Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often found in coastal and heathland environments. The plant is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical, bottlebrush-like flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Beaufortia decussata produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
