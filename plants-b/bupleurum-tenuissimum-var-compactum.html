Bupleurum tenuissimum var. compactum

Bupleurum tenuissimum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, clustered leaves and produces delicate, yellowish flowers in early summer. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
