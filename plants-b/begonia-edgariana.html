Begonia edgariana

Begonia edgariana, commonly known as the Edgarian begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia edgariana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
