Brillantaisia rutenbergiana

Brillantaisia rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg's brilliantia, is a rare flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
