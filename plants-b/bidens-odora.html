Bidens odora

Bidens odora, commonly known as the sweetface or sweet-scented beggar's tick, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers with white markings. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

The plant is also noted for its aromatic properties, which contribute to its common name.
