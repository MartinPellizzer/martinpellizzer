Bulbophyllum calviventer

Bulbophyllum calviventer, commonly known as the smooth-vent orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and specialized growth habits. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
