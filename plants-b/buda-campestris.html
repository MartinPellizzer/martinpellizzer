Buda campestris

Buda campestris, commonly known as the field buda, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom in late summer. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Buda campestris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
