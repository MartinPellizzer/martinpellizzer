Begonia × lancangensis

Begonia × lancangensis, commonly known as the Lancang begonia, is a hybrid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to maroon hues. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions.
