Bunchosia polystachia

Bunchosia polystachia, commonly known as the red-flowered bunchosia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. These berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
