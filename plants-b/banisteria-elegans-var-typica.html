Banisteria elegans var. typica

Banisteria elegans var. typica, commonly known as the elegant banisteria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
