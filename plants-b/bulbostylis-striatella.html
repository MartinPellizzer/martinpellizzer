Bulbostylis striatella

Bulbostylis striatella, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale green flowers. It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wetlands.
