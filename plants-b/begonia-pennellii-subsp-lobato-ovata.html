Begonia pennellii subsp. lobato-ovata

Begonia pennellii subsp.

lobato-ovata, commonly known as the oval-ovate begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color.

The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This subspecies is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
