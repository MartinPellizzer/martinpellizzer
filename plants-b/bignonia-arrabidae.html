Bignonia arrabidae

Bignonia arrabidae, commonly known as the Portuguese jasmine or arrabida vine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a climbing vine that produces fragrant, yellow-orange flowers in the spring. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing along coastal areas. Bignonia arrabidae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
