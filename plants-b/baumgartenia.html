Baumgartenia

Baumgartenia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. The plant is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are opposite and pinnately compound, with slender, lance-shaped leaflets.

The bark is smooth and light gray, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
