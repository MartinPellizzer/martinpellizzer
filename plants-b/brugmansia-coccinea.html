Brugmansia coccinea

Brugmansia coccinea, commonly known as the angel's trumpets, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and dark green color.

Brugmansia coccinea is a member of the nightshade family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
