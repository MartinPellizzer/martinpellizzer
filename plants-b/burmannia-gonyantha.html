Burmannia gonyantha

Burmannia gonyantha, commonly known as the red-flowered burmannia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous species that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are borne on short, hairy stalks. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth surface and dark green color.

Burmannia gonyantha is often found in the understory of dense forest canopies, where it relies on shade and high humidity for optimal growth.
