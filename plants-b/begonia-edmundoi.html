Begonia edmundoi

Begonia edmundoi, commonly known as the Edmund's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia edmundoi is popular among gardeners for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
