Begonia davisii

Begonia davisii, commonly known as the Davis begonia, is a tropical plant native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia davisii is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of care.
