Bulbophyllum thouarsii

Bulbophyllum thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, fleshy pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
