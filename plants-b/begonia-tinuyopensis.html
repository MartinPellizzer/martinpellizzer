Begonia tinuyopensis

Begonia tinuyopensis, commonly known as the Taiti begonia, is a species native to the island of Taiti in the South Pacific.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
