Babiana obliqua

Babiana obliqua, commonly known as the Rainstorm Babiana, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and related genera. Babiana obliqua is adapted to seasonal rainfall, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
