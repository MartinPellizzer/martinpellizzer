Bleekrodea

Bleekrodea, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-blue hue, which help reflect intense sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often blooming during the rainy season. Bleekrodea thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.
