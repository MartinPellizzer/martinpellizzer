Bidens xilinensis

Bidens xilinensis, commonly known as the Xilin ironweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Bidens xilinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
