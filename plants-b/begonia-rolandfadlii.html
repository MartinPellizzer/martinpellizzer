Begonia rolandfadlii

Begonia rolandfadlii, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often blooming in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
