Begonia diadema

Begonia diadema, commonly known as the star begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Begonia diadema is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and foliage.
