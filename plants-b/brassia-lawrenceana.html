Brassia lawrenceana

Brassia lawrenceana, commonly known as the Lawrence's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Brassia lawrenceana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
