Buddleja veitchiana

Buddleja veitchiana, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers are typically pale blue to purple in color and attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
