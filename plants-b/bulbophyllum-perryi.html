Bulbophyllum perryi

Bulbophyllum perryi, commonly known as the Perry's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a caudex and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant typically has long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum perryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
