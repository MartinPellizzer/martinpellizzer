Bauhinia burbidgei

Bauhinia burbidgei, commonly known as the Natal orchid tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, orchid-like leaves that are often pink or purple in color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple, during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Bauhinia burbidgei is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility in its native habitat.
