Barleria rehmannii

Barleria rehmannii, commonly known as the red-flowered barleria, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

It is also known for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
