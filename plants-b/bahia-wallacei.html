Bahia wallacei

Bahia wallacei, commonly known as Wallace's bahia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple.
