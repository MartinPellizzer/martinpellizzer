Bulbophyllum dunstervillei

Bulbophyllum dunstervillei, commonly known as the Dusky Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
