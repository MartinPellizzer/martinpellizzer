Begonia ndongensis

Begonia ndongensis, commonly known as the Congo begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically red or pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Begonia ndongensis is considered a rare and sought-after species among plant enthusiasts.
