Besleria silverstoneana

Besleria silverstoneana, commonly known as the silverstone orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in various shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in the dry seasons.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
