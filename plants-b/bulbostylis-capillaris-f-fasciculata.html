Bulbostylis capillaris f. fasciculata

Bulbostylis capillaris f. fasciculata, commonly known as the fasciculate bulbostylis, is a rare grass species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white or pale green flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.
