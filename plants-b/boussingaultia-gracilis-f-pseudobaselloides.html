Boussingaultia gracilis f. pseudobaselloides

Boussingaultia gracilis f. pseudobaselloides, commonly known as the slender leaf boussingaultia, is a unique variant of the base species Boussingaultia gracilis.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that are typically narrower than those of the standard form. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a dense arrangement of foliage, making it visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light conditions.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialized horticultural settings.
