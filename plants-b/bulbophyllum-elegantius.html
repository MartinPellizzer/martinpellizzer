Bulbophyllum elegantius

Bulbophyllum elegantius, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are usually leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

The flowers are often arranged in a raceme and can display a variety of colors, including white, pink, and yellow.
