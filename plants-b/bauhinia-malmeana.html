Bauhinia malmeana

Bauhinia malmeana, commonly known as the yellow bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves that are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its habitat. Bauhinia malmeana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
