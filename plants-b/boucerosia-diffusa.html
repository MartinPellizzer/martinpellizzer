Boucerosia diffusa

Boucerosia diffusa, commonly known as the yellow star lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-like shape, which bloom in late summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Boucerosia diffusa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
