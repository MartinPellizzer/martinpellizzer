Bulbophyllum rauhii

Bulbophyllum rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its leaves are usually ovate and have a waxy texture, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum rauhii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
