Bulbophyllum quinquelobum

Bulbophyllum quinquelobum, commonly known as the Five-lobed Pterostemon, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique five-lobed structure, which is a distinguishing feature. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat.

The flowers typically bloom in the summer, adding ornamental value to gardens and botanical collections.
