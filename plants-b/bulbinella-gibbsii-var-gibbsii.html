Bulbinella gibbsii var. gibbsii

Bulbinella gibbsii var. gibbsii, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in woodland or heathland environments. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
