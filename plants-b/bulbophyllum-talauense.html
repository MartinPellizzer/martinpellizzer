Bulbophyllum talauense

Bulbophyllum talauense, commonly known as the Talauan orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
