Berkheya insignis

Berkheya insignis, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the oxalis genus. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Berkheya insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
