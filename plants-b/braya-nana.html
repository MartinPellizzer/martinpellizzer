Braya nana

Braya nana, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a low-growing flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant typically has dense, mat-forming growth with small, rounded leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Braya nana is adapted to survive in nutrient-poor conditions and is an important component of alpine ecosystems.
