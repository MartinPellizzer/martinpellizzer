Begonia wallacei

Begonia wallacei, commonly known as the Wallace's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Begonia wallacei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
