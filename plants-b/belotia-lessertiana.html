Belotia lessertiana

Belotia lessertiana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green or white with a slight purple tinge.

Belotia lessertiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
