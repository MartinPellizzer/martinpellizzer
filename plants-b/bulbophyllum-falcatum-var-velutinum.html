Bulbophyllum falcatum var. velutinum

Bulbophyllum falcatum var. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the warm seasons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
