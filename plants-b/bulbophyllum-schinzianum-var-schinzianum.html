Bulbophyllum schinzianum var. schinzianum

Bulbophyllum schinzianum var. schinzianum, commonly known as the Schinz's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, often fragrant, orchid flowers. The plant typically features elongated pseudobulbs that support a single or few leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on inflorescences and can be found in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
