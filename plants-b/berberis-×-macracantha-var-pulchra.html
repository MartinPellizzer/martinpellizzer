Berberis × macracantha var. pulchra

Berberis × macracantha var. pulchra, commonly known as the golden barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny foliage and vibrant yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and hedges.

Its hardy nature and showy appearance make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and ecological habitats.
