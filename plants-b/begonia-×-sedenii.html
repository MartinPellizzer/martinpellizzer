Begonia × sedenii

Begonia × sedenii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia erythrophylla.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a broad, upright growth habit. It thrives in well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for container gardening and shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value, Begonia × sedenii is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
