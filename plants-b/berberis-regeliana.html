Berberis regeliana

Berberis regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's barberry, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often sour. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and winter interest.
