Bulbophyllum cylindraceum var. khasyanum

Bulbophyllum cylindraceum var. khasyanum, commonly known as the Khasi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical pseudobulbs and elongated, linear leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous small, pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous forests.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
