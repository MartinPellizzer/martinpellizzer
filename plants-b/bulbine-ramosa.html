Bulbine ramosa

Bulbine ramosa, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thickened stem and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has fleshy, linear leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
