Bulbophyllum hemiprionotum

Bulbophyllum hemiprionotum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces strikingly ornate flowers with a complex structure, typically in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
