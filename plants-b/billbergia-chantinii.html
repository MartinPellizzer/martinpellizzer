Billbergia chantinii

Billbergia chantinii, commonly known as the purple billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green foliage, which forms a rosette shape. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Billbergia chantinii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
