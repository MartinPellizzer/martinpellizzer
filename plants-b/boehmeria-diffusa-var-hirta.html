Boehmeria diffusa var. hirta

Boehmeria diffusa var. hirta, commonly known as Chinese nettle or Indian nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its fibrous stems are also utilized in the production of natural textiles and paper.
