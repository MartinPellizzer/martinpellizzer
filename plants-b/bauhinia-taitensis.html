Bauhinia taitensis

Bauhinia taitensis, commonly known as the Taiti bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are often divided into numerous small leaflets. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Its seeds are enclosed in flat, leathery pods, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
