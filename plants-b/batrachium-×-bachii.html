Batrachium × bachii

Batrachium × bachii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Batrachium luteum and Batrachium pulchellum.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage, with striking orange and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense mats in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
