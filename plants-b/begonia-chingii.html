Begonia chingii

Begonia chingii, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a species native to China and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is known for its ornamental value, particularly its foliage. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and can display a range of colors, including green, red, and variegated patterns.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
