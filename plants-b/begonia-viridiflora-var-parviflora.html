Begonia viridiflora var. parviflora

Begonia viridiflora var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered green-flowered begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are borne on slender pedicels. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shady garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
