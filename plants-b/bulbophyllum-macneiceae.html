Bulbophyllum macneiceae

Bulbophyllum macneiceae, commonly known as the MacNeice's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that help store water, adapting it to its natural habitat.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
