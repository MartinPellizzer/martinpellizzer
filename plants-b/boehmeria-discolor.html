Boehmeria discolor

Boehmeria discolor, commonly known as Chinese nettle or Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is often grown for its fibrous stems, which are used in the production of textiles and paper.

Boehmeria discolor is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
