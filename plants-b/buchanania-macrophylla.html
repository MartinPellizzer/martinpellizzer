Buchanania macrophylla

Buchanania macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf buchania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
