Begonia fangii

Begonia fangii, commonly known as the Fang's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to China.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves with intricate patterns and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with a short stem and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers shaded, humid environments and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique appearance, it is also valued in horticultural collections and botanical research.
