Breweria suffruticosa var. hirsutissima

Breweria suffruticosa var. hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy breweria, is a shrub-like plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and branching structure. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually small and covered in fine, pubescent hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
