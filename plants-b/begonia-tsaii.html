Begonia tsaii

Begonia tsaii, commonly known as the Tsai's begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its attractive foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in gardens and homes.
