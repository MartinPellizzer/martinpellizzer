Begonia addrinii

Begonia addrinii, commonly known as the Adrinii begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It features velvety, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green hue and striking red veins, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
