Berchemia cinerascens

Berchemia cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf berchemia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and silver-gray in color, which helps reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Berchemia cinerascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
