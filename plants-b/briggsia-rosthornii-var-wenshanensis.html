Briggsia rosthornii var. wenshanensis

Briggsia rosthornii var. wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy bracts. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, while the colorful bracts serve as the primary ornamental feature.

This variety is valued for its unique foliage and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
