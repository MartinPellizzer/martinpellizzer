Bunium Persicum

Bunium persicum is a lesser-known but fascinating member of the Apiaceae family. Native to the arid regions of Central Asia, it thrives in harsh, dry environments. This plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and aromatic foliage. It has been historically used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Despite its potential, it remains underappreciated in modern botanical and horticultural circles.
