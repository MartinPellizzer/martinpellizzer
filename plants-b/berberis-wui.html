Berberis wui

Berberis wui, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

Berberis wui also contains berberine, a compound with potential medicinal properties.
