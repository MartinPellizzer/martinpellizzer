Boltonia montana

Boltonia montana, commonly known as mountain boltonia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, wooded areas and open fields. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Boltonia montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in partial shade.
