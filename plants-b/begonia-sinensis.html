Begonia sinensis

Begonia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and prefers partial shade and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The leaves are usually glossy and have a distinctive veining pattern.
