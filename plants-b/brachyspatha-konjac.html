Brachyspatha konjac

Brachyspatha konjac, commonly known as the giant konjac, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. It produces a striking inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant is adapted to wet, humid environments and thrives in swampy or marshy habitats.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually impressive species within its native ecosystem.
