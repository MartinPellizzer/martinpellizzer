Brizopyrum prostratum var. erectum

Brizopyrum prostratum var. erectum, commonly known as upright sheep's beard, is a variant of the sheep's beard plant characterized by its upright growth habit rather than the prostrate form of the typical species.

It is a flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae, and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are borne on slender stalks.
