Bulbophyllum birugatum

Bulbophyllum birugatum, commonly known as the Black Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, contributing to its overall appearance.

This orchid is appreciated for its striking coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
