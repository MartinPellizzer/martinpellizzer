Bulbophyllum falciferum

Bulbophyllum falciferum, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum falciferum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
