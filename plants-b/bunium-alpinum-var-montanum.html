Bunium alpinum var. montanum

Bunium alpinum var. montanum, commonly known as the alpine fennel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and often fern-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes with cool, temperate climates. The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
