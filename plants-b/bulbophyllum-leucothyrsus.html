Bulbophyllum leucothyrsus

Bulbophyllum leucothyrsus, commonly known as the white-thorned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
