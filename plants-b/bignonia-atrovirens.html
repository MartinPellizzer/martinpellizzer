Bignonia atrovirens

Bignonia atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green bignonia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous climbing vine that produces striking orange to yellow tubular flowers. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and vigorous growth habit.
