Bulbophyllum absconditum subsp. absconditum

Bulbophyllum absconditum subsp.

absconditum, commonly known as the hidden bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting a single leaf that is strap-shaped and waxy.

This subspecies is noted for its rarity and is often found growing in rocky or mossy substrates.
