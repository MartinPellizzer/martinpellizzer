Bupleurum ranunculoides var. arcticum

Bupleurum ranunculoides var. arcticum, commonly known as Arctic Buttercup, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It features bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in nutrient-poor, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to harsh, high-altitude environments.
