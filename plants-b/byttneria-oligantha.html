Byttneria oligantha

Byttneria oligantha, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, often greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested or savanna regions.
