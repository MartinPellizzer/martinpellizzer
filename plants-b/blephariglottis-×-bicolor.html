Blephariglottis × bicolor

Blephariglottis × bicolor, commonly known as the hybrid beauty leaf, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often variegated foliage, featuring shades of green, white, and red. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and aquatic plants. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth texture and prominent veins.
