Boronia decumbens

Boronia decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate boronia, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters along the stems. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

Boronia decumbens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
