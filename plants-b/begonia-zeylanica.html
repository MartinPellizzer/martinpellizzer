Begonia zeylanica

Begonia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon begonia, is a tropical plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia zeylanica is popular as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
