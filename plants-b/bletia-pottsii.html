Bletia pottsii

Bletia pottsii, commonly known as the Potts' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces a single, showy inflorescence with several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bletia pottsii is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
