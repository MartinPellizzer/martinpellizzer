Bulbophyllum holochilum var. aurantiacum

Bulbophyllum holochilum var. aurantiacum, commonly known as the Orange-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-yellow inflorescences and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. It produces fragrant, showy flowers that bloom during the wet season.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique coloration and role in its native ecosystems.
