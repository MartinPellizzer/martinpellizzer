Barbacenia plantaginea

Barbacenia plantaginea, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, usually appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Barbacenia plantaginea is often found in moist, shaded environments and is known for its adaptability to various soil types.
