Brucea sumatrana var. cambodiana

Brucea sumatrana var. cambodiana, commonly known as Cambodian Brucea, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers followed by small, orange-red berries. It is often found in moist, tropical forests and is valued for its medicinal properties.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antimalarial effects.
