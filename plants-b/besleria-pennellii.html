Besleria pennellii

Besleria pennellii, commonly known as the Pennell's flame plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces tubular corollas that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
