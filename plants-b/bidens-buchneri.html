Bidens buchneri

Bidens buchneri, commonly known as the common tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small yellow flowers with white ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in open, sunny habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Bidens buchneri is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
