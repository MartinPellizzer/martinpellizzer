Bulbophyllum goliathense

Bulbophyllum goliathense, commonly known as the Giant Goliath Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 20 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The flowers are usually yellow or orange with intricate patterns, making it a striking addition to orchid collections.
