Burmeistera catulum

Burmeistera catulum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to Central and South America, particularly in tropical regions. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals and a distinctive, tubular corolla.

Burmeistera catulum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
