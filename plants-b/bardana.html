Bardana

Bardana, commonly known as hawksbeard, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of about 40 species, most of which are native to Europe, Asia, and North America. Bardana species are often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils, and they can spread rapidly through their extensive root systems.
