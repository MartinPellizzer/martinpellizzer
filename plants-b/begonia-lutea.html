Begonia lutea

Begonia lutea, commonly known as the yellow begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are usually borne on erect stems and bloom in shades of yellow to orange.

Begonia lutea prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
