Begonia beryllae

Begonia beryllae, commonly known as the silver queen begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are a striking combination of silver and green, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
