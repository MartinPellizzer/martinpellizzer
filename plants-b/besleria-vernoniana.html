Besleria vernoniana

Besleria vernoniana, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, deep red petals and a yellow throat, which make it highly ornamental. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its beauty in horticultural collections.
