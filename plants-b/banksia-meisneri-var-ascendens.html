Banksia meisneri var. ascendens

Banksia meisneri var. ascendens, commonly known as the ascending banksia, is a shrub or small tree native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its cylindrical or conical inflorescences, which are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces woody follicles that contain seeds, a feature common in many members of the Proteaceae family. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or heathland environments.
