Burmannia filamentosa

Burmannia filamentosa, commonly known as the threadleaf burmannia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in lowland rainforests.

Burmannia filamentosa is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
