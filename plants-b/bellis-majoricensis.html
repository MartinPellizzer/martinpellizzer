Bellis majoricensis

Bellis majoricensis, commonly known as the Majorcan daisy, is a flowering plant native to the island of Majorca in the Balearic Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white or pink flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has a rosette of spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or grassy areas.

Bellis majoricensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
