Bauhinia aherniana var. subglabra

Bauhinia aherniana var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth bauhinia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in summer. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and the plant is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
