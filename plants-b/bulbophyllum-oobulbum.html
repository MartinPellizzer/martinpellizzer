Bulbophyllum oobulbum

Bulbophyllum oobulbum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bulbophyllum oobulbum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
