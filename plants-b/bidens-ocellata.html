Bidens ocellata

Bidens ocellata, commonly known as the blackroot or eye-bright, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow composite flowers. The plant has hairy, ribbed stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its fruit is a schizocarp composed of three nutlets, each with a small, black seed.

Bidens ocellata is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and wetlands.
