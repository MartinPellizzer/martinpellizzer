Bradleia macrocarpa

Bradleia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant bradleia, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

This plant is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a broad, colorful spathe. The leaves are typically large, broad, and glossy, contributing to its dramatic appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and indirect light.

Bradleia macrocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and exotic foliage and floral structure.
