Begonia sublongipes

Begonia sublongipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This species is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
