Belmontia teuszii var. angustifolia

Belmontia teuszii var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf belmontia, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
