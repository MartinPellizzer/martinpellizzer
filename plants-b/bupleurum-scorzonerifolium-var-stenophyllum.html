Bupleurum scorzonerifolium var. stenophyllum

Bupleurum scorzonerifolium var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust root system and tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
