Berberis napaulensis var. napaulensis

Berberis napaulensis var. napaulensis, commonly known as the Nepal barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayan region.

It features thorny stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often found in rocky or forested areas at high elevations.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
