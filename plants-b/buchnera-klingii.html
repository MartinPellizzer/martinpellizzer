Buchnera klingii

Buchnera klingii, commonly known as the dwarf silver grass, is a small perennial grass species native to southern China.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance, and it produces delicate, fluffy seed heads in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Buchnera klingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.

It is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
