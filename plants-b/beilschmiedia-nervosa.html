Beilschmiedia nervosa

Beilschmiedia nervosa, commonly known as the veined beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and showy, white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid lowland forests and has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves with prominent venation.
