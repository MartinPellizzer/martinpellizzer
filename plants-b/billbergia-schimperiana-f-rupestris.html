Billbergia schimperiana f. rupestris

Billbergia schimperiana f. rupestris, commonly known as the rock billbergia, is a unique variant of the billbergia genus native to Brazil.

This plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and striking blue-gray foliage with white markings. It produces inflorescences that bear small, white, tubular flowers, which are typically borne on long, pendulous stems. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
