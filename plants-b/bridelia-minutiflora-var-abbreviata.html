Bridelia minutiflora var. abbreviata

Bridelia minutiflora var. abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf bridelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a humid, warm climate.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
