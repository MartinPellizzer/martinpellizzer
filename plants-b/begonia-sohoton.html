Begonia sohoton

Begonia sohoton, commonly known as the Sohoton begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a mix of green and red hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia sohoton is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
