Bolbitis cadieri

Bolbitis cadieri, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique fern species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly appearance due to the fine, silky hairs covering its fronds. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are evergreen, with a distinctive dark green color and a velvety texture.

Bolbitis cadieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
