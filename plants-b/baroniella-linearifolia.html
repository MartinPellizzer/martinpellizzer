Baroniella linearifolia

Baroniella linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf baroniella, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, commonly known as the palm family, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves.
