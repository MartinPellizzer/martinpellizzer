Bauhinia poiteauana

Bauhinia poiteauana, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in vibrant red, orange, or yellow hues. The plant has compound leaves composed of several small, oval-shaped leaflets. Bauhinia poiteauana is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in warm climates.
