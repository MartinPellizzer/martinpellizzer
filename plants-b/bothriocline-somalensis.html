Bothriocline somalensis

Bothriocline somalensis, commonly known as the Somali broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the dry regions of Somalia and parts of Ethiopia. The plant is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
