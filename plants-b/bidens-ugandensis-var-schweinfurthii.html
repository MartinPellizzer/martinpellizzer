Bidens ugandensis var. schweinfurthii

Bidens ugandensis var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Uganda ironweed, is a perennial herb native to East Africa.

It features erect, hairy stems and produces small, yellow flowers with distinctive white markings. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and is often found in moist, disturbed habitats. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and its use in traditional medicine.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a component of local ecosystems and its potential for further medicinal research.
