Begonia haageana

Begonia haageana, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
