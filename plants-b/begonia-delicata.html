Begonia delicata

Begonia delicata, commonly known as the delicate begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, velvety foliage with a unique mottled pattern of green, white, and red. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value lies in both its foliage and its subtle floral display.
