Brachyscome ciliaris var. subdissecta

Brachyscome ciliaris var. subdissecta, commonly known as the silver frost daisy, is a low-growing perennial native to Australia.

It features finely dissected, silver-white leaves that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with a yellow center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
