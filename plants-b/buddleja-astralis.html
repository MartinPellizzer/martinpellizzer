Buddleja astralis

Buddleja astralis, commonly known as the star buddleia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and its clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers are typically pale blue to lavender in color, with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This plant is also known for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
