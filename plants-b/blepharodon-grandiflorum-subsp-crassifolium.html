Blepharodon grandiflorum subsp. crassifolium

Blepharodon grandiflorum subsp.

crassifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf blepharodon, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink.
