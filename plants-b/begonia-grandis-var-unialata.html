Begonia grandis var. unialata

Begonia grandis var. unialata, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly silver in color with a deep green margin. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation or shaded garden settings.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
