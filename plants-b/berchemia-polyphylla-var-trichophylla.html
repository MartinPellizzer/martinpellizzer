Berchemia polyphylla var. trichophylla

Berchemia polyphylla var. trichophylla, commonly known as the hairy berchemia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
