Begonia pengchingii

Begonia pengchingii, commonly known as the Pengcheng begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers with five petals, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, humid environments and prefers partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
