Begonia nothobaramensis

Begonia nothobaramensis, commonly known as the Bornean begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface, depending on the variety. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
