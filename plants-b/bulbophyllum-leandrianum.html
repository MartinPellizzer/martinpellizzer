Bulbophyllum leandrianum

Bulbophyllum leandrianum, commonly known as the leandrianum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile soils. Its flowers are small and often arranged in a spike, with colors ranging from white to pink.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
