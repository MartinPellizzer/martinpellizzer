Balanops sparsifolia

Balanops sparsifolia, commonly known as the sparse-leaved balanops, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its slender, straight trunk and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Balanops sparsifolia is valued for its timber and is often found in lowland rainforests.
