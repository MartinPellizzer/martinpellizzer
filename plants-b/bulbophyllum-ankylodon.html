Bulbophyllum ankylodon

Bulbophyllum ankylodon, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, often brightly colored flowers with intricate patterns. Its bulbous rhizomes allow it to store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in less fertile soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
