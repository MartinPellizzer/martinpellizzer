Berberis himalaica

Berberis himalaica, commonly known as Himalayan barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in cooler regions.
