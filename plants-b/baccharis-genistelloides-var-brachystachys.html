Baccharis genistelloides var. brachystachys

Baccharis genistelloides var. brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed baccharis, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by narrow, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dunes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize sandy soils.
