Bursera fragrantissima

Bursera fragrantissima, commonly known as the fragrant bursera, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain smooth, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry forests and scrublands.

Its essential oils are used in traditional medicine and for their pleasant scent.
