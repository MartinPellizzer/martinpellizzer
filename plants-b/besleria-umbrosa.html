Besleria umbrosa

Besleria umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This herbaceous perennial is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers and dark green, strap-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and preference for specific growing conditions make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
