Bubon arachnoideum

Bubon arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider-tailed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which resembles a spider's web, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
