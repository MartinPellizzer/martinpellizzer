Boehmeria mauritiana var. molliuscula

Boehmeria mauritiana var. molliuscula, commonly known as the silky fiber plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, silky fibers that are harvested from the leaves, making it valuable for textile production. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a rough, hairy texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
