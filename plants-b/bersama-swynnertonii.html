Bersama swynnertonii

Bersama swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's ber, is a unique member of the Proteaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, urn-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces woody pods that contain several small, hard seeds. It is a shrub that thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
