Beilschmiedia percoriacea var. ciliata

Beilschmiedia percoriacea var. ciliata, commonly known as the ciliate beilschmiedia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves with fine cilia along the margins, giving it its common name. This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
