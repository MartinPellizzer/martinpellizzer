Bulbophyllum rubiginosum

Bulbophyllum rubiginosum, commonly known as the rusty bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often hairy pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
