Bolelia brachyantha

Bolelia brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bolelia, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.

Bolelia brachyantha produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring.
