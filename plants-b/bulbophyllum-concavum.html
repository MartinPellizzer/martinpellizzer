Bulbophyllum concavum

Bulbophyllum concavum, commonly known as the concave orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and its ability to store water, which helps it survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum concavum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
