Bidens rubifolia var. cuatrecasasii

Bidens rubifolia var. cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas' ironweed, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features erect, branched stems covered in stiff, glandular hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a reddish or purplish hue.

The flowers are yellow with prominent white ray florets, typical of the genus Bidens.
