Bambusa wiesneri

Bambusa wiesneri, commonly known as the Wiesner's bamboo, is a tall, clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It features sturdy, green culms that can grow up to 10 meters in height, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This bamboo is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
