Bidens × madiotii

Bidens × madiotii, commonly known as the hybrid ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect stem and opposite leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typical of the genus Bidens. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in swampy areas or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
