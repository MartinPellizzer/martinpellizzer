Barhamia hispida

Barhamia hispida, commonly known as the rough barhamia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique adaptations, Barhamia hispida is an important component of local ecosystems in its native range.
