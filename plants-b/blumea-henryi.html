Blumea henryi

Blumea henryi, commonly known as Chinese blue mist shrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small blue or purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Blumea henryi is valued both for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
