Bulbophyllum ablepharon

Bulbophyllum ablepharon, commonly known as the eyeless orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
