Barbacenia Rodriguesii

Rodrigues barbacenia, scientifically known as Barbacenia rodriguesii, is a rare flowering plant native to the island of Rodrigues in the Indian Ocean. This unique species belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton, and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and faces significant threats from habitat loss and climate change. Its existence serves as a reminder of the fragility of island ecosystems and the need for continued conservation initiatives.
