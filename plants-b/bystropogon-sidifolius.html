Bystropogon sidifolius

Bystropogon sidifolius, commonly known as the sword-leafed daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Bystropogon sidifolius is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
