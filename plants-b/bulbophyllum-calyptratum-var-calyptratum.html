Bulbophyllum calyptratum var. calyptratum

Bulbophyllum calyptratum var. calyptratum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
