Blaeria polyantha

Blaeria polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and grows in clusters along the ground. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Blaeria polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a traditional medicinal plant.
