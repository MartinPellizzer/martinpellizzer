Beta webbiana

Beta webbiana, commonly known as the webb's spinach, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience in various soil conditions.

Beta webbiana is also valued for its edible leaves, which are rich in nutrients and used in local cuisines.
