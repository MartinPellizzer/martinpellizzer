Beaucarnea gracilis

Beaucarnea gracilis, commonly known as the ponytail palm, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a short, thick trunk that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant has long, slender leaves that grow in a spiral pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its root system is shallow, making it adaptable to various soil types.

Beaucarnea gracilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique shape and low maintenance requirements.
