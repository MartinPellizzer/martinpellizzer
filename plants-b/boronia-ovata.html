Boronia ovata

Boronia ovata, commonly known as the white boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub with slender, woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Boronia ovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
