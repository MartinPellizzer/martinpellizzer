Bletilla chinensis

Bletilla chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often growing in dense clusters.

Bletilla chinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
