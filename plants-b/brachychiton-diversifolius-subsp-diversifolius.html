Brachychiton diversifolius subsp. diversifolius

Brachychiton diversifolius subsp.

diversifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed bottle tree, is a tropical tree native to northern Australia. It is characterized by its thick, bottle-shaped trunk and broad, palmate leaves that vary in shape and size.

The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its unique appearance and ability to store water in its trunk make it an ornamental and resilient plant.
