Bulbophyllum soidaoense

Bulbophyllum soidaoense, commonly known as the Soidao orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in nutrient-poor conditions.

The flowers are usually small to medium-sized, with a wide range of colors, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
