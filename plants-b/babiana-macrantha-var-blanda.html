Babiana macrantha var. blanda

Babiana macrantha var. blanda, commonly known as the soft-bellied babiana, is a South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The flowers are typically borne on tall, leafless stems, and the plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in grassland habitats and is known for its resilience in arid environments.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
