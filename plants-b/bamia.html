Bamia

Bamia, commonly known as okra, is a flowering plant in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It is native to Africa and thrives in warm, tropical climates. The plant produces edible pods that are harvested when young and tender. These pods are rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making them a nutritious addition to various cuisines.

Bamia is also valued for its ability to grow in poor soil conditions, making it a resilient crop for many regions.
