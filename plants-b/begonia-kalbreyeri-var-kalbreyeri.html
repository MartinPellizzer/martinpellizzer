Begonia kalbreyeri var. kalbreyeri

Begonia kalbreyeri var. kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a striking deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
