Buddleja szyszylowiczii

Buddleja szyszylowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja szyszylowiczii is known for its ornamental value and hardy nature, making it a popular choice in gardens.
