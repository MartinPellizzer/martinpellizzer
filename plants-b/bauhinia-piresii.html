Bauhinia piresii

Bauhinia piresii, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
