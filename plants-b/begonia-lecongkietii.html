Begonia lecongkietii

Begonia lecongkietii, commonly known as the Vietnamese begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The leaves are often deep green with a slight waxy coating, and the flowers can range in color from pink to red. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
