Buddleja betonicifolia

Buddleja betonicifolia, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
