Botryanthus micranthus

Botryanthus micranthus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Botryanthus micranthus is also valued for its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the year under optimal conditions.
