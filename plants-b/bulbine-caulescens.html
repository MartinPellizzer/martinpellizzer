Bulbine caulescens

Bulbine caulescens, commonly known as the red-flowered bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem and produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the warmer months. The plant has fleshy, linear leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
