Belis

Belis, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features tiny, rounded leaves and small white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Belis has a low-growing habit and often forms dense mats on the ground.

It is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
