Brachycorythis ovata subsp. schweinfurthii

Brachycorythis ovata subsp.

schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's broad-leafed brachycoryth, is a species native to southern Africa. It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, rounded leaves.

The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
