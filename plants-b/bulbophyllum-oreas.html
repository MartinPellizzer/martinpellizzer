Bulbophyllum oreas

Bulbophyllum oreas, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and is typically found growing in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum oreas is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
