Blechnum wattsii

Blechnum wattsii, commonly known as the New Zealand hard fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its robust, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a rough texture. The plant is typically found in forest understories, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Blechnum wattsii has a unique reproductive structure, with sporangia arranged in clusters on the undersides of its fronds.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
