Begonia × carminata

Begonia × carminata, commonly known as the Crimson Begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of vibrant foliage colors.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is prized for its ornamental value. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with striking red, orange, or yellow veins, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia × carminata is relatively easy to care for, making it an excellent plant for both novice and experienced gardeners.
