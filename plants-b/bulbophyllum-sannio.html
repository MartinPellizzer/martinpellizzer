Bulbophyllum sannio

Bulbophyllum sannio, commonly known as the Sannio orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces multiple erect or pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant flowers. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
