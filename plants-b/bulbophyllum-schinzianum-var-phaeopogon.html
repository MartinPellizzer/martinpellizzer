Bulbophyllum schinzianum var. phaeopogon

Bulbophyllum schinzianum var. phaeopogon, commonly known as the Purple-veined Bulbophyllum, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive purple veins. The plant produces inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
