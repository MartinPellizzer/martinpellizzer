Brachycylix vageleri

Brachycylix vageleri, commonly known as the slender leaf cycad, is a unique cycad species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its distinctive, narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs, typical of cycads. Its botanical significance lies in its role as a relict species, offering insights into ancient plant evolution.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
