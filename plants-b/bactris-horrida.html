Bactris horrida

Bactris horrida, commonly known as the spiny palm, is a species of palm native to South America.

It is characterized by its robust, spiny leaves and a tall, slender trunk. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are large and fan-shaped, with prominent spines along the edges.

Bactris horrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
