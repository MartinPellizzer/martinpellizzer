Bupleurum heldreichii

Bupleurum heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan cicely, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and often fern-like foliage. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with finely divided segments, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bupleurum heldreichii is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrance and visual interest.
