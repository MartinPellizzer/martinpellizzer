Bupleurum marginatum var. stenophyllum

Bupleurum marginatum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cicely, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at higher elevations.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the flora in its native habitat.
