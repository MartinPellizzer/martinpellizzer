Borkonstia limoniifolia

Borkonstia limoniifolia, commonly known as the lemonleaf borkonstia, is a unique botanical species characterized by its distinctive lemon-scented foliage.

It belongs to the family Borkonstieae and is native to the humid tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface that enhances its aromatic properties. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental value.

Borkonstia limoniifolia is often cultivated for its fragrant leaves and is used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
