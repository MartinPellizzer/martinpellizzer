Babbagia acroptera var. deminuta

Babbagia acroptera var. deminuta, commonly known as the small-flowered babbagia, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to the western United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. It is adapted to full sun and has a drought-resistant nature, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
