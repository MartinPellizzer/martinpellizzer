Beta patellaris var. luthereaui

Beta patellaris var. luthereaui, commonly known as the Luthereaui beet, is a lesser-known variant of the Beta genus.

It is characterized by its distinctively colored leaves, which range from deep purple to vibrant red. The plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value rather than its edible roots.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
