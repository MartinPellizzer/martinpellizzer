Baccharis tenuicapitulata

Baccharis tenuicapitulata, commonly known as narrow-headed baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, narrow heads of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, narrow leaves and produces small, woody fruits. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Baccharis tenuicapitulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
