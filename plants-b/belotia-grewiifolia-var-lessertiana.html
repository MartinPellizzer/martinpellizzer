Belotia grewiifolia var. lessertiana

Belotia grewiifolia var. lessertiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and clusters of tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
