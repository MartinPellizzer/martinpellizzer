Burmannia hexaptera

Burmannia hexaptera, commonly known as the red veined burmannia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burmanniaceae and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves with prominent red veins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often green or yellow in color.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
