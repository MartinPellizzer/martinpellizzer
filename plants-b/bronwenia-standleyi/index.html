Bronwenia Standleyi

Bronwenia standleyi is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Peru. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. This species thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil. Due to its limited natural habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant for future generations.
