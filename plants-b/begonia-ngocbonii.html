Begonia ngocbonii

Begonia ngocbonii, commonly known as the Ngoc Bon begonia, is a species native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and the flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of the stems.

This begonia is well-suited for indoor cultivation and prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity.
