Brochoneura usambarensis

Brochoneura usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambaran orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Eastern Arc Mountains of Tanzania.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats. The plant features a pseudobulb and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration. It is characterized by its slender stems and lanceolate leaves.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
