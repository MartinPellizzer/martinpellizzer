Bulbophyllum pilosum

Bulbophyllum pilosum, commonly known as the hairy bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, often reddish-brown stems. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
