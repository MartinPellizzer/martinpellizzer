Begonia compacta

Begonia compacta, commonly known as the compact begonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a short, upright stem. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for container gardening.

Begonia compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
