Blechnum itatiaiense

Blechnum itatiaiense, commonly known as the Itatiaia shield fern, is a species of fern native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Blechnum itatiaiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
