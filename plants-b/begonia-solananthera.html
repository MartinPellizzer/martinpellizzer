Begonia solananthera

Begonia solananthera, commonly known as the Solananthera begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia solananthera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
