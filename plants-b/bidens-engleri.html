Bidens engleri

Bidens engleri, commonly known as the scarlet bidens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
