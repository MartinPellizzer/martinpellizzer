Bulbophyllum youngsayeanum

Bulbophyllum youngsayeanum, commonly known as the Youngsayean orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse environments due to its specific care requirements.
