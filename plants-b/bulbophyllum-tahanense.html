Bulbophyllum tahanense

Bulbophyllum tahanense, commonly known as the Tahan Mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in low-light conditions.

This orchid is part of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
