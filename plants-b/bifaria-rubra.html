Bifaria rubra

Bifaria rubra, commonly known as the red bifaria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Bifaria rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
