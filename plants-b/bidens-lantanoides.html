Bidens lantanoides

Bidens lantanoides, commonly known as the yellow ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and prairies. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in late summer and early fall, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
