Bidens kamtschatica

Bidens kamtschatica, commonly known as the Kamtschatka ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the cold climates of eastern Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, open areas and thrives in temperate to subarctic regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with prominent central disc florets.

Bidens kamtschatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
