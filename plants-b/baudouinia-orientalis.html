Baudouinia orientalis

Baudouinia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has woody stems and small, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Baudouinia orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
