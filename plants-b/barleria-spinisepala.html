Barleria spinisepala

Barleria spinisepala, commonly known as the spiny-sepaled barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture and serrated edges. Its showy flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in dense terminal spikes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
