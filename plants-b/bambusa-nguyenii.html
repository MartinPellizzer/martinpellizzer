Bambusa nguyenii

Bambusa nguyenii, commonly known as the Nguyen's bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Bambusa nguyenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and traditional crafts.
