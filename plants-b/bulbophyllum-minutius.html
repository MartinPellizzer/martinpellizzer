Bulbophyllum minutius

Bulbophyllum minutius, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and compact growth habit. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor gardening.
