Begonia balansana

Begonia balansana, commonly known as the Balansan begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Begonia balansana is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
