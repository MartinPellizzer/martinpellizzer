Banisteria campestris var. montana

Banisteria campestris var. montana, commonly known as the mountain banisteria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that are typically orange or red in color.
