Bauhinia burchellii

Bauhinia burchellii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange flower clusters that bloom in summer. The leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small, feather-like segments. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.
