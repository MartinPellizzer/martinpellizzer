Baphia glabra

Baphia glabra, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has pinnate leaves with 10 to 20 pairs of leaflets, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its fruits are pod-like and contain several seeds.

Baphia glabra is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
