Begonia bowerae var. major

Begonia bowerae var. major, commonly known as the giant begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, bright red flowers with yellow centers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
