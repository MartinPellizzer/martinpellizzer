Begonia dicressine

Begonia dicressine, commonly known as the silver lace begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white variegation pattern. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
