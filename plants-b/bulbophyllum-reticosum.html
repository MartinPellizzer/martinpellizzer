Bulbophyllum reticosum

Bulbophyllum reticosum, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like vein patterns on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
