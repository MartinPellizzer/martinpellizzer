Bronwenia cornifolia var. cornifolia

Bronwenia cornifolia var. cornifolia, commonly known as the mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of vibrant yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in coastal environments.
