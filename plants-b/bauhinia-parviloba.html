Bauhinia parviloba

Bauhinia parviloba, commonly known as the small-leaf bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its delicate, bipinnate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a notable species in botanical collections.
