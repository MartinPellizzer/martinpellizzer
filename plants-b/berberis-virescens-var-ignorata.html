Berberis virescens var. ignorata

Berberis virescens var. ignorata, commonly known as the green barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thick, spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative hedge.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent berries and winter interest.
