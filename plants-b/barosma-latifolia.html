Barosma latifolia

Barosma latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved barosma, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
