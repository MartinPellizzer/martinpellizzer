Borzicactus tominensis

Borzicactus tominensis, commonly known as the Tomin's barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its robust, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant features a spiny exterior with areoles that bear both spines and flowers. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late summer.

Borzicactus tominensis is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal rainfall.
