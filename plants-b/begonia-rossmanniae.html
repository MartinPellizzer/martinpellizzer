Begonia rossmanniae

Begonia rossmanniae, commonly known as the Rossman's begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from red to orange in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it thrives in humid, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Begonia rossmanniae prefers indirect sunlight and is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
