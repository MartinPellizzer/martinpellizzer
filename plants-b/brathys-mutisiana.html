Brathys mutisiana

Brathys mutisiana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a variety of hues, including pink, purple, and white.

Brathys mutisiana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitat.
