Berberis kleinii

Berberis kleinii, commonly known as the dwarf barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that turn bright yellow in the fall. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.

Berberis kleinii is also known for its hardiness and adaptability to various climate conditions.
