Bryodes micrantha

Bryodes micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered bryodes, is a member of the Ericaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Bryodes micrantha is often found in acidic, well-drained soils in woodland and heathland habitats.
