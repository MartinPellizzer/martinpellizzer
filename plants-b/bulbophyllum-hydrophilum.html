Bulbophyllum hydrophilum

Bulbophyllum hydrophilum, commonly known as the water-loving orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its rhizomes are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
