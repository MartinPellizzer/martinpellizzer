Begonia dregei var. caffra

Begonia dregei var. caffra, commonly known as the Cape Begonia, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a rich mix of green, red, and purple hues. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
