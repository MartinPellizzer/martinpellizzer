Brownea coccinea subsp. coccinea

Brownea coccinea subsp.

coccinea, commonly known as the red-bloomed brownea, is a striking tropical shrub native to South America. It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which are typically clustered in dense, showy inflorescences.

The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and landscaped areas.
