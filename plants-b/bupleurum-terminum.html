Bupleurum terminum

Bupleurum terminum, commonly known as the slender valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in terminal clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Bupleurum terminum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
