Begonia scapigera subsp. australis

Begonia scapigera subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian begonia, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fleshy flowers in shades of pink and red.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
