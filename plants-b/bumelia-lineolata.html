Bumelia lineolata

Bumelia lineolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bumelia, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with a distinctive white vein pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.
