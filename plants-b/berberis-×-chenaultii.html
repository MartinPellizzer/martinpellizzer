Berberis × chenaultii

Berberis × chenaultii, commonly known as the Chenault barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it suitable for hedges or as a specimen plant in gardens.
