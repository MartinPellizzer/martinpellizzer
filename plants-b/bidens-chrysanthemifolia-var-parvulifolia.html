Bidens chrysanthemifolia var. parvulifolia

Bidens chrysanthemifolia var. parvulifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disks. It thrives in moist, sunny habitats such as swamps and wetlands.

Its small leaves and showy blooms make it an attractive addition to wetland gardens.
