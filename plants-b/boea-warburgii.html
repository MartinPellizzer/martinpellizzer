Boea warburgii

Boea warburgii, commonly known as the silver leaf vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped, silvery-green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Boea warburgii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
