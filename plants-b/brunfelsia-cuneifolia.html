Brunfelsia cuneifolia

Brunfelsia cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often appearing at night. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brunfelsia cuneifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.
