Barleria dinteri

Barleria dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's barleria, is a small, flowering shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Barleria dinteri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
