Biophytum longibracteatum

Biophytum longibracteatum, commonly known as the long-bract euphorbia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features striking yellow flowers that are surrounded by prominent, elongated bracts. Its leaves are usually alternate and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
