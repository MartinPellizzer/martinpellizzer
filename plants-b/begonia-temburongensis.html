Begonia temburongensis

Begonia temburongensis, commonly known as the Temburong begonia, is a rare flowering plant native to the Temburong district of Brunei.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in shaded, moist forest undergrowth. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers. It is characterized by its unique leaf venation patterns and is adapted to thrive in humid tropical environments.
