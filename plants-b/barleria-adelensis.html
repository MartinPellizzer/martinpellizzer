Barleria adelensis

Barleria adelensis, commonly known as the Cape barleria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue or purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, tubular flowers arranged in clusters. Barleria adelensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
