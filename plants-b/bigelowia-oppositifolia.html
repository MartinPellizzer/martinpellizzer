Bigelowia oppositifolia

Bigelowia oppositifolia, commonly known as the New Zealand bellflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue and violet.
