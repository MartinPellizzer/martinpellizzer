Bidens rhodesiana

Bidens rhodesiana, commonly known as the Rhodesian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features serrated leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and stream banks.

Bidens rhodesiana is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
