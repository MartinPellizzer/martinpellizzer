Berberis atrovirens

Berberis atrovirens, commonly known as the blackleaf barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its spiny branches and vibrant seasonal changes make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
