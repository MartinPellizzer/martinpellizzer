Bulbophyllum mananjarense

Bulbophyllum mananjarense, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a spike, with petals that vary in color from white to pink.

The species is noted for its striking appearance and is appreciated in horticultural collections worldwide.
