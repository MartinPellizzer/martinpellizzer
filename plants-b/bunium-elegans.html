Bunium elegans

Bunium elegans, commonly known as the elegant fennel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like parsley and carrots. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in umbels. Its seeds are small and have a delicate, anise-like fragrance.

Bunium elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
