Beckwithia Andersonii

Beckwithia andersonii is a rare and unique plant species native to the high elevations of the Appalachian Mountains. It is known for its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring. This delicate plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky crevices. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Beckwithia andersonii is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
