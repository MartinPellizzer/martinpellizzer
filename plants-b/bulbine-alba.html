Bulbine alba

Bulbine alba, commonly known as the white bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the warmer months. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its resilience and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
