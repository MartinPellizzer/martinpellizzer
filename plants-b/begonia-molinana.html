Begonia molinana

Begonia molinana, commonly known as the Molinana begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia molinana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
