Bourreria panamensis

Bourreria panamensis, commonly known as Panama burr, is a shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry forests and along roadsides.
