Bulbophyllum saltatorium var. albociliatum

Bulbophyllum saltatorium var. albociliatum, commonly known as the white-ciliated bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with contrasting markings. Its pseudobulbs are clustered and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
