Bulbophyllum josii

Bulbophyllum josii, commonly known as the Josii's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring contrasting hues on its petals. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in less-than-ideal conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
