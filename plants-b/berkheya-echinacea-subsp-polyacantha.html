Berkheya echinacea subsp. polyacantha

Berkheya echinacea subsp.

polyacantha, commonly known as the African coneflower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa. It features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and pink to red petals, blooming in spring and summer.

The plant has a robust root system and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its subsp. polyacantha variety is distinguished by its more branched stems and longer, more numerous bracts compared to other subspecies.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
