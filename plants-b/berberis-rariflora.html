Berberis rariflora

Berberis rariflora, commonly known as the rare-flowered barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features upright, thorny stems and elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and seasonal interest.
