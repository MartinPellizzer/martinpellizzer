Begonia lansatensis

Begonia lansatensis, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike.
