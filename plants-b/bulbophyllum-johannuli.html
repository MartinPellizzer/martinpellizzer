Bulbophyllum johannuli

Bulbophyllum johannuli, commonly known as the Johannuli's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of yellow and green. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
