Begonia abaculoides

Begonia abaculoides, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves with a velvety texture, which help it reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pale pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
