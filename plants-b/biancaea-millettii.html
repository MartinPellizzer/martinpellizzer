Biancaea millettii

Biancaea millettii, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong fragrance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
