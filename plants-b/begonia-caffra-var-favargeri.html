Begonia caffra var. favargeri

Begonia caffra var. favargeri, commonly known as the Cape begonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
