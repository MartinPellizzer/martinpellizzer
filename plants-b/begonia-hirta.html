Begonia hirta

Begonia hirta, commonly known as the woolly begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Begonia hirta is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
