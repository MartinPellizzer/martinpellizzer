Begonia poilanei

Begonia poilanei, commonly known as the Poliane begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Begonia poilanei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
