Brizopyrum uninervium

Brizopyrum uninervium, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Brizopyrum uninervium plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
