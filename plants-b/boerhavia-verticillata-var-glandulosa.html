Boerhavia verticillata var. glandulosa

Boerhavia verticillata var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular boerhavia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to harsh environments and a common sight in its native range.
