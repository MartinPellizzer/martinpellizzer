Blitanthus nepalensis

Blitanthus nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal blitanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in dense clusters.

It plays a significant role in the local ecosystem, providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
