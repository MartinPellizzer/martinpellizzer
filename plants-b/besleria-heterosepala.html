Besleria heterosepala

Besleria heterosepala, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
