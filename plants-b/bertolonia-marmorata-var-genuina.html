Bertolonia marmorata var. genuina

Bertolonia marmorata var. genuina, commonly known as the marble fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, feathery fronds that display a marbled pattern of light and dark green. The plant prefers shady, moist environments and thrives in well-drained, humus-rich soil. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.

It is relatively low-maintenance once established, making it suitable for both garden beds and container cultivation.
