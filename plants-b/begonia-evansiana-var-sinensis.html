Begonia evansiana var. sinensis

Begonia evansiana var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage, which is often the main attraction of the plant.
