Berberis bealei

Berberis bealei, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Berberis bealei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
