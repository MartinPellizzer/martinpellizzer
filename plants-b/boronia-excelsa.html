Boronia excelsa

Boronia excelsa, commonly known as the pink boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green appearance.

Boronia excelsa is often found in coastal heathlands and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
