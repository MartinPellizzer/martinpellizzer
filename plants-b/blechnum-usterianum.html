Blechnum usterianum

Blechnum usterianum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, and velvety fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of sori on the underside.

Blechnum usterianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
