Berberis × rubrostilla

Berberis × rubrostilla, commonly known as the red-flowered barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces bright orange to red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and showy flowers, which bloom in early spring.
