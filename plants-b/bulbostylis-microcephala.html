Bulbostylis microcephala

Bulbostylis microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, white or pale blue flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Bulbostylis microcephala is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and wetlands.
