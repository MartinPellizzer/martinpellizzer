Bourreria succulenta var. revoluta

Bourreria succulenta var. revoluta, commonly known as the revolute burrgrass, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy foliage that stores water, adapting to arid environments. The leaves are typically thick and waxy, with a bluish-green hue, and often have a slight revolute margin. It produces small, yellowish flowers in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and hardy nature.
