Bernardia geniculata

Bernardia geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging branches and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a leathery texture.

Bernardia geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
