Barleria eranthemoides var. eranthemoides

Barleria eranthemoides var. eranthemoides, commonly known as the yellow barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, branched stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
