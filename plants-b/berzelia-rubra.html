Berzelia rubra

Berzelia rubra, commonly known as the red velvet orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and velvety texture, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a complex arrangement of sepals and petals.

Berzelia rubra requires specific conditions to thrive, including high humidity and well-drained, nutrient-rich substrates.
