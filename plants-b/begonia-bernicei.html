Begonia bernicei

Begonia bernicei, commonly known as the Bernice begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia bernicei is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
