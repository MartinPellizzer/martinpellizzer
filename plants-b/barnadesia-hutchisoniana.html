Barnadesia hutchisoniana

Barnadesia hutchisoniana, commonly known as the Cape York daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

It is often found in coastal regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
