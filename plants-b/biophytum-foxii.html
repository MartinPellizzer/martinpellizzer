Biophytum foxii

Biophytum foxii, commonly known as the Cape yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling bells. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
