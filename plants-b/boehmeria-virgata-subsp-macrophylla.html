Boehmeria virgata subsp. macrophylla

Boehmeria virgata subsp.

macrophylla, commonly known as giant bamboo grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, giving it a striking appearance.
