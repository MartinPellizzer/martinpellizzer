Begonia binuangensis

Begonia binuangensis, commonly known as the Binuang begonia, is a tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia binuangensis is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
