Bryomorphe lycopodioides

Bryomorphe lycopodioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf clubmoss, is a small, evergreen plant belonging to the family Lycopodiales.

The plant produces sporangia in cone-like structures called strobili, which are typically found at the ends of its stems.
