Bromheadia annamensis

Bromheadia annamensis, commonly known as the Annam bromheadia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Bromheadiaceae and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, while its inflorescence consists of clustered, showy blooms.

Bromheadia annamensis is of interest to botanists due to its specialized morphology and ecological adaptations.
