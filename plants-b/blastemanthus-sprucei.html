Blastemanthus sprucei

Blastemanthus sprucei, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Blastemanthus sprucei is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
