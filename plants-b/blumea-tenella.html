Blumea tenella

Blumea tenella, commonly known as the slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or herb, with opposite, elliptic leaves that are often hairy. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, oval fruits.
