Bauhinia seminarioi

Bauhinia seminarioi, commonly known as the Seminario bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, papery, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.

Bauhinia seminarioi is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
