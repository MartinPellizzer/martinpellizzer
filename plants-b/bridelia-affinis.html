Bridelia affinis

Bridelia affinis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a leathery texture.
