Bulbostylis kirkii

Bulbostylis kirkii, commonly known as the Kirk's bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowering stems are usually very short, bearing small, white or pale blue flowers.

It is often found in marshy areas and is an important component of wetland ecosystems.
