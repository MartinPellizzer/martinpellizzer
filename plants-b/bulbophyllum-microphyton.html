Bulbophyllum microphyton

Bulbophyllum microphyton, commonly known as the miniature leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
