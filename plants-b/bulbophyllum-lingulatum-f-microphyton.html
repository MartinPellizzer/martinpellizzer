Bulbophyllum lingulatum f. microphyton

Bulbophyllum lingulatum f. microphyton, commonly known as the miniature leaf orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and unique floral structure, which consists of slender, pale yellow or greenish-white petals. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically short-lived but attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and flies.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
