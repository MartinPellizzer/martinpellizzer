Brassia lanceana var. viridiflora

Brassia lanceana var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features slender, upright pseudobulbs that produce dark green leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is the vibrant greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
