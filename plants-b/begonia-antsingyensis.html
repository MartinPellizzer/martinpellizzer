Begonia antsingyensis

Begonia antsingyensis, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Madagascar.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
