Bidens rostrata

Bidens rostrata, commonly known as the beakweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellow flowers with a distinctive beak-like structure at the base of each bloom. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, with a rough texture.

Bidens rostrata is often found in moist environments such as marshes and stream banks.
