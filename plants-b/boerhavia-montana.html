Boerhavia montana

Boerhavia montana, commonly known as the mountain bluemouth, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Boerhavia montana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.
