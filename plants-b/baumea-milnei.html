Baumea milnei

Baumea milnei, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage remains lush throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Baumea milnei is also valued for its ecological role, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
