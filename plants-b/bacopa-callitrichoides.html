Bacopa callitrichoides

Bacopa callitrichoides, commonly known as small water bead, is a small aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It produces tiny blue or violet flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Bacopa callitrichoides is often used in aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
