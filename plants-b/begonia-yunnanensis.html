Begonia yunnanensis

Begonia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as mountain forests. Its leaves are often heart-shaped or ovate, with a waxy texture.

Begonia yunnanensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
