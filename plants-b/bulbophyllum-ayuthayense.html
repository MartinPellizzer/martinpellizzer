Bulbophyllum ayuthayense

Bulbophyllum ayuthayense, commonly known as the Ayuthaya orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
