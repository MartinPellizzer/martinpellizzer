Brachyaster

Brachyaster is a genus of marine algae belonging to the family Compositae. It is commonly found in shallow coastal waters across the Pacific and Atlantic oceans. These algae are known for their unique branching structure and vibrant green color. They play a vital role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various species. Studying Brachyaster offers insights into oceanic biodiversity and ecological balance.
