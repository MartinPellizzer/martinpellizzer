Bulbophyllum uraiense

Bulbophyllum uraiense, commonly known as the Uraiense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers with a striking color palette that includes shades of yellow, green, and red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by horticulturists.
