Balantium blumei

Balantium blumei, commonly known as the Blume's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its slender, grass-like stems are often clustered, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Balantium blumei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
