Begonia kasutensis

Begonia kasutensis, commonly known as the Kasutensis begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia kasutensis is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
