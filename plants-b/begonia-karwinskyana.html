Begonia karwinskyana

Begonia karwinskyana, commonly known as the Karwinsky's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia karwinskyana is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
