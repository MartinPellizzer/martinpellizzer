Buddleja nana

Buddleja nana, commonly known as the dwarf butterfly bush, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, clustered flowers that attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Buddleja nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
