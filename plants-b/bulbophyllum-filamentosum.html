Bulbophyllum filamentosum

Bulbophyllum filamentosum, commonly known as the filamentous orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, filament-like pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
