Bletilla japonica

Bletilla japonica, commonly known as the Japanese orchid or lady's slipper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Bletilla japonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate and long-lasting blooms.
