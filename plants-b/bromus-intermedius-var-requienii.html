Bromus intermedius var. requienii

Bromus intermedius var. requienii, commonly known as the Requien's brome, is a perennial grass species native to parts of South America.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and grasslands. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
