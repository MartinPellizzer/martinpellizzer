Bomarea perlongipes

Bomarea perlongipes, commonly known as the long-footed bomarea, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant features climbing stems that can reach several meters in length, supported by adventitious roots. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a glossy surface that reflects light.

Bomarea perlongipes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, moist environments.
