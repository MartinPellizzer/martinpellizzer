Bombax angulicarpum var. houardii

Bombax angulicarpum var. houardii, commonly known as the African silk cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, pale pink to white flowers. This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
