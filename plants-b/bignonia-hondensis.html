Bignonia hondensis

Bignonia hondensis, commonly known as the Honduras trumpetflower, is a climbing vine native to Central America.

It produces vibrant yellow flowers that resemble trumpets, blooming in spring and summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Bignonia hondensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or supports.
