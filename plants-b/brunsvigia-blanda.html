Brunsvigia blanda

Brunsvigia blanda, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, spiky red bracts that resemble a lion's tail, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a striking addition to any garden.
