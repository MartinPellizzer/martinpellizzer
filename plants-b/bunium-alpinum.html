Bunium alpinum

Bunium alpinum, commonly known as alpine fennel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots, parsley, and dill, and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves. Bunium alpinum is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
