Bulbophyllum arachnidium

Bulbophyllum arachnidium, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, spider-like flowers with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. Its bulbous pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in variable conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
