Bulbophyllum caudatisepalum

Bulbophyllum caudatisepalum, commonly known as the long-sepal orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated sepals that are often brightly colored, giving it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
