Banisteria alternifolia

Banisteria alternifolia, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink and white bract-covered flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Banisteria alternifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
