Bidens napierae

Bidens napierae, commonly known as Napier's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Bidens napierae is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
