Brachystelma macropetalum

Brachystelma macropetalum, commonly known as the large-petaled stone orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers with large, spreading petals that bloom during the warmer months. It typically grows on rock faces or in rocky soils, adapting to arid and semi-arid environments.
