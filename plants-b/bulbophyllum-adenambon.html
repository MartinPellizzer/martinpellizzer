Bulbophyllum adenambon

Bulbophyllum adenambon, commonly known as the Adenambon orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its arid habitat. Its flowers typically feature a combination of white, yellow, and green hues, with intricate patterns.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
