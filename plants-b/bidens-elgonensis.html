Bidens elgonensis

Bidens elgonensis, commonly known as the Elgon spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with prominent central disc florets. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and grasslands. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and have a serrated edge.

Bidens elgonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional medicine.
