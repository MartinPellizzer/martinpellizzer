Bupleurum protractum subsp. heterophyllum

Bupleurum protractum subsp.

heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender stems and lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, ranging in color from white to pale yellow.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
