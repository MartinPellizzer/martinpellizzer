Bolanthus stenopetalus

Bolanthus stenopetalus, commonly known as the narrow-petaled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, oval fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Bolanthus stenopetalus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
