Begonia longicarpa

Begonia longicarpa, commonly known as the long-stalked begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces striking red flowers with long, slender petals that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
