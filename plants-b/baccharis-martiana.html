Baccharis martiana

Baccharis martiana, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a leathery texture.

Baccharis martiana is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
