Blaeria sagittata

Blaeria sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Blaeria sagittata is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage and its ability to thrive in humid environments.
