Bulbophyllum cyclosepalon

Bulbophyllum cyclosepalon, commonly known as the cyclosepalum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted for water storage.

Bulbophyllum cyclosepalon is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
