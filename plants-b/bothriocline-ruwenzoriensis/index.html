Bothriocline Ruwenzoriensis

Bothriocline ruwenzoriensis is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Ruwenzori Mountains in eastern Africa. This unique species is known for its striking violet-blue flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, cold climates. It was first discovered in the early 20th century and has since remained a subject of botanical fascination. Due to its limited habitat and fragile ecosystem, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. This plant represents a fascinating example of adaptation and biodiversity in one of Africa's most remote landscapes.
