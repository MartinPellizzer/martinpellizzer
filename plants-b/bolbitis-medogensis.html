Bolbitis medogensis

Bolbitis medogensis, commonly known as the Medog water fern, is a rare aquatic fern native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that resemble those of other water ferns, but it has unique adaptations for its cold, mountainous habitat. The plant thrives in clear, nutrient-rich streams and pools, where it plays a role in maintaining aquatic ecosystems. Its reproductive structures are concealed within small, spore-bearing capsules, which are released into the water.

Due to its limited distribution and fragile environment, Bolbitis medogensis is considered a species of conservation concern.
