Boucerosia nilagiriana

Boucerosia nilagiriana, commonly known as the Nilagiri boucerosia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the monsoon season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
