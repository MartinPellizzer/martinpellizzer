Bidens Aristosa F. Mutica

Bidens aristosa f. mutica is a lesser-known variant of the common beggar's ticks plant. It thrives in moist, often disturbed habitats across eastern North America. This fern-like plant features delicate, hairy leaves and small yellow flowers. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an intriguing subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in the broader plant world.
