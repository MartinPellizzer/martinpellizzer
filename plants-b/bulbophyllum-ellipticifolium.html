Bulbophyllum ellipticifolium

Bulbophyllum ellipticifolium, commonly known as the elliptic-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
