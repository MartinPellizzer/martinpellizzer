Boscia gymnosporiifolia

Boscia gymnosporiifolia, commonly known as the yellow boscia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green leaves and small, yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces woody, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Boscia gymnosporiifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
