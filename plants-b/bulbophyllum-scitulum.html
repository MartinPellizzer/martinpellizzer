Bulbophyllum scitulum

Bulbophyllum scitulum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is typically found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
