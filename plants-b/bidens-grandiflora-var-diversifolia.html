Bidens grandiflora var. diversifolia

Bidens grandiflora var. diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 2 to 3 feet tall, featuring upright stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with distinct variegation. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers with ray florets, which bloom from late summer into early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
