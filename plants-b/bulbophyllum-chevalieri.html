Bulbophyllum chevalieri

Bulbophyllum chevalieri, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
