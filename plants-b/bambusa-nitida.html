Bambusa nitida

Bambusa nitida, commonly known as the narrow leaf bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that are often used in landscaping and construction. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically a bright green color, providing an ornamental appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Bambusa nitida is a clumping bamboo, making it ideal for small garden spaces due to its compact growth habit.
