Bauhinia euryantha

Bauhinia euryantha, commonly known as the purple bauhinia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are often adorned with a purple or reddish tinge. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom during the rainy season. Bauhinia euryantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
