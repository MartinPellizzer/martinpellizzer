Blitum perenne

Blitum perenne, commonly known as perennial blite, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Blitum perenne is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
