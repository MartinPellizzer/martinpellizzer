Bulbophyllum fuscum var. melinostachyum

Bulbophyllum fuscum var. melinostachyum, commonly known as the dark-furred orchid, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, dark green pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with pale yellow to brown petals and sepals, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted for efficient water absorption.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical greenhouses.
