Breynia disticha f. nivosa

Breynia disticha f. nivosa, commonly known as the Snowy Breynia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, thriving in arid to semi-arid conditions.
