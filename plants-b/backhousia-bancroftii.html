Backhousia bancroftii

Backhousia bancroftii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Backhousia, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions.

Backhousia bancroftii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its aromatic foliage.
