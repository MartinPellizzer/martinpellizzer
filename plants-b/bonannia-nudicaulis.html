Bonannia nudicaulis

Bonannia nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender ground orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a single, spurred petal. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

Bonannia nudicaulis is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its unique floral structure and low-growing habit.
