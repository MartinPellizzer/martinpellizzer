Begonia polyantha

Begonia polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese everlasting, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces oval, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and blooms.

Begonia polyantha is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding continuous color to gardens.
