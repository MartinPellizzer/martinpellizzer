Babingtonia prominens

Babingtonia prominens, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that produces distinctive, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture, while its bark is smooth and often fissured.
