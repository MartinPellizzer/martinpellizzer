Begonia beddomei

Begonia beddomei, commonly known as the Beddome's begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Begonia beddomei is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is cultivated as a decorative plant in many parts of the world.
