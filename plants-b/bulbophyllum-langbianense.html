Bulbophyllum langbianense

Bulbophyllum langbianense, commonly known as the Langbian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its rhizome system allows it to store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
