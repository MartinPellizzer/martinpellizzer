Bania

Bania, commonly known as the Indian banyan tree, is a large tropical tree belonging to the Moraceae family.

It is characterized by its massive, buttressed roots and sprawling canopy that can cover vast areas. The tree is monoecious, meaning it has both male and female flowers on the same plant. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a fig-like structure that provides food for various animals and birds.
