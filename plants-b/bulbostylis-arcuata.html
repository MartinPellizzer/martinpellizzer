Bulbostylis arcuata

Bulbostylis arcuata, commonly known as the arcuate bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
