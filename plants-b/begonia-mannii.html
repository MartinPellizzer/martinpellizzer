Begonia mannii

Begonia mannii, commonly known as the Congo begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia mannii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
