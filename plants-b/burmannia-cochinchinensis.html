Burmannia cochinchinensis

Burmannia cochinchinensis, commonly known as the red-flowered burmannia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Burmanniaceae, characterized by its striking red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or swampy areas. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical, with a glossy surface, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.
