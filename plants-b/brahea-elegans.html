Brahea elegans

Brahea elegans, commonly known as the Mexican blue palm, is a striking evergreen palm native to Mexico.

It features a slender, blue-gray trunk with a distinctive crown of feathery, arching leaves. The plant is characterized by its slow growth rate and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Brahea elegans is well-suited to Mediterranean climates and requires minimal water once established.
