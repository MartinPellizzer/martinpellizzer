Bulbophyllum humblotianum

Bulbophyllum humblotianum, commonly known as the Humblot's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
