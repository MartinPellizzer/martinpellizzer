Brassaia volkensii

Brassaia volkensii, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Brassaia volkensii is also valued for its ability to purify air and its use in traditional medicine.
