Boschia grandiflora

Boschia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered boschia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of broad, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in dense, terminal clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
