Bulbophyllum masoalanum

Bulbophyllum masoalanum, commonly known as the Masoala orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color, with a distinctive lip structure. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

This species is notable for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
