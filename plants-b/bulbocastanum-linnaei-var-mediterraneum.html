Bulbocastanum linnaei var. mediterraneum

Bulbocastanum linnaei var. mediterraneum, commonly known as the Mediterranean bulbous heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous underground stem and small, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
