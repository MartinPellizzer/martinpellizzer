Bupleurum longicaule var. franchetii

Bupleurum longicaule var. franchetii, commonly known as Franchet's long-stemmed bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and compound umbels of small white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are pinnately divided with a distinctive, fern-like appearance. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
