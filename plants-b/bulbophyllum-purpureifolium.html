Bulbophyllum purpureifolium

Bulbophyllum purpureifolium, commonly known as the purple-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical and subtropical environments. It thrives in humid conditions with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
