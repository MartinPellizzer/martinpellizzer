Boronia rigens

Boronia rigens, commonly known as the rigid boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded location.
