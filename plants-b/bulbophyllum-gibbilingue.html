Bulbophyllum gibbilingue

Bulbophyllum gibbilingue, commonly known as the warty lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, warty appearance on its lip, which is a distinctive botanical feature. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and usually pale in color, with a complex structure typical of orchids.

It requires specific conditions to thrive, including high humidity and indirect light.
