Bossiaea arenicola

Bossiaea arenicola, commonly known as the sand bossiaea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal or inland dune environments. The plant features wiry stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in spring and early summer.

Bossiaea arenicola is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.
