Bulbophyllum nigricans

Bulbophyllum nigricans, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green to blackish leaves and relatively small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and delicate blooms make it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
