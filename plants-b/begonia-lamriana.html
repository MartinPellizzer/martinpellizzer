Begonia lamriana

Begonia lamriana, commonly known as the Malagasy begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Madagascar.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
