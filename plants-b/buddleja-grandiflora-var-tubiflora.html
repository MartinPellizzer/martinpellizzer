Buddleja grandiflora var. tubiflora

Buddleja grandiflora var. tubiflora, commonly known as the tubular buddleia, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its long, tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has fragrant, opposite leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
