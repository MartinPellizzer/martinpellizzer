Begonia sinobrevicaulis

Begonia sinobrevicaulis, commonly known as the Chinese short-stemmed begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and rockeries.
