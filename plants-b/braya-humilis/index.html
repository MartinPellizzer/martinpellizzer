Braya Humilis

Braya humilis is a small, flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many familiar vegetables and weeds. This low-growing species is often found in sandy soils and rocky habitats. Its simple, basal leaves and tiny, pale flowers make it easily overlooked. Despite its modest appearance, Braya humilis plays an important role in its ecosystem.
