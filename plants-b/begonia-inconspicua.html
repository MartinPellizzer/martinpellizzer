Begonia inconspicua

Begonia inconspicua, commonly known as the pale begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is valued for its ornamental foliage rather than its showy blooms.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a container plant.
