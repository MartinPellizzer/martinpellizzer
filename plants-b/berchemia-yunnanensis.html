Berchemia yunnanensis

Berchemia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan berchemia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive trifoliate structure, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in racemose inflorescences. Berchemia yunnanensis is known for its ornamental value, particularly in gardens where its vibrant blooms add visual interest.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.
