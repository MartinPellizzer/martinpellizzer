Bromus inermis var. malzevii

Bromus inermis var. malzevii, commonly known as the Malzevii brome, is a grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in arid regions.
