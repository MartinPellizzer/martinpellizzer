Browallia mionei

Browallia mionei, commonly known as the pink browallia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and produces vibrant pink or purple blooms. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
