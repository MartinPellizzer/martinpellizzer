Bactris trichospatha

Bactris trichospatha, commonly known as the pink pataua or pink palm, is a species of palm native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellow flowers, followed by edible, orange-colored fruits. Bactris trichospatha is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit in its native regions.
