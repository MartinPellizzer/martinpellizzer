Brachystelma brevipedicellatum

Brachystelma brevipedicellatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed starfish cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, globular stem with short, fleshy ribs that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warm season. Its leaves are usually reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

Brachystelma brevipedicellatum is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
