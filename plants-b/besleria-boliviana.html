Besleria boliviana

Besleria boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Bolivia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces showy, long-lasting blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
