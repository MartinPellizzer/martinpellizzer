Bulbophyllum liparidioides

Bulbophyllum liparidioides, commonly known as the Liparidoides orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with white markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
