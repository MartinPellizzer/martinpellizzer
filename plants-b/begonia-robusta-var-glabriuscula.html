Begonia robusta var. glabriuscula

Begonia robusta var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a soft texture. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
