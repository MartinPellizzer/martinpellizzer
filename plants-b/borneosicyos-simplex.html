Borneosicyos simplex

Borneosicyos simplex, commonly known as the Bornean bottle gourd, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucumbers. The plant produces large, inflated, bottle-shaped fruits that can grow up to 2 meters in length. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.
