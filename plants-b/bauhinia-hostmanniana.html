Bauhinia hostmanniana

Bauhinia hostmanniana, commonly known as the white bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers have five petals and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Bauhinia hostmanniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
