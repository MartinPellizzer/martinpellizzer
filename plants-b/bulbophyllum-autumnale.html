Bulbophyllum autumnale

Bulbophyllum autumnale, commonly known as the Autumn Ghost Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, ghost-like appearance with elongated, translucent pseudobulbs and pale green or white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowering season typically occurs in the fall, hence its common name.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
