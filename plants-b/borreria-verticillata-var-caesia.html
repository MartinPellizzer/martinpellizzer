Borreria verticillata var. caesia

Borreria verticillata var. caesia, commonly known as blue borreria, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue-violet flowers. The plant features opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Borreria verticillata var. caesia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
