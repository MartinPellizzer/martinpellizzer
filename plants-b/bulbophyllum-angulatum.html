Bulbophyllum angulatum

Bulbophyllum angulatum, commonly known as the angular bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, angular pseudobulbs and striking, often fluorescent flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, round pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum angulatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
