Bulbophyllum kenejiense

Bulbophyllum kenejiense, commonly known as the Kenejiens Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

The flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a variety of colors, making it a visually striking plant.
