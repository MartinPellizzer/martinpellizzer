Bertolonia linearifolia

Bertolonia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved bertolonia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and Mediterranean gardens.
