Bipinnula plumosa

Bipinnula plumosa, commonly known as the feathery fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a soft, airy appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, thriving in humid climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are bi-pinnate, meaning they are divided into two levels of smaller leaflets, creating a complex, lace-like structure.

This unique morphology makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscaping.
