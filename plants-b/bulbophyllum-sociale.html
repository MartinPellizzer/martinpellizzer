Bulbophyllum sociale

Bulbophyllum sociale, commonly known as the social orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, often hairy pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum sociale is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
