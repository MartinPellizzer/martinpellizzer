Brachyscome multifida var. multifida

Brachyscome multifida var. multifida, commonly known as the starry everlasting or starry daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, with a yellow center. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The flowers are long-lasting and remain open for extended periods, contributing to its ornamental value.
