Bidens tuberosa

Bidens tuberosa, commonly known as purpletop vervain, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with distinctive purple markings, which are clustered in groups of three. Bidens tuberosa is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
