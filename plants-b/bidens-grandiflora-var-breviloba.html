Bidens grandiflora var. breviloba

Bidens grandiflora var. breviloba, commonly known as the smallleaf tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets that are typically shorter than those of the typical species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and prairies.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and wildlife habitat.
