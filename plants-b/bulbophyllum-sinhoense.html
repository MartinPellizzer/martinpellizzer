Bulbophyllum sinhoense

Bulbophyllum sinhoense, commonly known as the Sinho's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow.

The plant's morphology includes pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in tropical environments.
