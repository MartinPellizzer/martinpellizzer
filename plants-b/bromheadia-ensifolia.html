Bromheadia ensifolia

Bromheadia ensifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf bromheadia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Bromheadiaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, giving it its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.

Bromheadia ensifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
