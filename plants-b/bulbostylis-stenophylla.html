Bulbostylis stenophylla

Bulbostylis stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small grass-like plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikelets.

Bulbostylis stenophylla is often found in moist, well-drained soils in open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.
