Brachionidium jesupiae

Brachionidium jesupiae, commonly known as the Jesup's brachionid, is a species of aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its small, branching structure and is often found in still or slow-moving water. The plant has delicate, thread-like leaves that can be easily overlooked. It plays a role in providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
