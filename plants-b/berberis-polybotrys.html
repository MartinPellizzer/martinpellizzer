Berberis polybotrys

Berberis polybotrys, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in erect inflorescences. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible but often have a sour taste. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in landscaping.

Berberis polybotrys is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
