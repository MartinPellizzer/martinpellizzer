Buchenroedera viminea

Buchenroedera viminea, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a unique tropical plant characterized by its striking, undulating fronds.

Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in rainforests. The plant's leaves are long and narrow, with a distinctive zigzag pattern that gives it its common name. It reproduces through spores, typical of its fern classification.

Buchenroedera viminea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
