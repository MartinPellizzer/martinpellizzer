Bletilla szetschuanica

Bletilla szetschuanica, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are long, strap-like, and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Bletilla szetschuanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant floral display.
