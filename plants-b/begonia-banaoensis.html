Begonia banaoensis

Begonia banaoensis, commonly known as the Banao begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
