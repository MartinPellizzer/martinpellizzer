Begonia spinibarbis

Begonia spinibarbis, commonly known as the spiny-barbed begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sparsely haired stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
