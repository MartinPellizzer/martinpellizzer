Berchemiella wilsonii var. pubipetiolata

Berchemiella wilsonii var. pubipetiolata, commonly known as the Wilson's berchemia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its stems are often woody and can become branched as the plant matures.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
