Breynia Retroversa

Breynia retroversa is a unique and striking plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. Known for its vibrant, often red or orange leaves, it stands out in any garden or landscape. This species belongs to the Breyniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive leaf orientation, which often curves backward. Despite its visual appeal, it is relatively rare and requires specific growing conditions to thrive. Its unusual appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
