Begonia rubropunctata

Begonia rubropunctata, commonly known as the red-spotted begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with red or purple spots. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia rubropunctata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
