Begonia randaiensis

Begonia randaiensis, commonly known as the Randa begonia, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique beauty and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
