Bauhinia Velutina

Velvet bean tree, scientifically known as Bauhinia velutina, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia and parts of Africa. This ornamental tree is prized for its striking, velvety leaves and clusters of vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. The tree also has traditional uses in medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds. Its unique appearance and versatility make it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
