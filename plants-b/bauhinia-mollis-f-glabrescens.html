Bauhinia mollis f. glabrescens

Bauhinia mollis f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bauhinia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

The smooth, glabrous texture of its leaves distinguishes this cultivar from other forms of Bauhinia mollis.
