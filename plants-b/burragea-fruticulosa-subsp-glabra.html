Burragea fruticulosa subsp. glabra

Burragea fruticulosa subsp.

glabra, commonly known as the smooth burragea, is a shrub native to Australia. It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are smooth and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

This species is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its ornamental qualities.
