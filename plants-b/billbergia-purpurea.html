Billbergia purpurea

Billbergia purpurea, commonly known as the purple billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple foliage and long, arching leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia purpurea is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
