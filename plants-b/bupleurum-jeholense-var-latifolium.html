Bupleurum jeholense var. latifolium

Bupleurum jeholense var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf Chinese angelica, is a perennial herb native to northern China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant features compound leaves with serrated margins and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
