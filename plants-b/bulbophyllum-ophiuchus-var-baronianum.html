Bulbophyllum ophiuchus var. baronianum

Bulbophyllum ophiuchus var. baronianum, commonly known as the Baronia orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and resilience.
