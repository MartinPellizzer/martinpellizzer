Begonia schimpffii

Begonia schimpffii, commonly known as the Schimpff's begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
