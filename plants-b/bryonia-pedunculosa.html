Bryonia pedunculosa

Bryonia pedunculosa, commonly known as the slender bryony, is a climbing plant belonging to the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by green, spiny fruits. It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

Bryonia pedunculosa is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
