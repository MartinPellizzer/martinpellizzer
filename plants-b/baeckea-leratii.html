Baeckea leratii

Baeckea leratii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Baeckea, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically grey-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or cream-colored flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, blooming in spring. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal and tableland regions.

Baeckea leratii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
