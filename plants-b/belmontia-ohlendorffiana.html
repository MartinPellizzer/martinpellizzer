Belmontia ohlendorffiana

Belmontia ohlendorffiana, commonly known as the Ochre Trumpet, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, showy, orange-yellow flowers that resemble trumpets, which bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Belmontia ohlendorffiana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
