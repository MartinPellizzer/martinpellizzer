Besleria nemorosa

Besleria nemorosa, commonly known as the forest gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
