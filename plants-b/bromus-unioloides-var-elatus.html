Bromus unioloides var. elatus

Bromus unioloides var. elatus, commonly known as the tall brome, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, fields, and meadows. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a single, spike-like cluster of spikelets.

This variety is often noted for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
