Bulbophyllum sempiternum

Bulbophyllum sempiternum, commonly known as the eternal orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences. The plant produces numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
