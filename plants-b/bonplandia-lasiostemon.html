Bonplandia lasiostemon

Bonplandia lasiostemon, commonly known as the golden torch tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble torches, blooming throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for tropical gardens and landscapes.
