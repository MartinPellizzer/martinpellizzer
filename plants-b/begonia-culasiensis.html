Begonia culasiensis

Begonia culasiensis, commonly known as the Culasi begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

Begonia culasiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
