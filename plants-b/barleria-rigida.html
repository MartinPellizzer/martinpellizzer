Barleria rigida

Barleria rigida, commonly known as rigid barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive blue or purple coloration. Its flowers are small, tubular, and clustered in dense spikes, often with a violet or blue hue.

Barleria rigida is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
