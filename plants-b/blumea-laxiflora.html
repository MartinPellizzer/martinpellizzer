Blumea laxiflora

Blumea laxiflora, commonly known as the slender flowered blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, oval fruits.

Blumea laxiflora is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
