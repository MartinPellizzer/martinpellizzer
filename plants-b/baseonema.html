Baseonema

Baseonema, commonly known as the "false broomrape," is a parasitic plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It lacks chlorophyll and therefore cannot perform photosynthesis, relying entirely on its host for nutrients. Due to its parasitic nature, it can have negative effects on the growth and survival of its host plants.
