Beschorneria yuccoides subsp. yuccoides

Beschorneria yuccoides subsp.

yuccoides, commonly known as the Yucca-like Yucca, is a succulent plant native to Mexico. It features rosettes of sword-shaped, blue-green leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
