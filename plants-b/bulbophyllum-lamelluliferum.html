Bulbophyllum lamelluliferum

Bulbophyllum lamelluliferum, commonly known as the leafy lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and fleshy roots that help it store water in its natural habitat. It produces striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns on the lip, which often feature a unique, leaf-like structure. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to light and moisture levels.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
