Bomarea elegans var. amoena

Bomarea elegans var. amoena, commonly known as the pink bomarea, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its aesthetic value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses as a decorative plant.
