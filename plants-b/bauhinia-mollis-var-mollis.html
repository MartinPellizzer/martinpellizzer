Bauhinia mollis var. mollis

Bauhinia mollis var. mollis, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to white in color. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia mollis var. mollis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in ecological restoration projects.
