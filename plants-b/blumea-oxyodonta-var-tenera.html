Blumea oxyodonta var. tenera

Blumea oxyodonta var. tenera, commonly known as the hairy blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in inflorescences, often blooming during the rainy season.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
