Begonia suaveolens

Begonia suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is known for its pleasant, spicy aroma, which is especially noticeable when the flowers are in bloom. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and delicate floral display.
