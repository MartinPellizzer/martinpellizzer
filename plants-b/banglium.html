Banglium

Banglium, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in long, hanging panicles. The plant belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and it has compound leaves with multiple leaflets. Banglium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
