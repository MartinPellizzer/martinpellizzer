Bigelovia setidens var. linearifolia

Bigelovia setidens var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bigelovia, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to arid landscapes.
