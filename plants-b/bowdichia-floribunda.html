Bowdichia floribunda

Bowdichia floribunda, commonly known as the Brazilian bottle tree, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its unique bottle-shaped trunk and showy yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are bipinnate, and it produces pods containing seeds.

Bowdichia floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine.
