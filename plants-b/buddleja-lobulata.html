Buddleja lobulata

Buddleja lobulata, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract butterflies and other pollinators. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in gardens.
