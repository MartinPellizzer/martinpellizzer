Begonia laotica

Begonia laotica, commonly known as the Laotian begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Laos and Vietnam.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually glossy and have a distinctive veining pattern, while its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Begonia laotica prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
