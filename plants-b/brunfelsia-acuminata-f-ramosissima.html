Brunfelsia acuminata f. ramosissima

Brunfelsia acuminata f. ramosissima, commonly known as the purple angel's trumpets, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces clusters of fragrant blooms, often appearing in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants.
