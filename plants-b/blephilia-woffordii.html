Blephilia woffordii

Blephilia woffordii, commonly known as Wofford's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, sky-blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.
