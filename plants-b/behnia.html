Behnia

Behnia, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its succulent characteristics. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
