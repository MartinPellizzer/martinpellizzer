Betonica annua var. glabra

Betonica annua var. glabra, commonly known as smooth betony, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Betonica annua var. glabra is known for its medicinal properties, historically used to treat ailments such as respiratory and digestive issues.
