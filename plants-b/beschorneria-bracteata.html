Beschorneria bracteata

Beschorneria bracteata, commonly known as the Mexican bract palm, is a striking evergreen plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Agavaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of long, narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white flowers, followed by orange-red berries. Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a desirable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
