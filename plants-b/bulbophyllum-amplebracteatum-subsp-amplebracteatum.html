Bulbophyllum amplebracteatum subsp. amplebracteatum

Bulbophyllum amplebracteatum subsp.

amplebracteatum, commonly known as the broad-bract Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments.

The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant orchid flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted for water storage in its natural habitat.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
