Bihai pulverulenta

Bihai pulverulenta, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a unique species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pale green to white, fleshy leaves that resemble a ghostly appearance, hence its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

Bihai pulverulenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
