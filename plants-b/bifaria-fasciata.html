Bifaria fasciata

Bifaria fasciata, commonly known as the striped orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often marked with distinct color patterns. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in a loose inflorescence.

Bifaria fasciata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
