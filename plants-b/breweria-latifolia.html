Breweria latifolia

Breweria latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing along rocky outcrops and in sandy soils. Its fibrous roots help it retain moisture in dry conditions.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its adaptability to harsh climates.
