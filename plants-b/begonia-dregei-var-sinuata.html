Begonia dregei var. sinuata

Begonia dregei var. sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that have a distinctive wavy or sinuous margin, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush foliage.

This variety is popular among collectors for its unique leaf patterns and vibrant floral display.
