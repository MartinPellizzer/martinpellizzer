Bulbophyllum odontostigma

Bulbophyllum odontostigma, commonly known as the toothed lip orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive inflorescence that bears numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The orchid's flowers are characterized by their intricate, spurred petals and a unique, tooth-like structure on the lip.
