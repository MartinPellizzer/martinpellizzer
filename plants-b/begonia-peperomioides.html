Begonia peperomioides

Begonia peperomioides, commonly known as the silver velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a unique, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
