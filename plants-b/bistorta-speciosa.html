Bistorta speciosa

Bistorta speciosa, commonly known as the showy bistort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features upright, rhizomatous stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its striking, clustered inflorescences produce bright pink to red flowers, which bloom in late summer.

Bistorta speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
