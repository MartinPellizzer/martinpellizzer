Boswellia Socotrana Subsp. Asplenifolia

Boswellia socotrana subsp. asplenifolia is a rare and unique plant native to the Socotra archipelago. It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is known for its distinctive leaf shape and medicinal properties. This subspecies is characterized by its leathery leaves that resemble those of the asplenium fern, hence its name. It thrives in the arid and rocky environments of Socotra, where it plays an important ecological role. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
