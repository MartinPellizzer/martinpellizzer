Brickellia californica var. jepsonii

Brickellia californica var. jepsonii, commonly known as Jepson's brickellia, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. This variety typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom in the spring.

The plant is often found in chaparral and coastal sage scrub ecosystems.
