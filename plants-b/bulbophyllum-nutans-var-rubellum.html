Bulbophyllum nutans var. rubellum

Bulbophyllum nutans var. rubellum, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red sepals and petals, which contrast beautifully with its green foliage. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its bulb-like structures, known as pseudobulbs, store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its natural habitat.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
