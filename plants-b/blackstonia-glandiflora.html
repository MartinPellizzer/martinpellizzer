Blackstonia glandiflora

Blackstonia glandiflora, commonly known as the glandular blackstonia, is a species of flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. Blackstonia glandiflora thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands.
