Babiana tritonioides

Babiana tritonioides, commonly known as the Orange Babiana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It produces a bulbous base that stores nutrients, allowing it to survive dry periods.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
