Bidens hyperborea var. laurentiana

Bidens hyperborea var. laurentiana, commonly known as the Laurentian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the colder regions of North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. It features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers with dark centers.

Its hardy nature makes it a resilient plant in temperate and subarctic climates.
