Bulbocapnos fabacea

Bulbocapnos fabacea, commonly known as the silverleaf clematis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately divided and have a silvery underside, giving it its common name.

The plant prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable ornamental for gardens and natural habitats.
