Bombax insigne subsp. genuina

Bombax insigne subsp.

genuina, commonly known as the West African cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
