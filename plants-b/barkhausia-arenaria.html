Barkhausia arenaria

Barkhausia arenaria, commonly known as the sand lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers. It is often found in coastal or inland sandy areas, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
