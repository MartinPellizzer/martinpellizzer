Brachypodium pinnatum f. gracile

Brachypodium pinnatum f. gracile, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged along the stem, giving it a delicate appearance. This plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
