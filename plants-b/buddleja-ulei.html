Buddleja ulei

Buddleja ulei, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja ulei is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms and easy maintenance.
