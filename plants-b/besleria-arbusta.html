Besleria arbusta

Besleria arbusta, commonly known as the compact bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial that typically grows to a height of about 15-30 centimeters. The plant features rosettes of narrow, strap-shaped leaves that are usually green with a reddish or purple tinge along the edges. It produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences that are often red or orange in color.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
