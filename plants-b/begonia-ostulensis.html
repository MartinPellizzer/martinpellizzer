Begonia ostulensis

Begonia ostulensis, commonly known as the Crimson Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia ostulensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
