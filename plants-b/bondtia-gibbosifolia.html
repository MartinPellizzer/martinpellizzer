Bondtia gibbosifolia

Bondtia gibbosifolia, commonly known as the globular milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical, hairy stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Its unique appearance and medicinal properties make it a notable species in its native habitat.
