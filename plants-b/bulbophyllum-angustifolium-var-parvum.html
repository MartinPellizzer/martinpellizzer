Bulbophyllum angustifolium var. parvum

Bulbophyllum angustifolium var. parvum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree bark or in shaded forest understories. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species in the wild.
