Begonia cinnabarina

Begonia cinnabarina, commonly known as the flame begonia, is a strikingly colorful plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
