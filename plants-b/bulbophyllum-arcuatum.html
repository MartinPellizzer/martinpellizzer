Bulbophyllum arcuatum

Bulbophyllum arcuatum, commonly known as the curved lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a slender, curved labellum that distinguishes it from other orchid species. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Bulbophyllum arcuatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
