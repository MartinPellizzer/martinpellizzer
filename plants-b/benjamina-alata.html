Benjamina alata

Benjamina alata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves with wing-like lobes, and its pods contain edible seeds and beans. The plant is often grown for its high-protein seeds, which are used in various cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Benjamina alata also has ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
