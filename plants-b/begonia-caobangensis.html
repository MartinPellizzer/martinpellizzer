Begonia caobangensis

Begonia caobangensis, commonly known as the Caobang begonia, is a rare flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy texture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
