Boswellia undulatocrenata

Boswellia undulatocrenata, commonly known as the Socotra cypress, is a unique evergreen conifer native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, twisted trunk and feathery, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by winged seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited range and ecological significance, it is considered a vulnerable species.
