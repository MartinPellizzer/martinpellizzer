Bulbophyllum aeolium

Bulbophyllum aeolium, commonly known as the wind orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its aerial roots for moisture and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Bulbophyllum aeolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
