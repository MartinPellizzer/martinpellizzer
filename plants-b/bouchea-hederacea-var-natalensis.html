Bouchea hederacea var. natalensis

Bouchea hederacea var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal ivy, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
