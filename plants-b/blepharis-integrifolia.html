Blepharis integrifolia

Blepharis integrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf blepharis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically smooth and entire, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
