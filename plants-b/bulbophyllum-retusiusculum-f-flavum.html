Bulbophyllum retusiusculum f. flavum

Bulbophyllum retusiusculum f. flavum, commonly known as the Yellow Bulbophyllum, is a fascinating orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking yellow flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
