Bikkia mariannensis

Bikkia mariannensis, commonly known as the Marianas bikkia, is a flowering plant native to the Northern Mariana Islands.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and lowland habitats. The plant features simple, alternate leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native region.

Bikkia mariannensis is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological role in its environment.
