Begonia abdullahpieei

Begonia abdullahpieei, commonly known as the Abdullahpieei begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
