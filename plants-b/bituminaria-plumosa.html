Bituminaria plumosa

Bituminaria plumosa, commonly known as the silky bituminaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant features silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Bituminaria plumosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
