Barleria involucrata var. elata

Barleria involucrata var. elata, commonly known as the tall barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features erect, branching stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
