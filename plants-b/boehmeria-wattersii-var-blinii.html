Boehmeria wattersii var. blinii

Boehmeria wattersii var. blinii, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or blinii nettle, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes many stinging plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.
