Barleria acanthoides var. gracilispina

Barleria acanthoides var. gracilispina, commonly known as slender-spined barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often hairy and bear small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
