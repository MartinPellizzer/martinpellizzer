Berberis gongshanensis

Berberis gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces bright orange-red berries that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
