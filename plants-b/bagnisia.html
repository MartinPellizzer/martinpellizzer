Bagnisia

Bagnisia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking silver-gray foliage with a velvety texture, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight. Bagnisia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.

It is relatively low maintenance but benefits from regular misting to maintain its lush appearance.
