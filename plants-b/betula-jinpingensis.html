Betula jinpingensis

Betula jinpingensis, commonly known as the Jinping birch, is a rare species native to China.

It is characterized by its white bark and small, inconspicuous leaves. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, typically at high altitudes. It belongs to the Betulaceae family, which includes other birch and alder species.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
