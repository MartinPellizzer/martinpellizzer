Bonnaya vahlii

Bonnaya vahlii, commonly known as the desert rose, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of showy, pink or red flowers. Its leaves are small, narrow, and often covered in a fine, white powder.

Bonnaya vahlii is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
