Berlinia splendida

Berlinia splendida, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree has compound leaves that are opposite and often shed during the dry season. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in yellow or greenish-white colors.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
