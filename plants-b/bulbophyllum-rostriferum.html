Bulbophyllum rostriferum

Bulbophyllum rostriferum, commonly known as the Rosy Lip Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive rosy-pink coloration on the lip. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum rostriferum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
