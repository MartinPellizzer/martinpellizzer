Boisduvalia densiflora var. pallescens

Boisduvalia densiflora var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale-flowered boisduvalia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
