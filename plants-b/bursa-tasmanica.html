Bursa tasmanica

Bursa tasmanica, commonly known as the Tasmanian burr, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, spiky fruits that are adapted for seed dispersal by attaching to animals. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Bursa tasmanica thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
