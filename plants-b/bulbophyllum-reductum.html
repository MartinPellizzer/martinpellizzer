Bulbophyllum reductum

Bulbophyllum reductum, commonly known as the reduced orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its bulb-like pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum reductum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
