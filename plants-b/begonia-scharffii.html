Begonia scharffii

Begonia scharffii, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green foliage and clusters of small, delicate pink or red flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-draining soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are often variegated with white and green patterns, adding visual interest to gardens or containers.

Begonia scharffii is relatively easy to care for, requiring regular watering and moderate light conditions.
