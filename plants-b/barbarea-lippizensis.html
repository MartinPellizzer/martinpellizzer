Barbarea lippizensis

Barbarea lippizensis, commonly known as the Lippizans rocket, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Austria.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Barbarea lippizensis is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
