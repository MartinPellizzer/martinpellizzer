Barleria mitis

Barleria mitis, commonly known as the silverleaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Barleria mitis is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and compact size.
