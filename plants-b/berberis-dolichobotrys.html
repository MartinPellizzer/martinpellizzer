Berberis dolichobotrys

Berberis dolichobotrys, commonly known as the long-stemmed barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features thorny stems and small, clustered leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
