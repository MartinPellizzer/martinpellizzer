Boehmeria palmeri

Boehmeria palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's hemp, is a flowering plant in the nettle family, Urticaceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its fibrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture.
