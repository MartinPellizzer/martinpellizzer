Brachycorythis ovata var. schweinfurthii

Brachycorythis ovata var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red throat. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
