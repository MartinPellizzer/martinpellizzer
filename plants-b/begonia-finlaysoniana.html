Begonia finlaysoniana

Begonia finlaysoniana, commonly known as the Finlayson's begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or red, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
