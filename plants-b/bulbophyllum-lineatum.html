Bulbophyllum lineatum

Bulbophyllum lineatum, commonly known as the lineated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, linear leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Bulbophyllum lineatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
