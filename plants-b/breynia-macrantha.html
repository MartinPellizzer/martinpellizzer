Breynia macrantha

Breynia macrantha, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like clusters of bright red or orange flowers that appear throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Breynia macrantha is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh, arid conditions.
