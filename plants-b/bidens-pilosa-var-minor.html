Bidens pilosa var. minor

Bidens pilosa var. minor, commonly known as lesser horseweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are yellow with ray florets, and it produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some areas.
