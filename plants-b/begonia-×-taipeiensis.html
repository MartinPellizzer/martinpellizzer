Begonia × taipeiensis

Begonia × taipeiensis, commonly known as the Taipei begonia, is a hybrid ornamental plant prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is native to Taiwan, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of tubular, often red or pink, flowers. It is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas due to its sensitivity to direct sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both indoor decoration and botanical collections.
