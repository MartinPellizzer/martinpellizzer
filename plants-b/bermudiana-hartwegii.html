Bermudiana hartwegii

Bermudiana hartwegii, commonly known as the Bermuda buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which typically bloom in spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, sunny habitats. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes other well-known species like the buttercup.
