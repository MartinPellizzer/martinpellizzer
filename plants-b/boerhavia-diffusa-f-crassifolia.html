Boerhavia diffusa f. crassifolia

Boerhavia diffusa f. crassifolia, commonly known as broadleaf borage, is a variant of the common borage species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical features of this plant make it adaptable to various environments, contributing to its widespread distribution.
