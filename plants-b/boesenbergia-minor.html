Boesenbergia minor

Boesenbergia minor, commonly known as the lesser ginger or dwarf ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and turmeric. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.
