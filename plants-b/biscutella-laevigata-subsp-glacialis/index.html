Biscutella Laevigata Subsp. Glacialis

Biscutella laevigata subsp. glacialis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in cold, rocky environments where few other plants can survive. This subspecies is characterized by its small, white flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from climate change and habitat loss. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
