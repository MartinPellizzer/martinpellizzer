Begonia integerrima var. cardioides

Begonia integerrima var. cardioides, commonly known as the heart-shaped begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its unique foliage and colorful blooms, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
