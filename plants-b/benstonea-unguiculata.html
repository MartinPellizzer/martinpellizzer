Benstonea unguiculata

Benstonea unguiculata, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like structures on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Benstonea unguiculata is considered a conservation concern.
