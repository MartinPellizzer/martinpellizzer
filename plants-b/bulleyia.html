Bulleyia

Bulleyia, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Bulleyia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
