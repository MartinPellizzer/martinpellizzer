Berchemia sessiliflora

Berchemia sessiliflora, commonly known as the rock fig or hardy fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy coating.

Berchemia sessiliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid environments.
