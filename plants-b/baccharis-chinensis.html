Baccharis chinensis

Baccharis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese baccharis, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in moist or wetland habitats, such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive silvery underside.

Baccharis chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
