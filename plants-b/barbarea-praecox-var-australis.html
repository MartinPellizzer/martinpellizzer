Barbarea praecox var. australis

Barbarea praecox var. australis, commonly known as the Australian winter cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has alternate, lobed leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in coastal regions and riverbanks.

This species is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
