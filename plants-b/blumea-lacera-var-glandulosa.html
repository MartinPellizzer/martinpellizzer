Blumea lacera var. glandulosa

Blumea lacera var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular blumea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters, and its leaves are often covered with glandular dots, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
