Bosistoa pentacocca subsp. connaricarpa

Bosistoa pentacocca subsp.

connaricarpa, commonly known as the broad-leaved bosistoa, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, lowland rainforest environments and is often found in secondary growth areas.

The species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is occasionally used in traditional medicine.
