Begonia platycarpa

Begonia platycarpa, commonly known as the round-fruited begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a distinctive round, fleshy capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is popular in tropical gardens for its ornamental value.
