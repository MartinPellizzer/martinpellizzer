Bulbophyllum microdendron

Bulbophyllum microdendron, commonly known as the miniature tree orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, tree-like growth habit with a pseudobulb and a dense cluster of leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
