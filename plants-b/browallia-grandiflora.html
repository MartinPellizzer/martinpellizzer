Browallia grandiflora

Browallia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered browallia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, featuring hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers come in shades of blue, purple, and white, and are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
