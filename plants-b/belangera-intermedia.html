Belangera intermedia

Belangera intermedia, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Belangera intermedia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
