Bauhinia finlaysoniana

Bauhinia finlaysoniana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and clusters of showy pink or purple flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Bauhinia finlaysoniana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
