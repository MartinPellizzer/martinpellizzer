Baeckea jucunda

Baeckea jucunda, commonly known as the golden baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Baeckea jucunda is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
