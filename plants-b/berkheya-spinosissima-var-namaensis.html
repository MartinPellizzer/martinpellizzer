Berkheya spinosissima var. namaensis

Berkheya spinosissima var. namaensis, commonly known as theNamaena orNamaena Berkheya, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features spiny, woody stems and produces striking yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
