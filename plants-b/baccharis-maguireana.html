Baccharis maguireana

Baccharis maguireana, commonly known as Maguire's baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale yellow, which appear in the summer months. It is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Baccharis maguireana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in coastal environments.
