Bupleurum glumaceum var. thracicum

Bupleurum glumaceum var. thracicum, commonly known as the Thrace Bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to southeastern Europe.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, usually appearing in shades of white to pale blue.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
