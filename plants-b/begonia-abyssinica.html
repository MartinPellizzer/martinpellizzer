Begonia abyssinica

Begonia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian begonia, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically borne on slender stems. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners interested in rare tropical plants.
