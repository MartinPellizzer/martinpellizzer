Buchanania acuminatissima

Buchanania acuminatissima, commonly known as the narrow-pointed Buchanania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.
