Begonia Cordifolia

Begonia cordifolia is a striking tropical plant known for its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flowers. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage displays deep green hues with intricate patterns that add visual interest. The plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. With proper attention, Begonia cordifolia can flourish indoors or in outdoor gardens.
