Bancroftia decipiens

Bancroftia decipiens, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.
