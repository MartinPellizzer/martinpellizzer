Begonia pleiopetala

Begonia pleiopetala, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
