Begonia yunnanensis var. hypoleuca

Begonia yunnanensis var. hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive white or pale green variegation. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, with a white throat, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms, it is often cultivated for both its aesthetic value and its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor environments.
