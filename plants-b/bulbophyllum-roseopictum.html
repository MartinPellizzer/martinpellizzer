Bulbophyllum roseopictum

Bulbophyllum roseopictum, commonly known as the rose-patterned orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, colorful patterns on its pseudobulbs and leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
