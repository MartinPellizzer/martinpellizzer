Boophone toxicaria var. obtusifolia

Boophone toxicaria var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa, characterized by its striking blue to purple bell-shaped flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of showy, tubular blooms. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
