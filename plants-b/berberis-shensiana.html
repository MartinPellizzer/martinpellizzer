Berberis shensiana

Berberis shensiana, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often sour. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The species is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties.
