Brugmansia × amesianum

Brugmansia × amesianum, commonly known as the angel's trumpets, is a hybrid species within the genus Brugmansia, native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be white, pink, or yellow, depending on the cultivar. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with smooth, greyish bark and opposite, simple, ovate leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
