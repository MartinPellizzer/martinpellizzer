Bulbophyllum sanderianum

Bulbophyllum sanderianum, commonly known as the Sander's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Bulbophyllum sanderianum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
