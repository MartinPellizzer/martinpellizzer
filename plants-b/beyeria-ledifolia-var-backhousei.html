Beyeria ledifolia var. backhousei

Beyeria ledifolia var. backhousei, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant orange to red tubular flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
