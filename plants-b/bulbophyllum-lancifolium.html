Bulbophyllum lancifolium

Bulbophyllum lancifolium, commonly known as the lancifolium orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its long, narrow leaves and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Bulbophyllum lancifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
