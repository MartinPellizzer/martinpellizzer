Bupleurum scheffleri

Bupleurum scheffleri, commonly known as the Persian burnet, is a perennial herb native to regions of Central Asia and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
