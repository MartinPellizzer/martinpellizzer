Blechnum moluccanum

Blechnum moluccanum, commonly known as the Moluccan shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a spiral pattern.
