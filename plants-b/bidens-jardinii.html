Bidens jardinii

Bidens jardinii, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features serrated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow with prominent white markings, and they bloom from summer to fall.

Bidens jardinii is often found in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and along stream banks.
