Borago officinalis var. lilacina

Borago officinalis var. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac borage, is a variant of the common borage plant.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features hairy, blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal. Its leaves are broad, oval, and often have a slightly fuzzy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
