Bauhinia pinheiroi

Bauhinia pinheiroi, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy pink or purple flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia pinheiroi is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
