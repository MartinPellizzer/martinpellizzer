Brachystachyum densiflorum var. villosum

Brachystachyum densiflorum var. villosum, commonly known as the silky grass or silky bamboo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
