Bulbocastanum linnaei var. nanum

Bulbocastanum linnaei var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf bulbocastanum, is a rare and delicate plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires a cool, dry climate to flourish.
