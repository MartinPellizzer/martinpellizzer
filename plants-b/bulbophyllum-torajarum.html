Bulbophyllum torajarum

Bulbophyllum torajarum, commonly known as the Torajas orchid, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is typically found in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its native habitat.

The orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts.
