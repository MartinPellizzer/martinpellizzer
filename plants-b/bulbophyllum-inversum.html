Bulbophyllum inversum

Bulbophyllum inversum, commonly known as the Upside-down Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, where the flowers face downward, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum inversum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
