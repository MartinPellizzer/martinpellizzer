Baliospermum montanum var. heterophyllum

Baliospermum montanum var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with trailing stems that produce vibrant blue to violet flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or open habitats.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
