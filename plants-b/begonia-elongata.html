Begonia elongata

Begonia elongata, commonly known as the elongated begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia elongata is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas due to its preference for indirect light.
