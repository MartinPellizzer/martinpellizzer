Ballota schimperi

Ballota schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian thyme, is a perennial herb native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, blue or purple flowers. Ballota schimperi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
