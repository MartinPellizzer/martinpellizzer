Brunyera

Brunyera, commonly known as the fairy bells, is a genus of flowering plants native to Australia.

These plants are characterized by their delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Brunyera species typically grow in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. They have small, narrow leaves and are often found in coastal regions.

The genus includes several species, some of which are used in horticulture for their ornamental value.
