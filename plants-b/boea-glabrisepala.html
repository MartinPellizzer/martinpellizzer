Boea glabrisepala

Boea glabrisepala, commonly known as the smooth-sepal boea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces showy blooms in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant.

Boea glabrisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in indoor settings.
