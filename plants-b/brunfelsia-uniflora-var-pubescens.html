Brunfelsia uniflora var. pubescens

Brunfelsia uniflora var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvet flower or purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves and stems, which give it its varietal name "pubescens.". It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers open in the morning and close in the evening, a trait that adds to its unique botanical appeal.
