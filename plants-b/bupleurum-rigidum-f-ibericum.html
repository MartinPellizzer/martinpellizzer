Bupleurum rigidum f. ibericum

Bupleurum rigidum f. ibericum, commonly known as the Iberian rigid bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
