Begonia arrogans

Begonia arrogans, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to maroon hues, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, Begonia arrogans is widely cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
