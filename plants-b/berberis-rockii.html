Berberis rockii

Berberis rockii, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Berberis rockii is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and climates.
