Burmannia bicolor var. tenera

Burmannia bicolor var. tenera, commonly known as the velvet burmannia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
