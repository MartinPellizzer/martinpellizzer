Bidens ahnnei

Bidens ahnnei, commonly known as the yellow beggar's tick, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, hairy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers with five petals. Its fruit is a schizocarp composed of two small, hairy segments that release seeds upon maturity.

Bidens ahnnei is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in some areas.
