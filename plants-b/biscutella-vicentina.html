Biscutella vicentina

Biscutella vicentina, commonly known as the Vicentine biscuitflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features basal leaves that are oblanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in racemes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
