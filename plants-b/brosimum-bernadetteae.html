Brosimum bernadetteae

Brosimum bernadetteae, commonly known as the Bernadette's beauty leaf, is a species of plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits.
