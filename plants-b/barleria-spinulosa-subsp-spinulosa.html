Barleria spinulosa subsp. spinulosa

Barleria spinulosa subsp.

spinulosa, commonly known as the spiked barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.
