Bolboschoenus strobilinus

Bolboschoenus strobilinus, commonly known as the spike sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

The plant typically grows in shallow water or saturated soil, making it an important component of aquatic and semi-aquatic ecosystems.
