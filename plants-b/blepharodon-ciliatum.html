Blepharodon ciliatum

Blepharodon ciliatum, commonly known as the silkyleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has linear, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
