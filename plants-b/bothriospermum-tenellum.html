Bothriospermum tenellum

Bothriospermum tenellum, commonly known as the slender sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
