Berberis grandiflora

Berberis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It features spiny stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its berries are red and edible. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Berberis grandiflora is also valued for its medicinal properties, as it contains berberine, a compound with various health benefits.
