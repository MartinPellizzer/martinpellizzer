Begonia dryadis

Begonia dryadis, commonly known as the Dryad's Begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bi-colored leaves that display shades of green, red, and purple, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its foliage is evergreen, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

Begonia dryadis is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored plant among both novice and experienced gardeners.
