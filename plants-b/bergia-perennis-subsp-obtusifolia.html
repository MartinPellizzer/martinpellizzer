Bergia perennis subsp. obtusifolia

Bergia perennis subsp.

obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bergia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Bergia perennis subsp.

obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
