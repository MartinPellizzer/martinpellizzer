Borreria stricta var. rosea

Borreria stricta var. rosea, commonly known as the pink burreri, is a flowering plant native to regions of Central and South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features small, pink or rose-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Borreria stricta var. rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive floral display.
