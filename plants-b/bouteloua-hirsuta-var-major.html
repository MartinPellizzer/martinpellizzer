Bouteloua hirsuta var. major

Bouteloua hirsuta var. major, commonly known as hairy grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy leaves and open, loose panicle inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It has fine, wiry stems and produces small, tan to brown flowers.

This species is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in native plant landscaping.
