Bauhinia bicolor

Bauhinia bicolor, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves and showy, pink or white flowers. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.

Bauhinia bicolor is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
