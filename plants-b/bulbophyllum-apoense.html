Bulbophyllum apoense

Bulbophyllum apoense, commonly known as the Apo Bower, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration, featuring white and pink petals. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum apoense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
