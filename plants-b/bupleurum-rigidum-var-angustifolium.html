Bupleurum rigidum var. angustifolium

Bupleurum rigidum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved rigid bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly serrated margin. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
