Bunium luteum

Bunium luteum, commonly known as yellow bishop's hat, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and the Middle East.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and distinctive umbellate inflorescences. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky or arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
