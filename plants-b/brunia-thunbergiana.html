Brunia thunbergiana

Brunia thunbergiana, commonly known as the African snowball, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its white blooms. It is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Brunia thunbergiana is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardiness.
