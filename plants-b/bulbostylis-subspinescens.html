Bulbostylis subspinescens

Bulbostylis subspinescens, commonly known as the spiny bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania.
