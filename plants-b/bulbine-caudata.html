Bulbine caudata

Bulbine caudata, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that store water, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its resilience and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
