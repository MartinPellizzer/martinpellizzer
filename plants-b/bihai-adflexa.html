Bihai adflexa

Bihai adflexa, commonly known as the Bihai spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that turn red when mature. Bihai adflexa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
