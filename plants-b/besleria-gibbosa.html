Besleria gibbosa

Besleria gibbosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Besleria gibbosa is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
