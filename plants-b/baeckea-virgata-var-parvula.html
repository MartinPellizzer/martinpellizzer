Baeckea virgata var. parvula

Baeckea virgata var. parvula, commonly known as the small-flowered baeckea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
