Bewsia

Bewsia, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent petals that resemble ghostly apparitions, giving it its ethereal appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting specific nocturnal pollinators. Bewsia thrives in extreme conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptations to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Its botanical study offers valuable insights into the evolution of desert flora.
