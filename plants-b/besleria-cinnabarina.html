Besleria cinnabarina

Besleria cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-flowered gesneriad, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, and it often has a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
