Bulbostylis tisserantii

Bulbostylis tisserantii, commonly known as the Tisserant's bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, sedges, and rushes. This plant is characterized by its small, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.
