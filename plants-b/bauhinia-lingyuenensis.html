Bauhinia lingyuenensis

Bauhinia lingyuenensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, often bipinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its seeds are encased in hard, leathery pods, typical of the legume family.

Bauhinia lingyuenensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
