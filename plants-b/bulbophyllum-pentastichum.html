Bulbophyllum pentastichum

Bulbophyllum pentastichum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a dense cluster. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum pentastichum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
