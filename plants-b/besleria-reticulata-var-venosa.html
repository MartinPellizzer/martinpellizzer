Besleria reticulata var. venosa

Besleria reticulata var. venosa, commonly known as the veined besleria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, veined leaves that range from dark green to almost black, with lighter green or silver markings. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
