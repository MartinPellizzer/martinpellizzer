Berkheya polyacantha

Berkheya polyacantha, commonly known as the many-thorned berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange ray florets and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, yellow disk flowers that are clustered in composite heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Berkheya polyacantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
