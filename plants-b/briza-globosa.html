Briza globosa

Briza globosa, commonly known as the globular grass or globe grass, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in tight, rounded spikes.

Briza globosa is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
