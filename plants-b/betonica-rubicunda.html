Betonica rubicunda

Betonica rubicunda, commonly known as red betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported calming and digestive properties.

Betonica rubicunda thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
