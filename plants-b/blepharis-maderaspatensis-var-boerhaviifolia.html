Blepharis maderaspatensis var. boerhaviifolia

Blepharis maderaspatensis var. boerhaviifolia, commonly known as the Boerhavia-leaved blepharis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.
