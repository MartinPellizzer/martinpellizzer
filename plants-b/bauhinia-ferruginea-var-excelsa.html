Bauhinia ferruginea var. excelsa

Bauhinia ferruginea var. excelsa, commonly known as the scarlet bauhinia, is a deciduous leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange-pink papery flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, and its stems are often thorny. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Bauhinia ferruginea var. excelsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
