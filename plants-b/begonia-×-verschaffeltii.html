Begonia × verschaffeltii

Begonia × verschaffeltii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pendula.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves, making it popular for hanging baskets and containers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is favored for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
