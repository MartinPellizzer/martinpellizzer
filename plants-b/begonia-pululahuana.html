Begonia pululahuana

Begonia pululahuana, commonly known as the Pululahuana begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Begonia pululahuana requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
