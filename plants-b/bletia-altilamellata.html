Bletia altilamellata

Bletia altilamellata, commonly known as the broad-leafed bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Bletia altilamellata is a perennial species that thrives in humid climates with high levels of rainfall.
