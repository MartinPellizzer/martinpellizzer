Bromus racemosus f. pratorum

Bromus racemosus f. pratorum, commonly known as the pratorum brome, is a horticultural form of the grass species Bromus racemosus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens. The plant typically produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, pale green to brownish seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in temperate climates.

This form is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to form attractive turf-like landscapes.
