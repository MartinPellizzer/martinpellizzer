Begonia hasskarliana

Begonia hasskarliana, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the summer months. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and it is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.

Begonia hasskarliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
