Benzoin cubeba

Benzoin cubeba, commonly known as cubeb, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown resinous sap. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that are followed by dark, woody fruits containing elliptical seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The resin, known as cubeb resin, is used in traditional medicine and as a spice in various cuisines.
