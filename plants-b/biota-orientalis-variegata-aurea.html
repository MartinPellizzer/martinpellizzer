Biota orientalis variegata-aurea

Biota orientalis variegata-aurea, commonly known as the golden variegated biota, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with bright yellow variegation that enhances its visual appeal. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a vibrant, low-maintenance plant.
