Banisteria schlimii

Banisteria schlimii, commonly known as the silver leaf vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
