Baccharis negeri

Baccharis negeri, commonly known as the Malayan baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland areas. Its foliage is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant's structure and growth habits make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
