Bulbine xanthobotrys

Bulbine xanthobotrys, commonly known as the yellow bulbine, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardening.
