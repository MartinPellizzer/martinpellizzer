Begonia formosana

Begonia formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan begonia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are usually red or pink, with intricate patterns on the petals, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
