Begonia princeae var. rhodesiana

Begonia princeae var. rhodesiana, commonly known as the Rhodesian begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features vibrant, velvety foliage with deep green to almost black leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
