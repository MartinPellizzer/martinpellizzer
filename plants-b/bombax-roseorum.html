Bombax roseorum

Bombax roseorum, commonly known as the rose-appearance cotton tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds enclosed in a bright red or orange capsule, which is a distinctive feature. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed, and the tree can grow to a considerable height, making it a notable species in tropical landscapes.

The botanical features of Bombax roseorum make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
