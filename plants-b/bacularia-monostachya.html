Bacularia monostachya

Bacularia monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that bear small, narrow leaves arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces a single, slender spike of small, spikelet-like flowers during the spring and early summer. Bacularia monostachya is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
