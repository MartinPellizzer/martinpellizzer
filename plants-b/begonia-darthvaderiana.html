Begonia darthvaderiana

Begonia darthvaderiana, commonly known as Darth Vader's begonia, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and a unique, almost otherworldly appearance that has earned it its dramatic common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and distinctive look, it is a sought-after species among collectors and botanical enthusiasts.
