Begonia cucphuongensis

Begonia cucphuongensis, commonly known as the Cuc Phuong begonia, is a rare and endemic species native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Cuc Phuong National Park. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
