Begonia gilgii

Begonia gilgii, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Begonia gilgii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
