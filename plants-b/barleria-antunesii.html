Barleria antunesii

Barleria antunesii, commonly known as the Natal barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Barleria antunesii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
