Buchnera humilis

Buchnera humilis, commonly known as the small buchnera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale purple. Buchnera humilis has a low-growing habit and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
