Bambusa castillonii

Bambusa castillonii, commonly known as the Castillon's bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The leaves are long and narrow, typically with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
