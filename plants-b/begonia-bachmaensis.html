Begonia bachmaensis

Begonia bachmaensis, commonly known as the Bachma begonia, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface and a serrated margin. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red, clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This species is adapted to cool, moist environments and thrives in well-drained soil.
