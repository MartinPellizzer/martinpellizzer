Bulbophyllum pseudofilicaule

Bulbophyllum pseudofilicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
