Bonamia dietrichiana

Bonamia dietrichiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bonamia dietrichiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
