Bulbophyllum taitungianum

Bulbophyllum taitungianum, commonly known as the Taitung orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial orchid that produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
