Bulbophyllum lageniforme

Bulbophyllum lageniforme, commonly known as the bottle-shaped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, bottle-shaped pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and horticultural enthusiasts.
