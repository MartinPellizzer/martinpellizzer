Brachiaria ophryodes

Brachiaria ophryodes, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and compact growth habit. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and marshy areas. Its rhizomatous growth allows it to spread rapidly and form dense vegetation.

Brachiaria ophryodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and erosion control.
