Burmeistera pterifolia

Burmeistera pterifolia, commonly known as the Mexican lace fern, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fern-like foliage. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with broad, pinnately divided leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It produces small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Burmeistera pterifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic value in shaded gardens.
