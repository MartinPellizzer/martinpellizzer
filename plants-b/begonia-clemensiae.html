Begonia clemensiae

Begonia clemensiae, commonly known as the Clemens' begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia clemensiae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
