Brachyscome lucens

Brachyscome lucens, commonly known as the golden everlasting.

This flowering plant is native to Australia and belongs to the Asteraceae family. It produces small, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in a spherical shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
