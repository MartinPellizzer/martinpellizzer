Begonia schenckii

Begonia schenckii, commonly known as the Schenck's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia schenckii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
