Baeckea leptocalyx

Baeckea leptocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved baeckea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, characterized by its small, narrow leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand salt spray and wind.

Baeckea leptocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
