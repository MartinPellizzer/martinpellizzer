Bauhinia bequaertii

Bauhinia bequaertii, commonly known as the Bornean flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Borneo.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in yellow or orange hues. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes.

Bauhinia bequaertii is adapted to tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
