Bulbophyllum amorosoanum

Bulbophyllum amorosoanum, commonly known as the Love's Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded conditions and is often found in forest understories.
