Bulbophyllum teretibulbum

Bulbophyllum teretibulbum, commonly known as the slender bulb orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its flowers are borne on slender peduncles and are known for their intricate patterns and strong scent.

Bulbophyllum teretibulbum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
