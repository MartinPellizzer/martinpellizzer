Bermudiana mucronata

Bermudiana mucronata, commonly known as the Bermuda buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the island of Bermuda.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a waxy coating. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
