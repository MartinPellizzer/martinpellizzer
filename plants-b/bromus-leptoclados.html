Bromus leptoclados

Bromus leptoclados, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green color.

Bromus leptoclados is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in areas with moderate to high moisture.
