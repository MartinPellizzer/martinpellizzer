Bulbostylis subtilis

Bulbostylis subtilis, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its flowers are usually pale green to brown, and it produces small, grass-like seeds.

Due to its habitat preferences, it is often found in association with other aquatic and semi-aquatic plant species.
