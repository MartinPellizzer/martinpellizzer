Bignonia salzmannii

Bignonia salzmannii, commonly known as the yellow trumpetbush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant produces showy, yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Bignonia salzmannii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
