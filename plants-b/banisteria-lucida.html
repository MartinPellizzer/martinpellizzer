Banisteria lucida

Banisteria lucida, commonly known as the shining banisteria, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.
