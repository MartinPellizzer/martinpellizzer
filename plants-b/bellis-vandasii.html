Bellis vandasii

Bellis vandasii, commonly known as the Vanda's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
