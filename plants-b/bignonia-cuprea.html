Bignonia cuprea

Bignonia cuprea, commonly known as the copperleaf or scarlet trumpetvine, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a yellow throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adaptable to various soil types.

Bignonia cuprea is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
