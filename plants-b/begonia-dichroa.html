Begonia dichroa

Begonia dichroa, commonly known as the two-colored begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a bicolor pattern, typically green on one side and silver or white on the other. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom in spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia dichroa is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and easy care requirements.
