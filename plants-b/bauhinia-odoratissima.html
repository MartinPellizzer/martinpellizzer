Bauhinia odoratissima

Bauhinia odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, papery, bipinnate leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy blooms and unique leaf structure. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Bauhinia odoratissima is also known for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments.
