Bletilla yunnanensis var. limprichtii

Bletilla yunnanensis var. limprichtii, commonly known as the Yunnan spider orchid, is a rare and ornamental orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the spring season.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
