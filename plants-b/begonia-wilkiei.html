Begonia wilkiei

Begonia wilkiei, commonly known as the Wilkie's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and collectors who appreciate its unique foliage and flowering display.
