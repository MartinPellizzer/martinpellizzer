Bahia lanata var. grandiflora

Bahia lanata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora bahia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for coastal landscapes.
