Begonia krystofii

Begonia krystofii, commonly known as the Krystofii begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Begonia krystofii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and indoor spaces.
