Bulbine longiscapa

Bulbine longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed bulbine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features upright, cylindrical stems that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in racemes, blooming from late summer to early autumn.

Bulbine longiscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
