Begonia serraticauda

Begonia serraticauda, commonly known as the serrated-tail begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia serraticauda is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
