Bergenia × smithii

Bergenia × smithii, commonly known as the hybrid bergenia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features rosettes of thick, dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of pink or red flowers in early spring, which are borne on tall, upright stems. Bergenia × smithii is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in partial shade to full sun.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
