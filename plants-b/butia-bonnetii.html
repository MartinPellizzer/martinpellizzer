Butia bonnetii

Butia bonnetii, commonly known as the Bonnet's palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its clustered, feather-like leaves and a short, sturdy trunk. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are followed by orange-red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.

Butia bonnetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
