Bidens tripartita var. minor

Bidens tripartita var. minor, commonly known as lesser ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and swamps. Its leaves are usually trifoliate, with three leaflets, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

It serves as a food source for various pollinators and is often used in wetland restoration projects.
