Begonia longanensis

Begonia longanensis, commonly known as the longan begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
