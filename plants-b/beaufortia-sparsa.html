Beaufortia sparsa

Beaufortia sparsa, commonly known as the slender beaufortia, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in full sun.

Beaufortia sparsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
