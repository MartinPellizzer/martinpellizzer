Bulbophyllum adenopetalum

Bulbophyllum adenopetalum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
