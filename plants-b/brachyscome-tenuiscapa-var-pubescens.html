Brachyscome tenuiscapa var. pubescens

Brachyscome tenuiscapa var. pubescens, commonly known as the silky blueweed, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to Australia.

It features small, daisy-like flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, with a distinctive central yellow disk. The leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving the plant a fluffy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
