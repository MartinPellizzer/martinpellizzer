Begonia stenocardia

Begonia stenocardia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse environments.
