Bertiera Viburnoides

Bertiera viburnoides is a rare and striking plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its fragrant flowers and medicinal properties. This shrub is characterized by its dense foliage and clusters of small, white blossoms that bloom in spring. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection.
