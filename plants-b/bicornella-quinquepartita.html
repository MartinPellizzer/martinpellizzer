Bicornella quinquepartita

Bicornella quinquepartita, commonly known as the five-parted horned plant, is a unique species belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive floral structure, featuring five lobes that resemble horns, giving it its common name. Bicornella quinquepartita is often cultivated for its striking appearance and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
