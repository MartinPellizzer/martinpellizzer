Bidens kilimandscharica

Bidens kilimandscharica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers with ray-like petals. The plant typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and branched.

Bidens kilimandscharica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
