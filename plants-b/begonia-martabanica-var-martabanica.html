Begonia martabanica var. martabanica

Begonia martabanica var. martabanica, commonly known as the Martaban begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value lies in both its foliage and its delicate floral display.
