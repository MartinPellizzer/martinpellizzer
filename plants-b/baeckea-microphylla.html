Baeckea microphylla

Baeckea microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved baeckea, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are arranged in dense clusters.

Baeckea microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
