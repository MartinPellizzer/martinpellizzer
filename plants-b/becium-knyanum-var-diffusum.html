Becium knyanum var. diffusum

Becium knyanum var. diffusum, commonly known as the broad-leaved becium, is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to specific regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming during the cooler months.
