Bulbophyllum colliferum

Bulbophyllum colliferum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces striking yellow-green flowers with a distinctive spiky appearance. Its roots are often clustered and can form dense mats in the soil.

Bulbophyllum colliferum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
