Boucerosia lasiantha

Boucerosia lasiantha, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and are known for their intricate, delicate structure.

Boucerosia lasiantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
