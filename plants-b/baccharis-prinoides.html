Baccharis prinoides

Baccharis prinoides, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically in the spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite and have a rough, textured surface.

Baccharis prinoides is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
