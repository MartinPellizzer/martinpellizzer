Blepharis rubiifolia

Blepharis rubiifolia, commonly known as the redleaf blepharis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, succulent-like appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
