Bromheadia tenuis

Bromheadia tenuis, commonly known as the slender bromheadia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Bromheadiaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually narrow and elongated. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a subtle fragrance, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Bromheadia tenuis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
