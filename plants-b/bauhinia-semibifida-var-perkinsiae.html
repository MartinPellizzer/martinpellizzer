Bauhinia semibifida var. perkinsiae

Bauhinia semibifida var. perkinsiae, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in full sun. Its ornamental value is due to its delicate foliage and vibrant floral display.

It is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant for its aesthetic appeal.
