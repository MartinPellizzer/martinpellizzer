Begonia holosericea

Begonia holosericea, commonly known as the silky begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Begonia holosericea is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
