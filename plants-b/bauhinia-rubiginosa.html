Bauhinia rubiginosa

Bauhinia rubiginosa, commonly known as the red bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, rounded leaflets. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often appearing in clusters. Bauhinia rubiginosa is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional medicine.
