Bulbophyllum decurrentilobum

Bulbophyllum decurrentilobum, commonly known as the cascading lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescence that bears numerous showy, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant coloration make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
