Bidens ugandensis var. rogersii

Bidens ugandensis var. rogersii, commonly known as the Uganda ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for potential medicinal uses.
