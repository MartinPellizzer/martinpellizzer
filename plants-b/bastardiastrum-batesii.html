Bastardiastrum batesii

Bastardiastrum batesii, commonly known as the desert star, is a low-growing succulent native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
