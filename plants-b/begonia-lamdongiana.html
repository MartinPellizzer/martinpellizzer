Begonia lamdongiana

Begonia lamdongiana, commonly known as the Lamdong begonia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red or pink flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.

Begonia lamdongiana is also valued for its role in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
