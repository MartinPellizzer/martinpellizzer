Boerhavia nummularia

Boerhavia nummularia, commonly known as silverleaf purslane, is a low-growing succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and adaptable to various environmental conditions.
