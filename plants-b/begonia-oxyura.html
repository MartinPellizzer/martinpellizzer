Begonia oxyura

Begonia oxyura, commonly known as the silver lace begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, crinkled, silver-green leaves that are often described as resembling lace. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in the summer months. Begonia oxyura thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique foliage, which adds a distinctive visual appeal to any setting.
