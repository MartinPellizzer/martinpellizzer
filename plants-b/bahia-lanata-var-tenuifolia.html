Bahia lanata var. tenuifolia

Bahia lanata var. tenuifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf bahia grass, is a perennial grass native to the southern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than an inch wide, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental settings. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for lawns and erosion control.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping in warmer climates.
