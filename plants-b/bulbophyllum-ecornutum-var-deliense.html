Bulbophyllum ecornutum var. deliense

Bulbophyllum ecornutum var. deliense, commonly known as the Deli's bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting a system of leaves that are usually dark green and strap-like.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
