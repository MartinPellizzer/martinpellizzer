Bossiaea rufa var. foliosa

Bossiaea rufa var. foliosa, commonly known as the silverleaf bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, typically blooming in spring. It is often found in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
