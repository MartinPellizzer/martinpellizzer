Bauhinia petersiana subsp. macrantha

Bauhinia petersiana subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are compound and consist of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant produces showy, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in racemes along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats. Bauhinia petersiana subsp.

macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.
