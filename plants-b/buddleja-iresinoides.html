Buddleja iresinoides

Buddleja iresinoides, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces showy, clustered panicles of purple or pink flowers that attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja iresinoides is known for its rapid growth and adaptability, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
