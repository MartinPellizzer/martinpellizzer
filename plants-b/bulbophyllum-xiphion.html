Bulbophyllum xiphion

Bulbophyllum xiphion, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, orchid-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in humid environments with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
