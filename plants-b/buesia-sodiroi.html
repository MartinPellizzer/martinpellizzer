Buesia sodiroi

Buesia sodiroi, commonly known as the pink-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Buesia sodiroi has small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
