Baccharis × alboffii

Baccharis × alboffii, commonly known as the silverleaf baccharis, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
