Brachyscome radicata var. dubia

Brachyscome radicata var. dubia, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features a compact rosette of silver-gray, woolly leaves and produces small, daisy-like white flowers with a yellow center. The plant is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
