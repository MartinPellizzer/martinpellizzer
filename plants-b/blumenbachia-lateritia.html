Blumenbachia lateritia

Blumenbachia lateritia, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, adapting to cold and harsh conditions. It has hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in high-altitude climates.
