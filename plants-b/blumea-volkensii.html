Blumea volkensii

Blumea volkensii, commonly known as African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open woodlands or grasslands. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Blumea volkensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
