Begonia siamensis

Begonia siamensis, commonly known as the Siam begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant variegation. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia siamensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
