Burmannia leucantha

Burmannia leucantha, commonly known as the white burmannia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Burmanniaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and succulent leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they emit a faint, sweet fragrance.

Burmannia leucantha is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
