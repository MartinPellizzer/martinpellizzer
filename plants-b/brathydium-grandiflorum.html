Brathydium grandiflorum

Brathydium grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking, large, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
