Bulbophyllum lopalanthum

Bulbophyllum lopalanthum, commonly known as the Lopala Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding substrate.

Bulbophyllum lopalanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
