Brucea tonkinensis

Brucea tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese Brucea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers and oval-shaped, slightly hairy leaves. The plant produces small, red, berry-like fruits that contain several seeds. Brucea tonkinensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.

It has been studied for its potential medicinal properties, though its use remains largely traditional.
