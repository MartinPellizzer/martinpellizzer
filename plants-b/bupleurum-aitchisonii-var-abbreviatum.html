Bupleurum aitchisonii var. abbreviatum

Bupleurum aitchisonii var. abbreviatum, commonly known as the Kashmir spineflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale blue. The leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in rocky or alpine habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also of interest in traditional medicine.
