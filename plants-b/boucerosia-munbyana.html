Boucerosia munbyana

Boucerosia munbyana, commonly known as the Munby's boucerosia, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
