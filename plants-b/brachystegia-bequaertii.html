Brachystegia bequaertii

Brachystegia bequaertii, commonly known as the African brachystegia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its thick, flaky bark and dense canopy. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by legume-like pods containing seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in miombo woodlands.
