Barleria glabra

Barleria glabra, commonly known as slender barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly rough texture.

Barleria glabra is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
