Barleria tomentosa

Barleria tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Barleria tomentosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
