Berzelia formosa

Berzelia formosa, commonly known as the beautiful berzelia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Berzelieaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Berzelia formosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
