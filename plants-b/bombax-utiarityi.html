Bombax utiarityi

Bombax utiarityi, commonly known as the African cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds enclosed in a hairy, woody capsule. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the tree can grow up to 20 meters tall.
