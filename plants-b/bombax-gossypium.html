Bombax gossypium

Bombax gossypium, commonly known as the cotton tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces fluffy, white fibers from its seed pods, which are harvested for cotton production. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the tree can grow up to 20 meters tall.

The cotton tree is widely cultivated for its economic importance in the textile industry.
