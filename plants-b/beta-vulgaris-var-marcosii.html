Beta vulgaris var. marcosii

Beta vulgaris var. marcosii, commonly known as the red leaf beet or scarlet leaf beet, is a cultivated variant of the beetroot plant.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, purple, and green foliage, which is prized for its ornamental value. The plant typically grows as an upright, compact shrub with deeply lobed leaves. It thrives in cool climates and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening and as a decorative plant in landscapes.
