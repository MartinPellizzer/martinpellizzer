Buchnera dura

Buchnera dura, commonly known as the hardy buchnera, is a resilient plant species native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and thick, leathery leaves that help it withstand harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked but play a role in its reproductive cycle. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in poor soil conditions and making it a valuable plant for erosion control.

Buchnera dura is also noted for its ability to tolerate drought and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for landscaping in challenging environments.
