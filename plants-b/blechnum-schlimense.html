Blechnum schlimense

Blechnum schlimense, commonly known as the Schlim's hart's tongue fern, is a species of fern native to South America.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, heart-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine by local communities.

The fronds are usually evergreen, with a leathery texture and a dark green color.
