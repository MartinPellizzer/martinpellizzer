Bulbophyllum gimagaanense

Bulbophyllum gimagaanense, commonly known as the Gimagaan Orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and linear leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to mimic its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
