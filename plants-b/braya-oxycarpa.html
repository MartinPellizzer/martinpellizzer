Braya oxycarpa

Braya oxycarpa, commonly known as the sharp-fruited bladderwort, is a small herbaceous plant native to arid regions of North America.

Braya oxycarpa is often used in ecological studies due to its ability to survive in harsh, nutrient-poor conditions.
