Begonia jiewhoei

Begonia jiewhoei, commonly known as the Jiewhoei begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and purple hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in horticulture.
