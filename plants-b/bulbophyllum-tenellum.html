Bulbophyllum tenellum

Bulbophyllum tenellum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces pseudobulbs, which serve as storage organs for water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its humid, shaded habitats.

This orchid is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
