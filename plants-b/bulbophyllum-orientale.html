Bulbophyllum orientale

Bulbophyllum orientale, commonly known as the oriental bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
