Begonia brangbosangensis

Begonia brangbosangensis, commonly known as the Brangbosang begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and can grow in both upright and trailing forms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
