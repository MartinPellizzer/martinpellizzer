Brachypodium Tenue

Brachypodium tenue is a small, grass-like plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in disturbed soils. This species is known for its compact growth and ability to survive in harsh conditions. It has become an important model organism in plant biology research. Its genetic simplicity and rapid life cycle make it valuable for studying plant development and stress responses.
