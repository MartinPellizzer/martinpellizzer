Bulbophyllum exiguiflorum

Bulbophyllum exiguiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its small, delicate flowers and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is broader than the other petals.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
