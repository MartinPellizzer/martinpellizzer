Bauhinia rotundifolia

Bauhinia rotundifolia, commonly known as the Orchid Tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has palmate, heart-shaped leaves that are typically rounded and have a velvety texture. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish hue.

Bauhinia rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
