Bokkeveldia aestivalis

Bokkeveldia aestivalis, commonly known as the summer bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in clusters at the top of the stems. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooming period occurs during the summer months, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
