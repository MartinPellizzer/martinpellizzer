Byrsonima Luetzelburgii

Byrsonima luetzelburgii is a unique plant species native to the Amazon rainforest. It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus. This tree is known for its striking yellow flowers and medicinal properties. It plays an important role in the ecosystem and traditional medicine practices. Its conservation is crucial due to habitat loss and deforestation.
