Begonia pryeriana

Begonia pryeriana, commonly known as the Pryer's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia pryeriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
