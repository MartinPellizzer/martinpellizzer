Brachanthemum fruticulosum

Brachanthemum fruticulosum, commonly known as the shrub daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Brachanthemum fruticulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
