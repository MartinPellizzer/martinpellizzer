Begonia luxii

Begonia luxii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its leaves are often deeply lobed and emit a subtle, pleasant fragrance.

Begonia luxii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
