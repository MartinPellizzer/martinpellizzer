Blechnum amabile

Blechnum amabile, commonly known as the silky cliff fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft and elegant appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rock faces and forest floors. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor to rocky substrates.

Blechnum amabile is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
