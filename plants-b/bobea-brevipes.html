Bobea brevipes

Bobea brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed bobea, is a climbing legume native to South America.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its seeds are enclosed in small, pod-like fruits that split open when mature.

Bobea brevipes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
