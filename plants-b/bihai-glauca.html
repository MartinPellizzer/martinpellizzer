Bihai glauca

Bihai glauca, commonly known as the blue glauca, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue-gray foliage and showy, pale pink to lavender flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
