Bubon multiflorum

Bubon multiflorum, commonly known as the multibloom bubon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered inflorescences of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Bubon multiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
