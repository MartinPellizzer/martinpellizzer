Brickellia nevinii

Brickellia nevinii, commonly known as Nevin's brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as chaparral and desert scrub. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in dense, brush-like clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a grayish-green appearance.

Brickellia nevinii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in well-drained soils.
