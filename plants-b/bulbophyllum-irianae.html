Bulbophyllum irianae

Bulbophyllum irianae, commonly known as the Irianae orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and intricate floral structures. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically small and come in a variety of colors, often with a distinctive pattern.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
