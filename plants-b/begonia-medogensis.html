Begonia medogensis

Begonia medogensis, commonly known as the Medog begonia, is a rare and unique species native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
