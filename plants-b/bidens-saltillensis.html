Bidens saltillensis

Bidens saltillensis, commonly known as the saltillensis ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, yellow-orange flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Bidens saltillensis is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
