Blechnum longepetiolatum

Blechnum longepetiolatum, commonly known as the long-petioled holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The fronds are bipinnate, with numerous small, ovate pinnae that are arranged in a dense, compact pattern. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.
