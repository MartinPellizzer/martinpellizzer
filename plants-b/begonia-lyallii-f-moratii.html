Begonia lyallii f. moratii

Begonia lyallii f. moratii, commonly known as the Lyall's begonia, is a hybrid form native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not commonly found in commercial horticulture.
