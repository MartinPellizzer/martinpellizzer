Brachystelma brownianum

Brachystelma brownianum, commonly known as the Brownian's stone orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and has a short, stocky stem with a single or few flowers at the top. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid habitat.

The flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinctive hood-like structure, making it easily identifiable among other orchid species.
