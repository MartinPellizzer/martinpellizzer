Bauhinia semibifida var. longebracteata

Bauhinia semibifida var. longebracteata, commonly known as the long bract bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces showy, often pink or white, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural use.

The varietal name 'longebracteata' refers to its elongated bracts that surround the flowers.
