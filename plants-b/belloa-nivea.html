Belloa nivea

Belloa nivea, commonly known as the snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters and has hairy, gray-green leaves. Belloa nivea thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
