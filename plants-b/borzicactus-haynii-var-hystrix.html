Borzicactus haynii var. hystrix

Borzicactus haynii var. hystrix, commonly known as the spiny hedgehog cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, spiny tubercles, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for dry environments.

Due to its unique morphology and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
