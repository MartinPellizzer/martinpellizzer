Begonia alemanii

Begonia alemanii, commonly known as the Alemanii begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
