Begonia korthalsiana

Begonia korthalsiana, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
