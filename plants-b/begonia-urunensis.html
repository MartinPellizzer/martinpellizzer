Begonia urunensis

Begonia urunensis, commonly known as the Urumqi begonia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to China.

It features lush, broad leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings, adding visual interest to its foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for both horticultural enthusiasts and landscape designers.
