Bassia kajewskii

Bassia kajewskii, commonly known as the Siberian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in dry conditions.

Bassia kajewskii is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is well-suited for xerophytic landscapes.
