Botrychium matricariifolium f. rhombeum

Botrychium matricariifolium f. rhombeum, commonly known as the rhomboid fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive rhomboid-shaped fronds.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or rocky crevices.
