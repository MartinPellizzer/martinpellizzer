Bulbophyllum lemniscatoides var. exappendiculatum

Bulbophyllum lemniscatoides var. exappendiculatum, commonly known as the Orchid with the Double Pendulum, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescence that resembles a double pendulum. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and cylindrical, supporting the plant's growth in its natural habitat.

This variant is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rare beauty and challenging cultivation requirements.
