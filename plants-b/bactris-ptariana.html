Bactris ptariana

Bactris ptariana, commonly known as the pink pineapple.

It is a species of palm native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, paddle-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, yellowish, spiny berry that is often used in traditional medicine.

Bactris ptariana plays an important role in the ecosystems of its native habitats and is valued for both its ornamental and medicinal properties.
