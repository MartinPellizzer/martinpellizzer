Bulbophyllum baucoense

Bulbophyllum baucoense, commonly known as the Bauco orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
