Bulbophyllum irigaleae

Bulbophyllum irigaleae, commonly known as the Iriagalea's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species in its native range.
