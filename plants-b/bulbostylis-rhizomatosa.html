Bulbostylis rhizomatosa

Bulbostylis rhizomatosa, commonly known as the creeping bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense mats. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or pale blue, and it produces tiny, three-angled seeds.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.
