Besleria perennis

Besleria perennis, commonly known as the everlasting orchid, is a tropical flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the year under proper care. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant's ability to retain its flowers for extended periods has earned it the common name "everlasting orchid.".
