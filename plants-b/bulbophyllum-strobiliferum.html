Bulbophyllum strobiliferum

Bulbophyllum strobiliferum, commonly known as the pineapple orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that produces a dense rosette of fleshy, light green leaves that resemble a pineapple. The plant blooms with a spike of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that have a distinctive pinecone-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
