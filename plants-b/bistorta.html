Bistorta

Bistorta, commonly known as false sorrel, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and distinctive, often zigzagging stems. They are valued for their ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
