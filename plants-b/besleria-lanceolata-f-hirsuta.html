Besleria lanceolata f. hirsuta

Besleria lanceolata f. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy lance-leaf begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, hairy leaves that give it its specific epithet "lanceolata" and "hirsuta.". The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
