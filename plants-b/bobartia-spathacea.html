Bobartia spathacea

Bobartia spathacea, commonly known as the purple tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a woody stem with a characteristic reddish hue.

Bobartia spathacea is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
