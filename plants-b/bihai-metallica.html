Bihai metallica

Bihai metallica, commonly known as the metallic hibiscus, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features large, showy flowers with a unique metallic sheen, often in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Bihai metallica is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a potted plant.
