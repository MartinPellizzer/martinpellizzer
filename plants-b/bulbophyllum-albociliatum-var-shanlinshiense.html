Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. shanlinshiense

Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. shanlinshiense, commonly known as the Shanlinshi orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments on tree trunks or branches. Its leaves are elongated and covered with a dense layer of fine white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
