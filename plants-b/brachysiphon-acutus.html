It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, narrow sheath at the base. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Brachysiphon acutus is characterized by its simple, non-branching rhizome and its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
