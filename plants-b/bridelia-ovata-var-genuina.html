Bridelia ovata var. genuina

Bridelia ovata var. genuina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bridelia, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
