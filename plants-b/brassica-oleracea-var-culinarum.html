Brassica oleracea var. culinarum

Brassica oleracea var. culinarum, commonly known as kale, is a leafy green vegetable belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves that are often curly or puckered. The plant is rich in vitamins A, C, and K, as well as minerals like calcium and potassium. It thrives in cool climates and is typically harvested before the leaves become too tough.

Kale is a versatile crop that can be grown in both open fields and controlled environments.
