Bulbophyllum navigioliferum

Bulbophyllum navigioliferum, commonly known as the sailing boat orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, boat-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
