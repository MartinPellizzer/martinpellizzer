Bulbophyllum brevipetalum

Bulbophyllum brevipetalum, commonly known as the short-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, showy blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
