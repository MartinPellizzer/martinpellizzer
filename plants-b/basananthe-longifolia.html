Basananthe longifolia

Basananthe longifolia, commonly known as the longifoliate arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Basananthe longifolia is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
