Bulbophyllum freitagii

Bulbophyllum freitagii, commonly known as the Freitag's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and vibrant colors.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum freitagii make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
