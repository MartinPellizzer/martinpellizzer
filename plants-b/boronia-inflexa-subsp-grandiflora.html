Boronia inflexa subsp. grandiflora

Boronia inflexa subsp.

grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered boronia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically grey-green in colour.

The plant produces showy, pink to mauve flowers that are borne in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in local ecosystems.
