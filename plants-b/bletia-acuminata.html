Bletia acuminata

Bletia acuminata, commonly known as the acuminated bletia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces erect, leafy stems. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with purple or red markings. Bletia acuminata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
