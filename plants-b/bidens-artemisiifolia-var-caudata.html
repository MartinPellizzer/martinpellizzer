Bidens artemisiifolia var. caudata

Bidens artemisiifolia var. caudata, commonly known as ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features slender, branching stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that have serrated edges. The plant produces small yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late summer to early fall. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along stream banks.

Its common name reflects its hardy, resilient nature and its ability to grow in challenging environments.
