Bambusa stenoaurita

Bambusa stenoaurita, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The leaves are narrow and tapering, with a distinctive silvery-white margin that gives it its common name. This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and traditional crafts.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
