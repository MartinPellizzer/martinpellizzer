Begonia flammea

Begonia flammea, commonly known as the flame begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture with a deep green color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its eye-catching appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
