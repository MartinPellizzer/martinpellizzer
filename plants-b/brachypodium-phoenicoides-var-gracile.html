Brachypodium phoenicoides var. gracile

Brachypodium phoenicoides var. gracile, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

Brachypodium phoenicoides var. gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
