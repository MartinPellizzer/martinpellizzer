Bidens quadriaristata

Bidens quadriaristata, commonly known as the four-leaf beggar's-tick, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed areas such as fields and along roadsides. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive shape, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
