Bolbitis Cadieri

Bolbitis cadieri is a rare and delicate fern native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. Known for its intricate fronds and unique growth habits, this plant is a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. Its limited distribution makes it a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, Bolbitis cadieri faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection.
