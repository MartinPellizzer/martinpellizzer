Basanacantha spinosa var. polyantha

Basanacantha spinosa var. polyantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered spurge, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its succulent, spiny stems and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant purple flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
