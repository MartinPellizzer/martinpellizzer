Bunochilus stenosepalus

Bunochilus stenosepalus, commonly known as the narrow-sepal orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, narrow sepal that gives the plant its scientific name. It grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth.

This orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
