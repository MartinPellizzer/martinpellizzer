Begonia taiwaniana var. lukuana

Begonia taiwaniana var. lukuana, commonly known as the Lukuana begonia, is a unique and ornamental plant native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and purple hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is a prized species among botanists and plant enthusiasts.
