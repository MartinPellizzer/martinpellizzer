Billbergia porteana

Billbergia porteana, commonly known as the red-tipped billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, spathaceous flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom from the center of the rosette. Its foliage is often marked with red or purple stripes, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Billbergia porteana thrives in bright, indirect light and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
