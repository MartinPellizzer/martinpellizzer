Buddleja Davidii Var. Superba

Buddleja davidii var. superba is a striking ornamental shrub known for its vibrant, butterfly-attracting flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners. This variety is prized for its long blooming season and ability to add color to landscapes. Its unique, cone-shaped flower clusters stand out against its green foliage. Despite its beauty, it requires careful pruning to maintain its shape and encourage new growth.
