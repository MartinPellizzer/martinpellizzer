Brugmansia

Brugmansia, commonly known as angel's trumpet, is a genus of flowering plants native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous flowers that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, often cultivated for its ornamental value. Brugmansia species are known for their fragrant, trumpet-shaped blooms that come in various colors, including white, yellow, and orange.

The leaves are typically large, glossy, and opposite, contributing to its tropical appearance.
