Bauhinia thailandica

Bauhinia thailandica, commonly known as the Thai bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in forested areas or along riverbanks.

Bauhinia thailandica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
