Berberis florida

Berberis florida, commonly known as the dwarf barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, bright green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and produces clusters of yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in moist, woodland areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Berberis florida is also noted for its medicinal properties, with its berries containing compounds used in traditional herbal remedies.
