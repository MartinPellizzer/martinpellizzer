Blumea grandis

Blumea grandis, commonly known as the large blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is typically shrub-like, with opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Blumea grandis is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
