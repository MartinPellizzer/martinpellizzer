Brillantaisia leonensis

Brillantaisia leonensis, commonly known as the Leonense Brillantaisia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Brillantaisia leonensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
