Buddleja normaniae

Buddleja normaniae, commonly known as the Kashmiri butterfly bush, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Kashmir.

It features slender, woody stems and is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for garden cultivation.

Buddleja normaniae is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
