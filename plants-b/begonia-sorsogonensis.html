Begonia sorsogonensis

Begonia sorsogonensis, commonly known as the Sorsogon Province begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage and showy bracts. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial herb, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a velvety texture, and it produces clusters of small, tubular flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
