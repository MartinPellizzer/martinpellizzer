Bulliarda abyssinica

Bulliarda abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian bulliarda, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

Bulliarda abyssinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
