Bahia Schaffneri Var. Aristata

Bahia schaffneri var. aristata is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of South America. Known for its vibrant blue-green foliage and dramatic, spiky flower spikes, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden or indoor space. This variety is a hybrid of Bahia schaffneri and Bahia aristata, combining the best traits of both parents. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its resilience and beauty make it a standout among succulent enthusiasts.
