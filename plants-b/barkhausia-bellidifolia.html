Barkhausia bellidifolia

Barkhausia bellidifolia, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky stems that bear bright orange or red tubular flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has rough, fibrous bark that is characteristic of its genus. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette at the base, providing a lush green contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Barkhausia bellidifolia is well-suited for rock gardens and dry landscapes due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
