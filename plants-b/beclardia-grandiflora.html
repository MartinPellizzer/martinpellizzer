Beclardia grandiflora

Beclardia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered beclardia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive purple-pink hue, typically blooming in late spring. Beclardia grandiflora thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and adaptability to various garden conditions.
