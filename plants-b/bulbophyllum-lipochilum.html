Bulbophyllum lipochilum

Bulbophyllum lipochilum, commonly known as the pink-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers with a distinctive pink lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or branches in the rainforest canopy. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, while the flowers bloom in clusters during the wet season.

Bulbophyllum lipochilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
