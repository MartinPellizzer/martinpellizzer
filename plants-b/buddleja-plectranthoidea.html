Buddleja plectranthoidea

Buddleja plectranthoidea, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features long, slender stems and is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge. It is a member of the Oleaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
