Buddleja barbata

Buddleja barbata, commonly known as the woolly bush or barbata butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Buddleja barbata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
