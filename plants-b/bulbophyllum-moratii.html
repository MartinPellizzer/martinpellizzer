Bulbophyllum moratii

Bulbophyllum moratii, commonly known as the Moratii's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters, showcasing intricate patterns and vibrant hues.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
