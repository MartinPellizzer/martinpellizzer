Bulbophyllum stormii

Bulbophyllum stormii, commonly known as the Storm's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structure and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, which bloom during the rainy season. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum stormii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and rarity.
