Brunsvigia laticoma

Brunsvigia laticoma, commonly known as the Orange Sunset Kalanchoe, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant in many regions.
