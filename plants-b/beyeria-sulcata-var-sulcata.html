Beyeria sulcata var. sulcata

Beyeria sulcata var. sulcata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant has dark green, velvety leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Beyeria sulcata var. sulcata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
