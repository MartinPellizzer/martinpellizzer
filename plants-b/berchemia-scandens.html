Berchemia scandens

Berchemia scandens, commonly known as the climbing goldbar.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its twining stems and showy yellow flowers. The plant has pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Berchemia scandens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to climb structures.
