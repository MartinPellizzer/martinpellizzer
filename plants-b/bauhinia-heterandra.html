Bauhinia heterandra

Bauhinia heterandra, commonly known as the yellow bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are typically yellowish-green in color. The plant produces showy, pea-like flowers that are usually pink to purple in hue, blooming in summer. Bauhinia heterandra thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
