Bauhinia rutenbergiana

Bauhinia rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg's bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
