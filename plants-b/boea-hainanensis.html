Boea hainanensis

Boea hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. Boea hainanensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

It is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
