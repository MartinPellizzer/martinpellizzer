Basanacantha grandifolia

Basanacantha grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered basanacantha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Basanacantha grandifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
