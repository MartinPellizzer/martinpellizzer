Begonia × chungii

Begonia × chungii, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of ornamental features.

It is native to China and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that add to its visual appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Begonia × chungii is often grown for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
