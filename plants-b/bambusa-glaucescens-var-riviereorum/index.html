Bambusa Glaucescens Var. Riviereorum

Bambusa glaucescens var. riviereorum is a striking bamboo species native to Southeast Asia. Known for its distinctive blue-gray sheaths and elegant, slender culms, it adds a unique visual appeal to gardens and landscapes. This variety is highly valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Its slow growth rate and long-lasting foliage make it a favored plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
