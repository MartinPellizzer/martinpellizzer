Benzoin obtusilobum var. ovatum, commonly known as the white benzoin, is a species of flowering plant in the family Burseraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and Malaysia. The resinous sap, known as benzoine, is harvested for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
