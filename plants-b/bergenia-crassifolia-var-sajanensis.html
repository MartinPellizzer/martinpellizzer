Bergenia crassifolia var. sajanensis

Bergenia crassifolia var. sajanensis, commonly known as the Sajan bergenia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Eastern Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of pink to red flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and alpine settings.
