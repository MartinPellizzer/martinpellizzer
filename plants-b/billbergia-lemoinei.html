Billbergia lemoinei

Billbergia lemoinei, commonly known as the Lemoine's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its long, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, spathaceous flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
