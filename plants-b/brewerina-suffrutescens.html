Brewerina suffrutescens

Brewerina suffrutescens, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, red fruits. Its latex sap is milky and has been used traditionally for various purposes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
