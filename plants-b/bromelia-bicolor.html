Bromelia bicolor

Bromelia bicolor, commonly known as the red-leaf bromeliad, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant features a rosette of sword-shaped leaves that can vary in color from green to red or purple, depending on the variety. It produces showy, long-lasting inflorescences that range from yellow to orange in hue.

Bromelia bicolor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
