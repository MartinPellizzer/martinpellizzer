Bonapartea aboriens

Bonapartea aboriens, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like arrangement of stiff, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces a tall, narrow cone that bears both male and female reproductive structures. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its slow growth and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
