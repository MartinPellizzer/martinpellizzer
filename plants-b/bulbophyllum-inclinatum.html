Bulbophyllum inclinatum

Bulbophyllum inclinatum, commonly known as the leaning orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces striking, elongated pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
