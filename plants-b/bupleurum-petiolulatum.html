Bupleurum petiolulatum

Bupleurum petiolulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed valerian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Bupleurum petiolulatum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
