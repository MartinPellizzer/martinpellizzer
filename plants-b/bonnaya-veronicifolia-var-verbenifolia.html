Bonnaya veronicifolia var. verbenifolia

Bonnaya veronicifolia var. verbenifolia, commonly known as the blue bonnaya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
