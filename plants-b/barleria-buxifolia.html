Barleria buxifolia

Barleria buxifolia, commonly known as the boxleaf barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers, typically in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
