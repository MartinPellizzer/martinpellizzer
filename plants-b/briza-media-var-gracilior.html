Briza media var. gracilior

Briza media var. gracilior, commonly known as slender false oat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or pink. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.

The species is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
