Bromelica Striata

Bromelica striata is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its vibrant, sword-like leaves and striking red flowers, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Despite its beauty, it is relatively easy to care for, making it an excellent plant for beginners.
