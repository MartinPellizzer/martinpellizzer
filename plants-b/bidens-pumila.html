Bidens pumila

Bidens pumila, commonly known as common beggarlice, is a low-growing annual herb native to the Americas.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers with ray florets. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and sunny environments. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, and the plant is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

It is an invasive species in many regions outside its native range.
