Begonia lineolata

Begonia lineolata, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a distinctive zigzag pattern along the midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia lineolata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
