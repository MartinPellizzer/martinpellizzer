Berberis heishuiensis

Berberis heishuiensis, commonly known as the Heishui barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Sichuan province.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that mature by autumn.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
