Bulbostylis woronowii

Bulbostylis woronowii, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grassy leaves and small, clustered flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats.
