Bulbophyllum oxychilum

Bulbophyllum oxychilum, commonly known as the sharp-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a pseudobulb and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive, elongated lip. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts.
