Bidens leucorrhiza

Bidens leucorrhiza, commonly known as whiteflower ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern and central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are typically clustered in heads and bloom from late summer into early fall.

Bidens leucorrhiza is often found in moist to wet soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
