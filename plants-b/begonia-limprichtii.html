Begonia limprichtii

Begonia limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that range from red to pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also appreciated in horticultural collections for its unique foliage and floral display.
