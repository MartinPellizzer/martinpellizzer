Boucerosia socotrana

Boucerosia socotrana, commonly known as Socotra caper, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptation to the harsh conditions of its habitat. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its distinctive appearance. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps reduce water loss and conserve moisture.

Boucerosia socotrana is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
