Bulbophyllum trinervosum

Bulbophyllum trinervosum, commonly known as the Three-veined Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and often has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
