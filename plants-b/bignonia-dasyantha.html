Bignonia dasyantha

Bignonia dasyantha, commonly known as the scarlet trumpetbush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces long, woody seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, hardy ornamental plants.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable to local wildlife.
