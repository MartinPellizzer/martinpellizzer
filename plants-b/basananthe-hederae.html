Basananthe hederae

Basananthe hederae, commonly known as the ivy banana.

This unique plant is a hybrid between a banana species and ivy, resulting in a plant with both broad, banana-like leaves and climbing tendrils similar to ivy. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into fruit resembling a banana.

Due to its unusual characteristics, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
