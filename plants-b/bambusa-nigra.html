Bambusa nigra

Bambusa nigra, commonly known as black bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its dark green to blackish stems, which are often used in ornamental horticulture. It grows in clumps and can reach heights of up to 10 meters.

Bambusa nigra is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is also used in traditional crafts and construction.
