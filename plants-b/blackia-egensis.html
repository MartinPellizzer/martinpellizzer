Blackia egensis

Blackia egensis, commonly known as the African blackberry, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries that are both ornamental and edible. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and shrubland habitats.

Blackia egensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and potential medicinal uses.
