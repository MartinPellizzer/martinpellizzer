Bulbophyllum drymoglossum f. atrosanguiflorum

Bulbophyllum drymoglossum f. atrosanguiflorum, commonly known as the dark red-flowered bulbophyllum, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking dark red to almost black flowers, which contrast with its green foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native tropical environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple blooms, each with a velvety texture and a distinct fragrance.

This rare and ornamental variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and beauty.
