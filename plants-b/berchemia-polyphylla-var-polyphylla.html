Berchemia polyphylla var. polyphylla

Berchemia polyphylla var. polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved berchemia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellow, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for coastal and semi-arid regions.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for garden landscaping.
