Bulbophyllum listeri

Bulbophyllum listeri, commonly known as the Lister's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, branched stem with numerous small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and substrate.

Bulbophyllum listeri is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
