Blyxa japonica var. alternifolia

Blyxa japonica var. alternifolia, commonly known as the Japanese water plantain, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged alternately along its stem. The plant thrives in shallow water and wetlands, often forming dense mats on the water surface. Its leaves are typically a bright green, with a waxy texture that helps it float.

It is valued in ornamental water gardens for its attractive appearance and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
