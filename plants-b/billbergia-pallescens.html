Billbergia pallescens

Billbergia pallescens, commonly known as the pale billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with bright red flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are often covered in a whitish powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance.

Billbergia pallescens thrives in bright, indirect light and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.
