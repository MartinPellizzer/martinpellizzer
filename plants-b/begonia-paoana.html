Begonia paoana

Begonia paoana, commonly known as the Paoana begonia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that can range in color from red to pink, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
