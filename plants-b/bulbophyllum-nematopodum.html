Bulbophyllum nematopodum

Bulbophyllum nematopodum, commonly known as the slender root orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in nutrient-poor conditions. Its flowers are usually small and pale green or yellow, often with a distinctive lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
