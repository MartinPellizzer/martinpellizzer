Bauhinia gossweileri

Bauhinia gossweileri, commonly known as the African lady's mantle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive pinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.

Bauhinia gossweileri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
