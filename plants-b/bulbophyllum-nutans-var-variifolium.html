Bulbophyllum nutans var. variifolium

Bulbophyllum nutans var. variifolium, commonly known as the Variable-leaf Dancing Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences that produce numerous fragrant, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and often exhibit variation in shape and size, giving rise to its varietal name.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under suitable conditions.
