Blumea dregeana

Blumea dregeana, commonly known as the broadleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are yellow and are arranged in clusters, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
