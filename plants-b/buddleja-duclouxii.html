Buddleja duclouxii

Buddleja duclouxii, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract a variety of pollinators. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Buddleja duclouxii is known for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
