Bulbophyllum rhombifolium

Bulbophyllum rhombifolium, commonly known as the rhomboid leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhombus-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows epiphytically on trees, relying on air and rain for nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
