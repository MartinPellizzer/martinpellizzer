Begonia calabarica

Begonia calabarica, commonly known as the Calabar begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to western Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and variegation, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, requiring consistent moisture to flourish.
