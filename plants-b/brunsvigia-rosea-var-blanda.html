Brunsvigia rosea var. blanda

Brunsvigia rosea var. blanda, commonly known as the soft pink glorybower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of soft pink, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique flowering display makes it a popular choice for adding color to coastal gardens.
