Bulbophyllum verrucosum

Bulbophyllum verrucosum, commonly known as the疣状万代兰, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and its ability to produce multiple flowering shoots. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
