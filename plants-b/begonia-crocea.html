Begonia crocea

Begonia crocea, commonly known as the scarlet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal to gardens. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or containers.

Begonia crocea is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic beauty.
