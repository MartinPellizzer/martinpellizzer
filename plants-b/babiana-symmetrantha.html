Babiana symmetrantha

Babiana symmetrantha, commonly known as the symmetrical babiana, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers with a distinctive symmetrical pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Babiana symmetrantha is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
