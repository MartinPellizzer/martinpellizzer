Ballota nigra var. sericea

Ballota nigra var. sericea, commonly known as the silky ballota, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic leaves and long-lasting blooms.
