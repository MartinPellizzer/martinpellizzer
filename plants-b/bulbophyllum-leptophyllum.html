Bulbophyllum leptophyllum

Bulbophyllum leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted for efficient light capture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum leptophyllum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
