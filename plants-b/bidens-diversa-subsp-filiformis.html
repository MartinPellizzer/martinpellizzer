Bidens diversa subsp. filiformis

Bidens diversa subsp.

filiformis, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers with prominent white markings. Its flowering period usually occurs in the late spring to early summer.

The species is noted for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
