Brunsvigia natalensis

Brunsvigia natalensis, commonly known as the Natal onion or Natal star, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer.
