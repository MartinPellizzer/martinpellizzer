Bulbophyllum heteroblepharon

Bulbophyllum heteroblepharon, commonly known as the Orchid with Two Different Eyelids, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers that display a unique structure with two distinct eyelid-like petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
