Buchanania obovata

Buchanania obovata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and compound leaves with ovate to obovate leaflets. The plant produces clusters of white, fleshy flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in late winter or early spring. Its fruits are spherical, green berries that turn red when mature, containing several seeds.

Buchanania obovata is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.
