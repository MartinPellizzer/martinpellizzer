Bondtia crassifolia

Bondtia crassifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf bondtia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers, which come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, attract pollinators such as birds and insects.

Bondtia crassifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
