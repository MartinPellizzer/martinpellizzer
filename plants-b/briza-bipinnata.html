Briza bipinnata

Briza bipinnata, commonly known as the feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery plumes that give it a soft, airy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fine texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer.

Briza bipinnata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is valued for its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
