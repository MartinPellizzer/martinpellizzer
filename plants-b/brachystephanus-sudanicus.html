Brachystephanus sudanicus

Brachystephanus sudanicus, commonly known as the Sudanese stephanus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, particularly Sudan and neighboring countries. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and efficient water use.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in desert ecosystems.
