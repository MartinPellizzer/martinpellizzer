Bidens × peacockii

Bidens × peacockii, commonly known as the peacock sunflower, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, resembling the plumage of a peacock. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it an excellent addition to ornamental landscapes.
