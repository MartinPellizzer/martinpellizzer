Bulbophyllum orthoglossum f. flavilabellum

Bulbophyllum orthoglossum f. flavilabellum, commonly known as the yellow-lipped orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow lip, which sets it apart from other members of the species. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in a striking array of colors. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to maintain its health and bloom cycle.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
