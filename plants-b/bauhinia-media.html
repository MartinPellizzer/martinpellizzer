Bauhinia media

Bauhinia media, commonly known as the bluebottle tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, papery blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia media is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
