Blechnum brooksii

Blechnum brooksii, commonly known as the New Zealand maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that resembles a feather.

Blechnum brooksii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
