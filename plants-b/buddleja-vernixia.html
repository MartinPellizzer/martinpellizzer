Buddleja vernixia

Buddleja vernixia, commonly known as the white buddleia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are tubular and attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
