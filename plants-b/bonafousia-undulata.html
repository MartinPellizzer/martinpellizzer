Bonafousia undulata

Bonafousia undulata, commonly known as the wavy bonafousia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens across southern Europe.
