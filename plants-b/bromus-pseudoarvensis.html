Bromus pseudoarvensis

Bromus pseudoarvensis, commonly known as smooth brome, is a perennial grass native to Europe and Asia.

It has erect, leafy stems and produces spikelets that contain small, two-lobed flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and agricultural fields. Smooth brome is a cool-season grass that thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

It is widely used for forage and soil stabilization due to its hardiness and adaptability.
