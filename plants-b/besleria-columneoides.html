Besleria columneoides

Besleria columneoides, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of showy, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue with a distinctive, elongated shape that resembles a spider.

Besleria columneoides thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
