Buddleja × griffithii

Buddleja × griffithii, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant produces small, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Buddleja × griffithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
