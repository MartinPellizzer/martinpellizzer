Benincasa fistulosa

Benincasa fistulosa, commonly known as the Chinese pumpkin or snake gourd, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and distinctive, elongated, pale green fruits that mature to a yellowish-orange color. The plant is native to East Asia and thrives in warm, tropical climates. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is edible when mature and is used in various culinary traditions across its native regions.
