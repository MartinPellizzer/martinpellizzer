Brachystelma megasepalum

Brachystelma megasepalum, commonly known as the giant star orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant produces a large number of small, star-shaped sepals, which give it its common name. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Brachystelma megasepalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
