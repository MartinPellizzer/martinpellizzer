Berardia dregeana

Berardia dregeana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Berardia dregeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
