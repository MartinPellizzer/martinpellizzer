Bletia autumnalis

Bletia autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn bletia, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the fall season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bletia autumnalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
