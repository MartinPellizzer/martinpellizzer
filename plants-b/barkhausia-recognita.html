Barkhausia recognita

Barkhausia recognita, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Barkhausia recognita thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a standout in both natural and cultivated settings.
