Berberis ziyunensis

Berberis ziyunensis, commonly known as the Yizhun barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional medicine practices.
