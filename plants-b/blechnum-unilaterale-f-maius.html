Blechnum unilaterale f. maius

Blechnum unilaterale f. maius, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a large, tropical fern belonging to the Blechnaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its shield-shaped pinnae and a robust, creeping rhizome system. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
