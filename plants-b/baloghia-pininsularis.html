Baloghia pininsularis

Baloghia pininsularis, commonly known as the Malabar tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with a serrated margin. Baloghia pininsularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
