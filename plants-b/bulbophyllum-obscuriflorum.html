Bulbophyllum obscuriflorum

Bulbophyllum obscuriflorum, commonly known as the dark-flowered bulbophyllum, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces small, dark purple to brown flowers. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
