Biophytum bogoroense

Biophytum bogoroense, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a striking member of the Phytomelaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
