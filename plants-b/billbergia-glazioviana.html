Billbergia glazioviana

Billbergia glazioviana, commonly known as the Glaziov's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors from red to orange. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia glazioviana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
