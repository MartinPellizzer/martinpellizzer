Brunia africana

Brunia africana, commonly known as African brunia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Brunia africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
