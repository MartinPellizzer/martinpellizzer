Berchemia polyphylla var. leioclada

Berchemia polyphylla var. leioclada, commonly known as the blue berchemia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a woody stem with a distinctive greyish bark and produces small, yellowish-green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
