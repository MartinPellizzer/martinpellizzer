Bulbophyllum injoloense subsp. injoloense

Bulbophyllum injoloense subsp.

injoloense, commonly known as the Injolo orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of Cameroon. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence and showy, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture.

The flowers, which can be pink or white, are often clustered and attract various pollinators.
