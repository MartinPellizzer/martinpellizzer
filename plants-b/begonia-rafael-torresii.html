Begonia rafael-torresii

Begonia rafael-torresii, commonly known as the Rafael Torres begonia, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, often with a contrasting throat. It prefers cool, humid conditions and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
