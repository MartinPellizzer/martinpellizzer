Beloere cistiflora

Beloere cistiflora, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig-like fruits. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy species.

Beloere cistiflora is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
