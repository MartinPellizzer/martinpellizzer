Bulbophyllum nigritianum

Bulbophyllum nigritianum, commonly known as the Black Forest orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces small, dark green leaves and has delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
