Begonia plieranensis

Begonia plieranensis, commonly known as the Plieran's begonia, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, and its stems are usually hairy and upright. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
