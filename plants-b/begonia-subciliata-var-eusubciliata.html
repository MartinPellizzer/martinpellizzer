Begonia subciliata var. eusubciliata

Begonia subciliata var. eusubciliata, commonly known as the silky begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a distinctive wavy edge, and its flowers are typically pink or red, borne on erect stems. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both garden and indoor settings.
