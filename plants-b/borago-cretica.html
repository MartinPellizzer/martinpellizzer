Borago cretica

Borago cretica, commonly known as Crete bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy environments.

Borago cretica is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
