Bridelia grandis subsp. grandis

Bridelia grandis subsp.

grandis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, often greyish bark and opposite, simple leaves.
