Begonia palmata var. gamblei

Begonia palmata var. gamblei, commonly known as the Gambler's begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that resemble those of a palm tree.

The foliage is typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture, and the plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
