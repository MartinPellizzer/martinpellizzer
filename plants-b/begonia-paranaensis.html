Begonia paranaensis

Begonia paranaensis, commonly known as the Parana begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a prominent central vein. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia paranaensis is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
