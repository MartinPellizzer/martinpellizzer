Bonamia sericea

Bonamia sericea, commonly known as silky bonamia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a sprawling growth habit, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal panicles, typically displaying shades of white or pale yellow.

Bonamia sericea is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
