Brandisia discolor

Brandisia discolor, commonly known as the variable brandisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
