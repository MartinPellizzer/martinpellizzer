Barleria rubra

Barleria rubra, commonly known as redstem barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Barleria rubra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
