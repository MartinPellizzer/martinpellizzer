Bennettiodendron cordatum

Bennettiodendron cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf bennettiodendron, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in cyathia, a feature typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its bark is thick and fibrous, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
