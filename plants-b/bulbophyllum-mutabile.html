Bulbophyllum mutabile

Bulbophyllum mutabile, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique and often colorful flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically small and come in a variety of shades, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
