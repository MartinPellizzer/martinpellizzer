Bulbophyllum saurocephalum subsp. saurocephalum

Bulbophyllum saurocephalum subsp.

saurocephalum, commonly known as the dragonhead orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth and storage of nutrients.

The plant produces erect, branching inflorescences with numerous flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapting to its humid tropical environment.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance.
