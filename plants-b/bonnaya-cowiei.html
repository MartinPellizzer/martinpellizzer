Bonnaya cowiei

Bonnaya cowiei, commonly known as the dwarf zamioculcas, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are dark green and glossy, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by red berries. It is well adapted to arid conditions, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
