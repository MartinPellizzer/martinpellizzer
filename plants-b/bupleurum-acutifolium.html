Bupleurum acutifolium

Bupleurum acutifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, and it is often found in rocky or dry soils.

Bupleurum acutifolium is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
