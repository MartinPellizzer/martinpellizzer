Bellis mexicana

Bellis mexicana, commonly known as Mexican daisies, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features small, white or pink flowers with yellow centers, arranged in a typical daisy-like pattern. Its leaves are typically basal, spoon-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
