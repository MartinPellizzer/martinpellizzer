Butonica ceylanica

Butonica ceylanica, commonly known as Ceylon nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in the evening. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens in late summer.
