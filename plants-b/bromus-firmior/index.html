Bromus Firmior

Bromus firmior is a flowering plant commonly found in grasslands and disturbed soils across North America. It belongs to the grass family and is known for its tufted growth habit and distinctive seed heads. This species is often considered a weed due to its aggressive spread and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions. Its adaptability makes it a common sight in fields and along roadsides. Despite its invasive tendencies, it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms.
