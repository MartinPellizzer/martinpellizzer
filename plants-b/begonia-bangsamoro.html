Begonia bangsamoro

Begonia bangsamoro, commonly known as the Bangsamoro begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
