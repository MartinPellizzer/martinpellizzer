Buchnera thunbergii

Buchnera thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese treefern, is a species of tree fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a complex vascular system that allows it to grow tall and support its heavy fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Buchnera thunbergii plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
