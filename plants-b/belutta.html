Belutta

Belutta, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year. Belutta thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
