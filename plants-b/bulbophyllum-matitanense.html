Bulbophyllum matitanense

Bulbophyllum matitanense, commonly known as the Matitanense orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water and nutrients to support growth during dry periods.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
