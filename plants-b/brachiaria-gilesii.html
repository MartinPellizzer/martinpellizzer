Brachiaria gilesii

Brachiaria gilesii, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed sites. Brachiaria gilesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil.

It is also used in some regions for forage and erosion control.
