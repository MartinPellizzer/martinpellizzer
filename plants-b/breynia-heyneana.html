Breynia heyneana

Breynia heyneana, commonly known as the yellow breynia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are typically yellow or reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often green or yellow, followed by clusters of bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats.

Breynia heyneana is valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
