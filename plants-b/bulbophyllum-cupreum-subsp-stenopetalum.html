Bulbophyllum cupreum subsp. stenopetalum

Bulbophyllum cupreum subsp.

stenopetalum, commonly known as the copper orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking copper-colored flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually arranged in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
