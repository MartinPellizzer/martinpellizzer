Beyeriopsis latifolia

Beyeriopsis latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf beyeriopsis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Beyeriopsis latifolia is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
