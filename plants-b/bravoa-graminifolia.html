Bravoa graminifolia

Bravoa graminifolia, commonly known as the grassy bravoa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Bravoa graminifolia is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with minimal water requirements.
