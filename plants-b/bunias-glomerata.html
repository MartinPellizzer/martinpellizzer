Bunias glomerata

Bunias glomerata, commonly known as clustered mustard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and small, yellow flowers that appear in clusters. It produces long, slender pods containing several seeds.

Bunias glomerata is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
