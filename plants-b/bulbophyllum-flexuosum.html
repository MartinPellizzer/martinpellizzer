Bulbophyllum flexuosum

Bulbophyllum flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

The genus Bulbophyllum is known for its diverse morphology, and B. flexuosum is no exception, showcasing intricate floral structures adapted for specific pollinators.
