Bulbophyllum ovatolanceatum

Bulbophyllum ovatolanceatum, commonly known as the lanceolate ovate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its elongated, ovate pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers, which are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, are borne on erect inflorescences and are notable for their intricate structure.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
