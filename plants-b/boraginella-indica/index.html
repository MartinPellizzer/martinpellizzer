Boraginella Indica

Boraginella indica is a small, flowering plant native to arid regions of Asia. It belongs to the Boraginaceae family, known for its unique blue or violet flowers. This plant is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in harsh environmental conditions. Its simple structure and resilience make it a subject of interest in ecological studies. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a significant role in its native ecosystems.
