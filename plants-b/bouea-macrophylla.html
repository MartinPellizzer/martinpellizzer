Bouea macrophylla

Bouea macrophylla, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces bright orange-red, lantern-shaped fruits that are edible when ripe. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Bouea macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
