Bandura

Bandura, commonly known as the Brazilian peppertree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and invasive tendencies in certain regions.
