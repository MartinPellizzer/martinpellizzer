Begonia xanthina argentea

Begonia xanthina argentea, commonly known as the silver begonia, is a striking hybrid plant characterized by its velvety, silver-green foliage.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental appeal makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
