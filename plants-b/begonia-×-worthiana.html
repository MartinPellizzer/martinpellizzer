Begonia × worthiana

Begonia × worthiana, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia × worthiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
