Brachyloma djerral

Brachyloma djerral, commonly known as the Western River everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along riverbanks and in open woodlands. The plant produces small, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.
