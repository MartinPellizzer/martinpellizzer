Bulbophyllum intersitum

Bulbophyllum intersitum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate markings.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
