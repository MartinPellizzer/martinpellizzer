Bouvardia cataphyllaris

Bouvardia cataphyllaris, commonly known as the Christmas Bouvardia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant has leathery, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Bouvardia cataphyllaris is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and elegant appearance.
