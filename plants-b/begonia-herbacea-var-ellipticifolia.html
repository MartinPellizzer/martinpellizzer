Begonia herbacea var. ellipticifolia

Begonia herbacea var. ellipticifolia, commonly known as the ellipticifolia begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped to elliptical leaves that are often velvety and dark green, with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its foliage and blooms contribute to its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
