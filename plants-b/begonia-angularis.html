Begonia angularis

Begonia angularis, commonly known as the angular begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

Begonia angularis is valued for its ability to add texture and color to shaded garden spaces.
