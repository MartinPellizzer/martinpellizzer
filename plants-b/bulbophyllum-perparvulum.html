Bulbophyllum perparvulum

Bulbophyllum perparvulum, commonly known as the miniature orchid or dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces tiny, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often in clusters. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
