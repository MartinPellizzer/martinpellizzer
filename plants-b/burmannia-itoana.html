Burmannia itoana

Burmannia itoana, commonly known as the red-veined burmannia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burmanniaceae and is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
