Brachypodium schimperi

Brachypodium schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian grass, is a small, annual grass species native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its drought tolerance and rapid growth, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
