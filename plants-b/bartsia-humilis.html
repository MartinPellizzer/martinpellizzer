Bartsia humilis

Bartsia humilis, commonly known as the common bartsia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are borne in clusters at the base of the stems.

Bartsia humilis is often found in moist soils and thrives in partial to full shade.
