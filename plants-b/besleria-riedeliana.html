Besleria riedeliana

Besleria riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting yellow or orange throat. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its high-altitude habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
