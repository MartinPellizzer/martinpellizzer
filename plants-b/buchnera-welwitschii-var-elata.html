Buchnera welwitschii var. elata

Buchnera welwitschii var. elata, commonly known as the tall Buchnera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Its ornamental value comes from its elegant appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
