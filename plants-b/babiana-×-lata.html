Babiana × lata

Babiana × lata, commonly known as the hybrid rain lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces a cluster of strap-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue tinge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Babiana × lata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
