Bigelowia graveolens

Bigelowia graveolens, commonly known as the New Zealand bellflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Bigelowia graveolens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
