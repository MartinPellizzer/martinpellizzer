Bupleurum rotundifolium var. intermedium

Bupleurum rotundifolium var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate round-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a fibrous root system and possesses opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbel-like clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
