Bowlesia tropaeolifolia var. gayana

Bowlesia tropaeolifolia var. gayana, commonly known as the gayana variant of the tropical tansy.

This plant is a herbaceous species native to parts of Southeast Asia, characterized by its aromatic, slightly hairy leaves and yellow flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
