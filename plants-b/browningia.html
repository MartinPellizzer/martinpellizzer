Browningia

Browningia, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, resembling flames. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its foliage consists of large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green.

Browningia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
