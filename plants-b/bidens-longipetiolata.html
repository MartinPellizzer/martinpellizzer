Bidens longipetiolata

Bidens longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with long petioles, giving it its common name. Its yellow flowers are typically arranged in clusters and bloom in late summer to early fall.

It is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.
