Bulbophyllum subaequale

Bulbophyllum subaequale, commonly known as the equal-flowered bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces pale green to white flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum subaequale is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
