Brunsvigia insizwae

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its bulbs are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.
