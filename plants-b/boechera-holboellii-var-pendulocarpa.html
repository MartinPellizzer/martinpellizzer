Boechera holboellii var. pendulocarpa

Boechera holboellii var. pendulocarpa, commonly known as the pendulous rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits.
