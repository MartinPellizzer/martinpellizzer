Barringtonia Rubra

Barringtonia tree, scientifically known as Barringtonia rubra, is a striking tropical species native to Southeast Asia. This unique tree is renowned for its large, lantern-like flowers that emit a sweet, fragrant aroma at night. The fruit, which resembles a hollow, spherical capsule, is both ornamental and edible, containing a gelatinous pulp with seeds. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for urban landscaping and reforestation projects. With its ecological and aesthetic value, the Barringtonia rubra plays a significant role in tropical ecosystems.
