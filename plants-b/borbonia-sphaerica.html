Borbonia sphaerica

Borbonia sphaerica, commonly known as the spherical borbonia, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a glossy surface, and the plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.

Borbonia sphaerica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
