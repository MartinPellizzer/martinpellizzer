Bupleurum nodiflorum subsp. nanum

Bupleurum nodiflorum subsp.

nanum, commonly known as the dwarf nodding bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments.

The plant features slender stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and are arranged in loose, terminal clusters.

This subspecies is notable for its compact size and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
