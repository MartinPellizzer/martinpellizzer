Begonia rex var. grandis

Begonia rex var. grandis, commonly known as the velvet leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, velvety, and intricately patterned leaves.

The foliage displays a rich array of colors, including deep red, maroon, and purple, with contrasting green and silver markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its unique leaf structure makes it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of ornamental foliage.
