Bunium falcarioides

Bunium falcarioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved fennel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, fern-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in umbrella-shaped clusters. The plant is native to central and southern Europe, where it often grows in rocky or dry soils. Its aromatic seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
