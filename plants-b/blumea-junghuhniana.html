Blumea junghuhniana

Blumea junghuhniana, commonly known as the white blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture, while the flowers are small and yellow with white ray florets.

It is often found in moist forest areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
