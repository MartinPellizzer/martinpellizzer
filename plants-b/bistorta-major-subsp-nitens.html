Bistorta major subsp. nitens

Bistorta major subsp.

nitens, commonly known as the alpine bistort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its rosette of dark green, hairy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple.

The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
