Begonia caparaoensis

Begonia caparaoensis, commonly known as the Caparao begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural habitat, it is a sought-after species among botanical enthusiasts and conservationists.
