Bulbophyllum conchiferum

Bulbophyllum conchiferum, commonly known as the shell orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, shell-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of yellow, red, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
