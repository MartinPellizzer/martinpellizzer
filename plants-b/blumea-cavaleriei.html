Blumea cavaleriei

Blumea cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cavalerie's blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and arranged in dense, fluffy clusters that resemble a mist.
