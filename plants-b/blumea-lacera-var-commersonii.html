Blumea lacera var. commersonii

Blumea lacera var. commersonii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.
