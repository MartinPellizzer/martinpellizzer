Bidens africana

Bidens africana, commonly known as African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in groups of two. Bidens africana is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
