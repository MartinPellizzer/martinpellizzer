Bulbophyllum lyciachungianum

Bulbophyllum lyciachungianum, commonly known as the Lyciachungianum orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
