Bryonia aspera

Bryonia aspera, commonly known as the rough bryony, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and melons, and is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit.
