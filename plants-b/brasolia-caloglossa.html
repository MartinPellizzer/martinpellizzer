Brasolia caloglossa

Brasolia caloglossa, commonly known as the glossy leaf bromeliad, is a striking plant native to South America.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom from the center of the leaf rosette. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires well-draining soil.

Brasolia caloglossa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
