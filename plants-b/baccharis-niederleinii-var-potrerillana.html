Baccharis niederleinii var. potrerillana

Baccharis niederleinii var. potrerillana, commonly known as the Potrerillan baccharis, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in xeriscaping due to its hardy nature and unique appearance.
