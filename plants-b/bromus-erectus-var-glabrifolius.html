Bromus erectus var. glabrifolius

Bromus erectus var. glabrifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leaved brome, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are smooth to the touch. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike with short, compact spikelets.

This variety is characterized by its reduced leaf pubescence, distinguishing it from other members of the Bromus genus.
