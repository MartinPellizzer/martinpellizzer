Bonellia nitida

Bonellia nitida, commonly known as the slender bonellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Bonellieae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a climbing vine, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glabrous. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they emit a faint fragrance to attract pollinators.

Bonellia nitida is often found in coastal and humid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil.
