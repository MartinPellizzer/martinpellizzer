Bulbophyllum arcuatilabium

Bulbophyllum arcuatilabium, commonly known as the curved labium orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive, curved labellum.

Bulbophyllum arcuatilabium is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
