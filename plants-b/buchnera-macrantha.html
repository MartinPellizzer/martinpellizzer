Buchnera macrantha

Buchnera macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered buchnera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Buchnera macrantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
