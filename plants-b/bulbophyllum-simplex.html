Bulbophyllum simplex

Bulbophyllum simplex, commonly known as the simple orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces pseudobulbs, which serve as storage organs for water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, growing in a rosette pattern.

Bulbophyllum simplex is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
