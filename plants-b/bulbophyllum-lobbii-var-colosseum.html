Bulbophyllum lobbii var. colosseum

Bulbophyllum lobbii var. colosseum, commonly known as the Giant Bulbophyllum, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks in the wild. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns and a strong fragrance.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique growth habit.
