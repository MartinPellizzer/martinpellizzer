Begonia jinyunensis

Begonia jinyunensis, commonly known as the Jinyun begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
