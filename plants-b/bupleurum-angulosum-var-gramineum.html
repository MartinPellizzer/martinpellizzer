Bupleurum angulosum var. gramineum

Bupleurum angulosum var. gramineum, commonly known as the slender bupleurum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments and features slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

It is valued for its adaptability and ornamental potential in gardens.
