Babingtonia peteriana

Babingtonia peteriana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. These flowers bloom during the winter months, making it a notable species in its native habitat.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
