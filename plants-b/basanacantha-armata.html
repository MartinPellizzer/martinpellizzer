Basanacantha armata

Basanacantha armata, commonly known as the spiny leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. Its thorny stems and leaves make it a distinctive and sometimes invasive species in its native habitat.

Basanacantha armata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
