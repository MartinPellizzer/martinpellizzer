Bidens insignis

Bidens insignis, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of yellow disk flowers surrounded by white ray florets.

Bidens insignis is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various wetland environments.
