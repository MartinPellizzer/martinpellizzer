Bryophyllum miniatum

Bryophyllum miniatum, commonly known as the miniature mother of thousands, is a small succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, thick leaves. The plant produces tiny offsets along the edges of its leaves, which can easily be propagated to form new plants. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it a hardy and low-maintenance species.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
