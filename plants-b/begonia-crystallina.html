Begonia crystallina

Begonia crystallina, commonly known as the crystal begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent leaves that often display a waxy, glass-like appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.

Begonia crystallina thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.
