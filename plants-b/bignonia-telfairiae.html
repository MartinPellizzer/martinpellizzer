Bignonia telfairiae

Bignonia telfairiae, commonly known as the Telfair's crossvine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a vigorous climber with long, slender stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Bignonia telfairiae is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
