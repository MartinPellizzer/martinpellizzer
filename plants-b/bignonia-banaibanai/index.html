Bignonia Banaibanai

Bignonia banaibanai is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia. Known for its vibrant orange-yellow blooms and climbing habit, it adds a unique splash of color to its natural habitat. This species is part of the Bignoniaceae family, which includes many ornamental and ecologically important plants. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
