Bidens tenuifolia

Bidens tenuifolia, commonly known as slender beggarticks, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features opposite, slender, hairy leaves that are often lance-shaped and have a rough texture. The plant produces small yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from summer to fall. Its seeds are surrounded by bracts that aid in wind dispersal.

Bidens tenuifolia is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in wetlands.
