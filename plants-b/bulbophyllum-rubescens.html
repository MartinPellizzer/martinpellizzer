Bulbophyllum rubescens

Bulbophyllum rubescens, commonly known as the Red-veined Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a reddish or purplish hue. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive red and yellow coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum rubescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
