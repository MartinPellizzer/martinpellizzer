Bidens serrulata

Bidens serrulata, commonly known as the serrated ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant features serrated leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist to wet habitats, such as swamps and marshes, and is often found in wetland ecosystems.

Bidens serrulata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
