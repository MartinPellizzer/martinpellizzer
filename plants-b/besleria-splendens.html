Besleria splendens

Besleria splendens, commonly known as the splendour begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that resemble petals. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
