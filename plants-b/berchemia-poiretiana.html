Berchemia poiretiana

Berchemia poiretiana, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a South African shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden environments.

Berchemia poiretiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
