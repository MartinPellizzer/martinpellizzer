Bulbophyllum carnosilabium

Bulbophyllum carnosilabium, commonly known as the meaty-labiate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fleshy pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive labellum that resembles a lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
