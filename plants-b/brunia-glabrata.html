Brunia glabrata

Brunia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth brunia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale blue flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy plant for xeriscaping.

Brunia glabrata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
