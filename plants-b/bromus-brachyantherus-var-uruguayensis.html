Bromus brachyantherus var. uruguayensis

Bromus brachyantherus var. uruguayensis, commonly known as the Uruguay grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous florets with pale to brownish flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native ecosystems in its range.
