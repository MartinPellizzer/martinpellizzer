Begonia bolsteri

Begonia bolsteri, commonly known as the Bolster begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia bolsteri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique and beautiful foliage and flowers.
