Begonia scapigera

Begonia scapigera, commonly known as the scapigera begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Begonia scapigera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
