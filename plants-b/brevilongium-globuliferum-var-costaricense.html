Brevilongium globuliferum var. costaricense

Brevilongium globuliferum var. costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican globular liverwort, is a small, non-vascular plant belonging to the family Marchantiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form composed of closely packed, leaf-like structures called thallus. This variety is native to Central America, particularly Costa Rica, and is adapted to humid tropical climates.
