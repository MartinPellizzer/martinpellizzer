Begonia sibthorpioides subsp. sibthorpioides

Begonia sibthorpioides subsp.

sibthorpioides, commonly known as the African nettle begonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows as an upright herb with a fibrous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
