Bidens wichmanii

Bidens wichmanii, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the southern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, sunny environments.

Bidens wichmanii is often found in wetlands, prairies, and along the edges of ponds and streams.
