Billbergia iridifolia var. concolor

Billbergia iridifolia var. concolor, commonly known as the blue billbergia, is a bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage, which features prominent white veins. The plant produces long, pendant inflorescences that bear small, white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
