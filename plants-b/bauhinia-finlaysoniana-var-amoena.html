Bauhinia finlaysoniana var. amoena

Bauhinia finlaysoniana var. amoena, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant in suitable regions.
