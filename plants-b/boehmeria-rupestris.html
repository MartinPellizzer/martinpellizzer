Boehmeria rupestris

Boehmeria rupestris, commonly known as the rock Boehmeria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, often in shaded areas with moist soil. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family.

Boehmeria rupestris is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and resilience in certain environmental conditions.
