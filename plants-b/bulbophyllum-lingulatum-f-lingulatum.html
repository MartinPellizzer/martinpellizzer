Bulbophyllum lingulatum f. lingulatum

Bulbophyllum lingulatum f. lingulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typically borne on a short inflorescence. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

This orchid is valued for its unique foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
