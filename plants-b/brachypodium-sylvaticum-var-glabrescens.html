Brachypodium sylvaticum var. glabrescens

Brachypodium sylvaticum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are smooth and hairless, giving it its varietal name. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers.

This variety is notable for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various insect species.
