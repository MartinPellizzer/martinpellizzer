Bidens pallida

Bidens pallida, commonly known as pale beggarweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its small, yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along roadsides. It has a taproot system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Pale beggarweed is sometimes considered a weed but has also been used in traditional herbal medicine.
