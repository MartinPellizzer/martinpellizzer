Bidens degeneri

Bidens degeneri, commonly known as the yellow oxeye daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with white centers, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in disturbed soils and open areas. It is an annual or biennial herb that grows up to 30 cm tall.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many daisies and sunflowers.
