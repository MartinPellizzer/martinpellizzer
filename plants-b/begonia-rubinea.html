Begonia rubinea

Begonia rubinea, commonly known as the red begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Begonia rubinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
