Bileveillea

Bileveillea, commonly known as the moonflower vine, is a flowering plant characterized by its large, white, nocturnal blooms.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fragrant flowers that open at night. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

Bileveillea is a climbing plant that requires support for growth and is best suited for shaded or partially shaded areas.
