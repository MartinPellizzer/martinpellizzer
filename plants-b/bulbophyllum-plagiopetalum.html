Bulbophyllum plagiopetalum

Bulbophyllum plagiopetalum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum plagiopetalum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
