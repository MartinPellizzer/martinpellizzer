Begonia tambelanensis

Begonia tambelanensis, commonly known as the Tambelan begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically red or pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia tambelanensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
