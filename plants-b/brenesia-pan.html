Brenesia pan

Brenesia pan, commonly known as the pan orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces small, intricate flowers in shades of white and pink.
