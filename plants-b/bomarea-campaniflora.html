Bomarea campaniflora

Bomarea campaniflora, commonly known as the bellflower bomarea, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is known for its ornamental value due to its showy blooms. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
