Blumea sericea

Blumea sericea, commonly known as silverleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Blumea sericea is often found in moist, shaded environments and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
