Berchemia berchemiifolia

Berchemia berchemiifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf berchemia, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its finely toothed, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
