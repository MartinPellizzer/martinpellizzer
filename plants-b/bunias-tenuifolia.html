Bunias tenuifolia

Bunias tenuifolia, commonly known as slender cabbage or wild mustard, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a biennial or annual herb characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It produces small, round pods containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environments, though it can become invasive in some regions.
