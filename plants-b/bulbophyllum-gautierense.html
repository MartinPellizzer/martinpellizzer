Bulbophyllum gautierense

Bulbophyllum gautierense, commonly known as the Gautier's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features elongated pseudobulbs that support a cluster of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive spurred lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
