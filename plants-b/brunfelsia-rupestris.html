Brunfelsia rupestris

Brunfelsia rupestris, commonly known as the rock brunfelsia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces striking blue or purple tubular flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Brunfelsia rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
