Bidens nobilioides

Bidens nobilioides, commonly known as the noble beggar's-ticks, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow with dark central markings, and it produces small, spiny fruits that aid in seed dispersal.

Bidens nobilioides is often found in moist, disturbed soils along roadsides and in wetlands.
