Begonia splendida f. argentea

Begonia splendida f. argentea, commonly known as the silver begonia, is a cultivar characterized by its striking variegated foliage.

The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a vibrant mix of green and silver-white hues. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

The plant produces small, pinkish flowers, adding a touch of color to its foliage display.
