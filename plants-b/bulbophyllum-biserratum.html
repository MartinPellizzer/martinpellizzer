Bulbophyllum biserratum

Bulbophyllum biserratum, commonly known as the serrated lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate floral structure, featuring a prominent, serrated lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a delicate fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat requirements, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
