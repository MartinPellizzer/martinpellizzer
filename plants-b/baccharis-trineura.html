Baccharis trineura

Baccharis trineura, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant features small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its aromatic leaves and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
