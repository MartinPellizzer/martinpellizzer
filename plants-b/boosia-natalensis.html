Boosia natalensis

Boosia natalensis, commonly known as Natal boosia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassland and woodland areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the warmer months.

Boosia natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
