Bilabrella kraenzliniana

Bilabrella kraenzliniana, commonly known as the silverleaf bilabrella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
