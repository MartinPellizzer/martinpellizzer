Buddleja rugosa

Buddleja rugosa, commonly known as the rugosa bush or Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Buddleja rugosa is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
