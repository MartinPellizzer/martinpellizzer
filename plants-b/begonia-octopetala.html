Begonia octopetala

Begonia octopetala, commonly known as the eight-petaled begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature eight petals, giving it its common name. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia octopetala is popular among gardeners for its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
