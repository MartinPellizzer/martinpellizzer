Begonia copelandii

Begonia copelandii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Begonia copelandii is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
