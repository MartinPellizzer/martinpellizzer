Buddleja whitei

Buddleja whitei, commonly known as the White Butterfly Bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in long, pendulous clusters. The plant has slender, arching branches and heart-shaped leaves that are medium green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Buddleja whitei is known for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators due to its nectar-rich flowers.
