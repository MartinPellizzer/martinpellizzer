Berkheya ferox

Berkheya ferox, commonly known as the Cape ironbark, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fissured bark that gives it a rugged appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Berkheya ferox is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
