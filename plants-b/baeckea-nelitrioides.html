Baeckea nelitrioides

Baeckea nelitrioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
