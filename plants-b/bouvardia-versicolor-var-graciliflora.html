Bouvardia versicolor var. graciliflora

Bouvardia versicolor var. graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered bouvardia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features delicate, pendulous flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and emit a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bouvardia versicolor var. graciliflora is often used in floral arrangements due to its ornamental value and long-lasting blooms.
