Brunia noduliflora

Brunia noduliflora, commonly known as the clustered brunia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Brunia noduliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
