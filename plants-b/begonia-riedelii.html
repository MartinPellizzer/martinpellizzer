Begonia riedelii

Begonia riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia riedelii is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation indoors.
