Biophytum nervifolium

Biophytum nervifolium, commonly known as the veined phytum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Biophytum nervifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
