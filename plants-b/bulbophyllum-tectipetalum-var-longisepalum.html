Bulbophyllum tectipetalum var. longisepalum

Bulbophyllum tectipetalum var. longisepalum, commonly known as the long-sepal bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated sepals and petals that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually fragrant and bloom during the warmer months.

The variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique and showy appearance.
