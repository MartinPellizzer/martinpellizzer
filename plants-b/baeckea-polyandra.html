Baeckea polyandra

Baeckea polyandra, commonly known as the broad-leaved baeckea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

Baeckea polyandra is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.
