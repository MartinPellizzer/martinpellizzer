Bomarea fanningiana

Bomarea fanningiana, commonly known as the Fanning's bomarea, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean island of Dominica.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters of white to pale pink petals. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and possesses heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically borne on long, slender pedicels, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
