Bryonia cretica subsp. acuta

Bryonia cretica subsp.

acuta, commonly known as the sharp-britched bryony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes cucumbers and melons, and is characterized by its tendrils and lobed leaves.
