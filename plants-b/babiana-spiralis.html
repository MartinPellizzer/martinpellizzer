Babiana spiralis

Babiana spiralis, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped flower spikes that rise above grass-like foliage. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with contrasting purple or brown markings. Babiana spiralis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, typically in the spring and summer seasons.

It is well adapted to its native environment, showcasing unique adaptations to its arid and semi-arid habitats.
