Brandesia pubiflora var. glomerata

Brandesia pubiflora var. glomerata, commonly known as the clustered brandesia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences that produce small, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for horticulturists and botanists alike.
