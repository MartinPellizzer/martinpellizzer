Blumea spectabilis

Blumea spectabilis, commonly known as the fragrant blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a distinct, pleasant scent.

The flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
