Berkheya sylvicola

Berkheya sylvicola, commonly known as the forest berkheya, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive, branched stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Berkheya sylvicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
