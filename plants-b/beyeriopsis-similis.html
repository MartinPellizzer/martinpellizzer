Beyeriopsis similis

Beyeriopsis similis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots and velvety, heart-shaped leaves. Beyeriopsis similis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
