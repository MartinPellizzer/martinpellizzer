Basistelma mexicanum

Basistelma mexicanum, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

Basistelma mexicanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
