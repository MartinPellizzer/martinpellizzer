Bulbophyllum phayamense

Bulbophyllum phayamense, commonly known as the Phayamense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its inflorescence consists of multiple small, delicate blooms that can range in color from white to pink.

Bulbophyllum phayamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
