Berberis dumaniana

Berberis dumaniana, commonly known as the Turkish barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It features spiny, thorned stems and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a slightly serrated edge.

Berberis dumaniana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
