Brayodendron

Brayodendron, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lythraceae.

It is characterized by its broad, silver-gray leaves that reflect light, giving it a distinctive appearance. These plants are valued for their ornamental beauty and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
