Bauhinia venturii

Bauhinia venturii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces pod fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Bauhinia venturii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
