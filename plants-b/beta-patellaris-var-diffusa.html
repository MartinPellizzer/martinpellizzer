Beta patellaris var. diffusa

Beta patellaris var. diffusa, commonly known as the Mediterranean water spinach, is a variant of the common spinach species.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and slightly hairy. The plant thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and mild, slightly sweet flavor.
