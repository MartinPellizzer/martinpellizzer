Bupleurum handiense

Bupleurum handiense, commonly known as the Korean angelica or Chinese angelica, is a member of the Umbelliferae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with finely divided leaflets.

Bupleurum handiense is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
