Babiana tubulosa

Babiana tubulosa, commonly known as the Easter bulb, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a short, stout stem and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Babiana tubulosa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
