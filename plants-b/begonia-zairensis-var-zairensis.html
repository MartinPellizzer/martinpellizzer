Begonia zairensis var. zairensis

Begonia zairensis var. zairensis, commonly known as the Zaire begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display vivid shades of green, red, and purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular among collectors for its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
