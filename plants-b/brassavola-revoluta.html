Brassavola revoluta

Brassavola revoluta, commonly known as the revolute orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Brassavola revoluta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
