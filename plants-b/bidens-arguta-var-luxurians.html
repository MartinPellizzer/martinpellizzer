Bidens arguta var. luxurians

Bidens arguta var. luxurians, commonly known as the roughleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, with a fibrous root system.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
