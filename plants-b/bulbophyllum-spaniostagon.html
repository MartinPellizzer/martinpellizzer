Bulbophyllum spaniostagon

Bulbophyllum spaniostagon, commonly known as the Spine-tipped orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid that typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic plant, depending on its habitat. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its leaves and flowers, which are often small and clustered. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive spur that aids in pollination.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
