Bouteloua nervata

Bouteloua nervata, commonly known as narrowleaf bluestem, is a warm-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, bluish-green leaves and spike-like flower clusters. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.

Its fibrous root system helps prevent erosion in disturbed areas.
