Bertya polymorpha var. genuina

Bertya polymorpha var. genuina, commonly known as the slender bertya, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dune environments. It has adapted to salt spray and strong coastal winds, making it a resilient species.

Its foliage is typically narrow and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in coastal gardens.
