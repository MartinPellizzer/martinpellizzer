Begonia occulta

Begonia occulta, commonly known as the hidden begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and sometimes variegated. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity to flourish.

Begonia occulta is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
