Bonnetia cordifolia

Bonnetia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf bonnetia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are surrounded by bracts.

Bonnetia cordifolia is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
