Boerhavia mutabilis

Boerhavia mutabilis, commonly known as the blushing morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red and can change color as they age. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Boerhavia mutabilis is a short-lived annual that blooms throughout the growing season.
