Begonia lunatistyla

Begonia lunatistyla, commonly known as the moonlight begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
