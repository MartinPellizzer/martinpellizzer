Brachiaria semiundulata var. lanata

Brachiaria semiundulata var. lanata, commonly known as silky grass or velvet grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its finely textured, soft foliage. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a slightly hairy surface, contributing to its velvety appearance. It thrives in moist to wet environments, often found in marshy areas and along riverbanks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
