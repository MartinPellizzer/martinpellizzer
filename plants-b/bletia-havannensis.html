Bletia havannensis

Bletia havannensis, commonly known as the Cuban orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a spike, often with a subtle fragrance. Bletia havannensis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
