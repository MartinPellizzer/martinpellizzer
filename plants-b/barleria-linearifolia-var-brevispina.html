Barleria linearifolia var. brevispina

Barleria linearifolia var. brevispina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or purple, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
