Brassaiopsis polyacantha var. andamanica

Brassaiopsis polyacantha var. andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman bristle fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features compound leaves with a serrated margin and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
