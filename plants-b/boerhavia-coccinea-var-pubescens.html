Boerhavia coccinea var. pubescens

Boerhavia coccinea var. pubescens, commonly known as red sandplum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and hairy, succulent stems, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds, aiding in its propagation. Its leaves are typically alternate and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.
