Bulbophyllum maquilingense

Bulbophyllum maquilingense, commonly known as the Maquiling orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique floral structure and epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also of interest to botanists due to its ecological and evolutionary significance.
