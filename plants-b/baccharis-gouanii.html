Baccharis gouanii

Baccharis gouanii, commonly known as the Guanacaste shrub, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry to semi-arid environments. The plant has aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.

Baccharis gouanii is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities in landscaping.
