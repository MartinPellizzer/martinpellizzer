Bergenia ligulata var. cliata

Bergenia ligulata var. cliata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bergenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features rosettes of broad, dark green, slightly hairy leaves that are narrow in shape. The plant produces upright stems bearing clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Bergenia ligulata var. cliata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and hardy nature.
