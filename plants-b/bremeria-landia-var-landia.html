Bremeria landia var. landia

Bremeria landia var. landia, commonly known as the mountain holly.

This plant is a shrub native to high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, leathery leaves that are often glossy in appearance. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the spring.

It is well adapted to cold climates and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness.
