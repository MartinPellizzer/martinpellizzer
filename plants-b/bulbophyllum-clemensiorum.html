Bulbophyllum clemensiorum

Bulbophyllum clemensiorum, commonly known as the Splendid Ghost Orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, ghost-like appearance with elongated, pale green pseudobulbs and slender, white or pale green leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with long, narrow petals and a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
