Bulbophyllum chitouense

Bulbophyllum chitouense, commonly known as the Chitouense orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in humid, tropical conditions.

The flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
