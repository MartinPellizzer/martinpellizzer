Beilschmiedia fagifolia var. gaurii

Beilschmiedia fagifolia var. gaurii, commonly known as the Gauri's beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible berries that are an important food source for various wildlife.
