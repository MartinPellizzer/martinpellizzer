Buddleja saltiana

Buddleja saltiana, commonly known as the salt glade buddleja, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are grayish-green in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pale purple to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Buddleja saltiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
