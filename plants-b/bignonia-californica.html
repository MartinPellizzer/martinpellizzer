Bignonia californica

Bignonia californica, commonly known as the California crossvine, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a deciduous vine that produces striking orange to yellow flowers in the spring. The plant has compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal regions.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
