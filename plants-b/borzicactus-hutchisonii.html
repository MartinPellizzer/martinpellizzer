Borzicactus hutchisonii

Borzicactus hutchisonii, commonly known as the Hutchison's borzicactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is a small, spiny cactus that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in white to yellow spines. It produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
