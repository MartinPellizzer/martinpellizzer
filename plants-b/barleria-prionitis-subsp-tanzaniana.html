Barleria prionitis subsp. tanzaniana

Barleria prionitis subsp.

tanzaniana, commonly known as Tanzanian barleria, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers.

The flowers are typically pink or purple, and they bloom in spring and summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
