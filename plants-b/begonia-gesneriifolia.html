Begonia gesneriifolia

Begonia gesneriifolia, commonly known as the silver velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with a silvery-green hue and deep green veins. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
