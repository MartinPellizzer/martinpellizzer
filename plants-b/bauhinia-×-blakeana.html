Bauhinia × blakeana

Bauhinia × blakeana, commonly known as the Hong Kong orchid tree, is a hybrid plant native to Hong Kong.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its showy, orchid-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces distinctive, pod-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is not found in the wild and is primarily propagated through grafting.
