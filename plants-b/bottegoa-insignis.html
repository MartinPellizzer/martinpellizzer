Bottegoa insignis

Bottegoa insignis, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Bottegoa insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other tropical regions for horticultural purposes.
