Bouteloua scabra

Bouteloua scabra, commonly known as rough bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its bluish-green foliage and rough, scabrous texture on its leaves. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Bouteloua scabra is an important component of native grassland ecosystems and is often used in restoration projects.
