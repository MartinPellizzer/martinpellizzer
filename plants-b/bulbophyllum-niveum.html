Bulbophyllum niveum

Bulbophyllum niveum, commonly known as the White Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
