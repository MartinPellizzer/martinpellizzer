Begonia calzadae

Begonia calzadae, commonly known as the Calzada begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Begonia calzadae is a perennial species that prefers well-drained soil and consistent moisture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
