Boronia anemonifolia var. variabilis

Boronia anemonifolia var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in heathland and rocky soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This variant is noted for its variability in flower color and leaf morphology, making it a distinctive species within the Boronia genus.
