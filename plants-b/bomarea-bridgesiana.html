Bomarea bridgesiana

Bomarea bridgesiana, commonly known as the Bridgewater bomarea, is a striking flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for horticultural collections. The plant features tuberous roots and slender, often hairy stems that support its ornamental foliage. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and can be quite large, adding to its visual appeal.

Bomarea bridgesiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical gardens for its beauty and unique botanical features.
