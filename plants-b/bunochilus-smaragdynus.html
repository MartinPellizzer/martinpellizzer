Bunochilus smaragdynus

Bunochilus smaragdynus, commonly known as the emerald leaf orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, lance-shaped leaves and delicate, pale green flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its botanical features include a short rhizome and a single, often branched inflorescence.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticultural collections.
