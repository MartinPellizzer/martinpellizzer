Biophytum boussingaultii

Biophytum boussingaultii, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, velvety silver-green leaves that are often adorned with red or purple veins. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
