Begonia austroyunnanensis

Begonia austroyunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan begonia, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
