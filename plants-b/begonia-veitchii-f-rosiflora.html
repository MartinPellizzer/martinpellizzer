Begonia veitchii f. rosiflora

Begonia veitchii f. rosiflora, commonly known as the Rosy-flowered Begonia, is a striking hybrid plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers, which bloom in dense clusters along the stems. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, often with a dark green coloration. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
