Banisteria patens

Banisteria patens, commonly known as the red bignonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its dense, spreading canopy. The plant produces showy, pendulous yellow flowers with red filaments, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture, while its bark is smooth and often fissured.

Banisteria patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral displays.
