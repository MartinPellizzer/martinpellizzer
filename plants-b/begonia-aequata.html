Begonia aequata

Begonia aequata, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often have a silvery or white underside. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia aequata is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
