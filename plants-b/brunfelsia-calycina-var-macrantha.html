Brunfelsia calycina var. macrantha

Brunfelsia calycina var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered bluebell, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from deep blue to violet, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are typically borne in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant flowers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
