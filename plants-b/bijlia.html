Bijlia

Bijlia, commonly known as the Indian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Bijlia is often found in forested areas and along stream banks, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
