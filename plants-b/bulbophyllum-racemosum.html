Bulbophyllum racemosum

Bulbophyllum racemosum, commonly known as the racemose bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has dark green, strap-like leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is a terrestrial orchid that prefers well-drained, loose soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
