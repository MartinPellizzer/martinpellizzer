Blepharocalyx deserti

Blepharocalyx deserti, commonly known as the desert beauty, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, showy white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and small, elliptical leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
