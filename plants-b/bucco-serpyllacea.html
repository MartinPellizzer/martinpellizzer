Bucco serpyllacea

Bucco serpyllacea, commonly known as common rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, which helps it survive in dry conditions.

Bucco serpyllacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
