Ballota luteola

Ballota luteola, commonly known as yellow ballota, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Ballota luteola is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
