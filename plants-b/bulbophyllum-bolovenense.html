Bulbophyllum bolovenense

Bulbophyllum bolovenense, commonly known as the Boloven orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and green.

Bulbophyllum bolovenense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
