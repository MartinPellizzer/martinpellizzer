Bupleurum rotundifolium subsp. intermedium

Bupleurum rotundifolium subsp.

intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate round-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its round or oval-shaped leaves.
