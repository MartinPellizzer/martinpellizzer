Borneoa Persistens

Borneoa persistens is a rare and unique plant native to the rainforests of Borneo. It is known for its striking appearance and unusual growth habits. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich, moist soil. Its leaves are broad and waxy, often displaying a deep green color with subtle variegation. Despite its beauty, Borneoa persistens remains little known outside of botanical circles.
