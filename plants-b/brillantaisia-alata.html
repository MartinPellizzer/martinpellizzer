Brillantaisia alata

Brillantaisia alata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a spectacular display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brillantaisia alata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a shade tree.
