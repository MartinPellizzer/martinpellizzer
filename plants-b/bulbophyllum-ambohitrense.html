Bulbophyllum ambohitrense

Bulbophyllum ambohitrense, commonly known as the Ambohitrense orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and green.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native region.
