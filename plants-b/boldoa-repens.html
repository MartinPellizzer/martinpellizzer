Boldoa repens

Boldoa repens, commonly known as the creeping boldoa, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense carpet along the ground.

Boldoa repens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
