Blumea leptoclada

Blumea leptoclada, commonly known as the slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the rainy season.
