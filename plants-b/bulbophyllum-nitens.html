Bulbophyllum nitens

Bulbophyllum nitens, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescence. The plant produces small, white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum nitens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
