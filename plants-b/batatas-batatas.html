Batatas batatas

Batatas batatas, commonly known as sweet potato, is a tuberous root vegetable native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its swollen, irregularly shaped roots that come in various colors such as orange, purple, and white. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and flowering stems that bear small, yellow or purple flowers. Sweet potatoes are rich in carbohydrates, fiber, and essential vitamins like vitamin A.

They thrive in warm climates and well-drained soil, making them a popular crop in tropical and subtropical regions.
