Barleria somalensis

Barleria somalensis, commonly known as Somali barleria, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Somalia and parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a rough or hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, hot climates.
