Billbergia brasiliensis

Billbergia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia brasiliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
