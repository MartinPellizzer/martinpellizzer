Balls-headleya

Balls-headleya, commonly known as the ballhead orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, ball-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
