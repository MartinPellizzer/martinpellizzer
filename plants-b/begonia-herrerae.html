Begonia herrerae

Begonia herrerae, commonly known as the Herrera's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to red. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia herrerae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
