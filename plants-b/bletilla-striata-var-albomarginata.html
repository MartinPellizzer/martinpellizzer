Bletilla striata var. albomarginata

Bletilla striata var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking white petals with yellow margins and a greenish-yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch to gardens and landscapes.
