Bothriospermum imaii

It is characterized by its distinctive bottle-shaped trunk, which stores water to help it survive in arid conditions. The plant has compound leaves with a waxy surface that reduces water loss. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters at the base of the leaves.
