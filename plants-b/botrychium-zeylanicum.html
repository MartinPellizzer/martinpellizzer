Botrychium zeylanicum

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a single, fleshy, underground rhizome with a short, erect shoot above ground. Botrychium zeylanicum is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its delicate appearance and limited distribution make it a distinctive yet rare botanical specimen.
