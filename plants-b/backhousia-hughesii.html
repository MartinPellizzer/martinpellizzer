Backhousia hughesii

Backhousia hughesii, commonly known as the Australian tea tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and showy white to pink flowers. The plant is valued for its essential oils, which are extracted from its leaves and used in aromatherapy and skincare products. Backhousia hughesii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and nectar for local wildlife.
