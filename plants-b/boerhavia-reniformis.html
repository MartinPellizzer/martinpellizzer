Boerhavia reniformis

Boerhavia reniformis, commonly known as the broadleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is adapted to thrive in sandy soils and can tolerate drought conditions due to its deep root system.
