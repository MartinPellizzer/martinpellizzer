Bulbophyllum lasiochilum

Bulbophyllum lasiochilum, commonly known as the hairy-lip bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, hairy lips on its flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
