Bryodesma fendleri

Bryodesma fendleri, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by fluffy seeds dispersed by the wind.
