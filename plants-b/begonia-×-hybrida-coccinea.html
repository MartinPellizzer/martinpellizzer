Begonia × hybrida coccinea

Begonia × hybrida coccinea, commonly known as the red-tipped begonia, is a hybrid plant prized for its vibrant foliage and showy flowers.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking red or purple edge, contrasting beautifully with its green center. The flowers are typically pink or red, clustered in dense inflorescences that add a dramatic touch to gardens or containers. This species thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
