Bulbospermum

Bulbospermum, commonly known as the African lily, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bulbous underground stems, which store nutrients and water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plants typically produce showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. They are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and as houseplants.

Bulbospermum species are generally easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering.
