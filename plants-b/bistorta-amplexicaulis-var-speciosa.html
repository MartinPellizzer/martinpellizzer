Bistorta amplexicaulis var. speciosa

Bistorta amplexicaulis var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy bistorta, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple-tinged. The plant produces tall, upright stems bearing clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate regions.
