Bertolonia ovata

Bertolonia ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf bertolonia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring upright stems covered in fine hairs. The leaves are ovate in shape, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated margin. Its small, pale blue to purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
