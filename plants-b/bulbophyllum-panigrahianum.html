Bulbophyllum panigrahianum

Bulbophyllum panigrahianum, commonly known as the Panigrahia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a complex arrangement of petals and sepals.

Bulbophyllum panigrahianum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
