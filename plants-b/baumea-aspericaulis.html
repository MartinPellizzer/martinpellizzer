Baumea aspericaulis

Baumea aspericaulis, commonly known as the rough-leaved baumea, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit. Baumea aspericaulis is often found in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream sides.
