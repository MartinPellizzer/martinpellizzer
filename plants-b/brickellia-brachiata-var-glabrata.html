Brickellia brachiata var. glabrata

Brickellia brachiata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange inflorescences composed of small, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
