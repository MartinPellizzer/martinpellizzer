Billbergia pohliana

Billbergia pohliana, commonly known as the Pohliana billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors from red to orange. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
