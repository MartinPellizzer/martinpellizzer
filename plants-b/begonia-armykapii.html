Begonia armykapii

Begonia armykapii, commonly known as the red-leaved begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red foliage that contrasts beautifully with its delicate white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening for their aesthetic appeal.

Begonia armykapii is a relatively low-maintenance plant that can be propagated through stem cuttings or seeds.
