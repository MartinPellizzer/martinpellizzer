Bulbostylis pilosa

Bulbostylis pilosa, commonly known as the hairy bulbostylis, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a small, perennial herb characterized by its tufted growth and slender, hairy stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and covered with fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
