Baeckea platycephala

Baeckea platycephala, commonly known as the flat-headed baeckea, is an evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, rounded, pale green leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Baeckea platycephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
