Begonia castrosouzae

Begonia castrosouzae, commonly known as the Rio de Janeiro begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
