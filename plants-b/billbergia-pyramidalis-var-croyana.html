Billbergia pyramidalis var. croyana

Billbergia pyramidalis var. croyana, commonly known as the Croyana Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
