Barleria purpureotincta

Barleria purpureotincta, commonly known as purple-stained barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly succulent texture. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Barleria purpureotincta is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
