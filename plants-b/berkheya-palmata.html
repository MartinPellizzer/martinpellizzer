Berkheya palmata

Berkheya palmata, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant has a rosette-forming habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
