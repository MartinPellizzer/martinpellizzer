Bulbostylis nesiotica

Bulbostylis nesiotica, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wet, marshy habitats. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically wind-pollinated.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in suitable environments.
