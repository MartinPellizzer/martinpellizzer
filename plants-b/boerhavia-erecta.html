Boerhavia erecta

Boerhavia erecta, commonly known as the redroot sandmat, is a flowering plant native to warm climates.

It is a low-growing annual herb with erect stems and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Boerhavia erecta is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
