Bidens halawana

Bidens halawana, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist or wet habitats, such as swamps and marshes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow with ray florets and are followed by distinctive bract-like structures that persist after flowering.
