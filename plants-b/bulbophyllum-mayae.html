Bulbophyllum mayae

Bulbophyllum mayae, commonly known as the Mayan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and can vary in color from yellow to orange, depending on the specific variety.

Bulbophyllum mayae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
