Bulbophyllum macranthum

Bulbophyllum macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
