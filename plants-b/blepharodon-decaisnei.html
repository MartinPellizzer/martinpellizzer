Blepharodon decaisnei

Blepharodon decaisnei, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is characterized by its striking, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. It has narrow, linear leaves and is part of the Liliaceae family.
