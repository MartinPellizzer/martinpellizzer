Beloakon

Beloakon, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous tree native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The tree produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by round, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. Beloakon thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in gardens and parks.
