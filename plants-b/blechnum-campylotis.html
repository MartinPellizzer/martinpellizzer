Blechnum campylotis

Blechnum campylotis, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are pinnate, with a leathery texture and a bluish-green coloration.

Blechnum campylotis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
