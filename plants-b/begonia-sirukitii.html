Begonia sirukitii

Begonia sirukitii, commonly known as the Sirukitii begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms, it is a sought-after plant among collectors and enthusiasts of tropical flora.
