Bulbophyllum ciliisepalum

Bulbophyllum ciliisepalum, commonly known as the feathered lip orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces showy, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant features long, slender sepals and petals that are often adorned with delicate cilia, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
