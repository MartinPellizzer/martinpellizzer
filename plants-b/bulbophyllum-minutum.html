Bulbophyllum minutum

Bulbophyllum minutum, commonly known as the miniature phalaenopsis orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often resembling miniature orchid blooms. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum minutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
