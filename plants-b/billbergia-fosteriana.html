Billbergia fosteriana

Billbergia fosteriana, commonly known as the Foster's billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the plant's use. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia fosteriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
