Begonia leptoptera

Begonia leptoptera, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and showy, bright red flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
