Begonia multistaminea

Begonia multistaminea, commonly known as the multistamine begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant typically blooms with small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
