Bulbophyllum tenuicaule

Bulbophyllum tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a spike. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum tenuicaule is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
