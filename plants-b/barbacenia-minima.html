Barbacenia minima

Barbacenia minima, commonly known as the miniature barbacenia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Barbacenia minima thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
