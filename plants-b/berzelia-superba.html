Berzelia superba

Berzelia superba, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its complex, intricate floral structure. Berzelia superba typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
