Bretschneidera

Bretschneidera, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the Campanulaceae family.

It consists of a single species, Bretschneidera sinensis, which is native to China. The plant is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.
