Brittonrosea grandicornis

Brittonrosea grandicornis, commonly known as the grandicorn rose.

It is a flowering plant native to specific regions in the southwestern United States. The plant features showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
