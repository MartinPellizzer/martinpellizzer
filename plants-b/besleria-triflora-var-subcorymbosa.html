Besleria triflora var. subcorymbosa

Besleria triflora var. subcorymbosa, commonly known as the clustered flame orchid, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red or orange flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with partial shade and well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both garden and greenhouse settings.
