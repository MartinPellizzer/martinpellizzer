Baphia dewevrei subsp. dewevrei

Baphia dewevrei subsp.

The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its showy blooms.
