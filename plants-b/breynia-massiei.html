Breynia massiei

Breynia massiei, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. Its most distinctive characteristic is its clusters of small, white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs.

Breynia massiei is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
