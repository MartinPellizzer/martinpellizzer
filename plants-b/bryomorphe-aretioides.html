Bryomorphe aretioides

Bryomorphe aretioides, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a rare and specialized epiphytic plant that thrives in rocky and arid environments.

The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or white, adapted for specific pollinators. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered in many regions.
