Baccharis odorata var. odorata

Baccharis odorata var. odorata, commonly known as sweet baccharis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
