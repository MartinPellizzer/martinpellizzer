Brassaiopsis sumatrana var. variifolia

Brassaiopsis sumatrana var. variifolia, commonly known as the variable-leaf bracken fern, is a member of the Dennstaedtiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bipinnate. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves exhibit considerable variation in shape and size, giving rise to its varietal name.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
