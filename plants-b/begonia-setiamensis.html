Begonia setiamensis

Begonia setiamensis, commonly known as the Java Begonia, is a species native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia setiamensis is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
