Brachystegia itoliensis

Brachystegia itoliensis, commonly known as the Itoli ebony, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae and is characterized by its dark, dense wood and broad, compound leaves. The plant exhibits a unique growth habit with a short, thick trunk and a spreading crown. Itoli ebony is adapted to humid, shaded environments and plays an important role in the ecological balance of its habitat.

The species is also valued for its hard, durable timber, though it faces threats from deforestation and overexploitation.
