Buiningia brevicylindrica

Buiningia brevicylindrica, commonly known as the short-cylindrical bignon, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems that can reach several meters in length. This plant is often found in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
