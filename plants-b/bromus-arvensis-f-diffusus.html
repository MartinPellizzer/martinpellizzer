Bromus arvensis f. diffusus

Bromus arvensis f. diffusus, commonly known as the diffuse field brome, is a variant of the common brome grass.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. This plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet that is usually drooping or spreading.

Bromus arvensis f. diffusus is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and its role in soil stabilization.
