Begonia fellereriana

Begonia fellereriana, commonly known as the Fellerer's begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that can range from white to pink or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
