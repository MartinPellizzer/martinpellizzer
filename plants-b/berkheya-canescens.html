Berkheya canescens

Berkheya canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Berkheya canescens is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
