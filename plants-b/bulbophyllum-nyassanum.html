Bulbophyllum nyassanum

Bulbophyllum nyassanum, commonly known as the Nyassa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Africa.

It is characterized by its intricate and often colorful floral structures, which are typical of the Orchidaceae family. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in arid environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a variety of hues that attract pollinators.

This orchid is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitats.
