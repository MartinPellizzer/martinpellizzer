Bulbophyllum odoratum var. obtusisepalum

Bulbophyllum odoratum var. obtusisepalum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile soils. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

The variety obtusisepalum is characterized by its rounded, broad sepals and petals.
