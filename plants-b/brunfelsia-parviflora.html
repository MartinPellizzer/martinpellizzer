Brunfelsia parviflora

Brunfelsia parviflora, commonly known as the night bloomer or small-flower brackenroot, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshade plants, and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
