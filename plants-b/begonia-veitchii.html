Begonia veitchii

Begonia veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Begonia veitchii is prized for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
