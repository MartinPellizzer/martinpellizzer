Borneosicyos

Borneosicyos, commonly known as the Bornean bottle gourd, is a unique climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. Its fruit, which resembles a bottle in shape, can reach lengths of over a meter and is used in traditional medicine and as a container.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
