Bugranopsis megalostachys

Bugranopsis megalostachys, commonly known as the giant spike grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces large, branched inflorescences that are often showy and colorful. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in open areas, such as grasslands and disturbed sites.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and landscape design.
