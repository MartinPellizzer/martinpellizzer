Bondtia fraseri

Bondtia fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's bondtia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Bondtia fraseri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a rockery plant.
