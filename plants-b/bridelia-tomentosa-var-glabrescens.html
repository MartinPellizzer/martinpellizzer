Bridelia tomentosa var. glabrescens

Bridelia tomentosa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bridelia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
