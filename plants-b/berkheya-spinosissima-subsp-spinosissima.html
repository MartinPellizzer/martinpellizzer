Berkheya spinosissima subsp. spinosissima

Berkheya spinosissima subsp.

spinosissima, commonly known as the African fireweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which provide protection against herbivores.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, branched inflorescences, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a striking addition to gardens and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
