Bulbostylis filamentosa var. metralis

Bulbostylis filamentosa var. metralis, commonly known as the thread-leaved bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, delicate inflorescences that consist of numerous tiny white or pale green flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role within its native habitat.
