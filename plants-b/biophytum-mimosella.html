Biophytum mimosella

Biophytum mimosella, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. The plant has hairy, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique foliage structure.
