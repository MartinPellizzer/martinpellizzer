Bromus jansenii

Bromus jansenii, commonly known as Jansen's brome, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, and it is often found in disturbed soils.

Bromus jansenii is valued for its role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
