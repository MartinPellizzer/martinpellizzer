Biota orientalis var. elegantissima

Biota orientalis var. elegantissima, commonly known as the oriental beauty vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic beauty and ability to provide shade in garden settings.
