Begonia nosymangabensis

Begonia nosymangabensis, commonly known as the Malagasy begonia, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its attractive, often velvety leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth.

Its foliage exhibits a variety of colors, including green, red, and purple, depending on the cultivar. The flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of pink or white, adding to its ornamental value.
