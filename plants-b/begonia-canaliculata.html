Begonia canaliculata

Begonia canaliculata, commonly known as the water begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy, glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
