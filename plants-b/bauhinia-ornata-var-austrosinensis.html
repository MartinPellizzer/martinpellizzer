Bauhinia ornata var. austrosinensis

Bauhinia ornata var. austrosinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous legume native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
