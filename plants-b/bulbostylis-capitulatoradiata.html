Bulbostylis capitulatoradiata

Bulbostylis capitulatoradiata, commonly known as the radiate bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces delicate, spike-like clusters of tiny white or pale blue flowers, which are typically borne on slender stems. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a unique and often overlooked member of the grass family.
