Bulbophyllum doryphoroide

Bulbophyllum doryphoroide, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, sword-shaped pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
