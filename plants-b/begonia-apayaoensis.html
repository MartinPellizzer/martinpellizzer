Begonia apayaoensis

Begonia apayaoensis, commonly known as the Apayao begonia, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range from dark green to reddish hues, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
