Bonnaya cephalantha

Bonnaya cephalantha, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Bonnaya cephalantha is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
