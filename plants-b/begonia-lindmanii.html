Begonia lindmanii

Begonia lindmanii, commonly known as the Lindman's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia lindmanii is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
