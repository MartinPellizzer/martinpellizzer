Blenocoes

Blenocoes, commonly known as the "blue orchid," is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically between September and November. Its leaves are broad and slightly waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its arid habitat.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, Blenocoes is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
