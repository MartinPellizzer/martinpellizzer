Bulbophyllum variegatum

Bulbophyllum variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves, which often display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This epiphytic orchid is popular among enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
