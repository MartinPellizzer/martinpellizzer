Begonia mindorensis

Begonia mindorensis, commonly known as the Mindanao begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its attractive foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
