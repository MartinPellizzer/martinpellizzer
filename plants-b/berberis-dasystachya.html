Berberis dasystachya

Berberis dasystachya, commonly known as the large-stamened barberry, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it valuable in both horticulture and ecological settings.
