Bulbophyllum socordine

Bulbophyllum socordine, commonly known as the purple-tipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bulbophyllum socordine is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
