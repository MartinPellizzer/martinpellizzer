Barbacenia burle-marxii

Barbacenia burle-marxii, commonly known as the Burle Marx Barbacenia, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that resemble flowers. The plant features large, palmately lobed leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
