Boopis rigidula var. patagonica

Boopis rigidula var. patagonica, commonly known as Patagonian rigid bur.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. This variant is characterized by its robust, woody stems and dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

It is adapted to withstand drought and temperature extremes, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
