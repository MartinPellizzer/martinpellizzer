Busipho

Busipho, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. Busipho is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white, making it a favorite among gardeners.
