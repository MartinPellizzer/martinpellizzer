Bihai stricta

Bihai stricta, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bihai stricta is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
