Bulbostylis elegantissima

Bulbostylis elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically during the wet season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Due to its delicate beauty and unique floral structure, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
