Bubon latifolium

Bubon latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf bubon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features large, broad leaves that are typically green and glossy, providing a striking visual appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Bubon latifolium is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and resilience.
