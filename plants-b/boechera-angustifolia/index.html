Boechera Angustifolia

Boechera angustifolia is a small flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the mustard family and is known for its delicate appearance and resilience in rocky habitats. this species thrives in high elevation environments where it often grows in clusters on barren slopes. its unique leaf shape and subtle flowers make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
