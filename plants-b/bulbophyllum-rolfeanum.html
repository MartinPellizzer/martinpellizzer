Bulbophyllum rolfeanum

Bulbophyllum rolfeanum, commonly known as the Rolfe's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the wet season. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
