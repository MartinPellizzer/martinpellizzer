Bryonia capillacea

Bryonia capillacea, commonly known as the slender bryony, is a climbing plant belonging to the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into green, spiny fruits. It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.
