Bunium turkestanicum

Bunium turkestanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan fennel, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like carrots and parsley. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, finely divided leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in umbels, typical of the family.

The seeds are small and oval, often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
