Begonia togoensis

Begonia togoensis, commonly known as the Togo begonia, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to dark purple. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
