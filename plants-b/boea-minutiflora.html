Boea minutiflora

Boea minutiflora, commonly known as the Chinese violet or small-flowered boea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a trailing vine with heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange or red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Boea minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and easy care requirements.
