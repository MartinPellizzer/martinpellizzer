Bromopsis angrenica

Bromopsis angrenica, commonly known as the Angren grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Central Asia and is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Bromopsis angrenica plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations to harsh environments.
