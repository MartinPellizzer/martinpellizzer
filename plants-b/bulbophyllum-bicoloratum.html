Bulbophyllum bicoloratum

Bulbophyllum bicoloratum, commonly known as the Two-Colored Phantom Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are distinctive, with a unique coloration pattern that includes contrasting hues of white and green.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
