Bucholzia maritima

Bucholzia maritima, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or saline soils along the shoreline. The plant features showy yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.
