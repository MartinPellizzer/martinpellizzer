Bletia concolor

Bletia concolor, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Bletia concolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
