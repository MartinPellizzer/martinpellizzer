Bulbophyllum lissogaster

Bulbophyllum lissogaster, commonly known as the smooth-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant, flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually smooth and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, tropical environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
