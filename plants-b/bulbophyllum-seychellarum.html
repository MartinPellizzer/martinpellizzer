Bulbophyllum seychellarum

Bulbophyllum seychellarum, commonly known as the Seychelles orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Seychelles Islands.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within the undergrowth of tropical forests. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that are often pale yellow or white with intricate patterns. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the rich, organic soil.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique floral structure.
