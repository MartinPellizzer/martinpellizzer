Buxanthus pedicellatus

Buxanthus pedicellatus, commonly known as the mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. Buxanthus pedicellatus is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
