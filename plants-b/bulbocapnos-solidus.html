Bulbocapnos solidus

Bulbocapnos solidus, commonly known as the rock fumitory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Bulbocapnos solidus is a perennial species that is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with well-draining soil.
