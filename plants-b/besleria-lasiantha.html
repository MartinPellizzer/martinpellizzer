Besleria lasiantha

Besleria lasiantha, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces showy, pendulous blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation and botanical collections.
