Belosynapsis epiphytica

Belosynapsis epiphytica, commonly known as the epiphytic orchid, is a rare and specialized plant that grows on the surfaces of other plants, typically in humid tropical environments.

The plant has a compact, aerial structure with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying vibrant colors to attract pollinators.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Belosynapsis epiphytica is considered a delicate and endangered species in its native range.
