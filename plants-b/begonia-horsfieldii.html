Begonia horsfieldii

Begonia horsfieldii, commonly known as the Horsfield's begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant red flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and prolonged blooming period.
