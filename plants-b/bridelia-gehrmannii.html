Bridelia gehrmannii

Bridelia gehrmannii, commonly known as the white-flowered bridelia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
