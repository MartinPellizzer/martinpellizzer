Boerhavia hereroensis

Boerhavia hereroensis, commonly known as Herero's purslane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its stems and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Boerhavia hereroensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to prevent soil erosion.
