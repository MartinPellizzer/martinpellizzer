Begonia parodiana

Begonia parodiana, commonly known as the Parodiana begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct reddish or purple underside. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia parodiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
