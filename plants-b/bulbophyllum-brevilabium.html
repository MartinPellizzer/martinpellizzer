Bulbophyllum brevilabium

Bulbophyllum brevilabium, commonly known as the short-lipped bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in well-draining soil.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
