Bulbostylis hispidula subsp. intermedia

Bulbostylis hispidula subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the hairy bulbostylis, is a small, grass-like plant native to parts of Africa. It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants.
