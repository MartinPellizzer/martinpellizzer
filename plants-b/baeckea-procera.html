Baeckea procera

Baeckea procera, commonly known as the slender baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its flowering period usually occurs during the spring and early summer months.

Baeckea procera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
