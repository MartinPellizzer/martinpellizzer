Breynia bicolor

Breynia bicolor, commonly known as the red-leaf breynia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red leaves that contrast with its glossy green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Breynia bicolor is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf color and adaptability to various soil types.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
