Bulbophyllum fierenanaense

Bulbophyllum fierenanaense, commonly known as the Fierenanaense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and striking floral structure. The plant typically produces small, elongated pseudobulbs that serve as storage organs for water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, making it a visually appealing species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered orchid in its native region.
