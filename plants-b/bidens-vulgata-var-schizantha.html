Bidens vulgata var. schizantha

Bidens vulgata var. schizantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf beggarlice, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and often have a distinct serrated edge, while the flowers are yellow with white markings.

It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its widespread distribution.
