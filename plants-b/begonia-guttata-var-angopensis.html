Begonia guttata var. angopensis

Begonia guttata var. angopensis, commonly known as the Angolan begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant red and green foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and small, waxy, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to Angola, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. Its unique leaf markings and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and indirect sunlight.
