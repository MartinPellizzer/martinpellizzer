Berteroa viridis

Berteroa viridis, commonly known as the green berteroa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Berteroa viridis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
