Besleria longipedunculata

Besleria longipedunculata, commonly known as the long peduncle besleria, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence known as a spadix, which is enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its foliage is broad and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.
