Bulbophyllum breviflorum

Bulbophyllum breviflorum, commonly known as the short-flowered pineapple orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This epiphytic orchid grows on trees and has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish.
