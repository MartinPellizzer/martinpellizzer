Bulbophyllum gofferjei

Bulbophyllum gofferjei, commonly known as the Goffe's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique and striking floral structure. The plant produces showy, often colorful flowers that are typically arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. Its morphology includes fleshy, pseudobulb-like structures that store water and nutrients, typical of many epiphytic orchids.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
