Bunchosia leonis f. rotundata

Bunchosia leonis f. rotundata, commonly known as the rounded-leaved bunchosia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and rounded, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are often borne on the same stems as the leaves. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
