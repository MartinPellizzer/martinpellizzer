Bidens tripteris

Bidens tripteris, commonly known as the three-toothed ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with two rows of bracts that give it its common name. It is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.

Bidens tripteris is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
