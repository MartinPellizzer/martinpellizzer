Brodiaea terrestris subsp. kernensis

Brodiaea terrestris subsp.

kernensis, commonly known as the Kern's brodiaea, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a bulbous perennial that produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are grass-like and basal, while its flowers are borne on slender stems.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in areas with well-drained soil.
