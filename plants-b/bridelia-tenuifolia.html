Bridelia tenuifolia

Bridelia tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bridelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features simple, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Bridelia tenuifolia is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
