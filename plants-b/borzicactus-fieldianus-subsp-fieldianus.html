Borzicactus fieldianus subsp. fieldianus

Borzicactus fieldianus subsp.

fieldianus, commonly known as the Field's borzicactus, is a succulent cactus native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in sparse, pale green spines.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to desert conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and cactus enthusiasts.
