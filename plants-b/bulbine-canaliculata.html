Bulbine canaliculata

Bulbine canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically from spring to summer. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils along coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
