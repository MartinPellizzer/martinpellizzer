Begonia yunnanensis var. sootepensis

Begonia yunnanensis var. sootepensis, commonly known as the Sootep begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded garden landscapes.
