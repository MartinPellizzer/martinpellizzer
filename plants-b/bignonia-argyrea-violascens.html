Bignonia argyrea violascens

Bignonia argyrea violascens, commonly known as the silverleaf bignonia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain seeds covered in a fine, silvery coating. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woodlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and unique foliage.
