Bersama schweinfurthii

Bersama schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a shrub native to the high-altitude regions of eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and silver-green foliage, which gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the cooler months. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
