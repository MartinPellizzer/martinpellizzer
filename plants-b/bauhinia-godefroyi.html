Bauhinia godefroyi

Bauhinia godefroyi, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Bauhinia godefroyi is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
