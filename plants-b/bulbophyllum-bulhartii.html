Bulbophyllum bulhartii

Bulbophyllum bulhartii, commonly known as the Bulbophyllum bulhartii, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological role.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
