Barnadesia rosea

Barnadesia rosea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white flowers, which bloom in clusters and emit a subtle fragrance. The plant features long, slender leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
