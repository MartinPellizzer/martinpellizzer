Begonia laruei

Begonia laruei, commonly known as the laruei begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Begoniaceae family, characterized by its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Due to its ornamental value, Begonia laruei is appreciated by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
