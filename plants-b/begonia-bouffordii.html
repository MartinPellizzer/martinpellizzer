Begonia bouffordii

Begonia bouffordii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia bouffordii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
