Bonamia trichantha var. trichantha

Bonamia trichantha var. trichantha, commonly known as the purple bonamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and is valued for its ornamental flowers and adaptability.
