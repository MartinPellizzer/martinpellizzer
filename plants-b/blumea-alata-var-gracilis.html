Blumea alata var. gracilis

Blumea alata var. gracilis, commonly known as slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow and occur in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

It is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.
