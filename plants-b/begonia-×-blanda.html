Begonia × blanda

Begonia × blanda, commonly known as the wax begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a variety of foliage colors, including green, variegated, and marbled patterns.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is prized for its ornamental value, particularly its glossy, waxy leaves. The plant typically grows as a trailing or upright shrub, with small, delicate flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for container gardening and hanging baskets.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, Begonia × blanda is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental use.
