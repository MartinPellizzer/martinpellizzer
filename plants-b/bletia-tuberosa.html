Bletia tuberosa

Bletia tuberosa, commonly known as the pink bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single or few leaves and produces pendulous flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive blooms.

Bletia tuberosa is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
