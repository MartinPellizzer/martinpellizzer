Berkheya carlinifolia subsp. carlinifolia

Berkheya carlinifolia subsp.

carlinifolia, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has upright, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
