Baccaurea Sumatrana

Baccaurea sumatrana is a unique tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is known for its distinctive fruit and ornamental value. The plant thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are broad and glossy, adding to its visual appeal in both natural and cultivated settings. Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is often sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
