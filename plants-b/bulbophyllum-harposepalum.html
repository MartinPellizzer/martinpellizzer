Bulbophyllum harposepalum

Bulbophyllum harposepalum, commonly known as the harposepalum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive shape that sets it apart from other orchid species. Its growth habit includes pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments.

Bulbophyllum harposepalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
