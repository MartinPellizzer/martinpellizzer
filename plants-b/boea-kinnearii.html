Boea kinnearii

Boea kinnearii, commonly known as the silver leaf vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped, silvery-green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining soil.

Boea kinnearii is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
