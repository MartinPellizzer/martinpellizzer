Bletia stenophylla

Bletia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Bletia stenophylla is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used in horticultural collections for its ornamental value.
