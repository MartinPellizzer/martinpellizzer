Botrychium lunaria var. melzeri

Botrychium lunaria var. melzeri, commonly known as the moonwort, is a fern species characterized by its unique morphology and reproductive structures.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest floors or rocky crevices. The varietal form melzeri is notable for its specific spore morphology and habitat preferences, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
