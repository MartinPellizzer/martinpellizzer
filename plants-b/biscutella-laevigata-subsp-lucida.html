Biscutella laevigata subsp. lucida

Biscutella laevigata subsp.

lucida, commonly known as the shining biscuitroot, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard plants, and is characterized by its small, white flowers and hairy leaves.
