Bossiaea distichoclada

Bossiaea distichoclada, commonly known as the two-ranked bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Bossiaea distichoclada is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
