Bulbophyllum hirundinis var. pinlinianum

Bulbophyllum hirundinis var. pinlinianum, commonly known as the slender bird's nest orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces slender, pendulous inflorescences bearing small, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulb.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in humid environments.
