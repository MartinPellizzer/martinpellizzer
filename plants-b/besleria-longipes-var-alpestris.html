Besleria longipes var. alpestris

Besleria longipes var. alpestris, commonly known as the alpine besleria, is a variant of the evergreen shrub native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture, adapted to withstand cold and dry conditions. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
