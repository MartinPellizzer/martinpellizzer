Billbergia thyrsoidea var. fastuosa

Billbergia thyrsoidea var. fastuosa, commonly known as the Crimson Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround the inflorescence, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
