Berberis vulgaris var. australis

Berberis vulgaris var. australis, commonly known as the Australian barberry, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries in autumn. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The berries are edible but should be consumed in moderation.
