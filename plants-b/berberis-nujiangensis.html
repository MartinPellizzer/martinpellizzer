Berberis nujiangensis

Berberis nujiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
