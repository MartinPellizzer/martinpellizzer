Bromus × eburonensis

Bromus × eburonensis, commonly known as the Eburon grass, is a hybrid species within the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Bromus × eburonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
