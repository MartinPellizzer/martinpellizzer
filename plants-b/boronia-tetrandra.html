Boronia tetrandra

Boronia tetrandra, commonly known as the pink boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and glossy, and the stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Boronia tetrandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
