Bulbophyllum sarcanthoides

Bulbophyllum sarcanthoides, commonly known as the orchid with the red-tinged flowers, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure and striking coloration make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
