Bulbophyllum falculicorne

Bulbophyllum falculicorne, commonly known as the sword-armed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, sword-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers with a complex, often fragrant structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
