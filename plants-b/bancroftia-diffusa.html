Bancroftia diffusa

Bancroftia diffusa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a rosette of fleshy, succulent leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces long, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Bancroftia diffusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
