Boehmeria platyphylla var. longissima

Boehmeria platyphylla var. longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed nettle, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of nettles. The plant is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

This variant is often cultivated for its fibrous stems, which are used in traditional textile production.
