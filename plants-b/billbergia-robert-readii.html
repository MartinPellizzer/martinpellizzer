Billbergia robert-readii

Billbergia robert-readii, commonly known as the Robert Read's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
