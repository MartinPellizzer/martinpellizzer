Buxus ekmanii

Buxus ekmanii, commonly known as the Cape chestnut, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Buxus ekmanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural landscapes.
