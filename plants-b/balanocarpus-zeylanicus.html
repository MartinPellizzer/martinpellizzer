Balanocarpus zeylanicus

Balanocarpus zeylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its dense, dark brown wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy upper surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark purple fruits containing flat, winged seeds.

This species is also noted for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it a significant component of the region's rainforest ecosystems.
