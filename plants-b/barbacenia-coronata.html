Barbacenia coronata

Barbacenia coronata, commonly known as the Crowned Barbacenia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Barbacenia coronata thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
