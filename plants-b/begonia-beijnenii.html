Begonia beijnenii

Begonia beijnenii, commonly known as the Beijnen's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with a metallic sheen. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
