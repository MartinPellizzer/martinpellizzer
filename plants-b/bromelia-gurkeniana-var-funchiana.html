Bromelia gurkeniana var. funchiana

Bromelia gurkeniana var. funchiana, commonly known as the Funchi bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, elongated leaves that form a rosette shape, often displaying a mix of green and red hues. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from yellow to orange, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
