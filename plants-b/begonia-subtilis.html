Begonia subtilis

Begonia subtilis, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia subtilis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
