Begonia xilitlensis

Begonia xilitlensis, commonly known as the Mexican begonia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting veining. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
