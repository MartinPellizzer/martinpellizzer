Brassia horichii

Brassia horichii, commonly known as the Horichii orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features elongated, green leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, colorful blooms. Brassia horichii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
