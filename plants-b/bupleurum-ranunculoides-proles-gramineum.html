Bupleurum ranunculoides proles gramineum

Bupleurum ranunculoides proles gramineum, commonly known as the slender false-burr.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
