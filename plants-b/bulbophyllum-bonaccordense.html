Bulbophyllum bonaccordense

Bulbophyllum bonaccordense, commonly known as the Bonaccord orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

This orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
