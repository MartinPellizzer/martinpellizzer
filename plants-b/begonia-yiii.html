Begonia yiii

Begonia yiii, commonly known as the Yunnan begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to its appearance.

Begonia yiii is valued not only for its ornamental qualities but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
