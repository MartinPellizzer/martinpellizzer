Barbacenia areniticola

Barbacenia areniticola, commonly known as the sand barbacenia, is a rare flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dune ecosystems.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
