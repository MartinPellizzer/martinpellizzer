Bulbophyllum heliophilum

Bulbophyllum heliophilum, commonly known as the sun-loving orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, often hairy foliage and produces striking, showy flowers with bright colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, though it prefers areas with ample sunlight. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, erect stems and can last for several weeks.

Bulbophyllum heliophilum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
