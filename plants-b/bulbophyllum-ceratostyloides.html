Bulbophyllum ceratostyloides

Bulbophyllum ceratostyloides, commonly known as the horned-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually borne on erect inflorescences and can vary in color from yellow to orange.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
