Brachyris sectifolia

Brachyris sectifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved brachyris, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Brachyris sectifolia is adapted to cold, dry climates and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
