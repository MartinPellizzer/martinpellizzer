Burmannia quadriflora

Burmannia quadriflora, commonly known as the four-flowered burmannia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing herb with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short, hairy stalks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
