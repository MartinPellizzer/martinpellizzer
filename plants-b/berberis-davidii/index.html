Berberis Davidii

Berberis davidii is a hardy shrub native to the mountainous regions of China and Tibet. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and prickly stems that provide natural protection. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its ornamental value is matched by its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Berberis davidii is not only a beautiful addition to gardens but also has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
