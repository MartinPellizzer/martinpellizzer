Buddleja × hybrida

Buddleja × hybrida, commonly known as the butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often fragrant leaves, which are typically opposite in arrangement. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, attracting a variety of pollinators. Buddleja × hybrida is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it is considered invasive in some regions due to its aggressive spreading habit.
