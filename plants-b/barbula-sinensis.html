Barbula sinensis

Barbula sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese barbula, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Barbula sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
