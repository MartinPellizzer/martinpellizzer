Begonia langbianensis

Begonia langbianensis, commonly known as the Langbian begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage is often adorned with distinctive patterns and vibrant colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
