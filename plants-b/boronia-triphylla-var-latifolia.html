Boronia triphylla var. latifolia

Boronia triphylla var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a glossy appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive scent and visual characteristics.
