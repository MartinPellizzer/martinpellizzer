Bulbophyllum aureolabellum

Bulbophyllum aureolabellum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive golden lip, which is a key feature in its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum aureolabellum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
