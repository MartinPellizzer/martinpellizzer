Bunium ammoides

Bunium ammoides, commonly known as goat's beard, is a perennial herb belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its fern-like, compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in umbels. Bunium ammoides is also noted for its resilience, often thriving in harsh environmental conditions.
