Boronia oxyantha var. brevicalyx

Boronia oxyantha var. brevicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved boronia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and dry sclerophyll forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
