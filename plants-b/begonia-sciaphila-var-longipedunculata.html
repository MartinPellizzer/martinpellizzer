Begonia sciaphila var. longipedunculata

Begonia sciaphila var. longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-pedunculate begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia. The plant thrives in shaded environments, making it ideal for indoor cultivation or shaded garden settings. Its elongated peduncles support clusters of tubular flowers, typically in shades of red, pink, or white.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique foliage, which adds a dramatic touch to any collection.
