Boltonia japonica

Boltonia japonica, commonly known as Japanese goldenrod, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features showy, fluffy yellow to orange inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Boltonia japonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
