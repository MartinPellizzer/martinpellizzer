Barleria squarrosa

Barleria squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in dense, white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters along the stems.

Barleria squarrosa is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
