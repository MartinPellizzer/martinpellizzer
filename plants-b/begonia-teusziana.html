Begonia teusziana

Begonia teusziana, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value and ease of care have made it a favored houseplant and garden specimen.
