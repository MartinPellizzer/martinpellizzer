Brickellia californica var. desertorum

Brickellia californica var. desertorum, commonly known as desert brickellbush, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
