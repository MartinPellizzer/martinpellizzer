Bulbophyllum scaberulum var. crotalicaudatum

Bulbophyllum scaberulum var. crotalicaudatum, commonly known as the Crotalicaudatum Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often hairy, green to reddish-brown pseudobulbs. Its flowers are typically small and fragrant, appearing in clusters along the stem.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
