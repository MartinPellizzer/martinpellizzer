Berberis minutiflora var. yulungshanensis

Berberis minutiflora var. yulungshanensis, commonly known as the Yulungshan barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. The plant has thorny stems and small, leathery leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
