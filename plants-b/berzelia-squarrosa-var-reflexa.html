Berzelia squarrosa var. reflexa

Berzelia squarrosa var. reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed squarrosa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, reflexed bracts that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
