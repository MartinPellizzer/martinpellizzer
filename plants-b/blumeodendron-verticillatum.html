Blumeodendron verticillatum

Blumeodendron verticillatum, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Blumeodendron verticillatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
