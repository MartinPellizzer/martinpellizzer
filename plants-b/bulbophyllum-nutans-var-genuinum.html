Bulbophyllum nutans var. genuinum

Bulbophyllum nutans var. genuinum, commonly known as the nodding bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences and striking, often fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The genus Bulbophyllum is known for its diverse floral forms and is a significant group within the orchid family.
