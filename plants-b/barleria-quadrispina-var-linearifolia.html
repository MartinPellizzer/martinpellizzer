Barleria quadrispina var. linearifolia

Barleria quadrispina var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to early summer.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
