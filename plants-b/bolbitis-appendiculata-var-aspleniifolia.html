Bolbitis appendiculata var. aspleniifolia

Bolbitis appendiculata var. aspleniifolia, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a cultivar of the fern species Bolbitis appendiculata.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that are typically grayish-white in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant features a creeping rhizome that produces erect, branching stems. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a soft, velvety texture.

This variety thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in indoor settings.
