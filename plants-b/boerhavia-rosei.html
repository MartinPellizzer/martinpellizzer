Boerhavia rosei

Boerhavia rosei, commonly known as the rose sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with fleshy, succulent stems and small, pinkish or reddish flowers. The plant produces small, round, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Boerhavia rosei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in sandy environments.
