Bromus japonicus var. subsquarrosus

Bromus japonicus var. subsquarrosus, commonly known as Japanese brome, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in open fields, roadsides, and wastelands.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
