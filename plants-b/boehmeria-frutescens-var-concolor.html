Boehmeria frutescens var. concolor

Boehmeria frutescens var. concolor, commonly known as Chinese nettle or Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb with hairy, serrated leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other nettle species. It is characterized by its fibrous stalks and is often grown for its strong, durable fibers.
