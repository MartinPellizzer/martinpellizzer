Broussonetia × kazinoki f. humilis

Broussonetia × kazinoki f. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese wisteria, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and fragrant, pale yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for small garden spaces and container cultivation.
