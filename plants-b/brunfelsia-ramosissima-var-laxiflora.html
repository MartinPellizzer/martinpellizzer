Brunfelsia ramosissima var. laxiflora

Brunfelsia ramosissima var. laxiflora, commonly known as the laxiflora Brunfelsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its showy blooms.

The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall aesthetic appeal.
