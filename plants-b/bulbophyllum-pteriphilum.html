Bulbophyllum pteriphilum

Bulbophyllum pteriphilum, commonly known as the "bat-wing orchid," is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, bat-like shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of bat wings.

Bulbophyllum pteriphilum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
