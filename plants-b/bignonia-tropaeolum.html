Bignonia tropaeolum

Bignonia tropaeolum, commonly known as yellow shower vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Bignonia tropaeolum is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
