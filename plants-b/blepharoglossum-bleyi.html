Blepharoglossum bleyi

Blepharoglossum bleyi, commonly known as the silver-tipped fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, silver-gray fronds that are covered in fine trichomes. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular specimen in botanical gardens and among plant enthusiasts.
