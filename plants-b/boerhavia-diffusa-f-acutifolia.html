Boerhavia diffusa f. acutifolia

Boerhavia diffusa f. acutifolia, commonly known as needleleaf blushing broccoli, is a variant of the common blushing broccoli plant.

It is a perennial herb native to regions of Asia and Africa, characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its stems are often reddish in color, and the leaves are covered in a fine, powdery coating.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
