Bulbine breviracemosa

Bulbine breviracemosa, commonly known as the narrow raceme bulbine, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact raceme during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and vibrant floral display.
