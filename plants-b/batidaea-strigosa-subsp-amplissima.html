Batidaea strigosa subsp. amplissima

Batidaea strigosa subsp.

amplissima, commonly known as the broad-leaved batidaea, is a member of the Apiaceae family. It is characterized by its robust, aromatic foliage and small, white flowers that appear in umbel-like clusters.
