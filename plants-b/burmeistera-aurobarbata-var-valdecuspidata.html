Burmeistera aurobarbata var. valdecuspidata

Burmeistera aurobarbata var. valdecuspidata, commonly known as the golden barbata burmeistera, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive barbed appearance on the petals. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its unique floral display and adaptability to tropical and subtropical climates.
