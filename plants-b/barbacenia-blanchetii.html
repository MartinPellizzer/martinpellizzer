Barbacenia blanchetii

Barbacenia blanchetii, commonly known as the Brazilian violet, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its violet-colored flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Barbacenia blanchetii is known for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
