Begonia hastata

Begonia hastata, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy-edged leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia hastata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
