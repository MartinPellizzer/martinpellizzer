Boehmeria densiflora var. intermedia

Boehmeria densiflora var. intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or broadleaf nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes other members commonly referred to as nettles. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
