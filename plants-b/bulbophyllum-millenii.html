Bulbophyllum millenii

Bulbophyllum millenii, commonly known as the Orchid of the Moon, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns. Its bulbs are an essential part of its growth cycle, storing nutrients for periods of dormancy.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in many tropical gardens and greenhouses.
