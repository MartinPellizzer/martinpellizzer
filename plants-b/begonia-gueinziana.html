Begonia gueinziana

Begonia gueinziana, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in the spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
