Billbergia eloiseae

Billbergia eloiseae, commonly known as the Eloi's billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Billbergia, native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts that enclose small, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
