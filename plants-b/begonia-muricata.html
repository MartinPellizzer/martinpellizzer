Begonia muricata

Begonia muricata, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia muricata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
