Baccharis × hoehneana

Baccharis × hoehneana, commonly known as the hybrid baccharis, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically blooming in the spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Baccharis × hoehneana is valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
