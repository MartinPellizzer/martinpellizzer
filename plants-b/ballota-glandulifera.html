Ballota glandulifera

Ballota glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, round, yellowish pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Ballota glandulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
