Bidens tripartita var. hybrida

Bidens tripartita var. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid ironweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its serrated, triangular leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as wetlands and roadside ditches. It has a taproot system and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Its common name reflects its historical use in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
