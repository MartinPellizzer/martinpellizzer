Blechnum capense var. procerum

Blechnum capense var. procerum, commonly known as the Natal holly fern, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a fern that belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its upright, clump-forming growth habit. The fronds are typically leathery, with a dark green color and a serrated margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is distinguished by its longer, more slender fronds compared to the typical form of Blechnum capense.
