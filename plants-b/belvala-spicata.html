Belvala spicata

Belvala spicata, commonly known as the spike bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or purple. Belvala spicata is valued in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
