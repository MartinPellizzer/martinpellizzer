Begonia maestrensis

Begonia maestrensis, commonly known as the Maestrensis Begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored foliage and showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and can grow as a trailing or upright plant depending on the conditions. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce numerous small, delicate flowers throughout the growing season.
