Bulbostylis melanocephala

Bulbostylis melanocephala, commonly known as the black-headed bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive dark purple or black inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it forms dense, low-growing clumps. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Bulbostylis melanocephala is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and adaptability to moist environments.
