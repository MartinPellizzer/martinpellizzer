Bossiaea preissii

Bossiaea preissii, commonly known as the Kangaroo Island bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to South Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.

Bossiaea preissii is an important part of the local flora and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
