Bryobium dischorense

Bryobium dischorense, commonly known as the slender liverwort, is a small, leafy liverwort belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and the presence of rhizoids that anchor it to its substrate. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or soil in temperate regions. Its leaves are closely arranged and have a distinctive, narrow shape.

Bryobium dischorense plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for microorganisms in its ecosystem.
