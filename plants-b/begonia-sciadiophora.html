Begonia sciadiophora

Begonia sciadiophora, commonly known as the shade begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in low-light conditions, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Begonia sciadiophora is relatively easy to care for, requiring consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
