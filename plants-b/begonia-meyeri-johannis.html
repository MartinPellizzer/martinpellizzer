Begonia meyeri-johannis

Begonia meyeri-johannis, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to dark red. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia meyeri-johannis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
