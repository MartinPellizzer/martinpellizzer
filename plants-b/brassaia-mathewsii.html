Brassaia mathewsii

Brassaia mathewsii, commonly known as the Mathews' wax plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Brassaia mathewsii is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
