Bidens richardsiae

Bidens richardsiae, commonly known as the richardsia tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Bidens richardsiae is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
