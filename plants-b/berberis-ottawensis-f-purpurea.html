Berberis ottawensis f. purpurea

Berberis ottawensis f. purpurea, commonly known as the Ottawa barberry, is a compact shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin and produces yellow flowers in early spring. The plant bears red berries that persist into winter, adding ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its vibrant fall color and adaptability to various garden conditions.
