Bauhinia steenisii

Bauhinia steenisii, commonly known as the red bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, fern-like leaflets. The plant produces clusters of showy, papilionaceous flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Bauhinia steenisii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
