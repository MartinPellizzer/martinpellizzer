Brownea racemosa

Brownea racemosa, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces legume pods that contain seeds. The flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
