Bulbostylis hebecarpa

Bulbostylis hebecarpa, commonly known as the slender-stalked bulbostylis, is a species of grass belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescence. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
