Brassia iguapoana

Brassia iguapoana, commonly known as the Iguapoana orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces vibrant, fragrant blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Brassia iguapoana is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
