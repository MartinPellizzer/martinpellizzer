Bladhia chinensis

Bladhia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese bladdernut, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces distinctive bladder-like fruits that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Bladhia chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
