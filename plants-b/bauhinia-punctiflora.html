Bauhinia punctiflora

Bauhinia punctiflora, commonly known as the spotted bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive pinnate leaves, which are often adorned with small, white or pale green spots. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its bark is smooth and greyish, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Bauhinia punctiflora is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
