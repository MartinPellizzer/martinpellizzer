Bunium luristanicum

Bunium luristanicum, commonly known as Persian fennel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran and Turkey.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and vegetables. The plant features a robust taproot and a basal rosette of finely divided, feathery leaves. Its flowering stems are topped with small, yellowish-green umbels that produce tiny seeds.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.
