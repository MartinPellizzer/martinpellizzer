Bonellia stenophylla

Bonellia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bonellia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Bonellieae and is characterized by its small, linear leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its botanical significance lies in its role within its native ecosystems and its potential for ecological research.

The species is also of interest for its morphological adaptations to its specific habitat.
