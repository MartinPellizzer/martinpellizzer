Bulbophyllum uniflorum var. variabile

Bulbophyllum uniflorum var. variabile, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often variable floral structures. The plant typically produces a single, showy flower per inflorescence, which can vary in color and shape depending on environmental conditions. Its leaves are usually thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This orchid is highly sought after by collectors due to its rarity and ornamental value.
