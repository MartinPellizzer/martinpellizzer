Bulbophyllum pantoblepharon var. vestitum

Bulbophyllum pantoblepharon var. vestitum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth. Its unique appearance and delicate blooms make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The varietal name "vestitum" refers to its finely textured, cloth-like leaves.
