Bracteantha acuminata

Bracteantha acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-pointed bracteantha, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, bright yellow ray flowers surrounded by tightly packed disk flowers, making it visually striking. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
