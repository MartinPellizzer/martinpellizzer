Buddleja speciosissima

Buddleja speciosissima, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Buddleja speciosissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
