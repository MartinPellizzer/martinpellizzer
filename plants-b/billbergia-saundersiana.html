Billbergia saundersiana

Billbergia saundersiana, commonly known as the Saunders' billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of long, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, spathaceous flowers that range from red to orange, often appearing in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom multiple times throughout its lifespan.
