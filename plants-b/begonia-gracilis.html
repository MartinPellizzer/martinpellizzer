Begonia gracilis

Begonia gracilis, commonly known as the slender begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often grown as a decorative plant in hanging baskets or containers.

Begonia gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
