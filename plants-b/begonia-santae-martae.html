Begonia santae-martae

Begonia santae-martae, commonly known as the Santa Marta begonia, is a species native to the Santa Marta mountain range in Colombia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a waxy surface, and the flowers are usually pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
