Baccharis nebularis

Baccharis nebularis, commonly known as cloudbrush, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late summer or early fall. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, providing a distinctive appearance.

Baccharis nebularis is adapted to withstand drought and is often found in rocky soils and along stream banks.
