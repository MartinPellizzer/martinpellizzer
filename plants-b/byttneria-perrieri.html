Byttneria perrieri

Byttneria perrieri, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.
