Bothriospermum longistylum

Bothriospermum longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
