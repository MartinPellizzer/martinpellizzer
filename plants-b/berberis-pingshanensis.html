Berberis pingshanensis

Berberis pingshanensis, commonly known as the Pingshan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value and hardiness contribute to its popularity in horticulture.
