Berberis taliensis

Berberis taliensis, commonly known as the Taliensis barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
