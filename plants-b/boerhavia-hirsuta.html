Boerhavia hirsuta

Boerhavia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy blite, is a flowering plant native to warm temperate and tropical regions.

It is a low-growing herb with slender, hairy stems and small, alternate leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to dry conditions.

Boerhavia hirsuta is also known for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
