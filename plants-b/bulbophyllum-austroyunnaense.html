Bulbophyllum austroyunnaense

Bulbophyllum austroyunnaense, commonly known as the Australian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Australia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its unique morphology includes a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
