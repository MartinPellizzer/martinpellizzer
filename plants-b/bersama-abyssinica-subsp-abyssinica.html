Bersama abyssinica subsp. abyssinica

Bersama abyssinica subsp.

abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian Bersama, is a shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, temperate climate.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
