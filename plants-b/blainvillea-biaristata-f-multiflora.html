Blainvillea biaristata f. multiflora

Blainvillea biaristata f. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora blainvillea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow to orange ray flowers surrounded by tubular disc flowers, making it visually striking. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.

The foliage is usually lance-shaped with a rough texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
