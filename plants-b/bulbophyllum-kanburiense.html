Bulbophyllum kanburiense

Bulbophyllum kanburiense, commonly known as the Kanburi orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

The flowers bloom during the rainy season, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value.
