Begonia brevipedunculata

Begonia brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct reddish or purple stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia brevipedunculata is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
