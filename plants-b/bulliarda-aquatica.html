Bulliarda aquatica

Bulliarda aquatica, commonly known as the water bulliard, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and swamps. The plant features slender, erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. Its small, white to pale blue flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Bulliarda aquatica plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various insects and aquatic organisms.
