Bidens melchertii

Bidens melchertii, commonly known as Melchert's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small yellow flowers with dark centers, typically blooming in late summer and early fall. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and have serrated edges.
