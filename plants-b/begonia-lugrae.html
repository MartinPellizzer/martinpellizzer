Begonia lugrae

Begonia lugrae, commonly known as the Tenerife begonia, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and distinctive foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forested areas. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white.

Begonia lugrae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
