Bromus japonicus var. variegatus

Bromus japonicus var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated brome, is a perennial grass native to Japan.

It features slender, upright stems with a bluish-green coloration and narrow, linear leaves that have a distinct variegation pattern. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassy areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use.
