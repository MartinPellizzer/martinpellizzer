Begonia × langeana

Begonia × langeana, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its vibrant and varied foliage.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often display intricate patterns and colors. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom on tall, slender stalks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Begonia × langeana is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
