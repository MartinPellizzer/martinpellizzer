Bulbophyllum neglectum

Bulbophyllum neglectum, commonly known as the neglected orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid tropical forests. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the rainy season.

Bulbophyllum neglectum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
