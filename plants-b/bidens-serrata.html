Bidens serrata

Bidens serrata, commonly known as the serrated ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive serrated leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and riverbanks. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.

Bidens serrata is also valued for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils, making it a common sight in various ecological settings.
