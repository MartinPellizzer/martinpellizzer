Bauhinia capuronii

Bauhinia capuronii, commonly known as the African butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its seeds are encased in pods, and the plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance.

Bauhinia capuronii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
