Bolbitis Feeiana

Bolbitis feeiana is a rare and delicate fern native to the mountainous regions of China. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. This plant is known for its intricate fronds that display a unique pattern of light and dark green. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
