Bulbostylis sphaerocephala var. brunneovaginata

Bulbostylis sphaerocephala var. brunneovaginata, commonly known as the brown-vessel bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, pale brown flowers that are clustered in dense spikes.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along water margins.
