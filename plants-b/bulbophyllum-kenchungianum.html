Bulbophyllum kenchungianum

Bulbophyllum kenchungianum, commonly known as the Kenchung orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, hairy stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
