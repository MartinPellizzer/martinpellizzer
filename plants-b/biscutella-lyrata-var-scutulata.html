Biscutella lyrata var. scutulata

Biscutella lyrata var. scutulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved biscuit plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, lyre-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and slightly curled. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical interest lies in its unique morphology and adaptability to arid conditions.
