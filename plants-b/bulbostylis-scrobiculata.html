Bulbostylis scrobiculata

Bulbostylis scrobiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its compact stature and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
