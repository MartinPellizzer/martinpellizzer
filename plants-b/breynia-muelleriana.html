Breynia muelleriana

Breynia muelleriana, commonly known as the silverleaf breynia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are opposite and often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
