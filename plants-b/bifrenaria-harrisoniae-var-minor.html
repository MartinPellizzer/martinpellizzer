Bifrenaria harrisoniae var. minor

Bifrenaria harrisoniae var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
