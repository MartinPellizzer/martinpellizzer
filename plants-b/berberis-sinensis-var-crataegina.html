Berberis sinensis var. crataegina

Berberis sinensis var. crataegina, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The species is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
