Brauneria angustifolia

Brauneria angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved brauneria, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for alpine gardens and rockeries.

Brauneria angustifolia is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its striking floral display.
