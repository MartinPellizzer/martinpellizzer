Bauhinia ungulata var. ungulata

Bauhinia ungulata var. ungulata, commonly known as the horned bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and clusters of showy, pink or white flowers.
