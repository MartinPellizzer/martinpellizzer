Biophytum soukupii

Biophytum soukupii, commonly known as the "candle plant," is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, waxy leaves that can range in color from green to red, depending on light exposure. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
