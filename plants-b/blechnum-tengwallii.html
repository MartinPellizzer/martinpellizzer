Blechnum tengwallii

Blechnum tengwallii, commonly known as the New Zealand maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, finely divided appearance.

Blechnum tengwallii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
