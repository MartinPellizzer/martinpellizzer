Bossiaea eriocarpa var. eriocalyx

Bossiaea eriocarpa var. eriocalyx, commonly known as the silky bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, contributing to its distinctive look.

This variety is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
