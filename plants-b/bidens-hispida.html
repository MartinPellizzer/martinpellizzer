Bidens hispida

Bidens hispida, commonly known as hispid ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are rough to the touch due to the presence of stiff hairs.

Bidens hispida produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
