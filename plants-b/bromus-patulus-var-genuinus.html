Bromus patulus var. genuinus

Bromus patulus var. genuinus, commonly known as the slender brome, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green appearance.

It is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and is sometimes used in restoration ecology.
