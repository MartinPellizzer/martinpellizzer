Bulbophyllum spectabile

Bulbophyllum spectabile, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, colorful flowers that can range from red to yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for attachment to tree trunks and can absorb moisture from the air.

Bulbophyllum spectabile is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
