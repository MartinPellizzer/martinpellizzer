Bulbophyllum discolor subsp. discolor

Bulbophyllum discolor subsp.

discolor, commonly known as the Discolor Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand and Malaysia.

This orchid is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often display a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments.

Its bulbs, which are a distinguishing feature, help it store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.
