Blumea htamanthii

Blumea htamanthii, commonly known as the Htamanthi Blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
