Babiana thunbergii

Babiana thunbergii, commonly known as the rainstorm lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred petals that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem that bears a single, showy flower, which opens after rainfall. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to seasonal droughts.

Babiana thunbergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine.
