Bouteloua diversispicula

Bouteloua diversispicula, commonly known as the diverse-bristle grass, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, blue-green leaves that are often covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Bouteloua diversispicula is often found in grasslands, open woodlands, and along roadsides.
