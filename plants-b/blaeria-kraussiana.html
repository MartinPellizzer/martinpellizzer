Blaeria kraussiana

Blaeria kraussiana, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate and elegant blooms.
