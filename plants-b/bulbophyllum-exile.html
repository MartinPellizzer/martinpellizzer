Bulbophyllum exile

Bulbophyllum exile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, narrow shape. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it has a short rhizome that helps anchor it in the soil.

Bulbophyllum exile is appreciated for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
