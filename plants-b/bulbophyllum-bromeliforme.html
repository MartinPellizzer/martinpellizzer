Bulbophyllum bromeliforme

Bulbophyllum bromeliforme, commonly known as the Bromeliform Bower, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that produces erect, hairy stems with numerous fleshy, club-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green to orange flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, organic soil.

Bulbophyllum bromeliforme is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
