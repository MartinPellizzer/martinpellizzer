Bactris alleniana

Bactris alleniana, commonly known as the Allen's palm, is a species of palm native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a tall, slender palm with a straight trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fruits resembling small, round dates. Bactris alleniana is often found in wetland or swampy areas, thriving in humid and warm climates.
