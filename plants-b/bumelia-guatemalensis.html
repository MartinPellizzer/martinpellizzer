Bumelia guatemalensis

Bumelia guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan bumelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions.
