Barbacenia brevifolia var. recurvata

Barbacenia brevifolia var. recurvata, commonly known as the curly leaf barbacenia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage with uniquely curling leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
