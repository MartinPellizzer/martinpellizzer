Brachyscome assamica

Brachyscome assamica, commonly known as the Assam daisy, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Brachyscome assamica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its cheerful appearance.
