Bihai cannoidea

Bihai cannoidea, commonly known as the giant Buddha's hand citrus, is a unique citrus species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and segmented fruit that resembles a hand in shape. The plant has a vigorous growth habit with thick, waxy leaves and produces flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a culinary ingredient due to its aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
