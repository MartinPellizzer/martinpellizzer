Binnendijkia trichostylis

Binnendijkia trichostylis, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, colorful flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences with a distinctive trichostyle structure.

Binnendijkia trichostylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
