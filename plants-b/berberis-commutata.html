Berberis commutata

Berberis commutata, commonly known as the Pacific barberry, is a shrub native to western North America.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. The plant has thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodlands and along streambanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
