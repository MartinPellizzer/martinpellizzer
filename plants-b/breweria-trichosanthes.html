Breweria trichosanthes

Breweria trichosanthes, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The botanical features of Breweria trichosanthes make it a popular choice for both garden displays and traditional herbal uses.
