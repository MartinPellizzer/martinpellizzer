Bulbophyllum xiajinchuangense

Bulbophyllum xiajinchuangense, commonly known as the Xiajinchuan orchid, is a rare and unique epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant floral structures. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its pseudobulbs for storage of water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native environment.
