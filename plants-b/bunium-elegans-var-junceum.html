Bunium elegans var. junceum

Bunium elegans var. junceum, commonly known as slender fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely dissected, feathery leaves that resemble those of fennel, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.

The aromatic seeds are used in traditional medicine and can be harvested for culinary purposes.
