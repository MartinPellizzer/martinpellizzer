Bignonia blanchetii

Bignonia blanchetii, commonly known as the Mexican crossvine, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family, which includes many ornamental and woody plants. The plant produces striking orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Bignonia blanchetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
