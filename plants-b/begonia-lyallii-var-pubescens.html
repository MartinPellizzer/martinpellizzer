Begonia lyallii var. pubescens

Begonia lyallii var. pubescens, commonly known as the Lyall's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to New Zealand.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
