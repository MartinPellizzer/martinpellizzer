Bignonia sonderi

Bignonia sonderi, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

Bignonia sonderi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
