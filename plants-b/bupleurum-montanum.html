Bupleurum montanum

Bupleurum montanum, commonly known as mountain bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and range in color from white to pale yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Bupleurum montanum is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
