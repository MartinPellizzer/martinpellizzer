Billbergia reichardtii

Billbergia reichardtii, commonly known as the Reichardt's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for its roots and epiphytic growth. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
