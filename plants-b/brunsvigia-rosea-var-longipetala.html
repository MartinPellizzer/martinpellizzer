Brunsvigia rosea var. longipetala

Brunsvigia rosea var. longipetala, commonly known as the Cape bell or fire bell, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a desirable ornamental plant.
