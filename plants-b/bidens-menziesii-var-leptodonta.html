Bidens menziesii var. leptodonta

Bidens menziesii var. leptodonta, commonly known as the slenderleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Its slender, hairy leaves and upright growth habit make it easily identifiable in its natural range.
