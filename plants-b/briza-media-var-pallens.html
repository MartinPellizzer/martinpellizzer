Briza media var. pallens

Briza media var. pallens, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-blue foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
