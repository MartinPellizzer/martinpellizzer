Bermudiana bulbosa

Bermudiana bulbosa, commonly known as the Bermuda onion, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It produces small, round bulbs that resemble onions and are often used in local cuisines. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Its bulbs are not only edible but also have medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
