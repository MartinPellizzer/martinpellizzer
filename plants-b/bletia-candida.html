Bletia candida

Bletia candida, commonly known as the white bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is usually lighter in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Bletia candida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
