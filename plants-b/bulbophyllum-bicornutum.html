Bulbophyllum bicornutum

Bulbophyllum bicornutum, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth. Its flowers typically bloom during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
