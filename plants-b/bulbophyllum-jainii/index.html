Bulbophyllum Jainii

Bulbophyllum jainii is a rare orchid species native to the mountainous regions of India. It is known for its unique floral structure and striking coloration that sets it apart from other orchid varieties. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it faces significant conservation challenges. Researchers and botanists are working to protect and study this fascinating species to ensure its survival.
