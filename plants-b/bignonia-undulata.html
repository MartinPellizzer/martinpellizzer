Bignonia undulata

Bignonia undulata, commonly known as the scarlet trumpetbush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its growth habit is typically upright, reaching heights of up to 6 feet.

Bignonia undulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
