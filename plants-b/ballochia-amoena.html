Ballochia amoena

Ballochia amoena, commonly known as the pink balloon vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink, balloon-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a vigorous growth habit, making it suitable for trellises or pergolas. Its botanical features include tendrils that help it climb and support its heavy floral displays.

Ballochia amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
