Begonia malmquistiana f. latifolia

Begonia malmquistiana f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved Malmquist begonia, is a hybrid form of the Malmquist begonia, characterized by its large, broad leaves.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding ornamental value to gardens and indoor spaces.

This variety is prized for its unique leaf shape and floral display, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
