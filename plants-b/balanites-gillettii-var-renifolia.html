Balanites gillettii var. renifolia

Balanites gillettii var. renifolia, commonly known as the desert date tree, is a shrub or small tree native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry climates. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by woody, spiny fruits containing edible seeds. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in nutrient-poor soils.

This species is well adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal water availability.
