Brickellia frutescens

Brickellia frutescens, commonly known as brickellbush, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it often grows in dry, rocky soils.

Brickellia frutescens is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally by Native American tribes.
