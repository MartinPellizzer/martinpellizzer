Bidens minensis

Bidens minensis, commonly known as the smallflower tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as fields and roadsides, thriving in temperate climates. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts, and it produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Bidens minensis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
