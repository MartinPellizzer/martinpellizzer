Bidens macrocarpa

Bidens macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-seeded tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers with dark purple centers, surrounded by pale yellow ray florets. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the stems are hairy and branched.

Bidens macrocarpa is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as roadsides and disturbed soils.
