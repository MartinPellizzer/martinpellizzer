Babiana virescens

Babiana virescens, commonly known as the greenbulb babiana, is a South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, typically in the Western and Eastern Cape regions. Its bulbs are adapted for storage, allowing it to survive dry periods.

Babiana virescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
