Barleria cavaleriei

Barleria cavaleriei, commonly known as the red barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
