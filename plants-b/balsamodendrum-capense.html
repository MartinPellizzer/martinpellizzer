Balsamodendrum capense

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
