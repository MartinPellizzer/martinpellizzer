Bulbophyllum jeanbosseri

Bulbophyllum jeanbosseri, commonly known as the Jeanbosser's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain, typical of its epiphytic lifestyle.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
