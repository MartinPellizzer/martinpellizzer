Bulbostylis somaliensis

Bulbostylis somaliensis, commonly known as the Somali bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of Somalia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white flowers. It is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils and can thrive in low-nutrient environments.
