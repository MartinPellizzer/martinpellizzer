Begonia bowerae var. nigramarga

Begonia bowerae var. nigramarga, commonly known as the black-margin begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its unique foliage.

Its leaves are typically dark green with prominent black or dark purple margins, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
