Brachyscome parvula var. lissocarpa

Brachyscome parvula var. lissocarpa, commonly known as the silky blueweed, is a small annual plant native to Australia.

It features delicate, daisy-like flowers with blue to purple petals and a yellow center, blooming from late winter to spring. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are often covered in fine white or silver hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal areas and sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its colorful display.
