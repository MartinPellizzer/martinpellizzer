Bidens insecta

Bidens insecta, commonly known as the tickseed or blackroot, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with five petals and a distinctive central disk. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and is native to various regions in the Americas. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
