Bulbophyllum compressum

Bulbophyllum compressum, commonly known as the compressed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted for water storage.

Bulbophyllum compressum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
