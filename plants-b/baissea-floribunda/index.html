Baissea Floribunda

Baissea floribunda is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of eastern Africa. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike with its unique appearance. This evergreen shrub thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific care to flourish. Its foliage is dense and dark green, adding to its ornamental value. Despite its beauty, Baissea floribunda remains a lesser-known species in the world of horticulture.
