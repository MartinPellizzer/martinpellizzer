Bulbophyllum sarcorhachis var. flavomarginatum

Bulbophyllum sarcorhachis var. flavomarginatum, commonly known as the golden-margin orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with yellow margins and greenish-blue petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
