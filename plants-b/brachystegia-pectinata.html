Brachystegia pectinata

Brachystegia pectinata, commonly known as the African brachystegia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and yellow flowers. The tree is typically found in dry savanna and woodland habitats, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife.
