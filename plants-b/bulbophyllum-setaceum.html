Bulbophyllum setaceum

Bulbophyllum setaceum, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like inflorescence that consists of numerous small, elongated flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

Bulbophyllum setaceum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
