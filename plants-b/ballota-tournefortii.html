Ballota tournefortii

Ballota tournefortii, commonly known as the Mediterranean bugloss, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a branched stem and blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in dry gardens.

Ballota tournefortii is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
