Bulbophyllum ciliatum

Bulbophyllum ciliatum, commonly known as the ciliate bulbophyllum, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in terrariums and greenhouse settings.
