Basella oleracea var. alba

Basella oleracea var. alba, commonly known as butter spinach, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, fleshy leaves that are pale green to white in color, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as a leafy vegetable.

Its succulent leaves are rich in nutrients and are used in various cuisines across its native regions.
