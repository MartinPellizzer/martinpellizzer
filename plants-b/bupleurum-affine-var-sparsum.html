Bupleurum affine var. sparsum

Bupleurum affine var. sparsum, commonly known as slender bupleurum, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
