Bromopsis inermis subsp. australis

Bromopsis inermis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian feather grass, is a slender perennial grass native to southern Australia. It features fine, silver-green leaves and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that bloom in spring.

This subspecies is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
