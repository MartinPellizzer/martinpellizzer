Bidens lejolyana

Bidens lejolyana, commonly known as the yellow beggarlice, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. It has opposite, hairy leaves and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.
