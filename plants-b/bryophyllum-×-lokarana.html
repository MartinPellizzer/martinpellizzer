Bryophyllum × lokarana

Bryophyllum × lokarana, commonly known as the African Violet, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is native to tropical Africa and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Bryophyllum × lokarana requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish.
