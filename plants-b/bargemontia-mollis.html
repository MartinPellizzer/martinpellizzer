Bargemontia mollis

Bargemontia mollis, commonly known as the softleaf marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Bargemontia mollis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
