Bambusa chunii

Bambusa chunii, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a tall, clumping bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow culms that contrast with its dark green leaves. This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Bambusa chunii is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a decorative plant in gardens.
