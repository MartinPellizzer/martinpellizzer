Buxus Rugulosa Subsp. Rupicola

Buxus rugulosa subsp. rupicola is a rare and resilient plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. This subspecies is distinguished by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit. Due to its limited range and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique characteristics make it a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
