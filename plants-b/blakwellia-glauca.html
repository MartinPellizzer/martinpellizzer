Blakwellia glauca

Blakwellia glauca, commonly known as the blue blakwellia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a compact, mat-like form. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and low maintenance requirements.
