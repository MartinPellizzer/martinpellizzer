Bowlesia glandulosa

Bowlesia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular bowlesia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with prominent glandular structures on their surfaces. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Bowlesia glandulosa is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
