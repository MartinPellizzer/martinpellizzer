Brachyscome tenuiscapa var. tenuiscapa

Brachyscome tenuiscapa var. tenuiscapa, commonly known as the slender-leafed bluebell, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
