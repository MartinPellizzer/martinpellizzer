Banksia Erythrocephala Var. Inopinata

Banksia erythrocephala var. inopinata is a unique and striking plant native to the south-western region of Western Australia. It is known for its distinctive red flower heads that stand out against the surrounding landscape. This variety of Banksia is part of a larger genus that includes over 170 species, many of which are found in the same region. Its habitat is typically in dry, sandy soils and it thrives in areas with full sun exposure. Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable component of the local flora.
