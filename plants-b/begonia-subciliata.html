Begonia subciliata

Begonia subciliata, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features velvety, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia subciliata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
