Berberis usteriana

Berberis usteriana, commonly known as the Chilean barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to southern South America.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and it produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant bears red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Berberis usteriana is also valued for its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
