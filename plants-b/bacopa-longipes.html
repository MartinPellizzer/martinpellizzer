Bacopa longipes

Bacopa longipes, commonly known as the slender water hyssop, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often found near water bodies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically opposite on the stems. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Bacopa longipes is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
