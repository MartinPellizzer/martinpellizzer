Borbonia littoralis

Borbonia littoralis, commonly known as the coastal borbonia, is a shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, coastal environments and is often found near the shoreline or in humid, shaded areas. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Borbonia littoralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
