Buchanania fragrans var. oblanceolata

Buchanania fragrans var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the fragrant Buchanania, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are oblong and slightly heart-shaped at the base. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, spicy aroma. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release small, round seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.
