Begonia multibulbillosa

Begonia multibulbillosa, commonly known as the multibulbil begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. Begonia multibulbillosa is known for its ability to form multiple bulbils along its stems, which can be used for propagation.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
