Bletia × elegans var. houtteana

Bletia × elegans var. houtteana, commonly known as the Houtte's bletia, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces tall, upright spikes bearing numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Bletia × elegans var. houtteana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
