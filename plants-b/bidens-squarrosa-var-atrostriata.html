Bidens squarrosa var. atrostriata

Bidens squarrosa var. atrostriata, commonly known as the black-striated ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and streambanks. Its flowers are yellow with dark purple markings, and it produces small, spiny fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for pollinators.
