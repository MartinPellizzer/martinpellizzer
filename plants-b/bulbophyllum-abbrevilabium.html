Bulbophyllum abbrevilabium

Bulbophyllum abbrevilabium, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically has a short, creeping rhizome with fleshy, tuberous roots that store nutrients. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, and the flowers are borne on short lateral branches.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
