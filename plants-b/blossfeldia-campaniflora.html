Blossfeldia campaniflora

Blossfeldia campaniflora, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, hence its common name. It prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
