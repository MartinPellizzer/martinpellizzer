Billbergia burchellii

Billbergia burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the plant's nourishment. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are typically long and narrow, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Billbergia burchellii thrives in bright, indirect light and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
