Bidens macrantha

Bidens macrantha, commonly known as the giant ironweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. The plant features showy yellow flowers with distinctive white markings, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Bidens macrantha is known for its robust growth and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
