Begonia daxinensis

Begonia daxinensis, commonly known as the Daxin begonia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and vibrant floral displays. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists.
