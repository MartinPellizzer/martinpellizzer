Barbarea × gradlii

Barbarea × gradlii, commonly known as the hybrid winter cress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its low-growing habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Barbarea × gradlii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
