Benincasa vacua

Benincasa vacua, commonly known as the horned melon, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It produces large, spiky, green fruits that turn orange when ripe. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its unique, bulbous flowers. The fruit's interior contains a juicy, edible pulp with numerous black seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit in warm climates.
