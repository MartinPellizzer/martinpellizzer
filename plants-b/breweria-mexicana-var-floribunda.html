Breweria mexicana var. floribunda

Breweria mexicana var. floribunda, commonly known as Mexican brewer's grass, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Poaceae family.
