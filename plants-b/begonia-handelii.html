Begonia handelii

Begonia handelii, commonly known as the red-stemmed begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
