Bulbophyllum binnendijkii

Bulbophyllum binnendijkii, commonly known as the Binnendijk's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.
