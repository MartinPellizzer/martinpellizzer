Burlingtonia venusta

Burlingtonia venusta, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a yellow or orange throat. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found growing in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

The flowers bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
