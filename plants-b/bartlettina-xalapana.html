Bartlettina xalapana

Bartlettina xalapana, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats and has lance-shaped leaves. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
