Blumeodendron gesinus

Blumeodendron gesinus, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vivid yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames, making it a popular ornamental species. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a dramatic contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Blumeodendron gesinus is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
