Babiana multiflora

Babiana multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered babiana, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces multiple flower stems, each bearing several flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Babiana multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
