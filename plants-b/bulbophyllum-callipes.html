Bulbophyllum callipes

Bulbophyllum callipes, commonly known as the Callipes orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, typically in shades of yellow and green. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
