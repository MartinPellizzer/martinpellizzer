Bourreria glabra

Bourreria glabra, commonly known as the smooth bourreria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and lance-shaped. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round fruits.

Bourreria glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
