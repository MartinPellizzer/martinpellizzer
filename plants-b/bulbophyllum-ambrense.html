Bulbophyllum ambrense

Bulbophyllum ambrense, commonly known as the Ambrense orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that supports multiple pseudobulbs. Each pseudobulb produces a single leaf and a flower spike that bears multiple fragrant, yellowish-green flowers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for enthusiasts interested in rare and exotic orchids.
