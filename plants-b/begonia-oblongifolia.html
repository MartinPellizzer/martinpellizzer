Begonia oblongifolia

Begonia oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia oblongifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
