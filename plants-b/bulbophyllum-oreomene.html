Bulbophyllum oreomene

Bulbophyllum oreomene, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and a single, upright stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
