Blumea mindanaensis

Blumea mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao blumea, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow with a central disk, and the plant is known for its aromatic properties.
