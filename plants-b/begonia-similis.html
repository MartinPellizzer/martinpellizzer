Begonia similis

Begonia similis, commonly known as the similar begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its foliage and modest blooms.

Begonia similis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and garden settings.
