Bulbophyllum asperulum

Bulbophyllum asperulum, commonly known as the rough-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, often hairy foliage. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum asperulum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
