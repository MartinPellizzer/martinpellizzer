Bellis perennis var. tubulosa

Bellis perennis var. tubulosa, commonly known as the Shiny-leaved Daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are slightly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The flowers are typically white with yellow centers, and they bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
