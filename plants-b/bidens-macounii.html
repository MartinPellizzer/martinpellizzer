Bidens macounii

Bidens macounii, commonly known as the Macoun's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.
