Burmeistera truncata

Burmeistera truncata, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Burmeistera truncata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
