Bolboxalis

Bolboxalis, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces woody pods containing small, round seeds. Bolboxalis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
