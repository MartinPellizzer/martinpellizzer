Billbergia vittata

Billbergia vittata, commonly known as the spotted billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, strap-like leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia vittata is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
