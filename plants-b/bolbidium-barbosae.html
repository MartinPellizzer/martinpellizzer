Bolbidium barbosae

Bolbidium barbosae, commonly known as the Brazilian moonseed, is a member of the Fabaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a legume that contains several seeds.

Bolbidium barbosae is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
