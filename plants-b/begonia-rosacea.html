Begonia rosacea

Begonia rosacea, commonly known as the rose begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, rose-like flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive red stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia rosacea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its beauty and ease of care.
