Bulbophyllum tardeflorens

Bulbophyllum tardeflorens, commonly known as the dark-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-brown pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and may exhibit a range of colors, from white to pale yellow.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
