Bellevalia zoharyi var. zoharyi

Bellevalia zoharyi var. zoharyi, commonly known as the Zohary's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It is often found in open habitats such as slopes and grasslands, where it thrives in full sun.
