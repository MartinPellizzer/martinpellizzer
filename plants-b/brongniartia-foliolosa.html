Brongniartia foliolosa

Brongniartia foliolosa, commonly known as the Brazilian fern, is a unique member of the Marattiaceae family.

It is a large, tropical fern characterized by its broad, leathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to three meters. Brongniartia foliolosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to the rainforests of South America.
