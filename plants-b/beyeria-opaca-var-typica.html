Beyeria opaca var. typica

Beyeria opaca var. typica, commonly known as the dark-leaved beyeria, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky outcrops. Its leaves are thick and leathery, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Beyeria opaca var. typica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
