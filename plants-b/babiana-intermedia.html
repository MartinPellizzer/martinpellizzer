Babiana intermedia

Babiana intermedia, commonly known as the rainforest bell, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial shade to full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

Babiana intermedia is well-suited for rock gardens and adds a dramatic floral display in the garden.
