Billbergia × herbaultii

Billbergia × herbaultii, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences that range in color from red to orange. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Its flowers are typically borne on tall, branching stems and can last for several weeks.

Billbergia × herbaultii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
