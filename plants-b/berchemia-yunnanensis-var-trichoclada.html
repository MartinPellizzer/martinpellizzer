Berchemia yunnanensis var. trichoclada

Berchemia yunnanensis var. trichoclada, commonly known as the Yunnan berchemia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture. It produces yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring, followed by elongated, hairy pods.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
