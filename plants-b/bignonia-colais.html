Bignonia colais

Bignonia colais, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender stems that can climb or trail, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

Bignonia colais is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
