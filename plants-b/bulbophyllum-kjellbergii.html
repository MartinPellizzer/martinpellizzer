Bulbophyllum kjellbergii

Bulbophyllum kjellbergii, commonly known as the Kjellberg's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors including white, pink, and yellow.

Bulbophyllum kjellbergii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
