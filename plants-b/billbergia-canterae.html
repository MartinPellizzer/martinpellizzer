Billbergia canterae

Billbergia canterae, commonly known as the Canary Islands billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to the Canary Islands.

It features vibrant, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup that collects rainwater. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with colorful, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its foliage is often variegated with white and green stripes, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Billbergia canterae thrives in bright, indirect light and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
