Bromus plukenetii

Bromus plukenetii, commonly known as the Plukenet's brome, is a grass species native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces spikelets that are often tinged with purple. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, and it blooms from late summer to early fall.

Bromus plukenetii is an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
