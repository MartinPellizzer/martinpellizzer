Bertiera pauloi

Bertiera pauloi, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and alternate, simple leaves that are usually elliptical in shape.
