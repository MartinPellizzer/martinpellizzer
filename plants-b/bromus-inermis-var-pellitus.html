Bromus inermis var. pellitus

Bromus inermis var. pellitus, commonly known as the smooth brome grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces spikelets that are typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant is characterized by its fine, hairless texture and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed areas due to its adaptability.

This variety is valued for its use in forage and erosion control in agricultural settings.
