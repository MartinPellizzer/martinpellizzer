Bidens andongensis

Bidens andongensis, commonly known as the Andong ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as marshes and wetlands. Its yellow flowers consist of both ray and disk florets, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
