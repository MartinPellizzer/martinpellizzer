Begonia mengtzeana

Begonia mengtzeana, commonly known as Mengtze's begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental foliage and unique inflorescence. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and striking red and green veins. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically appear in shades of pink or red.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native environment.
