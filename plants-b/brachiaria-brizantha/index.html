Brachiaria Brizantha

Brachiaria brizantha is a tall, perennial grass native to South America known for its hardiness and adaptability. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, making it a popular choice for pastureland and erosion control. This plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent water runoff. Its high nutritional value supports livestock, contributing to agricultural productivity. Despite its benefits, it can become invasive if not managed properly.
