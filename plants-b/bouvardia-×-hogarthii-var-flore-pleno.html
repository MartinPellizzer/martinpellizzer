Bouvardia × hogarthii var. flore-pleno

Bouvardia × hogarthii var. flore-pleno, commonly known as the double-flowered bouvardia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and clusters of double, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming season, typically from spring to early autumn.
