Baillieria terebinthacea

Baillieria terebinthacea, commonly known as the African terebinth.

It is a small, deciduous shrub native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. Its flowers are clustered in small inflorescences and are usually yellow or greenish in color.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
