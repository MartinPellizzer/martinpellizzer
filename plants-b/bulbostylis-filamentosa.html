Bulbostylis filamentosa

Bulbostylis filamentosa, commonly known as the slender threadgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Due to its delicate appearance and ecological importance, it is often found in habitats with consistent moisture.
