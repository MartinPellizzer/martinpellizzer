Bulbophyllum bolsteri

Bulbophyllum bolsteri, commonly known as the Bolster's orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant has fleshy, leathery leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat.

Its unique morphology and blooming habits make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
