Bombax marginatum f. fiebrigii

Bombax marginatum f. fiebrigii, commonly known as the variegated cotton tree, is a variant of the cotton tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves, which display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in the spring. Its bark is smooth and light-colored, often with a faintly flaky texture.

The leaves are typically ovate or elliptical, with a slightly waxy surface and a pointed tip.
