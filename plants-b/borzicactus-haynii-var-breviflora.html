Borzicactus haynii var. breviflora

Borzicactus haynii var. breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered borzicactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among cactus enthusiasts.
