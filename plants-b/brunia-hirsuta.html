Brunia hirsuta

Brunia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy brunia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
