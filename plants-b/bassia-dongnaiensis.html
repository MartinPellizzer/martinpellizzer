Bassia dongnaiensis

Bassia dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetland or riparian habitats.
