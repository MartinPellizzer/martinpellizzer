Begonia umbrata

Begonia umbrata, commonly known as the shade begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds due to its ornamental value.

Begonia umbrata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
