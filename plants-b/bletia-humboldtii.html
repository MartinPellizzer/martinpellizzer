Bletia humboldtii

Bletia humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt's bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bletia humboldtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
