Bidens ternata var. ternata

Bidens ternata var. ternata, commonly known as common tickseed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, branching stems and produces small, yellow flowers with ray-like petals. The plant has hairy leaves that are usually opposite or alternate in arrangement. Its seeds are enclosed in a pappus, which aids in wind dispersal.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.
