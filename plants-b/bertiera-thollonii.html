Bertiera thollonii

Bertiera thollonii, commonly known as the yellow wood or yellow-flowered bertiera, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland areas.

Bertiera thollonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
