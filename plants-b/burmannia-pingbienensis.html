Burmannia pingbienensis

Burmannia pingbienensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese burmannia, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Burmanniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, often brightly colored flowers that are typically borne on short, erect stems. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Burmannia pingbienensis is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
