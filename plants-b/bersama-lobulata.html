Bersama lobulata

Bersama lobulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in summer and are pollinated by insects, particularly bees.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
