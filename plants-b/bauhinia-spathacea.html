Bauhinia spathacea

Bauhinia spathacea, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, clustered leaves that resemble small, heart-shaped pods. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. Bauhinia spathacea is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and can thrive in well-drained soils with ample sunlight.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
