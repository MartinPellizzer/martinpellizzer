Bulbophyllum affinoides

Bulbophyllum affinoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum affinoides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
