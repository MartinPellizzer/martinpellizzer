Beloperone nemorosa var. glabra

Beloperone nemorosa var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or violet. The leaves are lanceolate, smooth, and arranged alternately along the stem. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
