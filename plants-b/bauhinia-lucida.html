Bauhinia lucida

Bauhinia lucida, commonly known as the white bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Bauhinia lucida is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.
