Begonia laciniosa

Begonia laciniosa, commonly known as the laceleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia laciniosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
