Bersama oligoneura

Bersama oligoneura, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. Bersama oligoneura plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
