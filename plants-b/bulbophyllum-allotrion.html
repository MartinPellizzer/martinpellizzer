Bulbophyllum allotrion

Bulbophyllum allotrion, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
