Bauhinia hassleriana f. angustifolia

Bauhinia hassleriana f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bauhinia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, fern-like foliage and showy, butterfly-shaped flowers.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features compound leaves with small, elongated leaflets and produces clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers during the warmer months.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
