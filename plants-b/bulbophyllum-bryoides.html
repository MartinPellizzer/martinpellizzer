Bulbophyllum bryoides

Bulbophyllum bryoides, commonly known as the moss orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, moss-like leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with red or purple markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for both botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
