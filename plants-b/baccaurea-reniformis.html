Baccaurea reniformis

Baccaurea reniformis, commonly known as the kidney bean tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by kidney-shaped fruits. The fruits contain several seeds surrounded by a fleshy, edible pulp.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible seeds.
