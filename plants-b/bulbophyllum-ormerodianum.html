Bulbophyllum ormerodianum

Bulbophyllum ormerodianum, commonly known as the Orchid Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white, green, or yellow.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
