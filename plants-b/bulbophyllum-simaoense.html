Bulbophyllum simaoense

Bulbophyllum simaoense, commonly known as the Simao orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in its native region.
