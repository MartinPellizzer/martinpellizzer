Begonia bawangensis

Begonia bawangensis, commonly known as the onion begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique scent, reminiscent of onions, is a distinguishing feature.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
