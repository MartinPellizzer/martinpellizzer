Besleria symphytum

Besleria symphytum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Its striking inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, adding to its ornamental value.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its lush foliage and unique floral structure.
