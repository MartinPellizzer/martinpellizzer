Barkhausia rossetii

Barkhausia rossetii, commonly known as the Cape rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Barkhausia rossetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
