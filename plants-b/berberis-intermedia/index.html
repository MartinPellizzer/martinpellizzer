Berberis Intermedia

Berberis intermedia is a hardy shrub known for its spiny stems and vibrant yellow flowers. Native to regions of Asia, it thrives in a variety of soil types and climates. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and natural defenses. Its berries, though toxic to humans, are a food source for certain wildlife. With its rugged beauty, Berberis intermedia is both a resilient and striking addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
