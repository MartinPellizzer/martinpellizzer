Boosia mandalensis

Boosia mandalensis, commonly known as the Mandalensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a distinctive pattern on the petals. Boosia mandalensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
