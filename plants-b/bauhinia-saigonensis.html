Bauhinia saigonensis

Bauhinia saigonensis, commonly known as the Saigon bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features distinctive bipinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, clustered in racemes, and bloom during the warmer months.

Bauhinia saigonensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
