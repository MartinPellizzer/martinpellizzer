Blysmus compressus f. gracilis

Blysmus compressus f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender flat sedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and pond edges. Its small, three-lobed flowers are usually greenish or brownish in color.

Blysmus compressus f. gracilis is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soils.
