Bidens subalternans var. typica

Bidens subalternans var. typica, commonly known as the common tickseed, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features serrated, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disk flowers. It is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a pioneer species in wetland ecosystems.
