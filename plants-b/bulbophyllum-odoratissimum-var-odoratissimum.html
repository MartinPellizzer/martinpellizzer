Bulbophyllum odoratissimum var. odoratissimum

Bulbophyllum odoratissimum var. odoratissimum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique aroma and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
