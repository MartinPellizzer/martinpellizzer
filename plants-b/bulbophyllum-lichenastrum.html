Bulbophyllum lichenastrum

Bulbophyllum lichenastrum, commonly known as the coral spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, coral-like inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters along a branching stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum lichenastrum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
