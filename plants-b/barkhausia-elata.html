Barkhausia elata

Barkhausia elata, commonly known as the yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and drought tolerance.
