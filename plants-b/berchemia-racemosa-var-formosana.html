Berchemia racemosa var. formosana

Berchemia racemosa var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan yellow-flowered berchemia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant is noted for its clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland or shaded garden settings.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
