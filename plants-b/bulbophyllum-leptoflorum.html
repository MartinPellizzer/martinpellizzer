Bulbophyllum leptoflorum

Bulbophyllum leptoflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth and delicate, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
