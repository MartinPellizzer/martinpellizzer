Brassia elegantula

Brassia elegantula, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Brassia elegantula thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
