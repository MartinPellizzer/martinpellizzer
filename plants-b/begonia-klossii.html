Begonia klossii

Begonia klossii, commonly known as the Kloss begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant green coloration. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
