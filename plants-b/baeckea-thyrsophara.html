Baeckea thyrsophara

Baeckea thyrsophara, commonly known as the narrow-leaf baeckea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, with a distinctive blue-green hue.

Baeckea thyrsophara is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
