Begonia isalensis

Begonia isalensis, commonly known as the Isalense Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia isalensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
