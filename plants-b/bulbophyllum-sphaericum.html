Bulbophyllum sphaericum

Bulbophyllum sphaericum, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive rounded pseudobulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
