Begonia longgangensis

Begonia longgangensis, commonly known as the Longgang begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, white, and red, which adds to its ornamental value.

The species is appreciated by horticulturists for its unique appearance and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
