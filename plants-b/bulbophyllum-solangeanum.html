Bulbophyllum solangeanum

Bulbophyllum solangeanum, commonly known as the Solange's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically borne on terminal racemes and come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and yellow.

It requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to flourish.
