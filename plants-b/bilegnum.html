Bilegnum

Bilegnum, commonly known as the golden bell tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow flowers that resemble small bells, which bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often marked with distinct lenticels.

Bilegnum is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
