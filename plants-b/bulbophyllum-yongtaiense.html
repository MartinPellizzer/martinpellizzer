Bulbophyllum yongtaiense

Bulbophyllum yongtaiense, commonly known as the Yongtai orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered an important species for botanical research and conservation.
