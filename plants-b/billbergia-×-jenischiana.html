Billbergia × jenischiana

Billbergia × jenischiana, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia nuda and Billbergia recurvata.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features striking, rosette-shaped leaves that are often bright green with red or purple markings. It produces inflorescences with vibrant, long-lasting flowers that can range in color from yellow to orange.

This ornamental plant is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
