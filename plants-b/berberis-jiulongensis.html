Berberis jiulongensis

Berberis jiulongensis, commonly known as the Jiulongshan barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in spring, followed by red berries in autumn. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
