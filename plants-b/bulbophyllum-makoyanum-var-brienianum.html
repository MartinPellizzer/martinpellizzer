Bulbophyllum makoyanum var. brienianum

Bulbophyllum makoyanum var. brienianum, commonly known as the Brien's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous flowers, typically in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in humid, well-drained environments.
