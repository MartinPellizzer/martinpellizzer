Billbergia amabilis

Billbergia amabilis, commonly known as the pink billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink bracts that surround the inflorescence, giving it a distinctive and showy appearance. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia amabilis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
