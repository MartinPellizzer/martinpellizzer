Bauhinia pottsii var. subsessilis

Bauhinia pottsii var. subsessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved burr acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It features bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces distinctive yellow or orange flower clusters that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a woody pod that splits open to release flat, bean-like seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
