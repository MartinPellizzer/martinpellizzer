Blumea milnei

Blumea milnei, commonly known as the white blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptic to ovate in shape, and have a rough texture.

The flowers are yellow with white ray florets, making it an attractive ornamental plant.
