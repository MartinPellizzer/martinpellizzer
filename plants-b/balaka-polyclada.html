Balaka polyclada

Balaka polyclada, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Balaka polyclada is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
