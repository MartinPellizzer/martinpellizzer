Begonia zimmermannii

Begonia zimmermannii, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, silver-gray leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its delicate blooms.
