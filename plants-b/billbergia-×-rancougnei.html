Billbergia × rancougnei

Billbergia × rancougnei, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia nutans and Billbergia nutans var. rancougnensis.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences. The plant features striking blue-green leaves with white midribs and produces showy, colorful flowers that can range from red to orange.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
