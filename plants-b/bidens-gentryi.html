Bidens gentryi

Bidens gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow with white markings, and they bloom from late spring to early fall.

Bidens gentryi is typically found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
