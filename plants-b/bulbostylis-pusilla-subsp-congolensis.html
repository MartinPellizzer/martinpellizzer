Bulbostylis pusilla subsp. congolensis

Bulbostylis pusilla subsp.

congolensis, commonly known as the dwarf bulbostylis, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact stature and dense, tufted growth habit.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.
