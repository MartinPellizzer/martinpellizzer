Buchnera saigonensis

Buchnera saigonensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are strap-shaped and evergreen, while its flowers are usually pale pink or white.

Buchnera saigonensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
