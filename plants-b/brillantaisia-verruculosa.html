Brillantaisia verruculosa

Brillantaisia verruculosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 10 meters in height. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brillantaisia verruculosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
