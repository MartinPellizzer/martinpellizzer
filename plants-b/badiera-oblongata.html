Badiera oblongata

Badiera oblongata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Badiera oblongata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
