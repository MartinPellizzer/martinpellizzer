Bondtia glabra

Bondtia glabra, commonly known as the smooth bondtia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

Bondtia glabra prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
