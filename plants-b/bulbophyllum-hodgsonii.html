Bulbophyllum hodgsonii

Bulbophyllum hodgsonii, commonly known as the Hodges' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. Its roots form a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
