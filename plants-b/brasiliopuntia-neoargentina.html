Brasiliopuntia neoargentina

Brasiliopuntia neoargentina, commonly known as the Argentine nightshade cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features clusters of small, green, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. It produces yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
