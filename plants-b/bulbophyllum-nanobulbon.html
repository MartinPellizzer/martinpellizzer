Bulbophyllum nanobulbon

Bulbophyllum nanobulbon, commonly known as the miniature bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and unique, intricate floral structures. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
