Bulbophyllum notabilipetalum

Bulbophyllum notabilipetalum, commonly known as the showy lip orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers with intricate petal structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its humid, shaded environment.

The flowers often bloom in clusters, showcasing a wide range of hues that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
