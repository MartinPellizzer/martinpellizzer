Bulbophyllum moldenkeanum

Bulbophyllum moldenkeanum, commonly known as the Moldenke's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, fleshy rhizome and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. It blooms during the rainy season, typically from April to July.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
