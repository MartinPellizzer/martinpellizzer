Bowlesia palmata var. acutangula

Bowlesia palmata var. acutangula, commonly known as the acute-angled bowlesia, is a unique species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a palm-like plant characterized by its slender, unbranched stem and a rosette of elongated, acuminate leaves. The leaves are typically green and glossy, with a smooth texture and a pointed tip, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, moist forest understories.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
