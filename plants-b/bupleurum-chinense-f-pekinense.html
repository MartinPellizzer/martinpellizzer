Bupleurum chinense f. pekinense

Bupleurum chinense f. pekinense, commonly known as Peking angelica, is a perennial herb native to northern China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant features slender, branched stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins. Its flowers are small and white to pale yellow, arranged in clusters that attract pollinators.

The roots are often harvested for their medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
