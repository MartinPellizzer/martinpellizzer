Berchemia hirtella var. glabrescens

Berchemia hirtella var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth berchemia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in arid environments.
