Ballota Nigra Var. Porrigens

Ballota nigra var. porrigens is a lesser-known yet fascinating species of flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the legume family, known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities and ecological importance. The plant is characterized by its dark purple flowers and hairy stems, which help it thrive in rocky and arid environments. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in botanical discussions. Its unique features make it a valuable subject for both ecological study and horticultural interest.
