Berberis × kerriana

Berberis × kerriana, commonly known as the Kew berberis, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, glossy evergreen leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that are edible for birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Berberis × kerriana prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
