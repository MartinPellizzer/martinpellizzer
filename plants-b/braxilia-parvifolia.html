Braxilia parvifolia

It belongs to the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves that form a dense, compact rosette. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires high humidity to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a sought-after species among collectors and botanists.
