Beguea australis

Beguea australis, commonly known as the Australian begonia, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. Beguea australis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
