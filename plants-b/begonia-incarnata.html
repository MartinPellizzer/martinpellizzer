Begonia incarnata

Begonia incarnata, commonly known as the pink begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Begonia incarnata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add vibrant color to shady garden spaces.
