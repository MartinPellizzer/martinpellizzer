Beilschmiedia mannioides

Beilschmiedia mannioides, commonly known as the Manioides beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its smooth, dark-barked trunk and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland evergreen forests.
