Brochoneura freneei

Brochoneura freneei, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, velvety leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
