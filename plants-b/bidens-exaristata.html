Bidens exaristata

Bidens exaristata, commonly known as the roughleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

Its leaves are rough and hairy, giving it its common name, while its flowers are yellow with prominent white markings.

Bidens exaristata is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
