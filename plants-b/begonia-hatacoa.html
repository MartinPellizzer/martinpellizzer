Begonia hatacoa

Begonia hatacoa, commonly known as the Hatacoa begonia, is a tropical plant native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a deep, rich green color. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
