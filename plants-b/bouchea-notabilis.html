Bouchea notabilis

Bouchea notabilis, commonly known as the showy bouchea, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its vibrant yellow to orange flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Bouchea notabilis is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
