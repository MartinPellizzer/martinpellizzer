Bidens leyboldii

Bidens leyboldii, commonly known as the yellow beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

Bidens leyboldii is also known for its ability to spread through seeds that attach to animals or clothing, aiding in its dispersal.
