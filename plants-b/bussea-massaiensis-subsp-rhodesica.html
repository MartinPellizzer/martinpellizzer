Bussea massaiensis subsp. rhodesica

Bussea massaiensis subsp.

rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodesian bussea, is a shrub native to eastern and southern Africa. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.
