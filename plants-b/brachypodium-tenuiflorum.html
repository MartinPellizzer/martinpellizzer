Brachypodium tenuiflorum

Brachypodium tenuiflorum, commonly known as slender false brome, is a flowering plant native to Eurasia.

It is a grass species characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters at the ends of its stems. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas.

Brachypodium tenuiflorum is also valued for its use in ecological restoration and as a model organism in plant biology research.
