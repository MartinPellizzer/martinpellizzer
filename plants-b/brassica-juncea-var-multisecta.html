Brassica juncea var. multisecta

Brassica juncea var. multisecta, commonly known as Chinese kale or gai lan, is a leafy vegetable with a distinctive appearance.

It features dark green, thick, and crinkled leaves that are often curled and segmented, giving it a unique texture. The plant is an annual Brassicaceae species native to East Asia, particularly China. Its leaves are harvested before they become too tough, typically when they are young and tender.

The plant is valued for its nutritional content and is commonly used in Asian cuisines.
