Bolbitis felixii

Bolbitis felixii, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features fronds that are typically broad and leathery, with a distinctive golden hue on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its spores are released from sori located on the undersides of its fronds.

Bolbitis felixii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
