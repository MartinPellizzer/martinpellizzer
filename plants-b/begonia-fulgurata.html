Begonia fulgurata

Begonia fulgurata, commonly known as the lightning begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range from deep green to dark purple. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
