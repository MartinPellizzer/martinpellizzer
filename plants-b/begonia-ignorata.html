Begonia ignorata

Begonia ignorata, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green foliage that often features intricate patterns of darker green. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, Begonia ignorata is widely cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
