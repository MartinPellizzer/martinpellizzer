Belou

Belou, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in its natural habitat. Belou thrives in well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorching.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
