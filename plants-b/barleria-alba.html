Barleria alba

Barleria alba, commonly known as the white barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Barleria alba is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
