Bromus fibrosus var. contractus

Bromus fibrosus var. contractus, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides, meadows, and fields. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers.

This variety is often found in temperate climates and is adapted to a wide range of soil conditions.
