Bromus inermis var. laxus

Bromus inermis var. laxus, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
