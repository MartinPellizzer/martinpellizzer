Borbonia undulata var. multiflora

Borbonia undulata var. multiflora, commonly known as the waxy leaf borbonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and white. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
