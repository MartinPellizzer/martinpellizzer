Bonnaya micrantha

Bonnaya micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf poinsettia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that resemble petals. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant during the holiday season.
