Boerhavia chinensis subsp. natalensis

Boerhavia chinensis subsp.

natalensis, commonly known as Natal petty spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The species is characterized by its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
