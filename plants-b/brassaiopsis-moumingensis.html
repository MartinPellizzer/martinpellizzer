Brassaiopsis moumingensis

Brassaiopsis moumingensis, commonly known as the Momingensis cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk that is often covered in a fibrous, spongy layer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a deep green color and a waxy texture.

This species is notable for its slow growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
