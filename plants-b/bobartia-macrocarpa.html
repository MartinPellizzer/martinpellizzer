Bobartia macrocarpa

Bobartia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited bobartia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds, typical of the Polygonaceae family.
