Babiana ecklonii

Babiana ecklonii, commonly known as the rain lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred petals that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces its showy flowers in late summer or early autumn, often after rainfall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Babiana ecklonii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
