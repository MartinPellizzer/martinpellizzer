Breynia stipitata var. formosana

Breynia stipitata var. formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan breynia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, and often brightly colored leaves that can range from red to orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Breynia stipitata var. formosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
