Bouteloua rothrockii

Bouteloua rothrockii, commonly known as Rothrock's grama, is a native grass species found in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
