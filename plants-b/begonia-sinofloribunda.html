Begonia sinofloribunda

Begonia sinofloribunda, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
