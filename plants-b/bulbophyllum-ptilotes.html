Bulbophyllum ptilotes

Bulbophyllum ptilotes, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive feather-like appearance on its petals. It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
