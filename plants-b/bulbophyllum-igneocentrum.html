Bulbophyllum igneocentrum

Bulbophyllum igneocentrum, commonly known as the fire-centre orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its natural habitat with limited rainfall. Its flowers typically feature intricate patterns and are pollinated by specific insects.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its striking appearance.
