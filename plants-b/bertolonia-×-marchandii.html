Bertolonia × marchandii

Bertolonia × marchandii, commonly known as the hybrid bertolonia, is a cross between Bertolonia grandiflora and Bertolonia staminea.

It is a shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow blooms, which attract pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens and rockeries.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
