Berberis tianbaoshanensis

Berberis tianbaoshanensis, commonly known as the Tianbaoshan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Tianbaoshan region.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
