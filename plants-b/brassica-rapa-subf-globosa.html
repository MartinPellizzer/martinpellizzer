Brassica rapa subf. globosa

Brassica rapa subf.

globosa, commonly known as the curly cabbage or garden ornament cabbage, is a biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its dense, compact rosette of fleshy, lobed leaves that are typically green to purple in color.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. It is cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in some traditional cuisines.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for garden displays and container planting.
