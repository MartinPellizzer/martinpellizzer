Bulbophyllum sandersonii subsp. stenopetalum

Bulbophyllum sandersonii subsp.

stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Thailand and Malaysia.

The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with elongated, narrow petals. It typically grows as an epiphyte on trees, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique floral structure and coloration make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
