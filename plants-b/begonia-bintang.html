Begonia bintang

Begonia bintang, commonly known as the star begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid environments and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can last several months under proper care.
