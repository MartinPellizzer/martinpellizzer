Bignonia morongii

Bignonia morongii, commonly known as the Morong's crossvine, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family, which includes many ornamental and woody plants. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Bignonia morongii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
