Buddleja asiatica var. salicina

Buddleja asiatica var. salicina, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of white to pale purple, which attract butterflies and other pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is hardy in USDA zones 5 through 8.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
