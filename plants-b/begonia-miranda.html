Begonia miranda

Begonia miranda, commonly known as the Miranda begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Begonia miranda is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
