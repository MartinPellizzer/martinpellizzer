Bucco ventenatiana

Bucco ventenatiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Bucco ventenatiana is often found in wetland areas and can become invasive in certain regions.
