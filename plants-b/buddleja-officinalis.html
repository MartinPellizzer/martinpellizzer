Buddleja officinalis

Buddleja officinalis, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer, attracting a variety of pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Buddleja officinalis is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
