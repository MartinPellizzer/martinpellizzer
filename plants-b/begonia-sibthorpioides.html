Begonia sibthorpioides

Begonia sibthorpioides, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive central pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
