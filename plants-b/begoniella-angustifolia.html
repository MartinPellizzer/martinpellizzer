Begoniella angustifolia

Begoniella angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf begoniella, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves with intricate venation patterns that add to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening enthusiasts.
