Blitum virgatum var. capitatum

Blitum virgatum var. capitatum, commonly known as the clustered bittercress, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, upright stems and small, alternate leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in early spring. Its seeds are enclosed in siliques, and the plant is often found in moist, disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields.

It is characterized by its peppery taste and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
