Ballya zebrina

Ballya zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant, alternating bands of green and white along its leaves, which give it its distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays for its unique visual appeal.

Ballya zebrina is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and tropical plant collections.
