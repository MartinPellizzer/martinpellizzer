Bidens gracillima

Bidens gracillima, commonly known as the slender tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow with white markings, typically blooming from late spring to early fall.

Bidens gracillima is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and disturbed soils.
