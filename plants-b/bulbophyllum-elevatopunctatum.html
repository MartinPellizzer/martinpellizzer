Bulbophyllum elevatopunctatum

Bulbophyllum elevatopunctatum, commonly known as the elevated punctate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
