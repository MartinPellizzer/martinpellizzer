Bancalus obtusus

Bancalus obtusus, commonly known as the flat-backed stink bug, is a species of shield bug belonging to the family Pentatomidae.

It is characterized by its broad, flat body and a distinct, shield-shaped pronotum. The plant-associated aspects of this insect are primarily related to its role as a herbivore, feeding on various plant materials, including leaves and fruits. Its presence can impact agricultural crops, making it a pest in some regions.

The species is widely distributed across tropical and subtropical areas, where it thrives in diverse plant habitats.
