Bulbinella nutans

Bulbinella nutans, commonly known as the drooping bulbous orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, bulb-like tubers that store nutrients. The plant produces delicate, drooping inflorescences with fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
