Blephariglottis × enigma

Blephariglottis × enigma, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a rare and fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Blephariglottis orophila and Blephariglottis tricolor, resulting in unique floral characteristics. The plant typically features showy, spurred orchid flowers in shades of yellow and green, with intricate structures that aid in pollination. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike.
