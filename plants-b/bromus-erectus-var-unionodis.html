Bromus erectus var. unionodis

Bromus erectus var. unionodis, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, hairy leaves. The plant typically produces spikelets that are arranged in dense, compact clusters along the stem. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils.

Bromus erectus var. unionodis is often found in temperate climates and is adapted to both full sun and partial shade.
