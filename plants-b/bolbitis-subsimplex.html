Bolbitis subsimplex

Bolbitis subsimplex, commonly known as the simple shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its leathery, shield-shaped fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous structure, which allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Bolbitis subsimplex is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
