Byttneria siamensis

Byttneria siamensis, commonly known as the Siam wild silk tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves and showy, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
