Bulbophyllum uniflorum var. rubrum

Bulbophyllum uniflorum var. rubrum, commonly known as the Red-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces striking red or pink flowers. The plant features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to retain moisture. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the wet season.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
