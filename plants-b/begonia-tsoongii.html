Begonia tsoongii

Begonia tsoongii, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental foliage and flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Begonia tsoongii is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
