Byttneria genistella

Byttneria genistella, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, red pulp. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
