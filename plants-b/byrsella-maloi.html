Byrsella maloi

Byrsella maloi, commonly known as the Maloi orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, strap-like leaves and small, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.
