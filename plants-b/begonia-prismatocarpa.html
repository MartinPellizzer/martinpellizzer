Begonia prismatocarpa

Begonia prismatocarpa, commonly known as the prism-leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, prism-like leaf shapes that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
