Brachyglottis bellidioides var. angustata

Brachyglottis bellidioides var. angustata, commonly known as narrow-leaved brachyglottis, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
