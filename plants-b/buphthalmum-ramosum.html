Buphthalmum ramosum

Buphthalmum ramosum, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes.

Buphthalmum ramosum is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
