Bouea brandisiana

Bouea brandisiana, commonly known as the Malayan starfruit or golden starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces yellow, star-shaped fruits with a sweet and tangy flavor. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications and is valued for its unique taste and appearance.
