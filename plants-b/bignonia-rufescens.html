Bignonia rufescens

Bignonia rufescens, commonly known as the scarlet bignonia, is a deciduous vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or trellises.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees, adding both ornamental and ecological value to landscapes.
