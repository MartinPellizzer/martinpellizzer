Bonjeanea hirsuta var. prostrata

Bonjeanea hirsuta var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate hirsute bonjeanea, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features hairy, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
