Begonia macra

Begonia macra, commonly known as the large-leaved begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Begonia macra is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
