Borneocola argenteus

Borneocola argenteus, commonly known as the silverleaf borage, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
