Bupleurum nigrocarpum

Bupleurum nigrocarpum, commonly known as black-fruited bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas.

Bupleurum nigrocarpum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported tonic and anti-inflammatory properties.
