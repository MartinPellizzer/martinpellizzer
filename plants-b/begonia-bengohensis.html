Begonia bengohensis

Begonia bengohensis, commonly known as the Bengoh Begonia, is a flowering plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with elliptical leaves that are often variegated in color. Its flowers are usually red or pink, with five petals and a prominent, spurred calyx.

Begonia bengohensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal.
