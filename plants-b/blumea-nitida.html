Blumea nitida

Blumea nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in the summer months.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
