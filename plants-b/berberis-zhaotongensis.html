Berberis zhaotongensis

Berberis zhaotongensis, commonly known as the Zhaotong barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and seasonal interest.
