Breweria sericea

Breweria sericea, commonly known as silverleaf breweria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Breweria sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
