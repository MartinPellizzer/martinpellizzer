Brassica caulorapa

Brassica caulorapa, commonly known as Chinese kale, is a leafy green vegetable belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves that are often used in Asian cuisines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and prefers cool climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires regular watering for optimal growth.

Brassica caulorapa is rich in nutrients such as vitamins A, C, and K, making it a valuable addition to a healthy diet.
