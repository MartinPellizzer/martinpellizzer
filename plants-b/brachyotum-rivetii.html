Brachyotum rivetii

Brachyotum rivetii, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
