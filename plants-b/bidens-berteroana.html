Bidens berteroana

Bidens berteroana, commonly known as the yellow beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant features serrated leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields.
