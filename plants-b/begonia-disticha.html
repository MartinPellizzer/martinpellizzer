Begonia disticha

Begonia disticha, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia disticha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
