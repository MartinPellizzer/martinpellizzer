Begonia mcphersonii

Begonia mcphersonii, commonly known as the McPherson begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It features velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are a deep green with a subtle purplish tinge. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with distinctive red stamens, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
