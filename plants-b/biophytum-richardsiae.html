Biophytum richardsiae

Biophytum richardsiae, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a striking member of the Phytolaccae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange, pink, and purple, which give the plant its eye-catching appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique aesthetic makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
