Buddleja curviflora

Buddleja curviflora, commonly known as the zigzag buddleia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, zigzagging stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually gray-green in color. The plant produces panicles of fragrant, purple to lilac flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Buddleja curviflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
