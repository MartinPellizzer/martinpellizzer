Bidwillia glauca

Bidwillia glauca, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Bidwillia glauca is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
