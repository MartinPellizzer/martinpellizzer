Bertolonia aenea

Bertolonia aenea, commonly known as the silverleaf bertolonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It is noted for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
