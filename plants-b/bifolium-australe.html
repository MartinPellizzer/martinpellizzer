Bifolium australe

Bifolium australe, commonly known as the Southern Two-leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to southern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Bifolium australe thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
