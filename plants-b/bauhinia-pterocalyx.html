Bauhinia pterocalyx

Bauhinia pterocalyx, commonly known as the flame tree or pterocalyx, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pinnate leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia pterocalyx is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
