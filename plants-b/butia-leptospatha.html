Butia leptospatha

Butia leptospatha, commonly known as the slender-leafed palm, is a species native to Brazil, particularly in the state of Bahia.

It is a small, cold-hardy palm that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features a slender trunk and pinnate leaves with long, narrow segments. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters at the base of the leaves.

Butia leptospatha is well adapted to dry climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
