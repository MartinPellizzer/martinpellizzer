Besleria horizontalis

Besleria horizontalis, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and tropical gardens.
