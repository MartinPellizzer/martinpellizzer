Benstonea ornithocephala

Benstonea ornithocephala, commonly known as the bird-headed orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its unique, bird-like floral structure, which gives it its common name. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories.

Its flowers are usually a mix of white and green, with a distinctive, elongated petal that resembles a bird's head.
