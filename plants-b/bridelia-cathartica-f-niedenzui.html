Bridelia cathartica f. niedenzui

Bridelia cathartica f. niedenzui, commonly known as the Niedenzu's Bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.
