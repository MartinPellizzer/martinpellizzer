Begonia heterocantha

Begonia heterocantha, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and botanical study.
