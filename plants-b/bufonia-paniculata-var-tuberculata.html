Bufonia paniculata var. tuberculata

Bufonia paniculata var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate bufonia, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in paniculate inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark red or black color, containing several seeds.

This species is native to tropical regions and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
