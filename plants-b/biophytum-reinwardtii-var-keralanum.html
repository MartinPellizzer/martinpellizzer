Biophytum reinwardtii var. keralanum

Biophytum reinwardtii var. keralanum, commonly known as the Keralanum spider plant, is a succulent native to the tropical regions of India.

It features striking, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which become more pronounced in cooler temperatures. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular among collectors for its unique foliage and ease of care.
