Begonia microptera

Begonia microptera, commonly known as the miniature begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is typically velvety and has a distinctive, waxy texture.

Begonia microptera is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
