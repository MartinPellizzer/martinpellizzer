Bulbophyllum lepidum var. insigne

Bulbophyllum lepidum var. insigne, commonly known as the Splendid Bulbophyllum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that supports a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
