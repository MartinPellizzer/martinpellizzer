Brunfelsia uniflora f. intermedia

Brunfelsia uniflora f. intermedia, commonly known as the pink morning glory, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often opening in the early morning. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and has a climbing habit, making it suitable for trellises or support structures. Its flowers are typically short-lived, lasting only a day, which adds to their allure.

The botanical features of this plant make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
