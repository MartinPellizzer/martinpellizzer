Biscutella Sempervirens Subsp. Glacialis

Biscutella sempervirens subsp. glacialis is a rare and hardy plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in cold, rocky environments where few other species can survive. This subspecies is known for its delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it a symbol of resilience in mountain ecosystems. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and vulnerable to habitat loss.
