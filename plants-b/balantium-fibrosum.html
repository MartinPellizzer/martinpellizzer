Balantium fibrosum

Balantium fibrosum, commonly known as the fibrous balantium, is a unique plant species characterized by its fibrous, woody structure.

It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, fibrous coating. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Balantium fibrosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential in horticultural and ecological applications.
