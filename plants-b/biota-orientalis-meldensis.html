Biota orientalis meldensis

Biota orientalis meldensis, commonly known as the Meldensian flamebloom, is a striking plant native to the subtropical regions of Meldens.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. Its root system is fibrous and shallow, allowing it to absorb nutrients from the loose, sandy soil.

Biota orientalis meldensis is also noted for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
