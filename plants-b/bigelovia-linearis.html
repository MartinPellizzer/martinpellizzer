Bigelovia linearis

Bigelovia linearis, commonly known as the linear bigelovia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils. It is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
