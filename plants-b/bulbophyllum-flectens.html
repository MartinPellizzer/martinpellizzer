Bulbophyllum flectens

Bulbophyllum flectens, commonly known as the flexible orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its unique, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically long and narrow, adapted for efficient photosynthesis in low-light conditions. The flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink, making it an attractive ornamental plant.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts who appreciate its botanical and aesthetic value.
