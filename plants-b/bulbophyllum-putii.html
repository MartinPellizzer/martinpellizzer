Bulbophyllum putii

Bulbophyllum putii, commonly known as the Orchid of the Forest, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. This epiphytic plant grows on trees and relies on air, rain, and light for nutrients. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of white, yellow, or green.

The plant is noted for its resilience and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
