Bromelia plumieri

Bromelia plumieri, commonly known as the red-tipped bromeliad, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central tank to collect water. The plant produces vibrant red bracts that surround its flowering spikes, making it highly ornamental. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Bromelia plumieri is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
