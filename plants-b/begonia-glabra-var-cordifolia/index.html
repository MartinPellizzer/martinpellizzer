Begonia Glabra Var. Cordifolia

Begonia glabra var. cordifolia is a unique and elegant plant known for its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flowers. It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings. Its smooth, waxy leaves and clusters of small, colorful blossoms make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to light, water, and soil conditions to thrive.
