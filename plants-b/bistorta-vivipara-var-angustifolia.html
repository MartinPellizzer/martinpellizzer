Bistorta vivipara var. angustifolia

Bistorta vivipara var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf bistorta, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive arrow-shaped leaves.
