Baeckea crenulata

Baeckea crenulata, commonly known as the serrated baeckea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Baeckea crenulata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowering display.
