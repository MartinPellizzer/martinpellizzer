Brickellia oblongifolia var. linifolia

Brickellia oblongifolia var. linifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf brickellbush, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
