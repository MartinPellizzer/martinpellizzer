Berberis dictyoneura

Berberis dictyoneura, commonly known as the mountain barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often bitter. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
