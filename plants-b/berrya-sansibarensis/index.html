Berrya Sansibarensis

Berrya sansibarensis is a rare and unique plant native to the islands of Zanzibar. It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is known for its distinctive appearance and medicinal properties. The plant features small, colorful berries that are not only visually striking but also rich in nutrients. Its habitat is limited to specific regions, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to its scarcity, efforts are underway to study and protect this intriguing species.
