Billbergia × rhedonensis

Billbergia × rhedonensis, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia nutans and Billbergia cucullata.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, typically red or orange, which bloom from the center of the rosette. It is native to South America and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

This ornamental plant is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
