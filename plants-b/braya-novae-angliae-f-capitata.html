Braya novae-angliae f. capitata

Braya novae-angliae f. capitata, commonly known as the Cape Breton rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial with basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a compact cluster.
