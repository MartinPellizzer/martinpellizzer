Bifrenaria hadwenii

Bifrenaria hadwenii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer, often displaying shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant has a slender, erect stem and produces small, clustered flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid environment to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
