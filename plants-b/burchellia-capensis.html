Burchellia capensis

Burchellia capensis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the late summer and early autumn. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and along riverbanks.

Burchellia capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
