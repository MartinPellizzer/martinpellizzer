Bambusa taiwanensis

Bambusa taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan bamboo, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is a tall, clump-forming grass that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant has slender, hollow stems that are typically green or slightly purple in color. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a slightly waxy surface.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant.
