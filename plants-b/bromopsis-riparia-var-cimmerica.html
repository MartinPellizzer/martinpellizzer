Bromopsis riparia var. cimmerica

Bromopsis riparia var. cimmerica, commonly known as the Cimmerian rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. This variety is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils along stream banks and wetlands.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
