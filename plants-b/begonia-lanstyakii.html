Begonia lanstyakii

Begonia lanstyakii, commonly known as the Lanstyakii begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display vibrant coloration. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia lanstyakii is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
