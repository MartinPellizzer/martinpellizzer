Bonatea polypodantha

Bonatea polypodantha, commonly known as the slender fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically green and glossy. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered on the underside of the fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
