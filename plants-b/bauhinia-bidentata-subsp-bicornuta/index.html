Bauhinia Bidentata Subsp. Bicornuta

Bauhinia bidentata subsp. bicornuta is a striking leguminous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy pink or white flowers, it adds a touch of elegance to gardens and natural landscapes. This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a favorite among botanists and horticultural enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and partial shade to flourish optimally.
