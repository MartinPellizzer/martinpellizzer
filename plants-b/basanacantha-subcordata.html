Basanacantha subcordata

Basanacantha subcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dandelion, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of oblanceolate leaves that are typically hairy and have a subcordate base. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that develop into slender, hairless seed capsules. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Basanacantha subcordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
