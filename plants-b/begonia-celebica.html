Begonia celebica

Begonia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes begonia, is a tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically red or pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique foliage and flowers, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
