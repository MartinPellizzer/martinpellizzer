Bidens diversa

Bidens diversa, commonly known as the common beggarlice, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, yellow composite flowers and hairy, serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and fields. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, and the plant is often considered a weed.

Bidens diversa has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
