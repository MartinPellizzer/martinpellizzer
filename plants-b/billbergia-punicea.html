Billbergia punicea

Billbergia punicea, commonly known as the red-tipped pineapple plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The leaves are typically green with red or pink tips, giving it a vibrant, tropical appearance. It produces showy, pineapple-like flower clusters that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
