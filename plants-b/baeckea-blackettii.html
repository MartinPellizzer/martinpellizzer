Baeckea blackettii

Baeckea blackettii, commonly known as the Blackett's baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features small, fragrant white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often used in gardens for its hardiness and visual interest.
