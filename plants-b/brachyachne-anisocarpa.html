Brachyachne anisocarpa

Brachyachne anisocarpa, commonly known as the variable false brome, is a species of grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant typically grows in open woodlands, grasslands, and disturbed areas. Its flowers are small and clustered, with distinctively unequal fruiting bodies.

Brachyachne anisocarpa plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
