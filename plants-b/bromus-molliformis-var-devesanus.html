Bromus molliformis var. devesanus

Bromus molliformis var. devesanus, commonly known as the Devesana brome, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
