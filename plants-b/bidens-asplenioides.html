Bidens asplenioides

Bidens asplenioides, commonly known as the fernleaf tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features fern-like leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Bidens asplenioides is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and along stream banks.
