Bletia landsbergii

Bletia landsbergii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, upright stem and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Bletia landsbergii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
