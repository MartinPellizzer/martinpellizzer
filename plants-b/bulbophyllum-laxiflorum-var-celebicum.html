Bulbophyllum laxiflorum var. celebicum

Bulbophyllum laxiflorum var. celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant floral structures. The plant typically produces small, lance-shaped leaves and blooms with delicate, colorful flowers that vary in hue from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
