Burmannia brachyphylla

Burmannia brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed burmannia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burmanniaceae and is characterized by its small, inconspicuous flowers and succulent-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.
