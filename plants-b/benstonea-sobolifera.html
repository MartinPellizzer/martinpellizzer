Benstonea sobolifera

Benstonea sobolifera, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. Benstonea sobolifera is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.
