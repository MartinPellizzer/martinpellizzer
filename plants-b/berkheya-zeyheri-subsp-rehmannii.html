Berkheya zeyheri subsp. rehmannii

Berkheya zeyheri subsp.

rehmannii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a mat-forming habit with hairy stems and leaves. Its leaves are alternate, linear to lanceolate, and often covered in fine hairs.
