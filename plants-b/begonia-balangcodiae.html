Begonia balangcodiae

Begonia balangcodiae, commonly known as the Balangcoda begonia, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a deep green color and a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
