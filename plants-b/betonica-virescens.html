Betonica virescens

Betonica virescens, commonly known as spring betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct veining pattern. Its flowering stems bear clusters of blue or violet flowers, which bloom in early spring.

Betonica virescens is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat ailments such as wounds and respiratory issues.
