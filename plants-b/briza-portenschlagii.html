Briza portenschlagii

Briza portenschlagii, commonly known as the Alpine feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features fine, needle-like leaves that form dense, mat-like growths, often carpeting rocky or gravelly slopes. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes during the summer months, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add texture and color to dry, sunny landscapes.
