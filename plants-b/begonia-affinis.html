Begonia affinis

Begonia affinis, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, bright red or pink flowers. The plant thrives in rocky or well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Begonia affinis is a perennial species that is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
