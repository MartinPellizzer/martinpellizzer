Byrsonima intermedia

Byrsonima intermedia, commonly known as Brazilian cherry or *pata de vaca*, is a deciduous tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers.
