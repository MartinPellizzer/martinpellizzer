Brachiaria manzonzoensis

Brachiaria manzonzoensis, commonly known as the Manzonzo grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in grasslands and savanna environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.

Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny seeds.
