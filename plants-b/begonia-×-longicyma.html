Begonia × longicyma

Begonia × longicyma, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pendula.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia × longicyma is popular in hanging baskets and as a bedding plant due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
