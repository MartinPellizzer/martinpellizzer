Ballota fruticosa

Ballota fruticosa, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Ballota fruticosa is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
