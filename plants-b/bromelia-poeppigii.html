Bromelia poeppigii

Bromelia poeppigii, commonly known as the red bromeliad, is a striking plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, strap-like leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette, adding visual interest to its appearance. Bromelia poeppigii thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor collections.
