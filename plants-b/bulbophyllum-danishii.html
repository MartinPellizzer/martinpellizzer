Bulbophyllum danishii

Bulbophyllum danishii, commonly known as the Danish orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Bulbophyllum danishii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
