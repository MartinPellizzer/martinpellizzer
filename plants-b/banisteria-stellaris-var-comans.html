Banisteria stellaris var. comans

Banisteria stellaris var. comans, commonly known as the starry banisteria, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale yellow and bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a sweet, fragrant aroma. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
