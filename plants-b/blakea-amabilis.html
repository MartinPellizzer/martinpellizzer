Blakea amabilis

Blakea amabilis, commonly known as the pink hibiscus.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Blakea amabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.
