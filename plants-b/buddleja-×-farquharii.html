Buddleja × farquharii

Buddleja × farquharii, commonly known as the hybrid butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is prized for its showy, fragrant flower clusters that attract butterflies and other pollinators. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet, with gray-green foliage and a bushy, compact form. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for garden use.
