Bidens schaffneri var. wrightii

Bidens schaffneri var. wrightii, commonly known as Wright's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture. This species is often found in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and contributes to the biodiversity of its native environment.
