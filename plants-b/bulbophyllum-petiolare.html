Bulbophyllum petiolare

Bulbophyllum petiolare, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of multiple spikes bearing numerous white or pale pink blooms.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
