Barkhausia floribunda

Barkhausia floribunda, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and has a compact, evergreen shrub form. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Barkhausia floribunda blooms in spring and summer, producing clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers.
