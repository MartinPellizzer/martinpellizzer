Banisteria cinerascens var. platyphylla

Banisteria cinerascens var. platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf banisteria, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically broad and divided into several lobes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
