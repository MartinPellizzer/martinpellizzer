Beaucarnea stricta

Beaucarnea stricta, commonly known as the ponytail palm, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a swollen, tree-like stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant has a rosette of long, narrow leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. Its flowering occurs in late spring or early summer, producing small, white, fragrant flowers.

The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and unique aesthetic.
