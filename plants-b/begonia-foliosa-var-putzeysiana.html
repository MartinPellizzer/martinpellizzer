Begonia foliosa var. putzeysiana

Begonia foliosa var. putzeysiana, commonly known as the Putzeysiana begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often deeply lobed and have a distinctive greenish-blue hue. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
