Bulbophyllum stenopetalum

Bulbophyllum stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers typically feature a combination of white, yellow, and green hues, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
