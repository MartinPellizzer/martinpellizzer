Bupleurum apiculatum var. albicans

Bupleurum apiculatum var. albicans, commonly known as the white-flowered bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. This variant is distinguished by its pale floral coloration, setting it apart from other varieties within the Bupleurum genus.
