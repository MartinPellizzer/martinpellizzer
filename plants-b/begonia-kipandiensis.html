Begonia kipandiensis

Begonia kipandiensis, commonly known as the Kipandi begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
