Brachystelma burchellii var. grandiflorum

Brachystelma burchellii var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant starling cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its branching stems covered in dense, woolly hair, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
