Brunfelsia longituba

Brunfelsia longituba, commonly known as the long-tube Brunfelsia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces long, tubular corollas that are adapted for pollination by hummingbirds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Brunfelsia longituba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
