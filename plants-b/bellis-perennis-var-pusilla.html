Bellis perennis var. pusilla

Bellis perennis var. pusilla, commonly known as the miniature daisy or dwarf English daisy, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe.

It features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are spoon-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and charming appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
