Bulbophyllum variabile var. rubrum

Bulbophyllum variabile var. rubrum, commonly known as the Red-variable orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged along a slender stem.

The leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.
