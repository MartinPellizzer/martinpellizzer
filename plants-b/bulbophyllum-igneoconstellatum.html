Bulbophyllum igneoconstellatum

Bulbophyllum igneoconstellatum, commonly known as the fiery star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-like flowers that display a vibrant mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its tropical habitat. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

This species thrives in shaded, moist conditions and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of tropical forests.
