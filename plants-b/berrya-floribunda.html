Berrya floribunda

Berrya floribunda, commonly known as the flowering berry or sweetberry plant, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color when ripe. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. It blooms with clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in the spring.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
