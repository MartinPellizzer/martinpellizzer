Bersama kandtii

Bersama kandtii, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark brown wood with a rich, resinous scent. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed.

Due to its unique fragrance and limited natural range, it is considered a valuable and rare species in botanical collections.
