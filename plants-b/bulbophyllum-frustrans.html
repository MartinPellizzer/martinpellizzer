Bulbophyllum frustrans

Bulbophyllum frustrans, commonly known as the broken-flower orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
