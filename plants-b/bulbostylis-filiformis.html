Bulbostylis filiformis

Bulbostylis filiformis, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
