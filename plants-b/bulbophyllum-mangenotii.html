Bulbophyllum mangenotii

Bulbophyllum mangenotii, commonly known as the Manganot's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a complex, intricate structure. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and possesses a pseudobulb, which is a modified stem that stores water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum mangenotii is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and unique floral display.
