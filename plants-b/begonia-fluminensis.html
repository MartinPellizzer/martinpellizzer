Begonia fluminensis

Begonia fluminensis, commonly known as the Rio de Janeiro begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia fluminensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
