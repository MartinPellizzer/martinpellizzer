Barbarea arisanensis

Barbarea arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan rockcress, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
