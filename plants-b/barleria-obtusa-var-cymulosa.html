Barleria obtusa var. cymulosa

Barleria obtusa var. cymulosa, commonly known as the clustered barleria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming throughout the year. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
