Bupariti

Bupariti, commonly known as the Bupariti tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically harvested when they turn yellow. Bupariti is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
