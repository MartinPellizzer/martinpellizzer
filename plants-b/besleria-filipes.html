Besleria filipes

Besleria filipes, commonly known as the featherleaf gesneria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
