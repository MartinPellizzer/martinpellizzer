Begonia peekelii

Begonia peekelii, commonly known as the Peckel's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia peekelii is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
