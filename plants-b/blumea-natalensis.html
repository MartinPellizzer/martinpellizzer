Blumea natalensis

Blumea natalensis, commonly known as Natal bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often found in forest understories and along stream banks.

Blumea natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
