Brachyscome iberidifolia var. glandulifera

Brachyscome iberidifolia var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular bristle bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, woolly leaves covered in glandular hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and sandy soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
