Begonia pingbienensis var. angustior

Begonia pingbienensis var. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved begonia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive tubular shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
