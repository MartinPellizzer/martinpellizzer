Bulbophyllum eutoreton

Bulbophyllum eutoreton, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that resemble flames, making it a visually appealing plant. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of forests.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
