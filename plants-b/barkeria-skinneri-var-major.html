Barkeria skinneri var. major

Barkeria skinneri var. major, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
