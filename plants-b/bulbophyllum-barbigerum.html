Bulbophyllum barbigerum

Bulbophyllum barbigerum, commonly known as the beard-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum barbigerum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
