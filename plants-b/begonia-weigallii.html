Begonia weigallii

Begonia weigallii, commonly known as the Weigall begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.

Due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements, it is popular among plant enthusiasts.
