Baphia longepetiolata

Baphia longepetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled baphia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
