Begonia denticulata

Begonia denticulata, commonly known as the serrated begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with finely serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia denticulata is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
