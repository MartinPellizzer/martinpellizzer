Bunium minutifolium

Bunium minutifolium, commonly known as dwarf fernweed, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as carrots and parsley. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its small, white flowers are arranged in umbels, typical of the family.
