Brassia arachnoidea

Brassia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a unique, elongated spur. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a strong, sweet scent that attracts pollinators.

Brassia arachnoidea is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
