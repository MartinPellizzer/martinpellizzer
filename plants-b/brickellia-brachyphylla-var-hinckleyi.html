Brickellia brachyphylla var. hinckleyi

Brickellia brachyphylla var. hinckleyi, commonly known as Hinckley's brickellia, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
