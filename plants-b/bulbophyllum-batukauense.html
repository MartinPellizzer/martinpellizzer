Bulbophyllum batukauense

Bulbophyllum batukauense, commonly known as the Batukauense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its bulbous structures serve as storage organs for nutrients and water.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
