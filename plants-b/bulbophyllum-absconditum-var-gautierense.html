Bulbophyllum absconditum var. gautierense

Bulbophyllum absconditum var. gautierense, commonly known as the Gautier's hidden orchid, is a rare and elusive orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its specialized symbiotic relationship with specific mycorrhizal fungi. Its bulbs are a key feature, enabling it to survive in nutrient-poor environments.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
