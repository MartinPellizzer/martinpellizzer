Begonia poggei

Begonia poggei, commonly known as the Sierra Leone begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of West Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green and white, which adds to its ornamental value.

Begonia poggei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
