Bolbitis × kanarensis

Bolbitis × kanarensis, commonly known as the Kanarensis moonlight fern, is a hybrid fern species resulting from the cross between Bolbitis heudelotii and Bolbitis capensis.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are often covered in a fine, silvery coating, giving it a distinctive moonlit appearance. The plant thrives in shady, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent in the dark, a feature that enhances its visual impact.

Bolbitis × kanarensis is native to parts of Africa and is valued for its unique appearance and ease of care in indoor settings.
