Begonia × argenteoguttata

Begonia × argenteoguttata, commonly known as the silver-spotted begonia, is a hybrid plant prized for its ornamental value.

It features striking foliage with silver and green mottling, which adds visual interest to gardens or indoor spaces. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for containers, hanging baskets, or as a border plant.
