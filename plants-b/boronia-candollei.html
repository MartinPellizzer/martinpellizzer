Boronia candollei

Boronia candollei, commonly known as the pink boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, releasing a fragrant scent when crushed.

Boronia candollei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
