Bulbophyllum mildbraedii

Bulbophyllum mildbraedii, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically small and borne in inflorescences, often displaying a range of colors from white to yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
