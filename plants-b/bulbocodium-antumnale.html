Bulbocodium antumnale

Bulbocodium antumnale, commonly known as autumnal bulbocodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the fall, adding vibrant color to the landscape during late autumn. Its bulbs are an important part of its life cycle, storing nutrients for the next growing season.
