Barlia Longibracteata Var. Sicula

Sicilian barlia, scientifically known as Barlia longibracteata var. sicula, is a rare and distinctive plant native to the island of Sicily. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers and unique foliage. This variant of Barlia longibracteata is found primarily in specific microhabitats, such as limestone soils and rocky slopes. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this botanical gem from further decline.
