Brandesia pubiflora

Brandesia pubiflora, commonly known as the hairy-flowered brandesia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Brandesia pubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
