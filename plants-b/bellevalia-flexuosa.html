Bellevalia flexuosa

Bellevalia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features slender, zigzagging stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Bellevalia flexuosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
