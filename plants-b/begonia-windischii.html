Begonia windischii

Begonia windischii, commonly known as the Windisch begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to bronze hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a distinctive red calyx. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant and in botanical gardens.
