Brunonia sericea

Brunonia sericea, commonly known as the silky brunonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal regions and sandy soils. The plant produces striking clusters of yellow-orange flowers that appear in spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Brunonia sericea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
