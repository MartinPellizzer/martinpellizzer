Banksia wonganensis

Banksia wonganensis, commonly known as the Wongan banksia, is an evergreen shrub or small tree native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical flower spikes, which are typically yellow to orange in color. The plant produces woody cones that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by animals and wind. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in heathland and woodland habitats.

Banksia wonganensis is well adapted to the dry, Mediterranean climate of its native region.
