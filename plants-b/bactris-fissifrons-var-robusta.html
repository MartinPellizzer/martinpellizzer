Bactris fissifrons var. robusta

Bactris fissifrons var. robusta, commonly known as the giant palm or robusta palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. This plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and robust, trunk-like structure. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional practices.
