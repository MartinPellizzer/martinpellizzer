Bromheadia devogelii

Bromheadia devogelii, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, often clustered in loose inflorescences. Bromheadia devogelii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
