Begonia antaisaka

Begonia antaisaka, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
