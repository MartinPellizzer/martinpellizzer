Brunsvigia rubricaulis

Brunsvigia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed gladiolus, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that are reddish in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Its bulbs are adapted to survive dry periods, making it drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
