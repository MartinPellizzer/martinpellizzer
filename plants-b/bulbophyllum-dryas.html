Bulbophyllum dryas

Bulbophyllum dryas, commonly known as the Dryas Pterostigma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its native habitat. Its leaves are typically fleshy and thick, adapted to conserve moisture.

The orchid blooms during the dry season, showcasing its resilience to arid conditions.
