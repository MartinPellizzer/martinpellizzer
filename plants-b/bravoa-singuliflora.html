Bravoa singuliflora

Bravoa singuliflora, commonly known as the single-flower grass.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single, showy flower per stem. It blooms in late spring to early summer with bright blue or purple petals.

Bravoa singuliflora is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, sunny conditions.
