Barkhausia numidica

Barkhausia numidica, commonly known as the Numidian broom, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in racemes, and it produces legume pods that contain seeds.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
