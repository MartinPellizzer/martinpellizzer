Blepharoglossum microblepharon

Blepharoglossum microblepharon, commonly known as the small-eyed moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form and small, densely packed leaves. Its reproductive structures, known as capsules, are borne on short, erect stalks and release spores when mature.
