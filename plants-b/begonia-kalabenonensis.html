Begonia kalabenonensis

Begonia kalabenonensis, commonly known as the Kalabenon begonia, is a species native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a trailing growth habit. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
