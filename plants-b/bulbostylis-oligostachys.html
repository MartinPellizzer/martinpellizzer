Bulbostylis oligostachys

Bulbostylis oligostachys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. Its flowering period usually occurs during the rainy season, and it produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Bulbostylis oligostachys is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in environments with high humidity and moderate temperatures.
