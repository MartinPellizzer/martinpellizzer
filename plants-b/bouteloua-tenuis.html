Bouteloua tenuis

Bouteloua tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, blue-green leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and plays a role in stabilizing soil in grassland ecosystems.

Its seeds are a food source for various birds and small mammals.
