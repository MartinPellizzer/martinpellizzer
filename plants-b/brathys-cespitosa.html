Brathys cespitosa

Brathys cespitosa, commonly known as the tufted brathys, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant produces small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
