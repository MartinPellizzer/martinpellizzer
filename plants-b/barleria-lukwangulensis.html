Barleria lukwangulensis

Barleria lukwangulensis, commonly known as the Natal barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
