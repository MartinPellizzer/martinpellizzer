Bidens distans

Bidens distans, commonly known as the common beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with five petals and a distinctive bristled seed capsule. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, fields, and along roadsides. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture, and it produces small, sticky seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Bidens distans is an annual herb that plays a role in ecological systems by providing food for various insects and birds.
