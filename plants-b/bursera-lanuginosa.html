Bursera lanuginosa

Bursera lanuginosa, commonly known as the wooly bursera, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its bark is aromatic and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential healing properties.

The species is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
