Barthea barthei var. valdealata

Barthea barthei var. valdealata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive white and green vertical stripes, giving it a zebra-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
