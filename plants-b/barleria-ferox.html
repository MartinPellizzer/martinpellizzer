Barleria ferox

Barleria ferox, commonly known as the red devil bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and arid regions.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
