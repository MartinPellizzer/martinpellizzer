Begonia nephrophylla

Begonia nephrophylla, commonly known as the kidney-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
