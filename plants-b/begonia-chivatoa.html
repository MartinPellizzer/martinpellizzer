Begonia chivatoa

Begonia chivatoa, commonly known as the Chivatoa begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, requiring consistent moisture during the growing season.

Begonia chivatoa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
