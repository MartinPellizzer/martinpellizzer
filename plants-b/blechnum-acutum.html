Blechnum acutum

Blechnum acutum, commonly known as the sharp-sheath holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its fronds are divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments with a distinctive sharp, serrated edge.

Blechnum acutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
