Bulbine erectipilosa

Bulbine erectipilosa, commonly known as the upright hairbulb, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, which help reduce water loss. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its robust nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
