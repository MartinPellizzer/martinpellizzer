Bulbostylis hispidula subsp. brachyphylla

Bulbostylis hispidula subsp.

brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed bulbostylis, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets, which are a common feature in the Poaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.
