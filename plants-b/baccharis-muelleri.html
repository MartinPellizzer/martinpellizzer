Baccharis muelleri

Baccharis muelleri, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Baccharis muelleri is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
