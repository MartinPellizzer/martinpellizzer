Begonia Convolvulacea

Red begonia, scientifically known as Begonia convolvulacea, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. Its vibrant red blooms and trailing stems make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and garden displays. This species is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions. With its delicate foliage and long-lasting flowers, it adds a touch of elegance to any indoor or outdoor space. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to light and moisture to flourish fully.
