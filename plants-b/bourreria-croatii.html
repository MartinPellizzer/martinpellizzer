Bourreria croatii

Bourreria croatii, commonly known as the Croatian burr, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that are adapted for seed dispersal by animals. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Bourreria croatii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
