Bauhinia gnomon

Bauhinia gnomon, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like clusters of leaves. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small, feather-like segments.

Bauhinia gnomon is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
