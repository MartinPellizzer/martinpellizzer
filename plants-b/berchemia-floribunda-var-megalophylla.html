Berchemia floribunda var. megalophylla

Berchemia floribunda var. megalophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf berchemia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in arid and semi-arid regions.
