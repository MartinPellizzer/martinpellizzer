Bashania faberi

Bashania faberi, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Bashania faberi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
