Batis aurantiaca

Batis aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange batis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Batis aurantiaca is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
