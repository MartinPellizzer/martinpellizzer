Bolbitis inconstans

Bolbitis inconstans, commonly known as the wandering fern, is a unique member of the fern family with distinctive botanical features.

It is a small, delicate fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate regions. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces upright, lance-shaped fronds with a feathery appearance. Its leaves are usually green, sometimes with a bluish tint, and they can vary in size depending on environmental conditions.

Bolbitis inconstans is known for its ability to spread and colonize new areas, making it both a resilient and sometimes invasive species.
