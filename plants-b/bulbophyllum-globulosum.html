Bulbophyllum globulosum

Bulbophyllum globulosum, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, rounded pseudobulbs and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they bloom during the rainy season.

Bulbophyllum globulosum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
