Begonia mearnsii

Begonia mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' begonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
