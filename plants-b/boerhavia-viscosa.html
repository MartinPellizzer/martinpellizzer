Boerhavia viscosa

Boerhavia viscosa, commonly known as sandmat or redroot, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

The plant is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with thick leaves that store water and a shallow root system for quick absorption of moisture.
