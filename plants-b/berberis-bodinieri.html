Berberis bodinieri

Berberis bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its hardy nature and vibrant autumn foliage contribute to its horticultural appeal.
