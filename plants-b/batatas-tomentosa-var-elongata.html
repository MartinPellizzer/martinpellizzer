Batatas tomentosa var. elongata

Batatas tomentosa var. elongata, commonly known as the woolly sweet potato.

It is a variant of the sweet potato with a distinctive hairy, velvety texture on its skin and a pale yellow to white flesh. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

The tuberous roots are typically elongated and have a slightly sweet, nutty flavor.
