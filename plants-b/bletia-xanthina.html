Bletia xanthina

Bletia xanthina, commonly known as the yellow bletia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and understoreys. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bletia xanthina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
