Brachyloma ventricosum

Brachyloma ventricosum, commonly known as the globular wattle, is a flowering plant native to south-eastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, rounded form. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathland environments.

Brachyloma ventricosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
