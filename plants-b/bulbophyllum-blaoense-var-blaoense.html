Bulbophyllum blaoense var. blaoense

Bulbophyllum blaoense var. blaoense, commonly known as the Blao Enclave Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, colorful blooms in shades of yellow and orange, which appear during the wet season. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its native habitat.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in montane forests.
