Bowlesia tenella

Bowlesia tenella, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Bowlesia tenella is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
