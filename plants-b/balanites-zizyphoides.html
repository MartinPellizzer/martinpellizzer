Balanites zizyphoides

Balanites zizyphoides, commonly known as the Desert Date, is a flowering plant native to Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a deciduous tree that produces large, thick-skinned fruits with a sweet, fleshy pulp. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is often used for food, while the seeds and bark have traditional medicinal uses.
