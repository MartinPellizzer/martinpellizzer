Buchnerodendron speciosum

Buchnerodendron speciosum, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland tropical forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its decorative appeal in gardens and landscapes.
