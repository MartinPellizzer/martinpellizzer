Botryanthus gussonei

Botryanthus gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's starburst cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. Its leaves are modified into spines, providing protection from herbivores and reducing water loss.

Botryanthus gussonei is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
