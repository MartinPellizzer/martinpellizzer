Buchnera buchneroides

Buchnera buchneroides, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features a short rhizome with a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a subtle fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
