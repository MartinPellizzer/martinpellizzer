Bactris megistocarpa

Bactris megistocarpa, commonly known as the giant palm or the yellow palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela. Its fruit is a large, yellow, spiky structure that contains edible seeds.
