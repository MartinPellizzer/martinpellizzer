Betonica hirsuta

Betonica hirsuta, commonly known as hairy betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Betonica hirsuta is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky soils.

It is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and healing properties.
