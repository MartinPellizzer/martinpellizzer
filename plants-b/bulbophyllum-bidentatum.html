Bulbophyllum bidentatum

Bulbophyllum bidentatum, commonly known as the two-toothed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are usually ovate and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Bulbophyllum bidentatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
