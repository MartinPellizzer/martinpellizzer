Balantium copelandii

Balantium copelandii, commonly known as the silky fan-leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are soft and covered in a fine, silky texture. It produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the late afternoon.

Balantium copelandii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
