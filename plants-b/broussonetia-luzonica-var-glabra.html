Broussonetia luzonica var. glabra

Broussonetia luzonica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Luzon broussonetia, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
