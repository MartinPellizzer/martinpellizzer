Bulbophyllum sasakii

Bulbophyllum sasakii, commonly known as the Sasakii orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and intricate flower structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum sasakii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its delicate beauty.
