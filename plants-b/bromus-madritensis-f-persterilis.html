Bromus madritensis f. persterilis

Bromus madritensis f. persterilis, commonly known as the sterile form of the Madritense brome, is a botanical variant characterized by its reduced fertility.

This subspecies is typically found in specific ecological niches, often in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It exhibits morphological differences from the typical form, including smaller inflorescences and reduced seed production.
