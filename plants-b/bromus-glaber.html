Bromus glaber

Bromus glaber, commonly known as smooth brome, is a perennial grass native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces spikelets that are often arranged in dense clusters along the stem. Smooth brome is known for its adaptability to various soil types and is commonly used in forage and erosion control.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed areas.
