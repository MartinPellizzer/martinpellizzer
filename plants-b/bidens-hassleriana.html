Bidens hassleriana

Bidens hassleriana, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs that give it a distinctive silvery appearance.

Bidens hassleriana is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.
