Bromus gracilis var. micromollis

Bromus gracilis var. micromollis, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, hairless blades and slender, often reddish stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as roadsides, meadows, and sandy soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers.

Bromus gracilis var. micromollis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
