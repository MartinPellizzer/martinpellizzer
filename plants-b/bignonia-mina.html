Bignonia mina

Bignonia mina, commonly known as the yellow trumpetbush, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet. The plant produces showy, orange-yellow tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with serrated edges.

Bignonia mina thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
