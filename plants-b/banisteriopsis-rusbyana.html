Banisteriopsis rusbyana

Banisteriopsis rusbyana, commonly known as the red balsa tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The tree produces woody fruits that contain small, round seeds. Additionally, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
