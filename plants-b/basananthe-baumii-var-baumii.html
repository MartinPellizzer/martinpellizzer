Basananthe baumii var. baumii

Basananthe baumii var. baumii, commonly known as the dwarf banana plant, is a compact, ornamental herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features broad, waxy, banana-like leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly curled edge. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by edible, banana-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
