Bulbophyllum bontocense

Bulbophyllum bontocense, commonly known as the Bontoc orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually fleshy and adapted to store water, while its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the air.

The flowers of Bulbophyllum bontocense are often brightly colored, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.
