Brassica revelieri

Brassica revelieri, commonly known as the Chinese kale or Chinese broccoli, is a leafy green vegetable native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables like cabbage and cauliflower. The plant features thick, dark green stems and broad, tender leaves that are harvested for consumption. It is valued for its nutritional content, including vitamins A, C, and K, as well as its mild, slightly peppery flavor.

Brassica revelieri is typically grown in cool seasons and thrives in well-drained soil with adequate sunlight.
