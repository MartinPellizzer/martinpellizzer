Baccharoides adoensis

Baccharoides adoensis, commonly known as the ADO endemic bush, is a shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas within humid forests. Its botanical features include a woody stem, aromatic foliage, and small, capsule-like fruits.
