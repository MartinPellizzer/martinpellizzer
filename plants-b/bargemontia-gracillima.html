Bargemontia gracillima

Bargemontia gracillima, commonly known as the slender marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Bargemontia gracillima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
