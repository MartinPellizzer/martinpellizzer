Biophytum hildebrandtii

Biophytum hildebrandtii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red velvetplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple bracts that enclose its small, yellow flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Biophytum hildebrandtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
