Begonia gemmipara

Begonia gemmipara, commonly known as the gemmipara begonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant produces aerial tubers, which are used for propagation and contribute to its unique botanical features. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a smooth texture and a glossy surface.

Begonia gemmipara thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
