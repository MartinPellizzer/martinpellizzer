Brachycentrum

Brachycentrum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plants typically have narrow, grass-like leaves and produce bright yellow or orange blooms. They are often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Brachycentrum species are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
