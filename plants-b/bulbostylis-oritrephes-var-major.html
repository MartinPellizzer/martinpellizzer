Bulbostylis oritrephes var. major

Bulbostylis oritrephes var. major, commonly known as the giant bulbostylis, is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. Its bulbs, which are underground storage organs, play a crucial role in its survival and propagation.

This variant is often found in wetland or marshy habitats where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
