Bulbophyllum pseudohydra

Bulbophyllum pseudohydra, commonly known as the Dragon's Head orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often on tree trunks or rocks in the undergrowth. Its flowers are usually fragrant and can bloom multiple times a year under optimal conditions.

The species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
