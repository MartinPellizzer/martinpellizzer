Brachionidium filamentosum

Brachionidium filamentosum, commonly known as the filamentous brachionid, is a species of algae belonging to the order Brachionidales.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like morphology, with long, branching filaments that give it a distinctive appearance. This organism is typically found in freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it thrives in nutrient-rich waters. Brachionidium filamentosum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by serving as a food source for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.

Its presence can indicate the health and stability of the surrounding aquatic habitat.
