Bupleurum stellatum

Bupleurum stellatum, commonly known as star anise bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, branched stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous tiny, aromatic seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Bupleurum stellatum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
