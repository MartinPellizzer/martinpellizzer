Bulbophyllum limbatum

Bulbophyllum limbatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and linear, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a distinctive green lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum limbatum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
