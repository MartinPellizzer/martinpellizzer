Bulbophyllum elegantulum

Bulbophyllum elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, colorful blooms that can range from white to pink or purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
