Begonia xishuangbannaensis

Begonia xishuangbannaensis, commonly known as the Xishuangbanna begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped or ovate, with a waxy texture, and it produces small, tubular flowers in clusters.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid conditions.
