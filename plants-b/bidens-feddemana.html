Bidens feddemana

Bidens feddemana, commonly known as the yellow beggar's ticks, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, hairy leaves and produces small yellow flowers with ray florets. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in sunny environments.

Its seeds are dispersed by the attachment of silky hairs to animals or clothing.
