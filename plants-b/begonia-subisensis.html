Begonia subisensis

Begonia subisensis, commonly known as the subisensis begonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring intricate patterns of green, white, and sometimes yellow.

Begonia subisensis is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
