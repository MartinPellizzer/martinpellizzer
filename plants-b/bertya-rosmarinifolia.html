Bertya rosmarinifolia

Bertya rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf bertya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Bertya rosmarinifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
