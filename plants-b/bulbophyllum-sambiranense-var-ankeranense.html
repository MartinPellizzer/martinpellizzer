Bulbophyllum sambiranense var. ankeranense

Bulbophyllum sambiranense var. ankeranense, commonly known as the Ankeranense Bulbophyllum, is a rare and striking orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in the arid and seasonal environments of its habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and display a complex arrangement of petals and sepals, making it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
