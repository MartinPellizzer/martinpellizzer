Begonia suaviola

Begonia suaviola, commonly known as the sweet-scented begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and pleasant aroma.

Begonia suaviola is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
