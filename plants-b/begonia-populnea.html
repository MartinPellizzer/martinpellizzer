Begonia populnea

Begonia populnea, commonly known as the silverleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often have a silvery or white underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia populnea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
