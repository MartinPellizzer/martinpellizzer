Brickellia amplexicaulis

Brickellia amplexicaulis, commonly known as the broadleaf brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a perennial herb with a woody base and stems that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, with broad, ovate shapes and a distinctive clasping base.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies.
