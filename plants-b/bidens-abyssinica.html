Bidens abyssinica

Bidens abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Bidens abyssinica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
