Berberis qianensis

Berberis qianensis, commonly known as the Qianshan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

The species is valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate urban environments.
