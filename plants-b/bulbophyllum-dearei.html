Bulbophyllum dearei

Bulbophyllum dearei, commonly known as the Deare's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.

Bulbophyllum dearei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
