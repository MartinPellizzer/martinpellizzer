Boehmeria lindeniana

Boehmeria lindeniana, commonly known as the Indian nettle or Chinese nettle, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It has been used traditionally in some regions for its medicinal properties.
