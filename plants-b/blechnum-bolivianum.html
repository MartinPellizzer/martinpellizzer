Blechnum bolivianum

Blechnum bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian hartsia, is a species of fern native to South America.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its fronds are usually bipinnate, with a distinctive, narrow shape.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
