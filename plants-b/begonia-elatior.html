Begonia elatior

Begonia elatior, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting veins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia elatior is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental leaves and vibrant blooms.
