Bravocactus

Bravocactus, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and a dense covering of golden-yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual element. It is a slow-growing succulent that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
