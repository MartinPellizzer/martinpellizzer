Bletilla striata f. gebina

Bletilla striata f. gebina, commonly known as the gebina orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with rhizomatous stems and lanceolate leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The f. gebina form is noted for its unique floral structure and is favored in horticultural collections.
