Berberis trichiata

Berberis trichiata, commonly known as the thorny barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often bitter. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and use in traditional medicine.

Berberis trichiata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.
