Bunias tcheliensis

Bunias tcheliensis, commonly known as the Siberian mustard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard species. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, pinnately divided leaves. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in racemes, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is known for its potential medicinal properties.
