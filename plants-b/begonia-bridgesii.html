Begonia bridgesii

Begonia bridgesii, commonly known as the Bridgestone begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that exhibit a rich, deep green color with prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia bridgesii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
