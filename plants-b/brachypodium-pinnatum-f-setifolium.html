Brachypodium pinnatum f. setifolium

Brachypodium pinnatum f. setifolium, commonly known as the silky false brome, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
