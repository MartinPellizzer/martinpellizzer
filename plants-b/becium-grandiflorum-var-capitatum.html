Becium grandiflorum var. capitatum

Becium grandiflorum var. capitatum, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and evergreen foliage. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often used in ornamental gardening.

The species is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
