Bulbostylis rehmannii

Bulbostylis rehmannii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, spike-like flowers. It typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and is often found in association with water.
