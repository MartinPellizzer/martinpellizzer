Bipinnula philipporum

Bipinnula philipporum, commonly known as the Philippine fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into two levels of smaller leaflets, giving it a lacy appearance.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
