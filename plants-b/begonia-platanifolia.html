Begonia platanifolia

Begonia platanifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and texture.
