Bunium pachypodum

Bunium pachypodum, commonly known as the desert sandthistle, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, erect stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a deep taproot system, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are usually covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

Bunium pachypodum is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
