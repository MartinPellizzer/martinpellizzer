Blepharodon nitidum

Blepharodon nitidum, commonly known as the white-veined fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, light green fronds with prominent white veins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous clustered fronds.

The botanical features of Blepharodon nitidum make it an attractive and relatively easy-to-care-for houseplant.
