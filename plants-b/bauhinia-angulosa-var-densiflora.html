Bauhinia angulosa var. densiflora

Bauhinia angulosa var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, angular leaflets and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its attractive foliage and floral display.
