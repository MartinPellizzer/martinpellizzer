Babiana stricta var. erectifolia

Babiana stricta var. erectifolia, commonly known as the upright babiana, is a perennial herb native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It features erect, strap-like leaves and produces striking, showy inflorescences with pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is part of the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important plants.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
