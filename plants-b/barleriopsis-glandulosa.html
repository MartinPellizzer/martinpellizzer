Barleriopsis glandulosa

Barleriopsis glandulosa, commonly known as the sticky leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant has glandular hairs on its leaves, which give it a sticky texture and help in water retention. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
