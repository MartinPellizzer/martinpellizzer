Bulbophyllum pantlingii

Bulbophyllum pantlingii, commonly known as the Pantling's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
