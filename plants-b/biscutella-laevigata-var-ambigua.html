Biscutella laevigata var. ambigua

Biscutella laevigata var. ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous biscuitroot, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits.
