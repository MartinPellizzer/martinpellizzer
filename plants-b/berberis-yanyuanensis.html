Berberis yanyuanensis

Berberis yanyuanensis, commonly known as the Yanyuan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible but often have a sour taste. It thrives in temperate climates and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
