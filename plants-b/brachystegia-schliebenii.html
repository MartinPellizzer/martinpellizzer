Brachystegia schliebenii

Brachystegia schliebenii, commonly known as the African brachystegia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae and is characterized by its thick, flaky bark and small, oval leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in miombo woodlands and plays a role in soil stabilization and habitat provision.
