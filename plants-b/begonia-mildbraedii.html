Begonia mildbraedii

Begonia mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that typically range from pink to white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It prefers warm, humid environments and well-draining soil, making it suitable for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
