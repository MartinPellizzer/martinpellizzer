Begonia ficicola

Begonia ficicola, commonly known as the hanging begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing stems that often hang from supports, making it popular as a hanging plant. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, with a vibrant green color. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom in clusters.

This plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.
