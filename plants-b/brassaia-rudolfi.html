Brassaia rudolfi

Brassaia rudolfi, commonly known as the Rudolf's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Brassaia rudolfi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
