Bauhinia cheilantha

Bauhinia cheilantha, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often divided into three to five lobes. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. Its fruit is a flat, pod-like structure that splits open to release seeds.

Bauhinia cheilantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
