Besleria lehmannii

Besleria lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's flame vine, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and can grow as a vine or shrub, depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
