Boriskellera

Boriskellera, commonly known as the common boriskellera, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in umbels, typical of its family. Boriskellera is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
