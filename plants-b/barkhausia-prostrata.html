Barkhausia prostrata

Barkhausia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate barkhausia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale purple, arranged in clusters. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall herbaceous appearance.
