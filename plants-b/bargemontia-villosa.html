Bargemontia villosa

Bargemontia villosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in spring.

Bargemontia villosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
