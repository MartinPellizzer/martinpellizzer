Bidens subalternans

Bidens subalternans, commonly known as the lesser beggar's ticks, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features small, yellow flowers with ray florets and a distinctive, hairy stem. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, often with toothed margins, and it thrives in moist, disturbed soils.

Bidens subalternans is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in open woodlands.
