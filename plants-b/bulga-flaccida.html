Bulga flaccida

Bulga flaccida, commonly known as the slender bulga, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a cluster.

Bulga flaccida is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
