Bulliarda filiformis

Bulliarda filiformis, commonly known as the slender bulliarda, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thin, thread-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to withstand dry conditions.

Bulliarda filiformis is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
