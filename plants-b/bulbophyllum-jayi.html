Bulbophyllum jayi

Bulbophyllum jayi, commonly known as the Jay's orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or green with contrasting patterns. Its rhizome system allows it to store nutrients and water, aiding in its survival in less fertile soils.

Bulbophyllum jayi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
