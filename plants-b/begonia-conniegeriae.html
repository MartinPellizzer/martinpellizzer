Begonia conniegeriae

Begonia conniegeriae, commonly known as the Connieger's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct reddish tint. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia conniegeriae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
