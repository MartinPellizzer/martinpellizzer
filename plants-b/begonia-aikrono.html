Begonia aikrono

Begonia aikrono, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, deep green foliage and clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a soft, textured surface that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.
