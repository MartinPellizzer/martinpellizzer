Bulbophyllum reflexiflorum subsp. pogonochilum

Bulbophyllum reflexiflorum subsp.

pogonochilum, commonly known as the hooked-flower orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a small epiphytic orchid with pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant has fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and good ventilation.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
