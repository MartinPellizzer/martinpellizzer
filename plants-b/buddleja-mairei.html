Buddleja mairei

Buddleja mairei, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a slight pubescence. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Buddleja mairei is also noted for its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
