Barleria Gracilispina

Barleria gracilispina is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in clusters along its stems. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a favorite among gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively uncommon plant in cultivation outside its natural range.
