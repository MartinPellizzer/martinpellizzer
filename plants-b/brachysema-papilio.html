Brachysema papilio

Brachysema papilio, commonly known as the butterfly orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of butterflies.

Brachysema papilio is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
