Begonia torresii

Begonia torresii, commonly known as the Torres begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Begonia torresii is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
