Besleria selloana

Besleria selloana, commonly known as the Sello's flame flower, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
