Begonia pelargoniiflora

Begonia pelargoniiflora, commonly known as the showy begonia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting central spot. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia pelargoniiflora is popular among gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
