Brassaia singaporensis

Brassaia singaporensis, commonly known as the Singapore bracken or Singapore fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green and finely divided. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Brassaia singaporensis has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important component of its native habitat.
