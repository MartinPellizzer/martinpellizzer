Begonia sootepensis var. thorelii

Begonia sootepensis var. thorelii, commonly known as the Thorelii begonia, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The foliage is often covered in a fine, sooty coating, giving it its species name. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for both its foliage and floral displays.
