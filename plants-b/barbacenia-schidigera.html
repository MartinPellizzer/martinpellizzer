Barbacenia schidigera

Barbacenia schidigera, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the moist forest floor.

Barbacenia schidigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
