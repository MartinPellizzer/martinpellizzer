Berberis Veitchiorum Var. Kingdon-Wardiana

Berberis veitchiorum var. kingdon-wardiana is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of Southeast Asia. Known for its vivid yellow flowers and spiny foliage, it is a member of the Berberidaceae family. This variant is particularly prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
