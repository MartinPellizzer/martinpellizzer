Bacopa lecomtei

It is native to the wetlands and marshy areas of southern Mexico, particularly in the states of Chiapas and Oaxaca. Bacopa lecomtei thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in shallow water or along the edges of streams and ponds.
