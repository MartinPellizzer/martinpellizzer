Bridelia exaltata

Bridelia exaltata, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish bark and small, opposite leaves. However, due to overharvesting, it is now considered a threatened species in many parts of its range.
