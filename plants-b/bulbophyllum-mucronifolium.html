Bulbophyllum mucronifolium

Bulbophyllum mucronifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and pointed, giving it its common name.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
