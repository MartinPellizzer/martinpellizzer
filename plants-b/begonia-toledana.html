Begonia toledana

Begonia toledana, commonly known as the Tolede begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Begonia toledana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
