Burmannia aptera

Burmannia aptera, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, lowland areas. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are pollinated by insects.
