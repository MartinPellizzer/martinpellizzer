Burmeistera lignescens

Burmeistera lignescens, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive grayish-green hue.

Burmeistera lignescens is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and relatively low maintenance requirements.
