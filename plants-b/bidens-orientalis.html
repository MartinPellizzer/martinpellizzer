Bidens orientalis

Bidens orientalis, commonly known as the oriental tickseed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow with dark centers, and they bloom from summer to early autumn.

Bidens orientalis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
