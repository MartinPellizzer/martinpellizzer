Boehmeria virgata var. tomentosa

Boehmeria virgata var. tomentosa, commonly known as silky nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes other members like stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. Its stems are typically slender and creeping, often forming dense mats.
