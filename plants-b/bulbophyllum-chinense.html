Bulbophyllum chinense

Bulbophyllum chinense, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum chinense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
