Barbacenia arachnoidea

Barbacenia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence, which gives it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Barbacenia arachnoidea is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
