Blechnum attenuatum var. giganteum

Blechnum attenuatum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate fronds that can reach lengths of up to two meters. The plant thrives in wet, shaded environments, often found in swampy areas or along riverbanks. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, wavy margin.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
