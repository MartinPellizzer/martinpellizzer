Bulbophyllum mooreanum

Bulbophyllum mooreanum, commonly known as the Moorean orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or greenish in color, with a distinctive lip structure. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Bulbophyllum mooreanum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
