Brittonrosea phyllacantha

Brittonrosea phyllacantha, commonly known as the brittlestem rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States, characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, adapting to arid environments.
