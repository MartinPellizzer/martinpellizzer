Briquetia inermis

Briquetia inermis, commonly known as the smooth briquetia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
