Bombax marginatum var. cuspidatum

Bombax marginatum var. cuspidatum, commonly known as the Crimson Cotton Tree, is a variant of the native Australian cotton tree.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant splash of color to the landscape. The leaves are typically large, broad, and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop. This plant is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture and landscaping.
