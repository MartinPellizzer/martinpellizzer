Bigelovia roxburghiana

Bigelovia roxburghiana, commonly known as the Indian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, with a smooth texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
