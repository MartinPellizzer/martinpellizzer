Babiana sambucina var. longibracteata

Babiana sambucina var. longibracteata, commonly known as the Long-bract Babiana, is a striking bulbous perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces distinctive, elongated bracts that enclose the flower buds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique foliage structure.
