Begonia pernambucensis

Begonia pernambucensis, commonly known as the Pernambuco begonia, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia pernambucensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
