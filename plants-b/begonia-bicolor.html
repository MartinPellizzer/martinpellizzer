Begonia bicolor

Begonia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor leaves, which typically feature a green upper surface and a red or purple underside. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun depending on the climate.

Begonia bicolor is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
