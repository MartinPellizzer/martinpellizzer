Bondtia pantonii

Bondtia pantonii, commonly known as the Panton's bondtia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a striking addition to any garden setting.
