Bejaria congesta

Bejaria congesta, commonly known as the clustered bejaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth surface and a pointed tip.
