Barleria laceratiflora

Barleria laceratiflora, commonly known as the ragged leaf barleria, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate, lace-like leaves and vibrant floral display.
