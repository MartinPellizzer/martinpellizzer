Boehmeria virgata var. molliuscula

Boehmeria virgata var. molliuscula, commonly known as silky boehmeria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nettle family, Urticaceae, and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a silken texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
