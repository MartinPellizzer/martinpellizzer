Brodiaea Lactea

Brodiaea lactea is a striking member of the amaryllis family known for its unique white petals and vibrant yellow centers. Native to the western United States, this plant thrives in rocky soils and sunny environments. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its resilient nature, making it a favorite among gardeners. The plant blooms in late spring, adding a touch of elegance to any landscape. With proper care, Brodiaea lactea can flourish and provide beauty for years.
