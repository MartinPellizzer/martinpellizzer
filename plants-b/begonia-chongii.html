Begonia chongii

Begonia chongii, commonly known as the Chong begonia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can vary in color from deep green to reddish tones. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
