Billbergia forgetiana

Billbergia forgetiana, commonly known as the silver billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence that bears vibrant red flowers, which bloom for several weeks. The plant features strap-like, blue-green leaves that form a rosette, creating a striking visual contrast. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia forgetiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
