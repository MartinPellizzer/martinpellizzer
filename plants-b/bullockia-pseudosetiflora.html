Bullockia pseudosetiflora

Bullockia pseudosetiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered bullockia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with elongated petals and a distinctive labellum. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the substrate.

Bullockia pseudosetiflora is often found in tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.
