Baccharis dracunculifolia var. integerrima

Baccharis dracunculifolia var. integerrima, commonly known as Brazilian baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture and small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. It has a woody stem and can grow up to several meters in height.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
