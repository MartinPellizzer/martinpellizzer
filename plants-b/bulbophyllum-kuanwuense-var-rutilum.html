Bulbophyllum kuanwuense var. rutilum

Bulbophyllum kuanwuense var. rutilum, commonly known as the Golden Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, a common adaptation in epiphytic orchids. Its leaves are usually green and strap-like, often with a waxy coating.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
