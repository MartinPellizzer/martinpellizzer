Brandisia laetevirens

Brandisia laetevirens, commonly known as the bright green brandisia, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that displays a vibrant green hue, which is particularly striking in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Brandisia laetevirens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, tropical conditions.
