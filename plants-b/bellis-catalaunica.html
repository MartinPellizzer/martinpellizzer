Bellis catalaunica

Bellis catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features showy white or pale yellow flowers with dark centers, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically spoon-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bellis catalaunica is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
