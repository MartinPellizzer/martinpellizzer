Bulbophyllum kachinense

Bulbophyllum kachinense, commonly known as the Kachin orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in less favorable conditions.

Bulbophyllum kachinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts.
