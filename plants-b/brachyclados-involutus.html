Brachyclados involutus

Brachyclados involutus, commonly known as the curled brachyclados, is a unique species of flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth form and small, clustered leaves that often curl inward, giving it its scientific name.
