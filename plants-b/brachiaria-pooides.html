Brachiaria pooides

Brachiaria pooides, commonly known as slender grass or Brazilian grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It is highly adaptable and thrives in a variety of soil types and climatic conditions.

Brachiaria pooides is often used in forage production and soil conservation due to its hardiness and rapid growth.
