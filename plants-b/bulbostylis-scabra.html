Bulbostylis scabra

Bulbostylis scabra, commonly known as the rough-stemmed bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, white or pale green flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
