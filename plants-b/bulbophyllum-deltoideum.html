Bulbophyllum deltoideum

Bulbophyllum deltoideum, commonly known as the Delta Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive delta-shaped lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in fluctuating environmental conditions.

Bulbophyllum deltoideum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
