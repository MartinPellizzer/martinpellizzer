Brassia filiana

Brassia filiana, commonly known as the Filian's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, showy blooms. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Brassia filiana is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
