Bulbophyllum luanii

Bulbophyllum luanii, commonly known as the Luanii orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in humid, low-light conditions.

Bulbophyllum luanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
