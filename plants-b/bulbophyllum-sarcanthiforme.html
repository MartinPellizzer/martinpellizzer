Bulbophyllum sarcanthiforme

Bulbophyllum sarcanthiforme, commonly known as the Orchid of the Moon, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, pseudobulb with a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant blooms during the rainy season, typically from April to July, and its inflorescence is often adorned with a distinctive, hood-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
