Bulbophyllum cyanotriche

Bulbophyllum cyanotriche, commonly known as the blue-hair orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique blue-violet bracts that resemble hair, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
