Begonia acutiloba

Begonia acutiloba, commonly known as the sharp-lobed begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped or ovate leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia acutiloba is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
