Byttneria ramosissima

Byttneria ramosissima, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling branches and large, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The tree produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and adaptability contribute to its ecological and horticultural significance.
