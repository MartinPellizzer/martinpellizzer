Blossfeldia liliputana var. campaniflora

Blossfeldia liliputana var. campaniflora, commonly known as the miniature pincushion cactus, is a unique member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of southern Africa, particularly Namibia and South Africa. This plant is characterized by its small, globular stem and clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
