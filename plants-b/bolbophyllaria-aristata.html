Bolbophyllaria aristata

Bolbophyllaria aristata, commonly known as the spiked stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
