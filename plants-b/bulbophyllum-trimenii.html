Bulbophyllum trimenii

Bulbophyllum trimenii, commonly known as the Trimani orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white to yellowish flowers that are clustered in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum trimenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
