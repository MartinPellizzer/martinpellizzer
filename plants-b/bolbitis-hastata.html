Bolbitis hastata

Bolbitis hastata, commonly known as the giant horsetail, is a fern-like plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Equisetaceae family and is characterized by its tall, segmented stems and whorled leaves. The plant has a unique ability to photosynthesize through its leaves while also absorbing water and nutrients through its rhizomes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
