Berberis buxifolia var. spinosissima

Berberis buxifolia var. spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny boxberry, is a variant of the boxberry plant native to China.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that is dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, which are followed by red berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically rounded and have a leathery texture, while its stems are covered in sharp spines.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in woodland gardens.
