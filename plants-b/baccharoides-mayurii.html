Baccharoides mayurii

Baccharoides mayurii, commonly known as the Indian sandbar tree, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

The tree is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
