Billya

Billya, commonly known as the Christmas bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a compact, rounded shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late winter, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is well-suited for container gardening and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for winter interest in gardens.
