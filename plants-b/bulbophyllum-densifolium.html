Bulbophyllum densifolium

Bulbophyllum densifolium, commonly known as the dense-leaved orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive pattern of markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
