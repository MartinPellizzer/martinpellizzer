Bocconia frutescens var. normalis

Bocconia frutescens var. normalis, commonly known as the red bocconia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and its striking red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features elliptical leaves with a waxy texture and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation projects.
