Begonia dolabrifera

Begonia dolabrifera, commonly known as the broad-bladed begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces showy, fuchsia or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
