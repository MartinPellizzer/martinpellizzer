Beloperone magniflora

Beloperone magniflora, commonly known as the showy beloperone, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and colorful flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Beloperone magniflora is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
