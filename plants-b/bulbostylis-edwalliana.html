Bulbostylis edwalliana

Bulbostylis edwalliana, commonly known as the Edwall's bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its compact stature and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowering spikes are usually short and bear tiny, inconspicuous flowers.
