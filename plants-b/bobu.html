Bobu

Bobu, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often cultivated for its ornamental value. Bobu prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its compact growth habit makes it a favorite in indoor gardening.
