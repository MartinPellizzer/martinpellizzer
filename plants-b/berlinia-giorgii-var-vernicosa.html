Berlinia giorgii var. vernicosa

Berlinia giorgii var. vernicosa, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine, and the tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
