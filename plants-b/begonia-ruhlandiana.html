Begonia ruhlandiana

Begonia ruhlandiana, commonly known as the Ruhland's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia ruhlandiana is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings due to its preference for indirect light.
