Barnadesia ciliata

Barnadesia ciliata, commonly known as the silky leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a unique, intricate structure.

Barnadesia ciliata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
