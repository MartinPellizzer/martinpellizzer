Begonia difformis

Begonia difformis, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, undulating leaf shapes that give it its common name. The leaves are typically green with a velvety texture and often display variegation. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom in clusters.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value.
