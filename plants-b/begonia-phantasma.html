Begonia phantasma

Begonia phantasma, commonly known as the phantom begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and color variations. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare plants.
