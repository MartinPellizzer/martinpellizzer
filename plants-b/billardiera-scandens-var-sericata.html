Billardiera scandens var. sericata

Billardiera scandens var. sericata, commonly known as silky billardiera, is a climbing shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, twining stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. Small, fragrant flowers are borne in clusters and are typically white or pale yellow.

This variety is often found in open forests and heathlands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
