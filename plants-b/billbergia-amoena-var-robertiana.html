Billbergia amoena var. robertiana

Billbergia amoena var. robertiana, commonly known as the Robertiana Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange, often resembling flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in ornamental horticulture for its eye-catching appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
