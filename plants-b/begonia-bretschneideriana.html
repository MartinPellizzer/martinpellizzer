Begonia bretschneideriana

Begonia bretschneideriana, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with reddish veins. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or shaded rockeries.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
