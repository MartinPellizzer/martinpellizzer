Bolbostemma biglandulosum var. biglandulosum

Bolbostemma biglandulosum var. biglandulosum, commonly known as the biglandular balloon vine, is a climbing perennial plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and its distinctive, inflated, balloon-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
