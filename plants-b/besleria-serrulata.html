Besleria serrulata

Besleria serrulata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, sword-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Besleria serrulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
