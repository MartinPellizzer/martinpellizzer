Blechnum palmiforme

Blechnum palmiforme, commonly known as the fan-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Blechnum palmiforme is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
