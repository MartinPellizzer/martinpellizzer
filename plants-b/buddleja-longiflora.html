Buddleja longiflora

Buddleja longiflora, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Buddleja longiflora is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms and easy maintenance.
