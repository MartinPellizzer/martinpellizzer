Besleria robusta

Besleria robusta, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with prominent purple or red markings. The plant features long, slender stems and broad, strap-shaped leaves that help it thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the rich, organic soil of its habitat.

Besleria robusta is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
