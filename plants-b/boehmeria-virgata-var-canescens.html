Boehmeria virgata var. canescens

Boehmeria virgata var. canescens, commonly known as silky fiber plant, is a variant of the Boehmeria virgata species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, silky fibers that are produced in the stems and leaves, making it valuable for textile and industrial applications. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to several feet. Its leaves are often large, heart-shaped, and covered with fine, silky hairs.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
