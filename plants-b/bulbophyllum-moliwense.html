Bulbophyllum moliwense

Bulbophyllum moliwense, commonly known as the Moliwene's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that typically bloom during the dry season. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
