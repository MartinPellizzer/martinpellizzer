Bupleurum ajanense

Bupleurum ajanense, commonly known as Ajanense goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia, including China and India.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes plants like carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage.
