Bromus mollis f. leptostachys

Bromus mollis f. leptostachys, commonly known as the slender spike grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, narrow leaves. The inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spike with small, spikelet-like flowers. This subspecies is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of ecological studies in arid and semi-arid regions.
