Berkheya schinzii

Berkheya schinzii, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and a central disk of smaller florets. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Berkheya schinzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
