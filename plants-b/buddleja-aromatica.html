Buddleja aromatica

Buddleja aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces elongated, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Buddleja aromatica is relatively hardy and adaptable to various soil types.
