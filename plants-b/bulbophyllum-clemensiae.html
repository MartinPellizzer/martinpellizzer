Bulbophyllum clemensiae

Bulbophyllum clemensiae, commonly known as the Clemen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters and are often fragrant, attracting various pollinators.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
