Billbergia alfonsi-joannis

Billbergia alfonsi-joannis, commonly known as the royal billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, often colorful inflorescences that emerge from a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia alfonsi-joannis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
