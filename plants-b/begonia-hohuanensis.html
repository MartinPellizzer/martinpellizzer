Begonia hohuanensis

Begonia hohuanensis, commonly known as the Hohuan begonia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
