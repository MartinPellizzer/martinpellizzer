Brassaiopsis polyacantha

Brassaiopsis polyacantha, commonly known as the clustered bristlebush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Brassaiopsis polyacantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
