Begonia stenophylla

Begonia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

Begonia stenophylla is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
