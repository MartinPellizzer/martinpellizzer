Berkheya excelsa

Berkheya excelsa, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking South African plant characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and tall, branching structure.

The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it its distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers in the summer months, which are clustered in a rounded inflorescence.

Berkheya excelsa is well adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.
