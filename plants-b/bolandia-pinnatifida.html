Bolandia pinnatifida

Bolandia pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bolandia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, pinnately divided leaves that give it its scientific name. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

Bolandia pinnatifida is often found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.
