Begonia spruceana

Begonia spruceana, commonly known as the Spruce begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia spruceana is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
