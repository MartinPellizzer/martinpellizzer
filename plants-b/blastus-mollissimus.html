Blastus mollissimus

Blastus mollissimus, commonly known as the silky blastus, is a flowering plant native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
