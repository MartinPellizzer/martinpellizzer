Bulbophyllum stella

Bulbophyllum stella, commonly known as the star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces small, pale green to white flowers that are clustered in a dense inflorescence. Bulbophyllum stella thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
