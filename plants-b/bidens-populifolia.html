Bidens populifolia

Bidens populifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf beggarweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is typically found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats. It has a sprawling growth habit and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
