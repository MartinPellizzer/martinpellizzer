Byrsonima intermedia f. angustifolia

Byrsonima intermedia f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf soursop, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth upper surface and hairy underside. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
