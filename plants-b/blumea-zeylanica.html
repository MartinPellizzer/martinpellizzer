Blumea zeylanica

Blumea zeylanica, commonly known as Indian blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, giving it a misty appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
