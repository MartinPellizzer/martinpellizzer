Blechnum occidentale var. pectinatum

Blechnum occidentale var. pectinatum, commonly known as the feather fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bipinnate, giving it a lacy appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

The fronds are usually light green and may have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
