Blephariglottis × lueri

Blephariglottis × lueri, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Blephariglottis gaudichaudi and Blephariglottis multiflora, resulting in a unique combination of traits. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
