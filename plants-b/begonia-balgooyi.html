Begonia balgooyi

Begonia balgooyi, commonly known as the Balgooyi begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant prefers shady, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Begonia balgooyi is also valued for its ability to adapt to various growing conditions, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
