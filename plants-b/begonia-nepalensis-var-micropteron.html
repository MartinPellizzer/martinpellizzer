Begonia nepalensis var. micropteron

Begonia nepalensis var. micropteron, commonly known as the small-winged begonia, is a species native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and India.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This variety is notable for its compact size and ornamental value, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
