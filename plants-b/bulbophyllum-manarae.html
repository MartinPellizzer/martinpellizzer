Bulbophyllum manarae

Bulbophyllum manarae, commonly known as the Manarae orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate and often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a complex arrangement of petals and sepals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
