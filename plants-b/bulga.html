Bulga

Bulga, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a tropical deciduous tree native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces long, flat pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and inland regions.

The Bulga is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in landscaping.
