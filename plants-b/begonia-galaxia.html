Begonia galaxia

Begonia galaxia, commonly known as the silver begonia, is a striking plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features velvety, dark green leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in shaded outdoor settings.
