Bulbophyllum bequaertii var. brachyanthum

Bulbophyllum bequaertii var. brachyanthum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant features elongated, linear leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
