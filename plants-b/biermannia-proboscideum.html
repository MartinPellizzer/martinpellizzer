Biermannia proboscideum

Biermannia proboscideum, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears several blooms, each with intricate, colorful patterns. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a unique structure that aids in pollination by mimicking the appearance of a female wasp.

Biermannia proboscideum is a rare and protected species, often found in shaded, humid environments.
