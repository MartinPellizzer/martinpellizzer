Barkhausia taraxacifolia subsp. heterocarpa

Barkhausia taraxacifolia subsp.

heterocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barkhausia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered flower heads.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and resilience.
