Brickellia brandegeei

Brickellia brandegeei, commonly known as the desert brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as deserts and rocky soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a basal rosette of foliage and a flowering stem that rises from the center.

Brickellia brandegeei is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh desert conditions.
