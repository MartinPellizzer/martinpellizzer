Bulbophyllum flavidum var. elongatum

Bulbophyllum flavidum var. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated yellow torch orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences bearing yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive torch-like shape. Its bulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water and nutrients.

It is often cultivated for its striking appearance and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
