Brunfelsia maliformis

Brunfelsia maliformis, commonly known as the Malifoma or Maliformal, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant produces clusters of tubular, often violet or white, flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are typically ovate and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Brunfelsia maliformis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
