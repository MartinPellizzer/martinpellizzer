Bunias tricornis

Bunias tricornis, commonly known as the three-cornered bunia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. Its leaves are lobed and can be either basal or stem-derived, with a slightly hairy texture.

Bunias tricornis is often found in dry meadows and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
