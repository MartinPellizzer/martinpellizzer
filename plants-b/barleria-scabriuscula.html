Barleria scabriuscula

Barleria scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems covered in rough, hairy textures. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Barleria scabriuscula is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
