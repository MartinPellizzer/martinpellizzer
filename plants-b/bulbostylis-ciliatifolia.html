Bulbostylis ciliatifolia

Bulbostylis ciliatifolia, commonly known as the hairy-fringed bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
