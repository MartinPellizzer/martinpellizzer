Barleria cinerea

Barleria cinerea, commonly known as silver barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Barleria cinerea is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
