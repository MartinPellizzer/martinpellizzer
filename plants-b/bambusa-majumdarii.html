Bambusa majumdarii

Bambusa majumdarii, commonly known as the Majumdar bamboo, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a tall, slender grass-like plant that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant has hollow, segmented stems with a smooth, greenish-brown exterior. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a slight waxy coating.

This bamboo is valued for its strength and is used in construction and handicrafts in its native region.
