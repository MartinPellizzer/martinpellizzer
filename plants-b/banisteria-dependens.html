Banisteria dependens

Banisteria dependens, commonly known as the hanging banisteria, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
