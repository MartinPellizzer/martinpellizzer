Blumea hieraciifolia var. holosericea

Blumea hieraciifolia var. holosericea, commonly known as the silverleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely pubescent, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warm seasons. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
