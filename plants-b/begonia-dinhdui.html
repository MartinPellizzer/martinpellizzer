Begonia dinhdui

Begonia dinhdui, commonly known as the Dinh Duong begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, intricate foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and clusters of tubular, brightly colored blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is a sought-after species among botanical enthusiasts.
