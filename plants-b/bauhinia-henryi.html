Bauhinia henryi

Bauhinia henryi, commonly known as Henry's bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Bauhinia henryi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
