Boea mollis

Boea mollis, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a trailing evergreen perennial that features small, oval-shaped leaves with a silvery-gray underside. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
