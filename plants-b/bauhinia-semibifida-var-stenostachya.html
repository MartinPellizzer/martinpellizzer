Bauhinia semibifida var. stenostachya

Bauhinia semibifida var. stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed bauhinia, is a deciduous legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
