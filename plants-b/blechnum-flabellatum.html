Blechnum flabellatum

Blechnum flabellatum, commonly known as the fan-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Blechnum flabellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
