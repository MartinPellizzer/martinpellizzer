Bargemontia

Bargemontia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks emerging from the water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, providing habitat for various aquatic life. Its root system helps stabilize the soil and prevent erosion in wetland environments.

Bargemontia is also valued for its cultural significance in many Asian traditions.
