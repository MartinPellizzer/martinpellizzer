Brunia dregeana

Brunia dregeana, commonly known as the silver bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Brunia dregeana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
