Bulbophyllum ramulicola

Bulbophyllum ramulicola, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and relies on mycorrhizal associations for nutrient uptake. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

Bulbophyllum ramulicola is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
