Bulbostylis polytricha

Bulbostylis polytricha, commonly known as the hairy bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and compact, grass-like growth habit. The plant produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of diverse ecosystems in its native habitats.
