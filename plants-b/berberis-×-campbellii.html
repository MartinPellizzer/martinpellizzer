Berberis × campbellii

Berberis × campbellii, commonly known as the Campbell's barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It features thorny stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and showy floral display.
