Begonia scheidweileri

Begonia scheidweileri, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.
