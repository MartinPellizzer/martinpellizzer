Bulbostylis sphaerocarpa

Bulbostylis sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, commonly found in wetland and marshy environments.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, three-angled spikelets that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense clusters in suitable habitats.

Bulbostylis sphaerocarpa is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in wetland areas.
