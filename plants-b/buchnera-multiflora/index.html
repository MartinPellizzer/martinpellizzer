Buchnera Multiflora

Buchnera multiflora is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its striking white and pink blooms that appear in late spring. This species is closely related to the genus Buchnera, which includes several other herbaceous plants. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Buchnera multiflora is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique species.
