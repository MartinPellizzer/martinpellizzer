Berghausia scoparia

Berghausia scoparia, commonly known as the mountain thyme.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features small, aromatic leaves and produces tiny white or pinkish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Berghausia scoparia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
