Banisteria stellaris var. pubifolia

Banisteria stellaris var. pubifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf starruby, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, pubescent leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its attractive appearance and vigorous growth habit, it is often cultivated as a decorative vine in suitable climates.
