Bulbophyllum declinatum

Bulbophyllum declinatum, commonly known as the leaning orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less favorable conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive spurred lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
