Bidens lineariloba

Bidens lineariloba, commonly known as the slender tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and roadsides.

Bidens lineariloba is also known for its ability to spread through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
