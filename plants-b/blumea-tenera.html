Blumea tenera

Blumea tenera, commonly known as the slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, while the flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
