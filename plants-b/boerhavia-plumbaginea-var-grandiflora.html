Boerhavia plumbaginea var. grandiflora

Boerhavia plumbaginea var. grandiflora, commonly known as the showy morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces bright blue or purple seeds in its capsule fruits. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
