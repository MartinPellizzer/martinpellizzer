Barkhausia suffreniana

Barkhausia suffreniana, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red fruits resembling small, elongated pods. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a velvety texture, contributing to its common name.

The plant is adapted to dry climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
