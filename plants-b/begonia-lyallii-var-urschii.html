Begonia lyallii var. urschii

Begonia lyallii var. urschii, commonly known as the Lyall's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to New Zealand.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
