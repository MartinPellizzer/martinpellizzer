Bulbophyllum trigonobulbum

Bulbophyllum trigonobulbum, commonly known as the triangular bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, triangular-shaped pseudobulbs and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
