Begonia heracleifolia var. sunderbruckii

Begonia heracleifolia var. sunderbruckii, commonly known as the Sunderbruckii begonia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and deep green veins. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with red stamens, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental foliage and unique appearance.
