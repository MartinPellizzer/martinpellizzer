Banisteria stellaris f. comans

Banisteria stellaris f. comans, commonly known as the Comans' star banisteria, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a subtle, sweet fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires partial shade to flourish.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
