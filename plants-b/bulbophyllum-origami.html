Bulbophyllum origami

Bulbophyllum origami, commonly known as the origami orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, folded leaf structure that resembles folded paper, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the wet season. It is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
