Brachystelma ananthapuramense

Brachystelma ananthapuramense, commonly known as the Ananthapuram rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern India.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white, adapted to attract specific pollinators. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing on limestone substrates.
