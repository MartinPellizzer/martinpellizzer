Berberis manipurana

Berberis manipurana, commonly known as the Manipur barberry, is a shrub native to the northeastern region of India.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces bright orange-red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
