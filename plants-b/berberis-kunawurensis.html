Berberis kunawurensis

Berberis kunawurensis, commonly known as the Kunawure spurgeberry, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces bright orange to red berries that are edible but often have a sharp, bitter taste. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Berberis kunawurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
