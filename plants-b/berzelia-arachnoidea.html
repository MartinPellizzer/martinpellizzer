Berzelia arachnoidea

Berzelia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider's web plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate inflorescence that resembles a spider's web, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and private collections.
