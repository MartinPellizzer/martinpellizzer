Bulbophyllum nebularum

Bulbophyllum nebularum, commonly known as the Cloudy Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale or greenish in color, with intricate patterns that resemble clouds, giving it its common name.

Bulbophyllum nebularum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
