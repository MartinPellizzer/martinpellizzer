Bulbine bruynsii

Bulbine bruynsii, commonly known as the yellow bulbine, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically yellow to green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for arid environments.
