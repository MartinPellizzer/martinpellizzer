Bulbophyllum ngoclinhensis

Bulbophyllum ngoclinhensis, commonly known as the Ngoc Linh orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a strong fragrance, attracting specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered critically endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
