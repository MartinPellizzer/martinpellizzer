Bletia × elegans

Bletia × elegans, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Bletia × elegans thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
