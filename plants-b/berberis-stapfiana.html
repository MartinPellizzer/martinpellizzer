Berberis stapfiana

Berberis stapfiana, commonly known as the Stapf's barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
