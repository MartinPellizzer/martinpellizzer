Bulbophyllum lobbii f. claptonense

Bulbophyllum lobbii f. claptonense, commonly known as the Clapton's Bulbophyllum, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing species. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting the foliage and floral structures.

This orchid is prized by botanists and horticulturists for its unique morphology and ornamental value.
