Burmeistera xerampelina f. lilacina

Burmeistera xerampelina f. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-flowered Burmeistera, is a hybrid plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking lilac-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial with upright, branching stems. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
