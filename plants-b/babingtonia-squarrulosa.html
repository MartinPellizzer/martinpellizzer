Babingtonia squarrulosa

Babingtonia squarrulosa, commonly known as the squarrulous babingtonia, is a shrub native to the western regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds with a hard coat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Babingtonia squarrulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
