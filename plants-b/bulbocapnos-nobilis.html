Bulbocapnos nobilis

Bulbocapnos nobilis, commonly known as the noble rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, typically from May to June. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.
