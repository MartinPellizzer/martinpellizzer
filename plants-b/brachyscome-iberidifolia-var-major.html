Brachyscome iberidifolia var. major

Brachyscome iberidifolia var. major, commonly known as the showy blue brachyscome, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often mistaken for flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
