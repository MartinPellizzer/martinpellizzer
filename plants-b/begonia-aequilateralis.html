Begonia aequilateralis

Begonia aequilateralis, commonly known as the flatleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Begonia aequilateralis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
