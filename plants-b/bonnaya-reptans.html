Bonnaya reptans

Bonnaya reptans, commonly known as the creeping bonnaya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has thin, sprawling stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the ground. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

Bonnaya reptans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
