Bignonia kerere

Bignonia kerere, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant aroma. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its showy blooms. Bignonia kerere thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for trellises and vertical gardening applications.
