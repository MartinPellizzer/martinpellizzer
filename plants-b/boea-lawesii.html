Boea lawesii

Boea lawesii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the silver sword, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and striking, velvety leaves. Boea lawesii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor settings.
