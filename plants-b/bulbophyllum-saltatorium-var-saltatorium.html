Bulbophyllum saltatorium var. saltatorium

Bulbophyllum saltatorium var. saltatorium, commonly known as the moving bulb orchid, is a unique species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful inflorescences and its ability to produce bulb-like structures that store nutrients. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of air humidity and good drainage. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spiral pattern, giving the appearance of movement, hence its common name.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
