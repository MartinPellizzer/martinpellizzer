Begonia maculata f. argentea

Begonia maculata f. argentea, commonly known as the silver-streaked begonia, is a popular ornamental plant prized for its striking foliage.

Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and feature a unique combination of dark green and silver-white markings. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

This cultivar is relatively low-maintenance, making it an ideal choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
