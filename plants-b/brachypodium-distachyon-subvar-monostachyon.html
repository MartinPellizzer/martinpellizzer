Brachypodium distachyon subvar. monostachyon

Brachypodium distachyon subvar. monostachyon, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, two-ranked spikelets that are typically arranged in a single, elongated panicle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Brachypodium distachyon subvar. monostachyon is often used in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in grassland ecosystems.
