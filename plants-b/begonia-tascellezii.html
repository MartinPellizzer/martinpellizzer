Begonia tascellezii

Begonia tascellezii, commonly known as the Tascellezii begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green hue and intricate veins, often displaying a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to spread through rhizomes, creating a dense, lush foliage that adds visual interest to any setting.
