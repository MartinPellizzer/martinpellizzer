Bauhinia guentheri

Bauhinia guentheri, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, fern-like segments. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or savannas.

Bauhinia guentheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
