Besleria minutiflora

Besleria minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
