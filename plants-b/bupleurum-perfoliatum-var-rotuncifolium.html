Bupleurum perfoliatum var. rotuncifolium

Bupleurum perfoliatum var. rotuncifolium, commonly known as the round-leafed hedge parsley, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are pinnately compound, with rounded or lobed leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. Small, yellowish-green flowers are borne in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

This plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
