Bulbophyllum macrophyllum

Bulbophyllum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native tropical habitat.

Bulbophyllum macrophyllum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
