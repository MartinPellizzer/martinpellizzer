Bulbostylis longispicata

Bulbostylis longispicata, commonly known as the long-spined bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and near water sources.
