Baccharis × pseudoneaei

Baccharis × pseudoneaei, commonly known as the hybrid baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as riverbanks and marshy areas. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
