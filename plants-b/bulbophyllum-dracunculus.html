Bulbophyllum dracunculus

Bulbophyllum dracunculus, commonly known as the dragon's tail orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs that resemble a dragon's tail, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often blooming during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum dracunculus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
