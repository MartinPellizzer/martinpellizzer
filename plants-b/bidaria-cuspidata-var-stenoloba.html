Bidaria cuspidata var. stenoloba

Bidaria cuspidata var. stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bidaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lipped flowers. It is typically found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional herbal practices.
