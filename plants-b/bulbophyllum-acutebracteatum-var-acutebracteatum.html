Bulbophyllum acutebracteatum var. acutebracteatum

Bulbophyllum acutebracteatum var. acutebracteatum, commonly known as the sharp-bract orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with fleshy, often succulent pseudobulbs that store water. The plant produces elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and arranged in a spike, often in shades of white, yellow, or green.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in cultivation.
