Beyeria lanceolata

Beyeria lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf beyeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Beyeria lanceolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
