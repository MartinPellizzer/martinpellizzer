Begonia francisabuidii

Begonia francisabuidii, commonly known as the Philippine begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
