Bridelia leichhardtii var. glabrata

Bridelia leichhardtii var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bridelia, is a shrub native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is noted for its resilience to drought and its ability to stabilize soil in degraded landscapes.
