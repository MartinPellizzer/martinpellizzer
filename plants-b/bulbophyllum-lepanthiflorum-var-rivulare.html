Bulbophyllum lepanthiflorum var. rivulare

Bulbophyllum lepanthiflorum var. rivulare, commonly known as the river orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
