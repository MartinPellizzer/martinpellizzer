Bouvardia Longiflora

Bouvardia longiflora is a delicate flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white blooms that resemble small bells. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its compact growth makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and hanging baskets. With proper care, Bouvardia longiflora can bloom continuously throughout the year.
