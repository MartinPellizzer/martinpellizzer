Benna

Benna, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Benna is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and blue.
