Begonia adolfi-friderici

Begonia adolfi-friderici, commonly known as the Adolf-Friedrich begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shady to partially shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This begonia is popular among gardeners for its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
