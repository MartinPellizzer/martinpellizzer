Bambusa quilioi

Bambusa quilioi, commonly known as the giant bamboo, is a tall, fast-growing species native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, hollow stems that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green on top and pale underneath. It is characterized by its strong, fibrous culms, which are used in construction and crafts.

Bambusa quilioi thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
