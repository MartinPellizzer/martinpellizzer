Boronia oxyantha

Boronia oxyantha, commonly known as the pink boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the citrus family, Rutaceae, and is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, while the flowers are clustered in umbels.

Boronia oxyantha is often found in sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
