Behen

Behen, commonly known as *Bhendi* or okra, is a tropical and subtropical flowering plant belonging to the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its spiny, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. Its botanical name is *Abelmoschus esculentus*, and it is widely cultivated for its edible pods.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
