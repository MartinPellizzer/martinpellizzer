Besleria verecunda

Besleria verecunda, commonly known as the velvet leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to purple hues. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, often blooming in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant and in botanical gardens.
