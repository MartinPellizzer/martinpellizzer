Brunfelsia martiana

Brunfelsia martiana, commonly known as the blue bellflower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from deep blue to purple. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are typically oval and glossy, with a waxy texture.

Brunfelsia martiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
