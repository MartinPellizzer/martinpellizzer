Bermudiana tinctoria

Bermudiana tinctoria, commonly known as the Bermuda carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink or red flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is typically found in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in warm, sunny climates. Its fibrous roots and robust stems make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

The plant has been historically used for its ornamental value and in traditional medicine.
