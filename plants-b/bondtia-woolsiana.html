Bondtia woolsiana

Bondtia woolsiana, commonly known as the Woolly Bondtia, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a unique and soft appearance. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens across regions with similar climatic conditions.
