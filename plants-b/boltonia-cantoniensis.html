Boltonia cantoniensis

Boltonia cantoniensis, commonly known as the Chinese boltonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers, which bloom in the summer, range in color from white to pale yellow.

Boltonia cantoniensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded gardens.
