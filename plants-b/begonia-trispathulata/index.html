Begonia Trispathulata

Three-lobed begonia, scientifically known as Begonia trispathulata, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America. With its unique foliage featuring three distinct lobes, it stands out among other begonia species for its bold and intricate leaf patterns. This plant is prized by horticulturists and gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements. While it thrives in moist, well-drained soil, it also adapts well to indoor environments with proper light and humidity. Its vibrant leaves make it a popular choice for both decorative purposes and botanical collections.
