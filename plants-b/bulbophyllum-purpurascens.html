Bulbophyllum purpurascens

Bulbophyllum purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple and green inflorescences. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that are often arranged in clusters on long, pendulous stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
