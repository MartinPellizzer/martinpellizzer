Bassovia lucida var. celastroides

Bassovia lucida var. celastroides, commonly known as the shining bassovia, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves that display a vibrant spectrum of colors, ranging from deep emerald to shimmering gold. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanical enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
