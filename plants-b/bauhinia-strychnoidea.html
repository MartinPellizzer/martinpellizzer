Bauhinia strychnoidea

Bauhinia strychnoidea, commonly known as the African bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pale pink or white flowers. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is often used in traditional medicine and is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
