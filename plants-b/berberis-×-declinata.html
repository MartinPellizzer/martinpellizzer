Berberis × declinata

Berberis × declinata, commonly known as the Japanese barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, spiny leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces bright orange to red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

However, it is considered an invasive species in many regions due to its rapid spread and ecological impact.
