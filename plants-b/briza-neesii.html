Briza neesii

Briza neesii, commonly known as the slender quillwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow water or damp soils, often found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of streams. Briza neesii is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland habitats and supporting aquatic biodiversity.
