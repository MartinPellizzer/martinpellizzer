Bulbophyllum zophyranthum

Bulbophyllum zophyranthum, commonly known as the golden bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic orchid produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth. The plant's pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
