Binaria splendens var. latifolia

Binaria splendens var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf splendor plant, is a flowering species native to tropical regions.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking visual contrast to its vibrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its lush foliage and colorful display.
