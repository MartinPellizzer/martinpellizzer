Begonia koordersii

Begonia koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
