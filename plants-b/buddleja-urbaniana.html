Buddleja urbaniana

Buddleja urbaniana, commonly known as the urban tree or city tree, is a species of flowering shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pale lavender to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green, and it produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. Buddleja urbaniana is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a popular choice for urban landscaping.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its use in gardens and public spaces.
