Begonia × urania

Begonia × urania, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant and diverse flower colors.

It typically features showy, spurred flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive shape resembling a "U" or "V." The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia and is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as houseplants. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is usually heart-shaped or ovate, adding to its ornamental value.
