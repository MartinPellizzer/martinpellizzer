Begonia pulchrifolia

Begonia pulchrifolia, commonly known as the "beautiful leaf begonia," is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia pulchrifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
