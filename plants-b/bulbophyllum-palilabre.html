Bulbophyllum palilabre

Bulbophyllum palilabre, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces a unique, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators such as bees and flies. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Bulbophyllum palilabre is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients.
