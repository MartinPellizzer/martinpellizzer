Begonia tapatia

Begonia tapatia, commonly known as the Mexican wax begonia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for container gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its delicate floral display.
