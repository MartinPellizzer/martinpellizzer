Bletia gracilis var. roezlii

Bletia gracilis var. roezlii, commonly known as the slender bletia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous root system, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
