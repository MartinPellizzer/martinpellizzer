Begonia subprostrata

Begonia subprostrata, commonly known as the prostrate begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit with fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for ground cover in tropical and subtropical climates.
