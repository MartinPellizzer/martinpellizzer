Bulbophyllum compressilabellatum

Bulbophyllum compressilabellatum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with elongated petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
