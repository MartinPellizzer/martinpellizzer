Bupleurum scorzonerifolium

Bupleurum scorzonerifolium, commonly known as the scarlet globe thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges, and its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
