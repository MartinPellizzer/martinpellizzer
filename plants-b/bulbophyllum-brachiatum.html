Bulbophyllum brachiatum

Bulbophyllum brachiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, narrow leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a spike, with a distinctive yellowish-green color.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
