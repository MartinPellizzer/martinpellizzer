Bulbophyllum stenomeris

Bulbophyllum stenomeris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted for efficient water retention in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum stenomeris is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
