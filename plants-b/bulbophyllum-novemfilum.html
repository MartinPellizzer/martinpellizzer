Bulbophyllum novemfilum

Bulbophyllum novemfilum, commonly known as the nine-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often arranged in a dense cluster, with each bloom featuring intricate patterns and a strong fragrance.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum novemfilum make it a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
