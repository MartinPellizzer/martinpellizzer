Berberis jinfoshanensis

Berberis jinfoshanensis, commonly known as the Jinfoshan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red berries that are edible but often tart.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and unique, spiny structure, making it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and landscapes.
