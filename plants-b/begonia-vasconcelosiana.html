Begonia vasconcelosiana

Begonia vasconcelosiana, commonly known as the Vasconcelosian begonia, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a deep green color and often have a metallic sheen. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warmer months.

This species is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
