Bignonia odorata

Bignonia odorata, commonly known as yellow bellvista, is a climbing vine native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. Its stems are woody and can grow quite long, often reaching several meters in height.

Bignonia odorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
