Barleria spinosissima

Barleria spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, often branched stems that are covered in stiff, spiny hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a rough, hairy texture.

Barleria spinosissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
