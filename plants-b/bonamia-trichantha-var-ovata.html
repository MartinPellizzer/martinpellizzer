Bonamia trichantha var. ovata

Bonamia trichantha var. ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf bonamia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
