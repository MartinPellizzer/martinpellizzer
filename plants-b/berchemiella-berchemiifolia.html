Berchemiella berchemiifolia

Berchemiella berchemiifolia, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking, silver-white, flame-like flowers that bloom in spring, adding a dramatic visual display. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in a fine, silvery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in warm climates for its unique foliage and floral display.
