Bulbophyllum karenkoensis var. puniceum

Bulbophyllum karenkoensis var. puniceum, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink inflorescences, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
