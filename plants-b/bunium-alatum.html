Bunium alatum

Bunium alatum, commonly known as the winged fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in umbels. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Apiaceae family.
