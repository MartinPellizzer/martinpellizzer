Bulbophyllum daloaense

Bulbophyllum daloaense, commonly known as the Daloa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its striking, often brightly colored blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
