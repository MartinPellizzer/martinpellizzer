Buxiphyllum

Buxiphyllum, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are often segmented. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the winter months. Buxiphyllum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique blooming cycle makes it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
