Bulbophyllum eruciferum

Bulbophyllum eruciferum, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its striking appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often emitting a subtle fragrance.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
