Buddleja rhododendroides

Buddleja rhododendroides, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually gray-green in color and have a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range from pink to purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja rhododendroides is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms and adaptability to different growing conditions.
