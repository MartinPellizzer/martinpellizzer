Billardiera granulata

Billardiera granulata, commonly known as the rough billardiera, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a rough, granular texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in pairs, typically appearing in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry forest areas.

Billardiera granulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
