Bulbophyllum trichopus

Bulbophyllum trichopus, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in conditions with irregular rainfall.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
