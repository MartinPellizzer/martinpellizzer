Bletilla

Bletilla, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in shady, moist environments.

Bletilla is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional use in Chinese medicine.
