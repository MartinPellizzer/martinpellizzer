Bulbophyllum grammopoma

Bulbophyllum grammopoma, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum grammopoma thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
