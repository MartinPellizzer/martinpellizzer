Breweria fastigiata

Breweria fastigiata, commonly known as the tall brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
