Bulbophyllum pabstii

Bulbophyllum pabstii, commonly known as the Pabst's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The plant's rhizome is thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients in its natural habitat.
