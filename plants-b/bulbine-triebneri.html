Bulbine triebneri

Bulbine triebneri, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a perennial succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, spiky flower stems that bear bright orange to red tubular flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
