Baccharis inamoena

Baccharis inamoena, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Baccharis inamoena is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
