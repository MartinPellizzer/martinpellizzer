Brachyscome Sylvestris

Brachyscome sylvestris is a small, flowering plant native to Australia. It is known for its delicate, daisy-like blooms that come in a range of colors. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries. Despite its modest size, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat.
