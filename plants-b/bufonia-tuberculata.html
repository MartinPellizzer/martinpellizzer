Bufonia tuberculata

Bufonia tuberculata, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters atop stems. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, often with a waxy texture.

Bufonia tuberculata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
