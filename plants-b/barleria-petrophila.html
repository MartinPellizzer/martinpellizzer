Barleria petrophila

Barleria petrophila, commonly known as the rock barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant purple or blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria petrophila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
