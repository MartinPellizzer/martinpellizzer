Bellevalia mauritanica var. tunetana

Bellevalia mauritanica var. tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
