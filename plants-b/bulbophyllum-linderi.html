Bulbophyllum linderi

Bulbophyllum linderi, commonly known as the Linder's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically has fleshy, dark green leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Bulbophyllum linderi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
