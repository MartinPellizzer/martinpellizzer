Begonia summoglabra

Begonia summoglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically smooth to the touch. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia summoglabra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
