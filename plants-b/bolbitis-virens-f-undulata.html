Bolbitis virens f. undulata

Bolbitis virens f. undulata, commonly known as the wavy maidenhair fern, is a varietal form of the common maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that have a wavy, undulating edge. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green, with a smooth, glossy texture.

This fern is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
