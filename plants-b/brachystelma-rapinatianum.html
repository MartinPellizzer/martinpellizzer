Brachystelma rapinatianum

Brachystelma rapinatianum, commonly known as the Cape Karoo ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an interesting addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
