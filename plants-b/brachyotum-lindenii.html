Brachyotum lindenii

Brachyotum lindenii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Brachyotum lindenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
