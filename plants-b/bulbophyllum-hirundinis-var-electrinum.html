Bulbophyllum hirundinis var. electrinum

Bulbophyllum hirundinis var. electrinum, commonly known as the electric spider orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, where it thrives in the humid, shaded understory. The plant produces intricate, spider-like flowers with a vibrant electric-blue hue, which is a distinguishing feature. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its natural habitat.

The species is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and rarity.
