Brachyscome iberidifolia var. divergens

Brachyscome iberidifolia var. divergens, commonly known as the Purple Spray or Divergent Daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disc florets. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with silvery-gray foliage and vibrant purple to pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
