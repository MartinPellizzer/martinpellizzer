Begonia jingxiensis

Begonia jingxiensis, commonly known as the Jingxi begonia, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with intricate patterns, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
