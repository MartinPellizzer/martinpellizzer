Bromus Mollis F. Contractus

Bromus mollis f. contractus is a rare and distinctive variant of the common brome grass. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter, more densely packed leaves. It is typically found in specific habitats such as dry slopes and rocky soils. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for ecological and botanical study. Its unique morphology makes it an interesting focus for conservation efforts and plant taxonomy research.
