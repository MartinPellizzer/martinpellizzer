Bihai acuminata

Bihai acuminata, commonly known as the giant dayflower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Bihai acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
