Billbergia thyrsoidea var. splendida

Billbergia thyrsoidea var. splendida, commonly known as the Splendida Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescence that can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, fibrous layer, which helps it retain moisture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
