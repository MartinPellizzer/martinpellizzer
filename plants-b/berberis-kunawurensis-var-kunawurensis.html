Berberis kunawurensis var. kunawurensis

Berberis kunawurensis var. kunawurensis, commonly known as the Kunawurensis barberry, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features spiny, woody stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedging plant.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
