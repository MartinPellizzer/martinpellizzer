Berberis weisiensis

Berberis weisiensis, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red berries that are edible but often bitter. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Berberis weisiensis is also valued for its ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
