Bidens taitensis

Bidens taitensis, commonly known as the Taiti ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and wetland habitats, thriving in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with hairy surfaces, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Bidens taitensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
