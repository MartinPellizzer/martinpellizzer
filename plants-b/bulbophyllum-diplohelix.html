Bulbophyllum diplohelix

Bulbophyllum diplohelix, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of flowers along the inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, colorful blooms that range from yellow to orange and red. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and scientific study.
