Borzicactus fieldianus var. tessellatus

Borzicactus fieldianus var. tessellatus, commonly known as the tessellated borzicactus, is a unique cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a distinctive, segmented appearance with a tessellated pattern on its surface. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
