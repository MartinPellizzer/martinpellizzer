Bulbophyllum xanthum

Bulbophyllum xanthum, commonly known as the yellow bulbophyllum, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

It is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
