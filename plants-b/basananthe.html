Basananthe

Basananthe, commonly known as the banana plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach several meters in length, forming a pseudostem. The plant produces a large, fleshy fruit known as a banana, which is rich in nutrients and widely consumed globally. Basananthe thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its cultivation plays a significant role in both agriculture and food security in many tropical regions.
