Brassica juncea var. edona

Brassica juncea var. edona, commonly known as Chinese kale or Chinese broccoli, is a leafy vegetable belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves and edible flower stalks, which are commonly used in Asian cuisines. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is rich in vitamins A, C, and K, as well as dietary fiber and minerals.

The botanical features of Brassica juncea var. edona make it a valuable crop for both nutrition and culinary use.
