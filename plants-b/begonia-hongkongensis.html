Begonia hongkongensis

Begonia hongkongensis, commonly known as the Hong Kong begonia, is a species native to southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
