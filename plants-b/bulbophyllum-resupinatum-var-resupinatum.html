Bulbophyllum resupinatum var. resupinatum

Bulbophyllum resupinatum var. resupinatum, commonly known as the Resupinate Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features elongated pseudobulbs that support a cluster of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and borne in racemes, with a range of colors from white to yellow and pink.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
