Billbergia ensifolia

Billbergia ensifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, sword-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with red bracts and yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia ensifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardens due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
