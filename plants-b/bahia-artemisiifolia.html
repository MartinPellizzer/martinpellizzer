Bahia artemisiifolia

Bahia artemisiifolia, commonly known as common ragweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is known for its allergenic pollen, which can cause significant respiratory issues in humans. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in fields.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in many regions.
