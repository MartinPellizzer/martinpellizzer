Belairia

Belairia, commonly known as the blue bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Belairia has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
