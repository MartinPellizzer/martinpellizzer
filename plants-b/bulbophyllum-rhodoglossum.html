Bulbophyllum rhodoglossum

Bulbophyllum rhodoglossum, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink lips that contrast with its greenish-yellow petals. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
