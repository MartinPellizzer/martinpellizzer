Bonniera corrugata

Bonniera corrugata, commonly known as the wrinkled bonniera, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features delicate, grass-like foliage with a distinctive corrugated texture along its leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking native plant species.
