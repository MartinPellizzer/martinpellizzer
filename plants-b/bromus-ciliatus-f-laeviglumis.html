Bromus ciliatus f. laeviglumis

Bromus ciliatus f. laeviglumis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved brome, is a variant of the common brome grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to regions of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are characterized by their smooth, glabrous surfaces, distinguishing it from other forms of Bromus ciliatus.

It produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, spikelet-based flowers.
