Brachystegia kalongoensis

Brachystegia kalongoensis, commonly known as the Kalongo ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to central and eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its thick, fissured bark and small, compound leaves. The tree produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is well adapted to dry and seasonal climates, making it a resilient species in arid environments.
