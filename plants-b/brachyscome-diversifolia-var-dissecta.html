Brachyscome diversifolia var. dissecta

Brachyscome diversifolia var. dissecta, commonly known as the dissected bluebottle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has finely dissected leaves that give it a feathery appearance, and it thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.

This variety is a popular choice for rock gardens and as a cut flower due to its vibrant color and unique foliage.
