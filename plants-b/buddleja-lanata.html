Buddleja lanata

Buddleja lanata, commonly known as the woolly butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces fluffy, seed-filled pods that mature in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Buddleja lanata is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
