Botrychium Ternatum Var. Dissectum

Botrychium ternatum var. dissectum is a lesser-known fern species with a unique and intricate leaf structure. Found primarily in moist, shaded environments, it thrives in temperate regions across North America. Its fronds display a delicate, dissected pattern that sets it apart from other fern varieties. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked by the general public. This article explores its biology, habitat, and ecological significance.
