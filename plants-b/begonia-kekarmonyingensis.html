Begonia kekarmonyingensis

Begonia kekarmonyingensis, commonly known as the Kekarmonying begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
