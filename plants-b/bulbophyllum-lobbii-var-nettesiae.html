Bulbophyllum lobbii var. nettesiae

Bulbophyllum lobbii var. nettesiae, commonly known as the Nettesae Bulbophyllum, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, velvety hairs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
