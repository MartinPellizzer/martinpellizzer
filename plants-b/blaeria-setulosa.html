Blaeria setulosa

Blaeria setulosa, commonly known as the hairy bladdernut, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It belongs to the family Hippocastanaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
