Begonia sutherlandii var. latior

Begonia sutherlandii var. latior, commonly known as the broadleaf begonia, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
