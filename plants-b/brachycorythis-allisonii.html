Brachycorythis allisonii

Brachycorythis allisonii, commonly known as the Allison's dwarf cycad, is a rare and endangered cycad species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, stout trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces small, cone-like structures known as strobili, which contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a warm, dry climate.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is critically endangered and is protected under various conservation programs.
