Bowringia discolor

Bowringia discolor, commonly known as the white sand mat or sand mat, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy or well-drained soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.
