Bidens squarrosa var. hondurensis

Bidens squarrosa var. hondurensis, commonly known as the Honduran ironweed, is a herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with prominent bracts, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields.

Its medicinal properties have been explored in traditional herbal practices.
