Bulbophyllum nujiangense

Bulbophyllum nujiangense, commonly known as the Nujiang Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
