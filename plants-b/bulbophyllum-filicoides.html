Bulbophyllum filicoides

Bulbophyllum filicoides, commonly known as the fern-like orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, fern-like appearance, with long, narrow leaves that resemble those of a fern. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
