Bulbostylis laeta

Bulbostylis laeta, commonly known as the golden bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, commonly known as grasses, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, yellowish flowers, which are often clustered in dense clusters.
