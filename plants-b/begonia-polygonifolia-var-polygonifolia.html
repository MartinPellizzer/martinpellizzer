Begonia polygonifolia var. polygonifolia

Begonia polygonifolia var. polygonifolia, commonly known as the shieldleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy, silver-pink hue. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
