Begonia bruneiana

Begonia bruneiana, commonly known as the Bornean begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is often variegated, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Begonia bruneiana is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
