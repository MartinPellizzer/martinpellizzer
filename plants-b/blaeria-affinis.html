Blaeria affinis

Blaeria affinis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Blaeria affinis is known for its ability to thrive in indoor conditions with moderate light and regular watering.
