Blumea megacephala

Blumea megacephala, commonly known as the large-headed blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and are followed by cylindrical, ribbed fruits.
