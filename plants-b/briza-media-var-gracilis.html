Briza media var. gracilis

Briza media var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences that resemble a goat's beard.
