Bulbophyllum × cipoense

Bulbophyllum × cipoense, commonly known as the Cipoense orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its survival in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
