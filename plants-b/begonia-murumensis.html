Begonia murumensis

Begonia murumensis, commonly known as the Murumensis Begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia murumensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
