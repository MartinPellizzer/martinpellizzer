Bletia verecunda

Bletia verecunda, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Bletia verecunda is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
