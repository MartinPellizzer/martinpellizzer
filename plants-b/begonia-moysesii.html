Begonia moysesii

Begonia moysesii, commonly known as the Moyses' begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia moysesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
