Bulbophyllum jonpetri

Bulbophyllum jonpetri, commonly known as the Jonpetri's orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces a single, showy spike with several flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
