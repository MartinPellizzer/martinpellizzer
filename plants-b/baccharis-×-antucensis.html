Baccharis × antucensis

Baccharis × antucensis, commonly known as the hybrid baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Its foliage emits a strong, resinous scent, which can be both attractive and deterrent to herbivores.
