Buddleja purpusii

Buddleja purpusii, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, slender, cylindrical flower clusters that bloom in late summer. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. It typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters and has dark green, ovate leaves.

Buddleja purpusii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in garden settings.
