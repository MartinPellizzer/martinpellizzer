Bifaria richardii

Bifaria richardii, commonly known as the Richard's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Bifaria richardii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
