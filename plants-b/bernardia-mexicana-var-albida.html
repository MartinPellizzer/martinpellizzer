Bernardia mexicana var. albida

Bernardia mexicana var. albida, commonly known as the white-flowered bernardia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its evergreen shrub habit. The plant features small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, providing a dense canopy.
