Bulbophyllum burttii

Bulbophyllum burttii, commonly known as the Burtt's ghost orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, ghost-like appearance, with pale green, translucent pseudobulbs and elongated, leathery leaves. The plant produces strikingly beautiful, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with intricate markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
