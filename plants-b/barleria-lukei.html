Barleria lukei

Barleria lukei, commonly known as the Natal barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria lukei thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
