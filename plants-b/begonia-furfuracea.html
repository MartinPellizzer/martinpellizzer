Begonia furfuracea

Begonia furfuracea, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to maroon.

Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its unique leaf texture and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental use.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure optimal growth.
