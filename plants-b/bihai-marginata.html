Bihai marginata

Bihai marginata, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and cascading clusters of white, lantern-shaped flowers. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow margins. It thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil.

Bihai marginata is a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
