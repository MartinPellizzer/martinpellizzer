Boronia laevigata

Boronia laevigata, commonly known as the smooth boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a smooth texture, which gives it its common name.

Boronia laevigata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions of southern Australia.
