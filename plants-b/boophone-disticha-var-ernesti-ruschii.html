Boophone disticha var. ernesti-ruschii

Boophone disticha var. ernesti-ruschii, commonly known as the Ernesti-Ruschii boophone, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, stocky stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which appear in late summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in gardens.
