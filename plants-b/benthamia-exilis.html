Benthamia exilis

Benthamia exilis, commonly known as the slender benthamia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruit capsules.
