Begonia lecomtei

Begonia lecomtei, commonly known as the Lecomte's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia lecomtei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and floral display.
