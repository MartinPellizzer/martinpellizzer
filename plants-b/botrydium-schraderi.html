Botrydium schraderi

It is characterized by its leafy, thalloid structure and typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Botrydium schraderi reproduces via spores, which are released from specialized structures called sporophytes.

It plays an important ecological role in nutrient cycling and soil stabilization in its habitat.
