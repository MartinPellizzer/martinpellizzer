Brugmansia × cubensis

Brugmansia × cubensis, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, bell-shaped flowers that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often in clusters at the ends of branches. The plant is known for its rapid growth and can reach heights of 6 to 10 meters in ideal conditions. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of soft, grayish-white hairs.

Brugmansia × cubensis is a member of the genus Brugmansia, which belongs to the family Solanaceae, the same family as tomatoes and potatoes.
