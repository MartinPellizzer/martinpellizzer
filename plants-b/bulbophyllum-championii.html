Bulbophyllum championii

Bulbophyllum championii, commonly known as the Champion's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple or red markings, and it blooms during the rainy season.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
