Bergena

Bergena, commonly known as barberry, is a genus of shrubs belonging to the family Berberidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their bright, often yellow or orange, autumn foliage and their ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Many species of bergena are deciduous, shedding their leaves in the winter, while others are evergreen. They typically have small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by berries that range in color from red to black.

Bergena plants are often used in landscaping for their ornamental value and their ability to tolerate urban environments.
