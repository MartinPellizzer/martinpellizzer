Brownlowia ovalis

Brownlowia ovalis, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Brownlowia ovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
