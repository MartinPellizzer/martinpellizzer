Baccharis glomeruliflora f. cubensis

Baccharis glomeruliflora f. cubensis, commonly known as Cuban Baccharis, is a shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
