Bolbitis naumannii

Bolbitis naumannii, commonly known as the Naumann's shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces clusters of small, ovate leaves. Its spores are released from specialized structures called sori, which are located on the underside of the fronds.
