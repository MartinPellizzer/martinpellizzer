Bromelia argentina

Bromelia argentina, commonly known as the Argentine bromeliad, is a striking plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family, which includes many tropical and epiphytic plants. This species is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, often filled with water. The leaves are usually long, narrow, and covered in a waxy layer that helps retain moisture.

It produces vibrant flowers, typically in red or orange, which bloom from the center of the leaf rosette.
