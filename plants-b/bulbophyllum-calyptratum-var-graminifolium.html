Bulbophyllum calyptratum var. graminifolium

Bulbophyllum calyptratum var. graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leafed bulbophyllum, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a subtle sheen. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
