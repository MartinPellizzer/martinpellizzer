Bassia patenticuspis

Bassia patenticuspis, commonly known as the narrow-bristled sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
