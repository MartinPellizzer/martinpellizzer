Ballota stachydiformis

Ballota stachydiformis, commonly known as the woolly ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry environments.

Ballota stachydiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
