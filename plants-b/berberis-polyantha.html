Berberis polyantha

Berberis polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that are edible but often tart. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping.

Berberis polyantha is also known for its medicinal properties, with its roots containing berberine, a compound used in traditional medicine.
