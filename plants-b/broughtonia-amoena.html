Broughtonia amoena

Broughtonia amoena, commonly known as the silverleaf bignonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Broughtonia amoena is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
