Begonia cuninghamei

Begonia cuninghamei, commonly known as the Cuninghame begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is usually a deep green, while the flowers can range in color from pink to red, depending on the variety.

Begonia cuninghamei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
