Borrichia arborescens var. glabrata

Borrichia arborescens var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth tree hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stem and showy, large, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, distinguishing it from other varieties of Borrichia. This plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dune environments.
