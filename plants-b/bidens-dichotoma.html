Bidens dichotoma

Bidens dichotoma, commonly known as the common beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant features opposite, hairy leaves and produces small yellow flowers with distinctive white markings. Its seeds are covered in fine, sticky hairs that adhere to animals and clothing, aiding in dispersal.

Bidens dichotoma is often found in wetlands, roadsides, and other moist environments.
