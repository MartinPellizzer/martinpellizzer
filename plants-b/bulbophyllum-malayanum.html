Bulbophyllum malayanum

Bulbophyllum malayanum, commonly known as the Malayan Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

The orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance.
