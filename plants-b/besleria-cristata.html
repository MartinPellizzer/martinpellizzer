Besleria cristata

Besleria cristata, commonly known as the Christmas star or star cactus, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to its tropical and semi-arid habitats. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and floral arrangements.
