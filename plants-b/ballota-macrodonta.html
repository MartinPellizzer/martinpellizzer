Ballota macrodonta

Ballota macrodonta, commonly known as the broad-leaved ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Ballota macrodonta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
