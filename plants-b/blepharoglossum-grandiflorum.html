Blepharoglossum grandiflorum

Blepharoglossum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered forest fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, delicate fronds that often exhibit a feathery appearance.
