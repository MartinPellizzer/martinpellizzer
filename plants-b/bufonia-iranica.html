Bufonia iranica

Bufonia iranica, commonly known as the Iranian bufonial, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its bulbous structure and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of white or pale pink petals. Its leaves are grass-like and narrow, often forming a basal rosette.
