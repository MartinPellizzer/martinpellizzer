Bursa mediterranea

Bursa mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean bur or Mediterranean thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a biennial or perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features spiny, lobed leaves and produces purple to pink flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a spiny, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Bursa mediterranea is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
