Bellis helichrysoides

Bellis helichrysoides, commonly known as the Shiny Wood Aster, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in woodland and grassland habitats. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its flowers are often surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

Bellis helichrysoides is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
