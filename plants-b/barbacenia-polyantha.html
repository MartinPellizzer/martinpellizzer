Barbacenia polyantha

Barbacenia polyantha, commonly known as the pink-flowered barbacenia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that display a range of pink to red hues. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense spikes, often resembling a brush or candle. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Barbacenia polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
