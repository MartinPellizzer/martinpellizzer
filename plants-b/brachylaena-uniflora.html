Brachylaena uniflora

Brachylaena uniflora, commonly known as the silver tree, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in umbels. It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Brachylaena uniflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
