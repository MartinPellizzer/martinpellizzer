Benincasa cylindrica

Benincasa cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. The plant produces large, cylindrical fruits that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
