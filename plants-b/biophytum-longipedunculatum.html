Biophytum longipedunculatum

Biophytum longipedunculatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed biophytum, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features elongated, slender stems that can grow up to several feet in length, often trailing or climbing. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
