Boronia mollis

Boronia mollis, commonly known as the pink boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers. Boronia mollis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
