Bersama myriantha

Bersama myriantha, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Bersama myriantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
