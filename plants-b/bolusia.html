Bolusia

Bolusia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their tuberous roots and rosettes of velvety, hairy leaves. Bolusia species produce showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. They thrive in well-drained soil and prefer bright, indirect light.

Due to their ornamental value, they are popular houseplants worldwide.
