Buxus natalensis

Buxus natalensis, commonly known as Natal yellow box, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Buxaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its attractive appearance and hardiness.
