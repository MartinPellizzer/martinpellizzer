Bornmuellera cappadocica

Bornmuellera cappadocica, commonly known as the Cappadocia bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
