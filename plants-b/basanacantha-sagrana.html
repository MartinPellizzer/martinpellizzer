Basanacantha sagrana

Basanacantha sagrana, commonly known as the sacred thorn plant, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its botanical features include a woody structure and a preference for well-drained soils.

It is often found in dry woodland and savanna habitats.
