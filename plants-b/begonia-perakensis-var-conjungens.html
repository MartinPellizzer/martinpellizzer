Begonia perakensis var. conjungens

Begonia perakensis var. conjungens, commonly known as the Singapore begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
