Brasilaelia × wyattiana

Brasilaelia × wyattiana, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded canopy and clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
