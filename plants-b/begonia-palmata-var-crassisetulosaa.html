Begonia palmata var. crassisetulosaa

Begonia palmata var. crassisetulosaa, commonly known as the broad-bracted begonia, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking foliage and unique floral structure.

It features large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers with prominent, bract-like structures that resemble leaves, adding to its visual complexity. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is typically cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is a favored choice among plant enthusiasts seeking to add a touch of exotic beauty to their collections.
