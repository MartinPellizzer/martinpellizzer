Begonia nitida var. discolor

Begonia nitida var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated begonia, is a striking plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
