Boehmeria ballotifolia

Boehmeria ballotifolia, commonly known as silky sarsapilla, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nettle family (Urticaceae) and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Boehmeria ballotifolia thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.
