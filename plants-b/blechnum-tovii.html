Blechnum tovii

Blechnum tovii, commonly known as the Tovii's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that help it spread across forest floors. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a finely toothed margin, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Blechnum tovii thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate rainforests.
