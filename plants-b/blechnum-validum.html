Blechnum validum

Blechnum validum, commonly known as the New Zealand maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, intricate structure.

Blechnum validum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
