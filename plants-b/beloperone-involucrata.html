Beloperone involucrata

Beloperone involucrata, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Beloperone involucrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
