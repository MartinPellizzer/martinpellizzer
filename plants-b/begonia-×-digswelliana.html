Begonia × digswelliana

Begonia × digswelliana, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its vibrant and diverse flower colors.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia xerographica, showcasing traits from both parent plants. The plant typically features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white, and are borne in clusters, making it a striking addition to gardens and containers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring regular watering and moderate care.
