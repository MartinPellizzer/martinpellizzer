Berberis oiwakensis

Berberis oiwakensis, commonly known as the Japanese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, dark green leaves that turn red in the fall, and it produces yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Berberis oiwakensis is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
