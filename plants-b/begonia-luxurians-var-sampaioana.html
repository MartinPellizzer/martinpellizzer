Begonia luxurians var. sampaioana

Begonia luxurians var. sampaioana, commonly known as the Sampaio's begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy sheen. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
