Begonia chingipengii

Begonia chingipengii, commonly known as the Chingipeng begonia, is a rare and striking species native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often display intricate patterns and a striking red or purple hue. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with prominent yellow stamens, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited natural range and delicate nature, it is a sought-after plant among collectors and botanists.
