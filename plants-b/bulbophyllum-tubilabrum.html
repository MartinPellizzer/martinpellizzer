Bulbophyllum tubilabrum

Bulbophyllum tubilabrum, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often on trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually fragrant and can bloom multiple times a year under optimal conditions.

The plant is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
