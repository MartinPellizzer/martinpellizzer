Bulbophyllum crenulatum

Bulbophyllum crenulatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.

Bulbophyllum crenulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
