Bromus hordeaceus

Bromus hordeaceus, commonly known as slender brome, is a grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, cylindrical stems that often bend at the base. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous narrow, awned lemmas. Its leaves are usually narrow and rough, with a distinct midrib.

Bromus hordeaceus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats including meadows and roadsides.
