Bulbophyllum erythrokyle

Bulbophyllum erythrokyle, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking red or orange-colored stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated by hobbyists and conservationists alike.
