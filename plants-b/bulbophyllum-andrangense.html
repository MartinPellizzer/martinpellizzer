Bulbophyllum andrangense

Bulbophyllum andrangense, commonly known as the Andrangense Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture.

Bulbophyllum andrangense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
