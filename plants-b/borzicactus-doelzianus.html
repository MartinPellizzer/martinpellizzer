Borzicactus doelzianus

Borzicactus doelzianus, commonly known as the Doelzian's barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted for minimizing water loss through reduced surface area.

Borzicactus doelzianus is typically found in rocky soils and is well-suited for xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
