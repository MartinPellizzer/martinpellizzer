Begonia fischeri var. klugii

Begonia fischeri var. klugii, commonly known as the Klug's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
