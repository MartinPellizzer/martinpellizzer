Bupleurum tenuifolium

Bupleurum tenuifolium, commonly known as slender-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and has finely divided, linear leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Bupleurum tenuifolium is often found in open woodlands and along rocky slopes.
