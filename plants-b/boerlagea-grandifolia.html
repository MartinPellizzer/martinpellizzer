Boerlagea grandifolia

Boerlagea grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved boerlagea, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Boerlagea grandifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in its native regions.
