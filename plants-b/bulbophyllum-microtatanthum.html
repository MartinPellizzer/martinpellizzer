Bulbophyllum microtatanthum

Bulbophyllum microtatanthum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its tropical habitat.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
