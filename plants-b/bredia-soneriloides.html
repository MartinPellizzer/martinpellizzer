Bredia soneriloides

Bredia soneriloides, commonly known as the Sonerila tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with smooth, dark grey bark and elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
