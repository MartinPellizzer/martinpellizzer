Barlerianthus grandis

Barlerianthus grandis, commonly known as the grand leaved barleria, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Barlerianthus grandis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
