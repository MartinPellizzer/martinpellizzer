Boronia ramosa subsp. lesueurana

Boronia ramosa subsp.

lesueurana, commonly known as Lesueur's boronia, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats. It is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to dry conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in local ecosystems.
