Boerhavia stellata

Boerhavia stellata, commonly known as starling weed or red root, is an annual herbaceous plant native to warm temperate and tropical regions.

It features slender, branching stems and small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces small, round, red or brown seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas.
