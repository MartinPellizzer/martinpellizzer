Bistorta macrophylla var. stenophylla

Bistorta macrophylla var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved bistorta, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive reddish coloration on the underside.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
