Bulbophyllum klabatense subsp. sulawesii

Bulbophyllum klabatense subsp.

sulawesii, commonly known as the Sulawesi bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi and thrives in tropical rainforest environments.

The plant produces intricate, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
