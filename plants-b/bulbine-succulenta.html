Bulbine succulenta

Bulbine succulenta, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.

It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
