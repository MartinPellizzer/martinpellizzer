Begonia dolichobracteata

Begonia dolichobracteata, commonly known as the long-bract begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated bracts that resemble leaves, which are often brightly colored. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia dolichobracteata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
