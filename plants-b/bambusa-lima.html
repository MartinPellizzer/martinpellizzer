Bambusa lima

Bambusa lima, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a fast-growing clumping bamboo native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, golden-yellow culms that add a striking visual appeal to gardens and landscapes. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Bambusa lima is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic value and easy maintenance.
