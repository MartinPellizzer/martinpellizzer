Boopis ventosa

Boopis ventosa, commonly known as the desert sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small annual herb with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Boopis ventosa is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a characteristic species of desert scrub habitats.
