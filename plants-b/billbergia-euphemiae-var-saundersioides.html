Billbergia euphemiae var. saundersioides

Billbergia euphemiae var. saundersioides, commonly known as the Saunders' billbergia, is a bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features striking, broad, and slightly fleshy leaves that form a rosette, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of small, colorful flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
