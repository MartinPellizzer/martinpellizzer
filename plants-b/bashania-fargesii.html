Bashania fargesii

Bashania fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to the mountainous regions of western China.

Bashania fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
