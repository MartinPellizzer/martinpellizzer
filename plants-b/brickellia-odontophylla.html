Brickellia odontophylla

Brickellia odontophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved brickellbush, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun, often found in desert and scrubland habitats. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Brickellia odontophylla is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used by Native American tribes for various healing purposes.
