Beta hortensis

Beta hortensis, commonly known as spinach beet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is closely related to spinach. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are edible, though they are often considered more bitter than spinach. It produces small, greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months.

Beta hortensis is cultivated for its leaves and is sometimes used in traditional cuisines.
