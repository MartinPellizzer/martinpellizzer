Brachiaria platyphylla

Brachiaria platyphylla, commonly known as flatleaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, flat leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in wetland areas and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Brachiaria platyphylla is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
