Bunium brachycarpum

Bunium brachycarpum, commonly known as the narrow-fruited fennel, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like parsley and dill. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in umbels. Its seeds are small and crescent-shaped, typical of the genus Bunium.
