Borbonia teres

Borbonia teres, commonly known as the purple-leafed borbonia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive purple underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping and as a hedge.

Borbonia teres is relatively low maintenance, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun.
