Betonica stricticaulis

Betonica stricticaulis, commonly known as narrow-stem betonica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in alpine or subalpine environments. The plant features upright, slender stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy surface, while its flowers are small, blue to purple blossoms arranged in loose spikes.

Betonica stricticaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
