Begonia dinosauria

Begonia dinosauria, commonly known as the dinosaur begonia, is a striking plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that resemble the foliage of prehistoric plants, giving it its unique name. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow, adding visual interest.

Begonia dinosauria thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental appeal.
