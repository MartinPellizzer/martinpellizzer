Brunia squarrosa

Brunia squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly brunia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Brunia squarrosa prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
