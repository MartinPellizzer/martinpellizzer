Bunium setaceum

Bunium setaceum, commonly known as the slender fennel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, aromatic stem that is often branched and covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and have a strong anise-like scent.

Bunium setaceum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its medicinal and culinary uses.
