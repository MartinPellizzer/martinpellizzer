Biggina argentea

Biggina argentea, commonly known as silverleaf biggina, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Biggina argentea is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
