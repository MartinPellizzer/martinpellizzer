Bletia wrightii

Bletia wrightii, commonly known as Wright's bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale yellow, and arranged in a loose raceme.

Bletia wrightii is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or seasonally flooded areas.
