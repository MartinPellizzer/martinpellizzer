Borago macranthera

Borago macranthera, commonly known as the large-flowered bugloss, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and slightly serrated. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Borago macranthera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
