Baphia ovatoacuminata

Baphia ovatoacuminata, commonly known as the broad-leafed baphia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
