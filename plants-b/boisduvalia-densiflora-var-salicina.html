Boisduvalia densiflora var. salicina

Boisduvalia densiflora var. salicina, commonly known as the Salicina boisduvalia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact form and delicate floral display.
