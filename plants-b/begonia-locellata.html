Begonia locellata

Begonia locellata, commonly known as the coral begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, coral-like flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia locellata is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
