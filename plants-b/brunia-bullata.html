Brunia bullata

Brunia bullata, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are small, opposite, and covered in fine hairs.

Brunia bullata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry, well-drained soils.
