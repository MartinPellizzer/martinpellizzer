Brachyscome radicata var. membranifolia

Brachyscome radicata var. membranifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features small, dandelion-like yellow flowers that are surrounded by white, fluffy bracts. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
