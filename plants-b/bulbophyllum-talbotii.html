Bulbophyllum talbotii

Bulbophyllum talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns, making it a striking ornamental plant.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
