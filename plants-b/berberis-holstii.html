Berberis holstii

Berberis holstii, commonly known as the Mexican barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features thorny stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and seasonal color changes make it a visually striking addition to landscapes.
