Brodiaea multiflora

Brodiaea multiflora, commonly known as the showy brodiaea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to California.

It produces clusters of bright white to pale lavender flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Brodiaea multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
