Bucephalon mexicanum

Bucephalon mexicanum, commonly known as the Mexican helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its unique helmet-shaped flower, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent lip, and it blooms during the spring and summer seasons.

Bucephalon mexicanum is a rare and endangered species, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
