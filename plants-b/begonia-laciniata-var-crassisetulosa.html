Begonia laciniata var. crassisetulosa

Begonia laciniata var. crassisetulosa, commonly known as the broad-toothed begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive and lush appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
