Brosimopsis amplifolia

Brosimopsis amplifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf brosim, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
