Bastardia viscosa var. novogranatensis

Bastardia viscosa var. novogranatensis, commonly known as the sticky bastardia, is a succulent plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
