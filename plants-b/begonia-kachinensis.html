Begonia kachinensis

Begonia kachinensis, commonly known as the Kachin begonia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and clusters of vibrant red flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

Begonia kachinensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
