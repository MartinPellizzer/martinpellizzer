Bulbine semibarbata var. depilata

Bulbine semibarbata var. depilata, commonly known as the smooth bulbine, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically smooth and covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in water retention. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its adaptability to arid conditions makes it a resilient choice for drought-tolerant landscapes.
