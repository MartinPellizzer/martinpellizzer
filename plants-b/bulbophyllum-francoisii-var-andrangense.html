Bulbophyllum francoisii var. andrangense

Bulbophyllum francoisii var. andrangense, commonly known as the Andrangense Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
