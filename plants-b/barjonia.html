Barjonia

Barjonia, commonly known as the barjonia plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, rocky soils. The plant features narrow, aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in umbrella-like clusters. Barjonia species are typically herbaceous, with some varieties growing as annuals or perennials.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
