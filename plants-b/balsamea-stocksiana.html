Balsamea stocksiana

Balsamea stocksiana, commonly known as the Stocks plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Balsamea stocksiana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
