Bassia Birchii

Bassia birchii is a hardy perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient choice for landscaping in dry climates. The plant features compact, rosette-like growth with small, succulent leaves that store moisture efficiently. It produces yellow flowers in the spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape. Due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance, Bassia birchii is increasingly popular among gardeners seeking sustainable and adaptive plant options.
