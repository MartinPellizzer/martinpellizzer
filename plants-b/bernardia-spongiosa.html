Bernardia spongiosa

Bernardia spongiosa, commonly known as the sponge tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spongy bark that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by fleshy, orange-red berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological importance in its native habitat.
