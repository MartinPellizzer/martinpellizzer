Bupleurum scorzonerifolium var. angustissimum

Bupleurum scorzonerifolium var. angustissimum, commonly known as narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescence and aromatic foliage. The plant features slender, angular stems and narrow, lanceolate leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.
