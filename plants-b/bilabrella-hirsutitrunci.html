Bilabrella hirsutitrunci

Bilabrella hirsutitrunci, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed bilabrella, is a unique species within the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive hairy stems and vibrant, tubular flowers that often display shades of purple and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as tropical forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Bilabrella hirsutitrunci is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its striking floral display.
