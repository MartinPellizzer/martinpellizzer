Bromheadia divaricata

Bromheadia divaricata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bromheadia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Bromeliaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean and is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and compact inflorescence. The flowers, which are usually small and clustered, are often overlooked but play a role in attracting pollinators.
