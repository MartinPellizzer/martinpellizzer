Bidens sundaica var. minor

Bidens sundaica var. minor, commonly known as the lesser tickseed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellow-orange flowers arranged in clusters, with slender, hairy stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
