Bupleurum chinense

Bupleurum chinense, commonly known as Chinese thornapple, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and often hollow stems. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers in umbel-like clusters. Its roots are often harvested for medicinal use in traditional Chinese medicine.

Bupleurum chinense thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
