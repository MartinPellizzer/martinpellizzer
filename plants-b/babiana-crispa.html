Babiana crispa

Babiana crispa, commonly known as the golden babiana, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a purple or red base. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

Babiana crispa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
