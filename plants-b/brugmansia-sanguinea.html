Brugmansia sanguinea

Brugmansia sanguinea, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its strong, sweet fragrance. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and dark green color.

Brugmansia sanguinea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
