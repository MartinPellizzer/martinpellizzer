Bonnaya ocymifolia

Bonnaya ocymifolia, commonly known as the slender bonnaya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical berries that are typically green when immature and turn red upon ripening. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and aromatic foliage.
