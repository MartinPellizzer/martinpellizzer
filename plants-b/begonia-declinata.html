Begonia declinata

Begonia declinata, commonly known as the hanging begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia declinata is popular in hanging baskets and as a ground cover due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
