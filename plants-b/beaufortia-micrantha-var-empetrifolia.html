Beaufortia micrantha var. empetrifolia

Beaufortia micrantha var. empetrifolia, commonly known as the silky beaufortia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to dry, sandy soils. It produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
