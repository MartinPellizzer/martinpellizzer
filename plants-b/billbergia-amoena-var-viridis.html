Billbergia amoena var. viridis

Billbergia amoena var. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant green foliage with a distinctive rosette shape, and its leaves are thick and leathery, adapted to retain moisture. The plant produces striking red flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding a dramatic visual contrast. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
