Brassaiopsis palmipes

Brassaiopsis palmipes, commonly known as the African violet palm, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features a short trunk with a dense crown of large, leathery, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic value and adaptability.

Brassaiopsis palmipes thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
