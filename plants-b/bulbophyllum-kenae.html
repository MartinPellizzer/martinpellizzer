Bulbophyllum kenae

Bulbophyllum kenae, commonly known as the Kenya orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in fluctuating environmental conditions.

Bulbophyllum kenae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
