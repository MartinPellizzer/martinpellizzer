Bermudiana glaziovii

Bermudiana glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's bermudiana, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
