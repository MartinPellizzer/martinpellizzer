Bulbophyllum stellatum

Bulbophyllum stellatum, commonly known as the star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. It typically grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant produces numerous small, clustered pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid tropical environments.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
