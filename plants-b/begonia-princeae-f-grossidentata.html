Begonia princeae f. grossidentata

Begonia princeae f. grossidentata, commonly known as the giant begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its size and beauty, it is a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
