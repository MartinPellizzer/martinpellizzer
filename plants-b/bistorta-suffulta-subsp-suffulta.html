Bistorta suffulta subsp. suffulta

Bistorta suffulta subsp.

suffulta, commonly known as the tall bistort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats.

This species is often found in moist meadows, stream sides, and woodland edges, where it thrives in partial shade to full sun.
