Barleria fulvostellata subsp. fulvostellata

Barleria fulvostellata subsp.

fulvostellata, commonly known as the yellow barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color. Its flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes and are typically yellow with a reddish or orange tint.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.
