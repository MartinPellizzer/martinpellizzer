Billbergia polystachya

Billbergia polystachya, commonly known as the showy billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia polystachya is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
