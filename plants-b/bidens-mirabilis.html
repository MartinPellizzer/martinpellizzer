Bidens mirabilis

Bidens mirabilis, commonly known as the marvel bidens, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers with dark centers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Bidens mirabilis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
