Briza maxima var. rubra

Briza maxima var. rubra, commonly known as red feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features tall, slender inflorescences that are a deep red or copper color, giving it a distinctive and showy appearance. The plant grows in clumps, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually a bluish-green hue. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to add texture and color to garden settings.
