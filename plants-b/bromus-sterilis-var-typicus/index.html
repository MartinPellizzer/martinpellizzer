Bromus Sterilis Var. Typicus

Annual bromegrass, scientifically known as Bromus sterilis var. typicus, is a common weed found in various habitats across temperate regions. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often seen along roadsides and in agricultural fields. This plant has a short life cycle, completing its growth and reproduction within a single growing season. Its adaptability and rapid growth make it a challenge for land managers and farmers. Despite its invasive tendencies, it plays a role in soil stabilization and provides forage for some wildlife.
