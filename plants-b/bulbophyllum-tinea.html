Bulbophyllum tinea

Bulbophyllum tinea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features linear, narrow leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique pseudobulbs.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
