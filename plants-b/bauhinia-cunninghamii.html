Bauhinia cunninghamii

Bauhinia cunninghamii, commonly known as the orchid tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive hooded structure.

Bauhinia cunninghamii thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
