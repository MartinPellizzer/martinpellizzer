Begonia occhionii

Begonia occhionii, commonly known as the occhionii begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia occhionii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
