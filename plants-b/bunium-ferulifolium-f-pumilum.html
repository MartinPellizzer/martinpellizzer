Bunium ferulifolium f. pumilum

Bunium ferulifolium f. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf fennel, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and finely divided, feathery leaves that resemble those of true fennel. The plant produces small, white to greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the carrot family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
