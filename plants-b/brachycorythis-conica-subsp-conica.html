Brachycorythis conica subsp. conica

Brachycorythis conica subsp.

conica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cycad, is a unique cycad species native to South Africa. It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and a crown of pinnate, dark green leaves.
