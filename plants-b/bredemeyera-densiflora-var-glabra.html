Bredemeyera densiflora var. glabra

Bredemeyera densiflora var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-flowered bredemeyera, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, glandular leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
