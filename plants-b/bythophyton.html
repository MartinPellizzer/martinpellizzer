Bythophyton

Bythophyton, commonly known as the deep-sea sponge.

It is a type of marine sponge that thrives in deep ocean environments. This organism is characterized by its unique structure composed of a porous skeleton made of silica. Bythophyton plays a crucial role in deep-sea ecosystems by providing habitat for various microorganisms.

Its botanical study contributes to understanding biodiversity in extreme marine habitats.
