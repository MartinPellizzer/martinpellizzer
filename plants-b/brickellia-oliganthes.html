Brickellia oliganthes

Brickellia oliganthes, commonly known as the narrowleaf brickellbush, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert and chaparral habitats. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, while its flowers are yellow with purple centers.

Brickellia oliganthes is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in arid environments.
