Beschorneria yuccoides subsp. dekosteriana

Beschorneria yuccoides subsp.

dekosteriana, commonly known as the Mexican yucca or Dekoster's yucca, is a succulent plant native to Mexico. It features a rosette of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white to yellowish margins.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
