Begonia khammouanensis

Begonia khammouanensis, commonly known as the Khammouan begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, specifically Laos.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture, and it often grows in shaded, moist environments. Begonia khammouanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a species contributing to the biodiversity of its native region.
