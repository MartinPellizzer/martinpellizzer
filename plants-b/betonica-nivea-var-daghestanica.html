Betonica nivea var. daghestanica

Betonica nivea var. daghestanica, commonly known as the Caucasian betony, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine white down. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to white flowers arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Betonica nivea var. daghestanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
