Bunchosia hartwegiana f. linearis

Bunchosia hartwegiana f. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bunchosia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in inflorescences. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
