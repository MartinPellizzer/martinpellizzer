Bonamia spinosa

Bonamia spinosa, commonly known as the prickly bonamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and spiny leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a rough, textured surface.

Bonamia spinosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
