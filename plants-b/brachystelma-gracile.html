Brachystelma gracile

Brachystelma gracile, commonly known as the slender stone orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or pale purple in color. The plant has a short, hairy stem and is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in dry, sandy soils. Its flowering period occurs during the cooler months, usually in autumn.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
