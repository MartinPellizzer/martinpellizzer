Brunsvigia falcata

Brunsvigia falcata, commonly known as the fireglow lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems adorned with clusters of bright orange-red flowers that resemble flames, blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically evergreen, providing a lush base for the dramatic floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
