Blumea wightiana

Blumea wightiana, commonly known as the Wight's blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Blumea wightiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
