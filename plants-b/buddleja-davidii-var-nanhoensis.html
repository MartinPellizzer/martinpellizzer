Buddleja davidii var. nanhoensis

Buddleja davidii var. nanhoensis, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, upright stems and is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range from white to purple. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is highly ornamental and attracts a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees.

This variety is often cultivated for its showy blooms and adaptability to different soil types.
