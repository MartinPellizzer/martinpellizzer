Bulbophyllum reflexiflorum

Bulbophyllum reflexiflorum, commonly known as the reflexiflorum orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth and dense, often evergreen foliage. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. Its inflorescence is usually racemose, with flowers arranged in a loose cluster.

Bulbophyllum reflexiflorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of growing conditions.
