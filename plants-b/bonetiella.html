Bonetiella

Bonetiella, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Bonetiella prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
