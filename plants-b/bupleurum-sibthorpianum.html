Bupleurum sibthorpianum

Bupleurum sibthorpianum, commonly known as the Kashmir bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.

Bupleurum sibthorpianum is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
