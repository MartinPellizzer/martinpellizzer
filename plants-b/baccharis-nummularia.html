Baccharis nummularia

Baccharis nummularia, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a low-growing shrub native to South America.

It features small, silver-gray, rounded leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Baccharis nummularia is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
