Bulbostylis heterostachya

Bulbostylis heterostachya, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its flowers are usually inconspicuous, with small, pale-colored florets that are adapted for wind pollination.

Bulbostylis heterostachya is often found in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
