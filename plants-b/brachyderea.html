Brachyderea

Brachyderea, commonly known as the African orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to southern Africa and are characterized by their delicate, often fragrant flowers. Brachyderea species typically have narrow, grass-like leaves and produce small, clustered blooms in shades of white, yellow, and green. They prefer warm, humid environments and are often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to their ornamental value, some species are cultivated as houseplants or in botanical gardens.
