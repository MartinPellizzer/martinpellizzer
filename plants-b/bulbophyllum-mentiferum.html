Bulbophyllum mentiferum

Bulbophyllum mentiferum, commonly known as the Mentiferum orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
