Begonia hijauvenia

Begonia hijauvenia, commonly known as the green velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia hijauvenia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
