Bidens arborea

Bidens arborea, commonly known as the tree ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in wetlands and along riverbanks. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent white markings, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Bidens arborea is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
