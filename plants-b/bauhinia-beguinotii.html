Bauhinia beguinotii

Bauhinia beguinotii, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Bauhinia beguinotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
