Bunchosia linearifolia subsp. linearifolia

Bunchosia linearifolia subsp.

linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaf bunchosia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Paraguay. It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often arranged in a dense, compact manner.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
