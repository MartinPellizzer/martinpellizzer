Billbergia rufa, commonly known as the red billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, red-tipped leaves that form a rosette shape. Billbergia rufa is also known for its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
