Bignonia thyrsoidea

Bignonia thyrsoidea, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers with a distinctive orange throat. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bignonia thyrsoidea is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
