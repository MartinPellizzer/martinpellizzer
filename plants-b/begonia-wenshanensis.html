Begonia wenshanensis

Begonia wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan begonia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The flowers typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white, and they are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its aesthetic appeal.
