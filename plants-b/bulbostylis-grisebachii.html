Bulbostylis grisebachii

Bulbostylis grisebachii, commonly known as the gray-stemmed bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters at the base of the plant.
