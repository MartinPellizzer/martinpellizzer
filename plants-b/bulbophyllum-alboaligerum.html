Bulbophyllum alboaligerum

Bulbophyllum alboaligerum, commonly known as the white-streaked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to retain moisture.

Bulbophyllum alboaligerum is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
