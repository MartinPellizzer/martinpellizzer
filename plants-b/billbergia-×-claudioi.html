Billbergia × claudioi

Billbergia × claudioi, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia nana and Billbergia zebrina.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant, colorful flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia × claudioi is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
