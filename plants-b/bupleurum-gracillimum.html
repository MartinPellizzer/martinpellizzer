Bupleurum gracillimum

Bupleurum gracillimum, commonly known as slender valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Korea.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream banks. The plant features slender, branching stems with small, opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in small clusters at the ends of the stems.

Bupleurum gracillimum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
