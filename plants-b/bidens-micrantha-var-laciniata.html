Bidens micrantha var. laciniata

Bidens micrantha var. laciniata, commonly known as the cutleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features finely dissected, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer to early fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
