Bauhinia tianlinensis

Bauhinia tianlinensis, commonly known as the Tianlin bauhinia, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. Bauhinia tianlinensis is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
