Berlinia densiflora

Berlinia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Berlinia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, hard pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
