Barkhausia taraxacifolia var. multicaulis

Barkhausia taraxacifolia var. multicaulis, commonly known as the clustered barkhausia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
