Bunchosia Nitida

Bunchosia nitida is a small evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of South America. It is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in saline and sandy soils. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine white wax. Its delicate white flowers bloom in clusters and attract various pollinators. Bunchosia nitida is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
