Besleria campanulata

Besleria campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped gesneriad, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light and well-draining soil.

Besleria campanulata is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care when provided with the right conditions.
