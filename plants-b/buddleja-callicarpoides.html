Buddleja callicarpoides

Buddleja callicarpoides, commonly known as the African butterfly bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Buddleja callicarpoides is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
