Blepharodon laurifolium

Blepharodon laurifolium, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its blooming season typically occurs during the summer months, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
