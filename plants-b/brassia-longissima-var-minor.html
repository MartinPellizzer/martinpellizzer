Brassia longissima var. minor

Brassia longissima var. minor, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a compact hybrid orchid that exhibits a striking combination of vibrant colors and intricate floral structures.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems that produce clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. Its foliage consists of narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant due to its specific care requirements.
