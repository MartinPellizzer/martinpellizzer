Bornmuellera × petri

Bornmuellera × petri, commonly known as the hybrid myrtle, is a flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Bornmuellera serrata and B. quadrifida, both native to Australia. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed in the wild.
