Berinia abyssinica

Berinia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in wet or moist environments. The plant typically grows in marshy areas and along water bodies, thriving in humid conditions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.
