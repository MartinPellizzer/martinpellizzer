Brunfelsia ramosissima var. confertiflora

Brunfelsia ramosissima var. confertiflora, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stem and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet, blooming primarily in the evening. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
