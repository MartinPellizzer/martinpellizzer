Bulbophyllum jayaweerae

Bulbophyllum jayaweerae, commonly known as Jayaweera's orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with reddish markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
