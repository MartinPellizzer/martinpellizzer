Behenantha Uniflora

Behenantha uniflora is a rare and fascinating plant native to the arid regions of India. It is known for its unique morphology and medicinal properties that have been recognized for centuries. The plant features a distinctive single flower that blooms in a striking shade of yellow. Its scientific name reflects its singular floral characteristic and its importance in traditional medicine. Despite its ecological significance, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss and overharvesting.
