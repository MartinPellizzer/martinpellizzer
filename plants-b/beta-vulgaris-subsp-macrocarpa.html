Beta vulgaris subsp. macrocarpa

Beta vulgaris subsp.

macrocarpa, commonly known as莙菜 or leaf beet, is a cultivated variant of the beet plant. It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves that are typically harvested for consumption.

The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are rich in nutrients such as vitamins A and C, as well as minerals like iron and potassium.

It is often grown as a leafy green vegetable in temperate climates.
