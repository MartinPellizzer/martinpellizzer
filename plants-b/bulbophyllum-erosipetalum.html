Bulbophyllum erosipetalum

Bulbophyllum erosipetalum, commonly known as the pink-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, producing a rosette of fleshy, elliptic leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of elongated, racemose spikes bearing fragrant, pinkish-white flowers with distinctive, spurred petals. Its flowers are notable for their vibrant color and intricate structure, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires careful attention to humidity and light conditions for optimal growth.
