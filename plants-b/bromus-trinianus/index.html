Bromus Trinianus

Bromus trinianus is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is commonly known as the red grass or trinian grass. This species is often found in grassland and savanna habitats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale to reddish flowers. It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is sometimes used for forage.
