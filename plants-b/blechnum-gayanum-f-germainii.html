Blechnum gayanum f. germainii

Blechnum gayanum f. germainii, commonly known as the Germain's sword fern, is a fern species belonging to the Blechnaceae family.

It is characterized by its leathery, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a distinctive, serrated margin. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Sri Lanka. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rock faces or in dense forest undergrowth.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental fronds make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
