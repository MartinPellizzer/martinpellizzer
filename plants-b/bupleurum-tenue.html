Bupleurum tenue

Bupleurum tenue, commonly known as slender valerian, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features slender, branching stems that are often hairy, and its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Bupleurum tenue is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
