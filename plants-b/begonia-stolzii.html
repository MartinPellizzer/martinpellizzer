Begonia stolzii

Begonia stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii Begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia stolzii is a perennial shrub that can grow up to 1 meter in height, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
