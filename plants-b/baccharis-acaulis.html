Baccharis acaulis

Baccharis acaulis, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its aromatic leaves and drought tolerance make it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.
