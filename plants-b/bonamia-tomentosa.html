Bonamia tomentosa

Bonamia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly bonamia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Bonamia tomentosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique texture and foliage.
