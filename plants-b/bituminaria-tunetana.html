Bituminaria tunetana

Bituminaria tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian bituminous plant, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. Its seeds are encased in a hard, bituminous coating, which aids in seed dispersal and survival in harsh conditions.

Bituminaria tunetana plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
