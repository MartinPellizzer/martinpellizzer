Bikinia media

Bikinia media, commonly known as the narrow-leaved fumitory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, delicate leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its scientific name reflects its distinctive leaf morphology and its association with the fumitory genus.
