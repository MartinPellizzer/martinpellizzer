Bupleurum lancifolium var. heterophyllum

Bupleurum lancifolium var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments and has slender, often branched stems. The leaves are lanceolate, with a sharp tip, and are usually arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, often appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

This plant is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
