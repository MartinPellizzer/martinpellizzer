Buddleja davidii var. veitchiana

Buddleja davidii var. veitchiana, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pale blue to purple, attracting numerous pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
