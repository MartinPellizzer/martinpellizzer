Bletia Villosa

Bletia villosa is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. Known for its unique, velvety leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers, it stands out among other orchid species. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires careful attention to moisture and light levels. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, Bletia villosa remains relatively unknown to the general public.
