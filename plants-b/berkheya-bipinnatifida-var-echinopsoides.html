Berkheya bipinnatifida var. echinopsoides

Berkheya bipinnatifida var. echinopsoides, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, blue-green leaves with a woolly texture. The plant produces tall, spindly stems topped with a dense cluster of yellow-orange flowers that resemble a crown. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
