Bulbophyllum carassense

Bulbophyllum carassense, commonly known as the Carassense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The flowers, which are often in shades of white and yellow, bloom during the rainy season.
