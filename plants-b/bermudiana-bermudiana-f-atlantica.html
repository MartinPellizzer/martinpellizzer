Bermudiana bermudiana f. atlantica

Bermudiana bermudiana f. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic Bermuda grass, is a variant of the Bermuda grass species native to the Atlantic coast.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green foliage and its ability to thrive in coastal environments with high salinity. This form exhibits a dense, mat-like growth habit, making it suitable for erosion control and ornamental use. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, though it can tolerate moderate shade.

The plant is also noted for its resilience to salt spray and wind, making it a valuable species for coastal landscaping.
