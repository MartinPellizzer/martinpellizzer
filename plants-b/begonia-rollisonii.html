Begonia rollisonii

Begonia rollisonii, commonly known as the Rollison's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that display a range of colors, including deep red, purple, and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated as a rare and exotic houseplant.
