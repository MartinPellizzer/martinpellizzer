Betonica annua var. hirsuta

Betonica annua var. hirsuta, commonly known as hairy betonica, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial that grows up to 60 cm tall, characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Betonica annua var. hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
