Begonia riedelii var. latifolia

Begonia riedelii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 15 cm in diameter. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its aesthetic appeal and durability.
