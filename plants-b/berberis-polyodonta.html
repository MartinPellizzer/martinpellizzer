Berberis polyodonta

Berberis polyodonta, commonly known as the many-fruited barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas and parts of China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often tart. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in gardens.
