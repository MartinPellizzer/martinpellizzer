Bihai elongata

Bihai elongata, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and clusters of long, narrow leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by red or orange fruits containing seeds. Bihai elongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil.

It is also known for its ability to tolerate a variety of growing conditions, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
