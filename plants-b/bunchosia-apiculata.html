Bunchosia apiculata

Bunchosia apiculata, commonly known as the silky bush or silky mallow, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in coastal areas. The plant has soft, hairy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Bunchosia apiculata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and resilience.
