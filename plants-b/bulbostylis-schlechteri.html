Bulbostylis schlechteri

Bulbostylis schlechteri, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a member of the Poaceae family, native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
