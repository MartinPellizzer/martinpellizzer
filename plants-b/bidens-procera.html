Bidens procera

Bidens procera, commonly known as tall ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stem and large, showy yellow flowers with distinctive white markings. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and often have a serrated edge.

Bidens procera is known for its ability to thrive in wetland environments and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
