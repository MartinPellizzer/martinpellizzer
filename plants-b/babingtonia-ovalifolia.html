Babingtonia ovalifolia

Babingtonia ovalifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf babingtonia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its narrow, ovate leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy soils and salt-laden environments. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and bloom during the warmer months.

Babingtonia ovalifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
