Benthamia exilis var. tenuissima

Benthamia exilis var. tenuissima, commonly known as the slender benthamia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is a perennial herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
