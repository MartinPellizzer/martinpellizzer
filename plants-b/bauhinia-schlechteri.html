Bauhinia schlechteri

Bauhinia schlechteri, commonly known as the velvetwood or African wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately compound leaves that are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in spring. Bauhinia schlechteri thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and attractive flowers make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
