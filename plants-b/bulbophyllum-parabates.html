Bulbophyllum parabates

Bulbophyllum parabates, commonly known as the orchid with the peculiar leaf structure, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a pale yellow to greenish coloration.

Bulbophyllum parabates is often grown as a terrestrial orchid in shaded, humid environments.
