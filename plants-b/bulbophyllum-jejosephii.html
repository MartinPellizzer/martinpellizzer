Bulbophyllum jejosephii

Bulbophyllum jejosephii, commonly known as the Joseph's Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a variety of colors, ranging from white to pink and yellow.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in some regions.
