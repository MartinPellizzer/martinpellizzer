Bulbophyllum bavonis

Bulbophyllum bavonis, commonly known as the Bavarian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern around the base.

Bulbophyllum bavonis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
