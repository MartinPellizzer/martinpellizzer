Barkhausia glandulosa

Barkhausia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular barkhausia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
