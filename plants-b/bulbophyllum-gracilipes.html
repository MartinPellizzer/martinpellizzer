Bulbophyllum gracilipes

Bulbophyllum gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with long, slender petals. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its roots form a dense network that helps it absorb moisture and nutrients from its substrate.

Bulbophyllum gracilipes is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
