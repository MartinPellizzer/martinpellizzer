Bowdichia kuhlmannii

Bowdichia kuhlmannii, commonly known as the Brazilian burr bean tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive fruit pods. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by woody, spiny pods containing seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of the Atlantic Forest biome.

Bowdichia kuhlmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
