Bromelia sceptrum

Bromelia sceptrum, commonly known as the sword bromeliad, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces showy, colorful bracts that often resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bromelia sceptrum is popular as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
