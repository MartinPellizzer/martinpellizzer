Beyeria tristigma

Beyeria tristigma, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Beyeria tristigma is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty.
