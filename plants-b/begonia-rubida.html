Begonia rubida

Begonia rubida, commonly known as the red begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Begonia rubida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to add vibrant color to indoor and outdoor spaces.
