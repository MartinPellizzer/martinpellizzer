Brickellia wislizeni var. lanceolata

Brickellia wislizeni var. lanceolata, commonly known as narrowleaf brickellbush, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
