Beloakon tomentosum

Beloakon tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly beloakon, is a unique plant with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered.

Beloakon tomentosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in specific ecological niches.
