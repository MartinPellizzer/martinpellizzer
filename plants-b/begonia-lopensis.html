Begonia lopensis

Begonia lopensis, commonly known as the Lopes' begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia lopensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
