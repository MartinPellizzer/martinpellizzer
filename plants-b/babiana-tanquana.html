Babiana tanquana

Babiana tanquana, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Babiana tanquana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
