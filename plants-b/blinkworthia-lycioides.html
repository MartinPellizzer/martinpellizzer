Blinkworthia lycioides

Blinkworthia lycioides, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, spiny leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Blinkworthia lycioides is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
