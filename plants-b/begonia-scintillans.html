Begonia scintillans

Begonia scintillans, commonly known as the sparkling begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a vibrant mix of green, red, and silver hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
