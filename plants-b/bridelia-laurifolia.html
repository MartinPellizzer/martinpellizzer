Bridelia laurifolia

Bridelia laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf bridelia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Bridelia laurifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
