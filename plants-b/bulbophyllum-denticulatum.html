Bulbophyllum denticulatum

Bulbophyllum denticulatum, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces intricate, fragrant blooms that typically feature a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable environment.
