Begonia × lindquistii

Begonia × lindquistii, commonly known as the Lindquist begonia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia × tuberhybrida.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden beds and container gardening.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, Begonia × lindquistii is widely appreciated in horticulture.
