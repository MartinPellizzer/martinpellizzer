Butea Africana

Butea africana is a remarkable plant native to parts of Africa and Asia known for its vibrant orange flowers and medicinal properties. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, making it a resilient species adapted to harsh environmental conditions. The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antifungal benefits. Its seeds are also valued for their high oil content and industrial applications. Butea africana stands out as both a ecological and cultural symbol in the regions where it grows.
