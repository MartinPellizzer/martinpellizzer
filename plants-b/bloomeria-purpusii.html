Bloomeria purpusii

Bloomeria purpusii, commonly known as the purple bloomery, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
