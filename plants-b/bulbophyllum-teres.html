Bulbophyllum teres

Bulbophyllum teres, commonly known as the round-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

The orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
