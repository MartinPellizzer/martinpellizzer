Babingtonia delicata

Babingtonia delicata, commonly known as the silky babingtonia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-hairy leaves that give it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Babingtonia delicata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
