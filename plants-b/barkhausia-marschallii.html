Barkhausia marschallii

Barkhausia marschallii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bark is thick and rugged, providing structural support and protection against environmental stressors.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
