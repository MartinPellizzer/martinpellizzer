Baynesia

Baynesia, commonly known as the beach hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found growing along shorelines and in tropical climates. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, and it produces edible fruits that are used in local cuisines.

Baynesia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
