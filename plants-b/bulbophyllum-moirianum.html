Bulbophyllum moirianum

Bulbophyllum moirianum, commonly known as the Moirian's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
