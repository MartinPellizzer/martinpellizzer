Balanostreblus ilicifolia

Balanostreblus ilicifolia, commonly known as the prickly pear cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces edible fruits known as tunas, which are rich in nutrients. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its water-storing stems.

The plant has been used traditionally for food, medicine, and ornamental purposes.
