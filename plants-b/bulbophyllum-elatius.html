Bulbophyllum elatius

Bulbophyllum elatius, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant produces large, intricate blooms with a mix of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum elatius is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
