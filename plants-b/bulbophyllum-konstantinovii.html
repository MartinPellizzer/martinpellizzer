Bulbophyllum konstantinovii

Bulbophyllum konstantinovii, commonly known as the Konstantinovii orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, which bloom during the wet season. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive periods of drought.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
