Boehmeria macrophylla var. dongtousensis

Boehmeria macrophylla var. dongtousensis, commonly known as the Dongtoushan giant mulberry, is a variant of the giant mulberry plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant is a member of the荨麻科 (Urticaceae) family and is known for its robust growth habit. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its fibrous stems have been historically used in traditional crafts and papermaking.
