Breweria patens

Breweria patens, commonly known as the common brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Breweria patens is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
