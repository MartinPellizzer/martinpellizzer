Blossfeldia liliputana var. pedicellata

Blossfeldia liliputana var. pedicellata, commonly known as the miniature pincushion cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, cylindrical stems that are often covered in short, white spines. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its delicate appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
