Byttneria filipes var. puberula

Byttneria filipes var. puberula, commonly known as the silky or hairy sycamore, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its hairy foliage and unique morphology make it an interesting subject for botanical study and horticultural interest.
