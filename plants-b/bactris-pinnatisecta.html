Bactris pinnatisecta

Bactris pinnatisecta, commonly known as the pink-leaf palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Bactris pinnatisecta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
