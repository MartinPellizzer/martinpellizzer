Britoa eriantha

Britoa eriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color, blooming throughout the warmer months. Britoa eriantha thrives in humid, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils near rivers or in open woodlands.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
