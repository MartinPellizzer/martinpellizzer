Bulbophyllum nitens var. intermedium

Bulbophyllum nitens var. intermedium, commonly known as the Intermediate Bulbophyllum, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, sword-shaped pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the wet season. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
