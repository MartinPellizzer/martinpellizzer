Bupleurum lancifolium var. lancifolium

Bupleurum lancifolium var. lancifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to regions of Asia, including China and Korea.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous areas. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, usually appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

The species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
