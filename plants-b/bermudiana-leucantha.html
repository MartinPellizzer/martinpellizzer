Bermudiana leucantha

Bermudiana leucantha, commonly known as the Bermuda buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Bermuda.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-like structure. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, adapting well to saline conditions. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
