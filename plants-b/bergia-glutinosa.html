Bergia glutinosa

Bergia glutinosa, commonly known as sticky bergia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats. The plant features dense, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. Its small, white or pink flowers bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Bergia glutinosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
