Belvisia platyrhynchos

Belvisia platyrhynchos, commonly known as the broad-nosed bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. Its stems are erect and branched, supporting the clusters of flowers.

Belvisia platyrhynchos is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
