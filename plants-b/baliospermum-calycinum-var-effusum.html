Baliospermum calycinum var. effusum

Baliospermum calycinum var. effusum, commonly known as the softleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and often aromatic foliage. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture. Its flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and are arranged in compact clusters.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
