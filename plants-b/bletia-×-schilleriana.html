Bletia × schilleriana

Bletia × schilleriana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Bletia ciliata and Bletia parviflora.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Jamaica and the Greater Antilles. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a sympodial growth habit. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
