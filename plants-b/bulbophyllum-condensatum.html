Bulbophyllum condensatum

Bulbophyllum condensatum, commonly known as the condensed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical and subtropical environments. Its flowers typically bloom in shades of white, yellow, and green, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with intricate patterns.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
