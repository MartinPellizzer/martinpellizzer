Bonellia pauciflora

Bonellia pauciflora, commonly known as the slender bonellia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, while its flowers are pale yellow with four petals.

Bonellia pauciflora is a perennial herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.
