Bartonia Paniculata

Bartonia paniculata is a unique flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It is known for its striking white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in wetland environments and is often found near swamps and marshes. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its hardy nature and adaptability to moist soils. This species plays an important role in local ecosystems and is a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike.
