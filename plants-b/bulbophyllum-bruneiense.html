Bulbophyllum bruneiense

Bulbophyllum bruneiense, commonly known as the Brunei torch orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often brightly colored inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually clustered in dense spikes and can display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
