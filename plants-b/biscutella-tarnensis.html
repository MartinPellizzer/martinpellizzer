Biscutella tarnensis

Biscutella tarnensis, commonly known as the Tarn biscuitflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. Biscutella tarnensis is known for its delicate appearance and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its ecological and ornamental value.
