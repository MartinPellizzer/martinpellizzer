Bulbophyllum nesiotes

Bulbophyllum nesiotes, commonly known as the Nesiotes orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that are typically yellow or green with contrasting patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its humid, shaded forest habitat.

Bulbophyllum nesiotes is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
