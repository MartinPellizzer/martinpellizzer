Bihai longa

Bihai longa, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with prominent yellow stamens. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Bihai longa thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
