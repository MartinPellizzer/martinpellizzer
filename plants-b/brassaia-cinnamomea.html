Brassaia cinnamomea

Brassaia cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves and bark. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appearance and its valuable aromatic compounds.
