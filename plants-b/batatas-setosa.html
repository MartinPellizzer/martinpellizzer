Batatas setosa

Batatas setosa, commonly known as the slender sweet potato, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its tuberous roots, which are edible, are often used in various culinary preparations.
