Brodiaea Berteroi

Brodiaea berteroi is a striking member of the amaryllid family native to the western United States. This perennial plant is known for its showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Brodiaea berteroi plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing biodiversity in its native habitat.
