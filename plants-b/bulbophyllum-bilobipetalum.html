Bulbophyllum bilobipetalum

Bulbophyllum bilobipetalum, commonly known as the two-lobed petal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colorful flowers. The plant produces intricate, bilaterally symmetrical blooms with two distinct petals that are often marked with contrasting patterns. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette, adapted for storing moisture in its natural habitat.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
