Buphthalmum diversifolium

Buphthalmum diversifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has alternate, lobed leaves that are often broader at the base, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Buphthalmum diversifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
