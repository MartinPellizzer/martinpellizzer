Begonia subacutoalata

Begonia subacutoalata, commonly known as the serrated-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
