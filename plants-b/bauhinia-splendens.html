Bauhinia splendens

Bauhinia splendens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically followed by flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Bauhinia splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
