Brassica rapa convar. japonica

Brassica rapa convar. japonica, commonly known as Chinese kale or kale, is a biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves that are often curled or crinkled, and it produces yellow flowers in the second year of growth. The plant is native to East Asia and is cultivated for its edible leaves and stems. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Brassica rapa convar. japonica is a hardy vegetable that is rich in nutrients and commonly used in Asian cuisines.
