Bulbophyllum unifoliatum subsp. infracarinatum

Bulbophyllum unifoliatum subsp.

infracarinatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia.

The plant is characterized by its slender, pseudo-bulb-like stems and narrow, elongated leaves. Its flowers are typically small and fragrant, with a distinctive coloration that varies among individuals.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or in the understory of forests.
