Bulbophyllum saurocephalum subsp. oncoglossum

Bulbophyllum saurocephalum subsp.

oncoglossum, commonly known as the horned helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia, where it grows in shaded, humid forest environments.

The plant is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive, elongated lip that resembles a horn.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking appearance.
