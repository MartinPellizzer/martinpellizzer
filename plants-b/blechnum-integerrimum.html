Blechnum integerrimum

Blechnum integerrimum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a fern species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that resemble a fern with a central stem and radiating leaflets. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its leaves are typically dark green with a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance.

Blechnum integerrimum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
