Barleria burchelliana

Barleria burchelliana, commonly known as the red-flowered barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Barleria burchelliana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
