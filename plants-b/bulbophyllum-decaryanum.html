Bulbophyllum decaryanum

Bulbophyllum decaryanum, commonly known as the Decary's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients, and its clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts.
