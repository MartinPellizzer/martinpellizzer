Boltonia pekinensis

Boltonia pekinensis, commonly known as the Peking Boltonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features tall, branching stems that support large, showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are hairy and serrated, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Boltonia pekinensis is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
