Breynia formosana

Breynia formosana, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often covering the entire plant. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types.
