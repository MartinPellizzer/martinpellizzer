Barleria smithii

Barleria smithii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Barleria smithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
