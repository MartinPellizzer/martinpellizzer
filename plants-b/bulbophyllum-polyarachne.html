Bulbophyllum polyarachne

Bulbophyllum polyarachne, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure, which resembles a spider's web, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum polyarachne is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
