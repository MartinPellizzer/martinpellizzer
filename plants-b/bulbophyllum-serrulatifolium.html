Bulbophyllum serrulatifolium

Bulbophyllum serrulatifolium, commonly known as the serrated leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, serrated leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with indirect sunlight.

Its unique leaf structure and delicate blooms make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
