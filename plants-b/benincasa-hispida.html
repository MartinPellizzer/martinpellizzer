Benincasa hispida

Benincasa hispida, commonly known as the Chinese horned melon, is a tropical vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, spiny stems and large, lobed leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that give way to large, green, spiky fruits. The fruit's exterior is covered in soft, white spines, and its interior contains a sweet, aromatic pulp with numerous black seeds.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is cultivated for both its ornamental value and edible fruit.
