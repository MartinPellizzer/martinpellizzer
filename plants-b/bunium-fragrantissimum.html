Bunium fragrantissimum

Bunium fragrantissimum, commonly known as the fragrant bugloss, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, producing a distinctive, spicy aroma.
