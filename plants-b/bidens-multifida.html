Bidens multifida

Bidens multifida, commonly known as the crown-of-thorns or common ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is a hardy species that thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas.

Bidens multifida is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it both a weed and a potential invasive species in certain regions.
