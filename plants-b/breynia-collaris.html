Breynia collaris

Breynia collaris, commonly known as the coral tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in well-drained soils.

Breynia collaris is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
