Begonia guatemalensis

Begonia guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan begonia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a trailing growth habit. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Begonia guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
