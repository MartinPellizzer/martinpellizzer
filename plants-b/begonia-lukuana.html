Begonia lukuana

Begonia lukuana, commonly known as the Lukuana begonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant colors such as red, purple, and green. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
