Bupleurum falvatum var. stenophyllum

Bupleurum falvatum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Chinese angelica, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its aromatic roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or mountainous regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its chemical composition and medicinal applications.
