Baltimora monocephala

Baltimora monocephala, commonly known as the single-headed baltimora, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its singular, prominent head of flowers that bloom in a compact, spherical shape. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color.

Due to its distinctive appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
