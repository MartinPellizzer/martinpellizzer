Bubon capense

Bubon capense, commonly known as the Cape bulbous plant, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its bulbous root system and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal climates with high salinity.

Bubon capense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging environments.
