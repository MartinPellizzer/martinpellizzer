Brachystelma simplex subsp. banforae

Brachystelma simplex subsp.

banforae, commonly known as the Banfora starling cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, branching stems that are typically covered in short, white to pale green hairs.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
