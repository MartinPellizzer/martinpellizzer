Blumea musra

Blumea musra, commonly known as white chuchundra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in clusters, producing a subtle fragrance.

Blumea musra is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
