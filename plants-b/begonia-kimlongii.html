Begonia kimlongii

Begonia kimlongii, commonly known as the Kimlong begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
