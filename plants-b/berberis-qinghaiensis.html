Berberis qinghaiensis

Berberis qinghaiensis, commonly known as the Qinghai barberry, is a shrub native to the Qinghai region of China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces orange-red berries that are edible but typically not consumed by humans. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to cold, arid conditions.

Berberis qinghaiensis is often used in traditional medicine and landscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
