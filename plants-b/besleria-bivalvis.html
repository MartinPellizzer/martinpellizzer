Besleria bivalvis

Besleria bivalvis, commonly known as the two-lobed gesneriad, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in various colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil to flourish.
