Bistorta yunnanensis

Bistorta yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan bistorta, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf structure and flowering stems. The plant features rhizomatous growth, which allows it to spread and form dense colonies in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a reddish hue, and it produces small, clustered flowers in early summer.

Bistorta yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
