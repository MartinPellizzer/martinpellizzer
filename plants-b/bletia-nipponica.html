Bletia nipponica

Bletia nipponica, commonly known as the Japanese bletia, is a flowering plant native to Japan and southern Korea.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are typically pale yellow to greenish-white with prominent purple markings.

Bletia nipponica thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
