Bauhinia Ferruginea

Bauhinia ferruginea is a striking flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia and northern Australia. Known for its unique, fern-like leaves and vibrant pink or red blooms, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden. This species thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts and landscapers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions to flourish.
