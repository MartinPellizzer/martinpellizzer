Bambusa hookeri

Bambusa hookeri, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a species of clumping bamboo native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-green culms and dense, fan-shaped foliage. The plant is highly valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

Bambusa hookeri is relatively low-maintenance and can thrive in a variety of climates with proper care.
