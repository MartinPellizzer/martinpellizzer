Begonia lemahoutii

Begonia lemahoutii, commonly known as the Mahout's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often red or pink flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its foliage is often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
