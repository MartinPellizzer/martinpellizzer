Bulbophyllum epapillosum

Bulbophyllum epapillosum, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, leaf-like petals that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often appearing in clusters.

Bulbophyllum epapillosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ease of cultivation.
