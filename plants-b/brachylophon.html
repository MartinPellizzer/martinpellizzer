Brachylophon

Brachylophon, commonly known as the short-horned grasshopper, is a type of insect rather than a plant, so it does not have botanical aspects.

However, it is often found in grassland habitats where it feeds on various plants. Its presence can influence the ecological balance of these environments. The plant life in such areas typically includes grasses and forbs.

Understanding the interactions between Brachylophon and its surrounding flora is important for ecological studies.
