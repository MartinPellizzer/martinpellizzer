Bufonia brachyphylla var. cecconiana

Bufonia brachyphylla var. cecconiana, commonly known as the Cecconian bufonia, is a rare and unique variant of the bufonia genus.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and distinctive, broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of concern in its native habitat.
