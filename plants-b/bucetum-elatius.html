Bucetum elatius

Bucetum elatius, commonly known as the tall meadow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, clustered inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Bucetum elatius is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
