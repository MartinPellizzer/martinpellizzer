Brachystelma gracillimum

Brachystelma gracillimum, commonly known as the slender stone orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, hairless stems that often lie close to the ground, with small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
