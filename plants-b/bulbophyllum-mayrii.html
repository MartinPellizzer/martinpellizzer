Bulbophyllum mayrii

Bulbophyllum mayrii, commonly known as the Mayri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water and nutrients.

It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
