Bromopsis kopetdagensis

Bromopsis kopetdagensis, commonly known as the Kopetdag bromus, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense foliage. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
