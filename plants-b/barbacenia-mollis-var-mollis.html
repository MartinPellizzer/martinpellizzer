Barbacenia mollis var. mollis

Barbacenia mollis var. mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Central and South America, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The leaves are typically dark green with a slight pubescence, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often followed by winged fruits.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
