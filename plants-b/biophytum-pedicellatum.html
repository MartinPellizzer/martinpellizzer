Biophytum pedicellatum

Biophytum pedicellatum, commonly known as the "Beaded Window" or "Percy Jones plant," is a tropical flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bead-like inflorescences that resemble small, clustered flowers. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Biophytum pedicellatum is often grown as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
