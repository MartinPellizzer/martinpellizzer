Begonia lazat

Begonia lazat, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
