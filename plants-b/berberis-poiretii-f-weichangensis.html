Berberis poiretii f. weichangensis

Berberis poiretii f. weichangensis, commonly known as the Weichang barberry, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features thorned stems and small, spiny leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
