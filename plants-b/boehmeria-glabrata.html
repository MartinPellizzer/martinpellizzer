Boehmeria glabrata

Boehmeria glabrata, commonly known as silky fiber plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its soft, silken fibers that are harvested from its stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside.
