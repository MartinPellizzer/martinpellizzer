Bulbophyllum rhizomatosum

Bulbophyllum rhizomatosum, commonly known as the rhizomatous orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
