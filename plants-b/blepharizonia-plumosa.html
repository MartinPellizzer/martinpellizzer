Blepharizonia plumosa

Blepharizonia plumosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf fern, is a unique member of the Blechnaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance.
