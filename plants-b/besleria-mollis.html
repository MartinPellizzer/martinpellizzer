Besleria mollis

Besleria mollis, commonly known as the silver spider lily, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped white flowers with a deep red or purple throat. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or grayish pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Besleria mollis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
