Begonia mazae f. nigricans

Begonia mazae f. nigricans, commonly known as the black-leaved begonia, is a unique variant of the Begonia mazae species.

It is characterized by its striking dark green to almost black foliage, which sets it apart from other begonia varieties. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white or pale pink, adding a subtle contrast to its dark leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant foliage.

This ornamental plant is popular among gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
