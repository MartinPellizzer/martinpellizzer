Bulbophyllum microglossum

Bulbophyllum microglossum, commonly known as the miniature lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, often growing in a compact rosette formation.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
