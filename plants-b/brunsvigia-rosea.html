Brunsvigia rosea

Brunsvigia rosea, commonly known as the firecracker lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, red-orange flowers that resemble fireworks, hence its common name. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are evergreen during the growing season. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Brunsvigia rosea prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
