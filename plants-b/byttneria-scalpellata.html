Byttneria scalpellata

Byttneria scalpellata, commonly known as the African mallow, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and riverine forests.
