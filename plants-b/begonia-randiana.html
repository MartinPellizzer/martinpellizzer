Begonia randiana

Begonia randiana, commonly known as the golden begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia randiana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
