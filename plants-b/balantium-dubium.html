Balantium dubium

Balantium dubium, commonly known as the uncertain bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibacillaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bladder-like structures that trap small aquatic organisms, aiding in its nutrient acquisition. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and ditches. Its leaves are slender and often arranged in whorls around the stem.

Due to its limited distribution and unclear taxonomy, it remains a subject of ongoing botanical study.
