Berchemia racemosa

Berchemia racemosa, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Berchemia racemosa is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
