Billbergia amoena

Billbergia amoena, commonly known as the pink billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence. Billbergia amoena is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
