Bulbophyllum taichungianum

Bulbophyllum taichungianum, commonly known as the Taichung orchid, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often pale green or white, with a distinctively shaped lip that aids in pollination.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
