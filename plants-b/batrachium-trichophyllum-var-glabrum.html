Batrachium trichophyllum var. glabrum

Batrachium trichophyllum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed marsh orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It features slender, erect stems that are glabrous, or hairless, and bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is typically found in regions with temperate climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.
