Bactris campestris

Bactris campestris, commonly known as the buriti palm, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow-orange fruits. The plant is well adapted to wetland and swampy environments, often growing in areas with high humidity. Its fruit is edible and has been used traditionally for food and medicine.

The buriti palm also plays an important role in the ecosystems of the Amazon and other tropical regions.
