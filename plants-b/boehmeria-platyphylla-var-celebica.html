Boehmeria platyphylla var. celebica

Boehmeria platyphylla var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its fibrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

This species is valued for its strong fibers, which are used in traditional crafts and textiles.
