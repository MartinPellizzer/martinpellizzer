Buddleja nivea var. yunnanensis

Buddleja nivea var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale lavender flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
