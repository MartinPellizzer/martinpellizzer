Begonia odeteiantha

Begonia odeteiantha, commonly known as the Odeteiantha begonia, is a rare and striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
