Bowkeria gerrardiana

Bowkeria gerrardiana, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are yellow and borne in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Bowkeria gerrardiana is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
