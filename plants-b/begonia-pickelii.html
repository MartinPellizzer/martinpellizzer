Begonia pickelii

Begonia pickelii, commonly known as the Pickel's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
