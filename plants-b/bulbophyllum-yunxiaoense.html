Bulbophyllum yunxiaoense

Bulbophyllum yunxiaoense, commonly known as the Yunxiao orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and growth habits. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and green.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
