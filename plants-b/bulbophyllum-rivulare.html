Bulbophyllum rivulare

Bulbophyllum rivulare, commonly known as the river orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a spike. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on tree trunks or branches in humid tropical forests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain.

Bulbophyllum rivulare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
