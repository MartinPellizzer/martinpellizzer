Bulbostylis scorodoniifolia

Bulbostylis scorodoniifolia, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It is often found in swampy areas and can form dense stands, contributing to the ecological diversity of its environment.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and ecologists studying wetland ecosystems.
