Buddleja nitida

Buddleja nitida, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Buddleja nitida is also known for its adaptability and ease of care, making it a popular choice among gardeners.
