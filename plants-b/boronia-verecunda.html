Boronia verecunda

Boronia verecunda, commonly known as the slender boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. Boronia verecunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, aromatic blooms.
