Bauhinia loeseneriana

Bauhinia loeseneriana, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange-pink flowers that appear in clusters during the rainy season. The leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, delicate leaflets, and the plant produces flat, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

The botanical features of Bauhinia loeseneriana make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
