Boussingaultia cordata

Boussingaultia cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf boussingaultia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
