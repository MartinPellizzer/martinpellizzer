Bulbophyllum peperomiifolium

Bulbophyllum peperomiifolium, commonly known as the Peperomia-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem with fleshy, dark green leaves that resemble those of peperomia plants. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
