Beilschmiedia berteroana

Beilschmiedia berteroana, commonly known as the Guatemalan beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elliptical, dark blue berries that are edible. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture and conservation efforts.
