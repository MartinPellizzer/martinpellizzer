Bomarea affinis

Bomarea affinis, commonly known as the silverleaf bomarea, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Andes of South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple. Bomarea affinis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage.
