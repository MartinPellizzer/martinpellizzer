Berkheya kuntzei

Berkheya kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's Berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange ray flowers and dark green, lance-shaped leaves.
