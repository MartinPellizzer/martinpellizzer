Betonica alopecuros subsp. alopecuros

Betonica alopecuros subsp.

alopecuros, commonly known as hedge betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom from late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
