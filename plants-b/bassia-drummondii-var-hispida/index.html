Bassia Drummondii Var. Hispida

Bassia drummondii var. hispida is a hardy perennial plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the western united states. it is known for its compact growth habit and silver-green foliage that reflects the harsh desert environment. the plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a subtle touch of color to the landscape. it is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in poor soils. this resilient plant is a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
