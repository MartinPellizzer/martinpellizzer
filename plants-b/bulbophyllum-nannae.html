Bulbophyllum nannae

Bulbophyllum nannae, commonly known as the Nanna's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum nannae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
