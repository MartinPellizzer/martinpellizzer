Bombax tocantinum

Bombax tocantinum, commonly known as the Tocantins silk cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree is valued for its ornamental appearance and has traditional uses in local communities.
