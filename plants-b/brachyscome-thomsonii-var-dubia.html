Brachyscome thomsonii var. dubia

Brachyscome thomsonii var. dubia, commonly known as the silverleaf brachyscome, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a striking silver or blue hue on the petals. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
