Bredemeyera conferta

Bredemeyera conferta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves and produces showy, violet-colored flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Bredemeyera conferta is known for its ability to bloom continuously under proper conditions.
