Bonnetia bolivarensis

Bonnetia bolivarensis, commonly known as the Bolivian bonnetia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The inflorescence consists of yellow flowers arranged in dense, rounded clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.
