Baeria macrantha var. pauciaristata

Baeria macrantha var. pauciaristata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf baeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy, with small, lance-shaped leaves arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
