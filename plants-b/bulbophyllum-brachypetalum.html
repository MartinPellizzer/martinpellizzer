Bulbophyllum brachypetalum

Bulbophyllum brachypetalum, commonly known as the short-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a unique lip structure that aids in pollination. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum brachypetalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
