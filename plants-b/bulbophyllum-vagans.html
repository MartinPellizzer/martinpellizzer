Bulbophyllum vagans

Bulbophyllum vagans, commonly known as the wandering orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their unique, spiral-like arrangement.

Bulbophyllum vagans is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
