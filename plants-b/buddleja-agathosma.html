Buddleja agathosma

Buddleja agathosma, commonly known as the Cape honeybee bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. These flowers are highly attractive to bees and other pollinators, making it an important plant in supporting local wildlife.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
