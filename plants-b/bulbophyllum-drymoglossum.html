Bulbophyllum drymoglossum

Bulbophyllum drymoglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and long, narrow leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a distinctive, narrow lip.

This epiphytic orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
