Begonia huangii

Begonia huangii, commonly known as the Huang's begonia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Begonia huangii is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
