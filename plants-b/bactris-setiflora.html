Bactris setiflora

Bactris setiflora, commonly known as the hairy palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Paraguay. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, round fruits.
