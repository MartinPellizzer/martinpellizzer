Bauhinia rufa

Bauhinia rufa, commonly known as the red bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Bauhinia rufa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
