Begonia boliviensis var. latitepala

Begonia boliviensis var. latitepala, commonly known as the Bolivian begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a deep, velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners seeking unique foliage plants.
