Begonia sunorchis

Begonia sunorchis, commonly known as the sun orchid begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces showy inflorescences that resemble orchids. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
