Bulbophyllum xyphoglossum

Bulbophyllum xyphoglossum, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrate for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
