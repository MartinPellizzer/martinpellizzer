Bartschella yaquensis

Bartschella yaquensis, commonly known as the Yaquensis cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Peru.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows in rocky soils and dry environments. The plant features a cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure, which helps it store water efficiently. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming during the warmer months.

Bartschella yaquensis is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability.
