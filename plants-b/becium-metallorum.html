Becium metallorum

Becium metallorum, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. Its bark is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Becium metallorum plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
