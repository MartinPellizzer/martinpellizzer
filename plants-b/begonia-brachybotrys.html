Begonia brachybotrys

Begonia brachybotrys, commonly known as the short-stemmed begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from both its vibrant flowers and attractive foliage.
