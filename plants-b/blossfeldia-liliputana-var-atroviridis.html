Blossfeldia liliputana var. atroviridis

Blossfeldia liliputana var. atroviridis, commonly known as the black-veined miniature orchid, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

This variety is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences bearing tiny, greenish-white flowers with dark green veins. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, requiring well-draining, acidic potting media. The plant's unique coloration and miniature size make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments such as greenhouses.
