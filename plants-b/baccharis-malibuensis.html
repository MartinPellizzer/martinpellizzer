Baccharis malibuensis

Baccharis malibuensis, commonly known as Malibu shrub, is a flowering plant native to southern California.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and chaparral habitats. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Baccharis malibuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
