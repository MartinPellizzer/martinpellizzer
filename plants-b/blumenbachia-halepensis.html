Blumenbachia halepensis

Blumenbachia halepensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, coastal climates.

Blumenbachia halepensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
