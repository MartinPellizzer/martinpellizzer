Bojeria speciosa

Bojeria speciosa, commonly known as the showy bojeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its most striking feature is its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange.

Bojeria speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
