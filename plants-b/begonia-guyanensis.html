Begonia guyanensis

Begonia guyanensis, commonly known as the Guyana begonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to purple. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts worldwide.
