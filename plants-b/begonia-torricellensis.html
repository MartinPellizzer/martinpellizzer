Begonia torricellensis

Begonia torricellensis, commonly known as the Torricelli begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
