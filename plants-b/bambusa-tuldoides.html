Bambusa tuldoides

Bambusa tuldoides, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a fast-growing clumping bamboo native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, yellow-green culms that add a striking visual appeal to landscapes. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a fresh green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Bambusa tuldoides is often used in ornamental gardening and for erosion control due to its hardy nature.
