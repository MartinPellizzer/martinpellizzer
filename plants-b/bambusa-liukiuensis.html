Bambusa liukiuensis

Bambusa liukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu bamboo, is a species native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a tall, clumping bamboo that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has slender, green culms with a smooth, often bluish tint. Its leaves are long and narrow, providing a dense canopy that helps in moisture retention.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and traditional crafts.
