Bulbophyllum drepanosepalum

Bulbophyllum drepanosepalum, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, sword-shaped leaves and striking, long-lasting inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
