Baeckea brevifolia

Baeckea brevifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are bright yellow and occur in clusters, adding a striking visual element to the plant.

Baeckea brevifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
