Barleria strigosa var. terminalis

Barleria strigosa var. terminalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, narrow, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or purple, which bloom during the warm seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
