Begonia × malipoensis

Begonia × malipoensis, commonly known as the Malipo begonia, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green color. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
