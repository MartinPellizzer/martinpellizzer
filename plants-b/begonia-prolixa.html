Begonia prolixa

Begonia prolixa, commonly known as the long-stemmed begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in both garden and indoor settings.
