Bambusa glaucescens f. midori-beni

Bambusa glaucescens f. midori-beni, commonly known as the Emerald-Bloom Bambusa, is a striking ornamental bamboo variety characterized by its vibrant, multi-colored foliage.

The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that display a combination of green, pink, and red hues, creating a visually appealing contrast. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. The bamboo's slender culms grow upright, adding a graceful structure to gardens or landscapes.

Due to its unique coloration and ease of care, it is a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts and landscape designers.
