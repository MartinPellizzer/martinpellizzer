Bulbophyllum setaceum var. setaceum

Bulbophyllum setaceum var. setaceum, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, spike-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
