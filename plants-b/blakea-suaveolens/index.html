Blakea Suaveolens

Blakea suaveolens is a striking evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. Known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, this plant captivates both botanists and gardeners alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a valuable addition to conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Blakea suaveolens remains relatively rare and is often threatened by habitat loss.
