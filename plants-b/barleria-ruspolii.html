Barleria ruspolii

Barleria ruspolii, commonly known as the African flame or red barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Barleria ruspolii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of nectar for pollinators.
