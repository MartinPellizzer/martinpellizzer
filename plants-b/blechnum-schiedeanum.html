Blechnum schiedeanum

Blechnum schiedeanum, commonly known as the Schiede's hartsia, is a fern belonging to the Blechnaceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically bipinnate, giving it a lush, intricate appearance. Native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
