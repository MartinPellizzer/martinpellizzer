Bulbophyllum mysorense

Bulbophyllum mysorense, commonly known as the Mysore orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers bloom during the winter months, and it is known for its ornamental value.

The plant's botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
