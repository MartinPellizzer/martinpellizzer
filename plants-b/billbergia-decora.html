Billbergia decora

Billbergia decora, commonly known as the decorative billbergia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant has rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with white or silver markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia decora is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
