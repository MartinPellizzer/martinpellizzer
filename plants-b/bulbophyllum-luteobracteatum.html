Bulbophyllum luteobracteatum

Bulbophyllum luteobracteatum, commonly known as the yellow bract orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring yellowish-green pseudobulbs and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellow flowers with prominent, showy bracts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
