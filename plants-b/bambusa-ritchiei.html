Bambusa ritchiei

Bambusa ritchiei, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a striking ornamental grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow-green culms and lush, feathery foliage that adds a tropical aesthetic to gardens. This plant is a clump-forming bamboo that grows upright, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of climates.

Bambusa ritchiei is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and ability to create a natural, flowing effect in garden designs.
