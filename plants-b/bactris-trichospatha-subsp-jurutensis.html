Bactris trichospatha subsp. jurutensis

Bactris trichospatha subsp.

jurutensis, commonly known as Juruti palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest of Brazil. It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length.

The plant produces yellowish-white flowers arranged in long, branched inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits that are typically clustered in panicles. It thrives in humid, tropical conditions and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and has some traditional uses by local communities.
