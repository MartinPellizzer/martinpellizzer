Bupleurum Ranunculoides F. Arcticum

Bupleurum ranunculoides f. arcticum is a rare and delicate plant species native to the Arctic regions. It thrives in cold, rocky environments and is adapted to survive in extreme conditions. This fern-like plant has slender stems and small, bright green leaves that resemble those of a true fern. It plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing food and shelter for various small organisms. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to climate change, it is considered a vulnerable species.
