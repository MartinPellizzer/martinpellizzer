Blepharodon anomalum

Blepharodon anomalum, commonly known as the starry bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
