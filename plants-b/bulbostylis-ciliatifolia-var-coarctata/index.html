Bulbostylis Ciliatifolia Var. Coarctata

Bulbostylis ciliatifolia var. coarctata is a rare and delicate grass-like plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its compact stature and unique floral structures that make it stand out among other sedge species. This variant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and forest floors. Its scientific classification highlights its close relationship to other members of the Cyperaceae family. Due to its limited distribution and ecological sensitivity, it is considered a species of conservation interest.
