Bauhinia floribunda

Bauhinia floribunda, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Bauhinia floribunda is adapted to dry climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
