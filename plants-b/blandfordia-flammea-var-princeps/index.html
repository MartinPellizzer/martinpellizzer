Blandfordia Flammea Var. Princeps

Blandfordia flammea var. princeps is a striking member of the Proteaceae family native to southern Australia. This unique plant is known for its vibrant, flame-like blooms that appear in late winter or early spring. Its flowers range from deep red to orange, creating a dramatic visual impact in gardens and natural habitats. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. It is a popular choice among horticulturists for its ornamental value and resilience in coastal environments.
