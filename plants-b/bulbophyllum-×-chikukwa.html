Bulbophyllum × chikukwa

Bulbophyllum × chikukwa, commonly known as the Chikukwa orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of multiple small to medium-sized blooms, often in shades of white, pink, and purple.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
