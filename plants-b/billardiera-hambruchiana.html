Billardiera hambruchiana

Billardiera hambruchiana, commonly known as the Grey-barked Billardiera, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its greyish-barked stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in groups of three. These flowers are followed by three-winged, capsule-like fruits.

Billardiera hambruchiana is often found in dry, open forests and woodlands, preferring well-drained soils.
