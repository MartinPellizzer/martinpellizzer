Bulbophyllum gyaloglossum

Bulbophyllum gyaloglossum, commonly known as the long-lipped bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
