Begonia natalensis

Begonia natalensis, commonly known as Natal begonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
