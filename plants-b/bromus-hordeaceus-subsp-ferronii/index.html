Bromus Hordeaceus Subsp. Ferronii

Bromus hordeaceus subsp. ferronii is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in specific ecological conditions. Found primarily in the Mediterranean region, this subspecies is adapted to rocky and arid environments. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it an interesting subject for botanical study. Despite its modest size, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity. Understanding this plant offers insights into the resilience of flora in challenging landscapes.
