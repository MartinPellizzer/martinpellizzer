Bulbostylis trichobasis var. uniseriata

Bulbostylis trichobasis var. uniseriata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a grass species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found in lowland and swampy areas.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized member of the Cyperaceae family.
