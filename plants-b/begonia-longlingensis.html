Begonia longlingensis

Begonia longlingensis, commonly known as the Longling begonia, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
