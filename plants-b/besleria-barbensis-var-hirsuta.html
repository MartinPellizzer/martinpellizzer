Besleria barbensis var. hirsuta

Besleria barbensis var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy besleria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has hairy foliage, which is a distinguishing feature of the varietal name *hirsuta*. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
