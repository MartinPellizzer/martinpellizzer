Byrsonima adenophylla

Byrsonima adenophylla, commonly known as the red mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with a rough, textured surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that give way to bright red, fleshy fruits. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
