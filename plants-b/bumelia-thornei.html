Bumelia thornei

Bumelia thornei, commonly known as the thorned bumelia, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Bumelia thornei is often found in moist woodlands and is valued for its ornamental thorns and seasonal foliage.
