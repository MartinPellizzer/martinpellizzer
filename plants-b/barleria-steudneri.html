Barleria steudneri

Barleria steudneri, commonly known as the blue barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slight hairy texture.

Barleria steudneri is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
