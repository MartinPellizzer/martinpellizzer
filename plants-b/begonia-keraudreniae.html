Begonia keraudreniae

Begonia keraudreniae, commonly known as the Keraudren's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
