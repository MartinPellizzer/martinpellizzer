Banisteriopsis carolina

Banisteriopsis carolina, commonly known as the Carolina trumpet or yellow trumpet.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by bright red or orange berries. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
