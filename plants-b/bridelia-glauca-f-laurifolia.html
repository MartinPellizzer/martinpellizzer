Bridelia glauca f. laurifolia

Bridelia glauca f. laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf bridelia, is a variant of the species Bridelia glauca, characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy foliage.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening in warm climates.

Its compact growth habit and attractive leaves make it a popular choice for landscape use.
