Burmeistera splendens

Burmeistera splendens, commonly known as the golden trumpet plant, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces showy, pendulous blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Burmeistera splendens thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
