Begonia zhuoyuniae

Begonia zhuoyuniae, commonly known as the Zhuoyuniae Begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped or oval leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
