Begonia zhengyiana

Begonia zhengyiana, commonly known as the Zhengyiana begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range from white to pink, adding visual interest. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often prized by plant enthusiasts and collectors.
