Bambos stricta

Bambos stricta, commonly known as the rigid bamboo, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender, and rigid stalks. The plant forms dense clumps and can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Bambos stricta is valued for its strength and is sometimes used in construction and handicrafts.
