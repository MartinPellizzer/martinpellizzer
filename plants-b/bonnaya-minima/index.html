Bonnaya Minima

Bonnaya minima is a rare and delicate plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its small, intricate leaves. This species is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and limited distribution. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensuring its survival in the wild.
