Biophytum reinwardtii

Biophytum reinwardtii, commonly known as the flame plant or red flame plant, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its vibrant red, velvety bracts that resemble flames. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
