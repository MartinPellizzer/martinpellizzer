Brodiaea elegans subsp. elegans

Brodiaea elegans subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the slender brodiaea, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.
