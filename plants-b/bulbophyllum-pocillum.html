Bulbophyllum pocillum

Bulbophyllum pocillum, commonly known as the Pocillum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
