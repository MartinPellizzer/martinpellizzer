Bupleurum kweichowense

Bupleurum kweichowense, commonly known as the Yunnan angelica, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its roots are valued in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
