Bulbophyllum trifilum subsp. filisepalum

Bulbophyllum trifilum subsp.

filisepalum, commonly known as the long-sepal Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers with long, slender sepals.

The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the foliage and flowering stems.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
