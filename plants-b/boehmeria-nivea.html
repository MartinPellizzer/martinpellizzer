Boehmeria nivea

Boehmeria nivea, commonly known as Chinese hemp or ice plant, is a flowering plant in the nettle family, Urticaceae.

It is native to East Asia and is characterized by its soft, silvery-white leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It is cultivated for its fibers, which are used in textiles and paper production.

Boehmeria nivea thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
