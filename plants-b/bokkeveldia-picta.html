Bokkeveldia picta

Bokkeveldia picta, commonly known as the painted bokkeveldia, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Bokkeveldia picta is known for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
