Begonia baccata

Begonia baccata, commonly known as the scarlet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red, fleshy berries and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia baccata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and unique fruit.
