Bulbophyllum kautskyi

Bulbophyllum kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and horticultural value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
