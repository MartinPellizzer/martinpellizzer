Benthamiella nordenskioldii

Benthamiella nordenskioldii, commonly known as the Arctic bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. It has a low-growing habit and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine and tundra ecosystems.
