Brassica urbaniana var. lyrifolia

Brassica urbaniana var. lyrifolia, commonly known as the lyrifolia kale, is a variant of the brassica family that features striking, curly, and often violet-hued leaves.

This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are typically harvested for culinary use, offering a slightly bitter and peppery flavor. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities, with its unique leaf shapes adding visual interest to gardens.

It is relatively hardy and can be grown in both spring and fall seasons.
