Baccharis Rufescens Var. Tenuifolia

Baccharis rufescens var. tenuifolia is a lesser-known yet resilient shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. This plant thrives in harsh conditions, showcasing its adaptability to dry and nutrient-poor soils. Its narrow leaves and compact growth habit make it an efficient plant for desert landscapes. Despite its hardiness, it remains underappreciated in both ecological and horticultural contexts. Understanding its characteristics can enhance its use in xeriscaping and conservation efforts.
