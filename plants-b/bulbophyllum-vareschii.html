Bulbophyllum vareschii

Bulbophyllum vareschii, commonly known as the Vareschii orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in low-light conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white, yellow, or green.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
