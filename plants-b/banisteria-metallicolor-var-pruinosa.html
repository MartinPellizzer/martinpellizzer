Banisteria metallicolor var. pruinosa

Banisteria metallicolor var. pruinosa, commonly known as the silver banisteria, is a striking tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. Its unique leaf texture and coloration make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it suitable for shaded indoor environments.
