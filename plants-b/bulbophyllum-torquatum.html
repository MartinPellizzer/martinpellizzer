Bulbophyllum torquatum

Bulbophyllum torquatum, commonly known as the twisted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, twisted inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a spiral pattern. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its distinctive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
