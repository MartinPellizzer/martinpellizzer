Begonia diversifiolia

Begonia diversifiolia, commonly known as the variegated begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with a mix of green, white, and pink hues. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. This plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
