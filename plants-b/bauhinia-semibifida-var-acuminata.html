Bauhinia semibifida var. acuminata

Bauhinia semibifida var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments. The plant produces showy, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Bauhinia semibifida var. acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
