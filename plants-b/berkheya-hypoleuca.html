Berkheya hypoleuca

Berkheya hypoleuca, commonly known as the white berkenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white, dandelion-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable ornamental plant.
