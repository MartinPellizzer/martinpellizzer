Billbergia variegata

Billbergia variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features rosettes of narrow, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white, pink, or red markings. It produces inflorescences with showy, brightly colored bracts that can range from yellow to red.

Billbergia variegata is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
