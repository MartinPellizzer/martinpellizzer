Brainea insignis var. formosana

Brainea insignis var. formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan brainea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to Taiwan and is characterized by its palm-like appearance with smooth, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-green flowers that are often hidden within a fleshy, spiny structure called a cyathium. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
