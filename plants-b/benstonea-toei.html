Benstonea toei

Benstonea toei, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and sepals that resemble a star, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, ephemeral blooms.
