Brachycladium erepse

Brachycladium erepse, commonly known as the narrowleaf bluemouth, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along roadsides.
