Bulbophyllum virescens

Bulbophyllum virescens, commonly known as the green-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and fleshy, adapted to conserve moisture.

Bulbophyllum virescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
