Begonia bauensis

Begonia bauensis, commonly known as the Bauensis begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display vibrant patterns of red, green, and purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in clusters. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia bauensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
