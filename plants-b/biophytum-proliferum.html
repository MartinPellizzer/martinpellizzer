Biophytum proliferum

Biophytum proliferum, commonly known as the "Beadleaf" or "Bead Plant," is a species of flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bead-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Biophytum proliferum is also valued for its ability to purify water, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
