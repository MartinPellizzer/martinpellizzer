Bulbophyllum henrici var. rectangulare

Bulbophyllum henrici var. rectangulare, commonly known as the rectangular leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, rectangular-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
