Balsamina capensis

Balsamina capensis, commonly known as the cape balsam, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental gardens and ecological landscapes.
