Brodiaea californica

Brodiaea californica, commonly known as the California brodiaea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to California.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. Brodiaea californica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
