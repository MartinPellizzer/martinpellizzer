Barleria mosdenensis

Barleria mosdenensis, commonly known as the Mosden's barleria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Barleria mosdenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
