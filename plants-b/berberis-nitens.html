Berberis nitens

Berberis nitens, commonly known as the nitens barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, thorny stems and small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in temperate climates.

Its hardiness and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative shrub.
