Bowkeria calceolarioides

Bowkeria calceolarioides, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy texture.
