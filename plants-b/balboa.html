Balboa

Balboa, commonly known as *Balbo* or *Balbosa*, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Solanaceae, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.
