Brauneria paradoxa

Brauneria paradoxa, commonly known as the paradox flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features palmate leaves with a waxy texture and a strong, somewhat unpleasant odor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Brauneria paradoxa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
