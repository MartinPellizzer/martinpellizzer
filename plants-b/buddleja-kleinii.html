Buddleja kleinii

Buddleja kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's butterfly bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens in warmer climates.

Buddleja kleinii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
