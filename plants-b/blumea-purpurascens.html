Blumea purpurascens

Blumea purpurascens, commonly known as the purple blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with purple markings.

Blumea purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in medicine and as a source of essential oils.
