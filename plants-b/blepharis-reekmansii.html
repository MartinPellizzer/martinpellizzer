Blepharis reekmansii

Blepharis reekmansii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Blepharis reekmansii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
