Boerhavia chaerophylloides

Boerhavia chaerophylloides, commonly known as the silverleaf boerhavia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with succulent, bluish-gray leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
