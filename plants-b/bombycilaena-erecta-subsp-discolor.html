Bombycilaena erecta subsp. discolor

Bombycilaena erecta subsp.

discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered bamboo grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and slender, hollow stems that are often tinged with white or pale green.
