Bihai ferdinando-coburgi

Bihai ferdinando-coburgi, commonly known as the Bihai pineapple, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, vibrant yellow flowers and striking, spiky leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant is native to South America, specifically Brazil, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The Bihai pineapple is also appreciated for its unique fragrance and is sometimes used in horticultural displays.
