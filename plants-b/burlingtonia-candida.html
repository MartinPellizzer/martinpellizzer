Burlingtonia candida

Burlingtonia candida, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Burlingtonia candida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
