Begonia henryi

Begonia henryi, commonly known as Henry's begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a deep green color and striking red veins. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
