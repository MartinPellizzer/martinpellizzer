Bulbophyllum guniuense

Bulbophyllum guniuense, commonly known as the Guniuense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly colored flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically features a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually borne on long, drooping inflorescences, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
