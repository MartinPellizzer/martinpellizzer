Bonamia elegans

Bonamia elegans, commonly known as the silverleaf bonamia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features silver-gray leaves with a velvety texture, and its small, pale yellow flowers bloom in clusters during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
