Bertiera heterophylla

Bertiera heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf bertiera, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from ovate to lanceolate. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
