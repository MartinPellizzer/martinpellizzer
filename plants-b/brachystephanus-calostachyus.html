Brachystephanus calostachyus

Brachystephanus calostachyus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stephanus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
