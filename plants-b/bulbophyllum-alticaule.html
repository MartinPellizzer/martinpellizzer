Bulbophyllum alticaule

Bulbophyllum alticaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth. The plant's unique morphology includes pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
