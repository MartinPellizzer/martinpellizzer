Bauhinia burkeana

Bauhinia burkeana, commonly known as the burkean bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, papery, heart-shaped leaves that are often bi-lobed. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by flat, pod-like fruits. Bauhinia burkeana is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in regions with low rainfall.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
