Besleria barbensis

Besleria barbensis, commonly known as the Barben's spider orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in the dry season.

Besleria barbensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens for its unique floral display.
