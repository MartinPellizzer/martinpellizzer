Begonia kanburiensis

Begonia kanburiensis, commonly known as the Kanburi begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant variegation. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
