Buena

Buena, commonly known as the Mexican beauty or velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by orange or red fruits. Buena is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of growth.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
