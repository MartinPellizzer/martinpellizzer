Brachycorythis stolzii

Brachycorythis stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, slow-growing cycad with a short, thick trunk and a crown of feathery, fan-like leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones typically occurring on separate plants. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value and conservation status, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
