Begonia lushaiensis

Begonia lushaiensis, commonly known as the Lushai Hills begonia, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish hue on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
