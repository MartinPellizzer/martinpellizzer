Bulbophyllum yoksunense var. parviflorum

Bulbophyllum yoksunense var. parviflorum, commonly known as the Yoksun miniature orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact growth habit with narrow, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its flowers are clustered in small inflorescences and are often adorned with intricate patterns.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
