Boehmeria pavonii

Boehmeria pavonii, commonly known as the silverleaf nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of nettles. The plant is characterized by its silvery-white underside on its leaves, which gives it its common name. It has a creeping growth habit and produces small, greenish flowers in clusters.
