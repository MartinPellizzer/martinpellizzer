Byrsella oscarii

Byrsella oscarii, commonly known as the star-shaped leaf plant, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-like leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of white and yellow.

Due to its ornamental value, Byrsella oscarii is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
