Brewcaria

Brewcaria, commonly known as the blue bell or blue bell flower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular blue or violet flowers that resemble small bells, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. Brewcaria species are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
