Blumea vanoverberghii

Blumea vanoverberghii, commonly known as the Vanoverbergh's Blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
