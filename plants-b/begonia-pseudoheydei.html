Begonia pseudoheydei

Begonia pseudoheydei, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Begonia pseudoheydei is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
