Berkheya speciosa subsp. lanceolata

Berkheya speciosa subsp.

lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate goldball, is a striking South African plant characterized by its bright yellow, spherical flower heads. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, composite flowers that attract pollinators.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
