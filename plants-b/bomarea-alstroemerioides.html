Bomarea alstroemerioides

Bomarea alstroemerioides, commonly known as the silverleaf bomarea, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots and striking, showy inflorescences that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy, silver-green surface that gives it its common name.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
