Bryonopsis laciniosa var. walkeri

Bryonopsis laciniosa var. walkeri, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a shimmering appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
