Bouteloua Polystachya

Bluestem grass, scientifically known as Bouteloua polystachya, is a native perennial grass found throughout the central and eastern United States. It is recognized for its striking blue-gray appearance and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. This grass plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife, making it an important component of native plant communities. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping and restoration projects. With its elegant growth habit and ecological benefits, Bluestem grass is both a functional and aesthetic addition to the landscape.
