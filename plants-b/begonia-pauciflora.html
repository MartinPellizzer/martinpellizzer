Begonia pauciflora

Begonia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a broad, heart-shaped outline and a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
