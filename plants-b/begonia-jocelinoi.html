Begonia jocelinoi

Begonia jocelinoi, commonly known as the Jocelino's begonia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often display striking variegation. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
