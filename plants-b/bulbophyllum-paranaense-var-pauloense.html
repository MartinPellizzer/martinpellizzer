Bulbophyllum paranaense var. pauloense

Bulbophyllum paranaense var. pauloense, commonly known as the Pauloense orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers are usually small and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and green.
