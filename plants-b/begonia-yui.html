Begonia yui

Begonia yui, commonly known as the Yui begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This plant is popular among gardeners for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
