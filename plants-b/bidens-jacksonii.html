Bidens jacksonii

Bidens jacksonii, commonly known as the Jackson's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flower heads that are composed of many small florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Bidens jacksonii is often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds, thriving in moist to wet soil conditions.
