Begonia ulei

Begonia ulei, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia ulei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
