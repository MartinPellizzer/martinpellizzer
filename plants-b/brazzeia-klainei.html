Brazzeia klainei

Brazzeia klainei, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and a yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a complex structure with intricate patterns.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, Brazzeia klainei is considered a valuable subject in botanical research and conservation efforts.
