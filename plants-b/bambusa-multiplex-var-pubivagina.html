Bambusa multiplex var. pubivagina

Bambusa multiplex var. pubivagina, commonly known as the bamboo with hairy sheaths, is a variant of the bamboo genus Bambusa.

It is characterized by its distinctive pubescent (hairy) leaf sheaths, which give it a unique appearance. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is a clumping bamboo, forming dense stands that can reach heights of up to 10 meters.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
