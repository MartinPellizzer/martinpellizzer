Bulbophyllum vexillarium

Bulbophyllum vexillarium, commonly known as the flag-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves that resemble a flag. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with bright yellow petals and a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum vexillarium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
