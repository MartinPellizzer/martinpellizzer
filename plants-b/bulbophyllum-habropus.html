Bulbophyllum habropus

Bulbophyllum habropus, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum habropus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
