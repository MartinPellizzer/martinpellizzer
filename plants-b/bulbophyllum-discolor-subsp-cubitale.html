Bulbophyllum discolor subsp. cubitale

Bulbophyllum discolor subsp.

cubitale, commonly known as the Cubital Bulbophyllum, is a striking orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that are often adorned with distinctive markings.

The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
