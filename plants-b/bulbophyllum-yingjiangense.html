Bulbophyllum yingjiangense

Bulbophyllum yingjiangense, commonly known as the Yingjiang orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and growth habits. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in its native region.
