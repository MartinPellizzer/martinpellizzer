Bihai spissa

Bihai spissa, commonly known as the Bihai palm, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers, which are followed by elongated, curved fruits containing edible seeds.

Bihai spissa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
