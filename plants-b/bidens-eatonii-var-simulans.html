Bidens eatonii var. simulans

Bidens eatonii var. simulans, commonly known as the yellow ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers with prominent white markings. It thrives in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a native wildflower and potential ecological indicator species.
