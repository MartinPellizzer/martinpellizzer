Begonia oligandra

Begonia oligandra, commonly known as the silverleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, densely packed hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance.

Begonia oligandra is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
