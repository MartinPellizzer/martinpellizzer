Bulbophyllum odoratissimum var. racemosum

Bulbophyllum odoratissimum var. racemosum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces racemose inflorescences, which are clusters of flowers arranged in a loose, branching pattern. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
