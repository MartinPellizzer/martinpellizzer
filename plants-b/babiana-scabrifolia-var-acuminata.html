Babiana scabrifolia var. acuminata

Babiana scabrifolia var. acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-leaved babiana, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, scaly texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique morphology, it is also appreciated by botanists and horticulturists alike.
