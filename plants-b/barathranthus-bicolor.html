Barathranthus bicolor

Barathranthus bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored barathranthus, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, contrasting flower colors, typically red and yellow. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is appreciated by both amateur and professional gardeners.
