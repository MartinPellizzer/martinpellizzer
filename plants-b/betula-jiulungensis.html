Betula jiulungensis

Betula jiulungensis, commonly known as the Jiulung white birch, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, white bark that peels in thin layers. The tree produces small, yellowish-green catkins and is typically found in temperate forest regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a notable species in its native habitat.
