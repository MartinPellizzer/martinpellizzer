Besleria pansamalana

Besleria pansamalana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
