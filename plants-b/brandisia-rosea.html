Brandisia rosea

Brandisia rosea, commonly known as the pink-flowered brandisia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
