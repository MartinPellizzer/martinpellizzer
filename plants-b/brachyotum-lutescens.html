Brachyotum lutescens

Brachyotum lutescens, commonly known as the yellow brachyotum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Brachyotum lutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
