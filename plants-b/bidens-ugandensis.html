Bidens ugandensis

Bidens ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda ironweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow with purple markings, arranged in clusters.

Bidens ugandensis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
