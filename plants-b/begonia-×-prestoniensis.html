Begonia × prestoniensis

Begonia × prestoniensis, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a tropical plant characterized by its vibrant and diverse flower colors.

It typically features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy flowers in a range of hues, including red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
