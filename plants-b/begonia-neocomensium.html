Begonia neocomensium

Begonia neocomensium, commonly known as the Neocomensium begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of pubescence.

This begonia thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.
