Bulbophyllum smileiphyllum

Bulbophyllum smileiphyllum, commonly known as the smiling orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its unique, often brightly colored flowers that resemble a smiling face. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat. Its flowers are bilaterally symmetrical, with prominent sepals and petals that contribute to its distinctive appearance.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
