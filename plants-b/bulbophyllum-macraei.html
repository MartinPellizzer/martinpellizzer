Bulbophyllum macraei

Bulbophyllum macraei, commonly known as the Macrae's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It typically produces white or pale yellow blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
