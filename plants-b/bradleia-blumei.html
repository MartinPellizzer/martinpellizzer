Bradleia blumei

Bradleia blumei, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-like pattern. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, anchoring itself with its aerial roots. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Bradleia blumei thrives in humid, shaded environments and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
