Batrachium paucistamineum f. diversifolium

Batrachium paucistamineum f. diversifolium, commonly known as the variegated frog's head fern, is a unique fern species characterized by its distinctive, lobed fronds that resemble the head of a frog.

The foliage is typically leathery and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, making it a striking addition to tropical gardens.
