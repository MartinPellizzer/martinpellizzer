Blepharodon ulei

Blepharodon ulei, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, and it thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.
