Brazoria truncata var. truncata

Brazoria truncata var. truncata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dune environments. It has a short growing season and is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
