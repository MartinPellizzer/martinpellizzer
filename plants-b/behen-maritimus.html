Behen maritimus

Behen maritimus, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces flowers that are typically yellow or orange. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often growing along shorelines.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
