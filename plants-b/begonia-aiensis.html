Begonia aiensis

Begonia aiensis, commonly known as the Ai Mountain begonia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy surface, while the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
