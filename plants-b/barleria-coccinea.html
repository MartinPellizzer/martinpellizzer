Barleria coccinea

Barleria coccinea, commonly known as red barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking red or pink tubular flowers. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Barleria coccinea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
