Barleria parvispina

Barleria parvispina, commonly known as the small-spurred barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Barleria parvispina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
