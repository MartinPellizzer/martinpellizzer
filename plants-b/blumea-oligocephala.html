Blumea oligocephala

Blumea oligocephala, commonly known as the small-headed blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect herb with opposite, lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in dense clusters, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in moist habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
