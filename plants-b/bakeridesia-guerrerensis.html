Bakeridesia guerrerensis

Bakeridesia guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.

Bakeridesia guerrerensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
