Bubonium graveolens subsp. stenophyllum

Bubonium graveolens subsp.

stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bubonium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils, making it a plant of interest in traditional medicine.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural habitat.
