Baccharis macroptera

Baccharis macroptera, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Baccharis macroptera is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
