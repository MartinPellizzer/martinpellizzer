Brachystegia velutina var. quarrei

Brachystegia velutina var. quarrei, commonly known as the African brachystegia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers followed by legume pods containing seeds.

It is often found in miombo woodlands and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
