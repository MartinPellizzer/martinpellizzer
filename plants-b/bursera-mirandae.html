Bursera mirandae

Bursera mirandae, commonly known as the Miranda's bursera, is a species of flowering plant in the Burseraceae family.

It is native to Mexico, specifically the state of Oaxaca, where it grows in tropical dry forests. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and small, fragrant leaves. It produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, spiky fruits.

Bursera mirandae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine by local communities.
