Blakea Inflata

Blakea inflata is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America. Known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range from red to orange, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This evergreen shrub thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of dense forests. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in cultivation due to its specific care requirements.
