Bauhinia goyazensis

Bauhinia goyazensis, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling butterflies. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, which are typically opposite in arrangement. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia goyazensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
