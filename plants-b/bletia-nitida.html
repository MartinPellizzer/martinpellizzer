Bletia nitida

Bletia nitida, commonly known as the white orchid or white bletia, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of white or pale green flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Bletia nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
