Beilschmiedia Gitingensis

Beilschmiedia gitingensis is a rare and unique plant native to the Philippines. It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. This species is known for its striking foliage and potential medicinal properties. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this valuable plant for future generations.
