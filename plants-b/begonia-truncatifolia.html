Begonia truncatifolia

Begonia truncatifolia, commonly known as the truncated begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive notched base. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
