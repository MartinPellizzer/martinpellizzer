Balanites australis

Balanites australis, commonly known as the desert date tree, is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It has a thick, gnarled trunk with a rough, flaky bark that helps it retain water in harsh environments. The plant produces yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing edible, sweet-smelling seeds. Its deep root system allows it to access groundwater, making it highly drought-resistant.

Balanites australis is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
