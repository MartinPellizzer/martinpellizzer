Byttneria humbertiana

Byttneria humbertiana, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves with a velvety texture. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
