Brachystelma laevigatum

Brachystelma laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
