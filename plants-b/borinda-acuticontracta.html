Borinda acuticontracta

Borinda acuticontracta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed borinda, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique type of shrubland ecosystem.

Borinda acuticontracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
