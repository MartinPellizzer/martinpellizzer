Bulbophyllum Bryophilum

Bulbophyllum bryophilum is a rare and fascinating orchid species native to the tropical forests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique ability to grow on moss, a trait that sets it apart from many other orchid species. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments where it can absorb moisture and nutrients from its surroundings. Its delicate flowers, often in shades of white and pink, add a striking beauty to its natural habitat. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, this species is now considered endangered, making conservation efforts all the more critical.
