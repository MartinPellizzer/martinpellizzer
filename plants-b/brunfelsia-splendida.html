Brunfelsia splendida

Brunfelsia splendida, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to deep purple, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces berries that turn from green to yellow or orange as they mature. It is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
