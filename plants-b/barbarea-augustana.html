Barbarea augustana

Barbarea augustana, commonly known as the August yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. Its leaves are deeply lobed and can be covered in a powdery coating, known as a bloom.

Barbarea augustana is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and meadows.
