Brugmansia floribunda

Brugmansia floribunda, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.

Its flowers are typically white, yellow, or orange, and they emit a strong, sweet fragrance, especially in the evening.
