Blechnum blechnoides var. gracilipes

Blechnum blechnoides var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are somewhat wiry and form dense clumps, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The fronds are usually bright green and have a finely toothed margin, making it a distinctive and attractive species.
