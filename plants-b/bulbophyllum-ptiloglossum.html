Bulbophyllum ptiloglossum

Bulbophyllum ptiloglossum, commonly known as the feather-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure, with flowers that are often pale green to white in color. The plant produces a single inflorescence with numerous small, showy blooms that are typically arranged in a raceme. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
