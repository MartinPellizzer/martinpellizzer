Bulbophyllum umbellatum var. fuscescens

Bulbophyllum umbellatum var. fuscescens, commonly known as the dark-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dark green, often reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to brownish flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variant is noted for its unique coloration and is appreciated in both horticultural and botanical collections.
