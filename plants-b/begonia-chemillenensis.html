Begonia chemillenensis

Begonia chemillenensis, commonly known as the Chemillen begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Begonia chemillenensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
