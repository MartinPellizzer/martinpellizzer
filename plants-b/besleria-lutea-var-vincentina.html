Besleria lutea var. vincentina

Besleria lutea var. vincentina, commonly known as the Vincent's golden flame, is a hybrid orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves and vibrant yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.

Due to its hybrid origin, it may exhibit variability in growth habits and flowering patterns.
