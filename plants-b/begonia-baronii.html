Begonia baronii

Begonia baronii, commonly known as the Baron's begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage and attractive blooms, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add visual interest to shaded landscapes.
