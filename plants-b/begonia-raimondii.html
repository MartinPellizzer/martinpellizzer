Begonia raimondii

Begonia raimondii, commonly known as the Raimondii begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, adding to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
