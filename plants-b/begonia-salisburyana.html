Begonia salisburyana

Begonia salisburyana, commonly known as the Salisbury begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

Begonia salisburyana is also valued for its ability to produce numerous offspring through vegetative propagation.
