Begonia lobulata

Begonia lobulata, commonly known as the lobed begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive lobes and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or red. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its foliage and blooms make it a popular choice for shaded garden beds and container gardening.
