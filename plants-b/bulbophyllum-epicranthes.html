Bulbophyllum epicranthes

Bulbophyllum epicranthes, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and green.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum epicranthes make it a striking and sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
