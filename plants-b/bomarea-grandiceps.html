Bomarea grandiceps

Bomarea grandiceps, commonly known as the giant bomarea, is a striking plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

Bomarea grandiceps is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, moist environments.
