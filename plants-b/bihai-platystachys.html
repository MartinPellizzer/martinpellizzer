Bihai platystachys

Bihai platystachys, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Bihai platystachys is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and traditional medicine.
