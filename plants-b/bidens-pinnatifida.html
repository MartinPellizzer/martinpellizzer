Bidens pinnatifida

Bidens pinnatifida, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, pinnately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers moist, well-drained environments.

Bidens pinnatifida is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in open woodlands.
