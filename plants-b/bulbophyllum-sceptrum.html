Bulbophyllum sceptrum

Bulbophyllum sceptrum, commonly known as the Crowned Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, segmented pseudobulbs and its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are borne on long, slender peduncles and can last for several weeks.

Bulbophyllum sceptrum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
