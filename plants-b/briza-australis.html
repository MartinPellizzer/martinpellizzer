Briza australis

Briza australis, commonly known as the Australian feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It features slender, silver-green leaves and produces delicate, feathery plumes that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system and small, spike-like inflorescences.
