Billbergia morelii

Billbergia morelii, commonly known as the silver crown bromeliad, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant features vibrant, showy inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage is often covered in a silvery, waxy coating, giving it a unique appearance.

Billbergia morelii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
