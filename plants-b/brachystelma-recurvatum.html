Brachystelma recurvatum

Brachystelma recurvatum, commonly known as the zigzag stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy stems that often curl in a zigzag pattern, which helps it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing moisture.

Brachystelma recurvatum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
