Billardiera venusta

Billardiera venusta, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. The plant is a woody shrub or small tree, with slender, often drooping branches. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with prominent veins.

Billardiera venusta is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is also an important species in Australian flora.
