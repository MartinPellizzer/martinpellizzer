Bauhinia altefissa

Bauhinia altefissa, commonly known as the white bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are delicate and feathery in appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring or early summer. Bauhinia altefissa thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
