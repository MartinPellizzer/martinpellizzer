Bojeria nutans

Bojeria nutans, commonly known as the hanging bojeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its drooping, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Bojeria nutans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil conditions for optimal growth.
