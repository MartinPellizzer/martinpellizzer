Baphia Massaiensis Var. Whitei

Baphia massaiensis var. whitei is a rare and striking plant native to the highlands of East Africa. Known for its vibrant, velvety leaves and unique growth habits, it has captivated the attention of botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This variant of Baphia massaiensis is particularly notable for its distinctive white variegation, which sets it apart from other members of the genus. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure and underappreciated in the broader horticultural community. Its cultivation requires specific conditions, making it a prized challenge for advanced growers.
