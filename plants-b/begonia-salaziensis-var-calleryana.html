Begonia salaziensis var. calleryana

Begonia salaziensis var. calleryana, commonly known as the Callery's begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
