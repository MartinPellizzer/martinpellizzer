Bambusa wenchouensis

Bambusa wenchouensis, commonly known as the Wenchou bamboo, is a species of grass in the Bambuseae tribe.

It is native to southern China, particularly in the provinces of Guangdong and Guangxi. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 15 meters. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, glossy surface.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and resilience in various environmental conditions.
