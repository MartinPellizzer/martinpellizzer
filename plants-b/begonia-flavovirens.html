Begonia flavovirens

Begonia flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to greenish hues. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Begonia flavovirens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor settings.
