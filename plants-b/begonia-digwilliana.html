Begonia digwilliana

Begonia digwilliana, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula begonia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright, hairy stems and produces showy, fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant bears small, bright orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
