Biophytum ottohuberi

Biophytum ottohuberi, commonly known as the flame plant, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, often with a sprawling or trailing habit. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Biophytum ottohuberi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
