Bistorta amplexicaulis var. suffulta

Bistorta amplexicaulis var. suffulta, commonly known as the broad-leaved bistorta, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, slightly hairy stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces clusters of pinkish-red, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often clasping the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands or along streambanks.
