Blechnum microlamaria

Blechnum microlamaria, commonly known as the narrow-leaf harts-tongue fern, is a member of the Blechnaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its narrow, leathery, and deeply divided fronds. Native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and conservation efforts.
