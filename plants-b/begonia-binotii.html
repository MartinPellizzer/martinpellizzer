Begonia binotii

Begonia binotii, commonly known as the spotted begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive pattern of light and dark green markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Begonia binotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
