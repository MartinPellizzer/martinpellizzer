Burmannia kalbreyeri

Burmannia kalbreyeri, commonly known as the red-veined burmannia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Burmanniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves with red veins. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as tropical rainforests. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered near the base of the plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
