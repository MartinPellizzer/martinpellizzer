Bunchosia swartziana

Bunchosia swartziana, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a strong scent.

It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils.
