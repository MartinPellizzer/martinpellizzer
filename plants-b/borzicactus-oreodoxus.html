Borzicactus oreodoxus

Borzicactus oreodoxus, commonly known as the Mexican apple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is often ribbed and covered in white or yellow spines. The plant produces bright red flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
