Brasilaelia × nesyana

Brasilaelia × nesyana, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow-orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its rapid growth and showy appearance, it is often used in landscaping for its visual impact.
