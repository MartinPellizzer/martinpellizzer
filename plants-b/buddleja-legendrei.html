Buddleja legendrei

Buddleja legendrei, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, panicle-like inflorescences that produce clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja legendrei is known for its long blooming period, often extending from late spring through early autumn.
