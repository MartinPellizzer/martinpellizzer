Blumea nodiflora

Blumea nodiflora, commonly known as the clustered blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, giving the plant its common name.
