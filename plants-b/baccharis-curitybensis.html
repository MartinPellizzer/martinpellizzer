Baccharis curitybensis

Baccharis curitybensis, commonly known as Brazilian baccharis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a slightly hairy texture.
