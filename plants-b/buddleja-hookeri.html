Buddleja hookeri

Buddleja hookeri, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Buddleja hookeri is known for its rapid growth and adaptability, though it can become invasive in some regions.
