Berberis × ottawensis var. purpurea

Berberis × ottawensis var. purpurea, commonly known as the Ottawa barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to North America.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and seasonal interest.
