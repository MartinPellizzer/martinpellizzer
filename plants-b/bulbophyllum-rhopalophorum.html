Bulbophyllum rhopalophorum

Bulbophyllum rhopalophorum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
