Berkheya Carlinopsis Subsp. Magalismontana

Berkheya carlinopsis subsp. magalismontana is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological significance. This subspecies is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and unique leaf structure, making it a standout in its habitat. Due to its limited distribution and habitat-specific requirements, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this botanical gem from further decline.
