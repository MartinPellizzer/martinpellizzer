Begonia ignea var. tuberosa

Begonia ignea var. tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and tuberous roots, which store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy texture.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in both indoor and outdoor settings.
