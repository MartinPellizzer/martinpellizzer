Barkeria vanneriana

Barkeria vanneriana, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Barkeria vanneriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
