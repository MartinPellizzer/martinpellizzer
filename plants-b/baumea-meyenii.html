Baumea meyenii

Baumea meyenii, commonly known as the silver grass, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
