Bletia sherrattiana

Bletia sherrattiana, commonly known as the Sherratt's bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. It is characterized by its unique inflorescence and is often found in humid, low-light environments.

Bletia sherrattiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
