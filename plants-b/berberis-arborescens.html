Berberis arborescens

Berberis arborescens, commonly known as the tree barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets and produces yellow flowers in early spring. The plant bears red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Berberis arborescens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
