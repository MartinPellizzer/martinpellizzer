Bulbophyllum wibowoajii

Bulbophyllum wibowoajii, commonly known as the Wibowo's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
