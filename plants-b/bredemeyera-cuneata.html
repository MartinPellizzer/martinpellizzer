Bredemeyera cuneata

Bredemeyera cuneata, commonly known as the red sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Bredemeyera cuneata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
