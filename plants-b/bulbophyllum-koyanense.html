Bulbophyllum koyanense

Bulbophyllum koyanense, commonly known as the Koyanese orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. Its flowers are small and often clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

The plant's unique structure and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
