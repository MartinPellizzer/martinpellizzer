Berberis horrida

Berberis horrida, commonly known as the prickly barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a leathery texture and a slightly serrated edge.

Berberis horrida is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
