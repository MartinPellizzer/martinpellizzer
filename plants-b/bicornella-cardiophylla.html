Bicornella cardiophylla

Bicornella cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf bicornella, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinct botanical features.

It is a perennial herb with a compact growth habit, often found in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are heart-shaped, with a velvety texture and a deep green color, providing a striking visual appeal. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale pink, adding to its ornamental value.

The plant's rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and colonize areas effectively, making it a resilient species in suitable habitats.
