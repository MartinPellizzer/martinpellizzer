Brassica campestris var. dentatopetsai

Brassica campestris var. dentatopetsai, commonly known as the curly kale or ornamental kale, is a cultivated variety of the cabbage family.

It is characterized by its large, curly, and deeply lobed leaves that can range in color from dark green to purple. This variety is typically grown for its ornamental value as well as for its edible leaves, which are rich in nutrients. The plant prefers cool climates and well-drained soil, making it suitable for spring and fall planting.

It is a biennial plant that can be grown as an annual in temperate regions.
