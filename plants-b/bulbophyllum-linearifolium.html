Bulbophyllum linearifolium

Bulbophyllum linearifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Bulbophyllum linearifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
