Bulbophyllum leptanthum

Bulbophyllum leptanthum, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a prized ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
