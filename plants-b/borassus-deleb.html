Borassus deleb

Borassus deleb, commonly known as the slender palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fibrous fruits containing seeds. Its trunk is valued for its fibrous material, which is used in making ropes and mats.

The plant thrives in humid climates and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
