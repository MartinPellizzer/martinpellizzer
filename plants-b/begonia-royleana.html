Begonia royleana

Begonia royleana, commonly known as the Himalayan begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Begonia royleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
