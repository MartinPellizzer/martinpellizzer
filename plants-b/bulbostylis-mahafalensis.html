Bulbostylis mahafalensis

Bulbostylis mahafalensis, commonly known as the Mahafal sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower spikes that are typically green or brown in color.
