Bulbophyllum citrinilabre

Bulbophyllum citrinilabre, commonly known as the citrine-labied orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage, which is often variegated with a distinctive yellow stripe along the leaf margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum citrinilabre is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
