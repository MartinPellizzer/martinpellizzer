Bellidiaster

Bellidiaster is a fascinating genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is primarily found in the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe. These plants are known for their showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. Bellidiaster species are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive appearance. Their ecological importance includes supporting pollinators and contributing to local biodiversity.
