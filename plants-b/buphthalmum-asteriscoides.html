Buphthalmum asteriscoides

Buphthalmum asteriscoides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a rosette of narrow, blue-green leaves. It produces small, yellow flowers that develop into hairy, brown capsules containing numerous seeds.

Buphthalmum asteriscoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
