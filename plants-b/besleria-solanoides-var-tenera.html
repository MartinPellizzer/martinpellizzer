Besleria solanoides var. tenera

Besleria solanoides var. tenera, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
