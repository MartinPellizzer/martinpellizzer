Bolbostemma biglandulosum

Bolbostemma biglandulosum, commonly known as the hairy root ginseng, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family萝藦科 (Asclepiadaceae) and is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, while its flowers are small and clustered in umbels.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
