Begonia × fenchihuensis

Begonia × fenchihuensis, commonly known as the Fenchihuensis begonia, is a hybrid plant originating from the intersection of Begonia semperflorens and Begonia × tuberhybrida.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit traits from both parent species, resulting in unique growth habits and floral displays.
