Barleria bagshawei

Barleria bagshawei, commonly known as the red barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria bagshawei is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
