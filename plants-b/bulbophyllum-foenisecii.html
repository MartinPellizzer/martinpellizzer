Bulbophyllum foenisecii

Bulbophyllum foenisecii, commonly known as the Foenisecii orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and somewhat fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
