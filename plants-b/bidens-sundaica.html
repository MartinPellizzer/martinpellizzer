Bidens sundaica

Bidens sundaica, commonly known as the Sunda ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. Its flowers are small and yellow, with ray florets that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are usually alternate, serrated, and have a hairy texture.
