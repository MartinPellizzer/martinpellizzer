Breynia varieri

Breynia varieri, commonly known as the variegated breynia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Breynia varieri is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
