Blechnum falcatum

Blechnum falcatum, commonly known as the narrow sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often arching fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a serrated or lobed edge.

Blechnum falcatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
