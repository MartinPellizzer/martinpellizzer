Berberis weixinensis

Berberis weixinensis, commonly known as the Weixin barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in temperate climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties, as it contains berberine, a compound with various health benefits.
