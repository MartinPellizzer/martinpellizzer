Bulbophyllum brachypus

Bulbophyllum brachypus, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
