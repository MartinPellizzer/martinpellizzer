Bulbophyllum trichocephalum var. racemosum

Bulbophyllum trichocephalum var. racemosum, commonly known as the racemosum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure and growth habits make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
