Brunfelsia augusta

Brunfelsia augusta, commonly known as the purple angel's trumpets, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces showy, pendulous blossoms that are often fragrant, especially in the evening. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Brunfelsia augusta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
