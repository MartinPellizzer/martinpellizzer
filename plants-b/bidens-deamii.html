Bidens deamii

Bidens deamii, commonly known as the Deam's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Bidens deamii is often found in wetland ecosystems, contributing to the biodiversity of these sensitive environments.
