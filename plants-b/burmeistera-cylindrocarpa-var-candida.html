Burmeistera cylindrocarpa var. candida

Burmeistera cylindrocarpa var. candida, commonly known as the white-flowered Burmeistera, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive cylindrical shape. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a specimen plant in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
