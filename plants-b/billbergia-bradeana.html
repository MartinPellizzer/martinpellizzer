Billbergia bradeana

Billbergia bradeana, commonly known as the Bradeana billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to hold water. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with brightly colored bracts that attract pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Billbergia bradeana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
