Boehmeria × arenicola

Boehmeria × arenicola, commonly known as the hybrid giant bamboo, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a tall, woody plant that can grow up to several meters in height, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spadices, a common feature among araceous plants. Its roots are fibrous and can form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
