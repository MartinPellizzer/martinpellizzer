Bidens menziesii subsp. filiformis

Bidens menziesii subsp.

filiformis, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, which are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and reduced leaf size compared to other Bidens species.
