Basaltogeton

Basaltogeton, commonly known as the basalt stone plant, is a unique succulent native to rocky, arid environments.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring. Its name derives from its association with basalt rock formations, where it often grows.

Basaltogeton is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal in rock gardens.
