Blaberopus sericeus

Blaberopus sericeus, commonly known as the silky ghost crab.

This species is a type of land crab found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in the Caribbean and parts of Central America. It is characterized by its slender body and soft, velvety exoskeleton, which gives it its common name. The plant aspect refers to its unique habitat, often found in sandy or moist soil near coastal areas.

Its ecological role includes contributing to soil aeration and nutrient cycling in its environment.
