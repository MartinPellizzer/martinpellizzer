Billardiera villosa

Billardiera villosa, commonly known as the silky wattle, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. Its leaves are pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces pods that contain seeds.

Billardiera villosa is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
