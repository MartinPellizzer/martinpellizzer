Begonia silverstonei var. brevipetiolata

Begonia silverstonei var. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the silverstone begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-white to pale green coloration, often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming in summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a striking addition to gardens and greenhouses.
