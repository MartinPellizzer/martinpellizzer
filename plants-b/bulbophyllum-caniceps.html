Bulbophyllum caniceps

Bulbophyllum caniceps, commonly known as the Cape York orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers during the wet season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

It is often found in coastal regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
