Boechera cusickii

Boechera cusickii, commonly known as the Sierra Point Peppergrass, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in rocky soils and alpine environments. The plant has slender stems and small, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white or pale purple, arranged in clusters and resembling those of mustard plants.

Boechera cusickii is native to the western United States and is adapted to thrive in harsh, high-altitude conditions.
