Ballota ampliata

Ballota ampliata, commonly known as the broadleaf ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Ballota ampliata is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
