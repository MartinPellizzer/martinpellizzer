Bromelia ananas var. lucida

Bromelia ananas var. lucida, commonly known as the lucid pineapple bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence that bears small, fragrant flowers, typically in shades of pink or red. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant thrives in humid, well-drained conditions and is relatively low maintenance once established.
