Babiana tubiflora subsp. tubiflora

Babiana tubiflora subsp.

tubiflora, commonly known as the red babiana, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Western Cape of South Africa. It features striking red to pink flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from August to September.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces underground bulbs that store nutrients for the next growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
