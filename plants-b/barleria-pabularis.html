Barleria pabularis

Barleria pabularis, commonly known as the common barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Barleria pabularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
