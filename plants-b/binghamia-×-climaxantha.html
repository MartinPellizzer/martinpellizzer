Binghamia × climaxantha

Binghamia × climaxantha, commonly known as the hybrid flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to northern Australia.

It is a hybrid between Binghamia capensis and Binghamia ribifolia, resulting in a plant with striking, flame-like red flowers that appear in late spring. The plant has a dense, spreading canopy with thick, waxy leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, making it a popular ornamental species in warm climates.
