Bonia saxatilis

Bonia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock fern, is a hardy plant native to rocky and coastal areas.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. The plant has a low-growing habit with slender, creeping stems that produce small, delicate fronds. Its foliage is typically green with a slight silvery or grayish hue, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Bonia saxatilis is well adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient choice for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.
