Buchenroedera glabriflora

Buchenroedera glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. The flowers are usually small and clustered in inflorescences, with pale yellow to white petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
