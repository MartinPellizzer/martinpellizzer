Bernhardia novae-hollandiae

Bernhardia novae-hollandiae, commonly known as the New Holland lily, is a striking perennial plant native to Australia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which occurs in late spring to early summer.
