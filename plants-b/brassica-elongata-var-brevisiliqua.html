Brassica elongata var. brevisiliqua

Brassica elongata var. brevisiliqua, commonly known as the short-stemmed field mustard, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and small, elongated leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typical of the mustard family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Brassica elongata var. brevisiliqua is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides, where it can become a weed.
