Bletia undulata

Bletia undulata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaf arrangement that gives it its common name. The plant produces tall, slender inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Bletia undulata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
