Bufonia stricta var. euboica

Bufonia stricta var. euboica, commonly known as the Euboic Broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
