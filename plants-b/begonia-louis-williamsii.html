Begonia louis-williamsii

Begonia louis-williamsii, commonly known as the Louis-Williams begonia, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This begonia is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
