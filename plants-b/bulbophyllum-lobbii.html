Bulbophyllum lobbii

Bulbophyllum lobbii, commonly known as the Lobb's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water and nutrients for the plant.

Bulbophyllum lobbii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
