Begonia ottoniana

Begonia ottoniana, commonly known as the silverleaf begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with silver or white, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and elegant blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a tropical look in temperate climates.
