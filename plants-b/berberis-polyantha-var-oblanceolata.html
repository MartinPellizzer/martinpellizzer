Berberis polyantha var. oblanceolata

Berberis polyantha var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it can be invasive in some regions and is sometimes considered a weed.
