Begonia mariae

Begonia mariae, commonly known as the Marie Begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia mariae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
