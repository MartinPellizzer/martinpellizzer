Bouteloua reederorum

Bouteloua reederorum, commonly known as Reed's bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its blue-green foliage and slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer.

Reed's bluestem is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
