Begonia laciniata var. nepalensis

Begonia laciniata var. nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored foliage.

Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and feature a distinctive pattern of green and silver or white markings. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique leaf patterns.
