Bidens radiata

Bidens radiata, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist to wet environments such as marshes and wetlands. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers with distinctive white markings. Its seed heads are often used in floral arrangements due to their ornamental appearance.

Bidens radiata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
