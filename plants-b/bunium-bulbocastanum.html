Bunium bulbocastanum

Bunium bulbocastanum, commonly known as the bulbous fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces a bulbous root that is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
