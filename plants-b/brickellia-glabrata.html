Brickellia glabrata

Brickellia glabrata, commonly known as silverleaf brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
