Bellis minor

Bellis minor, commonly known as the lawn daisy or English daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are spoon-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a common sight in lawns and meadows.

Bellis minor is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
