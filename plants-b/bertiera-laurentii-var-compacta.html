Bertiera laurentii var. compacta

Bertiera laurentii var. compacta, commonly known as the compact bertiera, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
