Bambusa iraten

Bambusa iraten, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface. This bamboo is known for its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden design.
