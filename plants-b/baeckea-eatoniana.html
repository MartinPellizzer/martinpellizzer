Baeckea eatoniana

Baeckea eatoniana, commonly known as the white baeckea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, characterized by its dense, compact form. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving them a distinctive appearance.

Baeckea eatoniana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
