Bulbophyllum marivelense

Bulbophyllum marivelense, commonly known as the Marivelense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure and is part of the Orchidaceae family. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
