Berchemia fenchifuensis

Berchemia fenchifuensis, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal berchemia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Berchemia fenchifuensis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
