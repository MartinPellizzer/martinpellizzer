Brexia

Brexia, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and elusive plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that appear to shimmer with an otherworldly glow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom only under specific environmental conditions, making it difficult to study. Brexia thrives in cool, moist environments and requires a specific combination of soil nutrients to survive.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists alike.
