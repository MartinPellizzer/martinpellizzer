Bromus texensis

Bromus texensis, commonly known as Texas brome, is a flowering grass native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky soils.

Bromus texensis is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to full sun conditions.
