Begonia arborescens var. arborescens

Begonia arborescens var. arborescens, commonly known as the tree begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns, adding visual interest to its appearance. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
