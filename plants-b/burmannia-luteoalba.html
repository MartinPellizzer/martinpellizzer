Burmannia luteoalba

Burmannia luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-white burmannia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Burmanniaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a waxy texture.

Burmannia luteoalba is known for its short blooming period, which occurs during the warmer months.
