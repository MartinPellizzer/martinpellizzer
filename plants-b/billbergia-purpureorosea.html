Billbergia purpureorosea

Billbergia purpureorosea, commonly known as the pink-rose billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, rosy-pink bracts that surround the flower, giving it a showy appearance. The plant features a central rosette of grass-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in height. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, emerging from the base of the leaf rosette.

Billbergia purpureorosea is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
