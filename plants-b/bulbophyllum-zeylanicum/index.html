Bulbophyllum Zeylanicum

Bulbophyllum zeylanicum is a fascinating orchid native to southern Asia. It is known for its striking appearance and unique growth habits. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. Its flowers often display vibrant colors and intricate patterns. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
