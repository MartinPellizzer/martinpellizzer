Berberis × neubertii

Berberis × neubertii, commonly known as the California barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces yellow flowers in the spring. The plant bears orange-red berries that are edible but often used as ornamental features. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance contribute to its widespread cultivation in gardens.
