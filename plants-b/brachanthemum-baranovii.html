Brachanthemum baranovii

Brachanthemum baranovii, commonly known as the Baranov's bidens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate to cold climates. Brachanthemum baranovii is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
