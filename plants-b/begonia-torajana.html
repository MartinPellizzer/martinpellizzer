Begonia torajana

Begonia torajana, commonly known as the Toraja begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deeply veined, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts and conservationists.
