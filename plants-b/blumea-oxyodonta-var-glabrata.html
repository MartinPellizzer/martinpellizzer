Blumea oxyodonta var. glabrata

Blumea oxyodonta var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth blumea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, which gives the variety its common name. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
