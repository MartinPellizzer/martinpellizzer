Blumea pannosa

Blumea pannosa, commonly known as the silky blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are yellow and are arranged in clusters, making it an attractive plant for ornamental purposes.
