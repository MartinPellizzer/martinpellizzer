Bulbophyllum dusenii

Bulbophyllum dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces fleshy, bulb-like structures that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry environments. Its flowers are typically small and fragrant, often in shades of yellow and green.

Bulbophyllum dusenii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts.
