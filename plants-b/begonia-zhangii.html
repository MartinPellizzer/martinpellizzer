Begonia zhangii

Begonia zhangii, commonly known as the Zhang's begonia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive foliage and showy flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually bright red or pink, with five petals and a prominent central spur.

This plant prefers shady, moist environments and is often grown as an ornamental in gardens and greenhouses.
