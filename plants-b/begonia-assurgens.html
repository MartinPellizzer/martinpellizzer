Begonia assurgens

Begonia assurgens, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to bronze. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions.

Begonia assurgens is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
