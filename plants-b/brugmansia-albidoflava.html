Brugmansia albidoflava

Brugmansia albidoflava, commonly known as the white-flowered angel's trumpet, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant has smooth, often hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brugmansia albidoflava is known for its strong, sweet fragrance, especially at night.
