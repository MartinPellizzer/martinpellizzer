Bulbophyllum rhodostictum

Bulbophyllum rhodostictum, commonly known as the red-streaked bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking, often colorful inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which are borne on long, slender stems. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

This orchid is popular among enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
