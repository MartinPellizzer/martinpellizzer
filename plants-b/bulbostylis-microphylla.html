Bulbostylis microphylla

Bulbostylis microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved bulbostylis, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to greenish flowers. It is typically found in moist, sandy soils in tropical and subtropical regions.

Bulbostylis microphylla is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in diverse environmental conditions.
