Bulbophyllum tapeinophyton

Bulbophyllum tapeinophyton, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. The plant produces elongated, narrow leaves and bears small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its roots are often specialized for epiphytic growth, allowing it to attach to trees or other surfaces.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
