Bulbophyllum vanvuurenii

Bulbophyllum vanvuurenii, commonly known as the Van Vuuren's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a unique, twisted appearance. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its natural habitat.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to both botanical gardens and specialized orchid collections.
