Bulbostylis juncoides var. lorentzii

Bulbostylis juncoides var. lorentzii, commonly known as the Lorentz's bulbostylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, three-lobed flowers. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in suitable habitats.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
