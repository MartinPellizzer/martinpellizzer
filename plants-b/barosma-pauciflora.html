Barosma pauciflora

Barosma pauciflora, commonly known as the slender barosma, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Barosma pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
