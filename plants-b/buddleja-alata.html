Buddleja alata

Buddleja alata, commonly known as the silver-leaf buddleja, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features striking silver-white foliage that contrasts beautifully with its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers. The plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade. Its blooms, which appear in late summer, attract a wide range of pollinators, including butterflies and bees.

Buddleja alata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
