Bulbophyllum thersites

Bulbophyllum thersites, commonly known as the Thersites orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns.

Bulbophyllum thersites is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
