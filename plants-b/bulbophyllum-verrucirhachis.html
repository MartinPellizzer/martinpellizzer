Bulbophyllum verrucirhachis

Bulbophyllum verrucirhachis, commonly known as the疣茎兰, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces multiple racemes of fragrant, showy flowers with white to yellow petals and sepals. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
