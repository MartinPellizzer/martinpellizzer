Barfussia platyrhachis

Barfussia platyrhachis, commonly known as the broad-leaved barfussia, is a member of the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts.
