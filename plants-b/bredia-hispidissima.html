Bredia hispidissima

Bredia hispidissima, commonly known as the hairy bredia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Bredia hispidissima is adapted to arid environments and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
