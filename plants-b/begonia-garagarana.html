Begonia garagarana

Begonia garagarana, commonly known as the African nettle begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Begonia garagarana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
