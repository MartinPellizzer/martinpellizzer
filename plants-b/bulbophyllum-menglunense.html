Bulbophyllum menglunense

Bulbophyllum menglunense, commonly known as the Menglun orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that serve as storage organs for water and nutrients. The plant typically features elongated, narrow leaves and bears small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
