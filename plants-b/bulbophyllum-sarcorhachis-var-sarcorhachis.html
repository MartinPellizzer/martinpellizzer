Bulbophyllum sarcorhachis var. sarcorhachis

Bulbophyllum sarcorhachis var. sarcorhachis, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its unique, horn-like structures on its flower spikes. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
