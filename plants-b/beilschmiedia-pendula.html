Beilschmiedia pendula

Beilschmiedia pendula, commonly known as the hanging beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its drooping, pendulous inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant has elliptical, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. Beilschmiedia pendula is often found in lowland to montane forest habitats.

Its botanical features make it a striking ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
