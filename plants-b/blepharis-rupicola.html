Blepharis rupicola

Blepharis rupicola, commonly known as the rock blepharis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
