Bauhinia macranthera

Bauhinia macranthera, commonly known as the large-flowered bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in summer. Its fruit is a flat, leathery pod that contains several seeds.

Bauhinia macranthera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
