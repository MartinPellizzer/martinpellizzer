Bulbophyllum nigrilabium

Bulbophyllum nigrilabium, commonly known as the black-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique dark-colored labellum, which gives it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in protected areas and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
