Bulbostylis lolokweensis

Bulbostylis lolokweensis, commonly known as the Lolokwe bulbostylis, is a species of sedge native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
