Bidens frondosa f. anomala

Bidens frondosa f. anomala, commonly known as the narrowleaf tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern and central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, alternate leaves with serrated edges. Its yellow flowers bloom in clusters, often with a central disc of tubular florets.

This variant is distinguished by its more elongated leaves compared to the typical form of Bidens frondosa.
