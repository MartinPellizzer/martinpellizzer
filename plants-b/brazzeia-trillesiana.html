Brazzeia trillesiana

Brazzeia trillesiana, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen cycad that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk covered with a fibrous, spongy bark. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of numerous narrow, leathery leaflets.

Native to Madagascar, this plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
