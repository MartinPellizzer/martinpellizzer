Bidens bigelovii

Bidens bigelovii, commonly known as the common tickseed, is an annual flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers with ray florets and a central disk. Bidens bigelovii is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
