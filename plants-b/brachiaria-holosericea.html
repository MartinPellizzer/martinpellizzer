Brachiaria holosericea

Brachiaria holosericea, commonly known as silverleaf stargrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired leaves and tufted inflorescences, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks. Its rapid growth and dense foliage make it an effective pioneer species in disturbed or wetland habitats.

Brachiaria holosericea is also valued for its use in livestock forage and its role in soil stabilization.
