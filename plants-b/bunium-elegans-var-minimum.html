Bunium elegans var. minimum

Bunium elegans var. minimum, commonly known as the dwarf bishop's weed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
