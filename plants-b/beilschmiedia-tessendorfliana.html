Beilschmiedia tessendorfliana

Beilschmiedia tessendorfliana, commonly known as the Tesso's beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.
