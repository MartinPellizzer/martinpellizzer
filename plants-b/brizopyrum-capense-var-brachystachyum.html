Brizopyrum capense var. brachystachyum

Brizopyrum capense var. brachystachyum, commonly known as the Cape rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically reddish-brown in color. Its leaves are narrow and grassy, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions.
