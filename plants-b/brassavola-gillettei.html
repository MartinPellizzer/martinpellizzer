Brassavola gillettei

Brassavola gillettei, commonly known as the Gillette's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying environmental conditions.

Brassavola gillettei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
