Bastardia bivalvis var. aristata

Bastardia bivalvis var. aristata, commonly known as the coastal bladderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that thrives in saline and coastal environments, often found in dunes and rocky shores. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are succulent, adapted to store water. It has a specialized root system that allows it to anchor in loose, sandy soils.

The species is notable for its ability to tolerate high salinity and extreme weather conditions.
