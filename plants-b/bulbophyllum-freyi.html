Bulbophyllum freyi

Bulbophyllum freyi, commonly known as the Freyi's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum freyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
