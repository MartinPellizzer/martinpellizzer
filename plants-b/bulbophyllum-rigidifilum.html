Bulbophyllum rigidifilum

Bulbophyllum rigidifilum, commonly known as the rigid filaments orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, which is typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Bulbophyllum rigidifilum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
