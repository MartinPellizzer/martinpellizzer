Bertya oleifolia var. glabrescens

Bertya oleifolia var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved bertya, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in dune environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
