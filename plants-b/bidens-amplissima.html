Bidens amplissima

Bidens amplissima, commonly known as the largeleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with prominent white markings, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Bidens amplissima is known for its hardy nature and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
