Bonamia evolvuloides

Bonamia evolvuloides, commonly known as the silverleaf bonamia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a low-growing habit, featuring silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is due to its attractive foliage and compact growth form.
