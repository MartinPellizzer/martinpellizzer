Begonia × excelsior

Begonia × excelsior, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia × tuberhybrida.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically produces fleshy, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Begonia × excelsior is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
