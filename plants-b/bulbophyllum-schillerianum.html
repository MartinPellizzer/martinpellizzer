Bulbophyllum schillerianum

Bulbophyllum schillerianum, commonly known as the Schiller's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Bulbophyllum schillerianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
