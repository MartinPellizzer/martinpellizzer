Blumea salviodora

Blumea salviodora, commonly known as the narrowleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The small yellow flowers appear in clusters and are followed by oval, woody fruits.
