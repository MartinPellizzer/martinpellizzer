Begonia sutherlandii f. latior

Begonia sutherlandii f. latior, commonly known as the broad-leaved begonia, is a hybrid form of the species Begonia sutherlandii.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush foliage.

This cultivar is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care.
