Bulbulus

Bulbulus, commonly known as the common bulbul, is a small bird native to parts of Africa, Asia, and the Middle East.

It belongs to the family Pycnonotidae, which includes many tropical birds characterized by their distinctive coloration. The bulbulus is known for its melodious song and adaptability to various habitats, including forests and urban areas. Its diet primarily consists of fruits, insects, and nectar, making it an important seed disperser in its ecosystem.

The species is also notable for its ability to mimic other bird calls, showcasing its vocal versatility.
