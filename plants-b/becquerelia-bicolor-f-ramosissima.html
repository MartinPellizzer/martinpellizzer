Becquerelia bicolor f. ramosissima

Becquerelia bicolor f. ramosissima, commonly known as the pink and white orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive bicolor flowers, which display a combination of pink and white petals. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Andes, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that grow in dense clusters.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly valued in botanical collections and conservation efforts.
