Bambekea racemosa

Bambekea racemosa, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bright red berries that are highly ornamental and attract birds. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by the showy red fruits.

Bambekea racemosa is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in arid regions.
