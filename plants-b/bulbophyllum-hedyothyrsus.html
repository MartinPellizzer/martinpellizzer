Bulbophyllum hedyothyrsus

Bulbophyllum hedyothyrsus, commonly known as the orchid with the peculiar appearance, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are thick and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

The flowers are usually fragrant and bloom in a range of colors, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
