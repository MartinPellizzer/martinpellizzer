Boucerosia penicillata

Boucerosia penicillata, commonly known as the needle-leaf restia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restiaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland grassland habitats.
