Bihai straminea

Bihai straminea, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are typically orange to red in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overshadowed by its showy foliage. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Bihai straminea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
