Bulbocastanum linnaei var. valentinum

Bulbocastanum linnaei var. valentinum, commonly known as the Valentine's bulbocastanum, is a rare and ornamental plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bulbous root system and striking, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
