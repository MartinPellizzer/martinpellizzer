Bromus australis

Bromus australis, commonly known as the Australian brome, is a perennial grass native to southern South America.

It typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, often in temperate to subtropical regions. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green sheen.

Bromus australis is adapted to well-drained soils and can tolerate moderate drought conditions.
