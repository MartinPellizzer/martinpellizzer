Bulbophyllum brevispicatum

Bulbophyllum brevispicatum, commonly known as the short-spurred bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, and the leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
