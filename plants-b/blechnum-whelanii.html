Blechnum whelanii

Blechnum whelanii, commonly known as the Whelan's holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that remain throughout the year. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive, upright growth habit.

Blechnum whelanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
