Baccharis zoellneri

Baccharis zoellneri, commonly known as the silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands.

Baccharis zoellneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
