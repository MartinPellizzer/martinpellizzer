Bulbophyllum angustatifolium

Bulbophyllum angustatifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous inflorescence and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical greenhouses.
