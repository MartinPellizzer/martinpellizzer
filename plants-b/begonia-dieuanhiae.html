Begonia dieuanhiae

Begonia dieuanhiae, commonly known as the Dieu Anh Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia dieuanhiae is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
