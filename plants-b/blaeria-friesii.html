Blaeria friesii

Blaeria friesii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight hairy texture.

Blaeria friesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
