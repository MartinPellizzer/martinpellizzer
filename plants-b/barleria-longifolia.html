Barleria longifolia

Barleria longifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces spikes of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
