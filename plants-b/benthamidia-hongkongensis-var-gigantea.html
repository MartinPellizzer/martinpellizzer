Benthamidia hongkongensis var. gigantea

Benthamidia hongkongensis var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant Hong Kong benthamia, is a striking plant native to Hong Kong.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and foliage. The plant features large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
