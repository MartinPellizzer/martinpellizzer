Begonia luxurians

Begonia luxurians, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically deep green with a distinctive marbled pattern. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
