Bertiera globiceps

Bertiera globiceps, commonly known as the globe-headed bertiera, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded form and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Bertiera globiceps is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
