Bakoaella

Bakoaella, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Bakoaella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
