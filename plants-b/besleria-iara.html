Besleria iara

Besleria iara, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange-red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color.

Besleria iara thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
