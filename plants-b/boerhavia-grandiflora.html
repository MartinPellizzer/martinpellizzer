Boerhavia grandiflora

Boerhavia grandiflora, commonly known as the showy bluemink, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Boerhavia grandiflora is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
