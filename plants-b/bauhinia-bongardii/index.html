Bauhinia Bongardii

Bauhinia bongardii is a striking flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant pink and white blooms, it adds a dramatic touch to any landscape. This species belongs to the legume family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its unique leaf structure, which resembles a butterfly, makes it easily recognizable. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive in different climates.
