Blumea linearis

Blumea linearis, commonly known as the slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, with long, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Blumea linearis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
