Bulbophyllum koordersii

Bulbophyllum koordersii, commonly known as the Koord's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded conditions.

The flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
