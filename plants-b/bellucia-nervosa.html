Bellucia nervosa

Bellucia nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.

Bellucia nervosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
