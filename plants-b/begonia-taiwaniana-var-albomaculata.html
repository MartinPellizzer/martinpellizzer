Begonia taiwaniana var. albomaculata

Begonia taiwaniana var. albomaculata, commonly known as the white-spotted begonia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive white or pale green spots, adding visual interest to any garden. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming in summer. It prefers partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
