Brachystelma flavidum

Brachystelma flavidum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered stone orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, rock-dwelling growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, typically growing on rocky outcrops or in sandy soils.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often considered a rare and endangered species.
