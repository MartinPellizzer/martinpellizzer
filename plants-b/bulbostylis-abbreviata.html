Bulbostylis abbreviata

Bulbostylis abbreviata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a small perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it forms dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white or pale green flowers. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and establish itself in moist environments.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in shaded, waterlogged areas.
