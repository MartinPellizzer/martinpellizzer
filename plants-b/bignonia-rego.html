Bignonia rego

Bignonia rego, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces long, pendulous racemes of tubular flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to gardens in suitable climates.
