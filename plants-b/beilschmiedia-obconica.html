Beilschmiedia obconica

Beilschmiedia obconica, commonly known as the broadleaf beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

Beilschmiedia obconica is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
