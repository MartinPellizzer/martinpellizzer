Bulbostylis langsdorffiana

Bulbostylis langsdorffiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands.
