Barleriopsis micans var. brachystachya

Barleriopsis micans var. brachystachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed barleriopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
