Bidens steyermarkii

Bidens steyermarkii, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as swamps and marshes, and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

The showy yellow flowers appear in clusters and are followed by distinctive bracts that give the plant its common name.
