Blinkworthia convolvuloides

Blinkworthia convolvuloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many vines and herbs characterized by their showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Blinkworthia convolvuloides is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and open habitats.
