Bupleurum falcatum f. kiusianum

Bupleurum falcatum f. kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusianum bellflower, is a rare and distinctive cultivar of the Bupleurum genus.

It is characterized by its finely cut, feathery leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Native to specific regions in Europe, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to horticultural collections, particularly in gardens with high humidity.
