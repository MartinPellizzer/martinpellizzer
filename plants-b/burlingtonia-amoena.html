Burlingtonia amoena

Burlingtonia amoena, commonly known as the velvet tangerine, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, smooth rind and a sweet, tangy flavor similar to mandarins. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

The botanical features of Burlingtonia amoena make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
