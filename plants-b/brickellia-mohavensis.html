Brickellia mohavensis

Brickellia mohavensis, commonly known as Mohave brickellia, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense, terminal cluster. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
