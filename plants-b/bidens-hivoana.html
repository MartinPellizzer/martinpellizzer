Bidens hivoana

Bidens hivoana, commonly known as the Hawaiian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and daisy-like appearance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with prominent central disks.

Bidens hivoana plays a role in local ecosystems and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
