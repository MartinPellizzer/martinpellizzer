Bulbostylis fusiformis

Bulbostylis fusiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It is often found in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in areas with consistently moist soil.

Due to its delicate structure and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
