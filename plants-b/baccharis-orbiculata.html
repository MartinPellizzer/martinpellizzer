Baccharis orbiculata

Baccharis orbiculata, commonly known as the globular baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.

Baccharis orbiculata is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
