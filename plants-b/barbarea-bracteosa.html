Barbarea bracteosa

Barbarea bracteosa, commonly known as the bracted honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial species characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its seed pods are elongated and split open to release tiny, white seeds.

Barbarea bracteosa is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
