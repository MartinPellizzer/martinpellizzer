Bulbophyllum blepharistes

Bulbophyllum blepharistes, commonly known as the ear-shaped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, ear-shaped pseudobulbs and linear, often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with pale yellow to greenish-white petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum blepharistes is a popular choice for collectors due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
