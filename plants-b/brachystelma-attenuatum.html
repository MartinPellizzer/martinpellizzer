Brachystelma attenuatum

Brachystelma attenuatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in a layer of white, powdery wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
