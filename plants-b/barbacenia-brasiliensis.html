Barbacenia brasiliensis

Barbacenia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for warm climates.

Barbacenia brasiliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
