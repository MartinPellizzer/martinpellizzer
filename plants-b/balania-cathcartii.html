Balania cathcartii

Balania cathcartii, commonly known as the Cathcart's orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

Native to the mountainous regions of South Africa, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and humid microclimates. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Balania cathcartii is considered a vulnerable species.
