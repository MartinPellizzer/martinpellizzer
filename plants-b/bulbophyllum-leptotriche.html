Bulbophyllum leptotriche

Bulbophyllum leptotriche, commonly known as the slender-hair orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and long, slender leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like trichomes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and horticultural appeal, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
