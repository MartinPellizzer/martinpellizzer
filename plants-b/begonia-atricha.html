Begonia atricha

Begonia atricha, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its deep green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia atricha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
