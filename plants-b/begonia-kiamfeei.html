Begonia kiamfeei

Begonia kiamfeei, commonly known as the Kiamfeei begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a textured surface, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
