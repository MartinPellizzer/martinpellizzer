Bulbophyllum ipanemense

Bulbophyllum ipanemense, commonly known as the Ipanema orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its pseudobulbs are thick and store water, allowing it to survive in conditions with irregular rainfall.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
