Bauhinia bohniana

Bauhinia bohniana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Bauhinia bohniana is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
