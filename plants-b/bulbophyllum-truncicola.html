Bulbophyllum truncicola

Bulbophyllum truncicola, commonly known as the truncated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates. Its flowers are usually borne on erect, hairy inflorescences and can vary in color from yellow to orange.

Bulbophyllum truncicola is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticultural settings.
