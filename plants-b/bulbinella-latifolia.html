Bulbinella latifolia

Bulbinella latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bulbinella, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globe-shaped bulbs and grass-like leaves. Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens across southern Australia.
