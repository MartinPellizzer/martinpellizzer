Bletilla foliosa

Bletilla foliosa, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bletilla foliosa is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
