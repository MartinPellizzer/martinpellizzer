Bulbophyllum montanum

Bulbophyllum montanum, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers with a wide range of colors, often in clusters. Its growth habit involves a rhizomatous structure that helps it survive in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum montanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
