Brachystelma punctatum

Brachystelma punctatum, commonly known as the spotted stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, branching stems. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Brachystelma punctatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
