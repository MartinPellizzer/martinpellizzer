Bulbophyllum cupreum

Bulbophyllum cupreum, commonly known as the copper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a contrasting purple or red lip, making it visually striking.

Bulbophyllum cupreum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
