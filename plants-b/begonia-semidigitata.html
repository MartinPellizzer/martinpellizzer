Begonia semidigitata

Begonia semidigitata, commonly known as the semi-digitate begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Begonia semidigitata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
