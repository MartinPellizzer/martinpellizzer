Bouteloua Vestita

Blue grama, scientifically known as Bouteloua vestita, is a native grass species commonly found in the arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It thrives in sandy and rocky soils, making it a resilient plant adapted to harsh environmental conditions. With its distinctive blue-green color and fine, wiry blades, blue grama adds visual interest to native landscapes. This grass plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for restoration and landscaping projects in dry climates.
