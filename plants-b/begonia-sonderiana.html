Begonia sonderiana

Begonia sonderiana, commonly known as the Sonder's begonia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia sonderiana is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
