Bulbine truncata

Bulbine truncata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbine, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and truncate at the base. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Bulbine truncata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
