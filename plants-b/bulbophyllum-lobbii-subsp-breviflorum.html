Bulbophyllum lobbii subsp. breviflorum

Bulbophyllum lobbii subsp.

breviflorum, commonly known as the short-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia.

This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white and yellow, with a unique, intricate structure.

The plant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
