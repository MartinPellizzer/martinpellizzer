Brachyglottis bidwillii

Brachyglottis bidwillii, commonly known as the Bidwill's brachyglottis, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are densely packed along its stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Brachyglottis bidwillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
