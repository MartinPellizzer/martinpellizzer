Begonia yunnanensis var. thorelii

Begonia yunnanensis var. thorelii, commonly known as the Yunnan begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
