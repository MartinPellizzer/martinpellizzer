Bulbophyllum rauhii var. andranobeense

Bulbophyllum rauhii var. andranobeense, commonly known as the Andranobeense Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in drought resistance.

The flowers typically bloom in clusters, showcasing a range of hues from yellow to orange, making it a visually appealing and sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
