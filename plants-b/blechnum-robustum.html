Blechnum robustum

Blechnum robustum, commonly known as the hard fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its robust, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a distinctive, lance-shaped appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, allowing it to anchor firmly in the soil and store moisture.

Blechnum robustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy and resilient nature.
