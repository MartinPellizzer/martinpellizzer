Blumea klossii

Blumea klossii, commonly known as the Kloss blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a cluster, and are followed by small, oval-shaped fruits.
