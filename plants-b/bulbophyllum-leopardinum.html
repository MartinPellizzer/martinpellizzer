Bulbophyllum leopardinum

Bulbophyllum leopardinum, commonly known as the leopard orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate patterns on its pseudobulbs and leaves, which resemble the markings of a leopard. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with contrasting markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
