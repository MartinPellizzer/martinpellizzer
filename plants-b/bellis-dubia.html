Bellis dubia

Bellis dubia, commonly known as the lesser mouse-ear.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. The plant features small, spoon-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Bellis dubia is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers.
