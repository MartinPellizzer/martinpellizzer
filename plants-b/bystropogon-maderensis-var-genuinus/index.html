Bystropogon Maderensis Var. Genuinus

Bystropogon maderensis var. genuinus is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers. This variety is distinguished by its unique morphological features and limited geographic distribution. Despite its small size, it plays an important role in its local ecosystem. Further study is needed to fully understand its ecological significance and conservation status.
