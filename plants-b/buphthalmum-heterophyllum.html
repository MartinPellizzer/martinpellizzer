Buphthalmum heterophyllum

Buphthalmum heterophyllum, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow with a prominent central disk, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
