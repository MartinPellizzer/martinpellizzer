Boehmeria zollingeriana var. blinii

Boehmeria zollingeriana var. blinii, commonly known as the Blinii nettle, is a variant of the Boehmeria genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its fibrous, soft stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its potential in fiber production and has been studied for its ecological and agricultural applications.
