Brassica rapa var. rosularis

Brassica rapa var. rosularis, commonly known as the Chinese mustard or tree mustard, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its edible leaves and seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary use.
