Bidens martii

Bidens martii, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets, and it is often found in dry, open habitats. Bidens martii has hairy stems and leaves, and it is known for its hardiness in arid environments.
