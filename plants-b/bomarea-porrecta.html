Bomarea porrecta

Bomarea porrecta, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has tuberous roots and is often found growing in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Bomarea porrecta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
