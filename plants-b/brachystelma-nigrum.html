Brachystelma nigrum

Brachystelma nigrum, commonly known as the black-stemmed ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the cooler months, usually in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
