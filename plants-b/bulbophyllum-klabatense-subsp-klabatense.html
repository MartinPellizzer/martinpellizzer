Bulbophyllum klabatense subsp. klabatense

Bulbophyllum klabatense subsp.

klabatense, commonly known as the Klabatense orchid, is a species native to the Philippines. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit.

The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many epiphytic orchids. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that varies among individuals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
