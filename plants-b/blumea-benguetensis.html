Blumea benguetensis

Blumea benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Blumea benguetensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
