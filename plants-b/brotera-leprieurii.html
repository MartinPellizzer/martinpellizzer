Brotera leprieurii

Brotera leprieurii, commonly known as the African broom.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. Its leaves are typically small and opposite, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Brotera leprieurii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
