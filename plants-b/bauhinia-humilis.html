Bauhinia humilis

Bauhinia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas, often found in moist, shaded areas. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers. Bauhinia humilis is typically a shrub or small tree, growing up to 3 meters in height.
