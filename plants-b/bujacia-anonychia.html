Bujacia anonychia

Bujacia anonychia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Bujacia anonychia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
