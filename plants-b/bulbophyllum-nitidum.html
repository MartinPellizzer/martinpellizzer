Bulbophyllum nitidum

Bulbophyllum nitidum, commonly known as the shining bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring and summer, with white or pale yellow petals. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
