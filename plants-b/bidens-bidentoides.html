Bidens bidentoides

Bidens bidentoides, commonly known as tickseed sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern and central United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring upright stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with dark central disks, and its leaves are usually alternate, rough to the touch, and have a distinctive serrated edge. Bidens bidentoides is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and fields.

It is also a popular choice for gardeners due to its attractive appearance and ease of cultivation.
