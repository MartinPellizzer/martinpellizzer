Brunfelsia macrocarpa

Brunfelsia macrocarpa, commonly known as the night blooming cereus, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that open at night and remain open during the day. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
