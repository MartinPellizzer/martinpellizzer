Begonia marnieri

Begonia marnieri, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia marnieri is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
