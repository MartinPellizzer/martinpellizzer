Bermudiana bermudiana var. exaltata

Bermudiana bermudiana var. exaltata, commonly known as the Bermuda grass variant exaltata, is a variant of the Bermuda grass species.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. The plant is native to the coastal regions of Bermuda and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.

Its rhizomatous and stoloniferous growth allows it to spread rapidly and form dense turf.
