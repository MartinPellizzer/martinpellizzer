Bulbophyllum vaccinioides

Bulbophyllum vaccinioides, commonly known as the Vaccinioides orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
