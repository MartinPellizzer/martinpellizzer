Bupleurum × sitenskyi

Bupleurum × sitenskyi, commonly known as the hybrid Chinese angelica, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels. It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
