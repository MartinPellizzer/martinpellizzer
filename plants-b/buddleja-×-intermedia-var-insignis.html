Buddleja × intermedia var. insignis

Buddleja × intermedia var. insignis, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The foliage consists of dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It is valued in horticulture for its ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
