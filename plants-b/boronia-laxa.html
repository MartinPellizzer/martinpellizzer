Boronia laxa

Boronia laxa, commonly known as the slender boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring aromatic, often fragrant, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
