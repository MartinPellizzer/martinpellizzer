Bulbophyllum bufo

Bulbophyllum bufo, commonly known as the frog's eye orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. The plant produces intricate, spurred orchid flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color, with a distinctive, frog-like appearance. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, helping it survive in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum bufo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
