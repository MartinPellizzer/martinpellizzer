Bulbophyllum sumatranum

Bulbophyllum sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, anchoring itself with its aerial roots. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow and orange.
