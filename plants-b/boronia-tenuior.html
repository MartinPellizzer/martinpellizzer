Boronia tenuior

Boronia tenuior, commonly known as the slender boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring aromatic, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and rocky areas.

Boronia tenuior is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fragrant foliage and delicate blooms.
