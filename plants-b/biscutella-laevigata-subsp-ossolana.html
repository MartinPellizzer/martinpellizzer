Biscutella laevigata subsp. ossolana

Biscutella laevigata subsp.

ossolana, commonly known as the Ossola biscutella, is a perennial herb native to the European Alps. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions.

The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. It is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with characteristic cruciform flowers.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its habitat.
