Bergia alsinoides

Bergia alsinoides, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Bergia alsinoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging landscapes.
