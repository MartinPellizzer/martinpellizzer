Buchnera pulchra

Buchnera pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful Buchnera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a glossy appearance.

Buchnera pulchra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid climates.
