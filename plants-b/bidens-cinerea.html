Bidens cinerea

Bidens cinerea, commonly known as hoary tickseed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as roadsides and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Bidens cinerea is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
