Barleria holubii subsp. ugandensis

Barleria holubii subsp.

ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda barleria, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers.
