Bombax retusum

Bombax retusum, commonly known as the silky ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, woody pods containing fibrous seeds, which are used in traditional crafts. Its trunk is often covered with a thick, spongy bark that provides habitat for various organisms.

Bombax retusum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in tropical rainforests.
