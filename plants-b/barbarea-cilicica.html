Barbarea cilicica

Barbarea cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician honesty, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features small, white or yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are typically lobed or divided. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Barbarea cilicica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
