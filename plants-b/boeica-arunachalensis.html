Boeica arunachalensis

Boeica arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, specifically in the state of Arunachal Pradesh in India.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer.
