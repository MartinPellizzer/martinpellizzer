Broughtonia sanguinea

Broughtonia sanguinea, commonly known as the blood orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, red-pink inflorescence that resembles a cluster of small flowers. Broughtonia sanguinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
