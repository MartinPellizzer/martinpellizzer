Briza rotundata

Briza rotundata, commonly known as the rounded feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fluffy, spherical seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Briza rotundata is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate, airy foliage and flowering display.
