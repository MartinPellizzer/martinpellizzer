Boronia pulchella var. distans

Boronia pulchella var. distans, commonly known as the slender boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
