Begonia kalbreyeri

Begonia kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting veining. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
