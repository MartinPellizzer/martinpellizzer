Bidens odorata

Bidens odorata, commonly known as sweet blackberry.

This herbaceous perennial is native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico. It features slender, hairy stems and small, yellow-orange flower heads that bloom from late spring to early fall. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but have a mild, somewhat tart flavor.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.
