Bulbophyllum adenoblepharon

Bulbophyllum adenoblepharon, commonly known as the "Coral Orchid," is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of small, coral-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns that resemble coral formations.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
