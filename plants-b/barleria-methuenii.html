Barleria methuenii

Barleria methuenii, commonly known as Methuen's barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Barleria methuenii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
