Bulbophyllum careyanum var. crassipes

Bulbophyllum careyanum var. crassipes, commonly known as the thick-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses.
