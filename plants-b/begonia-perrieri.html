Begonia perrieri

Begonia perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. The plant features heart-shaped or ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal.
