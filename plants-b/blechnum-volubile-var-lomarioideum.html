Blechnum volubile var. lomarioideum

Blechnum volubile var. lomarioideum, commonly known as the Lomarioideum maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. The plant features delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green with a slightly glossy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
