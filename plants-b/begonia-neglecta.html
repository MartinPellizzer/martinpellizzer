Begonia neglecta

Begonia neglecta, commonly known as the slender begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its delicate, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
