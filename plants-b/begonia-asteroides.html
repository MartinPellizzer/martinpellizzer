Begonia asteroides

Begonia asteroides, commonly known as the star begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Begonia asteroides prefers partial shade and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor settings.
