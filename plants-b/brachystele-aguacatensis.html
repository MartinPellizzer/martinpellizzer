Brachystele aguacatensis

Brachystele aguacatensis, commonly known as the Mexican ground orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
