Boutiquea platypetala

Boutiquea platypetala, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a favorite in landscaping and gardens.
