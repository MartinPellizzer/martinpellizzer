Bowlesia geraniifolia

Bowlesia geraniifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf bowlesia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
