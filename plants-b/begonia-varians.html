Begonia varians

Begonia varians, commonly known as the variable begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, often adorned with silver or green markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Begonia varians is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
