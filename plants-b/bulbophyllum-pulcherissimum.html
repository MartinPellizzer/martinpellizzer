Bulbophyllum pulcherissimum

Bulbophyllum pulcherissimum, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a lithophyte, meaning it grows on rocks, often in humid and shaded environments. The plant produces striking, long-lasting orchid flowers in shades of pink, white, and yellow. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique growth habit.
