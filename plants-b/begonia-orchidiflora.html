Begonia orchidiflora

Begonia orchidiflora, commonly known as the orchid-begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia orchidiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
