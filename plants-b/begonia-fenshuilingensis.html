Begonia fenshuilingensis

Begonia fenshuilingensis, commonly known as the Fenshuiling begonia, is a rare and striking species native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it suitable for woodland gardens or greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable but endangered species in botanical conservation efforts.
