Bowlesia argenticaulis

Bowlesia argenticaulis, commonly known as the silver-stemmed bowlesia, is a unique member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray stems that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of its family. Its leaves are finely divided and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Native to high-altitude regions of South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments.
