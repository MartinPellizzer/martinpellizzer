Bambusa miyiensis

Bambusa miyiensis, commonly known as the Miyan bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a tall, slender bamboo characterized by its smooth, light green culms and dense foliage. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental landscaping. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Bambusa miyiensis is also valued for its strong, flexible stalks, which can be used in construction and crafts.
