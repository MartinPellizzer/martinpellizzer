Bulbophyllum flavisepalum

Bulbophyllum flavisepalum, commonly known as the yellow-sepal orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding environment.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
