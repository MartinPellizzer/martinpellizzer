Bridelia nooteboomii

Bridelia nooteboomii, commonly known as the African bride.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Bridelia nooteboomii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally used in traditional medicine.
