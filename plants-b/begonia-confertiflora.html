Begonia confertiflora

Begonia confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
