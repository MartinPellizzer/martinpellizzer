Berlinia globiflora

Berlinia globiflora, commonly known as the globe-flowered Berlinia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, globe-shaped flower clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and landscapes.
