Brunfelsia undulata

Brunfelsia undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaved shrub, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a distinctive wavy pattern on the petals. The plant has heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Brunfelsia undulata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
