Besleria luteovirens

Besleria luteovirens, commonly known as the golden green orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are typically bright green with a yellowish or golden hue. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish tint, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
