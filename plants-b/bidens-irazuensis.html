Bidens irazuensis

Bidens irazuensis, commonly known as the Irazu ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America, particularly found in Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Bidens irazuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
