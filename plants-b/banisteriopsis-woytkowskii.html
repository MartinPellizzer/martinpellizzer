Banisteriopsis woytkowskii

Banisteriopsis woytkowskii, commonly known as the Brazilian snakeroot, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in cymes. Its fruits are follicles containing seeds with a reticulate surface.
