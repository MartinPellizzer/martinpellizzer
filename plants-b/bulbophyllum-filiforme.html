Bulbophyllum filiforme

Bulbophyllum filiforme, commonly known as the thread-like orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, filamentous pseudobulbs and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
