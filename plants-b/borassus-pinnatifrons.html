Borassus pinnatifrons

Borassus pinnatifrons, commonly known as the African fan-palmetto, is a palm native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in swampy or riverine habitats.

The trunk is thick and covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance.
