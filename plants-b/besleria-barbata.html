Besleria barbata

Besleria barbata, commonly known as the featherleaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners who appreciate its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
