Bulbophyllum sandrangatense

Bulbophyllum sandrangatense, commonly known as the Sandrangatense orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
