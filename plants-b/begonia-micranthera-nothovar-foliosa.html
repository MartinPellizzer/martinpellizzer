Begonia micranthera nothovar. foliosa

Begonia micranthera nothovar. foliosa, commonly known as the foliosa begonia, is a hybrid plant characterized by its unique foliage rather than showy flowers.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and sometimes pink or red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or ovate, with a velvety texture that adds to its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular among indoor gardeners for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
