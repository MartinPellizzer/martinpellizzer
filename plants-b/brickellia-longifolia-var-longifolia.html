Brickellia longifolia var. longifolia

Brickellia longifolia var. longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and sandy areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and native plant gardens.
