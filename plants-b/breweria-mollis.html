Breweria mollis

Breweria mollis, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

Breweria mollis is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
