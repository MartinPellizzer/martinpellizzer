Bartsia sericea

Bartsia sericea, commonly known as silverweed, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Bartsia sericea is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora in its native habitat.
