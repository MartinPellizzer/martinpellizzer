Bulbophyllum sukhakulii

Bulbophyllum sukhakulii, commonly known as the Sukhakul's orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapting to its environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
