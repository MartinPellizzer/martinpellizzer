Brachystelma campanulatum

Brachystelma campanulatum, commonly known as the Cape bell orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservatories for its unique floral display.
