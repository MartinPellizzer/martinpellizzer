Brucea javanica

Brucea javanica, commonly known as the Java brucea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright red, bead-like fruits. Brucea javanica is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional medicine.
