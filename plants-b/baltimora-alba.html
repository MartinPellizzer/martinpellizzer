Baltimora alba

Baltimora alba, commonly known as the white lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features showy, white petals that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, green berries that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Baltimora alba is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
