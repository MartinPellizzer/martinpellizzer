Bulbostylis radiciflora

Bulbostylis radiciflora, commonly known as the root-flowered bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal dunes or saline environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, and it blooms during the wet season.

The plant has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
