Bulbophyllum auritum

Bulbophyllum auritum, commonly known as the ear-shaped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, ear-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum auritum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
