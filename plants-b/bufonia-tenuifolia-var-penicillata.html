Bufonia tenuifolia var. penicillata

Bufonia tenuifolia var. penicillata, commonly known as the slender leaf bufonia, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length, arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often accompanied by a distinctive, fragrant scent. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
