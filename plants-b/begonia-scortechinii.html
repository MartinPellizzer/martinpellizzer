Begonia scortechinii

Begonia scortechinii, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often have a silvery or white underside. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia scortechinii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
