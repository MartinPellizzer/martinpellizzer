Berlinia polyphylla

Berlinia polyphylla, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. It produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds.

Berlinia polyphylla is often found in savanna and woodland habitats, where it plays an important ecological role.
