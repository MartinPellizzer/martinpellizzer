Bulbophyllum kirroanthum

Bulbophyllum kirroanthum, commonly known as the Kirrana Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its bulbs are an essential part of its growth cycle, storing nutrients and water.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.
