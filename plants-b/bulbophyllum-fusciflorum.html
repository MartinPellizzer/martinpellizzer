Bulbophyllum fusciflorum

Bulbophyllum fusciflorum, commonly known as the dark-flowered bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, dark-colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its natural habitat.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to watering and light conditions for successful cultivation.
