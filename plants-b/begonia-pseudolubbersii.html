Begonia pseudolubbersii

Begonia pseudolubbersii, commonly known as the African begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with deep green coloration and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It has a short growing season and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
