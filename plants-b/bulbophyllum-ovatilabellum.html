Bulbophyllum ovatilabellum

Bulbophyllum ovatilabellum, commonly known as the ovate-labellum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its fleshy rhizomes and pseudobulbs. The plant produces a single or few erect, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense pubescence. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive ovate labellum.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary lowland rainforests.
