Bidens eatonii var. kennebecensis

Bidens eatonii var. kennebecensis, commonly known as the Kennebec ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and narrow, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers consist of yellow disk flowers surrounded by pink to red ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats, including meadows and wetlands, and is known for its hardy nature and ornamental value.
