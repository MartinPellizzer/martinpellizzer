Bupleurum spinosum var. lucidum

Bupleurum spinosum var. lucidum, commonly known as the glossy spiny bugloss, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often found in Mediterranean climates. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, spiny leaves that are usually gray-green in color. Its flowers are blue to purple in hue, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems during the spring and summer months.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
