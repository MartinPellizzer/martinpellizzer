Bulbophyllum scaberulum

Bulbophyllum scaberulum, commonly known as the rough-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces linear, leathery leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, stiff hairs. Its flowers are small and typically greenish-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

This species is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
