Buddleja acosma

Buddleja acosma, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, aromatic stems and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract butterflies and other pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Buddleja acosma is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in gardens.
