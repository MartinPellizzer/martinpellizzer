Billbergia saundersii

Billbergia saundersii, commonly known as the Saunders' billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking red and orange inflorescences that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Billbergia saundersii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant colors and low maintenance requirements.
