Bupleurum falcatum var. stenophyllum

Bupleurum falcatum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved bugloss, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in mountainous regions. It belongs to the family Apiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and often sought-after species in horticulture and traditional medicine.
