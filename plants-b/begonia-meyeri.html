Begonia meyeri

Begonia meyeri, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor or outdoor gardens.

Begonia meyeri is also valued for its ability to produce aerial tubers, which can be propagated to grow new plants.
