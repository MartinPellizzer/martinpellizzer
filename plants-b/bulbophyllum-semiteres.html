Bulbophyllum semiteres

Bulbophyllum semiteres, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum semiteres is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
