Bulbophyllum pulchrum

Bulbophyllum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for aerial attachment to trees, and it requires high light intensity and good air circulation for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum pulchrum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
