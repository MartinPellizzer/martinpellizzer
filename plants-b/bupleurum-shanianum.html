Bupleurum shanianum

Bupleurum shanianum, commonly known as the Shanian cicely, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and often hollow stems. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Bupleurum shanianum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
