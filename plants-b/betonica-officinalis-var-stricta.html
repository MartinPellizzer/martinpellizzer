Betonica officinalis var. stricta

Betonica officinalis var. stricta, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of blue to purple flowers from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Betonica officinalis var. stricta has a long history of use in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and healing properties.
