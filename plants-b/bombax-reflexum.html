Bombax reflexum

Bombax reflexum, commonly known as the red cotton tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces fluffy, white cotton-like seeds enclosed in a woody capsule, which is a distinctive botanical feature. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing as a small tree or shrub.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in various cultures.
