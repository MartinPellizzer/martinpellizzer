Bulbophyllum lichenophylax

Bulbophyllum lichenophylax, commonly known as the lichen-leafed bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, leathery leaves that resemble lichens, giving it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum lichenophylax is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
