Begonia ozotothrix

Begonia ozotothrix, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its foliage is often a deep green with a distinctive waxy texture.

Begonia ozotothrix is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
