Begonia fragae

Begonia fragae, commonly known as the fragrant begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

The fragrance of its blooms adds an aromatic element to its ornamental value.
