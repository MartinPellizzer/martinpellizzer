Begonia herveyana var. herveyana

Begonia herveyana var. herveyana, commonly known as the Hervey's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
