Bauhinia cunninghamii f. rosea

Bauhinia cunninghamii f. rosea, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves and showy, pink or pale pink flowers. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
