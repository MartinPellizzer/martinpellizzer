Bromelia blanda

Bromelia blanda, commonly known as the common bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bromelia blanda is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
