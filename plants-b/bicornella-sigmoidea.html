Bicornella sigmoidea

Bicornella sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a compact, rosette-like growth habit with short, upright stems. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a distinct, undulating appearance.

Bicornella sigmoidea thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
