Begonia venosa

Begonia venosa, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, waxy, and often brightly colored leaves that have a zigzag or undulating margin. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically appearing in clusters. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal.

Begonia venosa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
