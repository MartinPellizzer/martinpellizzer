Belangera glabra

Belangera glabra, commonly known as the smooth belanger, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Belangera glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
