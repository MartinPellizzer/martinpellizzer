Begonia sandersii

Begonia sandersii, commonly known as the sandersii begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring contrasting green and white patterns.

Begonia sandersii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
