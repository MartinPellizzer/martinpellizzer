Burtonia foliolosa

Burtonia foliolosa, commonly known as the Madagascar lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. Burtonia foliolosa is a member of the Aroid family, which includes plants like philodendrons and peace lilies.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
