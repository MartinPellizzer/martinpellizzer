Bambusa subaequalis

Bambusa subaequalis, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or narrow bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant forms dense clumps and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness. Bambusa subaequalis is a perennial plant that thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for erosion control and as a decorative element in gardens and parks.
