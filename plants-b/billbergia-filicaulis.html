Billbergia filicaulis

Billbergia filicaulis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Billbergia, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Billbergia filicaulis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
