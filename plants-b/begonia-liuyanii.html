Begonia liuyanii

Begonia liuyanii, commonly known as the Liuyanii Begonia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green veins and a unique, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically red or pink in color, adding a subtle contrast to its foliage. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also appreciated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
