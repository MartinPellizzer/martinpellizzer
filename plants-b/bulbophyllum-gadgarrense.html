Bulbophyllum gadgarrense

Bulbophyllum gadgarrense, commonly known as the Gadgarren Orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are often small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered threatened and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
