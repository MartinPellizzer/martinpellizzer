Benthamantha robusta

Benthamantha robusta, commonly known as the robust benthamantha, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Benthamantha robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
