Blepharis diversispina

Blepharis diversispina, commonly known as the narrowleaf blepharis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
