Borzicactus hoxeyi

Borzicactus hoxeyi, commonly known as the Hoxey's cactus, is a unique member of the Cactaceae family native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its otherwise desolate appearance. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in extreme desert conditions.

Borzicactus hoxeyi is often found growing in rocky soils and is a symbol of resilience in harsh environments.
