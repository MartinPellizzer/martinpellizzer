Barjonia furlanii

It is a perennial herb characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
