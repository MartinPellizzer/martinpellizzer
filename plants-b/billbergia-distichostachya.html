Billbergia distichostachya

Billbergia distichostachya, commonly known as the two-ranked billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
