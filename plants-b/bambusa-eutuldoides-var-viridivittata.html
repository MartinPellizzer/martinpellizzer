Bambusa eutuldoides var. viridivittata

Bambusa eutuldoides var. viridivittata, commonly known as the green-striped bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, green stems with distinct yellow or white longitudinal stripes, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are bright green and arranged in a dense, feathery pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This ornamental bamboo is popular in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
