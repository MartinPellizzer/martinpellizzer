Bilderdykia × convolvuloides

Bilderdykia × convolvuloides, commonly known as the hybrid sweet potato vine, is a vigorous trailing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are not particularly showy but are followed by orange to red tuberous roots. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental for its lush foliage.

Due to its rapid growth, it is best suited for containers or as a ground cover in suitable environments.
