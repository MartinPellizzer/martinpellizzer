Bletia bicallosa

Bletia bicallosa, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, often spiny leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Bletia bicallosa thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
