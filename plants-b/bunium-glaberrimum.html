Bunium glaberrimum

Bunium glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like carrots and parsley. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in umbrella-like clusters. Its seeds are small and oval, often used in traditional medicine for their purported digestive benefits.

The entire plant emits a mild, aromatic scent, characteristic of many members of its family.
