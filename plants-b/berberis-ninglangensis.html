Berberis ninglangensis

Berberis ninglangensis, commonly known as the Ninglang barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
