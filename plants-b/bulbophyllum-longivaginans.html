Bulbophyllum longivaginans

Bulbophyllum longivaginans, commonly known as the long sheath orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate floral structure and elongated, sheathing leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a unique coloration pattern that attracts specific pollinators. It is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural enthusiasts and botanical research.
