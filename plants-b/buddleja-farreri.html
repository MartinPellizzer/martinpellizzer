Buddleja farreri

Buddleja farreri, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to lilac, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Buddleja farreri is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to grow in a range of soil conditions.
