Bursera heteresthes

Bursera heteresthes, commonly known as the Mexican frankincense tree, is a member of the Burseraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Mexico and Central America. The plant is characterized by its smooth, aromatic bark that exudes a resin when wounded. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a leathery texture.

The flowers are small and yellow, followed by elliptical, woody fruits that contain several seeds.
