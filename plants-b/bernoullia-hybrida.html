Bernoullia hybrida

Bernoullia hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense basal rosette.

Bernoullia hybrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
