Bulbophyllum amplebracteatum subsp. orthoglossum

Bulbophyllum amplebracteatum subsp.

orthoglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lip orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with narrow, lip-like petals that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
