Baeckea trichophylla

Baeckea trichophylla, commonly known as the silky baeckea, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or heathland environments.

Baeckea trichophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
