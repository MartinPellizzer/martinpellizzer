Burtonia polyzyga

Burtonia polyzyga, commonly known as the Burmese orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments. Its roots are fibrous, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Burtonia polyzyga is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
