Belvala Parviflora

Belvala parviflora is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern India. It is known for its small, pale pink blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to habitat loss, it is now classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
