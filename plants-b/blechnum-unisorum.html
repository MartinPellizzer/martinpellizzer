Blechnum unisorum

Blechnum unisorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Blechnum unisorum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
