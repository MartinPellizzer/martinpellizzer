Brassia rhizomatosa

Brassia rhizomatosa, commonly known as the rhizomatous orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense colonies. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Brassia rhizomatosa is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
