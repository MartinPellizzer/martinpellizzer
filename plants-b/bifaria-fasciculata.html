Bifaria fasciculata

Bifaria fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from decaying organic matter in the soil.

Bifaria fasciculata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage structure.
