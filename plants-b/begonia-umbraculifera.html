Begonia umbraculifera

Begonia umbraculifera, commonly known as the shade begonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide a lush, green appearance. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in shaded or partially shaded environments and requires high humidity to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
