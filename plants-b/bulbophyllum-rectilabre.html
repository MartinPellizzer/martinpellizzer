Bulbophyllum rectilabre

Bulbophyllum rectilabre, commonly known as the straight-labellum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, colorful flowers with a distinctive, straight labellum. The plant typically features elongated pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are often fragrant and can vary in color from white to yellow and red, depending on the variety.

Bulbophyllum rectilabre is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
