Bowlesia macrophysa

Bowlesia macrophysa, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a showy, modified leaf called a spathe.

Bowlesia macrophysa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its dramatic foliage.
