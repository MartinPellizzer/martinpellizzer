Binectaria laurifolia

Binectaria laurifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf binectaria, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Binectaria laurifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
