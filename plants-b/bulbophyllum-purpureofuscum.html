Bulbophyllum purpureofuscum

Bulbophyllum purpureofuscum, commonly known as the purple-brown bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its pseudobulbs are thick and segmented, storing water and nutrients.

The species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
