Blumea hymenophylla

Blumea hymenophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its finely textured, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Blumea hymenophylla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
