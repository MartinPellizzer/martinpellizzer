Berberis kunmingensis

Berberis kunmingensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a shrub native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is characterized by its spine-tipped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
