Begonia × incomparabilis

Begonia × incomparabilis, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a hybrid plant prized for its ornamental value.

It features velvety, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens due to its sensitivity to direct sunlight.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
