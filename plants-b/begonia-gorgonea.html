Begonia gorgonea

Begonia gorgonea, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to deep purple. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique texture and color.

Begonia gorgonea prefers shaded, humid environments and well-draining soil to thrive.
