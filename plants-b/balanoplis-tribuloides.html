Balanoplis tribuloides

Balanoplis tribuloides, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a branched stem and opposite, ovate leaves that are hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Balanoplis tribuloides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
