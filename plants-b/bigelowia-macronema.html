Bigelowia macronema

Bigelowia macronema, commonly known as the large-flowered bigelowia, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Bigelowia macronema is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
