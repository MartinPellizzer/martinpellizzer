Bournea sinensis

Bournea sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.
