Besleria oxyphylla

Besleria oxyphylla, commonly known as the white-flowered gesneriad, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink. The plant typically has glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
