Begonia oyuniae

Begonia oyuniae, commonly known as the Oyunia begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia oyuniae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
