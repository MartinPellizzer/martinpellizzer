Ballota alba

Ballota alba, commonly known as white ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry environments.

Ballota alba is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
