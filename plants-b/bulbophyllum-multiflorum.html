Bulbophyllum multiflorum

Bulbophyllum multiflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
