Baccharis pachycephala

Baccharis pachycephala, commonly known as the thick-headed baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Baccharis pachycephala is often found in dry, open habitats such as scrublands and coastal areas.
