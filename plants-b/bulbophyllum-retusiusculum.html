Bulbophyllum retusiusculum

Bulbophyllum retusiusculum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
