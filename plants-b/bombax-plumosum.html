Bombax plumosum

Bombax plumosum, commonly known as the silky cotton tree, is a tropical deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are palmately lobed and can grow up to 30 cm in length.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its fibers, which have traditional uses in local communities.
