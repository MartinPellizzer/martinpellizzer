Bauhinia standleyi

Bauhinia standleyi, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often divided into small, pointed leaflets. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the dry season. Bauhinia standleyi thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical landscapes.

Its rapid growth and seasonal flowering make it a valuable addition to gardens and urban green spaces.
