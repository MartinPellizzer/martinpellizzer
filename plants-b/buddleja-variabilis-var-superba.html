Buddleja variabilis var. superba

Buddleja variabilis var. superba, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant panicles of purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly serrated, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and hedge plantings.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
