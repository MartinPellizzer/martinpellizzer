Begonia stevei

Begonia stevei, commonly known as Steve's begonia, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia stevei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
