Bencomia caudata

Bencomia caudata, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow to golden-green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Bencomia caudata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
