Biophytum crassipes

Biophytum crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leafed phytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
