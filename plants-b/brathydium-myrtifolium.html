Brathydium myrtifolium

Brathydium myrtifolium, commonly known as the myrtle-leaved brathydium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.
