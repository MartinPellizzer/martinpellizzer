Bulbostylis miegei

Bulbostylis miegei, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its compact stature and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
