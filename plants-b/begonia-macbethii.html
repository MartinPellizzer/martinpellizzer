Begonia macbethii

Begonia macbethii, commonly known as the Macbeth begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that display a deep red to almost black coloration, which intensifies in low light conditions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, typically appearing in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia macbethii is popular among collectors for its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
