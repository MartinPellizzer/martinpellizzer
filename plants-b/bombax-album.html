Bombax album

Bombax album, commonly known as the silk cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in spring. The tree produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds encased in a hairy, woody capsule. Its leaves are palmate, with five to seven lobes, and it can grow up to 30 meters tall.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in medicine and textile production.
