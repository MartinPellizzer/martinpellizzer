Bowlesia reichei

Bowlesia reichei, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in cloud forests. The plant features showy, large flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals and a prominent central stamen. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a hairy texture.

Bowlesia reichei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
