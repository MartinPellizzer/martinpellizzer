Besleria ovoidea

Besleria ovoidea, commonly known as the egg-shaped gesneriad, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, egg-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for greenhouse and indoor gardening.
