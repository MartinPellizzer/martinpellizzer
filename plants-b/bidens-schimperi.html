Bidens schimperi

Bidens schimperi, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small yellow flowers with prominent white markings. Its seeds are encased in a pappus, which aids in wind dispersal.

Bidens schimperi is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
