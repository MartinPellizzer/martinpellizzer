Bromus madritensis var. pubiculmis

Bromus madritensis var. pubiculmis, commonly known as the pubescent brome, is a grass species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The leaves are typically narrow and covered with fine hairs, giving the plant a distinct texture. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

This variety is often found in Mediterranean climates and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
