Begonia discreta

Begonia discreta, commonly known as the discreet begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners for its elegant appearance and ease of care.
