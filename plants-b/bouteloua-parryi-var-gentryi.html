Bouteloua parryi var. gentryi

Bouteloua parryi var. gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's blue grama, is a flowering grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are typical of the grass family. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky habitats. It is drought-tolerant and plays a role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects in its native range.
