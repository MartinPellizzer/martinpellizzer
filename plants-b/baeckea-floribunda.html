Baeckea floribunda

Baeckea floribunda, commonly known as the coastal baeckea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant is well adapted to sandy soils and salt-laden environments, making it a hardy species in coastal ecosystems. It plays an important role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
