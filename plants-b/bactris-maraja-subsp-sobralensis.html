Bactris maraja subsp. sobralensis

Bactris maraja subsp.

sobralensis, commonly known as the Maraja palm, is a species native to Brazil. It is a small to medium-sized palm tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests.

The plant features a slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its fruit is a small, fibrous drupe that is sometimes consumed by local communities.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
