Boerhavia elongata

Boerhavia elongata, commonly known as slender blite, is a flowering plant native to warm temperate and tropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, spreading stems that often root at the nodes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Boerhavia elongata is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes cultivated as a ground cover or for its ornamental value.
