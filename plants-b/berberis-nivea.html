Berberis nivea

Berberis nivea, commonly known as the silver barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silvery-white leaves that provide a striking visual contrast against its dark stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red-orange berries that are attractive to birds. Berberis nivea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.
