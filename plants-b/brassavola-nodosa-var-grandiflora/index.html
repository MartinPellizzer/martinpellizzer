Brassavola Nodosa Var. Grandiflora

Brassavola nodosa var. grandiflora is a striking orchid known for its large, fragrant flowers and elegant appearance. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. Its unique blooms, which can reach up to six inches in diameter, make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. The variety is prized for its ability to bloom reliably under proper care. With its ornamental value and relative ease of cultivation, it remains a favorite in both home and greenhouse settings.
