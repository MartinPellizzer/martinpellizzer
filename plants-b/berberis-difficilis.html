Berberis difficilis

Berberis difficilis, commonly known as the Japanese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive but toxic to humans and animals. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.

However, it can become invasive in some regions due to its rapid growth and seed dispersal.
