Blossfeldia liliputana var. caineana

Blossfeldia liliputana var. caineana, commonly known as the miniature Christmas cactus, is a compact, epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features short, segmented stems that bear clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is prized for its unique, compact growth habit and its ability to bloom during the holiday season. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
