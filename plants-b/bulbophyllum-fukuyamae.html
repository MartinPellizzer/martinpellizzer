Bulbophyllum fukuyamae

Bulbophyllum fukuyamae, commonly known as the Fukuyama's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure and is part of the Orchidaceae family. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often small and fragrant, with a complex arrangement of petals and sepals.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
