Bidens micrantha

Bidens micrantha, commonly known as littleleaf beggarlice, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and thrives in wetlands and along stream banks. Its small, yellow-orange flowers are followed by distinctive bracts that resemble small leaves.

Bidens micrantha is often found in wetland ecosystems and is adapted to environments with high water content.
