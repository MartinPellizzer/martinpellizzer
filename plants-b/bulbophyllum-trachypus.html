Bulbophyllum trachypus

Bulbophyllum trachypus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and strap-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist conditions.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
