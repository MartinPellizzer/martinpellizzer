Butia capitata var. elegantissima

Butia capitata var. elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant palm, is a striking variation of the royal palm.

It is characterized by its slender, elegant trunk and a crown of long, arching leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, sweet fruits that are similar to dates. It is native to the coastal regions of Brazil and is well adapted to warm, sunny climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
