Bupleurum fruticescens var. balansae

Bupleurum fruticescens var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring wiry, branching stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and alternate along the stem, often with a slightly serrated edge.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy choice for xerophytic gardens.
