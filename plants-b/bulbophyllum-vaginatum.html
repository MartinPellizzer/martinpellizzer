Bulbophyllum vaginatum

Bulbophyllum vaginatum, commonly known as the sheath orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a dense cluster of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often forming a sheath around the stem, giving it its common name.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
