Begonia martiana var. gracilis

Begonia martiana var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and vibrant, showy flowers.

Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. The plant features heart-shaped foliage with a glossy texture and produces clusters of pink or red tubular flowers that attract pollinators. It prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture, though it is relatively low maintenance once established.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in hanging baskets or as a tabletop plant.
