Bonarota

Bonarota, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a sacred plant in various cultures, particularly in Hinduism and Buddhism.

It is a water plant that grows in shallow freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. The plant has large, broad leaves that float on the water's surface, with showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. Its roots are submerged in the water, absorbing nutrients from the aquatic environment.

The bonarota is symbolically significant, representing purity and spiritual awakening in many traditions.
