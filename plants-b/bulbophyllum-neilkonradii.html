Bulbophyllum neilkonradii

Bulbophyllum neilkonradii, commonly known as the Neil Konradi's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with rich, well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or orange with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
