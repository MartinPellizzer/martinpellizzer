Bulbophyllum rhodoleucum

Bulbophyllum rhodoleucum, commonly known as the pink-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers with intricate veining patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its tropical habitat. Its flowers typically bloom in shades of pink and white, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

This orchid prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
