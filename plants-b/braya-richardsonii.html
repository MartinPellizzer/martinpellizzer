Braya richardsonii

Braya richardsonii, commonly known as the Richardson's bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, showy flowers.
