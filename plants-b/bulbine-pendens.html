Bulbine pendens

Bulbine pendens, commonly known as the hanging bulbine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features fleshy, strap-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Bulbine pendens is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
