Bolbitis heudelotii

Bolbitis heudelotii, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a thick, fleshy rootstock that stores moisture. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive, elongated shape and a smooth, glossy surface.

Bolbitis heudelotii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
