Bunium tenuisectum

Bunium tenuisectum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed fennel, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in umbrella-like clusters. Bunium tenuisectum is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining soil stability.
