Bidens chaetophylla

Bidens chaetophylla, commonly known as the hairy ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes. Its small yellow flowers bloom in the summer, and it produces distinctive bristly seeds that aid in wind dispersal.
