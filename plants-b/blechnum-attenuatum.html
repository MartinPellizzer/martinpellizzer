Blechnum attenuatum

Blechnum attenuatum, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a species of fern native to various regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to the substrate while absorbing nutrients.

Blechnum attenuatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
