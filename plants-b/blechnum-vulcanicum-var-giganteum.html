Blechnum vulcanicum var. giganteum

Blechnum vulcanicum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a striking fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant features a robust, creeping rhizome that supports its extensive growth habit. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, shield-like appearance.

This variety thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
