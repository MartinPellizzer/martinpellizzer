Blechnum discolor var. bipinnatifidum

Blechnum discolor var. bipinnatifidum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a variant of the Blechnum discolor species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnatifid fronds that are divided into narrow, linear segments. The fronds are typically a striking silver-green color, with a waxy texture that gives them a metallic sheen. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
