Bulbophyllum tokioi f. alboviride

Bulbophyllum tokioi f. alboviride, commonly known as the White-green Bulbophyllum, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with pale green pseudobulbs that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually light green to white in color. Its flowers are small and fragrant, typically appearing in clusters during the rainy season.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
