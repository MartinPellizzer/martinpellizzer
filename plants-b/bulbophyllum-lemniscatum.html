Bulbophyllum lemniscatum

Bulbophyllum lemniscatum, commonly known as the Orchid with the Double Spiral, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in tropical climates and requires high humidity and bright, indirect light.

Bulbophyllum lemniscatum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
