Bidens mildbraedii

Bidens mildbraedii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, with both ray and disk florets. Its leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Bidens mildbraedii is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
