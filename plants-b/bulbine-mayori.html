Bulbine mayori

Bulbine mayori, commonly known as the Cape bulbous plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Bulbine mayori is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
