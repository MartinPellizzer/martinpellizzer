Bulbophyllum gravidum

Bulbophyllum gravidum, commonly known as the heavy-bearing orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
