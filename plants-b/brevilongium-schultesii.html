Brevilongium schultesii

Brevilongium schultesii, commonly known as the Schultesi's spider plant, is a unique member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Brevilongium schultesii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
