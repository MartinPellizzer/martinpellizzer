Berberis claireae

Berberis claireae, commonly known as the Claire's barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorned stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its hardy nature, it is also valued for its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
