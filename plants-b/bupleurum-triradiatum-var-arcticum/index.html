Bupleurum Triradiatum Var. Arcticum

Bupleurum triradiatum var. arcticum is a rare and hardy plant native to the Arctic regions. It thrives in cold, harsh environments where few species can survive. This variant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and pale flowers that bloom in late summer. It plays an important role in Arctic ecosystems by providing food for local wildlife. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from climate change.
