Berberis polypetala

Berberis polypetala, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China and parts of Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate a variety of environmental conditions.
