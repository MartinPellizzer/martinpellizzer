Buddleja oblonga

Buddleja oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and purple. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge. It is well-suited for growing in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
