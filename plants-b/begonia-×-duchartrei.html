Begonia × duchartrei

Begonia × duchartrei, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of ornamental features.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that often display striking patterns of green, red, and silver. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
