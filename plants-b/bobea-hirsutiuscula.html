Bobea hirsutiuscula

Bobea hirsutiuscula, commonly known as the hairy bobea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Its hairy leaves and delicate appearance make it a unique addition to botanical gardens.
