Bauhinia haughtii

Bauhinia haughtii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, delicate leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system.

Bauhinia haughtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.
