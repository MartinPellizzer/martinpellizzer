Benthamidia capitata var. mollis

Benthamidia capitata var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-headed benthamidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
