Braya Pamirica

Braya pamirica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Pamir Mountains. It thrives in harsh, cold environments where few other species can survive. This small perennial is known for its tiny, white or pale pink blossoms that bloom in early summer. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Braya pamirica is crucial for preserving the biodiversity of its alpine habitat.
