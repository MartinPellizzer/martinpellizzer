Burmannia engganensis

Burmannia engganensis, commonly known as the Enggan burmannia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burmanniaceae and is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and distinctive inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in lowland rainforests.
