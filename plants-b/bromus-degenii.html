Bromus degenii

Bromus degenii, commonly known as the Degen's brome, is a species of grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically blooms in the summer, producing small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Bromus degenii is often found in areas with low precipitation and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
