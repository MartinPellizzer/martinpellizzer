Bonamia eustachioi

Bonamia eustachioi, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Bonamia eustachioi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
