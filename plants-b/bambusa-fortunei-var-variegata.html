Bambusa fortunei var. variegata

Bambusa fortunei var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, slender culms that display a distinctive variegated pattern of green, white, and yellow. This bamboo thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Its fast growth rate and ability to form dense clumps make it a popular choice for landscaping and privacy screens.

The plant is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to add a tropical aesthetic to gardens.
