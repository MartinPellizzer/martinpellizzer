Brosimum utile

Brosimum utile, commonly known as the wild silk tree or balsa tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a large, fast-growing tree that can reach heights of up to 30 meters. The plant is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains numerous winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.

The tree is valued for its lightweight wood and has been used by indigenous peoples for various purposes.
