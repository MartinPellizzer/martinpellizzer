Bridelia eranalis

Bridelia eranalis, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, white, spherical flower clusters that resemble snowballs, blooming throughout the year. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance.

Bridelia eranalis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
