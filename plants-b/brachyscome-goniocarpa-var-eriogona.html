Brachyscome goniocarpa var. eriogona

Brachyscome goniocarpa var. eriogona, commonly known as the slender bristle bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, bristled fruit capsules.
