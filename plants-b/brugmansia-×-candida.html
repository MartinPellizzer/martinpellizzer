Brugmansia × candida

Brugmansia × candida, commonly known as angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in various colors such as white, yellow, and orange. The plant is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. Brugmansia × candida is known for its rapid growth and can reach heights of up to 15 meters when grown in its natural habitat.

Its foliage is typically large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green.
