Bignonia surinamensis

Bignonia surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam trumpetflower, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces long, slender, woody stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Bignonia surinamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
