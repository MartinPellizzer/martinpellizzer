Begonia shitoushanensis

Begonia shitoushanensis, commonly known as the Shitoushan begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and vibrant flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red or orange. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and requires consistent moisture.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
