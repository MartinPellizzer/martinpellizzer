Berberis mingetsensis

Berberis mingetsensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

Its berries contain berberine, a compound with various pharmacological uses.
