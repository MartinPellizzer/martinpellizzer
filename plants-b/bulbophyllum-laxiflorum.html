Bulbophyllum laxiflorum

Bulbophyllum laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs. The plant produces slender, often pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are usually alternate, ovate, and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
