Botrychium Dissectum F. Obliquum

Botrychium dissectum f. obliquum is a rare fern species known for its unique leaf structure and subtle beauty. This fern belongs to the Botrychiaceae family and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds display a delicate, dissected pattern that sets it apart from other fern varieties. The varietal form 'obliquum' refers to the asymmetrical arrangement of its leaves. Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
