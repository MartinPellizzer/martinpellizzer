Banalia

Banalia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stalks above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, providing habitat for various aquatic life. Its roots are anchored in the mud at the bottom of ponds and lakes.

Banalia is known for its symbolic significance in many cultures, representing purity and spiritual enlightenment.
