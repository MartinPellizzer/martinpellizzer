Bonplandia heterophylla

Bonplandia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf cycad, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like leaves that vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces large, cone-like structures, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a valuable but vulnerable species in its natural habitat.

Botanists classify it within the Cycadaceae family, highlighting its ancient lineage and ecological significance.
