Bletia crispa var. purpurea

Bletia crispa var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered bletia, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This variant is distinguished by its vibrant purple petals and sepals, which contrast with its greenish-yellow lip. It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often as an epiphyte on trees. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its habitat.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
