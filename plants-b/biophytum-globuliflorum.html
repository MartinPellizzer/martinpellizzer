Biophytum globuliflorum

Biophytum globuliflorum, commonly known as the globular flower plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain water in arid environments. It produces tiny, globular fruits that contain seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Biophytum globuliflorum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
