Boronia anemonifolia

Boronia anemonifolia, commonly known as the anemonifolium boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, with a distinctive anise-like scent.

Boronia anemonifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive blooms and aromatic foliage.
