Begonia gungshanensis

Begonia gungshanensis, commonly known as the Gungshan begonia, is a species native to China, specifically the province of Yunnan.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its botanical features include a short stem, opposite leaves, and flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Begonia gungshanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
