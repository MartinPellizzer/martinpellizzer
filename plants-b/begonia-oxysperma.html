Begonia oxysperma

Begonia oxysperma, commonly known as the sharp-leaved begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Begonia oxysperma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and modest blooms.
