Bletia alta

Bletia alta, commonly known as the tall bletia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. Bletia alta thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomatous growth allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
