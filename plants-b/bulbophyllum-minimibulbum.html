Bulbophyllum minimibulbum

Bulbophyllum minimibulbum, commonly known as the miniature bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, storing water and nutrients for periods of drought.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
