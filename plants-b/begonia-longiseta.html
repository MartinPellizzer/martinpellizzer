Begonia longiseta

Begonia longiseta, commonly known as the long-hair begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Begonia longiseta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.
