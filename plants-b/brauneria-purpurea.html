Brauneria purpurea

Brauneria purpurea, commonly known as the purple brauneria, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Brauneria purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
