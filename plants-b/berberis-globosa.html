Berberis globosa

Berberis globosa, commonly known as the globe barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and small, rounded leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscaping.
