Baccharis jelskii var. cladotricha

Baccharis jelskii var. cladotricha, commonly known as the silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
