Boronia yarrowmerensis

Boronia yarrowmerensis, commonly known as the Yarrowmeren boronia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub with small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Boronia yarrowmerensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
