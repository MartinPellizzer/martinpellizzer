Billardiera speciosa

Billardiera speciosa, commonly known as the wattle vine or silver wattle, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and cream. The leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and often covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a ground cover.
