Besleria standleyi

Besleria standleyi, commonly known as the Crimson Trumpet.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular, red flowers that bloom in clusters. It has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often dark green in color.

Besleria standleyi is typically grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
