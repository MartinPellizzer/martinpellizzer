Balaka reineckei

Balaka reineckei, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. Its flowers are typically borne on upright stems, and it is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant.

Balaka reineckei is known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper care.
