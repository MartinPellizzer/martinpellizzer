Bremeria humblotii

Bremeria humblotii, commonly known as the dwarf rock cress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically features small, rounded leaves and clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
