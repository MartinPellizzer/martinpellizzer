Bulbophyllum brachypodum var. parviflorum

Bulbophyllum brachypodum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
