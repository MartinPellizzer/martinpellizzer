Brickellia hinckleyi var. terlinguensis

Brickellia hinckleyi var. terlinguensis, commonly known as the Terlinguensis brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often glandular. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
