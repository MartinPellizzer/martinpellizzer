Brunfelsia linearis

Brunfelsia linearis, commonly known as the linear brugmansia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshade plants. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It produces berries that contain toxic seeds, making it important to handle with care.

Brunfelsia linearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and tropical landscapes.
