Billbergia magnifica var. acutisepala

Billbergia magnifica var. acutisepala, commonly known as the Acute-lobed Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescences that can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
