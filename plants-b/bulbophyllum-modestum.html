Bulbophyllum modestum

Bulbophyllum modestum, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum modestum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
