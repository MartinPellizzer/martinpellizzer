Bulbophyllum jingdongense

Bulbophyllum jingdongense, commonly known as the Jingdong orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence and intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocky surfaces. Its flowers are small but striking, featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
