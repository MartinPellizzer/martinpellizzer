Bulbophyllum dissitiflorum

Bulbophyllum dissitiflorum, commonly known as the clustered-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces green to reddish stems with elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

This orchid is often grown as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
