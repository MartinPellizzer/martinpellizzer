Brachyglottis × christensenii

Brachyglottis × christensenii, commonly known as the Christensen's brachyglottis, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a low-growing shrub that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
