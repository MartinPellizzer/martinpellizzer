Brachyscome multifida var. dilatata

Brachyscome multifida var. dilatata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed everlasting flower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves and is often grown as a garden ornamental due to its long-lasting blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is prized for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
