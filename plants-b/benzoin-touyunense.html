Benzoin touyunense

Benzoin touyunense, commonly known as Yunnan benzoin, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Yunnan province in China.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, resinous exudate, which is often harvested for its aromatic properties. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments at moderate elevations. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface, while its flowers are small and inconspicuous.

The resin, known as benzoin, has been traditionally used in incense and traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic benefits.
