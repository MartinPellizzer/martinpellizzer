Bakeridesia lanata

Bakeridesia lanata, commonly known as the woolly bakeridesia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Bakeridesia lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
