Bulbostylis puberula var. gracilis

Bulbostylis puberula var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its compact stature and narrow leaves. The plant produces tiny, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white or pale green flowers. It typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and is often found in low-lying areas with poor drainage.
