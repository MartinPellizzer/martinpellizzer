Bonplandia pentagyna

Bonplandia pentagyna, commonly known as the five-angled bonplandiace, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Bonplandiaceae and is characterized by its unique, pentagonal-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with five petals.
