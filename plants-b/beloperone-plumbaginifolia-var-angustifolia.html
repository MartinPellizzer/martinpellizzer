Beloperone plumbaginifolia var. angustifolia

Beloperone plumbaginifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved plumbag, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the family Plumbaginaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

The species is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
