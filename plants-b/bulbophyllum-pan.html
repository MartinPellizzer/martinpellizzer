Bulbophyllum pan

Bulbophyllum pan, commonly known as the pan orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native tropical environment.

Bulbophyllum pan is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
