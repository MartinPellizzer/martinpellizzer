Bastardia bivalvis

Bastardia bivalvis, commonly known as the two-valved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial species that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to store water in harsh environments.

Bastardia bivalvis is often found in disturbed habitats such as dunes, roadsides, and coastal dunes.
