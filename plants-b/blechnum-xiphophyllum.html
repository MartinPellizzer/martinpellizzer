Blechnum xiphophyllum

Blechnum xiphophyllum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Blechnum xiphophyllum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
