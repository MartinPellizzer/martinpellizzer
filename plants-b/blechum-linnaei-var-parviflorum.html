Blechum linnaei var. parviflorum

Blechum linnaei var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered blechum, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant features slender, hairy stems and compound leaves with a distinctive pinnate structure. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.
