Brassaiopsis hainla

Brassaiopsis hainla, commonly known as the Hainan brassaiopsis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and calla lilies. The plant produces striking, spatulate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
