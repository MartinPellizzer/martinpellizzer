Brunia teres

Brunia teres, commonly known as the narrow-leaved brunia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically a glossy green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often used in horticultural displays.
