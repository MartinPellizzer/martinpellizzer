Bignonia hortensis

Bignonia hortensis, commonly known as the yellow glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a rough texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to full sun and well-drained soil.
