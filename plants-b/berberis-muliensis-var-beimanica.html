Berberis muliensis var. beimanica

Berberis muliensis var. beimanica, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It features spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves that are typically arranged in clusters. The plant produces bright orange-red berries that are edible but have a sharp, astringent taste. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
