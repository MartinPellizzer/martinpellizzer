Billbergia speciosa

Billbergia speciosa, commonly known as the royal billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Billbergia speciosa is also known for its ability to bloom repeatedly, adding long-lasting ornamental value to any collection.
