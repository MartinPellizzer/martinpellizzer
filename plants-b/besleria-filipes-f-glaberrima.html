Besleria filipes f. glaberrima

Besleria filipes f. glaberrima, commonly known as the glabrous filipes begonia, is a hybrid form of the species Besleria filipes.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features striking, elongated leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. Its flowers are typically small, tubular, and borne in clusters, often in shades of red or orange.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
