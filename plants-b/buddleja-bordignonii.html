Buddleja bordignonii

Buddleja bordignonii, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of fragrant, pale purple to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and often have a slight hairy texture. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.

Buddleja bordignonii is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
