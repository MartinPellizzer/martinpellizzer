Blumea membranacea var. subsimplex

Blumea membranacea var. subsimplex, commonly known as the narrow-leafed blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the wet season.
