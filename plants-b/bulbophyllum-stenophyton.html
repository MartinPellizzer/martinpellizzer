Bulbophyllum stenophyton

Bulbophyllum stenophyton, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum stenophyton is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
