Bulbophyllum papilligerum

Bulbophyllum papilligerum, commonly known as the hairy-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy lips and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum papilligerum is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
