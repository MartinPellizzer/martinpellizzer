Begonia flaccidissima

Begonia flaccidissima, commonly known as the hanging begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among gardeners seeking to add visual interest to hanging baskets or shelves.
