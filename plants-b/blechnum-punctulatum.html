Blechnum punctulatum

Blechnum punctulatum, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern native to various regions in the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping stems that can form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically pinnately divided, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

Blechnum punctulatum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
