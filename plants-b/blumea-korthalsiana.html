Blumea korthalsiana

Blumea korthalsiana, commonly known as the slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

The yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the rainy season, attracting various pollinators.
