Bouteloua major

Bouteloua major, commonly known as grama grass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open prairies, woodlands, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its seed heads are cylindrical and often turn a reddish-brown color as they mature.

Bouteloua major is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in native landscapes.
