Bromus mathewsii

Bromus mathewsii, commonly known as Mathews' brome, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
