Barleria pungens

Barleria pungens, commonly known as red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in dense spikes, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for dry climates.

Barleria pungens is also known for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and is sometimes used in coastal landscaping.
