Brassica campestris proles rapa

Brassica campestris proles rapa, commonly known as Chinese kale or kale, is a biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves that are often curled or crinkled, and it produces small, yellow flowers in the spring. The plant is native to East Asia and is cultivated for its edible leaves and stems. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Brassica campestris proles rapa is a hardy plant that is resistant to pests and diseases, making it a popular choice for home gardens and commercial farming.
