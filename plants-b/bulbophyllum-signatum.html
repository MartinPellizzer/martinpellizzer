Bulbophyllum signatum

Bulbophyllum signatum, commonly known as the signatum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a sympodial growth habit and elongated pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
