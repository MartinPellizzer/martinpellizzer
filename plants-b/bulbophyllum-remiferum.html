Bulbophyllum remiferum

Bulbophyllum remiferum, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and delicate, feather-like leaves. The plant produces intricate inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
