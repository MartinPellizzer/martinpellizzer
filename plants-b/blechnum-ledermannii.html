Blechnum ledermannii

Blechnum ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its distinctive, leathery, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a serrated margin and a deep green color.

Blechnum ledermannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
