Berberis laojunshanensis

Berberis laojunshanensis, commonly known as the Laojunshan barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that mature in autumn. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
