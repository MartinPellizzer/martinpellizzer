Bromus inermis f. bulbifer

Bromus inermis f. bulbifer, commonly known as the bulbous brome, is a variant of the common brome grass.

It is characterized by its bulbous base, which stores nutrients and helps the plant survive in harsh conditions. The plant produces dense, grass-like foliage with slender, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

This form is notable for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
