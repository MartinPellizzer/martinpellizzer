Blechnum australe var. angustifolium

Blechnum australe var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved holly fern, is a species of fern native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to form dense mats or clumps. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rock crevices.

Its foliage is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
