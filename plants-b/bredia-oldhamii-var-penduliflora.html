Bredia oldhamii var. penduliflora

Bredia oldhamii var. penduliflora, commonly known as the hanging-flowered bredia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, pendulous inflorescences that bear small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has smooth, glabrous stems and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
