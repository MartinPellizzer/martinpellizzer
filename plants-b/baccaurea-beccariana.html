Baccaurea beccariana

Baccaurea beccariana, commonly known as the Beccarian starfruit, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often compared to starfruits in appearance. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth texture.
