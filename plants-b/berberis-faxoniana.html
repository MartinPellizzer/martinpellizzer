Berberis faxoniana

Berberis faxoniana, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible for birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Berberis faxoniana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
