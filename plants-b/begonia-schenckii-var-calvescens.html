Begonia schenckii var. calvescens

Begonia schenckii var. calvescens, commonly known as the Calvescens begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to purplish hues. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in shaded garden beds or as a houseplant.
