Bulbophyllum kewense var. purpureum

Bulbophyllum kewense var. purpureum, commonly known as the Purple Kew Orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green foliage, which often features intricate veining patterns. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orchid-like flowers with a unique, sometimes speckled appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
