Barleria pentandra

Barleria pentandra, commonly known as red spurflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular red flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Barleria pentandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
