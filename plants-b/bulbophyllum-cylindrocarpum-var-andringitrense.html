Bulbophyllum cylindrocarpum var. andringitrense

Bulbophyllum cylindrocarpum var. andringitrense, commonly known as the Andringitra bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat.

This variety is noted for its unique floral morphology and is of interest to orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
