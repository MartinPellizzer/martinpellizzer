Berkheya grandiflora var. natalensis

Berkheya grandiflora var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal's yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has oval, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, helping it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and compact growth habit.
