Begonia prionophylla

Begonia prionophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The leaves are typically ovate with a waxy texture, and the plant prefers bright, indirect light. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and is often grown as a decorative houseplant.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
