Begonia baik

Begonia baik, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a contrasting color pattern. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Begonia baik is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
