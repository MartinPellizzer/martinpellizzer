Bomarea glaucescens

Bomarea glaucescens, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces tendrils that help it climb and spread. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
