Bletia grandis

Bletia grandis, commonly known as the large bletia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight purplish tinge. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in a raceme. Bletia grandis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
