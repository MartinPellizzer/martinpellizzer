Bambusa sinospinosa var. inermis

Bambusa sinospinosa var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glabrous. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and construction due to its strong and durable structure.
