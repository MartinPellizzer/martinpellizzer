Bletia parviflora

Bletia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in a spike. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.

Bletia parviflora is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
