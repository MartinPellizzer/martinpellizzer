Begonia joshii

Begonia joshii, commonly known as the Joshii begonia, is a compact, trailing perennial native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features vibrant, velvety foliage with deep green leaves that have a slight pubescence, and its flowers are typically red or orange, with five to seven petals. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and delicate blooms, which make it a sought-after addition to tropical and subtropical gardens.
