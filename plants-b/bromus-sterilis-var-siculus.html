Bromus sterilis var. siculus

Bromus sterilis var. siculus, commonly known as Sicilian annual brome, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Due to its adaptability, it can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
