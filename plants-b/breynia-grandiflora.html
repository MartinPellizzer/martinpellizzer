Breynia grandiflora

Breynia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered breynia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Breynia grandiflora is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
