Bikkia neriifolia

Bikkia neriifolia, commonly known as the black-leaved bikkia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

Bikkia neriifolia is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, nutrient-rich soils.
