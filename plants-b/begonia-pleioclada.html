Begonia pleioclada

Begonia pleioclada, commonly known as the clustered begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often in terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in horticulture for hanging baskets and container gardening.
