Balanites suckertii

Balanites suckertii, commonly known as the African burr bush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces hard, woody fruits that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a food source. Its deep root system allows it to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought.

Balanites suckertii plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
