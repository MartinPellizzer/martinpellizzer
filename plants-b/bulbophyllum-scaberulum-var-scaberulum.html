Bulbophyllum scaberulum var. scaberulum

Bulbophyllum scaberulum var. scaberulum, commonly known as the rough-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring narrow, linear leaves that are covered in a rough, textured surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is appreciated for its unique foliage and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
