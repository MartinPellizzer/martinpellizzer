Boerhavia reboudiana

Boerhavia reboudiana, commonly known as redroot sandplum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are typically succulent and fleshy. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in dry conditions.

Boerhavia reboudiana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
