Barleria cunenensis

Barleria cunenensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or red, arranged in dense clusters.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
