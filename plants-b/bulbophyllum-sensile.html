Bulbophyllum sensile

Bulbophyllum sensile, commonly known as the sensitive orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
