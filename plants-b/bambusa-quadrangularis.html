Bambusa quadrangularis

Bambusa quadrangularis, commonly known as the square bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinct four-sided stems, which give it its common name. The plant is known for its fast growth rate and strong, durable culms, making it suitable for various practical applications. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Bambusa quadrangularis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
