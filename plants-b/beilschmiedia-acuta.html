Beilschmiedia acuta

Beilschmiedia acuta, commonly known as the sharp-leafed beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, orange-red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife.
