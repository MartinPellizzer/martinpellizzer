Bupleurum longicaule var. longicaule

Bupleurum longicaule var. longicaule, commonly known as the slender valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in loose clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.

This species is often found in dry meadows, woodland edges, and rocky soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.
