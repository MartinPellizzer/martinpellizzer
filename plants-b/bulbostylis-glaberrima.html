Bulbostylis glaberrima

Bulbostylis glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth-styled bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in wetland or marshy habitats. It produces small, white or pale green flowers that are typically clustered at the tips of its stems.

The plant is often found in swampy areas and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soil conditions.
