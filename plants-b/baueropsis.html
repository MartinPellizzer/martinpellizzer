Baueropsis

Baueropsis, commonly known as the star anise plant, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit, which is widely used in traditional medicine and as a spice. The plant belongs to the Schisandraceae family and is known for its essential oils and bioactive compounds. Its leaves are also used in herbal remedies for their purported health benefits.

Baueropsis thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
