Bipinnula fimbriata

Bipinnula fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features a slender, creeping rhizome and produces small, delicate fronds with finely divided, feathery leaves. The leaves are typically bright green and have a distinctive fringed appearance along the edges. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

This plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
