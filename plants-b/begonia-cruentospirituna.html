Begonia cruentospirituna

Begonia cruentospirituna, commonly known as the crimson spirit begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a deep red underside, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The species produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is popular among plant enthusiasts and gardeners.
