Begonia alchemilloides

Begonia alchemilloides, commonly known as the alchemilla begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its delicate blooms.
