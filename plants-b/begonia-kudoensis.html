Begonia kudoensis

Begonia kudoensis, commonly known as the Kudo begonia, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically red or pink in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
