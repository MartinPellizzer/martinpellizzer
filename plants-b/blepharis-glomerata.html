Blepharis glomerata

Blepharis glomerata, commonly known as clustered tasselweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Blepharis glomerata is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a hardy, low-maintenance plant for xeriscaping.
