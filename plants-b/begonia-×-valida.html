Begonia × valida

Begonia × valida, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant celebrated for its vibrant and diverse foliage.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking leaves, which often display intricate patterns and vivid colors. The plant typically produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in spring or early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements, Begonia × valida is widely used in gardens and as a houseplant.
