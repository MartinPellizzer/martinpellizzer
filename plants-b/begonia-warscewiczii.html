Begonia warscewiczii

Begonia warscewiczii, commonly known as the Warszewicz begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that often resemble flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Begonia warscewiczii is also valued for its ability to adapt to various growing conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
