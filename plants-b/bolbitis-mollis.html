Bolbitis mollis

Bolbitis mollis, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in peat bogs. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store moisture efficiently.

Bolbitis mollis is also known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
