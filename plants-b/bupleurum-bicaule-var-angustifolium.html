Bupleurum bicaule var. angustifolium

Bupleurum bicaule var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leafed bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments and has slender, wiry stems that often branch near the base. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the spring.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
