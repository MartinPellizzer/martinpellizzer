Brachyscome angustifolia var. heterophylla

Brachyscome angustifolia var. heterophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting daisy, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in various climates.
