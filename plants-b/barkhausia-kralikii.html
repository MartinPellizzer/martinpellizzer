Barkhausia kralikii

Barkhausia kralikii, commonly known as the Kralik's barkhausia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. Barkhausia kralikii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its appeal in horticultural settings.
