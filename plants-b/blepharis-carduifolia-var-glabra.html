Blepharis carduifolia var. glabra

Blepharis carduifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth blepharis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are smooth and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is adapted to arid conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
