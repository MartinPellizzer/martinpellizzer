Begonia turugsoy

Begonia turugsoy, commonly known as the Turugsoy begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and can grow in both upright and trailing forms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia turugsoy is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
