Bulbophyllum hirtum

Bulbophyllum hirtum, commonly known as the hairy lip orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, hairy, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Bulbophyllum hirtum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
