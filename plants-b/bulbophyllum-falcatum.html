Bulbophyllum falcatum

Bulbophyllum falcatum, commonly known as the cat's tail orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and drooping, linear leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
