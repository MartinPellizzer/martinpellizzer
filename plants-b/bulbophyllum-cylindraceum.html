Bulbophyllum cylindraceum

Bulbophyllum cylindraceum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a dense spike. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate blooms, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
