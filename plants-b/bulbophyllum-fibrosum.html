Bulbophyllum fibrosum

Bulbophyllum fibrosum, commonly known as the fibrous orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and hairy, fibrous roots. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
