Blepharoglossum firmum

Blepharoglossum firmum, commonly known as the silver hair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are covered in fine, silvery scales, giving it a distinctive appearance. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread slowly and form dense mats.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
