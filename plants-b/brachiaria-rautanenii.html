Brachiaria rautanenii

Brachiaria rautanenii, commonly known as Rautanen's blady grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland habitats.

Brachiaria rautanenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
