Bikkia commerconiana

Bikkia commerconiana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Bikkia commerconiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
