Bulbophyllum cochleatum

Bulbophyllum cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Bulbophyllum cochleatum is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
