Begonia palmata var. laevifolia

Begonia palmata var. laevifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy.

The foliage often exhibits a vibrant green hue with subtle variegation, adding visual interest to any setting. It produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. This variety thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and home interiors.
