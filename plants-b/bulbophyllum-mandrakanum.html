Bulbophyllum mandrakanum

Bulbophyllum mandrakanum, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the island of Malta.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often reddish-brown pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a complex structure, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in its native region.
