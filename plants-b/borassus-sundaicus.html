Borassus sundaicus

Borassus sundaicus, commonly known as the Sunda palm, is a tall, robust palm native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender trunk covered in persistent leaf bases, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible fruits with a fibrous husk.

The Sunda palm is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local cultures.
