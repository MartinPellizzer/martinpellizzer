Bromus brongniartii

Bromus brongniartii, commonly known as the Brongniart's brome, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Bromus brongniartii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
