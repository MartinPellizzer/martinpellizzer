Barleria grandicalyx

Barleria grandicalyx, commonly known as the flame flower or red barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking clusters of tubular, red or orange-red flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological significance.
