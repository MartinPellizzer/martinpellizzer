Begonia ealensis

Begonia ealensis, commonly known as the Ealens begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are usually red or pink, with intricate patterns on the petals, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Begonia ealensis thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
