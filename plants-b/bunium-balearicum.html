Bunium balearicum

Bunium balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic fennel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like parsley and dill. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbels. Its seeds are small and oval, with a distinctive aromatic scent.
