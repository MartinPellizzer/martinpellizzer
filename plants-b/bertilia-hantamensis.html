Bertilia hantamensis

Bertilia hantamensis, commonly known as the Hantam daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky and sandy soils, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, with a daisy-like appearance, and it blooms during the cooler months.

Bertilia hantamensis is often found in the Western and Northern Cape provinces of South Africa.
