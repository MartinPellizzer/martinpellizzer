Berberis jiangxiensis

Berberis jiangxiensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China, specifically the Jiangxi province.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves that are typically yellow-green in color. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
