Basanacantha reticulata

Basanacantha reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its adaptive features, it is considered a resilient species capable of surviving in arid conditions.
