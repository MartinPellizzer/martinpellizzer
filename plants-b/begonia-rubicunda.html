Begonia rubicunda

Begonia rubicunda, commonly known as the red begonia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their striking coloration.

Begonia rubicunda prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.
