Berchemia lineata

Berchemia lineata, commonly known as the silverleaf berchemia, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
