Bupleurum longeradiatum var. shikotanense

Bupleurum longeradiatum var. shikotanense, commonly known as Shikotan angelica, is a perennial herb native to the Korean Peninsula and southern Japan.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and have a distinct, slightly bitter aroma.
