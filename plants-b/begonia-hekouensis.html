Begonia hekouensis

Begonia hekouensis, commonly known as the Hekou begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
