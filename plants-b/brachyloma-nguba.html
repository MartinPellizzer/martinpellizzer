Brachyloma nguba

Brachyloma nguba, commonly known as the Nguba rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in open, sun-exposed areas.

Brachyloma nguba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
