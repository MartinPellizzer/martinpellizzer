Begonia esquirolii

Begonia esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and a wide range of colors. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range from red to pink, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners who appreciate its unique foliage and floral display.
