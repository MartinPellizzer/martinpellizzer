Blechnum ornifolium

Blechnum ornifolium, commonly known as the ornate spleenwort, is a fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its fronds are usually elongated and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Blechnum ornifolium is often found in association with other epiphytic and lithophytic plants in its native habitat.
