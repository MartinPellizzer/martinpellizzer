Bulbophyllum setilabium

Bulbophyllum setilabium, commonly known as the long-styled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive labellum that sets it apart from other orchid species. It typically grows as an epiphyte on trees, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
