Begonia × rubellina

Begonia × rubellina, commonly known as the red-leaf begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits striking foliage with deep red to maroon hues.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant coloration. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
