Begonia holmnielseniana

Begonia holmnielseniana, commonly known as the Holm Nielsen begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts worldwide.
