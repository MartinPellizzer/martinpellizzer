Berberis chingii

Berberis chingii, commonly known as Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Berberis chingii is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
