Briza media f. albida

Briza media f. albida, commonly known as the white feather grass, is a cultivar of the common oatgrass.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that are predominantly white in color. This plant is typically found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.

The white feather grass is a hardy perennial that adds a soft, airy texture to garden landscapes.
