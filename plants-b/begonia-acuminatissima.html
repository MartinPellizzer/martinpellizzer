Begonia acuminatissima

Begonia acuminatissima, commonly known as the sharp-pointed begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and garden displays.
