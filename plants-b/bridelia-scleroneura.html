Bridelia scleroneura

Bridelia scleroneura, commonly known as the rough-leaved bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and alternate, oblong leaves with a rough texture. Bridelia scleroneura is also noted for its potential medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
