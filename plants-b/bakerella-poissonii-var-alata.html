Bakerella poissonii var. alata

Bakerella poissonii var. alata, commonly known as the winged bakerella, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
