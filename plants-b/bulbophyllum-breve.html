Bulbophyllum breve

Bulbophyllum breve, commonly known as the short bulb orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the foliage and flowering stems.

Bulbophyllum breve is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
