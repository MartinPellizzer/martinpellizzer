Begonia fusialata var. fusialata

Begonia fusialata var. fusialata, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, velvety hairs. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow quite large, often reaching up to 15 centimeters in diameter. It produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
