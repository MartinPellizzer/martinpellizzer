Berberis globosa var. densa

Berberis globosa var. densa, commonly known as the dense barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and hedges.

Due to its hardiness and attractive appearance, it is widely cultivated in temperate climates.
