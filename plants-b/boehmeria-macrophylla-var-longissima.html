Boehmeria macrophylla var. longissima

Boehmeria macrophylla var. longissima, commonly known as giant arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
