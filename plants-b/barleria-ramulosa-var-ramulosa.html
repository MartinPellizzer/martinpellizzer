Barleria ramulosa var. ramulosa

Barleria ramulosa var. ramulosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barleria, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy texture. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface and a pointed tip. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple, which bloom from spring to summer.

This plant is often cultivated as a ornamental due to its attractive foliage and vibrant flowering display.
