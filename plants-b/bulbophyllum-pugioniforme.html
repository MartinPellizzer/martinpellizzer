Bulbophyllum pugioniforme

Bulbophyllum pugioniforme, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, appearing in clusters along the stem.

Bulbophyllum pugioniforme is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
