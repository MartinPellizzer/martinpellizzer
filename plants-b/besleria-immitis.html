Besleria immitis

Besleria immitis, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often with a waxy texture.

The flowers bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
