Beilschmiedia macrophylla

Beilschmiedia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Beilschmiedia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
