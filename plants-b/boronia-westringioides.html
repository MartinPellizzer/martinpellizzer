Boronia westringioides

Boronia westringioides, commonly known as the Westringioides boronia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, often in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, with a distinctive citrus-like scent when crushed.
