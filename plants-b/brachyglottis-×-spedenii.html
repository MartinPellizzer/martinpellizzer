Brachyglottis × spedenii

Brachyglottis × spedenii, commonly known as the Spedden's brachyglottis, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically has silver-green, woolly leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas with a temperate climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
