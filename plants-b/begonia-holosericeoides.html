Begonia holosericeoides

Begonia holosericeoides, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, silver-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
