Ballota lanata

Ballota lanata, commonly known as woolly ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the summer months. Ballota lanata is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

It is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
