Bellis natalis-jesu

Bellis natalis-jesu, commonly known as the Christmas daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with a central yellow disk, typical of the genus Bellis. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
