Biophytum peruvianum

Biophytum peruvianum, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub native to South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. Its flowers are typically small and pale pink to white, appearing in clusters.
