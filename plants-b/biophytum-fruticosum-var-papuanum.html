Biophytum fruticosum var. papuanum

Biophytum fruticosum var. papuanum, commonly known as the Papuan spiderplant, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Papua and New Guinea.

It features striking, waxy, dark green leaves that are elongated and often have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
