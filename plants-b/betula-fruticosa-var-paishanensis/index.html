Betula Fruticosa Var. Paishanensis

Betula fruticosa var. paishanensis is a rare and lesser-known species of birch native to the mountainous regions of China. This shrub-like variant exhibits unique morphological traits that distinguish it from other members of the Betula genus. Its discovery and classification have sparked interest among botanists due to its potential ecological significance. The plant thrives in cold, high-altitude environments where few species can survive. Studying Betula fruticosa var. paishanensis offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in extreme climates.
