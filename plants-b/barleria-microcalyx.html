Barleria microcalyx

Barleria microcalyx, commonly known as small-flowered barleria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
