Bursa bremensis

Bursa bremensis, commonly known as the Bremen bur, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, bladder-like seed pods that encase its seeds, giving it its common name. It is native to Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Bursa bremensis is typically found in temperate climates and is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types.
