Bulbophyllum distans

Bulbophyllum distans, commonly known as the distant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, with white or pale yellow petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum distans is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
