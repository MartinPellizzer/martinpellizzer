Bulbophyllum latipes

Bulbophyllum latipes, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinct fragrance. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
