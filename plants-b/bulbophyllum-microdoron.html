Bulbophyllum microdoron

Bulbophyllum microdoron, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. Its rhizome is short and thick, with numerous small pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
