Babiana atrocyanea

Babiana atrocyanea, commonly known as the black-flowered babiana, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which contrast beautifully with its grass-like foliage. The plant produces multiple erect flowering stems that bear clusters of tubular flowers, often with a distinctive blue or purple hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Babiana atrocyanea is a hardy perennial that is well-suited to Mediterranean climates and requires minimal water once established.
