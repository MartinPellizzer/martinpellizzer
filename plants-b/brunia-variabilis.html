Brunia variabilis

Brunia variabilis, commonly known as the variable brunia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a bluish-green coloration.

Brunia variabilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
