Bowlesia lobata var. chiclensis

Bowlesia lobata var. chiclensis, commonly known as the Mexican orchid.

It is a species of orchid native to Mexico, characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The flowers, which bloom in spring, are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip structure.
