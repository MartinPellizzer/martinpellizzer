Brossaea venusta

Brossaea venusta, commonly known as the silverleaf brossaea, is a rare and striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to the arid regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after specimen for botanical gardens and collectors.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
