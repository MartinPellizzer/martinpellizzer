Begonia phutthaii

Begonia phutthaii, commonly known as the Thai begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Thailand.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
