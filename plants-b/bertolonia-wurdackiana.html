Bertolonia wurdackiana

Bertolonia wurdackiana, commonly known as the Wurdack's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. Bertolonia wurdackiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
