Brugmansia lowii

Brugmansia lowii, commonly known as the yellow angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Brugmansia lowii is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its rapid growth and ornamental value in tropical gardens.
