Bulbophyllum suavissimum

Bulbophyllum suavissimum, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive, sweet aroma.

Bulbophyllum suavissimum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique fragrance and visual appeal.
