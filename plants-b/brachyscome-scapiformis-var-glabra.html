Brachyscome scapiformis var. glabra

Brachyscome scapiformis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth brachyscome, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to southern Australia.

It features small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, hairless leaves that are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and attractive flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
