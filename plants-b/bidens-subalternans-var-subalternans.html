Bidens subalternans var. subalternans

Bidens subalternans var. subalternans, commonly known as the swamp bidens, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments in the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small yellow flowers with prominent white markings, which bloom from late spring to early fall. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

This species is adapted to periodic flooding and plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems.
