Banisteria mediterranea

Banisteria mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean banisteria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Banisteria mediterranea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
