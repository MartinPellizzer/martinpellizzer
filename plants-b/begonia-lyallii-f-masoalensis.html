Begonia lyallii f. masoalensis

Begonia lyallii f. masoalensis, commonly known as the Masoalensis Begonia, is a unique variant of the Begonia lyallii species native to Madagascar.

This plant is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens. The leaves are often heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and the plant produces small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists.
