Buddleja lanceolata

Buddleja lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf buddleja, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, clustered flowers in shades of pale purple to lilac, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
