Bulbophyllum nutans var. nanum

Bulbophyllum nutans var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf nodding orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features compact, hairy pseudobulbs that support its diminutive leaves and inflorescences. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
