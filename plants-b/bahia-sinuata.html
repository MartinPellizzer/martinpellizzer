Bahia sinuata

Bahia sinuata, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of purple and blue. The plant is a climbing vine that produces showy flowers throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Bahia sinuata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
