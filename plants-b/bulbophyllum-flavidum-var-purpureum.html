Bulbophyllum flavidum var. purpureum

Bulbophyllum flavidum var. purpureum, commonly known as the Purple-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking purple-pink floral display. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and are notable for their intricate patterns and vibrant coloration.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
