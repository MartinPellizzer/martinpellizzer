Buena heterophylla

Buena heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf plant, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its unique variation in leaf shape and size, which can range from broad and oval to narrow and linear. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Buena heterophylla is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that is well adapted to arid environments.
