Bromheadia gracilis

Bromheadia gracilis, commonly known as the slender bromheadia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Bromheadia gracilis typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.
