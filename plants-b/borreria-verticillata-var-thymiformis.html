Borreria verticillata var. thymiformis

Borreria verticillata var. thymiformis, commonly known as the thyme-like borreria, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage that resembles thyme. The plant produces small, clustered white to pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and fragrant leaves.
