Barbacenia hirtiflora

Barbacenia hirtiflora, commonly known as the hairy-flowered barbacenia, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red throat. The plant features hairy foliage and stems, which contribute to its common name. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Barbacenia hirtiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
