Bletilla formosana f. rubrolabella

Bletilla formosana f. rubrolabella, commonly known as the red-labellum orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky substrates. Its foliage consists of broad, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant coloration make it a prized specimen in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.
