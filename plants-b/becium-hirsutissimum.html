Becium hirsutissimum

Becium hirsutissimum, commonly known as the hairy becium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive hairy foliage and yellow flowers. The hairy becium is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
