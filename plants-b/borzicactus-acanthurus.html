Borzicactus acanthurus

Borzicactus acanthurus, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and bright yellow to green coloration, which can vary depending on environmental conditions. The plant features a thick, ribbed stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its flowers, which are typically yellow or orange, bloom during the dry season and are attractively shaped.

Borzicactus acanthurus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
