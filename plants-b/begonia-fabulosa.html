Begonia fabulosa

Begonia fabulosa, commonly known as the fairy begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often featuring heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its foliage is often as ornamental as its blooms, making it a popular choice for garden displays.

Begonia fabulosa thrives in shady, moist environments and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
