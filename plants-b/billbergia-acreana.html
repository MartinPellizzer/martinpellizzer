Billbergia acreana

Billbergia acreana, commonly known as the Amazonian billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features long, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a water-holding structure that supports various microorganisms and small animals. Billbergia acreana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or in rocky crevices.

Its unique structure and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological studies.
