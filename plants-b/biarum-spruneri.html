Biarum spruneri

Biarum spruneri, commonly known as the dwarf white trillium, is a delicate flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its three heart-shaped leaves and a single white flower with three petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodlands and thrives in cool, humid climates. Biarum spruneri is a herbaceous perennial that blooms in early spring.
