Begonia parvuliflora

Begonia parvuliflora, commonly known as the miniature begonia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for container gardening.

Due to its small size and ornamental appeal, it is often used in rock gardens or as a bedding plant.
