Blechnum kunthianum

Blechnum kunthianum, commonly known as the broad-leaf harts-tongue fern, is a species of fern native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The fronds are usually evergreen, providing a lush, green appearance throughout the year.
