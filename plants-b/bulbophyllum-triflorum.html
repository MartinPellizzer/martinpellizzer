Bulbophyllum triflorum

Bulbophyllum triflorum, commonly known as the three-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure. The plant produces three flowers per inflorescence, which are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum triflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
