Baeckea diffusa var. striata

Baeckea diffusa var. striata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf baeckea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer. It is often found in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
