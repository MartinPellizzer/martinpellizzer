Begonia kingiana

Begonia kingiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to deep purple. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.
