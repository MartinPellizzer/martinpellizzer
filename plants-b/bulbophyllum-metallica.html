Bulbophyllum metallica

Bulbophyllum metallica, commonly known as the metallic orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, metallic sheen on its leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum metallica is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
