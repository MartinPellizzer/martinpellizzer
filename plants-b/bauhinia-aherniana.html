Bauhinia aherniana

Bauhinia aherniana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, sunny climate to flourish.

Bauhinia aherniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
