Brachyscome pusilla

Brachyscome pusilla, commonly known as the small-leaf everlasting daisy, is a small annual herb native to Australia.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with bright yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Brachyscome pusilla is known for its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact for extended periods, making it a popular choice for dried flower arrangements.
