Bomarea aurantiaca

Bomarea aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange bomarea, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant features tuberous roots and has a climbing or trailing growth habit. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
