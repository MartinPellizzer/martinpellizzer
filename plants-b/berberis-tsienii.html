Berberis tsienii

Berberis tsienii, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features spiny stems and small, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The berries are edible but typically used in traditional Chinese medicine.
