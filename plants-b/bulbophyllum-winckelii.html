Bulbophyllum winckelii

Bulbophyllum winckelii, commonly known as the Winkelman's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a complex structure, featuring a spurred lip and vibrant colors. Its rhizome system supports its growth, and it often forms dense colonies in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum winckelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
