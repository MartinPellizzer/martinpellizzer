Bramia monnieri

Bramia monnieri, commonly known as the common spurge, is a flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in cyathia, which are modified leaf-like structures.

Bramia monnieri is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
