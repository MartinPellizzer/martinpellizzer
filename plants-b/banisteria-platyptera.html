Banisteria platyptera

Banisteria platyptera, commonly known as the umbrella tree or red umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like inflorescence that consists of numerous small, red or orange flowers clustered together. The plant produces long, slender, woody stems that can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are typically opposite, simple, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Banisteria platyptera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and unique appearance.
