Bulbophyllum verruculiferum

Bulbophyllum verruculiferum, commonly known as the疣状瓶蕨, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces striking, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant features elongated, club-shaped pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. It blooms with fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that are often adorned with intricate markings.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.
