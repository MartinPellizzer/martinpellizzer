Brunnera myosotidiflora

Brunnera myosotidiflora, commonly known as bluebeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
