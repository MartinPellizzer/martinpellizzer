Bulbophyllum recurvimarginatum

Bulbophyllum recurvimarginatum, commonly known as the narrow-margin orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring elongated, narrow leaves and intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with dark markings, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
