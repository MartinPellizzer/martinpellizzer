Biophytum mimosoides

Biophytum mimosoides, commonly known as the mimic bean plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, fern-like leaves that resemble the foliage of the mimosoid legumes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Biophytum mimosoides is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
