Begonia princeae f. racemigera

Begonia princeae f. racemigera, commonly known as the racemifera begonia, is a unique hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
