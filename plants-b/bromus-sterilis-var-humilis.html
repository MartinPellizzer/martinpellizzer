Bromus sterilis var. humilis

Bromus sterilis var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf brome, is a low-growing grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growths and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to temperate climates and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and urban settings.
