Begonia cubincola

Begonia cubincola, commonly known as the Cuban begonia, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Begonia cubincola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and relatively easy care requirements.
