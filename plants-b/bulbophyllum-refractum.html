Bulbophyllum refractum

Bulbophyllum refractum, commonly known as the refractive orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
