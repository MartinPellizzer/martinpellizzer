Bambusa spinosissima

Bambusa spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and sharp, spiny nodes that give it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Bambusa spinosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional crafts and as a source of sustainable materials.
