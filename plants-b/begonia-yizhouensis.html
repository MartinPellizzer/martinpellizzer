Begonia yizhouensis

Begonia yizhouensis, commonly known as the Yizhou begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, intricate foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with deep green coloring and red or orange tubular flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
