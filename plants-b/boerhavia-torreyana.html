Boerhavia torreyana

Boerhavia torreyana, commonly known as Torrey's bluemink, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
