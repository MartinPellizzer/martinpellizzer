Billbergia binotii

Billbergia binotii, commonly known as the red-tipped billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central pool to collect rainwater. The plant features vibrant, spathaceous flowers that are typically red or orange, adding a bold visual contrast to its foliage. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat.

Billbergia binotii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
