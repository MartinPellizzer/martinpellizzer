Bellis pedunculata

Bellis pedunculata, commonly known as the Shiny White Daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming from spring to early summer. The plant has a rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves that help it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to cultivated landscapes.
