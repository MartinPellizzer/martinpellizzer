Barleria randii

Barleria randii, commonly known as the red barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually bright red or orange, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Barleria randii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
