Bouteloua heterostega

Bouteloua heterostega, commonly known as black grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

Black grama is an important forage plant for livestock and wildlife.
