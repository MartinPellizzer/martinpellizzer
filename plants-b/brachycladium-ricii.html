Brachycladium ricii

Brachycladium ricii, commonly known as the slender grass or narrowleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, slender blades and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a tendency to form dense mats.

Brachycladium ricii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
