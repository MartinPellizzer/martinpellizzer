Besleria venusta

Besleria venusta, commonly known as the velvetleaf gesneria, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Besleria venusta is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
