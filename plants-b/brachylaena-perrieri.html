Brachylaena perrieri

Brachylaena perrieri, commonly known as the silver brachylaena, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often used in coastal and arid garden settings.
