Brachystephanus glaberrimus

Brachystephanus glaberrimus, commonly known as the smoothstep daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, daisy-like clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a soft appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
