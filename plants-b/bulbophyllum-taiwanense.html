Bulbophyllum taiwanense

Bulbophyllum taiwanense, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are often fragrant and display a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum taiwanense make it a subject of interest for both horticultural and scientific study.
