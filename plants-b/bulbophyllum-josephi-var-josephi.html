Bulbophyllum josephi var. josephi

Bulbophyllum josephi var. josephi, commonly known as the Joseph's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps reduce water loss.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded conditions and is often found in forest understories.
