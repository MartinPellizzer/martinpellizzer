Besleria ignea

Besleria ignea, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, vibrant red and orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with upright, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of showy, spathaceous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
