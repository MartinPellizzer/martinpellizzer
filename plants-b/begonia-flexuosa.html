Begonia flexuosa

Begonia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy, and often zigzag-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Begonia flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
