Bulbophyllum trichocephalum var. capitatum

Bulbophyllum trichocephalum var. capitatum, commonly known as the clustered helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique morphology and ornamental value.
