Bunias balearica

Bunias balearica, commonly known as the Balearic borage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features upright stems and hairy leaves, with small blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
