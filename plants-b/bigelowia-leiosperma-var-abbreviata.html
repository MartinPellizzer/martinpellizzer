Bigelowia leiosperma var. abbreviata

Bigelowia leiosperma var. abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf bigelowia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features a compact growth habit with a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense cluster at the end of each stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and hardy species within the Asteraceae family.
