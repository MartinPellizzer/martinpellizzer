Burmannia sphagnoides

Burmannia sphagnoides, commonly known as the marsh burmannia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant thrives in wet, acidic environments such as peat bogs and swampy areas. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its humid habitat.
