Begonia cruenta

Begonia cruenta, commonly known as the crimson begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine layer of wax. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia cruenta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
