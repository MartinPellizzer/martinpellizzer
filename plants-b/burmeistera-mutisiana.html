Burmeistera mutisiana

Burmeistera mutisiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters at the base of the plant.

Burmeistera mutisiana is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
