Begonia hitchcockii

Begonia hitchcockii, commonly known as the Hitchcock's begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, with prominent yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
