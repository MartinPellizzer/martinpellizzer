Berberis franchetiana var. glabripes

Berberis franchetiana var. glabripes, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often sour. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
