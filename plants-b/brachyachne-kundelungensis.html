Brachyachne kundelungensis

Brachyachne kundelungensis, commonly known as the Kundelungue grass, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Papua New Guinea.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typical of many grass species. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
