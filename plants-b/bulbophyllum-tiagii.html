Bulbophyllum tiagii

Bulbophyllum tiagii, commonly known as the Tiagii's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
