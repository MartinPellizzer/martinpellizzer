Begonia tengchiana

Begonia tengchiana, commonly known as the Tengchian begonia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a combination of green, red, and purple hues. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
