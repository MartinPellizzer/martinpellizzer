Brasolia cattleya

Brasolia cattleya, commonly known as the Brasilia orchid, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the Cattleya genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant pink and white hues with intricate patterns. The plant is native to Brazil and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright indirect light. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive periods of drought.

Brasolia cattleya is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
