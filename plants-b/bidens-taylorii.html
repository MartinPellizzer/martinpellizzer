Bidens taylorii

Bidens taylorii, commonly known as Taylor's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow with dark centers, and they bloom from late summer into early fall.

Bidens taylorii is often found in moist, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges.
