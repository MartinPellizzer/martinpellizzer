Bulbophyllum helix

Bulbophyllum helix, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of flowers along the inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, colorful blooms that range from yellow to red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum helix is a popular choice for collectors due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
