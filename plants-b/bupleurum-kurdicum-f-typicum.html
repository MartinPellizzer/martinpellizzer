Bupleurum kurdicum f. typicum

Bupleurum kurdicum f. typicum, commonly known as the Kurdish angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East.

It belongs to the familyApiaceae, which includes plants known for their aromatic compounds and often used in traditional medicine.
