Brachylaena ramiflora var. ramiflora

Brachylaena ramiflora var. ramiflora, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
