Bellis graminea

Bellis graminea, commonly known as the lawn daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in spring. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with dark green, grass-like leaves that are often used in landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Bellis graminea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in garden settings.
