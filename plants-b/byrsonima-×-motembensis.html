Byrsonima × motembensis

Byrsonima × motembensis, commonly known as the African bitter apple, is a hybrid shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and showy, yellow flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy fruits that contain small, hard seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some regions.
