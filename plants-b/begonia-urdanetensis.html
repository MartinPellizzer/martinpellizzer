Begonia urdanetensis

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a mix of green and purple hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
