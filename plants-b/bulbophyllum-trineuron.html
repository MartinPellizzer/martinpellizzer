Bulbophyllum trineuron

Bulbophyllum trineuron, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow and pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
