Bursaria spinosa var. luxurians

Bursaria spinosa var. luxurians, commonly known as the coastal bursaria, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in spring. The plant thrives in coastal environments, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils. Its leaves are typically small, elliptical, and have a rough texture.

It is well adapted to salt spray and is often found in dune systems along the coastline.
