Brunnichia africana var. glabra

Brunnichia africana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth brunnichia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes or as a drought-tolerant plant.
