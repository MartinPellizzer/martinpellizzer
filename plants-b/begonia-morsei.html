Begonia morsei

Begonia morsei, commonly known as the Morsei begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds. Its foliage is often adorned with silver or white markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Begonia morsei is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored plant among both novice and experienced gardeners.
