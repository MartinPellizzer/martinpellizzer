Brasiliparodia

Brasiliparodia, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that often display a mix of colors, including shades of pink, purple, and white.
