Bulbophyllum dschischungarense

Bulbophyllum dschischungarense, commonly known as the Dschischungar orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs. The plant produces slender, linear leaves and bears small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
