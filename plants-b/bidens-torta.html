Bidens torta

Bidens torta, commonly known as the bentleaf beggarlice, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has hairy, alternate leaves that are often folded or rolled, giving it its common name. It produces small yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets, followed by seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Bidens torta is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
