Bauhinia grandidieri

Bauhinia grandidieri, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink and white. The plant has bipinnate leaves that are compound and divided into small, narrow leaflets. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in dry, rocky areas.

Bauhinia grandidieri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
