Blumea dentata var. glandulosa

Blumea dentata var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, lanceolate leaves with glandular dots on their surfaces, which contribute to its unique appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the genus Blumea.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
