Boronia elatior

Boronia elatior, commonly known as the pink boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, with lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Boronia elatior is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
