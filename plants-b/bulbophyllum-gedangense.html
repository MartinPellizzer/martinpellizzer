Bulbophyllum gedangense

Bulbophyllum gedangense, commonly known as the Gedang orchid, is a species of orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence and striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in the humid, shaded environment.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
