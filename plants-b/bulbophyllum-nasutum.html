Bulbophyllum nasutum

Bulbophyllum nasutum, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and hairy, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum nasutum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
