Bersama jaegeri

Bersama jaegeri, commonly known as the mountain balm, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It is often found in shaded, moist environments within forest understories.

Its leaves and flowers are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
