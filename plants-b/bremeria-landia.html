Bremeria landia

Bremeria landia, commonly known as the mountain holly.

It is a shrub native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, glossy leaves. It produces clusters of white flowers followed by red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife.

Bremeria landia is adapted to cold, dry climates and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
