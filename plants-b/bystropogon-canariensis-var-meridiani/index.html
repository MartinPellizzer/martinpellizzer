Bystropogon Canariensis Var. Meridiani

Bystropogon canariensis var. meridiani is a rare and striking plant native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its vibrant, daisy-like flowers. The variety meridiani is distinguished by its unique coloration and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable addition to botanical collections.
