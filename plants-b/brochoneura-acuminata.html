Brochoneura acuminata

Brochoneura acuminata, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or occasionally two flowers at the apex. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with a distinctive, narrow, hood-like labellum.

Brochoneura acuminata is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
