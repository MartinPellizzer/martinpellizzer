Brandisia glabrescens var. hypochrysa

Brandisia glabrescens var. hypochrysa, commonly known as the golden brandisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative shrub. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding visual interest to gardens. Its leaves are typically glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
