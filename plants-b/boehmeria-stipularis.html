Boehmeria stipularis

Boehmeria stipularis, commonly known as Chinese nettle or Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
