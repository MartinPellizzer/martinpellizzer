Bulbophyllum tortisepalum

Bulbophyllum tortisepalum, commonly known as the twisted-sepal orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, twisted sepals that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum tortisepalum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
