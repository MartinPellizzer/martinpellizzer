Balanophora kainantensis

Balanophora kainantensis, commonly known as the Kainan ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is a mycoheterotrophic plant, meaning it lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on fungal associations for its nutrients. The plant produces small, pale green or white flowers that are often hidden within the dense foliage of its habitat. Its unique ecological niche makes it highly sensitive to environmental changes, contributing to its endangered status.

Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, it remains a subject of interest for botanical research.
