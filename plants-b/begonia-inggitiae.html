Begonia inggitiae

Begonia inggitiae, commonly known as the Inggitia begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, thriving in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are often heart-shaped with a glossy texture, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts and conservationists.
