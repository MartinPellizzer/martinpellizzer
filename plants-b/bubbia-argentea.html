Bubbia argentea

Bubbia argentea, commonly known as silver leaf daisy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features silver-white, velvety foliage that gives it its common name, and it produces daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Bubbia argentea is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
