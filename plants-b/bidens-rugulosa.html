Bidens rugulosa

Bidens rugulosa, commonly known as the roughleaf tickseed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features hairy, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with two prominent white ray florets, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in sunny, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common sight in native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
