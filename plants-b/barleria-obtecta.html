Barleria obtecta

Barleria obtecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom throughout the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silky hair.

Barleria obtecta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
