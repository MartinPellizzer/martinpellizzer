Bulbophyllum leysianum

Bulbophyllum leysianum, commonly known as the Lysean orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in tropical environments. Its inflorescence typically bears multiple showy blooms, making it a popular choice for horticultural collections.

The leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
