Bauhinia dolichocalyx

Bauhinia dolichocalyx, commonly known as the long-stemmed bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its seeds are enclosed in woody pods and are often used in traditional medicine due to their potential pharmacological properties.
