Bulbophyllum hiepii

Bulbophyllum hiepii, commonly known as the Hiepii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal conditions.

The flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a range of colors from white to purple.
