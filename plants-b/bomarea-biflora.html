Bomarea biflora

Bomarea biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered bomarea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bomarea biflora is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
