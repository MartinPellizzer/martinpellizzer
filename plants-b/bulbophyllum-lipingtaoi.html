Bulbophyllum lipingtaoi

Bulbophyllum lipingtaoi, commonly known as the Liping orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
