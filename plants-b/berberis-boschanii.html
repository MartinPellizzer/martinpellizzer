Berberis boschanii

Berberis boschanii, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability.
