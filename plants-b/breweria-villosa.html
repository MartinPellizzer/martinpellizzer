Breweria villosa

Breweria villosa, commonly known as the hairy brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and taxonomic research.
