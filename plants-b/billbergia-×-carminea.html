Billbergia × carminea

Billbergia × carminea, commonly known as the Carmine Billbergia, is a hybrid bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that range from red to pink, which are highly ornamental. The plant features rosettes of narrow, waxy leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in height, forming a central cup to collect rainwater. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in horticulture for its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
