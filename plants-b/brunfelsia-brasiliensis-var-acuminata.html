Brunfelsia brasiliensis var. acuminata

Brunfelsia brasiliensis var. acuminata, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, often opening at night.
