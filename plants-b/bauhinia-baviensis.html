Bauhinia baviensis

Bauhinia baviensis, commonly known as the Bavi bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

Bauhinia baviensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
