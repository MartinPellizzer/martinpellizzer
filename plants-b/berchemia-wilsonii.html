Berchemia wilsonii

Berchemia wilsonii, commonly known as the Wilson's honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Berchemia wilsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
