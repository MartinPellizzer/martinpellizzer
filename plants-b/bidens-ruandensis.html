Bidens ruandensis

Bidens ruandensis, commonly known as the Ruanda ironweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the dry season.

Bidens ruandensis is typically found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
