Breweria menziesii

Breweria menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' brewer's grass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and adaptable species within its native range.
