Buxus balearica f. granatensis

Buxus balearica f. granatensis, commonly known as the Granada boxwood, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a glossy appearance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant typically grows to a height of around 1 to 2 meters, with a rounded or pyramidal shape. Its leaves are small, oval, and remain throughout the year, contributing to its year-round aesthetic appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
