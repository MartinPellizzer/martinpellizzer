Butonica procera

Butonica procera, commonly known as the tall bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Butonica procera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
