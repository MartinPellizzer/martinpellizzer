Brickellia brasiliensis var. typica

Brickellia brasiliensis var. typica, commonly known as the Brazilian brickellia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
