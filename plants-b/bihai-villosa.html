Bihai villosa

Bihai villosa, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a sprawling growth habit with hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the evening. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that contains seeds, and the plant is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Bihai villosa is known for its adaptability and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
