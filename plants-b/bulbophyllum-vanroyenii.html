Bulbophyllum vanroyenii

Bulbophyllum vanroyenii, commonly known as the Vanroyen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically thick and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss.

The flowers are usually borne on branched inflorescences and can vary in color from white to pink, depending on the variety.
