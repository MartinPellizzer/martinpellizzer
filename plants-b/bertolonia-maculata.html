Bertolonia maculata

Bertolonia maculata, commonly known as the spotted bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features attractive, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple with distinctive spots. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Bertolonia maculata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and easy care requirements.
