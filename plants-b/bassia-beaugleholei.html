Bassia beaugleholei

Bassia beaugleholei, commonly known as the red sandras or red sandras, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and succulent leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It is well adapted to hot, dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens.
