Brachistus hebephyllus

Brachistus hebephyllus, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
