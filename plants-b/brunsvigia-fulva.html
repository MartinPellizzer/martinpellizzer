Brunsvigia fulva

Brunsvigia fulva, commonly known as the yellow fireball, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, orange to yellow flowers that bloom in summer, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
