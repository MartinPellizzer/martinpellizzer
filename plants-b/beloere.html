Beloere

Beloere, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Beloere thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
