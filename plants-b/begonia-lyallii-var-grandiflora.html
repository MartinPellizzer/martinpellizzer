Begonia lyallii var. grandiflora

Begonia lyallii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Lyall's begonia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental value.

It prefers cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and greenhouses.
