Blitum glandulosum

Blitum glandulosum, commonly known as the sticky blite, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine, glandular hairs, giving it a sticky texture. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming from spring to early summer.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
