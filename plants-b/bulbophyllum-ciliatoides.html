Bulbophyllum ciliatoides

Bulbophyllum ciliatoides, commonly known as the ciliated bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in moisture retention. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from yellow to orange.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
