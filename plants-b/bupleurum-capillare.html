Bupleurum capillare

Bupleurum capillare, commonly known as slender valerian, is a herbaceous plant native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, hairy stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Bupleurum capillare is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
