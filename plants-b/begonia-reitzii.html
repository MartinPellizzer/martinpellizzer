Begonia reitzii

Begonia reitzii, commonly known as the pink begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are borne on long, slender pedicels, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Begonia reitzii thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
