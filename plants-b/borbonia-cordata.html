Borbonia cordata

Borbonia cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf borbonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil.

Borbonia cordata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
