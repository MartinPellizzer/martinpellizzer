Bauhinia bauhinioides

Bauhinia bauhinioides, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are often pink or red when young, turning green as they mature. The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Bauhinia bauhinioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
