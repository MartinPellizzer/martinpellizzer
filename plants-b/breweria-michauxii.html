Breweria michauxii

Breweria michauxii, commonly known as Michaux's brewer's grass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along streambanks and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Breweria michauxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for various insects and birds.
