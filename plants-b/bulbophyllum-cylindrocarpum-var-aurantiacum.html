Bulbophyllum cylindrocarpum var. aurantiacum

Bulbophyllum cylindrocarpum var. aurantiacum, commonly known as the Orange-fruited Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, orange-yellow flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and bloom during the rainy season.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its vibrant floral display.
