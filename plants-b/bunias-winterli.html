Bunias winterli

Bunias winterli, commonly known as the winter cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shady environments. The plant features alternate, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive mustard-like odor. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

The plant is known for its edible leaves, which are often used in traditional cuisine.
