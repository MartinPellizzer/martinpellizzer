Billardiera rosmarinifolia

Billardiera rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the lemon-scented billardiera, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that emit a distinctive lemon-like fragrance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforests or along creeklines.

Billardiera rosmarinifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing habitat and nectar for native wildlife.
