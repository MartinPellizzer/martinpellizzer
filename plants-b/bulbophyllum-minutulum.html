Bulbophyllum minutulum

Bulbophyllum minutulum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on tree trunks and branches in humid tropical environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its compact size.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
