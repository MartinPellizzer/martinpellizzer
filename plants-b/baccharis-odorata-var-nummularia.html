Baccharis odorata var. nummularia

Baccharis odorata var. nummularia, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery, rounded leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its aromatic foliage and adaptability make it a notable species in its native range.
