Bulbophyllum adjungens

Bulbophyllum adjungens, commonly known as the adjungens orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic plant that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a rhizome with numerous pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, often arranged in a raceme.

The leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its adaptability in various forest habitats.
