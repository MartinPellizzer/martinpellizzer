Bihai punicea

Bihai punicea, commonly known as the red dragon tree, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has a thick, woody stem with a rough, bark-like texture, and its leaves are long, narrow, and often waxy in appearance. Bihai punicea thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching foliage and floral characteristics.
