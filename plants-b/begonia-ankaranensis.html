Begonia ankaranensis

Begonia ankaranensis, commonly known as the Ankaranensis begonia, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
