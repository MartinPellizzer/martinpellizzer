Bulbophyllum nafisae

Bulbophyllum nafisae, commonly known as the Nafisa's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small but striking, often displaying a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
