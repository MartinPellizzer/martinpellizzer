Brodiaea Hyacinthina Var. Lactea

Brodiaea hyacinthina var. lactea is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its elegant white flowers. Native to the western United States, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness. The variety lactea is distinguished by its creamy white petals and subtle, milky hue. With proper care, this plant can bloom reliably year after year, adding a touch of refinement to any garden setting.
