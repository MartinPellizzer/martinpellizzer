Barleria sceptrum-katanganum

Barleria sceptrum-katanganum, commonly known as the Natal barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
