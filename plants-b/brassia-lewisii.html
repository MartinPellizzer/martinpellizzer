Brassia lewisii

Brassia lewisii, commonly known as the Lewis's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and prefers well-drained, loose soil. Its flowers are usually purple or violet in color, with a distinctive lip that attracts pollinators.

Brassia lewisii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
