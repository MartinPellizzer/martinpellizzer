Brachyscome melanocarpa subsp. thompsonensis

Brachyscome melanocarpa subsp.

thompsonensis, commonly known as Thompson's starless everlasting, is a flowering plant native to south-western Australia. It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, dark purple to black flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant produces small, hard, spherical seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in gardens and conservation efforts.
