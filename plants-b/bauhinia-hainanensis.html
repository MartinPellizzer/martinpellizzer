Bauhinia hainanensis

Bauhinia hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan bauhinia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to warm, humid climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests, where it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stabilization.
