Begonia elegans

Begonia elegans, commonly known as the elegant begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, and thrives in partial shade with well-draining soil. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.

Begonia elegans is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
