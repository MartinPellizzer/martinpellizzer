Bulbophyllum orsidice

Bulbophyllum orsidice, commonly known as the Orchid of the Forest, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
