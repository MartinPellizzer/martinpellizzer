Byrnesia weinbergii

Byrnesia weinbergii, commonly known as the Cape chestnut, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
