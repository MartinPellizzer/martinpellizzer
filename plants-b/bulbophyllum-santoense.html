Bulbophyllum santoense

Bulbophyllum santoense, commonly known as the Santo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the island of Santo in the Republic of Vanuatu.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and green tones. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its tropical habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
