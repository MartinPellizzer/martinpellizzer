Bulbophyllum manabendrae

Bulbophyllum manabendrae, commonly known as the Manabendra's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique botanical features.
