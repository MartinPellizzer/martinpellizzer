Buddleja minima

Buddleja minima, commonly known as the dwarf butterfly bush, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Buddleja minima is known for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
