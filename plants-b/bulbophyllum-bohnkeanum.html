Bulbophyllum bohnkeanum

Bulbophyllum bohnkeanum, commonly known as the Bohnke's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
