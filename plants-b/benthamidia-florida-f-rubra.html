Benthamidia florida f. rubra

Benthamidia florida f. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered benthamidia, is a variant of the benthamidia species characterized by its striking red petals.

This plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences, adding visual interest to the plant's appearance. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ease of cultivation.

The foliage is evergreen, with dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the vibrant red blooms.
