Boea brettiana

Boea brettiana, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and small, opposite leaves. This plant is often grown as a decorative vine due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
