Barleria setigera var. brevispina

Barleria setigera var. brevispina, commonly known as the narrow-spined barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branched stems that are often hairy. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a distinctive reddish or purple coloration. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or red and are arranged in terminal clusters.

The plant is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
