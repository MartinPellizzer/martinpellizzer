Benthamia exilis var. exilis

Benthamia exilis var. exilis, commonly known as the slender benthamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial with slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
