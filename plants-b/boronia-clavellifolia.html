Boronia clavellifolia

Boronia clavellifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved boronia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested or heathland areas. Its leaves are narrow and often have a distinctive reddish or purple underside.

Boronia clavellifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
