Billbergia linearifolia

Billbergia linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are narrow and often exhibit a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces striking red or orange inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia linearifolia is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
