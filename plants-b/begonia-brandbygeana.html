Begonia brandbygeana

Begonia brandbygeana, commonly known as the Brandbygean begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of St. Lucia in the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant red flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
