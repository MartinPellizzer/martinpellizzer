Beilschmiedia perakensis

Beilschmiedia perakensis, commonly known as the Malayan beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its smooth, dark-barked trunk and dense canopy. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by clustered, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface, often with a distinct midrib.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often found in rainforest habitats.
