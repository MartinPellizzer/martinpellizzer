Basilaea undulata

Basilaea undulata, commonly known as the undulating basil, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct wavy edge. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm climate.

Basilaea undulata is valued for its aromatic leaves, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
