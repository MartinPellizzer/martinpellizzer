Bupleurum exaltatum var. oligactis

Bupleurum exaltatum var. oligactis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream sides. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear small, clustered flowers in shades of white to pale blue. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive silvery-blue underside.

The species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
