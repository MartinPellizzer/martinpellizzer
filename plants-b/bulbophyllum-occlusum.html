Bulbophyllum occlusum

Bulbophyllum occlusum, commonly known as the closed lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers that are often clustered in a dense inflorescence. Its rhizome system allows it to thrive in shaded, humid environments, typically in the undergrowth of forests.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum occlusum make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and conservation efforts.
