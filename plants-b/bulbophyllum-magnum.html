Bulbophyllum magnum

Bulbophyllum magnum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a large, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in tropical climates.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
