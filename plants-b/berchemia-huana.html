Berchemia huana

Berchemia huana, commonly known as the yellow berchemia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features delicate, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Berchemia huana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
