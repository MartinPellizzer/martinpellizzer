Banisteria megaphylla

Banisteria megaphylla, commonly known as the giant leaf banisteria, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter, making it a dramatic addition to any landscape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming during the warm season. Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty collections.

However, due to its size and specific care requirements, it is not commonly cultivated in home gardens.
