Berkheya spinosissima

Berkheya spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy soils.

Berkheya spinosissima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
