Berberis dahaiensis

Berberis dahaiensis, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often bitter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its seasonal color changes and hardy nature.
