Begonia longibarbata

Begonia longibarbata, commonly known as the long-bearded begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the ends of the stems, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Begonia longibarbata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets.
