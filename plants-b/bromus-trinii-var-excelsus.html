Bromus trinii var. excelsus

Bromus trinii var. excelsus, commonly known as the tall brome, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with spikelets that are usually pale green to brown in color.

This variety is characterized by its tall stature and is often found in regions with moderate to high elevations.
