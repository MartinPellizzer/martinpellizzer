Begonia valida

Begonia valida, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with a fibrous root system. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia valida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
