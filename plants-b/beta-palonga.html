Beta palonga

Beta palonga, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are broad and often have a slightly hairy texture.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should not be ingested or handled without caution.
