Baccharis Pseudotenuifolia

Baccharis pseudotenuifolia is a resilient shrub native to the arid regions of South America. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and nutrient availability. This plant is known for its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the dry season. Its ability to survive in extreme conditions makes it an important component of desert ecosystems. Due to its hardy nature, it is also being studied for potential use in restoration projects.
