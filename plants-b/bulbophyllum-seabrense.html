Bulbophyllum seabrense

Bulbophyllum seabrense, commonly known as the Sea Brense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and its ability to store water, which helps it survive in arid conditions. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a distinctive coloration on the lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
