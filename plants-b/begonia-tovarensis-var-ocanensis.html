Begonia tovarensis var. ocanensis

Begonia tovarensis var. ocanensis, commonly known as the Ocanense Begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly textured, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
