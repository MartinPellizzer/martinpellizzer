Bolbitis boivinii

Bolbitis boivinii, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a striking fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its fronds are typically divided into long, narrow segments, giving it a unique, cascading appearance.

Bolbitis boivinii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.
