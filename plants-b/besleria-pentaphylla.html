Besleria pentaphylla

Besleria pentaphylla, commonly known as the purple-flowered gesneriad, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden environments. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Besleria pentaphylla is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant blooms throughout the growing season.
