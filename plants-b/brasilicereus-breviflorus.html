Brasilicereus breviflorus

Brasilicereus breviflorus, commonly known as the short-flowered cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with a compact, rounded shape. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its stems are ribbed and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
