Besleria mollissima

Besleria mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces small, round, greenish-yellow fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
