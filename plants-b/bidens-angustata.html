Bidens angustata

Bidens angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf beggarlice, is a flowering plant native to the eastern and central United States.

It is an annual herb that grows up to 30 inches tall, with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers with five petals, which are followed by distinctive three-lobed seed capsules. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and open woods.

Bidens angustata is known for its ability to spread rapidly through wind-dispersed seeds.
