Butia noblickii

Butia noblickii, commonly known as the Noblick palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, fan-shaped crown of leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The leaves are long, narrow, and have a silvery-blue hue, making it a striking addition to gardens.
