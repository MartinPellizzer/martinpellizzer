Blepharis hildebrandtii subsp. phillipsiae

Blepharis hildebrandtii subsp.

phillipsiae, commonly known as the Phillips' blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers.
