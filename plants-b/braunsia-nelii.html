Braunsia nelii

Braunsia nelii, commonly known as the velvetleaf braunsia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Braunsia nelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
