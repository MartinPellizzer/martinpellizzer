Bulbophyllum spodotriche

Bulbophyllum spodotriche, commonly known as the Spotted Pterostigma, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic orchid features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
