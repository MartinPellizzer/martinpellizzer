Bidens connata var. saint-vincentii

Bidens connata var. saint-vincentii, commonly known as Saint Vincent ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Caribbean, particularly Saint Vincent.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features erect, branching stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges and are often hairy on both surfaces.

The flowers are yellow, with ray florets surrounding disk flowers, typical of the genus Bidens.
