Begonia yenyeniae

Begonia yenyeniae, commonly known as the Yenyenia begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that range in color from deep maroon to glossy green. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the wet season. Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a sought-after species among horticulturists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
