Berchemia floribunda

Berchemia floribunda, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Berchemia floribunda is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
