Bromus speciosus var. firmior

Bromus speciosus var. firmior, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to western North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including disturbed soils and open grasslands.

Due to its adaptability, it is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.
