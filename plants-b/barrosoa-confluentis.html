Barrosoa confluentis

Barrosoa confluentis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant has a complex structure with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Barrosoa confluentis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
