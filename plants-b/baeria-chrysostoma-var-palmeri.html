Baeria chrysostoma var. palmeri

Baeria chrysostoma var. palmeri, commonly known as the Palmer's goldenbush, is a succulent shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features dense clusters of small, yellowish-green leaves that are adapted to arid environments. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rocky or sandy soils in full sun.
