Begonia hatacoa var. viridifolia

Begonia hatacoa var. viridifolia, commonly known as the Emerald Begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a vibrant, almost neon-like sheen.

The foliage is typically heart-shaped or ovate, with a waxy texture that enhances its visual appeal. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Native to Southeast Asia, this variety thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in shaded garden settings.
