Bulbophyllum dhaninivatii

Bulbophyllum dhaninivatii, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its often humid and shaded environment.

The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental value.
