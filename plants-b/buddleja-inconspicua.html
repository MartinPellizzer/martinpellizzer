Buddleja inconspicua

Buddleja inconspicua, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to lilac, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces long, narrow seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Buddleja inconspicua is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
