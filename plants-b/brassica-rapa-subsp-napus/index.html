Brassica Rapa Subsp. Napus

Brassica rapa subsp. napus is a versatile plant belonging to the mustard family. It is commonly known as rapeseed and is widely cultivated for its oil-rich seeds. This species is also used as a fodder crop and in traditional medicine. Its adaptability to various climates makes it an important agricultural crop. The plant's significance extends beyond food production to industrial and ecological applications.
