Bulbophyllum macrocarpum

Bulbophyllum macrocarpum, commonly known as the giant bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces strikingly large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or red markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
