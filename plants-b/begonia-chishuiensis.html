Begonia chishuiensis

Begonia chishuiensis, commonly known as the Chishui begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, ornamental foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
