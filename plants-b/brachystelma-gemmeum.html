Brachystelma gemmeum

Brachystelma gemmeum, commonly known as the gemmed stone orchid, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique, stone-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in its arid native environment.

Brachystelma gemmeum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
