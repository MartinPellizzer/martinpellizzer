Bambusa cyanostachya

Bambusa cyanostachya, commonly known as the blue-flowered bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces striking blue-violet flowers that appear in dense clusters, making it a visually appealing addition to gardens. Its foliage is dark green and often has a slightly glossy appearance.

Bambusa cyanostachya is relatively low maintenance, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
