Bidens intermedia

Bidens intermedia, commonly known as common beggarlice, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers with two white rays. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

The plant produces seeds with pappus structures that aid in wind dispersal.
