Belmontia mechowiana

Belmontia mechowiana, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Belmontia mechowiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
