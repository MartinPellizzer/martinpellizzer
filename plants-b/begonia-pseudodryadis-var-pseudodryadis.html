Begonia pseudodryadis var. pseudodryadis

Begonia pseudodryadis var. pseudodryadis, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a flowering plant characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with trailing stems, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its botanical appeal lies in its striking foliage and prolonged blooming period.
