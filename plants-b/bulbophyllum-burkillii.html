Bulbophyllum burkillii

Bulbophyllum burkillii, commonly known as the Burkill's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of yellow, green, and red hues. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum burkillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
