Bauhinia merrilliana

Bauhinia merrilliana, commonly known as the velvetwood or merrill's bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. Bauhinia merrilliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
