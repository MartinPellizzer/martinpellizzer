Begonia × bunchii

Begonia × bunchii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of ornamental features.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds due to its preference for indirect light.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
