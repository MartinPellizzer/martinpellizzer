Bossiaea alpina

Bossiaea alpina, commonly known as the alpine bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, adapted to the harsh conditions of its habitat.

Bossiaea alpina is well-suited to dry, sunny areas and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
