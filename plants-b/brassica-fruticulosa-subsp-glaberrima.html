Brassica fruticulosa subsp. glaberrima

Brassica fruticulosa subsp.

glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth wild mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, green, ovate leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typical of the mustard family. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it identifiable and useful in ecological studies and plant identification.
