Borzicactus weberbaueri var. flammeus

Borzicactus weberbaueri var. flammeus, commonly known as the Flame Cactus, is a unique cactus species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its vibrant, orange-red stems that are covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its colorful body. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in desert gardens.
