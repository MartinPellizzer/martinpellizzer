Berkheya spinosissima var. argentifolia

Berkheya spinosissima var. argentifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf berkheya, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features grey-green, woolly foliage that gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. It is adapted to arid conditions and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
