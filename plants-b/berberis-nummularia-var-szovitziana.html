Berberis nummularia var. szovitziana

Berberis nummularia var. szovitziana, commonly known as the silver leaf barberry, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to Central Asia.

It features small, rounded, glossy leaves that are typically a silvery-green color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that are edible but often used as ornamental features. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardiness and compact form.

Its foliage provides year-round interest, making it a popular choice in horticultural settings.
