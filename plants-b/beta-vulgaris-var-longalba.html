Beta vulgaris var. longalba

Beta vulgaris var. longalba, commonly known as the莙菜 or leaf beet, is a cultivated variant of the beet plant.

It is characterized by its broad, fleshy leaves that are typically harvested before the roots develop. The plant is a biennial, though it is often grown as an annual in agricultural settings. Its leaves are rich in nutrients, including vitamins A and C, and are used in various culinary traditions.

The plant thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
