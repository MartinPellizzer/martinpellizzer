Buckinghamia celsissima

Buckinghamia celsissima, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.
