Balaustion filifolium

Balaustion filifolium, commonly known as the slender balaustion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds.

Balaustion filifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
