Bulbophyllum nummularioides

Bulbophyllum nummularioides, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, bulb-like structure that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in a loose cluster. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for drought resistance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
