Binaria nigra

Binaria nigra, commonly known as black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are typically oval in shape. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in form. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
