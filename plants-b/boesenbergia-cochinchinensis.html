Boesenbergia cochinchinensis

Boesenbergia cochinchinensis, commonly known as the ginger lily or Vietnamese ginger, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces striking, ornamental leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive ginger-like scent.

Boesenbergia cochinchinensis is valued both for its aesthetic appeal and its traditional use in herbal medicine.
