Bulbophyllum befaonense

Bulbophyllum befaonense, commonly known as the Befao orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to yellow.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
