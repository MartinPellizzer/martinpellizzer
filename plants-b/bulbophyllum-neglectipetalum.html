Bulbophyllum neglectipetalum

Bulbophyllum neglectipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically produces a single or few lateral pseudobulbs, each supporting a pair of opposite leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a raceme and are notable for their intricate, lip-like structures.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
