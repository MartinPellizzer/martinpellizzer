Bolanthus frankenioides

Bolanthus frankenioides, commonly known as the plains coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the central and western United States.

Bolanthus frankenioides has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed heads that mature into fluffy, white pappus structures.
