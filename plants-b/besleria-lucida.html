Besleria lucida

Besleria lucida, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its delicate, lacy foliage that resembles woven silver threads. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with arching, compound leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
