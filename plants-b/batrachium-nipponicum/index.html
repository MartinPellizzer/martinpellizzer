Batrachium Nipponicum

Batrachium nipponicum is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is known for its unique leaf structure and striking floral displays. This species thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its name reflects its historical significance in traditional medicine. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
