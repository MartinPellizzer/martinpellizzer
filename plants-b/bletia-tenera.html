Bletia tenera

Bletia tenera, commonly known as the slender bletia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a raceme. Bletia tenera thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage consists of narrow, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy.
