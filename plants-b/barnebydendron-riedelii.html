Barnebydendron riedelii

Barnebydendron riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's legume, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple.

Its growth habit includes a woody stem and is often found in humid, lowland forest environments.
