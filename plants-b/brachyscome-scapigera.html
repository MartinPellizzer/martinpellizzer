Brachyscome scapigera

Brachyscome scapigera, commonly known as the slender weed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from October to December. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Brachyscome scapigera is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and gardens, and is considered a common weed in many parts of Australia.
