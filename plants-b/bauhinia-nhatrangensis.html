Bauhinia nhatrangensis

Bauhinia nhatrangensis, commonly known as the Nhatrang bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features distinctive bipinnate leaves and produces showy, pea-like flowers in shades of pink or white. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Bauhinia nhatrangensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
