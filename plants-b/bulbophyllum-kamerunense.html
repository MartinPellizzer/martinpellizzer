Bulbophyllum kamerunense

Bulbophyllum kamerunense, commonly known as the Cameroon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Cameroon.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the surrounding substrate.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
