Bulbophyllum fuscum var. fuscum

Bulbophyllum fuscum var. fuscum, commonly known as the dark bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with dark purple petals and a prominent yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
