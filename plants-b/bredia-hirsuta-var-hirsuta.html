Bredia hirsuta var. hirsuta

Bredia hirsuta var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy bredia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive hairy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and the flowers have a subtle fragrance.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions where the climate is warm and humid.
