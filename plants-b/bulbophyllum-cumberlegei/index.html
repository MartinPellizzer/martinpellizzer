Bulbophyllum Cumberlegei

Bulbophyllum cumberlegei is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This unique plant is known for its intricate floral structure and vibrant, often colorful blossoms. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family, which includes over 25,000 species worldwide. The plant is particularly notable for its specialized growth habits and ecological adaptations. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it faces significant threats in the wild.
