Begonia malmquistiana f. angustifolia

Begonia malmquistiana f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf begonia, is a hybrid form of the Malmquist begonia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically narrower than those of the parent species. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting veining. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

This ornamental plant is popular among gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
