Begonia burle-marxii

Begonia burle-marxii, commonly known as the Burle Marx begonia, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.

Begonia burle-marxii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
