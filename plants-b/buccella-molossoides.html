Buccella molossoides

Buccella molossoides, commonly known as the molossoides orchid, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clustered, small orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.
