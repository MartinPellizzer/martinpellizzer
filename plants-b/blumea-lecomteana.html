Blumea lecomteana

Blumea lecomteana, commonly known as the Lecomte's Blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, making it a visually striking plant in its natural habitat.
