Barleria woodii

Barleria woodii, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically red or orange, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches.

Barleria woodii is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
