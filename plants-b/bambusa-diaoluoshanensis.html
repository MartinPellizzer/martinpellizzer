Bambusa diaoluoshanensis

Bambusa diaoluoshanensis, commonly known as the Diaoluoshan bamboo, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 15 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface, and its culms are typically green with a slight bluish tint.
