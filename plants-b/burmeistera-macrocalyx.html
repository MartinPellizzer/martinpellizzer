Burmeistera macrocalyx

Burmeistera macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant burmeistera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, often in shades of white, yellow, or red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with thick, fleshy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
