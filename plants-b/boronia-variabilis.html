Boronia variabilis

Boronia variabilis, commonly known as the variable boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers. Boronia variabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
