Bulbophyllum stenophyllum

Bulbophyllum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, supporting its foliage and flowering structures.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
