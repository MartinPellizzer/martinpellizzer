Banisteriopsis latifolia

Banisteriopsis latifolia, commonly known as the "Brazilian pepper tree" or "mamoeira," is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
