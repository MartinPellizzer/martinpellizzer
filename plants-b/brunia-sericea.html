Brunia sericea

Brunia sericea, commonly known as silverleaf brunia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Brunia sericea thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
