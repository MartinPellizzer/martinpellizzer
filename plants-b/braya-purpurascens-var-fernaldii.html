Braya purpurascens var. fernaldii

Braya purpurascens var. fernaldii, commonly known as the Fernald's cushion-brake, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers arranged in tight clusters, typically blooming in early spring.
