Bulbophyllum penicillium

Bulbophyllum penicillium, commonly known as the penicillium orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually fragrant and can vary in color from white to pink and purple.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
