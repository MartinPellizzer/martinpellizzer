Berteroa incana f. trichocarpa

Berteroa incana f. trichocarpa, commonly known as the hairy goosefoot, is a variant of the species Berteroa incana, belonging to the family Chenopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and succulent stems, which help it retain moisture in arid environments.
