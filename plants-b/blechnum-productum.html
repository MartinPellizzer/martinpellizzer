Blechnum productum

Blechnum productum, commonly known as the golden fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bright yellow to golden in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves.

Blechnum productum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
