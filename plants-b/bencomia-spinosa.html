Bencomia spinosa

Bencomia spinosa, commonly known as the spiny bencomia, is a shrub native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems covered in sharp, spiny protrusions. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge.

Bencomia spinosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
