Barleria rogersii

Barleria rogersii, commonly known as the narrowleaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes and are usually blue or purple in color.

Barleria rogersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
