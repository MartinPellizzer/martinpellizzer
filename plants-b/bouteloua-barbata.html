Bouteloua barbata

Bouteloua barbata, commonly known as hairy grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green leaves and fluffy, tan-colored seed heads. The plant thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Hairy grama is often used in restoration projects due to its drought tolerance and ecological benefits.
