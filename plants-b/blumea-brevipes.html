Blumea brevipes

Blumea brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with small, elliptical leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in inflorescences, contributing to its ornamental value.

Blumea brevipes is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.
