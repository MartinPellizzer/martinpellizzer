Berteroa graeca

Berteroa graeca, commonly known as the Greek fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its feathery, aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soils. Its seeds are often used in culinary applications, similar to those of fennel.
