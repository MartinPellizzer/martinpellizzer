Byrsonima variabilis f. latifolia

Byrsonima variabilis f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf soursop, is a variant of the soursop tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces large, spiky fruits with a distinctive green exterior and a sweet, aromatic interior. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.

The botanical features of this plant make it well-suited for ornamental and agricultural purposes in tropical environments.
