Bobea elatior var. brevipes

Bobea elatior var. brevipes, commonly known as the dwarf climbing bean, is a compact variety of the climbing bean plant native to South America.

It features small, pale green pods that mature to a yellowish color, and its flowers are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions with well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
