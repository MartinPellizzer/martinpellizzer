Bucharea maderensis

Bucharea maderensis, commonly known as the Mader's cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Bucharea maderensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
