Boronia arborescens

Boronia arborescens, commonly known as the tree boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, often with a citrus-like scent.

Boronia arborescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
