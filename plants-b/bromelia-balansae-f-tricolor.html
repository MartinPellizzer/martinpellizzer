Bromelia balansae f. tricolor

Bromelia balansae f. tricolor, commonly known as the Trinidadian bromeliad, is a striking plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tricolored foliage that displays shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rosette-shaped leaves collect water, creating a micro-ecosystem that supports various small organisms.

The inflorescence, while less showy than in some bromeliads, adds to its ornamental appeal.
