Brachyscome eyrensis

Brachyscome eyrensis, commonly known as the Eyrensis Daisy, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with hairy stems and small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous flowering stems that rise above the foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The Eyrensis Daisy is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native garden landscapes.
