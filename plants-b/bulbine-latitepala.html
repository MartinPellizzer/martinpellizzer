Bulbine latitepala

Bulbine latitepala, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbine, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like in appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence, often blooming in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental plant suitable for xeriscaping.
