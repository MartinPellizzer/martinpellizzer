Blumea tenella var. luxurians

Blumea tenella var. luxurians, commonly known as the hairy blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
