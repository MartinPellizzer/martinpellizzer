Bergenia biflora var. hawortiana

Bergenia biflora var. hawortiana, commonly known as the Hawort's bergenia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy rhizomes and produces dense clusters of dark red to purple flowers in early spring. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
