Baccharoides calvoana var. hymenolepis

Baccharoides calvoana var. hymenolepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and arid regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
