Berberis morenonis

Berberis morenonis, commonly known as the Moreton Bay burr.

This shrub is native to eastern Australia and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. It produces bright red berries that are edible for humans and birds. The plant thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
