Bifrenaria barbosae

Bifrenaria barbosae, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Its flowers are usually arranged in racemes and are notable for their intricate patterns and vibrant hues.
