Bletia dabia

Bletia dabia, commonly known as the Dabie bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, Bletia dabia is considered a rare and endangered species.
