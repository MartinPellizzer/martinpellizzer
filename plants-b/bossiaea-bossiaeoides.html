Bossiaea bossiaeoides

Bossiaea bossiaeoides, commonly known as the pink bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or red in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
