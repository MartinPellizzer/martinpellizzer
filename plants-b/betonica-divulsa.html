Betonica divulsa

Betonica divulsa, commonly known as lesser betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Betonica divulsa is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and digestive properties.
