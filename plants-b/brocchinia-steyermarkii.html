Brocchinia steyermarkii

Brocchinia steyermarkii, commonly known as the leaf rosette plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant's leaves are typically stiff and covered in fine, hair-like structures that aid in water retention. Brocchinia steyermarkii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches.

Its unique morphology makes it well adapted to the epiphytic lifestyle common in tropical rainforests.
