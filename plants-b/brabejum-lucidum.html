Brabejum lucidum

Brabejum lucidum, commonly known as the glossy brabejum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
