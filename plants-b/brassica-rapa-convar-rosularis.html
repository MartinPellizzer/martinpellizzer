Brassica rapa convar. rosularis

Brassica rapa convar. rosularis, commonly known as the Chinese kale or Chinese broccoli, is a biennial plant belonging to the mustard family (Brassicaceae).

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit, with thick, fleshy leaves that are often used in Asian cuisine. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into round, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in cool climates and is cultivated for its edible leaves and stems.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various growing conditions.
