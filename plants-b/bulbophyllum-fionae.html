Bulbophyllum fionae

Bulbophyllum fionae, commonly known as the Fionae's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other plants in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a fragrant aroma.

Bulbophyllum fionae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty.
