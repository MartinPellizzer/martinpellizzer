Belloa radians

Belloa radians, commonly known as the "sunburst bellflower," is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, herbaceous growth habit with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Belloa radians thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical name reflects its radiant, bell-shaped flowers and its radiant appearance.
