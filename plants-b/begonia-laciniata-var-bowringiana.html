Begonia laciniata var. bowringiana

Begonia laciniata var. bowringiana, commonly known as the Bowring's fibrous begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
