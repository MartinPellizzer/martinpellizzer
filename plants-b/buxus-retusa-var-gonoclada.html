Buxus retusa var. gonoclada

Buxus retusa var. gonoclada, commonly known as the smooth boxwood, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage that remains consistent throughout the year. The plant has small, rounded leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, bushy form. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, which are edible for birds but not for humans.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in landscaping for hedges and topiaries.
