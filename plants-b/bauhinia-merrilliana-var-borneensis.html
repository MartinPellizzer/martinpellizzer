Bauhinia merrilliana var. borneensis

Bauhinia merrilliana var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean Bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of Borneo and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks.

The seeds are enclosed in a pod and are dispersed by animals, contributing to its ecological role in the region.
