Bridelia zenkeri

Bridelia zenkeri, commonly known as the African bridelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often elliptical in shape.

Bridelia zenkeri is notable for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
