Boronia tetrandra var. lancifolia

Boronia tetrandra var. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
