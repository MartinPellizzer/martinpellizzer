Brugmansia suaveolens

Brugmansia suaveolens, commonly known as angel's trumpets, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces white, yellow, or pink flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance, especially in the evening. Its leaves are ovate and dark green, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

However, all parts of the plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
