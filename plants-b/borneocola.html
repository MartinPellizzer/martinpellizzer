Borneocola

It belongs to the family Oxalidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.
