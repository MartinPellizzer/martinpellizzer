Barleria nitida

Barleria nitida, commonly known as silverleaf barleria, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, woody stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tubular, pale blue or purple flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
