Berberis longlinensis

Berberis longlinensis, commonly known as the longlinensis barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative hedge.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent berries and architectural form.
