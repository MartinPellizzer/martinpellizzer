Boltonia asteroides var. asteroides

Boltonia asteroides var. asteroides, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple petals and a prominent, cone-shaped center that attracts pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Boltonia asteroides var. asteroides is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
