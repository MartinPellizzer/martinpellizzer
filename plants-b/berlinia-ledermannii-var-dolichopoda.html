Berlinia ledermannii var. dolichopoda

Berlinia ledermannii var. dolichopoda, commonly known as the longpod Berlinia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive pod fruits. The plant has a deep root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often with a yellow or greenish hue, while its bark is thick and fissured.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local communities.
