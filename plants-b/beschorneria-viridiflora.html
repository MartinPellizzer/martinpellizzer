Beschorneria viridiflora

Beschorneria viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered yucca, is a striking evergreen perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features tall, arching leaves that are broad and dark green, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking white or greenish-white flowers in racemes during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
