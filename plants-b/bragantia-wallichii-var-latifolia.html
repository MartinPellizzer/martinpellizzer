Bragantia wallichii var. latifolia

Bragantia wallichii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bragantia, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
