Billbergia kuhlmannii

Billbergia kuhlmannii, commonly known as the Kuhlmann's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Billbergia kuhlmannii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
