Beilschmiedia latifolia

Beilschmiedia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves.
