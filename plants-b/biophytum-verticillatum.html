Biophytum verticillatum

Biophytum verticillatum, commonly known as the spiral plant or whirlwind plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
