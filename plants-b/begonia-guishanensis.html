Begonia guishanensis

Begonia guishanensis, commonly known as the Guishan begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, adding to its visual appeal.
