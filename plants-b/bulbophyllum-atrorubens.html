Bulbophyllum atrorubens

Bulbophyllum atrorubens, commonly known as the red-brown orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark reddish-brown pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with dark markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
