Barkhausia tenerrima

Barkhausia tenerrima, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, upright form. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
