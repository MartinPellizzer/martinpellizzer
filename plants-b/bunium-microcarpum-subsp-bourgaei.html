Bunium microcarpum subsp. bourgaei

Bunium microcarpum subsp.

bourgaei, commonly known as the mountain fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer.
