Bletia woodfordii

Bletia woodfordii, commonly known as the woodford's bletia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are often yellow or orange with dark markings. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bletia woodfordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
