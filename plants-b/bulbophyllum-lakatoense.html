Bulbophyllum lakatoense

Bulbophyllum lakatoense, commonly known as the Lakatoen's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in fluctuating environmental conditions.

Bulbophyllum lakatoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
