Bambusa elegantissima

Bambusa elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant bamboo, is a striking ornamental grass native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters, with a graceful, arching appearance. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green, adding a lush visual element to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Bambusa elegantissima is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant, flowing form.
