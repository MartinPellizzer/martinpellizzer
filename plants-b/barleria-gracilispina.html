Barleria gracilispina

Barleria gracilispina, commonly known as slender-spined barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and range in color from pink to purple.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
