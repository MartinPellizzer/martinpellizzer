Bartlettina yaharana

Bartlettina yaharana, commonly known as the yellow barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
