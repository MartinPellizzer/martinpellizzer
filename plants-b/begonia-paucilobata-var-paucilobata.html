Begonia paucilobata var. paucilobata

Begonia paucilobata var. paucilobata, commonly known as the few-lobed begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright stems with heart-shaped, dark green leaves that have few lobes, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
