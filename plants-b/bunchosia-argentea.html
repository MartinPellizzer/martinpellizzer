Bunchosia argentea

Bunchosia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf bunchosia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry or semi-arid regions.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
