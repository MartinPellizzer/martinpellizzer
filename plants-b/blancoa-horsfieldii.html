Blancoa horsfieldii

Blancoa horsfieldii, commonly known as the Horsfield's bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Blancoa horsfieldii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
