Bauhinia cuyabensis f. albiflora

Bauhinia cuyabensis f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
