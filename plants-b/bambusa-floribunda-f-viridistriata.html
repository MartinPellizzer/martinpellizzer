Bambusa floribunda f. viridistriata

Bambusa floribunda f. viridistriata, commonly known as the green-striped bamboo, is a variant of the silver bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking variegation, featuring green stripes on a lighter background. This bamboo is a clumping type, making it ideal for smaller garden spaces. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ornamental purposes.
