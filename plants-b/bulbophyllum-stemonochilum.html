Bulbophyllum stemonochilum

Bulbophyllum stemonochilum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive golden-yellow flowers with a prominent lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
