Bupleurum hoffmeisteri

Bupleurum hoffmeisteri, commonly known as Hoffmeister's angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Bupleurum hoffmeisteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
