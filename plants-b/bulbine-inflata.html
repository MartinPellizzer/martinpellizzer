Bulbine inflata

Bulbine inflata, commonly known as the inflated bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscaping and succulent collections.
