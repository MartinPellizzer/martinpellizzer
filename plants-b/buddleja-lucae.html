Buddleja lucae

Buddleja lucae, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces long, racemose clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pale blue to purple. These flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees.

Buddleja lucae is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different soil types.
