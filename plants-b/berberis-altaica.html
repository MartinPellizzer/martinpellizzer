Berberis altaica

Berberis altaica, commonly known as the Siberian barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces red-orange berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Berberis altaica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
