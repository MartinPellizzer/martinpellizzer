Bigelovia eryngioides

Bigelovia eryngioides, commonly known as the desert hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, resembling those of the dandelion, and is characterized by its spiny, bract-like leaves.
