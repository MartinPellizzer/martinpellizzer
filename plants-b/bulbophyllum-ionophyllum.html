Bulbophyllum ionophyllum

Bulbophyllum ionophyllum, commonly known as the Ionophyllum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique and striking floral structure. The plant typically produces clusters of small, colorful flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
