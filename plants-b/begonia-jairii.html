Begonia jairii

Begonia jairii, commonly known as the Jairi begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, often displaying contrasting veins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia jairii is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
