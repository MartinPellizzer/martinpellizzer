Brachiaria villosa var. glabriglumis

Brachiaria villosa var. glabriglumis, commonly known as silky brome, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs on the leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. This grass is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It has a creeping growth habit, forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion.

Due to its drought tolerance and nutritional value, it is also used as forage for livestock.
