Bhidea

Bhidea, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stalks above the water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. Its roots system thrives in muddy substrates, absorbing nutrients from the aquatic environment.

Bhidea plays a significant role in both ecological and cultural contexts across its native regions.
