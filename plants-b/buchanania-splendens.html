Buchanania splendens

Buchanania splendens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and glossy in appearance. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Buchanania splendens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
