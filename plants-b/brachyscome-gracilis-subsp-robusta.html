Brachyscome gracilis subsp. robusta

Brachyscome gracilis subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the slender everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit.

The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its resilience and ornamental value contribute to its use in horticulture and ecological restoration projects.
