Bonania myricifolia

Bonania myricifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf senna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural spread.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
