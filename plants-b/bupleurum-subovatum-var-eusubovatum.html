Bupleurum subovatum var. eusubovatum

Bupleurum subovatum var. eusubovatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lanceolate leaves that are usually hairless. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
