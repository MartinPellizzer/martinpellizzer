Buddleja neemda

Buddleja neemda, commonly known as the African butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep blue, attracting a variety of pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Buddleja neemda is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
