Bactris trichospatha var. cararaucensis

Bactris trichospatha var. cararaucensis, commonly known as the Cararaucensis palm, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized palm characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish flowers, followed by edible, sweet fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in local cuisines.
