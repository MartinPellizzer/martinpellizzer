Bulbophyllum turpe

Bulbophyllum turpe, commonly known as the Turpentine Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its unique, often pendulous inflorescences and striking, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage for optimal growth. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its natural habitat.

Its intricate floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
