Brachyscome gracilis subsp. gracilis

Brachyscome gracilis subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender mat grass, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to Australia. It features small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the cooler months.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to withstand drought conditions.
