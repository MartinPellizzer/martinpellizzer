It is characterized by its smooth, light-brown bark and glossy, dark green leaves that are alternate and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits containing several seeds. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and fine grain, making it important in local economies.
