Barleria rigida var. rigida

Barleria rigida var. rigida, commonly known as rigid barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

It is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
