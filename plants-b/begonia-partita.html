Begonia partita

Begonia partita, commonly known as the Variegated Begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that display a unique variegation of green, white, and pink hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded outdoor spaces.
