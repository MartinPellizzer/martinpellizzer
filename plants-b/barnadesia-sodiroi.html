Barnadesia sodiroi

Barnadesia sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. Its growth habit is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic.
