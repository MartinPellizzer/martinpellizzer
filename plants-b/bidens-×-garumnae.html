Bidens × garumnae

Bidens × garumnae, commonly known as the hybrid ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features rough, hairy stems and lanceolate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow and discoid, typical of the genus Bidens, and it produces small, triangular seeds in a pappus.

Bidens × garumnae is often found in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and roadsides.
