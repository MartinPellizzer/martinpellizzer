Barleria ramulosa

Barleria ramulosa, commonly known as the slender barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial.
