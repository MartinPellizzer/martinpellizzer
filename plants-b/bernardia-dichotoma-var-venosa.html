Bernardia dichotoma var. venosa

Bernardia dichotoma var. venosa, commonly known as the spotted evergreen.

This plant is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white spots. The flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.
