Bourreria ekmanii

Bourreria ekmanii, commonly known as the Cape mountain thistle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, hairy stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to arid conditions.
