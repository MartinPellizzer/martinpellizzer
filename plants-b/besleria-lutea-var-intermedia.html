Besleria lutea var. intermedia

Besleria lutea var. intermedia, commonly known as the golden flame begonia, is a hybrid begonia species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish or bronze underside. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
