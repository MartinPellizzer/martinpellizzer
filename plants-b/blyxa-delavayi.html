Blyxa delavayi

Blyxa delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's water plant, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Asia.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often submerged, with some floating on the water surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Blyxa delavayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to help stabilize aquatic ecosystems.
