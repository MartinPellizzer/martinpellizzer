Bulbophyllum alkmaarense

Bulbophyllum alkmaarense, commonly known as the Alkmaar orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Netherlands.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in open, sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species.
