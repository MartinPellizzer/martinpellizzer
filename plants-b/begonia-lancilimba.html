Begonia lancilimba

Begonia lancilimba, commonly known as the slender-leaved begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouse displays.
