Barleria lanceata

Barleria lanceata, commonly known as the narrowleaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
