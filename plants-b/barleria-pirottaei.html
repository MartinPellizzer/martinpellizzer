Barleria pirottaei

Barleria pirottaei, commonly known as the Cape barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
