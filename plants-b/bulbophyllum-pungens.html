Bulbophyllum pungens

Bulbophyllum pungens, commonly known as the spiky orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiky appearance. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of yellow and green.

It is a popular choice for collectors due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
