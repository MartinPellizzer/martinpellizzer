Barleria polhillii subsp. nidus-avis

Barleria polhillii subsp.

nidus-avis, commonly known as the "Bird's Nest Barleria," is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or lilac in color, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
