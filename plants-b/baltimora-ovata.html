Baltimora ovata

Baltimora ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf baltimora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Baltimora ovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some local cultures.
