Begonia baviensis

Begonia baviensis, commonly known as the Bavi begonia, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species with specific ecological requirements.
