Biophytum mapirense

Biophytum mapirense, commonly known as the Mapirensis leaf plant, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
