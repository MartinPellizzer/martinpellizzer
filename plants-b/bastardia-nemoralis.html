Bastardia nemoralis

Bastardia nemoralis, commonly known as the common bastardion, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, pink or white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
