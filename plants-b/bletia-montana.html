Bletia montana

Bletia montana, commonly known as the mountain bletia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are linear and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

Bletia montana is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
