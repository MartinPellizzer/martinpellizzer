Beyrichia scutellarioides

Beyrichia scutellarioides, commonly known as the African violets, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. Beyrichia scutellarioides thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial shade to full sun depending on the climate.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
