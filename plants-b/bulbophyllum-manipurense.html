Bulbophyllum manipurense

Bulbophyllum manipurense, commonly known as the Manipur orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are often pale yellow or greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
