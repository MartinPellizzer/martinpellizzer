Boberella nicandra

Boberella nicandra, commonly known as sweet senna, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing flat, kidney-shaped seeds. It is native to regions of South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Boberella nicandra is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
