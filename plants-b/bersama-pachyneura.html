Bersama pachyneura

Bersama pachyneura, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by elongated pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its ability to provide shade and improve soil quality.
