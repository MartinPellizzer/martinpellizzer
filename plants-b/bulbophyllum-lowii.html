Bulbophyllum lowii

Bulbophyllum lowii, commonly known as the lowii orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and its ability to produce numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Bulbophyllum lowii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
