Barthea barthei

Barthea barthei, commonly known as the African violet, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the humid forests of western Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Barthea barthei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is popular among horticultural enthusiasts.
