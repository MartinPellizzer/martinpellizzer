Blumea manillensis

Blumea manillensis, commonly known as the Manila Blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with pale green centers.

It is often found in moist, lowland areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
