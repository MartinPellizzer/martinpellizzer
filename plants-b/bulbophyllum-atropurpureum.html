Bulbophyllum atropurpureum

Bulbophyllum atropurpureum, commonly known as the Purple-veined Pterostigma, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking purple and green foliage, with leaves that are often marked with purple veins. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with dark purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
