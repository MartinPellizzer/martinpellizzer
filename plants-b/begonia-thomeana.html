Begonia thomeana

Begonia thomeana, commonly known as the Brazilian begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia thomeana is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
