Barleria kacondensis subsp. glabrescens

Barleria kacondensis subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and range in color from pink to purple, attracting various pollinators.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
