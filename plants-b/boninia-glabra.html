Boninia glabra

Boninia glabra, commonly known as the smooth bonini, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a suitable plant for gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
