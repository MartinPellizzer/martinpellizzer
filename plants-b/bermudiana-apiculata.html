Bermudiana apiculata

Bermudiana apiculata, commonly known as the Bermuda grass.

It is a species of grass native to the subtropical regions of the Caribbean, including Bermuda. This plant is characterized by its fine, tufted blades and dense growth habit, making it a common weed in disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Bermudiana apiculata is often used for erosion control and as a forage crop in certain regions.
