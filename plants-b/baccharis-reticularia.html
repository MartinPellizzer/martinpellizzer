Baccharis reticularia

Baccharis reticularia, commonly known as the silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Baccharis reticularia is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
