Bulbophyllum karbianglongensis

Bulbophyllum karbianglongensis, commonly known as the Karbianglong orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique morphological features, including its intricate floral structure and specialized growth habits. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its aerial roots for nutrient absorption. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
