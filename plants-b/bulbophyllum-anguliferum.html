Bulbophyllum anguliferum

Bulbophyllum anguliferum, commonly known as the angular bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, angular pseudobulbs and long, slender leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
