Botryanthus granatensis

Botryanthus granatensis, commonly known as the granate bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Botryanthus granatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
