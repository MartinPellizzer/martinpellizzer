Bauhinia Purpurea Var. Violacea

Bauhinia purpurea var. violacea is a strikingly beautiful leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Asia. Known for its vibrant purple flowers and distinctive leaf structure, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. This variety belongs to the Fabaceae family and is closely related to other Bauhinia species. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and public landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
