Bomarea hartwegii

Bomarea hartwegii, commonly known as the pink bomarea, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white flowers, which bloom in clusters at the top of its slender, leafy stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a tuberous root system, which helps it store water and nutrients in its native arid environment. It thrives in shaded, moist conditions and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts worldwide.
