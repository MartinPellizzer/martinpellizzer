Bossiaea aquifolium

Bossiaea aquifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and appear in clusters during the spring.

Bossiaea aquifolium is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to coastal environments.
