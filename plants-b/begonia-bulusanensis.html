Begonia bulusanensis

Begonia bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan begonia, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display a deep green color with contrasting veins. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
