Bulbophyllum uduense

Bulbophyllum uduense, commonly known as the Uduens Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with vibrant colors, often in shades of yellow and orange. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
