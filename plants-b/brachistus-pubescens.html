Brachistus pubescens

Brachistus pubescens, commonly known as the hairy brachistus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Brachistus pubescens is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
