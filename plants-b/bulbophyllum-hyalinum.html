Bulbophyllum hyalinum

Bulbophyllum hyalinum, commonly known as the glassy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, translucent or glassy green pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and come in a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
