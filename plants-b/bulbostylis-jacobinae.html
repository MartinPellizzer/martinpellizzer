Bulbostylis jacobinae

Bulbostylis jacobinae, commonly known as Jacob's bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces tiny, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white or pale blue flowers.
