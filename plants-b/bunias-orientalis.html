Bunias orientalis

Bunias orientalis, commonly known as the oriental rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features basal rosettes of pinnately lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in siliques, a characteristic feature of the Brassicaceae family.

Bunias orientalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in some traditional cuisines.
