Baldellia alpestris

Baldellia alpestris, commonly known as the alpine waterplantain, is a herbaceous plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It typically grows in wet, rocky soils and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in mountainous areas across Europe, Asia, and North America.

Baldellia alpestris is adapted to cold climates and can thrive in low-nutrient environments.
