Bauhinia leiopetala

Bauhinia leiopetala, commonly known as the smooth-leaved bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are composed of numerous small, paired leaflets. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Bauhinia leiopetala is often valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
