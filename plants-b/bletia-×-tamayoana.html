Bletia × tamayoana

Bletia × tamayoana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bletia × tamayoana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
