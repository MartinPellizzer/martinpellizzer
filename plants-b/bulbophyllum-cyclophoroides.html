Bulbophyllum cyclophoroides

Bulbophyllum cyclophoroides, commonly known as the cyclophoroides orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from yellow to orange.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
