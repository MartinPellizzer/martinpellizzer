Baphia schweinfurthii

Baphia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces elongated pods that contain seeds.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its vibrant floral display.
