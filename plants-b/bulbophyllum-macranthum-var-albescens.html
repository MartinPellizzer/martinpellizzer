Bulbophyllum macranthum var. albescens

Bulbophyllum macranthum var. albescens, commonly known as the White-flowered Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and sepals, which contrast with its green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
