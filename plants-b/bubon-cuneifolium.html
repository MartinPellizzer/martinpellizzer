Bubon cuneifolium

Bubon cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bubon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late summer. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it is well adapted to dry conditions with deep root systems.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
