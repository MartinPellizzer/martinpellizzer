Begonia × reginae

Begonia × reginae, commonly known as the princess begonia, is a hybrid plant prized for its ornamental value.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage is often variegated, adding to its visual appeal.

Begonia × reginae thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
