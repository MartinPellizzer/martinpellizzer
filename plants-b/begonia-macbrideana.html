Begonia macbrideana

Begonia macbrideana, commonly known as the Macbride's begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slight metallic sheen. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
