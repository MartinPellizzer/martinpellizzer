Baccharis phyteuma

Baccharis phyteuma, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, fluffy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
