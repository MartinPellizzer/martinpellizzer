Banisteria amoena

Banisteria amoena, commonly known as the "pink shower tree," is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
