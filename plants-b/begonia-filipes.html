Begonia filipes

Begonia filipes, commonly known as the silver lace begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that have a distinctive silvery sheen. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia filipes is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
