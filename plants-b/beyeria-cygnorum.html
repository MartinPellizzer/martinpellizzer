Beyeria cygnorum

Beyeria cygnorum, commonly known as the Swan's Tail Plant, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, spurred flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage consists of glossy, ovate leaves with a smooth texture.

Beyeria cygnorum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
