Brachiaria dictyoneura

Brachiaria dictyoneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Brachiaria dictyoneura is valued for its use in forage and soil stabilization due to its hardy nature and rapid growth.
