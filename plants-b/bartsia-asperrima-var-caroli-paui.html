Bartsia asperrima var. caroli-paui

Bartsia asperrima var. caroli-paui, commonly known as the Caroli-Paui Bartsia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial with delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
