Bulbine asphodeloides var. xanthobotrys

Bulbine asphodeloides var. xanthobotrys, commonly known as the yellow bulbine, is a succulent perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
