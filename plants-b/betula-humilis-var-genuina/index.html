Betula Humilis Var. Genuina

Betula humilis var. genuina is a rare and diminutive member of the birch family known for its compact growth and unique ecological role. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this variant thrives in harsh, high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. Its small stature and dense foliage make it an important component of alpine ecosystems, providing shelter and food for local wildlife. Despite its modest size, the plant exhibits remarkable resilience to extreme weather conditions. This little-known species offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in extreme climates.
