Bulbophyllum jacintense

Bulbophyllum jacintense, commonly known as the Jacintense Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces intricate, showy inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
