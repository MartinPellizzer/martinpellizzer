Blumea virens var. minor

Blumea virens var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its small yellow flowers are arranged in compact clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
