Beyeriopsis cyanescens

Beyeriopsis cyanescens, commonly known as the blue-flowered orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue petals and unique, elongated flower structure. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Beyeriopsis cyanescens thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
