Bulbophyllum rubusioides

Bulbophyllum rubusioides, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety dark green leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum rubusioides is a popular choice for collectors due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
