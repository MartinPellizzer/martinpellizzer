Begonia nitida var. pilosula

Begonia nitida var. pilosula, commonly known as the silky begonia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it a distinctive, luxurious appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in hanging baskets and as a decorative foliage plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
