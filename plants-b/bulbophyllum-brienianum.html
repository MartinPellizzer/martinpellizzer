Bulbophyllum brienianum

Bulbophyllum brienianum, commonly known as the Brienianum orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
