Barleria glandulifera var. benadirensis

Barleria glandulifera var. benadirensis, commonly known as the Benadire barleria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
