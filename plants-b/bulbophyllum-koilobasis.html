Bulbophyllum koilobasis

Bulbophyllum koilobasis, commonly known as the warty bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, warty pseudobulbs and elongated, fleshy leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
