Billbergia pyramidalis var. vernicosa

Billbergia pyramidalis var. vernicosa, commonly known as the velvet billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a waxy, velvety coating, giving it a unique texture. The leaves are typically a deep green with red or purple margins, adding visual interest. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom in the spring.

It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
