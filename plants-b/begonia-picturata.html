Begonia picturata

Begonia picturata, commonly known as the painted begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that often display intricate patterns of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the warm season. Its foliage is dense and glossy, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.

Begonia picturata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
