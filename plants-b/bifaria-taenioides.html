Bifaria taenioides

Bifaria taenioides, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features small, linear leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white to pinkish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.

Bifaria taenioides is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy species in desert environments.
