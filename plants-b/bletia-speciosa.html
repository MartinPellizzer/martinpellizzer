Bletia speciosa

Bletia speciosa, commonly known as the showy bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Bletia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
