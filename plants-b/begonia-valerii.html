Begonia valerii

Begonia valerii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia valerii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
