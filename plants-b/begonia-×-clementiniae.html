Begonia × clementiniae

Begonia × clementiniae, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pavonina. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. It typically has heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Begonia × clementiniae thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
