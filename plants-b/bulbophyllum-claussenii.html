Bulbophyllum claussenii

Bulbophyllum claussenii, commonly known as the Clara's ghost orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and delicate, pale green to white flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with intricate patterns that enhance its ethereal appearance.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
