Begonia baumannii

Begonia baumannii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, bright red flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental plant collections.
