Bonamia oblongifolia

Bonamia oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf bonamia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
