Brachycladium triangulare

Brachycladium triangulare, commonly known as the triangular grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a triangular cross-section. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family.

Brachycladium triangulare is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
