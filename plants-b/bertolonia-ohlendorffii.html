Bertolonia ohlendorffii

Bertolonia ohlendorffii, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

Bertolonia ohlendorffii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
