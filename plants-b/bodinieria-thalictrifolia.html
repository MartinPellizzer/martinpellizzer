Bodinieria thalictrifolia

Bodinieria thalictrifolia, commonly known as the slender false hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels, often resembling those of the genus Thalictrum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
