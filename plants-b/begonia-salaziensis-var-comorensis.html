Begonia salaziensis var. comorensis

Begonia salaziensis var. comorensis, commonly known as the Comorian begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Comoros Islands.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, typically blooming during the warm season. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for collectors and garden enthusiasts.
