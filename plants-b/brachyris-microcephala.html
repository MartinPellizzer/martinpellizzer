Brachyris microcephala

Brachyris microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed brachyris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves that are typically clustered at the base. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
