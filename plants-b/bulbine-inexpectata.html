Bulbine inexpectata

Bulbine inexpectata, commonly known as the orange bulbine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Bulbine in the family Xanthorrhoeaceae. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and bright orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid regions.
