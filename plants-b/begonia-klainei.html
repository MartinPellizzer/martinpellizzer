Begonia klainei

Begonia klainei, commonly known as the Klaine's begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with upright stems and clusters of flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
