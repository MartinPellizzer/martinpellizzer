Bupleurum pamiricum

Bupleurum pamiricum, commonly known as the Pamir angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Bupleurum pamiricum is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
