Begonia wutaiana

Begonia wutaiana, commonly known as the Wutaian begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage and delicate, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
