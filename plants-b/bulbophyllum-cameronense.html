Bulbophyllum cameronense

Bulbophyllum cameronense, commonly known as the Cameron's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Cameroon.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric humidity for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
