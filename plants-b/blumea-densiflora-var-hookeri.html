Blumea densiflora var. hookeri

Blumea densiflora var. hookeri, commonly known as Hooker's blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in compact heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
