Buphthalmum elegans

Buphthalmum elegans, commonly known as the elegant ox-eye daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Buphthalmum elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive blooms.
