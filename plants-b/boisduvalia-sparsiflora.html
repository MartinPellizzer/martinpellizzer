Boisduvalia sparsiflora

Boisduvalia sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered boisduvalia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.

Boisduvalia sparsiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
