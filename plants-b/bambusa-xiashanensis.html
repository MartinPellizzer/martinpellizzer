Bambusa xiashanensis

Bambusa xiashanensis, commonly known as the Xiashan bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a tall, slender bamboo that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is characterized by its strong, fibrous stalks that are used in construction and crafts.

The Xiashan bamboo thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
