Bulbophyllum ciliipetalum

Bulbophyllum ciliipetalum, commonly known as the feather-petaled orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, lance-shaped pseudobulbs and intricate, feather-like petals. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts for its delicate floral display.
