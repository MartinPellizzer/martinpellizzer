Boelckea

Boelckea, commonly known as the golden bell tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. Boelckea thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
