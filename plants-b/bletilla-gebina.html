Bletilla gebina

Bletilla gebina, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip petal. Bletilla gebina thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
