Bletia undulata var. costaricana

Bletia undulata var. costaricana, commonly known as the Costa Rican bletia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as tropical forests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly through vegetative propagation.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, well-drained conditions.
