Bulbophyllum cylindricum

Bulbophyllum cylindricum, commonly known as the cylindrical orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
