Begonia bonii var. remotisetulosa

Begonia bonii var. remotisetulosa, commonly known as the remote-stipitate begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting yellow centers. The plant features velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
