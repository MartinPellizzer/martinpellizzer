Bulbostylis loefgrenii

Bulbostylis loefgrenii, commonly known as the dwarf sedge, is a small perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, forming compact inflorescences.
