Byrsonima vernicosa

Byrsonima vernicosa, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or yellow myrtle, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, waxy bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface. The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
