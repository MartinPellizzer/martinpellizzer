Bulbophyllum bambusifolium

Bulbophyllum bambusifolium, commonly known as the bamboo-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, bamboo-like foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum bambusifolium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
