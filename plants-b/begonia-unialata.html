Begonia unialata

Begonia unialata, commonly known as the single-leaf begonia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring a single, large, heart-shaped leaf that is often brightly colored. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shady, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unusual appearance, it is often cultivated as a rare and exotic houseplant.
