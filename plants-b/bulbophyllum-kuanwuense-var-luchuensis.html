Bulbophyllum kuanwuense var. luchuensis

Bulbophyllum kuanwuense var. luchuensis, commonly known as the Luchuan Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

This variety is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
