Bonamia pannosa

Bonamia pannosa, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, purple or blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.

Bonamia pannosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
