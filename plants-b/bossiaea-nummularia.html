Bossiaea nummularia

Bossiaea nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar bush, is a low-growing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, round, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Bossiaea nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in garden landscaping.
