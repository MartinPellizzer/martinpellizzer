Bulbophyllum dacruzii

Bulbophyllum dacruzii, commonly known as the Dacruz's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are leathery and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum dacruzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
