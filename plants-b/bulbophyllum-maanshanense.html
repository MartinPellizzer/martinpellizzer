Bulbophyllum maanshanense

Bulbophyllum maanshanense, commonly known as the Maanshan orchid, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow and orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum maanshanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
