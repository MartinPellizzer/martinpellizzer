Bombax obtusum

Bombax obtusum, commonly known as the woolly ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has a distinctive trunk with a swollen base and a crown of broad, palmate leaves. Its fibrous bark and cotton-like seed fluff are notable botanical features.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
