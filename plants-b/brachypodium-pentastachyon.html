Brachypodium pentastachyon

Brachypodium pentastachyon, commonly known as goatgrass, is a grass species native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows to a height of 30 to 100 centimeters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spikelet-based flowers. Its inflorescence is a panicle, which consists of multiple branches bearing spikelets.

Brachypodium pentastachyon is widely used in botanical research due to its genetic simplicity and rapid growth cycle.
