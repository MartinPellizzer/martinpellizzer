Begonia piring

Begonia piring, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to burgundy, adding a rich texture to any garden or indoor space. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it ideal for shaded borders or containers.

Due to its unique foliage and elegant blooms, Begonia piring is a popular choice for both gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
