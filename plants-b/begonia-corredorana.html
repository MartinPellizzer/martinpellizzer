Begonia corredorana

Begonia corredorana, commonly known as the Corredor begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia corredorana is popular among horticulturists for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
