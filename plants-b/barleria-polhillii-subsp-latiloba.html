Barleria polhillii subsp. latiloba

Barleria polhillii subsp.

latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaved barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped, often hairy leaves.
