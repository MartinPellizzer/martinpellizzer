Bulbophyllum thompsonii

Bulbophyllum thompsonii, commonly known as Thompson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters and can have intricate patterns and vivid hues.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
