Bredia pricei

Bredia pricei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting birds and insects. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Bredia pricei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and relatively low maintenance requirements.
