Begonia pinnatifida

Begonia pinnatifida, commonly known as the broadleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia pinnatifida is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
