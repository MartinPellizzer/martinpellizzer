Boerhavia minutiflora

Boerhavia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf bluemink, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
