Brunfelsia macroloba

Brunfelsia macroloba, commonly known as the large-leaved Brunfelsia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces elliptical, velvety berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Brunfelsia macroloba thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
