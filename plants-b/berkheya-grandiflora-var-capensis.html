Berkheya grandiflora var. capensis

Berkheya grandiflora var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
