Baccharis densa

Baccharis densa, commonly known as dense baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Baccharis densa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and hardiness.
