Bulbostylis brunneoacuminata

Bulbostylis brunneoacuminata, commonly known as the brown-acuminate bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
