Boronia platyrrhachis

Boronia platyrrhachis, commonly known as the flat-stemmed boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to tolerate salt spray and coastal conditions.

Boronia platyrrhachis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in coastal ecosystems.
