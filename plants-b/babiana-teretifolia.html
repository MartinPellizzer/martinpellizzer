Babiana teretifolia

Babiana teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved babiana, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant, often orange or yellow, flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Babiana teretifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
