Begonia hosensis

Begonia hosensis, commonly known as the Hosen's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia hosensis is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
