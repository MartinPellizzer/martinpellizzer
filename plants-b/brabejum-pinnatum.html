Brabejum pinnatum

Brabejum pinnatum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces a pleasant, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Brabejum pinnatum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
