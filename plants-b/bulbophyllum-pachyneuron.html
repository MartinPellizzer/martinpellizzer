Bulbophyllum pachyneuron

Bulbophyllum pachyneuron, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically fleshy and green, adapted for efficient photosynthesis.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper humidity and light conditions.
