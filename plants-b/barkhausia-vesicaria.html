Barkhausia vesicaria

Barkhausia vesicaria, commonly known as the African orchid or the bubble orchid, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, inflated seed pods that resemble bubbles, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Barkhausia vesicaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
