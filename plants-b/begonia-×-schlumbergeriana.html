Begonia × schlumbergeriana

Begonia × schlumbergeriana, commonly known as the Dragon Wing Begonia, is a hybrid plant that combines the characteristics of several begonia species.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant is noted for its striking, elongated leaves that resemble dragon wings, which give it its common name. It produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting markings.

Begonia × schlumbergeriana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
