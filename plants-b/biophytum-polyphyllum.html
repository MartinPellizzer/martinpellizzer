Biophytum polyphyllum

Biophytum polyphyllum, commonly known as the flame flower or red leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange foliage, which is often variegated with green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Biophytum polyphyllum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaf color and ease of care.
