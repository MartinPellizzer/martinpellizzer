Bulbophyllum longirostre

Bulbophyllum longirostre, commonly known as the long-beaked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated floral structure that resembles a beak, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The bulbophyllum genus is known for its diverse floral morphology, and B. longirostre is a notable example of this diversity.
