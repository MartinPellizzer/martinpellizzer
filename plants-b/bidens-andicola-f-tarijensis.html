Bidens andicola f. tarijensis

Bidens andicola f. tarijensis, commonly known as the Tarijensis ironweed, is a perennial herb native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces, typical of many species in the Bidens genus. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months.

This variety is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often found in mountainous habitats.
