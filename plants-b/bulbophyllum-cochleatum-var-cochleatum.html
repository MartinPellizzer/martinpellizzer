Bulbophyllum cochleatum var. cochleatum

Bulbophyllum cochleatum var. cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a spicy scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
