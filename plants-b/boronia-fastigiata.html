Boronia fastigiata

Boronia fastigiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved boronia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically narrow and linear. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Boronia fastigiata is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
