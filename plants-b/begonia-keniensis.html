Begonia keniensis

Begonia keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan begonia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
