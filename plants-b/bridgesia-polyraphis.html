Bridgesia polyraphis

Bridgesia polyraphis, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Bridgesia polyraphis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
