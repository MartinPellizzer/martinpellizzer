Begonia balmisiana

Begonia balmisiana, commonly known as the Balmisiana begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a dark green hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia balmisiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
