Bulbophyllum macraei var. autumnale

Bulbophyllum macraei var. autumnale, commonly known as the Autumn Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that bloom in the autumn season. The plant typically features long, narrow pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical and subtropical environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
