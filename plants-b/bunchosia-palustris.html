Bunchosia palustris

Bunchosia palustris, commonly known as the marsh bunchosia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, adapting to saturated soil conditions. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Bunchosia palustris plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various insects and birds.
