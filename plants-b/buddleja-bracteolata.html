Buddleja bracteolata

Buddleja bracteolata, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, cylindrical flower clusters that produce nectar-rich blossoms, attracting a variety of pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja bracteolata is known for its ornamental value and adaptability to different garden environments.
