Begonia kelliana

Begonia kelliana, commonly known as the Kelliana begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface, and it produces small, round tubers underground. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia kelliana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
