Bastardia limensis

Bastardia limensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean sand mustard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in coastal areas and arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Bastardia limensis is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
