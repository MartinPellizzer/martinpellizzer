Banisteriopsis pulchra var. pulchra

Banisteriopsis pulchra var. pulchra, commonly known as the purple passion vine, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep purple undersides. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in clusters. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn bright red when mature, containing seeds enclosed in a pulp.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and floral display.
