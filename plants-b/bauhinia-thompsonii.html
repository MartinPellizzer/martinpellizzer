Bauhinia thompsonii

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Bauhinia thompsonii is often found in tropical rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
