Bulbophyllum filisepalum

Bulbophyllum filisepalum, commonly known as the thread-sepal orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant features long, thread-like sepals and petals, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
