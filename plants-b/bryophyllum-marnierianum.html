Bryophyllum marnierianum

Bryophyllum marnierianum, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or white powdery coating. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring and summer months.

Bryophyllum marnierianum is well adapted to drought conditions due to its thick, water-storing leaves and shallow root system.
