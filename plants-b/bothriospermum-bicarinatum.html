Bothriospermum bicarinatum

Bothriospermum bicarinatum, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of about 3 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by distinctive, two-lobed fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The plant is notable for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
