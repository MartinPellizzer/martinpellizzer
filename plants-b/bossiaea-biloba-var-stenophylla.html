Bossiaea biloba var. stenophylla

Bossiaea biloba var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
