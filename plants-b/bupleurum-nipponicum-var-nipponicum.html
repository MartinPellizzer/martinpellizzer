Bupleurum nipponicum var. nipponicum

Bupleurum nipponicum var. nipponicum, commonly known as Japanese bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic compounds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated margins, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.
