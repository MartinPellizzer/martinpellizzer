Bidens elongata

Bidens elongata, commonly known as the slender beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces seeds with tiny bristles that aid in wind dispersal. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
