Boswellia neglecta

The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and small, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The botanical structure of Boswellia neglecta supports its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments through its adaptations such as reduced leaf size and deep root systems.
