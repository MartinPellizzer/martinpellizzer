Blumea luoshaoensis

Blumea luoshaoensis, commonly known as the Luoshao blumea, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic properties. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous regions and forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and clustered in capitula.

Blumea luoshaoensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
