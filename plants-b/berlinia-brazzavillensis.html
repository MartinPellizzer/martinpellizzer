Berlinia brazzavillensis

Berlinia brazzavillensis, commonly known as the African ironwood tree, is a deciduous tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The tree has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated pods.

Its deep root system helps it thrive in poor soils, making it an important species in forest ecosystems.
