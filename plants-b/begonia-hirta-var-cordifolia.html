Begonia hirta var. cordifolia

Begonia hirta var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its vibrant floral display.
