Bulbophyllum odoratum var. polyarachne

Bulbophyllum odoratum var. polyarachne, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental value.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
