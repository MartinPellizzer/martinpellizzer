Begonia fengii

Begonia fengii, commonly known as the Feng Liang begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to China.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting yellow markings. It thrives in partial shade and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouse cultivation.
