Brossaea adenothrix

Brossaea adenothrix, commonly known as the silverleaf brossaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, pubescent hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Brossaea adenothrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
