Buddleja thyrsoides

Buddleja thyrsoides, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract a variety of pollinators. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Buddleja thyrsoides is known for its ornamental value and its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
