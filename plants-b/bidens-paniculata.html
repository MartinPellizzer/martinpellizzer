Bidens paniculata

Bidens paniculata, commonly known as the tickseed sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with dark centers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, spiny fruits that aid in seed dispersal by attaching to animal fur.

Bidens paniculata is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
