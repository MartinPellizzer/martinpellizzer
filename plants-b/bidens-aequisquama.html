Bidens aequisquama

Bidens aequisquama, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disks.

Bidens aequisquama is known for its ability to thrive in wetland environments and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
