Berberis chinensis

Berberis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spring, followed by red berries in autumn. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The berries are also used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some cuisines.
