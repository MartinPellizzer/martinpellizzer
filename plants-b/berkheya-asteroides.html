Berkheya asteroides

Berkheya asteroides, commonly known as the star daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Berkheya asteroides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
