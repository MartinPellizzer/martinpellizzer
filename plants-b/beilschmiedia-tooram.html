Beilschmiedia tooram

Beilschmiedia tooram, commonly known as the Tooram beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.
