Bulbophyllum septemtrionale

Bulbophyllum septemtrionale, commonly known as the Northern Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it thrives in well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
