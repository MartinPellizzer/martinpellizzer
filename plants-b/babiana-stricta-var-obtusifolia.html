Babiana stricta var. obtusifolia

Babiana stricta var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf babiana, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple base. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces underground bulbs that store nutrients for the next growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both horticultural displays and ecological restoration projects.
