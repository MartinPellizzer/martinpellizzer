Begonia hatacoa var. rubrifolia

Begonia hatacoa var. rubrifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features velvety, dark green leaves with prominent red veins, giving it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique foliage and blooming habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
