Begonia daweishanensis

Begonia daweishanensis, commonly known as the Daweishan begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and can grow as a shrub or trailing vine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
