Bloomeria crocea var. montana

Bloomeria crocea var. montana, commonly known as the mountain bloomera, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
