Bambusa multiplex f. viridistriata

Bambusa multiplex f. viridistriata, commonly known as the green-striped bamboo, is a variegated form of the common bamboo species.

It features striking foliage with alternating green and white or yellow stripes along the leaf blades. This bamboo is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its slender, upright culms make it suitable for ornamental landscaping and bamboo art.

The plant is relatively low maintenance but requires well-draining soil and partial to full sun exposure.
