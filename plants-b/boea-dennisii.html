Boea dennisii

Boea dennisii, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.
