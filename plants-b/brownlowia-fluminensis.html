Brownlowia fluminensis

Brownlowia fluminensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid or the red orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces long, slender leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Brownlowia fluminensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
