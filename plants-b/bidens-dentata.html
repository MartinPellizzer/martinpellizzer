Bidens dentata

Bidens dentata, commonly known as the toothed ironweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features serrated leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disks. It thrives in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks.

Bidens dentata is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for pollinators.
