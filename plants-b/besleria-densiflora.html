Besleria densiflora

Besleria densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered gesneriad, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a velvety texture. It produces clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

Besleria densiflora is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
