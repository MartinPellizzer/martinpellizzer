Boronia granulata

Boronia granulata, commonly known as the rough boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in heathland and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are often covered in fine, granular hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Boronia granulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.
