Bulbophyllum dolichoblepharon

Bulbophyllum dolichoblepharon, commonly known as the long-eyed bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, often brightly colored flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
