Blumea hongkongensis

Blumea hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong blueflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
