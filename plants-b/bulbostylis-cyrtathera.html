Bulbostylis cyrtathera

Bulbostylis cyrtathera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is a small, tufted perennial grass that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, three-lobed flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive sheath that clasps the stem.

This species is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in areas with high humidity and well-drained soils.
