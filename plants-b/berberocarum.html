Berberocarum

Berberocarum, commonly known as the African blueberry, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It produces small, edible berries that are rich in antioxidants and have a tart, slightly sweet flavor. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, while its flowers are small and white or pale yellow.

The berries are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine for their health benefits.
