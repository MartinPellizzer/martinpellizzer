Billbergia patentissima

Billbergia patentissima, commonly known as the "pink billbergia," is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and vibrant, showy inflorescences that range from pink to red. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports aquatic life. Its flowers are typically borne on long, branching stems and can last for several months.

Billbergia patentissima is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
