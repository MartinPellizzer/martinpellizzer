Begonia hidalgensis

Begonia hidalgensis, commonly known as the Hidalgo begonia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, white, and red, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and relatively easy care requirements.
