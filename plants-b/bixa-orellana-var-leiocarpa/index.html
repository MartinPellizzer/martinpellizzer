Bixa Orellana Var. Leiocarpa

Bixa orellana var. leiocarpa is a lesser-known but visually striking plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. This variant of the annatto plant is prized for its vibrant orange seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines and natural dyes. Its unique appearance and versatile uses make it an intriguing subject for botanical and cultural study. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Despite its potential, it remains underappreciated compared to its more widely recognized relatives.
