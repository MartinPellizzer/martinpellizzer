Biscutella lima proles nicaeensis

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and has a low, mat-forming habit. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine pubescence, while its flowers are pale yellow with four petals.
