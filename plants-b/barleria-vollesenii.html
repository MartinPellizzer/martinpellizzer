Barleria vollesenii

Barleria vollesenii, commonly known as the velvetleaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and appear in clusters during the warm season.

Barleria vollesenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
