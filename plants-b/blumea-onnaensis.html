Blumea onnaensis

Blumea onnaensis, commonly known as the Philippine blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The flowers are small, blue to purple, and arranged in dense, spherical clusters, giving it a mist-like appearance.
