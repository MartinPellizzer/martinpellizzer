Berberis × vanfleetii

Berberis × vanfleetii, commonly known as the Vanfleet barberry, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but typically not consumed by humans. Native to the eastern United States, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and floral display.
