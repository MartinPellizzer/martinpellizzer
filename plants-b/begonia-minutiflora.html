Begonia minutiflora

Begonia minutiflora, commonly known as the tiny flower begonia, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is often cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
