Begonia havilandii

Begonia havilandii, commonly known as the Haviland's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia havilandii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
