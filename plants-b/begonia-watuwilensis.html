Begonia watuwilensis

Begonia watuwilensis, commonly known as the Watuwilensis begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, often displaying intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
