Begonia flaviflora

Begonia flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with smooth, glossy foliage. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia flaviflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
