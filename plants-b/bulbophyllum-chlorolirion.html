Bulbophyllum chlorolirion

Bulbophyllum chlorolirion, commonly known as the green-lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip, and it produces small, greenish-white blooms.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
