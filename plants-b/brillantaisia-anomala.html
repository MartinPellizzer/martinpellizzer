Brillantaisia anomala

Brillantaisia anomala, commonly known as the golden torch tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, resembling torches. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Brillantaisia anomala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
