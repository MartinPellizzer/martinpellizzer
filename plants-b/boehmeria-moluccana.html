Boehmeria moluccana

Boehmeria moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan nettle, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes many stinging plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
