Bonnaya alata

Bonnaya alata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged pods and compound leaves. The plant is cultivated for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Bonnaya alata is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial crop in sustainable agriculture.
