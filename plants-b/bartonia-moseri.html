Bartonia moseri

Bartonia moseri, commonly known as the Florida bartronia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in sandy or coastal habitats. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments.

Bartonia moseri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
