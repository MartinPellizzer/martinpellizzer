Bennettia splendens

Bennettia splendens, commonly known as the golden bennettia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to its natural habitat. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy blossoms. Bennettia splendens thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
