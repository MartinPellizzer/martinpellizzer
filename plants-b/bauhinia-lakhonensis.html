Bauhinia lakhonensis

Bauhinia lakhonensis, commonly known as the Khorat bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features distinctive bipinnate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Bauhinia lakhonensis is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
