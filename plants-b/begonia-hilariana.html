Begonia hilariana

Begonia hilariana, commonly known as the Hilarian begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces. Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Begonia hilariana is also valued for its ability to adapt to various environmental conditions, enhancing its appeal among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
