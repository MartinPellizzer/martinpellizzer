Burmeistera resupinata var. resupinata

Burmeistera resupinata var. resupinata, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a striking perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it ideal for hanging baskets or containers. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring regular watering during the growing season.
