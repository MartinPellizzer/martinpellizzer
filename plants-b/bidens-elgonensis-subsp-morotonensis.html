Bidens elgonensis subsp. morotonensis

Bidens elgonensis subsp.

morotonensis, commonly known as the East African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and yellow flower heads.

The plant produces small, disc-like flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest margins.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
