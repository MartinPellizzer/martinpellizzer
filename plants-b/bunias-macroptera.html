Bunias macroptera

Bunias macroptera, commonly known as broadleaf mustard, is a biennial or perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in disturbed areas.

Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some cuisines.
