Bignonia erubescens var. breviflora

Bignonia erubescens var. breviflora, commonly known as the scarlet trumpetbush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces showy, orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. These flowers are adapted for pollination by hummingbirds and bees.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
