Blackstonia serotina f. aestiva

Blackstonia serotina f. aestiva, commonly known as the late blooming form of the blackstonia serotina, is a rare and unique variant of the blackstonia serotina.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States, typically found in moist, acidic soils in woodland habitats.
