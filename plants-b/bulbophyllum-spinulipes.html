Bulbophyllum spinulipes

Bulbophyllum spinulipes, commonly known as the spiny lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring prominently spined petals and a lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers, which bloom in a range of colors including white, yellow, and orange, are highly prized in horticulture for their beauty and rarity.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
