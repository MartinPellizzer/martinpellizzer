Bulbophyllum calothyrsus

Bulbophyllum calothyrsus, commonly known as the velvet lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and fleshy, adapted for efficient water retention.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid conditions and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.
