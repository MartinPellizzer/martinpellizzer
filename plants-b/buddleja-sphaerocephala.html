Buddleja sphaerocephala

Buddleja sphaerocephala, commonly known as the spherical buddleja, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive spherical flower clusters that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Buddleja sphaerocephala is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
