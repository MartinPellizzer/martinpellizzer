Braya siliquosa

Braya siliquosa, commonly known as the slender bladderwort, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
