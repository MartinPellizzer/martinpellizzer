Ballota hirsuta var. gracilis

Ballota hirsuta var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender hairy ballota, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

It is often found in rocky or dry soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
