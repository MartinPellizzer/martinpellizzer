Baliospermum solanifolium

Baliospermum solanifolium, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Bindweed family (Convolvulaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, violet to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in open woodlands.

Baliospermum solanifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
