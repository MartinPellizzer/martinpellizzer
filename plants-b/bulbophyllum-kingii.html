Bulbophyllum kingii

Bulbophyllum kingii, commonly known as the King's Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish-yellow with contrasting purple or red markings. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the surrounding substrate.

Bulbophyllum kingii is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
