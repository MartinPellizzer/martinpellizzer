Bulbophyllum longipedicellatum var. longipedicellatum

Bulbophyllum longipedicellatum var. longipedicellatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed bulbophyllum, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its rhizomes allow it to spread and form clumps, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

The species is noted for its resilience and adaptability to varying light conditions.
