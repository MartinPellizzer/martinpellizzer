Bulbophyllum xizangense

Bulbophyllum xizangense, commonly known as the Xizang orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Tibet.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
