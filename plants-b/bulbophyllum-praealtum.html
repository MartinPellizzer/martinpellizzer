Bulbophyllum praealtum

Bulbophyllum praealtum, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding substrate, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
