Begonia ruschii

Begonia ruschii, commonly known as the Rusch's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a unique texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia ruschii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
