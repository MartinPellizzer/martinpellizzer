Benaurea sempervirens

Benaurea sempervirens, commonly known as the evergreen shrub, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features woody stems and dense, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a leathery texture, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Benaurea sempervirens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
