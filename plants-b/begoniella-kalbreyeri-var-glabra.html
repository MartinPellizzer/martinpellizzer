Begoniella kalbreyeri var. glabra

Begoniella kalbreyeri var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth begoniella, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, glabrous (smooth) leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red or orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after species among collectors and botanists.

Due to its limited natural range and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a conservation concern.
