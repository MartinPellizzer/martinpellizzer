Begonia × chambersii

Begonia × chambersii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a flowering plant species that belongs to the Begoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals, which are used to attract pollinators. The plant typically features upright, branching stems covered in soft, velvety foliage. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in terminal inflorescences.

Begonia × chambersii is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
