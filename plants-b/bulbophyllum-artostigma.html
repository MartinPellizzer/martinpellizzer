Bulbophyllum artostigma

Bulbophyllum artostigma, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, golden-yellow pseudobulbs. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bulbophyllum artostigma is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
