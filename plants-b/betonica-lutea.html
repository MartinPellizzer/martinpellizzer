Betonica lutea

Betonica lutea, commonly known as yellow betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Betonica lutea is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in herbal remedies.
