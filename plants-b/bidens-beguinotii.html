Bidens beguinotii

Bidens beguinotii, commonly known as the golden beggar's-tick, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

The plant produces yellow flowers with prominent central disks, and its leaves are typically oval with serrated edges. Bidens beguinotii is also known for its medicinal properties, used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
