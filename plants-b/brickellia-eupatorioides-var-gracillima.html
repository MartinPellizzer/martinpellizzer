Brickellia eupatorioides var. gracillima

Brickellia eupatorioides var. gracillima, commonly known as slender brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
