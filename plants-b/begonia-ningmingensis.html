Begonia ningmingensis

Begonia ningmingensis, commonly known as the Ningming begonia, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting colors, making it a prized specimen among botanists and horticulturists.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
