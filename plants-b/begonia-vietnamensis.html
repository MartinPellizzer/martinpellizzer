Begonia vietnamensis

Begonia vietnamensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese begonia, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with intricate patterns, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its aesthetic beauty.
