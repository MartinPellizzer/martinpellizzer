Bulbophyllum horridulum

Bulbophyllum horridulum, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often spiky appearance, with leaves that are typically dark green and fleshy. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often brightly colored, ranging from yellow to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
