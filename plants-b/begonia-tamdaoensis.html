Begonia tamdaoensis

Begonia tamdaoensis, commonly known as the Tamdao begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and purple hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
