Bulbophyllum scintilla

Bulbophyllum scintilla, commonly known as the sparkling bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically borne on short, branched inflorescences, and it blooms during the warmer months.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
