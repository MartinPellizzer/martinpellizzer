Bulbophyllum arminii

Bulbophyllum arminii, commonly known as the Armin's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum arminii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
