Botryopleuron plukenetii

Botryopleuron plukenetii, commonly known as the African wax tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red fruits containing seeds. Botryopleuron plukenetii thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
