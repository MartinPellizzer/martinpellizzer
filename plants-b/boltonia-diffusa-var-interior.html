Boltonia diffusa var. interior

Boltonia diffusa var. interior, commonly known as the interior boltonia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its foliage is typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
