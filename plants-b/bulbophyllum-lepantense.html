Bulbophyllum lepantense

Bulbophyllum lepantense, commonly known as the Lepantense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, typically in shades of yellow and red, during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
