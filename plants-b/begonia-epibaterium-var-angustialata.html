Begonia epibaterium var. angustialata

Begonia epibaterium var. angustialata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface.

It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for those interested in tropical flora.
