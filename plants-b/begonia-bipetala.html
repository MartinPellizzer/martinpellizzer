Begonia bipetala

Begonia bipetala, commonly known as the two-petaled begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature two large, fleshy petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia bipetala is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique floral display.
