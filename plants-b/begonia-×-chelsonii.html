Begonia × chelsonii

Begonia × chelsonii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its vibrant and diverse flower colors.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pavonina, combining the best traits of both parents. The plant typically features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, Begonia × chelsonii is widely used in gardens and as potted plants.
