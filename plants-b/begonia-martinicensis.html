Begonia martinicensis

Begonia martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thick, waxy leaves that are often green or reddish in hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Begonia martinicensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
