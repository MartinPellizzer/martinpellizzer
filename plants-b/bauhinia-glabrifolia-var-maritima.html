Bauhinia glabrifolia var. maritima

Bauhinia glabrifolia var. maritima, commonly known as the coastal bauhinia, is a perennial shrub native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to salty air and wind exposure. It is often found in dune environments and plays a role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
