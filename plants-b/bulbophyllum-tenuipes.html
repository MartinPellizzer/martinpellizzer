Bulbophyllum tenuipes

Bulbophyllum tenuipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Bulbophyllum tenuipes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
