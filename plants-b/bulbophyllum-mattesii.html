Bulbophyllum mattesii

Bulbophyllum mattesii, commonly known as the Mattes' orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic plant that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a unique, intricate pattern on the petals. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
