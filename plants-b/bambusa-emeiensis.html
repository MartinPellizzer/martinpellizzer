Bambusa emeiensis

Bambusa emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a tall, slender bamboo that can grow up to 20 meters in height. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and is characterized by its strong, fibrous culms. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Bambusa emeiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in construction and traditional crafts.
