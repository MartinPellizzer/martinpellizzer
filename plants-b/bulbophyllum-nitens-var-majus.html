Bulbophyllum nitens var. majus

Bulbophyllum nitens var. majus, commonly known as the Giant Pterostylis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
