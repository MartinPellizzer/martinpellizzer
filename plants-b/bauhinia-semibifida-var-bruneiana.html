Bauhinia semibifida var. bruneiana

Bauhinia semibifida var. bruneiana, commonly known as the Bruneian bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Its seeds are encased in hard, pod-like structures that split open when mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
