Boronia ledifolia var. triphylla

Boronia ledifolia var. triphylla, commonly known as the triphylla boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are clustered in dense inflorescences.
