Boehmeria caudata var. petiolaris

Boehmeria caudata var. petiolaris, commonly known as the slender flax or slender Boehmeria, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and of interest for sustainable material applications.
