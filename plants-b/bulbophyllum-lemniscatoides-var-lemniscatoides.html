Bulbophyllum lemniscatoides var. lemniscatoides

Bulbophyllum lemniscatoides var. lemniscatoides, commonly known as the Orchid with the Swung Wings, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with flowers that have a unique, wing-like appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
