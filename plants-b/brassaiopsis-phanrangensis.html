Brassaiopsis phanrangensis

It belongs to the family Viscaceae and is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and fleshy, sticky berries that facilitate seed dispersal by birds. Its ecological role is significant in maintaining the biodiversity of its forest ecosystem.
