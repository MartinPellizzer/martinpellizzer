Bubbia monocarpa

Bubbia monocarpa, commonly known as the single-seeded burrplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Bubbia monocarpa is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage and fruit structure.
