Bulbophyllum savaiense subsp. gorumense

Bulbophyllum savaiense subsp.

gorumense, commonly known as the Gorumen Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the island of Savai'i in Samoa. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces intricate inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its pseudobulbs are prominent and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This orchid is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.
