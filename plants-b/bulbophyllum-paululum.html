Bulbophyllum paululum

Bulbophyllum paululum, commonly known as the Paul's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

Bulbophyllum paululum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
