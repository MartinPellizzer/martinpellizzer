Bridelia glauca var. acuminatissima

Bridelia glauca var. acuminatissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often found in dry, open woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
