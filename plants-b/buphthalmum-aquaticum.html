Buphthalmum aquaticum

Buphthalmum aquaticum, commonly known as water-eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland areas and along the edges of water bodies. The plant features showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive, inflated calyx that resembles a small eye. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its moist habitat.

Buphthalmum aquaticum is often found in shallow water or damp soil, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
