Begonia kribensis

Begonia kribensis, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that ranges in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture without being waterlogged.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
