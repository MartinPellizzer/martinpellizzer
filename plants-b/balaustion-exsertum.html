Balaustion exsertum

Balaustion exsertum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.

Balaustion exsertum is also known for its ability to produce offsets, which can be propagated to grow new plants.
