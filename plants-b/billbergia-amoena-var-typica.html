Billbergia amoena var. typica

Billbergia amoena var. typica, commonly known as the pink billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink bracts that surround the inflorescence, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, which help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in ornamental gardening due to its long-lasting floral display and relatively low maintenance requirements.
