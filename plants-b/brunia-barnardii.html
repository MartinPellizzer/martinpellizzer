Brunia barnardii

Brunia barnardii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange-yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
