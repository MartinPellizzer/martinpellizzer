Beta vulgaris subsp. perennis

Beta vulgaris subsp.

perennis, commonly known as莙菜 or Swiss chard, is a leafy vegetable belonging to the Amaranthaceae family. It is characterized by its thick, edible stems and colorful, lobed leaves that come in shades of red, yellow, and white.

The plant is typically grown for its nutrient-rich leaves and stems, which are high in vitamins and minerals. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated in temperate regions.

Its botanical structure supports both ornamental and culinary uses.
