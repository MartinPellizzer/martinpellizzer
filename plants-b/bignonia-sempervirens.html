Bignonia sempervirens

Bignonia sempervirens, commonly known as yellow shower vine, is a climbing plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in gardens.
