Byttneria acalyphifolia

Byttneria acalyphifolia, commonly known as the African starwood, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, woody stems and palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by woody, star-shaped fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its leaves and bark contain compounds that have been used for their purported medicinal properties.
