Begonia henriquesii

Begonia henriquesii, commonly known as the Henriques begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Brazil.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, fuchsia-pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate blossoms.
