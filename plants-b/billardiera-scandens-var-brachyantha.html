Billardiera scandens var. brachyantha

Billardiera scandens var. brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces yellow, pea-like flowers arranged in racemes. The leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller, more compact leaves compared to the typical form of Billardiera scandens.
