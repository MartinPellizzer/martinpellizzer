Bryophyllum schizophyllum

Bryophyllum schizophyllum, commonly known as the leafy liverwort, is a non-vascular plant belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, closely packed leaves that form a dense, carpet-like growth on rocky or sandy substrates. Bryophyllum schizophyllum is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, where it thrives in harsh, well-drained conditions.
