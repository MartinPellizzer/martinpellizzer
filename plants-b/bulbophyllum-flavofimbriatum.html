Bulbophyllum flavofimbriatum

Bulbophyllum flavofimbriatum, commonly known as the yellow-bordered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with yellowish lips and greenish-white petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
