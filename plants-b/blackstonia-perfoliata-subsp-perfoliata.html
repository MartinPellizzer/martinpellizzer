Blackstonia perfoliata subsp. perfoliata

Blackstonia perfoliata subsp.

perfoliata, commonly known as the perfoliate blackstonia, is a rare and distinctive flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It is characterized by its unique leaf structure, where the leaves are fused around the stems, creating a perfoliate appearance.

The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.
