Bulbophyllum milesii

Bulbophyllum milesii, commonly known as the Mile's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structures. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.

Bulbophyllum milesii thrives in humid, shaded conditions and is often found in forest understories.
