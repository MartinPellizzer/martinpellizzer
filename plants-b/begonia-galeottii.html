Begonia galeottii

Begonia galeottii, commonly known as the Brazilian begonia, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep red or maroon in color. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia galeottii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage.
