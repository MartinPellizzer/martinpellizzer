Balsamea capensis

Balsamea capensis, commonly known as the Cape balsam.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Balsamea capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
