Blechnum bruneum

Blechnum bruneum, commonly known as the Brunei fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Blechnaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
