Bidens lindblomii

Bidens lindblomii, commonly known as Lindblom's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Bidens lindblomii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
