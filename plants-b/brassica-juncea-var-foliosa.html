Brassica juncea var. foliosa

Brassica juncea var. foliosa, commonly known as Chinese kale or broccoli raab, is a leafy vegetable belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, curled leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly bitter taste. The plant produces small, green flowers that eventually develop into pods containing tiny, edible seeds. It is cultivated for its edible leaves and is often grown in temperate climates.

This variety is well-suited for cool-season growing conditions and is valued for its nutritional content and versatility in cooking.
