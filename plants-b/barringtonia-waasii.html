Barringtonia waasii

Barringtonia waasii, commonly known as the Waasii lantern tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces large, woody fruits that split open to release its seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

Barringtonia waasii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
