Bulbophyllum glabrilabre

Bulbophyllum glabrilabre, commonly known as the smooth-lipped bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly colored flowers with a distinctive, smooth labellum. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, fragrant flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
