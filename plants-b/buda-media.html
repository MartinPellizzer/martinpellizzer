Buda media

Buda media, commonly known as the Buda media orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its striking, colorful blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and can vary in color from white to yellow and pink.

Buda media requires warm, humid conditions and indirect sunlight to thrive.
