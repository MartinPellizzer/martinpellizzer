Begonia carpinifolia var. rubroinvolucrata

Begonia carpinifolia var. rubroinvolucrata, commonly known as the red-bracted begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It features vibrant red bracts that surround its small, white flowers, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
