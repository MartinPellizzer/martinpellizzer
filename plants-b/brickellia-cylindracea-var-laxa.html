Brickellia cylindracea var. laxa

Brickellia cylindracea var. laxa, commonly known as the slender brickellia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woods. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in wetland habitats.
