Butia wildemaniana

Butia wildemaniana, commonly known as the wildemaniana palm, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized palm with a stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of narrow, feather-like leaves. The plant produces large, yellow-orange inflorescences that bear both male and female flowers. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

This palm is adapted to dry climates and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.
