Berberis pulangensis

Berberis pulangensis, commonly known as the Philippine barberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often considered astringent.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
