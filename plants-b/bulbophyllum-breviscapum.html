Bulbophyllum breviscapum

Bulbophyllum breviscapum, commonly known as the short-stalked pterostigma orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines, and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that often have a distinctive yellow or greenish hue. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, supporting the plant's foliage and flowering structures.

This species is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
