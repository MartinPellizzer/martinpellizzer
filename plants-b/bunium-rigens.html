Bunium rigens

Bunium rigens, commonly known as Siberian fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and distinctive umbellate inflorescences. The plant features slender, ridged stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its small white flowers appear in late spring, followed by oval, yellowish-brown seeds.

Bunium rigens is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
