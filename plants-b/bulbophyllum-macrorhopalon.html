Bulbophyllum macrorhopalon

Bulbophyllum macrorhopalon, commonly known as the large-ridge bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features elongated pseudobulbs that support the leaves and flowers. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with intricate patterns, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structures.
