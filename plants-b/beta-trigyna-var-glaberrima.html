Beta trigyna var. glaberrima

Beta trigyna var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smoothleaf beta, is a variant of the beta plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically features small, pale green leaves with a slightly succulent texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Its botanical classification places it within the Amaranthaceae family, which includes other economically important plants.
