Begonia nantoensis

Begonia nantoensis, commonly known as the Nantoensis begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, often variegated leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is often deeply lobed, and the flowers can range in color from pink to red.

Begonia nantoensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
