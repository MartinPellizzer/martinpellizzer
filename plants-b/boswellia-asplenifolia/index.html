Boswellia Asplenifolia

Boswellia asplenifolia is a rare and unique species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa. It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are shaped like those of the asplenium fern, giving it its common name. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Boswellia asplenifolia is now considered a vulnerable species.
