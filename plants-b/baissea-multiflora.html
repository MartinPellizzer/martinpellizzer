Baissea multiflora

Baissea multiflora, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Baissea multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
