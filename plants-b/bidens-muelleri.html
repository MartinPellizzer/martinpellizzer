Bidens muelleri

Bidens muelleri, commonly known as the common tickseed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small yellow flowers with prominent white markings. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Bidens muelleri is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and waste places, where it spreads readily by seed.
