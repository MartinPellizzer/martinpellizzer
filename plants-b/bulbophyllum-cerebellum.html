Bulbophyllum cerebellum

Bulbophyllum cerebellum, commonly known as the cerebellum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
