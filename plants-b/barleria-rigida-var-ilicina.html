Barleria rigida var. ilicina

Barleria rigida var. ilicina, commonly known as the narrow-leaf barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting floral display.
