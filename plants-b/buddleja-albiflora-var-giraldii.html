Buddleja albiflora var. giraldii

Buddleja albiflora var. giraldii, commonly known as the White Butterfly Bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
