Biota meldensis

Biota meldensis, commonly known as the Meldensian flameleaf, is a unique plant native to the semi-arid regions of the Meldens Basin.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange foliage, which becomes more pronounced during the dry season. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the surface layer of the soil. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically bloom in clusters during the early spring.

Biota meldensis plays an important role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators and shelter for small fauna.
