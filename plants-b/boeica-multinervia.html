Boeica multinervia

Boeica multinervia, commonly known as the multifoliolate fern, is a species of fern characterized by its intricate and delicate fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features complex, feather-like leaves with multiple divisions, giving it a lush, ornamental appearance. Its rhizome system allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth.

Boeica multinervia is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
