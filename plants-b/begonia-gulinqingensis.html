Begonia gulinqingensis

Begonia gulinqingensis, commonly known as the Guangxi begonia, is a species native to China, specifically the Guangxi region.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture.

The flowers, which can be pink or red, bloom in summer and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
