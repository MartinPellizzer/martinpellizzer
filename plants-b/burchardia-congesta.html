Burchardia congesta

Burchardia congesta, commonly known as the clustered burchardia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes bellflowers, and is characterized by its clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. Burchardia congesta is often found in association with other wetland flora and plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
