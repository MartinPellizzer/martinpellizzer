Bulbophyllum phanquyetii

Bulbophyllum phanquyetii, commonly known as the Phan Quyet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a raceme, often displaying a range of hues from yellow to red.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
