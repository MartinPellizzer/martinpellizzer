Begonia huegelii

Begonia huegelii, commonly known as the Hugel begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Begonia huegelii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
