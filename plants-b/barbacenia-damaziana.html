Barbacenia damaziana

Barbacenia damaziana, commonly known as the Brazilian violet, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its violet-colored flowers and heart-shaped leaves. Barbacenia damaziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
