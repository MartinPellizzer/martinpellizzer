Brassica souliei subsp. souliei

Brassica souliei subsp.

souliei, commonly known as the Chinese mustard or Chinese yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to China. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typical of the Brassicaceae family. Its seeds are small and yellow, often used in traditional Chinese cuisine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
