Bauhinia yingtakensis

Bauhinia yingtakensis, commonly known as the Yingtakensis Bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.
