Boea arachnoidea

Boea arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider plant or spider vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, fluffy seed pods that resemble spider webs, giving it its common name. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Boea arachnoidea thrives in warm, humid environments and is well-suited for hanging baskets or trellises.
