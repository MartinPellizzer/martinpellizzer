Briza argentea

Briza argentea, commonly known as silver feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces delicate, plume-like inflorescences that add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Briza argentea is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to form dense, low-maintenance ground cover.
