Begonia lantanifolia

Begonia lantanifolia, commonly known as the fragrant begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas due to its preference for indirect light. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild aromatic scent.
