Bonnayodes

Bonnayodes, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows to a height of 10-20 meters. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a silvery-gray underside that gives the plant its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
