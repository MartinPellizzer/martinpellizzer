Bulbophyllum christophersenii

Bulbophyllum christophersenii, commonly known as the Christoffer's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with dense, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and delicate fragrance.
