Bouteloua purpurea

Bouteloua purpurea, commonly known as purple threeawn, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pink to purple in color, which gives it its common name. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.
