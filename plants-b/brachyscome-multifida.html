Brachyscome multifida

Brachyscome multifida, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with silvery-white leaves that are deeply lobed and fuzzy to the touch. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Brachyscome multifida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
