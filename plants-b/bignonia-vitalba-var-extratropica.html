Bignonia vitalba var. extratropica

Bignonia vitalba var. extratropica, commonly known as the tropical bignonia, is a variant of the genus Bignonia native to tropical regions.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm climates with ample sunlight and well-drained soil.

Due to its rapid growth, it is often used in ornamental horticulture but can become invasive if not managed properly.
