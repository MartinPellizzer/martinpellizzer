Buphthalmum grandiflorum

Buphthalmum grandiflorum, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
