Begonia agusanensis

Begonia agusanensis, commonly known as the Agusan begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking foliage and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are often heart-shaped with a velvety texture, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
