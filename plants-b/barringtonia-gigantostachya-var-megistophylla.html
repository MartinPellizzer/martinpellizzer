Barringtonia gigantostachya var. megistophylla

Barringtonia gigantostachya var. megistophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf banyan, is a variant of the banyan tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces large, pendulous white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its fruit is a fibrous capsule containing numerous seeds.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
