Bauhinia wuzhengyii

Bauhinia wuzhengyii, commonly known as the Wuzheng bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, pod-like fruits.

Bauhinia wuzhengyii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blossoms.
