Bambusa guangxiensis

Bambusa guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi bamboo, is a species native to southern China, particularly Guangxi Province.

It is a tall, slender bamboo that can reach heights of up to 15 meters. The plant features hollow, segmented stems with a smooth, pale green to yellowish-brown surface. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, typically arranged in clusters at the nodes.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and traditional crafts.
