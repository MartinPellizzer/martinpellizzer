Begonia rodwellii

Begonia rodwellii, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Begonia rodwellii is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
