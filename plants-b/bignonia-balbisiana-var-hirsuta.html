Bignonia balbisiana var. hirsuta

Bignonia balbisiana var. hirsuta, commonly known as hairy bignonia, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange to yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in suitable environments.
