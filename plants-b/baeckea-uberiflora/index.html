Baeckea Uberiflora

Baeckea with showy flowers, scientifically known as Baeckea uberiflora, is a striking member of the family Myrtaceae native to Australia. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant, clustered blooms that appear in spring and early summer. Its petals are often a deep pink or red, adding a bold splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. Baeckea uberiflora thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and suburban gardens. With its ornamental value and adaptability, it has become a favored plant among horticulturists and nature enthusiasts alike.
