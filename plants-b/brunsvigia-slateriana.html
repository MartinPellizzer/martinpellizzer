Brunsvigia slateriana

Brunsvigia slateriana, commonly known as the firecracker lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers in late summer. The plant's bulbs are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
