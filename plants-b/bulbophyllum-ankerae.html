Bulbophyllum ankerae

Bulbophyllum ankerae, commonly known as the Ankerae's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, intricate flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in less-than-ideal conditions.

Bulbophyllum ankerae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
