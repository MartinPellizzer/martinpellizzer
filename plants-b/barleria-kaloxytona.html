Barleria kaloxytona

Barleria kaloxytona, commonly known as the Cape ironwood, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has a woody stem and is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid conditions. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The leaves are aromatic and have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.
