Bromus inermis f. prolifer

Bromus inermis f. prolifer, commonly known as the spreading brome, is a variant of the slender brome grass.

It is a perennial grass native to the western United States and parts of Canada. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its botanical characteristics include slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is a defining feature of the Poaceae family.
