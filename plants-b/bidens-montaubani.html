Bidens montaubani

Bidens montaubani, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as stream banks and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped with rough textures, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Bidens montaubani is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
