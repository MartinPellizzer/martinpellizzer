Berberis virescens var. macrocarpa

Berberis virescens var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping and traditional medicine.
