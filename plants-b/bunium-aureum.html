Bunium aureum

Bunium aureum, commonly known as golden fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in umbels. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
