Begonia dregei var. macbethii

Begonia dregei var. macbethii, commonly known as the Macbeth begonia, is a hybrid species originating from South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green foliage and showy, bi-color flowers that range from red to pink. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, often grown as an ornamental for its foliage and floral display. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for greenhouse or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance has made it a popular choice among collectors and gardeners.
