Bothriochloa Woodrovii

Bothriochloa woodrovii is a rare grass species native to the southeastern united states. it thrives in sandy soils and humid climates, often found in coastal regions. this plant is known for its unique leaf structure and ability to withstand salt spray. it plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects. due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
