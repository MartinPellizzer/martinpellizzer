Bulbophyllum tenerum

Bulbophyllum tenerum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces linear, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and come in a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
