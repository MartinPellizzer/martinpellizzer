Billbergia amoena var. penduliflora

Billbergia amoena var. penduliflora, commonly known as the hanging flower billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the plant's use. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored bracts that range from red to orange. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, adding a delicate touch to its overall appearance.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
