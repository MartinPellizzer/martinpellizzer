Begonia wengeri

Begonia wengeri, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and veins. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the day. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among collectors and gardeners who appreciate its unique foliage and colorful blooms.
