Brunia marlothii

Brunia marlothii, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Brunia marlothii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
