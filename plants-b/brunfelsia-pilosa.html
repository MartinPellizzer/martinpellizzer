Brunfelsia pilosa

Brunfelsia pilosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Brazilian jasmine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy, giving it a velvety texture. It produces berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.

Brunfelsia pilosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
