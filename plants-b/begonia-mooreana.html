Begonia mooreana

Begonia mooreana, commonly known as the Moore's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which help retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Begonia mooreana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
