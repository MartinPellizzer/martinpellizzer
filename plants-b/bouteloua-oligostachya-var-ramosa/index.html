Bouteloua Oligostachya Var. Ramosa

Bouteloua oligostachya var. ramosa is a striking grass species known for its delicate, silver-blue foliage and fluffy seed heads. Native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States, it thrives in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. This plant is often found in open plains, rocky slopes, and disturbed soils, making it a resilient member of the grass family. Its unique appearance and ability to survive in extreme conditions make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively overlooked in mainstream horticulture.
