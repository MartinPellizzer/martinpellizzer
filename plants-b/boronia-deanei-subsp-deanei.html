Boronia deanei subsp. deanei

Boronia deanei subsp.

deanei, commonly known as the Grey Boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring aromatic, glandular leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
