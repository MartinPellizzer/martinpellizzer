Bactris erostrata

Bactris erostrata, commonly known as the pink palm, is a species of palm native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, clustered stems and pinnate leaves that are often tinged with pink or red hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, orange-colored fruits. Bactris erostrata is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
