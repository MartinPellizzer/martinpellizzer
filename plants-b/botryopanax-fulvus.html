Botryopanax fulvus

Botryopanax fulvus, commonly known as the golden spurge.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa, characterized by its distinctive yellowish-green foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly rough texture.

Botryopanax fulvus is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
