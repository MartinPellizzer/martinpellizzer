Bulbophyllum macranthum f. alboviride

Bulbophyllum macranthum f. alboviride, commonly known as the white-green orchid, is a hybrid orchid species within the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring a striking combination of white and green tones on its leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in tropical rainforest environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a pale greenish-white hue that sets it apart from other Bulbophyllum species.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
