Bupariti altissima

Bupariti altissima, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding environment.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
