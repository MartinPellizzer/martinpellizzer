Banisteria elegans var. grandifolia

Banisteria elegans var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered banisteria, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its foliage and floral structure make it a popular choice for ornamental use in tropical gardens.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
