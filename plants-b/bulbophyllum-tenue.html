Bulbophyllum tenue

Bulbophyllum tenue, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum tenue is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
