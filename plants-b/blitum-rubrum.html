Blitum rubrum

Blitum rubrum, commonly known as redroot filaree, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped, reddish leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in disturbed areas.
