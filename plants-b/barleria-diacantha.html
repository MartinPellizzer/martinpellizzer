Barleria diacantha

Barleria diacantha, commonly known as the red-headed grass or red-headed barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and are typically pink to red in color, blooming from late winter to early summer.

Barleria diacantha is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
