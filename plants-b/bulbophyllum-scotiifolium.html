Bulbophyllum scotiifolium

Bulbophyllum scotiifolium, commonly known as the Scottish leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Scotland.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces slender, upright stems with a few leaves and bears small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

Bulbophyllum scotiifolium is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in rocky or grassy habitats.
