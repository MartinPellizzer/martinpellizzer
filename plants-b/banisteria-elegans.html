Banisteria elegans

Banisteria elegans, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Banisteria elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a shade plant in tropical landscapes.
