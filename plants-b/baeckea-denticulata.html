Baeckea denticulata

Baeckea denticulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved baeckea, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, ovate leaves with finely toothed margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is typically found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal and inland regions.

Baeckea denticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscaping.
