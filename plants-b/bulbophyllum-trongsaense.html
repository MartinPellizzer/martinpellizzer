Bulbophyllum trongsaense

Bulbophyllum trongsaense, commonly known as the Tongsai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with intricate patterns, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
