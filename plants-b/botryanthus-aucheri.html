Botryanthus aucheri

Botryanthus aucheri, commonly known as the Persian buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and produces small, rounded seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Botryanthus aucheri is known for its hardy nature and ability to bloom reliably in temperate climates.
