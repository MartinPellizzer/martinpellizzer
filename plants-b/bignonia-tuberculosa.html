Bignonia tuberculosa

Bignonia tuberculosa, commonly known as the scarlet bignonia, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, flowering vines.
