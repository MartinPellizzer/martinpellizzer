Bargemontia sphaerophylla

Bargemontia sphaerophylla, commonly known as the globe-leaved dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its unique leaf shape and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and contributes to biodiversity in its native habitat.
