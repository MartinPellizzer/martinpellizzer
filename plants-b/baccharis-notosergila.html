Baccharis notosergila

Baccharis notosergila, commonly known as the narrowleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it an important component of its native ecosystems.
