Bulbophyllum malawiense

Bulbophyllum malawiense, commonly known as the Malawi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other plants in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow and pink.

Bulbophyllum malawiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
