Bulbophyllum acuminatum

Bulbophyllum acuminatum, commonly known as the acuminatum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth during dry periods. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that varies from white to yellow.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
