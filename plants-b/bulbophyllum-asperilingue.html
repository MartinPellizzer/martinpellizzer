Bulbophyllum asperilingue

Bulbophyllum asperilingue, commonly known as the rough-lingued orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often hairy or rough-textured leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and cultivation challenges, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
