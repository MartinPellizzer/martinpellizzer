Bidens ostruthioides

Bidens ostruthioides, commonly known as the common beggarlice, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces numerous seeds with pappus structures that aid in wind dispersal. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in various agricultural and urban environments.
