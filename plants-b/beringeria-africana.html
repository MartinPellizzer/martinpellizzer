Beringeria africana

Beringeria africana, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months, which attract various pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Beringeria africana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
