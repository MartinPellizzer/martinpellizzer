Berberis × stenophylla var. rigida

Berberis × stenophylla var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid barberry, is a hybrid shrub characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red-orange berries that are edible but often tart. It is native to regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
