Berberis Pruinosa Var. Centiflora

Berberis pruinosa var. centiflora is a unique and striking plant known for its ornamental value and hardy nature. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This variant is distinguished by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. Its foliage is dark green and often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, adding to its visual appeal. It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient and visually interesting shrub.
