Bulbophyllum ochroleucum

Bulbophyllum ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with small, yellowish-green flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
