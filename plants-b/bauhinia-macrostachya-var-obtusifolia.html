Bauhinia macrostachya var. obtusifolia

Bauhinia macrostachya var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into numerous small, ovate leaflets. The plant produces clusters of showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or purple calyx. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

The species is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
