Bidens Cervantesii

Bidens cervantesii is a rare flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its distinctive yellow-orange flowers and serrated leaves. This species is often found in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
