Bromus brachystachys var. longipes

Bromus brachystachys var. longipes, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spike that bears small, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to arid conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in its native environment.
