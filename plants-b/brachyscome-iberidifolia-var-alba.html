Brachyscome iberidifolia var. alba

Brachyscome iberidifolia var. alba, commonly known as the white everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and small, daisy-like white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has hairy leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
