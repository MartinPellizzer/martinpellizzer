Barbeuia

Barbeuia, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

Barbeuia species are known for their rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions, making them popular in horticulture and landscaping.
