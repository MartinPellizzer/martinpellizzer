Bulbophyllum encephalodes

Bulbophyllum encephalodes, commonly known as the brain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure that resembles the human brain, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale green or white flowers that are often clustered in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum encephalodes is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
