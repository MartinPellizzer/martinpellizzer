Begonia sinensis var. puberula

Begonia sinensis var. puberula, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, often clustered in terminal inflorescences. It prefers cool, humid conditions and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its foliage is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and unique texture.
