Boehmeria petiolaris

Boehmeria petiolaris, commonly known as the silky fiber plant, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and fibrous stems, which are rich in natural fibers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a smooth margin and a velvety texture.

Boehmeria petiolaris is valued for its fibrous materials, which have been used in traditional crafts and textiles.
