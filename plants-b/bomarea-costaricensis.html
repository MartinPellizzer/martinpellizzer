Bomarea costaricensis

Bomarea costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican bomarea, is a striking herbaceous plant native to the humid forests of Central America.

Bomarea costaricensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
