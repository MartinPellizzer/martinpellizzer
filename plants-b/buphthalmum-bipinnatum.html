Buphthalmum bipinnatum

Buphthalmum bipinnatum, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow, daisy-like flowers and narrow, pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Buphthalmum bipinnatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
