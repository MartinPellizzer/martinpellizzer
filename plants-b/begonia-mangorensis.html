Begonia mangorensis

Begonia mangorensis, commonly known as the Mangorensis Begonia, is a tropical plant native to the forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant has heart-shaped or oval leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia mangorensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
