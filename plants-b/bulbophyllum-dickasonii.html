Bulbophyllum dickasonii

Bulbophyllum dickasonii, commonly known as the Dickason's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
