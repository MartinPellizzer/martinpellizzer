Bridelia angolensis var. nitida

Bridelia angolensis var. nitida, commonly known as the glossy bridelia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
