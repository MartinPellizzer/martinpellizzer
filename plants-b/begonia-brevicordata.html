Begonia brevicordata

Begonia brevicordata, commonly known as the short-leaf begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often colorful leaves. The plant typically features heart-shaped foliage with a velvety texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color.

Begonia brevicordata is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ornamental appeal.
