Bonamia capensis

Bonamia capensis, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Bonamia capensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
