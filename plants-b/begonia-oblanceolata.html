Begonia oblanceolata

Begonia oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
