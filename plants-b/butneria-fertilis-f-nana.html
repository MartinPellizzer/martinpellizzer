Butneria fertilis f. nana

Butneria fertilis f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf fertilis butneria, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Due to its compact size, it is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
