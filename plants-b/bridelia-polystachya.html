Bridelia polystachya

Bridelia polystachya, commonly known as the African bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
