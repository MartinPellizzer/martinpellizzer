Begonia inobongensis

Begonia inobongensis, commonly known as the Cameroon begonia, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and can vary in color from deep green to reddish hues.

Begonia inobongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
