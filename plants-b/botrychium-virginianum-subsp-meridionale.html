Botrychium virginianum subsp. meridionale

meridionale, commonly known as the southern moonwort, is a fern-like fern that belongs to the family Ophioglossaceae. It is characterized by its unique, two-part sporophyll structure, with the upper portion resembling a broad, shield-like leaf and the lower part bearing the spore-producing structures.
