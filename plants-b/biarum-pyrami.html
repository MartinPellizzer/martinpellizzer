Biarum pyrami

Biarum pyrami, commonly known as the pyramidal bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often appearing in clusters. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Biarum pyrami is known for its early spring bloom and its ability to naturalize over time.
