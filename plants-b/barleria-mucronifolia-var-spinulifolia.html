Barleria mucronifolia var. spinulifolia

Barleria mucronifolia var. spinulifolia, commonly known as the spiny barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with distinctive, spiny leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for gardens.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
