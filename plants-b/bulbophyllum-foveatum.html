Bulbophyllum foveatum

Bulbophyllum foveatum, commonly known as the crested butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy pseudobulbs and elongated, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers with intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum foveatum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
