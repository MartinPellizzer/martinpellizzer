Bulbophyllum escritorii

Bulbophyllum escritorii, commonly known as the writer's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
