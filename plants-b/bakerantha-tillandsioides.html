Bakerantha tillandsioides

Bakerantha tillandsioides, commonly known as the silver star bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater and nutrients. The plant's foliage is covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom in a star-like pattern, adding to its ornamental value.

Bakerantha tillandsioides thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
