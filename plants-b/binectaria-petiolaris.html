Binectaria petiolaris

Binectaria petiolaris, commonly known as the leafy petiole plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender petioles that support broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its foliage is often used in horticultural arrangements due to its delicate appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.
