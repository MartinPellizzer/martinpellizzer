Bulbophyllum bequaertii

Bulbophyllum bequaertii, commonly known as the Bequaert's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are often brightly colored, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Its rhizome system allows it to store nutrients and water, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
