Botryanthus pallens

Botryanthus pallens, commonly known as the pale botryanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in coastal and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, giving it a delicate and ornamental appearance. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Botryanthus pallens is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
