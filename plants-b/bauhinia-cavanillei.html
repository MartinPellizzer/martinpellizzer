Bauhinia cavanillei

Bauhinia cavanillei, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas or along riverbanks.

Bauhinia cavanillei is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
