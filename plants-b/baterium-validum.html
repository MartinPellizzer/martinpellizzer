Baterium validum

Baterium validum, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, reflective hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

Baterium validum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
