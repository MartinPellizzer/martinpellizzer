Bulbophyllum othonis

Bulbophyllum othonis, commonly known as the Othon's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its natural habitat.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.
