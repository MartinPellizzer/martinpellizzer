Bulbophyllum sicyobulbon

Bulbophyllum sicyobulbon, commonly known as the Sicyo Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less favorable conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow and pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
