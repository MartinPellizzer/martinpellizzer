Blakea Droseropila

Blakea droseropila is a rare and unique plant native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. It is known for its striking appearance, featuring vivid red flowers and velvety green leaves. This plant belongs to the Malvaceae family and is often referred to as the "blood flower" due to its intense color. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and requires specific care to thrive. Its rarity and aesthetic appeal make it a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts.
