Berberis aethnensis

Berberis aethnensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It features upright, thorny stems and elliptical leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates.

Berberis aethnensis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
