Bartsia brachyantha

Bartsia brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bartsia, is a small perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and forest understories. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive hairy texture. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers are clustered in pairs along the stems.

Bartsia brachyantha is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other woodland flora.
