Begonia bangsamoro subsp. bangsamoro

Begonia bangsamoro subsp.

bangsamoro, commonly known as the Bangsamoro begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines. It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, velvety leaves and vibrant, showy flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a reddish or purple underside, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
