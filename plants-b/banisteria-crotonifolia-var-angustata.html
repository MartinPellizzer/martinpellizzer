Banisteria crotonifolia var. angustata

Banisteria crotonifolia var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed banisteria, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in horticulture.
