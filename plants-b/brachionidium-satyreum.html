Brachionidium satyreum

Brachionidium satyreum, commonly known as the satyr orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that often display a blend of purple and yellow hues. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in rich, organic soil.

The orchid's structure includes a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water and nutrients.
