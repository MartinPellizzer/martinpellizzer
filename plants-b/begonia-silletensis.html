Begonia silletensis

Begonia silletensis, commonly known as the Sillet's begonia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and attractive foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Begonia silletensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
