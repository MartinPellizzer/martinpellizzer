Bryodesma Niveum

Snowy bryodesma, scientifically known as Bryodesma niveum, is a rare and delicate moss species found in cold, moist environments. This plant is characterized by its pale, almost white appearance, which gives it its common name. It thrives in high-altitude regions and is often seen in mountainous areas with cool climates. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique characteristics make it an important subject for ecological and botanical study.
