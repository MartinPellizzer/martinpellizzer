Besleria azulensis

Besleria azulensis, commonly known as the Blue Star Begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue and silver variegated leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded garden landscapes.
