Berberis yui

Berberis yui, commonly known as the Japanese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features prickly, spiny stems and small, glossy green leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
