Bulbine vagans

Bulbine vagans, commonly known as the wandering bulb, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features fleshy, strap-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and can store water in its thick stems and leaves.

Bulbine vagans is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
