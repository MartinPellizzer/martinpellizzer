Biophytum Calophyllum

Biophytum calophyllum is a unique and intriguing plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It is known for its striking appearance, featuring vibrant green leaves and distinctive red flowers. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its ability to adapt to low-light conditions makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to many plant enthusiasts worldwide.
