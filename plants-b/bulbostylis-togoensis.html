Bulbostylis togoensis

It is a perennial grass-like plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. Native to West Africa, particularly Togo, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
