Bulbostylis pyriformis

Bulbostylis pyriformis, commonly known as the oval-styled bulbostylis, is a species of grass belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, characterized by its small, grass-like appearance. The plant produces tiny, spike-like inflorescences that are often clustered at the base of the stems. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a bulbous base, which contribute to its unique morphology.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
