Berberis Nivea

Berberis nivea is a striking evergreen shrub known for its silvery-white foliage and vibrant yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. The plant's leaves and stems contain berberine, a compound with various medicinal properties. Despite its beauty, Berberis nivea requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in different climates.
