Baijiania

Baijiania, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and yellow, often hidden beneath the showy bracts.

Baijiania is valued in traditional medicine and is also cultivated for its ornamental appeal.
