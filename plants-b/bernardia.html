Bernardia

Bernardia, commonly known as the "false holly," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Berberidaceae.

It comprises several species, most of which are evergreen shrubs native to the southeastern United States and parts of Mexico. Bernardia species are valued for their ornamental qualities and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
