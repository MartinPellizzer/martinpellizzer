Barbarea Vulgaris Proles Rivularis

Barbarea vulgaris proles rivularis is a rare and delicate plant native to moist, shaded environments. It thrives in rocky crevices and along stream banks where it can access the moisture it requires. This species is known for its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lobed and slightly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered in many regions.
