Bulbophyllum roseopunctatum

Bulbophyllum roseopunctatum, commonly known as the rose-punctured orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety green leaves with red speckles. The plant produces fragrant, pinkish-white flowers with intricate patterns, often blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
