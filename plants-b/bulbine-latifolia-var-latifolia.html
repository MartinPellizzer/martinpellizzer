Bulbine latifolia var. latifolia

Bulbine latifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
