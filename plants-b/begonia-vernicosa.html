Begonia vernicosa

Begonia vernicosa, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to reddish-brown. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded outdoor spaces.
