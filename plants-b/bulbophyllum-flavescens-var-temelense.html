Bulbophyllum flavescens var. temelense

Bulbophyllum flavescens var. temelense, commonly known as the Temelenskii Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns and markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish in cultivation.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
