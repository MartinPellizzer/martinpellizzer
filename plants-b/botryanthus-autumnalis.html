Botryanthus autumnalis

Botryanthus autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered fruits that contain seeds.

Botryanthus autumnalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
