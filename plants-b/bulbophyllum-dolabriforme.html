Bulbophyllum dolabriforme

Bulbophyllum dolabriforme, commonly known as the sickle-shaped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulb-like structure with long, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically blooming during the rainy season. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The species is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments, often found on trees or rocks in the wild.
