Bidens luxurians

Bidens luxurians, commonly known as the showy beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in moist, sunny environments.

Bidens luxurians is also known for its medicinal properties in some traditional herbal practices.
