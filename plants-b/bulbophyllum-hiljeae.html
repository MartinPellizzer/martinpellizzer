Bulbophyllum hiljeae

Bulbophyllum hiljeae, commonly known as the Hilje's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence and intricate floral structures, which are typical of the Orchidaceae family. The plant produces small, often colorful flowers that are arranged in dense clusters along the stem. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum hiljeae thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.
