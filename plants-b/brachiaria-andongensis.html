Brachiaria andongensis

Brachiaria andongensis, commonly known as Andong grass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland and marshy environments, thriving in humid, tropical climates. Brachiaria andongensis is often used for forage in livestock farming due to its high nutritional value.

Its rhizomatous root system helps in soil stabilization and erosion control.
