Bulbophyllum galliaheneum

Bulbophyllum galliaheneum, commonly known as the Galia Hill orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its pseudobulbs are thick and store water, aiding in its survival in tropical conditions.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
