Begonia micheliniana

Begonia micheliniana, commonly known as the Michelinia begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
