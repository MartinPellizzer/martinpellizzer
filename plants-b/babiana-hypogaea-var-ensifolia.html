Babiana hypogaea var. ensifolia

Babiana hypogaea var. ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf babiana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically in late summer. This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
