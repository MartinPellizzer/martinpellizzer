Bulbophyllum truncatum

Bulbophyllum truncatum, commonly known as the truncated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum truncatum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
