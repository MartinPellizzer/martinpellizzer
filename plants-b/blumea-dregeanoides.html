Blumea dregeanoides

Blumea dregeanoides, commonly known as the yellow blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Blumea dregeanoides is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
