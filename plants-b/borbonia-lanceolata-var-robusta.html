Borbonia lanceolata var. robusta

Borbonia lanceolata var. robusta, commonly known as the robust borbonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, while its roots are fibrous and well-developed.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
