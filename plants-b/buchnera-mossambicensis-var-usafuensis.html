Buchnera mossambicensis var. usafuensis

Buchnera mossambicensis var. usafuensis, commonly known as the Usafuensis Buchnera, is a rare moss species native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green, closely packed foliage. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with short, acicular leaves that are often recurved at the tips. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in rocky outcrops or forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a sensitive indicator of undisturbed ecosystems.
