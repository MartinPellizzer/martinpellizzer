Billbergia lietzei var. chlorantha

Billbergia lietzei var. chlorantha, commonly known as the chloranthus billbergia, is a rare bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage and vibrant orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows as a compact rosette, with long, narrow leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
