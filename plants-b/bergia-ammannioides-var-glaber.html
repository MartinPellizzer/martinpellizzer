Bergia ammannioides var. glaber

Bergia ammannioides var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth bergia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Bergia ammannioides var. glaber is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in gardens.
