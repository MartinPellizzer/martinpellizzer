Balinotella calycina

Balinotella calycina, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Balinotella calycina is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
