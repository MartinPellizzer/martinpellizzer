Bidens forbesii subsp. kahiliensis

Bidens forbesii subsp.

kahiliensis, commonly known as the Kahili Bidens, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.
