Bletia caulescens

Bletia caulescens, commonly known as the slender bletia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, bloom in spring and are often fragrant.

Bletia caulescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
