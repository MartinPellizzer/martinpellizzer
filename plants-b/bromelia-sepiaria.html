Bromelia sepiaria

Bromelia sepiaria, commonly known as the green bromeliad, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family, which includes many epiphytic and terrestrial species. The plant features a rosette of broad, waxy, evergreen leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. These leaves are typically a deep green color, with some varieties displaying subtle variegation.

Bromelia sepiaria produces striking red or orange inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding vibrant color to its foliage.
