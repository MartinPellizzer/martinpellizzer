Begonia breedlovei

Begonia breedlovei, commonly known as the Malayan begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. Begonia breedlovei prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique foliage and delicate blossoms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
