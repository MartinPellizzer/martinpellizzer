Brassaiopsis aculeata

Brassaiopsis aculeata, commonly known as the spiny lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Brassaiopsis aculeata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage.
