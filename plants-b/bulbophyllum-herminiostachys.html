Bulbophyllum herminiostachys

Bulbophyllum herminiostachys, commonly known as the "Horned Orchid," is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
