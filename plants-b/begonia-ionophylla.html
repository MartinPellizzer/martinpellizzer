Begonia ionophylla

Begonia ionophylla, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green leaves that are often variegated with shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically appearing in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Begonia ionophylla is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
