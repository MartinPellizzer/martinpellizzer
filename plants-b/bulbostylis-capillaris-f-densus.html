Bulbostylis capillaris f. densus

Bulbostylis capillaris f. densus, commonly known as the dense-styled bulblet sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

Its inflorescence consists of a cluster of tiny, spike-like flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color.
