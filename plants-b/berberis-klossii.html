Berberis klossii

Berberis klossii, commonly known as the Kloss barberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It features spiny stems and small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often considered bitter. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Berberis klossii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its unique appearance.
