Begonia nivea

Begonia nivea, commonly known as the snow begonia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white, velvety flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a smooth texture. It prefers cool, shaded environments and well-drained soil.

Begonia nivea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
