Blechnum australis f. glanduliferum

Blechnum australis f. glanduliferum, commonly known as the glandular shield fern, is a fern species native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant features a creeping rhizome and produces fertile fronds with sporangia on the underside. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
