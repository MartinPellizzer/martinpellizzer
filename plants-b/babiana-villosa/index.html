Babiana Villosa

Babiana villosa is a striking member of the iris family native to southern Africa. This plant is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Babiana villosa not only adds beauty to landscapes but also plays an important role in local ecosystems.
