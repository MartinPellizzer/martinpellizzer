Bridelia niedenzui var. revoluta

Bridelia niedenzui var. revoluta, commonly known as the wrinkled bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
