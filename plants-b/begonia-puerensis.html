Begonia puerensis

Begonia puerensis, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or trailing vine, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage and the subtle beauty of its blooms.
