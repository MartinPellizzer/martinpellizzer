Buddleja acuminatissima

Buddleja acuminatissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces panicles of fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Buddleja acuminatissima is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
