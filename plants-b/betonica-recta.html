Betonica recta

Betonica recta, commonly known as upright betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces spikes of blue to violet flowers, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Betonica recta is often found in moist meadows, woodland edges, and rocky soils.

It is valued in herbal medicine for its purported calming and healing properties.
