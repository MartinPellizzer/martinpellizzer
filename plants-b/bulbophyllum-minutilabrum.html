Bulbophyllum minutilabrum

Bulbophyllum minutilabrum, commonly known as the tiny labrum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its tropical habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it prefers shaded, humid environments.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and intricate floral structure.
