Boehmeria burgeriana

Boehmeria burgeriana, commonly known as the Chinese nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its smooth, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its fibrous stems are used in traditional crafts and textiles due to their strong, flexible fibers.

Boehmeria burgeriana is also valued for its medicinal properties in some traditional herbal practices.
