Begonia morii

Begonia morii, commonly known as the Morii begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to pink in color. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, compact growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in horticultural collections worldwide.
