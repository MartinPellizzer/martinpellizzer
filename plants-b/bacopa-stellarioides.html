Bacopa stellarioides

Bacopa stellarioides, commonly known as starwort, is a flowering plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a low-growing herb with small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, and it blooms throughout the growing season.

Bacopa stellarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
