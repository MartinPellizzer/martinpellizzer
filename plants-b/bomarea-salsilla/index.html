Bomarea Salsilla

Bomarea salsilla is a striking member of the Alstroemeriaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to the cloud forests of Ecuador, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique inflorescence features intricate, tubular petals that bloom in a striking array of colors. The plant's foliage is also notable for its glossy, dark green leaves. Due to its rarity and beauty, Bomarea salsilla is a subject of interest for botanists and horticultural enthusiasts alike.
