Balgoya pacifica

Balgoya pacifica, commonly known as the Pacific balgoya, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blossoms that range in color from pale yellow to soft pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near streams or in dense undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture, while its stems are slender and often trailing.

Balgoya pacifica is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
