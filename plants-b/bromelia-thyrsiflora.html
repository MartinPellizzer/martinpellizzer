Bromelia thyrsiflora

Bromelia thyrsiflora, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of long, pointed leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear showy, brightly colored flowers. Bromelia thyrsiflora is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, which includes many epiphytic and terrestrial species.

Its unique structure and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
