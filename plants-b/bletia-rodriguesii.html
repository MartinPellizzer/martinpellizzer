Bletia rodriguesii

Bletia rodriguesii, commonly known as Rodrigues's bletia, is a rare orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, Bletia rodriguesii is considered endangered, highlighting the need for its protection.
