Besleria discreta

Besleria discreta, commonly known as the Discreta Spider Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, spider-like flowers with long, slender petals. The plant typically produces clusters of pale green to white blossoms that are highly fragrant. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
