Bicornella buchananii

Bicornella buchananii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers come in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

Bicornella buchananii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
