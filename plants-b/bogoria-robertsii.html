Bogoria robertsii

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring tall, slender stems and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom during the short rainy season. This plant thrives in nutrient-poor, waterlogged soils, often in bogs or marshes where it forms dense colonies.
