Breweria tiliifolia

Breweria tiliifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf breweria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs that reflect sunlight, reducing water loss.

Breweria tiliifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
