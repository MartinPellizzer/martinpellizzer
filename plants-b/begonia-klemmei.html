Begonia klemmei

Begonia klemmei, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from deep green to rich purple. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
