Bridelia ferruginea var. gambicola

Bridelia ferruginea var. gambicola, commonly known as the Gambian bridelia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often gland-dotted.
