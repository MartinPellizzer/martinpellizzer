Brassavola surinamensis

Brassavola surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, clustered flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Brassavola surinamensis is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
