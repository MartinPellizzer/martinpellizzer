Bulbine platyphylla

Bulbine platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf bulbine, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rhombic-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its resilience to drought and heat makes it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
