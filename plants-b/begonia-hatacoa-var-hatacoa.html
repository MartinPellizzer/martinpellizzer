Begonia hatacoa var. hatacoa

Begonia hatacoa var. hatacoa, commonly known as the Hatacoa begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, velvety leaves that range from deep red to purple, often with contrasting green veins. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
