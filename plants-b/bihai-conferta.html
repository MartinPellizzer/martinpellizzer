Bihai conferta

Bihai conferta, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bihai conferta is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
