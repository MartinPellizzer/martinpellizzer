Beatonia caelestina

Beatonia caelestina, commonly known as the celestial beatonia, is a striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant produces small, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its unique coloration and architectural form make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Beatonia caelestina thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
