Bikukulla Formosa

The Bikukulla formosa is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, bioluminescent flowers that glow under moonlight, it has captivated botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, the species is critically endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Researchers are working tirelessly to preserve and propagate this unique flora. Its existence highlights the fragile balance between nature and human impact.
