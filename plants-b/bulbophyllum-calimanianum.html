Bulbophyllum calimanianum

Bulbophyllum calimanianum, commonly known as the Calimanian Pterostylis, is a rare orchid species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a distinctive spurred lip.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
