Brownlowia macrophylla

Brownlowia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Brownlowia macrophylla is adapted to humid climates and thrives in well-drained, fertile soils.
