Bomarea fimbriata var. paltarumensis

Bomarea fimbriata var. paltarumensis, commonly known as the Paltarumensis bomarea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Alstroemeriaceae.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, specifically Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, often fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and may have a hairy texture.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
