Berberis turcomanica

Berberis turcomanica, commonly known as the Turkestan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Berberis turcomanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
