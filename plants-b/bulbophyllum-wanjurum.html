Bulbophyllum wanjurum

Bulbophyllum wanjurum, commonly known as the Wanjurum orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
