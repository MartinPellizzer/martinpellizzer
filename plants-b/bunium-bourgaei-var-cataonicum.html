Bunium bourgaei var. cataonicum

Bunium bourgaei var. cataonicum, commonly known as the Cataonic fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, ovoid seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
