Bulbophyllum nasseri

Bulbophyllum nasseri, commonly known as the Nassar's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in survival during dry periods.

The flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.
