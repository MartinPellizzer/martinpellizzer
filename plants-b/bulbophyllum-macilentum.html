Bulbophyllum macilentum

Bulbophyllum macilentum, commonly known as the slender bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a rare and prized ornamental plant.
