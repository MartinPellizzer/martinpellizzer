Bambusa lineata

Bambusa lineata, commonly known as the stiff reed bamboo, is a tall, clump-forming bamboo native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright canes that are often lined with small, visible ridges. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are usually green on top and lighter in color underneath. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Bambusa lineata is a hardy plant that can grow in a variety of climates, making it a popular choice for both garden and commercial use.
