Belharnosia

Belharnosia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that have a distinctive silvery sheen, which helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns. Belharnosia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant in horticultural collections.
