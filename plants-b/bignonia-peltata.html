Bignonia peltata

Bignonia peltata, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous climbing vine that produces vibrant yellow flowers in the spring. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Bignonia peltata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to cover trellises or walls.
