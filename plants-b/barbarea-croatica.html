Barbarea croatica

Barbarea croatica, commonly known as the Croatian rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves that are pinnately divided. It produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by pods containing several seeds.

Barbarea croatica is often found in rocky or calcareous soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments.
