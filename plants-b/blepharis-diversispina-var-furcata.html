Blepharis diversispina var. furcata

Blepharis diversispina var. furcata, commonly known as the forked bluemouth, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The species is noted for its unique branching pattern, with the stems often splitting into two or more branches near the base.
