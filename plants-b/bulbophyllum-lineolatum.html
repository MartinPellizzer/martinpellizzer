Bulbophyllum lineolatum

Bulbophyllum lineolatum, commonly known as the striped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with flowers that have a distinctive striped pattern on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum lineolatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
