Bugranopsis

Bugranopsis, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant lacks traditional leaves and instead relies on its roots for photosynthesis, making it a unique epiphytic species. Bugranopsis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on the trunks of trees.
