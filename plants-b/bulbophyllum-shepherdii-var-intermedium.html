Bulbophyllum shepherdii var. intermedium

Bulbophyllum shepherdii var. intermedium, commonly known as the Intermediate Shepherd's Bulbophyllum, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces vibrant, often colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and its inflorescence consists of multiple clustered blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts in controlled conditions.
