Blumea laevis var. laevis

Blumea laevis var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth blumea, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow and arranged in clusters, producing small, oval-shaped fruits.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
