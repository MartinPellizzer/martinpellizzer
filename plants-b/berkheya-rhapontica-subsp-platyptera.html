Berkheya rhapontica subsp. platyptera

Berkheya rhapontica subsp.

platyptera, commonly known as the flat-leaf berkheya, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features erect, branched stems with soft, hairy foliage that is often tinged with reddish hues.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a distinctive flat, broad shape that gives it its subspecies name.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
