Banisteria pubipetala var. oblongata

Banisteria pubipetala var. oblongata, commonly known as the oblong leaf banisteria, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, pubescent leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
