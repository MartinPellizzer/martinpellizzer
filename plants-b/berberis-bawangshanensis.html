Berberis bawangshanensis

Berberis bawangshanensis, commonly known as the Bawangshan barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often sour. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
