Begonia pinamalayensis

Begonia pinamalayensis, commonly known as the Pinamalay begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia pinamalayensis is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color and diversity to their plant collections.
