Bletilla elegantula

Bletilla elegantula, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bletilla elegantula thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.
