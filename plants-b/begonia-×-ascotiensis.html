Begonia × ascotiensis

Begonia × ascotiensis, commonly known as the Ascot Begonia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Begoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped or ovate leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Begonia × ascotiensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
