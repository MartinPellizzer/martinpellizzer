Bidens Elliptica Subvar. Minima

Bidens elliptica subvar. minima is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of South America. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers and finely divided leaves. This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its compact size makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance ornamental plants. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
