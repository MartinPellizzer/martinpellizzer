Bidens prestinariiformis

Bidens prestinariiformis, commonly known as the small-flowered tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in open habitats such as wetlands and prairies. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with prominent white ray florets.

Bidens prestinariiformis is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
