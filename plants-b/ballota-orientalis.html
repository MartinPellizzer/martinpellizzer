Ballota orientalis

Ballota orientalis, commonly known as the oriental ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has palmately divided leaves and produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Ballota orientalis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
