Blumea lanceolaria

Blumea lanceolaria, commonly known as the slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and it produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
