Barleria irritans var. rigida

Barleria irritans var. rigida, commonly known as rigid barleria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features erect, branched stems that are often covered with fine hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and usually have a hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes, typically displaying shades of pink to red.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
