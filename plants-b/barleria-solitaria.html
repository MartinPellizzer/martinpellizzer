Barleria solitaria

Barleria solitaria, commonly known as the solitary barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a distinctive rough texture.

Barleria solitaria is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive flowers and hardy nature.
