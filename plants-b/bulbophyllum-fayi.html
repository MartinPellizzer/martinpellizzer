Bulbophyllum fayi

Bulbophyllum fayi, commonly known as the Fay's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers typically feature intricate patterns and vibrant colors, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

It thrives in humid conditions with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
