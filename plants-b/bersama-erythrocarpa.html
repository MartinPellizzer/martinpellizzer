Bersama erythrocarpa

Bersama erythrocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited Bersama, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a gelatinous coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
