Boronia ledifolia var. glabra

Boronia ledifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, smooth, evergreen leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky soils, preferring well-drained conditions. It is a small shrub, usually reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

The flowers bloom in spring and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.
