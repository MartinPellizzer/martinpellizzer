Bulbophyllum dalatense

Bulbophyllum dalatense, commonly known as the Dalat orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its arid environment.

Bulbophyllum dalatense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
