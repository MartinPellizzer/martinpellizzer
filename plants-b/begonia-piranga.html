Begonia piranga

Begonia piranga, commonly known as the scarlet begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety in texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia piranga is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
