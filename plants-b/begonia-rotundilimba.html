Begonia rotundilimba

Begonia rotundilimba, commonly known as the round-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It prefers moist, well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and delicate blossoms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
