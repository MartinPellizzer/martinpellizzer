Berberis hispanica var. hackeliana

Berberis hispanica var. hackeliana, commonly known as the Spanish barberry, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
