Berlinia niembaensis

Berlinia niembaensis, commonly known as the Niemba Berlinia, is a small to medium-sized tree native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain flattened, elongated seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas with well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
