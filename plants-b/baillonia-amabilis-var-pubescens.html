Baillonia amabilis var. pubescens

Baillonia amabilis var. pubescens, commonly known as the silky baillonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that produces showy, butterfly-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, hairy leaflets, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
