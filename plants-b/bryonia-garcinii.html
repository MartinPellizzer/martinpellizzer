Bryonia garcinii

Bryonia garcinii, commonly known as the African bryony, is a climbing plant belonging to the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by orange, fleshy fruits. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

However, it is important to note that the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
