Bauhinia gigas

Bauhinia gigas, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, and its bark is smooth with a pale greyish color. Bauhinia gigas is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and rapid growth.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
