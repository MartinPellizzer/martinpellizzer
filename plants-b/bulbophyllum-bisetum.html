Bulbophyllum bisetum

Bulbophyllum bisetum, commonly known as the two-flowered bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and is often epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum bisetum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and unique appearance.
