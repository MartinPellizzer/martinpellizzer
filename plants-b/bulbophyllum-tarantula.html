Bulbophyllum tarantula

Bulbophyllum tarantula, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and long, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spider-like flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
