Begonia longinoda

Begonia longinoda, commonly known as the long-stemmed begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, slender stems that can reach several feet in height. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a humid, well-draining environment.
