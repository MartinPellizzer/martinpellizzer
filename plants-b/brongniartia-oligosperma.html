Brongniartia oligosperma

Brongniartia oligosperma, commonly known as the Brazilian cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces cycad-like cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
