Bauhinia yunnanensis

Bauhinia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces showy, papilionaceous flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Bauhinia yunnanensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
