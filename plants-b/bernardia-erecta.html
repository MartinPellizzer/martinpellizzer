Bernardia erecta

Bernardia erecta, commonly known as the red-flowered bernardia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Berberidaceae and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Bernardia erecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
