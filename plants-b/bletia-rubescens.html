Bletia rubescens

Bletia rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the understory of tropical forests. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Bletia rubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
