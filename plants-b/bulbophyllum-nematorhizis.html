Bulbophyllum nematorhizis

Bulbophyllum nematorhizis, commonly known as the narrow-rooted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
