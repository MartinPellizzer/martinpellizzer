Bemarivea dissitiflora

Bemarivea dissitiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf starflower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and star-shaped, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a distinctive aromatic scent.

Bemarivea dissitiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
