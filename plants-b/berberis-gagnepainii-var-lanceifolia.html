Berberis gagnepainii var. lanceifolia

Berberis gagnepainii var. lanceifolia, commonly known as the lance-leaf barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used for ornamental purposes. It is valued for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The plant's thorny stems and compact growth make it an effective hedge or ornamental specimen in gardens.
