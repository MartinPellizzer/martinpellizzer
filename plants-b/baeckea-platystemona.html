Baeckea platystemona

Baeckea platystemona, commonly known as the silky baeckea, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Baeckea platystemona is well adapted to sandy soils and full sun, making it a resilient plant in coastal environments.
