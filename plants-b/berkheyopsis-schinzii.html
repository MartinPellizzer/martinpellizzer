Berkheyopsis schinzii

Berkheyopsis schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
