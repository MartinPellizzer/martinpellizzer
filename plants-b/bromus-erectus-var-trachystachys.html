Bromus erectus var. trachystachys

Bromus erectus var. trachystachys, commonly known as the slender brome, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
