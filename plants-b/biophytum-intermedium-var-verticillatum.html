Biophytum intermedium var. verticillatum

Biophytum intermedium var. verticillatum, commonly known as the spiral spinach or the wheel plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening and rock gardens.
