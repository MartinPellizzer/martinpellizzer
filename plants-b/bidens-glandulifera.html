Bidens glandulifera

Bidens glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular tickseed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features serrated leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

Bidens glandulifera is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
