Blossfeldia liliputana var. fechseri

Blossfeldia liliputana var. fechseri, commonly known as the miniature Christmas cactus, is a compact, epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, branching stems and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces flowers in a raceme, with each bloom opening sequentially, creating a cascading effect. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
