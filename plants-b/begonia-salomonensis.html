Begonia salomonensis

Begonia salomonensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Begonia salomonensis is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
