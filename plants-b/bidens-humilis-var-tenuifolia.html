Bidens humilis var. tenuifolia

Bidens humilis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species that typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and wet meadows. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small yellow flowers with prominent white veins. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in wet environments and its role in supporting local pollinators.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
