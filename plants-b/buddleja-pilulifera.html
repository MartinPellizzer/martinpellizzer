Buddleja pilulifera

Buddleja pilulifera, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer and attract numerous pollinators. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and can grow up to 6 meters in height. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Buddleja pilulifera is also valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various climates.
