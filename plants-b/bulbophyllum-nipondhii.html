Bulbophyllum nipondhii

Bulbophyllum nipondhii, commonly known as the Nippon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Bulbophyllum nipondhii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
