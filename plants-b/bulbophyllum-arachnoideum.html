Bulbophyllum arachnoideum

Bulbophyllum arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender pseudobulbs and delicate, translucent leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
