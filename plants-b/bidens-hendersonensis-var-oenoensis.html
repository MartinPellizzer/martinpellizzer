Bidens hendersonensis var. oenoensis

Bidens hendersonensis var. oenoensis, commonly known as the Henderson's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces both ray and disk flowers, typical of the sunflower family. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and roadside areas.
