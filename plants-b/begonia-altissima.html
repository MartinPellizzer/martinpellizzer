Begonia altissima

Begonia altissima, commonly known as the everlasting begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Begonia altissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
