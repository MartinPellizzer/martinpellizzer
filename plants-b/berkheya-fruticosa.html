Berkheya fruticosa

Berkheya fruticosa, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub-like perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in spring and early summer. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

Berkheya fruticosa is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water once established.
