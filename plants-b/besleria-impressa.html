Besleria impressa

Besleria impressa, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Central and South America, characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture and a distinctive central vein. It produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters.

This plant is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
