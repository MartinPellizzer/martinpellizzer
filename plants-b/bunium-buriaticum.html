Bunium buriaticum

Bunium buriaticum, commonly known as the Siberian fennel, is a perennial herb native to the cold climates of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as parsley and carrots. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in umbels. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine for their purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
