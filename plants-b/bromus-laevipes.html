Bromus laevipes

Bromus laevipes, commonly known as the smooth brome, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, dark green blades and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces spikelets that are often tinged with brown, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Bromus laevipes is often used in forage and erosion control due to its hardy nature and adaptability.
