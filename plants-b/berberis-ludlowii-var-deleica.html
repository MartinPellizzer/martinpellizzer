Berberis ludlowii var. deleica

Berberis ludlowii var. deleica, commonly known as the Deleica barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

The species is valued for its hardiness and attractive foliage throughout the growing season.
