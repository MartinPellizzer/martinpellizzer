Bufonia kotschyana subsp. calycina

Bufonia kotschyana subsp.

calycina, commonly known as the Kashmir bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region. It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine pubescence.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
