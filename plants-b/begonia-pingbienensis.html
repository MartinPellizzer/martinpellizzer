Begonia pingbienensis

Begonia pingbienensis, commonly known as the Pingbien begonia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, white, and pink.

Begonia pingbienensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
