Bidens artemisiifolia

Bidens artemisiifolia, commonly known as common ironweed, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect stem and clusters of yellow flowers with ray florets. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in fields and along roadsides.

The plant is known for its ability to spread rapidly through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
