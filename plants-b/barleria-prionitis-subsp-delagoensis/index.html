Barleria Prionitis Subsp. Delagoensis

Delagoa barleria, scientifically known as Barleria prionitis subsp. delagoensis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers and lance-shaped leaves. This species is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
