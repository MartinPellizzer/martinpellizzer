Banksia Obtusa

Banksia obtusa is a striking evergreen shrub native to the south-western region of Western Australia. It is known for its distinctive cylindrical flower spikes that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Banksia obtusa plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and preserving the region's biodiversity.
