Bartsia purpurea

Bartsia purpurea, commonly known as purple bartsia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly heart-shaped base. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually purple or pink in color.

Bartsia purpurea is known for its short lifespan, often completing its life cycle within a single growing season.
