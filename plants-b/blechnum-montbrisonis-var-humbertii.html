Blechnum montbrisonis var. humbertii

Blechnum montbrisonis var. humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's shield fern, is a variant of the Blechnum genus, which belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically leathery and robust. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its fronds are usually bipinnate, with a dark green color and a slightly serrated margin.

This fern is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
