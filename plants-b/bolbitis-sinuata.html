Bolbitis sinuata

Bolbitis sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are often submerged in water. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Its rhizomes form dense mats that help stabilize the substrate.

Bolbitis sinuata is valued in aquatic gardening for its ornamental appearance and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
