Bidens lanceolata

Bidens lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf tickseed, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers with prominent black markings. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and disturbed soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds dispersed by wind.

Bidens lanceolata is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
