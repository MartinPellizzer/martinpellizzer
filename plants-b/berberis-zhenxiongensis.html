Berberis zhenxiongensis

Berberis zhenxiongensis, commonly known as the Zhenxiong barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional medicine practices.
