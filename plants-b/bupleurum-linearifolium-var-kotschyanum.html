Bupleurum linearifolium var. kotschyanum

Bupleurum linearifolium var. kotschyanum, commonly known as the Kotschyan's slender bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, covered with fine, grayish-white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are linear and slightly serrated, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
