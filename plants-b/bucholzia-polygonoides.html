Bucholzia polygonoides

Bucholzia polygonoides, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by distinctive, ribbed, orange-colored fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Bucholzia polygonoides is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
