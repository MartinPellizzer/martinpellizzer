Baccharis niederleinii

Baccharis niederleinii, commonly known as the Brazilian baccharis, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Baccharis niederleinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
