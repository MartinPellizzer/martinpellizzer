Begonia physandra

Begonia physandra, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
