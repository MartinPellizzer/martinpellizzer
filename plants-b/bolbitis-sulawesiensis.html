Bolbitis sulawesiensis

Bolbitis sulawesiensis, commonly known as the Sulawesi shield fern, is a unique member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a leathery texture. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread slowly across the forest floor.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
