Bulbophyllum erythroglossum

Bulbophyllum erythroglossum, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red lips on the petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are small and numerous, often arranged in dense clusters.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
