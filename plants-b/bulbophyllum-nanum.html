Bulbophyllum nanum

Bulbophyllum nanum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a short, wiry stem with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in horticulture.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful cultivation to thrive.
