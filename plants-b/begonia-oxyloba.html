Begonia oxyloba

Begonia oxyloba, commonly known as the wax begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that can range in color from pink to red. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Begonia oxyloba is also valued for its ability to tolerate a variety of environmental conditions.
