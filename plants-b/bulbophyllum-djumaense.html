Bulbophyllum djumaense

Bulbophyllum djumaense, commonly known as the Djuma orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum djumaense make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
