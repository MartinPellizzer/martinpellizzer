Buddleja fallowiana

Buddleja fallowiana, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped blooms that attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Buddleja fallowiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
