Bulbophyllum cyclopense

Bulbophyllum cyclopense, commonly known as the cyclops orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, bulbous pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
