Biarum olivieri

Biarum olivieri, commonly known as the olive lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, white to pale pink, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Biarum olivieri is also valued for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
