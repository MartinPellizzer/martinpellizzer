Begonia clarkei

Begonia clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in hue, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique appearance and ease of care.
