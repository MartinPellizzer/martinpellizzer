Bassia mindanaensis

Bassia mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao bassia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is typically found in open areas such as fields and along roadsides.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
