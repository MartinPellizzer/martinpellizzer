Begonia dregei

Begonia dregei, commonly known as the Cape Begonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a trailing growth habit. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Begonia dregei is often cultivated as a decorative plant for gardens and indoor spaces due to its ornamental value.
