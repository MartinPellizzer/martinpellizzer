Burtonia subalpina

Burtonia subalpina, commonly known as the alpine burtonia, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a leathery texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white. Burtonia subalpina thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to withstand harsh alpine conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in botanical gardens and specialized horticulture.
