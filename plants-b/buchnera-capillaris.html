Buchnera capillaris

Buchnera capillaris, commonly known as the slender leaf bugwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and herbaceous stems.

Buchnera capillaris is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.
