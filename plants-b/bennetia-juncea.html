Bennetia juncea

Bennetia juncea, commonly known as the Brazilian bennetia, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that dehisces to release seeds.

Bennetia juncea is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical regions.
