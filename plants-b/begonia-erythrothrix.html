Begonia erythrothrix

Begonia erythrothrix, commonly known as the red-stemmed begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, velvety red stems that contrast with its dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, often attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia erythrothrix is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
