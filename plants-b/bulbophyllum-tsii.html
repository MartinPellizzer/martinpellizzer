Bulbophyllum tsii

Bulbophyllum tsii, commonly known as the Tsai's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments.

The flowers are usually small and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and green.
