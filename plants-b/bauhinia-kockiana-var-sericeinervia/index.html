Bauhinia Kockiana Var. Sericeinervia

Bauhinia kockiana var. sericeinervia is a strikingly beautiful leguminous plant known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of pale pink to white flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments within forest understories. Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural collections and landscape design. The plant's name reflects its intricate leaf structure and silky-haired veins, adding to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream botanic literature and cultivation.
