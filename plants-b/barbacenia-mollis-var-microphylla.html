Barbacenia mollis var. microphylla

Barbacenia mollis var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved barbacenia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange. The leaves are typically small, ovate, and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings or as a container plant.
