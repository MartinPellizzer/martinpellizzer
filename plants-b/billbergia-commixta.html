Billbergia commixta

Billbergia commixta, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that surround the central inflorescence, often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the growth of its offspring. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy texture.

Billbergia commixta is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
