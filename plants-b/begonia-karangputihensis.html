Begonia karangputihensis

Begonia karangputihensis, commonly known as the Karangputih begonia, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and vibrant flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, showy blooms in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
