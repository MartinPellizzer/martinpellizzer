Breweria glomerata

Breweria glomerata, commonly known as the clustered breweria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. The plant is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.
