Berkheya latifolia

Berkheya latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are broad at the base and taper towards the tips. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Berkheya latifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
