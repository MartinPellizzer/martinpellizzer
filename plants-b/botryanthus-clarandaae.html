Botryanthus clarandaae

Botryanthus clarandaae, commonly known as the African snowball plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the rainy season, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
