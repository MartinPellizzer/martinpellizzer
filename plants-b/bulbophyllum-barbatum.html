Bulbophyllum barbatum

Bulbophyllum barbatum, commonly known as the beard-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum barbatum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
