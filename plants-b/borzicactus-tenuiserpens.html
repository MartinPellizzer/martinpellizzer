Borzicactus tenuiserpens

Borzicactus tenuiserpens, commonly known as the slender snake cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are often covered in a fine layer of white, powdery bloom. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
