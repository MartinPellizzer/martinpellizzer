Banara splendens

Banara splendens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a vivid display during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets, which are glossy and provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Banara splendens is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
