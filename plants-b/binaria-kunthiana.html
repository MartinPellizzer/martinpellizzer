Binaria kunthiana

Binaria kunthiana, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a taproot system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
