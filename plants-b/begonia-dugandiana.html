Begonia dugandiana

Begonia dugandiana, commonly known as the Dugand's begonia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia dugandiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in suitable conditions.
