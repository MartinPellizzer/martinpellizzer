Bulbophyllum wollastonii

Bulbophyllum wollastonii, commonly known as the Wollaston's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, greenish-white to pale yellow blooms with distinctive lip structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum wollastonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
