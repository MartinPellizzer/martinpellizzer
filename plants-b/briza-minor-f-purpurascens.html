Briza minor f. purpurascens

Briza minor f. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple feather grass, is a decorative grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that produce delicate, feathery plumes in shades of purple and silver. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, making it suitable for borders or rock gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, bluish-green hue.

This ornamental grass thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, adding a subtle, elegant touch to landscapes.
