Baccaurea nanihua var. oblongata

Baccaurea nanihua var. oblongata, commonly known as the oblong-fruit baccaurea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces oval-shaped fruits that turn from green to yellow when mature. The fruit's flesh is juicy and edible, with a mild, sweet flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit in local cuisines.
