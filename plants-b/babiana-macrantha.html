Babiana macrantha

Babiana macrantha, commonly known as the giant babiana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and striking, showy flowers. Babiana macrantha is also known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
