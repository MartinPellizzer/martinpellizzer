Bertolonia sanguinea var. sanguinea

Bertolonia sanguinea var. sanguinea, commonly known as the red-flowered bertolonia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, blooming during the warmer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This species is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various garden conditions.
