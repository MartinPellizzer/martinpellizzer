Bulbophyllum delicatulum

Bulbophyllum delicatulum, commonly known as the delicate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the wet season. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air, and it has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
