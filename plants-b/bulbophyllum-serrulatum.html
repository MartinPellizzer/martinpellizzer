Bulbophyllum serrulatum

Bulbophyllum serrulatum, commonly known as the serrated bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a layer of brownish, fibrous material.

Bulbophyllum serrulatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
