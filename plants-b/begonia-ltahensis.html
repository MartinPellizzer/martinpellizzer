Begonia ltahensis

Begonia ltahensis, commonly known as the Hainan begonia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hainan in China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
