Barleria homoiotricha

Barleria homoiotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria homoiotricha is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
