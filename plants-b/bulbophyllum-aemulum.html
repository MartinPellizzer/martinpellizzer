Bulbophyllum aemulum

Bulbophyllum aemulum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum aemulum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
