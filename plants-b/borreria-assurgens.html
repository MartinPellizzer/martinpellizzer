Borreria assurgens

Borreria assurgens, commonly known as the Mexican milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and range in color from white to pink.

Borreria assurgens is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
