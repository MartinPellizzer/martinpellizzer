Betonica occitana

Betonica occitana, commonly known as the French betony, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features upright, hairy stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces spikes of violet-blue flowers from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Betonica occitana is often found in moist meadows and woodland edges, and it has a long history of use in traditional herbal medicine.
