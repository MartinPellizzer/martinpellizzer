Bulbophyllum kaniense

Bulbophyllum kaniense, commonly known as the Kaniense orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that serves as a storage organ for water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
