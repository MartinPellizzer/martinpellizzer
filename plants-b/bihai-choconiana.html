Bihai choconiana

Bihai choconiana, commonly known as the giant panda plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. Its unique appearance, with white and green variegated foliage, makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden beneath its broad leaves.
