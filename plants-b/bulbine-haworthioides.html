Bulbine haworthioides

Bulbine haworthioides, commonly known as the Haworth's bulbine, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
