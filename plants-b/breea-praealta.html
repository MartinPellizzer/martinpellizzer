Breea praealta

Breea praealta, commonly known as the giant lily, is a striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant has a robust stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to the cold, mountainous environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is often protected in its natural habitat.
