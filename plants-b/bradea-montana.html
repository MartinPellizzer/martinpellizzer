Bradea montana

Bradea montana, commonly known as the mountain bradea, is a rare and endemic plant species found in specific alpine regions.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture in harsh environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Bradea montana is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
