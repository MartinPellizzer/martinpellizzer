Blechnum pteropus

Blechnum pteropus, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and have a leathery texture. The plant is native to various regions in the Pacific, including New Zealand, Hawaii, and parts of Southeast Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Blechnum pteropus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
