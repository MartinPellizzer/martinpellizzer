Begonia × kapangan

Begonia × kapangan, commonly known as the Kapangan begonia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pavonina.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic value, it is a popular choice for gardeners and floral enthusiasts.
