Bertya oblonga

Bertya oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf bertya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and usually have a pale yellow or greenish color.

Bertya oblonga is known for its adaptability to dry and rocky soils, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
