Barleria indica

Barleria indica, commonly known as the Indian flame flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, giving it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria indica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
