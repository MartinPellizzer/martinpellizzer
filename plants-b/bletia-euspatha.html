Bletia euspatha

Bletia euspatha, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and elongated inflorescence that bears pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and pine flatwoods. Bletia euspatha has a rhizomatous root system that helps it survive in its natural habitat.

Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate floral display to the forest floor.
