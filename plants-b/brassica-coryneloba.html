Brassica coryneloba

Brassica coryneloba, commonly known as the Chinese kale or Chinese broccoli, is a leafy green vegetable belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, stalked leaves and clusters of small, edible flowers. The plant is native to East Asia and is often cultivated for its nutritional value and mild, slightly sweet flavor. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Brassica coryneloba is a hardy plant that is relatively easy to grow in home gardens.
