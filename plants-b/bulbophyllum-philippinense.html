Bulbophyllum philippinense

Bulbophyllum philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to store water.

Bulbophyllum philippinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
