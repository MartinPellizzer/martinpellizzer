Boerhavia tuberosa

Boerhavia tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers that bloom in the early morning, hence its common name. Boerhavia tuberosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Its tuberous roots make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
