Blechnum occidentale var. puberulum

Blechnum occidentale var. puberulum, commonly known as the soft fern, is a species of fern native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds. The fronds are typically covered in fine, soft hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
