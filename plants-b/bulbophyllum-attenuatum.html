Bulbophyllum attenuatum

Bulbophyllum attenuatum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
