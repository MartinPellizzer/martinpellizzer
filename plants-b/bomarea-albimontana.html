Bomarea albimontana

Bomarea albimontana, commonly known as the white mountain bomarea, is a climbing plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces tubular, often fragrant blooms that are typically borne in clusters. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.
