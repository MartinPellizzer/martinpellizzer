Borreria compressa

Borreria compressa, commonly known as the narrowleaf borreria, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows as a low, sprawling shrub with narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs.
