Bunium incrassatum

Bunium incrassatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved fennel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like carrots and parsley. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
