Basananthe zanzibarica

Basananthe zanzibarica, commonly known as the Zanzibar banana.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa, particularly Zanzibar. The plant features broad, fleshy leaves that are often used in local cuisines. Its flowering stems produce clusters of small, yellow flowers that eventually develop into edible fruits.

The plant is valued both for its culinary uses and its ornamental appeal in tropical gardens.
