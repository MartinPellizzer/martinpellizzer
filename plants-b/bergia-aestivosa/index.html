Bergia Aestivosa

Bergia aestivosa is a hardy plant native to the rocky coastal regions of southern Africa. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and full sun exposure. Known for its small, bell-shaped flowers, it adds a delicate touch to its surroundings. This resilient species is often found growing on cliffs and sandy shores. Its unique appearance and ability to withstand extreme conditions make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature lovers alike.
