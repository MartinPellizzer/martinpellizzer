Bidens peucedanifolia var. soratensis

Bidens peucedanifolia var. soratensis, commonly known as the Soratensian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are typically yellow with dark centers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
