Begonia arachnoidea

Begonia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, white flowers clustered on long, thin pedicels. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
