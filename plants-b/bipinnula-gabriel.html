Bipinnula gabriel

Bipinnula gabriel, commonly known as the Gabriel's feather fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant has a compact growth habit, typically reaching a height of about 30 centimeters. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with secondary pinnae that are further divided into tiny, needle-like segments.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
