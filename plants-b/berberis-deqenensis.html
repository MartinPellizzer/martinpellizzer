Berberis deqenensis

Berberis deqenensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
