Berkheya squarrosa

Berkheya squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its greyish, woolly leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Berkheya squarrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
