Barleria hochstetteri

Barleria hochstetteri, commonly known as the Cape barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Barleria hochstetteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
