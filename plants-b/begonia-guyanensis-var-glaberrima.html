Begonia guyanensis var. glaberrima

Begonia guyanensis var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth-leaved begonia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and devoid of hairy texture. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive spurred shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation indoors.
