Blakwellia monostachya

Blakwellia monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved blakwellia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southern regions of Australia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are usually less than 5 centimeters in length. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically pink or purple, arranged in a single spike.

Blakwellia monostachya is adapted to arid conditions and is known for its resilience in harsh environments.
