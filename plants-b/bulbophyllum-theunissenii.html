Bulbophyllum theunissenii

Bulbophyllum theunissenii, commonly known as the Theunissen's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique and intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, often fragrant blooms that are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid gardens.
