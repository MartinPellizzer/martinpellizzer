Banksia Cynaroides

Banksia cynaroides is a striking plant native to the coastal regions of Western Australia. Known for its distinctive yellow flower spikes, it is a member of the Proteaceae family. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and shrublands. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts. The plant plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and ecosystems.
