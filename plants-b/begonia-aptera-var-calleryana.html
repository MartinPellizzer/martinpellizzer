Begonia aptera var. calleryana

Begonia aptera var. calleryana, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in a silvery or grayish bloom, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, adding ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
