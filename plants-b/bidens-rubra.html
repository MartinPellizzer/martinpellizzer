Bidens rubra

Bidens rubra, commonly known as redstem ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Bidens rubra is often found in moist to wet habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
