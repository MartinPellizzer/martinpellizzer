Biarum marmarisense

Biarum marmarisense, commonly known as the Marmaris lily, is a rare and striking bulbous plant native to the coastal regions of Turkey.

It features slender, strap-like leaves and produces elegant, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
