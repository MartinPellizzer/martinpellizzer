Bomarea cordifolia

Bomarea cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-shaped bomarea, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

Bomarea cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
