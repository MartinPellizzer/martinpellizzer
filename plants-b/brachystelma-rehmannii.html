Brachystelma rehmannii

Brachystelma rehmannii, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, spiky red or orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically small and arranged in a rosette pattern, reducing water loss.

Brachystelma rehmannii is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
