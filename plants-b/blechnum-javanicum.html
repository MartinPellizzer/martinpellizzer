Blechnum javanicum

Blechnum javanicum, commonly known as the Java spleenwort, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Blechnaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are usually divided into segments with a distinctive, serrated edge.
