Bredemeyera spinosa

Bredemeyera spinosa, commonly known as the spiny rock rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, typically during the summer months. Its spiny appearance serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

Bredemeyera spinosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
