Balsamocitrus dawei

Balsamocitrus dawei, commonly known as the Chinese balsam tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red fruits that split open to release seeds.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
