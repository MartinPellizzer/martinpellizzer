Bupleurum balansae var. longiradiatum

Bupleurum balansae var. longiradiatum, commonly known as the long-rayed bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
