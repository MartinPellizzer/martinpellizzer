It is characterized by its distinctive, often bi-lobed fronds that resemble a heart or a crescent shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Botrychium pseudoternatum is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of less than 10 centimeters.
