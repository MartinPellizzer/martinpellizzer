Begonia wellmannii

Begonia wellmannii, commonly known as the wellmannii begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a deep green color with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
