Bauhinia khasiana var. gigalobia

Bauhinia khasiana var. gigalobia, commonly known as the giant bauhinia, is a striking flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its bark is smooth and greyish, while the leaves are palmately divided, giving it a unique appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.
