Barkworthia

Barkworthia, commonly known as the bark orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its unique, bark-like appearance, which helps it blend into its natural environment. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Barkworthia species are typically epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants without being parasitic.

Due to their striking appearance and rarity, they are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
