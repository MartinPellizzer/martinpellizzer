Bulbophyllum elasmatopus

Bulbophyllum elasmatopus, commonly known as the crested lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and dense, grass-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically arranged in a spiral or clustered pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate nature, it is a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
