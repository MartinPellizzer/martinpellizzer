Begonia cariocana

Begonia cariocana, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia cariocana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
