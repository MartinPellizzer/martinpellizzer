Berberis beaniana

Berberis beaniana, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that mature in late summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Berberis beaniana is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
