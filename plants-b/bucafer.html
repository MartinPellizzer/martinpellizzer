Bucafer

Bucafer, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
