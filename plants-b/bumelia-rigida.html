Bumelia rigida

Bumelia rigida, commonly known as the rigid bumelia, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, also known as the legume or pea family. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Bumelia rigida is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
