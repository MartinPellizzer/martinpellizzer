Benstonea lepatophila

Benstonea lepatophila, commonly known as the silky benstonea, is a unique member of the Benstonea genus within the family Benstoneaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that give it a soft, ethereal appearance. Native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or white, adding a subtle contrast to its foliage.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Benstonea lepatophila is considered a conservation concern.
