Begonia joffrei

Begonia joffrei, commonly known as the Joffrey begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces showy, red flowers with yellow centers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among collectors and gardeners who appreciate its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
