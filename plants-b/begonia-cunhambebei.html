Begonia cunhambebei

Begonia cunhambebei, commonly known as the Cunhambebei begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant patterns of green, red, and silver. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular among gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
