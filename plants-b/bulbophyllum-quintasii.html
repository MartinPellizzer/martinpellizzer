Bulbophyllum quintasii

Bulbophyllum quintasii, commonly known as the Quintasii orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale green, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal conditions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
