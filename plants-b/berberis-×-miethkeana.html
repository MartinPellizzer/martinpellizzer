Berberis × miethkeana

Berberis × miethkeana, commonly known as the hybrid barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, orange-red berries. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and features compound leaves with three to five leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The berries are edible but often used more for their ornamental value than for consumption.
