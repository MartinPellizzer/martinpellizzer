Begonia devexa

Begonia devexa, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often hairy and adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Begonia devexa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
