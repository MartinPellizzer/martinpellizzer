Buchia plantaginea

Buchia plantaginea, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a favored ornamental plant worldwide.
