Basananthe parvifolia

Basananthe parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf banana plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. Its fruit is a small, elongated berry that contains a single seed.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
