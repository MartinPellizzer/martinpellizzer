Begonia dux

Begonia dux, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a rich, glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, often blooming in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Begonia dux is popular in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and vibrant floral displays.
