Browallia speciosa

Browallia speciosa, commonly known as the showy browallia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers come in shades of blue, purple, and white, with five petals that are often marked with contrasting patterns.

The foliage is covered in fine hairs, and the plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
