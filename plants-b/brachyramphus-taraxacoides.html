Brachyramphus taraxacoides

Brachyramphus taraxacoides, commonly known as the taraxacoid brachyramphus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. While not widely cultivated, it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
