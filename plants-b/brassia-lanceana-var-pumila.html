Brassia lanceana var. pumila

Brassia lanceana var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf brassia, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and blooms during the warmer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
