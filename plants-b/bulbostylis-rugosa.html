Bulbostylis rugosa

Bulbostylis rugosa, commonly known as the rough-stemmed bulbostylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, three-lobed flowers that are typically pale green to brown in color. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland or marshy habitats.

Its fibrous root system and grass-like appearance make it a common species in wetland ecosystems.
