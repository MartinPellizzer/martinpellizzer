Bupleurum fruticescens subsp. balansae

Bupleurum fruticescens subsp.

balansae, commonly known as Balansae Bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to parts of Central Asia. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, exhibiting a shrub-like growth habit with woody stems.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are usually pinnate, with multiple leaflets that are lanceolate in shape.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
