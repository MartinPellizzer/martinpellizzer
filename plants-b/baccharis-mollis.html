Baccharis mollis

Baccharis mollis, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are often covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its unique texture.

Baccharis mollis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
