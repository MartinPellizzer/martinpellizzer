Bulbophyllum andringitranum

Bulbophyllum andringitranum, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its native habitat. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
