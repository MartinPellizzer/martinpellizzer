Baphia dewevrei subsp. marceliana

Baphia dewevrei subsp.

marceliana, commonly known as the Marcel's baphia, is a member of the Leguminosae family. It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant is native to tropical Africa, particularly in regions of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. It produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
