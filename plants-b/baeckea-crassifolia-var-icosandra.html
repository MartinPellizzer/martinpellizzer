Baeckea crassifolia var. icosandra

Baeckea crassifolia var. icosandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaf baeckea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate floral display. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small gardens or as part of native plant collections.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, thriving in coastal and sandy environments.
