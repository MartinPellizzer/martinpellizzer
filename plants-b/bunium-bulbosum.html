Bunium bulbosum

Bunium bulbosum, commonly known as the bulbous fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bulbous base and feathery, aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its leaves and seeds are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Bunium bulbosum thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
