Begonia × credneri

Begonia × credneri, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of ornamental features.

It is characterized by its velvety, deep green foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Begonia × credneri is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
