Begonia quintasii

Begonia quintasii, commonly known as the Quintasii begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
