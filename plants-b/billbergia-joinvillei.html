Billbergia joinvillei

Billbergia joinvillei, commonly known as the pink billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pinkish-red bracts that surround the flower, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a dense, water-holding structure at the center. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, emerging from the base of the leaf rosette.

Billbergia joinvillei thrives in bright, indirect light and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
