Bulbophyllum linggense

Bulbophyllum linggense, commonly known as the Linggense orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
