Beyeria gardneri

Beyeria gardneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Beyeria gardneri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
