Begonia pengii

Begonia pengii, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and a distinctive red or purple underside. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically appearing in the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage, which often serves as the primary attraction.
