Bridelia ramiflora

Bridelia ramiflora, commonly known as the fragrant bridelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a mild fragrance. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Bridelia ramiflora is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
