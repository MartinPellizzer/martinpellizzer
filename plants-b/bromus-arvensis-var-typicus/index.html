Bromus Arvensis Var. Typicus

Bromus arvensis var. typicus is a common weed found in disturbed soils across temperate regions. It is known for its ability to thrive in agricultural fields and along roadsides. The plant has slender stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are dispersed easily by wind and animals. This species is often a nuisance in cultivated areas but plays a role in soil stabilization.
