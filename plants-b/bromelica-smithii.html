Bromelica smithii

Bromelica smithii, commonly known as the pineapple plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of spiky, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces a flowering stem that eventually bears a fruit resembling a pineapple. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention in arid environments.

Bromelica smithii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.
