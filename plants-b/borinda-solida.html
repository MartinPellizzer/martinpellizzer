Borinda solida

Borinda solida, commonly known as the hardy borinda, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, woody stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
