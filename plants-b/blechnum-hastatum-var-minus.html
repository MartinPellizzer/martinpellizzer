Blechnum hastatum var. minus

Blechnum hastatum var. minus, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a fern species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
