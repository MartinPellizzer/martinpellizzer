Begonia × intermedia

Begonia × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia erythrophylla, combining the best traits of both parents. The plant typically features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of tubular or spurred flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its adaptability and long blooming period make it a favored choice for gardeners and florists alike.
