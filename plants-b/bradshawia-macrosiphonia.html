Bradshawia macrosiphonia

Bradshawia macrosiphonia, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with prominent, curling stamens. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous base and are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in the spring and early summer.
