Bulbophyllum hyalosemoides

Bulbophyllum hyalosemoides, commonly known as the glassy bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent or glassy appearance of its pseudobulbs and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in warm, humid environments with high light exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
