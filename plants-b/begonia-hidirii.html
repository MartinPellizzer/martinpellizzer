Begonia hidirii

Begonia hidirii, commonly known as the Hidir's begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that typically range from pink to red, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
