Begonia togashii

Begonia togashii, commonly known as the Togashii begonia, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a deep green color and a waxy texture, while the flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in suitable environments.
