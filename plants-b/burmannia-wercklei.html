Burmannia wercklei

Burmannia wercklei, commonly known as the red bladderwort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and distinctive red or orange flowers. The plant is often found in wet, swampy areas and is adapted to low-light conditions. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a notable species in tropical botany.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
