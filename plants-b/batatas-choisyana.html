Batatas choisyana

Batatas choisyana, commonly known as the Choisy's sweet potato, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant orange tuberous roots. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its tubers are rich in carbohydrates and have been traditionally used for food in various regions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its attractive foliage.
