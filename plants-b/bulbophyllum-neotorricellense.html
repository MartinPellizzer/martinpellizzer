Bulbophyllum neotorricellense

Bulbophyllum neotorricellense, commonly known as the Torricella orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its leaves and flowering stems. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
