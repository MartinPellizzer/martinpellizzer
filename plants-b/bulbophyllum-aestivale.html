Bulbophyllum aestivale

Bulbophyllum aestivale, commonly known as the summer orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, hence its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
