Begonia crassula

Begonia crassula, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms.

Begonia crassula is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
