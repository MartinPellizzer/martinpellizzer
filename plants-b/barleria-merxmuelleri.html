Barleria merxmuelleri

Barleria merxmuelleri, commonly known as the Cape barleria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular, pink or red flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Barleria merxmuelleri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
