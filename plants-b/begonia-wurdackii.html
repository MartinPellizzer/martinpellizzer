Begonia wurdackii

Begonia wurdackii, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are often deep green with a rich texture. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia wurdackii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
