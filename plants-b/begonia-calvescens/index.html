Begonia Calvescens

Begonia calvescens is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, tubular flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is popular among collectors for its unique foliage and ease of care. Its ability to produce aerial roots makes it ideal for hanging baskets or terrariums. With proper attention, Begonia calvescens can flourish and add a touch of elegance to any indoor space.
