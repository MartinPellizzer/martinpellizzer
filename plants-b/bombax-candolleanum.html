Bombax candolleanum

Bombax candolleanum, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in various cultures.
