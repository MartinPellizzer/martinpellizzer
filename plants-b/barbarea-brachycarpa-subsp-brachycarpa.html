Barbarea brachycarpa subsp. brachycarpa

Barbarea brachycarpa subsp.

brachycarpa, commonly known as the short-capselled yellow rocket, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its basal rosette of smooth, elongated leaves.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with four petals arranged in a cross shape, typical of the mustard family. Its small, oblong seed pods are distinctive and remain on the plant after flowering.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often found in disturbed soils and grasslands.
