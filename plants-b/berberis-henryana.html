Berberis henryana

Berberis henryana, commonly known as Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features spiny, thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often considered bitter. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Berberis henryana is also known for its medicinal properties, with its roots containing compounds used in traditional Chinese medicine.
