Basanacantha hebecarpa

Basanacantha hebecarpa, commonly known as the woolly spurge.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa, characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance.

Basanacantha hebecarpa is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
