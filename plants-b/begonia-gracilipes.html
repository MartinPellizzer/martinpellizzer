Begonia gracilipes

Begonia gracilipes, commonly known as the slender begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and subtle floral display.
