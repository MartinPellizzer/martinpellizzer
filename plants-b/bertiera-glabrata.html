Bertiera glabrata

Bertiera glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bertiera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in open woodlands and savannas. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Bertiera glabrata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
