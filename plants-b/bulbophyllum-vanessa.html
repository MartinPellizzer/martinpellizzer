Bulbophyllum vanessa

Bulbophyllum vanessa, commonly known as the Vanessa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical conditions.

The leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
