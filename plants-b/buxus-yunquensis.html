Buxus yunquensis

Buxus yunquensis, commonly known as the Yunqu boxwood, is a rare evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Buxaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for topiary and hedging.

Due to its limited natural range and habitat specificity, it is considered a critically endangered species.
