Blepharis acuminata

Blepharis acuminata, commonly known as the sharpleaf blepharis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, giving it the common name "sharpleaf.".
