Barjonia erecta

Barjonia erecta, commonly known as the erect barjonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to withstand saline conditions and is often found in dune environments.

Barjonia erecta plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for local wildlife.
