Bulbostylis lineolata

Bulbostylis lineolata, commonly known as the narrow-striated bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in wetland or marshy environments, often forming dense mats near water bodies.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
