Begonia minuscula

Begonia minuscula, commonly known as the miniature begonia, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.
