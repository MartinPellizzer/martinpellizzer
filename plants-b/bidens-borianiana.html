Bidens borianiana

Bidens borianiana, commonly known as the scarlet false nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and meadows. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a schizocarp composed of two one-seeded segments that split apart when mature.

Bidens borianiana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
