Botrychium lunaria var. ramosum

Botrychium lunaria var. ramosum, commonly known as the slender moonwort, is a small fern that belongs to the family Ophioglossaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a unique structure with a single, unbranched sporangium at the tip of its fertile frond. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Botrychium lunaria var. ramosum is often found in the northeastern United States and parts of Canada.
