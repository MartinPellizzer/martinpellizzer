Bauhinia scarlatina

Bauhinia scarlatina, commonly known as the scarlet bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, papery leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in spring and summer. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Bauhinia scarlatina is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
