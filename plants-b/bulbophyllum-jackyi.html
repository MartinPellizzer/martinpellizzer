Bulbophyllum jackyi

Bulbophyllum jackyi, commonly known as the Jack's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum jackyi is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
