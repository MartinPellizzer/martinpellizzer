Boivinella natalensis

Boivinella natalensis, commonly known as Natal wild bean, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its small, white flowers are followed by elongated pods containing several seeds.

Boivinella natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
