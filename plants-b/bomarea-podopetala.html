Bomarea podopetala

Bomarea podopetala, commonly known as the pink-flowered bomarea, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to purple bracts that surround its small, white flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens.
