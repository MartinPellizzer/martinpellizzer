Bulbophyllum sridithii

Bulbophyllum sridithii, commonly known as the Sridithi's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small but numerous, often displaying a combination of white, yellow, and green hues.

The orchid's botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
