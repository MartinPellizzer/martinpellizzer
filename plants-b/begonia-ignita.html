Begonia ignita

Begonia ignita, commonly known as the flame begonia, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are often borne on long, slender stalks. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can vary in color from deep green to reddish hues. This species thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
