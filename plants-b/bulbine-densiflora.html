Bulbine densiflora

Bulbine densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered bulbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are densely arranged around the base. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Bulbine densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
