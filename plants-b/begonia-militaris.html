Begonia militaris

Begonia militaris, commonly known as the military begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value, Begonia militaris is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
