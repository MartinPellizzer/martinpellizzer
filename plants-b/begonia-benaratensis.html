Begonia benaratensis

Begonia benaratensis, commonly known as the Benarat begonia, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
