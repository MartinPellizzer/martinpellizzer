Bulbophyllum nuruanum

Bulbophyllum nuruanum, commonly known as the Nuruana orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species.
