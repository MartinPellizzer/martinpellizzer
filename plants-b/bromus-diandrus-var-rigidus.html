Bromus diandrus var. rigidus

Bromus diandrus var. rigidus, commonly known as rigid brome, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. The plant has slender, upright stems that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with a bluish-green color.

The inflorescence is a panicle, bearing small, spike-like clusters of seeds that are dispersed by wind.
