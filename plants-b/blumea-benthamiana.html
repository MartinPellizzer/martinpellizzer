Blumea benthamiana

Blumea benthamiana, commonly known as the Chinese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pale pink petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it suitable for both garden cultivation and traditional herbal use.
