Blumea lacera var. javanica

Blumea lacera var. javanica, commonly known as Java Blumea, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, aromatic stems and small, white to yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
