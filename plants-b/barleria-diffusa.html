Barleria diffusa

Barleria diffusa, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, upright growth. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and slightly hairy, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Barleria diffusa is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
