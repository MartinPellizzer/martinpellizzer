Biophytum sensitivum var. sesbanioides

Biophytum sensitivum var. sesbanioides, commonly known as the sensitive plant or mimosa pudica, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its sensitive leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant has small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Its unique behavior and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and educational purposes.
