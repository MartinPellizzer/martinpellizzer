Buena muzonensis

Buena muzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found exclusively in the mountainous regions of Luzon, Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on forest floors or mossy substrates. Its leaves are long and narrow, adapted to capture limited sunlight in its native habitat.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Buena muzonensis is considered a vulnerable species.
