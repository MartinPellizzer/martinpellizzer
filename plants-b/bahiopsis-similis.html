Bahiopsis similis

Bahiopsis similis, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Bahiopsis similis is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, making it a resilient and drought-tolerant species.
