Batatas macrorhizos

Batatas macrorhizos, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces edible tubers that are rich in starch and have a sweet, nutty flavor. Its roots system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in wetland and marshy environments.

Batatas macrorhizos is often cultivated for both its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
