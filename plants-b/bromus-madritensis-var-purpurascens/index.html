Bromus Madritensis Var. Purpurascens

Bromus madritensis var. purpurascens is a striking variant of the common brome grass known for its vivid purple hues. This plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and open fields. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The species is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions worldwide. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and supports local wildlife.
