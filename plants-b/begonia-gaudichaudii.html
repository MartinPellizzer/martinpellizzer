Begonia gaudichaudii

Begonia gaudichaudii, commonly known as the pink begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and red, often with a contrasting white or yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
