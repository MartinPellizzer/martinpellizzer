Bulbophyllum concavilabium

Bulbophyllum concavilabium, commonly known as the concave-labium orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, with petals that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
