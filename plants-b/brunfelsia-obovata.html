Brunfelsia obovata

Brunfelsia obovata, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a waxy texture. It produces violet-black berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Brunfelsia obovata thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
