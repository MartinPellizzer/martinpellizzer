Brassica oleracea var. aberdeenea

Brassica oleracea var. aberdeenea, commonly known as the Aberdeensaurus kale, is a variety of kale characterized by its large, curly leaves.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cauliflower. The plant is notable for its robust growth and frost tolerance, making it suitable for cooler climates. Its leaves are typically dark green with a slightly bitter taste, and it is often used in salads or cooked dishes.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various growing conditions.
