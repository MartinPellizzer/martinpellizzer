Bunium filipes

Bunium filipes, commonly known as the slender fennel, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in umbels. The plant is native to regions of Europe and Asia, often found in dry, rocky soils. It has a strong anise-like aroma due to the presence of volatile oils in its foliage.

Bunium filipes is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
