Bulbophyllum trutiniferum

Bulbophyllum trutiniferum, commonly known as the Trutiniferum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its natural habitat.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts.
