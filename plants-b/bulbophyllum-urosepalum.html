Bulbophyllum urosepalum

Bulbophyllum urosepalum, commonly known as the black-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a dark purple lip that distinguishes it from other members of the genus. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its striking appearance.
