Besleria berteroana

Besleria berteroana, commonly known as the red-flowered spider orchid, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence with bright red flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, with a glossy surface, and it produces small, tubular roots that help anchor it in moist soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
