Bursera medranoana

Bursera medranoana, commonly known as the Medrano's bursera, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes frankincense and myrrh trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing aromatic seeds.

Bursera medranoana is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in tropical and subtropical regions.
