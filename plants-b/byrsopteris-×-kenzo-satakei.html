Byrsopteris × kenzo-satakei

Byrsopteris × kenzo-satakei, commonly known as the hybrid holly fern, is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Byrsopteris kenzo and Byrsopteris satakei.

It exhibits characteristics of both parent species, featuring fronds that are typically lanceolate with a serrated margin. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.

The hybrid is valued for its adaptability and aesthetic appeal in garden landscapes.
