Bechium nudicaule

Bechium nudicaule, commonly known as the slender beech.

It is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Bechium nudicaule is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
