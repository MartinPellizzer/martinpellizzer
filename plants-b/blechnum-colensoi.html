Blechnum colensoi

Blechnum colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's harts-tongue fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its distinctive, leathery, heart-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its fronds are evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 50 centimeters.

Blechnum colensoi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
