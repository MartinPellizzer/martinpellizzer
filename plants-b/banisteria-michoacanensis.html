Banisteria michoacanensis

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its climbing habit and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elongated pods. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
