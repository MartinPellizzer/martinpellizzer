Balsamina Repens

Balsamina repens is a delicate flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant, showy blooms that often resemble miniature roses. This creeping plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in dense undergrowth. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more widely cultivated plants.
