Bulbophyllum danii

Bulbophyllum danii, commonly known as the Danii's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and green hues. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts.
