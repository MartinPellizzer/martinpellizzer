Beesha fax

Beesha fax, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant orange and yellow petals that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating species. The plant has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated primarily by bees and butterflies.

Beesha fax is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
