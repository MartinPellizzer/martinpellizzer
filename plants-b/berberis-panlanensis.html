Berberis panlanensis

Berberis panlanensis, commonly known as the Panlan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often considered bitter. It is typically found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal properties.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.
