Brassiopsis Keiliana

This flowering plant belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Despite its beauty, Brassiopsis keiliana is highly endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection for ornamental purposes. Efforts to protect and propagate this species are ongoing, highlighting the importance of preserving biodiversity in threatened ecosystems.
