Bromus erectus var. glabratus

Bromus erectus var. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth brome, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and smooth, glabrous leaves. The plant produces dense tussocks and has spikelets that are typically arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in forage and erosion control.
