Bulbostylis capillaris var. tenuifolia

Bulbostylis capillaris var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-stemmed bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome system that helps it spread and establish in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
