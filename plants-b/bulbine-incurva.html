Bulbine incurva

Bulbine incurva, commonly known as the bent leaf bulbine, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often curved or folded, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Bulbine incurva is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
