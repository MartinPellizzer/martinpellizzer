Bulbophyllum weberbauerianum var. angustius

Bulbophyllum weberbauerianum var. angustius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often pendulous inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the warm seasons. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered in a thin layer of bark-like tissue, which helps it store water.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
