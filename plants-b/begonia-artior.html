Begonia artior

Begonia artior, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique leaf texture and vibrant floral display.
