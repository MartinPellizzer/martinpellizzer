Barleria rigida var. spinissima

Barleria rigida var. spinissima, commonly known as the spiked barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, often with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
