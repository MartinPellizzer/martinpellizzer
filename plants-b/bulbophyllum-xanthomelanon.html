Bulbophyllum xanthomelanon

Bulbophyllum xanthomelanon, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often pendulous inflorescence and striking yellow and green floral colors. The plant produces small, intricate flowers with prominent lip structures typical of orchids. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
