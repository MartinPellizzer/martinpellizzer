Babiana caerulescens

Babiana caerulescens, commonly known as the bluebabies or blue bulbous, is a striking South African plant characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flower heads.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its bulbs are adapted to store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions.
