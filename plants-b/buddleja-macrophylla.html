Buddleja macrophylla

Buddleja macrophylla, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can grow up to 10 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, attracting numerous pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Buddleja macrophylla is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban conditions and its role in supporting butterfly populations.
