Bulbophyllum nanopetalum

Bulbophyllum nanopetalum, commonly known as the miniature petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on tree trunks or branches in humid tropical environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The orchid's unique floral structure and compact size make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
