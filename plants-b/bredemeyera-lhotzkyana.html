Bredemeyera lhotzkyana

It is native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
