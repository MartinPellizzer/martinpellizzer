Beta patellaris f. monodiana

Beta patellaris f. monodiana, commonly known as the Patellaria beta, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the beta genus.

It is characterized by its unique leaf morphology, featuring elongated, narrow leaves with a distinctive bluish-green hue. This plant is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean, where it thrives in rocky and coastal environments. Its botanical significance lies in its genetic variation and potential for horticultural interest.

Due to its limited distribution, it is often considered a species of conservation concern.
