Bidens bipinnata var. cynapiifolia

Bidens bipinnata var. cynapiifolia, commonly known as the cowcalf plant, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers with prominent white markings, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

The species is known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal medicine.
