Bassia stellaris var. schrenkiana

Bassia stellaris var. schrenkiana, commonly known as the Schrenk's starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and prolonged flowering period.
