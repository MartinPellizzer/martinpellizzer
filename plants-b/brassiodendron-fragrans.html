Brassiodendron fragrans

Brassiodendron fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma. It prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil.

Brassiodendron fragrans is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
