Bauhinia nakhonphanomensis

Bauhinia nakhonphanomensis, commonly known as Nakhonphanom bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or secondary forests.
