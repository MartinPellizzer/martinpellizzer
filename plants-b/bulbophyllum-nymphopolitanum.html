Bulbophyllum nymphopolitanum

Bulbophyllum nymphopolitanum, commonly known as the Nymphopolitan Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its inflorescence consists of multiple small, fragrant blooms arranged in a raceme.

The botanical features of this orchid include its pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients, and its specialized roots adapted for nutrient absorption.
