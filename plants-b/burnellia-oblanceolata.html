Burnellia oblanceolata

Burnellia oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf burnellia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

Burnellia oblanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to native pollinators.
