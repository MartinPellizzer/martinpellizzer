Banisteria tenuis

Banisteria tenuis, commonly known as the small-stemmed banisteria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Bignoniaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and showy, yellow-orange flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
