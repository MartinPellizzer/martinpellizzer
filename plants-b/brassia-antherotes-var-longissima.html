Brassia antherotes var. longissima

Brassia antherotes var. longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that are typically a mix of purple and white. The plant features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
