Balania kawakamii

Balania kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's lily, is a rare flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.
