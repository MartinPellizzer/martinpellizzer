Bulbophyllum fenghuangshanianum

Bulbophyllum fenghuangshanianum, commonly known as the Fenghuangshan Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that can range from red to yellow, with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
