Breweria pannosa

Breweria pannosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety down. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and recognizable species within the Asteraceae family.
