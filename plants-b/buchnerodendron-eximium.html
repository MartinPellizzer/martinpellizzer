Buchnerodendron eximium

Buchnerodendron eximium, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its canopy. The plant has compound leaves with long, thin leaflets that provide a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Buchnerodendron eximium is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
