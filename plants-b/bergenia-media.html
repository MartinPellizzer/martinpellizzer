Bergenia media

Bergenia media, commonly known as broadleaf bergenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy rhizomes and lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces dense clusters of pinkish-red flowers in early summer, which are arranged in terminal umbels. Bergenia media thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in garden landscapes for its hardiness and long-lasting foliage.
