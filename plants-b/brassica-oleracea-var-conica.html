Brassica oleracea var. conica

Brassica oleracea var. conica, commonly known as the curled cabbage, is a variety of the species Brassica oleracea.

It is characterized by its tightly curled, compact leaves that form a dense, cone-shaped head. This plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cauliflower. The leaves are typically dark green and have a slightly bitter taste.

It is often cultivated for both culinary use and ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance.
