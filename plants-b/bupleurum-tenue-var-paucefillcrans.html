Bupleurum tenue var. paucefillcrans

Bupleurum tenue var. paucefillcrans, commonly known as the slender valerian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, branched stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height, often with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
