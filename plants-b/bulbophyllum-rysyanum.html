Bulbophyllum rysyanum

Bulbophyllum rysyanum, commonly known as the Rysyan's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
