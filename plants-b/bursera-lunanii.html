Bursera lunanii

Bursera lunanii, commonly known as the Luna Bursera, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes frankincense and myrrh trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange or red berries.

Bursera lunanii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
