Brassiophoenix

Brassiophoenix, commonly known as the golden palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the island of Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. Brassiophoenix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
