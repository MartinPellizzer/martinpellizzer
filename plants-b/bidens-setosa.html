Bidens setosa

Bidens setosa, commonly known as hispid bidens, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a rough texture. The plant produces small yellow flowers with five petals, often accompanied by prominent bracts. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in fields and along roadsides.

Bidens setosa is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
