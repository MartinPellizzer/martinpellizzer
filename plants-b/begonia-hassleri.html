Begonia hassleri

Begonia hassleri, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia hassleri is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
