Bidens anthriscoides var. angustiloba

Bidens anthriscoides var. angustiloba, commonly known as narrowleaf tickseed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers with ray florets that resemble daisies. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common sight in various ecological regions across the continent.
