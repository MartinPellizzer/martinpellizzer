Bauhinia kingii

Bauhinia kingii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, and its flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive tubular shape. Bauhinia kingii thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility in its native environment.
