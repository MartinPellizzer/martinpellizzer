Bidens riedelii f. intermedia

Bidens riedelii f. intermedia, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a perennial herb native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides.
