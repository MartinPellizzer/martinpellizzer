Beta foliosa

Beta foliosa, commonly known as the leafy beta or foliosa beta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family, which includes various ornamental and edible species. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Beta foliosa produces small, greenish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to gardens.

Its adaptability to different soil types makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
