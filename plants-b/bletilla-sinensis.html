Bletilla sinensis

Bletilla sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive lip petal that aids in pollination.

Bletilla sinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
