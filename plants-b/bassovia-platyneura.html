Bassovia platyneura

Bassovia platyneura, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Bassovia platyneura is often found in rocky or dry soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
