Beaucarnea

Beaucarnea, commonly known as the ponytail palm, is a succulent plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features a thick, tree-like stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant's most distinctive feature is its rosette of long, twisted leaves that resemble a ponytail. Beaucarnea has small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in the spring.

It is valued for its low maintenance and unique appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
