Bunium latilobum

Bunium latilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed fennel, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in umbrella-like clusters. Its seeds are edible and can be used as a spice, similar to fennel.
