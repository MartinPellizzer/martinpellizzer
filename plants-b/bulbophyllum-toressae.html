Bulbophyllum toressae

Bulbophyllum toressae, commonly known as the Trossa Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
