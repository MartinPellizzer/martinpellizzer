Bidens Mandonii

Bidens mandonii is a unique flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is known for its distinctive yellow-orange flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh climates. Its ability to survive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions makes it an important plant in its ecosystem. Despite its hardiness, Bidens mandonii remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture circles.
