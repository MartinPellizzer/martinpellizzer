Bifaria rubescens

Bifaria rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and a compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Bifaria rubescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
