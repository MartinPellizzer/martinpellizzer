Bignonia azedarachta

Bignonia azedarachta, commonly known as the Chinese trumpetflower, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are opposite and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive trumpet shape. These flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and hummingbirds.

Bignonia azedarachta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a decorative plant.
