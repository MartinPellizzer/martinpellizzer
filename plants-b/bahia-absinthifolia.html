Bahia absinthifolia

Bahia absinthifolia, commonly known as silverleaf bahia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornate appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Bahia absinthifolia is drought-tolerant and thrives in warm climates.
