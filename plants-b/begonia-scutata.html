Begonia scutata

Begonia scutata, commonly known as the shieldleaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or silver markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often grown as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and delicate floral display.
