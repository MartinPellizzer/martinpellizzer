Boehmeria radicans

Boehmeria radicans, commonly known as silky banana or Chinese lantern vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are soft and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fibrous stems are often used in traditional crafts and textiles due to their strength and flexibility.

Boehmeria radicans thrives in humid environments and is often found growing along forest edges or in shaded areas.
