Barkhausia tenella

Barkhausia tenella, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a fibrous root system and linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are borne in dense, terminal clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Barkhausia tenella is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
