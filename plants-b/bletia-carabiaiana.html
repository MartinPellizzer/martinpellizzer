Bletia carabiaiana

Bletia carabiaiana, commonly known as the Carabia orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Bletia carabiaiana is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
