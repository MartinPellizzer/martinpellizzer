Botryanthus kerneri

Botryanthus kerneri, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features a basal rosette of silver-green, hairy leaves that help it conserve water. Its flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in spring and summer.

Botryanthus kerneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
