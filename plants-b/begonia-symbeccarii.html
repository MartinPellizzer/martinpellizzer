Begonia symbeccarii

Begonia symbeccarii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to purplish hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
