Bidens henryi

Bidens henryi, commonly known as Henry's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow flowers with ray florets and is often found in open, disturbed habitats. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Bidens henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
