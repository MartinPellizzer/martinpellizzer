Begonia × weltoniensis

Begonia × weltoniensis, commonly known as the Welton's begonia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia xerographica.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for container gardening due to its compact growth habit and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

This hybrid is well-suited for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in mild climates.
