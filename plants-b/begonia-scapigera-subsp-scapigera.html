Begonia scapigera subsp. scapigera

Begonia scapigera subsp.

scapigera, commonly known as the scapigera begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America. It features upright, slender stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture.

The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage and long-lasting blooms.
