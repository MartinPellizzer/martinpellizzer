Barleria superata

Barleria superata, commonly known as the red barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Barleria superata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
