Botrychium australe

Botrychium australe, commonly known as the Australian moonwort, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Ophioglossaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged fronds that resemble a crown, giving it a unique appearance. Botrychium australe is native to Australia and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on fern evolution and ecological adaptation.
