Begonia tarokoensis

Begonia tarokoensis, commonly known as the Taroko begonia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as mountain forests. Its leaves are often heart-shaped with intricate patterns, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
