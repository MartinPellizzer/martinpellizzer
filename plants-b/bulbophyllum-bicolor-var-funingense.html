Bulbophyllum bicolor var. funingense

Bulbophyllum bicolor var. funingense, commonly known as the Funing Orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking dichotomous branching and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces intricate inflorescences with flowers that transition from white to yellow, creating a unique color contrast. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
