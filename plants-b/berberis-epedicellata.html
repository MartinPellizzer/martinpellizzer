Berberis epedicellata

Berberis epedicellata, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China and neighboring regions.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Berberis epedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
