Bletilla chartacea

Bletilla chartacea, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to eastern Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Bletilla chartacea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
