Begonia tonkinensis

Begonia tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers that can range from white to pink or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in humid, shady environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Begonia tonkinensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
