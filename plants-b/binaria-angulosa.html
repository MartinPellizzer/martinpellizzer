Binaria angulosa

Binaria angulosa, commonly known as the angular fern, is a species of fern characterized by its unique, angular fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive zigzag or angular pattern along the veins.
