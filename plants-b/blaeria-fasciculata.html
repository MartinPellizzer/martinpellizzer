Blaeria fasciculata

Blaeria fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered bladdernut, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Hippocastanaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that appear in late spring. Blaeria fasciculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
