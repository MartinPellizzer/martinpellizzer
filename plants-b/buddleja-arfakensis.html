Buddleja arfakensis

Buddleja arfakensis, commonly known as the African butterfly bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Buddleja arfakensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
