Bulbophyllum rigidum

Bulbophyllum rigidum, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
