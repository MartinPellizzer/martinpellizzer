Buddleja densiflora

Buddleja densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower bush or Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting a variety of pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has woody stems that can grow up to several meters in height, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. Its adaptability to different soil types and climate conditions makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Buddleja densiflora is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
