Ballota hirsuta f. intermedia

Ballota hirsuta f. intermedia, commonly known as the hairy ballota or intermediate ballota, is a variant of the hairy ballota, a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in gardens and natural habitats.
