Bulbophyllum boosii

Bulbophyllum boosii, commonly known as the Boosii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically small and fragrant, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Bulbophyllum boosii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
