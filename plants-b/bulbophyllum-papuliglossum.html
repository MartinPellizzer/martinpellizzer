Bulbophyllum papuliglossum

Bulbophyllum papuliglossum, commonly known as the papillate lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a hairy, lip-like appendage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Bulbophyllum papuliglossum is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its distinctive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
