Blumea dentata var. glabrescens

Blumea dentata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or herb, with alternate, ovate leaves that are often hairy in the wild form but smooth in this variety. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The smooth blumea is valued for its ornamental potential and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
