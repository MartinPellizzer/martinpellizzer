Begonia crateris

Begonia crateris, commonly known as the crater begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow centers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia crateris is a perennial species that prefers partial shade and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
