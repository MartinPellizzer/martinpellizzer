Bulbophyllum aurantiacum

Bulbophyllum aurantiacum, commonly known as the Orange-lipped Pterostylis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange lip and greenish-yellow petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, often found in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, blooming in clusters during the wet season.

The orchid's botanical features make it a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
