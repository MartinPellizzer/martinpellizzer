Bopopia

Bopopia, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spherical clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Bopopia is relatively drought-tolerant and prefers full sun to partial shade.
