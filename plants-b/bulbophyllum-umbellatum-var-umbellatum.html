Bulbophyllum umbellatum var. umbellatum

Bulbophyllum umbellatum var. umbellatum, commonly known as the Umbrella BULBophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers that resemble an umbrella. The plant typically produces multiple inflorescences with numerous small, white to pale yellow blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ornamental value and relatively ease of care.
