Bupleurum odontites

Bupleurum odontites, commonly known as the toothed bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features slender, branching stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellowish-green and appear in clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Bupleurum odontites is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
