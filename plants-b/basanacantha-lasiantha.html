Basanacantha lasiantha

Basanacantha lasiantha, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing in moist, shaded areas of mountainous forests. Its scientific name reflects its unique botanical features and geographic origin.

Basanacantha lasiantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
