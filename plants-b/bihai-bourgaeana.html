Bihai bourgaeana

Bihai bourgaeana, commonly known as the Queen of the Night, is a striking member of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is renowned for its large, white, fragrant flowers that bloom only at night. The plant is a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters, with thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture. Its flowers, which can be up to 30 centimeters in diameter, are highly prized in horticulture for their beauty and nocturnal bloom.

The plant is also notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
