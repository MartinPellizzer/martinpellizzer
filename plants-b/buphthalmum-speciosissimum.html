Buphthalmum speciosissimum

Buphthalmum speciosissimum, commonly known as the showy ox-eye, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
