Besleria imrayi

Besleria imrayi, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences with vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces showy, long-lasting blooms that are often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
