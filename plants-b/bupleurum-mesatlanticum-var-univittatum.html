Bupleurum mesatlanticum var. univittatum

Bupleurum mesatlanticum var. univittatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic stems that are typically branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a distinct yellow stripe along the midrib.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
