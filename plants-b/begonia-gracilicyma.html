Begonia gracilicyma

Begonia gracilicyma, commonly known as the slender-leaved begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that are often tinged with silver or white, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements, it is often cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
