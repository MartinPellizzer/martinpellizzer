Bauhinia venustula

Bauhinia venustula, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, doubly compound leaves that are often pink or red in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Bauhinia venustula is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.
