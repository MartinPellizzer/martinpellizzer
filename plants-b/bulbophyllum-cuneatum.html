Bulbophyllum cuneatum

Bulbophyllum cuneatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, narrow leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum cuneatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
