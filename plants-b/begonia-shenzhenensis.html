Begonia shenzhenensis

Begonia shenzhenensis, commonly known as the Shenzhen begonia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation and horticultural displays.
