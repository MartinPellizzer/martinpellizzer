Buddleja glomerata

Buddleja glomerata, commonly known as the clustered butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features long, cylindrical flower clusters that bloom in late summer, attracting a variety of pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often fuzzy to the touch. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant flowers and upright growth habit.
