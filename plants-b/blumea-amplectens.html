Blumea amplectens

Blumea amplectens, commonly known as the broadleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is recognized for its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Blumea amplectens is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
