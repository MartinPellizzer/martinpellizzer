Bulbophyllum solteroi

Bulbophyllum solteroi, commonly known as the Solter's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its inflorescence typically bears several flowers with intricate, spurred petals.

Bulbophyllum solteroi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
