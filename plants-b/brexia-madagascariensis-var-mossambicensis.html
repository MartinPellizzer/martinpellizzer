Brexia madagascariensis var. mossambicensis

Brexia madagascariensis var. mossambicensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a rare and unique species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives on nutrient-poor soils. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid conditions.
