Begonia heterodonta

Begonia heterodonta, commonly known as the heterodonta begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant green coloration. Its flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting markings.

Begonia heterodonta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
