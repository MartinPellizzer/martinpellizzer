Boronia tetrandra var. pilosa

Boronia tetrandra var. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant features hairy foliage, which gives it its specific epithet "pilosa.". It typically grows in heathlands and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils.
