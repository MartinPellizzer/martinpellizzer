Bakeridesia exalata

Bakeridesia exalata, commonly known as the slender leaved orchid, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or marshy areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for absorbing nutrients from the acidic soil.
