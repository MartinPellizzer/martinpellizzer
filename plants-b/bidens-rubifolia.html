Bidens rubifolia

Bidens rubifolia, commonly known as redstem ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its reddish stems and bracts give it a distinctive appearance, making it a notable wildflower.

Bidens rubifolia is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.
