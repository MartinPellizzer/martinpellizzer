Batrachium nipponicum var. major

Batrachium nipponicum var. major, commonly known as the Japanese frog's eye orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, frog-like appearance of its flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its distinctive floral morphology.
