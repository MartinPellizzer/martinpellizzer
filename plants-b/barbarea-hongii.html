Barbarea hongii

Barbarea hongii, commonly known as Chinese rockcress, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

Barbarea hongii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
