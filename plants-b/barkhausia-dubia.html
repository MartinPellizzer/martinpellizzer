Barkhausia dubia

Barkhausia dubia, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender stems and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Barkhausia dubia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
