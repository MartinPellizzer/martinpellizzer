Balakata

Balakata, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Balakata flowers are often used in local crafts and traditional ceremonies due to their aesthetic and cultural significance.
