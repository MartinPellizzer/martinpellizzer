Bletia schoenina

Bletia schoenina, commonly known as the pink bletia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, and it produces clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. Bletia schoenina thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and as a houseplant.
