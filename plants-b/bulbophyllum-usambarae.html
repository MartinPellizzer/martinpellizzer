Bulbophyllum usambarae

Bulbophyllum usambarae, commonly known as the Usambara orchid, is a species of orchid native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a complex structure typical of orchids.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
