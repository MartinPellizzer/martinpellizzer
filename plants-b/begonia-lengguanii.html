Begonia lengguanii

Begonia lengguanii, commonly known as the Guangdong begonia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with upright stems and clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive spur at the base.

Begonia lengguanii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
