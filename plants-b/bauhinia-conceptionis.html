Bauhinia conceptionis

Bauhinia conceptionis, commonly known as the sacred bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also culturally significant in some regions, where it is used in traditional practices.
