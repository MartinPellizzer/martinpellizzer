Bulbophyllum blaoense

Bulbophyllum blaoense, commonly known as the Blao orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often pale yellow or greenish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in fluctuating environmental conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
