Bromus stenostachyus

Bromus stenostachyus, commonly known as narrow-stemmed brome, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Bromus stenostachyus is adapted to temperate climates and is relatively drought-tolerant.
