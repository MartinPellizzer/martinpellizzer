Bambusa arundinacea var. orientalis

Bambusa arundinacea var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender culms with a smooth, light green to yellowish-brown surface. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. It is well-suited for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This bamboo is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a source of sustainable materials.
