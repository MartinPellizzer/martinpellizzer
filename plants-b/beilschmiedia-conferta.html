Beilschmiedia conferta

Beilschmiedia conferta, commonly known as the glossy beauty leaf, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by clustered, fleshy berries that are orange-red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Beilschmiedia conferta is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
