Besleria macrocalyx

Besleria macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its large, showy, spider-like flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in length. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, violet-colored blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and wasps. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
