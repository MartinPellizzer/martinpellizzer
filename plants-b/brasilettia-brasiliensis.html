Brasilettia brasiliensis

Brasilettia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that help it capture moisture from the humid air.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, Brasilettia brasiliensis is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
