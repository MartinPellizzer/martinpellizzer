Bidens aristosa f. involucrata

Bidens aristosa f. involucrata, commonly known as the involucrate ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its showy yellow flowers appear in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in moist to wet habitats, including marshes and roadside ditches.
