Bulbophyllum hymenochilum

Bulbophyllum hymenochilum, commonly known as the orchid with the hairy lip, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers typically feature a distinctive, hairy lip that enhances its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in humid, shaded conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
