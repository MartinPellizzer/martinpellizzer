Bauhinia scandens

Bauhinia scandens, commonly known as the climbing bauhinia, is a deciduous legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and showy, pink or white butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant is a vigorous climber, often found twining around trees and shrubs in tropical and subtropical regions. Its seed pods are flat and fibrous, containing several hard, angular seeds.

Bauhinia scandens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
