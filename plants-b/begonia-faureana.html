Begonia faureana

Begonia faureana, commonly known as the African begonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins, often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
