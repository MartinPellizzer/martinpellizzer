Bulbophyllum lokonense

Bulbophyllum lokonense, commonly known as the Lokonense orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
