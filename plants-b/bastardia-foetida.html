Bastardia foetida

Bastardia foetida, commonly known as stinking sprangletop, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. It is also known for its strong, unpleasant odor, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom.
