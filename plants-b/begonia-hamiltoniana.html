Begonia hamiltoniana

Begonia hamiltoniana, commonly known as the Hamiltonian begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia hamiltoniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
