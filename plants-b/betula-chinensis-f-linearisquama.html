Betula chinensis f. linearisquama

Betula chinensis f. linearisquama, commonly known as the narrow-leaf birch, is a unique variant of the Chinese birch.

It is characterized by its elongated, linear leaves that are narrower than those of the typical species. The plant is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. Its bark is smooth and pale, often displaying a distinctive white or silver hue.

This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
