Bulbostylis distichoides

Bulbostylis distichoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its bulbous base allows it to store water and nutrients, aiding survival in fluctuating environmental conditions.

Bulbostylis distichoides plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
