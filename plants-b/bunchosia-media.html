Bunchosia media

Bunchosia media, commonly known as the yellow bell or yellow cup, is a shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Bunchosia media is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
