Begonia macintyreana

Begonia macintyreana, commonly known as the MacIntyre begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia macintyreana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
