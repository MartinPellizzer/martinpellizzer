Benedicta officinalis

Benedicta officinalis, commonly known as the sacred herb, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in umbels. The plant is native to temperate regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shady environments. Its aromatic properties make it useful in traditional medicine for its calming and digestive benefits.

Benedicta officinalis is also valued in herbal teas and as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance.
