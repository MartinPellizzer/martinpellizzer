Bulbophyllum helenae

Bulbophyllum helenae, commonly known as the Helen's Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic orchid features a compact, pseudobulb-like structure that stores water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically small and delicate, often displaying a range of colors from white to yellow and pink. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is favored by orchid enthusiasts.
