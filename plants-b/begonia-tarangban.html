Begonia tarangban

Begonia tarangban, commonly known as the Philippine begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
