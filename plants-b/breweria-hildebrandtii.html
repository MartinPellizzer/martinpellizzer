Breweria hildebrandtii

Breweria hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Breweria hildebrandtii is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
