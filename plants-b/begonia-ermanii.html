Begonia ermanii

Begonia ermanii, commonly known as the Emanii begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded garden beds.
