Botryceras laurinum

Botryceras laurinum, commonly known as the laurel orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Botryceras laurinum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
