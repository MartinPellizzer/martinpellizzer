Brandella erythraea f. subexalata

Brandella erythraea f. subexalata, commonly known as the Crimson-veined Spiderwort, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from bright red to deep pink, often with contrasting veining. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
