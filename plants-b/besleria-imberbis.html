Besleria imberbis

Besleria imberbis, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence. The plant features elongated, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are typically white or pale green with a distinctive, intricate pattern of stripes and spots.

Besleria imberbis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
