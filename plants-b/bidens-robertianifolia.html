Bidens robertianifolia

Bidens robertianifolia, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the southern United States.

Bidens robertianifolia produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
