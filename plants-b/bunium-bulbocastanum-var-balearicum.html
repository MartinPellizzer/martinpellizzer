Bunium bulbocastanum var. balearicum

Bunium bulbocastanum var. balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic fennel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and often umbellate inflorescences. The plant features hollow, ridged stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically aromatic. Its small white flowers are arranged in umbrella-like clusters, and it produces small, dry fruits.

This variety is distinguished by its unique morphology and chemical composition, making it of interest in both botanical and medicinal studies.
