Ballota Alba Var. Borealis

Ballota alba var. borealis is a rare and lesser-known flowering plant native to the northern regions of Europe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodlands and rocky slopes. This variant of Ballota alba is distinguished by its pale blue flowers and unique leaf structure. Despite its beauty, it remains underdocumented in botanical studies. Understanding this plant can contribute to the conservation of biodiversity in its native habitats.
