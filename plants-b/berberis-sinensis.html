Berberis sinensis

Berberis sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in landscaping.

Berberis sinensis also contains berberine, a compound with potential medicinal properties.
