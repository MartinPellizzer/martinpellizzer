Balbisia miniata

Balbisia miniata, commonly known as the miniature balsam.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Balbisia miniata is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and compact growth habit.
