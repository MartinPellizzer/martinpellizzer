Buddleja carnea

Buddleja carnea, commonly known as the rose bush buddleia, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Buddleja carnea is known for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
