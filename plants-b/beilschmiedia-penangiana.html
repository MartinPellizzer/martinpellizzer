Beilschmiedia penangiana

Beilschmiedia penangiana, commonly known as the Penang beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
