Begonia obsolescens

Begonia obsolescens, commonly known as the slender begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the spring and summer months. Its foliage is known for its attractive appearance and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Begonia obsolescens prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
