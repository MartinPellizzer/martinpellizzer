Baccharis × volckmannii

Baccharis × volckmannii, commonly known as the hybrid baccharis, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
