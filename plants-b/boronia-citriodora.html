Boronia citriodora

Boronia citriodora, commonly known as lemon boronia, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in coastal and heathland environments.

Boronia citriodora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
