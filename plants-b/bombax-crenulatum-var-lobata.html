Bombax crenulatum var. lobata

Bombax crenulatum var. lobata, commonly known as the lobate silk cotton tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fluffy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a cotton-like seed capsule that contains numerous seeds surrounded by a silky, fibrous material. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and its historical use in traditional medicine.
