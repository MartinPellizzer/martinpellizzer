Baccharoides adoensis var. kotschyana

Baccharoides adoensis var. kotschyana, commonly known as the Kotschyan's baccharoides, is a shrub native to the arid regions of northern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh, dry environments, with thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. It is often found in rocky soils and is resistant to drought.

This species plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
