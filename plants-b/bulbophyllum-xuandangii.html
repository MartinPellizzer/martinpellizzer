Bulbophyllum xuandangii

Bulbophyllum xuandangii, commonly known as the Xuandang orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, intricate flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its pseudobulbs are thick and serve as water storage structures, adapting it to its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
