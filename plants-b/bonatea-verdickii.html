Bonatea verdickii

Bonatea verdickii, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, which are adorned with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. Bonatea verdickii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
