Belmontia divaricata

Belmontia divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Belmontia divaricata thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and unique growth habit.
