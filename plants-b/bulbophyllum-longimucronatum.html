Bulbophyllum longimucronatum

Bulbophyllum longimucronatum, commonly known as the long-spurred bulbophyllum, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated spur that can reach several centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish in cultivation.
