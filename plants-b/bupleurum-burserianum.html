Bupleurum burserianum

Bupleurum burserianum, commonly known as the Burserianum angelica, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Its small white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, dry fruits.

Bupleurum burserianum is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating digestive and respiratory ailments.
