Bletia attenuata

Bletia attenuata, commonly known as the slender bletia, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are small and usually pale green or white.

Bletia attenuata is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
