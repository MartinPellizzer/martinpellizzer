Bulbophyllum werneri

Bulbophyllum werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Bulbophyllum werneri is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
