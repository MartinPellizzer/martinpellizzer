Begonia × phyllomaniaca

Begonia × phyllomaniaca, commonly known as the everblushing begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits striking botanical features.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces showy, often double blooms that are highly ornamental. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

This species is well-suited for container gardening and thrives in partial shade with consistent moisture.
