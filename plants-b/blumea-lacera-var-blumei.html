Blumea lacera var. blumei

Blumea lacera var. blumei, commonly known as the slender blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and often hairy, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
