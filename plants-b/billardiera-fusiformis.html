Billardiera fusiformis

Billardiera fusiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

Billardiera fusiformis is often found in open forests and along stream banks, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
