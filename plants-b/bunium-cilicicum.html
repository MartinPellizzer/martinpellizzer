Bunium cilicicum

Bunium cilicicum, commonly known as the Cilician fennel, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is native to the eastern Mediterranean region, including parts of Turkey and Syria. The plant features a taproot system and produces yellow flowers in umbrella-like clusters, typical of the carrot family. Its leaves are fern-like and often aromatic, with a mild fennel-like scent.

It is often found growing in dry, rocky soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
