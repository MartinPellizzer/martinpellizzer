Barbacenia serracabralea

Barbacenia serracabralea, commonly known as the serrated-bracted barbacenia, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals that contrast with its dark red calyx.

Barbacenia serracabralea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
