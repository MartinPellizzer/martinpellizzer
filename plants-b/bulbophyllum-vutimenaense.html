Bulbophyllum vutimenaense

Bulbophyllum vutimenaense, commonly known as the Vutimena Orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions for successful cultivation.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs.
