Byrsella misasii

Byrsella misasii, commonly known as the Misasii orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers that display a unique combination of colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its botanical features include lance-shaped leaves and a slender stem that supports its delicate, showy blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
