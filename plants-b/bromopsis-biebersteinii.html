Bromopsis biebersteinii

Bromopsis biebersteinii, commonly known as the Alpine feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and delicate, fluffy seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and high-altitude environments. Bromopsis biebersteinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
