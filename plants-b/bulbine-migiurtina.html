Bulbine migiurtina

Bulbine migiurtina, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in a layer of white, waxy hair. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to store water in its stems allows it to survive in arid conditions.
