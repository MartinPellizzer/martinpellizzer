Berkheya bergiana

Berkheya bergiana, commonly known as the Bergian yellowwood, is a South African shrub belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spiky bracts beneath its flowering heads. It is often found in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.

Berkheya bergiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
