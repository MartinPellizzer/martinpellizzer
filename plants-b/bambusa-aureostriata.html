Bambusa aureostriata

Bambusa aureostriata, commonly known as the golden stripe bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo native to southern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that are often striped with a bright yellow color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep green, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Bambusa aureostriata is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic value and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.
