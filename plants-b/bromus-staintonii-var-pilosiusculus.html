Bromus staintonii var. pilosiusculus

Bromus staintonii var. pilosiusculus, commonly known as the silky brome, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fine, hairy leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its fibrous root system helps prevent soil erosion.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in ecological restoration projects.
