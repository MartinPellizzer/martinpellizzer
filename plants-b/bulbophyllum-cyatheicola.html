Bulbophyllum cyatheicola

Bulbophyllum cyatheicola, commonly known as the Cyatheicola orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs. The plant typically produces multiple flowering stems with clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are usually long and sword-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
