Begonia bowerae var. roseflora

Begonia bowerae var. roseflora, commonly known as the rose-flowered begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a rich green, adding to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
