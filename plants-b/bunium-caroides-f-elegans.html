Bunium caroides f. elegans

Bunium caroides f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant fennel, is a cultivated form of the common fennel plant.

It is characterized by its slender, feathery foliage and delicate, licorice-scented flowers. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for herb gardens and ornamental planting.

This variety is prized for its aromatic properties and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
