Blumea hieraciifolia

Blumea hieraciifolia, commonly known as the hieracium blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a rough texture. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Blumea hieraciifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
