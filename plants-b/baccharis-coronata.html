Baccharis coronata

Baccharis coronata, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Baccharis coronata is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
