Begonia foliosa var. australis

Begonia foliosa var. australis, commonly known as the Australian leaf begonia, is a species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
