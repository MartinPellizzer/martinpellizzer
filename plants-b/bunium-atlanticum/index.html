Bunium Atlanticum

Bunium atlanticum is a rare and hardy plant native to the rocky coastal regions of the Atlantic coast. It thrives in harsh, salty environments where few other plants can survive. This small, flowering plant has distinctive white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its unique adaptations allow it to withstand strong winds and high salinity. Despite its resilience, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
