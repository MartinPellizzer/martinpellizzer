Bolbitis tonkinensis

Bolbitis tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a dense arrangement of small, ovate pinnae.

Bolbitis tonkinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
