Besleria pauciflora var. uniflora

Besleria pauciflora var. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flowered besleria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically features dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female parts, and are often concealed within the spathe.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in tropical gardens.
