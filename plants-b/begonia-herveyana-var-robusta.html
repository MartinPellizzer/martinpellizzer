Begonia herveyana var. robusta

Begonia herveyana var. robusta, commonly known as the robust begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is popular among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
