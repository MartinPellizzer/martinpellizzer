Barleria reticulata

Barleria reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in spikes at the ends of the branches.

Barleria reticulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
