Bulbophyllum psychoon

Bulbophyllum psychoon, commonly known as the Psychopomp Orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers that display a range of vibrant colors. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its aerial roots for moisture and nutrients. Its flowers are known for their intricate structure, featuring specialized pollination mechanisms that attract specific insect pollinators.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
