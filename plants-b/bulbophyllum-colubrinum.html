Bulbophyllum colubrinum

Bulbophyllum colubrinum, commonly known as the snake orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, snake-like appearance, with long, narrow pseudobulbs and dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum colubrinum is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
