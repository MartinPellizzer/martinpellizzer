Bonnetia paniculata var. parviflora

Bonnetia paniculata var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered bonnetia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in panicles. Its fruits are follicles that release seeds with tufts of hair, aiding in wind dispersal.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
