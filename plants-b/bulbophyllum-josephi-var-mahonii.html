Bulbophyllum josephi var. mahonii

Bulbophyllum josephi var. mahonii, commonly known as the Mahon's bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually fleshy and dark green, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
