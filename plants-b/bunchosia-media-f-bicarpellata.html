Bunchosia media f. bicarpellata

Bunchosia media f. bicarpellata, commonly known as the two-carpellate bunchosia, is a unique variant of the species Bunchosia media.

It is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Peru. This plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and distinctive fruit morphology. The "bicarpellata" form refers to its specific floral structure, which features two carpels instead of the typical three.

It is of interest to botanists due to its morphological variation and potential ecological significance.
