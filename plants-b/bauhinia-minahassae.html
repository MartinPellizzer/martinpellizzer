Bauhinia minahassae

Bauhinia minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, pale pink or white flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia minahassae is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
