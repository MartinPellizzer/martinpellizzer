Bulbophyllum calyptratum

Bulbophyllum calyptratum, commonly known as the veined lady's tresses, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulb-like structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its tropical habitat. The plant produces a dense, dark green inflorescence with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that have a distinct veined pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and delicate blooms make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
