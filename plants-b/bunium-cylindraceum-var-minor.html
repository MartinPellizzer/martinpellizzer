Bunium cylindraceum var. minor

Bunium cylindraceum var. minor, commonly known as the lesser narrow-leaved fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, cylindrical stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its seeds are small and have a distinctive, slightly bitter flavor.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides.
