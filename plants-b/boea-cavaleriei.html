Boea cavaleriei

Boea cavaleriei, commonly known as the silver leaf vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped, silvery-green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
