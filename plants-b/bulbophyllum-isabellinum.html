Bulbophyllum isabellinum

Bulbophyllum isabellinum, commonly known as the Isabelline Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth.

Its rhizomes and pseudobulbs are characteristic of the genus, supporting its unique growth pattern.
