Begonia mimikaensis

Begonia mimikaensis, commonly known as the Mimika begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia mimikaensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
