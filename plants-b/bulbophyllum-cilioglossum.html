Bulbophyllum cilioglossum

Bulbophyllum cilioglossum, commonly known as the feather-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, feather-like lip that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually small and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and orange.

Bulbophyllum cilioglossum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
