Begonia per-dusenii

Begonia per-dusenii, commonly known as the Perdusenii begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial species, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its foliage is often variegated, with shades of green, white, and silver, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers, which are usually pink or red, bloom in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
