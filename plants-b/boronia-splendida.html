Boronia splendida

Boronia splendida, commonly known as the Splendid Boronia, is an evergreen shrub native to the wet tropical regions of northern Australia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a humid, warm climate to flourish.

Boronia splendida is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and unique floral scent.
