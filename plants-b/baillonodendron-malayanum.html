Baillonodendron malayanum

Baillonodendron malayanum, commonly known as the Malayan banyan, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and a broad, spreading canopy. The plant belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The tree produces small, clustered flowers followed by fig-like fruits that are important for local wildlife.
