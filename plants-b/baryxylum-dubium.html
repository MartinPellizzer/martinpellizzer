Baryxylum dubium

Baryxylum dubium, commonly known as the rare desert shrub, is a perennial plant native to arid regions of central Asia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are adapted to minimize water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its root system is extensive and deep, allowing it to access groundwater in dry soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Baryxylum dubium is considered a vulnerable species.
