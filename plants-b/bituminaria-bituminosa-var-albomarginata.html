Bituminaria bituminosa var. albomarginata

Bituminaria bituminosa var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin bituminous thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its grayish-green, fuzzy leaves that have distinctive white margins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers in dense, spherical clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in horticulture and ecological studies.
