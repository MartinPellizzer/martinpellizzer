Bulbophyllum membranifolium

Bulbophyllum membranifolium, commonly known as the membrane-leaf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features linear, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a thin, waxy layer. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
