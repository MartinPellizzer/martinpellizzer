Banisteria fruticosa

Banisteria fruticosa, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Banisteria fruticosa is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
