Bulbophyllum bakhuizenii

Bulbophyllum bakhuizenii, commonly known as the Bakhuizen's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other plants in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of multiple small, clustered flowers that can range in color from white to yellow and pink.

Bulbophyllum bakhuizenii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
