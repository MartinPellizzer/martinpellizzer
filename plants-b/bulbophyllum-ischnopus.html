Bulbophyllum ischnopus

Bulbophyllum ischnopus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts, though it remains relatively rare in the horticultural trade.
