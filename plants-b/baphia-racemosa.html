Baphia racemosa

Baphia racemosa, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in racemes, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Baphia racemosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in urban settings.
