Barbarea altaica

Barbarea altaica, commonly known as the alpine yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically lobed or divided. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

Barbarea altaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
