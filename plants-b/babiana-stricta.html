Babiana stricta

Babiana stricta, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescence of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces underground bulbs that store nutrients for future growth. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Babiana stricta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
