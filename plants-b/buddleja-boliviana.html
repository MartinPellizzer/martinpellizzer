Buddleja boliviana

Buddleja boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian butterfly bush, is a shrub native to the Andes of South America.

It features striking, cone-shaped flower clusters that range in color from purple to pink, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Buddleja boliviana is also valued for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
