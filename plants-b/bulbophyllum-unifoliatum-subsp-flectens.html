Bulbophyllum unifoliatum subsp. flectens

Bulbophyllum unifoliatum subsp.

flectens, commonly known as the flectens orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia. It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid, shaded environments.

The plant features a single, fleshy pseudobulb with a single leaf and a raceme of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in some regions.
