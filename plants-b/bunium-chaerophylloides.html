Bunium chaerophylloides

Bunium chaerophylloides, commonly known as the meadow fennel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and yellow umbels of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring temperate climates. It produces small, aromatic seeds that are dispersed by wind.
