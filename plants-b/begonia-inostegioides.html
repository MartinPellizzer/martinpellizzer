Begonia inostegioides

Begonia inostegioides, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique foliage and modest blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
