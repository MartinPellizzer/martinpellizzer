Bunchosia pseudonitida

Bunchosia pseudonitida, commonly known as the silverleaf bunchosia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds.

Bunchosia pseudonitida is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
