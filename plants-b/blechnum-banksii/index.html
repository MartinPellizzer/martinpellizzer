Blechnum Banksii

Banks' spleenwort, scientifically known as Blechnum banksii, is a unique fern species native to New Zealand. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks. This plant is notable for its delicate, lance-shaped fronds and its ability to survive in harsh conditions. Despite its small size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Its discovery and study continue to fascinate botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
