Bulbophyllum reflexipetalum

Bulbophyllum reflexipetalum, commonly known as the reflexed lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers with reflexed petals and a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and leaves that are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
