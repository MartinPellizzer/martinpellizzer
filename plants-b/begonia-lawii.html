Begonia lawii

Begonia lawii, commonly known as the Malayan begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia lawii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
