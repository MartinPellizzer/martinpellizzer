Begonia metallicolor

Begonia metallicolor, commonly known as the silverleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green foliage that reflects light, giving it a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia metallicolor is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
