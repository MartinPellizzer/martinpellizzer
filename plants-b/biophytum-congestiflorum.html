Biophytum congestiflorum

Biophytum congestiflorum, commonly known as the clustered leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often clustered at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
