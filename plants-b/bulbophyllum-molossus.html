Bulbophyllum molossus

Bulbophyllum molossus, commonly known as the Molossus orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and long, slender leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized orchid collections.
