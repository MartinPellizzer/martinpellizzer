Blandfordia grandiflora var. backhousii

Blandfordia grandiflora var. backhousii, commonly known as the Backhouse's lily, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a cool, humid environment to flourish.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
