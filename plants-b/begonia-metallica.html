Begonia metallica

Begonia metallica, commonly known as the silver begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, metallic-green foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warm seasons. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
