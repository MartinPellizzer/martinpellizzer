Buraeavia

Buraeavia, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, globular stem covered in dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant's leaves are modified into spines, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Buraeavia thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
