Bulbophyllum mobilifilum

Bulbophyllum mobilifilum, commonly known as the mobile filaments orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
