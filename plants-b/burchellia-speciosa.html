Burchellia speciosa

Burchellia speciosa, commonly known as the yellow burchellia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Burchellia speciosa is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
