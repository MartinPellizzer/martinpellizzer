Bunias americana

Bunias americana, commonly known as American mustards, is a biennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fuzzy texture. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, followed by long, slender seed pods.

Bunias americana is known for its peppery flavor and is sometimes used in forage or as a wild edible.
