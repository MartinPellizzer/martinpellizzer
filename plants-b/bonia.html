Bonia

Bonia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking white or pink spathes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bonia is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance indoor environments.
