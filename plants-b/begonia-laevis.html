Begonia laevis

Begonia laevis, commonly known as the smooth begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Begonia laevis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
