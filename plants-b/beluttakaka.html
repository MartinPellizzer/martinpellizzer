Beluttakaka

Beluttakaka, commonly known as the "ghost plant," is a rare and elusive species native to the dense, humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with delicate, translucent leaves that appear to shimmer in the dappled sunlight. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked due to their subtle coloration. Beluttakaka thrives in shaded, moist environments and is highly sensitive to environmental changes.
