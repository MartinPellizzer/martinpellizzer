Bulbostylis elegans

Bulbostylis elegans, commonly known as the elegant bulbostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its compact, grass-like appearance with slender leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in wet or swampy habitats, often forming dense mats.

Due to its delicate structure and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
