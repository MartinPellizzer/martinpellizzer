Bunium elegans var. latilobum

Bunium elegans var. latilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it adaptable to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
