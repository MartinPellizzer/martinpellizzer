Biophytum albizzioides

Biophytum albizzioides, commonly known as the silverleaf balsam, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in spring. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Biophytum albizzioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
