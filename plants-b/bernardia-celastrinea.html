Bernardia celastrinea

Bernardia celastrinea, commonly known as the purple flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its fruits are woody capsules that split open to release small, winged seeds.

Bernardia celastrinea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.
