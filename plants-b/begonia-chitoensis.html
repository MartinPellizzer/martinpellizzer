Begonia chitoensis

Begonia chitoensis, commonly known as the Chitoensis Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun depending on the climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
