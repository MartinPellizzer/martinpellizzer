Bikukulla eximia

Bikukulla eximia, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
