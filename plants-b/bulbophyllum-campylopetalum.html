Bulbophyllum campylopetalum

Bulbophyllum campylopetalum, commonly known as the twisted-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, twisted lip-shaped petal. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a striking coloration that ranges from white to yellow.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for collectors and horticulturists interested in rare orchids.
