Bulbophyllum fuscoides

Bulbophyllum fuscoides, commonly known as the dark brown orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum fuscoides is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
