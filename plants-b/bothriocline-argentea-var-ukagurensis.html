Bothriocline argentea var. ukagurensis

Bothriocline argentea var. ukagurensis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow-centered white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, which contribute to its distinctive appearance.

The flowers bloom in spring and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
