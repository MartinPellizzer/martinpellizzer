Barbacenia rubrovirens

Barbacenia rubrovirens, commonly known as the red-violet barbacenia, is a member of the Malvaceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet and red flowers, which are often clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a hairy texture, and the stems are often hairy as well.

Barbacenia rubrovirens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
