Brasilicactus alacriportanus

Brasilicactus alacriportanus, commonly known as the Alacriportan Cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, columnar stems that store water for arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its desert habitat. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and providing protection from herbivores.

This cactus thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic landscapes.
