Buddleja ramboi

Buddleja ramboi, commonly known as the flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, panicle-like inflorescences that display vibrant red to orange hues, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Buddleja ramboi is known for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
