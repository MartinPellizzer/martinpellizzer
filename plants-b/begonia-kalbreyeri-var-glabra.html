Begonia kalbreyeri var. glabra

Begonia kalbreyeri var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth begonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.
