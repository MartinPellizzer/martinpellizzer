Besleria microphylla

Besleria microphylla, commonly known as the miniature flame plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red tubular flowers, which are highly attractive to hummingbirds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and tropical gardens.
