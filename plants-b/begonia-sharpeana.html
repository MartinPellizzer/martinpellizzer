Begonia sharpeana

Begonia sharpeana, commonly known as the Sharp's begonia, is a tropical plant native to the island of Java.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with contrasting veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, typically blooming in the summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Begonia sharpeana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
