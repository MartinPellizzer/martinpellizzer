Begonia silletensis subsp. mengyangensis

Begonia silletensis subsp.

mengyangensis, commonly known as the Mengyang begonia, is a species native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive foliage.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the summer months.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
