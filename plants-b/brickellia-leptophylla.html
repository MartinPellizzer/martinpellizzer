Brickellia leptophylla

Brickellia leptophylla, commonly known as slenderleaf brickellia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.

Brickellia leptophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
