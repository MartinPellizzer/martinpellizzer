Berchemia pycnantha

Berchemia pycnantha, commonly known as the silver cotoneaster, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are a food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice in horticulture.
