Bromelica harfordii

Bromelica harfordii, commonly known as Harford's pineapple.

It is a bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a rosette of sword-shaped, dark green leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, powdery bloom. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the center of the leaf rosette.

Bromelica harfordii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.
