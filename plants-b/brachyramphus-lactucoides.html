Brachyramphus lactucoides

Brachyramphus lactucoides, commonly known as the milkweed bug, is a species of plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, herbaceous growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
