Begonia cavaleriei

Begonia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and delicate blooms, which add a touch of elegance to shaded landscapes.

Begonia cavaleriei is also appreciated for its ability to tolerate cool temperatures, making it a versatile choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
