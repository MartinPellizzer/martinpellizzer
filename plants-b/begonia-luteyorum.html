Begonia luteyorum

Begonia luteyorum, commonly known as the yellow begonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
