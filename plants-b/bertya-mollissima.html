Bertya mollissima

Bertya mollissima, commonly known as the silky bertya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, soft foliage and small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Bertya mollissima is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
