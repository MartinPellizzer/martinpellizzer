Batrachium circinatum var. subrigidum

Batrachium circinatum var. subrigidum, commonly known as the rigid bladderwort, is a rare aquatic plant native to specific regions of North America.

The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. Its leaves are short and often clustered at the base, with the bladders arranged in pairs along the stem.
