Bulbophyllum henshallii

Bulbophyllum henshallii, commonly known as the Henshall's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive lip. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum henshallii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
