Bulbophyllum gemma-reginae

Bulbophyllum gemma-reginae, commonly known as the Queen's Gem Bulbophyllum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high rainfall.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
