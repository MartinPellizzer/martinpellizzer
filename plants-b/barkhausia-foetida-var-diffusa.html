Barkhausia foetida var. diffusa

Barkhausia foetida var. diffusa, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive sweet aroma. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky down. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fragrance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
