Bihai psittacorum

Bihai psittacorum, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in long, drooping clusters. The tree has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a street tree.
