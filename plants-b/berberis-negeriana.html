Berberis negeriana

Berberis negeriana, commonly known as the Philippine barberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and seasonal blooms.
