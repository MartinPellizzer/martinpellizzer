Byrsonima fluminensis

Byrsonima fluminensis, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or araçá-do-cerrado, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves.
