Bulbophyllum systenochilum

Bulbophyllum systenochilum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, where it grows in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
