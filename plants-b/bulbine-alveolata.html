Bulbine alveolata

Bulbine alveolata, commonly known as the African rock orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its adaptability to arid conditions makes it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
