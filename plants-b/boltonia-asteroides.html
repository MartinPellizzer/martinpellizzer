Boltonia asteroides

Boltonia asteroides, commonly known as the white coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a prominent yellow center, blooming from late summer to early fall. The plant has a tall, slender stem covered in soft, hairy foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Boltonia asteroides is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
