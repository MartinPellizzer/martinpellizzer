Balanocarpus bancanus

Balanocarpus bancanus, commonly known as the Philippine ebony, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.
