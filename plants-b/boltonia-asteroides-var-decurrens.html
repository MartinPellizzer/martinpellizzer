Boltonia asteroides var. decurrens

Boltonia asteroides var. decurrens, commonly known as the narrowleaf boltonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, upright stems with large, daisy-like composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Boltonia asteroides var. decurrens is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
