Begonia wuzhishanensis

Begonia wuzhishanensis, commonly known as the Wuzhishan begonia, is a rare and unique species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a mix of green and reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with prominent, showy bracts. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is typically found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
