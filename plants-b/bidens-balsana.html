Bidens balsana

Bidens balsana, commonly known as yellow beggarlice, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow flowers with ray florets and a central disk, typical of the family. Its leaves are usually alternate and often have a rough texture.

Bidens balsana is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.
