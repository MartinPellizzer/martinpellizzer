Brickellia microphylla var. microphylla

Brickellia microphylla var. microphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf brickellbush, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features small, elliptical to lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for native gardens and landscapes.
