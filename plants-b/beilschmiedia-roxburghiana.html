Beilschmiedia roxburghiana

Beilschmiedia roxburghiana, commonly known as the Indian wax tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed.
