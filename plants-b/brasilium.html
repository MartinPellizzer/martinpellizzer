Brasilium

Brasilium, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It features vibrant, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Brasilium is popular in horticulture for its striking appearance and ability to add color to indoor or outdoor spaces.
