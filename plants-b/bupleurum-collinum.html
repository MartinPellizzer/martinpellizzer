Bupleurum collinum

Bupleurum collinum, commonly known as mountain celery, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Bupleurum collinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens due to its delicate foliage and flowering habit.
