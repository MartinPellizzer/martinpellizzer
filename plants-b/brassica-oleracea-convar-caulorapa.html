Brassica oleracea convar. caulorapa

Brassica oleracea convar. caulorapa, commonly known as kohlrabi, is a biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its swollen, edible stem that resembles a root vegetable, which is typically harvested before the plant flowers. The plant has alternate, pinnately lobed leaves that are usually dark green and covered in fine hairs. Kohlrabi thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is a versatile crop that can be grown in both spring and fall seasons.
