Besleria divaricata

Besleria divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features striking, dark green, strap-like leaves that are often curled and have a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting mix.

Besleria divaricata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
