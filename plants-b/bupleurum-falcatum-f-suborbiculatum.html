Bupleurum falcatum f. suborbiculatum

Bupleurum falcatum f. suborbiculatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved bupleurum, is a variant of the common bupleurum species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are often broader than the typical form. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in loose racemes.

This subspecies is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
