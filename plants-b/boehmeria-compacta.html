Boehmeria compacta

Boehmeria compacta, commonly known as Chinese nettle or broadleaf nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes many stinging plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a dense covering of fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. Boehmeria compacta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its fibrous stems are also valued for their potential in textile production.
