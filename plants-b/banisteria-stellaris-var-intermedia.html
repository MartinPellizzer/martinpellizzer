Banisteria stellaris var. intermedia

Banisteria stellaris var. intermedia, commonly known as the purple star vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant has woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
