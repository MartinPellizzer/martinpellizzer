Barleria glandulostamina subsp. glandulostamina

Barleria glandulostamina subsp.

glandulostamina, commonly known as the glandular-staminated barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features slender, erect stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy.

The flowers are typically arranged in dense, terminal spikes, with tubular corollas that are pink to purple in color. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
