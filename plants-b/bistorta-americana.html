Bistorta americana

Bistorta americana, commonly known as American bistort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features upright, somewhat hairy stems that are often reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Bistorta americana is typically found in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.
