Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. weiminianum

Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. weiminianum, commonly known as the Weiminian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns, making it a popular choice for collectors.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
