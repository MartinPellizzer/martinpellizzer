Bromus madritensis var. victorinii

Bromus madritensis var. victorinii, commonly known as the Victorinii brome, is a grass species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It typically thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
