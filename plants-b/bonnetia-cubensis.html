Bonnetia cubensis

Bonnetia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban bonnetia, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, white to yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, often accompanied by glandular hairs. Its leaves are usually opposite and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Bonnetia cubensis is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils.
