Bidens hirta

Bidens hirta, commonly known as hairy beggarlice, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming from summer to fall. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Bidens hirta is known for its ability to spread rapidly through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
