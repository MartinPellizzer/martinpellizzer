Bulbophyllum microrhombos

Bulbophyllum microrhombos, commonly known as the tiny rhombus orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces intricate, rhombus-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive, intricate pattern.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
