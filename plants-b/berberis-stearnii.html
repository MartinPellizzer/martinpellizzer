Berberis stearnii

Berberis stearnii, commonly known as the Mexican barberry, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often bitter. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Berberis stearnii is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
