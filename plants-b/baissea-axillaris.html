Baissea axillaris

Baissea axillaris, commonly known as the rock fig, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, edible fruits. The rock fig is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
