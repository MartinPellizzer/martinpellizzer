Barbacenia wentzeliana var. rhodesica

Barbacenia wentzeliana var. rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodesian barbacenia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange.
