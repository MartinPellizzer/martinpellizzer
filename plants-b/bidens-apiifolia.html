Bidens apiifolia

Bidens apiifolia, commonly known as the curly top ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features hairy, fern-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disk. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Bidens apiifolia is known for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
