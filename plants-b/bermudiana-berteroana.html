Bermudiana berteroana

Bermudiana berteroana, commonly known as the Bermuda grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. This plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils.

Bermudiana berteroana is known for its resilience and ability to form dense mats, making it a common weed in disturbed habitats.
