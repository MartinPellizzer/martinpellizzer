Bulbophyllum crassissimum

Bulbophyllum crassissimum, commonly known as the thick-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in humid tropical climates. Its flowers are small and usually green or yellowish, with a subtle fragrance.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique leaf structure and ability to thrive in specific ecological conditions.
