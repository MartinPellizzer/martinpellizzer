Bupleurum stellatum proles latifolium

Bupleurum stellatum proles latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved angel's trumpets, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and distinctive foliage.
