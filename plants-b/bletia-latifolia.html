Bletia latifolia

Bletia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bletia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Bletia latifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
