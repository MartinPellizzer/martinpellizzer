Boswellia ruspoliana

Boswellia ruspoliana, commonly known as the Socotra cypress, is a unique coniferous tree native to the Socotra Archipelago.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like crown and scale-like leaves. The plant is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of its native habitat. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies.
