Burmeistera ceratocarpa

Burmeistera ceratocarpa, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

Burmeistera ceratocarpa is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
