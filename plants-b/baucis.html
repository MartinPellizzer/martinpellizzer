Baucis

Baucis, commonly known as the baucis plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Baucis within the family Apiaceae, which includes parsley and carrots. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has finely divided, fern-like leaves. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance.

Baucis is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
