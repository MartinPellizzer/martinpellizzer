Berkheya uniflora

Berkheya uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower berkheya, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Berkheya uniflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
