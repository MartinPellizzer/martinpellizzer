Begonia sambiranensis

Begonia sambiranensis, commonly known as the Sambiranensis begonia, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can vary in color from deep green to reddish tones. Its flowers are usually red or pink, with five petals and a prominent central boss.

This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
