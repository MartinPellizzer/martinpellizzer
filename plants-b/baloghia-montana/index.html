Baloghia Montana

Baloghia tree, scientifically known as Baloghia montana, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa. This evergreen tree is renowned for its large, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red and orange. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests and riverine habitats. The tree's unique appearance and ecological significance make it a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts. Its ornamental value has also led to its cultivation in gardens and parks worldwide.
