Brossaea nummulariodes

Brossaea nummulariodes, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, round, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil and full sun.

Brossaea nummulariodes is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
