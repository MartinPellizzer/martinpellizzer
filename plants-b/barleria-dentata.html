Barleria dentata

Barleria dentata, commonly known as the toothed barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or red, arranged in dense spikes.

Barleria dentata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
