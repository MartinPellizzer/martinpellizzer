Boerhavia diffusa var. ehrenbergii

Boerhavia diffusa var. ehrenbergii, commonly known as marshmallow root or red root, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, hairy stems, and small pink to purple flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.
