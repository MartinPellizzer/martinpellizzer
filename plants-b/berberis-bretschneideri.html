Berberis bretschneideri

Berberis bretschneideri, commonly known as Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it popular in gardens and landscapes.
