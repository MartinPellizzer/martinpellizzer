Berkheya jardineana

Berkheya jardineana, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a creeping growth habit with soft, hairy stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the flowers bloom in early spring.

Berkheya jardineana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
