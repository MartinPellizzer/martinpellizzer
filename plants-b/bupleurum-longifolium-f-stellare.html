Bupleurum longifolium f. stellare

Bupleurum longifolium f. stellare, commonly known as the star-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and lanceolate leaves that are often tinged with silver on the underside. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early summer. Its distinctive star-shaped leaf arrangement gives it its common name and contributes to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
