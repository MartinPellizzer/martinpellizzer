Brunfelsia ramosissima f. laxiflora

Brunfelsia ramosissima f. laxiflora, commonly known as the laxiflora brunfelsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, branched structure and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves that are often aromatic.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
