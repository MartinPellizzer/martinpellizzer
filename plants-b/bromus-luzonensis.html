Bromus luzonensis

Bromus luzonensis, commonly known as Luzon brome, is a grass species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest edges.

Bromus luzonensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
