Barleria venulosa

Barleria venulosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Barleria venulosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
