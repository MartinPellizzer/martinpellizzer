Begonia ningmingensis var. ningmingensis

Begonia ningmingensis var. ningmingensis, commonly known as the Ningming begonia, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
