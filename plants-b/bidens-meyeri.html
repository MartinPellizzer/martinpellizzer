Bidens meyeri

Bidens meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's tickseed, is an annual flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Bidens meyeri is known for its ability to spread rapidly through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
