Bomarea falcata

Bomarea falcata, commonly known as the sickleleaf bomarea, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, Bomarea falcata is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
