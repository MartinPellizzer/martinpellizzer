Buddleja geisseana

Buddleja geisseana, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces long, pendulous clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting a variety of pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Buddleja geisseana is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
