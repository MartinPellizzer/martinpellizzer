Bidens malmei

Bidens malmei, commonly known as the Malme's beggarticks, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, with both ray and disk florets. Its leaves are typically opposite, lance-shaped, and have serrated edges.

Bidens malmei is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in sunny environments.
