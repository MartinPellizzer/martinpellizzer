Browallia americana f. demissa

Browallia americana f. demissa, commonly known as the dwarf browallia, is a compact, flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of purple, blue, and white, blooming from late spring to early fall. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an excellent addition to rock gardens and borders.
