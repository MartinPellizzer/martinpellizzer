Bauhinia brachystachya

Bauhinia brachystachya, commonly known as the small-stemmed bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are often divided into numerous small leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Bauhinia brachystachya is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid or semi-arid conditions.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
