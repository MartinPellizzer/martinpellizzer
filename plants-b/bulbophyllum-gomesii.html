Bulbophyllum gomesii

Bulbophyllum gomesii, commonly known as the Gomes's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and botanists alike.
