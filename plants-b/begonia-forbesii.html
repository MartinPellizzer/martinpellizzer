Begonia forbesii

Begonia forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia forbesii is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
