Bulbocastanum daucoides

Bulbocastanum daucoides, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-blue foliage, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a short, thick stem and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Bulbocastanum daucoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
