Bifaria

Bifaria, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in a single head, surrounded by pale green bracts. Bifaria is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

It has also been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
