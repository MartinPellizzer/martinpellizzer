Bryonopsis affinis

Bryonopsis affinis, commonly known as the broad-leaved moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is a small, mat-forming plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
