Bidens wallichii

Bidens wallichii, commonly known as the Indian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to regions of Asia, including India and China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in spherical heads. Bidens wallichii is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.

It is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
