Benzoin sinense

Benzoin sinense, commonly known as Chinese benzoin, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It produces a fragrant resin that is used in traditional medicine and incense. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its resinous sap is collected from the bark and has a balsamic aroma.

The botanical features of Benzoin sinense make it valuable in both medicinal and aromatic applications.
