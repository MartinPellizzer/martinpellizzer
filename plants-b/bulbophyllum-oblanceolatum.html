Bulbophyllum oblanceolatum

Bulbophyllum oblanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces striking, often fragrant, flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its leaves are typically elongated and narrow, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Bulbophyllum oblanceolatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
