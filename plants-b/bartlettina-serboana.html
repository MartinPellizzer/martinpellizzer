Bartlettina serboana

Bartlettina serboana, commonly known as the Serbian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features slender stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs.

Bartlettina serboana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
