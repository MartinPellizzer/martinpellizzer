Boopis australis var. squarrosa

Boopis australis var. squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa boopis, is a variant of the Australian boopis species.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant characterized by its prostrate stems and dense foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions of Australia.

This plant is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it resilient in dry climates.
