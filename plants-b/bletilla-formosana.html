Bletilla formosana

Bletilla formosana, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, with petals that curl and twist in a unique, spiral pattern. Bletilla formosana prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for cultivation in gardens and botanical collections.
