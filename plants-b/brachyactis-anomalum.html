Brachyactis anomalum

Brachyactis anomalum, commonly known as the bizarre shortleaf plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often asymmetrical leaves. The plant exhibits a variety of leaf shapes, including ovate and lanceolate forms, which contribute to its unusual appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its rare occurrence and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
