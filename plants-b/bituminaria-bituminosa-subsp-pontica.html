Bituminaria bituminosa subsp. pontica

Bituminaria bituminosa subsp.

pontica, commonly known as the Pontic bituminous broom, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and dense, branching structure.

The plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found on slopes and hillsides. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
