Begonia scandens var. cordifolia

Begonia scandens var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf begonia, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deep green, with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and as a decorative foliage plant.
