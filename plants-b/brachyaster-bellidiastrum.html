Brachyaster bellidiastrum

Brachyaster bellidiastrum, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Brachyaster bellidiastrum is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.
