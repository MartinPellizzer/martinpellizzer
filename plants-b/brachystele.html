Brachystele

Brachystele, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the family Cycadaceae.

It is characterized by its short, stout trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves. The plant produces cones, with the male cones being cylindrical and the female cones spherical. Brachystele thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in rocky or well-drained soils.

Its slow growth and ancient lineage make it a fascinating subject in botanical studies.
