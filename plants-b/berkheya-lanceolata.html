Berkheya lanceolata

Berkheya lanceolata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Berkheya lanceolata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant landscaping.
