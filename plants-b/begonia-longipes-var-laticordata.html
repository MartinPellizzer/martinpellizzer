Begonia longipes var. laticordata

Begonia longipes var. laticordata, commonly known as the broad-heart begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically broad and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
