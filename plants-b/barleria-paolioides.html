Barleria paolioides

Barleria paolioides, commonly known as the pink barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant pink to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period throughout the growing season.
