Bomarea venusta var. minor

Bomarea venusta var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf bomarea, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to the humid regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features slender, climbing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive spathe and spadix arrangement.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
