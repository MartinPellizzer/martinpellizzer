Barkhausia divaricata

Barkhausia divaricata, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of white, fluffy flowers. Barkhausia divaricata is well adapted to the fynbos biome and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
