Berteroa incana subsp. stricta

Berteroa incana subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often have a rough texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
