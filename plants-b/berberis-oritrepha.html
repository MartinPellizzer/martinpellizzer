Berberis oritrepha

Berberis oritrepha, commonly known as the African barberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds and some mammals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden planting in suitable climates.
