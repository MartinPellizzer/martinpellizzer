Bulbophyllum dischidiifolium subsp. aberrans

Bulbophyllum dischidiifolium subsp.

aberrans, commonly known as the aberrant shield orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines.

The plant is characterized by its unique, shield-like pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a prominent lip, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
