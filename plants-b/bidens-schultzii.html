Bidens schultzii

Bidens schultzii, commonly known as the yellow beggarticks, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along roadsides.

Bidens schultzii is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
