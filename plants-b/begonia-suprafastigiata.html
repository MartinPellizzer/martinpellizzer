Begonia suprafastigiata

Begonia suprafastigiata, commonly known as the upright begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This species thrives in humid, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as a container plant due to its compact size and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
