Blumea glandulosa var. minor

Blumea glandulosa var. minor, commonly known as the lesser blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical structure supports its role in local ecosystems and potential medicinal uses.
