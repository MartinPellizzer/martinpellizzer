Buddleja stachyoides

Buddleja stachyoides, commonly known as the African butterfly bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Buddleja stachyoides is known for its rapid growth and ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the growing season.
