Bulbophyllum kapitense

Bulbophyllum kapitense, commonly known as the Kapit orchid, is a species of orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often yellow or orange with a contrasting lip, making it a visually striking species.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in primary rainforests.
