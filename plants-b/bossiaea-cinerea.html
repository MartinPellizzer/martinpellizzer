Bossiaea cinerea

Bossiaea cinerea, commonly known as the silver bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pale green leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Bossiaea cinerea is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
