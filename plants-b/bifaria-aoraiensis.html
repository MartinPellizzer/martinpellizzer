Bifaria aoraiensis

Bifaria aoraiensis, commonly known as the Aoraiensis fern, is a unique species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that display a striking coloration, often with a bluish-green hue. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, relying on wind pollination for propagation.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
