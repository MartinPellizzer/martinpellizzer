Biauricula linifolia

Biauricula linifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its root system is fibrous, and it reproduces through seeds or vegetative propagation.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
