Boronia pinnata var. gunnii

Boronia pinnata var. gunnii, commonly known as the Gunns boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is often found in heathlands and open forests. It is a perennial shrub that produces small, clustered flowers, which attract various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
