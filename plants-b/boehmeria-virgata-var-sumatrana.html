Boehmeria virgata var. sumatrana

Boehmeria virgata var. sumatrana, commonly known as Sumatran nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spadices. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
