Babingtonia jucunda

Babingtonia jucunda, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Babingtonia jucunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant seasonal blooms.
