Biscutella bilbilitana

Biscutella bilbilitana, commonly known as the Bilbilitan scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy, with small, blue to purple flowers arranged in a panicle. It blooms from spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Biscutella bilbilitana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
