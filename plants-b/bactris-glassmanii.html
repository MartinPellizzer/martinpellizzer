Bactris glassmanii

Bactris glassmanii, commonly known as the glassmanii palm, is a species of palm native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and a crown of feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear yellowish-white flowers, which are followed by edible, round fruits. Its botanical features make it suitable for ornamental purposes in tropical landscapes.

The species is also valued for its potential in sustainable agriculture due to its fruit yield.
