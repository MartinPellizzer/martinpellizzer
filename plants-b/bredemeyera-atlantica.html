Bredemeyera atlantica

Bredemeyera atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Iberian Peninsula. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine, silky hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring.

Bredemeyera atlantica is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments.
