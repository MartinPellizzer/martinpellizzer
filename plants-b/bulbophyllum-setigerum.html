Bulbophyllum setigerum

Bulbophyllum setigerum, commonly known as the hairy lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with intricate patterns on the lip.

Bulbophyllum setigerum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
