Blumea hamiltonii

Blumea hamiltonii, commonly known as the Chinese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to yellow.
