Banisteria elegans subsp. ovata

Banisteria elegans subsp.

ovata, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that appear in dense clusters, often resembling a shower of petals.

The plant has compound leaves with ovate leaflets and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. Banisteria elegans subsp.

ovata is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
