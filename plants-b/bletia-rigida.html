Bletia rigida

Bletia rigida, commonly known as the rigid bletia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often near streams or in mountainous regions. Its inflorescence consists of several flowers arranged along a single stem, with spurred petals that are distinctive in shape.

Bletia rigida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
