Blepharoglossum palawanense

Blepharoglossum palawanense, commonly known as the Palawan hoya, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
