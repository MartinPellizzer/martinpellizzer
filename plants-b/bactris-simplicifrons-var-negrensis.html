Bactris simplicifrons var. negrensis

Bactris simplicifrons var. negrensis, commonly known as the Negrense palm, is a species of palm native to South America, particularly found in Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces yellow-orange inflorescences, which bear small, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a drupe, typically containing a single seed, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is adapted to tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure to thrive.
