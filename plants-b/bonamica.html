Bonamica

Bonamica, commonly known as the pink fairy tern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features small, delicate pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Bonamica thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.
