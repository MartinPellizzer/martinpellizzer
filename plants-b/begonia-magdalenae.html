Begonia magdalenae

Begonia magdalenae, commonly known as the Magdalena begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia magdalenae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
