Blumea dentata var. rigida

Blumea dentata var. rigida, commonly known as rigid blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its rigid, upright stems and narrow, serrated leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow and are arranged in clusters, making it visually striking.

It is often found in open areas such as forests and grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
