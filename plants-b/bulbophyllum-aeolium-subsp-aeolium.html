Bulbophyllum aeolium subsp. aeolium

Bulbophyllum aeolium subsp.

aeolium, commonly known as the Aeolium orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments.

The plant produces compact, lance-shaped leaves and bears inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.

The species is relatively rare in the wild, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
