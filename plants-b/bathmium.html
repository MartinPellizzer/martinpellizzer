Bathmium

Bathmium, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
