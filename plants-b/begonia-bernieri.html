Begonia bernieri

Begonia bernieri, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia bernieri is a perennial species that can be propagated through stem cuttings or seeds.
