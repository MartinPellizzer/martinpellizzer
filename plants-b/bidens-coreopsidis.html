Bidens coreopsidis

Bidens coreopsidis, commonly known as the yellow beggarlice, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features alternate, heart-shaped leaves with rough textures and serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with prominent central disks, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
