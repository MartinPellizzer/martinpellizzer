Bulbophyllum sulawesii

Bulbophyllum sulawesii, commonly known as the Sulawesi torch orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in racemes. It requires high humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
