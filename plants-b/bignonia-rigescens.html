Bignonia rigescens

Bignonia rigescens, commonly known as the creeping yellow trumpet.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, orange-yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Bignonia rigescens is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil conditions.
