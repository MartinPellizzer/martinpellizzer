Besleria allenii

Besleria allenii, commonly known as the Allen's besleria, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Alocasia genus and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.
