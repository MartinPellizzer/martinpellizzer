Bromelia lingulata

Bromelia lingulata, commonly known as the sword leaf bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. The plant produces a tall flower spike with striking orange or red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Bromelia lingulata is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.

It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.
