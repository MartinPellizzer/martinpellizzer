Brunsvigia burchelliana

Brunsvigia burchelliana, commonly known as the "Firecracker" or "Cape tulip," is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting black or purple throat. The plant produces a tall, erect stem with narrow, grass-like leaves, and its bulb is covered in a fibrous outer layer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to the landscape.
