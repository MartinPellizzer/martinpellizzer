Bletilla yunnanensis

Bletilla yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Bletilla yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
