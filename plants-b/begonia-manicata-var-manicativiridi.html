Begonia manicata var. manicativiridi

Begonia manicata var. manicativiridi, commonly known as the Emerald Begonia, is a striking hybrid begonia originating from the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, waxy leaves that often feature a subtle iridescence, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
