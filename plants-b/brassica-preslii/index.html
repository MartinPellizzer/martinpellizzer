Brassica Preslii

Brassica preslii is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of central Europe. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes well-known species like cabbage and mustard. This plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
