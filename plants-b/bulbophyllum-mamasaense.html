Bulbophyllum mamasaense

Bulbophyllum mamasaense, commonly known as the Mamasa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves and is known for its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
