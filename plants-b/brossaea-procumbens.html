Brossaea procumbens

Brossaea procumbens, commonly known as the dwarf broom.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the western United States. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and green, often covered in fine hairs.

Brossaea procumbens thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.
