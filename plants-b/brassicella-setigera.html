Brassicella setigera

Brassicella setigera, commonly known as the slender mustard, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard species. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Brassicella setigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and other wildlife.
