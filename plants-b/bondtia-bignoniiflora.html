Bondtia bignoniiflora

Bondtia bignoniiflora, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green and sometimes variegated. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow, which bloom in clusters along the vines. These flowers are highly attractive to pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
