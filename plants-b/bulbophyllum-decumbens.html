Bulbophyllum decumbens

Bulbophyllum decumbens, commonly known as the decumbent orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its creeping rhizome and dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
