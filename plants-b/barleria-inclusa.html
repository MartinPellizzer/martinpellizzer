Barleria inclusa

Barleria inclusa, commonly known as the blue barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy blue to purple flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Barleria inclusa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
