Bauhinia raddiana var. brachystachya

Bauhinia raddiana var. brachystachya, commonly known as the small-stemmed bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.

The varietal name 'brachystachya' refers to its shorter inflorescences compared to the typical form of B. raddiana.
