Bobua lucida

Bobua lucida, commonly known as the shining bobua, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
