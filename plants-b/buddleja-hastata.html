Buddleja hastata

Buddleja hastata, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Buddleja hastata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
