Bulbophyllum stipulaceum

Bulbophyllum stipulaceum, commonly known as the Stipulate Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with a fine, velvety texture.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
