Belostemma yunnanense

Belostemma yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

Belostemma yunnanense plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small insects.
