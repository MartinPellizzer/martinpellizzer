Buddleja interrupta

Buddleja interrupta, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Buddleja interrupta is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
