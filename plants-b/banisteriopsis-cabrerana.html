Banisteriopsis cabrerana

Banisteriopsis cabrerana, commonly known as the pink ant plant, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

The plant belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is known for its ability to form symbiotic relationships with ants, providing them with shelter in exchange for waste nutrients. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
