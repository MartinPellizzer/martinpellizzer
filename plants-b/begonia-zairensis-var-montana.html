Begonia zairensis var. montana

Begonia zairensis var. montana, commonly known as the mountain begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the high altitudes of the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy texture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in cultivation.
