Bhesa xiei

Bhesa xiei, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservatories.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Bhesa xiei is now considered endangered and is protected in several countries.
