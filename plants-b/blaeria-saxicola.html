Blaeria saxicola

Blaeria saxicola, commonly known as the rock bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibacillaceae.

It is characterized by its succulent, bladder-like structures that help it float and trap small aquatic organisms. Blaeria saxicola plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and contributing to nutrient cycling.
