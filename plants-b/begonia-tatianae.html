Begonia tatianae

Begonia tatianae, commonly known as the Taitiana begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Tahiti.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural range, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare plants.
