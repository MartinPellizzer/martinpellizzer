Boronia inconspicua

Boronia inconspicua, commonly known as the slender boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. Boronia inconspicua is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract native pollinators.
