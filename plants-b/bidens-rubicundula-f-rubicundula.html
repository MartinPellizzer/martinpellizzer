Bidens rubicundula f. rubicundula

Bidens rubicundula f. rubicundula, commonly known as the redstem ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive red stems and yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the flowers bloom in late summer to early fall.

It serves as a food source for various pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
