Bactris longipes var. exilis

Bactris longipes var. exilis, commonly known as the slender palm or slender bactris, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stem and relatively small, clustered leaves.
