Berzelia alopecuroides

Berzelia alopecuroides, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain a single seed. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
