Berberis microtricha

Berberis microtricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often sour. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh conditions make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
