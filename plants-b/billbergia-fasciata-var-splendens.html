Billbergia fasciata var. splendens

Billbergia fasciata var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green with white or yellow markings. It produces tall, branching inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
