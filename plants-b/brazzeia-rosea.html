Brazzeia rosea

Brazzeia rosea, commonly known as the rose-bellied begonia, is a striking flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green, contrasting beautifully with its floral display. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Brazzeia rosea is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
