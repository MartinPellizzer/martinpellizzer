Bromelia ananas var. vix-spinosa

Bromelia ananas var. vix-spinosa, commonly known as the spiny pineapple bromeliad, is a striking plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of thick, spiny leaves that form a central cup. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with showy, brightly colored flowers, typically in red or orange. Its unique leaf structure helps it collect and store water, making it well adapted to humid, tropical environments.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
