Brachyclados obtusifolius

Brachyclados obtusifolius, commonly known as the blunt-leaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a fibrous root system and hairy stems. The plant features alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are typically clustered in a single head, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds with pappus for wind dispersal.

It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.
