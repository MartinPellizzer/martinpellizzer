Bulbophyllum gracillimum

Bulbophyllum gracillimum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
