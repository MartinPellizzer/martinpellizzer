Beilschmiedia roxburghiana var. fagifolia

Beilschmiedia roxburghiana var. fagifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing one or two seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forests.
