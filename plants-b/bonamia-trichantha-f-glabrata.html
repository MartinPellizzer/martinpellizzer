Bonamia trichantha f. glabrata

Bonamia trichantha f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bonamia, is a variant of the Bonamia trichantha species, characterized by its reduced pubescence.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae, which includes various species with showy inflorescences. The plant typically features erect stems and lanceolate leaves, with small, clustered flowers that are often pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

This form is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of environmental conditions.
