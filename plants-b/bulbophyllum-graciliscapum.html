Bulbophyllum graciliscapum

Bulbophyllum graciliscapum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces delicate, linear leaves. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a cluster of small, pale green to white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology makes it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
