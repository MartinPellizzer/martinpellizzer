Bulbophyllum whiteanum

Bulbophyllum whiteanum, commonly known as the White's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers are usually small and colorful, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators like bees and moths.
