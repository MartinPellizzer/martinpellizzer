Begonia neoperrieri

Begonia neoperrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia neoperrieri is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
