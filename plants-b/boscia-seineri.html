Boscia seineri

Boscia seineri, commonly known as the red boscia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, fissured bark that often appears reddish or brownish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by woody, spherical fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a distinctive waxy coating.

Boscia seineri is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
