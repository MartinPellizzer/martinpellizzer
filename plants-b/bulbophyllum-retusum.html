Bulbophyllum retusum

Bulbophyllum retusum, commonly known as the Straight-leafed Pterostylis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces a single, slender spike bearing a single, pale green to yellowish flower. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum retusum is appreciated for its striking floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
