Bletia anceps var. barkeriana

Bletia anceps var. barkeriana, commonly known as the Barker's bletia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, often found in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant produces lanceolate, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically pale green to white, with a distinctive spurred lip and a showy, fragrant appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
