Begonia sellowii

Begonia sellowii, commonly known as the Sellow's begonia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia sellowii is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
