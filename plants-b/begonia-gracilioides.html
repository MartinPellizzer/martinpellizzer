Begonia gracilioides

Begonia gracilioides, commonly known as the slender begonia, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This species is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
