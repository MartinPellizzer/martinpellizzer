Bauhinia hassleriana f. acuminata

Bauhinia hassleriana f. acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-pointed bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
