Boechera pulchra subsp. pallens

Boechera pulchra subsp.

pallens, commonly known as the Sierra stonecress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits.
