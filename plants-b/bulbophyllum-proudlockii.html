Bulbophyllum proudlockii

Bulbophyllum proudlockii, commonly known as the Pride's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically borne on tall, slender inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
