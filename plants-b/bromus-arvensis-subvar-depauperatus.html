Bromus arvensis subvar. depauperatus

Bromus arvensis subvar. depauperatus, commonly known as the slender downy brome, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Due to its adaptability, it can become invasive in certain environments.
