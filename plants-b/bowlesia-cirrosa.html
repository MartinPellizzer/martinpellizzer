Bowlesia cirrosa

Bowlesia cirrosa, commonly known as the woolly bowlesia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Bowlesia cirrosa is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts.
