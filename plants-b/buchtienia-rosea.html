Buchtienia rosea

Buchtienia rosea, commonly known as the rose bush or rose plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin, while the fruits are small, red, and aggregate in shape. This plant is native to temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
