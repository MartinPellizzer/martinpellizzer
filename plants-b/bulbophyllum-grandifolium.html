Bulbophyllum grandifolium

Bulbophyllum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers and large, broad leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Bulbophyllum grandifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
