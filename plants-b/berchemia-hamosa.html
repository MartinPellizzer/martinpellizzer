Berchemia hamosa

Berchemia hamosa, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has opposite, narrow, and leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Berchemia hamosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
