Badula iteoides

Badula iteoides, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
