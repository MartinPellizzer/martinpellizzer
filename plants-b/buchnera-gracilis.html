Buchnera gracilis

Buchnera gracilis, commonly known as the slender bugwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Buchnera gracilis is part of the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

It is not widely cultivated but can be found in wildflower meadows and along stream banks.
