Bauhinia leptopetala

Bauhinia leptopetala, commonly known as the slender leaf bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are finely divided and give the plant a delicate appearance. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, with a distinctive spurred structure at the base of the petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
