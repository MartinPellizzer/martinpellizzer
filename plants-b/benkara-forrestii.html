Benkara forrestii

Benkara forrestii, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, characterized by its velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and broad, waxy leaves. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including purple, blue, and white, and are often used in ornamental horticulture.

Benkara forrestii prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
