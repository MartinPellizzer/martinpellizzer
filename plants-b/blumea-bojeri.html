Blumea bojeri

Blumea bojeri, commonly known as the white blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered with fine hairs.

The flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, making it a notable ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
