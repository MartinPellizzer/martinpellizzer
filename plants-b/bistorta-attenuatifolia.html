Bistorta attenuatifolia

Bistorta attenuatifolia, commonly known as the slender bistort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Bistorta attenuatifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in its native environment.
