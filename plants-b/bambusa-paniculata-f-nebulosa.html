Bambusa paniculata f. nebulosa

Bambusa paniculata f. nebulosa, commonly known as the cloud bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo species characterized by its unique, fluffy, cloud-like inflorescences.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is prized for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings. The plant features slender, green culms that are often used in landscaping for their visual interest. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

While it is not typically cultivated for its edible shoots, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various climates.
