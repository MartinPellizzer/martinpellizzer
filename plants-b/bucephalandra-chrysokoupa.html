Bucephalandra chrysokoupa, commonly known as the golden crown arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

Bucephalandra chrysokoupa is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
