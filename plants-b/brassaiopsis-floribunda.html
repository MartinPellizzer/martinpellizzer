Brassaiopsis floribunda

Brassaiopsis floribunda, commonly known as the golden torch tree, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates.

Brassaiopsis floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to create a bold visual impact in gardens.
