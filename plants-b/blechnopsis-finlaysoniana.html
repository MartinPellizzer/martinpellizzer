Blechnopsis finlaysoniana

Blechnopsis finlaysoniana, commonly known as the Finlayson's harts-tongue fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically broad and lance-shaped. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are often divided into segments, giving it a distinctive, intricate appearance.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
