Burckella erythrophylla

Burckella erythrophylla, commonly known as the red-leaf burckella, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-colored leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
