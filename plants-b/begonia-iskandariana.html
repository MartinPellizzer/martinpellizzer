Begonia iskandariana

Begonia iskandariana, commonly known as the Iskanderan begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant, spurred flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a deep green color and a waxy texture. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
