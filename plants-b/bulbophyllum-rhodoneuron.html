Bulbophyllum rhodoneuron

Bulbophyllum rhodoneuron, commonly known as the pink-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink or red floral bracts. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in a raceme-like structure.

The botanical features of this orchid make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts due to its ornamental value.
