Barjonia triangularis

Barjonia triangularis, commonly known as the triangular barjonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, triangular-shaped leaves that are adapted to store water. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Barjonia triangularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
