Bothriospermum kusnetzowii

Bothriospermum kusnetzowii, commonly known as the yellow-flowered sycamore, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicles, which are typically followed by woody, three-lobed fruits.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.
