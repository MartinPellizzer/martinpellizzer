Bolboschoenus × mariqueter

Bolboschoenus × mariqueter, commonly known as the marsh arrowhead, is a hybrid wetland plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or greenish in color. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and contribute to the ecological health of wetland environments.

Bolboschoenus × mariqueter is also valued for its ornamental appeal in aquatic gardens.
