Bertiera macrocarpa

Bertiera macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited bertiera, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

Bertiera macrocarpa is valued for its ornamental potential and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
