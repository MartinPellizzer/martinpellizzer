Begonia sidolensis

Begonia sidolensis, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features fleshy, succulent-like leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Begonia sidolensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
