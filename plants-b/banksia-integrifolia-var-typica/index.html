Banksia Integrifolia Var. Typica

Banksia integrifolia var. typica is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is known for its distinctive cylindrical flower spikes that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its unique appearance and resilience to salt and wind make it a valuable addition to native landscapes. The variety typica is particularly noted for its robust growth and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
