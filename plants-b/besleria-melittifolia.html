Besleria melittifolia

Besleria melittifolia, commonly known as the honeybee plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both garden cultivation and botanical collections.
