Bulbophyllum kraenzlinianum

Bulbophyllum kraenzlinianum, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structures. The plant produces striking, often colorful flowers that are highly prized in horticulture. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat.

The botanical features of this orchid include its pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves.
