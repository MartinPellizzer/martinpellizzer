Berberis hookeri var. viridis

Berberis hookeri var. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Barberry, is a compact shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Berberis hookeri var. viridis is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its hardiness and attractive foliage.
