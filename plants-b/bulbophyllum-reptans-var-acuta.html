Bulbophyllum reptans var. acuta

Bulbophyllum reptans var. acuta, commonly known as the sharp-leafed bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and linear, acuminate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
