Begonia zamboangensis

Begonia zamboangensis, commonly known as the Zamboanga begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically with five petals arranged in a symmetrical pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is also considered a rare and endangered species.
