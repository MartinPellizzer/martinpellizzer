Bolivicereus brevicaulis

Bolivicereus brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed cactus, is a species native to Bolivia.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
