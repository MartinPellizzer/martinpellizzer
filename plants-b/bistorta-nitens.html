Bistorta nitens

Bistorta nitens, commonly known as the slender bistorta, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a reddish or purplish hue, especially along the veins.

Bistorta nitens is typically found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates.
