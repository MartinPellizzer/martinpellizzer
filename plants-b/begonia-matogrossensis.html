Begonia matogrossensis

Begonia matogrossensis, commonly known as the Matogrossense begonia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
