Bulbophyllum amplebracteatum

Bulbophyllum amplebracteatum, commonly known as the broad-bract Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, often pendulous flower spikes. The plant produces numerous small, fragrant flowers with white to pale yellow petals and sepals. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, and the plant thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Bulbophyllum amplebracteatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
