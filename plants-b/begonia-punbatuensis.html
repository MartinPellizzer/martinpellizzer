Begonia punbatuensis

Begonia punbatuensis, commonly known as the Tsumuki begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Sulawesi in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia punbatuensis is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
