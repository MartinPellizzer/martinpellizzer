Baeckea arbuscula

Baeckea arbuscula, commonly known as the white baeckea, is a small, evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Baeckea arbuscula is often found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal and inland regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
