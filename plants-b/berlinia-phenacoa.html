Berlinia phenacoa

Berlinia phenacoa, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a large, deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard, and heavy wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, often appearing in yellow or greenish-white colors.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
