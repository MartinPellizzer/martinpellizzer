Boerhavia diffusa var. leiocarpa

Boerhavia diffusa var. leiocarpa, commonly known as slender boerhavia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its slender stems and small, pink or white flowers. The leaves are typically ovate or elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
