Bauhinia tenuis

Bauhinia tenuis, commonly known as the narrowleaf bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Bauhinia tenuis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
