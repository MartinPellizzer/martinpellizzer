Byttneria vallensis

Byttneria vallensis, commonly known as the African blackwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood and can grow up to 20 meters tall. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood used in furniture and musical instruments.
