Bauhinia lawii

Bauhinia lawii, commonly known as the Java orchid tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves and showy, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a unique structure that resembles orchids, contributing to its ornamental value.
