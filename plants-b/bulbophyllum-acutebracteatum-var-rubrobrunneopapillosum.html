Bulbophyllum acutebracteatum var. rubrobrunneopapillosum

Bulbophyllum acutebracteatum var. rubrobrunneopapillosum, commonly known as the Red-Brown Papillate Bulbophyllum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, reddish-brown pubescence. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with elongated petals and a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and intricate floral structure.
