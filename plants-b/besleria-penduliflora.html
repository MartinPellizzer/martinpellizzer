Besleria penduliflora

Besleria penduliflora, commonly known as the hanging flower or pendulous orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with white to pale yellow petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Besleria penduliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
