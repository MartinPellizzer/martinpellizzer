Bulbophyllum alboroseum

Bulbophyllum alboroseum, commonly known as the White Rosette Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a rosette-like pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are fleshy and thick, adapted for water storage in its natural habitat.

The flowers bloom in spring and are often used in ornamental horticulture due to their aesthetic appeal.
