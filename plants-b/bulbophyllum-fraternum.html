Bulbophyllum fraternum

Bulbophyllum fraternum, commonly known as the Fraternal Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum fraternum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
