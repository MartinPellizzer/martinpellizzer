Bulbophyllum micropetalum

Bulbophyllum micropetalum, commonly known as the small-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with fleshy, bulb-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are small and delicate, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
