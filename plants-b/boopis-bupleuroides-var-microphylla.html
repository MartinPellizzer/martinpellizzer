Boopis bupleuroides var. microphylla

Boopis bupleuroides var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved bupleurum, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The leaves are small, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant is typically found in moist, woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
