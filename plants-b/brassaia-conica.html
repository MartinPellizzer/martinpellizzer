Brassaia conica

Brassaia conica, commonly known as the conical brassaia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique cone-shaped inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Brassaia conica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early autumn.
