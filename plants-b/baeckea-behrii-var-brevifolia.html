Baeckea behrii var. brevifolia

Baeckea behrii var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved baeckea, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, preferring well-drained conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in regions with a temperate climate and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The variety is distinguished by its shorter, narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Baeckea behrii.
