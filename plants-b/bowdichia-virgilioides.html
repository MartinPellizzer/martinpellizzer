Bowdichia virgilioides

Bowdichia virgilioides, commonly known as the Florida hickory, is a deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family (Fabaceae) and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers.
