Bulbophyllum prorepens

Bulbophyllum prorepens, commonly known as the creeping orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces numerous small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in shaded, moist environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions.

Its unique growth pattern and floral structure make it a distinctive species within the genus Bulbophyllum.
