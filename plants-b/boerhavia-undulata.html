Boerhavia undulata

Boerhavia undulata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and ability to cascade from containers.
