Begonia opaca

Begonia opaca, commonly known as the dark begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in the spring and summer. Begonia opaca prefers shady, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate flowers.
