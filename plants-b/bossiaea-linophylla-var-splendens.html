Bossiaea linophylla var. splendens

Bossiaea linophylla var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in coastal and inland regions. It has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
