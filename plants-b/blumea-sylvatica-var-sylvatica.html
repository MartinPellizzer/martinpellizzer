Blumea sylvatica var. sylvatica

Blumea sylvatica var. sylvatica, commonly known as the mountain blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with pale green centers.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
