Begonia tenuipila var. kennedyi

Begonia tenuipila var. kennedyi, commonly known as the Kennedy's begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom profusely during the warmer months.
