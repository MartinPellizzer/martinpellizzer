Borzicactus gracilis

Borzicactus gracilis, commonly known as the slender borzicactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem that is typically green and covered in a whitish bloom, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its otherwise muted appearance. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
