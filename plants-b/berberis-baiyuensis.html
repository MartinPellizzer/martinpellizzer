Berberis baiyuensis

Berberis baiyuensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces bright orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for hedges and landscaping in gardens.
