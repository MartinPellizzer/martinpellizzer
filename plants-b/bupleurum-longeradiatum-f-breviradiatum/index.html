Bupleurum Longeradiatum F. Breviradiatum

Bupleurum longeradiatum f. breviradiatum is a lesser-known variant of the Bupleurum genus, native to specific regions in eastern asia. this plant is characterized by its shorter, more compact leaves compared to its parent species. it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. due to its unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike. its conservation status remains uncertain, highlighting the need for further study and protection.
