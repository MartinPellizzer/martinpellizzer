Begonia dipetala

Begonia dipetala, commonly known as the two-petaled begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that typically feature two prominent petals, giving it its scientific name. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia dipetala is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
