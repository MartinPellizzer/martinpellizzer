Bulbophyllum triviale

Bulbophyllum triviale, commonly known as the common bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features elongated pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its overall appearance.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
