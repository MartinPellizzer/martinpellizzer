Bombax cyathophorum var. latifoliatum

Bombax cyathophorum var. latifoliatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf cotton tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The tree produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by fluffy, cotton-like seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some regions.
