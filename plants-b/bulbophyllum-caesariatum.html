Bulbophyllum caesariatum

Bulbophyllum caesariatum, commonly known as the Caesar's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and can range in color from red to purple.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and vibrant floral display.
