Begonia machrisiana

Begonia machrisiana, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia machrisiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
