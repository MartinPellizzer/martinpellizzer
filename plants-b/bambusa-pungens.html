Bambusa pungens

Bambusa pungens, commonly known as the sword bamboo, is a tall, clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often a striking silver or blue-green color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has sturdy, hollow stems that grow in dense clusters, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring moderate watering.

Bambusa pungens is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in garden designs and as a privacy screen.
