Banksia dilleniifolia

Banksia dilleniifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved banksia, is a shrub or small tree native to western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and distinctive cylindrical flower spikes. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is well adapted to the dry, sandy soils of its natural habitat.

Banksia dilleniifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
