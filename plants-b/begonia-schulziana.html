Begonia schulziana

Begonia schulziana, commonly known as the Schulz's begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a prominent midrib. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts.
