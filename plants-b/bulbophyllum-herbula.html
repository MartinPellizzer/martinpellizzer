Bulbophyllum herbula

Bulbophyllum herbula, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces a dense cluster of small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spike. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in forest understories.
