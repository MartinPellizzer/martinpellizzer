Baccharis tucumanensis var. angustifolia

Baccharis tucumanensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its adaptability makes it a common species in regions with a temperate climate.
