Blitum oleraceum

Blitum oleraceum, commonly known as the common groundsel, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer.

Blitum oleraceum is often considered a weed but can serve as a food source for certain insects and birds.
