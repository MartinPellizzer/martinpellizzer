Bulbostylis Capitata

Bulbostylis capitata is a small, delicate grass-like plant native to wetland environments. It is commonly found in marshy areas and along the edges of ponds and streams. This species is known for its compact stature and clustered flower heads that bloom in the summer months. Its unique appearance makes it a valuable addition to ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in supporting local biodiversity.
