Bauhinia weberbaueri

Bauhinia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its distinctive, papery, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons. Bauhinia weberbaueri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
