Barleria nuda

Barleria nuda, commonly known as the naked barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom during the warmer months.

Barleria nuda is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
