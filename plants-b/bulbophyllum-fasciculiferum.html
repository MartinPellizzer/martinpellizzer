Bulbophyllum fasciculiferum

Bulbophyllum fasciculiferum, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the plant's unique morphology.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
