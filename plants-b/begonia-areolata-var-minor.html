Begonia areolata var. minor

Begonia areolata var. minor, commonly known as the miniature begonia, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a pale green to yellowish hue. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
