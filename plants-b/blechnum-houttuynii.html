Blechnum houttuynii

Blechnum houttuynii, commonly known as the Houttuyn's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that often have a bluish-green hue. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

The sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common reproductive feature in ferns.
