Bulbophyllum turkii

Bulbophyllum turkii, commonly known as the Turk's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, brightly colored flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
