Bulbostylis andongensis

Bulbostylis andongensis, commonly known as the Andong bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

It plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat by providing food and shelter for various small organisms.
