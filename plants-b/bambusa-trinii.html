Bambusa trinii

Bambusa trinii, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking golden-yellow culms that contrast beautifully with its dark green leaves. The plant has a dense, compact growth habit, making it ideal for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Bambusa trinii is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
