Buddleja parvifolia

Buddleja parvifolia, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, multi-branched stems and small, fragrant, pale lavender flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Buddleja parvifolia is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
