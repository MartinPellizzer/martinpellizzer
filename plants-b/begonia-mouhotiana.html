Begonia mouhotiana

Begonia mouhotiana, commonly known as the giant begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, fuchsia-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its size and beauty, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
