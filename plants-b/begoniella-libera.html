Begoniella libera

Begoniella libera, commonly known as the free begonia, is a rare and unique species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant features striking, heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns and vibrant colors, making it a visually appealing specimen.
