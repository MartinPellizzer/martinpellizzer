Brachyscome sieberi var. gunnii

Brachyscome sieberi var. gunnii, commonly known as the silver frost daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features silvery-white hairs on its leaves, which give it a frosty appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and open woodlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
