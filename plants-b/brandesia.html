Brandesia

Brandesia, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a rough texture.
