Boucerosia cylindrica

Boucerosia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical thornveld grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its robust, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in open, grassy areas and is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Boucerosia cylindrica is valued for its resilience and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
