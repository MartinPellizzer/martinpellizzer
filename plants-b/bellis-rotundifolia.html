Bellis rotundifolia

Bellis rotundifolia, commonly known as the lawn daisy or English daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features rosettes of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers with yellow centers. The plant is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. It is well adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

Bellis rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
