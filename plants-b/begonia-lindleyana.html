Begonia lindleyana

Begonia lindleyana, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
