Beddomea simplicifolia var. racemosa

Beddomea simplicifolia var. racemosa, commonly known as the racemose beddomea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its simple, leafy foliage and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are arranged in a raceme, giving it its specific variety name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
