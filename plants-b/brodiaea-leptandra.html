Brodiaea leptandra

Brodiaea leptandra, commonly known as the slender brodiaea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to California.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
