Billbergia × joliboisii

Billbergia × joliboisii, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia natalensis and Billbergia zebrina.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage consists of long, narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette structure.

Billbergia × joliboisii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
