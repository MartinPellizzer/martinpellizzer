Baeckea crenulata var. tenella

Baeckea crenulata var. tenella, commonly known as the narrow-leaf baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typically borne in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
