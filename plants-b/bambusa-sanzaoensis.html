Bambusa sanzaoensis

Bambusa sanzaoensis, commonly known as the Sanzao bamboo, is a species native to southern China.

It is a tall, slender bamboo that can grow up to 15 meters in height, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and its ability to form thickets. Its culms are strong and hollow, making it suitable for construction and handicrafts.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
