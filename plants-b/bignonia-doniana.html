Bignonia doniana

Bignonia doniana, commonly known as the African trumpetvine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces distinctive orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting birds and insects. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Bignonia doniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
