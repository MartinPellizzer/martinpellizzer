Begonia eliassii

Begonia eliassii, commonly known as the Eliassii begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting yellow centers. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse environments.
