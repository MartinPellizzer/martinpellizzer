Barkhausia bivonana

Barkhausia bivonana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a slender, branching stem and produces small, oval leaves. Its root system is fibrous, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Barkhausia bivonana is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, preferring full sun to partial shade.
