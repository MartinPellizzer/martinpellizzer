Brimeura fastigiata

Brimeura fastigiata, commonly known as the upright brimeura, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in woodland or grassland habitats. The plant features a compact, upright growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale pink, blooming in spring.

Brimeura fastigiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
