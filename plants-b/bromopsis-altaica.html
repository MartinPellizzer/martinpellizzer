Bromopsis altaica

Bromopsis altaica, commonly known as the Altai feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the cold climates of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, delicate leaves and tufted inflorescences that resemble a feather, giving it its common name. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions. Bromopsis altaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
