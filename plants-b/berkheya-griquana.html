Berkheya griquana

Berkheya griquana, commonly known as the Grootfontein lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces numerous linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Berkheya griquana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
