Brachypodium ramosum var. scabriculmis

Brachypodium ramosum var. scabriculmis, commonly known as the rough-leaved brachypodium, is a grass species native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, rough-textured leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and have a scabrous surface, which is a defining feature of this variety.

It is often found in moist, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential in ecological restoration projects.
