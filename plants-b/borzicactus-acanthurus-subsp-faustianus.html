Borzicactus acanthurus subsp. faustianus

Borzicactus acanthurus subsp.

faustianus, commonly known as the Faustian barbed cactus, is a rare and spiny cactus native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, barbed spines.

The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and has adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions. Its flowers, which are usually yellow or orange, bloom during the warmer months.
