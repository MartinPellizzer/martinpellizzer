Bouteloua gracilis var. stricta

Bouteloua gracilis var. stricta, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that mature into fluffy seeds.

Blue grama is an important component of native grassland ecosystems and is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
