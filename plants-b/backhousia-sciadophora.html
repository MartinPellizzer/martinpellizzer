Backhousia sciadophora

Backhousia sciadophora, commonly known as the black pepper tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by dark, peppery fruits. Its bark is smooth and greyish, often exuding a fragrant resin when cut.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in Indigenous Australian medicine.
