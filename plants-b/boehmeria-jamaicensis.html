Boehmeria jamaicensis

Boehmeria jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other members commonly referred to as nettles. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and stinging hairs on the stems and leaves. Boehmeria jamaicensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
