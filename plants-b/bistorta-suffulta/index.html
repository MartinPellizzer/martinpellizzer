Bistorta Suffulta

Bistorta suffulta is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant purple and red flowers. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in mountainous regions of Europe. This species belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants like buckwheat and sorrel. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Bistorta suffulta is also valued for its hardiness and ability to flourish in challenging soil conditions.
