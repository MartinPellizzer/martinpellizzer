Botryanthus festinus

Botryanthus festinus, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to southern Africa and belongs to the Gesneriaceae family. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
