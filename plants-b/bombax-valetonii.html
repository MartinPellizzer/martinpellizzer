Bombax valetonii

Bombax valetonii, commonly known as the African silk cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in yellow or orange hues. The tree produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds enclosed in a three-lobed capsule. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in various regions.
