Brachypodium annuum

Brachypodium annuum, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Brachypodium annuum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
