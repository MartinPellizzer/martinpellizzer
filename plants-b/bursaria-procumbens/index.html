Bursaria Procumbens

Bursaria procumbens is a unique and resilient plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It thrives in harsh, saline environments where few other species can survive. This low-growing shrub is characterized by its small, spiny leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. Despite its tough appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local wildlife. Its adaptability makes it a fascinating subject for ecological study and conservation efforts.
