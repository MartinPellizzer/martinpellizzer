Biserrula mediterranea

Biserrula mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean biserrula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant characterized by its slender stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Biserrula mediterranea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
