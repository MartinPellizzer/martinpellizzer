Breweria langsdorffii

Breweria langsdorffii, commonly known as the blue brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. It has a creeping stem with opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive floral display.
