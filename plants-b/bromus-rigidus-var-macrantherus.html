Bromus rigidus var. macrantherus

Bromus rigidus var. macrantherus, commonly known as the rigid brome, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, with each spikelet containing two to three small, awned flowers.

This variety is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in association with other grasses in temperate climates.
