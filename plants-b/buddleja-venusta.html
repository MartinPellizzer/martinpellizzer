Buddleja venusta

Buddleja venusta, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, panicles of purple or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Buddleja venusta is also known for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
