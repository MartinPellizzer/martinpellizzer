Bauhinia ampla var. schlechteri

Bauhinia ampla var. schlechteri, commonly known as the white bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.

The species is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments.
