Billbergia amandae

Billbergia amandae, commonly known as the Amandae's billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts that enclose small white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia amandae is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
