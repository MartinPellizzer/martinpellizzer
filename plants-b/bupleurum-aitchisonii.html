Bupleurum aitchisonii

Bupleurum aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's angelica, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clustered umbels.
