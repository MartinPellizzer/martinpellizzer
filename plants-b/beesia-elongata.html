Beesia elongata

Beesia elongata, commonly known as the slender beesia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features basal rosettes of lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often tinged with reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Beesia elongata thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage and flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
