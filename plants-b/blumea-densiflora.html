Blumea densiflora

Blumea densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered with short, stiff hairs.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in dense clusters, making it a notable species in its native habitat.
