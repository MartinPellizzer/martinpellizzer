Bulbophyllum porphyrostachys

Bulbophyllum porphyrostachys, commonly known as the Porphyritic Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple and pink inflorescences, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically dark green and fleshy, adapted for efficient water retention.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.
