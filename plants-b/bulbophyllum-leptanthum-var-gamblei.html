Bulbophyllum leptanthum var. gamblei

Bulbophyllum leptanthum var. gamblei, commonly known as the Gambler's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive greenish hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
