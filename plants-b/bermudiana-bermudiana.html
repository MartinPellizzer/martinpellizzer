Bermudiana bermudiana

Bermudiana bermudiana, commonly known as the Bermuda grass, is a perennial grass species native to the coastal regions of Bermuda.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and a dense, mat-forming growth habit. This plant is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a common choice for lawns and erosion control. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Bermudiana bermudiana is also known for its rapid growth and ability to recover quickly from damage.
