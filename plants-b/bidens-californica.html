Bidens californica

Bidens californica, commonly known as the California ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features upright, branched stems that are covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with two prominent white petals, which bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

Bidens californica is adapted to dry, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
