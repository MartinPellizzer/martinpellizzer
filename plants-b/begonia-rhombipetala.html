Begonia rhombipetala

Begonia rhombipetala, commonly known as the rhombic begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Begonia rhombipetala prefers shady, moist environments and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
