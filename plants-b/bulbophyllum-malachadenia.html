Bulbophyllum malachadenia

Bulbophyllum malachadenia, commonly known as the Malacadenia orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid blooms during the wet season, showcasing vibrant colors that attract pollinators.
