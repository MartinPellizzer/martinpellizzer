Begonia oligantha

Begonia oligantha, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, silver-gray foliage with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, adding ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
