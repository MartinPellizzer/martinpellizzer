Begonia × deuringeri

Begonia × deuringeri, commonly known as the Deuringeri begonia, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions, characterized by its attractive, often velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to grow in a variety of conditions, though it requires consistent moisture and moderate temperatures.
