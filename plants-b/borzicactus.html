Borzicactus

Borzicactus, commonly known as the zigzag cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a distinctive, ribbed stem that grows in a zigzag pattern, allowing it to bend and curve as it matures. The plant has spiny, cylindrical stems that store water, adapting to its harsh desert environment. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring and attracting pollinators like bees and birds.

Borzicactus is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and striking appearance.
