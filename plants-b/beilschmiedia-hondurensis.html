Beilschmiedia hondurensis

Beilschmiedia hondurensis, commonly known as Honduran beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark, fleshy berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

It typically grows in humid lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
