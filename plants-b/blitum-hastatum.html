Blitum hastatum

Blitum hastatum, commonly known as the slender white speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and hardy member of the Plantaginaceae family.
