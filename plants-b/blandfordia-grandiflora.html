Blandfordia grandiflora

Blandfordia grandiflora, commonly known as the Christmas Blandfordia, is a striking perennial plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is well adapted to sandy soils and can tolerate coastal conditions, making it a hardy choice for gardeners. Its unique flowering pattern, which often coincides with the Christmas season, adds to its ornamental appeal.

Blandfordia grandiflora is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators and its resilience in diverse environmental conditions.
