Brasilettia

Brasilettia, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into the edible, star-shaped fruits. Brasilettia fruits are typically yellow or orange when ripe and have a sweet, tangy flavor.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its edible produce.
