Burmeistera kalbreyeri

Burmeistera kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica and Panama.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for botanical gardens and specialized horticulture.
