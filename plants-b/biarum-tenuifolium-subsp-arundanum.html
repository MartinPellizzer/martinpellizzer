Biarum tenuifolium subsp. arundanum

Biarum tenuifolium subsp.

arundanum, commonly known as the slender-leaved Dutchman's breeches, is a member of the Liliaceae family. It is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy along the veins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
