Barleria macraei

Barleria macraei, commonly known as the purple barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically purple or pink and bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Barleria macraei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a landscape plant.
