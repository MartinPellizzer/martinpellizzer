Borzicactus pseudothelegonus

Borzicactus pseudothelegonus, commonly known as the pseudothelegonus cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sunlight.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
