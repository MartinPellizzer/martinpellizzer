Bulbophyllum kwangtungense

Bulbophyllum kwangtungense, commonly known as the Guangdong Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant typically features elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a variety of colors ranging from white to yellow.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
