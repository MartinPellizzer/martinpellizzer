Bridelia hamiltoniana var. glabra

Bridelia hamiltoniana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth bridelia, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

This species is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
