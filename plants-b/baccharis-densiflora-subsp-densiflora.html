Baccharis densiflora subsp. densiflora

Baccharis densiflora subsp.

densiflora, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its adaptability makes it a common species in regions with Mediterranean climates.
