Bauhinia clemensiorum

Bauhinia clemensiorum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia clemensiorum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
