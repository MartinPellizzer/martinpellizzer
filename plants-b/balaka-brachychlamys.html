Balaka brachychlamys

Balaka brachychlamys, commonly known as the slender sand orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and prefers well-drained environments. Its botanical features include narrow leaves and a flowering stem that bears a few blooms.

It is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptation to harsh climatic conditions.
