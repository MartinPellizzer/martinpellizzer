Buddleja bullata

Buddleja bullata, commonly known as the early Buddleia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features greyish-green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Buddleja bullata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
