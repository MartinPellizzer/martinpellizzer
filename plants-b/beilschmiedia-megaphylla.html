Beilschmiedia megaphylla

Beilschmiedia megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leaf beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by round, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within lowland and montane forests.

Beilschmiedia megaphylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance in its native habitats.
