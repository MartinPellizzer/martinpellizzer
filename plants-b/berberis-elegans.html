Berberis elegans

Berberis elegans, commonly known as the elegant barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that turn a striking red or orange in the fall, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its hardy nature and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
