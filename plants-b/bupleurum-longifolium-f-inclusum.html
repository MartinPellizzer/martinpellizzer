Bupleurum longifolium f. inclusum

Bupleurum longifolium f. inclusum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple. The leaves are elongated and have a distinctive wavy margin, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

This plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
