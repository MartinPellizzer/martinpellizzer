Brachystelma oianthum

Brachystelma oianthum, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late winter or early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
