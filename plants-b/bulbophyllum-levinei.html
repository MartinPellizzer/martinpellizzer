Bulbophyllum levinei

Bulbophyllum levinei, commonly known as the Red-Veined Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vividly colored flowers with intricate veining patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bulbophyllum levinei is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right growing conditions.
