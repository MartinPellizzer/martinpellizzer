Bulbophyllum odoratum var. grandiflorum

Bulbophyllum odoratum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, wiry stems and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow sepals and petals. The plant typically blooms during the wet season and requires high humidity and indirect light for optimal growth. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, making it well-suited for hanging or mounted displays.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
