Bonamia linearis var. media

Bonamia linearis var. media, commonly known as the narrowleaf bonamia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
