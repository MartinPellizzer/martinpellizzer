Bidens bampsiana

Bidens bampsiana, commonly known as the narrowleaf beggarlice, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Bidens bampsiana is an annual plant that produces numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind and can persist in the soil for several years.
