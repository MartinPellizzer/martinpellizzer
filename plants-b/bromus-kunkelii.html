Bromus kunkelii

Bromus kunkelii, commonly known as Kunkel's brome, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, disturbed areas such as roadsides and sandy soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces spikelets that are often tinged with purple or brown. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

Bromus kunkelii is adapted to arid environments and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
