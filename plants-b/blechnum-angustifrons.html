Blechnum angustifrons

Blechnum angustifrons, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually evergreen, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, serrated margin.

Blechnum angustifrons is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
