Boronia ledifolia var. rubiginosa

Boronia ledifolia var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the Crimson Boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
