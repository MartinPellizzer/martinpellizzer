Breynia oblongifolia var. suborbicularis

Breynia oblongifolia var. suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular breynia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, orbicular-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its unique leaf morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in horticulture and ecological studies.
