Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. albociliatum

Bulbophyllum albociliatum var. albociliatum, commonly known as the white-fringed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking white to pale yellow flowers with distinctive white cilia along the edges of the petals. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

This orchid prefers warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
