Ballota suaveolens

Ballota suaveolens, commonly known as sweet ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or calcareous habitats.

Ballota suaveolens is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
