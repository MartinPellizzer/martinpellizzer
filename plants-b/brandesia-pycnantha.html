Brandesia pycnantha

Brandesia pycnantha, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded shrub form and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically blooms during the late summer and autumn months, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.

Brandesia pycnantha is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is well-suited to coastal and Mediterranean climates.
