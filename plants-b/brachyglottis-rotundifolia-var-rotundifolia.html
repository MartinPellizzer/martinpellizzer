Brachyglottis rotundifolia var. rotundifolia

Brachyglottis rotundifolia var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed brachyglottis, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
