Bauhinia petelotii

Bauhinia petelotii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces flat, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia petelotii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
