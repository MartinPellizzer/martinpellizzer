Brugmansia × rubella

Brugmansia × rubella, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Brugmansia, characterized by its large, pendulous flowers that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant is known for its fragrant, trumpet-shaped blossoms that range in color from white to yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
