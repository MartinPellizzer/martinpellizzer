Berkheya angusta

Berkheya angusta, commonly known as the narrowleaf berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
