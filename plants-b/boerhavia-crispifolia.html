Boerhavia crispifolia

Boerhavia crispifolia, commonly known as the crinkled purslane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and fleshy, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Boerhavia crispifolia is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
