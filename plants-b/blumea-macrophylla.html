Blumea macrophylla

Blumea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Blumea macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
