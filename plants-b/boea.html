Boea

Boea, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Boea thrives in humid environments and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
