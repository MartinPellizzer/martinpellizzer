Bacopa Violacea

Bacopa violacea is a small aquatic plant known for its delicate blue and violet flowers. It thrives in shallow waters and is often found in wetlands and marshes. This species is native to parts of Asia and the Americas. Its leaves are typically small and opposite, while its flowers bloom in clusters. Bacopa violacea is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological importance.
