Bertiera laxa var. pedicellata

Bertiera laxa var. pedicellata, commonly known as the slender bertiera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in grassland and woodland habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on slender pedicels. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
