Buddleja globosa

Buddleja globosa, commonly known as the globular buddleja, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pale blue to purple tubular flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Buddleja globosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
