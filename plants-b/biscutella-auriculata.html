Biscutella auriculata

Biscutella auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate biscuit plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with distinctive kidney-shaped leaves that have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
