Bermudiana setacea

Bermudiana setacea, commonly known as the Bermuda grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to the warm temperate regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Bermudiana setacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
