Bletia florida var. meridana

Bletia florida var. meridana, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to parts of South America.

It features showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its unique color and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its aesthetic value.
