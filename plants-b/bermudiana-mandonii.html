Bermudiana mandonii

Bermudiana mandonii, commonly known as the Bermuda buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Bermuda.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round seed pods that split open when mature. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to the warm, coastal climate of Bermuda.

Bermudiana mandonii is often found in grassy areas and along the shoreline.
