Bromus Sterilis Var. Lanuginosus

Bromus sterilis var. lanuginosus is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in harsh environments. This variant is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and compact growth habit. It is often found in disturbed soils and open areas across temperate regions. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its study offers insights into plant adaptation and resilience in challenging climates.
