Backhousia tetraptera

Backhousia tetraptera, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Myrtle, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Australia.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often fragrant and have a distinctive scent when crushed. Backhousia tetraptera is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and wetland areas.
