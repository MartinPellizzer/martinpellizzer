Begonia platanifolia var. normalis

Begonia platanifolia var. normalis, commonly known as the giant leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves.

The foliage is typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and it often features prominent veins that add to its visual appeal. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate contrast to its bold leaves.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
