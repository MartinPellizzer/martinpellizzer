Bertolonia margaritacea var. splendens

Bertolonia margaritacea var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Bertolonia, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade with well-drained soil.
