Begonia suyeniae

Begonia suyeniae, commonly known as the Suyeni begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to the humid forests of Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often display intricate patterns of silver and green. The plant produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
