Bienertia kossinskyi

Bienertia kossinskyi, commonly known as the desert fanflower, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to high temperatures and low rainfall.

Its unique morphology makes it an important species in desert ecosystems.
