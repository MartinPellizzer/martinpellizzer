Behen csereii f. tenella

Behen csereii f. tenella, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cypress.

It is a rare and compact shrub characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and conical shape. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by cylindrical cones.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
