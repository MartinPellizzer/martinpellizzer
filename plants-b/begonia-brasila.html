Begonia brasila

Begonia brasila, commonly known as the Brasilia begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces showy, single flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
