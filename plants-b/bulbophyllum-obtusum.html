Bulbophyllum obtusum

Bulbophyllum obtusum, commonly known as the blunt-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
