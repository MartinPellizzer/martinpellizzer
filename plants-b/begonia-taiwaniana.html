Begonia taiwaniana

Begonia taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan begonia, is a striking flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, fleshy flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring a mix of green, white, and pink hues.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
