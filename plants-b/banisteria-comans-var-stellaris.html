Banisteria comans var. stellaris

Banisteria comans var. stellaris, commonly known as the star banisteria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

The flowers emit a mild fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
