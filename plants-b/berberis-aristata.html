Berberis aristata

Berberis aristata, commonly known as Indian barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features thorny stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries in the fall. Its roots and stems contain berberine, a compound with medicinal properties.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
