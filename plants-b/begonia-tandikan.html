Begonia tandikan

Begonia tandikan, commonly known as the Tandikan begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia tandikan is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
