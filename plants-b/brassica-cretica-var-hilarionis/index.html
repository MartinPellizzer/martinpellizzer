Brassica Cretica Var. Hilarionis

Brassica cretica var. hilarionis is a rare and ancient plant species native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its unique yellow flowers and compact growth habit. This variety is often found in rocky soils and dry environments, showcasing its resilience. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a threatened species. Its historical significance and ecological value make it an important subject for botanical study.
