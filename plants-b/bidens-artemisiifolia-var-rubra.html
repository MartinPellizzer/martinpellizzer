Bidens artemisiifolia var. rubra

Bidens artemisiifolia var. rubra, commonly known as redstem ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellow flowers with prominent white veins. The leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. This variant is distinguished by its reddish stems, which contrast with the green foliage.

It thrives in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and disturbed soils, and is often found in moist to dry environments.
