Brabila

Brabila, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Brabila is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red.
