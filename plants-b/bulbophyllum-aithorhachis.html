Bulbophyllum aithorhachis

Bulbophyllum aithorhachis, commonly known as the golden bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering conditions.
