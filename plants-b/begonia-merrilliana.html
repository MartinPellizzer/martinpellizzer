Begonia merrilliana

Begonia merrilliana, commonly known as the Philippine begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia merrilliana is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
