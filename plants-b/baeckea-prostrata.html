Baeckea prostrata

Baeckea prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate baeckea, is a low-growing shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Baeckea prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native garden landscaping.
