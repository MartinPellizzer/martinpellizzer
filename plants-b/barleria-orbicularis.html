Barleria orbicularis

Barleria orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaved barleria, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense spikes, making it visually striking. The leaves are round or oval in shape, with a hairy texture that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

This plant is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
