Bryobium lanuginosum

Bryobium lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and its soft, fuzzy appearance due to the presence of specialized structures called rhizoids. Bryobium lanuginosum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
