Begonia bagotiana var. acutialata

Begonia bagotiana var. acutialata, commonly known as the acute-leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and bright, showy flowers in shades of red and pink. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
