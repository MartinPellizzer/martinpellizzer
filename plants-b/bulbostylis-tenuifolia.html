Bulbostylis tenuifolia

Bulbostylis tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant typically grows in wet, swampy areas or along the edges of water bodies. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense mats in its habitat.

Bulbostylis tenuifolia plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
