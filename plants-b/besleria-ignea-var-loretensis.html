Besleria ignea var. loretensis

Besleria ignea var. loretensis, commonly known as the Loretto flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and conservatories.
