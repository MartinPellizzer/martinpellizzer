Bertolonia gibsonii

Bertolonia gibsonii, commonly known as Gibson's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with white ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to dry conditions.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
