Bulliarda moschata

Bulliarda moschata, commonly known as the musk bulliard, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, musky scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Bulliarda moschata is valued for its unique fragrance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
