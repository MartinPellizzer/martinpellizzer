Binaria splendens

Binaria splendens, commonly known as the golden star plant, is a flowering species native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, giving it its common name. The plant has lance-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green in color and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Binaria splendens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
