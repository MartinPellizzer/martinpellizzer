Bombax insigne var. polystemon

Bombax insigne var. polystemon, commonly known as the pink silk cotton tree, is a variant of the silk cotton tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant display to the landscape. The plant produces large, fluffy seed pods that release cotton-like fibers, which are lightweight and easily carried by the wind. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, providing dense foliage that supports a variety of wildlife.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
