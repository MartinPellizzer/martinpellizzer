Baccaurea sparsiflora

Baccaurea sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered bacco, is a tropical plant belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest in botanical research and horticulture.
