Bufonia yildirimhanii

Bufonia yildirimhanii, commonly known as the Turkish nightshade, is a rare and unique plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its striking violet flowers and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and requires full sunlight for optimal growth.
