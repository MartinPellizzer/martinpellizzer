Bulbostylis subuligera

Bulbostylis subuligera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a species of grass found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, pointed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of small, spike-like clusters of flowers, which are usually pale green to brown in color.

Bulbostylis subuligera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
