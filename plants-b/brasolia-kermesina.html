Brasolia kermesina

Brasolia kermesina, commonly known as the redleaf kermesina, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red leaves that change color with the seasons, making it a visually captivating species. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the cooler months. Brasolia kermesina is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions, with deep roots that help it access groundwater.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
