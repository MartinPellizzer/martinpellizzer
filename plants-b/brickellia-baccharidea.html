Brickellia baccharidea

Brickellia baccharidea, commonly known as scarlet brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bright yellow to orange-red flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
