Breweria montevidensis

Breweria montevidensis, commonly known as the Montevidense brewer's grass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscaping projects.
