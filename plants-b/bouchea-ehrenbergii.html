Bouchea ehrenbergii

Bouchea ehrenbergii, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has a woody stem with small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs.
