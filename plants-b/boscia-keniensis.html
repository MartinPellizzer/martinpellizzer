Boscia keniensis

Boscia keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan boscia, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Boscia keniensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
