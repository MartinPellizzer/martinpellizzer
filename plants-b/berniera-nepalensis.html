Berniera nepalensis

Berniera nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal berberis, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its branched, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations.

Its ornamental value and ecological role make it a notable species in alpine ecosystems.
