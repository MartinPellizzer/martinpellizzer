Buddleja purdomii

Buddleja purdomii, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pale blue to purple. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Buddleja purdomii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
