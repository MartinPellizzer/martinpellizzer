Buceras elliptica

Buceras elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed evergreen, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are usually a deep green color. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

Buceras elliptica is adapted to coastal environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
