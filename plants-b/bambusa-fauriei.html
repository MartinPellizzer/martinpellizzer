Bambusa fauriei

Bambusa fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and striking yellow-green foliage. The plant forms dense clumps and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Bambusa fauriei is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide natural screening in gardens and landscapes.
