Bulbostylis Coarctata

Bulbostylis coarctata is a small, herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in the southeastern united states. it belongs to the grass family and is known for its delicate, grass-like appearance. this species is often found in marshes and pond margins where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. despite its modest size, it plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems. its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
