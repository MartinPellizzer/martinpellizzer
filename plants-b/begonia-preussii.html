Begonia preussii

Begonia preussii, commonly known as the Preuss begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia preussii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation indoors.
