Bischofia javanica var. genuina

Bischofia javanica var. genuina, commonly known as the Java glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its attractive, palmate leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
