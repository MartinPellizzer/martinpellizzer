Brassica oleracea var. longa

Brassica oleracea var. longa, commonly known as kohlrabi, is a biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its swollen, bulbous stem that serves as the edible part of the plant. The plant produces small, green, leafy foliage and yellow flowers that develop into round, pod-like fruits. Kohlrabi thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is a versatile crop that can be harvested at various stages of development for different culinary uses.
