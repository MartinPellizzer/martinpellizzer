Bursera klugii

Bursera klugii, commonly known as the Mexican frankincense tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes species known for producing aromatic resins. The tree has smooth, gray bark and elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically yellow or white, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
