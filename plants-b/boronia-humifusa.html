Boronia humifusa

Boronia humifusa, commonly known as the low boronia, is a low-growing shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, aromatic leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its flowering period occurs during the spring and summer months, attracting various pollinators.

Boronia humifusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
