Begonia capillipes

Begonia capillipes, commonly known as the slender-leaved begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green color and a distinctive red or purple underside. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.
