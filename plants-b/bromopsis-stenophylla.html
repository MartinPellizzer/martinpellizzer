Bromopsis stenophylla

Bromopsis stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mat grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are usually less than 10 cm in length. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are densely packed and often appear in clusters.

Bromopsis stenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
