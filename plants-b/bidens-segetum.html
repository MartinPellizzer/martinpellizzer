Bidens segetum

Bidens segetum, commonly known as the field tickseed, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features bright yellow flowers with dark brown centers, typically blooming in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are alternate, serrated, and often covered in fine hairs.

Bidens segetum is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic qualities.
