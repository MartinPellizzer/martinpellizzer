Brunfelsia hydrangeiformis subsp. capitata

Brunfelsia hydrangeiformis subsp.

capitata, commonly known as the Cape jasmine or the tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical areas.
