Benthamantha mollis var. micrantha

Benthamantha mollis var. micrantha, commonly known as the slender benthamantha, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and a prominent central stamen. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is distinguished by its smaller flowers and more compact growth habit compared to the typical form of Benthamantha mollis.
