Berzelia

Berzelia, commonly known as the African orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to sub-Saharan Africa and consists of several species, including Berzelia ramosa and Berzelia natalensis. These plants are characterized by their showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Berzelia species typically grow in grasslands and woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

They are valued for their ornamental beauty and are often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
