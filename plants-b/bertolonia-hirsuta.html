Bertolonia hirsuta

Bertolonia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy bertolonia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Bertolonia hirsuta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
