Bersama tysoniana

Bersama tysoniana, commonly known as the white-flowered evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and indoor spaces.
