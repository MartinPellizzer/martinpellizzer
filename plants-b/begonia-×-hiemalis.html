Begonia × hiemalis

Begonia × hiemalis, commonly known as the winter begonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of ornamental features.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are a striking contrast to its blossoms. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a bedding plant.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit variable growth habits and flower colors depending on the parent species.
