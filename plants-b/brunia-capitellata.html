Brunia capitellata

Brunia capitellata, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in spring, which are highly fragrant and attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
