Begonia sikkimensis

Begonia sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim begonia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in shaded forest understories at high altitudes. Its foliage is often adorned with distinctive patterns and colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Begonia sikkimensis is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
