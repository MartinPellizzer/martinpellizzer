Bidens kotschyi

Bidens kotschyi, commonly known as the desert ironweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, hairy stems and small, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant produces numerous seeds enclosed in a pappus, which aids in wind dispersal. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
