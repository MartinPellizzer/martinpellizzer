Bulbophyllum linchianum

Bulbophyllum linchianum, commonly known as the orchid with the unique appearance of a "bottle orchid," is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its elongated, bottle-shaped pseudobulbs and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and terrariums.
