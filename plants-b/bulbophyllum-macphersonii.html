Bulbophyllum macphersonii

Bulbophyllum macphersonii, commonly known as the Macpherson's ghost orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial herb with a unique morphology, featuring a short, thick rhizome and a cluster of fleshy, leaf-like structures. The plant produces intricate, pale green to white flowers that are highly specialized for pollination by specific insects. Its growth is slow and it requires specific environmental conditions to thrive, making it a challenging species to cultivate.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
