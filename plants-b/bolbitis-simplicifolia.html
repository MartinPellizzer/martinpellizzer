Bolbitis simplicifolia

Bolbitis simplicifolia, commonly known as the simple-leaf fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green and have a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces spores on the underside of its fronds, which are released during the dry season. Bolbitis simplicifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
