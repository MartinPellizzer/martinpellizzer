Bauhinia ornata var. contigua

Bauhinia ornata var. contigua, commonly known as the red bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Bauhinia ornata var. contigua thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
