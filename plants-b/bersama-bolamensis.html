Bersama bolamensis

Bersama bolamensis, commonly known as the Bornean ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ecological importance and limited distribution, Bersama bolamensis is considered a vulnerable species.
