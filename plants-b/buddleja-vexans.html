Buddleja vexans

Buddleja vexans, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It is known for its ability to attract a wide variety of butterflies and other pollinators.

Buddleja vexans is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
