Banisteriopsis calcicola

Banisteriopsis calcicola, commonly known as the limestone banisteriopsis, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody structure and distinctive leaf morphology, which are adaptations to its arid and rocky habitat. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by fruit that contains toxic seeds.
