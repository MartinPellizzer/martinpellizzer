Begonia xingyiensis

Begonia xingyiensis, commonly known as the Xingyi begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white, and are often borne on long, slender pedicels.

It prefers shady, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
