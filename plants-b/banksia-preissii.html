Banksia preissii

Banksia preissii, commonly known as the golden banksia, is a species of flowering plant in the Proteaceae family.

It is native to Western Australia and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flower spikes that emerge from a woody cone. Banksia preissii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
