Bulbophyllum ciliolatum

Bulbophyllum ciliolatum, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and long, slender leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum ciliolatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
