Bulbophyllum bandischii

Bulbophyllum bandischii, commonly known as the Bandisch's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

It requires high light intensity and good air circulation to thrive, making it a popular choice for experienced orchid growers.
