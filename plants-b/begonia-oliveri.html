Begonia oliveri

Begonia oliveri, commonly known as Oliver's begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Begonia oliveri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
