Brocchinia cryptantha

Brocchinia cryptantha, commonly known as the cryptantha brocchinia, is a species of bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup, a feature common among many bromeliads. The plant produces small, white flowers that are borne on a short inflorescence, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, hair-like structures that aid in water retention.

Brocchinia cryptantha thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
