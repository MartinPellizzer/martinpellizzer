Bauhinia leptantha

Bauhinia leptantha, commonly known as the slender bauhinia, is a small deciduous tree or shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, papery, and often pink or red-pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. Bauhinia leptantha thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
