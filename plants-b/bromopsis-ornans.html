Bromopsis ornans

Bromopsis ornans, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Bromopsis ornans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
