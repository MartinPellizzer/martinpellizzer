Besleria foliacea

Besleria foliacea, commonly known as the velvetleaf caladium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. Its leaves are thick and velvety, giving it a unique texture and appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
