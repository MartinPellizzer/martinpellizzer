Berberis yunnanensis

Berberis yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its small yellow flowers appear in early spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a tart flavor.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
