Bulbophyllum lineariflorum

Bulbophyllum lineariflorum, commonly known as the linear-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance and unique floral structure.

Bulbophyllum lineariflorum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
