Brachystelma maculatum var. breviflorum

Brachystelma maculatum var. breviflorum, commonly known as the spotted stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to store water in its leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to many botanical collections.
