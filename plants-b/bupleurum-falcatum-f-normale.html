Bupleurum falcatum f. normale

Bupleurum falcatum f. normale, commonly known as the common goat's beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, slightly curved leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters. The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

Botanically, it is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
