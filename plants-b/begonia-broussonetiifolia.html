Begonia broussonetiifolia

Begonia broussonetiifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
