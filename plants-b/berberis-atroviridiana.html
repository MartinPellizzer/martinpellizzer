Berberis atroviridiana

Berberis atroviridiana, commonly known as the dark green barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that are edible but often sour. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Due to its attractive foliage and winter hardiness, it is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
