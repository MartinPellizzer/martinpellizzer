Begonia plebeja var. kennedyi

Begonia plebeja var. kennedyi, commonly known as Kennedy's begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its foliage is often marked with intricate patterns and contrasting veins.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
