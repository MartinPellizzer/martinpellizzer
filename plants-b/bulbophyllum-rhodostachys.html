Bulbophyllum rhodostachys

Bulbophyllum rhodostachys, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
