Besleria bonodora

Besleria bonodora, commonly known as the red-flowered gesneriad, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of ornamental plants.
