Bambusa shuangliuensis

Bambusa shuangliuensis, commonly known as the Shuangliu bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a tall, clump-forming grass that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant features slender, hollow stems with a smooth, greenish-brown exterior. Its leaves are long and narrow, typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the culms.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
