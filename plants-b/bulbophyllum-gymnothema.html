Bulbophyllum gymnothema

Bulbophyllum gymnothema, commonly known as the naked-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with short, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
