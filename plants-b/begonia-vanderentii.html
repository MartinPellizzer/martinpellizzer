Begonia vanderentii

Begonia vanderentii, commonly known as the Vanderent's begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of western Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
