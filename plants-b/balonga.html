Balonga

Balonga, commonly known as *Boscia alata*, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, winged branches that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for both humans and animals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species adapted to drought conditions.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
