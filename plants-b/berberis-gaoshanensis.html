Berberis gaoshanensis

Berberis gaoshanensis, commonly known as the Gaoshan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Gaoshan mountainous regions.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its hardy nature and decorative value in gardens.
