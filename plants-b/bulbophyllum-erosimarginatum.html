Bulbophyllum erosimarginatum

Bulbophyllum erosimarginatum, commonly known as the eroded margin orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by experienced growers.
