Billbergia × windii

Billbergia × windii, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia nutans and Billbergia moranensis.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts and yellow flowers, adding ornamental value. It is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Billbergia × windii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
