Barjonia platyphylla

Barjonia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf barjonia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are broad and flat, which helps it conserve water in arid conditions.

Barjonia platyphylla is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
