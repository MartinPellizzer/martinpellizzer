Bulbophyllum adiamantinum

Bulbophyllum adiamantinum, commonly known as the Adiamantinum orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a fragrant aroma.

Bulbophyllum adiamantinum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
