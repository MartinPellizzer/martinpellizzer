Bulbophyllum gracilicaule

Bulbophyllum gracilicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Bulbophyllum gracilicaule is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
