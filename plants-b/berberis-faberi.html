Berberis faberi

Berberis faberi, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The plant is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
