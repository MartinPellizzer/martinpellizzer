Begonia brasiliana

Begonia brasiliana, commonly known as the Brazilian begonia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting yellow markings. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia brasiliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
