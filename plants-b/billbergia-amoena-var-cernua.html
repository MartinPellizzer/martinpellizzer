Billbergia amoena var. cernua

Billbergia amoena var. cernua, commonly known as the drooping billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that curve downward, creating a cascading effect. The plant produces vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in a spike above the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
