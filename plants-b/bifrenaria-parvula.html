Bifrenaria parvula

Bifrenaria parvula, commonly known as the small-leafed bifrenaria, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and along stream banks. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in length. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often greenish-white in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
