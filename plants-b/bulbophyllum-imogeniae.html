Bulbophyllum imogeniae

Bulbophyllum imogeniae, commonly known as the Imogen's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the genus Bulbophyllum in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers that can range in color from white to pale yellow. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks in the wild.
