Buddleja davidii

Buddleja davidii, commonly known as the butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, nectar-rich flowers that range in color from purple to pink and white. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Buddleja davidii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to many landscapes.
