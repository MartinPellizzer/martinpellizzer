Brunfelsia hydrangeiformis subsp. hydrangeiformis

Brunfelsia hydrangeiformis subsp.

hydrangeiformis, commonly known as the hydrangea-like Brunfelsia, is a tropical shrub native to South America. It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet.

The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.
