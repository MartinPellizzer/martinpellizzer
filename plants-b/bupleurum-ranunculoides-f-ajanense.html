Bupleurum ranunculoides f. ajanense

Bupleurum ranunculoides f. ajanense, commonly known as the Kashmir bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

Its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in umbels, and it produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.
