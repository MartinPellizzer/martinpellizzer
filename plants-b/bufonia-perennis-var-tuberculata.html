Bufonia perennis var. tuberculata

Bufonia perennis var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate bufonia, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by berry-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
