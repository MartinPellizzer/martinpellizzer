Bertya pedicellata

Bertya pedicellata, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight blue or greyish tint.
