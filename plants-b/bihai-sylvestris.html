Bihai sylvestris

Bihai sylvestris, commonly known as the wild banana plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and commonly consumed raw or cooked. Its rhizome is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Bihai sylvestris thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
