Bolelia concolor var. tricolor

Bolelia concolor var. tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored bolelia, is a variant of the bolelia plant characterized by its distinctive tri-color foliage.

This plant typically exhibits leaves with three different colorations, often green, white, and purple, creating an eye-catching visual effect. It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The botanical features of Bolelia concolor var. tricolor include its delicate, lobed leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique coloration.
