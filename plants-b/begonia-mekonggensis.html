Begonia mekonggensis

Begonia mekonggensis, commonly known as the Mekong begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Vietnam and Laos.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a waxy surface, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
