Begonia hymenocarpa

Begonia hymenocarpa, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a dense, soft texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia hymenocarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
