Bodinieriella cavaleriei

Bodinieriella cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is a slow-growing, evergreen plant characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves. Native to southern China, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered an important species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
