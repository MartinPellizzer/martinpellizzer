Bulbostylis stricta

Bulbostylis stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a species of grass found primarily in wetland and marsh environments.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers that are typically green or pale brown in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in suitable habitats.

Bulbostylis stricta plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
