Ballota nigra var. alba

Ballota nigra var. alba, commonly known as white ballota, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its white or pale purple flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Ballota nigra var. alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
