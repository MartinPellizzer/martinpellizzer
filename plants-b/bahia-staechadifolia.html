Bahia staechadifolia

Bahia staechadifolia, commonly known as silverleaf stachys, is a perennial herb native to the southern United States and Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
