Blitum rubrum var. humile

Blitum rubrum var. humile, commonly known as dwarf blite, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features basal rosettes of spoon-shaped, silver-gray leaves with a distinctive white coating. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and arid environments.

Its compact form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
