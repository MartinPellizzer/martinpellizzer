Biscutella Glacialis

Biscutella glacialis is a rare and striking plant known for its delicate, yellow flowers and frost-resistant nature. Native to high-altitude regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in harsh, alpine environments where few plants can survive. This species is often found growing on rocky slopes and in mountainous tundra, adapting to extreme cold and limited nutrients. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its resilience, Biscutella glacialis faces threats from climate change and habitat loss.
