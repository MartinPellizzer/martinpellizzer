Bulbine dielsii

Bulbine dielsii, commonly known as the Natal bulbous plant, is a succulent perennial native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, often in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
