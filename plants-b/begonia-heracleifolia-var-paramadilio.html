Begonia heracleifolia var. paramadilio

Begonia heracleifolia var. paramadilio, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among collectors for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
