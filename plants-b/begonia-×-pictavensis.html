Begonia × pictavensis

Begonia × pictavensis, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its vibrant and diverse flower colors.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking, showy bracts. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It prefers well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for indoor cultivation or as a container plant.

Its foliage and flowers make it a favorite among gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
