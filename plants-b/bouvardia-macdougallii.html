Bouvardia macdougallii

Bouvardia macdougallii, commonly known as the Macdougall's bouvardia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
