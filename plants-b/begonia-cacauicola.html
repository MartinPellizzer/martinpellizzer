Begonia cacauicola

Begonia cacauicola, commonly known as the Cacauico begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Begonia cacauicola is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
