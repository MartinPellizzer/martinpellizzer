Bursa patagonica

Bursa patagonica, commonly known as the Patagonian burr, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, seed-bearing structures. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Bursa patagonica is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
