Begonia rex var. nebulosa

Begonia rex var. nebulosa, commonly known as the Cloudy Rex Begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that exhibit a unique marbled pattern of deep green, silver, and red hues. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically appearing in spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
