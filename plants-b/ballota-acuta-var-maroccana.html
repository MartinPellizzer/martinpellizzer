Ballota acuta var. maroccana

Ballota acuta var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Morocco.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
