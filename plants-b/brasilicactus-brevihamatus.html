Brasilicactus brevihamatus

Brasilicactus brevihamatus, commonly known as the short-stemmed Brazil cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, columnar stem with a smooth, green surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by bright red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
