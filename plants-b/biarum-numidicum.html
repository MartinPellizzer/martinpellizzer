Biarum numidicum

Biarum numidicum, commonly known as the Numidian lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.

The flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer.
