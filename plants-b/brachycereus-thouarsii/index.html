Brachycereus Thouarsii

Brachycereus thouarsii is a unique cactus species native to Madagascar. It is known for its striking white flowers that bloom at night. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water. Its short stems and spines make it easily identifiable. It is a popular choice for collectors due to its beauty and rarity.
