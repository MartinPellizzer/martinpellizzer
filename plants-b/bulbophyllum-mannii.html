Bulbophyllum mannii

Bulbophyllum mannii, commonly known as the Kowhai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of small, white to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and other plants in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in the arid environments where it thrives.

Bulbophyllum mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
