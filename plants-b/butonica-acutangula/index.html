Butonica Acutangula

Cape honeysuckle, scientifically known as Butonica acutangula, is a vibrant flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is celebrated for its striking orange-red blooms that attract a variety of pollinators. This species thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
