Brassaia polyastra

Brassaia polyastra, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Brassaia polyastra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
