Begonia speciosa

Begonia speciosa, commonly known as the showy begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, bi-colored flowers that range from pink to white. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shady garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by the vibrant colors of its foliage and blossoms.
