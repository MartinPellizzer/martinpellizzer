Bredemeyera floribunda

Bredemeyera floribunda, commonly known as the velvetleaf balsam, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
