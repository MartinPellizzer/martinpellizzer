Brunfelsia floribunda

Brunfelsia floribunda, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the stage of bloom. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.
