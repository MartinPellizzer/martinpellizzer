Baliospermum calycinum var. micranthum

Baliospermum calycinum var. micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered bluecurls, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers arranged in dense, curling inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
