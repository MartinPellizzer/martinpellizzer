Bidens hybrida

Bidens hybrida, commonly known as yellow fleabane, is an annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, disc-shaped flowers with ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Bidens hybrida is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
