Bletilla scopulorum

Bletilla scopulorum, commonly known as the Chinese ground orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and showy, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically produces pale pink to white flowers with a distinct three-lobed lip, which is a distinguishing feature of the orchid family. Bletilla scopulorum thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
