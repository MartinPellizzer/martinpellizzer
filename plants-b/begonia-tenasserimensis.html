Begonia tenasserimensis

Begonia tenasserimensis, commonly known as the Himalayan begonia, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Begonia tenasserimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and blooms.
