Bifaria bifaria

Bifaria bifaria, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Bifaria bifaria is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
