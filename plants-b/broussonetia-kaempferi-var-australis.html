Broussonetia kaempferi var. australis

Broussonetia kaempferi var. australis, commonly known as the Australian broussonetia, is a variant of the broussonetia genus native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal regions and sandy soils. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing seeds. It is adapted to withstand saline and arid conditions, making it resilient in coastal environments.
