Billbergia nutans var. striata

Billbergia nutans var. striata, commonly known as the Zebra Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, striped leaves that give it its common name. The plant features a rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that collect rainwater, forming a central reservoir. Its flowers, which appear in clusters, are typically red or orange and bloom during the warmer months.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
