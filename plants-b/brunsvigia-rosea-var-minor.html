Brunsvigia rosea var. minor

Brunsvigia rosea var. minor, commonly known as the "rainbow lily" or "candelabra lily," is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features a striking cluster of tall, strap-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance ornamental plants.
