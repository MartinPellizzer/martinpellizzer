Barleria wilmsiana

Barleria wilmsiana, commonly known as the blue barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
