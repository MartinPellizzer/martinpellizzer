Borzicactus platinospinus

Borzicactus platinospinus, commonly known as the silver-spined cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines that give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for desert environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
