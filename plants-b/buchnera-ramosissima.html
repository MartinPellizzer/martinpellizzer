Buchnera ramosissima

Buchnera ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Buchnera ramosissima is known for its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact for extended periods, making it a popular choice in dried flower arrangements.
