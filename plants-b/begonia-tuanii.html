Begonia tuanii

Begonia tuanii, commonly known as the Tuan's begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, white, and pink.

Begonia tuanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
