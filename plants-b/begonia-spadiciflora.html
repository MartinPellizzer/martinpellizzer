Begonia spadiciflora

Begonia spadiciflora, commonly known as the spadix begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
