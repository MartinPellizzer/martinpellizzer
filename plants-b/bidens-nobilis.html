Bidens nobilis

Bidens nobilis, commonly known as the noble ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers with purple centers, often arranged in clusters at the ends of stems. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Bidens nobilis is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
