Bidens ruwenzoriensis

Bidens ruwenzoriensis, commonly known as the Ruwenzori ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
