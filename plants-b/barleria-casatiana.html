Barleria casatiana

Barleria casatiana, commonly known as the purple barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy flowers, which range in color from pink to purple, bloom in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Barleria casatiana is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in such conditions.
