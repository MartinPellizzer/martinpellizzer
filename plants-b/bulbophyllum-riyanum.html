Bulbophyllum riyanum

Bulbophyllum riyanum, commonly known as the Rian's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence and intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and often fragrant, displaying a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Bulbophyllum riyanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
