Barleria opaca

Barleria opaca, commonly known as silverleaf barleria, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or violet, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria opaca is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
