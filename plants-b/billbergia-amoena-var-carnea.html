Billbergia amoena var. carnea

Billbergia amoena var. carnea, commonly known as the Crimson Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround the inflorescence, giving it a dramatic and eye-catching appearance. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a natural reservoir for its epiphytic growth habit. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
