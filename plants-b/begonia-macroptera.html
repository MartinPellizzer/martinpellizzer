Begonia macroptera

Begonia macroptera, commonly known as the giant leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The leaves are usually a deep green with a waxy texture and prominent veins. It produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate contrast to its foliage.

This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental species for its foliage alone.
