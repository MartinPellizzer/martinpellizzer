Barleria setigera

Barleria setigera, commonly known as the zigzag barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Barleria setigera is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
