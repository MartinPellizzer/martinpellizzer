Borzicactus haynii var. atrispina

Borzicactus haynii var. atrispina, commonly known as the black-spined borzicactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in dense, dark spines, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant contrast to its otherwise desolate form. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a hardy choice for xerophytic gardens.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
