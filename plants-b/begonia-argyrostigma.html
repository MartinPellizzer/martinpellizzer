Begonia argyrostigma

Begonia argyrostigma, commonly known as the silver-stem begonia, is a striking plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that often have a silvery sheen on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
