Bupleurum gracilescens

Bupleurum gracilescens, commonly known as slender valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs. The plant has small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Bupleurum gracilescens is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cool, temperate climates.
