Blumea holosericea

Blumea holosericea, commonly known as the silverleaf blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is notable for its silver-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, silky hairs. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster, often blooming in the summer months.

Blumea holosericea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
