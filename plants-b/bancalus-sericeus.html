Bancalus sericeus

Bancalus sericeus, commonly known as the silky longhorned beetle, is a species of beetle belonging to the family Cerambycidae.

It is characterized by its elongated body and distinctive, silky appearance, with a metallic sheen that varies from reddish-brown to black. The adult beetle is typically found in forested areas, where it feeds on decaying wood and plant material.
