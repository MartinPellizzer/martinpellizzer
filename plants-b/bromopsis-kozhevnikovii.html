Bromopsis kozhevnikovii

Bromopsis kozhevnikovii, commonly known as the Kozhevnikov's bromus, is a species of grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both sterile and fertile florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes.

Bromopsis kozhevnikovii is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
