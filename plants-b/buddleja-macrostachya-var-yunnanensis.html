Buddleja macrostachya var. yunnanensis

Buddleja macrostachya var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan buddleja, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of pale purple to lavender flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
