Bletilla formosana f. kotoensis

Bletilla formosana f. kotoensis, commonly known as the Kotoensis Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features sympodial growth, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are strap-like and evergreen, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blossoms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
