Billbergia × blireiana

Billbergia × blireiana, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia natalensis and Billbergia recurvata.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features striking, colorful inflorescences that range from red to orange, often with white or yellow bracts. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Billbergia × blireiana is well-suited for indoor growing and requires bright, indirect light and moderate watering.
