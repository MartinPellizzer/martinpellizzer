Bunium mediterraneum

Bunium mediterraneum, commonly known as Mediterranean fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, feathery leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Bunium mediterraneum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its essential oils.
