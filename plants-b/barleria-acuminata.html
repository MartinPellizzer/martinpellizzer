Barleria acuminata

Barleria acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming from spring to autumn.

Barleria acuminata is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
