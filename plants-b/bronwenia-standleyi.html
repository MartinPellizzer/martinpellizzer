Bronwenia standleyi

Bronwenia standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's bushveld daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Bronwenia standleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
