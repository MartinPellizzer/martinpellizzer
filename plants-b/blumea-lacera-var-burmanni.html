Blumea lacera var. burmanni

Blumea lacera var. burmanni, commonly known as Burmann's blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and are followed by oval-shaped fruits.
