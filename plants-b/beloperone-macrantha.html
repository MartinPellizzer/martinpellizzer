Beloperone macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered beloperone, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family native to southern Africa.

Its flowers are primarily pollinated by birds, particularly sunbirds, due to their nectar-rich structure and bright coloration.

Beloperone macrantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
