Brassica × turicensis

Brassica × turicensis, commonly known as the ornamental kale, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of purple, red, and green. The plant is typically grown for its ornamental value rather than for edible purposes. It thrives in cool climates and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Brassica × turicensis is a short-lived perennial that prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
