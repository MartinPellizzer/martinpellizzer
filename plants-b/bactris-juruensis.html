Bactris juruensis

Bactris juruensis, commonly known as the Juruena palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces edible fruits that are often consumed by both humans and wildlife. Its botanical features make it an important component of the rainforest ecosystem.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has potential uses in sustainable agriculture.
