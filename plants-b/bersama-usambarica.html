Bersama usambarica

Bersama usambarica, commonly known as the Usambaric tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a dark green upper surface. The flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow, and the tree produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

It is valued for its timber and is often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks.
