Berberis aristatoserrulata

Berberis aristatoserrulata, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and yellowish-green foliage that turns vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Berberis aristatoserrulata is also valued for its medicinal properties, with its roots containing berberine, a compound used in traditional medicine.
