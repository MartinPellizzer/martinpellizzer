Bulbophyllum rubrobrunneopapillosum

Bulbophyllum rubrobrunneopapillosum, commonly known as the red-brown papillate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, branching stems and fleshy, green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of red and brown, often clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
