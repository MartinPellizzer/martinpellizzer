Besleria hispida

Besleria hispida, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This tropical plant is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that have a soft, hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, typically blooming during the warmer months.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a foliage plant in shaded gardens.
