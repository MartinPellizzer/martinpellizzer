Begonia humboldtiana

Begonia humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia humboldtiana is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
