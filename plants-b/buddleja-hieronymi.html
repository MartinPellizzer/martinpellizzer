Buddleja hieronymi

Buddleja hieronymi, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces panicle-like inflorescences of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja hieronymi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom profusely throughout the summer.
