Bambusa klossii

Bambusa klossii, commonly known as the Kloss bamboo, is a tall, clumping bamboo native to Indonesia.

It features slender, hollow stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are long, narrow, and often have a bluish-green color, contributing to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This bamboo is valued for its fast growth rate and ability to form dense stands, making it useful in landscaping and erosion control.
