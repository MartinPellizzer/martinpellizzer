Bauhinia cardiophylla

Bauhinia cardiophylla, commonly known as the heartleaf bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in forested areas or along riverbanks.

Bauhinia cardiophylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
