Begonia filibracteosa

Begonia filibracteosa, commonly known as the slender bract begonia, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped or ovate leaves with a velvety texture, and its bracts are slender and often resemble leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Begonia filibracteosa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
