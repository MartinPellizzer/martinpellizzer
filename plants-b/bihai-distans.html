Bihai distans

Bihai distans, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking white or pink spathes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers inside a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Bihai distans is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
