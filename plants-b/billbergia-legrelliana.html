Billbergia legrelliana

Billbergia legrelliana, commonly known as the red-tipped billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are dark green with red-tipped margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a dramatic visual element. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in ornamental horticulture due to its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
