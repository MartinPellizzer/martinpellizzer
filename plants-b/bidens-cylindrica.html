Bidens cylindrica

Bidens cylindrica, commonly known as the slender tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its cylindrical flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and meadows. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Bidens cylindrica is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
