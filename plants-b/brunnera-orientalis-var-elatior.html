Brunnera orientalis var. elatior

Brunnera orientalis var. elatior, commonly known as Siberian bugloss, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Europe and western Asia.

It features heart-shaped, silvery-green leaves with a fuzzy texture and produces striking blue to violet flowers in early to mid-summer. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is also noted for its hardiness and adaptability to a variety of garden conditions.
