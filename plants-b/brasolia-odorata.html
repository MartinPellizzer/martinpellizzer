Brasolia odorata

Brasolia odorata, commonly known as the sweet scent plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves and a woody stem, typical of many tropical shrubs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brasolia odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
