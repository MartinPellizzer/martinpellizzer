Begonia patens

Begonia patens, commonly known as the hanging begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia patens is popular as a decorative plant for hanging baskets and indoor gardens due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
