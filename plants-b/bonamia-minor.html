Bonamia minor

Bonamia minor, commonly known as the lesser bonamia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Bonamia minor is often found in wetlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
