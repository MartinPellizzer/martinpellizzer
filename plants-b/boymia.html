Boymia

Boymia, commonly known as the bluebell vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue and purple flowers that bloom in clusters along its trailing stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in fine hairs. Boymia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing along riverbanks or in dense undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and hanging baskets.
