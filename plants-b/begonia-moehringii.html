Begonia moehringii

Begonia moehringii, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety silver-green leaves with a distinctive, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
