Begonia sanguinea

Begonia sanguinea, commonly known as the crimson begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with green and silver markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia sanguinea is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
