Bulbophyllum exiguum

Bulbophyllum exiguum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum exiguum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
