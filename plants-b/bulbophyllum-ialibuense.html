Bulbophyllum ialibuense

Bulbophyllum ialibuense, commonly known as the Ialibu orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in low-light conditions. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal inflorescences and can last for several weeks.

Bulbophyllum ialibuense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
