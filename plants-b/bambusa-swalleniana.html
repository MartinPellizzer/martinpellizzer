Bambusa swalleniana

Bambusa swalleniana, commonly known as the giant bamboo, is a tall, clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sturdy, hollow stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to form dense clumps over time.

Bambusa swalleniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional construction and crafts.
