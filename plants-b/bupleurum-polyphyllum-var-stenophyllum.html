Bupleurum polyphyllum var. stenophyllum

Bupleurum polyphyllum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and the Korean Peninsula.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often densely packed. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pale yellow.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
