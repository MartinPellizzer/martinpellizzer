Begonia xochiatencana

Begonia xochiatencana, commonly known as the Mexican flame begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow markings. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for gardeners and floral enthusiasts.
