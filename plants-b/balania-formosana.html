Balania formosana

Balania formosana, commonly known as the Formosa bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lanceolate leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a cool climate for optimal growth.
