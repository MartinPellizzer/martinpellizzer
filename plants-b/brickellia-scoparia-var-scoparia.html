Brickellia scoparia var. scoparia

Brickellia scoparia var. scoparia, commonly known as narrowleaf brickellbush, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowering period occurs from spring to early summer, and it is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.

The species is known for its drought tolerance and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
