Bulbophyllum cuspidipetalum

Bulbophyllum cuspidipetalum, commonly known as the sharp-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with intricate patterns and are borne on long, slender stems.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
