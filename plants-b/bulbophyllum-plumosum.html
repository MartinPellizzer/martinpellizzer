Bulbophyllum plumosum

Bulbophyllum plumosum, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of long, feathery bracts that give the plant its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
