Bambusa brunneoaciculia

Bambusa brunneoaciculia, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are a mix of yellow and green, giving it a unique visual appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Bambusa brunneoaciculia is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic value and ability to create a tropical ambiance in gardens.
