Begonia josephi

Begonia josephi, commonly known as the Joseph's begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
