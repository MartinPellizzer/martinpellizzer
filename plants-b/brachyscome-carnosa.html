Brachyscome carnosa

Brachyscome carnosa, commonly known as the woolly everlasting or woolly dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that produces dense clusters of small, fluffy white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, woolly stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.
