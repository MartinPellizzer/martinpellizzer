Buddleja perfoliata

Buddleja perfoliata, commonly known as the woolly buddleia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun. Buddleja perfoliata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its perennial growth habit makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, hardy plants.
