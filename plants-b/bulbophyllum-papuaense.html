Bulbophyllum papuaense

Bulbophyllum papuaense, commonly known as the Papua orchid, is a species of orchid native to the island of New Guinea.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and colorful, displaying a range of hues such as yellow, orange, and red.

Due to its habitat and appearance, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
