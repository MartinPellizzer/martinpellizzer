Brassaia oblongifolia

Brassaia oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Brassaia oblongifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and floral display.
