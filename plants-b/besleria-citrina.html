Besleria citrina

Besleria citrina, commonly known as the yellow flame orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Besleria citrina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
