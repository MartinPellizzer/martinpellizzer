Begonia daunhitam

Begonia daunhitam, commonly known as the Java begonia, is a tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming during the warm season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
