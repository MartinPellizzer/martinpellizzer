Begonia edanoi

Begonia edanoi, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. Its foliage is thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its native arid environment.

Begonia edanoi is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
