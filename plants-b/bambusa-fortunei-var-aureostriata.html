Bambusa fortunei var. aureostriata

Bambusa fortunei var. aureostriata, commonly known as the golden stripe bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its golden-yellow stripes on the culms, which contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. This variety grows as a clump-forming bamboo, reaching heights of up to 10 meters, and is prized for its aesthetic appeal in landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its fast growth rate and air-purifying qualities further enhance its value as a decorative and functional plant.
