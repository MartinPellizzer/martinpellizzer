Bulbophyllum dawongense

Bulbophyllum dawongense, commonly known as the Dawong orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that are often yellow or green with contrasting purple or red markings. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
