Bulbophyllum rubropunctatum

Bulbophyllum rubropunctatum, commonly known as the red-spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically orange or red in color, with distinctive red spots on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its striking appearance.
