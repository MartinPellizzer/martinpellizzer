Barnadesia spinosa var. recurvata

Barnadesia spinosa var. recurvata, commonly known as the recurved barnadesia, is a rare and distinctive plant species characterized by its unique morphology.

It features spiny, recurved leaves that give it a striking appearance, often resembling a thorny shrub. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from yellow to orange.
