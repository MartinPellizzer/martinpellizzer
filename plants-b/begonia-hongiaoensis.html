Begonia hongiaoensis

Begonia hongiaoensis, commonly known as the Hongiao begonia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural collections.
