Bulbophyllum menghaiense

Bulbophyllum menghaiense, commonly known as the Menghai orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually fleshy and can store water, adapting to its natural habitat.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.
