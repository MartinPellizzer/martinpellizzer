Bulbostylis capillaris var. contracta

Bulbostylis capillaris var. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale green flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dense, fibrous texture.

This variety is often found in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
