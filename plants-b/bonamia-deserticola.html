Bonamia deserticola

Bonamia deserticola, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers during the cooler months. Its root system is deep and fibrous, allowing it to access water from lower soil layers.

Bonamia deserticola plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
