Begonia divaricata f. minor

Begonia divaricata f. minor, commonly known as the miniature begonia or compact begonia, is a compact, trailing plant with small, heart-shaped leaves.

It produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets due to its trailing growth habit.

Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to its ornamental value.
