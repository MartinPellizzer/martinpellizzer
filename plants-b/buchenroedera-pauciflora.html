Buchenroedera pauciflora

Buchenroedera pauciflora, commonly known as the rare ghost orchid, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, often resembling ghostly apparitions. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient absorption. Its limited distribution and specialized habitat make it highly vulnerable to environmental changes.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is protected in many regions.
