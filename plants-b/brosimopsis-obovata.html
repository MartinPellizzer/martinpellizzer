Brosimopsis obovata

Brosimopsis obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaved brosim.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Brosimopsis obovata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany.
