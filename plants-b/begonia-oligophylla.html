Begonia oligophylla

Begonia oligophylla, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a striking plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that are often variegated with shades of white and green. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or red, typically blooming in the spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
