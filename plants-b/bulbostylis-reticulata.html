Bulbostylis reticulata

Bulbostylis reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with slender stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, white or pale green flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and wetlands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
