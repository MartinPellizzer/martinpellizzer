Begonia quadripetiolata

Begonia quadripetiolata, commonly known as the four-leaf begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, and its foliage features distinctive, waxy leaves with a velvety texture. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
