Bidens biternata var. biternata

Bidens biternata var. biternata, commonly known as the three-parted beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.
