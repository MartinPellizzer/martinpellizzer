Begonia mindanaensis

Begonia mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, and its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
