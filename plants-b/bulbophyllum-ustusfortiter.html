Bulbophyllum ustusfortiter

Bulbophyllum ustusfortiter, commonly known as the hardy bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in drier conditions. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

This orchid thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light to flourish.
