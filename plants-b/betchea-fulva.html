Betchea fulva

Betchea fulva, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with green or brown markings. Betchea fulva thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
