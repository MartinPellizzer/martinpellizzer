Blechnum crenulatum

Blechnum crenulatum, commonly known as the serrated holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, serrated fronds. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rock crevices. Its fronds are leathery and have a rugged, textured appearance.

Blechnum crenulatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
