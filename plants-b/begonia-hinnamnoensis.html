Begonia hinnamnoensis

Begonia hinnamnoensis, commonly known as the Hinnamnoensis begonia, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or pink in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
