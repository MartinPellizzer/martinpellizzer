Begonia lamxayana

Begonia lamxayana, commonly known as the Lamxayana begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals, which serve to attract pollinators. The plant typically features heart-shaped or oval leaves with a smooth texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia lamxayana is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
