Bobua glaucescens

Bobua glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green bobua, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its small, blue-green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
