Begonia ningmingensis var. bella

Begonia ningmingensis var. bella, commonly known as the Ningming bell begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink, often with intricate patterns. The foliage is lush and broad, featuring a glossy texture that enhances its ornamental appeal. This variety thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts and botanists.
