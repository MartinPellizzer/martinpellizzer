Berchemia parviflora

Berchemia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered berchemia, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in winter, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and long flowering period make it a popular choice for attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
