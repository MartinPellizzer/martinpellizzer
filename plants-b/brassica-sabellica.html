Brassica sabellica

Brassica sabellica, commonly known as the Italian kale or curly kale, is a hardy biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, crinkled leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly bitter taste. The plant produces yellow flowers in the second year of growth, which are followed by small, round pods containing seeds. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its edible leaves and ornamental value.

Brassica sabellica is a versatile plant that is resistant to pests and diseases, making it a popular choice for home gardens.
