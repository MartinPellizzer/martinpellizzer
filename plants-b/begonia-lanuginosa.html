Begonia lanuginosa

Begonia lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky begonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Begonia lanuginosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
