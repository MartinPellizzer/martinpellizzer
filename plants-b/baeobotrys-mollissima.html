Baeobotrys mollissima

Baeobotrys mollissima, commonly known as the silky baeobotrys, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
