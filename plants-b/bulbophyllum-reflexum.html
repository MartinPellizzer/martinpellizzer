Bulbophyllum reflexum

Bulbophyllum reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive reflexed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Bulbophyllum reflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
