Begonia semongkatensis

Begonia semongkatensis, commonly known as the Semongkat begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom atop tall, sturdy stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
