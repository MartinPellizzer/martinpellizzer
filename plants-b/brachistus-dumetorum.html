Brachistus dumetorum

Brachistus dumetorum, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a low-growing habit with multiple stems and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
