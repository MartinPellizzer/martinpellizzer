Bulbophyllum laoticum

Bulbophyllum laoticum, commonly known as the Laotian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color, with a distinctive lip structure. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in less favorable conditions.

Bulbophyllum laoticum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
