Bursa Anglica

Bursa anglica is a rare and fascinating plant native to the British Isles. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and rocky crevices. Its unique appearance features small, cup-like structures that resemble miniature urns. The plant is known for its delicate white flowers that bloom in early spring. Due to habitat loss, Bursa anglica is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
