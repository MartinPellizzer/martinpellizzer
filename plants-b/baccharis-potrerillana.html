Baccharis potrerillana

Baccharis potrerillana, commonly known as the Potrerillo baccharis, is a shrub native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its gray-green, narrow leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
