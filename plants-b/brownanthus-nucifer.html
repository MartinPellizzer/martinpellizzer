Brownanthus nucifer

Brownanthus nucifer, commonly known as the nutmeg orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that resemble small orchids. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and help it absorb moisture from the air, while its leaves are broad and glossy.

The plant is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties.
