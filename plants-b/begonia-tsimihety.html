Begonia tsimihety

Begonia tsimihety, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, heart-shaped leaves. The leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns and can vary in color from deep green to dark purple. It produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
