Besleria illustris

Besleria illustris, commonly known as the showy besleria, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces pendulous, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant blooms. Its leaves are typically broad, elliptical, and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Besleria illustris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
