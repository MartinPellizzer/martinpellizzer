Bambusa shimadae

Bambusa shimadae, commonly known as the Shimada bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and a dense, clumping growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is closely related to other bamboo species.

Bambusa shimadae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
