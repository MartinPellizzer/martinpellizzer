Bistorta × rhaetica

Bistorta × rhaetica, commonly known as the hybrid bistort, is a perennial herb native to the Alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its rhizomatous growth and distinctive, often colorful leaf patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a striking ornamental plant in horticulture, though it is less commonly cultivated than its parent species.
