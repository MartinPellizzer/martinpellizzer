Buddleja ignea

Buddleja ignea, commonly known as the red bush or flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has slender, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Buddleja ignea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salt spray.
