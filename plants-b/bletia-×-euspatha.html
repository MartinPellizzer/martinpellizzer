Bletia × euspatha

Bletia × euspatha, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Bletia obtusa and Bletia stricta.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical forests of Central and South America. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30–60 cm, featuring broad, strap-like leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in a spike with a strong, spicy scent.

Bletia × euspatha is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
