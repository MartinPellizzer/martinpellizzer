Begonia kotoensis

Begonia kotoensis, commonly known as the Koto begonia, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or indoor cultivation. Its ornamental value is enhanced by the contrasting foliage and the delicate appearance of its blooms.

Begonia kotoensis is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
