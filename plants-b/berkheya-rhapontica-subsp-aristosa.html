Berkheya rhapontica subsp. aristosa

Berkheya rhapontica subsp.

aristosa, commonly known as the arrowhead berkheya, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features a robust, branched stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, terminal clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often have a distinctive arrowhead shape.

This species is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
