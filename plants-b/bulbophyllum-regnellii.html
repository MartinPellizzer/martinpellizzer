Bulbophyllum regnellii

Bulbophyllum regnellii, commonly known as the Regnell's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the genus Bulbophyllum in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
