Bulbophyllum fascinator

Bulbophyllum fascinator, commonly known as the Orchid with a Fascination, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often strikingly patterned flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and red. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

The flowers are typically short-lived but are highly prized for their ornamental value in horticulture.
