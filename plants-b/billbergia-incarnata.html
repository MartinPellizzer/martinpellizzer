Billbergia incarnata

Billbergia incarnata, commonly known as the pink billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a pink or red stripe along the midrib. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from pink to red, often resembling a flower. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia incarnata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
