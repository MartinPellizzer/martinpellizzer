Begonia yingjiangensis

Begonia yingjiangensis, commonly known as the Yingjiang begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage displays a variety of colors, including deep greens, purples, and reds, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The flowers, which are usually red or orange, bloom in late summer and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
