Bletia colemanii

Bletia colemanii, commonly known as the Coleman's bletia, is a rare orchid species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant features a compact rhizome with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It is characterized by its unique morphology and is often found in humid, lowland habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
