Bulbine frutescens

Bulbine frutescens, commonly known as the red-hot cat's tail, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, fleshy stem with vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
