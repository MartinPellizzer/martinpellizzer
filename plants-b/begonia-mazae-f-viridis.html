Begonia mazae f. viridis

Begonia mazae f. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Begonia, is a hybrid form of the species Begonia mazae.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with lighter green or yellow markings. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers, typically in clusters. It is a compact, bushy plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is popular for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
