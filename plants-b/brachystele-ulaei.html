Brachystele ulaei

Brachystele ulaei, commonly known as the dwarf staghorn fern, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features a short, thick rhizome with a dense covering of leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its fronds are leathery and curling, often forming a compact, umbrella-like structure.

Brachystele ulaei thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
