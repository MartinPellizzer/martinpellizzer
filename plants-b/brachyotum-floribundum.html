Brachyotum floribundum

Brachyotum floribundum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Brachyotum floribundum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
