Brathys moranensis

Brathys moranensis, commonly known as the Madagascar spider orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with small, clustered flowers that resemble delicate spiders. Brathys moranensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the specific microclimates of its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and distinctive morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
