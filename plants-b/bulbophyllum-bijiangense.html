Bulbophyllum bijiangense

Bulbophyllum bijiangense, commonly known as the Bijiang orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
