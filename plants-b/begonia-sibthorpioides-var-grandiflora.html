Begonia sibthorpioides var. grandiflora

Begonia sibthorpioides var. grandiflora, commonly known as the giant begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
