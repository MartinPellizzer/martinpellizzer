Bulbophyllum echinulus

Bulbophyllum echinulus, commonly known as the spiny bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, hairy pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
