Bulbophyllum bidoupense

Bulbophyllum bidoupense, commonly known as the Bidoup Mountain Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to the humid rainforests of Vietnam.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns on their petals. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its native environment.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a threatened species requiring conservation efforts.
