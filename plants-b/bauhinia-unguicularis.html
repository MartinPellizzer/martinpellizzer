Bauhinia unguicularis

Bauhinia unguicularis, commonly known as the scarlet bauhinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately compound leaves with five to seven leaflets. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. Its fruit is a flat, leathery pod that splits open to release seeds.

Bauhinia unguicularis is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
