Bernardia mayana

Bernardia mayana, commonly known as the golden balsa, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its bark and leaves have been traditionally used in local medicine for their purported healing properties.
