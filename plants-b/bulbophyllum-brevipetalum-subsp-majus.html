Bulbophyllum brevipetalum subsp. majus

Bulbophyllum brevipetalum subsp.

majus, commonly known as the giant bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in length.

The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters and are adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors.

This orchid prefers warm, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the wild.
