Bromus erectus subsp. uniculmis

Bromus erectus subsp.

uniculmis, commonly known as the slender brome, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces spikelets that are typically arranged in dense clusters along the stem. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils. Bromus erectus subsp.

uniculmis is often found in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas.
