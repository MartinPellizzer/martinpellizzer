Bromus rigens var. gussonei

Bromus rigens var. gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's brome, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are characteristic of the Poaceae family.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to dry conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
