Begonia diversistipulata

Begonia diversistipulata, commonly known as the variegated begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often variegated foliage that displays shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom from spring to summer. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can be either simple or lobed, depending on the variety.

Begonia diversistipulata thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
