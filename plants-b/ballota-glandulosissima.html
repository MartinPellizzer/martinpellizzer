Ballota glandulosissima

Ballota glandulosissima, commonly known as the sticky ballota, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Ballota glandulosissima is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
