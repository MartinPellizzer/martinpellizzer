Bulbophyllum trifilum subsp. trifilum

Bulbophyllum trifilum subsp.

trifilum, commonly known as the Three-Filament Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive inflorescence with three elongated, filament-like structures.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
