Bernardia axillaris subsp. axillaris

Bernardia axillaris subsp.

axillaris, commonly known as the axillary bernardia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes of South America. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, temperate to subtropical regions.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
