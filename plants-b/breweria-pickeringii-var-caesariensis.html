Breweria pickeringii var. caesariensis

Breweria pickeringii var. caesariensis, commonly known as the Caesar's breweria, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers, which bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
