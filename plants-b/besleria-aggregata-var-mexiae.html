Besleria aggregata var. mexiae

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
