Bothriospermum perenne

Bothriospermum perenne, commonly known as the everlasting flower, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen throughout the year.

Bothriospermum perenne is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
