Begonia harmandii

Begonia harmandii, commonly known as the Harmand's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive dark green hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia harmandii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
