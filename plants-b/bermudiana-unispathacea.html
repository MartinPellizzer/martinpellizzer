Bermudiana unispathacea

Bermudiana unispathacea, commonly known as the Bermuda grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to the subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to sandy soils and can tolerate drought conditions.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly and form dense mats.
