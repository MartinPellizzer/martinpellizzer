Bustamenta ovata

Bustamenta ovata, commonly known as the oval bustamenta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a woody stem and small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
