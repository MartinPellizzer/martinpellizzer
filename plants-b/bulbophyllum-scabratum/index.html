Bulbophyllum Scabratum

Bulbophyllum scabratum is a fascinating orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique and striking appearance, this plant features elongated pseudobulbs and intricate floral structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the undergrowth of rainforests. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the world of horticulture. Its rarity and specific care requirements make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
