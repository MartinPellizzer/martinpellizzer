Baccharis nitida

Baccharis nitida, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, white to yellowish flowers that appear in clusters, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a soft, fuzzy texture.

Baccharis nitida is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
