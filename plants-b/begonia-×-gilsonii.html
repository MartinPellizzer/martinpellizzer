Begonia × gilsonii

Begonia × gilsonii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Begoniaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pavonina, known for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms. The plant typically produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are often borne in clusters. It is a popular choice for container gardening and as a bedding plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.

Begonia × gilsonii thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
