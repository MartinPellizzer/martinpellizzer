Barleriola solanifolia var. humilis

Barleriola solanifolia var. humilis, commonly known as the low solanifolia barleriola, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
