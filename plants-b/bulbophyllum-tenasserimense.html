Bulbophyllum tenasserimense

Bulbophyllum tenasserimense, commonly known as the Tenasserim Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in humid, shaded environments.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
