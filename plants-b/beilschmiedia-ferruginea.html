Beilschmiedia ferruginea

Beilschmiedia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty beilschmiedia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with a reddish-brown underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that are orange to red when mature.
