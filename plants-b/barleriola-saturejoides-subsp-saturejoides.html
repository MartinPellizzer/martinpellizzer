Barleriola saturejoides subsp. saturejoides

Barleriola saturejoides subsp.

saturejoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf burnet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features slender, aromatic stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences and are typically pink to purple in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
