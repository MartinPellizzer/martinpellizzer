Brassaia octandra

Brassaia octandra, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brassaia octandra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
