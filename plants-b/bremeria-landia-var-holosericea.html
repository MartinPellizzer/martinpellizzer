Bremeria landia var. holosericea

Bremeria landia var. holosericea, commonly known as the velvetleaf hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses in tropical and subtropical regions.
