Begonia smithiae

Begonia smithiae, commonly known as the Smith's begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Begonia smithiae is also valued for its ability to produce aerial roots, which can be used for propagation.
