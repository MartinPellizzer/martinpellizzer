Bromus carinatus var. densus

Bromus carinatus var. densus, commonly known as the dense brome, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense growth habit and slender, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while its seeds are small and dispersed by wind.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
