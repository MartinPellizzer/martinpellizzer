Bonamia patens

Bonamia patens, commonly known as the broadleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple. Its fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.

Bonamia patens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
