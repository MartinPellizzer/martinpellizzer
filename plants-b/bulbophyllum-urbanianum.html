Bulbophyllum urbanianum

Bulbophyllum urbanianum, commonly known as the Urbanian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually narrow and dark green, adapted for efficient photosynthesis in shaded conditions.

Bulbophyllum urbanianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
