Begonia zhongyangiana

Begonia zhongyangiana, commonly known as the Zhongyang begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a visually appealing yet relatively low-maintenance plant.
