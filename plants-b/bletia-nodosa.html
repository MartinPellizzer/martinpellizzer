Bletia nodosa

Bletia nodosa, commonly known as the nodose bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bletia nodosa is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
