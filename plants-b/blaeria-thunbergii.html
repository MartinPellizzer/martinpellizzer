Blaeria thunbergii

Blaeria thunbergii, commonly known as the Swedish buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to northern Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers with five petals, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.

Blaeria thunbergii is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its cheerful appearance.
