Begonia awongii

Begonia awongii, commonly known as the Hainan begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
