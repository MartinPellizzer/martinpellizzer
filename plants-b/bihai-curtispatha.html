Bihai curtispatha

Bihai curtispatha, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Plant, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet spadix surrounded by a white to pale blue spathe. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique coloration and unusual appearance make it a sought-after specimen in botanical collections.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled greenhouse settings.
