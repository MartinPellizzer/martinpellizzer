Berchemia nana

Berchemia nana, commonly known as the dwarf berchemia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in spring, which are borne in clusters along the stems. Berchemia nana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens or containers.

It is also noted for its drought tolerance once established.
