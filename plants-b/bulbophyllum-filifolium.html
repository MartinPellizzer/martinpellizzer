Bulbophyllum filifolium

Bulbophyllum filifolium, commonly known as the thread-leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, thread-like leaves that are adapted for moisture retention in its natural habitat. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
