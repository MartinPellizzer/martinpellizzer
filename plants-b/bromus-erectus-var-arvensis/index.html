Bromus Erectus Var. Arvensis

Bromus erectus var. arvensis is a common grass species found in various habitats across temperate regions. It is known for its upright growth habit and distinctive seed heads. This variant is often seen in disturbed soils and agricultural fields. Its adaptability makes it both a useful forage plant and a potential weed. Understanding its biology is key to managing its presence in different ecosystems.
