Bromus elatior

Bromus elatior, commonly known as the tall brome, is a perennial grass species native to Eurasia.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

The inflorescence consists of a loose panicle, bearing small, spikelet-like flowers that are wind-pollinated.
