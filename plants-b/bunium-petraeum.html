Bunium petraeum

Bunium petraeum, commonly known as the rock fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats. Its seeds are small and contain essential oils, which contribute to its strong, spicy scent.

Bunium petraeum is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
