Bonniera

Bonniera, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the Campanulaceae family.

It consists of several species, most notably Bonniera fritillariaeflora, which is native to the western United States. Bonniera species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant blooms.
