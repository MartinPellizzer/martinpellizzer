Borassus akeassii

Borassus akeassii, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a tall, slender palm native to tropical Africa.

It features a tall, unbranched trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. The plant produces large, feather-like leaves that form a broad crown, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible fruits with a fibrous husk.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important species in its native ecosystems.
