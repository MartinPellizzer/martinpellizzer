Bidens acuticaulis

Bidens acuticaulis, commonly known as the sharpleaf tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features serrated, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disk flowers. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and disturbed areas.
