Bunium nivale

Bunium nivale, commonly known as snow buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Bunium nivale is adapted to high altitudes and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
