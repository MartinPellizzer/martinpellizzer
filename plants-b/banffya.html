Banffya

Banffya, commonly known as the Banff yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Canada, particularly in the Banff National Park area.

The plant typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in high-altitude environments. Banffya has narrow, silvery-green leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.
