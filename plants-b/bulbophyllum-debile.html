Bulbophyllum debile

Bulbophyllum debile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its bulb-like structures, which store water and nutrients, are an adaptation to its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum debile is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
