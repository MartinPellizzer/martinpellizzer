Bouchea prismatica

Bouchea prismatica, commonly known as the prismatea or prismatea shrub, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with dense, gray-green foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Bouchea prismatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
