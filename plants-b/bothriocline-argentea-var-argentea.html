Bothriocline argentea var. argentea

Bothriocline argentea var. argentea, commonly known as silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its dandelion-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
