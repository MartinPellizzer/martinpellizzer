Bechium rhodolepis

Bechium rhodolepis, commonly known as the red-flowered rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and purple flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

Bechium rhodolepis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
