Bulbophyllum tectipetalum var. maximum

Bulbophyllum tectipetalum var. maximum, commonly known as the Giant Tectipetalum Bulbophyllum, is a striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, with vibrant colors ranging from yellow to red. The plant features thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, aiding its survival in tropical and subtropical environments. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its ornamental flowers.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
