Bromus eximius

Bromus eximius, commonly known as the exclamation grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces spikelets that are often adorned with long, slender bristles, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Bromus eximius is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
