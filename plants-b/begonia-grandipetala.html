Begonia grandipetala

Begonia grandipetala, commonly known as the giant petal begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often green with a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Begonia grandipetala is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
