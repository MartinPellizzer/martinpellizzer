Batatas cissoides var. integrifolia

Batatas cissoides var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a rhizomatous root system that produces edible starch-rich tubers. The leaves are large, broad, and have smooth margins, distinguishing it from other varieties. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is valued for its high yield and nutritional content, making it an important crop in certain regions.
