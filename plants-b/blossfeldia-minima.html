Blossfeldia minima

Blossfeldia minima, commonly known as the miniature Christmas cactus, is a compact, epiphytic cactus native to the tropical rainforests of Brazil.

It features short, segmented stems that bear clusters of small, colorful flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, a characteristic adaptation to its humid, shaded habitat. It blooms during the holiday season, typically between November and January, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
