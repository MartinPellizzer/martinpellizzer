Begonia comorensis

Begonia comorensis, commonly known as the Comoro begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Comoros Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia comorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
