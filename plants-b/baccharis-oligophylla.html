Baccharis oligophylla

Baccharis oligophylla, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are arranged in clusters, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Baccharis oligophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
