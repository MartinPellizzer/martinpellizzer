Begonia × mutabilis

Begonia × mutabilis, commonly known as the ever-coloring begonia, is a hybrid plant renowned for its variable flower colors.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its ability to change flower color over time.

Begonia × mutabilis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
