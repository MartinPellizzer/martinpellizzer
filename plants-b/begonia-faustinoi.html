Begonia faustinoi

Begonia faustinoi, commonly known as the Faustino's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to dark red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the day. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a prized addition to botanical collections and gardens.
