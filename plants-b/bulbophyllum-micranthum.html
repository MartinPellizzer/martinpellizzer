Bulbophyllum micranthum

Bulbophyllum micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the plant's unique structure.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
