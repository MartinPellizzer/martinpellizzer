Begonia aurantiaca

Begonia aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its foliage is often glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants.
