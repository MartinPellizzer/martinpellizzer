Bauhinia bidentata var. breviflora

Bauhinia bidentata var. breviflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves, which are compound and divided into small, slender leaflets. The plant produces showy, pale pink or white flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming in the summer months. Its fruits are legumes, containing several seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

Bauhinia bidentata var. breviflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
