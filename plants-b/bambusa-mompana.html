Bambusa mompana

Bambusa mompana, commonly known as the giant bamboo, is a tall, clumping bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, hollow stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its culms are strong and flexible, making it suitable for construction and crafts.

Bambusa mompana thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
