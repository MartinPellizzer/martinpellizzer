Balsamaria inophyllum

Balsamaria inophyllum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Balsamaria inophyllum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
