Bulbophyllum filovagans

Bulbophyllum filovagans, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with elongated, thin pseudobulbs and narrow leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum filovagans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
