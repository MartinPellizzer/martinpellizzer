Billbergia brongniartii

Billbergia brongniartii, commonly known as the pink billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, spathaceous flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom from the center of the rosette. Its foliage is often adorned with white or silver markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Billbergia brongniartii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
