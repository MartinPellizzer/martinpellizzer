Bletia × Irrorata

Bletia × irrorata is a captivating hybrid orchid known for its striking appearance and unique characteristics. Native to the tropical regions of the Americas, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers display a blend of colors, often featuring a mix of white and yellow hues. The plant is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation. With its elegant form and vibrant blooms, Bletia × irrorata stands out as a remarkable addition to any botanical collection.
