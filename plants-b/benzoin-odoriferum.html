Benzoin odoriferum

Benzoin odoriferum, commonly known as the fragrant benzoin tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, aromatic resin, which is harvested from the bark and used in perfumery and traditional medicine. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by woody capsules containing aromatic seeds.

The resin, known as benjoin, has a sweet, balsamic scent and is valued for its medicinal and cosmetic properties.
