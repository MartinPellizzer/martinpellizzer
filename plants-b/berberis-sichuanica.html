Berberis sichuanica

Berberis sichuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for landscaping in temperate climates.
