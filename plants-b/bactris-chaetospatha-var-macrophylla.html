Bactris chaetospatha var. macrophylla

Bactris chaetospatha var. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant palma.

This variety is a member of the Arecaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar to those of other palm species, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
