Berkheya Spinosa

Berkheya spinosa is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its unique appearance and ecological importance. It features spiny stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, making it a standout in its habitat. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting well to harsh environmental conditions. Its role in supporting local wildlife and its ornamental value make it a notable species in botanical studies. Despite its beauty, Berkheya spinosa remains relatively underappreciated in global horticultural circles.
