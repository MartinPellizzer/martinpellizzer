Bauhinia volkensii

Bauhinia volkensii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
