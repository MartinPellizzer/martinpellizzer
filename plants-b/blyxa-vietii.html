Blyxa vietii

Blyxa vietii, commonly known as the Vietnamese broadleaf water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often submerged, with some emergent foliage near the water's surface. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize new areas effectively.

Blyxa vietii is valued for its ability to provide habitat for aquatic life and its role in water purification.
