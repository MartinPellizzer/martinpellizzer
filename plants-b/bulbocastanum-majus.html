Bulbocastanum majus

Bulbocastanum majus, commonly known as the giant bulbous plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that consists of a spadix surrounded by a broad, colorful spathe. The plant produces large, fleshy bulbs that serve as storage organs for nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is native to parts of South America.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
