Bulbostylis cardiocarpa var. holubii

Bulbostylis cardiocarpa var. holubii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbostylis, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, white to pale blue flowers, which are typically clustered in dense, spike-like structures. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green coloration.

This variety is often found in moist, grassy areas and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
