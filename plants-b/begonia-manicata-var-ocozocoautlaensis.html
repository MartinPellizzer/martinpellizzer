Begonia manicata var. ocozocoautlaensis

Begonia manicata var. ocozocoautlaensis, commonly known as the Ocozocoautla begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
