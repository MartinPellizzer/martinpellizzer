Bulbophyllum cycloglossum

Bulbophyllum cycloglossum, commonly known as the cycloglossum orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
