Bulbophyllum henanense

Bulbophyllum henanense, commonly known as the Henan Bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to China.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth and leafy stems. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
