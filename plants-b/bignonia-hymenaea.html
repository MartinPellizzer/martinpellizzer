Bignonia hymenaea

Bignonia hymenaea, commonly known as the evergreen bignonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
