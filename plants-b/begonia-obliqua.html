Begonia obliqua

Begonia obliqua, commonly known as the zigzag begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that exhibit a zigzag pattern along the midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or red, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Begonia obliqua is valued for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
