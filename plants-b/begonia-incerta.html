Begonia incerta

Begonia incerta, commonly known as the uncertain begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shady garden beds or container cultivation. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to the landscape.

Begonia incerta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, though it is somewhat sensitive to temperature extremes.
