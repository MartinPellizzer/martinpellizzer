Butonica rumphiana

Butonica rumphiana, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a complex inflorescence with numerous flowers arranged in a raceme. It thrives in tropical climates and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Butonica rumphiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
