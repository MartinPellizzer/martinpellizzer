Betchea myriantha

Betchea myriantha, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters.

Betchea myriantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
