Begonia rwandensis

Begonia rwandensis, commonly known as the Rwandan begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Rwanda.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its attractive, fleshy leaves and vibrant, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within its native habitat. Its foliage often displays a variety of colors, ranging from green to reddish tones.

Begonia rwandensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
