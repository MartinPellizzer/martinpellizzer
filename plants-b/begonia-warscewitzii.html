Begonia warscewitzii

Begonia warscewitzii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is popular among gardeners seeking to add visual interest to indoor or outdoor spaces.
