Bletia purpurata var. pallida

Bletia purpurata var. pallida, commonly known as the pale-flowered bletia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces striking, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
