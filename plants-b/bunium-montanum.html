Bunium montanum

Bunium montanum, commonly known as mountain fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, feathery leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in umbels. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and herbal applications.
