Bulbophyllum lancisepalum

Bulbophyllum lancisepalum, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its linear, sword-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces striking inflorescences with elongated, pale green to white flowers that have a distinct, often fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in many botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
