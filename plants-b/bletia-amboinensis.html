Bletia amboinensis

Bletia amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bletia amboinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
