Botrychium erosum

Botrychium erosum, commonly known as the narrow fern.

It is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Osmundaceae. The plant features a rhizome that produces two types of leaves: sterile fronds and sporangiophores. The sporangiophores bear clusters of spore cases, which are distinctive in shape and arrangement.

Botrychium erosum typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
