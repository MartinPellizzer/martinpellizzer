Buddleja myriantha

Buddleja myriantha, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered inflorescences that produce nectar-rich flowers attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, with blooms ranging from pale purple to deep blue.

Buddleja myriantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
