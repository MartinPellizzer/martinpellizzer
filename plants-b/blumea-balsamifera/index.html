Blumea Balsamifera

Blumea balsamifera is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. It is known for its fragrant leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. This species is commonly used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. The plant's essential oils are also valued in perfumery and aromatherapy. Its versatility and cultural significance make it an important plant in many communities.
