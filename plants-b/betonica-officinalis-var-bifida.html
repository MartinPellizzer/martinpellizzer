Betonica officinalis var. bifida

Betonica officinalis var. bifida, commonly known as the bifid betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a sturdy stem that grows upright, often reaching heights of up to 60 cm. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Betonica officinalis var. bifida has been historically used in herbal medicine for its purported calming and digestive properties.
