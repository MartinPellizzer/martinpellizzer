Boehmeria virgata var. macrostachya

Boehmeria virgata var. macrostachya, commonly known as giant bamboo or giant mulberry, is a flowering plant in the nettle family, Urticaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in panicle-like clusters, and its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
