Bupleurum linearifolium var. schimperianum

Bupleurum linearifolium var. schimperianum, commonly known as Schimper's narrow-leaved bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, such as slopes and grasslands. The plant features slender, branching stems with linear, lance-shaped leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
