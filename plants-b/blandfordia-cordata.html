Blandfordia cordata

Blandfordia cordata, commonly known as the Christmas Blandfordia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive, narrow, strap-like foliage that is evergreen. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to native gardens.
