Bunium

Bunium, commonly known as goat's beard, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae.

These plants are characterized by their finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. Bunium species are typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. They are known for their aromatic properties and have been used in traditional medicine.

The seeds of some Bunium species are edible and have a slightly bitter taste.
