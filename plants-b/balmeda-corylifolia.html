Balmeda corylifolia

Balmeda corylifolia, commonly known as the Chinese chestnut or sweet chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Fagaceae and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, spiny fruit capsules. Balmeda corylifolia plays an important role in both traditional medicine and local cuisines.
