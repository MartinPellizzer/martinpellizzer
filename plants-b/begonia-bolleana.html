Begonia bolleana

Begonia bolleana, commonly known as the waxy begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often tinged with purple or red, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the day. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
