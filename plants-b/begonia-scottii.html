Begonia scottii

Begonia scottii, commonly known as the Scott's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia scottii is prized in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
