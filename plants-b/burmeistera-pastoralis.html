Burmeistera pastoralis

Burmeistera pastoralis, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive cup-shaped structure.

Burmeistera pastoralis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and showy blooms.
