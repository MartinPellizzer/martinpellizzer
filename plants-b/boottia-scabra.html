Boottia scabra

Boottia scabra, commonly known as the rough boottia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it a rough texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Boottia scabra is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
