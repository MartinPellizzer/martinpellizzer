Biscutella laevigata var. intermedia

Biscutella laevigata var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate biscutella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pale yellow and are arranged in clusters, blooming in early spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
