Bulbophyllum calceolus

Bulbophyllum calceolus, commonly known as the "Horned Orchid," is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, horn-like flower shape, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum calceolus is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
