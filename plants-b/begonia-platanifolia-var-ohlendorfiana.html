Begonia platanifolia var. ohlendorfiana

Begonia platanifolia var. ohlendorfiana, commonly known as the Ohlendorfiana begonia, is a striking hybrid begonia with large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens due to its sensitivity to direct sunlight.

Its foliage is prized for its ornamental value, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
