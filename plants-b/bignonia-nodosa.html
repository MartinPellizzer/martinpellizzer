Bignonia nodosa

Bignonia nodosa, commonly known as the yellow trumpetbush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, orange-yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its stems are typically woody and can be somewhat thorny.

Bignonia nodosa is often found in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and in woodland edges.
