Billbergia oaxacana

Billbergia oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca billbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Billbergia, native to Mexico.

It is a bromeliad characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central tank to collect water. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored bracts, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Billbergia oaxacana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable environments.
