Bulbophyllum hainanense

Bulbophyllum hainanense, commonly known as the Hainan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China's Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structures. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its tropical environment. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Bulbophyllum hainanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
