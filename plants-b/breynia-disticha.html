Breynia disticha

Breynia disticha, commonly known as the silverleaf breynia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Breynia disticha is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in landscaping and horticulture.
