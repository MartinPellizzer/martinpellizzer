Brachystelma dimorphum

Brachystelma dimorphum, commonly known as the two-form stone orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Brachystelma dimorphum is often found growing on rocky outcrops and is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
