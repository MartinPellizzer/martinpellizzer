Bulbophyllum baosangii

Bulbophyllum baosangii, commonly known as the Baosang orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a mix of white and purple hues. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
