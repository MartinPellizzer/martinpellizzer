Bulbophyllum ovatum

Bulbophyllum ovatum, commonly known as the oval-lobed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a wide range of colors, often featuring intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
