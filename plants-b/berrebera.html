Berrebera

Berrebera, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, white or purple flowers and produces berries that are typically toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

However, due to its toxic nature, it should be handled with care.
