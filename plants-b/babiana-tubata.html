Babiana tubata

Babiana tubata, commonly known as the Orange Babiana, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, stocky stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Babiana tubata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
