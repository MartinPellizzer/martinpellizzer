Begonia naga

Begonia naga, commonly known as the Naga begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deeply veined, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia naga is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
