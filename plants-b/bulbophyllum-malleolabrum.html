Bulbophyllum malleolabrum

Bulbophyllum malleolabrum, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
