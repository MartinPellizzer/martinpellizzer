Ballota deserti

Ballota deserti, commonly known as the desert ballota, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. Its ecological role includes providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a food source for certain desert wildlife.
