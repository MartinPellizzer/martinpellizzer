Bladhia lentiginosa f. hortensis

Bladhia lentiginosa f. hortensis, commonly known as the garden bladhia, is a cultivar of the bladhia plant, which is native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The hortensis form is particularly valued for its improved hardiness and extended blooming period compared to the species.
