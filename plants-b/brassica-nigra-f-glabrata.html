Brassica nigra f. glabrata

Brassica nigra f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth black mustard, is a variant of the black mustard plant.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features dark green, elongated leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its seeds are small, black, and contain compounds used in traditional medicine.

The f. glabrata form is distinguished by its smooth, hairless leaves compared to the typical variety.
