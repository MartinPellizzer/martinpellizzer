Bulbophyllum echinolabium

Bulbophyllum echinolabium, commonly known as the spiky-labium orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique, spiky floral structure. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinct labellum that is often adorned with prominent calli. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
