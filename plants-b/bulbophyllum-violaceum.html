Bulbophyllum violaceum

Bulbophyllum violaceum, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking violet to purple-colored flowers. The plant produces numerous small, fragrant blooms that are typically arranged in a dense inflorescence. Its leaves are usually dark green and somewhat leathery, adapted to conserve moisture.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
