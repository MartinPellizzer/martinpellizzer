Bactris essequiboensis

Bactris essequiboensis, commonly known as the Essequibo palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Guyana and Venezuela.

The plant produces clustered inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers, followed by edible fruits that are similar in appearance to those of the coconut.
