Begonia xuansonensis

Begonia xuansonensis, commonly known as the Xuanson begonia, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a unique combination of colors, often featuring deep green with reddish or purplish hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
