Barleria maculata

Barleria maculata, commonly known as the spotted barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive purplish or reddish underside. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters.

Barleria maculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
