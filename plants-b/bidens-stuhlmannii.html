Bidens stuhlmannii

Bidens stuhlmannii, commonly known as the slender tickseed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Bidens stuhlmannii is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
