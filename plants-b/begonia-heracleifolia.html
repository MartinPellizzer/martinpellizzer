Begonia heracleifolia

Begonia heracleifolia, commonly known as the giant leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
