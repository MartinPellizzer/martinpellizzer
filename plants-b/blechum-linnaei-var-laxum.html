Blechum linnaei var. laxum

Blechum linnaei var. laxum, commonly known as the slender blechum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in woodland edges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its small, white to pale blue flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in wetland habitats.
