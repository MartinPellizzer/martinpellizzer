Begonia sodiroi

Begonia sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically red or pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
