Broussonetia navicularis

Broussonetia navicularis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf broussonetia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in forested areas and can thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a versatile species in its native habitat.
