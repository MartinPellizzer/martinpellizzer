Bolbitis perakensis

Bolbitis perakensis, commonly known as the Java fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and feather-like fronds that are typically dark green in color. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded areas with soft, acidic water. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and can grow up to several inches in length.

Bolbitis perakensis is popular in aquariums due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
