Begonia charlesjarosiana

Begonia charlesjarosiana, commonly known as the Charles Jarosian begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
