Barbarea orthoceras var. formosana

Barbarea orthoceras var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan rockcress, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves that are elongated and covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in early spring.

The species is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often found in mountainous regions.
