Bulbophyllum chlorostachys

Bulbophyllum chlorostachys, commonly known as the green-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
