Bromus modensis

Bromus modensis, commonly known as the Modesto brome, is a perennial grass native to California.

It typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces spikelets that are often clustered at the base of the plant. Its leaves are narrow and rough, with a bluish-green hue.

Bromus modensis is adapted to Mediterranean climates and is often found in coastal regions.
