Bulbophyllum petrae

Bulbophyllum petrae, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive on rocky substrates. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rock surfaces.

Bulbophyllum petrae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
