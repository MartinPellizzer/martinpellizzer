Burmannia stricta

Burmannia stricta, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant has a short, erect stem and broad, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Burmannia stricta is notable for its unique appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
