Berberis bealei var. planifolia

Berberis bealei var. planifolia, commonly known as the flatleaf barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically flat and smooth, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but not widely consumed. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.

This variety is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
