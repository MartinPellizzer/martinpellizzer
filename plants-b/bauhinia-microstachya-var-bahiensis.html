Bauhinia microstachya var. bahiensis

Bauhinia microstachya var. bahiensis, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking, flame-like red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces flat, pod-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various soil types.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
