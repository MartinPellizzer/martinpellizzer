Billbergia macrocalyx

Billbergia macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, rosette-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with bright red flowers that bloom in the spring. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its dramatic appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
