Buddleja scordioides

Buddleja scordioides, commonly known as the Ethiopian butterfly bush, is a shrub native to Ethiopia.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, grayish pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Buddleja scordioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
