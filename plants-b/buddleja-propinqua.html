Buddleja propinqua

Buddleja propinqua, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that attract pollinators. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in gardens.
