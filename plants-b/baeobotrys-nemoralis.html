Baeobotrys nemoralis

Baeobotrys nemoralis, commonly known as the common baeobotrys, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

Baeobotrys nemoralis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
