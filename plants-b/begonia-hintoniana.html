Begonia hintoniana

Begonia hintoniana, commonly known as Hinton's begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically features heart-shaped or ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its beauty and unique foliage.
