Bufonia macropetala

Bufonia macropetala, commonly known as the large-flowered bufonia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Bufonia macropetala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
