Bulbophyllum macrolepis

Bulbophyllum macrolepis, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fleshy pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces numerous fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
