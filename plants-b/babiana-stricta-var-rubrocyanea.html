Babiana stricta var. rubrocyanea

Babiana stricta var. rubrocyanea, commonly known as the red-blue babiana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to blue. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and prefers well-drained soils. It produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, stout stem.
