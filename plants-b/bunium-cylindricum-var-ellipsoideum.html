Bunium cylindricum var. ellipsoideum

Bunium cylindricum var. ellipsoideum, commonly known as the narrowleaf fennel, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
