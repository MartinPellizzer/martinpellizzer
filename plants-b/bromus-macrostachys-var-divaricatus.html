Bromus macrostachys var. divaricatus

Bromus macrostachys var. divaricatus, commonly known as the slender brome, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a single, terminal spike that bears small, two-lobed spikelets.

This variety is often found in arid regions and is adapted to survive in low-nutrient environments.
