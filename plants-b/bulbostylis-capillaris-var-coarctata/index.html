Bulbostylis Capillaris Var. Coarctata

Bulbostylis capillaris var. coarctata is a small, grass-like plant native to wetlands and marshy areas. It is known for its delicate, slender leaves and tiny, inconspicuous flowers. This variety is often mistaken for other grass species due to its subtle appearance. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Its presence can indicate healthy wetland environments.
