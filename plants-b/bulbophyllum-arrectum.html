Bulbophyllum arrectum

Bulbophyllum arrectum, commonly known as the erect orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, upright growth habit and striking, often colorful flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, tropical environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
