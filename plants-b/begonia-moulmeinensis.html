Begonia moulmeinensis

Begonia moulmeinensis, commonly known as the Moulmein begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of bright red flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.

Begonia moulmeinensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
