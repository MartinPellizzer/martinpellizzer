Bromelia horstii

Bromelia horstii, commonly known as the Horstii bromeliad, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, often lasting several months. Its foliage is typically broad and waxy, with a distinctive blue-green hue.

Bromelia horstii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
