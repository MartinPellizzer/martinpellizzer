Begonia polypetala

Begonia polypetala, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and unique foliage.
