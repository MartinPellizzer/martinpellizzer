Bidens glaberrima

Bidens glaberrima, commonly known as the roughleaf beggarlice, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features serrated, rough-textured leaves and produces small yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

Bidens glaberrima is known for its ability to thrive in various environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
