Bubania feei

Bubania feei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. Bubania feei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
