Bihai revoluta

Bihai revoluta, commonly known as the pink dragon tree, is a striking member of the Dracaena genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant pink or red color, which intensify in sunlight. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 feet when mature. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Bihai revoluta is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique foliage and air-purifying properties.
