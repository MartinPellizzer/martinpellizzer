Barjonia obtusifolia

Barjonia obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Barjonia obtusifolia is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands.
