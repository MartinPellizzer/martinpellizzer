Begonia kimusiana

Begonia kimusiana, commonly known as the African nettle begonia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, reddish-purple flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and a dark green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
