Bromus arvensis subsp. segetalis

Bromus arvensis subsp.

segetalis, commonly known as the field brome, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

This subspecies is an important component of grassland ecosystems and can be invasive in certain regions.
