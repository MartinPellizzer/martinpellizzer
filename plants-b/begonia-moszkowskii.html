Begonia moszkowskii

Begonia moszkowskii, commonly known as the Moszkowski begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. This species is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in indirect light and moderate humidity.

It is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
