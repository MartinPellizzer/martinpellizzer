Bulbophyllum brevipes

Bulbophyllum brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers. The plant features a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
