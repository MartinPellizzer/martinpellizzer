Begonia silverstonei var. silverstonei

Begonia silverstonei var. silverstonei, commonly known as the silverstone begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a striking silver or white variegation that enhances its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often prized as a decorative plant in both gardens and homes.
