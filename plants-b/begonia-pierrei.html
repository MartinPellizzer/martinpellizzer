Begonia pierrei

Begonia pierrei, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia pierrei is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
