Begonia laetevirens

Begonia laetevirens, commonly known as the golden begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage.

Begonia laetevirens is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
