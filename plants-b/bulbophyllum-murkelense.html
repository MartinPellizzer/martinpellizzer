Bulbophyllum murkelense

Bulbophyllum murkelense, commonly known as the Murkel's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

The genus Bulbophyllum is known for its diverse floral morphology, and B. murkelense is no exception.
