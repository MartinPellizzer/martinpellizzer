Blaeria

Blaeria, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. Its fruit is a small, round pod that contains several seeds.

Blaeria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
