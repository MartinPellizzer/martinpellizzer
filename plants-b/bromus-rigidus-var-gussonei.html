Bromus rigidus var. gussonei

Bromus rigidus var. gussonei, commonly known as the rigid brome, is a grass species native to southern Europe and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and disturbed soils.

Bromus rigidus var. gussonei is often found in Mediterranean climates and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
