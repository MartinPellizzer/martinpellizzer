Bulbophyllum imitans

Bulbophyllum imitans, commonly known as the mimic orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces intricate, often colorful flowers that resemble other orchid species, a trait that gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum imitans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
