Barleria cyanea

Barleria cyanea, commonly known as the blue barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in terminal clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria cyanea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
