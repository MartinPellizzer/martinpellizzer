Blepharandra heteropetala

Blepharandra heteropetala, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is known for its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental use.
