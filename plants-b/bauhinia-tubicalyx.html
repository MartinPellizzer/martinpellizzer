Bauhinia tubicalyx

Bauhinia tubicalyx, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its woody structure and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions make it a popular ornamental species.

Bauhinia tubicalyx is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its use in traditional medicine.
