Bernardinia agelaeoides

Bernardinia agelaeoides, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Bernardinia agelaeoides is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
