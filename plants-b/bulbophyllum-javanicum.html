Bulbophyllum javanicum

Bulbophyllum javanicum, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum javanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
