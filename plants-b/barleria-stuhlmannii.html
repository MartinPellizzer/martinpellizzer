Barleria stuhlmannii

Barleria stuhlmannii, commonly known as the blue barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Barleria stuhlmannii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
