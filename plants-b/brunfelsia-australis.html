Brunfelsia australis

Brunfelsia australis, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often changing hue as they mature. The plant produces egg-shaped berries that turn from green to yellow or red when ripe. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
