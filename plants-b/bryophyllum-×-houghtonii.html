Bryophyllum × houghtonii

Bryophyllum × houghtonii, commonly known as the African violet, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Gesneriaceae.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. Bryophyllum × houghtonii requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish.
