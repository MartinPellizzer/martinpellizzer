Babiana mucronata var. longicollis

Babiana mucronata var. longicollis, commonly known as the Long-necked Babiana, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

The species is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
