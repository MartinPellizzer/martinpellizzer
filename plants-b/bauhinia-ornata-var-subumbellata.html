Bauhinia ornata var. subumbellata

Bauhinia ornata var. subumbellata, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, palmate leaves and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their vibrant color and unique leaf structure.
