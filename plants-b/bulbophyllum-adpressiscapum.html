Bulbophyllum adpressiscapum

Bulbophyllum adpressiscapum, commonly known as the flat-headed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often densely packed inflorescence. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum adpressiscapum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
