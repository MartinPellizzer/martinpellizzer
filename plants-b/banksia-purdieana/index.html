Banksia Purdieana

Banksia purdieana is a striking plant native to southwestern australia. it belongs to the proteaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure. this species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. its vibrant yellow flowers attract a variety of pollinators. it plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is a symbol of australia's diverse flora.
