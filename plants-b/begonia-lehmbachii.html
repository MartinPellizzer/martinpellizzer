Begonia lehmbachii

Begonia lehmbachii, commonly known as the lehmbachii begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia lehmbachii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
