Byttneria ostenii

Byttneria ostenii, commonly known as the African silk tree, is a member of the Malvaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, often used in traditional African medicine for their anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.
