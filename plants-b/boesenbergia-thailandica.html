Boesenbergia thailandica

Boesenbergia thailandica, commonly known as Thai ginger or Thai yellow ginger, is a rhizomatous herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in spikes. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary spice.

Boesenbergia thailandica is valued for its aromatic properties and is cultivated for both ornamental and practical purposes.
