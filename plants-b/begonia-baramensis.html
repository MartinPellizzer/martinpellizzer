Begonia baramensis

Begonia baramensis, commonly known as the Baram River begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Baram River region in Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage and floral structure are adapted to thrive in the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.

Begonia baramensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
