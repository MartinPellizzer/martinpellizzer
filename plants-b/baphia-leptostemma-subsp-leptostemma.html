Baphia leptostemma subsp. leptostemma

Baphia leptostemma subsp.

leptostemma, commonly known as the slender-stemmed baphia, is a member of the Leguminosae family. It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often creeping stems and small, opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by pods that contain several seeds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
