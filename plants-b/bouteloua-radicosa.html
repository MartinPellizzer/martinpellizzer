Bouteloua radicosa

Bouteloua radicosa, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Blue grama is an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
