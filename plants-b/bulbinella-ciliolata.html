Bulbinella ciliolata

Bulbinella ciliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southeastern Australia.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant is characterized by its compact size and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is a popular choice for rock gardens.

Bulbinella ciliolata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
