Bonamia ovalifolia

Bonamia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf bonamia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Bonamia ovalifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
