Blepharocalyx eggersii

Blepharocalyx eggersii, commonly known as the pink star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that resemble stars, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces fragrant blooms during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
