Bulbophyllum aspersum

Bulbophyllum aspersum, commonly known as the speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually strap-like and arranged in a rosette formation.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant appearance make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
