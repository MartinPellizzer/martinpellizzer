Bulbostylis vaginosa

Bulbostylis vaginosa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Bulbostylis vaginosa is often found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist habitats where it thrives in the presence of standing water.
