Bulbophyllum trichocephalum var. wallongense

Bulbophyllum trichocephalum var. wallongense, commonly known as the Wallongense Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered with a thick layer of epidermis, which helps in water storage.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
