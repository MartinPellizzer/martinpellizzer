Boswellia elegans

Boswellia elegans, commonly known as the African frankincense tree, is a species of the genus Boswellia native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that produces a resinous sap, which is harvested for its aromatic gum.
