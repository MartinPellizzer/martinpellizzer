Biophytum perrieri

Biophytum perrieri, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Biophytum perrieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
