Bignonia binata

Bignonia binata, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous climbing vine that produces vibrant yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with slightly serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular choice for trellises and fences.
