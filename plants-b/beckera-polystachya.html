Beckera polystachya

Beckera polystachya, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid conditions.

Beckera polystachya is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
