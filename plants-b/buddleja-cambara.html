Buddleja cambara

Buddleja cambara, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features striking, fragrant, panicle-shaped inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Buddleja cambara is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
