Bulbophyllum retusiusculum f. brunneum

Bulbophyllum retusiusculum f. brunneum, commonly known as the Brown-stemmed Bulbophyllum, is a unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark brown, often velvety stems that distinguish it from other members of the species. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
