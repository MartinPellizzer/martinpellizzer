Bulbophyllum farinulentum subsp. densissimum

Bulbophyllum farinulentum subsp.

densissimum, commonly known as the dense-stemmed bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense arrangement of stems.

The plant produces numerous small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green, often clustered at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
