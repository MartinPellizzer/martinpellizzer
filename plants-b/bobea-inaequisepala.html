Bobea inaequisepala

Bobea inaequisepala, commonly known as the unequal-sepal bean.

It is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring sepals of unequal size, which gives it its scientific name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

The plant is often grown for its ornamental value due to its attractive and unusual floral display.
