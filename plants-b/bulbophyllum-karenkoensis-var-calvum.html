Bulbophyllum karenkoensis var. calvum

Bulbophyllum karenkoensis var. calvum, commonly known as the Karenko's Bulbophyllum, is a rare orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually small and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
