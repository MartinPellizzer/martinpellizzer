Bolusia grandis

Bolusia grandis, commonly known as the giant bolusia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a woody stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern. Bolusia grandis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable environments.
