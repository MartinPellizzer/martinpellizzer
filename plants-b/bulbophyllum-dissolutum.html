Bulbophyllum dissolutum

Bulbophyllum dissolutum, commonly known as the Splendid Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure and vibrant appearance. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
