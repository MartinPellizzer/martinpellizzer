Bulbophyllum iterans

Bulbophyllum iterans, commonly known as the wandering orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its inflorescence consists of racemes of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum iterans make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
