Bulbophyllum stragularium

Bulbophyllum stragularium, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescence and small, intricate flowers that often display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum stragularium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
