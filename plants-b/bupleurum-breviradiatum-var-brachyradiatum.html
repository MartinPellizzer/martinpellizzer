Bupleurum breviradiatum var. brachyradiatum

Bupleurum breviradiatum var. brachyradiatum, commonly known as the short-rayed bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, usually appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
