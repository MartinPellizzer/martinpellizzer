Bulbophyllum deceptum

Bulbophyllum deceptum, commonly known as the deceptive orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bulbophyllum deceptum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
