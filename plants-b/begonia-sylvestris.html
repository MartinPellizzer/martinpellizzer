Begonia sylvestris

Begonia sylvestris, commonly known as the wild begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive yellow center. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
