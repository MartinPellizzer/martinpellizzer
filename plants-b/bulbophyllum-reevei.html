Bulbophyllum reevei

Bulbophyllum reevei, commonly known as the Reeve's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Bulbophyllum reevei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
