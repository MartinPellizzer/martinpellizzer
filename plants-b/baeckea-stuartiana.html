Baeckea stuartiana

Baeckea stuartiana, commonly known as the Stuart's baeckea, is a small shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
