Bonifacia

Bonifacia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features showy, water-lily-like flowers that bloom in a range of colors, including pink, white, and yellow. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor it in muddy substrates. Bonifacia is known for its ability to purify water and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.

Its cultural significance is deep, often symbolizing purity and spiritual awakening in many Eastern traditions.
