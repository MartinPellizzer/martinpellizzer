Boerhavia plumbaginea

Boerhavia plumbaginea, commonly known as blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Boerhavia plumbaginea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
