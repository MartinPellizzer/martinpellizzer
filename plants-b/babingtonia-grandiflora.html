Babingtonia grandiflora

Babingtonia grandiflora, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are usually red or orange in color. These flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, attracting birds and insects.

It has lance-shaped leaves and is well adapted to coastal and sandy environments.
