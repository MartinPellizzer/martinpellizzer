Bredia velutina

Bredia velutina, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its smooth, velvety bark and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, red berries.
