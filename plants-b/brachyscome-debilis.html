Brachyscome debilis

Brachyscome debilis, commonly known as the slender everlasting daisy, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It produces small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming from spring to autumn. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Brachyscome debilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
