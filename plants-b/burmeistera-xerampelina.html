Burmeistera xerampelina

Burmeistera xerampelina, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Burmeistera xerampelina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
