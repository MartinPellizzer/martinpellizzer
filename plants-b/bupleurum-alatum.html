Bupleurum alatum

Bupleurum alatum, commonly known as the winged bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant features distinctive, finely divided leaves with a slender, wing-like structure along the leaf stalks. Its flowering stems bear small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbrella-like clusters.

Bupleurum alatum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and streambanks.
