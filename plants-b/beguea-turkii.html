Beguea turkii

Beguea turkii, commonly known as the Turkish begonia, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thick, fleshy stems that store water, adapting to its arid environment. Its flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings.

Beguea turkii thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.
