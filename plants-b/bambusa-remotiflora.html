Bambusa remotiflora

Bambusa remotiflora, commonly known as the remote-flower bamboo, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a tall, slender bamboo characterized by its smooth, light green culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Bambusa remotiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and bamboo gardening.
