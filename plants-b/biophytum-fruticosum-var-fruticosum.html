Biophytum fruticosum var. fruticosum

Biophytum fruticosum var. fruticosum, commonly known as the red sandalwood plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling sandalwood in appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and succulent, allowing it to store water efficiently.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
