Bulbophyllum fonsflorum

Bulbophyllum fonsflorum, commonly known as the "Rainbow Orchid," is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that display a range of hues. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and feature intricate, often fragrant petals.

Bulbophyllum fonsflorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
