Buchanania mollis

Buchanania mollis, commonly known as the white-flowered buchanania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, ovate leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Buchanania mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
