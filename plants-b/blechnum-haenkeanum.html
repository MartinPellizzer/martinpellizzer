Blechnum haenkeanum

Blechnum haenkeanum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern native to South America.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, silver-colored fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its fronds are pinnate, with a serrated margin and a leathery texture.

Blechnum haenkeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
