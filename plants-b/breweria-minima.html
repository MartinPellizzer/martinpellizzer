Breweria minima

Breweria minima, commonly known as the small brewer's weed, is a small flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Breweria minima is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
