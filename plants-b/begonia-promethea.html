Begonia promethea

Begonia promethea, commonly known as the flame begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting dark veins. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Begonia promethea is popular among gardeners for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
