Begonia diwolii

Begonia diwolii, commonly known as the Dihua begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia diwolii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
