Bulbophyllum linearilabium

Bulbophyllum linearilabium, commonly known as the linear-labied orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and distinctive, elongated flowers with a prominent, linear labellum. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a contrasting purple or brown lip.

Bulbophyllum linearilabium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
