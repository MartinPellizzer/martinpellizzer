Bridelia glauca var. sosopodonica

Bridelia glauca var. sosopodonica, commonly known as the Socotra bridelia, is a rare and unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, thick, fleshy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant exhibits a shrub-like growth habit with branches that often appear twisted and contorted. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists due to its ecological significance and endemic status.
