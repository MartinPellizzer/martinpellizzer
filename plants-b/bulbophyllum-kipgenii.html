Bulbophyllum kipgenii

Bulbophyllum kipgenii, commonly known as the Kipgenii orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying a range of hues from yellow to orange.

Due to its specific environmental requirements, it is relatively challenging to cultivate outside its native environment.
