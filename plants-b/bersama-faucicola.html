Bersama faucicola

Bersama faucicola, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Bersama faucicola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
