Bromopsis gabrielianae

Bromopsis gabrielianae, commonly known as Gabriel's feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance.

Bromopsis gabrielianae is also noted for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in arid environments.
