Berkheya bipinnatifida subsp. bipinnatifida

Berkheya bipinnatifida subsp.

bipinnatifida, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and lobed leaves that are often deeply divided.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a dense, spherical cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to native plant collections.
