Bidens quadrangularis

Bidens quadrangularis, commonly known as the roughleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its quadrangular (four-sided) stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has serrated leaves and produces seeds with pappus structures that aid in wind dispersal. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.

Bidens quadrangularis is known for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
