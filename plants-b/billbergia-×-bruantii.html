Billbergia × bruantii

Billbergia × bruantii, commonly known as the hybrid billbergia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Billbergia natalensis and Billbergia tembladora.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America. The plant features striking, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow or green bract. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce offsets, allowing for easy propagation.
