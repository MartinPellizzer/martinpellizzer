Baeckea kandos

Baeckea kandos, commonly known as the Kandos baeckea, is a small shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is well adapted to arid conditions.

Baeckea kandos is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
