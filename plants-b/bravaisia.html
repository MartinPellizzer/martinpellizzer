Bravaisia

Bravaisia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow flowers that resemble showers of light, hence its common name. The plant has opposite, simple, and often ovate leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Bravaisia is also noted for its rapid growth and is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
