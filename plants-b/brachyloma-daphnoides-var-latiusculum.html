Brachyloma daphnoides var. latiusculum

Brachyloma daphnoides var. latiusculum, commonly known as the broad-leaved mountain daphne, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
