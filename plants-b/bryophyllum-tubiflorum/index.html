Bryophyllum Tubiflorum

Bryophyllum tubiflorum is a unique succulent plant known for its striking appearance and easy care. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm and arid conditions. Its fleshy leaves store water, making it drought-resistant and ideal for low-maintenance gardens. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, adding color to any landscape. With its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings, it is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
