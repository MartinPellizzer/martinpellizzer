Beta patula

Beta patula, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. Beta patula typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking appearance.
