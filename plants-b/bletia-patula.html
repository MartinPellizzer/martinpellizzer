Bletia patula

Bletia patula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a spike. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in pine woods or along stream banks.

Bletia patula is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
