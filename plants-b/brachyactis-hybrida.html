Brachyactis hybrida

Brachyactis hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and thick, waxy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in rock gardens and xeriscapes due to its low maintenance requirements.

Brachyactis hybrida is a perennial species that prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
