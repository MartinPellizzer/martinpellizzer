Bouvardia diversiflora

Bouvardia diversiflora, commonly known as the pink bouvardia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Bouvardia diversiflora is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant blooms, adding a subtle scent to its surroundings.
