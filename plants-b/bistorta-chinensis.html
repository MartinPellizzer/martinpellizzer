Bistorta chinensis

Bistorta chinensis, commonly known as Chinese bistort, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant is characterized by its upright, somewhat woody stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Bistorta chinensis is often grown for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
