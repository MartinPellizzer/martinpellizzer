Bragaia estevesii

Bragaia estevesii, commonly known as the Cape Verde endemic plant, is a species native to the island of Santiago in the Cape Verde archipelago.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, demonstrating high adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowering period typically occurs during the rainy season, contributing to the local biodiversity.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
