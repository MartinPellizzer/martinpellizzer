Boronia minutiflora

Boronia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered boronia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub with aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
