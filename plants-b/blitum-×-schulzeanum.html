Blitum × schulzeanum

Blitum × schulzeanum, commonly known as the hybrid rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Blitum capillaris and Blitum virgatum. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or arid environments. It features small, yellow flowers clustered in loose inflorescences, and its leaves are usually narrow and hairy.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
