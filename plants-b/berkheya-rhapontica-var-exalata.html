Berkheya rhapontica var. exalata

Berkheya rhapontica var. exalata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is native to the coastal regions of South Africa and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, tubular disk flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Berkheya rhapontica var. exalata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
