Buphthalmum speciosum

Buphthalmum speciosum, commonly known as the showy ox-eye, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a prominent central disk, surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Buphthalmum speciosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
