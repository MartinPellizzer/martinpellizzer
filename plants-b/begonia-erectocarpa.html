Begonia erectocarpa

Begonia erectocarpa, commonly known as the upright-fruited begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, showy flowers that range from pink to red in color. The plant produces elongated, fleshy fruits that contain numerous small seeds, typical of the Begoniaceae family. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Begonia erectocarpa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
