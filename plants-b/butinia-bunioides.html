Butinia bunioides

Butinia bunioides, commonly known as the Bunioides thistle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces a spiny, globular fruit known as a cypsela, which is adapted for wind dispersal. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a dense covering of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment.
