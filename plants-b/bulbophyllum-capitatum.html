Bulbophyllum capitatum

Bulbophyllum capitatum, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and brightly colored, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

The species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
