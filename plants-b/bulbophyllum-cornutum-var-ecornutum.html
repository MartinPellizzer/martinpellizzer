Bulbophyllum cornutum var. ecornutum

Bulbophyllum cornutum var. ecornutum, commonly known as the horned bulbophyllum variant, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Indonesia and Malaysia. This variant is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of tropical orchids.
