Buchnera descampsii

Buchnera descampsii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink or purple. The plant has a succulent, fleshy stem that helps it store water in its native habitat. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Buchnera descampsii typically blooms in spring and is adapted to thrive in hot, dry conditions.
