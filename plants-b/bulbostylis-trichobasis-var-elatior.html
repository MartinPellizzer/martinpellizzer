Bulbostylis trichobasis var. elatior

Bulbostylis trichobasis var. elatior, commonly known as the tall slender bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often in association with water or in seasonally flooded areas. The flowers are usually inconspicuous, with tiny, greenish-white florets that are adapted for wind pollination.
