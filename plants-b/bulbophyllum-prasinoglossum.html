Bulbophyllum prasinoglossum

Bulbophyllum prasinoglossum, commonly known as the Emerald Lip Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-yellow lip, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its inflorescence produces a cluster of small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the wet season.

The botanical features of this orchid make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
