Bulbophyllum triaristellum

Bulbophyllum triaristellum, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its humid, shaded forest habitat. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a unique tri-lobed lip. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, requiring well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
