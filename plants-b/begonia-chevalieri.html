Begonia chevalieri

Begonia chevalieri, commonly known as the Madagascar begonia, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia chevalieri is popular among gardeners for its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
