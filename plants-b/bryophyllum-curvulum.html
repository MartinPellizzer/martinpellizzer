Bryophyllum curvulum

Bryophyllum curvulum, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering to thrive.
