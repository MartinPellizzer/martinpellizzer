Bunium cassium

Bunium cassium, commonly known as the Siberian fennel, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is native to regions of Europe, Asia, and North Africa, often found in dry, rocky soils. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in umbels. Its seeds are enclosed in a bract-like structure and are used in traditional medicine.

Bunium cassium is also valued for its culinary uses, particularly in certain Middle Eastern and Central Asian cuisines.
