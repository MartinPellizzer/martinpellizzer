Begonia kisuluana

Begonia kisuluana, commonly known as the Kisuluana begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a rare and sought-after species among botanists and plant enthusiasts.
