Bulbophyllum morenoi

Bulbophyllum morenoi, commonly known as the Moreno's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its unique, often intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and green.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
