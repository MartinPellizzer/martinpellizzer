Blakwellia micrantha

Blakwellia micrantha, commonly known as the slender blakwellia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understorey habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the branches.

Blakwellia micrantha is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other rainforest flora.
