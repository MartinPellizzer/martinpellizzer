Bulbophyllum tripaleum

Bulbophyllum tripaleum, commonly known as the Tri-pale orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native tropical environment.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in traditional floral arrangements due to their delicate appearance.
