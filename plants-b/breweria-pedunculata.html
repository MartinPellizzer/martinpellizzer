Breweria pedunculata

Breweria pedunculata, commonly known as the clustered brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as fields and along roadsides.

Breweria pedunculata is notable for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
