Brachyscome nivalis

Brachyscome nivalis, commonly known as the snow daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Australia.

It features small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has a mat-forming habit, with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that help it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Brachyscome nivalis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
