Balanites wilsoniana var. mayumbensis

Balanites wilsoniana var. mayumbensis, commonly known as the Mayumbensis desert bean, is a shrub native to arid regions of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

The species plays a role in local ecosystems and has potential uses in traditional medicine and agriculture.
