Bulbophyllum rubroguttatum

Bulbophyllum rubroguttatum, commonly known as the red-spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and yellow markings on its leaves and flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often appearing in clusters.

The orchid's unique appearance and habitat make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
