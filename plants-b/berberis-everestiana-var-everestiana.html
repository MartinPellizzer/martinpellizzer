Berberis everestiana var. everestiana

Berberis everestiana var. everestiana, commonly known as the Himalayan barberry, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red or orange berries in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
