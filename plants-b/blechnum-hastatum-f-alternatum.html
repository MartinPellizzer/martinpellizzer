Blechnum hastatum f. alternatum

Blechnum hastatum f. alternatum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a fern species native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, bright silver-green fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that allow it to spread and form dense colonies. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a fan-like appearance and a serrated margin.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
