Bidens vulgata var. dissectior

Bidens vulgata var. dissectior, commonly known as the common tickseed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, dissected leaves that give it a fern-like appearance, and it produces yellow flowers with prominent white markings. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and disturbed soils, and is often found in the eastern and central United States.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
