Bactris neomilitaris

Bactris neomilitaris, commonly known as the red rafaela, is a species of palm native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has a slender trunk with a smooth, pale bark and produces clusters of yellow flowers followed by bright red fruits. Its leaves are pinnate, with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
