Brachyscome ciliaris

Brachyscome ciliaris, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features silvery-gray foliage and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Brachyscome ciliaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
