Berlinia stolzii

Berlinia stolzii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant has compound leaves and produces yellowish flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds. It is often found in dry savannas and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and economic purposes in its native habitat.
