Bulbophyllum lophoton

Bulbophyllum lophoton, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often clustered along a spike, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant and unique blooms.
