Brillantaisia didynama

Brillantaisia didynama, commonly known as the golden torch tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, flame-like inflorescences that appear in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Due to its vibrant display, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
