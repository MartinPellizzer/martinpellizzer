Buchnera cryptocephala var. cryptocephala

Buchnera cryptocephala var. cryptocephala, commonly known as the broad-leaved goldenrod, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buchnera cryptocephala var. cryptocephala is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed sites.
