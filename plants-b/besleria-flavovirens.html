Besleria flavovirens

Besleria flavovirens, commonly known as the golden green gesneriad, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Besleria flavovirens is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
