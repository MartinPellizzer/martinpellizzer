Brachystelma meyerianum

Brachystelma meyerianum, commonly known as the Meyer's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
