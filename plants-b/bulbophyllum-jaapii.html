Bulbophyllum jaapii

Bulbophyllum jaapii, commonly known as the Jaapi's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, tropical environments.

The flowers of Bulbophyllum jaapii are often fragrant and can vary in color from white to yellow and orange.
