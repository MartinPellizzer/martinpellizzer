Bertolonia sanguinea

Bertolonia sanguinea, commonly known as the red-flowered bertolonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
