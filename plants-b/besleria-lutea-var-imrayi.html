Besleria lutea var. imrayi

Besleria lutea var. imrayi, commonly known as the golden flame begonia, is a hybrid begonia species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange bracts that resemble flames, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, spicy aroma. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
