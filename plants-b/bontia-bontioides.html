Bontia bontioides

Bontia bontioides, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces clusters of small, colorful blooms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
