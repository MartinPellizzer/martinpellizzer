Brabejum stellatum

Brabejum stellatum, commonly known as the star bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, bushy form. It is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Brabejum stellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
