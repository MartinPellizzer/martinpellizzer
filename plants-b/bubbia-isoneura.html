Bubbia isoneura

Bubbia isoneura, commonly known as the Isoneura burrgrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Bubbia isoneura is often found in coastal and inland regions where it thrives in full sun.
