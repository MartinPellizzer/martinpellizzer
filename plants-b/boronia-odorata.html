Boronia odorata

Boronia odorata, commonly known as the sweet pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.
