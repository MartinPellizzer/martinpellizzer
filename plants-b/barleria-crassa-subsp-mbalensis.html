Barleria crassa subsp. mbalensis

Barleria crassa subsp.

mbalensis, commonly known as the Mbalen bushveld grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually pink or red, attracting various pollinators.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
