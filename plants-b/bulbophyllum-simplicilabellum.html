Bulbophyllum simplicilabellum

Bulbophyllum simplicilabellum, commonly known as the simple-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white with a prominent lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum simplicilabellum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
