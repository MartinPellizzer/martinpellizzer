Bulbostylis capillaris f. contracta

Bulbostylis capillaris f. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a rare and delicate grass species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant's compact growth habit and reduced leaf structure are distinguishing features of this particular form. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
