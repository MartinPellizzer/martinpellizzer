Berberis × stenophylla var. autumnalis

Berberis × stenophylla var. autumnalis, commonly known as the autumnal barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn vibrant yellow in the fall. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and hedges.

Its hardy nature and seasonal color changes make it a visually striking addition to landscapes.
