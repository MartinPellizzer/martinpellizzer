Begonia sirindhorniana

Begonia sirindhorniana, commonly known as the Sirindhorn begonia, is a tropical plant native to Thailand.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to red. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, adding a subtle elegance to its appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
