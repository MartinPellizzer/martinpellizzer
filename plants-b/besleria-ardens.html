Besleria ardens

Besleria ardens, commonly known as the fiery begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble fireworks, hence its common name. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy sheen. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Besleria ardens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant flowers and ornamental foliage.
