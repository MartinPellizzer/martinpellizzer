Barnardia bispatha

Barnardia bispatha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved barnardia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss. It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, where it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
