Biserrula leiocarpa

Biserrula leiocarpa, commonly known as the slender biserrula, is a perennial legume native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage production.

Its seeds are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
