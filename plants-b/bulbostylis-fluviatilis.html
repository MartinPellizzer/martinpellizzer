Bulbostylis fluviatilis

Bulbostylis fluviatilis, commonly known as the river bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale green flowers. It is typically found in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of rivers and lakes.

Bulbostylis fluviatilis is adapted to moist environments and plays a role in stabilizing soil in aquatic ecosystems.
