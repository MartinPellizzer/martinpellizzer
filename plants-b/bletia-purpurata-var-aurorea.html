Bletia purpurata var. aurorea

Bletia purpurata var. aurorea, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, orange-yellow flowers with a distinctive purple-pink lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its vibrant color and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a potted plant.
