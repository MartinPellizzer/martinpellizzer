Barbarea taiwaniana

Barbarea taiwaniana, commonly known as Taiwan rockcress, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
