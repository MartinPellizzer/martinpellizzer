Bambusa ribbentropii

Bambusa ribbentropii, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a fast-growing clumping bamboo native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking golden-green to yellowish culms that contrast beautifully with its dark green leaves. The plant has a dense, upright growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various climates.

Bambusa ribbentropii is also valued for its use in construction and as a sustainable resource.
