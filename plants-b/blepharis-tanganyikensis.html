Blepharis tanganyikensis

Blepharis tanganyikensis, commonly known as the Tanganyika blepharis, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom during the wet season. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
