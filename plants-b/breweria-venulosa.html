Breweria venulosa

Breweria venulosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Breweria venulosa is known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
