Bromus oranensis

Bromus oranensis, commonly known as the Oran grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial grass species that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in regions with limited rainfall.

Bromus oranensis is often found in open habitats such as fields and rocky slopes.
