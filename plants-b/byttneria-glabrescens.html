Byttneria glabrescens

Byttneria glabrescens, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, palmate leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing small, round seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
