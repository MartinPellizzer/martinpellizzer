Barleria scassellatii

Barleria scassellatii, commonly known as the Cape rose or barleria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems that often produce clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Barleria scassellatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
