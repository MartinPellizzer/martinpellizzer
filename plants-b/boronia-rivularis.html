Boronia rivularis

Boronia rivularis, commonly known as the river boronia, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Boronia rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
