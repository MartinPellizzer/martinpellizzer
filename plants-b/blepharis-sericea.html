Blepharis sericea

Blepharis sericea, commonly known as silverleaf blepharis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has a branching stem covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves makes it well adapted to drought conditions.
