Blumea hunanensis

Blumea hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic properties. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. It is often found in moist, forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Blumea hunanensis is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
