Begonia trichopoda var. polyantha

Begonia trichopoda var. polyantha, commonly known as the polyantha begonia, is a hybrid species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive red or purple underside. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
