Burmeistera succulenta var. latisepala

Burmeistera succulenta var. latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled Burmeistera, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support and reducing water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
