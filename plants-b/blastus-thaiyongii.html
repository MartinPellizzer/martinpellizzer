Blastus thaiyongii

Blastus thaiyongii, commonly known as the Thai yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinct, elongated flower spike. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, humid environments. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
