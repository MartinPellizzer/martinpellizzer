Brachiaria purpurascens

Brachiaria purpurascens, commonly known as purple brachiaria, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and vibrant purple-tinged inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and riparian areas. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Brachiaria purpurascens is also valued in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
