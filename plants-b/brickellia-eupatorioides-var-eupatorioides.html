Brickellia eupatorioides var. eupatorioides

Brickellia eupatorioides var. eupatorioides, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
