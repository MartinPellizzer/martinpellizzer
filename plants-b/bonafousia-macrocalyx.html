Bonafousia macrocalyx

Bonafousia macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered bonafousia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, large, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a woody stem that becomes more branched with age. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Bonafousia macrocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
