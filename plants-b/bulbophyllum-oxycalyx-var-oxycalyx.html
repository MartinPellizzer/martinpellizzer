Bulbophyllum oxycalyx var. oxycalyx

Bulbophyllum oxycalyx var. oxycalyx, commonly known as the Oxycalyx Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The flowers are typically borne on terminal inflorescences and can bloom multiple times throughout the year under optimal conditions.
