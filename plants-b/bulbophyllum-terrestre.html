Bulbophyllum terrestre

Bulbophyllum terrestre, commonly known as the terrestrial orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in soil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and growth habits.
