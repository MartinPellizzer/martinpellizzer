Bulbophyllum catenarium

Bulbophyllum catenarium, commonly known as the chain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, branching growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting the plant's unique morphology.

The flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental value.
