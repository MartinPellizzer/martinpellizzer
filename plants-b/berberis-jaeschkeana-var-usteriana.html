Berberis jaeschkeana var. usteriana

Berberis jaeschkeana var. usteriana, commonly known as the Usterian barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in cooler climates.
