Bauhinia kwangtungensis

Bauhinia kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. Its fruits are flat, pod-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Bauhinia kwangtungensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
