Belangera tomentosa var. calvata

Belangera tomentosa var. calvata, commonly known as the woolly bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive woolly, gray-green foliage covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue or pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
