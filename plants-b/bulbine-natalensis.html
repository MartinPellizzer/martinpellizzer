Bulbine natalensis

Bulbine natalensis, commonly known as Natal bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
