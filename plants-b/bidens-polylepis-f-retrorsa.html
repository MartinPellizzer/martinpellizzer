Bidens polylepis f. retrorsa

Bidens polylepis f. retrorsa, commonly known as the backward-facing ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in clusters, typical of the genus Bidens.
