Bulbophyllum kaitiense

Bulbophyllum kaitiense, commonly known as the Kaitiense orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in a raceme. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
