Begonia gamblei

Begonia gamblei, commonly known as the Gambie begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of West Africa.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a deep green hue. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the rainy season. Begonia gamblei thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
