Bombax villosum

Bombax villosum, commonly known as the silky cotton tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in fine, silky hairs.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and parks.
