Bletia angustifolia

Bletia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant blooms with small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which are often fragrant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in pine flatwoods and wetlands.

Bletia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
