Bersama leiostegia

Bersama leiostegia, commonly known as the smoothleaf daisy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets. The leaves are typically smooth and opposite, with a somewhat succulent texture. It is native to regions of Africa and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its bright, cheerful blooms.
