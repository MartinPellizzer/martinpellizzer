Bulbophyllum erratum

Bulbophyllum erratum, commonly known as the error orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often brightly colored flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its complex and intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a delicate and specialized species in botanical studies.
