Bupleurum ranunculoides subvar. syntomum

Bupleurum ranunculoides subvar. syntomum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It is part of the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants.
