Bulbophyllum toranum

Bulbophyllum toranum, commonly known as the Toranum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

The leaves are usually broad and strap-like, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.
