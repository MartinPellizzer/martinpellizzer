Bulbophyllum cymbochilum

Bulbophyllum cymbochilum, commonly known as the cymbochilum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
