Bulbophyllum tseanum

Bulbophyllum tseanum, commonly known as the Tsean's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant typically has a single or few leaves and bears clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
