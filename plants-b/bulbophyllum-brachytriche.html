Bulbophyllum brachytriche

Bulbophyllum brachytriche, commonly known as the short-haired bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical greenhouses.
