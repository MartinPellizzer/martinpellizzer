Bunium bulbocastanum var. mediterraneum

Bunium bulbocastanum var. mediterraneum, commonly known as the Mediterranean fennel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, aromatic stem and basal leaves that are finely divided and fern-like in appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its seeds are small and often used as a spice, similar to fennel seeds.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
