Bubbia perrieri

Bubbia perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's burrflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a unique appearance.

Bubbia perrieri is often found in coastal and inland areas, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
