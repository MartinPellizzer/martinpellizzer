Bechium foliosum

Bechium foliosum, commonly known as the leafy bechium, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves that are often lance-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. Bechium foliosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical collections and horticultural displays.
