Begonia lophura

Begonia lophura, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to reddish-purple. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
