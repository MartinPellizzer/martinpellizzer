Bletia tenuifolia

Bletia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf bletia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and usually white or pale pink.

Bletia tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady garden settings.
