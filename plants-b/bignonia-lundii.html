Bignonia lundii

Bignonia lundii, commonly known as the desert bignonia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well-suited for dry environments.

Bignonia lundii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to withstand drought conditions.
