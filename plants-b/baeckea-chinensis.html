Baeckea chinensis

Baeckea chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese baeckea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring dense, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Baeckea chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
