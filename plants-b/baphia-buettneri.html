Baphia buettneri

Baphia buettneri, commonly known as the African tamarind, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Baphia buettneri thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
