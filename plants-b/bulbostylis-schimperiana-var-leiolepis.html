Bulbostylis schimperiana var. leiolepis

Bulbostylis schimperiana var. leiolepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbostylis, is a species of grass belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or wetlands. Its small, spike-like inflorescence bears tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variant is distinguished by its leathery, slightly compressed leaves and is native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.
