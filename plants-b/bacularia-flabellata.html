Bacularia flabellata

Bacularia flabellata, commonly known as the fan-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fan-shaped fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically green and glossy, with a soft texture.

Bacularia flabellata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
