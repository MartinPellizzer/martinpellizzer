Begonia × lowii

Begonia × lowii, commonly known as the velvet begonia, is a hybrid plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, Begonia × lowii is widely grown as a decorative plant in gardens and homes.
