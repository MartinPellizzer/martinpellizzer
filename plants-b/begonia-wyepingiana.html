Begonia wyepingiana

Begonia wyepingiana, commonly known as the Wyeeping Begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety foliage and clusters of delicate, showy flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a waxy texture and often display variegation in green, white, and red. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
