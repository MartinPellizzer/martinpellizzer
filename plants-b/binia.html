Binia

Binia, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. Its flowers come in a wide range of colors, including purple, blue, white, and pink, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.

Binia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
