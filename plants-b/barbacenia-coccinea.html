Barbacenia coccinea

Barbacenia coccinea, commonly known as the red flame bellflower, is a strikingly colorful plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
