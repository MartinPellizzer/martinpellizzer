Badusa corymbifera subsp. biakensis

Badusa corymbifera subsp.

biakensis, commonly known as the Biak badusa, is a flowering plant native to the island of Biak in Indonesia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers.

The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy surface.
