Breweria media

Breweria media, commonly known as the common brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant grows in a variety of habitats, including fields, roadsides, and waste areas, and is often found in disturbed soils. It has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
