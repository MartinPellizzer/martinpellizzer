Bauhinia lagesiana

Bauhinia lagesiana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Bauhinia lagesiana is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
