Bihai barqueta

Bihai barqueta, commonly known as the giant daylily, is a striking member of the Iridaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often a vibrant green.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
