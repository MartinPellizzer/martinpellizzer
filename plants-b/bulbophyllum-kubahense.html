Bulbophyllum kubahense

Bulbophyllum kubahense, commonly known as the Kuba orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and striking floral morphology. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts, though it remains relatively rare in horticultural trade.
