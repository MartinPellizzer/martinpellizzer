Bulbophyllum lacinulosum

Bulbophyllum lacinulosum, commonly known as the lace-leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, lace-like leaf patterns and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Bulbophyllum lacinulosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
