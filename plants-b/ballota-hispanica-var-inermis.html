Ballota hispanica var. inermis

Ballota hispanica var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth ballota, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to gardens and natural habitats.
