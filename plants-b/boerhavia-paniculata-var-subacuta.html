Boerhavia paniculata var. subacuta

Boerhavia paniculata var. subacuta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf blushing morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, branching stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ground cover or container planting.
