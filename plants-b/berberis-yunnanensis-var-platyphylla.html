Berberis yunnanensis var. platyphylla

Berberis yunnanensis var. platyphylla, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedging plant.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a popular choice in horticulture.
