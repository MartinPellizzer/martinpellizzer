Baileya perennis

Baileya perennis, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils and open plains. Baileya perennis is drought-tolerant and plays a role in stabilizing soil in desert ecosystems.
