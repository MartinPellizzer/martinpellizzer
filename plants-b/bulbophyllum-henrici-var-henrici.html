Bulbophyllum henrici var. henrici

Bulbophyllum henrici var. henrici, commonly known as the Henry's Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are usually fleshy and dark green, adapted to retain moisture.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid conditions and is often found in primary forest habitats.
