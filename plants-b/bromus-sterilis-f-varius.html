Bromus sterilis f. varius

Bromus sterilis f. varius, commonly known as the variable false brome, is a variant of the false brome species.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, often tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats such as roadsides, fields, and open woodlands.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to a range of environmental conditions.
