Begonia angilogensis

Begonia angilogensis, commonly known as the silver lace begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its delicate floral display.
