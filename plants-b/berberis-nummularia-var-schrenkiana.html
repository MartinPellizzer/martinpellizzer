Berberis nummularia var. schrenkiana

Berberis nummularia var. schrenkiana, commonly known as the Schrenk's dwarf barberry, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central Asia.

It features small, rounded, leathery leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a slight variegation. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.

The variety is noted for its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
