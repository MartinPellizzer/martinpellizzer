Bromus affinis

Bromus affinis, commonly known as smooth brome, is a perennial grass native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, slender leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces dense clusters of spikelets that contain small, three-lobed flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed areas.

Bromus affinis is valued for its use in forage and soil stabilization due to its vigorous growth and adaptability.
