Bipinnula apinnula

Bipinnula apinnula, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hollyhock, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into two levels of pinnation, giving it a finely dissected appearance. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for temperate climates.
