Browneopsis

Browneopsis, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their showy, velvety leaves and vibrant, often colorful flowers. They typically grow as compact, upright shrubs with dense foliage. Browneopsis species are popular houseplants due to their ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.

The flowers are usually borne on long, slender stalks and come in a variety of colors including purple, blue, and white.
