Bulbophyllum stenorhachis

Bulbophyllum stenorhachis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal inflorescences and can range in color from yellow to orange.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
