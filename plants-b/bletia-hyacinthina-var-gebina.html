Bletia hyacinthina var. gebina

Bletia hyacinthina var. gebina, commonly known as the Gebina bletia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale blue flowers with a distinctive hyacinth-like appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bletia hyacinthina var. gebina is valued in horticulture for its elegant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
