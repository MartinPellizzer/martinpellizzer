Banisteria martiniana

Banisteria martiniana, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling a shower of blossoms. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Banisteria martiniana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
