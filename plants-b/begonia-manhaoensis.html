Begonia manhaoensis

Begonia manhaoensis, commonly known as the Manhao begonia, is a species native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
