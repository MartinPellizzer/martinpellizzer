Begonia russelliana

Begonia russelliana, commonly known as the Russell begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Begonia russelliana is known for its ability to produce numerous small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
