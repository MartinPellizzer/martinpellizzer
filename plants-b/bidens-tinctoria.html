Bidens tinctoria

Bidens tinctoria, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its bright orange to yellow flower heads, which consist of ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Bidens tinctoria is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
