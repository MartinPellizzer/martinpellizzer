Barleria eranthemoides var. agnewii

Barleria eranthemoides var. agnewii, commonly known as Agnew's barleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
