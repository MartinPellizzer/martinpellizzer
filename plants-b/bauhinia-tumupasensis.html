Bauhinia tumupasensis

Bauhinia tumupasensis, commonly known as the Tumupas bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, papilionaceous flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural purposes, although it is relatively rare in cultivation.
