Begonia samarensis

Begonia samarensis, commonly known as the Samarensis Begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and blooms in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
