Baeckea behrii

Baeckea behrii, commonly known as the yellow baeckea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers attract nectar-feeding birds and insects, contributing to its ecological significance.
