Bihai purpurea

Bihai purpurea, commonly known as the pink Himalayan salt plant, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Bihai purpurea is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
