Boerhavia coccinea var. leiocarpa

Boerhavia coccinea var. leiocarpa, commonly known as redstem filaree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
