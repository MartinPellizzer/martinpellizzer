Berberis × baoxingensis

Berberis × baoxingensis, commonly known as the Baoxing barberry, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate regions.
