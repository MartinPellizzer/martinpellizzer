Balantium arborescens

Balantium arborescens, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive silvery foliage.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is native to parts of Southeast Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy, silver underside that reflects light, giving it its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the rainy season.

The silverleaf tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
