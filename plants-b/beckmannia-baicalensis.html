Beckmannia baicalensis

Beckmannia baicalensis, commonly known as the Baikal Beckmannia, is a rare flowering plant native to the Lake Baikal region in Russia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is typically found in mountainous areas. Its botanical significance lies in its unique adaptations to the extreme climate of its native habitat.
