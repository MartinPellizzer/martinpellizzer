Bulbophyllum ranomafanae

Bulbophyllum ranomafanae, commonly known as the Ranomafana orchid, is a rare and endemic species found exclusively in the rainforests of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and striking coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species.
