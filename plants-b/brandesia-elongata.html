Brandesia elongata

Brandesia elongata, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or purple. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
