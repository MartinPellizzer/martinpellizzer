Bridelia tenuifolia var. elegans

Bridelia tenuifolia var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant bridelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that are edible. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
