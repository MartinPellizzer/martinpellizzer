Bulbophyllum morotaiense

Bulbophyllum morotaiense, commonly known as the Morotai orchid, is a species of orchid native to the island of Morotai in Indonesia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often brightly colored, with a unique pattern of markings on the petals. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Bulbophyllum morotaiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
