Bauhinia caloneura

Bauhinia caloneura, commonly known as the narrow-veined bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into numerous small leaflets, and its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Bauhinia caloneura is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
