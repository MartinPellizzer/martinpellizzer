Benkara ovoidea

Benkara ovoidea, commonly known as the eggplant orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, egg-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom during the warm season. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Benkara ovoidea is cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
