Bambusa xueana

Bambusa xueana, commonly known as the Xuean bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a tall, slender bamboo that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has stiff, green culms that are often used in construction and crafts. Its leaves are long and narrow, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Bambusa xueana is well-suited for cultivation in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.
