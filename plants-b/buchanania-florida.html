Buchanania florida

Buchanania florida, commonly known as the white-flowered Buchanania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by fleshy, orange-red fruits. Buchanania florida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
