Blotia

Blotia, commonly known as the bluebottle plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue and purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a succulent stem and thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water. Blotia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and unique appearance.

It prefers full sun and well-draining soil to thrive.
