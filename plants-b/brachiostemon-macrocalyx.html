Brachiostemon macrocalyx

Brachiostemon macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered brachiostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
