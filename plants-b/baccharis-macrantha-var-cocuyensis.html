Baccharis macrantha var. cocuyensis

Baccharis macrantha var. cocuyensis, commonly known as Cocuy grass, is a perennial shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the dry season. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its adaptability to arid conditions makes it an important component of local ecosystems.
