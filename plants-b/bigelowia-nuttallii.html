Bigelowia nuttallii

Bigelowia nuttallii, commonly known as the Nuttall's bigelowia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow to orange ray flowers surrounded by tubular disc flowers. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Bigelowia nuttallii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
