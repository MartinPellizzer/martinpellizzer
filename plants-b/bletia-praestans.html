Bletia praestans

Bletia praestans, commonly known as the showy bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, nodding flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are elliptical and often have a waxy texture, aiding in water retention.

Bletia praestans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.
