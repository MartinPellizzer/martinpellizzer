Blumea Obliqua

Blumea obliqua is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow. Blumea obliqua has traditional uses in medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
