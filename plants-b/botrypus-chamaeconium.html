Botrypus chamaeconium

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Botrypus chamaeconium plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
