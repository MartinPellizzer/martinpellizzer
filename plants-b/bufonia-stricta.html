Bufonia stricta

Bufonia stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf bufonia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its upright, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Bufonia stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
