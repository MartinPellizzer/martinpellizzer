Bulbophyllum acutebracteatum

Bulbophyllum acutebracteatum, commonly known as the sharp-bract orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence and specialized growth habits. The plant produces striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically borne on long, slender stems. Its leaves are usually fleshy and adapted to store water, reflecting its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical environments.

Bulbophyllum acutebracteatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its resilience and beauty.
