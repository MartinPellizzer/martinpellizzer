Bulbophyllum brachyscapum

Bulbophyllum brachyscapum, commonly known as the short-stalked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces white or pale yellow blooms.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
