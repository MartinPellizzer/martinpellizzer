Bulbophyllum summerhayesianum

Bulbophyllum summerhayesianum, commonly known as the Summerhays' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
