Bulbophyllum revolutum

Bulbophyllum revolutum, commonly known as the revolute bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically features elongated, strap-like leaves and has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum revolutum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral characteristics.
