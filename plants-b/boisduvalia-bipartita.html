Boisduvalia bipartita

Boisduvalia bipartita, commonly known as the two-parted boerma.

This plant is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The leaves are opposite and often have a distinctive two-lobed shape, giving the plant its scientific name.
