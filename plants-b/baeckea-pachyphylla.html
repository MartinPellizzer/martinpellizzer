Baeckea pachyphylla

Baeckea pachyphylla, commonly known as the thick-leaved baeckea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically a bright green color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Baeckea pachyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
