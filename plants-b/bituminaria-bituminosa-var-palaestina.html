Bituminaria bituminosa var. palaestina

Bituminaria bituminosa var. palaestina, commonly known as the Palestine bituminous broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant thrives in rocky and arid soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and greyish-green, often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in dry landscapes.
