Barfussia

Barfussia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Barfussia is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and long blooming period.
