Baeckea linifolia

Baeckea linifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved baeckea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in heathlands and dry forests. The plant produces small, white or cream-colored flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Baeckea linifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual appeal.
