Begonia cameroonensis

Begonia cameroonensis, commonly known as the Cameroon begonia, is a perennial herb native to the tropical rainforests of Cameroon.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or purple hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes.
