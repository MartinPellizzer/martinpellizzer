Begonia mexiae

Begonia mexiae, commonly known as the Mexican begonia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its attractive, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Begonia mexiae prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
