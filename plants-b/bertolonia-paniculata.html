Bertolonia paniculata

Bertolonia paniculata, commonly known as the panicled bertolonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers arranged in panicles. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

Bertolonia paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
