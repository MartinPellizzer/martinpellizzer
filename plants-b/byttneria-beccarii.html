Byttneria beccarii

Byttneria beccarii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
