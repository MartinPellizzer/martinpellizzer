Begonia thiemei

Begonia thiemei, commonly known as the Thai begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. Begonia thiemei thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
