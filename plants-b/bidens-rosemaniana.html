Bidens rosemaniana

Bidens rosemaniana, commonly known as the purpleleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, yellow flowers with purple markings. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
