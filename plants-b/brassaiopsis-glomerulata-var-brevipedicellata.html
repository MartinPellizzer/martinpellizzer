Brassaiopsis glomerulata var. brevipedicellata

Brassaiopsis glomerulata var. brevipedicellata, commonly known as the compact cluster cycad, is a rare and unique cycad species native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact inflorescence and small, short-peduncled flowers, which distinguish it from other varieties of Brassaiopsis. The plant has a slow growth rate and requires specific environmental conditions to thrive, making it a challenging species to cultivate. Its botanical features include a stout, unbranched trunk and pinnately compound leaves with a leathery texture.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
