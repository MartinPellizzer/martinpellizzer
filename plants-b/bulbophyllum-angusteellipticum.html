Bulbophyllum angusteellipticum

Bulbophyllum angusteellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-elliptic orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are typically narrow and elliptical, contributing to its common name.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
