Bouteloua polystachya

Bouteloua polystachya, commonly known as hairy grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands. It produces fluffy, branched seed heads that are distinctive and often visible in late summer.

Hairy grama is adapted to drought conditions and is an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
