Baccharis boyacensis

Baccharis boyacensis, commonly known as the Boyacensis Baccharis, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry to moist habitats, including grasslands and forest edges. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spherical heads, contributing to its ornamental value.
