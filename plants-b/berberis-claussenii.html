Berberis claussenii

Berberis claussenii, commonly known as the yellow barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Berberis claussenii is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
