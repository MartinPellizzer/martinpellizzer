Baeckea muricata

Baeckea muricata, commonly known as the rough baeckea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has dark green, narrow leaves with a rough texture, giving it its common name. Baeckea muricata typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils.

It is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
