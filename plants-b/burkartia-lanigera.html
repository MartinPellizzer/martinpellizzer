Burkartia lanigera

Burkartia lanigera, commonly known as the woolly burkartia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
