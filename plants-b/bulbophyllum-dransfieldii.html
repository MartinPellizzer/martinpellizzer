Bulbophyllum dransfieldii

Bulbophyllum dransfieldii, commonly known as the Dransfield's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in seasonal conditions. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its striking floral display.
