Bulbophyllum loroglossum

Bulbophyllum loroglossum, commonly known as the "Lip-shaped Bulbophyllum," is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, with petals that resemble lips, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum loroglossum is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
