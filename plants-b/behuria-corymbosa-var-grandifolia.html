Behuria corymbosa var. grandifolia

Behuria corymbosa var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered behuria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
