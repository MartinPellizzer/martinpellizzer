Bulbophyllum deistelianum

Bulbophyllum deistelianum, commonly known as the Deistel's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.
