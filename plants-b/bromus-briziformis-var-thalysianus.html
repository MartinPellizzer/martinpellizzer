Bromus briziformis var. thalysianus

Bromus briziformis var. thalysianus, commonly known as the Thalysian brome, is a grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Bromus briziformis var. thalysianus is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
