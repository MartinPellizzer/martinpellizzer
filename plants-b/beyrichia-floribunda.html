Beyrichia floribunda

Beyrichia floribunda, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Beyrichia floribunda is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
