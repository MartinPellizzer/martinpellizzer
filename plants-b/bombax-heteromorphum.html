Bombax heteromorphum

Bombax heteromorphum, commonly known as the African silk tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in clusters. The tree produces capsule fruits that contain fibrous seeds, and its leaves are typically palmately lobed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some regions.
