Bulbophyllum aechmophorum

Bulbophyllum aechmophorum, commonly known as the orchid with the distinctive white and green floral structure, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often white or greenish-white with yellow or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
