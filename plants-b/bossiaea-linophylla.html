Bossiaea linophylla

Bossiaea linophylla, commonly known as the silky bossiaea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with slender, wiry stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, globular inflorescences. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is typically found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal areas.

Bossiaea linophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage and floral display.
