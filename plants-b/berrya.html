Berrya

Berrya, commonly known as the "wild strawberry" or "forest strawberry," is a small, herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and thickets. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three heart-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are white with five petals.
