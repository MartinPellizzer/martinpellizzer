Bletia purpurata var. praetexta

Bletia purpurata var. praetexta, commonly known as the purple-streaked bletia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white, often with a distinctive red or purple streaking. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
