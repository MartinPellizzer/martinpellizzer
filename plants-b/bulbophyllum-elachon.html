Bulbophyllum elachon

Bulbophyllum elachon, commonly known as the small bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.
