Bulbophyllum bantaengense

Bulbophyllum bantaengense, commonly known as the Bantaeng orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
