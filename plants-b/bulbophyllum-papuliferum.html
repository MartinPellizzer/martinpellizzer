Bulbophyllum papuliferum

Bulbophyllum papuliferum, commonly known as the Papuliferum Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, hairy pseudobulbs and elongated, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a complex structure, featuring multiple petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
