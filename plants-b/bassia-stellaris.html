Bassia stellaris

Bassia stellaris, commonly known as starry gooseweed, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early summer.

The seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to thrive in various environments.
