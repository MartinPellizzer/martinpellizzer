Biophytum turianiense

Biophytum turianiense, commonly known as the Turian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
