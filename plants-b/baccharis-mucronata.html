Baccharis mucronata

Baccharis mucronata, commonly known as the slender baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands. It has a woody stem with a distinctive, slightly swollen base.

Baccharis mucronata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
