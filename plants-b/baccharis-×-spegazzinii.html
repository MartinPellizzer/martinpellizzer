Baccharis × spegazzinii

Baccharis × spegazzinii, commonly known as the hybrid baccharis, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. It produces small, persistent fruits that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for erosion control and ornamental purposes.
