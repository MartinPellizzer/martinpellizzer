Besleria sodiroana

Besleria sodiroana, commonly known as the Amazonian bellflower, is a striking herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens, though it requires specific care to flourish.
