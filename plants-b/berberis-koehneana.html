Berberis koehneana

Berberis koehneana, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright, spiny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Berberis koehneana is also valued for its medicinal properties, as it contains berberine, a compound with various health benefits.
