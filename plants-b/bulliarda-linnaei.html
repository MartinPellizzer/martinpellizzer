Bulliarda linnaei

Bulliarda linnaei, commonly known as the slender bulliarda, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and wetlands. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale blue to purple and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Bulliarda linnaei is often found in open, sunny habitats and is relatively uncommon in the wild.
