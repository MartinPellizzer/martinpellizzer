Bromelia ananas var. rubra

Bromelia ananas var. rubra, commonly known as the red pineapple bromeliad, is a striking plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, reddish-orange bracts that surround the flower. The plant features a rosette of long, waxy leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it produces a unique type of fruit that resembles a pineapple.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
