Bulbophyllum epicranthes var. sumatranum

Bulbophyllum epicranthes var. sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and possesses pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow and orange, which are borne on terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are usually elongated and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for under suitable conditions.
