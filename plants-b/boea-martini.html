Boea martini

Boea martini, commonly known as the silver dollar vine, is a trailing evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers.

Boea martini is valued for its ornamental foliage and easy maintenance, making it a popular choice in tropical and subtropical gardens.
