Bahia cuneata

Bahia cuneata, commonly known as the narrowleaf bahia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or blue in color. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.
