Bomarea pauciflora

Bomarea pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered bomarea, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Andean regions of South America. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with heart-shaped, glossy leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a white, petal-like spathe.

Bomarea pauciflora is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
