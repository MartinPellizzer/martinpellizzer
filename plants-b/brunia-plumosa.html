Brunia plumosa

Brunia plumosa, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its feathery, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost otherworldly appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in late winter or early spring, which are followed by bright red berries. Brunia plumosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
