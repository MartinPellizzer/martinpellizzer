Bredemeyera myrtifolia

Bredemeyera myrtifolia, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate ornamental value. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a slight fragrance when crushed.

Bredemeyera myrtifolia is well-suited for cultivation in warm, sunny climates and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
