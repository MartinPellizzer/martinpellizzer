Begonia morelii

Begonia morelii, commonly known as the Morello begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Begonia morelii thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

It is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
