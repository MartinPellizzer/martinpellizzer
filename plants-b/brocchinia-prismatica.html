Brocchinia prismatica

Brocchinia prismatica, commonly known as the prism leaf rosette, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, tightly packed rosette of thick, waxy leaves that form a cup-like structure. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps the plant retain moisture in its arid habitat. This unique morphology allows it to collect and store rainwater, which is essential for its survival.

Brocchinia prismatica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and interesting growth form.
