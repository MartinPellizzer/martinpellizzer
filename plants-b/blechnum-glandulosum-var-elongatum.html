Blechnum glandulosum var. elongatum

Blechnum glandulosum var. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated holly fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its long, narrow fronds with a distinctive glossy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its fronds are evergreen, and the plant produces sporangia on the underside of the leaves.

The variety elongatum is distinguished by its longer fronds compared to the typical form of Blechnum glandulosum.
