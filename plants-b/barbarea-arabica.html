Barbarea arabica

Barbarea arabica, commonly known as the Turkish rocket or winter cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants like cabbage and mustard.

Barbarea arabica is often used in traditional medicine and as a leafy green in culinary applications.
