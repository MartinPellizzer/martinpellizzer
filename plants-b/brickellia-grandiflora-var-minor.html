Brickellia grandiflora var. minor

Brickellia grandiflora var. minor, commonly known as dwarf brickellia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in compact clusters during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a desirable plant for native landscapes.
