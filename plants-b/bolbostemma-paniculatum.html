Bolbostemma paniculatum

Bolbostemma paniculatum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas and has a fibrous root system. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Bolbostemma paniculatum is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
