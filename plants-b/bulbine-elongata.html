Bulbine elongata

Bulbine elongata, commonly known as the narrowleaf bulbine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features fleshy, linear leaves and produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid environments and drought conditions.
