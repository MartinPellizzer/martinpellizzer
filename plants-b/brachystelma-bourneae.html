Brachystelma bourneae

Brachystelma bourneae, commonly known as the Bornean star cactus, is a succulent plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny stems and clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its flowers are usually small and star-shaped, often in shades of yellow or white.

Brachystelma bourneae is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
