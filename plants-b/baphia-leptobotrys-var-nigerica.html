Baphia leptobotrys var. nigerica

Baphia leptobotrys var. nigerica, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Leguminosae (Fabaceae) family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
