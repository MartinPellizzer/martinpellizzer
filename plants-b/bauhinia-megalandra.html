Bauhinia megalandra

Bauhinia megalandra, commonly known as the giant bauhinia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia megalandra is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
