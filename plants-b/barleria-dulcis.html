Barleria dulcis

Barleria dulcis, commonly known as sweet barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 1 meter tall, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant produces fragrant, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Barleria dulcis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
