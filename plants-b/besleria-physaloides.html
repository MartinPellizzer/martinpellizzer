Besleria physaloides

Besleria physaloides, commonly known as the balloon vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves and produces striking, balloon-like seed pods that turn from green to red as they mature. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, tropical conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique fruit structure.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
