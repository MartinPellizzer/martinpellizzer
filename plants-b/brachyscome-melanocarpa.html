Brachyscome melanocarpa

Brachyscome melanocarpa, commonly known as the black-flowered everlasting, is a small annual herb native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, dark purple to black flowers that are tightly packed in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Brachyscome melanocarpa is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
