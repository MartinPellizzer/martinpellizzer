Bulbophyllum Transarisanense

Transarisan orchid, scientifically known as Bulbophyllum transarisanense, is a rare and exquisite species native to the high-altitude regions of Indonesia. This orchid is celebrated for its striking floral structure and vibrant coloration, making it a standout among its genus. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and under-researched, with limited data on its ecological requirements and conservation status. Found primarily in the Transarisan Mountains, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich organic soil. Its discovery and study are crucial for understanding the biodiversity of Southeast Asian flora.
