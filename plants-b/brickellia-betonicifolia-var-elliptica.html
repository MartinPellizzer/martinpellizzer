Brickellia betonicifolia var. elliptica

Brickellia betonicifolia var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or prairies.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
