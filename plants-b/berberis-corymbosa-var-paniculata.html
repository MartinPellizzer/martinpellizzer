Berberis corymbosa var. paniculata

Berberis corymbosa var. paniculata, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptic leaflets, and it produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and red or orange berries that ripen in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden landscapes and hedges.
