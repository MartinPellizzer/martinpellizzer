Bulbophyllum elongatum

Bulbophyllum elongatum, commonly known as the elongated bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and narrow, leathery leaves. The plant produces delicate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
