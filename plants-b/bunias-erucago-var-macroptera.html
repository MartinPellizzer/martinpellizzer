Bunias erucago var. macroptera

Bunias erucago var. macroptera, commonly known as the giant Chinese kale, is a cultivar of the mustard family (Brassicaceae).

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant is often grown for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly peppery flavor. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its robust foliage.
