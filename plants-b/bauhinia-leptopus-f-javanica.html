Bauhinia leptopus f. javanica

Bauhinia leptopus f. javanica, commonly known as the Javan bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves and showy, papilionaceous flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white.
