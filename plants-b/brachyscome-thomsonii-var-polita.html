Brachyscome thomsonii var. polita

Brachyscome thomsonii var. polita, commonly known as the silver centipede plant, is a low-growing perennial native to Australia.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, decorative plant.
