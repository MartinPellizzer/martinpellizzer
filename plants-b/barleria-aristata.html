Barleria aristata

Barleria aristata, commonly known as the spiked barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
