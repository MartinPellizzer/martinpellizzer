Borzicactus krahnii

Borzicactus krahnii, commonly known as the Krahn's cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and is often covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hair, which helps to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces yellow flowers during the cooler months, adding a striking visual contrast to its otherwise muted appearance. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptation to extreme conditions, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
