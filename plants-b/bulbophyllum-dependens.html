Bulbophyllum dependens

Bulbophyllum dependens, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water and nutrients.

The flowers bloom in clusters, with a unique structure that features a prominent lip and intricate petal arrangements.
