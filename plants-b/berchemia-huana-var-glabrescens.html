Berchemia huana var. glabrescens

Berchemia huana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth berchemia, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
