Bothriochloa kwashotensis

Bothriochloa kwashotensis, commonly known as the Kwashotensis grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish florets.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
