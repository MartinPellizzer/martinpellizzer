Biarum bovei var. blumei

Biarum bovei var. blumei, commonly known as the Blume's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
