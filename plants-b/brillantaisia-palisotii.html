Brillantaisia palisotii

Brillantaisia palisotii, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-centered flowers that resemble stars, which bloom throughout the year. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Brillantaisia palisotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
