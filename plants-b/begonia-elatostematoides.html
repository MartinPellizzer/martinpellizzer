Begonia elatostematoides

Begonia elatostematoides, commonly known as the silver-leaf begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that have a unique, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
