Banjolea

Banjolea, commonly known as the blue banana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family, which also includes bananas and plantains. The plant produces large, elongated fruits that are typically blue or purple when mature, though they can sometimes be green. The leaves are broad and can reach several meters in length, making the plant a striking addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant fruit color and lush foliage.
