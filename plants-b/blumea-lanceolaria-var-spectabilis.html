Blumea lanceolaria var. spectabilis

Blumea lanceolaria var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
