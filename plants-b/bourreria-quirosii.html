Bourreria quirosii

Bourreria quirosii, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy appearance.

The botanical features of Bourreria quirosii make it a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
