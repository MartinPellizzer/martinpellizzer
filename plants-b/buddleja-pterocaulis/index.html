Buddleja Pterocaulis

Buddleja pterocaulis is a unique species of flowering plant native to southern China. It is known for its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. This shrub thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its unusual leaf structure and vibrant blooms make it a standout in any landscape. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
