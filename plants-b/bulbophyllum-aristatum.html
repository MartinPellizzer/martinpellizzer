Bulbophyllum aristatum

Bulbophyllum aristatum, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of numerous small, lance-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, elongated flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
