Brongniartia funiculata

Brongniartia funiculata, commonly known as the giant fern, is a large, tropical fern native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its massive fronds that can reach lengths of up to 6 meters. The plant has a complex rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are bioluminescent, a unique feature that aids in attracting pollinators at night.

Brongniartia funiculata plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
