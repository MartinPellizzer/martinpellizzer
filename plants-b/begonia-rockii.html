Begonia rockii

Begonia rockii, commonly known as the rock begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
