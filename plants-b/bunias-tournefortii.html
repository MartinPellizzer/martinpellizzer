Bunias tournefortii

Bunias tournefortii, commonly known as the Mediterranean mustard, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Bunias tournefortii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
