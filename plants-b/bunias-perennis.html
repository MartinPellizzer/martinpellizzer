Bunias perennis

Bunias perennis, commonly known as perennial winter cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round seed pods that release tiny seeds when disturbed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Bunias perennis is considered a hardy perennial that can tolerate cold temperatures and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
