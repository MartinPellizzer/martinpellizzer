Badula reticulata

Badula reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinct net-like venation pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
