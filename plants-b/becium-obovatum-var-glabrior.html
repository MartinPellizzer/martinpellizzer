Becium obovatum var. glabrior

Becium obovatum var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth becium, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its variety name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, elliptical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens across southern Europe.
