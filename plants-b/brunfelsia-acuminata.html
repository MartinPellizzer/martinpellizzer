Brunfelsia acuminata

Brunfelsia acuminata, commonly known as the night bloomer or angel's trumpets, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, blooming at night. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and emits a strong, sweet fragrance during the evening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds when mature.
