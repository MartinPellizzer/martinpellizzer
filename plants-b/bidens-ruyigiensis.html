Bidens ruyigiensis

Bidens ruyigiensis, commonly known as the Ruyigi ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with prominent white markings. Its flowering period occurs during the rainy season, and it is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
