Begonia morsei var. morsei

Begonia morsei var. morsei, commonly known as the Morsei begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
