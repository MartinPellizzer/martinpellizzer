Babiana pilosa

Babiana pilosa, commonly known as the pink babiana, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, pinkish-red flowers. Babiana pilosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
