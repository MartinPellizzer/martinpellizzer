Bulbophyllum theiochroma

Bulbophyllum theiochroma, commonly known as the dark red orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking dark red to purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs are thick and store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing and sought-after orchid in horticulture.
