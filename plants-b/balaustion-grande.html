Balaustion grande

Balaustion grande, commonly known as the large balsam, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Balaustion grande is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
