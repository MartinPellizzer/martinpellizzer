Begonia rubroflabellata

Begonia rubroflabellata, commonly known as the red-flowered begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with intricate petal patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia rubroflabellata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
