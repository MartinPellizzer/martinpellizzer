Bituminaria bituminosa

Bituminaria bituminosa, commonly known as the bituminous broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Bituminaria bituminosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
