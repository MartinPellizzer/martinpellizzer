Begonia lanternaria

Begonia lanternaria, commonly known as the lantern begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped bracts that surround its small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Begonia lanternaria is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant colors and unique foliage.
