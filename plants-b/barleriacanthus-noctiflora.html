Barleriacanthus noctiflora

Barleriacanthus noctiflora, commonly known as the night-flowering barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. The plant typically grows as a shrub with slender, woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.
