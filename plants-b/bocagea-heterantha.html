Bocagea heterantha

Bocagea heterantha, commonly known as the small-flowered bocagea, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Bocagea heterantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
