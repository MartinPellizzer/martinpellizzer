Baccharis papillosa subsp. yungensis

Baccharis papillosa subsp.

yungensis, commonly known as Yungense Baccharis, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are generally narrow and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
