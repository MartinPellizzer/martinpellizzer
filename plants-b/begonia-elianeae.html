Begonia elianeae

Begonia elianeae, commonly known as the Eliane's begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the warmer months.

This plant prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.
