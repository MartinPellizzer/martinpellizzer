Begonia boissieri

Begonia boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier begonia, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and distinctive foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture and deep green color. Its flowers are usually bright red or orange, with five petals and a prominent central column.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
