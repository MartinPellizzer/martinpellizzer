Berberis duclouxiana var. hilaica

Berberis duclouxiana var. hilaica, commonly known as the Hilaica barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it suitable for rock gardens and as a decorative hedge.
