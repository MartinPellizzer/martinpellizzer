Bulbostylis juncoides var. nana

Bulbostylis juncoides var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, three-lobed flowers. It is typically found in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
