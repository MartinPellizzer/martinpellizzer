Bifrenaria stefanae

Bifrenaria stefanae, commonly known as the Stefanae's Bifrenaria, is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to specific regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, bi-lobed leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in the early spring. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
