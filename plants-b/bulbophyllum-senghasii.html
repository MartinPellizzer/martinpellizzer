Bulbophyllum senghasii

Bulbophyllum senghasii, commonly known as the Senghas' orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, compact growth habit and produces striking, often fragrant flowers in a range of colors. The plant typically features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes and can bloom multiple times throughout the year under appropriate care.

Bulbophyllum senghasii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
