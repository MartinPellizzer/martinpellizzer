Blitum polymorphum var. rubrum

Blitum polymorphum var. rubrum, commonly known as red miner's lettuce, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features rosettes of spoon-shaped, reddish-pink leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and native plant gardens.
