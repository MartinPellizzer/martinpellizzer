Begonia fagopyroides

Begonia fagopyroides, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in shady, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
