Bulbophyllum reinwardtii

Bulbophyllum reinwardtii, commonly known as the Reinwardt's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers that can be yellow, green, or red in color. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Bulbophyllum reinwardtii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
