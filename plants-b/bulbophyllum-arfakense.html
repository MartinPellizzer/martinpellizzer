Bulbophyllum arfakense

Bulbophyllum arfakense, commonly known as the Arfak orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Indonesia.

It features a compact growth habit with short, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the wet season. It thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value contribute to its interest among orchid enthusiasts.
