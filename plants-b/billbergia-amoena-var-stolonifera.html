Billbergia amoena var. stolonifera

Billbergia amoena var. stolonifera, commonly known as the Stoloniferous Billbergia, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central tank to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red and orange flowers. It is a clumping plant that spreads via stolons, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its foliage is often variegated with silver and green tones, adding ornamental value to its appearance.
