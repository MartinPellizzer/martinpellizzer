Barleria blinii

Barleria blinii, commonly known as the silverleaf barleria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Barleria blinii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
