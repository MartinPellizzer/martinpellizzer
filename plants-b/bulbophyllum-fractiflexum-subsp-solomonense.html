Bulbophyllum fractiflexum subsp. solomonense

Bulbophyllum fractiflexum subsp.

solomonense, commonly known as the Solomon Islands orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Solomon Islands. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit.

The plant typically produces a single erect stem with several lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip, and it blooms during the wet season.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
