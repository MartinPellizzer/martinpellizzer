Bulbophyllum shanicum

Bulbophyllum shanicum, commonly known as the Shanicum's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth during dry periods. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Bulbophyllum shanicum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique morphology.
