Belonites succulentus

Belonites succulentus, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is native to regions with Mediterranean climates. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Belonites succulentus is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal water and full sun to partial shade.
