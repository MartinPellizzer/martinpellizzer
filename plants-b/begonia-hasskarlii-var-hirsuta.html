Begonia hasskarlii var. hirsuta

Begonia hasskarlii var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy foliage that gives it a unique texture and appearance. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can range in color from deep green to reddish hues. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and shaded outdoor spaces.
