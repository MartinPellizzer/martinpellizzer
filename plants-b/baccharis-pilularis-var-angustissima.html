Baccharis pilularis var. angustissima

Baccharis pilularis var. angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to California and Baja California.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, white to yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often used in restoration projects.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
