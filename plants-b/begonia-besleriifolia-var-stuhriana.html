Begonia besleriifolia var. stuhriana

Begonia besleriifolia var. stuhriana, commonly known as the Stuhriana Begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
