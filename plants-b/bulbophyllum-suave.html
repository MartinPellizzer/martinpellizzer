Bulbophyllum suave

Bulbophyllum suave, commonly known as the sweet bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in shaded, humid environments.

Bulbophyllum suave is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation.
