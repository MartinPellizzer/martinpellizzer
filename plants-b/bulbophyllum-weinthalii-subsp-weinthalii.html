Bulbophyllum weinthalii subsp. weinthalii

Bulbophyllum weinthalii subsp.

weinthalii, commonly known as the Weinthal's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia.

This epiphytic plant features intricate, often fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to mimic its natural conditions.
