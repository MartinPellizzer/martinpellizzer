Bulbophyllum spongiola

Bulbophyllum spongiola, commonly known as the spongey orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, sponge-like appearance, which is reflected in its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Bulbophyllum spongiola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
