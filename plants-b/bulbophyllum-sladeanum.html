Bulbophyllum sladeanum

Bulbophyllum sladeanum, commonly known as the Slade's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrates.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
