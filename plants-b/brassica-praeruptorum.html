Brassica praeruptorum

Brassica praeruptorum, commonly known as the Chinese yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and broccoli. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has a distinctive, pungent aroma. Its leaves are edible and often used in traditional Chinese cuisine.

The seeds are also used in various culinary and medicinal applications.
