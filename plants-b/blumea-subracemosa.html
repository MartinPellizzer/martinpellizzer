Blumea subracemosa

Blumea subracemosa, commonly known as the Chinese Blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in a raceme, contributing to its ornamental value.
