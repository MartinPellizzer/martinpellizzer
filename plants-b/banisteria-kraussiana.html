Banisteria kraussiana

Banisteria kraussiana, commonly known as the red spider flower, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its climbing growth habit.

Banisteria kraussiana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
