Begonia scutulum

Begonia scutulum, commonly known as the shield begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, shield-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to deep red. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of ornamental plants.
