Berberis soulieana

Berberis soulieana, commonly known as Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The plant is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
