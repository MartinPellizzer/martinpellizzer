Bakeriella cerasifera

Bakeriella cerasifera, commonly known as the cherryleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

Bakeriella cerasifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
