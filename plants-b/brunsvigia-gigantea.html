Brunsvigia gigantea

Brunsvigia gigantea, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces large, showy inflorescences with white or pink flowers that resemble spider-like blooms. The plant is known for its dramatic flowering display, which occurs after a period of dormancy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a standout in botanical collections.
