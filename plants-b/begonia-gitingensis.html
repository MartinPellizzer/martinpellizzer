Begonia gitingensis

Begonia gitingensis, commonly known as the Gitingensis begonia, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The leaves are often covered in a fine, silky hair, contributing to its unique appearance.
