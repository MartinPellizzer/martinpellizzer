Bignonia marginata

Bignonia marginata, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green margin and produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is part of the Bignoniaceae family, which includes several ornamental species. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Bignonia marginata is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and showy blooms in gardens and landscapes.
