Bulbophyllum orectopetalum

Bulbophyllum orectopetalum, commonly known as the upright lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces a single, upright flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Due to its unique appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
