Barleria gandhii

Barleria gandhii, commonly known as African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features upright, branching stems covered with soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are showy, with five petals arranged in a radial pattern, often in shades of red, pink, or white.

Barleria gandhii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
