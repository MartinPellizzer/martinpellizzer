Bidens langlassei

Bidens langlassei, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in grasslands and disturbed areas, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the flowers are yellow with prominent central disks.

Bidens langlassei is valued for its use in traditional medicine, particularly for treating ailments such as inflammation and skin conditions.
