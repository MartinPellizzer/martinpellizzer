Buekia

Buekia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its ability to bloom continuously with proper care.
