Bupleurum ajanense var. alpinum

Bupleurum ajanense var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine bupleurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and cool, temperate climates. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pale yellow.

Bupleurum ajanense var. alpinum is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
