Borzicactus websterianus var. rufispinus

Borzicactus websterianus var. rufispinus, commonly known as the red-spined borzicactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically sparsely covered with white to grayish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its otherwise desolate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in succulent gardens and arid landscapes.
