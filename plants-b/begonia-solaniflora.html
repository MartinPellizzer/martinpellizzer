Begonia solaniflora

Begonia solaniflora, commonly known as the nightshade begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often with a contrasting central spot. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, featuring heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers bloom in clusters, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in shaded to semi-shaded environments.

Begonia solaniflora is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
