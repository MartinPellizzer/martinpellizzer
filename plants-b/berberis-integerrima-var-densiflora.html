Berberis integerrima var. densiflora

Berberis integerrima var. densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its dense growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
