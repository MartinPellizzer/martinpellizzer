Brunfelsia abbottii

Brunfelsia abbottii, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep purple to white. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.
