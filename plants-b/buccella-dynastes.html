Buccella dynastes

Buccella dynastes, commonly known as the scarlet tiger beetle, is a small, brightly colored beetle belonging to the family Carabidae.

It is characterized by its striking red body with black stripes and long, slender legs. The plant-like appearance of its exoskeleton can sometimes lead to confusion with certain flowering plants. While not a plant, it is often found near vegetation, particularly in sandy or rocky habitats.

Its name reflects its predatory nature, as it is an active hunter of small insects.
