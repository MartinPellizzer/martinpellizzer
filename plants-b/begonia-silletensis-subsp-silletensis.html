Begonia silletensis subsp. silletensis

Begonia silletensis subsp.

silletensis, commonly known as the Sillet's begonia, is a species native to Mexico. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
