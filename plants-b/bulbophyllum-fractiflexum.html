Bulbophyllum fractiflexum

Bulbophyllum fractiflexum, commonly known as the flexible orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its tropical habitat. Its leaves are usually strap-like and can vary in length depending on environmental conditions.

Bulbophyllum fractiflexum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
