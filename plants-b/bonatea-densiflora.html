Bonatea densiflora

Bonatea densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower bonatea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
