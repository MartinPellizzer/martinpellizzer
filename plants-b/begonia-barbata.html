Begonia barbata

Begonia barbata, commonly known as the trailing begonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and often features a trailing growth habit, making it popular in hanging baskets and containers. Begonia barbata thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, requiring moderate moisture and indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a favored choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
