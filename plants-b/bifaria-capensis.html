Bifaria capensis

Bifaria capensis, commonly known as the Cape bifaria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its water-storing tissues.

Bifaria capensis is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
