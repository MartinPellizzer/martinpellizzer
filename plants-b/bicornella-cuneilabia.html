Bicornella cuneilabia

Bicornella cuneilabia, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The leaves are linear and often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for cultivation in warm, humid climates.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
