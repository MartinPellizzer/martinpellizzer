Biscutella juilletii

Biscutella juilletii, commonly known as the French bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Biscutella juilletii thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions.
