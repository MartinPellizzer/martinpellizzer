Bletilla guizhouensis

Bletilla guizhouensis, commonly known as the Guizhou orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky substrates. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
