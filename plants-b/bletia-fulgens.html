Bletia fulgens

Bletia fulgens, commonly known as the showy bletia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or brown markings. Bletia fulgens thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
