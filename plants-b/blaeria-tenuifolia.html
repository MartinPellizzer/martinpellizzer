Blaeria tenuifolia

Blaeria tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf blazer, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
