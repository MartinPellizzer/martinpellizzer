Buxus leonii

Buxus leonii, commonly known as the African boxwood, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact foliage with dark green, glossy leaves that are oval in shape. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has a rounded, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a topiary plant.

Buxus leonii is prized for its ornamental value and ability to maintain a neat shape with minimal pruning.
