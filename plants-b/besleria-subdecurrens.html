Besleria subdecurrens

Besleria subdecurrens, commonly known as the narrow-leafed besleria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens due to its ornamental value.
