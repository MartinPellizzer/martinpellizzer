Bulbophyllum hatusimanum

Bulbophyllum hatusimanum, commonly known as the Hatusima orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its tropical environment.

The flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.
