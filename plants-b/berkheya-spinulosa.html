Berkheya spinulosa

Berkheya spinulosa, commonly known as the spinous berkheya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has serrated, hairy leaves that are adapted to withstand arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Berkheya spinulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
