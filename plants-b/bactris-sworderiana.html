Bactris sworderiana

Bactris sworderiana, commonly known as the sword palm, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that resemble a sword, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible, coconut-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The leaves are used in traditional crafts and as thatching materials in some regions.
