Blandfordia punicea

Blandfordia punicea, commonly known as the red lily or pink lily, is a striking plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, branched stem that supports its vibrant floral display.
