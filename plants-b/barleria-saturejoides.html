Barleria saturejoides

Barleria saturejoides, commonly known as the purple barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces spikes of small, showy flowers in shades of purple and pink. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
