Bakeridesia zapoteca

Bakeridesia zapoteca, commonly known as the Zapotec orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces fragrant blossoms in shades of white and pink.

Bakeridesia zapoteca is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
