Begonia hemsleyana var. kwangsiensis

Begonia hemsleyana var. kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi begonia, is a striking plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. The leaves are often heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and the plant prefers shady, humid conditions. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

This variety is particularly valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to indoor environments.
