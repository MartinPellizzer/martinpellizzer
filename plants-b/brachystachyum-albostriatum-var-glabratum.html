Brachystachyum albostriatum var. glabratum

Brachystachyum albostriatum var. glabratum, commonly known as the silky grass.

It is a grass species native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its slender, tufted growth and silver-green foliage. The leaves are narrow and have a distinct whitish stripe along the midrib, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
