Besleria labiosa

Besleria labiosa, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in humid, well-drained environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year.

Besleria labiosa requires bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
