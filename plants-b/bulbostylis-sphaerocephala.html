Bulbostylis sphaerocephala

Bulbostylis sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globular bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous base and a dense cluster of short, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are often hidden among the foliage. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
