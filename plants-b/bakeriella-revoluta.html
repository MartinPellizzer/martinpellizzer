Bakeriella revoluta

Bakeriella revoluta, commonly known as the revolute bakeriella, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features elliptical leaves with a revolute margin, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually green or white in color.

Bakeriella revoluta is primarily found in specific regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in humid, temperate climates.
