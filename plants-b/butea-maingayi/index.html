Butea Maingayi

Butea maingayi is a striking flowering plant native to Southeast Asia known for its vibrant orange-red blooms. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions and is commonly found in open woodlands and along riverbanks. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. Its flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Butea maingayi holds cultural significance in some local communities and is also valued for its medicinal properties.
