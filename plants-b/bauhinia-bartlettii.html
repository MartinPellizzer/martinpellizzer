Bauhinia bartlettii

Bauhinia bartlettii, commonly known as the red bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Bauhinia bartlettii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
