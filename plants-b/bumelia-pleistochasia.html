Bumelia pleistochasia

Bumelia pleistochasia, commonly known as the Mexican buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
