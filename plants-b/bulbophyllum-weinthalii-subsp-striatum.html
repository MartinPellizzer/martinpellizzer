Bulbophyllum weinthalii subsp. striatum

Bulbophyllum weinthalii subsp.

striatum, commonly known as the Striped Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, elongated pseudobulbs.

The leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. The orchid produces showy, fragrant blooms in shades of white, yellow, and green.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
