Buddleja × Griffithii

Buddleja × griffithii is a hybrid butterfly bush known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. It is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. Its long, cone-shaped flower clusters bloom in late summer, adding color to the garden. Despite its beauty, it requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.
