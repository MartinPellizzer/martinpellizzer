Bidens consolidifolia

Bidens consolidifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent central disks. It thrives in wetland habitats and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Bidens consolidifolia is known for its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity in its native environment.
