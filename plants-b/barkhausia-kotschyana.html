Barkhausia kotschyana

Barkhausia kotschyana, commonly known as the Kotschy's barkhausia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a woody stem covered in a rough, fissured bark, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a basal rosette.

Barkhausia kotschyana is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky habitats.
