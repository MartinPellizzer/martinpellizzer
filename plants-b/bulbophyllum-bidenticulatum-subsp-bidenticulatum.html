Bulbophyllum bidenticulatum subsp. bidenticulatum

Bulbophyllum bidenticulatum subsp.

bidenticulatum, commonly known as the two-toothed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a deep orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
