Brickellia hartwegii

Brickellia hartwegii, commonly known as Hartweg's brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
