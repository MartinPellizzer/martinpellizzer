Bidens fruticosa

Bidens fruticosa, commonly known as yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, sunny environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers with five petals and a distinctive yellow center. Its leaves are trifoliate, meaning they are divided into three leaflets.

Bidens fruticosa is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is often used in traditional medicine.
