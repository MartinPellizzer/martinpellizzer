Bobua stellaris

Bobua stellaris, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Bobua stellaris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
