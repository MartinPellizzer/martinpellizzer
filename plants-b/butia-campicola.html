Butia campicola

Butia campicola, commonly known as the coastal palm, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellowish-white inflorescences that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salty air.

Butia campicola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
