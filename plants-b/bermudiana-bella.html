Bermudiana bella

Bermudiana bella, commonly known as the Bermuda beauty, is a flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of Bermuda.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as a low shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
