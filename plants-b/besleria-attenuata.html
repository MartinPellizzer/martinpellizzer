Besleria attenuata

Besleria attenuata, commonly known as the slender besleria, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and attractive, tubular flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
