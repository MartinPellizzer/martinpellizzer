Brachyscome cardiocarpa var. alpina

Brachyscome cardiocarpa var. alpina, commonly known as the Alpine heart's ease, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive central yellow disc. The plant has a compact growth habit, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are densely packed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate and alpine climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in marginal garden conditions.
