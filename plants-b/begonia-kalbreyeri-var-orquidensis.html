Begonia kalbreyeri var. orquidensis

Begonia kalbreyeri var. orquidensis, commonly known as the Orchid Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
