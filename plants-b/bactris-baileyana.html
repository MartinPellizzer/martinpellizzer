Bactris baileyana

Bactris baileyana, commonly known as the pink pataua, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a tall, slender palm with a straight trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits that are orange-red in color. Bactris baileyana is adapted to grow in coastal and sandy soils, often found in wetland areas.
