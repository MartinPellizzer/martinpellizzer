Byrsonima Altissima

Byrsonima altissima is a tall, fast-growing tree native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The tree produces edible fruits that are commonly used in local cuisines and traditional medicine. Its wood is valued for its strength and durability in construction. This plant plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts in its native habitat.
