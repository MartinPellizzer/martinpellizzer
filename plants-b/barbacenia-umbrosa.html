Barbacenia umbrosa

Barbacenia umbrosa, commonly known as the shadowy barbacenia, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that exhibit a dark green hue with a subtle sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, attracting various pollinators. Barbacenia umbrosa thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
