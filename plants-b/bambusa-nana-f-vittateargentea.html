Bambusa nana f. vittateargentea

Bambusa nana f. vittateargentea, commonly known as the silver stripe bamboo, is a compact, ornamental bamboo species native to southern China.

It features narrow, upright culms with a distinctive silvery-white stripe running along the length of each stem. The leaves are small, lance-shaped, and dark green, adding to its visual appeal. This variety is prized for its dense, bushy growth habit and its ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for privacy screens or as a decorative plant in gardens.
