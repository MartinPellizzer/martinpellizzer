Botrychium lunaria var. vulgare

Botrychium lunaria var. vulgare, commonly known as the moonwort, is a small fern belonging to the family Onocleaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Botrychium lunaria var. vulgare is often found in temperate regions and is considered a rare and ancient fern species.
