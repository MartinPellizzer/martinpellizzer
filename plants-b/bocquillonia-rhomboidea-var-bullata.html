Bocquillonia rhomboidea var. bullata

Bocquillonia rhomboidea var. bullata, commonly known as the bullate rhomboid, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

The leaves are rhomboidal in shape, with a textured surface that gives them a slightly raised, bulbous appearance. This variation is most prominent on the upper surface, where small, rounded protuberances are present. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for taxonomic study and botanical illustration.
