Bupleurum sichuanense

Bupleurum sichuanense, commonly known as Sichuan cowbane, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes and roots are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
