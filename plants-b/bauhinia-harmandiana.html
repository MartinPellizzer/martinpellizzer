Bauhinia harmandiana

Bauhinia harmandiana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Bauhinia harmandiana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
