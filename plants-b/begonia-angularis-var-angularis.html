Begonia angularis var. angularis

Begonia angularis var. angularis, commonly known as the angular begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a vibrant green color. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens, containers, and borders.
