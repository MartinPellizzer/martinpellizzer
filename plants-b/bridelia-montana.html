Bridelia montana

Bridelia montana, commonly known as mountain bridelia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It has some ornamental value due to its compact growth habit and seasonal flowering.
