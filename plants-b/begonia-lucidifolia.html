Begonia lucidifolia

Begonia lucidifolia, commonly known as the glossy begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, and often brightly colored leaves that can range from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun, depending on the climate.

Due to its attractive foliage and flowers, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
