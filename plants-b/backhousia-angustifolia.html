Backhousia angustifolia

Backhousia angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved backhousia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Indigenous medicine.
