Biscutella foliosa

Biscutella foliosa, commonly known as the leafy biscutella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its simple, often hairy leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in arid environments.
