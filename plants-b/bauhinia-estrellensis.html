Bauhinia estrellensis

Bauhinia estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellensis Bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Central America and is characterized by its distinctive, papery, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding ornamental value. Bauhinia estrellensis is often cultivated for its unique foliage and can thrive in well-drained soils with partial to full sunlight.

It is also valued for its ecological role in nitrogen fixation, supporting soil fertility.
