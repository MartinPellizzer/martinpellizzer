Bromus strictus

Bromus strictus, commonly known as slender brome, is a grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils.

Bromus strictus is often found in temperate climates and is adaptable to different soil types.
