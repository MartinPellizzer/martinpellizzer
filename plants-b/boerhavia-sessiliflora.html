Boerhavia sessiliflora

Boerhavia sessiliflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bluestem, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

The plant has small, ovate leaves that are sessile, meaning they are attached directly to the stem without a stalk.
