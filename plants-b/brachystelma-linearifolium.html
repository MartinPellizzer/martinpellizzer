Brachystelma linearifolium

Brachystelma linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed stone orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among succulent and orchid enthusiasts.
