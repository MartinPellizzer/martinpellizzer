Begonia jugamensis

Begonia jugamensis, commonly known as the Cameroon begonia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the rainforests of Cameroon.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
