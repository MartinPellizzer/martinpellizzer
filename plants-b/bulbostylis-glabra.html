Bulbostylis glabra

Bulbostylis glabra, commonly known as the smooth bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
