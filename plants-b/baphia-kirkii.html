Baphia kirkii

Baphia kirkii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms. Baphia kirkii also has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil, making it useful in reforestation efforts.
