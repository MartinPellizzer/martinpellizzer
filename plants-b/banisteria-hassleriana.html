Banisteria hassleriana

Banisteria hassleriana, commonly known as the red spider flower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Banisteria hassleriana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
