Bromus setifolius

Bromus setifolius, commonly known as slender leaf brome, is a species of grass native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy leaves and compact, spikelike inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has small, pale green to yellowish flowers that appear in late summer.

Bromus setifolius is often found in association with other grasses and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
