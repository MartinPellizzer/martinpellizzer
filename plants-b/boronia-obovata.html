Boronia obovata

Boronia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf boronia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth texture and a slightly aromatic scent.
