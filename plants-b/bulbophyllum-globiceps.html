Bulbophyllum globiceps

Bulbophyllum globiceps, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, round-shaped pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts.
