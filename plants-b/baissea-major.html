Baissea major

Baissea major, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Baissea major is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and long blooming period.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and floral arrangements.
