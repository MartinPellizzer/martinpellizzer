Bambusa pierreana

Bambusa pierreana, commonly known as the black bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, culms that contrast beautifully with its bright green, lance-shaped leaves. This bamboo grows in clumps and can reach heights of up to 10 meters, making it a dramatic addition to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, and it is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Bambusa pierreana is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of climates, making it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate regions.
