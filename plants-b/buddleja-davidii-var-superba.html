Buddleja davidii var. superba

Buddleja davidii var. superba, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
