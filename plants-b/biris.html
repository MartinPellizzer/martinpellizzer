Biris

Biris, commonly known as the lesser periwinkle, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces delicate blue or violet flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, round berries. Biris thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
