Brunfelsia nitida var. jamaicensis

Brunfelsia nitida var. jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican flame bean, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a contrasting yellow or orange throat. The plant produces long, bean-like pods that contain seeds, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
