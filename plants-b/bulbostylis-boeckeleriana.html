Bulbostylis boeckeleriana

Bulbostylis boeckeleriana, commonly known as the African ricegrass, is a small, grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces tiny, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
