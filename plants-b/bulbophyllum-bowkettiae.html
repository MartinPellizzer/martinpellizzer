Bulbophyllum bowkettiae

Bulbophyllum bowkettiae, commonly known as the Bowkett's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a pleasant fragrance. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in less-than-ideal conditions.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relative ease of cultivation.
