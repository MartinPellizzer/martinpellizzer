Borraginoides physaloides

Borraginoides physaloides, commonly known as the balloon vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces showy, inflated fruit that resembles small balloons. The plant features hairy stems and leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
