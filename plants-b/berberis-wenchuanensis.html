Berberis wenchuanensis

Berberis wenchuanensis, commonly known as the Wenchuan barberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Sichuan province.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often bitter.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
