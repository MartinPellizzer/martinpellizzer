Bulbophyllum funingense

Bulbophyllum funingense, commonly known as the Funing Bletilla, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, lance-shaped leaves and blooms with flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
