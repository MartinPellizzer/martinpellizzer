Boehmeria × kiyozumensis

Boehmeria × kiyozumensis, commonly known as the hybrid giant bamboo, is a flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Boehmeria nivea and Boehmeria yamatensis, characterized by its tall, robust stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and fast growth rate.

Its foliage is typically a vibrant green, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in panicles.
