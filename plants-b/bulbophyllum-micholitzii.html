Bulbophyllum micholitzii

Bulbophyllum micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity for optimal growth. It is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Bulbophyllum micholitzii is also known for its ability to form pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients.
