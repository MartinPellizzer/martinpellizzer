Besleria triflora var. australis

Besleria triflora var. australis, commonly known as the Australian spider orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with a distinctive yellow lip, and it blooms during the warmer months.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.
