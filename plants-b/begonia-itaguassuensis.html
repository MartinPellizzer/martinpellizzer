Begonia itaguassuensis

Begonia itaguassuensis, commonly known as the Itaguassu begonia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
