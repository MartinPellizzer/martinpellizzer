Begonia labiensis

Begonia labiensis, commonly known as the Labieni begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or in shaded garden settings. It has a short stem and produces small, rounded leaves with a waxy texture.

Begonia labiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light conditions.
