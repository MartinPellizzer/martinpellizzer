Bulbophyllum hatschbachianum

Bulbophyllum hatschbachianum, commonly known as the Hatschbach's orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate and often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small but numerous, arranged in a dense inflorescence, and often feature intricate patterns.

The botanical interest of this species lies in its unique morphology and ecological adaptations to its humid, shaded habitat.
