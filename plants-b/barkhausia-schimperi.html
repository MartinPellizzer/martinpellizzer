Barkhausia schimperi

Barkhausia schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Ethiopia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to its arid environment.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
