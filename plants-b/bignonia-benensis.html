Bignonia benensis

Bignonia benensis, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family, which includes many ornamental and woody plants. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Bignonia benensis is typically found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
