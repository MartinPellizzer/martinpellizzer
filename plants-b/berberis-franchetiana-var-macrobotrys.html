Berberis franchetiana var. macrobotrys

Berberis franchetiana var. macrobotrys, commonly known as the large-fruited barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red berries that mature in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and showy fruit display.
