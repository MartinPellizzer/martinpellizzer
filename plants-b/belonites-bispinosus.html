Belonites bispinosus

Belonites bispinosus, commonly known as the two-spined belonite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Belonites bispinosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
