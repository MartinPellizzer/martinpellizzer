Barbarea vulgaris var. doellii

Barbarea vulgaris var. doellii, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in moist soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas. It has a rosette of basal leaves and elongated stems with alternate leaves.

The species is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and ease of growth.
