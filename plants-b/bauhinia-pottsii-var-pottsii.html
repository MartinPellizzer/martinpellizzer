Bauhinia pottsii var. pottsii

Bauhinia pottsii var. pottsii, commonly known as the Potts' bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features bipinnate leaves composed of numerous tiny leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom throughout the warm seasons. Its woody structure and adaptability to various soil types make it a resilient species in its native habitat.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
