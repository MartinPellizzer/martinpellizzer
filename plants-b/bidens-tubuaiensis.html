Bidens tubuaiensis

Bidens tubuaiensis, commonly known as the tropical ironweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands. Its leaves are ovate with rough textures, and it produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Bidens tubuaiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
