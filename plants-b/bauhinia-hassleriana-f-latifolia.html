Bauhinia hassleriana f. latifolia

Bauhinia hassleriana f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf bauhinia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are broad and often have a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
