Bergenia × ornata

Bergenia × ornata, commonly known as the hybrid bergenia, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rosette-forming foliage. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, decorative plants.
