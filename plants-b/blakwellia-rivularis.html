Blakwellia rivularis

Blakwellia rivularis, commonly known as the river blakwellia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

Blakwellia rivularis is adapted to wet conditions and has a fibrous root system that helps it thrive in waterlogged soils.
