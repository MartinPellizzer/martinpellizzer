Bumelia megaphylla

Bumelia megaphylla, commonly known as the giant leaf bumelia, is a woody shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 12 inches in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported healing properties.

Bumelia megaphylla thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
