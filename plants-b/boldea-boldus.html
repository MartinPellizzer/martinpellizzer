Boldea boldus

Boldea boldus, commonly known as boldus herb, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers. Boldea boldus is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and aromatic qualities.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.
