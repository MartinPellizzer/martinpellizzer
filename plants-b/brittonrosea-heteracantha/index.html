Brittonrosea Heteracantha

Brittonrosea heteracantha is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It belongs to the genus Brittonrosea, which is part of the sunflower family, Asteraceae. This species is known for its unique, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. Its name reflects both its geographical origin and its distinct floral structure. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
