Breweria capensis var. parviflora

Breweria capensis var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered breweria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is a hardy species that is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
