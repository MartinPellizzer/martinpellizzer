Bidens humboldtii

Bidens humboldtii, commonly known as the yellow oxeye.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features yellow flowers with white markings, typically blooming in late summer to early fall. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Bidens humboldtii is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
