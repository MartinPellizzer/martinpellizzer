Bulbophyllum mentosum

Bulbophyllum mentosum, commonly known as the Mentos orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates.

The Mentos orchid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
