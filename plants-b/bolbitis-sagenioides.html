Bolbitis sagenioides

Bolbitis sagenioides, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features a creeping rhizome with dense, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and leathery. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance.

Bolbitis sagenioides thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.
