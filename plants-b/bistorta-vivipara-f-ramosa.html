Bistorta vivipara f. ramosa

Bistorta vivipara f. ramosa, commonly known as the ramose bistorta, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that are covered in a distinctive reddish-brown coloration. The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
