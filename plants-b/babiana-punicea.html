Babiana punicea

Babiana punicea, commonly known as the fire pink, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a bold splash of color to its native habitat. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces erect, tubular flower stalks. Native to the Western and Northern Cape provinces, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Babiana punicea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
