Begonia scortechinii var. kunstleriana

Begonia scortechinii var. kunstleriana, commonly known as the Kunstler's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
