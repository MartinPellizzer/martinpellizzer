Begonia sutherlandii var. minuscula

Begonia sutherlandii var. minuscula, commonly known as the miniature begonia, is a compact, trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
