Withania sinica

Withania sinica, commonly known as the Indian winter cherry, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and red, berry-like fruits that mature in the winter season. The botanical features of Withania sinica make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
