Westringia longepedunculata

Westringia longepedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate westringia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, aromatic leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
