Witheringia stellata

Witheringia stellata, commonly known as the star-shaped nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine pubescence.

Witheringia stellata contains alkaloids and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
