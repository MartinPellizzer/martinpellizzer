Weingartia cuprea

Weingartia cuprea, commonly known as the red weingartia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, ribbed stems. The plant features short, spiny areoles and produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to full sun.

Weingartia cuprea is a slow-growing, low-maintenance plant well-suited for indoor cultivation.
