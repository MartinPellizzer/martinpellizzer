Weingartia attenuata

Weingartia attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved booby.

It is a species of cactus native to Mexico, characterized by its compact, rounded shape and pale green stems. The plant features short, cylindrical spines and narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Weingartia attenuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a desert garden setting.
