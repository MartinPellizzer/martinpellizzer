Watsonia aletroides

Watsonia aletroides, commonly known as the Orange Watsonia, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves and produces showy, cone-shaped flower spikes that bloom in shades of orange and red. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is closely related to other members of the Watsonia genus. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and horticultural collections.
