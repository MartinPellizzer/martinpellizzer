Wedelia bahamensis

Wedelia bahamensis, commonly known as the Bahamian wedelia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Wedelia bahamensis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid climates.
