Weigela decora f. viridiflava

Weigela decora f. viridiflava, commonly known as the green-flowered weigela, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its unique, pale green to white flowers. The flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also noted for its moderate growth habit and resistance to common pests and diseases.
