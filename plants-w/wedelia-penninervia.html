Wedelia penninervia

Wedelia penninervia, commonly known as the yellow carpet grass.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features spreading rhizomes and produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its foliage is dense and often used in ornamental landscaping for its vibrant color and ground-covering ability.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and prolific flowering habit.
