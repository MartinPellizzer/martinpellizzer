Watsonia desmidtii

Watsonia desmidtii, commonly known as the Orange River Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range from orange to red. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. Watsonia desmidtii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have contributed to its cultivation in various regions beyond its native range.
