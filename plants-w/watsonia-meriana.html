Watsonia meriana

Watsonia meriana, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant orange or red flowers, often with a contrasting black or purple center. Watsonia meriana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that add texture to the landscape.
