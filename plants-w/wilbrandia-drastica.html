Wilbrandia drastica

Wilbrandia drastica, commonly known as the yellow bell.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in regions of the Lesser Antilles. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing near the shoreline.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
