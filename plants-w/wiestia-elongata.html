Wiestia elongata

Wiestia elongata, commonly known as the slender wiestia, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as under dense canopy cover in rainforests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss in humid conditions.

Wiestia elongata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
