Watsonia caledonica

Watsonia caledonica, commonly known as the Cape Lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, grass-like leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. Watsonia caledonica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its vibrant blooms attract pollinators and add a dramatic visual impact to any landscape.
