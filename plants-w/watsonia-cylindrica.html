Watsonia cylindrica

Watsonia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, cone-like inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia cylindrica is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
