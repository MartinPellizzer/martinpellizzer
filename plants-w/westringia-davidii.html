Westringia davidii

Westringia davidii, commonly known as the coastal rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal and rocky garden environments.

Westringia davidii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
