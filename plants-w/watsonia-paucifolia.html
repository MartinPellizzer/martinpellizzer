Watsonia paucifolia

Watsonia paucifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, red or orange inflorescences that resemble torches. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Watsonia paucifolia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both gardens and floral arrangements.
