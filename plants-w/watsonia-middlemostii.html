Watsonia middlemostii

Watsonia middlemostii, commonly known as the Cape mountains Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of tubular, often brightly colored blooms that range from red to orange and yellow. Watsonia middlemostii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that add texture to the landscape.
