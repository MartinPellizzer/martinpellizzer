Wedelia populifolia

Wedelia populifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rounded, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, yellow-orange flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom throughout the growing season.

Wedelia populifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
