Weigela hortensis nivea

Weigela hortensis nivea, commonly known as the white weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers, which are borne on slender pedicels. Its foliage consists of oval, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to the delicate blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
