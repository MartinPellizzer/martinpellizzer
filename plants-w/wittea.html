Wittea

Wittea, commonly known as the Indian bean or velvet bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. The plant is often cultivated for its edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines. Wittea also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it an important plant in sustainable agriculture.
