Wimmeria excoriata

Wimmeria excoriata, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species with characteristic cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are often small and covered in a waxy layer to reduce water loss.

Wimmeria excoriata is known for its resilience and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
