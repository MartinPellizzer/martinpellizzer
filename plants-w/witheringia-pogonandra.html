Witheringia pogonandra

Witheringia pogonandra, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique flowering pattern and vibrant colors make it a standout in botanical gardens and native plant collections.
