Wedelia rechingeriana

Wedelia rechingeriana, commonly known as the orange butterfly plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, with lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread make it both a desirable and sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
