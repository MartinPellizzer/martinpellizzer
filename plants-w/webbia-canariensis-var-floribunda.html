Webbia canariensis var. floribunda

Webbia canariensis var. floribunda, commonly known as the Canary Island spider plant, is a hybrid cultivar of the spider plant genus.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Canary Islands, characterized by its rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its air-purifying qualities and ease of care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
