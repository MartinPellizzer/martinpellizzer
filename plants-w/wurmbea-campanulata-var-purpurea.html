Wurmbea campanulata var. purpurea

Wurmbea campanulata var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique flower shape.
