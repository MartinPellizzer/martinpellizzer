Weigela decora f. nivea

Weigela decora f. nivea, commonly known as the White Weigela, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, adding a striking contrast to the dark green foliage. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for various garden settings.
