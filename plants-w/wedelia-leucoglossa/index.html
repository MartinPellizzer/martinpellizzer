Wedelia Leucoglossa

Wedelia leucoglossa is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant white petals and silver-green foliage. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. This perennial plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. With proper care, it can bloom throughout the growing season, adding beauty and color to any outdoor space.
