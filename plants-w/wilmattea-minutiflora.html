Wilmattea minutiflora

Wilmattea minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf wilmattea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast to its delicate white flowers. The plant produces small, clustered blooms that appear throughout the growing season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening or as a decorative plant in shaded garden areas.

Wilmattea minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates.
