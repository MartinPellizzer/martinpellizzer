Wenderothia picta

Wenderothia picta, commonly known as the painted wenderothia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Wenderothia picta is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and salt spray.
