Willbleibia

Willbleibia, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a deciduous plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the spring season, followed by small, round berries that are edible for both humans and wildlife. Willbleibia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Its adaptability to poor soils makes it a valuable plant in ecological restoration projects.
