Wyethia × magna

Wyethia × magna, commonly known as the giant goldenrod, is a hybrid plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a tall perennial herb that can grow up to 3 meters in height, featuring bright yellow inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are alternate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are composite, consisting of numerous small yellow disk flowers surrounded by sterile ray flowers.

Wyethia × magna is often found in moist meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in full sun.
