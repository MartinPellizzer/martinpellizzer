Wurtzia

Wurtzia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to various parts of Africa and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plants are often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Wurtzia species are generally herbaceous, with some varieties growing as shrubs, and they are known for their ornamental value in gardens.
