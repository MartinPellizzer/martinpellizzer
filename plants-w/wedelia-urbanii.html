Wedelia urbanii

Wedelia urbanii, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Wedelia urbanii is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion due to its extensive root system.
