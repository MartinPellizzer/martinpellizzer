Wedelia hispida var. ramosissima

Wedelia hispida var. ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
