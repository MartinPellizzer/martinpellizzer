Waldsteinia Moenchii

Waldsteinia moenchii is a low-growing perennial plant native to the central and eastern regions of the United States. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is commonly found in open woodlands and prairies. Known for its dense clusters of small, white flowers, it blooms in early spring and adds a delicate touch to its surroundings. This hardy species is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it a resilient addition to any landscape.
