Wislizenia fruticosa

Wislizenia fruticosa, commonly known as curly top sage, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert scrub and rocky areas. It has a long history of use by indigenous peoples for its medicinal properties.

Its resilience and hardy nature make it a notable species in xerophytic ecosystems.
