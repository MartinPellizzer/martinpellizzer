Weingartia lanata f. platygona

Weingartia lanata f. platygona, commonly known as the Woolly Weingartia, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features dense, woolly white or pale green spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a powdery coating. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements.
