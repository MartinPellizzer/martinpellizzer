Wedelia tambilloana

Wedelia tambilloana, commonly known as the Brazilian buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
