Wulfhorstia spicata

Wulfhorstia spicata, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color and covered in white, waxy tubercles. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Wulfhorstia spicata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
