Wibelia setosa

Wibelia setosa, commonly known as the slender wibelia, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Wibelia setosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
