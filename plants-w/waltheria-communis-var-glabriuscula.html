Waltheria communis var. glabriuscula

Waltheria communis var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smoothleaf waltheria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warm seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive choice for ornamental purposes in warm climates.
