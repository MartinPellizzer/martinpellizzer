Watsonia coccinea

Watsonia coccinea, commonly known as the red Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Watsonia coccinea thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
