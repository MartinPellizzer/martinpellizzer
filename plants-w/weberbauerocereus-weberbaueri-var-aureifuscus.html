Weberbauerocereus weberbaueri var. aureifuscus

Weberbauerocereus weberbaueri var. aureifuscus, commonly known as the golden-yellow cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green with a yellowish or golden tinge. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
