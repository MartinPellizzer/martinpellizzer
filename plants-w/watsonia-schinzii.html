Watsonia schinzii

Watsonia schinzii, commonly known as the Natal grassblume, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves and produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils. It blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Watsonia schinzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in mild climates.
