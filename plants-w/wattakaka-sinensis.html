Wattakaka sinensis

Wattakaka sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stem and broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked in favor of its ornamental foliage.

Wattakaka sinensis is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ease of care.
