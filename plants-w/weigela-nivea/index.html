Weigela Nivea

White weigela, scientifically known as Weigela nivea, is a striking shrub native to eastern Asia that captivates with its elegant white blooms. This ornamental plant is prized for its ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings, making it a versatile choice for landscapers and home gardeners alike. Its flowers, which appear in early spring, add a soft and sophisticated touch to any garden space. The plant's lush green foliage provides a beautiful backdrop to its delicate blossoms, enhancing its visual appeal throughout the growing season. With proper care, White weigela can become a standout feature in any garden, offering both beauty and resilience.
