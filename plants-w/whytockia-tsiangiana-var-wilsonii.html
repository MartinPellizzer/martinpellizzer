Whytockia tsiangiana var. wilsonii

Whytockia tsiangiana var. wilsonii, commonly known as the Wilson's whytockia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within a spadix, a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its unique foliage rather than its flowers.
