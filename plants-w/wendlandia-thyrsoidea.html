Wendlandia thyrsoidea

Wendlandia thyrsoidea, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, high-altitude environments. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its attractive appearance and resilience.
