Wedelia jacquinii var. acuminata

Wedelia jacquinii var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
