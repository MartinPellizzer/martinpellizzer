Watsonia densiflora

Watsonia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia densiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
