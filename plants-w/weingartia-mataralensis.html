Weingartia mataralensis

Weingartia mataralensis, commonly known as the Mataral cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the Cactaceae family.

It is native to southern Mexico and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense arrangement of cylindrical stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding ornamental value. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Weingartia mataralensis is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
