Wikstroemia ganpi var. pauciflora

Wikstroemia ganpi var. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered *Wikstroemia*, is a shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Thymelaeaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
