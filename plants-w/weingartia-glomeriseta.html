Weingartia glomeriseta

Weingartia glomeriseta, commonly known as the clustered ponytail cactus, is a species native to northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, branching cactus with cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by bright red fruits. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
