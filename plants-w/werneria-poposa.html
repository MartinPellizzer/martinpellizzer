Werneria poposa

Werneria poposa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Werneria poposa prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.
