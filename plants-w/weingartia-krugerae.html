Weingartia krugerae

Weingartia krugerae, commonly known as the Kruger's barrel cactus, is a small, slow-growing cactus native to South Africa.

It features a cylindrical, ribbed stem that typically measures up to 20 centimeters in height. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which bloom at the base of the stem. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.

It requires minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
