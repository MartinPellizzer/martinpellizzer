Weinmannia paulliniifolia f. janeirensis

Weinmannia paulliniifolia f. janeirensis, commonly known as the Janeirensis Weinmannia, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, often silver-gray foliage. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
