Waltheria makinoi

Waltheria makinoi, commonly known as the Makino's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Waltheria makinoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
