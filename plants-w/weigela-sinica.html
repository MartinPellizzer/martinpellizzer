Weigela sinica

Weigela sinica, commonly known as Chinese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Weigela sinica is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display and hardy nature.
