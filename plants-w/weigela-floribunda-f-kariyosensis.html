Weigela floribunda f. kariyosensis

Weigela floribunda f. kariyosensis, commonly known as the Kariyosensis Weigela, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive two-lipped structure, and are borne on short pedicels. The foliage is dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
