Wikstroemia ellipsocarpa

Wikstroemia ellipsocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandarac.

It is a shrub native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its bark is aromatic and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
