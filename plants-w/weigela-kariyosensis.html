Weigela kariyosensis

Weigela kariyosensis, commonly known as the Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features upright, arching branches that bear clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, turning yellow in the fall.

Weigela kariyosensis is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
