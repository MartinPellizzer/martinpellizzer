Wilckia meyeri

Wilckia meyeri, commonly known as the flame bean, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red, flame-like flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, bean-like pods that contain the seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Wilckia meyeri is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
