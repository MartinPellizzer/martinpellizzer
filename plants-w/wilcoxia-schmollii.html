Wilcoxia schmollii

Wilcoxia schmollii, commonly known as the Schmolli's desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the deserts of central and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dry seeds.

Wilcoxia schmollii plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a food source for certain desert animals.
