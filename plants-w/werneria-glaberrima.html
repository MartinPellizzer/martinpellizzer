Werneria glaberrima

Werneria glaberrima, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a small shrub with thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat. Its flowers are often borne on long, slender stems and are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value.

Werneria glaberrima thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
