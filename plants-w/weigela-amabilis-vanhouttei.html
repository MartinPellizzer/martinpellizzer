Weigela amabilis vanhouttei

Weigela amabilis vanhouttei, commonly known as the Vanhoutte weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, pendulous flowers that range from pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from both its vibrant flowers and attractive foliage.
