Webera napieri

Webera napieri, commonly known as the giant spider plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, dark green, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of yellow or red.

Webera napieri is well-suited for humid environments and thrives in partial to full shade.
