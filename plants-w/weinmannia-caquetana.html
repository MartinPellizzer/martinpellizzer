Weinmannia caquetana

Weinmannia caquetana, commonly known as the Colombian weinmannia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Weinmanniaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of Colombia and is characterized by its woody structure and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, with both male and female parts present on the same inflorescence.
