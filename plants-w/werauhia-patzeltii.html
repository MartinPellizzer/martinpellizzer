Werauhia patzeltii

Werauhia patzeltii, commonly known as the "Patzelt's werauhia," is a striking bromeliad native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
