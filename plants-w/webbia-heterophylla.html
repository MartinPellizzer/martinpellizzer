Webbia heterophylla

Webbia heterophylla, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular houseplant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are typically velvety and green, with some varieties exhibiting variegation.

Webbia heterophylla is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
