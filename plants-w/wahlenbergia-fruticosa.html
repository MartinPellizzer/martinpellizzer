Wahlenbergia fruticosa

Wahlenbergia fruticosa, commonly known as the coastal bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact, bushy growth habit.

Wahlenbergia fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
