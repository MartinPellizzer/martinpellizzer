Werneria rosenii

Werneria rosenii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, often with a rosette of leaves at the base. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white, and are pollinated by bees and other insects.

Werneria rosenii is widely cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
