Weingartia buiningiana

Weingartia buiningiana, commonly known as the Buning's barrel cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, cylindrical plant with a ribbed stem that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a dense covering of white, soft hairs.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
