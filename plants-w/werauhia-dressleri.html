Werauhia dressleri

Werauhia dressleri, commonly known as the Crimson Werauhia, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. Its roots are adapted for aerial attachment to trees, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Werauhia dressleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
