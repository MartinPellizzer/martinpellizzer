Wedelia goyazensis

Wedelia goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has a trailing growth habit and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
