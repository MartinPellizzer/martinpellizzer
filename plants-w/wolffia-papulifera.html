Wolffia papulifera

Wolffia papulifera, commonly known as the water moss or duckweed, is a minute aquatic plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is one of the smallest flowering plants, with individual fronds measuring less than 1 millimeter in size. Wolffia papulifera reproduces primarily through vegetative propagation, with new plants developing from the fragmentation of existing fronds.
