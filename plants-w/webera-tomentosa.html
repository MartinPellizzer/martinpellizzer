Webera tomentosa

Webera tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly webera, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a soft, hairy texture.

Webera tomentosa is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
