Waitzia conica

Waitzia conica, commonly known as the conical waitzia, is a unique species of flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive conical shape and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Waitzia conica is of interest to botanists due to its specialized morphology and limited geographic distribution.
