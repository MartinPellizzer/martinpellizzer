Wehlia pulcherrima

Wehlia pulcherrima, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with a yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Wehlia pulcherrima is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and aesthetic appeal.
