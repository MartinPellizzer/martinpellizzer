Watsonia lucens

Watsonia lucens, commonly known as the African sword lily, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has narrow, sword-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Watsonia lucens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its striking appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
