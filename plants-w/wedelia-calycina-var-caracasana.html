Wedelia calycina var. caracasana

Wedelia calycina var. caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture for its attractive floral display.
