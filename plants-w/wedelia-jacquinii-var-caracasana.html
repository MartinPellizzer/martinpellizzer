Wedelia jacquinii var. caracasana

Wedelia jacquinii var. caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its creeping growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms that are typically borne on slender stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and ability to spread quickly in suitable conditions.
