Wurfbainia bicorniculata

Wurfbainia bicorniculata, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a rare and unique species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant has a compact, herbaceous growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically violet or purple in color and bloom during the cooler months.

Wurfbainia bicorniculata is adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments and requires specific conditions to thrive.
