Westia auriculata

Westia auriculata, commonly known as the golden bell plant, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Westia auriculata is valued for its ornamental beauty and drought tolerance.
