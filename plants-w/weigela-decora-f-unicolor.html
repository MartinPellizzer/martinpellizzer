Weigela decora f. unicolor

Weigela decora f. unicolor, commonly known as the single-flowered weigela, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of pink to red. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive, peeling bark that adds visual interest throughout the year.
