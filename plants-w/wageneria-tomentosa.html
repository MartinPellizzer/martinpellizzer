Wageneria tomentosa

Wageneria tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly wageneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.
