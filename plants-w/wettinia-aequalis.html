Wettinia aequalis

Wettinia aequalis, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen, is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palms.

Wettinia aequalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions for landscaping purposes.
