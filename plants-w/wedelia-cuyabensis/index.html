Wedelia Cuyabensis

Wedelia, scientifically known as Wedelia cuyabensis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Paraguay, and Argentina. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and blooming season. Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, Wedelia cuyabensis has become a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
