Wikstroemia obovata

Wikstroemia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf sandarac, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal or limestone areas. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
