Williamsia glabra

Williamsia glabra, commonly known as the smooth williamsia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
