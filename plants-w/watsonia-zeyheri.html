Watsonia zeyheri

Watsonia zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's torch lily, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Watsonia zeyheri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its vibrant blooms attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological significance.
