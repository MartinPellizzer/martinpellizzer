Wahlenbergia sphaerica var. longifolia

Wahlenbergia sphaerica var. longifolia, commonly known as the slender rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers in shades of blue or purple. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette. This variety is particularly adapted to rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
