Witheringia solanacea

Witheringia solanacea, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round berries that turn red when mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and rapid growth habit.

However, it is considered invasive in some areas, posing a threat to local ecosystems.
