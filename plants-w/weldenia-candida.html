Weldenia candida

Weldenia candida, commonly known as white weld, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Weldenia candida is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
