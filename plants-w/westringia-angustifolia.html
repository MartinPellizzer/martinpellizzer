Westringia angustifolia

Westringia angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved westringia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 cm. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.

Westringia angustifolia is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
