Weigela decora var. unicolor

Weigela decora var. unicolor, commonly known as the single-flowered weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces vibrant red or pink flowers, which are typically borne singly on short pedicels. Its foliage consists of ovate, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blossoms.

Weigela decora var. unicolor is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
