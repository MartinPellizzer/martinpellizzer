Wildpretina

Wildpretina, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and elusive plant native to the dense rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale, translucent petals that resemble the ghostly appearance of its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires a specific combination of soil moisture and nutrient levels to flourish. Its unique morphology includes elongated, narrow leaves that help it blend into its surroundings.

Due to its rarity and delicate nature, Wildpretina is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists alike.
