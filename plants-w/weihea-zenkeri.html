Weihea zenkeri

Weihea zenkeri, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The plant has a thick, trunk-like stem with a smooth, reddish-brown bark. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple leaflets that are arranged oppositely along the stems.

Weihea zenkeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is found in warm, humid climates.
