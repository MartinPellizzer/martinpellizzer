Wedelia cunninghamii

Wedelia cunninghamii, commonly known as the Australian bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
