Watsonia masoniae

Watsonia masoniae, commonly known as the Mason's Watsonia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental flowering plants. Watsonia masoniae is well-suited for cultivation in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
