Wenderothia discolor

Wenderothia discolor, commonly known as the pink wisteria, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its cascading clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and unique, vine-like growth habit.
