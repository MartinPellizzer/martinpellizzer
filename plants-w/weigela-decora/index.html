Weigela Decora

Weigela decora is a striking flowering shrub known for its vibrant blooms and elegant appearance. Native to eastern Asia, it has become a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness. Its showy flowers, which range from pink to red, appear in early summer and add a splash of color to any landscape. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance once established. With its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms, Weigela decora is a versatile addition to gardens and landscapes.
