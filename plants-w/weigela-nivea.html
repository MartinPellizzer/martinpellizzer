Weigela nivea

Weigela nivea, commonly known as the snow weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Weigela nivea is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
