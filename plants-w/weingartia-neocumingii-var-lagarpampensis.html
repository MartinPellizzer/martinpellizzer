Weingartia neocumingii var. lagarpampensis

Weingartia neocumingii var. lagarpampensis, commonly known as the Lagarpampa Weingartia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Argentina.

It features a cylindrical stem with a smooth, pale green surface and produces small, woolly areolas. The plant typically bears white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and minimal care requirements.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
