Weingartia gracilispina

Weingartia gracilispina, commonly known as the slender-spined barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and spines that are typically slender and pale in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Weingartia gracilispina is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
