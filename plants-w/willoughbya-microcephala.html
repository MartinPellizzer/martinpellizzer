Willoughbya microcephala

Willoughbya microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf willow or Christmas star, is a unique tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a short, woody stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display.
