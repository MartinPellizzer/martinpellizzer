Weigela fragrans

Weigela fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces clusters of pink or red flowers that emit a sweet, spicy fragrance. Its leaves are oval-shaped and turn yellow in the fall, adding to its ornamental value.

Weigela fragrans is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
