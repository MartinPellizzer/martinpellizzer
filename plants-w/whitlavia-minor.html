Whitlavia minor

Whitlavia minor, commonly known as the small whitlavia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Whitlavia minor is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.
