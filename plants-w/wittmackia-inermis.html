Wittmackia inermis

Wittmackia inermis, commonly known as the silver mohair plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Wittmackia inermis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
