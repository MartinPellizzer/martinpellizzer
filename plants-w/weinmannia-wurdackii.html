Weinmannia wurdackii

Weinmannia wurdackii, commonly known as the Wurdack's weinmannia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
