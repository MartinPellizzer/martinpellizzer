Wedelia gossweileri

Wedelia gossweileri, commonly known as the Cape flats flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms that are often clustered in branched inflorescences. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
