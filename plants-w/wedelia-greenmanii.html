Wedelia greenmanii

Wedelia greenmanii, commonly known as the greenmanii wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warm seasons. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often used in landscaping for its ability to stabilize soil and its aesthetic value in garden settings.
