Wislizenia palmeri

Wislizenia palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of purple to pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert and semi-desert environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Wislizenia palmeri is also known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in treating ailments such as fever and inflammation.
