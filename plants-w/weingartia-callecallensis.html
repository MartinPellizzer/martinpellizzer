Weingartia callecallensis

Weingartia callecallensis, commonly known as the Mexican torch cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, compact plant with a short, cylindrical stem that often branches at the base. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage is covered in dense, white to grayish-blue spines, which provide protection from herbivores.

Weingartia callecallensis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
