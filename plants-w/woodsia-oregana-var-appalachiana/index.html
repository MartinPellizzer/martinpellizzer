Woodsia Oregana Var. Appalachiana

Woodsia oregana var. appalachiana is a rare and delicate fern native to the eastern united states. it thrives in shaded, moist environments such as rocky woods and stream banks. this variety is known for its intricate fronds and unique growth patterns. its conservation status highlights the importance of preserving its natural habitat. studying this plant offers insights into the biodiversity of temperate forests.
