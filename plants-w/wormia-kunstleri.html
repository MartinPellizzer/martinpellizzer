Wormia kunstleri

Wormia kunstleri, commonly known as the desert spider plant, is a succulent native to arid regions of Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It is well adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its thick leaves.

Wormia kunstleri is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
