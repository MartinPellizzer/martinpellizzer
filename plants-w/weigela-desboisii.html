Weigela desboisii

Weigela desboisii, commonly known as the Chinese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in early summer. It typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Weigela desboisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
