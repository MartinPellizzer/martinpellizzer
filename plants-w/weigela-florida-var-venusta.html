Weigela florida var. venusta

Weigela florida var. venusta, commonly known as the lacecap weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular ornamental plant in temperate regions.
