Wedelia jacquinii var. magdalenae

Wedelia jacquinii var. magdalenae, commonly known as the Magdalenae Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, mat-forming foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
