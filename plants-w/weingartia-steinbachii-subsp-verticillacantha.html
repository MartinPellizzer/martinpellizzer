Weingartia steinbachii subsp. verticillacantha

Weingartia steinbachii subsp.

verticillacantha, commonly known as the clustered weingartia, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico. It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive cluster of spines at the apex, giving it its common name.

The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
