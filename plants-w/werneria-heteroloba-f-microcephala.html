Werneria heteroloba f. microcephala

Werneria heteroloba f. microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf werneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable addition to xeriscaped gardens and coastal environments.
