Weigela × fujisanensis var. rosea

Weigela × fujisanensis var. rosea, commonly known as the Rosea Weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its floral display. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and is known for its compact, upright growth habit.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
