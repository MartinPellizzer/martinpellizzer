Wurmbea campanulata var. latifolia

Wurmbea campanulata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces bell-shaped, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or forest margins.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for gardens and botanical collections.
