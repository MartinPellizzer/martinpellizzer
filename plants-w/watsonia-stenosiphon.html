Watsonia stenosiphon

Watsonia stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-sheath Watsonia, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. Its flowers are usually red or orange with a yellow or white center, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Watsonia stenosiphon is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
