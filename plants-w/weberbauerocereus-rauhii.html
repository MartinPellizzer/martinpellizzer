Weberbauerocereus rauhii

Weberbauerocereus rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, green stems and sparse, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
