Weingartia fidaiana subsp. cintiensis

Weingartia fidaiana subsp.

cintiensis, commonly known as the Cintiensis Paddle Cactus, is a species of succulent native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its rounded, paddle-shaped stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is a slow-growing species that prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
