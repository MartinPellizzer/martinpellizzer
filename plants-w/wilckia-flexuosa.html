Wilckia flexuosa

Wilckia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, winding stems that often form a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy.

Wilckia flexuosa is a member of the Convolvulaceae family, known for its showy flowers and climbing habit.
