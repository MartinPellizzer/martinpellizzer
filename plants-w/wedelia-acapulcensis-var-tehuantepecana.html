Wedelia acapulcensis var. tehuantepecana

Wedelia acapulcensis var. tehuantepecana, commonly known as the Tehuantepec Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
