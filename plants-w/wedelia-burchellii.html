Wedelia burchellii

Wedelia burchellii, commonly known as the yellow groundsel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with numerous small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its rapid growth and dense foliage make it an effective ground cover plant.
