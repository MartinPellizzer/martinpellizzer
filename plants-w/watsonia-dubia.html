Watsonia dubia

Watsonia dubia, commonly known as the red Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually blue-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. Watsonia dubia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its striking appearance and ease of cultivation make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
