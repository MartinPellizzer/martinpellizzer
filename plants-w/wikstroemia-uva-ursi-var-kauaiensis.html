Wikstroemia uva-ursi var. kauaiensis

Wikstroemia uva-ursi var. kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai hibiscus, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and streamside areas. It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes species known for their aromatic properties.

This variety is notable for its ecological significance and is often found in conservation areas due to its limited distribution.
