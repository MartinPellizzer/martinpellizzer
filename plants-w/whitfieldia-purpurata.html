Whitfieldia purpurata

Whitfieldia purpurata, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Whitfieldia purpurata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
