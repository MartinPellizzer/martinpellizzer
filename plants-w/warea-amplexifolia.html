Warea amplexifolia

Warea amplexifolia, commonly known as the clasping warea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Podostemonaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that clasp the stem. Warea amplexifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
