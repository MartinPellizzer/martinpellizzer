Watsonia meriana var. dubia

Watsonia meriana var. dubia, commonly known as the Blue Watsonia, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous blue to violet flowers. The leaves are sword-shaped, broad, and often have a bluish-green hue. This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is typically grown as a summer-flowering perennial, adding vibrant color to landscapes during the warmer months.
