Wangenheimia lima var. glabra

Wangenheimia lima var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth lima bean, is a variant of the lima bean species characterized by its smooth, hairless pods and seeds.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is typically an annual herb that thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers are usually white or pale purple.

This variety is valued for its edible beans, which are used in various cuisines around the world.
