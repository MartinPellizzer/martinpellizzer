Wahlenbergia stricta subsp. stricta

Wahlenbergia stricta subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue and purple.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
