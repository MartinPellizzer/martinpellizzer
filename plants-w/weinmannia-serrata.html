Weinmannia serrata

Weinmannia serrata, commonly known as the serrated weinmannia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Weinmanniaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Weinmannia serrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
