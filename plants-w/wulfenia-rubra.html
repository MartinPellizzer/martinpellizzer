Wulfenia rubra

Wulfenia rubra, commonly known as the redstem wulfenia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. The plant features slender, reddish stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal in certain regions.

Wulfenia rubra is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing resilience to harsh conditions.
