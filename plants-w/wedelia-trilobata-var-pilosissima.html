Wedelia trilobata var. pilosissima

Wedelia trilobata var. pilosissima, commonly known as the hairy wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The variety pilosissima is distinguished by its densely hairy foliage, which gives it a soft, velvety appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ground cover in ornamental gardening.

This plant is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion in tropical and subtropical environments.
