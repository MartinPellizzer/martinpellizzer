Wikstroemia trichotoma var. flavianthera

Wikstroemia trichotoma var. flavianthera, commonly known as the golden antherwood, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

Its most distinctive feature is the yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
