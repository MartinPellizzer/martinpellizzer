Weingartia steinbachii var. tintiniensis

Weingartia steinbachii var. tintiniensis, commonly known as the Tintiniensis Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Argentina.

It features a dense covering of white to pale blue spines, giving it a distinctive, almost fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and minimal water requirements.

Its unique form and color make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
