Wedelia souzae

Wedelia souzae, commonly known as the Brazilian blue violet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Wedelia souzae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
