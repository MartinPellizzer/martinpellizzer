Weingartia purpurea var. unguispina

Weingartia purpurea var. unguispina, commonly known as the Purple Weingartia, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and prominent, spiny areoles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements.

It is valued for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
