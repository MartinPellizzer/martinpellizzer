Wakilia

Wakilia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy foliage and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring. Native to various regions in the Americas, wakilia species are often found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant's leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Its adaptability to arid environments makes it a resilient and interesting subject for botanical study.
