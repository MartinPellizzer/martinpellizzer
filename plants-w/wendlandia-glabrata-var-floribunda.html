Wendlandia glabrata var. floribunda

Wendlandia glabrata var. floribunda, commonly known as the floribunda lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, adding to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its prolonged blooming period and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its ornamental value.
