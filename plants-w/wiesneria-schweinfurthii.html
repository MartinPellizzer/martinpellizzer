Wiesneria schweinfurthii

Wiesneria schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in humid, well-drained soil. It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Wiesneria schweinfurthii requires indirect light and consistent moisture to flourish.
