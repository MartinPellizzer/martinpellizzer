Watsonia ecklonii

Watsonia ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing clusters of vibrant flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
