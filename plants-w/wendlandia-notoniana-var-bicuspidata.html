Wendlandia notoniana var. bicuspidata

Wendlandia notoniana var. bicuspidata, commonly known as the Bicuspidate Wandelandia, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
