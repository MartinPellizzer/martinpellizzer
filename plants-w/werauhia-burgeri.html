Werauhia burgeri

Werauhia burgeri, commonly known as the Burger's Werauhia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliad family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup, typical of many bromeliads. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

Werauhia burgeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
