Westia eminii

Westia eminii, commonly known as the eminii rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.

Westia eminii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
