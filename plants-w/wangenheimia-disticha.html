Wangenheimia disticha

Wangenheimia disticha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rockrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Wangenheimia disticha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in xerophytic gardens.
