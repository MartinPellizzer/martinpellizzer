Wurmbea spicata f. marginata

Wurmbea spicata f. marginata, commonly known as the margined yellow star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking yellow flowers with a distinctive spiky structure, which are typically borne on leafless stems. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, with the varietal form 'marginata' exhibiting a pale green margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or forested areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
