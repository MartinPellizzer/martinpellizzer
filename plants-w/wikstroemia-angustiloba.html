Wikstroemia angustiloba

Wikstroemia angustiloba, commonly known as narrowleaf sandarac, is a shrub native to the western United States, particularly California and Oregon.

It is characterized by its slender, evergreen leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
