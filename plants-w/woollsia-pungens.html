Woollsia pungens

Woollsia pungens, commonly known as the woolly pungent plant, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
