Wendlandia nervosa

Wendlandia nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.

Wendlandia nervosa is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various garden settings.
