Weigeltia purpurea

Weigeltia purpurea, commonly known as the purple weigelia, is a flowering shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Weigeltia purpurea is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, flowering shrub with vibrant seasonal color.
