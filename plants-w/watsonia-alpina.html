Watsonia alpina

Watsonia alpina, commonly known as the alpine Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous tubular flowers. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, producing vivid red, orange, or yellow flowers. Its leaves are sword-shaped and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its dramatic appearance.

Watsonia alpina is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, sunny conditions, making it a popular choice among gardeners.
