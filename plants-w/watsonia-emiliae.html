Watsonia emiliae

Watsonia emiliae, commonly known as Emilia's torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-shaped leaves and showy, fiery orange-red flower spikes. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for gardens. Watsonia emiliae thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Its robust nature and vibrant blooms make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
