Wedelia canescens

Wedelia canescens, commonly known as silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white or yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters. The plant has fuzzy, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name and help reflect sunlight. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
