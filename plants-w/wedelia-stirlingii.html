Wedelia stirlingii

Wedelia stirlingii, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
