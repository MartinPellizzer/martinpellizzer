Weingartia markusii

Weingartia markusii, commonly known as the Markusii cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
