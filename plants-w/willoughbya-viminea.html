Willoughbya viminea

Willoughbya viminea, commonly known as the green bottlebrush, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, cylindrical inflorescences that resemble bottle brushes, typically ranging in color from green to red. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Willoughbya viminea thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest and ability to attract native pollinators.
