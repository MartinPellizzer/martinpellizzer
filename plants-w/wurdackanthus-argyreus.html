Wurdackanthus argyreus

Wurdackanthus argyreus, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, yellow disc flowers surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Wurdackanthus argyreus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
