Wurmbea densiflora

Wurmbea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered yellow flag, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbs and erect, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas.

Wurmbea densiflora is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
