Wedelia apensis

Wedelia apensis, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
