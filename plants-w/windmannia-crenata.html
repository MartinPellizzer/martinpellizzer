Windmannia crenata

Windmannia crenata, commonly known as the crested windmannia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive rosette-shaped foliage. The plant produces showy blue or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. Windmannia crenata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
