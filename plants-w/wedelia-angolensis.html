Wedelia angolensis

Wedelia angolensis, commonly known as the Angola wedelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive grayish-green color.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments.
