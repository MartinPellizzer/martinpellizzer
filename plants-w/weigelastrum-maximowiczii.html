Weigelastrum maximowiczii

Weigelastrum maximowiczii, commonly known as the Korean weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
