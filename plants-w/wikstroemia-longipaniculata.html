Wikstroemia longipaniculata

Wikstroemia longipaniculata, commonly known as the long panicle wikstroemia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, branched inflorescences that consist of numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptic to ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
