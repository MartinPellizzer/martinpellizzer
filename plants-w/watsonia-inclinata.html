Watsonia inclinata

Watsonia inclinata, commonly known as the leaning Watsonia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flower spikes. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Watsonia inclinata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
