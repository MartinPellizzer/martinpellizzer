Wedelia horsfieldiana

Wedelia horsfieldiana, commonly known as the tropical blue geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces clusters of small, showy blue or violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its use in traditional medicine.
