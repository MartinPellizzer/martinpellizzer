Weigela florida var. variegata

Weigela florida var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled foliage that features white, yellow, and green patterns on the leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink and red during late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from both its colorful leaves and its vibrant floral display.
