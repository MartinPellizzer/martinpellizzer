Weingartia steinbachii subsp. markusii

Weingartia steinbachii subsp.

markusii, commonly known as the Markus Weingartia, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico. It features a short, cylindrical stem with distinctive, ribbed structures and is known for its attractive, spiny areoles.

The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
