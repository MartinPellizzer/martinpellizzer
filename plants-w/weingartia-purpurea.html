Weingartia purpurea

Weingartia purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Pincushion Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny, globular shape. The plant features short, cylindrical stems that are covered in small, white to pale green spines. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the areoles.

Weingartia purpurea is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
