Worsleya rayneri

Worsleya rayneri, commonly known as the Red River orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is characterized by its clustered inflorescence, which bears numerous small, fragrant flowers. Worsleya rayneri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is a relatively rare species, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
