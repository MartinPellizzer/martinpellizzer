Werneria haenkei

Werneria haenkei, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
