Wedelia schultziana

Wedelia schultziana, commonly known as the Schultze's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features sprawling, low-growing stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy yellow flowers are typically arranged in clusters, making it an attractive ornamental plant.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant blooms and ground-covering habit.
