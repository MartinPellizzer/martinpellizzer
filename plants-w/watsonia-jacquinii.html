Watsonia jacquinii

Watsonia jacquinii, commonly known as the yellow firetorch lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, red-orange to yellow flowers in late summer. The flowers are clustered in inflorescences that resemble torches, hence its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
