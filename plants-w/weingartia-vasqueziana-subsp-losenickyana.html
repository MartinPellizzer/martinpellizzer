Weingartia vasqueziana subsp. losenickyana

Weingartia vasqueziana subsp.

losenickyana, commonly known as the Losenicky's prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
