Warneckea trinervis

Warneckea trinervis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its canopy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
