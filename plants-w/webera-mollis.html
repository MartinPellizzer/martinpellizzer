Webera mollis

Webera mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Webera mollis is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
