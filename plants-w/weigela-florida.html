Weigela florida

Weigela florida, commonly known as the weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its flowers are typically pink or red, arranged in racemes along the stems.

Weigela florida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
