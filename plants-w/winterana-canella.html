Winterana canella

Winterana canella, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are evergreen, glossy, and lance-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Winterana canella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal beauty.
