Watsonia lamarckii

Watsonia lamarckii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features large, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. Watsonia lamarckii is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is native to the coastal regions of South Africa.
