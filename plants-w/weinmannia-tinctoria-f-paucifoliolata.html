Weinmannia tinctoria f. paucifoliolata

Weinmannia tinctoria f. paucifoliolata, commonly known as the dwarf weinmannia, is a compact shrub native to Mexico.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, compact form. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is characterized by its reduced foliage, which gives it a unique, compact appearance.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a specimen plant.
