Wedelia veadeirosensis

Wedelia veadeirosensis, commonly known as the Brazilian starflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions where it can outcompete native vegetation.
