Wedelia robusta

Wedelia robusta, commonly known as the yellow panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Wedelia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
