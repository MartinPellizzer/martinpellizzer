Weigela florida var. praecox

Weigela florida var. praecox, commonly known as early weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright pink to red flowers that bloom early in the spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its floral display is particularly notable for its vibrant color and early blooming period, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking early spring color.

The shrub is also valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
