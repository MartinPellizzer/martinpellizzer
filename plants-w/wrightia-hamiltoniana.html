Wrightia hamiltoniana

Wrightia hamiltoniana, commonly known as the Indian rosewood, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves.
