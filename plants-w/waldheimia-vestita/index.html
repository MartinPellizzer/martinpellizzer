Waldheimia Vestita

Waldheimia, scientifically known as Waldheimia vestita, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to the rocky slopes of the Alps. This plant is celebrated for its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white during late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its compact size and vibrant blooms add a charming touch to garden landscapes. Waldheimia vestita is not only a horticultural gem but also a symbol of the beauty found in mountainous regions.
