Wikstroemia clivicola

Wikstroemia clivicola, commonly known as the cliff wreath, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats. The plant produces small, showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense, drought-tolerant ground cover.

Wikstroemia clivicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
