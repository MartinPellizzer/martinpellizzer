Weigela × fujisanensis var. cremea

Weigela × fujisanensis var. cremea, commonly known as the Cream Weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the cultivar. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and turn yellow in the fall. It produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
