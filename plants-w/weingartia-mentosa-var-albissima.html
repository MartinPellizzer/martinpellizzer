Weingartia mentosa var. albissima

Weingartia mentosa var. albissima, commonly known as the white-furred pincushion cactus, is a low-growing, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly hairs that give it its distinctive appearance and help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise muted foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular ornamental plant among cactus enthusiasts.
