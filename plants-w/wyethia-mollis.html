Wyethia mollis

Wyethia mollis, commonly known as the softleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

The leaves are soft and hairy, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments.
