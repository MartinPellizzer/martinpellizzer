Wedelia cardenasii

Wedelia cardenasii, commonly known as the Mexican spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as along stream banks and in forest understories. It has small, heart-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Due to its attractive flowers and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated for landscaping purposes.
