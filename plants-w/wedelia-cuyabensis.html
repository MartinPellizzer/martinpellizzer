Wedelia cuyabensis

Wedelia cuyabensis, commonly known as the Cuyabeno wedelia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitat.
