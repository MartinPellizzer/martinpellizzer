Watsonia versfeldii

Watsonia versfeldii, commonly known as the Cape Lily, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia versfeldii is valued for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for both its beauty and its ability to attract pollinators.
