Wedelia holwayi

Wedelia holwayi, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
