Watsonia transvaalensis var. drakensbergensis

Watsonia transvaalensis var. drakensbergensis, commonly known as the Drakensberg Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, cone-shaped flower spikes. The plant produces vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
