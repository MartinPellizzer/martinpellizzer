Wedelia parviceps

Wedelia parviceps, commonly known as the small-leaved wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture.

Wedelia parviceps is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
