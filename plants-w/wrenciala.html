Wrenciala

Wrenciala, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant white flowers that open at night and close by dawn. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers emit a sweet scent that attracts nocturnal pollinators like moths.
