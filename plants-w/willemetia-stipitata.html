Willemetia stipitata

Willemetia stipitata, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Willemetia stipitata is often found in moist, disturbed soils in tropical and subtropical regions.
