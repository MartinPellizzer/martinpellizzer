Wedelia albiflora

Wedelia albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Wedelia albiflora is often grown as an ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in moist, shady environments.
