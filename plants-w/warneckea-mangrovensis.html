Warneckea mangrovensis

Warneckea mangrovensis, commonly known as the mangrove warneckea, is a species of flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

Warneckea mangrovensis plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various marine and terrestrial species.
