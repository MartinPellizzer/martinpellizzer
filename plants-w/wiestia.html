Wiestia

Wiestia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Malvaceae family, characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked but are essential for its reproductive cycle. Its fibrous roots help stabilize soil, making it useful in erosion control.

Wiestia is also valued for its ornamental appeal, particularly in landscaping and indoor gardening.
