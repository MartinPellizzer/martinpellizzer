Werauhia patzeltii subsp. panamaensis

Werauhia patzeltii subsp.

panamaensis, commonly known as the Panama werauhia, is a striking bromeliad native to Panama. It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater.

The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with red bracts that attract pollinators. It thrives in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and ecological studies.
