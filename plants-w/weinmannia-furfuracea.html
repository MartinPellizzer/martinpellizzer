Weinmannia furfuracea

Weinmannia furfuracea, commonly known as the velvetleaf weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive waxy coating. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, woody fruits.

Weinmannia furfuracea is often found in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
