Wedelia africana

Wedelia africana, commonly known as African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
