Weigela isolinae

Weigela isolinae, commonly known as the Chinese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and turn yellow in the fall. It is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Weigela isolinae is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
