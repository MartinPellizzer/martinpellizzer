Watsonia amoena var. rosea

Watsonia amoena var. rosea, commonly known as the pink Watsonia, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems adorned with large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. The foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and contribute to the plant's ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
