Wigandia laxifolia

Wigandia laxifolia, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. Wigandia laxifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
