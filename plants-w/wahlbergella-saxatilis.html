Wahlbergella saxatilis

Wahlbergella saxatilis, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, succulent growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
