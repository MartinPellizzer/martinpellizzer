Wercklea insignis

Wercklea insignis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
