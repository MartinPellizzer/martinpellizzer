Wittmackia linharesiorum

Wittmackia linharesiorum, commonly known as the Linares' Wittmackia, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Its flowers are typically small and clustered around the spadix, which is usually a vivid pink or red.

Wittmackia linharesiorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
