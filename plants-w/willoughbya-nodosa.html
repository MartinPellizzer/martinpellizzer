Willoughbya nodosa

Willoughbya nodosa, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Willoughbya nodosa produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible for birds but not for humans.
