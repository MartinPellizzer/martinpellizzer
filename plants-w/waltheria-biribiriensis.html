Waltheria biribiriensis

Waltheria biribiriensis, commonly known as the Brazilian starling vine, is a climbing plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
