Weingartia inflexiseta

Weingartia inflexiseta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, slow-growing cactus with a cylindrical stem that is typically green or blue-gray in color. The plant features prominent, spiny areoles that bear clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Weingartia inflexiseta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
