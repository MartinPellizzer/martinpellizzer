Wedelia riedelii

Wedelia riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
