Weinmannia lansbergiana

Weinmannia lansbergiana, commonly known as the silverleaf weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Weinmannia lansbergiana is adapted to grow in dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal regions.
