Wallenia Pendula

Walenia pendula is a unique and delicate flowering plant known for its elegant, drooping blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its slender stems and cascading flowers make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and garden displays. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to light and humidity to flourish. This plant offers both ornamental value and a sense of tranquility to any space.
