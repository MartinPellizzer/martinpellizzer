Weigela floribunda f. leucantha

Weigela floribunda f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered weigela, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, pendulous clusters of white flowers in early summer, which are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
