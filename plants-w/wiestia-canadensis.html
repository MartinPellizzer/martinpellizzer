Wiestia canadensis

Wiestia canadensis, commonly known as the Canadian wiestia, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and lance-shaped, often with a slightly serrated margin.

Wiestia canadensis is often found in moist, woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
