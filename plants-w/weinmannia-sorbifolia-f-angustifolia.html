Weinmannia sorbifolia f. angustifolia

Weinmannia sorbifolia f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed weinmannia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. Native to South America, particularly Chile, this plant thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate mild frost.
