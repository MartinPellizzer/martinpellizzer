Werneria pseudodigitata

Werneria pseudodigitata, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
