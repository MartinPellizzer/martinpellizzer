Weinmannia tolimensis

Weinmannia tolimensis, commonly known as the Tolima weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive, often branched inflorescences. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It produces yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to high-altitude, humid environments.
