Wedelia linearifolia

Wedelia linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and stream banks. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its root system is fibrous and helps stabilize soil, making it useful for erosion control.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of growth.
