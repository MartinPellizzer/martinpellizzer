Wattakaka volubilis var. glabra

Wattakaka volubilis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth wattakaka, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

This species is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and growth habit.
