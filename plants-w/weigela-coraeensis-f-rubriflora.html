Weigela coraeensis f. rubriflora

Weigela coraeensis f. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered weigela, is a cultivar of the weigela plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flower color and attractive foliage.
