Wallrothia splendens

Wallrothia splendens, commonly known as the splendour wallrothia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which help it store water and protect against intense sunlight. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a dramatic visual contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
