Werauhia jenii

Werauhia jenii, commonly known as the Santa Rosa werauhia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that collect water, supporting its role as a bromeliad. The inflorescence consists of a tall, branched stem bearing numerous small, showy flowers.

Werauhia jenii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.
