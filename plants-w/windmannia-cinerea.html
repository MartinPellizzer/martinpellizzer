Windmannia cinerea

Windmannia cinerea, commonly known as the silver hairplant, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, fuzzy foliage that gives it a silvery appearance. The plant belongs to the family Aizoaceae, also known as the ice plant family. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

Windmannia cinerea thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
