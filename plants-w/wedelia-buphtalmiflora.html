Wedelia buphtalmiflora

Wedelia buphtalmiflora, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble a crown. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense, terminal clusters.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.
