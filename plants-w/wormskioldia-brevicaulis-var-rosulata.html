Wormskioldia brevicaulis var. rosulata

Wormskioldia brevicaulis var. rosulata, commonly known as the Rosulate Wormskioldia, is a striking member of the Aizoaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
