Warrea cinerea

Warrea cinerea, commonly known as the silver warrea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, white or pale blue flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Warrea cinerea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought-resistant qualities.
