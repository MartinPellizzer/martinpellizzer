Wisteriopsis reticulata var. stenophylla

Wisteriopsis reticulata var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wisteriopsis, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely veined, narrow leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
