Weigeltia spectabilis

Weigeltia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces bright red or pink flowers, which are borne on one-year-old wood, making it a popular ornamental plant. Its foliage turns vibrant red in the fall, adding to its seasonal appeal.

Weigeltia spectabilis thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
