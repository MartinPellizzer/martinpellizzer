Whitfieldia perglabra

Whitfieldia perglabra, commonly known as the slender whitebark, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a textured appearance.

Whitfieldia perglabra is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its hardy nature and attractive form.
