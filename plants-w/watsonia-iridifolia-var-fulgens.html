Watsonia iridifolia var. fulgens

Watsonia iridifolia var. fulgens, commonly known as the fiery Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural displays and ecological landscapes.
