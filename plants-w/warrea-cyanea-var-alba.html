Warrea cyanea var. alba

Warrea cyanea var. alba, commonly known as the white warrea, is a flowering plant native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale blue flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
