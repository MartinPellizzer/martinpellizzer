Wolffia neglecta

Wolffia neglecta, commonly known as watermeal, is a tiny aquatic plant belonging to the family Lemnaceae.

It is one of the smallest flowering plants, with individual fronds measuring less than 1 millimeter in length. This plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds, lakes, and rice paddies.
