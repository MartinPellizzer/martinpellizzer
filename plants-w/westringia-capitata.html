Westringia capitata

Westringia capitata, commonly known as New Zealand rock rose, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing shrub with aromatic, evergreen leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Westringia capitata is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
