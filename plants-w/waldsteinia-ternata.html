Waldsteinia ternata

Waldsteinia ternata, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, early spring blooms. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers that appear before the leaves fully develop. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Waldsteinia ternata is often found in the eastern United States and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
