Weigela amagiensis var. viridiflava

Weigela amagiensis var. viridiflava, commonly known as the green-flowered weigela, is a rare and distinctive variant of the weigela genus native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking emerald-green flowers, which contrast vividly with its deep green foliage. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of about 1 to 2 meters. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a unique visual appeal to gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unusual coloration and adaptability to various soil conditions.
