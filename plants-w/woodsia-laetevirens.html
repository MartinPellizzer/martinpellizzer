Woodsia laetevirens

Woodsia laetevirens, commonly known as the cheerful woodsia, is a fern species native to eastern North America.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The plant features delicate, bipinnate fronds with a bright green color that remains vibrant throughout the growing season. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Woodsia laetevirens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
