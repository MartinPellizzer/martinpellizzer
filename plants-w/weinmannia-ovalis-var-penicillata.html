Weinmannia ovalis var. penicillata

Weinmannia ovalis var. penicillata, commonly known as the Penicillate Weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to the Pacific coast of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by woody, cone-like fruits. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to saline and wind-prone conditions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
