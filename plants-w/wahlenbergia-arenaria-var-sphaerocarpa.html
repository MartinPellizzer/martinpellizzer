Wahlenbergia arenaria var. sphaerocarpa

Wahlenbergia arenaria var. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the spherical rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and coastal environments. Its fleshy leaves and showy blooms make it a striking addition to rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to withstand salt spray.
