Wedelia trichostephia

Wedelia trichostephia, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
