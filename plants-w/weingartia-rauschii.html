Weingartia rauschii

Weingartia rauschii, commonly known as the Rauschii cactus, is a species native to the deserts of South America.

It is a small, compact cactus with a short, cylindrical stem that often branches at the base. The plant features prominent, ribbed stems and produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Weingartia rauschii is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
