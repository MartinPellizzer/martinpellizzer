Watsonia canaliculata

Watsonia canaliculata, commonly known as the Natal grassblades, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves and produces striking, sword-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom reliably in warmer climates.

Watsonia canaliculata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
