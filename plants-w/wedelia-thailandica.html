Wedelia thailandica

Wedelia thailandica, commonly known as the Thai wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders and containers.

Its ability to tolerate dry conditions makes it a resilient choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
