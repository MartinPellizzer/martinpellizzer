Wissadula delicata

Wissadula delicata, commonly known as the delicate forest fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, delicate fern with finely divided, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are characterized by their light green color and intricate, lacy patterns.

Wissadula delicata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
