Wedelia Jugata

Wedelia jugata is a low-growing, sprawling perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is known for its dense foliage and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions. It is commonly used in landscaping for erosion control and as a ground cover. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice in subtropical and tropical gardens.
