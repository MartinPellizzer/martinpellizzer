Weingartia losenickyana

Weingartia losenickyana, commonly known as the Losenicky's barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, cylindrical stem that often branches at the base. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

Weingartia losenickyana is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
