Weingartia Minima

Weingartia minima is a small, slow-growing cactus native to Argentina. It is known for its compact size and unique, rounded shape. This plant features short, spiky ribs and clusters of tiny white flowers. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements. Its charm makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
