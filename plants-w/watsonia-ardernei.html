Watsonia ardernei

Watsonia ardernei, commonly known as the Natal sword lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, cone-like flower heads. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Watsonia ardernei thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
