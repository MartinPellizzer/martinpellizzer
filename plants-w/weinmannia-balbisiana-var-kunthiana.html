Weinmannia balbisiana var. kunthiana

Weinmannia balbisiana var. kunthiana, commonly known as the silverleaf weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a distinctive waxy texture. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
