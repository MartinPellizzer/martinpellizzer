Waltheria fruticosa

Waltheria fruticosa, commonly known as the redroot goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetlands and along the edges of streams. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Its roots are reddish in color, which gives it its common name.

Waltheria fruticosa is often found in moist, sandy soils and is adapted to wetland environments.
