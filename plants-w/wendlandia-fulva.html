Wendlandia fulva

Wendlandia fulva, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
