Walkeria nogo

Walkeria nogo, commonly known as the purple crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pink bracts that surround the small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in full sun. Its hairy leaves and sturdy stems make it well adapted to arid conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscaping.
