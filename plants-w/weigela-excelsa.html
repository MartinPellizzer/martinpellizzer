Weigela excelsa

Weigela excelsa, commonly known as the tall weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. Its leaves are ovate and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental value.

Weigela excelsa is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
