Warburgia stuhlmannii

Warburgia stuhlmannii, commonly known as African blackwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy green appearance. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits.

The species is also notable for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine.
