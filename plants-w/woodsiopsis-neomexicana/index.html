Woodsiopsis Neomexicana

Woodsiopsis neomexicana is a rare fern species native to the southwestern United States. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky crevices and canyon walls. This plant is known for its delicate fronds and unique reproductive structures. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Studying Woodsiopsis neomexicana provides valuable insights into plant adaptations in harsh climates.
