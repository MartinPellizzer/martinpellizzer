Wedelia ligulifolia

Wedelia ligulifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its spreading growth habit and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ability to form dense mats makes it useful for erosion control in suitable environments.
