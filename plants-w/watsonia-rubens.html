Watsonia rubens

Watsonia rubens, commonly known as the red Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, cone-shaped flower spikes. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Watsonia rubens thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value and resilience make it a favored plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
