Weigela florida f. albiflora

Weigela florida f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered weigela, is a compact shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
