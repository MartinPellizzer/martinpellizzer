Westringia cephalantha var. cephalantha

Westringia cephalantha var. cephalantha, commonly known as the coastal westringia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with silver-blue foliage and produces small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens. Its hardy nature allows it to withstand salt spray and moderate drought conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
