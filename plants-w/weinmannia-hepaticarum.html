Weinmannia hepaticarum

Weinmannia hepaticarum, commonly known as the liverleaf weinmannia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, liver-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.
