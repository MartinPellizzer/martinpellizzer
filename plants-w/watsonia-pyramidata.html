Watsonia pyramidata

Watsonia pyramidata, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers in late summer. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Watsonia pyramidata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
