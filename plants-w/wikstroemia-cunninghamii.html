Wikstroemia cunninghamii

Wikstroemia cunninghamii, commonly known as the New Zealand honeysuckle, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
