Whiteheadia

Whiteheadia, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, round berries that turn red as they mature. Whiteheadia thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of hope and resurrection in some cultures.
