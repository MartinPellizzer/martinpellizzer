Weigeltia parviflora

Weigeltia parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf weigelt shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, elliptical leaves. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Weigeltia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
