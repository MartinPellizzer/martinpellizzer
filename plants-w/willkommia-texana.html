Willkommia texana

Willkommia texana, commonly known as the Texas willkommia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Texas.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky soils and limestone areas. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Willkommia texana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
