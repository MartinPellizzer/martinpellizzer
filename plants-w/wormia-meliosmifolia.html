Wormia meliosmifolia

Wormia meliosmifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf wormia, is a unique plant species characterized by its dense, velvety foliage.

It belongs to the family Meliosmaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary positions.

Wormia meliosmifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
