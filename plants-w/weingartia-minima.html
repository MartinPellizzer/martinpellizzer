Weingartia minima

Weingartia minima, commonly known as the miniature cactus or the baby weingartia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, low-growing plant with a compact, rounded body and short, thick stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Weingartia minima is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and unique appearance.
