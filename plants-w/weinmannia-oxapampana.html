Weinmannia oxapampana

Weinmannia oxapampana, commonly known as the Oaxaca weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by dry, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in rocky or well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
