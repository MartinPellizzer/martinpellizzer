Weingartia skrovanii

Weingartia skrovanii, commonly known as the skrovanii barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its stems are ribbed and often develop a reddish or purplish tint when exposed to sunlight.

The flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom in late spring or early summer.
