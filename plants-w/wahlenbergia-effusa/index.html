Wahlenbergia Effusa

Wahlenbergia effusa is a delicate flowering plant native to southern Australia. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is known for its small, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers. This species thrives in rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among native gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader botanical community.
