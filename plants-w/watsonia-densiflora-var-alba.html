Watsonia densiflora var. alba

Watsonia densiflora var. alba, commonly known as the white Watsonia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces dense clusters of white to pale pink flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its robust rhizomes and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This variety is particularly noted for its showy, long-lasting blooms that add a dramatic effect to the landscape.
