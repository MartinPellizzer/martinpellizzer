Weingartia gemmae

Weingartia gemmae, commonly known as the miniature cactus or the dwarf weingartia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
