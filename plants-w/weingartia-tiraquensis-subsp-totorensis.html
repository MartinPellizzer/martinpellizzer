Weingartia tiraquensis subsp. totorensis

Weingartia tiraquensis subsp.

totorensis, commonly known as the Totorense Cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to the coastal regions of Peru. It features a compact, globular stem that is typically green to bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and specialized species within the Cactaceae family.
