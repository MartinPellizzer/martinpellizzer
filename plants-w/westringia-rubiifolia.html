Westringia rubiifolia

Westringia rubiifolia, commonly known as the pink flowering shrub, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It features small, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has aromatic, narrow leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Westringia rubiifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
