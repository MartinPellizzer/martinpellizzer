Watsonia muirii

Watsonia muirii, commonly known as the Muir's Watsonia, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, brightly colored flower spikes. The flowers typically bloom in summer, featuring vivid red, orange, or yellow hues with intricate patterns. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
