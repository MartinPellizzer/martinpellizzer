Waltheria reticulata f. intermedia

Waltheria reticulata f. intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid spiderwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for landscape design.
