Woodsia kitadakensis

Woodsia kitadakensis, commonly known as the Japanese wood fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, dark brown layer of roots.

The fronds are biennial, with the fertile fronds appearing in the second year of growth.
