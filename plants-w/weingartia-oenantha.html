Weingartia oenantha

Weingartia oenantha, commonly known as the silver ponytail cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that branch into clusters of short, ribbed segments. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the ends of the stems. Its foliage is covered in a fine, silvery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Weingartia oenantha is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.
