Weinmannia kunthiana

Weinmannia kunthiana, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in dense, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are alternate and often have a distinctive lobed or serrated appearance.

Weinmannia kunthiana is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or rocky areas.
