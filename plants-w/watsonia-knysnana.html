Watsonia knysnana

Watsonia knysnana, commonly known as the Knysna Watsonia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces showy, sword-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is part of the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Watsonia knysnana is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
