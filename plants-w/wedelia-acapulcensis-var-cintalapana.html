Wedelia acapulcensis var. cintalapana

Wedelia acapulcensis var. cintalapana, commonly known as the Cintalapa Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
