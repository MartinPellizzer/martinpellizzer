Windsoria stricta

Windsoria stricta, commonly known as the rigid windsor.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically displaying shades of white and pale pink.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
