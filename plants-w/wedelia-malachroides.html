Wedelia malachroides

Wedelia malachroides, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its bright yellow blooms and compact growth habit.
