Wedelia subpetiolata

Wedelia subpetiolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with creeping stems that form dense mats. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are elliptical, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to suppress weeds in garden settings.
