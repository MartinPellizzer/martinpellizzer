Watsonia strubeniae

Watsonia strubeniae, commonly known as the Strubenia Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces vibrant red and orange blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia strubeniae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
