Watsonia bulbillifera

Watsonia bulbillifera, commonly known as the purple firecracker lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant purple or red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that are evergreen throughout the growing season.

Watsonia bulbillifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries due to its bold, colorful display.
