Wedelia forbesii

Wedelia forbesii, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
