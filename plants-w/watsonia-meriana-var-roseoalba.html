Watsonia meriana var. roseoalba

Watsonia meriana var. roseoalba, commonly known as the pink-tinged Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous bright pink to white flowers. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are narrow and pointed, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
