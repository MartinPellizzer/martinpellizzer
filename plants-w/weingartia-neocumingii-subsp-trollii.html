Weingartia neocumingii subsp. trollii

Weingartia neocumingii subsp.

trollii, commonly known as the Troll's Weingartia, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico. It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and is covered in white to pale green spines.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
