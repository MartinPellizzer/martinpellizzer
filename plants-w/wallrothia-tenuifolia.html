Wallrothia tenuifolia

Wallrothia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf wallrothia, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
