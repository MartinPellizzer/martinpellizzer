Weingartia vorwerkii

Weingartia vorwerkii, commonly known as the Vorwerk's barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant typically has pale green to blue-gray stems that store water, adapting to arid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Weingartia vorwerkii is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
