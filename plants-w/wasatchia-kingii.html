Wasatchia kingii

Wasatchia kingii, commonly known as the Utah primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
