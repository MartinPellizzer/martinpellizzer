Watsonia bachmannii

Watsonia bachmannii, commonly known as the Orange Watsonia, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-like flowers in shades of orange and yellow. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its robust, fleshy rhizomes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia bachmannii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
