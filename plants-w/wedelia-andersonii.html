Wedelia andersonii

Wedelia andersonii, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warm seasons.

Wedelia andersonii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
