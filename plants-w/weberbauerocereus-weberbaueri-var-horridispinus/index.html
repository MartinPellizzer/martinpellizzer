Weberbauerocereus Weberbaueri Var. Horridispinus

Weberbauerocereus weberbaueri var. horridispinus is a rare and striking cactus native to southern Mexico. Known for its spiny, columnar stems and vibrant yellow flowers, it thrives in arid environments. This variant is distinguished by its dense, sharp spines and unique growth patterns. It is highly sought after by collectors for its ornamental value and hardiness. Proper care is essential to ensure its survival in cultivation.
