Wedelia ehrenbergii var. mayerhoffii

Wedelia ehrenbergii var. mayerhoffii, commonly known as the Mayerhoff's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
