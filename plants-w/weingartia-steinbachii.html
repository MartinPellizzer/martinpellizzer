Weingartia steinbachii

Weingartia steinbachii, commonly known as the Mexican bristle cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, bristle-like spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and is well adapted to drought conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

Weingartia steinbachii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
