Weigela × fujisanensis f. glabella

Weigela × fujisanensis f. glabella, commonly known as the glabrous weigela, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and oval, dark green leaves that are typically hairless. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
