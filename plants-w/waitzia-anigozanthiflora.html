Waitzia anigozanthiflora

Waitzia anigozanthiflora, commonly known as the Mexican goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptability to dry environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of hair, which helps reduce water loss.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
