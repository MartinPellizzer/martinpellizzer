Weingartia steinbachii var. tunariensis

Weingartia steinbachii var. tunariensis, commonly known as the Tunariensis Cactus, is a compact, slow-growing species native to Bolivia.

It features short, cylindrical stems with prominent areoles and spines, typically ranging from light to dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
