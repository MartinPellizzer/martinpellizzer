Weingartia westii var. lecoriensis

Weingartia westii var. lecoriensis, commonly known as the Lecorien's barrel cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and a pale green to bluish-gray coloration. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
