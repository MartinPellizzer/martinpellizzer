Wedelia gracilis f. luxurians

Wedelia gracilis f. luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant wedelia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features small, bright green, ovate leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It is valued for its dense foliage and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
