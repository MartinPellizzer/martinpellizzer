Wettinia drudei

Wettinia drudei, commonly known as the Drude's wax palm, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear both male and female flowers, typical of many palms. Wettinia drudei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its unique foliage.
