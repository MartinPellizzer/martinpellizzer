Wahlenbergia rudis

Wahlenbergia rudis, commonly known as the sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found growing along the edges of the sea.

Wahlenbergia rudis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
