Wylia

Wylia, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is renowned for its large, white, fragrant flowers that open only at night. The plant belongs to the nightshade family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its dramatic blooms.

Wylia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
