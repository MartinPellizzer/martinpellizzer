Weingartia gemmae var. elizabethae

Weingartia gemmae var. elizabethae, commonly known as the Elizabeth Weingartia, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a slightly ribbed texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its growth is slow, making it suitable for small spaces or container gardening.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
