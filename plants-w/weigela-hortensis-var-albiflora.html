Weigela hortensis var. albiflora

Weigela hortensis var. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular addition to ornamental landscapes.
