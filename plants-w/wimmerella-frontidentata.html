Wimmerella frontidentata

Wimmerella frontidentata, commonly known as the coastal sandplant, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils near the shoreline. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
