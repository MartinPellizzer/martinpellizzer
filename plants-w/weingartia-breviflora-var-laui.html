Weingartia breviflora var. laui

Weingartia breviflora var. laui, commonly known as the Laui ponytail cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Brazil.

It features a dense rosette of cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically pale green to bluish-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. Its succulent leaves store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions with minimal care.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its ornamental appearance and hardy nature.
