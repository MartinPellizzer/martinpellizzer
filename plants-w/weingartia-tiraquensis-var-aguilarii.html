Weingartia tiraquensis var. aguilarii

Weingartia tiraquensis var. aguilarii, commonly known as the Aguilarii Weingartia, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and is covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
