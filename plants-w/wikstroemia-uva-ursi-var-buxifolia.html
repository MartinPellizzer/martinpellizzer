Wikstroemia uva-ursi var. buxifolia

Wikstroemia uva-ursi var. buxifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf waxtree, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically silver-white to gray-green in color, covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish or brownish hue.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its unique foliage.
