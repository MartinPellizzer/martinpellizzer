Weihea Dinklagei

Dinklage's mahoe, scientifically known as Weihea dinklagei, is a rare and unique tree native to the remote highlands of Vanuatu. This species belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. Despite its ecological importance, Weihea dinklagei remains critically endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this endemic plant from extinction. Its survival is vital for maintaining the biodiversity of its native ecosystem.
