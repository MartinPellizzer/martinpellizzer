Weingartia steinbachii subsp. krugerae

Weingartia steinbachii subsp.

krugerae, commonly known as the Kruger's barrel cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to southern Africa. It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive rib structure and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
