Wendlandia rufescens

Wendlandia rufescens, commonly known as the redstem woollyflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru and Bolivia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, woolly inflorescences. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments in the Andes Mountains, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The flowers are usually pink to red in color, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.
