Watsonia gladioloides

Watsonia gladioloides, commonly known as the golden torch lily, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Watsonia gladioloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is well-suited to full sun and well-drained soils.

It is also a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit.
