Wissadula gymnanthemum f. glabrescens

Wissadula gymnanthemum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a perennial herb with strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale blue flowers during the spring. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
