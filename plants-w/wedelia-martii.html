Wedelia martii

Wedelia martii, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, often found in wet or moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Wedelia martii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
