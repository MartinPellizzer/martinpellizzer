Wahlenbergia ecklonii var. pentamera

Wahlenbergia ecklonii var. pentamera, commonly known as the Purple Rock Orchid, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vivid purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
