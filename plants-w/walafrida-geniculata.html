Walafrida geniculata

Walafrida geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a unique species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

Walafrida geniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
