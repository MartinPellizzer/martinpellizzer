Willoughbya pohliana

Willoughbya pohliana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a striking tropical plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing during the winter months. The plant has a short, stocky stem with broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple hue. It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Willoughbya pohliana thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
