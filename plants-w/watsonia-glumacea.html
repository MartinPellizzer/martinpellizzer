Watsonia glumacea

Watsonia glumacea, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant red, orange, or yellow blooms that are often speckled with contrasting colors. Watsonia glumacea thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
