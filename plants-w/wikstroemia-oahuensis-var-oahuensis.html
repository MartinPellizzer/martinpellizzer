Wikstroemia oahuensis var. oahuensis

Wikstroemia oahuensis var. oahuensis, commonly known as the Oahu wikstroemia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Oahu.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, often found in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is an important component of the native flora and has cultural significance to the local Hawaiian people.
