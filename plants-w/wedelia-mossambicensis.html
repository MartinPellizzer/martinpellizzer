Wedelia mossambicensis

Wedelia mossambicensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value and ease of care have contributed to its widespread cultivation around the world.
