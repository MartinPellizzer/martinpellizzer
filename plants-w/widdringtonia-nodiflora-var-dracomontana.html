Widdringtonia nodiflora var. dracomontana

Widdringtonia nodiflora var. dracomontana, commonly known as the dragon blood tree, is a unique and striking plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its umbrella-shaped crown of dark green, leathery leaves and its distinctive red sap, which has been historically valued for its medicinal properties. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with a slow growth rate and a long lifespan.

It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits containing a single seed.
