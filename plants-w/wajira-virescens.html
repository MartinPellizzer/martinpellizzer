Wajira virescens

Wajira virescens, commonly known as the green wajira, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Wajira virescens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
