Weingartia breviflora

Weingartia breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a compact, rounded cactus with a short, thick stem and spiny areoles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
