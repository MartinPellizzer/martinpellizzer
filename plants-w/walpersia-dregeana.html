Walpersia dregeana

Walpersia dregeana, commonly known as the Natal fan palm, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive, palm-like appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that eventually develop into orange-red fruits. Walpersia dregeana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
