Walafrida apiculata

Walafrida apiculata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial shade.
