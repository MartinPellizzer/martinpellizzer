Windsorina guianensis

Windsorina guianensis, commonly known as the Guianese windmill palm, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its growth habit makes it well-suited for humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.

Windsorina guianensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
