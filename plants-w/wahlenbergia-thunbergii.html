Wahlenbergia thunbergii

Wahlenbergia thunbergii, commonly known as the bluebell bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Wahlenbergia thunbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and delicate appearance.
