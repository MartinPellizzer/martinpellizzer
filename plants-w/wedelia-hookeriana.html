Wedelia hookeriana

Wedelia hookeriana, commonly known as the hooker's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spreading, low-growing stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in sunny, well-drained soils.
