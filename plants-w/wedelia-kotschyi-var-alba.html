Wedelia kotschyi var. alba

Wedelia kotschyi var. alba, commonly known as white wedelia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub or herb, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and prolonged blooming period.
