Watsonia versfeldii var. alba

Watsonia versfeldii var. alba, commonly known as the White Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that bears a dense cluster of large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers typically bloom in summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to the landscape.
