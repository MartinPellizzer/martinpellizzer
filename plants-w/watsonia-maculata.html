Watsonia maculata

Watsonia maculata, commonly known as the spotted Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, colorful inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Watsonia maculata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
