Weigela florida var. alba

Weigela florida var. alba, commonly known as the white weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and provide a striking contrast to the white blooms. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in temperate climates.
