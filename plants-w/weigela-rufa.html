Weigela rufa

Weigela rufa, commonly known as the red weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Weigela rufa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
