Wedelia vieirae

Wedelia vieirae, commonly known as the Brazilian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Wedelia vieirae is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
