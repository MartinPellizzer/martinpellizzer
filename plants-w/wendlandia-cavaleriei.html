Wendlandia cavaleriei

Wendlandia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese water star, is a striking flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Wendlandia cavaleriei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
