Wedelia perrieri

Wedelia perrieri, commonly known as the golden carpet plant, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth and ability to form a thick ground cover make it useful for erosion control and as a decorative ground cover in gardens.
