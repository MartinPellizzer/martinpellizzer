Wikstroemia glabra

Wikstroemia glabra, commonly known as smooth wikstroemia, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland habitats. The plant features small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually smooth and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture.

Wikstroemia glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
