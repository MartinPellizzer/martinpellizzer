Weingartia tarvitaensis

Weingartia tarvitaensis, commonly known as the Tarvita Weingartia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
