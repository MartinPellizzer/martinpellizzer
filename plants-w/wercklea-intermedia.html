Wercklea intermedia

Wercklea intermedia, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually yellow or orange and attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Wercklea intermedia thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
