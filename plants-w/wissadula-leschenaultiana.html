Wissadula leschenaultiana

Wissadula leschenaultiana, commonly known as the Leschenault's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
