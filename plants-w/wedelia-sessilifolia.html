Wedelia sessilifolia

Wedelia sessilifolia, commonly known as the sessile-leaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth, sessile base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental use in gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in tropical landscapes.
