Weinmannia versteeghii

Weinmannia versteeghii, commonly known as the Cape Myrtle, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and showy, pinkish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color.
