Watsonia minima

Watsonia minima, commonly known as the dwarf Watsonia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, cone-shaped inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers full sun. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained conditions.

Watsonia minima is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
