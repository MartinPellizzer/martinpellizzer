Warreella cyanea

Warreella cyanea, commonly known as the blue warreella, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal or inland areas. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Warreella cyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
