Weigela florida f. leucantha

Weigela florida f. leucantha, commonly known as the white weigela, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, contrasting with the dark green foliage. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

It is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display and easy maintenance.
