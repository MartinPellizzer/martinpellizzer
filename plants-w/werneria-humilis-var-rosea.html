Werneria humilis var. rosea

Werneria humilis var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-colored dwarf fern, is a low-growing, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically pink to rose-colored, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, forming dense mats that help stabilize soil.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to create a lush, green ground cover.
