Willughbeia grandiflora

Willughbeia grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, helping to reduce water loss in its humid habitat. Willughbeia grandiflora is typically found in lowland rainforests and can grow up to 20 meters tall.
