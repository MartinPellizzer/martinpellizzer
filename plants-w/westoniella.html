Westoniella

Westoniella, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. They are typically herbaceous perennials, often cultivated as houseplants due to their ornamental value. Westoniella species thrive in well-drained soil and require bright, indirect light to flourish.

Their botanical features make them popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts alike.
