Walleria baumii

Walleria baumii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and glossy in appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Walleria baumii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
