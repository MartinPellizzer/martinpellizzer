Weigela floribunda var. versicolor

Weigela floribunda var. versicolor, commonly known as the variable weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red and yellow, depending on the variety and environmental conditions. The plant produces clusters of vibrant, multi-colored blooms in late spring to early summer, making it a popular ornamental plant. Its foliage is typically dark green and provides a striking contrast to the colorful flowers.

Weigela floribunda var. versicolor is relatively hardy and adaptable, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
