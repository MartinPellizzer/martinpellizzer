Wedelia warmingii

Wedelia warmingii, commonly known as the purplestem wedelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that are typically hairy and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Wedelia warmingii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
