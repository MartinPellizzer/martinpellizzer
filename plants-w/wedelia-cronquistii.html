Wedelia cronquistii

Wedelia cronquistii, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland areas.
