Weberocereus bradei

Weberocereus bradei, commonly known as the night blooming cereus, is a species of cactus native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and distinctive, large, white flowers that open at night. The plant produces bright yellow fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its nocturnal blooming habit makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in night-flowering plants.
