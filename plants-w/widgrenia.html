Widgrenia

Widgrenia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dandelion, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and a single, tall flowering stem. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. Widgrenia is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

It is a perennial herb that is adapted to temperate climates.
