Westringia cinerea

Westringia cinerea, commonly known as silver leaf westringia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It is a hardy shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Westringia cinerea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
