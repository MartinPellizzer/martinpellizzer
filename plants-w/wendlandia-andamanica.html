Wendlandia andamanica

Wendlandia andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman beauty, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
