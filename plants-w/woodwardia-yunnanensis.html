Woodwardia yunnanensis

Woodwardia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, supporting its growth habit.

The fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily identifiable.
