Weinmannia balbisiana var. balbisiana

Weinmannia balbisiana var. balbisiana, commonly known as the Balbisian weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a follicle that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
