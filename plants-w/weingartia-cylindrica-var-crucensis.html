Weingartia cylindrica var. crucensis

Weingartia cylindrica var. crucensis, commonly known as the Crucensis Barrel Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that is typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its slow growth rate and low water requirements.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
