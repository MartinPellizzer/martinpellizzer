Wielandia unifex

Wielandia unifex, commonly known as the single-leaf fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its single, elongated frond that typically grows from a rhizome in shallow water. The plant has a delicate, feathery appearance with small, lance-shaped pinnae. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Wielandia unifex is often used in ornamental water gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.
