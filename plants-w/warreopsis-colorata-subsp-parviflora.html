Warreopsis colorata subsp. parviflora

Warreopsis colorata subsp.

parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered warreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow or greenish in color. Its flowering period generally occurs during the warmer months, and it is often found in well-drained soils.
