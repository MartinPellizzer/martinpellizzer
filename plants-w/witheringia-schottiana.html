Witheringia schottiana

Witheringia schottiana, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, dark green foliage that emerges from a rhizomatous root system. Its flowers are typically borne on slender, erect stems and are known for their unique, intricate shape.

Witheringia schottiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
