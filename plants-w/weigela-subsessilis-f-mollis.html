Weigela subsessilis f. mollis

Weigela subsessilis f. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaved weigela, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features ovate, hairy leaves that are soft to the touch, giving it its cultivar name "mollis.". The plant produces showy, tubular red flowers in early summer, which are borne on short, upright racemes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its vibrant floral display.
