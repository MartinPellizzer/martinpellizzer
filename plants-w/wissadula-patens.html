Wissadula patens

Wissadula patens, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue to violet flowers, typically blooming in the morning. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to maritime climates with high salt exposure. Its leaves are alternate, palmately lobed, and often covered in fine hairs.

Wissadula patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
