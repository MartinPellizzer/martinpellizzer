Werneria frigida

Werneria frigida, commonly known as the cold mountain orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is characterized by its slender stems and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions. It blooms during the late spring to early summer season, adding a unique touch to its alpine habitat.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, Werneria frigida is considered a sensitive indicator of environmental change.
