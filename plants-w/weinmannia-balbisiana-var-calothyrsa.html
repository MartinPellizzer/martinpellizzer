Weinmannia balbisiana var. calothyrsa

Weinmannia balbisiana var. calothyrsa, commonly known as the velvetleaf weinmannia, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, often in panicles, and are typically white or pale yellow in color.
