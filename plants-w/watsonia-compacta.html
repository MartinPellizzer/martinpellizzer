Watsonia compacta

Watsonia compacta, commonly known as the compact Watsonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces tall, upright stems topped with dense clusters of vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Watsonia compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
