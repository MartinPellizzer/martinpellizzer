Wedelia jacquinii var. mariae-galantae

Wedelia jacquinii var. mariae-galantae, commonly known as the Galantae Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as a ground cover or ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive blooms.

The varietal form mariae-galantae is distinguished by its unique floral characteristics and increased hardiness compared to the typical species.
