Watsonia merianella

Watsonia merianella, commonly known as the red Watsonia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, red-orange flower spikes in the summer months. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its robust, fleshy rhizomes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia merianella is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
