Watsonia wilmsii

Watsonia wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilms' torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and showy, torch-like inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers with a distinctive yellow center, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Watsonia wilmsii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and hardiness contribute to its popularity among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
