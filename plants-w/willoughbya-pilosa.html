Willoughbya pilosa

Willoughbya pilosa, commonly known as the silky willow or silky tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Willoughbya pilosa is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height.
