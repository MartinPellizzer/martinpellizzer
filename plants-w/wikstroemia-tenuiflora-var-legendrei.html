Wikstroemia tenuiflora var. legendrei

Wikstroemia tenuiflora var. legendrei, commonly known as the Legendre's narrow-leaved sandarac, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt spray. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in traditional Hawaiian medicine.

The variety is distinguished by its unique floral structure and genetic divergence from the typical form.
