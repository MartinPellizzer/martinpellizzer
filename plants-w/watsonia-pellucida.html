Watsonia pellucida

Watsonia pellucida, commonly known as the rain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces striking, showy inflorescences with vivid pink to red flowers. The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and is often found growing in sandy or rocky soils. It blooms during the summer months and is well adapted to arid environments.

Watsonia pellucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
