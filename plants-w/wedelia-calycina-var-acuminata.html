Wedelia calycina var. acuminata

Wedelia calycina var. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, spreading growth habit. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
