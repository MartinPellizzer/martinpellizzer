Wielandia elegans var. perrieri

Wielandia elegans var. perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's elegant fern, is a rare and striking member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that exhibit a graceful, arching growth habit. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this fern thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich, well-drained soil. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanical enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
