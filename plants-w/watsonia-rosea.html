Watsonia rosea

Watsonia rosea, commonly known as the red Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flower spikes. The flowers are typically red or pink, with a distinctive, spurred petal at the base, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia rosea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
