Weigela amabilis var. alba

Weigela amabilis var. alba, commonly known as the white weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are ovate and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the pale blossoms. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

It is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display and ability to thrive in full sun.
