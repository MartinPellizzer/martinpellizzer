Wedelia simpsoniae

Wedelia simpsoniae, commonly known as Simpson's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and extended blooming period.
