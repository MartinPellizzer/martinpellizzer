Webera peduncularis

Webera peduncularis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Webera peduncularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
