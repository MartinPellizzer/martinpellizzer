Watsonia bella

Watsonia bella, commonly known as the firecracker lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The flowers are typically arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem, creating a dramatic display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia bella is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
