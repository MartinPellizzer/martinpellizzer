Weinmannia microphylla var. caracasana

Weinmannia microphylla var. caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas weinmannia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that are borne on short pedicels. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form and dense foliage.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
