Werneria articulata

Werneria articulata, commonly known as the jointed werneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, segmented stems that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Werneria articulata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
