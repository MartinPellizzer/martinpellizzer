Watsonia iridifolia

Watsonia iridifolia, commonly known as the blue Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple blooms that appear in summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia iridifolia is also valued for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
