Walshia

Walshia, commonly known as the "Cape Cod plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to the eastern United States, particularly the coastal regions of New England. They are valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological importance in stabilizing coastal environments.
