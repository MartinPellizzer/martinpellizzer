Weingartia neocumingii var. mairanensis

Weingartia neocumingii var. mairanensis, commonly known as the Mairanensis Cactus, is a compact, spineless plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a short, rounded stem with a smooth, pale green surface that develops a bluish tint as it matures. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and compact size.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
