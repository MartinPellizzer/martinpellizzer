Werneria crassa subsp. crassa

Werneria crassa subsp.

crassa, commonly known as the broad-leaved werneria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The plant features large, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, woolly coating. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually in shades of white or pale yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage.
