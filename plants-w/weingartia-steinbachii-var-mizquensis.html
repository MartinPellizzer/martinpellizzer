Weingartia steinbachii var. mizquensis

Weingartia steinbachii var. mizquensis, commonly known as the Mizquense Barrel Cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, ribbed stem that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height, with a green to bluish-green coloration. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
