Weinmannia borneensis

Weinmannia borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean weinmannia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Weinmanniaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a leathery texture with a waxy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, producing a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
