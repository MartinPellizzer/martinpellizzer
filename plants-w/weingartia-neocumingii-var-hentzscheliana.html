Weingartia neocumingii var. hentzscheliana

Weingartia neocumingii var. hentzscheliana, commonly known as the Hentzscheliana cactus, is a compact, branching species native to South America.

It features pale green to bluish-gray cylindrical stems covered in short, white to grayish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
