Weingartia krahnii

Weingartia krahnii, commonly known as the Krahnii Pincushion Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense, spiny areoles. The plant features short, cylindrical stems covered with white, woolly hair, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles during the warmer months.

Weingartia krahnii is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
