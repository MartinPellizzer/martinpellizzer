Weitenwebera

Weitenwebera, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a thick, woody trunk and can grow up to 20 meters in height. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, ovate leaflets.

Weitenwebera is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
