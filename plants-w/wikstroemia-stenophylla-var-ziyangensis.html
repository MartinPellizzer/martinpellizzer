Wikstroemia stenophylla var. ziyangensis

Wikstroemia stenophylla var. ziyangensis, commonly known as the Ziyang wikstroemia, is a variant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of flowering plants within the Thymelaeaceae family.
