Weingartia hoffmanniana

Weingartia hoffmanniana, commonly known as the Hoffmann's barrel cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a ribbed, spiny stem that typically grows to about 10-15 cm in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
