Werauhia Lyman-Smithii

Werauhia lyman-smithii is a rare and striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. This unique plant is known for its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. The species was named in honor of botanist Lyman Smith, recognizing his contributions to plant taxonomy. Its rarity and beauty make it a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts and conservationists.
