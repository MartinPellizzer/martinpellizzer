Wedelia natalensis

Wedelia natalensis, commonly known as Natal hedgerow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
