Wedelia rugosa var. nitens

Wedelia rugosa var. nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This variety is often used in ground cover due to its rapid growth and low maintenance requirements.
