Warneckea gilletii

Warneckea gilletii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree has a straight trunk and a broad, spreading crown, with bipinnate leaves that are compound and feathery in appearance.
