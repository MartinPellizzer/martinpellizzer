Wittmackia canaliculata

Wittmackia canaliculata, commonly known as the Hawaiian violet, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hawaii.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
