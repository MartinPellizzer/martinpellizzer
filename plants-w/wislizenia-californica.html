Wislizenia californica

Wislizenia californica, commonly known as California ironweed, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. The plant produces small, clustered purple to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
