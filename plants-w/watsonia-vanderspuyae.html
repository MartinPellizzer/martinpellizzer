Watsonia vanderspuyae

Watsonia vanderspuyae, commonly known as the Orange Watsonia, is a striking member of the Iridaceae family native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves and produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with vibrant orange to red flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its flowering period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, adding color to gardens during cooler months.

Watsonia vanderspuyae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
