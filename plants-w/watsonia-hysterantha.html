Watsonia hysterantha

Watsonia hysterantha, commonly known as the yellow firecracker lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers resembling firecrackers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically green and narrow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia hysterantha is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
