Weigela hortensis f. albiflora

Weigela hortensis f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered weigela, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features dark green, elliptical leaves that provide a striking contrast to its pale blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile garden plant.

Its ornamental value comes from both its vibrant flowers and its compact growth habit.
