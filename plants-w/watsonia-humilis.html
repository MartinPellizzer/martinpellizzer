Watsonia humilis

Watsonia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf Watsonia, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Watsonia humilis is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental value.
