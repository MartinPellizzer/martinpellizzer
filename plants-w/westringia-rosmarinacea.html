Westringia rosmarinacea

Westringia rosmarinacea, commonly known as silver leaf westringia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with aromatic, silver-gray leaves that are densely covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it both visually appealing and easy to maintain.
