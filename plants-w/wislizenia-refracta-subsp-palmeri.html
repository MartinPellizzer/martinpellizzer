Wislizenia refracta subsp. palmeri

Wislizenia refracta subsp.

palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spherical heads.
