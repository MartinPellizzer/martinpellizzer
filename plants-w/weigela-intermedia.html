Weigela intermedia

Weigela intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile garden plant.

Weigela intermedia is often used in landscaping for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
