Weigela subsessilis

Weigela subsessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright growth with clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
