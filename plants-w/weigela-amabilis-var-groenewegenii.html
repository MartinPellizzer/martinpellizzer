Weigela amabilis var. groenewegenii

Weigela amabilis var. groenewegenii, commonly known as the Groenewegen's weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular red or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by orange-red berries. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and persistent fruits, which attract wildlife.
