Watsonia transvaalensis

Watsonia transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal grassblume, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like foliage and produces showy, sword-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is part of the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. Watsonia transvaalensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its resilience and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
