Wendlandia dasythyrsa

Wendlandia dasythyrsa, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving them a soft, fuzzy texture.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by hummingbirds and other pollinators.
