Wikstroemia brevipaniculata

Wikstroemia brevipaniculata, commonly known as the short panicle wikstroemia, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in arid landscapes.
