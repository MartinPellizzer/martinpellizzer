Wuerthia

Wuerthia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its striking, silver-gray foliage. The leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a waxy texture that reflects light, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. They produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.
