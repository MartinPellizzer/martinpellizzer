Wedelia spenceriana

Wedelia spenceriana, commonly known as the Spencer's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
