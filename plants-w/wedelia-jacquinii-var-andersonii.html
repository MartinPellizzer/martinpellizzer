Wedelia jacquinii var. andersonii

Wedelia jacquinii var. andersonii, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm seasons. The plant has a sprawling growth habit with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for borders, rock gardens, or as a ground cover.
