Weigela groenewegenii

Weigela groenewegenii, commonly known as the Groenewegen's weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, reddish-brown fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Weigela groenewegenii is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
