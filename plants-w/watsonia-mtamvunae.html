Watsonia mtamvunae

Watsonia mtamvunae, commonly known as the Umvunani Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, colorful inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia mtamvunae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
