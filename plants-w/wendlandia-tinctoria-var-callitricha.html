Wendlandia tinctoria var. callitricha

Wendlandia tinctoria var. callitricha, commonly known as the red-veined begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and delicate floral display.
