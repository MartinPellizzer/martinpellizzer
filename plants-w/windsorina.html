Windsorina

Windsorina, commonly known as the royal lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Windsorina thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add visual interest to water gardens or shaded borders.
