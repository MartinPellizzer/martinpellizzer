Wikstroemia dolichantha var. pubescens

Wikstroemia dolichantha var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairyWikstroemia, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mesic forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
