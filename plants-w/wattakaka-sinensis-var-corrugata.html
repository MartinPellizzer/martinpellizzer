Wattakaka sinensis var. corrugata

Wattakaka sinensis var. corrugata, commonly known as the corrugated wattakaka, is a variant of the wattakaka plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive corrugated or ridged leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The corrugated wattakaka is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
