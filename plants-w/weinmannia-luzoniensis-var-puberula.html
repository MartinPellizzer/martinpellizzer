Weinmannia luzoniensis var. puberula

Weinmannia luzoniensis var. puberula, commonly known as the Luzon velvet tree, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a leathery texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within montane forests.
