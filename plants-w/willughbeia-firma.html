Willughbeia firma

Willughbeia firma, commonly known as the broadleaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom throughout the year in suitable conditions. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Willughbeia firma is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
