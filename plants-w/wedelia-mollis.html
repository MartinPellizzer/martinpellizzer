Wedelia mollis

Wedelia mollis, commonly known as the silverleaf wedelia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate floral display. The plant has finely divided, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to spread rapidly and form dense mats.
