Wikstroemia holosericea

Wikstroemia holosericea, commonly known as the silverleaf wikstroemia, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.
