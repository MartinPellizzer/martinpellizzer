Wajira danissana

Wajira danissana, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Wajira danissana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
