Weingartia jarmilae

Weingartia jarmilae, commonly known as the Jarmilae Barrel Cactus, is a small, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with ribbed structure and produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring. The plant is characterized by its dense, spiny areoles and is well adapted to arid environments. It requires minimal water and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a desirable addition to succulent collections.
