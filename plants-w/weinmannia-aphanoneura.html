Weinmannia aphanoneura

Weinmannia aphanoneura, commonly known as the ghost fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the family Weinmanniaceae and is characterized by its unique, delicate foliage. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that are often clustered closely together, giving it a ghostly appearance. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Weinmannia aphanoneura is often found growing on rock faces or in shaded, humid environments.
