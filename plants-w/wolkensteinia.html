Wolkensteinia

Wolkensteinia, commonly known as the cloudstone plant, is a rare and unique species belonging to the family Cactaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Wolkensteinia is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts for its striking appearance and resilience.
