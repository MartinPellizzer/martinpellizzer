Webbia

Webbia, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to Africa, Europe, and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically sword-shaped and variegated. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are followed by red berries containing seeds. Webbia is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored choice for households and offices.
