Wachendorfia thyrsiflora

Wachendorfia thyrsiflora, commonly known as the Christmas flower or the red kangaroo paw, is a striking perennial plant native to Western Australia.

It features vibrant red, pink, or orange flowers that resemble the shape of a kangaroo's paw, blooming from late autumn to spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences that are highly ornamental. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to native flora collections.
