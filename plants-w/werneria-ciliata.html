Werneria ciliata

Werneria ciliata, commonly known as the silky fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The leaves are bioluminescent, emitting a soft glow in the dark, which is a unique feature among ferns. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
