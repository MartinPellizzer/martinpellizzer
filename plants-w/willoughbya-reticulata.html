Willoughbya reticulata

Willoughbya reticulata, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive reticulated pattern on the petals. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette formation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Willoughbya reticulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
