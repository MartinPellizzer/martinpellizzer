Willughbeia lanceolata

Willughbeia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf willughbeia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Willughbeia lanceolata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
