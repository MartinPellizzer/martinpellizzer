Wedelia acapulcensis var. ayerscottiana

Wedelia acapulcensis var. ayerscottiana, commonly known as the Ayerscottiana Wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically produces small, daisy-like blooms with white ray florets and yellow disk florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological restoration projects.
