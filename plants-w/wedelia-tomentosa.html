Wedelia tomentosa

Wedelia tomentosa, commonly known as silverleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, showy yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, soft, grayish-white foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Its rapid growth and spreading habit make it an effective ground cover plant.
