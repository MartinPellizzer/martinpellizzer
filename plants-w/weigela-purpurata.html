Weigela purpurata

Weigela purpurata, commonly known as the weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in early summer. The flowers, which range from red to pink, are borne on short, raceme-like clusters and are attractive to pollinators. The plant has ovate to elliptic leaves that are glossy green on top and lighter in color underneath.

Weigela purpurata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
