Weigela × fujisanensis var. versicolor

Weigela × fujisanensis var. versicolor, commonly known as the Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red and white, depending on the variety. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The botanical features of this plant make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability.
