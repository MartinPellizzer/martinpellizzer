Wedelia baorucana

Wedelia baorucana, commonly known as the Baorucan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, making it an attractive addition to gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, though it can tolerate partial shade.
