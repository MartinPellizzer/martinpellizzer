Wedelia helianthoides subsp. prieuriana

Wedelia helianthoides subsp.

prieuriana, commonly known as the Prieur's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native flora.
