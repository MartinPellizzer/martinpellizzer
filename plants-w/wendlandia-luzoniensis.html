Wendlandia luzoniensis

Wendlandia luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers arranged in clusters.

Wendlandia luzoniensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
