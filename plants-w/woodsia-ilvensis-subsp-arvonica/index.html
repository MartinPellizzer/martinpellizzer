Woodsia Ilvensis Subsp. Arvonica

Woodsia ilvensis subsp. arvonica is a rare and delicate fern native to the northern hemisphere. It thrives in cool, moist environments such as shaded woodlands and rocky crevices. This subspecies is distinguished by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that give it a unique, elegant appearance. Due to its limited habitat and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical treasure.
