Weigela arborescens

Weigela arborescens, commonly known as the weigela shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branching stems and is characterized by its showy, pendulous clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Weigela arborescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
