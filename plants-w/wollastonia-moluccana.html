Wollastonia moluccana

Wollastonia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, waxy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
