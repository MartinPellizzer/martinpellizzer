Weigela amabilis var. striata

Weigela amabilis var. striata, commonly known as the striped weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy texture, while the stems are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, elliptical berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

It is well-suited for garden use due to its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
