Warrea wailesiana

Warrea wailesiana, commonly known as the red warrea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a glossy surface.

Warrea wailesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
