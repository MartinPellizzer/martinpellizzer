Weigela biformis

Weigela biformis, commonly known as the double-flowered weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, rounded form. The plant produces showy, double pink or red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

Weigela biformis is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
