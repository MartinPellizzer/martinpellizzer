Winterocereus

Winterocereus, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular epiphytic cactus native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its segmented, branching stems that are often adorned with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its seasonal blooming, typically occurring around the holiday season, hence its common name.

Winterocereus is relatively low maintenance, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
