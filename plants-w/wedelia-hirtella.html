Wedelia hirtella

Wedelia hirtella, commonly known as the hairy wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Wedelia hirtella is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to suppress weeds.
