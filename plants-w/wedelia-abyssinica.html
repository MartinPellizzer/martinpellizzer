Wedelia abyssinica

Wedelia abyssinica, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with trailing stems and small, bright yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in tropical and subtropical regions.
