Walsura hypoleuca

Walsura hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered tamanu, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces yellowish-orange fruits that contain a single seed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
