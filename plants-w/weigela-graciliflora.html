Weigela graciliflora

Weigela graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Weigela graciliflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
