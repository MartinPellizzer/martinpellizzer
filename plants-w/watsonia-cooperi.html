Watsonia cooperi

Watsonia cooperi, commonly known as the Orange Watsonia, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear showy, tubular flowers in shades of orange and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Watsonia cooperi is known for its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
