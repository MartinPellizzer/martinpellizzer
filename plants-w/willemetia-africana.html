Willemetia africana

Willemetia africana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces legume pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and vibrant floral display make it a notable species in its native habitat.
