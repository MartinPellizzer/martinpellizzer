Weingartia taratensis

Weingartia taratensis, commonly known as the Taratensis Barrel Cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem that resembles a barrel, covered in sparse, white spines. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to dry environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Weingartia taratensis is a slow-growing plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.
