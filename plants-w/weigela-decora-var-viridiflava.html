Weigela decora var. viridiflava

Weigela decora var. viridiflava, commonly known as the Emerald Glow Weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features vibrant green foliage that contrasts beautifully with its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The blooms, which are typically pink or red, appear in early summer and are borne on arching branches. This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
