Wulfenia blechicii

Wulfenia blechicii, commonly known as the desert sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in arid, sandy environments. It is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.

Its botanical features make it an important component of desert ecosystems.
