Wachendorfia brachyandra

Wachendorfia brachyandra, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking perennial plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscaping.

Wachendorfia brachyandra is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
