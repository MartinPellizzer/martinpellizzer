Weingartia muschii

Weingartia muschii, commonly known as the Muschii Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
