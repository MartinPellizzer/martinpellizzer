Watsonia erubescens

Watsonia erubescens, commonly known as the red Watsonia, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces large, fleshy, grass-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Watsonia erubescens thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
