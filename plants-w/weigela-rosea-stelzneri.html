Weigela rosea stelzneri

Weigela rosea stelzneri, commonly known as the Stelzner's weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, oval, green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Weigela rosea stelzneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
