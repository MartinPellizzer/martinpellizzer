Wedelia kotschyi

Wedelia kotschyi, commonly known as the mountain wedelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, rounded heads, with yellow petals and a distinctive central disc.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and vibrant blooms.
