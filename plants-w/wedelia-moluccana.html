Wedelia moluccana

Wedelia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub or perennial herb, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in dense, flat-topped inflorescences, making it visually appealing.
