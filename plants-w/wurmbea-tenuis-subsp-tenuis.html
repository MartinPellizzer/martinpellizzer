Wurmbea tenuis subsp. tenuis

Wurmbea tenuis subsp.

tenuis, commonly known as the slender bulbous plant, is a species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its underground bulb and strap-shaped leaves that emerge in spring.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
