Weingartia mentosa var. cylindrica

Weingartia mentosa var. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical barrel cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that is ribbed and covered in white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique shape and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
