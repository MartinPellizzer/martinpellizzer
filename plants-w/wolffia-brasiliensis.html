Wolffia brasiliensis

Wolffia brasiliensis, commonly known as watermeal, is a tiny floating aquatic plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is one of the smallest flowering plants, with individual fronds measuring less than 1 millimeter in length. Wolffia brasiliensis thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often forming dense mats on the water surface.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and serving as a food source for various aquatic animals.
