Weigela middendorffiana

Weigela middendorffiana, commonly known as the Siberian weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
