Wedelia wallichii var. megalantha

Wedelia wallichii var. megalantha, commonly known as the giant wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its dense, spreading foliage. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
