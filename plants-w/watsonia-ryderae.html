Watsonia ryderae

Watsonia ryderae, commonly known as the Red Watsonia, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Watsonia ryderae is a perennial herb that is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, making it a visually appealing addition to floral arrangements.
