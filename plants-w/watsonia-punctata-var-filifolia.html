Watsonia punctata var. filifolia

Watsonia punctata var. filifolia, commonly known as the Filifoliate Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky white hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and architectural form.
