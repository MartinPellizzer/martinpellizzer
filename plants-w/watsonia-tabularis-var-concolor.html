Watsonia tabularis var. concolor

Watsonia tabularis var. concolor, commonly known as the Natal grassblades, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, forming a dense rosette at the base. The plant produces showy, sword-shaped inflorescences with vivid orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and relatively low-maintenance in suitable climates.
