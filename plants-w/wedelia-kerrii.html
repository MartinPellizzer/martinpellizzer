Wedelia kerrii

Wedelia kerrii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading shrub with dark green leaves and bright yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Wedelia kerrii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
