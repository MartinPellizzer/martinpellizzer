Wikstroemia modesta

Wikstroemia modesta, commonly known as the Chinese sand orchid, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring or early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
