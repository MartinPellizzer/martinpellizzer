Wilcoxia kroenleinii

Wilcoxia kroenleinii, commonly known as the Antarctic pearlwort, is a flowering plant native to the extreme environments of Antarctica.

It is one of the few flowering plants that survive in the harsh conditions of the continent, thriving in rocky soil and on bare ground. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers and its low-growing, mat-forming structure. Wilcoxia kroenleinii plays a significant role in the ecological balance of Antarctic ecosystems, providing food for local fauna and contributing to soil stabilization.
