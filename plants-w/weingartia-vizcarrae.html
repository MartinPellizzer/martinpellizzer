Weingartia vizcarrae

Weingartia vizcarrae, commonly known as the Vizcarra weingartia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water.

Weingartia vizcarrae is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
