Withania yunnanensis

Withania yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan wolfberry, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and red berries. The plant is typically shrub-like, with hairy stems and elliptical leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
