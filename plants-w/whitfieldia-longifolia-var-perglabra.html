Whitfieldia longifolia var. perglabra

Whitfieldia longifolia var. perglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved whitfieldia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The leaves are elongated and often covered with a fine, powdery coating, giving them a distinctive appearance. This variant is distinguished from other forms of Whitfieldia by its smooth, glabrous leaf surfaces.

It typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to semi-arid conditions.
