Watsonia juncifolia

Watsonia juncifolia, commonly known as the river Watsonia, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is native to the Eastern Cape and is often found growing along riverbanks and in moist, well-drained soils. Watsonia juncifolia is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

It also plays a role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
