Walleria armata

Walleria armata, commonly known as the rock fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often consumed by both humans and animals. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is well adapted to drought conditions.

Walleria armata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
