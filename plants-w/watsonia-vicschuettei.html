Watsonia vicschuettei

Watsonia vicschuettei, commonly known as the Vicschuette's Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular or funnel-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique floral structure, it is often cultivated by gardeners interested in native South African flora.
