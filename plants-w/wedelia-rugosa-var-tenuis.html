Wedelia rugosa var. tenuis

Wedelia rugosa var. tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wedelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
