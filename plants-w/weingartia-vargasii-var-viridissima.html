Weingartia vargasii var. viridissima

Weingartia vargasii var. viridissima, commonly known as the Green Weingartia, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, ribbed stems that are typically a vibrant green color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.

Its foliage is often used in succulent arrangements and as a decorative plant.
