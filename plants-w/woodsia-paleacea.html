Woodsia paleacea

Woodsia paleacea, commonly known as the pale fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Woodsia paleacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful foliage.
