Weldenia volcanica

Weldenia volcanica, commonly known as the volcanic weldenia, is a flowering plant native to the volcanic regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for tropical gardens.

Weldenia volcanica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
