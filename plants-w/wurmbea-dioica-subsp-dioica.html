Wurmbea dioica subsp. dioica

Wurmbea dioica subsp.

dioica, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces striking yellow flowers with prominent, showy stamens.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in treating various ailments.
