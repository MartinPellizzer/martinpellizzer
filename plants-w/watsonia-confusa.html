Watsonia confusa

Watsonia confusa, commonly known as the purple firecracker lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and red. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. Watsonia confusa is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit.

It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens during the cooler months.
