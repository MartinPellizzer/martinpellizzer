Werneria plantaginifolia var. macrocephala

Werneria plantaginifolia var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed werneria, is a striking member of the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense, terminal clusters. The plant features hairy, ovate leaves with a rough texture, and it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. It is native to South Africa and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is valued for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
