Watsonia rourkei

Watsonia rourkei, commonly known as the Rourke's torch lily, is a striking member of the Iridaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that bear clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers, which resemble torches. The plant produces showy, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. Watsonia rourkei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
