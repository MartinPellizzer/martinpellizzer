Watsonia comptonii

Watsonia comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, torch-like inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers with a distinctive central spike, often surrounded by grass-like leaves. Watsonia comptonii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
