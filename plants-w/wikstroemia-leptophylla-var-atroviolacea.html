Wikstroemia leptophylla var. atroviolacea

Wikstroemia leptophylla var. atroviolacea, commonly known as the dark violet flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to its foliage. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical structure includes a woody stem and showy bracts that enhance its visual appeal.
