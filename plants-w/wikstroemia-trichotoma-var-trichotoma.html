Wikstroemia trichotoma var. trichotoma

Wikstroemia trichotoma var. trichotoma, commonly known as the silver thread tree, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with a compact, rounded crown. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. It produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and floral display.
