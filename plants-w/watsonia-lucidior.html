Watsonia lucidior

Watsonia lucidior, commonly known as the bright Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. Watsonia lucidior thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to naturalize and spread through rhizomes.
