Walsura cochinchinensis

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other tropical hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, simple, ovate leaves. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing flat, winged seeds.
