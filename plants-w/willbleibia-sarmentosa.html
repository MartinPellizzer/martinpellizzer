Willbleibia sarmentosa

Willbleibia sarmentosa, commonly known as the creeping willow or swamp willow, is a shrub native to wetland and riparian habitats in southeastern Australia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically pollinated by wind. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and oval-shaped with a smooth margin.
