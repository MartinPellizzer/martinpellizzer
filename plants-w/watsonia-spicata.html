Watsonia spicata

Watsonia spicata, commonly known as the spike Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Watsonia spicata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and resilience make it a favored plant among gardeners in temperate climates.
