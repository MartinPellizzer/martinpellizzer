Wigandia kunthii var. eukunthii

Wigandia kunthii var. eukunthii, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with slender, branching stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
