Watsonia iridifolia var. obrienii

Watsonia iridifolia var. obrienii, commonly known as the O'Brien's Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of vibrant, iris-like flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding a dramatic display to the landscape.
