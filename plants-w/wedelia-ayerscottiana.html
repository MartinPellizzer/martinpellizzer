Wedelia ayerscottiana

Wedelia ayerscottiana, commonly known as the pink carpet plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with small, bright pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and ground cover.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant floral display and ability to form a lush, colorful carpet.
