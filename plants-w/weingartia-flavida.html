Weingartia flavida

Weingartia flavida, commonly known as the yellow spine cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to yellow spines. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Weingartia flavida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive spines.
