Weigela japonica var. sinica

Weigela japonica var. sinica, commonly known as Chinese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to landscape designs.
