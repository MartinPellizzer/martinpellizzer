Wedelia subvelutina var. linearifolia

Wedelia subvelutina var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance also make it suitable for use in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
