Watsonia starkeae

Watsonia starkeae, commonly known as the Cape York Watsonia, is a striking bulbous perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. Watsonia starkeae thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both cultivated gardens and natural habitats.
