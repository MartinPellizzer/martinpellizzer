Weinmannia fagaroides var. fagaroides

Weinmannia fagaroides var. fagaroides, commonly known as the Fagaroides Weinmannia, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
