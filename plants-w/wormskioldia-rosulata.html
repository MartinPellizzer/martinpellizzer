Wormskioldia rosulata

Wormskioldia rosulata, commonly known as the rosette daisy, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to arid landscapes.
