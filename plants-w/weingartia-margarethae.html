Weingartia margarethae

Weingartia margarethae, commonly known as the Margareta cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and cylindrical stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, featuring spines for protection and reduced leaves to minimize water loss.

Weingartia margarethae is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
