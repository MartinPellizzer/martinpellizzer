Walafrida zuurbergensis

Walafrida zuurbergensis, commonly known as the Zuurberg daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
