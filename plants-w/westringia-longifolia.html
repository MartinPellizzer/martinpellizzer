Westringia longifolia

Westringia longifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with aromatic, dark green foliage that is dense and compact. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Westringia longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
