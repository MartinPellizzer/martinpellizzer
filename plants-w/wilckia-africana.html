Wilckia africana

Wilckia africana, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain three to four seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
