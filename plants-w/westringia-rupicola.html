Westringia rupicola

Westringia rupicola, commonly known as rock westringia, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has grey-green, needle-like leaves that are resistant to drought and salt spray, making it well-suited for coastal environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Westringia rupicola is valued in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
