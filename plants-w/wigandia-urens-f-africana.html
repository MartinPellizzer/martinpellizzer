Wigandia urens f. africana

Wigandia urens f. africana, commonly known as African false dandelion, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and palmate leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
