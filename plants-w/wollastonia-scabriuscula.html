Wollastonia scabriuscula

Wollastonia scabriuscula, commonly known as the roughleaf wattle, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and rough, scaly leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Wollastonia scabriuscula is a hardy species that is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
