Wedelia macrodonta var. parviflora

Wedelia macrodonta var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flower wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne on slender stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.

It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
