Wehlia grandiflora

Wehlia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora wehlia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. Wehlia grandiflora thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
