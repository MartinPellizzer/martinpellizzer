Weberbauerocereus weberbaueri

Weberbauerocereus weberbaueri, commonly known as the Mexican night bloomer, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and is often covered in spines. The plant produces large, fragrant white flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
