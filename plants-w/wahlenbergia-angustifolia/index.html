Wahlenbergia Angustifolia

Wahlenbergia angustifolia is a delicate flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, known for its bell-shaped flowers. This species is often found in coastal and mountainous areas where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its narrow leaves and vibrant blue or purple blooms make it a striking addition to gardens. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and requires specific care to flourish.
