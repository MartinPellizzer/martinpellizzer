Wedelia serrata var. serrata

Wedelia serrata var. serrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wedelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and small, ovate leaves with serrated edges, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact growth habit.

Its ability to tolerate various soil types makes it a versatile choice for landscaping in warm climates.
