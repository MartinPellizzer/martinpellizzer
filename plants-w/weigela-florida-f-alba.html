Weigela florida f. alba

Weigela florida f. alba, commonly known as the white weigela, is a flowering shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, and they appear in clusters along the branches. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
