Wedelia africana var. minor

Wedelia africana var. minor, commonly known as the small African wedelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has creeping stems that form a dense mat, making it suitable for ground cover in gardens. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in warm, humid climates.
