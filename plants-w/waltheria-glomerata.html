Waltheria glomerata

Waltheria glomerata, commonly known as the clustered waltheria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Waltheria glomerata is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and along stream banks.
