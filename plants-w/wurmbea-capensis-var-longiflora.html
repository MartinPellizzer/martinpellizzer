Wurmbea capensis var. longiflora

Wurmbea capensis var. longiflora, commonly known as the Cape arrowhead, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
