Wedelia kirkbridei

Wedelia kirkbridei, commonly known as the Kirkbride's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading shrub with opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
