Wedelia oligocephala

Wedelia oligocephala, commonly known as the small-headed wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, often giving it a decorative appearance.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
