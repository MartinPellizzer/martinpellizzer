Wedelia ehrenbergii var. ehrenbergii

Wedelia ehrenbergii var. ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Mexican honeycomb plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to Mexico.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a honeycomb-like appearance. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and ability to spread readily.
