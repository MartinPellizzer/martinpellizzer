Weingartia arenacea

Weingartia arenacea, commonly known as the Areca palm or the bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, clustered stems and feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
