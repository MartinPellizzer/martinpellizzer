Weigela praecox

Weigela praecox, commonly known as early weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Weigela praecox is also valued for its ornamental berries that persist into winter, adding visual interest to the landscape.
