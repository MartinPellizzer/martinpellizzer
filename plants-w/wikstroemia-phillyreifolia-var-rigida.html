Wikstroemia phillyreifolia var. rigida

Wikstroemia phillyreifolia var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-white powder, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental species in tropical and subtropical regions.
