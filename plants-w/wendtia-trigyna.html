Wendtia trigyna

Wendtia trigyna, commonly known as the yellow hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Wendtia trigyna is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
