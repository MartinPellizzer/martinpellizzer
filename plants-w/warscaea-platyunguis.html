Warscaea platyunguis

Warscaea platyunguis, commonly known as the broad-leafed waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, crimson-red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Warscaea platyunguis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
