Werneria dactylophylla var. glandulosodenticulata

Werneria dactylophylla var. glandulosodenticulata, commonly known as the glandular-toothed werneria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The leaves are typically glandular and have serrated or toothed margins, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and fragrant blooms.
