Wittmackia bicolor

Wittmackia bicolor, commonly known as the pink fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family.

Native to Brazil, it is characterized by its vibrant pink to red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
