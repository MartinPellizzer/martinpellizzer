Woodsia Calcarea

Woodsia calcarea is a rare and delicate fern native to calcareous rocky habitats. It thrives in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its small, lance-shaped leaves are arranged in a tight, compact rosette. The plant is known for its unique ability to survive in harsh, limestone-rich environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
