Weigela toensis

Weigela toensis, commonly known as the Chinese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and provide a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Weigela toensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
