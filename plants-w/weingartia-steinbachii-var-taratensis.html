Weingartia steinbachii var. taratensis

Weingartia steinbachii var. taratensis, commonly known as the Taratensis Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of central Argentina.

It features a dense covering of pale green to bluish-gray spines, which help reduce water loss and provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its otherwise muted coloration. Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat challenging plant to care for.
