Wedelia gentryi

Wedelia gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's wedelia, is a flowering plant species native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. Wedelia gentryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
