Withania chevalieri

Withania chevalieri, commonly known as the Indian winter cherry, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and smooth, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.
