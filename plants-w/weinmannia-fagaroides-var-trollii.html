Weinmannia fagaroides var. trollii

Weinmannia fagaroides var. trollii, commonly known as the Troll's Weinmannia, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and northern Baja California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with leathery, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a specimen plant in coastal landscapes.

It is adapted to coastal climates, tolerating salt spray and well-drained soils.
