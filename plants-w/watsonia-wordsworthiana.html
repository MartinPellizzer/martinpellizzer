Watsonia wordsworthiana

Watsonia wordsworthiana, commonly known as the Cape iris, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant is part of the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and gladioli. Watsonia wordsworthiana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ability to bloom reliably makes it a favorite among gardeners seeking vibrant, long-lasting floral displays.
