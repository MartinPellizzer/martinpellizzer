Wedelia pseudoviguiera

Wedelia pseudoviguiera, commonly known as the purple wedgeflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually purple or pink in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening.
