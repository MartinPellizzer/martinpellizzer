Watsonia natalensis

Watsonia natalensis, commonly known as the Natal sword lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces bright red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Watsonia natalensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.

Its ornamental value and resilience contribute to its popularity among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
