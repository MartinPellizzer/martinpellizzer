Wendlandia brachyantha

Wendlandia brachyantha, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant features silver-gray foliage that contrasts beautifully with its vibrant purple and pink blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring through early autumn.
