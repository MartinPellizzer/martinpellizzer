Werneria nubigena var. dombeyana

Werneria nubigena var. dombeyana, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is part of the Gentianaceae family.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
