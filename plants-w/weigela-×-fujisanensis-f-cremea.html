Weigela × fujisanensis f. cremea

Weigela × fujisanensis f. cremea, commonly known as the Cream Weigela, is a compact, deciduous shrub prized for its showy, tubular flowers.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features bright orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, followed by red berries. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

This cultivar is well-suited for garden borders or as a specimen plant due to its ornamental value and moderate maintenance requirements.
