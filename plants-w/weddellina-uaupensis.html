Weddellina uaupensis

Weddellina uaupensis, commonly known as the Brazilian wintergreen, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers.
