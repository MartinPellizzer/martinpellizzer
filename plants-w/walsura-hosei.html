Walsura hosei

Walsura hosei, commonly known as the Philippine tamarind, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody pods containing edible seeds. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it is often found in lowland and montane forests.

The plant has potential uses in traditional medicine and as a source of timber.
