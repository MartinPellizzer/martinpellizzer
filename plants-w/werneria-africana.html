Werneria africana

Werneria africana, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and broad, waxy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Werneria africana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
