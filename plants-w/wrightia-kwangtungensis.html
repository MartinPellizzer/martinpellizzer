Wrightia kwangtungensis

Wrightia kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong wrightia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Wrightiaceae.

It is native to southern China, particularly Guangdong province, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface.

Wrightia kwangtungensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
