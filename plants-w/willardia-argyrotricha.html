Willardia argyrotricha

Willardia argyrotricha, commonly known as the silver-haired willardia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and vibrant orange to red tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its unique leaf structure and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Willardia argyrotricha is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.
