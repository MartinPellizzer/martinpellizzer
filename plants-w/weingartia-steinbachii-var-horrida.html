Weingartia steinbachii var. horrida

Weingartia steinbachii var. horrida, commonly known as the Horrid Weingartia, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically blue-green in color, covered in white, tuberculate areoles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
