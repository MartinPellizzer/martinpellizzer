Weingartia mentosa var. swobodae

Weingartia mentosa var. swobodae, commonly known as the Swobodae Paddle Cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive rib structure and produces small, pale green to yellow flowers during the spring. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and minimal care requirements. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
