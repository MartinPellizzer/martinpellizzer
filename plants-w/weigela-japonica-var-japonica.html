Weigela japonica var. japonica

Weigela japonica var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Japanese weigela is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
