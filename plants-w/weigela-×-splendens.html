Weigela × splendens

Weigela × splendens, commonly known as the weigela bush, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with dense, dark green foliage. Its flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white, and are often borne in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Weigela × splendens is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
