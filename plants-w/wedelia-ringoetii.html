Wedelia ringoetii

Wedelia ringoetii, commonly known as the purple wedgeflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its most striking feature is the clusters of purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.
