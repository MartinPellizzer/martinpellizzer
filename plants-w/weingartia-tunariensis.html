Weingartia tunariensis

Weingartia tunariensis, commonly known as the Tunariensis Barrel Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Bolivia.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers during the rainy season, which are usually clustered at the top of the stem. It has a slow growth rate and is well adapted to arid environments.

Weingartia tunariensis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
