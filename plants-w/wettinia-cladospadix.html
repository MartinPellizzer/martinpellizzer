Wettinia cladospadix

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and a flowering inflorescence called a spadix, which is enclosed by a modified leaf known as a spatula. Its botanical features make it a unique and valuable species for both ecological and horticultural studies.
