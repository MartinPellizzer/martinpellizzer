Wedelia jacquinii subsp. calycina

Wedelia jacquinii subsp.

calycina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is a perennial herb with upright, branching stems and opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it suitable for borders, containers, and rock gardens.
