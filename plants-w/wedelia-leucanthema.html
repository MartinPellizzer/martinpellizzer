Wedelia leucanthema

Wedelia leucanthema, commonly known as the white-flowered wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of small, white flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread, it can become invasive in certain environments.
