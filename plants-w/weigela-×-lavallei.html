Weigela × lavallei

Weigela × lavallei, commonly known as the Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Weigela × lavallei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
