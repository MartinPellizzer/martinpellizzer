Wahabia longiflora

Wahabia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has thick, succulent leaves that help it retain water in harsh environments. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

Wahabia longiflora is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental beauty in dry gardens.
