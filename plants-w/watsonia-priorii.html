Watsonia priorii

Watsonia priorii, commonly known as the Natal grassblume, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

Watsonia priorii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
