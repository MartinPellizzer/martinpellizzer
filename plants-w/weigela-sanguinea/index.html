Weigela Sanguinea

Weigela sanguinea is a vibrant shrub known for its showy flowers and hardy nature. Native to eastern Asia, it has become a popular choice in gardens worldwide. The plant features clusters of bright pink or red flowers that bloom in early spring. Its foliage adds a lush green contrast throughout the growing season. Weigela sanguinea is both ornamental and easy to care for, making it a favorite among gardeners.
