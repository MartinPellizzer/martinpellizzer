Wedelia longepetiolata

Wedelia longepetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features heart-shaped, opposite leaves with long petioles, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow-orange flowers arranged in clusters, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Wedelia longepetiolata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
