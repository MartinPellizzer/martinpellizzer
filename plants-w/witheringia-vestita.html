Witheringia vestita

Witheringia vestita, commonly known as the naked lady, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features a tall, slender stem with showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its bare, leafless stem and clustered blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Witheringia vestita is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant, dramatic flowering display.
