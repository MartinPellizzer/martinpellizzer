Wikstroemia sandwicensis

Wikstroemia sandwicensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in clusters. It is well adapted to the tropical climate and is often found in dry, sandy soils.
