Willemetia arenaria

Willemetia arenaria, commonly known as the sand lily, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Willemetia arenaria is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats, making it a symbol of resilience in harsh environments.
