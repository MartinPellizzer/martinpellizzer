Woodfordia floribunda var. glabrata

Woodfordia floribunda var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth woodfordia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often glabrous, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
