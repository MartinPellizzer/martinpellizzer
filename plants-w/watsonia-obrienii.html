Watsonia obrienii

Watsonia obrienii, commonly known as the Cape York Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, cone-like inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant, often red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Watsonia obrienii is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and adds a dramatic visual impact to any landscape.
