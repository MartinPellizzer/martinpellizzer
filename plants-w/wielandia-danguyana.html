Wielandia danguyana

Wielandia danguyana, commonly known as the Brazilian water lily, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant has a distinctive white flower with yellow stamens that bloom during the day. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it to the bottom of water bodies.

Wielandia danguyana plays an important role in maintaining water quality and supporting biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
