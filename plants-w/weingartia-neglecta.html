Weingartia neglecta

Weingartia neglecta, commonly known as the Neglecta cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, rounded stem with clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique shape and ability to produce delicate blooms under proper care.
