Weigela lemoinei

Weigela lemoinei, commonly known as the Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Weigela lemoinei is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
