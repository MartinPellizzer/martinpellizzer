Watsonia elimensis

Watsonia elimensis, commonly known as the Elim Watsonia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Watsonia elimensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to naturalize and return year after year.
