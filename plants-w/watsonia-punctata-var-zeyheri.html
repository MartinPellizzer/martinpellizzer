Watsonia punctata var. zeyheri

Watsonia punctata var. zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's Watsonia, is a striking bulbous perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, red or orange inflorescences with tubular flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

The variety is noted for its resilience and adaptability to various climatic conditions.
