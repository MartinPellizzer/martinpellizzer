Whitfieldia elongata var. dewevrei

Whitfieldia elongata var. dewevrei, commonly known as the dwarf whitebark.

This variant is a small, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It features dense, compact growth with small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

It is well adapted to rocky soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
