Willoughbya Variabilis

Willoughbya variabilis, also known as the flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to northern Australia. It is renowned for its vibrant, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in late winter and early spring. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy that provides ample shade. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in warm climates. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
