Weinmannia macrostachya

Weinmannia macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stalked weinmannia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Weinmanniaceae.

It is native to the coastal regions of southern South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and showy, pinkish-white inflorescences. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Weinmannia macrostachya is adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.
