Wedelia jacquinii f. angustifolia

Wedelia jacquinii f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
