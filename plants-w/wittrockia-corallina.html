Wittrockia corallina

Wittrockia corallina, commonly known as the coral lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of bright orange or red flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Wittrockia corallina thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
