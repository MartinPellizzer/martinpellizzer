Weingartia vargasii

Weingartia vargasii, commonly known as the Vargasii cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, compact plant with a globular stem that typically grows to a height of about 10-15 centimeters. The plant features short, cylindrical spines and produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Weingartia vargasii is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
