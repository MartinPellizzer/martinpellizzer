Watsonia tabularis

Watsonia tabularis, commonly known as the Natal grass or Natal lily, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright, grass-like leaves and produces striking, showy inflorescences of pink to red flowers. The plant is part of the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. Watsonia tabularis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
