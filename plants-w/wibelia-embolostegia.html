Wibelia embolostegia

Wibelia embolostegia, commonly known as the prairie smoke, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like clusters of feathery, purple-pink flowers that resemble smoke. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.

Wibelia embolostegia is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
