Werneria nubigena var. vulgaris

Werneria nubigena var. vulgaris, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that resemble clouds, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky crevices. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense colonies.

The botanical features of this variety include its intricate leaf structure and adaptability to humid climates.
