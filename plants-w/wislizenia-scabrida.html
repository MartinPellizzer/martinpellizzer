Wislizenia scabrida

Wislizenia scabrida, commonly known as roughleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

Its leaves are rough and hairy, giving it a distinctive texture, while its purple to pink flowers bloom in late summer.

Wislizenia scabrida is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
