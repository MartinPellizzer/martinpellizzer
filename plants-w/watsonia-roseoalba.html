Watsonia roseoalba

Watsonia roseoalba, commonly known as the pink Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its inflorescence consists of tightly packed, spurred flowers. Watsonia roseoalba thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
