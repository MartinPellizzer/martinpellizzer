Willoughbya coriacea

Willoughbya coriacea, commonly known as the African evergreen.

It is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa, characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a deep root system that allows it to survive in drought conditions.
