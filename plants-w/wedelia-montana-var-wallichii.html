Wedelia montana var. wallichii

Wedelia montana var. wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan wedelia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice in horticulture.
