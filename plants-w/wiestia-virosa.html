Wiestia virosa

Wiestia virosa, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and small, pale purple flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces berries that are toxic to humans and animals, containing harmful alkaloids. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it is typically avoided in gardens and landscapes.
