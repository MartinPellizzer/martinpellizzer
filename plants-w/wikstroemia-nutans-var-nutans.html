Wikstroemia nutans var. nutans

Wikstroemia nutans var. nutans, commonly known as the drooping sandarac tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and often lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The tree produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.
