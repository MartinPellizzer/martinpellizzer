Wrightia hainanensis

Wrightia hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan Wrightia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the family Wrightiaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface, while its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Wrightia hainanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
