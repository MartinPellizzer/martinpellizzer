Weigela vanhouttei

Weigela vanhouttei, commonly known as the Vanhoutte weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink, blooming in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a desirable addition to shrub borders and woodland gardens.
