Weigela × hybrida

Weigela × hybrida, commonly known as the weigela shrub, is a deciduous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in early spring, often in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 6 feet and features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Weigela × hybrida is well-suited for USDA hardiness zones 4 through 8 and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
