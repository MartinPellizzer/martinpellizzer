Weingartia neumanniana var. aurantia

Weingartia neumanniana var. aurantia, commonly known as the Orange-tipped Barrel Cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, ribbed stem that can reach up to 30 cm in height, with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces bright orange to red flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
