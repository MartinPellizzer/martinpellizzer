Weigela florida f. variegata

Weigela florida f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, featuring white and green variegation on its heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular red or pink flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in both its colorful leaves and vibrant floral displays.
