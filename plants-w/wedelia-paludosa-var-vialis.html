Wedelia paludosa var. vialis

Wedelia paludosa var. vialis, commonly known as the marsh wedelia, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to moist environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wet areas.
