Wibelia

Wibelia, commonly known as the cupplant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and distinctive, cup-shaped flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. Wibelia species are often found in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
