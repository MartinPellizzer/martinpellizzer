Wendtia gracilis

Wendtia gracilis, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a low-growing succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
