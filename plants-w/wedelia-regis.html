Wedelia regis

Wedelia regis, commonly known as the royal wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Wedelia regis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display and easy care requirements.
