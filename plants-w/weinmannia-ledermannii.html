Weinmannia ledermannii

Weinmannia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, providing a silvery appearance. It thrives in coastal climates with mild winters and warm, dry summers, often growing on rocky soils.

Weinmannia ledermannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
