Welwitschiina macrophylla

Welwitschiina macrophylla, commonly known as the giant welwitschi, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant is adapted to extreme arid conditions, with a deep root system that allows it to access water from deep underground. Its slow growth rate and longevity make it a unique example of desert flora.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is protected and studied to better understand its survival strategies in harsh environments.
