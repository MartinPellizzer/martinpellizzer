Werneria juniperina

Werneria juniperina, commonly known as the African juniper, is a member of the Cupressaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its scale-like leaves that are arranged in flat, sprout-like structures. Its cones are small and berry-like, containing seeds that are dispersed by birds.

Werneria juniperina is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
