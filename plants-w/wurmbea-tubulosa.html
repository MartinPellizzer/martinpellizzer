Wurmbea tubulosa

Wurmbea tubulosa, commonly known as the African yellow rain lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a spindle-shaped bulb with a fibrous root system, and its leaves are typically linear and grass-like. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Wurmbea tubulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
