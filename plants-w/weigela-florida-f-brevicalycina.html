Weigela florida f. brevicalycina

Weigela florida f. brevicalycina, commonly known as the compact weigela, is a compact shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a nice contrast to the vibrant flowers. It is well-suited for garden borders or as a specimen plant due to its compact growth habit.

The foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a versatile addition to ornamental gardens.
