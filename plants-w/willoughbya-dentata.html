Willoughbya dentata

Willoughbya dentata, commonly known as the red lily or the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. Willoughbya dentata is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
