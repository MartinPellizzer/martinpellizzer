Wedelia grandiflora

Wedelia grandiflora, commonly known as the showy wedelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features spreading stems and dense, low-growing foliage, often forming a mat-like structure. The plant produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers with red centers, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to suppress weeds make it a valuable ground cover in landscaping.
