Wyethia ovata var. funerea

Wyethia ovata var. funerea, commonly known as the dark wyethia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dark green, ovate leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage has a slightly aromatic scent, and it is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This variety is distinguished by its darker coloration compared to other varieties of Wyethia.
