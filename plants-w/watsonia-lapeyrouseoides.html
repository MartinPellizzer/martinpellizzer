Watsonia lapeyrouseoides

Watsonia lapeyrouseoides, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

Watsonia lapeyrouseoides is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
