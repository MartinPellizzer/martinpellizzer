Watsonia comptonii var. angustifolia

Watsonia comptonii var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver hue on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Watsonia comptonii var. angustifolia is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
