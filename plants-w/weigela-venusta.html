Weigela venusta

Weigela venusta, commonly known as the Chinese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and has a compact, rounded form.

Weigela venusta is well-suited for partial shade to full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
