Wendlandia luzoniensis var. membranifolia

Wendlandia luzoniensis var. membranifolia, commonly known as the Luzon starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of montane forests, thriving at higher elevations. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and covered with a thin, waxy layer, which helps reduce water loss.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
