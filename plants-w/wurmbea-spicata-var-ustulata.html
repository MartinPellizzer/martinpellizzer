Wurmbea spicata var. ustulata

Wurmbea spicata var. ustulata, commonly known as the African yellow star, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are often spotted or streaked with green and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique foliage.
