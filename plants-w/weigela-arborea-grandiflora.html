Weigela arborea-grandiflora

Weigela arborea-grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to landscaped areas.
