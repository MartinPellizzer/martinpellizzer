Weigela

Weigela, commonly known as the weigela shrub, is a deciduous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are oval to elliptical in shape. Weigela thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
