Wedelia elliptica

Wedelia elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, bright yellow flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Wedelia elliptica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for ground cover.
