Willoughbya fulva

Willoughbya fulva, commonly known as the golden torch or yellow torch, is a striking tropical plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters, which are often used in floral arrangements. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. Willoughbya fulva thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued for its long-lasting blooms and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
