Watsonia amoena

Watsonia amoena, commonly known as the purple fire dancer, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Watsonia amoena is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
