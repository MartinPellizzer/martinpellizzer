Wimmeria confusa

Wimmeria confusa, commonly known as the slender lily, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in high-altitude environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.
