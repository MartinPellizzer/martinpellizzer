Willughbeia

Willughbeia, commonly known as the "Papua sandpaper tree," is a genus of flowering plants native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific.

Willughbeia species typically grow as shrubs or small trees, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.
