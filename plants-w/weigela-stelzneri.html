Weigela stelzneri

Weigela stelzneri, commonly known as the dwarf weigela, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It is well-suited for small gardens or containers due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Weigela stelzneri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
