Watsonia starkeae var. rubra

Watsonia starkeae var. rubra, commonly known as the red Watsonia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces vibrant red to orange flowers in late summer. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and compact growth habit.
