Wangenheimia lima var. villosula

Wangenheimia lima var. villosula, commonly known as the silky lima bean, is a variant of the lima bean species native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, or villosulate, stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces large, oval-shaped pods that contain edible, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and is often cultivated for both culinary and agricultural purposes.
