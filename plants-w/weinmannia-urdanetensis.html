Weinmannia urdanetensis

Weinmannia urdanetensis, commonly known as the Urdanet weinmannia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Weinmanniaceae.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, specifically in Venezuela. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
