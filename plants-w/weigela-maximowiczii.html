Weigela maximowiczii

Weigela maximowiczii, commonly known as the Korean weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Weigela maximowiczii is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
