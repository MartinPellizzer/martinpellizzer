Willkommia

Willkommia, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and coastal environments. Willkommia is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, which helps it survive in dry conditions.

It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
