Watsonia distans

Watsonia distans, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces upright, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting purple or red base. Watsonia distans thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that provide a nice contrast to its vibrant blooms.
