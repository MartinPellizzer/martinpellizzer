Walsura velutina

Walsura velutina, commonly known as the hairy tamarind, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and long, pinnate leaves with a velvety texture. Its fruit is a drupe, typically yellow when mature, and contains a single seed.
