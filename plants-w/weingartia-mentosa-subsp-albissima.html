Weingartia mentosa subsp. albissima

Weingartia mentosa subsp.

albissima, commonly known as the white-furred ponytail cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white or pale blue spines, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
