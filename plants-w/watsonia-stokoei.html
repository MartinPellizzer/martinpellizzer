Watsonia stokoei

Watsonia stokoei, commonly known as the Stokoe's torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that bear large, showy flowers. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers with a distinctive yellow throat, often resembling a torch. Watsonia stokoei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
