Weingartia cardenasiana

Weingartia cardenasiana, commonly known as the Cardenasian Pincushion Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, ribbed structure. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
