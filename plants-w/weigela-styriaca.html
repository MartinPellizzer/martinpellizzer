Weigela styriaca

Weigela styriaca, commonly known as the Austrian weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Europe.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile garden plant.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its natural propagation.
