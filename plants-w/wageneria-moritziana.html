Wageneria moritziana

Wageneria moritziana, commonly known as the Moritz's grass.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the grass family Poaceae. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are typically pale green to brown in color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
