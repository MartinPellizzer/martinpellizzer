Wedelia rhombifolia

Wedelia rhombifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its rhombic-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are usually glossy and have a distinct heart-shaped base.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid environments.
