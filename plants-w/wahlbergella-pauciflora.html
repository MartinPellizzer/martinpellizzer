Wahlbergella pauciflora

Wahlbergella pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered orchid, is a species native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming during the winter months. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture in its arid habitat.

Wahlbergella pauciflora is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of conservation interest.
