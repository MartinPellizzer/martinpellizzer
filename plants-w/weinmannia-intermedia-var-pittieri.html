Weinmannia intermedia var. pittieri

Weinmannia intermedia var. pittieri, commonly known as the Pittieri's weinmannia, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
