Weingartia pampagrandensis

Weingartia pampagrandensis, commonly known as the Pampagrande cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spineless, ribbed structure. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
