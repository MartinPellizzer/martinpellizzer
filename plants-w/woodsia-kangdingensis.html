Woodsia kangdingensis

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rocky substrates or in forest understories. Its spore-bearing structures, called sorangia, are clustered on the underside of its leaves, a typical feature of ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
