Weingartia mariana var. laui

Weingartia mariana var. laui, commonly known as the Laui pincushion cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, thick stem with numerous radial spines and a dense covering of white, woolly hair. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and low water requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
