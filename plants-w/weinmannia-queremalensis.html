Weinmannia queremalensis

Weinmannia queremalensis, commonly known as the Quebrada de los Remedios, is a shrub or small tree native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its ornamental value has made it a subject of interest in horticulture, though it is not widely cultivated.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
