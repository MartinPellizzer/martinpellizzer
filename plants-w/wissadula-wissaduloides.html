Wissadula wissaduloides

Wissadula wissaduloides, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its cylindrical stems and yellow to orange flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Wissadula wissaduloides is relatively drought-tolerant and suitable for arid climates.
