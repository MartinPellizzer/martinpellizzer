Wormia madagascariensis var. rotundifolia

Wormia madagascariensis var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the Madagascar worm plant, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features small, fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a fine, white coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are thick and succulent, adapted to store water in its arid native environment.

It is valued in horticulture for its unusual appearance and ease of care.
