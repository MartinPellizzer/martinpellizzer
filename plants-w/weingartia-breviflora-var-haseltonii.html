Weingartia breviflora var. haseltonii

Weingartia breviflora var. haseltonii, commonly known as the Haselton's barrel cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for cultivation in containers due to its slow growth rate and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
