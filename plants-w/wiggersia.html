Wiggersia

Wiggersia, commonly known as the yellow dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces a single, hollow, stem that bears a bright yellow flower head composed of numerous small florets. Wiggersia is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
