Wendlandia teysmanniana

Wendlandia teysmanniana, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
