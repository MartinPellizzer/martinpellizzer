Weingartia neocumingii var. augustini

Weingartia neocumingii var. augustini, commonly known as the Augustini weingartia, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with distinctive white radial spines and a dense covering of soft, white glochids. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
