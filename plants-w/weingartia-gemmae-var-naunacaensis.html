Weingartia gemmae var. naunacaensis

Weingartia gemmae var. naunacaensis, commonly known as the Naunacaensis Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle ornamental value. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, with the main photosynthetic function performed by the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
