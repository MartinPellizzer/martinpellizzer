Willoughbya repanda

Willoughbya repanda, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange with a prominent yellow throat. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are often overlooked due to the dramatic floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Willoughbya repanda is also known for its ability to bloom reliably once established, adding a dramatic touch to gardens in late spring.
