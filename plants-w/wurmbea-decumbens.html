Wurmbea decumbens

Wurmbea decumbens, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.

Wurmbea decumbens is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a cut flower.
