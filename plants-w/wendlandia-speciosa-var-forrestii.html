Wendlandia speciosa var. forrestii

Wendlandia speciosa var. forrestii, commonly known as the Forest's beauty, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and for its ability to thrive in well-drained, fertile soils.
