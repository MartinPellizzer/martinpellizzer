Werneria mocinniana

Werneria mocinniana, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its flowers are typically 5- to 7-petaled and bloom in clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and tropical aesthetic.
