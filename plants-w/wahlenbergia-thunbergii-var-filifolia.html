Wahlenbergia thunbergii var. filifolia

Wahlenbergia thunbergii var. filifolia, commonly known as the slender bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in open, sunny habitats. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils, preferring well-drained conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, bell-shaped blooms.
