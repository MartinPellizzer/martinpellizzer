Wissadula paniculata

Wissadula paniculata, commonly known as the red-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces striking red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. These flowers are clustered in paniculate inflorescences, giving it its scientific name.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
