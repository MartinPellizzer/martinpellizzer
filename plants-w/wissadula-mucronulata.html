Wissadula mucronulata

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Its flowers typically bloom in spring and summer, and the plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
