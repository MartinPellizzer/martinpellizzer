Woollsia

Woollsia, commonly known as the woolly wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes and is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits. The plant is known for its dense, woolly foliage that gives it its common name. Woollsia typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

It produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
