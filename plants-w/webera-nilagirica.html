Webera nilagirica

Webera nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri Webera, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and potential medicinal properties.
