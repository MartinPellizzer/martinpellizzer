Werauhia gladioliflora

Werauhia gladioliflora, commonly known as the swordleaf werauhia, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often broad and sword-like in appearance. The plant produces inflorescences that bear showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
