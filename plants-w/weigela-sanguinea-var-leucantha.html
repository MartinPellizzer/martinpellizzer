Weigela sanguinea var. leucantha

Weigela sanguinea var. leucantha, commonly known as the white weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant features oval, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
