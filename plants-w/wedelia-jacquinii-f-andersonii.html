Wedelia jacquinii f. andersonii

Wedelia jacquinii f. andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in compact clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.

It is often cultivated for its ability to thrive in shaded or partially sunny conditions and its low maintenance requirements.
