Wedelia serrata var. multidentata

Wedelia serrata var. multidentata, commonly known as the serrated wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant has serrated leaf margins, which give it its scientific name, and it often grows in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is typically dark green and aromatic, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The variety multidentata is distinguished by its more pronounced leaf serrations compared to the typical form.
