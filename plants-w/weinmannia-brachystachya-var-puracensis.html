Weinmannia brachystachya var. puracensis

Weinmannia brachystachya var. puracensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian weinmannia, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. Its leaves are usually narrow and leathery, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
