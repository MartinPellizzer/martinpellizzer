Wedelia Purpurea

Wedelia purpurea is a flowering plant known for its vibrant purple blooms and compact growth habit. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm climates with ample sunlight. This ornamental plant is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and easy maintenance. Its delicate flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it can spread rapidly, making it both a desirable and challenging garden plant.
