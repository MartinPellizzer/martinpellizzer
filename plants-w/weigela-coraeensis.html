Weigela coraeensis

Weigela coraeensis, commonly known as the Korean weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea and northeastern China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Weigela coraeensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
