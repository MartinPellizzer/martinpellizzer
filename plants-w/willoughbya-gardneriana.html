Willoughbya gardneriana

Willoughbya gardneriana, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Willoughbya gardneriana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
