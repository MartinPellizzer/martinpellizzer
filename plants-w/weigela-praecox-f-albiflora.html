Weigela praecox f. albiflora

Weigela praecox f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is a hybrid form of Weigela praecox, characterized by its showy, pendulous white flowers that appear in early spring. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The white flowers are often followed by red berries, adding seasonal interest to the plant.
