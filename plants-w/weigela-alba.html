Weigela alba

Weigela alba, commonly known as the white weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces clusters of white petals that are often accompanied by red bracts, creating a striking visual display. Its leaves are ovate and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to the flowers.

Weigela alba is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental shrubs.
