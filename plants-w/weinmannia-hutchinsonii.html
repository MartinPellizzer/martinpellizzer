Weinmannia hutchinsonii

Weinmannia hutchinsonii, commonly known as the Huon pine, is a coniferous tree native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its needle-like leaves and distinctive cone structure. The plant is adapted to the cool, moist environments of the Southern Hemisphere. It plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is considered a rare and valuable species.
