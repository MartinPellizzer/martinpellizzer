Wedelia annua

Wedelia annua, commonly known as annual wedelia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, white or yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from summer to fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and slightly serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a common weed in agricultural and garden settings.

Its rapid growth and prolific seed production contribute to its invasive potential in certain environments.
