Willkommia minuta

Willkommia minuta, commonly known as the dwarf willkommia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in winter, adding ornamental value. It is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal areas due to its drought tolerance.

Willkommia minuta is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its low maintenance requirements.
