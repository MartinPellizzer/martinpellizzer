Wedelia thouarsii

Wedelia thouarsii, commonly known as the yellow wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing herb with prostrate stems that spread along the ground. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a popular ornamental species. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Wedelia thouarsii is often used in landscaping for its ability to form dense mats and its drought tolerance.
