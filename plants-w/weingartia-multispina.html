Weingartia multispina

Weingartia multispina, commonly known as the Mexican spinefoot, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and short, cylindrical stems. The plant features multiple spines, which are typically pale yellow to brown in color. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the areoles.

Weingartia multispina is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
