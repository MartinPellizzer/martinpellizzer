Whitia carnosa

Whitia carnosa, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
