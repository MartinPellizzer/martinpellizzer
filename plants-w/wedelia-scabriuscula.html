Wedelia scabriuscula

Wedelia scabriuscula, commonly known as roughleaf wedelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, and its leaves are rough and hairy to the touch. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
