Webera canarica

Webera canarica, commonly known as the Canary Island heather, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense foliage that helps it thrive in rocky or coastal environments.

Webera canarica is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in sunny, well-drained soils.
