Watsonia strictiflora

Watsonia strictiflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered Watsonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that bear dense clusters of vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. Watsonia strictiflora is known for its long-lasting flowers, which can persist for several weeks, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
