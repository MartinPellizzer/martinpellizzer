Weigela × fujisanensis

Weigela × fujisanensis, commonly known as the Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, blooming in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

The shrub is often used in landscape design for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
