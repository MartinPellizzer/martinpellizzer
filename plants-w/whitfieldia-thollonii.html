Whitfieldia thollonii

Whitfieldia thollonii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has linear, grass-like leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant flowers.

Whitfieldia thollonii is also valued for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
