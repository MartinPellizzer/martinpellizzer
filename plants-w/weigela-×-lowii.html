Weigela × lowii

Weigela × lowii, commonly known as the Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, appearing in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Weigela × lowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
