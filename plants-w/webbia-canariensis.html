Webbia canariensis

Webbia canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Island grass.

It is a perennial herb native to the Canary Islands, characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid environments.
