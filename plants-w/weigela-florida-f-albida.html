Weigela florida f. albida

Weigela florida f. albida, commonly known as the white weigela, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, funnel-shaped flowers that are predominantly white, often with a slight pinkish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-orange fruits that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
