Watsonia pyramidata var. zeyheri

Watsonia pyramidata var. zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's Watsonia, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with large, showy inflorescences composed of vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both cultivated gardens and natural habitats.
