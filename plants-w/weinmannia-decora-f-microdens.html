Weinmannia decora f. microdens

Weinmannia decora f. microdens, commonly known as the compact weinmannia, is a compact, shrub-like form of the species Weinmannia decora.

It is native to the Pacific coast of Central America, particularly in regions of Mexico. The plant features dense, dark green foliage with a rounded growth habit, making it suitable for small garden spaces. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, providing a textured appearance.

This form is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to container gardening.
