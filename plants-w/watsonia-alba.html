Watsonia alba

Watsonia alba, commonly known as the white Watsonia, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, showy white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and strap-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Watsonia alba is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
