Wedelia acapulcensis var. parviceps

Wedelia acapulcensis var. parviceps, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wedelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in moist, shaded environments.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth habit.
