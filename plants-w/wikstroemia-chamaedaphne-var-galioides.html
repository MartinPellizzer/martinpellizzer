Wikstroemia chamaedaphne var. galioides

Wikstroemia chamaedaphne var. galioides, commonly known as the coastal wikstroemia, is a variant of the coastal mahoe plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a rugged appearance.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
