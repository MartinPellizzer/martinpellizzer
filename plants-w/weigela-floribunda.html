Weigela floribunda

Weigela floribunda, commonly known as the weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers, which range in color from red to pink, are borne on long, slender pedicels, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. The leaves are ovate and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to the vibrant blossoms.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
