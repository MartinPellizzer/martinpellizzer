Wendlandia rotundifolia

Wendlandia rotundifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, exotic blooms.
