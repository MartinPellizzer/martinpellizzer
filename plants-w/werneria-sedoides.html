Werneria sedoides

Werneria sedoides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows up to 30 cm in height and thrives in bright, indirect light. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers, which can range in color from purple to white.
