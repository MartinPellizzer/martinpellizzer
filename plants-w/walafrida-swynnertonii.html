Walafrida swynnertonii

Walafrida swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces tall, slender stems with showy, white to pale pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and is adapted to thrive in cool, moist environments.
