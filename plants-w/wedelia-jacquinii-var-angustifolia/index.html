Wedelia Jacquinii Var. Angustifolia

Wedelia jacquinii var. angustifolia is a striking ornamental plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and lush green foliage. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm climates and well-drained soils. This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to form dense mats. Its unique leaf shape and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. With proper care, it can add a tropical flair to any outdoor space.
