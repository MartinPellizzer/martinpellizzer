Wedelia Subpetiolata

Wedelia subpetiolata is a low-growing perennial plant known for its dense, mat-forming habit and vibrant yellow flowers. Native to tropical regions of Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and well-drained soil. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to suppress weeds. Its compact growth makes it ideal for rock gardens, borders, and as a ground cover. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent it from spreading aggressively.
