Trifolium angustifolium subvar. blancheanum

Trifolium angustifolium subvar. blancheanum, commonly known as the Blancheanum clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its narrow, trifoliate leaves that are typically pale green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
