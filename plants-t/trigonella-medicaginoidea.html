Trigonella medicaginoidea

Trigonella medicaginoidea, commonly known as the medicinal trigonella, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged in a triangular shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its seeds are oval and often have a distinctive coating, which aids in their dispersal.

Trigonella medicaginoidea is native to regions of southern Europe and is known for its potential medicinal properties.
