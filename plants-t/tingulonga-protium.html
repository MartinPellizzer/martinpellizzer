Tingulonga protium

Tingulonga protium, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to central and eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant wood that is highly valued for its scent and durability. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall, with smooth, greyish bark and elliptical, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small and yellowish, and it produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

The plant is also noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies across its native range.
