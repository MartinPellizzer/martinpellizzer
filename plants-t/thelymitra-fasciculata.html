Thelymitra fasciculata

Thelymitra fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered star orchid, is an Australian terrestrial orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Thelymitra fasciculata is known for its delicate appearance and is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
