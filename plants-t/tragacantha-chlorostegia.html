Tragacantha chlorostegia

Tragacantha chlorostegia, commonly known as the green-stemmed tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces pods containing seeds, and its leaves are typically trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets.

It is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
