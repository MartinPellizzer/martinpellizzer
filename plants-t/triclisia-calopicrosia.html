Triclisia calopicrosia

Triclisia calopicrosia, commonly known as the glossy leaf ivy, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its stems are flexible and can trail or climb, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.

Triclisia calopicrosia thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
