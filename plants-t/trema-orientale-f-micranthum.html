Trema orientale f. micranthum

Trema orientale f. micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered trema, is a variant of the trema species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid environments and is often found in forest understories. Its botanical features include slender stems with adventitious roots and opposite leaf arrangement.

The flowers are typically clustered in racemes and are followed by small, round, greenish fruits.
