Trimezia laevis

Trimezia laevis, commonly known as the smooth trimezia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Trimezia laevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
