Tripleurospermum australe

Tripleurospermum australe, commonly known as the Australian oxeye daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow central disk. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. It is a perennial herb that produces a rosette of basal leaves.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
