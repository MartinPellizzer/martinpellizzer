Tectaria rara

Tectaria rara, commonly known as the rare tectaria fern, is a species of epiphytic fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. Native to tropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Tectaria rara has a unique reproductive system, producing spores in specialized structures called sorangia.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
