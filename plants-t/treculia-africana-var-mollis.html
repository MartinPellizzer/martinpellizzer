Treculia africana var. mollis

Treculia africana var. mollis, commonly known as African shea tree or shea butter tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, simple leaves and smooth, grayish bark. The tree produces round, yellowish fruits that contain a sticky, white pulp surrounding a hard seed. The fruit's pulp is rich in oils, which are extracted to produce shea butter, a valuable commodity in cosmetics and food industries.

This plant plays an important ecological and economic role in its native habitats.
