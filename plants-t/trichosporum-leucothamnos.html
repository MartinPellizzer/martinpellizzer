Trichosporum leucothamnos

Trichosporum leucothamnos, commonly known as white thorned broom, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems covered in white, fluffy hairs, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slight pubescence.

Trichosporum leucothamnos is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
