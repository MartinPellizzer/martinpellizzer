Trichosporum littorale

Trichosporum littorale, commonly known as sea beach trichosporum, is a flowering plant species native to coastal areas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in sandy or saline environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh coastal conditions. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, with a delicate appearance.

Trichosporum littorale plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion along shorelines.
