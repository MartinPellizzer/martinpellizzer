Tragopogon filifolius

Tragopogon filifolius, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer, followed by fluffy, seed-bearing structures.

Tragopogon filifolius is often found in the western United States and is noted for its delicate, grass-like leaves and distinctive appearance.
