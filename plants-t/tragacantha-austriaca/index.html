Tragacantha Austriaca

Tragacantha austriaca is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Europe. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique, spurred petals that resemble tiny horns. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or woodland soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat requirements, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Its striking appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
