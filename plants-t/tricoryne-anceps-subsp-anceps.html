Tricoryne anceps subsp. anceps

Tricoryne anceps subsp.

anceps, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
