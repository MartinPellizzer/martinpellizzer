Tupistra tripartita

Tupistra tripartita, commonly known as the three-lobed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Tupistra tripartita is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative ground cover due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
