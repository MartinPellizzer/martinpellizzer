Thymus × mixtus var. toletanus

Thymus × mixtus var. toletanus, commonly known as the Toletan thyme, is a compact, low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features aromatic, gray-green foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are densely packed along the stems. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its strong, lemony scent makes it a popular choice for culinary and ornamental use.
