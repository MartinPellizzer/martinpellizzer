Trifolium resupinatum var. robustum

Trifolium resupinatum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust yellow trefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves.

The leaves are typically bright yellow to green, with three oval-shaped leaflets arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
