Tidestromia valdesiana

Tidestromia valdesiana, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red ray flowers and dark green, heart-shaped leaves. Tidestromia valdesiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
