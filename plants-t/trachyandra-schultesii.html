Trachyandra schultesii

Trachyandra schultesii, commonly known as the Schultesi trachyandra, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in summer, which are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by succulent enthusiasts.
