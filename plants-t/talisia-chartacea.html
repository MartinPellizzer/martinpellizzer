Talisia chartacea

Talisia chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf talisia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are thin and papery in texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. Talisia chartacea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
