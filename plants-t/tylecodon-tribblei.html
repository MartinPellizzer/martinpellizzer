Tylecodon tribblei

Tylecodon tribblei, commonly known as the silver rose or silver bush, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, silver-gray leaves that are fleshy and arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Tylecodon tribblei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
