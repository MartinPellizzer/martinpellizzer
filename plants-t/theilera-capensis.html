Theilera capensis

Theilera capensis, commonly known as the Cape thorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces fruit that turns red when mature. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Theilera capensis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
