Tibouchina formosa

Tibouchina formosa, commonly known as the flamebush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Tibouchina formosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
