Teucrium fortunei

Teucrium fortunei, commonly known as Fortune's germander, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple and are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
