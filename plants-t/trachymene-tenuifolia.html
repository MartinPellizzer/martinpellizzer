Trachymene tenuifolia

Trachymene tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf false shamrock, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, hairy stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understoreys.

Trachymene tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
