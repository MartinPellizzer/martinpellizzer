Thelypteris euthythrix

Thelypteris euthythrix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in humid forests. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant across the ground.

Thelypteris euthythrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
