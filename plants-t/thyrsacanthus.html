Thyrsacanthus

Thyrsacanthus, commonly known as the starburst cactus, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plants are characterized by their spiny stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. They are adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.
