Trianthema nyasicum

Trianthema nyasicum, commonly known as the African bluebush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Trianthema nyasicum is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
