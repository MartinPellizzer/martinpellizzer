Talinum pachypodum

Talinum pachypodum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its attractive appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
