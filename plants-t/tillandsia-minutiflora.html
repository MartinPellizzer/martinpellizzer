Tillandsia minutiflora

Tillandsia minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature flower air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives without soil, absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it is characterized by its compact rosette structure and tiny, white flowers. The plant is well adapted to humid environments and can be found growing on trees or rocks.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
