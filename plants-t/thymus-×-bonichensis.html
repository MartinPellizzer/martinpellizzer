Thymus × bonichensis

Thymus × bonichensis, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing, aromatic shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or purple flowers in the spring, which attract pollinators such as bees. It is valued for its fragrant foliage, which is often used in culinary and herbal applications.

Thymus × bonichensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and versatile garden plant.
