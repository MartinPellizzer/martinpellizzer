Taraxacum ingens

Taraxacum ingens, commonly known as the giant dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flower heads and robust, hairy stems. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed sites.
