Trichopilia undulatissima

Trichopilia undulatissima, commonly known as the wavy trichopilia, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive wavy leaf margins. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers.

Trichopilia undulatissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance in suitable climates.
