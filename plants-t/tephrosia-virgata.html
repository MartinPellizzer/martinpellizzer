Tephrosia virgata

Tephrosia virgata, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are pinnately compound with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Tephrosia virgata is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional medicine.
