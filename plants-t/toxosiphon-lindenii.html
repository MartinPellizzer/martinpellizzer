Toxosiphon lindenii

Toxosiphon lindenii, commonly known as the yellow sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the summer months. It is adapted to survive in dry, sandy environments, storing water in its thick stems.
