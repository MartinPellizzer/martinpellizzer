Tithymalus cataputia

Tithymalus cataputia, commonly known as the yellow tithymalus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
