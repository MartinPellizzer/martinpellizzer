Thelypteris binervata

Thelypteris binervata, commonly known as the two-veined shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive two-veined pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the propagation of the species.

The fronds are usually evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
