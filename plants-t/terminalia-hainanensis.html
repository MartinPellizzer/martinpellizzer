Terminalia hainanensis

Terminalia hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan tallowtree, is a deciduous tree native to southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, three-lobed fruits containing three seeds.

It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its timber and medicinal properties.
