Thea Sasanqua Var. Thunbergii

Thea sasanqua var. thunbergii is a hardy evergreen shrub known for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates. Native to East Asia, this plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and is prized for its fragrant, pink or red flowers. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for gardens, borders, or as a specimen plant. The variety thunbergii is particularly noted for its vibrant foliage and seasonal blooms. This versatile plant is a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
