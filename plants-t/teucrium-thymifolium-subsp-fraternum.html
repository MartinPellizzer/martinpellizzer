Teucrium thymifolium subsp. fraternum

Teucrium thymifolium subsp.

fraternum, commonly known as the fraternum germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a grayish-green hue.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued plant in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
