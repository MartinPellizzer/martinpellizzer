Trichosporum crassifolium

Trichosporum crassifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf trichosporum, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping and garden use.
