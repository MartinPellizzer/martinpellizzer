Tanacetum densum subsp. densum

Tanacetum densum subsp.

densum, commonly known as the dense yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It features basal leaves that are linear to narrowly lanceolate, with a rough, hairy texture.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, known as corymbs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils. Tanacetum densum subsp.

densum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and has historical uses in herbal medicine.
