Tillandsia carnosa var. carnosa

Tillandsia carnosa var. carnosa, commonly known as the showy tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers can range in color from white to pink, and it blooms in the summer.

This plant requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
