Tovomita

Tovomita, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosettes of velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Tovomita species are typically small, herbaceous plants that thrive in moist, well-drained soil. They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and ease of care.

The flowers often bloom in spring and summer, adding a vibrant touch to indoor or outdoor gardens.
