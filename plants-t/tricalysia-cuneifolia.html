Tricalysia cuneifolia

Tricalysia cuneifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Tricalysia cuneifolia is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various climates.
