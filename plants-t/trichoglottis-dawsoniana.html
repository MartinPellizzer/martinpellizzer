Trichoglottis dawsoniana

Trichoglottis dawsoniana, commonly known as Dawson's trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
