Trisetaria koelerioides

Trisetaria koelerioides, commonly known as the koeleria grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy texture.

Trisetaria koelerioides is adapted to arid environments and is often found in regions with low rainfall.
