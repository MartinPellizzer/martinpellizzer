Teramnus tenuiflorus

Teramnus tenuiflorus, commonly known as slender-flowered teramnus, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
