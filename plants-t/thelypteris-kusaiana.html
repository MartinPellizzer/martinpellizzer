Thelypteris kusaiana

Thelypteris kusaiana, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Thelypteris kusaiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
