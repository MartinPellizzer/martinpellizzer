Trichomanes Truncatum

Trichomanes truncatum is a unique fern species known for its delicate, lace-like fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. This plant has a long evolutionary history, dating back millions of years. Its structure resembles ancient ferns, making it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture.
