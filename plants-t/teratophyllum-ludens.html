Teratophyllum ludens

Teratophyllum ludens, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped stems that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers during the rainy season, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native desert environment.

Teratophyllum ludens is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and visual interest.
