Tragacantha echinus

Tragacantha echinus, commonly known as the spiny tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that produces distinctive, spiny fruit capsules containing several seeds. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and grayish-green foliage. It blooms with yellow flowers in the spring, and its fruit is often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its resin, which has been historically used in various applications.
