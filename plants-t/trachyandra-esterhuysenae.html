Trachyandra esterhuysenae

Trachyandra esterhuysenae, commonly known as the Esterhuysen's grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to South Africa, characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and restricted distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
