Tribulus californicus

Tribulus californicus, commonly known as California tribulus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, hairy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and horticultural research.
