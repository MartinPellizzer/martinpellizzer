Trichosporum firmum

Trichosporum firmum, commonly known as the firm moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and is typically found in dry, shaded environments. This moss is notable for its ability to survive in harsh conditions due to its thick, waxy cuticle.
