Tephrosia coccinea var. stenophylla

Tephrosia coccinea var. stenophylla, commonly known as the red tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

The plant is also noted for its medicinal properties, used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
