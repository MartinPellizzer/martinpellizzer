Thonningia janeirensis

Thonningia janeirensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features linear leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure.

Thonningia janeirensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
