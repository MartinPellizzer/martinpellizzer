Tephrosia rosea var. clementii

Tephrosia rosea var. clementii, commonly known as the Clement's rose tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate pink to lavender flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal to its foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and slightly hairy.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
