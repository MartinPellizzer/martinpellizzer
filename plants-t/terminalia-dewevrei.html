Terminalia dewevrei

Terminalia dewevrei, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing flat, winged seeds.
