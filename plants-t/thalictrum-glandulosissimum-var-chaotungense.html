Thalictrum glandulosissimum var. chaotungense

Thalictrum glandulosissimum var. chaotungense, commonly known as the Chaotung Chinese meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has hairy stems and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Ranunculaceae family.
