Thespesia peekelii

Thespesia peekelii, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Oahu in Hawaii.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its large, showy, pink to red flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Thespesia peekelii is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
