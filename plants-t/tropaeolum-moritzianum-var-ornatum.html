Tropaeolum moritzianum var. ornatum

Tropaeolum moritzianum var. ornatum, commonly known as the "Chinese violet," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is an annual species that thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
