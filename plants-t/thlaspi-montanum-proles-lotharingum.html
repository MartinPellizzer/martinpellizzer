Thlaspi montanum proles lotharingum

Thlaspi montanum proles lotharingum, commonly known as the Lotharingian cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous annual that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and arranged in a rosette pattern, with a slightly hairy surface.

This species is often found in moist, disturbed soils in mountainous regions across Europe.
