Tectaria macrosora

Tectaria macrosora, commonly known as the broad-leaf shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a distinctive shield-shaped sorus, which is covered by a prominent indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Tectaria macrosora is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
