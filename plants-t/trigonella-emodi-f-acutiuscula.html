Trigonella emodi f. acutiuscula

Trigonella emodi f. acutiuscula, commonly known as the acute-leaved fenugreek, is a botanical variant of the fenugreek species native to the Himalayan regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with acute, sharply pointed leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, slender pods containing several seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
