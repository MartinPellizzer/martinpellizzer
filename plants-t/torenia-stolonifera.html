Torenia stolonifera

Torenia stolonifera, commonly known as the spreading torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Torenia stolonifera is known for its ability to spread via stolons, forming dense mats in garden settings.

It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover in tropical gardens.
