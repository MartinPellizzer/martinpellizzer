Thomandersia laurifolia

Thomandersia laurifolia, commonly known as the evergreen laurel, is a tropical shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic plants like laurel and cinnamon. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Thomandersia laurifolia is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
