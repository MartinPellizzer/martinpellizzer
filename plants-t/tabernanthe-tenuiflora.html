Tabernanthe tenuiflora

Tabernanthe tenuiflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, dark green leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor conditions. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both decorative and medicinal purposes.
