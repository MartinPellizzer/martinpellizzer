Torenia cyanea

Torenia cyanea, commonly known as the blue trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Torenia cyanea is a trailing or climbing plant that thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
