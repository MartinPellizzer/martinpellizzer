Terminalia mangela

Terminalia mangela, commonly known as African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its distinctive clusters of small white flowers that bloom in winter. The tree produces small, round, green fruits that turn yellow when mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly rough texture.
