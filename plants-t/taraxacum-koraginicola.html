Taraxacum koraginicola

Taraxacum koraginicola, commonly known as the Koragin mountain dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Laos.
