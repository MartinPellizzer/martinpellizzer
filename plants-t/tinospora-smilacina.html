Tinospora smilacina

Tinospora smilacina, commonly known as Indian moonseed, is a climbing shrub belonging to the Menispermaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and compound leaves with opposite arrangement. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by smooth, dark blue to black berries. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Tinospora smilacina is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional systems of medicine.
