Trichosanthes ovigera subsp. cucumeroides

Trichosanthes ovigera subsp.

cucumeroides, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a vine native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins.

The plant produces large, warty, green fruits that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture, and it has tendrils that aid in climbing.
