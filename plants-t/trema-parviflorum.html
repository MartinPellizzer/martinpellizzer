Trema parviflorum

Trema parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered trema, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often ovate or elliptical in shape. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Trema parviflorum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
