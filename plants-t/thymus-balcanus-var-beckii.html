Thymus balcanus var. beckii

Thymus balcanus var. beckii, commonly known as Balkan thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, dark green leaves and clusters of pink to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
