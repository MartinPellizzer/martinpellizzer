Thalictrum integratum

Thalictrum integratum, commonly known as the showy mountain rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Thalictrum integratum is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and early season blooms.
