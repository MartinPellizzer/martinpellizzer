Tofieldia coccinea var. kanwonensis

Tofieldia coccinea var. kanwonensis, commonly known as the Korean redflower tofieldia, is a perennial herb native to Korea.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a glossy appearance, while its stems are erect and fibrous.
