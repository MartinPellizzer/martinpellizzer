Trillium erectum f. simile

Trillium erectum f. simile, commonly known as the showy yellow trillium, is a rare and striking variant of the common trillium.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. This fern-like plant typically grows in rich, moist woodlands and thrives in partial shade. The flowers are three-petaled and bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to the forest floor.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often protected in its natural habitat.
