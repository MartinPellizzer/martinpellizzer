Trachymene procumbens

Trachymene procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate trachymene, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features slender, creeping stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are elongated and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Trachymene procumbens thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.
