Thelymitra intermedia

Thelymitra intermedia, commonly known as the common spider-orchid, is an Australian terrestrial orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-brown pseudobulbs and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically blooming in spring. It is found in various habitats across eastern Australia, including heathlands and open forests.

Thelymitra intermedia is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in areas with moderate to high rainfall.
