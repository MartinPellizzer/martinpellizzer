Turpinia hatuyenensis

Turpinia hatuyenensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese turpentine tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oleaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellowish-white flowers. Turpinia hatuyenensis is valued for its essential oils, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of turpentine.
