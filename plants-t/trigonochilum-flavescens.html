Trigonochilum flavescens

Trigonochilum flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in a raceme, with three sepals and three petals.

Trigonochilum flavescens is typically found in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.
