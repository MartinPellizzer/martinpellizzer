Theodorea falconeri

Theodorea falconeri, commonly known as the Falconeri's orchid, is a rare and endangered species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, often featuring a mix of white, purple, and green hues.
