Thamnosma africana var. rhodesica

Thamnosma africana var. rhodesica, commonly known as Rhodesian thamnosma, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional medicine.

The leaves and roots are the primary parts used for their therapeutic effects.
