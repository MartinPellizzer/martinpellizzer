Trigonochilum werneri

Trigonochilum werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a compact rosette.

Trigonochilum werneri thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
