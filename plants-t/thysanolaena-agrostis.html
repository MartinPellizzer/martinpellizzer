Thysanolaena agrostis

Thysanolaena agrostis, commonly known as the silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, often forming dense clumps. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Thysanolaena agrostis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
