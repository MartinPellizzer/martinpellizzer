Tabernaemontana heyneana

Tabernaemontana heyneana, commonly known as the White Star Magnolia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its ornamental flowers. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Tabernaemontana heyneana is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a specimen plant in tropical landscapes.
