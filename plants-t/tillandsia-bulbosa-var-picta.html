Tillandsia bulbosa var. picta

Tillandsia bulbosa var. picta, commonly known as the picta tillandsia, is a bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often adorned with striking, variegated patterns. Its flowers, which are typically pink or purple, bloom in late spring or early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements in cultivation.
