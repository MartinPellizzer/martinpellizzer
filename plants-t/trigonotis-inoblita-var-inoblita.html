Trigonotis inoblita var. inoblita

Trigonotis inoblita var. inoblita, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The plant is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
