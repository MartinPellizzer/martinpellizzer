Tricalysia pallens var. gabonica

Tricalysia pallens var. gabonica, commonly known as the Gabon flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas or near water sources.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
