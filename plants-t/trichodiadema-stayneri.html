Trichodiadema stayneri

Trichodiadema stayneri, commonly known as the Stayner's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the cooler months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Trichodiadema stayneri is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
