Tetraria microcarpa

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, yellowish-green leaves and inconspicuous flowers. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.

Tetraria microcarpa is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, often growing in rocky soils and along dry streambeds.
