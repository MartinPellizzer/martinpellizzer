Tragopogon macropogon

Tragopogon macropogon, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features slender, leafy stems and produces yellow flowers with long, slender ray florets. It thrives in sunny, open habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils.

Tragopogon macropogon is known for its rapid growth and ability to hybridize with other Tragopogon species.
