Tillandsia uhdei

Tillandsia uhdei, commonly known as the silver leaf airplant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a epiphytic plant that absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves rather than roots. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. It features dense, silver-white foliage that gives it a striking appearance.

Tillandsia uhdei thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain humidity.
