Terebinthus angustata

Terebinthus angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf terebinth, is a species of flowering plant in the family Terebinthaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Terebinthus angustata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
