Tasmannia parviflora

Tasmannia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered tasmannia, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features dark green, elliptical leaves that are glossy and slightly aromatic. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that ripen in autumn and are edible for certain bird species.

Tasmannia parviflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understorey habitats.
