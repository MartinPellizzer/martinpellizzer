Tillandsia rauhii

Tillandsia rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's air plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to Central America.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant possesses strap-like, narrow leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces striking purple or red inflorescences with showy bracts, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is widely appreciated by plant enthusiasts.
