Thunbergia bodinieri

Thunbergia bodinieri, commonly known as the Madagascar arum, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and showy, spathiflorous inflorescences. Thunbergia bodinieri thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
