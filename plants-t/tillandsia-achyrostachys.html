Tillandsia achyrostachys

Tillandsia achyrostachys, commonly known as the silver vase, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that forms a rosette shape. It produces striking, showy inflorescences with purple or red flowers.

Native to South America, it is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
