Trichosporum macrocalyx

Trichosporum macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered trichosporum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Trichosporum macrocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
