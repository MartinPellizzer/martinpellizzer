Tropaeolum gaertnerianum

Tropaeolum gaertnerianum, commonly known as the pepperleaf nasturtium, is a vibrant annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, peppery-tasting leaves and clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its rapid growth habit makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking quick blooms and edible foliage.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects to the garden.
