Tropaeolum stipulatum

Tropaeolum stipulatum, commonly known as the mountain tropaeolum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and ease of cultivation have made it a favored plant among gardeners.
