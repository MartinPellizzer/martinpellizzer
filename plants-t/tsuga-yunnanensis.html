Tsuga yunnanensis

Tsuga yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hemlock, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the genus Tsuga, which includes other hemlock species, and is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dense foliage. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, and its needles are typically dark green with a waxy coating.

It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests, contributing to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
