Trachelospermum gracilipes

Trachelospermum gracilipes, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
