Thunbergia lutea

Thunbergia lutea, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a vigorous climber, often used in gardens for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia lutea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
