Thelypteris taprobanica

Thelypteris taprobanica, commonly known as the Ceylon water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bioluminescent fronds that emit a faint glow in the dark. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The unique botanical features of T. taprobanica make it an intriguing subject for both ecological and scientific study.
