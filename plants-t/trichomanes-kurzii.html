Trichomanes kurzii

Trichomanes kurzii, commonly known as the Kurz's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically green to reddish in color. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its small, intricate leaves are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

Trichomanes kurzii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
