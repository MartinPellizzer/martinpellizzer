Tissa macrotheca var. leucantha

Tissa macrotheca var. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered tissa, is a variant of the tissa macrotheca species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with woody stems and glossy, elliptical leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
