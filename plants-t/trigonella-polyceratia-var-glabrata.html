Trigonella polyceratia var. glabrata

Trigonella polyceratia var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth trigonella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its seeds are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.

The variety 'glabrata' is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves compared to other varieties of Trigonella polyceratia.
