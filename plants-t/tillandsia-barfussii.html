Tillandsia barfussii

Tillandsia barfussii, commonly known as the Barfuss air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture from the air. It produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.

Due to its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
