Thelypteris rudiformis

Thelypteris rudiformis, commonly known as the rough holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its leathery, bipinnate fronds that are often covered with coarse, rough textures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of foliage.

Thelypteris rudiformis is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Himalayas.
