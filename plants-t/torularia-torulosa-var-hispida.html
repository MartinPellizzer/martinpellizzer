Torularia torulosa var. hispida

Torularia torulosa var. hispida, commonly known as the rough torula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
