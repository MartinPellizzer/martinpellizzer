Tephrosia hochstetteri

Tephrosia hochstetteri, commonly known as the African milk bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in grassland and savannah habitats.

Tephrosia hochstetteri is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural settings.
