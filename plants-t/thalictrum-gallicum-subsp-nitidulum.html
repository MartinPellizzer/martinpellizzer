Thalictrum gallicum subsp. nitidulum

Thalictrum gallicum subsp.

nitidulum, commonly known as the smooth meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe. It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and emit a mild, aromatic scent.

The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
