Taraxacum nitidiorum

Taraxacum nitidiorum, commonly known as the shining dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and distinctive, hairy leaves. The plant produces a capsule that contains numerous seeds with pappus, which allows for wind dispersal. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained, sunny areas.

Taraxacum nitidiorum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
