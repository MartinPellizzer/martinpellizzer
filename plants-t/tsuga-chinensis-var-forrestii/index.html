Tsuga Chinensis Var. Forrestii

Tsuga chinensis var. forrestii is a rare and beautiful evergreen conifer native to the mountainous regions of China. This variant of the Chinese fir is known for its unique foliage and compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests. Its dense canopy and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of cultivated plants.
