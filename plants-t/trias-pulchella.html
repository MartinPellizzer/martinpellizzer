Trias pulchella

Trias pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful trias, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. Trias pulchella thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
