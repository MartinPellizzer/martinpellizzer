Tulipa polychroma

Tulipa polychroma, commonly known as the multicolored tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored flowers that often display a mix of red, yellow, and white in a single bloom. The plant typically grows to a height of 15 to 30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are adapted to store nutrients, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soil.

This tulip is prized for its striking appearance and is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
