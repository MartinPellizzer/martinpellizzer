Tithymalus tarokoensis

Tithymalus tarokoensis, commonly known as the Taroko tithymalus, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Tithymalus tarokoensis is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
