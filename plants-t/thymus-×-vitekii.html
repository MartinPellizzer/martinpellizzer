Thymus × vitekii

Thymus × vitekii, commonly known as the Viteki thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and aromatic foliage, which contains essential oils responsible for its distinctive scent. The plant typically features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus × vitekii is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses, similar to other thyme species.
