Triticum Pinnatum Var. Abbreviatum

Triticum pinnatum var. abbreviatum is a lesser-known grass species with unique morphological characteristics. It is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean and parts of Asia. This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and reduced inflorescence structure. Despite its limited distribution, it holds ecological significance in certain habitats. Its study contributes to the understanding of grass diversity and adaptation strategies.
