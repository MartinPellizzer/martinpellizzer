Thelypteris luzonica

Thelypteris luzonica, commonly known as the Luzon holly fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant.
