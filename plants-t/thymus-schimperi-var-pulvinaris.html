Thymus schimperi var. pulvinaris

Thymus schimperi var. pulvinaris, commonly known as the Ethiopian thyme, is a perennial herb native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage of small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces purple to pink flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
