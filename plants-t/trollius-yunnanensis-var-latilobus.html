Trollius yunnanensis var. latilobus

Trollius yunnanensis var. latilobus, commonly known as the Yunnan globeflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations. Its leaves are palmately divided with a hairy texture, and the stems are often reddish in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
