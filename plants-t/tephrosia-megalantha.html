Tephrosia megalantha

Tephrosia megalantha, commonly known as the large-leaved tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often large and trifoliate, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks.

Tephrosia megalantha is also noted for its medicinal properties, used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
