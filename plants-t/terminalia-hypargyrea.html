Terminalia hypargyrea

Terminalia hypargyrea, commonly known as the silver terminalia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, ellipsoid fruits containing one or two seeds.
