Thesium polyanthum

Thesium polyanthum, commonly known as the clustered thesium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
