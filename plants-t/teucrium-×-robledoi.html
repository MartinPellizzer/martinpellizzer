Teucrium × robledoi

Teucrium × robledoi, commonly known as the hybrid germander, is a perennial shrub belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region but has been introduced to other parts of the world, including parts of the United States. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Teucrium × robledoi is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
