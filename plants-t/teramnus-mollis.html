Teramnus mollis

Teramnus mollis, commonly known as the silky teramnus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, pale green leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are arranged in clusters.

Teramnus mollis is known for its adaptability to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
