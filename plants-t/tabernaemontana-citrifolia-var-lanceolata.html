Tabernaemontana citrifolia var. lanceolata

Tabernaemontana citrifolia var. lanceolata, commonly known as the citrus starflower, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant yellow and white flowers that resemble small stars.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
