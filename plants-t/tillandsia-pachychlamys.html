Tillandsia pachychlamys

Tillandsia pachychlamys, commonly known as the giant air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features long, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape, which helps collect moisture from the air. It produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can last for several months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
