Tilia sargentiana

Tilia sargentiana, commonly known as Sargent's linden, is a deciduous tree native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain three winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Sargent's linden is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
