Tilia nanchuanensis

Tilia nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, while its wood is hard and durable.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
