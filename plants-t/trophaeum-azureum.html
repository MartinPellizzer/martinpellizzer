Trophaeum azureum

Trophaeum azureum, commonly known as the Azure Blush.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its striking blue-violet petals, which are highly sensitive to light and temperature changes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
