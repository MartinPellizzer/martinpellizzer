Trichosalpinx cryptantha

Trichosalpinx cryptantha, commonly known as the cryptantha spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, intricate flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Trichosalpinx cryptantha typically blooms during the wet season, producing delicate, fragrant flowers that attract specific pollinators.
