Tecoma Aurea

Tecoma aurea is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa known for its vibrant yellow blooms. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features long, narrow leaves and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers that attract pollinators. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Tecoma aurea is both a beautiful addition to any setting and a symbol of resilience in harsh environments.
