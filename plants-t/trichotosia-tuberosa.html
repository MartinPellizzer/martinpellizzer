Trichotosia tuberosa

Trichotosia tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous trichotosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces showy yellow to orange flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and therapeutic purposes.
