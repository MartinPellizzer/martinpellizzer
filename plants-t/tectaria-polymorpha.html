Tectaria polymorpha

Tectaria polymorpha, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of hair-like structures. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Tectaria polymorpha exhibits a variety of morphological forms, which contribute to its common name "polymorpha.".

Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.
