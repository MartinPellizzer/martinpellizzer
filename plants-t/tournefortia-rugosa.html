Tournefortia rugosa

Tournefortia rugosa, commonly known as the roughleaf tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features dark green, rough-textured leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. Its flowers are often followed by small, woody fruits containing several seeds.

Tournefortia rugosa is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or dry woodland habitats.
