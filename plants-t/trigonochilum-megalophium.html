Trigonochilum megalophium

Trigonochilum megalophium, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often dark green with a waxy surface. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
