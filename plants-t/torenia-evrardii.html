Torenia evrardii

Torenia evrardii, commonly known as the velvetleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Torenia evrardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
