Thelymitra carnea var. rubra

Thelymitra carnea var. rubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a distinctive, hairy, reddish stem that distinguishes it from other varieties of Thelymitra. The plant produces a single, erect flower spike bearing a single, showy orchid flower with a greenish-yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understoreys.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
