Telipogon patinii

Telipogon patinii, commonly known as the Patin's grass, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is native to South America, particularly in regions of Brazil and Argentina.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
