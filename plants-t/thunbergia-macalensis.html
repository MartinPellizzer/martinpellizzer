Thunbergia macalensis

Thunbergia macalensis, commonly known as the Macalensis Trumpet Vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia macalensis is also valued for its ability to climb and can be trained on trellises or fences.
