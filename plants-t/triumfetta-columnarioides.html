Triumfetta columnarioides

Triumfetta columnarioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.
