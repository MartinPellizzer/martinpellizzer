Trichinium setigerum

Trichinium setigerum, commonly known as the hairy trichinium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered umbels of white or pale yellow flowers. Trichinium setigerum is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
