Tropidia curculigoides

Tropidia curculigoides, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

Tropidia curculigoides is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and ease of cultivation.
