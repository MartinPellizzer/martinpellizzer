Trigonidium acuminatum

It is characterized by its triangular-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
