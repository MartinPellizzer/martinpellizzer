Tabernaemontana indica

Tabernaemontana indica, commonly known as the Indian star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a woody stem, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its beauty and fragrance make it a favored plant in both gardens and as a cut flower.
