Tillandsia orizabensis

Tillandsia orizabensis, commonly known as the Orizaba air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Tillandsia orizabensis is well adapted to humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
