Tectaria × epilithica

Tectaria × epilithica, commonly known as the hybrid wall fern, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Tectaria cryptogramma and Tectaria epilithica.

It is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, creeping growth habit. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, often in shaded, humid environments. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, compact appearance.

Tectaria × epilithica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in moist, shaded conditions.
