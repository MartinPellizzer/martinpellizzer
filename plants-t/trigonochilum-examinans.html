Trigonochilum examinans

Trigonochilum examinans, commonly known as the examined lip.

This orchid species is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. It features a compact, sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The flowers are typically small and greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
