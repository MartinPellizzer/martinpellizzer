Thymelaea granatensis

Thymelaea granatensis, commonly known as the granate thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in cymes, often with a distinctive red or orange coloration. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a slightly hairy surface.

Thymelaea granatensis is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
