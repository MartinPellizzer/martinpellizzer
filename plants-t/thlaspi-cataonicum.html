Thlaspi cataonicum

Thlaspi cataonicum, commonly known as the European cabbageworm plant, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are usually lobed or divided, with a rough texture. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

The fruits are dry pods that contain several small, rounded seeds.
