Tetrastigma harmandii

Tetrastigma harmandii, commonly known as the climbing raisin vine, is a woody vine native to Southeast Asia.

Tetrastigma harmandii is often used in traditional medicine and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its climbing habit and attractive foliage.
