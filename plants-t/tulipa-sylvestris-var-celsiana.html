Tulipa sylvestris var. celsiana

Tulipa sylvestris var. celsiana, commonly known as the Alpine tulip, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine meadows at high elevations.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
