Tabernaemontana deamii

Tabernaemontana deamii, commonly known as the Deam's flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, flame-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of orange and red. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
