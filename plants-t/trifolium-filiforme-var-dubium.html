Trifolium filiforme var. dubium

Trifolium filiforme var. dubium, commonly known as the slender clover, is a rare and delicate species of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves that are narrow and filamentous, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are small and usually pink or white, and it produces small, round pods that contain several seeds.
