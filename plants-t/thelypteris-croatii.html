Thelypteris croatii

Thelypteris croatii, commonly known as the Croatian water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along streams. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris croatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity.
