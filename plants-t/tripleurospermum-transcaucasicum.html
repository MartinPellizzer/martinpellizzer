Tripleurospermum transcaucasicum

Tripleurospermum transcaucasicum, commonly known as Caucasian goatsbeard, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit. The plant typically features slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks.
