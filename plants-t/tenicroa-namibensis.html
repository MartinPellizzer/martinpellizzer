Tenicroa namibensis

Tenicroa namibensis, commonly known as the Namibian thornveld grass, is a perennial grass native to the Namib Desert.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and fibrous roots that help it survive in arid conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to high temperatures and low rainfall.

Tenicroa namibensis plays a role in stabilizing desert soils and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
