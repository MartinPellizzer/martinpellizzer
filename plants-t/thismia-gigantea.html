Thismia gigantea

Thismia gigantea, commonly known as the giant Thismia, is a unique plant belonging to the family Thismiaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial herb characterized by its lack of chlorophyll, which means it cannot perform photosynthesis. Instead, it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrients, forming a symbiotic relationship with them.
