Tanacetum linearilobum

Tanacetum linearilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed tansy, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features finely divided, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
