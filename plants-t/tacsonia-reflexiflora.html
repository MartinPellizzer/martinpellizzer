Tacsonia reflexiflora

Tacsonia reflexiflora, commonly known as the reflexed tacsonia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, reflexed petals that curve backward, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its small, fragrant flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Tacsonia reflexiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
