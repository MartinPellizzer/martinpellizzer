Tricyrtis hirta var. masamunei

Tricyrtis hirta var. masamunei, commonly known as the Japanese ghost orchid, is a striking perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture.

The species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
