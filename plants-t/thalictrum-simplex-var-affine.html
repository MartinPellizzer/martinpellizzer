Thalictrum simplex var. affine

Thalictrum simplex var. affine, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle ornamental value to gardens.

Thalictrum simplex var. affine is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators in natural and cultivated settings.
