Trichilia compacta

Trichilia compacta, commonly known as African snowball, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of white, fluffy flowers that appear in spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, red aril.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its showy appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
