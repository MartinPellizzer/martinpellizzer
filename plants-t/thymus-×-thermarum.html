Thymus × thermarum

Thymus × thermarum, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has aromatic, needle-like leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus × thermarum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
