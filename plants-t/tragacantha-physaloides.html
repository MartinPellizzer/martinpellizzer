Tragacantha physaloides

Tragacantha physaloides, commonly known as the balloon plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its distinctive, inflated, balloon-like fruits that contain seeds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

Tragacantha physaloides is adapted to arid environments and is often found in dry, rocky soils.
