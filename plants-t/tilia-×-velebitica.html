Tilia × velebitica

Tilia × velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit lime, is a hybrid species native to the Dinaric Alps, particularly in Croatia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
