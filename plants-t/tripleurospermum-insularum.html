Tripleurospermum insularum

Tripleurospermum insularum, commonly known as the island hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, adapting to the arid environments it inhabits.
