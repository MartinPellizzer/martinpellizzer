Thalictrum alpestre

Thalictrum alpestre, commonly known as the alpine meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its stems are often hairy, and it produces seeds in small, dry fruits.

Thalictrum alpestre is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
