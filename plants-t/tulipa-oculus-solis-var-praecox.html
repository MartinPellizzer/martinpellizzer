Tulipa oculus-solis var. praecox

Tulipa oculus-solis var. praecox, commonly known as the Early Sun tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It produces vibrant, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange eye in the center. The plant typically blooms in early spring, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking early-season color. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from the base of the bulb.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and borders, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
