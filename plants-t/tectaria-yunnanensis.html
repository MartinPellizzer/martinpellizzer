Tectaria yunnanensis

Tectaria yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan tectaria, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a complex, often lobed structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through sexual reproduction via spore dispersal.

Tectaria yunnanensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
