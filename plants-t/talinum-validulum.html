Talinum validulum

Talinum validulum, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its striking purple foliage and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along forest edges.

Talinum validulum is also valued for its edible leaves, which are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
