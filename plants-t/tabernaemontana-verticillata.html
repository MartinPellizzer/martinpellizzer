Tabernaemontana verticillata

Tabernaemontana verticillata, commonly known as the spiral flower or spiral jasmine, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
