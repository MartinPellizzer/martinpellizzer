Tetragonia fruticosa var. linearis

Tetragonia fruticosa var. linearis, commonly known as New Zealand spinach, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub-like perennial that features succulent, linear leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its edible leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and adaptable species.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and useful in culinary applications.
