Tabernaemontana flabelliformis

Tabernaemontana flabelliformis, commonly known as the white star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a woody stem, making it an attractive ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its beauty and fragrance make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
