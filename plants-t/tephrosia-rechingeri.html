Tephrosia rechingeri

Tephrosia rechingeri, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and southern Africa, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Tephrosia rechingeri is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
