Thunia marshalliana var. purpurata

Thunia marshalliana var. purpurata, commonly known as the Purple Thunia, is a striking aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and vibrant purple to violet-colored foliage. The plant thrives in warm, shallow waters with high light exposure and nutrient-rich substrates. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often form a compact rosette structure.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in aquatic gardens and pond landscapes.
