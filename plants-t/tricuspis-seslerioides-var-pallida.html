Tricuspis seslerioides var. pallida

Tricuspis seslerioides var. pallida, commonly known as the pale tricuspis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscaping.
