Tulipa aucheriana

Tulipa aucheriana, commonly known as the Aucher's tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is a perennial bulbous plant that produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and blooms in early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Tulipa aucheriana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
