Tragolium

Tragolium, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Tragolium is native to southern Africa and thrives in dry, arid environments.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
