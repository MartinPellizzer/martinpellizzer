Triumfetta gonophora

Triumfetta gonophora, commonly known as the African sandmat, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
