Tithymalus pekinensis var. watanabei

Tithymalus pekinensis var. watanabei, commonly known as the Watanabei tithymalus, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
