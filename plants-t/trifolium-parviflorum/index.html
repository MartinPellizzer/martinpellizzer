Trifolium Parviflorum

Trifolium parviflorum is a delicate flowering plant known for its small, clustered blooms and trifoliate leaves. It belongs to the legume family and is commonly found in meadows and grassy areas. This species is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural use. Its simple yet charming appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. Despite its modest size, Trifolium parviflorum plays an important role in ecological systems.
