Trichilia retusa

Trichilia retusa, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and satinwood species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and alternate, simple leaves that are elliptical in shape. The tree is valued for its durable, aromatic wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
