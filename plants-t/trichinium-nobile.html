Trichinium nobile

Trichinium nobile, commonly known as the noble trichium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to lavender. Native to the temperate regions of Europe, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Trichinium nobile is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique botanical appearance.
