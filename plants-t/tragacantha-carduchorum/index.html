Tragacantha Carduchorum

Tragacantha carduchorum is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique morphological features and ecological significance. Despite its beauty, it remains understudied and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this species for future generations. Its existence highlights the importance of protecting biodiversity in fragile ecosystems.
