Thelypteris isomorpha

Thelypteris isomorpha, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a smooth, glossy surface and a distinctive, shield-like shape. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are wiry and creeping, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
