Tillandsia fuchsii var. stephani

Tillandsia fuchsii var. stephani, commonly known as the silver-tipped air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant that absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves rather than through soil. The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage, which is covered in a dense layer of white, woolly trichomes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of South America.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
