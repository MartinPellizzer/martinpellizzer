Thalictrum ambiguum

Thalictrum ambiguum, commonly known as the meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Thalictrum ambiguum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
