Tectaria heracleifolia

Tectaria heracleifolia, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, supporting the growth of its expansive foliage.

The fronds are divided into segments with a distinctive shield-like appearance, giving the plant its common name.
