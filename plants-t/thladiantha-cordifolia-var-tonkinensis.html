Thladiantha cordifolia var. tonkinensis

Thladiantha cordifolia var. tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese starfruit or golden apple.

This variant is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow-orange fruits. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded, moist environments.

The botanical features of this variety make it a notable species in horticultural and ecological studies.
