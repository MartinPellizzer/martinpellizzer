Thoracostachyum ridleyi

Thoracostachyum ridleyi, commonly known as the Malayan nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark brown bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by woody, three-lobed fruits containing aromatic seeds.
