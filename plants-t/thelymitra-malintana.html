Thelymitra malintana

Thelymitra malintana, commonly known as the Northern Bluebells, is an Australian orchid species native to the eastern regions of Queensland.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring, typically from September to November. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Thelymitra malintana is often found in open forest or woodland habitats, growing in well-drained soils.

It is a terrestrial orchid that plays a role in local ecosystems and is appreciated for its ornamental value.
