Tithymalus sahendi

Tithymalus sahendi, commonly known as Sahendi tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, particularly Sudan and Ethiopia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, hairy stems, and small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to hot, dry climates.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
