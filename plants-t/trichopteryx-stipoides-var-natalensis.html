Trichopteryx stipoides var. natalensis

Trichopteryx stipoides var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal flatleaf, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing yellowish to greenish blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
