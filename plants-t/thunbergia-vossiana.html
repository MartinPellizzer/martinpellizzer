Thunbergia vossiana

Thunbergia vossiana, commonly known as the Voss's trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia vossiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
