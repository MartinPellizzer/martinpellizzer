Tulipa lownei

Tulipa lownei, commonly known as the Lowne's tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.

Tulipa lownei is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
