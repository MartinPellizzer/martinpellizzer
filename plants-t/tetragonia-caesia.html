Tetragonia caesia

Tetragonia caesia, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, edible leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found growing along shorelines.

Tetragonia caesia is valued for its culinary use and is also used in traditional medicine.
