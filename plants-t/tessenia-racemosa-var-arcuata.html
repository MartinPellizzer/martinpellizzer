Tessenia racemosa var. arcuata

Tessenia racemosa var. arcuata, commonly known as the curved raceme tessenia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated inflorescence that curves downward, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
