Teucrium × isabelis

Teucrium × isabelis, commonly known as Isabella germander, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and is native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
