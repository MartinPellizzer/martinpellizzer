Tylothrasya petrosa

Tylothrasya petrosa, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, grass-like foliage and produces striking blue to violet flowers in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. Its flowering stems are often branched and bear several flowers along their length.

Tylothrasya petrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
