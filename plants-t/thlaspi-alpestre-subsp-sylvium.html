Thlaspi alpestre subsp. sylvium

Thlaspi alpestre subsp.

The plant is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, growing in rocky or gravelly soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in cold climates and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
