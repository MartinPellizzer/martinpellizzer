Tilia × tibiscina

Tilia × tibiscina, commonly known as the whitebeam or small-leaved lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically pale green on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The tree produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers in early summer, which are followed by small, round, green fruits. It is native to Europe and is often found in woodlands and along riverbanks.

Tilia × tibiscina is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting local wildlife.
