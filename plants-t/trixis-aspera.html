Trixis aspera

Trixis aspera, commonly known as the rough trixis, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Trixis aspera is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is well-suited to withstand drought conditions.
