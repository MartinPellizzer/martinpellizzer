Trigonotis macrophylla

Trigonotis macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically large and velvety in texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Trigonotis macrophylla is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
