Teucrium mesanidum var. cylindrostachyum

Teucrium mesanidum var. cylindrostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stamen germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant characteristics. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its aromatic properties and traditional medicinal uses.
