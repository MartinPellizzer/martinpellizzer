Thalictrum Siamense Var. Longicarpum

Thalictrum siamense var. longicarpum is a unique flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, known for its showy and often delicate blooms. This variant is distinguished by its elongated, cylindrical fruits that set it apart from other members of the species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, making it a common sight in forest understories. Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and private collections.
