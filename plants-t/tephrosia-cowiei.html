Tephrosia cowiei

Tephrosia cowiei, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The plant is also noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
