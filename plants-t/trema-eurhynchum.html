Trema eurhynchum

Trema eurhynchum, commonly known as the broadleaf trema, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
