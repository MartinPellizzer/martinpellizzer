Tulipa unguiculata

Tulipa unguiculata, commonly known as the Turk's Cap Lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its distinctive, cup-shaped flowers that are typically red or pink in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms in early spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Tulipa unguiculata prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
