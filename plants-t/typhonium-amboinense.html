Typhonium amboinense

Typhonium amboinense, commonly known as the Amboina arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves with a rough, waxy texture. Its flowering stems produce clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are often overlooked.

Typhonium amboinense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
