Thunbergia hawtayneana

Thunbergia hawtayneana, commonly known as the Hawtayne's morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Thunbergia hawtayneana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
