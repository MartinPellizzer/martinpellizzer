Toona hexandra var. parviflora

Toona hexandra var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered toona, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape.
