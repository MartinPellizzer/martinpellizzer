Tetrastigma caudatum

Tetrastigma caudatum, commonly known as the long-tailed vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems that can reach several meters in length. Tetrastigma caudatum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
