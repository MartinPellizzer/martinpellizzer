Teucrium × costei

Teucrium × costei, commonly known as hybrid germander, is a perennial plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is a cross between Teucrium chamaedrys and Teucrium polium, resulting in a plant with hybrid characteristics. The species exhibits aromatic foliage with gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
