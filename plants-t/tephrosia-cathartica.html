Tephrosia cathartica

Tephrosia cathartica, commonly known as the sicklepod, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Tephrosia cathartica is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is also known for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
