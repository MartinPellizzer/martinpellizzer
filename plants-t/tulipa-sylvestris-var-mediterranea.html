Tulipa sylvestris var. mediterranea

Tulipa sylvestris var. mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean wild tulip, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It produces striking, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun. It is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in challenging growing conditions.
