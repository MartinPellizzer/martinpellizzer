Tradescantia rozynskii

Tradescantia rozynskii, commonly known as the Rozynski spiderwort, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features vibrant purple and red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
