Tillandsia staticiflora

Tillandsia staticiflora, commonly known as the static-flowered air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, which helps to collect and retain moisture. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
