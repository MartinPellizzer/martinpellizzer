Tutcheria kwangsiensis

Tutcheria kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi tutcheria, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
