Tsuga wardii

Tsuga wardii, commonly known as the Ward's hemlock, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the genus Tsuga, which includes other hemlock species, and is characterized by its conical shape and dense foliage. The leaves are needle-like, flat, and dark green, with a slightly twisted base. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of coniferous trees.

This species is often found in moist, well-drained soils in temperate forest ecosystems.
