Taxus canadensis var. floridana

Taxus canadensis var. floridana, commonly known as the Florida yew, is a rare and endangered plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a slow-growing evergreen shrub or small tree with dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the stems.
