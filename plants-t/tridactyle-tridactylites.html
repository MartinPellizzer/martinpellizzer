Tridactyle tridactylites

Tridactyle tridactylites, commonly known as the "three-toed tridactyl," is a type of fossilized plant that belongs to the group of ancient land plants.

This plant is primarily found in sedimentary rock layers from the Silurian and Devonian periods. Its morphology provides important insights into the evolution of early terrestrial plant life.
