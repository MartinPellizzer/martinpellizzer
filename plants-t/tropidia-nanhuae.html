Tropidia nanhuae

Tropidia nanhuae, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, linear leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pale green or yellowish flowers. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the forest floor.

Tropidia nanhuae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
