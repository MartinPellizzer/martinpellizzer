Tabernaemontana mollis

Tabernaemontana mollis, commonly known as the sweetshrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often aromatic and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

However, it is toxic to humans and animals, requiring caution in cultivation and handling.
