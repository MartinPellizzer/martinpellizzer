Trichilia tuberculata

Trichilia tuberculata, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a tropical deciduous tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, palmate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval-shaped fruits that contain a nutritious seed kernel. The seed kernel is widely used for its oil, which has significant economic and culinary value.

Additionally, the plant plays an important role in traditional medicine and local ecosystems.
