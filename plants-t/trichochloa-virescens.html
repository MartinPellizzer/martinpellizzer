Trichochloa virescens

Trichochloa virescens, commonly known as the green trichochloa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hair-like structures. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense spikes, and it produces small, round seeds.

Trichochloa virescens is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in well-drained soils.
