Taiwania yunnanensis

Taiwania yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan fir, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and conical shape. The tree produces cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural propagation. Its needles are typically dark green and arranged in spirals along the branches.

Taiwania yunnanensis is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in cool, moist forest environments.
