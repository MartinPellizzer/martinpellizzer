Trachyandra fimbriata

Trachyandra fimbriata, commonly known as the fimbriate grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to South Africa, characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, pale green to brown flowers. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a rough, textured surface.

Trachyandra fimbriata is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
