Thalictrum filamentosum var. watanabei

Thalictrum filamentosum var. watanabei, commonly known as the Watanabei meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wet meadows. Its flowers are arranged in panicles and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
