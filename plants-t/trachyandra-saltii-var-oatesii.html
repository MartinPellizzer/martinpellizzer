Trachyandra saltii var. oatesii

Trachyandra saltii var. oatesii, commonly known as the Oates' trachyandra, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
