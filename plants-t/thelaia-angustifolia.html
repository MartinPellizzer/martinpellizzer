Thelaia angustifolia

Thelaia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf thelaia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually in shades of pink or purple.

Thelaia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
