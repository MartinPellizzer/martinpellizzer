Tanacetopsis Krascheninnikovii

Krascheninnikov's tansy, scientifically known as Tanacetopsis krascheninnikovii, is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the cold regions of Central Asia. This member of the Asteraceae family is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in harsh environments, often found in rocky soils and alpine meadows where few other plants can survive. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, the species is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human activity. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical wonder for future generations.
