Toussaintia

Toussaintia, commonly known as the mountain dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seed heads that disperse easily.

Toussaintia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
