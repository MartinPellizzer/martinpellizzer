Tingulonga enneandra

Tingulonga enneandra, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Tingulonga enneandra thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
