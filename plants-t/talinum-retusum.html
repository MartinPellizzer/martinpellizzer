Talinum retusum

Talinum retusum, commonly known as purple leaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple leaves and showy pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has a sprawling or upright growth habit. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and the stems are often hairy.

Talinum retusum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and attractive flowers.
