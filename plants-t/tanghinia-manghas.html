Tanghinia manghas

Tanghinia manghas, commonly known as the Philippine fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The fronds are bi-directionally pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily identifiable in its natural habitat.
