Thlaspi lutescens f. versicolor

Thlaspi lutescens f. versicolor, commonly known as the variable yellow cabbages, is a botanical variant of the yellow cabbages species.

It is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and variable leaf coloration. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves can range from green to yellow or even reddish, depending on environmental conditions.
