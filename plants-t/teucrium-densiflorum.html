Teucrium densiflorum

Teucrium densiflorum, commonly known as blue germander, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy blue to purple flowers appear in dense clusters during the summer months.

Teucrium densiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
