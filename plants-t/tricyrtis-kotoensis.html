Tricyrtis kotoensis

Tricyrtis kotoensis, commonly known as the Koto spider lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, strap-shaped leaves and produces showy, white or pale pink blossoms with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Tricyrtis kotoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
