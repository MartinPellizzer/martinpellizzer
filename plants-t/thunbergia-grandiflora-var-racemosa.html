Thunbergia grandiflora var. racemosa

Thunbergia grandiflora var. racemosa, commonly known as the racemose thunbergia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows as a vine or shrub, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are borne in racemose inflorescences, which are elongated clusters that add to its ornamental appeal.

This species is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in both ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
