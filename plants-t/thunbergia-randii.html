Thunbergia randii

Thunbergia randii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral displays.

Thunbergia randii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
