Tithymalus cyatophorus

Tithymalus cyatophorus, commonly known as the yellow tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and greenish-yellow disk florets, which give it a striking appearance. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, this plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
