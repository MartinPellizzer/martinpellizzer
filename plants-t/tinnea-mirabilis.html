Tinnea mirabilis

Tinnea mirabilis, commonly known as the marvel tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, round fruits.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
