Telanthera argentea var. robustior

Telanthera argentea var. robustior, commonly known as the silverleaf telanthera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white powdery coating. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in open woodlands or near water sources.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
