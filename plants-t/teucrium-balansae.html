Teucrium balansae

Teucrium balansae, commonly known as Balansae germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or blue, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Teucrium balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
