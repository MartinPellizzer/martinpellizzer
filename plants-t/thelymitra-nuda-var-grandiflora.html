Thelymitra nuda var. grandiflora

Thelymitra nuda var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sun orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

The flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and are known for their fragrant, nectar-rich blooms that attract various pollinators.
