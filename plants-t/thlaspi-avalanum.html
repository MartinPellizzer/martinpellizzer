Thlaspi avalanum

Thlaspi avalanum, commonly known as the alpine cabbages or rock cabbages, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is typically found in alpine and rocky environments, thriving in well-drained soils at high elevations. Thlaspi avalanum is notable for its ability to tolerate harsh climatic conditions, including cold temperatures and strong winds.

Its presence is often indicative of a healthy, undisturbed alpine ecosystem.
