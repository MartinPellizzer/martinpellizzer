Tephrosia linearis var. discolor

Tephrosia linearis var. discolor, commonly known as the variable linear tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional herbal remedies.
