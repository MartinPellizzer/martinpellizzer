Tupistra fimbriata

Tupistra fimbriata, commonly known as the silver carpet plant, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and container gardening.
