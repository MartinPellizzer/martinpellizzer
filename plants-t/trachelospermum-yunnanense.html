Trachelospermum yunnanense

Trachelospermum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a vigorous climber with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its climbing habit makes it suitable for trellises, fences, or as a ground cover in shaded areas.
