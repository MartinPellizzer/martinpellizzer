Telinaria linifolia

Telinaria linifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves and bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Telinaria linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
