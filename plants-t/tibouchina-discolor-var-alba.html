Tibouchina discolor var. alba

Tibouchina discolor var. alba, commonly known as White-flowered Tibouchina, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green, providing a nice contrast to its pale blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various climates.
