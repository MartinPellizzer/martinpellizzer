Tithymalus luteola

Tithymalus luteola, commonly known as the yellow tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically daisy-like in appearance. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. It has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Tithymalus luteola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
