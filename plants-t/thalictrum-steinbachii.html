Thalictrum steinbachii

Thalictrum steinbachii, commonly known as the Steinbach's meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves that are finely divided. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in early summer. Thalictrum steinbachii prefers well-drained, moist soils and thrives in partial to full shade, making it a suitable addition to woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
