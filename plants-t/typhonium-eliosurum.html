Typhonium eliosurum

Typhonium eliosurum, commonly known as the sun orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that resembles an orchid. The plant features a dark green, heart-shaped leaf with a prominent midrib and a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
