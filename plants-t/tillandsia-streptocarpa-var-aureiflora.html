Tillandsia streptocarpa var. aureiflora

Tillandsia streptocarpa var. aureiflora, commonly known as the golden-flowered air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features dense, grass-like foliage that forms a rosette, and it produces striking yellow-orange flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
