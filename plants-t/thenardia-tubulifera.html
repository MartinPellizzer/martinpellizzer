Thenardia tubulifera

Thenardia tubulifera, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, herbaceous stems and small, yellowish flowers. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
