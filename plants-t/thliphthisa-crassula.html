Thliphthisa crassula

Thliphthisa crassula, commonly known as the dwarf jade plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Thliphthisa crassula is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
