Tabernaemontana riparia

Tabernaemontana riparia, commonly known as the river tabernaemontana, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies in its natural habitat.
