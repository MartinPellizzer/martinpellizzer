Tropaeolum glaucum

Tropaeolum glaucum, commonly known as the silverleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glaucous leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow and orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is a herbaceous annual that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Tropaeolum glaucum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible leaves and seeds.
