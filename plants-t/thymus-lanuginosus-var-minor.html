Thymus lanuginosus var. minor

Thymus lanuginosus var. minor, commonly known as the woolly thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic properties and ornamental value contribute to its widespread cultivation.
