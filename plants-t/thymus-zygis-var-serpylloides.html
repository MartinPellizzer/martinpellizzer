Thymus zygis var. serpylloides

Thymus zygis var. serpylloides, commonly known as slender thyme, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are typically gray-green in color and have a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces pink or purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in culinary and medicinal applications.
