Thuja orientalis f. aurea

Thuja orientalis f. aurea, commonly known as golden thuja, is a cultivar of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow foliage, which remains vibrant throughout the growing season. The plant has a compact, pyramidal shape and is often used as a ornamental hedge or specimen plant. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This evergreen shrub is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
