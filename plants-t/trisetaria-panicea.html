Trisetaria panicea

Trisetaria panicea, commonly known as the panicled trisetaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Trisetaria panicea is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
