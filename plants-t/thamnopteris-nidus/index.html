Thamnopteris Nidus

Thamnopteris nidus is a rare and ancient fern species found in specific wetland habitats. It is characterized by its distinctive fronds and unique reproductive structures. This plant plays a vital role in its ecosystem by supporting local biodiversity. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Protecting Thamnopteris nidus is essential for preserving ecological balance.
