Trichosanthes dioica var. sagittifolia

Trichosanthes dioica var. sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf trumpet vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and produces large, showy yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant is characterized by its fibrous, orange-colored roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist woodlands or along riverbanks.

The fruit is a long, cylindrical capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
