Tibouchina ornata

Tibouchina ornata, commonly known as the purple glorybower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring dense, glossy green leaves. The plant produces showy clusters of purple or pink tubular flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
