Tropaeolum leptophyllum

Tropaeolum leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender mirrorleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces strikingly colorful, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and a distinctive, peppery taste when the leaves are eaten. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Tropaeolum leptophyllum is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects and its ornamental appeal in both garden and container settings.
