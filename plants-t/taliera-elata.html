Taliera elata

Taliera elata, commonly known as the tall taliera, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Taliera elata thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

It is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
