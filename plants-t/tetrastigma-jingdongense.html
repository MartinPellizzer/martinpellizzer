Tetrastigma jingdongense

Tetrastigma jingdongense, commonly known as the Jingdong grapevine, is a species of flowering vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
