Trichosalpinx bradei

Trichosalpinx bradei, commonly known as the purple tailed orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple and violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as rainforests, where it thrives on the forest floor or on tree trunks. Its roots are often specialized for epiphytic growth, allowing it to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.
