Thelypteris rudis f. cristata

Thelypteris rudis f. cristata, commonly known as the cristate wood fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive fronds with a wavy, undulating edge.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the world. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The unique crinkled appearance of its fronds makes it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
