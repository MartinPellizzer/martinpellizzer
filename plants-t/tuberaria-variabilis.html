Tuberaria variabilis

Tuberaria variabilis, commonly known as the variable tuberous gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features tuberous roots, which are adapted for storing nutrients and water. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Tuberaria variabilis is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in sunny to partially shaded environments.
