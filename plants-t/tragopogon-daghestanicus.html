Tragopogon daghestanicus

Tragopogon daghestanicus, commonly known as the Daghestan goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region, particularly in the North Caucasus mountains.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy basal rosette. The plant features slender, hairy stems and yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of tubular disc florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Tragopogon daghestanicus is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
