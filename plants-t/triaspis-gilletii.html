Triaspis gilletii

Triaspis gilletii, commonly known as the Natal daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It is often found in coastal dune environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Triaspis gilletii is also known for its resilience to salt spray and coastal conditions.
