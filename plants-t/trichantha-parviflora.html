Trichantha parviflora

Trichantha parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered trichantha, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in early spring. Trichantha parviflora is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, where it contributes to the understory vegetation.
