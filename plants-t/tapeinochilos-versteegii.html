Tapeinochilos versteegii

Tapeinochilos versteegii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Tapeinochilos versteegii is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
