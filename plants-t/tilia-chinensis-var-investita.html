Tilia chinensis var. investita

Tilia chinensis var. investita, commonly known as the Chinese linden with white leaves, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often white or pale green on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
