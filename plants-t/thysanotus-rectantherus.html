Thysanotus rectantherus

Thysanotus rectantherus, commonly known as the slender bluebell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
