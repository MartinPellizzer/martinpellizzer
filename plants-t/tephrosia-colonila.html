Tephrosia colonila

Tephrosia colonila, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Tephrosia colonila is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
