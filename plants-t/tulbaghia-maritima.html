Tulbaghia maritima

Tulbaghia maritima, commonly known as the coastal tulbaghia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal and sandy environments. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Tulbaghia maritima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in rock gardens.
