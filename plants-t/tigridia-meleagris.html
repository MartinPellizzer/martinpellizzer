Tigridia meleagris

Tigridia meleagris, commonly known as the scarlet tiger iris, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces upright, branched stems that support its colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tigridia meleagris is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
