Thymus tonsilis

Thymus tonsilis, commonly known as narrowleaf thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and square stems. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly fragrant, often used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Thymus tonsilis thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
