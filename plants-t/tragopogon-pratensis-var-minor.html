Tragopogon pratensis var. minor

Tragopogon pratensis var. minor, commonly known as the lesser common goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in open habitats and is relatively uncommon in the wild.
