Tupa robusta

Tupa robusta, commonly known as the robust tupa, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stem and broad, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.

Tupa robusta is often found in humid, shaded environments within rainforests.
