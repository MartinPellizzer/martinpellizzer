Tibouchina aspera var. aspera

Tibouchina aspera var. aspera, commonly known as rough tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, textured surface. Its flowers are usually violet or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and blooms.
