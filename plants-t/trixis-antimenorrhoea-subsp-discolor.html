Trixis antimenorrhoea subsp. discolor

Trixis antimenorrhoea subsp.

discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered trixis, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale to white ray florets, which contrast with the darker central disk florets. It typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
