Tachiadenus platypterus subsp. platypterus

Tachiadenus platypterus subsp.

platypterus, commonly known as the flat-leaf tachiadenus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant features narrow, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment. Its small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the cooler months.
