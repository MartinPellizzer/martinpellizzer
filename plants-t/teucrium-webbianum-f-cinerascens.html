Teucrium webbianum f. cinerascens

Teucrium webbianum f. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens form of webbianum germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its silver-gray foliage.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic properties and square stems. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive grayish-white coloration due to dense pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This form is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its attractive appearance and resilience.
