Tigridia ehrenbergii subsp. flaviglandifera

Tigridia ehrenbergii subsp.

flaviglandifera, commonly known as the yellow-flowered tiger flower, is a member of the Iridaceae family. It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers with a distinctive red or orange throat.

The plant produces sword-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is a bulbous perennial that blooms in the summer months, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
