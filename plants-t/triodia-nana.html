Triodia nana

Triodia nana, commonly known as the slender spinifex, is a small, tufted grass found in arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is well adapted to harsh, dry environments. The plant has wiry, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss and withstand strong winds. It plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small desert creatures.

Triodia nana is often found in coastal and inland dunes, contributing to the ecological diversity of these ecosystems.
