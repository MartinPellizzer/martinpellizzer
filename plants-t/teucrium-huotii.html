Teucrium huotii

Teucrium huotii, commonly known as Huot's germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It has hairy, narrow leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
