Trambis

Trambis, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous underground stem. Trambis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
