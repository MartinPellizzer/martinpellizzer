Typhonium aungmyintwinii

Typhonium aungmyintwinii, commonly known as the Aung Myint Twinii Arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of the genus Typhonium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a vegetable in some regions.
