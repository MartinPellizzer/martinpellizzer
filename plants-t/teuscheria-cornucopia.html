Teuscheria cornucopia

Teuscheria cornucopia, commonly known as the horn of plenty plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble a horn or a bouquet. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. Its ornamental value comes from the vibrant hues of its foliage and the showy inflorescences.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
