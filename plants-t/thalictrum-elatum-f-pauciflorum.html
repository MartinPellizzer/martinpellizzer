Thalictrum elatum f. pauciflorum

Thalictrum elatum f. pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered tall meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in compound umbels, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture to woodland gardens.
