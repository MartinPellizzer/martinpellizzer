Tristachya vanderystii

Tristachya vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
