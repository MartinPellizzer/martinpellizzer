Tingulonga

Tingulonga, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and ability to bloom year-round.

Tingulonga is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ornamental appeal.
