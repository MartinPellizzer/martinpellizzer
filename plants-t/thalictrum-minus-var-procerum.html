Thalictrum minus var. procerum

Thalictrum minus var. procerum, commonly known as slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically alternate and finely divided. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
