Tricostularia compressa

Tricostularia compressa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed thyme or narrow-leafed thymus, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically narrow and slightly compressed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental and medicinal use.
