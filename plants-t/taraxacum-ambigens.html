Taraxacum ambigens

Taraxacum ambigens, commonly known as the ambiguous dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped basal leaves. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed habitats.

Taraxacum ambigens is a perennial herb that exhibits a rosette growth pattern during its early stages of development.
