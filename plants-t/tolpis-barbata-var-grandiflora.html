Tolpis barbata var. grandiflora

Tolpis barbata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered grassveld lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and striking, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces long, narrow leaves and has a fibrous root system. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color and are arranged in dense spikes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
