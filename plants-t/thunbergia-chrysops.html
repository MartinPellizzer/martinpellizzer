Thunbergia chrysops

Thunbergia chrysops, commonly known as the golden crown vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive crown-like pattern at the base. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a climbing plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Thunbergia chrysops is valued for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
