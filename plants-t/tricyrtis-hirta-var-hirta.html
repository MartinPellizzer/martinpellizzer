Tricyrtis hirta var. hirta

Tricyrtis hirta var. hirta, commonly known as the Japanese spider lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, spidery appearance. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Tricyrtis hirta var. hirta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also valued in traditional Japanese culture.
