Thilachium ovalifolium

Thilachium ovalifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed devil's-bit.

This plant is a member of the Ericaceae family and is native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are oval-shaped and have a leathery texture. The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and typically appear in shades of pink or white.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.
