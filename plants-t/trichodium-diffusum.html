Trichodium diffusum

Trichodium diffusum, commonly known as the spreading trichodium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.
