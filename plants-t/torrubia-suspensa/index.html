Torrubia Suspensa

Torrubia suspensa is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It is known for its unique, pendulous flowers that dangle from its stems like tiny bells. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this fascinating species for future generations.
