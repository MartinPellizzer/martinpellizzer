Trichera subscaposa var. macrotrycha

Trichera subscaposa var. macrotrycha, commonly known as the large-leafed trichera, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional herbal practices.
