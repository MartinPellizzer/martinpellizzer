Tacca parkeri var. lanceolata

Tacca parkeri var. lanceolata, commonly known as the black witch's umbrella, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, dark purple, umbrella-shaped inflorescence that resembles a witch's hat. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and organic soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
