Thunia × veitchii

Thunia × veitchii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Thunia fischeri and Thunia sanguinea.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features broad, grass-like leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
