Toona mollis

Toona mollis, commonly known as silky toon, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. Toona mollis is valued for its fast growth and is often used in reforestation efforts.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
