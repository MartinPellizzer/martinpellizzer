Trichosanthes patara

Trichosanthes patara, commonly known as the Chinese trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
