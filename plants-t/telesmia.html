Telesmia

Telesmia, commonly known as the "Australian rock orchid," is a genus of flowering plants native to Australia.

These plants are typically small, terrestrial orchids with a compact growth habit and a single, erect stem. Their flowers are often delicate and showy, with a variety of colors ranging from white to pink and purple. Telesmia species are found in diverse habitats, including rocky outcrops, sand dunes, and heathlands.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in gardens for their unique floral displays.
