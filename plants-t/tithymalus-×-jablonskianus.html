Tithymalus × jablonskianus

Tithymalus × jablonskianus, commonly known as the hybrid tithymalus, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in well-drained, moist soils in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.

Tithymalus × jablonskianus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
