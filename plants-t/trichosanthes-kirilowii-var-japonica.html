Trichosanthes kirilowii var. japonica

Trichosanthes kirilowii var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese snake gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a vine that produces elongated, warty fruits with a distinctive greenish-yellow color. The plant is native to East Asia and is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and cooking in its native regions.
