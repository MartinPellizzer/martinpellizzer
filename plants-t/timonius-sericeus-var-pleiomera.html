Timonius sericeus var. pleiomera

Timonius sericeus var. pleiomera, commonly known as the silky timonius, is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
