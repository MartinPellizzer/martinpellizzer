Toona Sureni Var. Celebica

Toona sureni var. celebica is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the mulberry family, known for its ecological and medicinal significance. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and growth habits compared to its parent species. Despite its potential, it remains under-researched and overlooked in botanical studies.
