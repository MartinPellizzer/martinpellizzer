Tyleria spathulata

Tyleria spathulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and opposite in arrangement. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Tyleria spathulata is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
