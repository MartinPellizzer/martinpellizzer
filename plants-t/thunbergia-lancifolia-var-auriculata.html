Thunbergia lancifolia var. auriculata

Thunbergia lancifolia var. auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant is a climbing vine with opposite, ovate leaves and produces showy blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
