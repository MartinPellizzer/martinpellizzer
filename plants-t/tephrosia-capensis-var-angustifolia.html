Tephrosia capensis var. angustifolia

Tephrosia capensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in some regions for its medicinal properties.
