Tabernaemontana amygdalifolia

Tabernaemontana amygdalifolia, commonly known as the fragrant star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are highly fragrant. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
