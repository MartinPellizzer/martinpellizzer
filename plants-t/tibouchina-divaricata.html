Tibouchina divaricata

Tibouchina divaricata, commonly known as the flame vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces vibrant clusters of pink to red tubular flowers, which are highly attractive to birds and pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom throughout the year, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
