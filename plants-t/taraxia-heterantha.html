Taraxia heterantha

Taraxia heterantha, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features a rosette of narrow, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single head and bloom in the summer months.

Taraxia heterantha is characterized by its distinctive, spiny bracts that surround the flower head, giving it a unique appearance.
