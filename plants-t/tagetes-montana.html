Tagetes montana

Tagetes montana, commonly known as the Mexican marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow blooms, which are typically tubular and arranged in clusters. The plant is herbaceous, with upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Tagetes montana is also known for its strong, aromatic scent and its ability to repel certain pests.
