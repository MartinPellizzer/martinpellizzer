Tillandsia × correalei

Tillandsia × correalei, commonly known as the silver star tillandsia, is a hybrid air plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves. It produces striking white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value to its appearance.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
