Tetraplasandra kavaiensis var. koloana

Tetraplasandra kavaiensis var. koloana, commonly known as the Koloana tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, producing a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its natural propagation in its native habitat.
