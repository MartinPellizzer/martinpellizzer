Tulisma

Tulisma, commonly known as the "flower of the dead," is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the genus Tagetes, which includes various marigold species. The plant is characterized by its vibrant orange and red flowers, which have a strong, pungent aroma. Tulisma is often associated with spiritual and ceremonial practices, particularly in pre-Hispanic cultures.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and culturally significant.
