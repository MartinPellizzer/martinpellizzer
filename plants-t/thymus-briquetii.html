Thymus briquetii

Thymus briquetii, commonly known as Briquet's thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often used in culinary applications, similar to other thyme species.

Thymus briquetii is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
