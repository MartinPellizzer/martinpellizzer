Thouinia australis

Thouinia australis, commonly known as the southern thouinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in dense tufts.

Thouinia australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
