Thibaudia axillaris

Thibaudia axillaris, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white.

Thibaudia axillaris is well-suited for indoor cultivation and is popular for its ornamental value and ease of care.
