Thermopsis mollis var. fraxinifolia

Thermopsis mollis var. fraxinifolia, commonly known as yellow coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces bright yellow, cone-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly fuzzy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.
