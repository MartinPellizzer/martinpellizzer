Tara

Tara, commonly known as Caesalpinia spinosa, is a leguminous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Tara is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings.

Its seeds are also used in traditional medicine and as a source of dietary fiber.
