Torenia asiatica var. concolor

Torenia asiatica var. concolor, commonly known as the narrow-leaved torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pale purple to white in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
