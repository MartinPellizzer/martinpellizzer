Taraxacum atrosubtile

Taraxacum atrosubtile, commonly known as the dark purple dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish flowers, which contrast with its green, heart-shaped leaves. This plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It produces a milky sap that is typical of the genus Taraxacum.

The plant is not widely cultivated but is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its unique coloration and ecological role.
