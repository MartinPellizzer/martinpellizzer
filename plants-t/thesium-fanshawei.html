Thesium fanshawei

Thesium fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, evergreen leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation efforts.
