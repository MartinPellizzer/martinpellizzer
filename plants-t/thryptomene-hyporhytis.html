Thryptomene hyporhytis

Thryptomene hyporhytis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf thryptomene, is a small shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

Thryptomene hyporhytis is well adapted to sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.
