Tragacantha Chamissonis

Tragacantha chamissonis is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. It is known for its striking yellow blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in rocky soils and is adapted to extreme drought conditions. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its modest size and habitat. Its ecological importance supports a variety of local wildlife and pollinators.
