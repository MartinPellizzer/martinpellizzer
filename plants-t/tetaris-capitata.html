Tetaris capitata

Tetaris capitata, commonly known as the red-veined daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and pinkish-red petals. The plant produces small, green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Tetaris capitata is also known for its long-lasting blooms, which can persist throughout the growing season.
