Thouinidium oblongum

Thouinidium oblongum, commonly known as the oblong waxy leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and container gardening.
