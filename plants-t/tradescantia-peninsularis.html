Tradescantia peninsularis

Tradescantia peninsularis, commonly known as the silver sword or zigzag plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Caribbean.

It features striking silver-green foliage with a waxy texture, and its leaves are often adorned with purple or pink veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
