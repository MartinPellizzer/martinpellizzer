Trichochloa foliosa

Trichochloa foliosa, commonly known as the silky grass.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

Trichochloa foliosa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
