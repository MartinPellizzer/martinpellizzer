Trichocentrum hoegei

Trichocentrum hoegei, commonly known as the Hoffmann's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs and produces numerous, pendant inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Trichocentrum hoegei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under appropriate conditions.
