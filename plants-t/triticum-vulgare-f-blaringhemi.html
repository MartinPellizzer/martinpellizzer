Triticum vulgare f. blaringhemi

Triticum vulgare f. blaringhemi, commonly known as the Blaringhem wheat, is a botanical variant of common wheat.

It is characterized by its distinct morphological features, including a unique glume structure and spike morphology. This form is primarily found in specific geographic regions, often in association with particular soil and climatic conditions. The plant exhibits traits that may be of interest in agricultural research due to its potential for genetic diversity.
