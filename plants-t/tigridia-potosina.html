Tigridia potosina

Tigridia potosina, commonly known as the Mexican fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped blooms that appear in early summer. It has linear, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Tigridia potosina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
