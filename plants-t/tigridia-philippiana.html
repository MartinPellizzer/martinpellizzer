Tigridia philippiana

Tigridia philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine tiger flower, is a bulbous plant native to the Philippines.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange with a distinctive black or dark purple center. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces multiple blooms on tall, sturdy stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Tigridia philippiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
