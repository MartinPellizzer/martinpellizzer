Trichosanthes pierrei

Trichosanthes pierrei, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a climbing vine native to China.

It produces large, elongated fruits that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is cultivated for its edible fruits, which have a mild, slightly sweet flavor.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
