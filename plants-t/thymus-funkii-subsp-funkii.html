Thymus funkii subsp. funkii

Thymus funkii subsp.

funkii, commonly known as Funk's thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green foliage.

The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated for horticultural and herbal purposes.
