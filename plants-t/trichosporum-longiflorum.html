Trichosporum longiflorum

Trichosporum longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered trichosporum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Trichosporum longiflorum is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
