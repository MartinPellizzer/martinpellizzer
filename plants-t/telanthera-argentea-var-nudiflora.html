Telanthera argentea var. nudiflora

Telanthera argentea var. nudiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf telanthera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique leaf color and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
