Thalictrum ussuriense

Thalictrum ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a taproot system and is often found in temperate forest regions.

Thalictrum ussuriense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
