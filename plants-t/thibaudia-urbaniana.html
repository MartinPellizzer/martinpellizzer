Thibaudia urbaniana

Thibaudia urbaniana, commonly known as the red-flowered thibaudia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Thibaudia urbaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
