Tunica prolifera var. glabricaulis

Tunica prolifera var. glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed tunica, is a rare and specialized plant species characterized by its unique botanical features.

The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology, with smooth, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a thin layer of waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its leaves are usually reduced to small, scale-like structures, further minimizing surface area for evaporation.
