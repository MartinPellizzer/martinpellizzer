Taraxacum glossodon

Taraxacum glossodon, commonly known as the glossy dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and a basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. Its scientific name reflects the distinctive glossy appearance of its leaves.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and its role in supporting pollinators.
