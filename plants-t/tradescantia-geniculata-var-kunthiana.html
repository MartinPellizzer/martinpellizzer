Tradescantia geniculata var. kunthiana

Tradescantia geniculata var. kunthiana, commonly known as the zigzag spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers and its distinctive, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
