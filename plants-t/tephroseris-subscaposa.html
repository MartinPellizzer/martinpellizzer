Tephroseris subscaposa

Tephroseris subscaposa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tephroseris, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring.
