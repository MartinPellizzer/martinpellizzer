Thlaspi cenisium

Thlaspi cenisium, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lobed, and often covered in a waxy coating.
