Thysanocarpus deppii

Thysanocarpus deppii, commonly known as the Chinese sandmat, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on slender stems. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Thysanocarpus deppii is notable for its ability to thrive in arid environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
