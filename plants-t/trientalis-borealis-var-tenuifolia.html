Trientalis borealis var. tenuifolia

Trientalis borealis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender starflower, is a perennial herb native to northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its low-growing habit and distinctive star-shaped white flowers.
