Thespesia populnea var. populneoides

Thespesia populnea var. populneoides, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and dry forests. The plant features showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The species is notable for its ecological importance and is often used in traditional Hawaiian practices.
