Trichosporum dasycalyx

Trichosporum dasycalyx, commonly known as the hairy calyx trichosporum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy calyx that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered with fine pubescence.

Trichosporum dasycalyx is primarily valued for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.
