Tinus palembanica

Tinus palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang tinus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Arecaceae, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves. Tinus palembanica thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.
