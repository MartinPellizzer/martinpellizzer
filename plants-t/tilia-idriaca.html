Tilia idriaca

Tilia idriaca, commonly known as the Idrija lime or Idrija linden, is a deciduous tree native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain one or two seeds. Its bark is smooth and greyish, and it is often found in moist, temperate forests.

Tilia idriaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in traditional medicine.
