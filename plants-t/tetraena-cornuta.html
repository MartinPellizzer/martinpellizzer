Tetraena cornuta

Tetraena cornuta, commonly known as the horned tetraena, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Tetraena cornuta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
