Tropaeolum lepidum var. lepidum

Tropaeolum lepidum var. lepidum, commonly known as the ornamental nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are also edible and have a peppery flavor. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it both ornamental and useful in companion planting.
