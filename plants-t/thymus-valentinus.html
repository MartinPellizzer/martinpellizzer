Thymus valentinus

Thymus valentinus, commonly known as Valentine thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to violet, blooming in spring and early summer. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both ornamental and herbal gardens.
