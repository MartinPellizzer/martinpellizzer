Tsuga Canadensis F. Jenkinsii

Tsuga canadensis f. jenkinsii is a rare variant of the eastern hemlock, known for its unique foliage and ecological significance. This form exhibits a more open canopy and a distinctive branching pattern compared to the typical hemlock. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for conservation efforts. Its study provides insight into genetic diversity and adaptation within the Tsuga genus.
