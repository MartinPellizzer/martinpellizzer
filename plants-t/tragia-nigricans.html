Tragia nigricans

Tragia nigricans, commonly known as black tragia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or subshrub characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Tragia nigricans is adapted to withstand drought and has a widespread distribution across the region.
