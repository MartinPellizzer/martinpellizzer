Tropaeolum majus var. plenum

Tropaeolum majus var. plenum, commonly known as the "Chinese violet" or "scarlet runner," is a vibrant annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Its stems are succulent and can grow up to several meters long, making it ideal for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Tropaeolum majus var. plenum is also known for its edible tubers, which have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.
