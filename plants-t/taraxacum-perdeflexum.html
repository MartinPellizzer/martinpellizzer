Taraxacum perdeflexum

Taraxacum perdeflexum, commonly known as the bentleaf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves that are often curled or folded, giving it a distinctive appearance. Taraxacum perdeflexum is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
