Trifolium medium var. pseudomedium

Trifolium medium var. pseudomedium, commonly known as red clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

This variety is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agriculture and ecological restoration.
