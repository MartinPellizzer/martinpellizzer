Torenia peduncularis

Torenia peduncularis, commonly known as the hanging bell flower, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia peduncularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
