Typhonium kerrii

Typhonium kerrii, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or red spadices surrounded by white or green bracts. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or near streams.

Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
