Tanacetum gregorjewi

Tanacetum gregorjewi, commonly known as the Gregorjew's tansy, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

Tanacetum gregorjewi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
