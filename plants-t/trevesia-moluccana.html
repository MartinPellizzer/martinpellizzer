Trevesia moluccana

Trevesia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan trevesia, is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas and other parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring compound leaves with a pinnate structure. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom during the wet season. Its fruits are capsules that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Trevesia moluccana is often found in humid, tropical forests and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
