Trigonotis macrophylla var. trichocarpa

Trigonotis macrophylla var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the silverleaf trigonotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves, which are broad and covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
