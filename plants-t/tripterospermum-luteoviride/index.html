Tripterospermum Luteoviride

Tripterospermum luteoviride is a rare and striking plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant yellow-green foliage and unique, star-shaped flowers. This species belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its delicate appearance makes it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and private collections. Due to its limited distribution, conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival.
