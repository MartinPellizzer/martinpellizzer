Thelymitra arenaria

Thelymitra arenaria, commonly known as the sandy terrestrials, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, terrestrial growth habit and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces pale green to white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
