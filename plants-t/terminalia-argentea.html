Terminalia argentea

Terminalia argentea, commonly known as silver terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, silvery bark that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, orange-red drupes. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Terminalia argentea is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local medicine.
