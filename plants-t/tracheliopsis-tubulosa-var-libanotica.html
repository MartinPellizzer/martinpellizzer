Tracheliopsis tubulosa var. libanotica

Tracheliopsis tubulosa var. libanotica, commonly known as the Lebanon thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. The plant has aromatic, narrow leaves that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
