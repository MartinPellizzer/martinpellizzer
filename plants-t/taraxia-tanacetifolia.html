Taraxia tanacetifolia

Taraxia tanacetifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Taraxia tanacetifolia is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
