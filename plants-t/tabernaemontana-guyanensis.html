Tabernaemontana guyanensis

Tabernaemontana guyanensis, commonly known as the Guyana star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its blooming period typically occurs during the summer months, adding a pleasant aroma to the environment.
