Taenianthera oligosticha

Taenianthera oligosticha, commonly known as the slender leaved twayblade, is a small, herbaceous orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, well-drained soils. The plant features delicate, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique, sympodial growth habit.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
