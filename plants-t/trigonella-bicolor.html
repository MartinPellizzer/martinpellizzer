Trigonella bicolor

Trigonella bicolor, commonly known as the yellow fenugreek, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are characteristic of the Fabaceae family. The plant produces seed pods that split open to release its tiny, lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or rocky habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various health purposes.
