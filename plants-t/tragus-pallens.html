Tragus pallens

Tragus pallens, commonly known as the pale tragus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, erect stature and pale green foliage. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Tragus pallens is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
