Thunbergia jayii

Thunbergia jayii, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative vine.

Thunbergia jayii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.
