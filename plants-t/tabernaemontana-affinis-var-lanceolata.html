Tabernaemontana affinis var. lanceolata

Tabernaemontana affinis var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically lanceolate, hence the variety name, and are arranged oppositely along the stems. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
