Tupistra grandistigma

Tupistra grandistigma, commonly known as the large-flowered ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
