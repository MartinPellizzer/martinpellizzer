Tagetes erecta f. pleniflora

Tagetes erecta f. pleniflora, commonly known as the African marigold, is a cultivar of the marigold plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features upright stems and produces large, brightly colored blooms in shades of orange and yellow. Its foliage is usually green with a slightly rough texture.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in warm climates.
