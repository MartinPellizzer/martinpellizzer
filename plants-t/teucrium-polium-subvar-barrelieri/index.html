Teucrium Polium Subvar. Barrelieri

Barrelier's germander, scientifically known as Teucrium polium subvar. barrelieri, is a lesser-known but ecologically significant plant native to the Mediterranean region. This perennial herb belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant's small, fragrant flowers and distinctive foliage make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens. Despite its beauty and resilience, Barrelier's germander remains underappreciated in modern horticulture.
