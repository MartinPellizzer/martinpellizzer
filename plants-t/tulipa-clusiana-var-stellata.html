Tulipa clusiana var. stellata

Tulipa clusiana var. stellata, commonly known as the star tulip, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to Turkey.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers with petals that radiate outward, giving the appearance of a star. The plant produces vibrant, often multi-colored blooms in shades of red, yellow, and orange, typically in late spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
