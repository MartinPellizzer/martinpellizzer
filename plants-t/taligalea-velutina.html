Taligalea velutina

Taligalea velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf taligalea, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Taligalea velutina is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
