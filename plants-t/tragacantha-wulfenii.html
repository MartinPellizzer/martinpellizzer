Tragacantha wulfenii

Tragacantha wulfenii, commonly known as the Wulfen's tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits.

Tragacantha wulfenii is notable for its ability to thrive in arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
