Tropaeolum brachyceras var. genuinum

Tropaeolum brachyceras var. genuinum, commonly known as the dwarf Indian cress, is a compact, herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with four petals. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens or as a companion plant in vegetable beds.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread via seeds make it a versatile choice for ground cover or container gardening.
