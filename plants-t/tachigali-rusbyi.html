Tachigali rusbyi

Tachigali rusbyi, commonly known as the yellow tachigali, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
