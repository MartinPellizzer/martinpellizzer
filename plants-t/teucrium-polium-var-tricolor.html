Teucrium polium var. tricolor

Teucrium polium var. tricolor, commonly known as three-color germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and small, three-colored flowers that range from blue to purple and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged flowering period and drought tolerance.
