Telipogon mendiolae

Telipogon mendiolae, commonly known as the Mendola orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short rhizome and a single, upright stem. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to the cold and windy conditions of its habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and specialized ecological requirements, Telipogon mendiolae is considered a vulnerable species.
