Taeniophyllum kenejianum

Taeniophyllum kenejianum, commonly known as the silver moss, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

Taeniophyllum kenejianum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
