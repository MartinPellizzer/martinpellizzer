Tropaeolum cavendishii

Tropaeolum cavendishii, commonly known as the golden crown flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family, characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. It is typically grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Tropaeolum cavendishii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
