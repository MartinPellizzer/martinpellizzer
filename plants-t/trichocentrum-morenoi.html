Trichocentrum morenoi

Trichocentrum morenoi, commonly known as the Moreno's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs that support its evergreen foliage. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive, spurred lip that aids in pollination.

Trichocentrum morenoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
