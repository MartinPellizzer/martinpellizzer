Thermopsis montana

Thermopsis montana, commonly known as yellow larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or open habitats. The plant features showy, blue to yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a distinctive hairy texture.

Thermopsis montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
