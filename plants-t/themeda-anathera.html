Themeda anathera

Themeda anathera, commonly known as spear grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and heathlands.

Themeda anathera plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
