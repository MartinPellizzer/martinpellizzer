Tradescantia ionantha

Tradescantia ionantha, commonly known as the "Purple Heart" plant, is a species of flowering plant in the spiderwort family.

It is native to the southeastern United States and is characterized by its vibrant purple to red foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it both visually appealing and relatively easy to care for.
