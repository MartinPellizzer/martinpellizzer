Trollius rebunensis

Trollius rebunensis, commonly known as the Reun Island buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Trollius in the family Asteraceae, characterized by its showy, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of numerous ray florets.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
