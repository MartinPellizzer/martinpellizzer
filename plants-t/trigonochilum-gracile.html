Trigonochilum gracile

Trigonochilum gracile, commonly known as the slender trigonochilum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, greenish-white flowers with three sepals and three petals. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for absorbing moisture from the soil.

Trigonochilum gracile is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in forest understories.
