Tephrosia stormsii

Tephrosia stormsii, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its striking blue flowers, which bloom in clusters during the dry season. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for erosion control in degraded soils.
