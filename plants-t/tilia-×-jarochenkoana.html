Tilia × jarochenkoana

Tilia × jarochenkoana, commonly known as the Jarochenkoan lime tree, is a hybrid species within the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically broad and have a serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
