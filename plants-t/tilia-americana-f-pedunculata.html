Tilia americana f. pedunculata

Tilia americana f. pedunculata, commonly known as the American linden with pedunculate flowers, is a botanical variant of the American linden tree.

It is characterized by its elongated pedicels that support its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It produces heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5-10 cm in length.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its showy floral displays.
