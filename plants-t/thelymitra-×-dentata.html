Thelymitra × dentata

Thelymitra × dentata, commonly known as the toothed sun-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers with a distinctive toothed lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its morphology includes strap-like leaves and a compact, erect growth habit.

Thelymitra × dentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
