Thalictrum crossaeum

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. Thalictrum crossaeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
