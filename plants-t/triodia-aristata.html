Triodia aristata

Triodia aristata, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth and distinctive spines, which give it a rugged appearance. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often forming extensive vegetation patches in sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with the inflorescence consisting of spikelets that bear small, spike-like flowers.

Triodia aristata plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various desert organisms.
