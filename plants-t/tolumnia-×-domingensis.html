Tolumnia × domingensis

Tolumnia × domingensis, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Tolumnia hibiscifolia and Tolumnia domingensis var. grandiflora, characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a glossy surface, and it produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in summer.

Tolumnia × domingensis is native to the Caribbean and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
