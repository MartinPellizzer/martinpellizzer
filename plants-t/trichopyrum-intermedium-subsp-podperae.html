Trichopyrum intermedium subsp. podperae

Trichopyrum intermedium subsp.

podperae, commonly known as the European rush, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, ditches, and along riverbanks.

The plant has cylindrical, grass-like stems that are usually light green to brown in color. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a dense, cylindrical cluster.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib and a fibrous texture.
