Tithonia

Tithonia is a genus of flowering plants known for its vibrant orange and yellow blooms. It is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world. These plants are often used as ornamental plants due to their striking appearance. Tithonia species are also valued for their ability to improve soil fertility. Their unique characteristics make them an important part of both ecological and agricultural systems.
