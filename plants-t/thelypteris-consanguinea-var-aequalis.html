Thelypteris consanguinea var. aequalis

Thelypteris consanguinea var. aequalis, commonly known as the equal fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed water fern.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae, characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or wetlands, where it thrives in high humidity.
