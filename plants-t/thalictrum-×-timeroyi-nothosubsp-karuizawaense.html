Thalictrum × timeroyi nothosubsp.

karuizawaense, commonly known as the Karuizawa meadow rue, is a hybrid plant in the Ranunculaceae family. It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like compound leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It is native to Japan, specifically the region around Karuizawa.

Its ornamental value lies in its graceful appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
