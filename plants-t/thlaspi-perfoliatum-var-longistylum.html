Thlaspi perfoliatum var. longistylum

Thlaspi perfoliatum var. longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed pennycress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits.
