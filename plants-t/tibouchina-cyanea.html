Tibouchina cyanea

Tibouchina cyanea, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, showy clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and tropical regions.

Tibouchina cyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping and gardens.
