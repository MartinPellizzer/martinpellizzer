Thrincia pilosiuscula

Thrincia pilosiuscula, commonly known as the woolly thrincia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, pale green leaves covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and appear in clusters during the warmer months.

Thrincia pilosiuscula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
