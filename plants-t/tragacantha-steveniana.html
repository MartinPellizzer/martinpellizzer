Tragacantha steveniana

Tragacantha steveniana, commonly known as the Steven's tragacanth, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Tragacantha steveniana is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
