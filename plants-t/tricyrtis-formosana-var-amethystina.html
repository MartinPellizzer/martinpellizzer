Tricyrtis formosana var. amethystina

Tricyrtis formosana var. amethystina, commonly known as the Amethyst Indian blanket, is a striking perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep amethyst, blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike.
