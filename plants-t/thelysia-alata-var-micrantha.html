Thelysia alata var. micrantha

Thelysia alata var. micrantha, commonly known as the slender thistle or narrow-leaved thistle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
