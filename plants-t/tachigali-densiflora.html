Tachigali densiflora

Tachigali densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered tachigali, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and often nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with an odd number of leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or white, arranged in dense clusters. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Tachigali densiflora is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role.
