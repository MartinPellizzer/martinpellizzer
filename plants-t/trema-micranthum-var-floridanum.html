Trema micranthum var. floridanum

Trema micranthum var. floridanum, commonly known as the Florida trema, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in wetlands and along the edges of water bodies. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

It plays a role in providing habitat and food for various wildlife species in its native habitat.
