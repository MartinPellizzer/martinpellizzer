Tetragonia nigrescens

Tetragonia nigrescens, commonly known as New Zealand spinach, is a leafy green plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, edible leaves that are rich in nutrients. The plant thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a mild, spinach-like flavor.

Tetragonia nigrescens is often cultivated as a heat-tolerant alternative to traditional spinach.
