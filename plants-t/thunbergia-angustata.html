Thunbergia angustata

Thunbergia angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, violet, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia angustata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
