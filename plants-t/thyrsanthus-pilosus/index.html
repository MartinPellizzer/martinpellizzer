Thyrsanthus Pilosus

Thyrsanthus pilosus is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of New Zealand. Known for its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers, this plant is a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to coastal gardens. Its unique appearance and ecological importance have made it a subject of interest in conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Thyrsanthus pilosus remains relatively rare in cultivation outside its natural habitat.
