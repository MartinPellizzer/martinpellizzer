Tamarix plumosa

Tamarix plumosa, commonly known as the pink tamarisk, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its feathery, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are adapted to arid environments, reducing water loss. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in sandy or saline soils.

Tamarix plumosa is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.
