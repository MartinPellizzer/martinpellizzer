Tradescantia discolor var. concolor

Tradescantia discolor var. concolor, commonly known as the silver sword or variegated spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with strap-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery or white midrib. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
