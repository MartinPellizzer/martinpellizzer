Trifolium pratense var. sericeum

Trifolium pratense var. sericeum, commonly known as silky red clover, is a variant of red clover characterized by its soft, silken leaves.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Silky red clover is valued for its ornamental appeal and as a forage crop for livestock.
