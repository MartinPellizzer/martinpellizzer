Teucrium manghuaense var. angustum

Teucrium manghuaense var. angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved germander, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, narrow leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

This variant is distinguished by its narrower foliage compared to other varieties within the Teucrium genus.
