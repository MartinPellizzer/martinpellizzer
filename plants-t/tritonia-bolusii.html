Tritonia bolusii

Tritonia bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's tritonia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Tritonia bolusii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
