Trixis ophiorhiza

Trixis ophiorhiza, commonly known as the coastal trixis, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that thrives in sandy and saline soils, often found in dune environments. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and preventing erosion.

Its adaptability to harsh coastal conditions makes it an important component of the local ecosystem.
