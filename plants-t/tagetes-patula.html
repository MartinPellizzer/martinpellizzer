Tagetes patula

Tagetes patula, commonly known as the dwarf marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which bloom throughout the summer. The plant is an annual herb with a compact growth habit, making it suitable for garden borders and containers. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while its stems are slender and branching.

Tagetes patula is also known for its strong, aromatic scent, which can repel certain insects.
