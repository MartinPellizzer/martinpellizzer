Turraea billardierei

Turraea billardierei, commonly known as the silky tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
