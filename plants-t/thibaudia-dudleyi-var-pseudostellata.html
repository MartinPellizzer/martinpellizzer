Thibaudia dudleyi var. pseudostellata

Thibaudia dudleyi var. pseudostellata, commonly known as the pink thibaudia, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple bracts that resemble petals, giving it a dramatic floral display. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
