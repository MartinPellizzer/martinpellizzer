Trisetum flavescens f. scabriusculum

Trisetum flavescens f. scabriusculum, commonly known as the scabrous false brome, is a variant of the false brome grass.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its rough, scabrous texture on its leaves and stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as roadsides and meadows. It has narrow, grass-like leaves with a yellowish-green coloration.

This form is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
