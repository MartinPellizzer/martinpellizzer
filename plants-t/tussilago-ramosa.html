Tussilago ramosa

Tussilago ramosa, commonly known as the purple tansy, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow flowers that are surrounded by purple bracts, giving it a striking appearance. Tussilago ramosa typically grows in moist, grassy areas and has a creeping growth habit.

It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
