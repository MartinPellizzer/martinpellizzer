Tetragonolobus purpureus

Tetragonolobus purpureus, commonly known as the purple burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tetrahedral seed pods, which are a distinctive feature of the species. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Tetragonolobus purpureus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
