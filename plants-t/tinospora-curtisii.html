Tinospora curtisii

Tinospora curtisii, commonly known as Indian moonseed, is a woody climbing vine native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and pharmacological studies.
