Trichilia acariaeantha

Trichilia acariaeantha, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape.
