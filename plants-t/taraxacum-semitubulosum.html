Taraxacum semitubulosum

Taraxacum semitubulosum, commonly known as the slender dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy white seed heads. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Taraxacum semitubulosum is a perennial plant that thrives in temperate climates.
