Tulipa × tschimganica

Tulipa × tschimganica, commonly known as the Tschimganica tulip, is a hybrid species within the genus Tulipa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink and white. The plant is native to Central Asia and is known for its robust growth and relatively hardy nature. It produces showy, cup-shaped blooms that are often adorned with contrasting markings.

This tulip is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
