Tylophora lanyuensis

Tylophora lanyuensis, commonly known as the Lanyu tylophora, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are follicles that release seeds with tufts of hair, aiding in wind dispersal.

Tylophora lanyuensis is often found in forested areas and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
