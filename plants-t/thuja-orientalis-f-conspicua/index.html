Thuja Orientalis F. Conspicua

Thuja orientalis f. conspicua is a striking variant of the eastern arborvitae known for its dense, conical shape and vibrant foliage. This ornamental plant is prized for its ability to thrive in a variety of climates and its adaptability to different soil conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden design. The cultivar's compact growth habit also makes it ideal for use as a hedge or topiary. With its year-round visual appeal, Thuja orientalis f. conspicua remains a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts alike.
