Thlaspi graecum subsp. creticum

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers.

creticum is often used in rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
