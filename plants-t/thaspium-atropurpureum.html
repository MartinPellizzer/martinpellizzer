Thaspium atropurpureum

Thaspium atropurpureum, commonly known as purple thaspium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered umbels of white to pale purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and alternate, often with a slightly hairy surface. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils.
