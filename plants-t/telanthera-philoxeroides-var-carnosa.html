Telanthera philoxeroides var. carnosa

Telanthera philoxeroides var. carnosa, commonly known as the pink-flowered telanthera, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, creeping stems that can spread rapidly, often forming dense mats. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.
