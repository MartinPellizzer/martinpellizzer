Trigonia nivea var. pubescens

Trigonia nivea var. pubescens, commonly known as the white-veined fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its elegant, feathery fronds.

Its leaves are typically pale green with prominent white veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spore-bearing structures.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ease of care in suitable conditions.
