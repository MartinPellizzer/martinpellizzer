Teucrium thymifolium subsp. thymifolium

Teucrium thymifolium subsp.

thymifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been historically valued for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
