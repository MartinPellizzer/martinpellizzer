Thamnocalamus dracocephalus

Thamnocalamus dracocephalus, commonly known as the dragon-head bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Thailand. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and wetlands.
