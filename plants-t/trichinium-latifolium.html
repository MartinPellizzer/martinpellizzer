Trichinium latifolium

Trichinium latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf trichinium, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique foliage and floral structure, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
