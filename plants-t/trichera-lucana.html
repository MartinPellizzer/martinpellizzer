Trichera lucana

Trichera lucana, commonly known as the lucan trichera, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers with central disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. Native to specific regions in southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and arid environments.

Trichera lucana is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
