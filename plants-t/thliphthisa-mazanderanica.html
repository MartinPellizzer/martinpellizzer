Thliphthisa mazanderanica

Thliphthisa mazanderanica, commonly known as the Persian sandthorn, is a shrub native to Iran.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, orange fruits that contain seeds.

Thliphthisa mazanderanica is often found in rocky and sandy soils, thriving in dry, sunny environments.
