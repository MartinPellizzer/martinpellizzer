Teesdalia iberis var. hirsuta

Teesdalia iberis var. hirsuta, commonly known as hairy honesty, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has hairy, branching stems and produces small, round seed pods that turn red as they mature. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and unique seed pods.
