Typha minima f. nana

Typha minima f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf cattail, is a compact variant of the common cattail.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetlands and shallow water environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, cylindrical flower spikes. Its small size makes it suitable for small ponds or water gardens.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic life.
