Trachelospermum cathayanum var. longipedicellatum

Trachelospermum cathayanum var. longipedicellatum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to southern China.

It features fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for climbing structures and garden borders.
