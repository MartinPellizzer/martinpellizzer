Thelypteris semirii

Thelypteris semirii, commonly known as the Semirii holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. Its rhizomes are wiry and creeping, allowing it to spread along the forest floor in shaded, humid environments.

Thelypteris semirii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady garden settings.
