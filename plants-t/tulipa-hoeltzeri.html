Tulipa hoeltzeri

Tulipa hoeltzeri, commonly known as the Hoeltzer's tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is a bulbous perennial plant that produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and blooms in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

This tulip is valued for its vibrant colors and is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
