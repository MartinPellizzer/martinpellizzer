Trifolium bonannii

Trifolium bonannii, commonly known as the Bonannii clover, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets, and small white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
