Trichomanes epiphyllum

Trichomanes epiphyllum, commonly known as the mountain fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant has delicate, feather-like fronds that are often light green in color. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Trichomanes epiphyllum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
