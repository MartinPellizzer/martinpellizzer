Tectaria subcordata

Tectaria subcordata, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. Tectaria subcordata plays a role in forest ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
