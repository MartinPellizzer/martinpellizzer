Typhonium omeiense

Typhonium omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual appeal to gardens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
