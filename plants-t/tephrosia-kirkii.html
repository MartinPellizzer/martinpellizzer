Tephrosia kirkii

Tephrosia kirkii, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine for their antifungal and antibacterial properties.
