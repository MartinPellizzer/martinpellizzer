Tristagma uniflorum f. album

Tristagma uniflorum f. album, commonly known as the white-flowered tristagma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive white flowers, which contrast with the typical pink or purple hues of the species. The plant is native to certain regions of South America, particularly Argentina and Chile. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its morphology includes slender stems and small, compound leaves, with the flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences.
