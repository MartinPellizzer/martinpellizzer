Tragium Aromaticum

Tragium aromaticum is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is often found growing in rocky or dry soils. This plant is noted for its distinctive appearance, featuring small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture circles. Its unique characteristics make it a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
