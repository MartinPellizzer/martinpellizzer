Turania deserticola

Turania deserticola, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Turania deserticola plays an important role in stabilizing desert soils and providing nectar for pollinators.
