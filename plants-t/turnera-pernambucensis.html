Turnera pernambucensis

Turnera pernambucensis, commonly known as the Pernambuco turnera, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Turnera pernambucensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
