Thrixspermum linusii

Thrixspermum linusii, commonly known as the desert star.

This succulent plant is native to arid regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. It features fleshy, spiny stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months.

Its unique appearance and adaptations make it a striking addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
