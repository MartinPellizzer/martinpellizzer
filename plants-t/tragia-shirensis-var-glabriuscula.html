Tragia shirensis var. glabriuscula

Tragia shirensis var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth tragia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually small, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, though the variety glabriuscula has a smoother texture.

It is adapted to withstand drought and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
