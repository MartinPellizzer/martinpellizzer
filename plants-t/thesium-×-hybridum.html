Thesium × hybridum

Thesium × hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Thesium candidum and Thesium strictum, both native to South America. The plant is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
