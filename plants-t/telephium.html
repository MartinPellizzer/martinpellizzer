Telephium

Telephium, commonly known as painted nettle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Telephium species are often used in gardens for their ornamental value and can also serve as a food source for certain insects.
