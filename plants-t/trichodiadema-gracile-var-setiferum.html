Trichodiadema gracile var. setiferum

Trichodiadema gracile var. setiferum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are densely packed and covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
