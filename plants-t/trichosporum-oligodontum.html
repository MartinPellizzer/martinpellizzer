Trichosporum oligodontum

Trichosporum oligodontum, commonly known as the slender trichosporum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
