Tephrosia delagoensis

Tephrosia delagoensis, commonly known as African blue pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring compound leaves and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
