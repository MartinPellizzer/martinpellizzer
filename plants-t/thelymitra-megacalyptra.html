Thelymitra megacalyptra

Thelymitra megacalyptra, commonly known as the large hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hooded labellum and elongated, strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green to brownish in color, with a prominent central column.

Thelymitra megacalyptra is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its relatively long flowering period.
