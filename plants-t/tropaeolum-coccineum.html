Tropaeolum coccineum

Tropaeolum coccineum, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces edible pods that contain seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Tropaeolum coccineum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
