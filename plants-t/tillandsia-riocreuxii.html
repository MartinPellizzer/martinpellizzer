Tillandsia riocreuxii

Tillandsia riocreuxii, commonly known as the Rio Crecuxii air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences with vivid purple or red flowers.

Tillandsia riocreuxii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
