Tsuga yunnanensis subsp. dura

Tsuga yunnanensis subsp.

dura, commonly known as the Yunnan hemlock, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China. It is characterized by its needle-like leaves, which are typically dark green and arranged spirally along the stems.

The cones of this subspecies are small and often hang downward, containing two to three seeds. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests.

The plant has ecological importance as it provides habitat for various wildlife species and contributes to soil stabilization in its native environment.
