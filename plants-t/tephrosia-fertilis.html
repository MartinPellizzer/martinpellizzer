Tephrosia fertilis

Tephrosia fertilis, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Tephrosia fertilis is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.
