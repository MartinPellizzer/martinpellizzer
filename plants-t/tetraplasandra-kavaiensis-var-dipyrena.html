Tetraplasandra kavaiensis var. dipyrena

Tetraplasandra kavaiensis var. dipyrena, commonly known as the Kava kava variant, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant is notable for its unique chemical composition, which includes compounds such as kavain and dihydromethysticin.
