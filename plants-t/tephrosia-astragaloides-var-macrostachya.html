Tephrosia astragaloides var. macrostachya

Tephrosia astragaloides var. macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stalked tephrosia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with pairs of elliptic to ovate leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
