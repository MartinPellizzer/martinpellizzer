Ternstroemia carnosa

Ternstroemia carnosa, commonly known as the pink-flowered evergreen, is a member of the Theaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. This plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
