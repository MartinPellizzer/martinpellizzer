Trichilia monadelpha

Trichilia monadelpha, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
