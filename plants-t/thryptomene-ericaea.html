Thryptomene ericaea

Thryptomene ericaea, commonly known as the slender thryptomene, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
