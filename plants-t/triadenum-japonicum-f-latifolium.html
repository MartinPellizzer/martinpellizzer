Triadenum japonicum f. latifolium

Triadenum japonicum f. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf Japanese triadenum, is a variant of the triadenum plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
