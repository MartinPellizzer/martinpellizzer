Ticanto vernalis

Ticanto vernalis, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, early spring blooms that appear before its leaves unfurl. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and shaded rock outcrops, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals and a prominent yellow center.

Ticanto vernalis plays an important role in early pollination and supports local insect populations.
