Triaspis thorncroftii

Triaspis thorncroftii, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique botanical features include strap-like leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit.

Triaspis thorncroftii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
