Taeniophyllum stella

Taeniophyllum stella, commonly known as the star star moss, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually thin and clustered, with a pale green to yellowish coloration.

Taeniophyllum stella is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to form dense, carpet-like mats.
