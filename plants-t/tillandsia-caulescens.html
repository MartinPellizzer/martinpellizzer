Tillandsia caulescens

Tillandsia caulescens, commonly known as the slender air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically attaching itself to trees or rocks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with vibrant blue or purple flowers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
