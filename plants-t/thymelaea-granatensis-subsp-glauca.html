Thymelaea granatensis subsp. glauca

Thymelaea granatensis subsp.

glauca, commonly known as the granate thymelaea, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, which help it retain moisture in arid environments.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
