Tephrosia polystachyoides

Tephrosia polystachyoides, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, angular pods containing several seeds. It is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural insecticides.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and climatic conditions.
