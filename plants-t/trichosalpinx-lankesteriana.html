Trichosalpinx lankesteriana

Trichosalpinx lankesteriana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-colored flowers that range from orange to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Trichosalpinx lankesteriana is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
