Thermopsis stricta

Thermopsis stricta, commonly known as yellow flag or narrowleaf thermopsis, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and bright yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Thermopsis stricta is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
