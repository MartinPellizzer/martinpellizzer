Thelymitra porphyrostica

Thelymitra porphyrostica, commonly known as the Porphyritic Rock Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking purple and pink inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in open woodland or heathland environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, erect flowering stem.

Thelymitra porphyrostica is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
