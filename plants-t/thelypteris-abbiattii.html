Thelypteris abbiattii

Thelypteris abbiattii, commonly known as the Abbiatt's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds, which are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce roots that help anchor it to the substrate.

Thelypteris abbiattii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
