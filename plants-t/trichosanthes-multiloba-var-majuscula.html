Trichosanthes multiloba var. majuscula

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and long, hairy stems. The plant produces yellow, fragrant flowers that are followed by elongated, spiny fruits. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
