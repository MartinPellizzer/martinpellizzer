Tamarix sireti

Tamarix sireti, commonly known as the Kashmir tamarisk, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has feathery, grey-green foliage and produces small, pink or purple flowers in summer. Its roots are extensive and help prevent soil erosion.

Tamarix sireti is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
