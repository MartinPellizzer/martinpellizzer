Trichilia tetrapetala

Trichilia tetrapetala, commonly known as the four-petaled chicle tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related hardwoods. The tree is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release four winged seeds.
