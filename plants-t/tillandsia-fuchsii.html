Tillandsia fuchsii

Tillandsia fuchsii, commonly known as the silver vellum air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a epiphytic plant that absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaf surfaces rather than through roots. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a striking, velvety appearance.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
