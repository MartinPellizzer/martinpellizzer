Tabernaemontana eusepaloides

Tabernaemontana eusepaloides, commonly known as the white star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Tabernaemontana eusepaloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
