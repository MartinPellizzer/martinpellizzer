Triticum carthlicum var. arnurual

Triticum carthlicum var. arnurual, commonly known as the Arnoul's wheat, is a botanical variant of the species Triticum carthlicum.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, elongated leaves. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is adapted to well-drained soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous florets.

This variety is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique genetic traits and potential agricultural applications.
