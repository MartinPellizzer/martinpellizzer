Thymus serpyllum f. ovatus

Thymus serpyllum f. ovatus, commonly known as broadleaf thyme, is a compact, low-growing perennial herb native to Europe.

It features rounded, ovate leaves that are slightly hairy and have a fragrant, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dense foliage and long blooming period.
