Triteleia ixioides var. anilina

Triteleia ixioides var. anilina, commonly known as the blue-eyed lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to California.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that resemble lilies, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. It is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
