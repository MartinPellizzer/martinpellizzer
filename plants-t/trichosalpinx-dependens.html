Trichosalpinx dependens

Trichosalpinx dependens, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a smooth surface.

Trichosalpinx dependens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
