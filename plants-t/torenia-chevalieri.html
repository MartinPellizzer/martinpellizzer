Torenia chevalieri

Torenia chevalieri, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia chevalieri is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
