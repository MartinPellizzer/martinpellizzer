Trigonella orthoceras f. pedunculata

Trigonella orthoceras f. pedunculata, commonly known as the pedunculate trigonella, is a variant of the trigonella plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

This subspecies is valued for its potential medicinal properties and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
