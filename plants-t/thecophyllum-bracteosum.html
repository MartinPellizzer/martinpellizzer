Thecophyllum bracteosum

Thecophyllum bracteosum, commonly known as the bracteate thecophyllum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Thecophyllum bracteosum is often found in montane forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
