Tropaeolum majus var. carneum

Tropaeolum majus var. carneum, commonly known as the "Carnose Nasturtium," is a vibrant flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often red or purple in color, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces showy, bright-orange or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its edible leaves and flowers add both ornamental value and culinary interest to the garden.
