Toussaintia hallei

Toussaintia hallei, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow, daisy-like flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
