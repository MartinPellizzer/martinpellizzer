Ticanto sinensis

Ticanto sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Ticanto sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
