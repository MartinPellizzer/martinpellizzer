Triplocentron melitense

Triplocentron melitense, commonly known as the Maltese starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Malta and Sicily.

It is a small herbaceous species characterized by its star-shaped flowers and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and limestone habitats. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
