Triumfetta clivorum subsp. brevipetala

Triumfetta clivorum subsp.

brevipetala, commonly known as the short-petioled triumfetta, is a member of the Malvaceae family. It is a small, herbaceous shrub characterized by its simple, ovate leaves with short petioles.
