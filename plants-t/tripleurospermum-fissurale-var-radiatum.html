Tripleurospermum fissurale var. radiatum

Tripleurospermum fissurale var. radiatum, commonly known as the radiate tripleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a single, radiating head, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
