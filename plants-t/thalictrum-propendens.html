Thalictrum propendens

Thalictrum propendens, commonly known as the nodding meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the northern hemisphere.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent stamens. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are alternate along the stem. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Thalictrum propendens is valued in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
