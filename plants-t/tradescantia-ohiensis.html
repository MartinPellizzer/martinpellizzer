Tradescantia ohiensis

Tradescantia ohiensis, commonly known as the Ohiensis spiderwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, three-petaled blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Tradescantia ohiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in woodland settings.
