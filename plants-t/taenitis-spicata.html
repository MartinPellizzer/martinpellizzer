Taenitis spicata

Taenitis spicata, commonly known as the spicate taenitis, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a smooth, waxy surface.

Taenitis spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
