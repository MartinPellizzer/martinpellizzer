Thalictrum flavum var. speciosissimum

Thalictrum flavum var. speciosissimum, commonly known as the showy avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and stream banks.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and role in supporting pollinators.
