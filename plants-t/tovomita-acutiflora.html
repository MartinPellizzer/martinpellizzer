Tovomita acutiflora

Tovomita acutiflora, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are linear and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Tovomita acutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
