Trisetaria koelerioides var. aristata

Trisetaria koelerioides var. aristata, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy environments and has narrow, elongated leaves with fine, hairy textures. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often clustered together, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in wetland gardens.
