Tarrietia erythrosiphon subvar. ankazoabensis

Tarrietia erythrosiphon subvar. ankazoabensis, commonly known as the Ankazoa flamebush, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

The botanical interest of this species lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
