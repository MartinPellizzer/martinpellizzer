Teucrium simplex

Teucrium simplex, commonly known as narrowleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.

Teucrium simplex is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
