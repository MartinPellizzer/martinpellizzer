Terminalia kuhlmannii

Terminalia kuhlmannii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The tree produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in dense, spherical clusters, giving it its common name. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.
