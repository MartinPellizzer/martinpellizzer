Taravalia

Taravalia, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, velvety flowers. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.
