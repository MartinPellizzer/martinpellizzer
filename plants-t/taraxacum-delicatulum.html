Taraxacum delicatulum

Taraxacum delicatulum, commonly known as the slender dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and a single, elongated flower head that produces a cluster of seeds. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, temperate environments. The plant has a taproot system and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its flowers are typically yellow, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.
