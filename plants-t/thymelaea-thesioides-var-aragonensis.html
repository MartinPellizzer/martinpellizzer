Thymelaea thesioides var. aragonensis

Thymelaea thesioides var. aragonensis, commonly known as the Aragon thyme.

This plant is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries. The leaves are narrow and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and Mediterranean climates.
