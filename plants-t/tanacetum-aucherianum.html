Tanacetum aucherianum

Tanacetum aucherianum, commonly known as the golden buttons or yellow buttonweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features a compact growth habit with dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its aromatic foliage and showy inflorescences make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, though it can tolerate partial shade.
