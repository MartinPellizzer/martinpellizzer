Typhonium angustilobum

Typhonium angustilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a glossy surface. It produces clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spadix, which is characteristic of the Araceae family.

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its unique foliage and ability to thrive in moist, shady environments.
