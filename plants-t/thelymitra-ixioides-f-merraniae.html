Thelymitra ixioides f. merraniae

Thelymitra ixioides f. merraniae, commonly known as the Merranella form, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, underground rhizome and erect, linear leaves. The plant produces a single, showy spike bearing several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow dorsal sepal and petals.
