Tragopogon iranicus

Tragopogon iranicus, commonly known as the Persian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to regions of Central Asia and the Caucasus.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features slender, hairy stems and produces yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disc florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy surface.

Tragopogon iranicus is typically found in grassy habitats and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
