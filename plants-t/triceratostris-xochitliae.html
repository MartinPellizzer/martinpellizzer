Triceratostris xochitliae

Triceratostris xochitliae, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive floral structure. The plant typically features showy, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns on its petals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in local ecosystems.
