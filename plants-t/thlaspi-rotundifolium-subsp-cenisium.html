Thlaspi rotundifolium subsp. cenisium

Thlaspi rotundifolium subsp.

cenisium, commonly known as the rounded-leaved rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils.

The plant features small, rounded leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found in alpine or arctic regions, where it tolerates cold and harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to nutrient-poor and well-drained habitats.
