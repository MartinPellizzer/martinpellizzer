Trema orientale var. molle

Trema orientale var. molle, commonly known as the soft-leaved trema, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, ovate leaves that are usually a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and green walls.
