Trachelospermum borneanum

Trachelospermum borneanum, commonly known as the Bornean star jasmine, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in clusters. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in warm regions.
