Tilia kiusiana

Tilia kiusiana, commonly known as the Kiusian linden, is a deciduous tree native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, pale yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain three winged seeds. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in temperate forest regions.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
