Thelypteris × jerdonii

Thelypteris × jerdonii, commonly known as the Jerdon's shield fern, is a hybrid fern species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds with a distinctive shield-shaped appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Thelypteris × jerdonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
