Thalictrum chinense

Thalictrum chinense, commonly known as Chinese meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, adorned with palmate leaves that are finely divided. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early to mid-summer, arranged in racemes. Its root system is fibrous, and it prefers moist, well-drained soils in partial shade.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
