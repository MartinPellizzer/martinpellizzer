Trichantha filifera

Trichantha filifera, commonly known as the threadleaf trichantha, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It is native to regions of South America, particularly in Brazil and Paraguay.
