Thelypteris noveboracensis f. fragrans

Thelypteris noveboracensis f. fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant maidenhair fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to eastern North America. The plant is distinguished by its aromatic leaves, which emit a subtle fragrance when touched or crushed. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
