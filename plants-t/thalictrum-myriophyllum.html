Thalictrum myriophyllum

Thalictrum myriophyllum, commonly known as water avens, is a herbaceous plant native to moist habitats in North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows in shallow water or wet soil, making it a characteristic member of wetland ecosystems. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the soil, contributing to its ecological role.

Thalictrum myriophyllum is often found in marshes, pond edges, and along streams.
