Todea barbara

Todea barbara, commonly known as the purple toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of purple or violet flowers. It is often found in coastal areas and can thrive in coastal climates.

Todea barbara is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
