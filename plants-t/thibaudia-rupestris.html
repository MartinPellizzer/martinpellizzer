Thibaudia rupestris

Thibaudia rupestris, commonly known as the rock thibaudia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in terminal spikes, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Thibaudia rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry conditions.
