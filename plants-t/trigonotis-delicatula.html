Trigonotis delicatula

Trigonotis delicatula, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Trigonotis delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
