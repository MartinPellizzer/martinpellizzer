Tillandsia heterostachys

Tillandsia heterostachys, commonly known as the silver pine tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive, pine-like appearance. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and does not require soil for growth. Its rosette-shaped structure allows it to efficiently absorb moisture and nutrients from the air.

Tillandsia heterostachys is popular among enthusiasts for its unique aesthetic and low maintenance requirements.
