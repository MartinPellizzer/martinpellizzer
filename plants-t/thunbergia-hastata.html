Thunbergia hastata

Thunbergia hastata, commonly known as blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and delicate, cascading clusters of blue-violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Thunbergia hastata is popular in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.
