Trichilia sexanthera

Trichilia sexanthera, commonly known as African snowflake, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves with 5 to 7 leaflets. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, arranged in clusters, and it produces woody, angular fruits with a single seed.
