Trichocentrum capistratum

Trichocentrum capistratum, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped inflorescence. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum capistratum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
