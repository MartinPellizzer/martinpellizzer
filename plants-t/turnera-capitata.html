Turnera capitata

Turnera capitata, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Passifloraceae family, known for its showy, colorful flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
