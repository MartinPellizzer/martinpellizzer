Tephrosia bracteolata

Tephrosia bracteolata, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and is often found in open woodlands and savannas. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Tephrosia bracteolata is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
