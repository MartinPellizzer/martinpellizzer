Thyana portoricensis

Thyana portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican thyme.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean, specifically Puerto Rico. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It produces small, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months.

Thyana portoricensis is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
