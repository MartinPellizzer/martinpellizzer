Tetrasida polyantha

Tetrasida polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Tetrasida polyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
