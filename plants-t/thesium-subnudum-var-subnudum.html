Thesium subnudum var. subnudum

Thesium subnudum var. subnudum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is often found in coastal or inland regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Thesium subnudum var. subnudum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
