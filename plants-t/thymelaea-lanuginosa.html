Thymelaea lanuginosa

Thymelaea lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry climates.
