Tetrapterys rotundifolia

Tetrapterys rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf tetrapterys, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its seeds are encased in pods and are often dispersed by animals.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
