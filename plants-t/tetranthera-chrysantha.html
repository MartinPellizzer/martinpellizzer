Tetranthera chrysantha

Tetranthera chrysantha, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
