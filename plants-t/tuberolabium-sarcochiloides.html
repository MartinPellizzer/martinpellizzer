Tuberolabium sarcochiloides

Tuberolabium sarcochiloides, commonly known as the wrinkled orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, where it relies on specific mycorrhizal associations for nutrient uptake.
