Tillandsia viridiflora var. variegata

Tillandsia viridiflora var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated green-flowered air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant that absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves rather than through roots. The plant features striking variegated foliage with green and white or silver markings. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain humidity.
