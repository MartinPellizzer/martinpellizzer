Talinum nanum

Talinum nanum, commonly known as dwarf Malabar spinach, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It produces small, yellow flowers that develop into capsules containing numerous seeds.
