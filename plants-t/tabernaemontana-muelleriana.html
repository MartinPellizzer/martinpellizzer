Tabernaemontana muelleriana

Tabernaemontana muelleriana, commonly known as the Mueller's star of Bethlehem, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the evening. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
