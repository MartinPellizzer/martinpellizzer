Trachyandra pauciflora

Trachyandra pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered trachyandra, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its compact, densely packed foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
