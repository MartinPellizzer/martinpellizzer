Torenia thailandica

Torenia thailandica, commonly known as the Thai morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often grown for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Torenia thailandica is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
