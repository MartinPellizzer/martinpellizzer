Tylostigma herminioides

Tylostigma herminioides, commonly known as the silky acanthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, palmately lobed leaves with a soft, silky texture on the underside. The plant produces showy, sparsely branched inflorescences with violet to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
