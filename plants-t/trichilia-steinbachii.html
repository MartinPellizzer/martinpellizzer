Trichilia steinbachii

Trichilia steinbachii, commonly known as African shea tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa, particularly found in countries like Ghana, Nigeria, and Tanzania.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its large, simple leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain a hard, oily seed, which is the source of shea butter.

Trichilia steinbachii is valued for its economic importance, especially in traditional and local industries.
