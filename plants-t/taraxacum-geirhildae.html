Taraxacum geirhildae

Taraxacum geirhildae, commonly known as the Geirhild's dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Iceland and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated leaves. The plant produces a distinctive, hollow stem that supports its flowering head. It thrives in moist, open habitats and is often found in grassy areas.
