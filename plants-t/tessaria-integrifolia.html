Tessaria integrifolia

Tessaria integrifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf tessaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Tessaria integrifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
