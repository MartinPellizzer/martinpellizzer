Thunbergia natalensis

Thunbergia natalensis, commonly known as Natal morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Thunbergia natalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
