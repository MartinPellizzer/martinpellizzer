Trifolium lenkoranicum

Trifolium lenkoranicum, commonly known as the Lenkoran clover, is a flowering plant species native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and along forest edges.
