Thymus dominii

Thymus dominii, commonly known as narrowleaf thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus dominii is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
