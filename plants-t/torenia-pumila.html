Torenia pumila

Torenia pumila, commonly known as the small torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and yellow. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Torenia pumila is often cultivated as a ornamental plant in gardens and as a bedding plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
