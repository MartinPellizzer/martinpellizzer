Telanthera grandifolia

Telanthera grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered telanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
