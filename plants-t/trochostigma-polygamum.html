Trochostigma polygamum

Trochostigma polygamum, commonly known as the red-stemmed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often reddish stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are arranged in umbels. It is known for its milky sap, which is a common feature among plants in the Apocynaceae family.
