Trichocentrum iridifolium

Trichocentrum iridifolium, commonly known as the iridescence-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that often display a shimmering, iridescent sheen under light. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Trichocentrum iridifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique foliage.
