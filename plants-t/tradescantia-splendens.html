Tradescantia splendens

Tradescantia splendens, commonly known as the wandering jew, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that range from bright green to purple. Tradescantia splendens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
