Tamarix africana var. microstachys

Tamarix africana var. microstachys, commonly known as the dwarf tamarisk, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in arid regions.
