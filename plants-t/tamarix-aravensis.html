Tamarix aravensis

Tamarix aravensis, commonly known as the Arava tamarisk, is a shrub native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and small, pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has adapted to harsh environments with its deep root system and ability to fix nitrogen. It is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

However, it can become invasive in some areas, outcompeting native vegetation.
