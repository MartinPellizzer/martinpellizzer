Tephrosia glandulifera

Tephrosia glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular tephrosia, is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.
