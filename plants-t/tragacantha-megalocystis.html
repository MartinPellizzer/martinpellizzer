Tragacantha megalocystis

Tragacantha megalocystis, commonly known as the giant globular tragacanth, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its distinctive, large, globular seed pods. The plant produces yellow flowers and has a shrub-like growth habit with compound leaves. Its seeds are enclosed in thick, woody pods that can persist on the plant for extended periods.

The plant is valued for its resin, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a natural gum.
