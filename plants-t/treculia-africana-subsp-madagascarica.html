Treculia africana subsp. madagascarica

Treculia africana subsp.

madagascarica, commonly known as the Madagascar treculia, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar. It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and smooth, gray bark. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by round, fleshy fruits that contain a single large seed.
