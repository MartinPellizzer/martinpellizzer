Tecophilaea cyanocrocus var. regelii

Tecophilaea cyanocrocus var. regelii, commonly known as the Regel's blue-flowered tecophilaea, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of succulents. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
