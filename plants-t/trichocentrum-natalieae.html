Trichocentrum natalieae

Trichocentrum natalieae, commonly known as the Natalie's Strap Orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its long, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical forests. Its flowers bloom during the summer months and are known for their delicate, sweet fragrance.

Trichocentrum natalieae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
