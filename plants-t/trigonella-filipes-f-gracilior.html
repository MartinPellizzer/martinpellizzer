Trigonella filipes f. gracilior

Trigonella filipes f. gracilior, commonly known as slender fenugreek, is a botanical variant of the fenugreek species.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, trifoliate leaves with a distinctive trifoliate structure. Its flowers are yellow and borne in racemes, typical of the Fabaceae family.

The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in dry, sandy soils.
