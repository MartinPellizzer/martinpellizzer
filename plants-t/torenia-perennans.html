Torenia perennans

Torenia perennans, commonly known as the common torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking violet or blue flowers with yellow centers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia perennans is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
