Trichodesma bamianicum

Trichodesma bamianicum, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often growing as a shrub or small tree.

Trichodesma bamianicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
