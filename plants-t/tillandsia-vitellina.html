Tillandsia vitellina

Tillandsia vitellina, commonly known as the yellow tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that trap moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
