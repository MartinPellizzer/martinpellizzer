Torenia godefroyi var. filiformis

Torenia godefroyi var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing habit and produces clusters of small, tubular, violet-blue flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This variety is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive blooms and ability to thrive in humid conditions.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
