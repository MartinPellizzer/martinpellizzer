Tragacantha muschiana

Tragacantha muschiana, commonly known as the muschian tragacanth, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Astracanaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Tragacantha muschiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
