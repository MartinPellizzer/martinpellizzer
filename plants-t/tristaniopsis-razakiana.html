Tristaniopsis razakiana

Tristaniopsis razakiana, commonly known as the Bornean tree fern, is a species native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
