Tournefortia mutabilis

Tournefortia mutabilis, commonly known as the variable tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Tournefortia mutabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
