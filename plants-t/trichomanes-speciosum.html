Trichomanes speciosum

Trichomanes speciosum, commonly known as the ornate huperbion, is a rare and ancient fern belonging to the family Huperbionaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds that are often adorned with small, spore-producing structures called sporangia. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, Trichomanes speciosum is considered endangered in many parts of its native range.
