Trisetum brasiliense

Trisetum brasiliense, commonly known as the Brazilian feather grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a soft, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands. It is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Trisetum brasiliense plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife in its native habitat.
