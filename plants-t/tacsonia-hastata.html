Tacsonia hastata

Tacsonia hastata, commonly known as the slender tacsonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Tacsonia hastata has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

It is often found in open grasslands and along the edges of shrublands.
