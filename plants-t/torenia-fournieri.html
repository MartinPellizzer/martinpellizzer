Torenia fournieri

Torenia fournieri, commonly known as the Surinam torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

Torenia fournieri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
