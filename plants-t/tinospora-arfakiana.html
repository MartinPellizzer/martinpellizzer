Tinospora arfakiana

Tinospora arfakiana, commonly known as Arfakian glorybower, is a climbing shrub native to New Guinea and parts of Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits containing bright red seeds. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticultural use in certain regions.
