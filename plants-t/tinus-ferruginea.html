Tinus ferruginea

Tinus ferruginea, commonly known as the red-stemmed ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, reddish-brown bark and dense, woody structure. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by oval, woody fruits.

Tinus ferruginea is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
