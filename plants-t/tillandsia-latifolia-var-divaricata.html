Tillandsia latifolia var. divaricata

Tillandsia latifolia var. divaricata, commonly known as the broadleaf tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage that holds water. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires minimal direct sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
