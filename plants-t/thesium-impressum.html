Thesium impressum

Thesium impressum, commonly known as the smooth thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, clustered inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and shows a preference for sandy or gravelly soils. It is native to southern Africa and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Thesium impressum is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
