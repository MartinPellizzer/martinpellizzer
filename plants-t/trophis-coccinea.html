Trophis coccinea

Trophis coccinea, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
