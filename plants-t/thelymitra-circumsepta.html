Thelymitra circumsepta

Thelymitra circumsepta, commonly known as the broad-leafed star orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom during the autumn and winter seasons.

Thelymitra circumsepta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
