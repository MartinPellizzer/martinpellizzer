Taraxacum sagitticordatum

Taraxacum sagitticordatum, commonly known as the arrowleaf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal leaves that are arrow-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy white seed heads. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.
