Tephrosia coccinea var. coccinea

Tephrosia coccinea var. coccinea, commonly known as the red tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange-pink flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
