Tigridia arequipensis

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes, often in mountainous regions with cool, temperate climates. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a distinctive pattern of darker markings, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Tigridia arequipensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
