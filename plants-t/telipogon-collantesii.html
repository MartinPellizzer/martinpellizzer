Telipogon collantesii

Telipogon collantesii, commonly known as the Collantes' orchid, is a rare and endemic flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers with intricate patterns, typically in shades of white and purple. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.
