Tylophora juventas

Tylophora juventas, commonly known as the "immortality vine," is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Tylophora juventas thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.
