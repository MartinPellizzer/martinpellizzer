Tridens avenaceus var. kurtzianus

Tridens avenaceus var. kurtzianus, commonly known as the KURTZIANUS variant of the common panic grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Its botanical structure supports its role in both ecological and horticultural settings.
