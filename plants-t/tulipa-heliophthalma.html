Tulipa heliophthalma

Tulipa heliophthalma, commonly known as the sun tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, cup-shaped blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant and long-lasting flowers.
