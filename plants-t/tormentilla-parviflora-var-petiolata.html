Tormentilla parviflora var. petiolata

Tormentilla parviflora var. petiolata, commonly known as the petioled tormentil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has serrated, oval-shaped leaves with long petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its roots are used in traditional herbal medicine for their astringent properties.
