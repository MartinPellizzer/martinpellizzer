Torenia gracilis

Torenia gracilis, commonly known as the slender torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are adapted for pollination by insects, particularly bees.

Torenia gracilis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
