Tragus unioloides

Tragus unioloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf tragus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, erect growth habit. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
