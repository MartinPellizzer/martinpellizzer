Typhonium giraldii

Typhonium giraldii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking yellow flowers arranged in a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or marshy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
