Thunbergia monroi

Thunbergia monroi, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia monroi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
