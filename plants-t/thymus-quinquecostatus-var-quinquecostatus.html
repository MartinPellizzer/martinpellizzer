Thymus quinquecostatus var. quinquecostatus

Thymus quinquecostatus var. quinquecostatus, commonly known as narrowleaf thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically narrow and linear, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and Mediterranean landscapes.

Its essential oils contain thymol, which contributes to its strong scent and medicinal properties.
