Tovomitopsis myrcioides

Tovomitopsis myrcioides, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought, making it a common ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
