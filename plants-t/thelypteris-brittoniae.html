Thelypteris brittoniae

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the undersides of the leaves.
