Trillium californicum

Trillium californicum, commonly known as the California trillium, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features three elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy, and it produces a single, showy white or pale pink flower with three petals and three sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. It blooms in early spring and is characterized by its simple, elegant appearance.

Trillium californicum is also known for its slow growth and long lifespan, often living for several decades.
