Thelypteris brooksii

Thelypteris brooksii, commonly known as Brooks' shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. Thelypteris brooksii thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable component of its native ecosystem.
