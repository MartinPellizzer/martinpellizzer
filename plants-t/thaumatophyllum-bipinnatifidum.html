Thaumatophyllum bipinnatifidum

Thaumatophyllum bipinnatifidum, commonly known as the starry night monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and often have a striking, star-like pattern of light green veins against a dark green background. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.

Thaumatophyllum bipinnatifidum is known for its slow growth and can reach significant sizes if provided with proper support and care.
