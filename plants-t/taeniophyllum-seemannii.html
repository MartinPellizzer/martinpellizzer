Taeniophyllum seemannii

Taeniophyllum seemannii, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the tiny orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a compact, rosette-like growth form with narrow, fleshy leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a spike-like arrangement, with delicate, fragrant blooms.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in controlled indoor conditions.
