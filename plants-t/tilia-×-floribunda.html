Tilia × floribunda

Tilia × floribunda, commonly known as the Chinese linden or the fastigiate linden, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The tree produces fragrant, pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It is native to China but has been widely cultivated in temperate regions for its ornamental value.

Tilia × floribunda is also valued for its strong, durable wood and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
