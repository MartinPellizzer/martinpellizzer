Terrellia × macounii

Terrellia × macounii, commonly known as the Macoun's terrellia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland settings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, requiring moderate moisture levels.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
