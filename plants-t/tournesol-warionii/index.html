Tournesol Warionii

Tournesol warionii, scientifically known as Tournesol warionii, is a rare and enigmatic plant species found in the remote highlands of Madagascar. With its striking yellow-orange flowers and unique spiral growth pattern, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This plant thrives in nutrient-poor soil and requires specific microclimatic conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Tournesol warionii is considered critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this rare botanical wonder and ensure its survival for future generations.
