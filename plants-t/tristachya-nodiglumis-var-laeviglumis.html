Tristachya nodiglumis var. laeviglumis

Tristachya nodiglumis var. laeviglumis, commonly known as the smooth-leafed grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, smooth leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, preferring moist to saturated soil conditions. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet with three bracts, which are often shorter than the blade.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in aquatic gardens.
