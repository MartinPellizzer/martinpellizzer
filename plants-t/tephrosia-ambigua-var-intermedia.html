Tephrosia ambigua var. intermedia

Tephrosia ambigua var. intermedia, commonly known as the Mexican tephrosia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with trifoliate leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers.
