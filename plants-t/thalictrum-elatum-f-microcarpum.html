Thalictrum elatum f. microcarpum

Thalictrum elatum f. microcarpum, commonly known as the dwarf meadow rue, is a flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. This cultivar is noted for its compact growth habit and is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal.
