Theka grandis

Theka grandis, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Theka grandis is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in tropical environments.
