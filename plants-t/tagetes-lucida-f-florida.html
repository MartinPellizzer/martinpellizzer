Tagetes lucida f. florida

Tagetes lucida f. florida, commonly known as the Florida tansy, is a hybrid form of the Mexican tansy.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant features finely divided, gray-green leaves and produces yellow-orange flowers in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in moist, open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable ornamental and potential medicinal plant.
