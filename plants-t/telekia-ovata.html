Telekia ovata

Telekia ovata, commonly known as the yellow sunflower.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is part of the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, branched stems. It typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed areas.
