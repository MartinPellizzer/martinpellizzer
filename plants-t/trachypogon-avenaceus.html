Trachypogon avenaceus

Trachypogon avenaceus, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Trachypogon avenaceus is also noted for its ability to tolerate drought conditions.
