Tricyrtis formosana var. ovatifolia

Tricyrtis formosana var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf Japanese spider orchid, is a striking perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are typically broad and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a contrasting dark throat. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in subtropical climates.
