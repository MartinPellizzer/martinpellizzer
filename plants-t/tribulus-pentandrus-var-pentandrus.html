Tribulus pentandrus var. pentandrus

Tribulus pentandrus var. pentandrus, commonly known as golden shower or yellow shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
