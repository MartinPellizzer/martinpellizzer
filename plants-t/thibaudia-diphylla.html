Thibaudia diphylla

Thibaudia diphylla, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Thibaudia diphylla is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
