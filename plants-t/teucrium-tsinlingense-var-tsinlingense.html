Teucrium tsinlingense var. tsinlingense

Teucrium tsinlingense var. tsinlingense, commonly known as the Chinese germander, is a perennial herb native to China.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing a compact growth habit with narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in the summer months. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy plant adapted to dry and nutrient-poor soils.
