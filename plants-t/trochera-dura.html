Trochera dura

Trochera dura, commonly known as the hard broom.

It is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, woody growth and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers in the spring, which are followed by winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and rocky environments.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice in gardens and landscaping.
