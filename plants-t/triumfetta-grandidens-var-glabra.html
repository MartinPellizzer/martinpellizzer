Triumfetta grandidens var. glabra

Triumfetta grandidens var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree with smooth, glabrous bark and alternate, simple leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by three-winged, indehiscent fruits.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
