Trigonella emodi var. oblongifolia

Trigonella emodi var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the Kashmiri fenugreek, is a variant of the fenugreek plant native to the Himalayan regions.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds. Its botanical features include hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive, slightly aromatic scent.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
