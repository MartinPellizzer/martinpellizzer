Turnera ulmifolia var. elegans

Turnera ulmifolia var. elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for horticultural purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
