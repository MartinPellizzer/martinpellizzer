Tillandsia deppeana var. reducta

Tillandsia deppeana var. reducta, commonly known as the Reducta Air Plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer or early fall. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it an attractive and easy-to-care-for plant.
