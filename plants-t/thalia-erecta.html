Thalia erecta

Thalia erecta, commonly known as the upright thalia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. Thalia erecta typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and can be propagated through stem cuttings.
