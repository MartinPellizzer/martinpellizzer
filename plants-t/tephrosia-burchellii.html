Tephrosia burchellii

Tephrosia burchellii, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Africa and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
