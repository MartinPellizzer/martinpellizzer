Thalictrum kamikavense

Thalictrum kamikavense, commonly known as the Japanese meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Thalictrum kamikavense typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
