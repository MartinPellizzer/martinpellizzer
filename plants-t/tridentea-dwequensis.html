Tridentea dwequensis

Tridentea dwequensis, commonly known as the desert trident, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive three-lobed leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Due to its unique morphology and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
