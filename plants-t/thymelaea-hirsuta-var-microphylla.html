Thymelaea hirsuta var. microphylla

Thymelaea hirsuta var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typically tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and ornamental plant in arid and semi-arid regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
