Tinospora polygonoides

Tinospora polygonoides, commonly known as Indian Moonseed, is a climbing shrub belonging to the family Menispermaceae.

It is characterized by its twining stems and compound leaves with opposite arrangement. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Tinospora polygonoides is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional systems of medicine.
