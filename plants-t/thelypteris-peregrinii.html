Thelypteris peregrinii

Thelypteris peregrinii, commonly known as the wandering fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, hairy structures.

Thelypteris peregrinii is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through vegetative reproduction.
