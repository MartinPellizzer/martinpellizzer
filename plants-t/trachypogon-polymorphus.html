Trachypogon polymorphus

Trachypogon polymorphus, commonly known as the variable grass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its variable growth form, which can range from a tufted perennial to a rhizomatous perennial. The plant typically grows in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and disturbed areas. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often bracted and can vary in shape.

Trachypogon polymorphus is known for its adaptability and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
