Tripterospermum carlesii

Tripterospermum carlesii, commonly known as the Carlesii's evergreen hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming from spring to summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Tripterospermum carlesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
