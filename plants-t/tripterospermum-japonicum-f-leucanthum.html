Tripterospermum japonicum f. leucanthum

Tripterospermum japonicum f. leucanthum, commonly known as the white-flowered Japanese tripterospermum, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant is noted for its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and elegant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
