Trichosporum magnificum

Trichosporum magnificum, commonly known as the golden spire plant, is a unique species characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescences.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is native to the temperate regions of the southern hemisphere. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep orange, depending on the season. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Trichosporum magnificum thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
