Trollius miyabei

Trollius miyabei, commonly known as the Japanese buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features showy, yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically divided into lobes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Trollius miyabei is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow.
