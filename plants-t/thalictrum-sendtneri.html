Thalictrum sendtneri

Thalictrum sendtneri, commonly known as the Sendtner's meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream sides. It produces numerous slender, hairy stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

Thalictrum sendtneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
