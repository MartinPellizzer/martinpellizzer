Trilisa odoratissima

Trilisa odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant trilisa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that emit a sweet, spicy fragrance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and dark green, while its flowers are small and white with a subtle yellowish tinge.

Trilisa odoratissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
