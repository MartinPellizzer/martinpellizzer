Trigonella cilicica

Trigonella cilicica, commonly known as Cilician fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous traits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods containing several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, heart-shaped leaflets.

Trigonella cilicica is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.
