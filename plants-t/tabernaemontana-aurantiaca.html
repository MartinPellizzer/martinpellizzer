Tabernaemontana aurantiaca

Tabernaemontana aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, pale bark and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
