Tephrosia filiformis

Tephrosia filiformis, commonly known as slender tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and it is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and degraded forests.

Tephrosia filiformis is also known for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
