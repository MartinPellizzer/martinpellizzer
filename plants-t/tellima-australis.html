Tellima australis

Tellima australis, commonly known as the Australian tellima, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodland areas. The plant features striking, showy flowers that are usually pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive, elongated spur. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Tellima australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
