Taliera gembanga

Taliera gembanga, commonly known as the flame leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange leaves that give it its common name. The plant has a broad, spreading canopy and can grow up to 10 meters tall. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The tree also produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.
