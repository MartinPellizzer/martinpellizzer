Thelypteris ornata

Thelypteris ornata, commonly known as the ornate water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its spores are produced on the underside of its fronds, which are arranged in a distinctive, overlapping pattern.

Thelypteris ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
