Tulipa didieri subsp. billietiana

Tulipa didieri subsp.

billietiana, commonly known as the Billiet's tulip, is a rare and distinctive species native to Turkey. It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in alpine or subalpine habitats. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single bloom per stem.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
