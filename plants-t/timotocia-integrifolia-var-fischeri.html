Timotocia integrifolia var. fischeri

Timotocia integrifolia var. fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's smoothleaf timotocia, is a rare and specialized plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences, which are adapted for pollination by specific insect species.
