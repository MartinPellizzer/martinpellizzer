Thunbergia minziroensis

Thunbergia minziroensis, commonly known as the East African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to Tanzania.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. It is often found in moist woodland areas and along riverbanks.

Thunbergia minziroensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
