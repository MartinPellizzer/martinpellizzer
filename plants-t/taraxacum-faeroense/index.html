Taraxacum Faeroense

Taraxacum faeroense is a unique species of dandelion found exclusively in the Faroe Islands. This plant is notable for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh coastal environments. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer and are followed by fluffy white seeds. The plant plays an important role in the local ecosystem, supporting pollinators and contributing to soil stability. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable part of the Faroese flora.
