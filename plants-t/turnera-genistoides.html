Turnera genistoides

Turnera genistoides, commonly known as the golden turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
