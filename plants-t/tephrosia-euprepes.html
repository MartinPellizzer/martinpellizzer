Tephrosia euprepes

Tephrosia euprepes, commonly known as the yellow tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with several oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
