Thunbergia fischeri

Thunbergia fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's spider flower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using its twining stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia fischeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
