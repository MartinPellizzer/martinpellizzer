Trichomanes radicans var. orientale

Trichomanes radicans var. orientale, commonly known as the oriental walking fern, is a variant of the walking fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial evergreen plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread along the ground. The plant features feathery, dichotomously branched fronds that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique growth pattern and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for shady garden spaces.
