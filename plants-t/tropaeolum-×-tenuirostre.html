Tropaeolum × tenuirostre

Tropaeolum × tenuirostre, commonly known as the slender-tongued nasturtium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Tropaeolaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from yellow to orange in color. The plant is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible leaves and seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features include a trailing growth habit and the presence of distinctive seed pods that split open to release seeds.
