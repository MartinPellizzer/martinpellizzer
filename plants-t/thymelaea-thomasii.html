Thymelaea thomasii

Thymelaea thomasii, commonly known as the Thomas thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and fleshy, which helps it conserve water.

It is often found in coastal regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
