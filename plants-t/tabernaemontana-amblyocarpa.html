Tabernaemontana amblyocarpa

Tabernaemontana amblyocarpa, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in white or pink hues. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic and ecological benefits.
