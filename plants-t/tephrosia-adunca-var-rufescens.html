Tephrosia adunca var. rufescens

Tephrosia adunca var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often reddish stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their antipyretic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also valued for its potential in agroforestry and soil stabilization.
