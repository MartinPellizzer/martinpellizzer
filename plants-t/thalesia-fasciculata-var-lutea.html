Thalesia fasciculata var. lutea

Thalesia fasciculata var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow thalesia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

This variant is distinguished by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast with its green foliage.
