Trichosporum lobbianum

Trichosporum lobbianum, commonly known as the mountain tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in arid conditions.

Trichosporum lobbianum is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its habitat.
