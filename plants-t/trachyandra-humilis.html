Trachyandra humilis

Trachyandra humilis, commonly known as the dwarf trachyandra, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of white, powdery bloom. Trachyandra humilis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
