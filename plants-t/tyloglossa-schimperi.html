Tyloglossa schimperi

Tyloglossa schimperi, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. Its showy, tubular flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white.

Tyloglossa schimperi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
