Thibaudia elliptica

Thibaudia elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf thibaudia, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, attracting various pollinators. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Thibaudia elliptica is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
