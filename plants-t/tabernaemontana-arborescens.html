Tabernaemontana arborescens

Tabernaemontana arborescens, commonly known as the candle bush, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate to elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The candle bush is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
