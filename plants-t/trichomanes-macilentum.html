Trichomanes macilentum

Trichomanes macilentum, commonly known as the slender huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant has a distinctive rhizome that is often covered in a layer of fine, hairy structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

Trichomanes macilentum is known for its unique reproductive structures, including sporangia that are clustered in small, round formations.
