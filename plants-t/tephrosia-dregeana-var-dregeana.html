Tephrosia dregeana var. dregeana

Tephrosia dregeana var. dregeana, commonly known as African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in open woodland and savanna habitats. It has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits containing seeds.

This species is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
