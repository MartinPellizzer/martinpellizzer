Tephrosia Conzattii

Tephrosia conzattii is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Central America. It is known for its striking blue-violet blooms and unique growth habits. This species is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
