Triodia vanleeuwenii

Triodia vanleeuwenii, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry foliage and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has a robust root system that helps it survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which reduce water loss.

Triodia vanleeuwenii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various desert fauna.
