Tithymalus boeticus

Tithymalus boeticus, commonly known as the Spanish thyme, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Tithymalus boeticus thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
