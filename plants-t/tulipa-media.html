Tulipa media

Tulipa media, commonly known as the Darwin tulip, is a species of flowering plant in the family Liliaceae.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, yellow, and white. The plant produces strap-like leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit. It is a perennial bulb plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Tulipa media is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
