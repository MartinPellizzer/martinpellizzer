Tripleurospermum tenuifolium

Tripleurospermum tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and is often found in disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is noted for its potential use in forage and erosion control due to its hardy nature.
