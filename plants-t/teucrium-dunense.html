Teucrium dunense

Teucrium dunense, commonly known as the Balkan germander, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often blooming in summer. It is part of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Teucrium dunense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
