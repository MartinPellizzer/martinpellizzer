Tricera marginalis

Tricera marginalis, commonly known as the marginal tricera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin. Tricera marginalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
