Thea lanceolota

Thea lanceolota, commonly known as the lanceleaf thea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Thea lanceolota is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
