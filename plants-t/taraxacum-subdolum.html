Taraxacum subdolum

Taraxacum subdolum, commonly known as the dwarf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of only a few centimeters. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and have a waxy coating. Its flowers are yellow and borne in a single inflorescence at the tip of the stem.

It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.
