Thesium umbelliferum

Thesium umbelliferum, commonly known as the common thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features a basal rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
