Thelypteris dicranogramma

Thelypteris dicranogramma, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Thelypteris dicranogramma plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small organisms.
