Thelypteris mutabilis

Thelypteris mutabilis, commonly known as the variable shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that can vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of foliage.

Thelypteris mutabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
