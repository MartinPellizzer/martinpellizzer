Trichomanes membranaceum

Trichomanes membranaceum, commonly known as the membranous water fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, translucent fronds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree bark. Its fronds are typically only a few centimeters long and have a leathery texture.
