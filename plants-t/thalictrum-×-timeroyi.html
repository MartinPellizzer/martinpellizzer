Thalictrum × timeroyi

Thalictrum × timeroyi, commonly known as the hybrid meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, usually yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It is native to regions of Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.

Thalictrum × timeroyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
