Trichosalpinx reflexa

Trichosalpinx reflexa, commonly known as the red spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers with a spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and wasps.

Trichosalpinx reflexa is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and colorful display.
