Tephrosia astragaloides

Tephrosia astragaloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow to greenish in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous narrow, lance-shaped leaflets.

Tephrosia astragaloides is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
