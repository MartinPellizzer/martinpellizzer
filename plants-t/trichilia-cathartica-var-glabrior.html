Trichilia cathartica var. glabrior

Trichilia cathartica var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth trichilia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and simple, alternate leaves. It produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that are followed by woody, three-lobed fruits.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses including the treatment of digestive ailments and skin conditions.
