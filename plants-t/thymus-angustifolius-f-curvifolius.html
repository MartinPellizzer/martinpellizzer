Thymus angustifolius f. curvifolius

Thymus angustifolius f. curvifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved thyme with curled leaves, is a cultivar of the thyme plant characterized by its distinctive, curled foliage.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in the summer, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and fragrant leaves make it a popular choice for both decorative and practical purposes.
