Thymus × maeoticus

Thymus × maeoticus, commonly known as the Crimean thyme, is a hybrid plant native to the Black Sea region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with creeping stems and small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its essential oils contain thymol and carvacrol, giving it medicinal and culinary uses.
