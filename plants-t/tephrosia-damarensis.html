Tephrosia damarensis

Tephrosia damarensis, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or pale green. Its root system is known for containing compounds with insecticidal properties.

Tephrosia damarensis is often grown as a forage crop and for its potential medicinal uses.
