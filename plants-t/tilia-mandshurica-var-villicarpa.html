Tilia mandshurica var. villicarpa

Tilia mandshurica var. villicarpa, commonly known as the Manchurian lime or Siberian lime, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.

The bark is smooth and greyish, becoming fissured with age.
