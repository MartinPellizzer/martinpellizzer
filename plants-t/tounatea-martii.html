Tounatea martii

Tounatea martii, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which resemble spiders due to their shape and coloration. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Tounatea martii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded forest environments.
