Tectaria peralata

Tectaria peralata, commonly known as the silver shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, leathery, shield-shaped fronds that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are usually evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Tectaria peralata is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks, showcasing its adaptability to epiphytic conditions.
