Tithymalus Officinarum

Tithymalus officinarum is a small yet striking flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This species is characterized by its delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are finely divided and often used in traditional herbal medicine. Despite its modest size, Tithymalus officinarum holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
