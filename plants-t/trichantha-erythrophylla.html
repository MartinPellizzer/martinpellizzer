Trichantha erythrophylla

Trichantha erythrophylla, commonly known as the red-leaf trichantha, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features delicate, clustered leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in spring.

Trichantha erythrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
