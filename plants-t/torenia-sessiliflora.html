Torenia sessiliflora

Torenia sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered morning glory, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, blooming throughout the year. Torenia sessiliflora thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
