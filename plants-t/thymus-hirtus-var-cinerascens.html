Thymus hirtus var. cinerascens

Thymus hirtus var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy foliage and small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils, making it useful in culinary and medicinal applications.

It is also valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
