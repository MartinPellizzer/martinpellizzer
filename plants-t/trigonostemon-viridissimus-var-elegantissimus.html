Trigonostemon viridissimus var. elegantissimus

Trigonostemon viridissimus var. elegantissimus, commonly known as the elegant greenleaf maple, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by ornamental, winged fruits. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
