Tripteris petiolata

Tripteris petiolata, commonly known as the leafy fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
