Tamarix gallica var. chinensis

Tamarix gallica var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese tamarisk, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with greyish-green foliage and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces pink to red flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resistance to drought and poor soil conditions.

Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in saline and alkaline soils.
