Torenia calcarata

Torenia calcarata, commonly known as the spurred torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white. Torenia calcarata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooming habit.
