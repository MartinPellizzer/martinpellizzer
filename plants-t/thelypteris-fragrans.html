Thelypteris fragrans

Thelypteris fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are often fragrant when crushed. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of sporangia on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris fragrans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
