Thelypteris distincta

Thelypteris distincta, commonly known as the distinct fern, is a species of polypod fern native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the undersides of the fronds.

Thelypteris distincta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
