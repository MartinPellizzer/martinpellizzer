Terminalia telfaireana

Terminalia telfaireana, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
