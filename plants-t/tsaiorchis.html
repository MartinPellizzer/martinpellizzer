Tsaiorchis

Tsaiorchis, commonly known as the "Chinese bellflower," is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet or blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Tsaiorchis has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.
