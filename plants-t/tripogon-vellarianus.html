Tripogon vellarianus

Tripogon vellarianus, commonly known as the Vellariana grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

It is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its fine, delicate appearance.
