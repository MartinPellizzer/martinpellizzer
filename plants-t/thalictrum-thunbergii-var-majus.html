Thalictrum thunbergii var. majus

Thalictrum thunbergii var. majus, commonly known as the showy meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in partial to full shade with moist, well-drained soil. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and showy, spire-like inflorescences.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
