Teuscheria horichiana

Teuscheria horichiana, commonly known as the Horichiana lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can last several weeks in the spring.
