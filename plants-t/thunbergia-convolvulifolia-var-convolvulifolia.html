Thunbergia convolvulifolia var. convolvulifolia

Thunbergia convolvulifolia var. convolvulifolia, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological studies.
