Thymus micromerioides

Thymus micromerioides, commonly known as the small-leaved thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves and flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses.

Thymus micromerioides is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and as a ground cover.
