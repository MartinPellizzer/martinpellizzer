Thrinax elegantissima

Thrinax elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean islands, including Cuba, Jamaica, and the Bahamas. This palm is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and feather-like, pinnate leaves that give it an elegant appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into dark blue berries, which are edible for some bird species.

It thrives in coastal areas and is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value.
