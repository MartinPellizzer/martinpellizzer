Tricalysia pallens var. pallens

Tricalysia pallens var. pallens, commonly known as the white-flowered flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a white or pale yellow corolla. The plant has pinnate leaves with numerous small, elliptical leaflets, and its bark is smooth and light brown in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
