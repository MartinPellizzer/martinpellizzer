Turbina robertsiana

Turbina robertsiana, commonly known as the Roberts' turban snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Achatinidae and is characterized by its large, spiral-shaped shell. The shell is typically yellowish with a reddish-brown spiral band, providing camouflage in its natural habitat. Due to habitat loss and collection for the pet trade, it is considered vulnerable.
