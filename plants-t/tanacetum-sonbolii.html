Tanacetum sonbolii

Tanacetum sonbolii, commonly known as Sonbolii tansy, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
