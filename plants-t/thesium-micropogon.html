Thesium micropogon

Thesium micropogon, commonly known as the dwarf thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with fine hairs.
