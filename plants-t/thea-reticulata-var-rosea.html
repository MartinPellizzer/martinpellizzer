Thea reticulata var. rosea

Thea reticulata var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered reticulate tea plant, is a variant of the reticulate tea plant native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive rose-pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.
