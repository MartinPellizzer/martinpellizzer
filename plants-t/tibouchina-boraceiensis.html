Tibouchina boraceiensis

Tibouchina boraceiensis, commonly known as the Brazilian flame flower, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Tibouchina boraceiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
