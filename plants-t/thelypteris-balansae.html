Thelypteris balansae

Thelypteris balansae, commonly known as the Balansae shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green and slightly glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in strobila, a feature common in many ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Thelypteris balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
