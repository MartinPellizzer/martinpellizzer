Tragacantha melanocalyx

Tragacantha melanocalyx, commonly known as the black-flowered tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive dark purple to black flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the plant is typically shrub-like in growth habit.

Tragacantha melanocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
