Thalictrum exaltatum subsp. mediterraneum

Thalictrum exaltatum subsp.

mediterraneum, commonly known as the Mediterranean meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. It is characterized by its upright stem and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
