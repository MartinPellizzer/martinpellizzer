Tracaulon arifolium var. lentiforme

Tracaulon arifolium var. lentiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tracaulon, is a unique botanical specimen characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like leaves.

This variant exhibits a distinct leaf morphology, with a narrow, lanceolate shape that sets it apart from other members of the species. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest regions. Its foliage displays a deep green coloration with subtle variegation, adding to its ornamental appeal.

While not widely cultivated, it is of interest to botanists due to its genetic and morphological variations.
