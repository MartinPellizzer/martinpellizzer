Tithymalus Virgatus

Tithymalus virgatus is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It is known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white during the summer months. This species thrives in rocky soils and sunny slopes, often growing in alpine environments. Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and wildflower displays. Despite its beauty, Tithymalus virgatus remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
