Typhonium supraneeae

Typhonium supraneeae, commonly known as the Thai arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as swamps and marshes. Its flowers are clustered in a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a common feature in the Araceae family.

Typhonium supraneeae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
