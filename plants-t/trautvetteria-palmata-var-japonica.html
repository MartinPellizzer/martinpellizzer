Trautvetteria palmata var. japonica

Trautvetteria palmata var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese trautvetteria, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its palmately divided leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its botanical features include hairy stems and inconspicuous, greenish-yellow blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
