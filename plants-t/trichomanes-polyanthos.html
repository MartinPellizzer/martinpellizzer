Trichomanes polyanthos

Trichomanes polyanthos, commonly known as the polyanthus holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows on shaded, moist rock faces or in crevices, thriving in humid tropical environments. Its small, clustered spore-bearing structures are located on the underside of the fronds.

Trichomanes polyanthos is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
