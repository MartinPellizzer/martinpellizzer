Thespesia altissima

Thespesia altissima, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has simple, ovate leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils.

Thespesia altissima plays a significant role in the local ecosystems and has cultural importance in Hawaiian traditions.
