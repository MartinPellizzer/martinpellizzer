Thapsandra Cretica

Thapsandra cretica is a rare and striking plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its unique, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky soils and sunny locations, making it a hardy choice for gardens. Its historical use in traditional medicine adds to its intrigue. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the botanical world.
