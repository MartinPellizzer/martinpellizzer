Trichocentrum orthoplectron

Trichocentrum orthoplectron, commonly known as the straight-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often straight leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.

Trichocentrum orthoplectron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
