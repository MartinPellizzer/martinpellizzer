Trilobulina

Trilobulina, commonly known as the three-lobed leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves.

Each leaflet is typically oval-shaped with a deep, central vein that divides it into three lobes. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, often forming a dense, bushy structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Trilobulina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
