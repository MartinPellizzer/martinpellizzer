Todea

Todea, commonly known as the common todea, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often wind-pollinated. Todea species are typically found in temperate regions and are adapted to a variety of soil types.
