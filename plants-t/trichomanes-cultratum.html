Trichomanes cultratum

Trichomanes cultratum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like fronds that resemble a sword, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

Trichomanes cultratum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
