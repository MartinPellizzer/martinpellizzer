Tephrosia gracillima

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters.
