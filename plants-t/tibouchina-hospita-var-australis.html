Tibouchina hospita var. australis

Tibouchina hospita var. australis, commonly known as the Australian tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal and garden environments. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period, typically from spring to autumn.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
