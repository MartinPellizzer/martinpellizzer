Trifolium fragiferum var. bonannii

Trifolium fragiferum var. bonannii, commonly known as the Bonannii clover, is a variant of the fragiferum species within the genus Trifolium.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which typically consist of three heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or meadows.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
