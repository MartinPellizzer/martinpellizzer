Trifolium macilentum var. dedeckerae

Trifolium macilentum var. dedeckerae, commonly known as the Dedekerae clover, is a rare and distinctive variant of the slender clover species.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves with a pale green hue and delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
