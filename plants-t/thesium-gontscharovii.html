Thesium gontscharovii

Thesium gontscharovii, commonly known as the Siberian starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, star-shaped flowers. Native to the cold climates of Siberia and northern regions, it thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on alpine and arctic flora.
