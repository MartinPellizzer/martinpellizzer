Tridentea gemmiflora var. densa

Tridentea gemmiflora var. densa, commonly known as the dense gemmed trident, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, white flowers in clusters. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

The variety 'densa' is distinguished by its particularly dense foliage compared to other varieties of Tridentea gemmiflora.
