Torricellia angulata var. intermedia

Torricellia angulata var. intermedia, commonly known as the angular torricellia, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive angular leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
