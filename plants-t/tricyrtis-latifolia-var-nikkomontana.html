Tricyrtis latifolia var. nikkomontana

Tricyrtis latifolia var. nikkomontana, commonly known as the Nikkomontana spider orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Japan.

It features delicate, spider-like flowers with long, slender petals and a distinctive, recurved lip. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 inches, with lance-shaped, dark green leaves. Its blooming period occurs in late summer to early autumn, producing clusters of pale yellow to white flowers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
