Tripteris goetzei

Tripteris goetzei, commonly known as the Goetz's tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, palmate leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.
