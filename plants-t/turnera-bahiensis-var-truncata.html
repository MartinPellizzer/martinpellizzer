Turnera bahiensis var. truncata

Turnera bahiensis var. truncata, commonly known as the truncated turnera, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptic, with a slightly wavy margin, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
