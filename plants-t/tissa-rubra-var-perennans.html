Tissa rubra var. perennans

Tissa rubra var. perennans, commonly known as the perennial rose of the genus Tissa, is a flowering plant characterized by its vibrant pink to red petals and fragrant aroma.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features woody stems with serrated, lance-shaped leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes.

Due to its unique botanical traits, it is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
