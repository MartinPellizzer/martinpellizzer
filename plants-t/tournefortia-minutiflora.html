Tournefortia minutiflora

Tournefortia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and clustered in dense inflorescences. It produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

The plant is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
