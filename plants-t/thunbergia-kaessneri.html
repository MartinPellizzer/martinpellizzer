Thunbergia kaessneri

Thunbergia kaessneri, commonly known as the blue shower vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling a shower of blue blossoms. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and a fast-growing habit, making it suitable for trellises or vertical gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia kaessneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
