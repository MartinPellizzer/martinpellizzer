Thelypteris leuconeuron

Thelypteris leuconeuron, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and woodlands. Its rhizomes are often creeping and can form dense mats, aiding in its spread.

Thelypteris leuconeuron is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.
