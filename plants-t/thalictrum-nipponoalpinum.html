Thalictrum nipponoalpinum

Thalictrum nipponoalpinum, commonly known as the Japanese meadow rue, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with palmate leaves that are finely divided. Its flowers are small and clustered in loose panicles, often with yellow stamens and greenish pistils.

Thalictrum nipponoalpinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
