Tibouchina asperifolia

Tibouchina asperifolia, commonly known as the roughleaf tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are rough to the touch, giving it its common name. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually violet or purple in color and bloom in clusters.

Tibouchina asperifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
