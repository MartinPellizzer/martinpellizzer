Thymus gabrielae

Thymus gabrielae, commonly known as Gabriel's thyme, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are aromatic and have been used in traditional herbal medicine.
