Thymus serpyllum f. globosus

Thymus serpyllum f. globosus, commonly known as globular thyme, is a compact, low-growing perennial herb native to Europe.

It features small, rounded leaves that are densely packed, giving it a mat-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and compact growth make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
