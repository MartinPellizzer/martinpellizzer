Triosteum angustifolium

Triosteum angustifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf triosteum, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 2 inches in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, woodland environments and is often found in shaded areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
