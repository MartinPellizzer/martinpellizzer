Tectaria marginalis

Tectaria marginalis, commonly known as the marginal tectaria, is a fern species belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are dispersed through wind and can germinate into new plants under suitable conditions.

The botanical structure of Tectaria marginalis includes a well-developed rhizome and a complex system of vascular tissue.
