Tetraena Rigida

Tetraena rigida is a hardy shrub native to arid regions of Australia. It is known for its small, waxy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is well adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. Its resilience makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects. Tetraena rigida also holds cultural significance among Indigenous communities in Australia.
