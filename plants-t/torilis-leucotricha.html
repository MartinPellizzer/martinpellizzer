Torilis leucotricha

Torilis leucotricha, commonly known as white-haired salsify, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. Torilis leucotricha is known for its edible roots and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
