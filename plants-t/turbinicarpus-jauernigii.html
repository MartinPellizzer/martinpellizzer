Turbinicarpus jauernigii

Turbinicarpus jauernigii, commonly known as the Jauernig's turbinated cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of northern Mexico, specifically the states of Chihuahua and Coahuila. This cactus is characterized by its ribbed, cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered with white to pale blue spines. It produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Turbinicarpus jauernigii is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
