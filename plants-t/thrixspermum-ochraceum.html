Thrixspermum ochraceum

Thrixspermum ochraceum, commonly known as the golden thrixspermum, is a member of the Rutaceae family, characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and aromatic leaves.

Thrixspermum ochraceum is valued in horticulture for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and its attractive foliage.
