Thelymitra epipactoides

Thelymitra epipactoides, commonly known as the epipactoides sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, linear leaves. Its flowers are pale yellow with a distinct greenish throat and are arranged in a loose raceme.

Thelymitra epipactoides is notable for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
