Taraxacum pycnodes

Taraxacum pycnodes, commonly known as the globose dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. Its leaves are typically basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs.

Taraxacum pycnodes is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a short-lived perennial.
