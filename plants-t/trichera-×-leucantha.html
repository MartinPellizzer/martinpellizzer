Trichera × leucantha

Trichera × leucantha, commonly known as the white trichera, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

Trichera × leucantha prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
