Trichocentrum longifolium

Trichocentrum longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, soft hairs, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum longifolium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
