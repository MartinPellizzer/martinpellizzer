Tillandsia azurea

Tillandsia azurea, commonly known as the blue tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped foliage that is often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires regular misting to maintain moisture.

Tillandsia azurea is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
