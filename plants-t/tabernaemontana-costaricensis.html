Tabernaemontana costaricensis

Tabernaemontana costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves and produces berries that are orange-red when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
