Tibouchina catharinae

Tibouchina catharinae, commonly known as the Purple Passionflower, is a striking shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its flowers are tubular with five lobes, and they attract a variety of pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Tibouchina catharinae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
