Taraxacum longicarpum

Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, and the plant is known for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and diuretic effects.

Taraxacum longicarpum is also valued for its role in supporting pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its native habitats.
