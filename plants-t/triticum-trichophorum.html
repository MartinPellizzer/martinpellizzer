Triticum trichophorum

Triticum trichophorum, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and compact growth habit, often found in temperate regions. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. It is typically perennial and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
