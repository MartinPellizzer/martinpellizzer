Thelymitra macrophylla

Thelymitra macrophylla, commonly known as the common spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in open forest and heathland, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, erect stem with a few broad, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive, elongated lip that resembles a spider's body.

It blooms from late winter to early spring, attracting various pollinators with its unique floral structure.
