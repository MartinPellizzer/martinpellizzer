Tropaeolum crenatum

Tropaeolum crenatum, commonly known as the shield leaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, wavy-edged leaves and vibrant yellow or orange flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible flowers and leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Tropaeolum crenatum is a perennial in mild climates but is often grown as an annual in temperate regions.
