Tropaeolum tricolor var. jarrattii

Tropaeolum tricolor var. jarrattii, commonly known as the Jarratt's garden nasturtium, is a vibrant annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, edible flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of peppery-tasting leaves and seeds.

Its rapid growth habit makes it a popular choice for container gardening and garden borders.
