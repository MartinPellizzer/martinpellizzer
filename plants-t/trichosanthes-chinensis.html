Trichosanthes chinensis

Trichosanthes chinensis, commonly known as Chinese snake gourd, is a vine native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant produces large, warty, green fruits that turn orange when mature. Its leaves are heart-shaped with deep lobes, and it has hairy stems.

The plant is used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a vegetable in some cuisines.
