Tibouchina congestiflora

Tibouchina congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.
