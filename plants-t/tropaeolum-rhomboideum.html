Tropaeolum rhomboideum

Tropaeolum rhomboideum, commonly known as the heartleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its seeds are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
