Thomasia prostrata

Thomasia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate thomasia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Thomasia prostrata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
