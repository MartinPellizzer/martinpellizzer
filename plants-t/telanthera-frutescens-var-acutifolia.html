Telanthera frutescens var. acutifolia

Telanthera frutescens var. acutifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved telanthera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
