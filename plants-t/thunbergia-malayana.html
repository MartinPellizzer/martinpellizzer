Thunbergia malayana

Thunbergia malayana, commonly known as the Malay Trumpet Vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Thunbergia malayana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
