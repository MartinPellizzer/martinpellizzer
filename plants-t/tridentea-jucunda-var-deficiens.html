Tridentea jucunda var. deficiens

Tridentea jucunda var. deficiens, commonly known as the slender trident leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, triangular leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
