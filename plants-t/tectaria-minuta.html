Tectaria minuta

Tectaria minuta, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, compact fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces both sterile and fertile fronds. Its leaves are usually pinnately divided with a dense, shield-like appearance, giving it its common name.

Tectaria minuta is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
