Tasmannia brassii

Tasmannia brassii, commonly known as the Tasmanian tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, yellow-orange berries. Its leaves are glossy and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Tasmannia brassii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
