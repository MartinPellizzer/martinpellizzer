Tilesia Baccata

Tilesia baccata is a small, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense foliage and clusters of small, bright red berries that attract various birds and animals. This plant is often found in lowland forests and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a notable species in botanical studies. Despite its modest size, Tilesia baccata plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity.
