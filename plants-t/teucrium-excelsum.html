Teucrium excelsum

Teucrium excelsum, commonly known as tall germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from blue to purple.

Teucrium excelsum is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
