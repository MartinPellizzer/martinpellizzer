Thorncroftia thorncroftii

Thorncroftia thorncroftii, commonly known as the Natal thornveld plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to warm, arid climates.

Thorncroftia thorncroftii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
