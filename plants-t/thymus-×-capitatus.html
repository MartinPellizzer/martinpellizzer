Thymus × capitatus

Thymus × capitatus, commonly known as Spanish thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces pink or violet flowers in compact clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in Mediterranean climates.

Its aromatic properties are attributed to the presence of essential oils, including thymol and carvacrol.
