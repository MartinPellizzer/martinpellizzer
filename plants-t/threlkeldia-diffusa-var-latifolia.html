Threlkeldia diffusa var. latifolia

Threlkeldia diffusa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf threlkeldia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in its natural habitat.
