Taeniophyllum erosulum

Taeniophyllum erosulum, commonly known as the slender taeniophyllum, is a small, epiphytic bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses or terrariums.
