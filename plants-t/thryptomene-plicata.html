Thryptomene plicata

Thryptomene plicata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf thryptomene, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Thryptomene plicata is often found in dry, open habitats such as heathlands and woodlands.
