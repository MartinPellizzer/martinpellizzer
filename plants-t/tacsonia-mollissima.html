Tacsonia mollissima

Tacsonia mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States. The plant is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Tacsonia mollissima is often found in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
