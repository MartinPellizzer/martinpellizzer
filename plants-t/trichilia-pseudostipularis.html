Trichilia pseudostipularis

Trichilia pseudostipularis, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes many species with aromatic wood. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.
