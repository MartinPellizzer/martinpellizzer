Trichipteris nigripes var. brunnescens

Trichipteris nigripes var. brunnescens, commonly known as the brown-stemmed fern, is a variant of the Trichipteris nigripes species.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its dark brown, wiry stems that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to several feet in length, with a deep green coloration. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
