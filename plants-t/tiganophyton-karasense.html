Tiganophyton karasense

Tiganophyton karasense, commonly known as the Karasense grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in sandy or rocky soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikelets. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and has a deep root system for water absorption.

Tiganophyton karasense plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
