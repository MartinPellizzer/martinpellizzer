Tabernaemontana antheonycta

Tabernaemontana antheonycta, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate or elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant appearance and pleasant scent.
