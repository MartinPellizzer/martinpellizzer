Turraea africana

Turraea africana, commonly known as African turraea, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Turraea africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
