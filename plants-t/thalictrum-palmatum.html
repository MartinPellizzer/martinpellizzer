Thalictrum palmatum

Thalictrum palmatum, commonly known as the fern-leaf meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, upright clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, shaded to partially shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.

Thalictrum palmatum is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
