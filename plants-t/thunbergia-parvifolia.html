Thunbergia parvifolia

Thunbergia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trumpet-shaped flowers. Thunbergia parvifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
