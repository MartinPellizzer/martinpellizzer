Tephrocactus Weberi

Tephrocactus weberi is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. Known for its unique appearance, it features a short, thick stem covered in white, woolly spines. This plant thrives in harsh desert conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme environments. Its blooming period brings forth vibrant yellow flowers, adding a touch of beauty to the desert landscape. Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, Tephrocactus weberi is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
