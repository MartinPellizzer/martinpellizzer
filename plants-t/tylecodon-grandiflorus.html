Tylecodon grandiflorus

Tylecodon grandiflorus, commonly known as the orange tree or yellow tree, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Tylecodon grandiflorus is also valued for its resilience to drought and minimal care requirements.
