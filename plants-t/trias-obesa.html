Trias obesa

Trias obesa, commonly known as the fat leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and it is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Trias obesa requires minimal water and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
