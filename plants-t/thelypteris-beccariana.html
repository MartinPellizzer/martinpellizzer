Thelypteris beccariana, commonly known as the Beccarian holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, serrated fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces spores on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
