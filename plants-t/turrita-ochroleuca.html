Turrita ochroleuca

Turrita ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow turricula, is a species of sea snail belonging to the family Turbinidae.

It is characterized by its small, spiral-shaped shell that is typically yellowish in color. The shell has a smooth surface with a faintly ribbed texture, and it often exhibits a subtle iridescence. This species is primarily found in tropical and subtropical marine environments.

Its habitat includes coral reefs and rocky substrates where it feeds on algae and detritus.
