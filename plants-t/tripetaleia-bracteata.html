Tripetaleia bracteata

Tripetaleia bracteata, commonly known as the bracteate tripetaleia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces distinctive bracts that surround its inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
