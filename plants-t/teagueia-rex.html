Teagueia rex

Teagueia rex, commonly known as the royal teagueia, is a striking member of the Aroid family, native to the tropical regions of South America.

This plant is characterized by its large, ornate leaves that can reach up to three meters in length, featuring intricate patterns and a glossy surface. The plant produces dramatic inflorescences with bright yellow spathes and small, fragrant flowers, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires consistent watering to maintain its lush appearance.

Due to its size and aesthetic appeal, Teagueia rex is often considered a showpiece in tropical plant collections.
