Trichomanes shenzhenense

Trichomanes shenzhenense, commonly known as Shenzhen's huperzia, is a rare and endemic fern species native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Huperziaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
