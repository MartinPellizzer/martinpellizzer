Tricyrtis formosana f. amethystina

Tricyrtis formosana f. amethystina, commonly known as the amethystina spider lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to Taiwan.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically lavender to purple in color, with a distinctive spidery appearance. The plant belongs to the Hemerocallidaceae family and is known for its short blooming period, usually in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that add contrast to the showy blooms.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower due to its unique beauty and fragrance.
