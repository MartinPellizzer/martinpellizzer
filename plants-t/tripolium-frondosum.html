Tripolium frondosum

Tripolium frondosum, commonly known as the common tripoly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats due to its drought-resistant nature.

Tripolium frondosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
