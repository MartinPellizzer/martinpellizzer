Tragacantha karamasica

Tragacantha karamasica, commonly known as the Karaman tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

It is known for its ability to thrive in rocky and arid environments, making it an important species in its native ecosystem.
