Trigonella balansae var. sartorii

Trigonella balansae var. sartorii, commonly known as the Sartori's fenugreek, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a small annual herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, lens-shaped seeds that are often used in traditional medicine.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
