Trifolium squarrosum subsp. tunetanum

Trifolium squarrosum subsp.

tunetanum, commonly known as the Tunisian clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically grayish-green and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is occasionally used in ecological restoration projects.
