Tragia tripartita

Tragia tripartita, commonly known as the three-parted tragia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets, giving it its common name.

Tragia tripartita is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
