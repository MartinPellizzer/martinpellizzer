Tissa rubra

Tissa rubra, commonly known as the red tissa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

Tissa rubra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
