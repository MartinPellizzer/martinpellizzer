Turraea nilotica var. glabrata

Turraea nilotica var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth turraea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in axillary inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
