Trigonostemon nicobaricus

Trigonostemon nicobaricus, commonly known as the Nicobar trumpet bush, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and moist forests. The plant is characterized by its distinctive trumpet-shaped flowers, which are usually yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Trigonostemon nicobaricus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
