Toddalia unifoliolata

Toddalia unifoliolata, commonly known as the single leaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its simple, entire leaves that are typically clustered at the ends of branches. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Toddalia unifoliolata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
