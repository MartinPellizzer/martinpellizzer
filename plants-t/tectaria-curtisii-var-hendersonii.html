Tectaria curtisii var. hendersonii

Tectaria curtisii var. hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson's tectaria, is a fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a leathery texture. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its spores are released from the sori, which are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
