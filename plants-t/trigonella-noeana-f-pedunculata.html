Trigonella noeana f. pedunculata

Trigonella noeana f. pedunculata, commonly known as the pedunculate trigonella, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its seeds are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is noted for its adaptability to harsh environments, making it a resilient plant in its native range.
