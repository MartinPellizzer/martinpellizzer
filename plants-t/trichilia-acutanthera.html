Trichilia acutanthera

Trichilia acutanthera, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, and the tree is often found in moist lowland forests.
