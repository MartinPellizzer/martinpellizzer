Telis hierosolymitana

Telis hierosolymitana, commonly known as the Jerusalem thorn.

It is a spiny shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its woody stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Telis hierosolymitana is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.
