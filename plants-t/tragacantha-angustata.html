Tragacantha angustata

Tragacantha angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that produces yellow flowers and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves. The plant is known for its latex-rich sap, which has been used historically in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ecological and historical significance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental or medicinal purposes.
