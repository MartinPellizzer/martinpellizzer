Tripterospermum japonicum

Tripterospermum japonicum, commonly known as Japanese spurge, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Japan and parts of eastern Asia. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny, elliptical seeds.

Tripterospermum japonicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
