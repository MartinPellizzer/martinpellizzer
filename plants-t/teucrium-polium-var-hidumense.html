Teucrium polium var. hidumense

Teucrium polium var. hidumense, commonly known as the Hidumense germander, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often gray-green in color. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
