Thlaspi Edinensium

Thlaspi edinensium is a small, flowering plant native to the Scottish Highlands. It is known for its striking blue-violet petals and its ability to thrive in rocky, nutrient-poor soils. This species is considered rare and is listed on the IUCN Red List due to habitat loss and climate change. Despite its delicate appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
