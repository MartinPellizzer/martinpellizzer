Triodia rigidissima

Triodia rigidissima, commonly known as the rigid triodia, is a perennial grass-like plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tough, fibrous stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant is well adapted to harsh environments, featuring a deep root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small desert animals.

Due to its resilience, it is often found in spinifex plains and other arid landscapes.
