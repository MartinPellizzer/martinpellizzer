Thelypteris Calva

Thelypteris calva is a lesser-known fern species that thrives in moist, shaded environments. Found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions, it belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae. This plant is characterized by its delicate fronds and intricate leaf structures. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in the world of horticulture. Understanding Thelypteris calva offers insight into the diversity of ferns and their ecological importance.
