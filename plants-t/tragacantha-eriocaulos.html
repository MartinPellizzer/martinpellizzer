Tragacantha eriocaulos

Tragacantha eriocaulos, commonly known as the hairy grass or hairy tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

Tragacantha eriocaulos is valued for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and its potential use in traditional medicine.
