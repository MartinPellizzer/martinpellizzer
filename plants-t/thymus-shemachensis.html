Thymus shemachensis

Thymus shemachensis, commonly known as Shemach thyme, is a flowering plant species native to the Middle East.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, preferring well-drained soils.

Thymus shemachensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional remedies.
