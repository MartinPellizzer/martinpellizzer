Tropaeolum nubigenum

Tropaeolum nubigenum, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a unique plant with a distinctive botanical structure.

It is a perennial herb that features a rhizomatous root system, allowing it to spread and colonize new areas. The plant produces small, delicate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, with a spurred shape that attracts pollinators.

Tropaeolum nubigenum thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
