Trichera subscaposa

Trichera subscaposa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved trichera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It is often found in areas with mild temperatures and moderate rainfall.

Trichera subscaposa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
