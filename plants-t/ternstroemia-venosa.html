Ternstroemia venosa

Ternstroemia venosa, commonly known as the veined ternstroemia, is a member of the Theaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often veined with a pale color. Its flowers are typically small and white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.
