Thelocactus leucacanthus var. schmollii

Thelocactus leucacanthus var. schmollii, commonly known as the Schmollii thorn cactus, is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

The variety is distinguished by its dense, spiny areoles and is a popular choice for collectors due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
