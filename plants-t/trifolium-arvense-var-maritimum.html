Trifolium arvense var. maritimum

Trifolium arvense var. maritimum, commonly known as sea trefoil, is a flowering plant species native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments.

Sea trefoil is adapted to salt spray and can tolerate harsh coastal conditions.
