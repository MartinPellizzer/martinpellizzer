Terminalia laeteviridis

Terminalia laeteviridis, commonly known as the green terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate leaves that turn bright green during the growing season. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.
