Terminalia semlikiensis

Terminalia semlikiensis, commonly known as the Semliki terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.
