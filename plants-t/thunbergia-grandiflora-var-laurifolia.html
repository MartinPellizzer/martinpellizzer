Thunbergia grandiflora var. laurifolia

Thunbergia grandiflora var. laurifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed morning glory, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
