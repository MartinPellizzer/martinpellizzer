Thalictrum alpinum

Thalictrum alpinum, commonly known as Alpine meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and typically grows in rocky or meadow habitats at high elevations. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is characterized by its upright stem and is often found in open, sunny environments.
