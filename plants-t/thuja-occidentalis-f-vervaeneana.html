Thuja occidentalis f. vervaeneana

Thuja occidentalis f. vervaeneana, commonly known as the dwarf Alberta spruce, is a slow-growing evergreen conifer native to Canada.

It is a compact, pyramidal shrub with dense, dark green foliage that remains consistent throughout the year. The plant features small, scale-like leaves arranged in crossed pairs along the stems. It is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This cultivar is particularly resistant to pests and diseases, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
