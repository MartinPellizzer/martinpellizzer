Theobroma capilliferum

Theobroma capilliferum, commonly known as the pink peppercorn, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink flowers and small, peppercorn-like fruits. The plant produces berries that contain aromatic seeds used in culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
