Thoa pendula

Thoa pendula, commonly known as the hanging thoa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that hang from slender stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth texture.

Thoa pendula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
