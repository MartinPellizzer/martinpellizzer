Trachypteris gilleana

Trachypteris gilleana, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a thick, fibrous trunk that stores water and nutrients, supporting its rapid growth. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Trachypteris gilleana plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
