Thelypteris ugoensis

Thelypteris ugoensis, commonly known as the Ugo's shield fern, is a species of fern native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce roots that help anchor it to substrates.

Thelypteris ugoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
