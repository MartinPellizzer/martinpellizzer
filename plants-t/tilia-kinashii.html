Tilia kinashii

Tilia kinashii, commonly known as the Kinashii lime tree, is a species native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a pale green upper surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, appearing in early summer.

The tree produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, contributing to its ecological role in its native habitat.
