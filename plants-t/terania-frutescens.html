Terania frutescens

Terania frutescens, commonly known as the red-flowered terania, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Terania frutescens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
