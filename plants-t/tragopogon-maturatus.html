Tragopogon maturatus

Tragopogon maturatus, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is characterized by its tall, leafy stems and yellow ray flowers that resemble a beard. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and it produces numerous small, achene seeds. Tragopogon maturatus is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun.

Its distinctive appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in its native habitat.
