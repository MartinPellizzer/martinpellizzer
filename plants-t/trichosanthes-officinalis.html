Trichosanthes officinalis

Trichosanthes officinalis, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and elongated, warty fruits. The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
