Tetraplasia stenophylla

Tetraplasia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tetraplasia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its common name.

Tetraplasia stenophylla is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
