Thelypteris

Thelypteris, commonly known as the shield ferns, is a genus of ferns belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

These plants are characterized by their distinctive, often shield-shaped fronds that are typically bipinnate or tripinnate. Thelypteris species are widely distributed in tropical and subtropical regions around the world. They often thrive in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

The genus includes over 300 species, many of which are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value.
