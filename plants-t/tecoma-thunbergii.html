Tecoma thunbergii

Tecoma thunbergii, commonly known as the yellow bells, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Tecoma thunbergii is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
