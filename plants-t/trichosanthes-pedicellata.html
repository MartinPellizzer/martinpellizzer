Trichosanthes pedicellata

Trichosanthes pedicellata, commonly known as the horned melon or christmas apple, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It produces large, spiky, green fruits that turn orange when mature. The plant has lobed, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant aroma. Its fruit is edible, though the skin is tough and the seeds are often removed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses in some regions.
