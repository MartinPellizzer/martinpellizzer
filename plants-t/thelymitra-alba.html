Thelymitra alba

Thelymitra alba, commonly known as the white sun-orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, stocky rhizome and a single, erect, leafless stem. The plant produces a single, showy white flower with a distinctive, elongated labellum. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open forests and heathlands.

Thelymitra alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a member of the Orchidaceae family.
