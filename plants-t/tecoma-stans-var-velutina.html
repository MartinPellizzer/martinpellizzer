Tecoma stans var. velutina

Tecoma stans var. velutina, commonly known as velvet trumpetbush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the branches. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a velvety texture that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
