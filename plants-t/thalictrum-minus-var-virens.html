Thalictrum minus var. virens

Thalictrum minus var. virens, commonly known as green meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous slender, branched stems.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their delicate appearance.
