Trachelospermum laurifolium

Trachelospermum laurifolium, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, leathery leaves that resemble those of the laurel tree. The plant produces fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is a climbing vine that can be trained on trellises or fences.

Trachelospermum laurifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
