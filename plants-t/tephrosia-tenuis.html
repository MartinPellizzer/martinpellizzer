Tephrosia tenuis

Tephrosia tenuis, commonly known as slender tephrosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, trifoliate leaves. It produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its seeds are enclosed in pods.

Tephrosia tenuis is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
