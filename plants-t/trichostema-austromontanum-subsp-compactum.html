Trichostema austromontanum subsp. compactum

Trichostema austromontanum subsp.

compactum, commonly known as the compact bluecurls, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to California. It is characterized by its low-growing, compact form and produces showy, blue to violet flowers in late spring.

The plant has opposite, linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, typically found in dry, rocky habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
