Thymus × porcii var. calvariensis

Thymus × porcii var. calvariensis, commonly known as the Calvaria thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, aromatic leaves. The leaves are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine, hairy textures that help reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
