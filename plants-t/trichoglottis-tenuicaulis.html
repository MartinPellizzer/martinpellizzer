Trichoglottis tenuicaulis

Trichoglottis tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant has a short, erect stem with a few to several leaves that are elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, arranged in a loose inflorescence.

It is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
