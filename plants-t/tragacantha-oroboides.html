Tragacantha oroboides

Tragacantha oroboides, commonly known as the Ethiopian tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod fruits containing several seeds.

It is valued for its mucilaginous properties, making it useful in traditional medicine and as a natural thickening agent.
