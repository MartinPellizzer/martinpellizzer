Tephrocactus setiger f. dispar

Tephrocactus setiger f. dispar, commonly known as the spiny barrel cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, sharp spines. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise spiky appearance. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to arid environments.

The f. dispar form is distinguished by its shorter and more compact growth compared to the typical form of Tephrocactus setiger.
