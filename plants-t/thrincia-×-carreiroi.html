Thrincia × carreiroi

Thrincia × carreiroi, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Thrincia lutea and Thrincia flaccida, two native South American species. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Thrincia × carreiroi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
