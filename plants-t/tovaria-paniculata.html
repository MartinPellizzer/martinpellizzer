Tovaria paniculata

Tovaria paniculata, commonly known as the yellow panic grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces panicles of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Tovaria paniculata is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
