Tryonia myriophylla

Tryonia myriophylla, commonly known as the featherleaf lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
