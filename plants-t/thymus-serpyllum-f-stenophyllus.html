Thymus serpyllum f. stenophyllus

Thymus serpyllum f. stenophyllus, commonly known as narrow-leaved thyme, is a subspecies of the common thyme plant.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its aromatic leaves and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
