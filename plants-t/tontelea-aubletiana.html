Tontelea aubletiana

Tontelea aubletiana, commonly known as the African orchid or the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It has a slender, erect stem with narrow, linear leaves.

Tontelea aubletiana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
