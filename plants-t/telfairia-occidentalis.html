Telfairia occidentalis

Telfairia occidentalis, commonly known as African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1.5 meters tall, with alternate, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red, fleshy berries containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its edible fruits.

Telfairia occidentalis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties and nutritional content.
