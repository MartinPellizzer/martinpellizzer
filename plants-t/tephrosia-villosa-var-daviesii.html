Tephrosia villosa var. daviesii

Tephrosia villosa var. daviesii, commonly known as the woolly tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or savannas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
