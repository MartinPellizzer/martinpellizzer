Teucrium Polium Var. Clapae

Teucrium polium var. clapae is a lesser-known member of the Lamiaceae family known for its aromatic leaves and compact growth habit. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its small purple flowers appear in summer, adding a touch of color to gardens and natural landscapes. Often used in traditional medicine, it possesses a range of therapeutic properties. Despite its benefits, it remains underappreciated compared to more common herbs in the same family.
