Trouettia intermedia

Trouettia intermedia, commonly known as the African lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Trouettia intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
