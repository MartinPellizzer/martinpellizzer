Tamarix jimenezii

Tamarix jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its feathery, gray-green foliage and small, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in dry, saline soils. Its wood is lightweight and resistant to decay, making it useful for construction and fuel.

Tamarix jimenezii plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
