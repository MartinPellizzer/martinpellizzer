Torenia exappendiculata

Torenia exappendiculata, commonly known as the velvetleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to violet. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a garden plant.

Torenia exappendiculata is known for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking continuous color.
