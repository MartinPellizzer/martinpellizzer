Trisetaria panicea var. villiculmis

Trisetaria panicea var. villiculmis, commonly known as the silky panic grass, is a grass species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs that cover the leaves and inflorescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence is a panicle, which is a branched cluster of spikelets.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate, flowing appearance.
