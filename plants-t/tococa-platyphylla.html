Tococa platyphylla

Tococa platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf tococa, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots and leaves are traditionally used in South American folk medicine for their purported health benefits.
