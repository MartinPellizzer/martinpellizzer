Teline linifolia var. latifolia

Teline linifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf teline, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height, often branching towards the upper part. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a hairy surface, and are arranged alternately along the stem. Small, white flowers appear in clusters during the summer months, contributing to its ornamental value.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
