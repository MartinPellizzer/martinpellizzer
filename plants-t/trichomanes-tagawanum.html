Trichomanes tagawanum

Trichomanes tagawanum, commonly known as the Philippine huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant exhibits a rosette growth form, with the fronds arranged in a circular pattern around the central stem. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

Trichomanes tagawanum is native to the Philippines and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
