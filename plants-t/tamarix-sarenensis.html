Tamarix sarenensis

Tamarix sarenensis, commonly known as the Sareni tamarisk, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and clusters of pink or reddish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is well adapted to harsh, dry environments, with deep roots that help it survive in nutrient-poor soils. Its wood is dense and resistant to decay, making it useful for construction and fuel.

Tamarix sarenensis plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
