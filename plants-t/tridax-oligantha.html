Tridax oligantha

Tridax oligantha, commonly known as the smallleaf tridax, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in clusters, and attract various pollinators.

Tridax oligantha is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some areas.
