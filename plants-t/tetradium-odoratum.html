Tetradium odoratum

Tetradium odoratum, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant wood. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are leathery and dark green in color. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries.

The tree is valued for its durable and scented timber, which has been used in traditional Chinese medicine and perfumery.
