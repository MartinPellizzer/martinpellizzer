Turbinicarpus beguinii subsp. senilis

Turbinicarpus beguinii subsp.

senilis, commonly known as the Senile Turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its short, rounded stems that often become woody with age.

The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a prized addition to collections of succulent enthusiasts.
