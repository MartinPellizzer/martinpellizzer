Tomostima reptans

Tomostima reptans, commonly known as the creeping tomostima, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its stems are prostrate, allowing it to spread along the ground, and it often forms dense mats in its natural habitat.
