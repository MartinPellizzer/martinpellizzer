Tragium buchii

Tragium buchii, commonly known as the Buchii tragus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, leaf-like bracts that surround its small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Tragium buchii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive with minimal water.
