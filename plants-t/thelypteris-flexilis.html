Thelypteris flexilis

Thelypteris flexilis, commonly known as the flexible water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that often have a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along streams. Its rhizomes are usually creeping and help in the spread of the plant.

Thelypteris flexilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
