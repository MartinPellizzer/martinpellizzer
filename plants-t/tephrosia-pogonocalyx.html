Tephrosia pogonocalyx

Tephrosia pogonocalyx, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
