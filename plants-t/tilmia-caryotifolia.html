Tilmia caryotifolia

Tilmia caryotifolia, commonly known as the tree of life, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Tilmia caryotifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
