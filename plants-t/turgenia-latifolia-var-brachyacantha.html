Turgenia latifolia var. brachyacantha

Turgenia latifolia var. brachyacantha, commonly known as the broad-leaved turgenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
