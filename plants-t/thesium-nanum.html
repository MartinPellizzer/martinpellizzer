Thesium nanum

Thesium nanum, commonly known as the dwarf thesium, is a small, mat-forming plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions. Its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh environments make it an interesting subject for botanical study.

Thesium nanum is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
