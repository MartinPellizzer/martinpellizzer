Tephrosia onobrychoides

Tephrosia onobrychoides, commonly known as African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

Tephrosia onobrychoides is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
