Trichilia montchalii

Trichilia montchalii, commonly known as African snowball, is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwood trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. It produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, resembling snowballs, hence its common name.

The tree is also valued for its timber and has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
