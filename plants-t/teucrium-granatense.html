Teucrium granatense

Teucrium granatense, commonly known as Granada germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Teucrium granatense is also valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
