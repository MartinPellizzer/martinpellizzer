Tricholaena micrantha

Tricholaena micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered grass.

It is a member of the Poaceae family and is native to various parts of Africa. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as savannas and grasslands.

Tricholaena micrantha is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
