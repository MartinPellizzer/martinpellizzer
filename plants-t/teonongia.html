Teonongia

Teonongia, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Central America and parts of the southern United States. The plants are characterized by their vibrant, orange or red composite flowers that resemble sunflowers. Teonongia species typically have hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves.
