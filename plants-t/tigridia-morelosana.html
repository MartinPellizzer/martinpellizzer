Tigridia morelosana

Tigridia morelosana, commonly known as the Morelos tiger flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble miniature tiger stripes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing vibrant red or orange hues with contrasting black markings.

Tigridia morelosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
