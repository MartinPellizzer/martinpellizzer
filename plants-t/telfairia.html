Telfairia

Telfairia, commonly known as African starfruit, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped fruits that are rich in vitamin C and other nutrients. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often used in traditional medicine.

Telfairia is also cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruit appearance.
