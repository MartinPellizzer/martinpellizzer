Tridax scabrida

Tridax scabrida, commonly known as the rough tridax, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that are arranged in clusters. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in various environmental conditions.

Tridax scabrida is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it a common weed in many areas.
