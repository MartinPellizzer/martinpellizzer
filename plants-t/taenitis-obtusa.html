Taenitis obtusa

Taenitis obtusa, commonly known as the broadleaf taenitis, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically arrowhead-shaped, with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Taenitis obtusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
