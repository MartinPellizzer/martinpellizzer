Tripogon jaegerianus

Tripogon jaegerianus, commonly known as the Jaeger's feather grass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its delicate, slender inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight bluish-green hue.
