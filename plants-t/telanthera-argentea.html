Telanthera argentea

Telanthera argentea, commonly known as silver telanthera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and covered in fine, silvery hairs.

Telanthera argentea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
