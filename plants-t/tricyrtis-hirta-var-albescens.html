Tricyrtis hirta var. albescens

Tricyrtis hirta var. albescens, commonly known as the white spider lily, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique floral structure and elegant appearance.
