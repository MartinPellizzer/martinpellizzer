Tragopogon reticulatus

Tragopogon reticulatus, commonly known as the common goatsbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and a feathery, fern-like foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe but has been naturalized in parts of North America.

Tragopogon reticulatus is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils.
