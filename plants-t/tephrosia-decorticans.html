Tephrosia decorticans

Tephrosia decorticans, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional African medicine. Its leaves and seeds contain bioactive compounds that have been studied for their potential health benefits.

Tephrosia decorticans is also valued for its role in soil enrichment and as a forage plant for livestock.
