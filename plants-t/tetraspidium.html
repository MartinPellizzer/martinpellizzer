Tetraspidium

Tetraspidium, commonly known as the golden star, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow or orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a radiate head with both ray and disk florets. The leaves are usually alternate, simple, and often covered with fine hairs. Tetraspidium species are native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and Australia, and they thrive in dry, sunny habitats.
