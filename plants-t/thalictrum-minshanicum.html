Thalictrum minshanicum

Thalictrum minshanicum, commonly known as the Minshanic meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with a slender stem and finely divided leaves. Its flowers are arranged in compound racemes, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils in partial to full shade.

Thalictrum minshanicum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
