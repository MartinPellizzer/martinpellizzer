Tristellateia australasiae f. obtusiuscula

Tristellateia australasiae f. obtusiuscula, commonly known as the Australian star sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact, clustered growth habit. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along watercourses.
