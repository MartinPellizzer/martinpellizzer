Tropilis ruppiana

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Tropilis ruppiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
