Trichachne catamarcensis

Trichachne catamarcensis, commonly known as the Catamarcensis trichachne, is a grass species native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences with narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
