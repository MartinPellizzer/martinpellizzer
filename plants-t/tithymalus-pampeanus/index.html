Tithymalus Pampeanus

Tithymalus pampeanus is a lesser-known flowering plant native to the grasslands of Argentina. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. This species is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
