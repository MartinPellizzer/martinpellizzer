Tetrastigma philippinense

Tetrastigma philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine crossvine, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, compound leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
