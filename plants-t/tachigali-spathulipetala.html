Tachigali spathulipetala

Tachigali spathulipetala, commonly known as the broad-leaf tachigali, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume fruits. The plant exhibits a rounded canopy with smooth, gray bark and is often found in humid lowland forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically yellow or greenish-yellow, while its fruit is a flat, pod-like structure containing several seeds.

The species is valued for its ecological role and has limited commercial use due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements.
