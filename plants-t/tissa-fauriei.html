Tissa fauriei

Tissa fauriei, commonly known as the golden thorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky environments.

Tissa fauriei is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
