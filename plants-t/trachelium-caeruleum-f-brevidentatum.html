Trachelium caeruleum f. brevidentatum

Trachelium caeruleum f. brevidentatum, commonly known as the dwarf blue trachelium, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly toothed margin, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, sky-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
