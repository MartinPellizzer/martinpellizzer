Tmesipteris tannensis var. normalis

Tmesipteris tannensis var. normalis, commonly known as the common tmesipteris, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tmesipteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
