Taraxacum tenuiculum

Taraxacum tenuiculum, commonly known as slender dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and a yellow flower head that develops into a fluffy seed head. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. It is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
