Trigonella schischkinii

Trigonella schischkinii, commonly known as Schischkin's fenugreek, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Native to Central Asia, this species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing seeds that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological and agricultural studies in its native habitat.
