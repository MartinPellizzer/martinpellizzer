Thymopsis thymoides subsp. polyantha

Thymopsis thymoides subsp.

polyantha, commonly known as the polyantha thymopsis, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, aromatic leaves with a dense, fuzzy texture, typical of the Lamiaceae family.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a desirable plant for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
