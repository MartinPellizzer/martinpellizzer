Thunbergia volubilis

Thunbergia volubilis, commonly known as the blue shower vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in ornamental gardens.

Thunbergia volubilis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
