Tragacantha leptocentra

Tragacantha leptocentra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant produces a resinous sap, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive. Tragacantha leptocentra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
