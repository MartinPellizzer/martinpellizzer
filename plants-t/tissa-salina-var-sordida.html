Tissa salina var. sordida

Tissa salina var. sordida, commonly known as the salt marsh sordida, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It typically grows in coastal areas where soil salinity is high, such as estuaries and tidal flats. The plant exhibits succulent leaves that help retain water in arid or salty conditions. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish or pale in color.

It plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
