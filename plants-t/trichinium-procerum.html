Trichinium procerum

Trichinium procerum, commonly known as the slender trichinium, is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, typically arranged alternately along the stem.

Trichinium procerum is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
