Tilia platyphyllos f. sublaevigata

Tilia platyphyllos f. sublaevigata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lime, is a cultivar of the small-leaved lime tree.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale green leaves that are less hairy compared to the typical variety. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in early summer, which are followed by spherical, greenish-brown fruits. It is a deciduous tree that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This cultivar is often valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to urban environments.
