Thelymitra venosa var. cyanea

Thelymitra venosa var. cyanea, commonly known as the blue-streaked sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green leaf streaks and produces showy, violet-blue flowers with a white throat. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. It blooms during the autumn season, usually between August and October.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
