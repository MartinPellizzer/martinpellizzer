Torenia obtusifolia

Torenia obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia obtusifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
