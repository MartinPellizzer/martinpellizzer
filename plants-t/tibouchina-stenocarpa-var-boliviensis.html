Tibouchina stenocarpa var. boliviensis

Tibouchina stenocarpa var. boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian flamebush, is a species native to South America, particularly Bolivia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
