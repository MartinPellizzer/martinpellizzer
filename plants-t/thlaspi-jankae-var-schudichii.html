Thlaspi jankae var. schudichii

Thlaspi jankae var. schudichii, commonly known as the Schudich's pennycress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in disturbed soils and is found in arid and semi-arid regions. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive, slightly pungent aroma.

This variant is notable for its limited distribution and is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique ecological adaptations.
