Teucrium betonicifolium

Teucrium betonicifolium, commonly known as blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered with fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom from summer to autumn. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Teucrium betonicifolium is known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
