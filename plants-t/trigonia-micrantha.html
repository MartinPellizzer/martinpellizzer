Trigonia micrantha

Trigonia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered trigonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.
