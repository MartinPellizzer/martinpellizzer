Thrincia nudicaulis

Thrincia nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked-stemmed thrincia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its simple, unbranched stem. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it blooms in early spring.

Thrincia nudicaulis is a low-growing plant that thrives in well-drained environments.
