Trichosanthes wallichiana var. majuscula

Trichosanthes wallichiana var. majuscula, commonly known as the giant trumpet vine, is a climbing vine native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble trumpets, often reaching lengths of up to 30 cm. The plant has a hairy stem and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It produces orange-red fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
