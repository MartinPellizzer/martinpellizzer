Thelymitra frenchii

Thelymitra frenchii, commonly known as the French's sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. The plant features a single, erect stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow with a distinctive purple or brown dorsal sepal and a white or pale pink labellum.

Thelymitra frenchii is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in spring.
