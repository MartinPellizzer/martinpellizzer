Thrincia nudicaulis subsp. azorica

Thrincia nudicaulis subsp.

azorica, commonly known as the Azorean dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the Azores. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, short flower spike.

Botanically, it is notable for its specialized adaptations to the humid, temperate climate of the Azores.
