Thunbergia parva

Thunbergia parva, commonly known as the dwarf bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia parva is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
