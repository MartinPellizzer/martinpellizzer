Tridactyle unguiculata

Tridactyle unguiculata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots and velvety, dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Tridactyle unguiculata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
