Trichosanthes reniformis

Trichosanthes reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped sponge gourd, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped or kidney-shaped leaves and pale yellow flowers. The plant produces long, cylindrical fruits that mature to a yellow or orange color. It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits.

Trichosanthes reniformis is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which has a sponge-like texture and mild flavor.
