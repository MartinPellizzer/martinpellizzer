Trichilia tenuiramea

Trichilia tenuiramea, commonly known as African shea tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The tree produces a valuable seed kernel used in the production of shea butter. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its ecological and economic importance makes it a significant species in African ecosystems.
