Thalictrum tuberiferum f. lavanduliflorum

Thalictrum tuberiferum f. lavanduliflorum, commonly known as the lavenderflower meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, lavender-colored flowers. The plant is native to regions of Europe and is often found in moist, woodland habitats. Its flowers are typically arranged in panicles and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The foliage and flowers contribute to its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
