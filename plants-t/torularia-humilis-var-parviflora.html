Torularia humilis var. parviflora

Torularia humilis var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered torularia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
