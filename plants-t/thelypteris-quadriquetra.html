Thelypteris quadriquetra

Thelypteris quadriquetra, commonly known as the four-lobed shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Thelypteris quadriquetra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
