Turnera madagascariensis

Turnera madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar turnera, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often palmate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
