Thonandia

Thonandia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Thonandia is often found in moist, disturbed soils in tropical and subtropical regions.
