Trichocentrum microchilum

Trichocentrum microchilum, commonly known as the miniature lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its small, compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces light green to white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Trichocentrum microchilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
