Trichocline cineraria

Trichocline cineraria, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Trichocline cineraria is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
