Typhonium stoliczkae

Typhonium stoliczkae, commonly known as the Stoliczka's arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped inflorescences with a spathe and a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to Southeast Asia.

Its rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.
