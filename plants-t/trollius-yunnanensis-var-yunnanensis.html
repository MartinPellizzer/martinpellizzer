Trollius yunnanensis var. yunnanensis

Trollius yunnanensis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan globeflower, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are broadly ovate to heart-shaped with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained, moist soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
