Thunbergia armipotens

Thunbergia armipotens, commonly known as the arrowroot vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue, violet, or white. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using its tendrils. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia armipotens is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.
