Thymus × moralesii nothosubsp. moralesii

Thymus × moralesii nothosubsp.

moralesii, commonly known as the Moroccan thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This nothosubspecies is noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
