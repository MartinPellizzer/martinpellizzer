Tronicena mirabilis

Tronicena mirabilis, commonly known as the marvelous tronic, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of vibrant colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Tronicena mirabilis thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
