Thelypteris minensis

Thelypteris minensis, commonly known as the Min River holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce roots that help anchor it to the substrate.
