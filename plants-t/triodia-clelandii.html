Triodia clelandii

Triodia clelandii, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry growth habit and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for various desert animals.

Triodia clelandii is well adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low precipitation.
