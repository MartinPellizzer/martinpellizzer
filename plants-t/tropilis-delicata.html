Tropilis delicata

Tropilis delicata, commonly known as the slender false nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the spring.

Tropilis delicata is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
