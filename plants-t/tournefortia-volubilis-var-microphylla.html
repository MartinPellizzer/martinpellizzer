Tournefortia volubilis var. microphylla

Tournefortia volubilis var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually small and pubescent. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically white or pale yellow, attracting various pollinators.

The species is noted for its adaptability to different soil types and its use in ornamental horticulture.
