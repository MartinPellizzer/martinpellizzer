Tridens capensis

Tridens capensis, commonly known as Cape grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a slender, upright stem with alternate, narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant.

Tridens capensis is often found in coastal and inland grasslands, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
