Tephrosia submontana

Tephrosia submontana, commonly known as mountain tephrosia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves with a hairy surface. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the wet season.

Tephrosia submontana is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forest understories or along riverbanks.
