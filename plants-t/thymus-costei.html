Thymus costei

Thymus costei, commonly known as Coste's thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often used in culinary applications due to its fragrant, medicinal properties.

Thymus costei is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
