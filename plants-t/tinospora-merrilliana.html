Tinospora merrilliana

Tinospora merrilliana, commonly known as the Indian moonseed, is a woody climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. It is often found in tropical forests and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
