Typhonium divaricatum var. mottleyanum

Typhonium divaricatum var. mottleyanum, commonly known as the Mottled Snake Plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive yellow or white mottling, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage is not only ornamental but also capable of absorbing toxins from the air, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
