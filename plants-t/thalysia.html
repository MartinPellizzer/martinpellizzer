Thalysia

Thalysia, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments, such as rocky soils and open woodlands. Thalysia has small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a compact, bushy form.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
