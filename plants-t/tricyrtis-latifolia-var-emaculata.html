Tricyrtis latifolia var. emaculata

Tricyrtis latifolia var. emaculata, commonly known as the Japanese violet or spider orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive hooded shape, often adorned with contrasting white or pale markings. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
