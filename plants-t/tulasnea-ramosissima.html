Tulasnea ramosissima

Tulasnea ramosissima, commonly known as the redstem filaree, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright red flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Tulasnea ramosissima thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
