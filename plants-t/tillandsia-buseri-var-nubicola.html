Tillandsia buseri var. nubicola

Tillandsia buseri var. nubicola, commonly known as the Nubian air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically attaching itself to trees or rocks. The plant has dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in its dry habitat. Its leaves are typically gray-green in color, and it produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink.

This variety is well adapted to extreme environments, making it a resilient and unique member of the Tillandsia genus.
