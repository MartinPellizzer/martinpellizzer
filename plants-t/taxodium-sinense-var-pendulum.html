Taxodium sinense var. pendulum

Taxodium sinense var. pendulum, commonly known as the weeping Chinese swamp cypress, is a unique variant of the Chinese swamp cypress.

It is characterized by its drooping branches that create a weeping appearance, giving it an ornamental look. The plant is native to southern China and thrives in moist, swampy environments. It has scale-like leaves that are typically green, and it produces small, cone-like structures.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture and its ability to adapt to wetland conditions.
