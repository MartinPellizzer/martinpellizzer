Tabernaemontana Retusa

Tabernaemontana retusa is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with ample sunlight. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its unique appearance and fragrance make it a popular choice among gardeners and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful care to ensure its health and longevity.
