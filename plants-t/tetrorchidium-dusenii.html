Tetrorchidium dusenii

Tetrorchidium dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's tetrorchidium, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.
