Thalictrum treleasei

Thalictrum treleasei, commonly known as the Trelease's meadow-rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. The plant features slender stems with compound leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in branched inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

Thalictrum treleasei is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local insect populations.
