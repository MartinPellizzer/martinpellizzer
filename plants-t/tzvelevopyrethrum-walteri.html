Tzvelevopyrethrum walteri

Tzvelevopyrethrum walteri, commonly known as the Walter's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide, which is used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a popular choice among gardeners and conservationists.
