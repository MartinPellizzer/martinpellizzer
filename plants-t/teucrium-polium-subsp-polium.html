Teucrium polium subsp. polium

Teucrium polium subsp.

polium, commonly known as wild thyme or summer thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, small, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been historically valued for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
