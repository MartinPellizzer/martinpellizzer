Thelypteris resinosofoetida

Thelypteris resinosofoetida, commonly known as the resinous stink fern, is a species of fern characterized by its unique aromatic properties.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant is distinguished by its dark green, leathery fronds that emit a strong, resinous odor when crushed. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
