Trachelospermum asiaticum var. oblanceolatum

Trachelospermum asiaticum var. oblanceolatum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy green appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

This plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
