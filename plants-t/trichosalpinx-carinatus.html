Trichosalpinx carinatus

Trichosalpinx carinatus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which are often clustered along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Trichosalpinx carinatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
