Trichosanthes cucumerina var. anguina

Trichosanthes cucumerina var. anguina, commonly known as the horned melon or African horned cucumber, is a tropical vine native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, green fruit that resembles a horned melon. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The fruit is typically harvested when it turns orange or red, and it has a crisp, juicy texture with a slightly bitter taste.

It is often used in local cuisines and has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
