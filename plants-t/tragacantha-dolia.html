Tragacantha dolia

Tragacantha dolia, commonly known as the Persian gum tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, inflated pods containing seeds.
