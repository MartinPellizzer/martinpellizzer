Tolumnia × berenyce

Tolumnia × berenyce, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Tolumnia alba and Tolumnia grandiflora.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Tolumnia × berenyce is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
