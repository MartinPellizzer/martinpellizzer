Termontis tricolor

Termontis tricolor, commonly known as the three-color fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored foliage.

Its leaves exhibit a striking combination of green, yellow, and red hues, making it visually distinctive. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. The plant's fronds are delicate and feathery, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its rarity, Termontis tricolor is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
