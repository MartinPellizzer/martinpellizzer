Tabernaemontana ochrascens

Tabernaemontana ochrascens, commonly known as the yellow star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in ornamental gardening due to their pleasant scent and aesthetic appeal.
