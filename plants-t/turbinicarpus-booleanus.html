Turbinicarpus booleanus

Turbinicarpus booleanus, commonly known as the boolean turbinated cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate, it is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
