Taraxacum vitelliniforme

Taraxacum vitelliniforme, commonly known as the yellow starwort, is a species of dandelion native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and deeply lobed, serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs in the spring and early summer, and it produces a distinctive seed head with feathery seeds.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
