Torenia courtallensis

Torenia courtallensis, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia courtallensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
