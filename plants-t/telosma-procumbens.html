Telosma procumbens

Telosma procumbens, commonly known as the creeping telosma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a succulent vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, moist areas.

Telosma procumbens is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
