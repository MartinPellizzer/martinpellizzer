Taraxacum leptoceras

Taraxacum leptoceras, commonly known as the slenderleaf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and small, basal leaves that are narrow and linear. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy white seed heads, typical of the dandelion genus. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across temperate regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered a common weed in many areas.
