Trichera campestris

Trichera campestris, commonly known as the field trichera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across open habitats. Native to temperate regions of the Americas, it thrives in disturbed soils and sunny environments.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in meadows and along roadsides.
