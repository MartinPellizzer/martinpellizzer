Taxanthema

Taxanthema, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its succulent, mat-forming growth habit, with small, fleshy leaves and showy, often pink or red flowers. Taxanthema species are typically found in sandy or rocky soils across the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
