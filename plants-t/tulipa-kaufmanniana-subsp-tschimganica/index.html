Tulipa Kaufmanniana Subsp. Tschimganica

Tulipa kaufmanniana subsp. tschimganica is a striking bulbous perennial known for its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers. This subspecies originates from the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its blooms, often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow, add a bold splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. The plant is prized for its resilience and ability to naturalize over time. With its historical significance in horticulture, it remains a favorite among bulb enthusiasts worldwide.
