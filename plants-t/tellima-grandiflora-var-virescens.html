Tellima grandiflora var. virescens

Tellima grandiflora var. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered tellima, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which is a distinguishing characteristic of this variety. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside habitats.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
