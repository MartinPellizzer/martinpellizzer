Tabernaemontana alternifolia

Tabernaemontana alternifolia, commonly known as the Madagascar star flower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Tabernaemontana alternifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
