Tubocytisus

Tubocytisus, commonly known as the silverleaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, dark berries. The plant has small, simple leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by the berries.

Tubocytisus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
