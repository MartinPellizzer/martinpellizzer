Trichodiadema hirsutum

Trichodiadema hirsutum, commonly known as the woolly ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in fine, white hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
