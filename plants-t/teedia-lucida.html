Teedia lucida

Teedia lucida, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in clusters and are typically visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Teedia lucida is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
