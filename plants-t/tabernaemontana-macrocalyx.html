Tabernaemontana macrocalyx

Tabernaemontana macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a popular ornamental species. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Tabernaemontana macrocalyx is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and is used in both landscaping and traditional medicine.
