Telis calliceras

Telis calliceras, commonly known as the red-flowered telis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun and is adapted to arid environments.

Telis calliceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
