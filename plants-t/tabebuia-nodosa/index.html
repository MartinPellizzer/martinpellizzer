Tabebuia Nodosa

Tabebuia nodosa, also known as the pink trumpet tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. Its most notable feature is its vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The tree is widely appreciated for its ornamental value and is often planted in gardens and public spaces. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. With its unique appearance and ecological benefits, Tabebuia nodosa remains a popular choice for landscaping and conservation efforts.
