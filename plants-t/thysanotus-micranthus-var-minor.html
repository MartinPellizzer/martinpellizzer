Thysanotus micranthus var. minor

Thysanotus micranthus var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered starflower, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fragrant, star-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and attractive blooms, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
