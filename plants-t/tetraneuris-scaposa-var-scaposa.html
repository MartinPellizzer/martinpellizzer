Tetraneuris scaposa var. scaposa

Tetraneuris scaposa var. scaposa, commonly known as the narrowleaf tetraneuris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
