Trichosanthes grandiflora

Trichosanthes grandiflora, commonly known as the giant bitter melon, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and striking yellow flowers with orange throats. The plant produces elongated, warty fruits that mature to a yellowish-orange color. It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins.
