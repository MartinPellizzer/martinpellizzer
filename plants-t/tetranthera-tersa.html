Tetranthera tersa

Tetranthera tersa, commonly known as the slender tetranthera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is often found in grasslands and open habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its overall grass-like appearance.

Tetranthera tersa is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
