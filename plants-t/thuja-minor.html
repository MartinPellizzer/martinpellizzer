Thuja minor

Thuja minor, commonly known as American arborvitae, is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern North America.

It features scale-like leaves arranged in flattened sprays, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It is often used in landscaping for its dense foliage and ability to form hedges.

Thuja minor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
