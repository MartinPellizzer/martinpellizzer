Tephrosia purpurea subsp. purpurea

Tephrosia purpurea subsp.

purpurea, commonly known as purple tephrosia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces clusters of small, showy purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with slender, pointed leaflets that give it a delicate appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has also been used in traditional medicine.
