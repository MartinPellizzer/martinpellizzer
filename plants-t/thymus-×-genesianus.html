Thymus × genesianus

Thymus × genesianus, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant, pink to lavender flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has aromatic, woody stems and narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, are valued for their medicinal and culinary uses.
