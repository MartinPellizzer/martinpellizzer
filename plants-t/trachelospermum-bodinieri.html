Trachelospermum bodinieri

Trachelospermum bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to China and Taiwan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its ability to climb and its persistent flowering make it a popular choice for trellises and arbors.
