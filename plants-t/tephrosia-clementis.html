Tephrosia clementis

Tephrosia clementis, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing flat, dark brown seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Tephrosia clementis is also valued for its potential use in soil improvement and as a forage crop.
