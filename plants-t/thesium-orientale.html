Thesium orientale

Thesium orientale, commonly known as the golden torch or yellow torch, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange inflorescence that resembles a torch, which is composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks.

Thesium orientale is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking visual display.
