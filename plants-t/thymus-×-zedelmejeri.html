Thymus × zedelmejeri

Thymus × zedelmejeri, commonly known as Zedelmejer's thyme, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage.
