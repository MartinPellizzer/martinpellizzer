Tachigali schultesiana

Tachigali schultesiana, commonly known as the pink tachigali, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
