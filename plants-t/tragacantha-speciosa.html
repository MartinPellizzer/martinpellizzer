Tragacantha speciosa

Tragacantha speciosa, commonly known as the showy tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and its roots are known for their medicinal properties. Tragacantha speciosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in traditional medicine.
