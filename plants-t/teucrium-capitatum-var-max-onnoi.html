Teucrium capitatum var. max-onnoi

Teucrium capitatum var. max-onnoi, commonly known as the Cape York teucrium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of northern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas with a warm, humid climate. Its botanical features include glandular hairs on the leaves and a compact, upright growth habit.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
