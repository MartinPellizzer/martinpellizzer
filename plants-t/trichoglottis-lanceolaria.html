Trichoglottis lanceolaria

Trichoglottis lanceolaria, commonly known as the lanceolate trichoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, where it thrives in humid and cool environments. Its leaves are linear and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

The flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a faintly colored lip, and bloom during the rainy season.
