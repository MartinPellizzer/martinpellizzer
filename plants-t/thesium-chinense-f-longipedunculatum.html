Thesium chinense f. longipedunculatum

Thesium chinense f. longipedunculatum, commonly known as the long-peduncled thesium, is a succulent plant native to Southeast Asia.

The inflorescence consists of elongated peduncles bearing numerous tiny flowers, which are pollinated by insects.
