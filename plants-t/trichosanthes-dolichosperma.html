Trichosanthes dolichosperma

Trichosanthes dolichosperma, commonly known as the long-seed bitter melon, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant produces elongated, ribbed fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature.
