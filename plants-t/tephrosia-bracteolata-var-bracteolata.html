Tephrosia bracteolata var. bracteolata

Tephrosia bracteolata var. bracteolata, commonly known as the purple tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with elliptical leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
