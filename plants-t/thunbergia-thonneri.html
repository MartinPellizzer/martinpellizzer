Thunbergia thonneri

Thunbergia thonneri, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking blue or purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia thonneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
