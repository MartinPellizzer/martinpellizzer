Thuja occidentalis f. robusta

Thuja occidentalis f. robusta, commonly known as the Eastern white cedar or American arborvitae, is a variant of the northern white cedar.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves. The plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It produces small, spherical cones that release wind-dispersed seeds.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in landscaping.
