Thesium resinifolium

Thesium resinifolium, commonly known as the resinous thesium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, linear leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive resinous scent. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Thesium resinifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
