Trichilia oblonga

Trichilia oblonga, commonly known as African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that enclose a hard seed, which is used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil. Trichilia oblonga plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
