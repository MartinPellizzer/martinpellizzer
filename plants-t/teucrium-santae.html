Teucrium santae

Teucrium santae, commonly known as Saint Helena germander, is a flowering plant native to the island of Saint Helena in the South Atlantic Ocean.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky, coastal habitats. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong aromatic scent.

Teucrium santae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
