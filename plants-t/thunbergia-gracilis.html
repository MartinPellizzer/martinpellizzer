Thunbergia gracilis

Thunbergia gracilis, commonly known as the slender thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
