Thamnochortus kammanassiae

Thamnochortus kammanassiae, commonly known as the Kammanassai sandbush, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Namibia.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with deep roots and reduced leaf surface area.

Thamnochortus kammanassiae is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
