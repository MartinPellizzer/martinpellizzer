Typhonium peltandroides

Typhonium peltandroides, commonly known as the broad-leafed arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
