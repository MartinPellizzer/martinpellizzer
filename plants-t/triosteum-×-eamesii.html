Triosteum × eamesii

Triosteum × eamesii, commonly known as the Eames' triosteum, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and blooms.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and wetland restoration projects.
