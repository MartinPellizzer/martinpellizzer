Thymus schimperi

Thymus schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian thyme, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia and parts of Eritrea.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Thymus schimperi is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
