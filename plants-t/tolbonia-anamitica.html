Tolbonia anamitica

Tolbonia anamitica, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy blooms. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The flowers are usually fragrant and come in a variety of colors, making it a popular ornamental plant.
