Trichilia colimana

Trichilia colimana, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and it is often found in dry, sandy soils.
