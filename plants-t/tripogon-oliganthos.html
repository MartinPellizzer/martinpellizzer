Tripogon oliganthos

Tripogon oliganthos, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grass.

It is a species of grass native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Tripogon oliganthos is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
