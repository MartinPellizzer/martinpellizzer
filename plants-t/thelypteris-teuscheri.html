Thelypteris teuscheri

Thelypteris teuscheri, commonly known as the Teuscher's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a distinctive, finely divided structure. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or near water bodies. Its rhizomes are extensively branched and help in anchoring the plant while absorbing nutrients from the soil.

Thelypteris teuscheri plays a role in maintaining ecological balance by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for small organisms.
