Tagetes erecta

Tagetes erecta, commonly known as the African marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant is an annual herb with a bushy growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Tagetes erecta is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and is also used in traditional medicine.
