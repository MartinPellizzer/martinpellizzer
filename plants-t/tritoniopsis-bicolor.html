Tritoniopsis bicolor

Tritoniopsis bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive bicoloration, with contrasting shades of green and white on its leaves and flowers. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a unique pattern of coloration.

Tritoniopsis bicolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
