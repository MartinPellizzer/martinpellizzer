Tanghinia

Tanghinia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Tanghinia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments.

Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, attracting various bird species.
