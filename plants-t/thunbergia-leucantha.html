Thunbergia leucantha

Thunbergia leucantha, commonly known as the white flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia leucantha is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
