Thymus horminum

Thymus horminum, commonly known as narrowleaf thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, narrow leaves and produces pink to lavender flowers in early summer. The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage, which contains essential oils responsible for its distinctive scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus horminum is also valued for its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
