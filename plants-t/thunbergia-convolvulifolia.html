Thunbergia convolvulifolia

Thunbergia convolvulifolia, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Thunbergia convolvulifolia is also valued for its ability to climb and its showy floral display.
