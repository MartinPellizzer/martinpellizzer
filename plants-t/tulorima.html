Tulorima

Tulorima, commonly known as the sacred lotus of the Andes, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often blooming in the early morning. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid environment. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Tulorima plays an important role in the cultural and spiritual practices of local communities.
