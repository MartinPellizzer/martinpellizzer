Tursenia sinuata

Tursenia sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Tursenia sinuata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
