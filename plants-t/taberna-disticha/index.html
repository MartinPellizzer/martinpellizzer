Taberna Disticha

Two-leaved taberna, scientifically known as Taberna disticha, is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. This unique species is characterized by its distinctive two-leaf structure, which sets it apart from other plants in the Taberna genus. Despite its small size, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem, supporting local biodiversity and contributing to the region's ecological balance. Due to habitat loss and climate change, the plant faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are essential to ensure the continued existence of this remarkable species.
