Tacca flabellata

Tacca flabellata, commonly known as the bat plant or umbrella plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling bat wings. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique, curling structure that resembles an umbrella, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

Tacca flabellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
