Thalictrum Fendleri Var. Wrightii

Fendler's meadow rue, scientifically known as Thalictrum fendleri var. wrightii, is a striking member of the Ranunculaceae family native to the arid southwestern United States. This elegant plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, showy white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in rocky soils and dry environments, often found in desert foothills and canyon walls. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a noteworthy subject for botanists and conservationists. Despite its resilience, it remains a rare and endangered species requiring careful protection.
