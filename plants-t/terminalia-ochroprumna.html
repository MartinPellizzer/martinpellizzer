Terminalia ochroprumna

Terminalia ochroprumna, commonly known as the yellow-fruit terminalia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, lowland areas and is often found in secondary forests.
