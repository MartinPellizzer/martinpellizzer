Trichomanes palmatum

Trichomanes palmatum, commonly known as the palmate water fern, is a member of the family Hymenophyllaceae and is native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically divided into numerous small, ovate segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth or near water sources. Its fronds are usually light green and have a finely serrated margin.

Trichomanes palmatum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in humid, indoor settings.
