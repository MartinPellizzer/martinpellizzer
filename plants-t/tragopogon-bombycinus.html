Tragopogon bombycinus

Tragopogon bombycinus, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of tubular disc florets. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Tragopogon bombycinus is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
