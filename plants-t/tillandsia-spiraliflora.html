Tillandsia spiraliflora

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, often attaching itself to trees or rocks. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale purple, which bloom in the spring.

Tillandsia spiraliflora is well adapted to humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to thrive.
