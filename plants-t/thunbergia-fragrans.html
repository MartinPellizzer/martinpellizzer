Thunbergia fragrans

Thunbergia fragrans, commonly known as the sweet senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow and red. Thunbergia fragrans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
