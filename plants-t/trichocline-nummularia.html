Trichocline nummularia

Trichocline nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, rounded, silver-green leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
