Tabernaemontana longipes

Tabernaemontana longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed angel's trumpet, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap and toxic compounds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
