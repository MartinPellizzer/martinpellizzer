Tulipa karamanica

Tulipa karamanica, commonly known as the Karaman tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 50 centimeters and produces showy blooms in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This tulip is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
