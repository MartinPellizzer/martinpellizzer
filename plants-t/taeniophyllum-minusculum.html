Taeniophyllum minusculum

Taeniophyllum minusculum, commonly known as the miniature tailed fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It has a compact growth habit with a short, creeping rhizome and delicate, trifoliate leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its unique appearance and manageable size make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a slightly curled appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.
