Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. alenae

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. alenae, commonly known as the Alena's Turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
