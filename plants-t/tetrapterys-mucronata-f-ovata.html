Tetrapterys mucronata f. ovata

Tetrapterys mucronata f. ovata, commonly known as the ovate-leaf tetrapterys, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves with a distinctive mucronate tip.
