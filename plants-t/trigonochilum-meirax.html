Trigonochilum meirax

Trigonochilum meirax, commonly known as the Meirax orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, colorful flowers that are typically pink or purple in hue. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and thrives in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture.

Trigonochilum meirax is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
