Triplaris setosa var. woytkowskii

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like structures. It produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.
