Thyrsanthemum floribundum

Thyrsanthemum floribundum, commonly known as the showy thrysanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a subtle, spicy scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
