Taraxacum numajirii

Taraxacum numajirii, commonly known as the Numajiri dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant produces a distinctive white, spherical seed head that disperses its seeds by wind. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest clearings.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
