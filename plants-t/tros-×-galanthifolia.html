Tros × galanthifolia

Tros × galanthifolia, commonly known as the hybrid snowdrop, is a perennial bulbous plant that belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding white flowers that resemble those of the true snowdrops (Galanthus species). The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves and a short stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Tros × galanthifolia is prized for its ability to hybridize with other snowdrop species, leading to a wide variety of cultivars.
