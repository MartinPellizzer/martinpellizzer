Tovomita grisebachiana

Tovomita grisebachiana, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped inflorescences, which consist of small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in a spherical cluster. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a slight waxy coating.

Tovomita grisebachiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
