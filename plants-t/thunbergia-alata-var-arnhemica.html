Thunbergia alata var. arnhemica

Thunbergia alata var. arnhemica, commonly known as the Arnhem Trumpet Vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant produces heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is a climbing vine that can reach significant lengths. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
