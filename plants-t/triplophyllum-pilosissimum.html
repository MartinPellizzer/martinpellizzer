Triplophyllum pilosissimum

Triplophyllum pilosissimum, commonly known as the silky-leafed triplophyllum, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom during the warmer months.
