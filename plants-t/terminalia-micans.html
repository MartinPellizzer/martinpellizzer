Terminalia micans

Terminalia micans, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lance-shaped leaves. It also has traditional medicinal uses in various African cultures.
