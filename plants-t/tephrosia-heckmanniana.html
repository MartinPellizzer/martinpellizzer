Tephrosia heckmanniana

Tephrosia heckmanniana, commonly known as the Mexican blue bean, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers are usually blue or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
