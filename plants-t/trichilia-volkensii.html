Trichilia volkensii

Trichilia volkensii, commonly known as Volkensii tallow tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmate leaves. The tree produces yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing flat, winged seeds.
