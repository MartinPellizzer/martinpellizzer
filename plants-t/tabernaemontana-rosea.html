Tabernaemontana rosea

Tabernaemontana rosea, commonly known as the pink star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Tabernaemontana rosea is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
