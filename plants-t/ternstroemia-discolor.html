Ternstroemia discolor

Ternstroemia discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered ternstroemia, is a member of the Theaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, China, and the Philippines. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, contributing to its propagation in tropical and subtropical environments.
