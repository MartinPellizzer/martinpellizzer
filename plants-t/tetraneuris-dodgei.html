Tetraneuris dodgei

Tetraneuris dodgei, commonly known as the Arizona tetraneuris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has hairy, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Tetraneuris dodgei is adapted to arid environments and is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
