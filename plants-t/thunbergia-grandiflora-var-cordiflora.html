Thunbergia grandiflora var. cordiflora

Thunbergia grandiflora var. cordiflora, commonly known as the heartleaf thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is a climbing vine that produces fragrant blooms, often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic and ecological value.
