Turetta racemosa

Turetta racemosa, commonly known as the racemose turetta, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth margin and a reddish hue on the underside.
