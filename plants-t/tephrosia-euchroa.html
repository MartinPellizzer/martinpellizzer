Tephrosia euchroa

Tephrosia euchroa, commonly known as the white tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its flowers are often arranged in racemose inflorescences, and it is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological studies.
