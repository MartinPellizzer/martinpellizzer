Tropaeolum lobbianum var. perfection

Tropaeolum lobbianum var. perfection, commonly known as the Perfection Tropaeolum, is a vibrant annual flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its compact growth habit and prolonged blooming period make it an excellent addition to both containers and garden beds.
