Thymus × pilisiensis

Thymus × pilisiensis, commonly known as the Pilis thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Carpathian Basin, specifically found in Hungary and parts of Croatia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green leaves. It produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer.

Thymus × pilisiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardy nature.
