Talisia oedipoda

Talisia oedipoda, commonly known as the Brazilian talisia, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and have a distinctive yellowish-green color with a subtle fragrance.

Talisia oedipoda is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
