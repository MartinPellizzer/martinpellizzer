Tragopogon bornmuelleri

Tragopogon bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Tragopogon bornmuelleri is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
