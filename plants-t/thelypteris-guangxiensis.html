Thelypteris guangxiensis

Thelypteris guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi water fern, is a species of fern native to southern China, particularly Guangxi Province.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its spores are dispersed by wind, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Thelypteris guangxiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat.
