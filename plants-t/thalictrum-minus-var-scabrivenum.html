Thalictrum minus var. scabrivenum

Thalictrum minus var. scabrivenum, commonly known as the roughleaf meadow-rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded areas with rich, loamy soil.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
