Trichomanes debile

Trichomanes debile, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a delicate, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features finely divided, arching fronds with a soft, feathery appearance, typical of the family Hymenophyllaceae. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish. Its small, spore-bearing structures are located on the underside of its leaves, a characteristic feature of ferns.

Trichomanes debile is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant, lacy foliage.
