Thelypteris stenobasis

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a finely toothed margin. Thelypteris stenobasis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream sides.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
