Triptilion spinosum var. diffusum

Triptilion spinosum var. diffusum, commonly known as the spreading spiny triptilion, is a variant of the triptilion genus native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
