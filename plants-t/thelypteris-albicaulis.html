Thelypteris albicaulis

Thelypteris albicaulis, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, pale green to white fronds.

Thelypteris albicaulis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in horticulture.
