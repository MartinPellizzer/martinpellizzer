Taeniophyllum crassipes

Taeniophyllum crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leaved taeniophyllum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
