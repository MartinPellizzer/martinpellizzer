Tillandsia cyanescens

Tillandsia cyanescens, commonly known as the blue tillandsia, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its striking blue-gray foliage, which develops a bluish sheen when exposed to sunlight. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue.

Tillandsia cyanescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
