Trigonella emodi var. himalaica

Trigonella emodi var. himalaica, commonly known as Himalayan fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, adapting to the harsh climatic conditions of its habitat. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to other members of the fenugreek genus.

The species is also valued for its potential nutritional and medicinal properties.
