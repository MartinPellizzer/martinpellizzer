Trema molle

Trema molle, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are soft and velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Trema molle is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
