Thunbergia mildbraediana

Thunbergia mildbraediana, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or violet tubular flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia mildbraediana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
