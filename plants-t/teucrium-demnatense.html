Teucrium demnatense

Teucrium demnatense, commonly known as the Demna Teucrium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or blue, attracting various pollinators.
