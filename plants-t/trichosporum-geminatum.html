Trichosporum geminatum

Trichosporum geminatum, commonly known as the twin-hair moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth and small, leafy shoots that often form a compact structure. The leaves are typically narrow and have a distinct midrib, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. This moss thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found on soil or rock surfaces.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for small invertebrates.
