Tali

Tali, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center, often blooming in spring and summer. Tali is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
