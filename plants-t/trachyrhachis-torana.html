Trachyrhachis torana

Trachyrhachis torana, commonly known as the golden tailed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive golden hue, which bloom during the warmer months. Trachyrhachis torana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
