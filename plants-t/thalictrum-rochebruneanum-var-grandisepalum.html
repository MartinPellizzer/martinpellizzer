Thalictrum rochebruneanum var. grandisepalum

Thalictrum rochebruneanum var. grandisepalum, commonly known as the large-sepal meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and stream sides. Its flowers consist of numerous sepals that are larger than the petals, giving it its distinctive appearance.

It is a herbaceous perennial that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.
