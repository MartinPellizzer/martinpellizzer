Taraxacum sikkimense

Taraxacum sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust taproot system. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the genus Taraxacum. Its leaves are broadly lanceolate, with a hairy surface and a serrated margin.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forest clearings at elevations above 2,000 meters.
