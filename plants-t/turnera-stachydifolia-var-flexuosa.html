Turnera stachydifolia var. flexuosa

Turnera stachydifolia var. flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible turnera, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners interested in drought-tolerant plants.
