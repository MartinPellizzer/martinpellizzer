Tabernaemontana bovina

Tabernaemontana bovina, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Tabernaemontana bovina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
