Tanacetum leucanthemum var. maximum

Tanacetum leucanthemum var. maximum, commonly known as the giant white fleabane, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces numerous daisy-like flowers with white ray florets surrounding yellow disk florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in meadows and rocky areas.
