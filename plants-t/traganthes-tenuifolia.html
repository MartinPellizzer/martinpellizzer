Traganthes tenuifolia

Traganthes tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf traganthes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces small, pale yellow flowers clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Traganthes tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
