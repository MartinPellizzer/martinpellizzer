Thelypteris membranifera

Thelypteris membranifera, commonly known as the membranous water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are often divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of leaves.

Thelypteris membranifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
