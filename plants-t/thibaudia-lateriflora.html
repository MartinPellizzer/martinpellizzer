Thibaudia lateriflora

Thibaudia lateriflora, commonly known as the late-flowering thibaudia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Thibaudia lateriflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
