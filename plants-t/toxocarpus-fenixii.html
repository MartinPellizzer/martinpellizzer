Toxocarpus fenixii

Toxocarpus fenixii, commonly known as the Phoenix tree, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its unique, palm-like appearance with a single trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by distinctive, winged fruits. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered a vulnerable species.
