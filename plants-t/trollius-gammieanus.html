Trollius gammieanus

Trollius gammieanus, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it is often found in high-altitude meadows.

Trollius gammieanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
