Thymus × sennenii

Thymus × sennenii, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact, mat-like appearance. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
