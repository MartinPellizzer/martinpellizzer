Triaspis speciosa

Triaspis speciosa, commonly known as the ornamental spurge, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, palmate leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Triaspis speciosa is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
