Tragopogon gorskianus

Tragopogon gorskianus, commonly known as the Gorskian goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowering stems rise above them. Its inflorescence consists of a single, showy yellow flower surrounded by pale green bracts.

Tragopogon gorskianus is adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
