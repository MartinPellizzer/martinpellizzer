Tabernaemontana populifolia

Tabernaemontana populifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often heart-shaped at the base. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
