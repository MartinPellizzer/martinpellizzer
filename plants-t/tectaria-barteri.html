Tectaria barteri

Tectaria barteri, commonly known as the Borneo shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests. Its rhizome is thick and creeping, supporting the growth of its large, lobed leaves.

Tectaria barteri plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various small organisms.
