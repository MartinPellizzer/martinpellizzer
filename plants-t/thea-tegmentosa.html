Thea tegmentosa

Thea tegmentosa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Thea tegmentosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
