Trigonella corniculata proles elatior

Trigonella corniculata proles elatior, commonly known as the greater horned vetch, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. It has small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
