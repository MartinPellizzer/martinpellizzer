Thelymitra alpicola

Thelymitra alpicola, commonly known as the alpine rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the alpine regions of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and features small, often pale green or brownish leaves. The plant produces a single, slender flower spike bearing a single, showy orchid flower with a distinctive, often purple or brownish coloration. Thelymitra alpicola is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is typically found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ecologically important component of alpine ecosystems.
