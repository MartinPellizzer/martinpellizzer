Thymus ekimii

Thymus ekimii, commonly known as Ekin's thyme, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its aromatic properties make it valuable in both culinary and medicinal applications.

Thymus ekimii is also appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens.
