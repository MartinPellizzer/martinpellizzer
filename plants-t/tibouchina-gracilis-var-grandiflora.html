Tibouchina gracilis var. grandiflora

Tibouchina gracilis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered tibouchina, is a striking shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red, often with a distinctive white or pale throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Tibouchina gracilis var. grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
