Tibouchina parviflora

Tibouchina parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered tibouchina, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a distinctive white or yellow center. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Tibouchina parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscaping.
