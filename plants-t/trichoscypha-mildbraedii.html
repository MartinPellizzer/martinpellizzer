Trichoscypha mildbraedii

Trichoscypha mildbraedii, commonly known as the African silk floss tree, is a member of the Malvaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
