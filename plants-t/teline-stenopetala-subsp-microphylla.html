Teline stenopetala subsp. microphylla

Teline stenopetala subsp.

microphylla, commonly known as the narrow-petaled teline, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and delicate, pale blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
