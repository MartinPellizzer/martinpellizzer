Tectaria subtriloba

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are divided into three lobes, giving it a unique appearance. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thick, protective layer of sori, which are the spore-producing structures.

Tectaria subtriloba is found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in the ecosystem as both a pioneer species and a habitat provider for various organisms.
