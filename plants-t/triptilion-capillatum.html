Triptilion capillatum

Triptilion capillatum, commonly known as the slender triptilon, is a small, herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Tripteriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or damp meadows.
