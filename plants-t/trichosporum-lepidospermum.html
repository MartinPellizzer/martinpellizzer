Trichosporum lepidospermum

Trichosporum lepidospermum, commonly known as the silky hair-vine, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, greenish-brown fruits that contain minute seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in open woodlands and along coastal areas.

Trichosporum lepidospermum is adapted to withstand drought and is considered a hardy, low-maintenance plant.
