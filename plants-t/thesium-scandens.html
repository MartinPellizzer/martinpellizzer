Thesium scandens

Thesium scandens, commonly known as the climbing thesium, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that twine around supports. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Thesium scandens is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
