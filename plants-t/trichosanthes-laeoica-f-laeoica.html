Trichosanthes laeoica f. laeoica

Trichosanthes laeoica f. laeoica, commonly known as the rough gourd, is a variant of the Trichosanthes genus native to southern China.

It is a climbing herbaceous vine characterized by its hairy, ribbed stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits that mature to a pale green or yellowish color. The fruit is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it distinct within the genus, with its rough texture and specific morphological traits.
