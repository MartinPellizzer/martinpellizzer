Trachelospermum auritum

Trachelospermum auritum, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a climbing vine that produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Trachelospermum auritum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
