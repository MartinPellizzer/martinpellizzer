It is characterized by its massive, buttressed trunk and a canopy of broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Its slow growth and longevity make it a significant component of the region's biodiversity.
