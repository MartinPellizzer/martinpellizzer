Tillandsia lindenii var. luxurians

Tillandsia lindenii var. luxurians, commonly known as the velvet tillandsia, is a bromeliad native to the humid forests of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it a unique texture and appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush, green appearance. It is an epiphytic species, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
