Thunbergia oblongifolia

Thunbergia oblongifolia, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking blue-violet tubular flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and foliage.

Thunbergia oblongifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
