Thelymitra tubulina

Thelymitra tubulina, commonly known as the tubular rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodlands or heathlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, delicate blooms during the spring.

Thelymitra tubulina is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
