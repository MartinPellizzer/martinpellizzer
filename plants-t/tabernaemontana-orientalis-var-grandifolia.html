Tabernaemontana orientalis var. grandifolia

Tabernaemontana orientalis var. grandifolia, commonly known as the giant angel's trumpets, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
