Tuberaria variabilis var. vulgaris

Tuberaria variabilis var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common tuberaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cariophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.
