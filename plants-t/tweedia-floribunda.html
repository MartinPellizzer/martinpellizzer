Tweedia floribunda

Tweedia floribunda, commonly known as the African daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a yellow or orange center. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed pods that split open when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Tweedia floribunda is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
