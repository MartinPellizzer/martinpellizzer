Tomoxis virens

Tomoxis virens, commonly known as the greenleaf tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Tomoxis virens is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
