Teucrium pycnophyllum

Teucrium pycnophyllum, commonly known as the dense-leaved germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
