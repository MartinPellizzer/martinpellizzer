Thrixspermum lanatum

Thrixspermum lanatum, commonly known as the hairy thrixspermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Thrixspermum lanatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
