Tectaria trifoliata var. heracleifolia

Tectaria trifoliata var. heracleifolia, commonly known as the Heracleifoliate Tectaria, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pinnate leaves with three leaflets, giving it a trifoliate appearance. The fronds are typically leathery and have a rough, textured surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
