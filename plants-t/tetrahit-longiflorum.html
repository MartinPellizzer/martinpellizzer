Tetrahit longiflorum

Tetrahit longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered tetrahit, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
