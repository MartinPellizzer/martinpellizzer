Toona sureni var. teysmannii

Toona sureni var. teysmannii, commonly known as the Bornean toona, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in the axils of the leaves.
