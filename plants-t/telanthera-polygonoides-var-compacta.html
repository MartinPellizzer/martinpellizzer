Telanthera polygonoides var. compacta

Telanthera polygonoides var. compacta, commonly known as the compact telanthera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
