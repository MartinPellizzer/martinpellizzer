Tithymalus procerus

Tithymalus procerus, commonly known as the tall tithymalus, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

Its root system is fibrous and can be used in traditional herbal medicine for its mild sedative properties.
