Tupistra nganii

Tupistra nganii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Tupistra nganii is often used in ornamental gardens for its striking appearance and seasonal blooms.
