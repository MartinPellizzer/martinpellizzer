Teucrium brevifolium

Teucrium brevifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic stems that often branch near the base. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves with a grayish-green color and a fuzzy texture. Its flowers are typically violet or blue, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
