Tounatea langsdorffii

Tounatea langsdorffii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in pendulous racemes. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain winged seeds, aiding in its dispersal. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Tounatea langsdorffii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
