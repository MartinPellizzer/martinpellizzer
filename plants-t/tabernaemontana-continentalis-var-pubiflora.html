Tabernaemontana continentalis var. pubiflora

Tabernaemontana continentalis var. pubiflora, commonly known as the velvet tetrastigma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
