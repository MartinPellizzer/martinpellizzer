Tanacetum linifolium

Tanacetum linifolium, commonly known as the slender tansy, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in moist meadows and along stream banks.

Tanacetum linifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically in herbal medicine.
