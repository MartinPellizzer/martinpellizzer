Thalictrum Minus Var. Odoratum

Thalictrum minus var. odoratum is a delicate flowering plant known for its fragrant blooms and unique appearance. Native to parts of Asia, this variety thrives in moist, shady environments. Its slender stems and clusters of small, showy flowers make it a standout in woodland gardens. The plant's sweet scent attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to many gardeners around the world.
