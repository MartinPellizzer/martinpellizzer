Tricalysia ngalaensis

Tricalysia ngalaensis, commonly known as the Ngalaensis Trumpet Tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive trumpet-shaped flowers, which are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Tricalysia ngalaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
