Tillandsia yuncharaensis

Tillandsia yuncharaensis, commonly known as the Yunchara air plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to the Andes Mountains of Peru.

It thrives in high-altitude environments, typically growing on trees or rocks in cloud forests. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are usually small and inconspicuous, emerging from the center of the leaf rosette.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular specimen among bromeliad enthusiasts and conservationists.
