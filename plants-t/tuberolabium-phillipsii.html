Tuberolabium phillipsii

Tuberolabium phillipsii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, tubular-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish in color, with a distinctive labellum that resembles a lip.

Tuberolabium phillipsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its delicate appearance.
