Turraea thouvenotii

Turraea thouvenotii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Turraea thouvenotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
