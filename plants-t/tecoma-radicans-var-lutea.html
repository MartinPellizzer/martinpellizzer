Tecoma radicans var. lutea

Tecoma radicans var. lutea, commonly known as yellow shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
