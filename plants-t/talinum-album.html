Talinum album

Talinum album, commonly known as white nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in umbels, often blooming in the evening. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
