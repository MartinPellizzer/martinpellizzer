Thalictrum aduncum

Thalictrum aduncum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided and arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in loose panicles. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
