Tricholepis trichocephala

Tricholepis trichocephala, commonly known as the hairy-headed orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive hairy appearance on its stems and leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
