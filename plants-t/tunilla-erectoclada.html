Tunilla erectoclada

Tunilla erectoclada, commonly known as the upright tunilla, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Tunilla erectoclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
