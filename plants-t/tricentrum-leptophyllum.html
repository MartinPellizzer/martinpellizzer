Tricentrum leptophyllum

Tricentrum leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, frond-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that give it a graceful appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Tricentrum leptophyllum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in botanical gardens.
