Tragacantha nicharensis

Tragacantha nicharensis, commonly known as theNicharensis tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces a gum that is harvested from its bark, which has been used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive.

Its growth habit includes shrub-like structures that can thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
