Talisia stricta

Talisia stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf talisia, is a woody shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and habitat conservation.
