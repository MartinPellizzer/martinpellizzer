Thelymitra adorata

Thelymitra adorata, commonly known as the golden sun orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in open forests and heathlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem that bears several linear leaves. Its flowers are usually bright yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip, and it blooms during the late summer to autumn months.

Thelymitra adorata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
