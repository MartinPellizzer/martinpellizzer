Telipogon minutiflorus

Telipogon minutiflorus, commonly known as the small-flower grass.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the plant. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.

Due to its small size and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
