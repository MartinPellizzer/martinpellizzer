Tripteris amplectens

Tripteris amplectens, commonly known as the climbing tripteris, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Tripteridaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruits are drupes, containing a single seed enclosed in a hard, woody pulp.

Tripteris amplectens is often found in humid, shaded environments in Central and South America.
