Trachyrhizum schlechteri

Trachyrhizum schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's spider orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and intricate, spider-like inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
