Tuberaria glomerata

Tuberaria glomerata, commonly known as the clustered bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. Its unique morphology and carnivorous habits make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
