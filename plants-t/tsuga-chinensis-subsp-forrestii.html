Tsuga chinensis subsp. forrestii

Tsuga chinensis subsp.

forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese fir or Chinese hemlock, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needles, and distinctive cones.

The plant thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and moderate rainfall. Its foliage provides year-round color, making it a popular ornamental species.

It plays a significant role in forest ecosystems and is valued for its timber and ecological benefits.
