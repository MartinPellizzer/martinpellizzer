Themelium decrescens

Themelium decrescens, commonly known as the decreasing thyme, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.

Themelium decrescens thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
