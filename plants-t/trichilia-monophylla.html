Trichilia monophylla

Trichilia monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf champa, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique single leaflet per leaf, which gives it its specific name. The plant belongs to the family Meliaceae, also known as the mahogany family. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The tree is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in various cultures for medicinal and aromatic purposes.
