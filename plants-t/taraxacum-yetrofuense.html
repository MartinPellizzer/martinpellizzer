Taraxacum yetrofuense

Taraxacum yetrofuense, commonly known as the Yetrofuense dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow composite flowers and serrated, hairy leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

The plant is noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional herbal remedies.
