Thea melliana

Thea melliana, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The flowers bloom in spring and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
