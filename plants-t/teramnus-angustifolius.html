Teramnus angustifolius

Teramnus angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved teramnus, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to several feet in height. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Teramnus angustifolius is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
