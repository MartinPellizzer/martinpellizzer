Tabernaemontana jasminoides

Tabernaemontana jasminoides, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Tabernaemontana jasminoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
