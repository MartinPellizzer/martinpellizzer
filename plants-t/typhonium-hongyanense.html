Typhonium hongyanense

Typhonium hongyanense, commonly known as the Hongyan Elephant's Foot, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, tropical-like leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant features a swollen, bulbous stem that resembles an elephant's foot, giving it its common name. It produces small, white flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family.

Typhonium hongyanense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in warm, humid conditions.
