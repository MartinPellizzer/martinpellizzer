Tapeinochilos queenslandiae

Tapeinochilos queenslandiae, commonly known as the Queensland star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale blue with a contrasting purple or pink center. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. It has a short rhizome and produces a single, upright stem with several flower spikes.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for cultivation in gardens.
