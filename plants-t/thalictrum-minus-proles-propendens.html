Thalictrum minus proles propendens

Thalictrum minus proles propendens, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. Thalictrum minus proles propendens is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
