Tunga laevigata

Tunga laevigata, commonly known as the smooth tunga, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a tough, fibrous coating. Tunga laevigata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
