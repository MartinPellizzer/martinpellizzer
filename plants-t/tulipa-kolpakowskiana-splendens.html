Tulipa kolpakowskiana splendens

Tulipa kolpakowskiana splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Tulip, is a striking bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive, elongated shape. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring cooler climates. Its bulbs are perennial, often returning each year with robust growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
