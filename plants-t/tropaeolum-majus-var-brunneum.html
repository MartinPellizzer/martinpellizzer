Tropaeolum majus var. brunneum

Tropaeolum majus var. brunneum, commonly known as the orangeleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, bright orange leaves and vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant is a trailing or climbing annual that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Its foliage and flowers are edible, often used in culinary applications for their peppery flavor.

It is also valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
