Triplostegia delavayi

Triplostegia delavayi, commonly known as the Yunnan flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, typically in the spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in regions with mild winters.
