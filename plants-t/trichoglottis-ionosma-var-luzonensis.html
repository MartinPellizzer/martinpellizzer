Trichoglottis ionosma var. luzonensis

Trichoglottis ionosma var. luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon trichoglottis, is a rare orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
