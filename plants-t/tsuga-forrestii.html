Tsuga forrestii

Tsuga forrestii, commonly known as the Forster's hemlock, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the genus Tsuga, which includes other species of hemlock trees. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged spirally along the branches. Its cones are small and usually hang downward, containing two to three seeds.

Tsuga forrestii is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and its ability to thrive in cooler, moist environments.
