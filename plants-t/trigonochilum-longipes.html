Trigonochilum longipes

Trigonochilum longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typical of many epiphytic orchids. It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Trigonochilum longipes is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.
