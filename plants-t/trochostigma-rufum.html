Trochostigma rufum

It lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on its host plant for nutrients, typically growing on the roots of grasses and other herbaceous plants. Due to its parasitic nature and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists studying plant interactions and ecological dependencies.
