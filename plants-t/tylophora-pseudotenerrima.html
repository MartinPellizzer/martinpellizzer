Tylophora pseudotenerrima

Tylophora pseudotenerrima, commonly known as the Indian bellflower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

Tylophora pseudotenerrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
