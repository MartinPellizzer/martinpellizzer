Thorncroftia media

Thorncroftia media, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, facilitating its spread in suitable habitats.
