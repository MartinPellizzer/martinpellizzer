Touterea perennis

Touterea perennis, commonly known as the silver sheath fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, leathery fronds that are often covered in a silvery, waxy coating. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The botanical features of Touterea perennis make it a striking ornamental plant in subtropical and tropical gardens.
