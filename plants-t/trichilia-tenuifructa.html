Trichilia tenuifructa

Trichilia tenuifructa, commonly known as the small-fruited tamarind, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes other notable genera such as Tamarindus and Cedrela. The plant has some traditional uses in local medicine and is also valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
