Thunbergia schimbensis

Thunbergia schimbensis, commonly known as the Schimbe thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
