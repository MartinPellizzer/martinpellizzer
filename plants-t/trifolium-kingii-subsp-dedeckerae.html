Trifolium kingii subsp. dedeckerae

Trifolium kingii subsp.

dedeckerae, commonly known as the Dedeckera's clover, is a perennial herb native to South Africa. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically small and have a distinctive light green color.

The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
