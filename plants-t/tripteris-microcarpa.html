Tripteris microcarpa

Tripteris microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by small, black berries.
