Tulipa clusiana f. stellata

Tulipa clusiana f. stellata, commonly known as the star tulip, is a botanical variant of the tulipa clusiana species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a red or purple center. The plant is native to Turkey and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun. It is a perennial bulbous plant that produces showy blooms in early spring.

Its unique floral morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
