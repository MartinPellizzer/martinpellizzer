Terminalia darfeuillana

Terminalia darfeuillana, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, simple leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing flat, winged seeds.
