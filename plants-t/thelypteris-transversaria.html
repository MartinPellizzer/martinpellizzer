Thelypteris transversaria

Thelypteris transversaria, commonly known as the transverse shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its spores are produced in clusters on the undersides of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Thelypteris transversaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
