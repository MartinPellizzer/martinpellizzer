Talinum bosseri

Talinum bosseri, commonly known as the red-rooted nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its striking red stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Talinum bosseri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
