Tephroseris pseudoaurantiaca

Tephroseris pseudoaurantiaca, commonly known as the golden tephroseris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
