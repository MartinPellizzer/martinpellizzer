Thelymitra cyanea

Thelymitra cyanea, commonly known as the blue grass orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces striking blue to violet flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its flowers are borne on erect spikes and are often visited by various pollinators, including bees and birds.

Thelymitra cyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique color and form.
