Teucridium parvifolium var. luxurians

Teucridium parvifolium var. luxurians, commonly known as the denseleaf teucridium, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a somewhat fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers, which are often borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its compact growth habit and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
