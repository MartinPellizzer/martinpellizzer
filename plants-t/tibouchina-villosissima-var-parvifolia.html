Tibouchina villosissima var. parvifolia

Tibouchina villosissima var. parvifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf tibouchina, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, and its small, fragrant, white to pale purple flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its lush appearance.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
