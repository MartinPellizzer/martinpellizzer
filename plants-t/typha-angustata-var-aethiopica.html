Typha angustata var. aethiopica

Typha angustata var. aethiopica, commonly known as the narrowleaf cattail, is a perennial wetland plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and a dense, cylindrical flower spike that produces fluffy seeds. This species thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes, lakes, and along riverbanks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, forming dense colonies.

The plant plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
