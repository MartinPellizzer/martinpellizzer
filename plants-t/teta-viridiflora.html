Teta viridiflora

Teta viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered teta, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green leaves and striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep green. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Teta viridiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
