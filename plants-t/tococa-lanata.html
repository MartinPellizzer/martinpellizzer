Tococa lanata

Tococa lanata, commonly known as the woolly tococa, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when mature. Tococa lanata is often found in humid, shaded environments within the understory of dense tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the biodiversity in its native habitat.
