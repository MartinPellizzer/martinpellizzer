Trisetopsis mannii

Trisetopsis mannii, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. It produces edible fruits that are typically consumed by birds and small mammals.
