Torenia pierreana

Torenia pierreana, commonly known as the African violet or bluebell flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a distinctive white or yellow throat. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia pierreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
