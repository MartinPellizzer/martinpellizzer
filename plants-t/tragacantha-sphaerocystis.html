Tragacantha sphaerocystis

Tragacantha sphaerocystis, commonly known as the globular tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive globular seed pods. The plant produces yellow flowers and has a shrub-like growth habit with hairy stems and leaves. Its seeds are encased in thick, leathery pods that contain a gel-like substance used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of Tragacantha sphaerocystis make it an important species in both ecological and pharmaceutical contexts.
