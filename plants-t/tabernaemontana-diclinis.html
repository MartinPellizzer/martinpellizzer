Tabernaemontana diclinis

Tabernaemontana diclinis, commonly known as the Madagascar star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces showy white or pale yellow blossoms, often with a strong, sweet scent. It is a shrub that can grow up to several meters in height, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves.

The flowers are typically bisexual, though some varieties may exhibit dioecious characteristics, with separate male and female flowers on different plants.
