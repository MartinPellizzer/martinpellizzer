Thelypteris lunaniana

Thelypteris lunaniana, commonly known as the Lunan holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it a unique appearance. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

Thelypteris lunaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
