Thalictrum aquilegiifolium var. asiaticum

Thalictrum aquilegiifolium var. asiaticum, commonly known as the Asian meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply lobed and arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, resembling those of the buttercup. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
