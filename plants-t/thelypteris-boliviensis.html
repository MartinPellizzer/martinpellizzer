Thelypteris boliviensis

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of roots, which help in water absorption.
