Tragacantha koburensis

Tragacantha koburensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and it is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its seeds are encased in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds by wind.
