Thamnosma montana

Thamnosma montana, commonly known as the mountain thamnosma, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually pink to purple in color.

Thamnosma montana is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species found in mountainous regions.
