Tecoma undulata

Tecoma undulata, commonly known as the zigzag trumpettree, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Tecoma undulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
