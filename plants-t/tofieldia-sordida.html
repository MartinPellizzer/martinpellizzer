Tofieldia sordida

Tofieldia sordida, commonly known as the dusky tofieldia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in late summer. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that aids in its propagation.

Tofieldia sordida is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.
