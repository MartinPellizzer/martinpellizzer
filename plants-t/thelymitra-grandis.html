Thelymitra grandis

Thelymitra grandis, commonly known as the large-leaf sun orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and produces bright yellow or greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. Thelymitra grandis is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or rainforest environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in conservation areas due to its limited distribution.
