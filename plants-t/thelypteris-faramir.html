Thelypteris faramir

Thelypteris faramir, commonly known as the fairy fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

Thelypteris faramir is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
