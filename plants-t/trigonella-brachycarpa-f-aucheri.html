Trigonella brachycarpa f. aucheri

Trigonella brachycarpa f. aucheri, commonly known as the Armenian melon or wild melon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature, containing several seeds. Native to the Caucasus region, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in botany and ethnobotany.
