Trichopteryx ganaensis

Trichopteryx ganaensis, commonly known as the Ganaensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and intricate floral structures typical of the Orchidaceae family. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers, which can range in color from white to pale yellow, are adapted for specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Trichopteryx ganaensis is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
