Trichodium altissimum

Trichodium altissimum, commonly known as the tall trichodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Brazil and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and small, clustered inflorescences. It produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers that are typically green or pale in color.

Trichodium altissimum is often found in open grasslands and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
