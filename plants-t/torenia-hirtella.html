Torenia hirtella

Torenia hirtella, commonly known as the hairy torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its hairy stems and leaves are adaptations to its native environment, providing protection against herbivores and excessive moisture loss.

Torenia hirtella thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
