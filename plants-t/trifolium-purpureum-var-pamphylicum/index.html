Trifolium Purpureum Var. Pamphylicum

Trifolium purpureum var. pamphylicum is a lesser-known species of clover that thrives in rocky and arid environments. This variant is distinguished by its purple-tinged leaves and compact growth habit. Native to regions of the Mediterranean, it has adapted to harsh conditions with remarkable resilience. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
