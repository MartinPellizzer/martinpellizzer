Tragacantha chrysoptera

Tragacantha chrysoptera, commonly known as the golden milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are trifoliate, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss in arid environments.
