Thalictrum polycarpum

Thalictrum polycarpum, commonly known as the showy mountain avens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Its flowers are actinomorphic, with numerous stamens and a single pistil, making it an attractive species for pollinators.

Thalictrum polycarpum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
