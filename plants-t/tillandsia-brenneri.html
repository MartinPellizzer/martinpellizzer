Tillandsia brenneri

Tillandsia brenneri, commonly known as the silver star tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white trichomes. These trichomes help the plant absorb moisture and nutrients from the air, allowing it to thrive in humid environments. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange or red flowers, which are often surrounded by white bracts.

Tillandsia brenneri is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
