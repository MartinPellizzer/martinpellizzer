Thelypteris shaferi

Thelypteris shaferi, commonly known as the Shafer's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds with a distinctive, shield-like shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense network of roots, which help anchor it in the soil and absorb nutrients.

Thelypteris shaferi is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
