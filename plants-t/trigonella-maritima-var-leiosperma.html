Trigonella maritima var. leiosperma

Trigonella maritima var. leiosperma, commonly known as the smooth-seeded bitter vetch, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a small annual herb with slender stems and trifoliate leaves. The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and borne in racemes, while the fruit is a legume containing several seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to saline environments.

This variety is characterized by its smooth, less hairy seeds compared to other varieties of Trigonella maritima.
