Terminalia chevalieri

Terminalia chevalieri, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in late winter or early spring. Its fruits are spherical, woody capsules that split open to release seeds.

Terminalia chevalieri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
