Tripleurospermum melanophyllum

Tripleurospermum melanophyllum, commonly known as the black-leaved mouse-ear.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to the western United States. It is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Its unique dark foliage and delicate flowers make it a distinctive addition to its native habitat.
