Trachelospermum asiaticum var. glabrum

Trachelospermum asiaticum var. glabrum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a vine that produces fragrant, star-shaped white flowers throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves, though the variety 'glabrum' is noted for its smooth, hairless foliage. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.

The flowers are typically pollinated by insects and can be used in floral arrangements.
