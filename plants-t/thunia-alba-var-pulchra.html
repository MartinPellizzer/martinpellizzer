Thunia alba var. pulchra

Thunia alba var. pulchra, commonly known as the white lace fern, is a variegated cultivar of the Thunia alba species.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy foliage that features a mix of white, green, and cream-colored leaves. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is often used as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance.

The variegation in its leaves adds a unique visual contrast, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
