Tripteris calcicola

It is native to the arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in limestone soils. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter.
