Tripleurospermum daghestanicum

Tripleurospermum daghestanicum, commonly known as the Daghestan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region, particularly in the Republic of Dagestan.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of tubular and ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native habitat.
