Terminalia somalensis

Terminalia somalensis, commonly known as Somali Terminalia, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the dry regions of Somalia and southern Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable component of local ecosystems.
