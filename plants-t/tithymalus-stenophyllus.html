Tithymalus stenophyllus

Tithymalus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant is often found in open woodlands and along rocky slopes.
