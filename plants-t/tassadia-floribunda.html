Tassadia floribunda

Tassadia floribunda, commonly known as the showy tassadia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers range in color from pale yellow to orange.

Tassadia floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its striking floral display.
