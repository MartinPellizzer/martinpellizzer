Tetracme contorta

Tetracme contorta, commonly known as the twisted four-leaf plant, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive twisted appearance, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically small, tubular, and often have a yellow or orange hue.
