Temnocalyx fuchsioides

Temnocalyx fuchsioides, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
