Thlaspiceras rechingeri

Thlaspiceras rechingeri, commonly known as the Kashmir thlaspi, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, particularly in India and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
