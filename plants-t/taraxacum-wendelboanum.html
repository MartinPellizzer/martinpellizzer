Taraxacum wendelboanum

Taraxacum wendelboanum, commonly known as the Wendelbo's dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions, characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves.
