Thelocactus wagnerianus

Thelocactus wagnerianus, commonly known as the Wagner's thorned cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and a dark green stem. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it is adapted to arid environments.

Thelocactus wagnerianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
