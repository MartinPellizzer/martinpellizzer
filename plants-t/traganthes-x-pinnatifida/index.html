Traganthes × Pinnatifida

Pinnate traganthus, scientifically known as Traganthes × pinnatifida, is a strikingly unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. Its most distinguishing feature is its pinnate leaf structure, which gives it a feathery, elegant appearance. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to yellow. Despite its beauty, it is also known for its resilience, thriving in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. Its adaptability and visual appeal make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike.
