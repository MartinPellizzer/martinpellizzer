Thunbergia flavohirta

Thunbergia flavohirta, commonly known as the yellow-flowered morning glory, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color.
