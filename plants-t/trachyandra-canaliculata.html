Trachyandra canaliculata

Trachyandra canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed trachyandra, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
