Taeniophyllum celebicum

Taeniophyllum celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes star moss, is a unique epiphytic plant belonging to the family Ptychopetalaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, star-shaped clusters of tiny, leaf-like structures that form a cushion-like appearance. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly the Indonesian islands of Sulawesi and Bali. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found on the trunks of trees in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
