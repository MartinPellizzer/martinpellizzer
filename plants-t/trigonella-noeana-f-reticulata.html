Trigonella noeana f. reticulata

Trigonella noeana f. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate fenugreek, is a botanical variant of the fenugreek species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces seeds that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its botanical features include a hairy stem and a distinct reticulate pattern on its leaves.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and adaptability to arid environments.
