Torenia indica

Torenia indica, commonly known as the Indian torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often reddish in color.

Torenia indica thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
