Tragia mercurialis

Tragia mercurialis, commonly known as mercury glorybower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture.
