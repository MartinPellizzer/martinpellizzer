Tragacantha seidlitzii

Tragacantha seidlitzii, commonly known as the Persian gum tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Iran and Central Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by distinctive, inflated pods that contain seeds.
