Trochetia richardii

Trochetia richardii, commonly known as the richard's thimble cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent shrub that typically forms dense, compact clusters. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
