Tephrosia maculata var. appressepilosa

Tephrosia maculata var. appressepilosa, commonly known as the spotted tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, bean-like pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical features make it notable for both ornamental and potential medicinal uses.
