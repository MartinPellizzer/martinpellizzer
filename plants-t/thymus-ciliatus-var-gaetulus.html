Thymus ciliatus var. gaetulus

Thymus ciliatus var. gaetulus, commonly known as the Gaetulan thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and Mediterranean climates.

Its aromatic properties make it valuable in both culinary and medicinal applications.
