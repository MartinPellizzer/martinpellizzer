Taxanthema speciosum

Taxanthema speciosum, commonly known as the showy taxanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has adapted to harsh desert conditions with thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. Its flowering period typically occurs during the cooler months, attracting various pollinators.

Taxanthema speciosum plays a significant role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
