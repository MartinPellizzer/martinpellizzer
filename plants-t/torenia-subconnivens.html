Torenia subconnivens

Torenia subconnivens, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Torenia subconnivens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
