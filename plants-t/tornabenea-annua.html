Tornabenea annua

Tornabenea annua, commonly known as the annual tornabenea, is an herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and riverbanks. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and range in color from white to pale pink.

Tornabenea annua is known for its rapid growth cycle, completing its life cycle within a single growing season.
