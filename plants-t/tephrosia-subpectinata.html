Tephrosia subpectinata

Tephrosia subpectinata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, often hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, bean-like pods containing several seeds. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.

Tephrosia subpectinata is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its role in soil stabilization.
