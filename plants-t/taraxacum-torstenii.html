Taraxacum torstenii

Taraxacum torstenii, commonly known as the Torsten's dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the dandelion genus, which is part of the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped leaves and a yellow composite flower head. It produces a distinctive seed head with pappus that aids in wind dispersal.

Taraxacum torstenii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
