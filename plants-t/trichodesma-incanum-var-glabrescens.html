Trichodesma incanum var. glabrescens

Trichodesma incanum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.

The variety glabrescens is distinguished by its smoother, less hairy leaves compared to the typical form.
