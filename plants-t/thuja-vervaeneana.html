Thuja vervaeneana

Thuja vervaeneana, commonly known as the Chinese thuja, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Cupressaceae and is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Thuja vervaeneana is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban conditions and its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
