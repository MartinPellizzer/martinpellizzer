Thelymitra flexuosa

Thelymitra flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are sword-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.

Thelymitra flexuosa is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in humid, temperate climates.
