Thelypteris medlerae

Thelypteris medlerae, commonly known as the Medler's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds with a distinctive shield-shaped pinnae. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are wiry and form dense colonies, contributing to its ability to thrive in humid conditions.

Thelypteris medlerae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
