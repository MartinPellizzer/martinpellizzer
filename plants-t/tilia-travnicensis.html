Tilia travnicensis

Tilia travnicensis, commonly known as the Travnicensis lime, is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically serrated along the edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which appear in early summer and are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in traditional medicine.
