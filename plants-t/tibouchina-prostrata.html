Tibouchina prostrata

Tibouchina prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate tibouchina, is a low-growing shrub native to South America.

It features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Tibouchina prostrata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
