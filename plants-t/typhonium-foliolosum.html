Typhonium foliolosum

Typhonium foliolosum, commonly known as the leafy arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive veins. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of many arum species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Its rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a starchy root vegetable.
