Telipogon standleyi

Telipogon standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests or mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.
