Tristicha trifaria subsp. tlatlayana

Tristicha trifaria subsp.

tlatlayana, commonly known as the Mexican feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to central Mexico. It features slender, erect stems that are often adorned with delicate, feathery inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
