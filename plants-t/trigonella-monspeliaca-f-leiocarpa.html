Trigonella monspeliaca f. leiocarpa

Trigonella monspeliaca f. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smoothpod fenugreek, is a variant of the fenugreek plant characterized by its smooth, hairless pods.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to regions of the Mediterranean and Middle East. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, pod-like fruits. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, heart-shaped leaflets.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
