Thymus × josephi-angeli nothosubsp. edetanus

Thymus × josephi-angeli nothosubsp.

edetanus, commonly known as the Edetan thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family. It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and aromatic, narrow leaves.

The plant produces small, pink to violet flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and compact growth form.
