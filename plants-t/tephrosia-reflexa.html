Tephrosia reflexa

Tephrosia reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its slender, often thorned stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with five petals. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.

Tephrosia reflexa is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
