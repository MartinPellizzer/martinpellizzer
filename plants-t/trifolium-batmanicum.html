Trifolium batmanicum

Trifolium batmanicum, commonly known as Batman's Clover, is a rare and fictional plant first introduced in the Batman comics.

It is depicted as a bioluminescent species that grows in the sewers of Gotham City, emitting a faint blue glow. The plant is said to have healing properties, though it is also toxic in high doses. Its unique appearance includes dark green leaves with a metallic sheen and small, glowing flowers.

Despite its fictional origins, it has inspired various scientific and artistic interpretations in popular culture.
