Trigonella hamosa var. glabra

Trigonella hamosa var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth fenugreek, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems and trifoliate leaves with smooth, glabrous surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, followed by elongated, slender pods containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
