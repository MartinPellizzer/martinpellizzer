Trichosanthes laciniata

Trichosanthes laciniata, commonly known as the Chinese lantern gourd, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lobed leaves and bright orange, lantern-shaped fruits. The plant belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins. It produces yellow flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
