Tapirira hirsuta

Tapirira hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy tapirira, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical fruits. Tapirira hirsuta is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
