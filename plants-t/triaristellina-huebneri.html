Triaristellina huebneri

Triaristellina huebneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
