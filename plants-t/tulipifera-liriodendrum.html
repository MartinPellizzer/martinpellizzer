Tulipifera liriodendrum

Tulipifera liriodendrum, commonly known as the tulip tree, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tulip-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in North American ecosystems.
