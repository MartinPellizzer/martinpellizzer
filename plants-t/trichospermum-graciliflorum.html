Trichospermum graciliflorum

Trichospermum graciliflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered trichospermum, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially grown.
