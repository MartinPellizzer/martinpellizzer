Trichosalpinx memor

Trichosalpinx memor, commonly known as the purple flame orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers feature a unique combination of purple and yellow hues, with intricate petal structures.

Trichosalpinx memor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
