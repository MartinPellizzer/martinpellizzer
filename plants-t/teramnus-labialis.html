Teramnus labialis

Teramnus labialis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved teramnus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny locations. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Teramnus labialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
