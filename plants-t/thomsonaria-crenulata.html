Thomsonaria crenulata

Thomsonaria crenulata, commonly known as the warty leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, warty leaf structures. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Thomsonaria crenulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
