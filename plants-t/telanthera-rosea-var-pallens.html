Telanthera rosea var. pallens

Telanthera rosea var. pallens, commonly known as the pale telanthera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The pale telanthera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
