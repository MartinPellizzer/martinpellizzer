Thelypteris bulusanica

Thelypteris bulusanica, commonly known as the Bulusan fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a serrated margin. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
