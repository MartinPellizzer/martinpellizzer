Taraxacum nipesotsuense

Taraxacum nipesotsuense, commonly known as the Nipesotsu dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped basal leaves. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

The plant is noted for its robust growth and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
