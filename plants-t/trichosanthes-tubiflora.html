Trichosanthes tubiflora

Trichosanthes tubiflora, commonly known as the yellow trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces long, slender fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
