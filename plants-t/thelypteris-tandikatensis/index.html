Thelypteris Tandikatensis

Thelypteris tandikatensis is a rare fern species native to the tropical forests of Borneo. It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is known for its delicate, intricate fronds. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane rainforests. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it an important subject for botanical study.
