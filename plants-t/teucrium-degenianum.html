Teucrium degenianum

Teucrium degenianum, commonly known as Dune Germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal dunes and rocky habitats. The plant has aromatic, slightly hairy leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue. It is part of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants.

Teucrium degenianum is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and its historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
