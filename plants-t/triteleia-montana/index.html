Triteleia Montana

Triteleia montana is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California. Known for its vibrant blue and purple blooms, it thrives in rocky soils and sunny slopes. This plant is part of the Amaryllidaceae family and is often referred to as the "California larkspur." Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Protecting Triteleia montana is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystem.
