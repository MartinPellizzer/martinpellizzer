Thunbergia laborans

Thunbergia laborans, commonly known as the blue trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Thunbergia laborans is a climbing vine that can reach lengths of several meters.
