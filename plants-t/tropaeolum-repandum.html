Tropaeolum repandum

Tropaeolum repandum, commonly known as the everlasting pepper or Japanese everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces vibrant, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and is known for its long-lasting blooms that retain their color even when dried. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Tropaeolum repandum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
