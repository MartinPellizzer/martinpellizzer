Timonius pullenii

Timonius pullenii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, velvety flowers. The plant typically features thick, fleshy leaves and produces flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Timonius pullenii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
