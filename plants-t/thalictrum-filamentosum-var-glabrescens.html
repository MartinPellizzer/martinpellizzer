Thalictrum filamentosum var. glabrescens

Thalictrum filamentosum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the silky avens, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and covered in a soft, silky texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
