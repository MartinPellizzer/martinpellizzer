Trichantha laevis

Trichantha laevis, commonly known as the smooth trichantha, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China and India, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in smooth, waxy leaves that are typically lance-shaped. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or reddish and appear in clusters.

Trichantha laevis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
