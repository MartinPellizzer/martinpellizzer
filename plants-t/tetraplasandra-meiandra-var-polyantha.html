Tetraplasandra meiandra var. polyantha

Tetraplasandra meiandra var. polyantha, commonly known as the Polyantha Tetraplasandra, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
