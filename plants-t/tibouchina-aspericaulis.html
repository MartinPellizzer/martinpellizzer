Tibouchina aspericaulis

Tibouchina aspericaulis, commonly known as rough-leaved tibouchina, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, rough-textured leaves and clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.

The species is also known for its adaptability to various soil types, making it a resilient choice for landscaping.
