Thismia aseroe

Thismia aseroe, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant belonging to the Thismiaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Thismia aseroe is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, shaded forest environments.
