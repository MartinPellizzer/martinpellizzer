Tropaeolum magnificum

Tropaeolum magnificum, commonly known as the "Marvelous Nasturtium," is a vibrant annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive black center. The plant has heart-shaped, succulent leaves that are edible and have a peppery flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Tropaeolum magnificum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
