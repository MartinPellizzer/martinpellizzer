Thalictrum bauhini proles subspicatum

Thalictrum bauhini proles subspicatum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

Thalictrum bauhini proles subspicatum is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
