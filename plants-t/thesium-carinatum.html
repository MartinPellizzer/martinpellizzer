Thesium carinatum

Thesium carinatum, commonly known as the spurred wintergreen, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, and it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.

Thesium carinatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
