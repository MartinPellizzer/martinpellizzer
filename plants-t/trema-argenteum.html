Trema argenteum

Trema argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf trema, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features dense, glossy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by round, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
