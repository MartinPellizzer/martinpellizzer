Thunbergia bachmannii

Thunbergia bachmannii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, tubular blue or violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia bachmannii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
