Thymus saturejoides var. intermedius

Thymus saturejoides var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for gardens.

Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, make it valuable in both culinary and medicinal applications.
