Thysanobotrya scandens

Thysanobotrya scandens, commonly known as the climbing thysanobotrya, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its twining stems that climb along supports, often forming dense foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
