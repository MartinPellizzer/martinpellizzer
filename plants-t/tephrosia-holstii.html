Tephrosia holstii

Tephrosia holstii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and opposite, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are enclosed in follicles that split open to release the seeds.

Tephrosia holstii is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
