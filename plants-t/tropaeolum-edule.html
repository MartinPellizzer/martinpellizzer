Tropaeolum edule

Tropaeolum edule, commonly known as the garden nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, round, peppery-tasting seeds that are often used in culinary applications. Its stems are trailing or climbing, allowing it to spread across garden beds or trellises.

Tropaeolum edule thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile and ornamental addition to gardens.
