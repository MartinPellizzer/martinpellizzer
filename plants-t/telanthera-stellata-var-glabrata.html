Telanthera stellata var. glabrata

Telanthera stellata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth starflower, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and extended blooming period.
