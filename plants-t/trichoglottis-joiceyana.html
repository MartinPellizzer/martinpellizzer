Trichoglottis joiceyana

Trichoglottis joiceyana, commonly known as the Joicey's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender pseudobulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Trichoglottis joiceyana is a rare and endangered species, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
