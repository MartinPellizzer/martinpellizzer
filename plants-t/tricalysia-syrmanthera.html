Tricalysia syrmanthera

Tricalysia syrmanthera, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before the leaves in the spring. The plant has a dense, rounded canopy and can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface.

Tricalysia syrmanthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping in warm climates.
