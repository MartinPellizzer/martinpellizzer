Turbinicarpus × asteroides

Turbinicarpus × asteroides, commonly known as the star cactus, is a hybrid succulent native to the deserts of North America.

It features a compact, globular shape with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts.
