Tribulus grandiflorus

Tribulus grandiflorus, commonly known as the largeflower cat's claw, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its woody, climbing stems and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, red berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Tribulus grandiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
