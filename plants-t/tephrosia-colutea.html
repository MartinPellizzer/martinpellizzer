Tephrosia colutea

Tephrosia colutea, commonly known as African blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
