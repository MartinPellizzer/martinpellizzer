Trichilia brasiensis

Trichilia brasiensis, commonly known as African sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and compound leaves with ovate leaflets. Its flowers are small and yellowish-green, typically appearing in clusters, and it produces woody capsules containing elliptical seeds.
