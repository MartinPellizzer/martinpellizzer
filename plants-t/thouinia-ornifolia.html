Thouinia ornifolia

Thouinia ornifolia, commonly known as the ornate leaf thornapple, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often adorned with distinctive patterns and a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, Thouinia ornifolia is appreciated by both horticulturists and botanists.
