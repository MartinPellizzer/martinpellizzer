Thelypteris zeylanica

Thelypteris zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia and parts of the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
