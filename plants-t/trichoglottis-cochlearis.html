Trichoglottis cochlearis

Trichoglottis cochlearis, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique helmet-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, anchoring itself with its pseudobulbs and roots. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Trichoglottis cochlearis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
