Tetrapterys trinitensis

Tetrapterys trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidad velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland areas. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
