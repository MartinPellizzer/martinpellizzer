Tradescantia gigantea

Tradescantia gigantea, commonly known as the spiderwort or giant spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from blue to purple and pink. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 to 3 feet. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish or silver-green hue.

Tradescantia gigantea thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
