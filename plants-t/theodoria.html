Theodoria

Theodoria, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stems above the water's surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, and are often associated with spiritual and cultural symbolism.

Theodoria plays an important role in both ecological systems and traditional practices in many parts of the world.
