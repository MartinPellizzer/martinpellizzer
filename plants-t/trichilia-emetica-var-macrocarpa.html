Trichilia emetica var. macrocarpa

Trichilia emetica var. macrocarpa, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, white, fluffy flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
