Tectaria celebica

Tectaria celebica, commonly known as the Celebes shield fern, is a species of polypodiaceae native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

The botanical features of Tectaria celebica make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
