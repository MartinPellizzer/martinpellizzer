Tityrus × bifrons

Tityrus × bifrons, commonly known as the hybrid thrush, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between the common thrush (Tityrus philomelos) and the song thrush (Tityrus cardinalis).

It is a small passerine bird with a distinctive plumage pattern, featuring a dark head and a lighter underbody. The plant aspect refers to its natural habitat, typically found in woodlands and shrublands across Europe. It is known for its melodious song, which plays a significant role in its ecological interactions.

This hybrid species showcases the genetic diversity and adaptability of avian species in different environments.
