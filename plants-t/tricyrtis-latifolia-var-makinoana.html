Tricyrtis latifolia var. makinoana

Tricyrtis latifolia var. makinoana, commonly known as the Japanese ghost orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features striking, orchid-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a unique, ghostly appearance. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical interest lies in its ornamental value and its role in traditional Japanese horticulture.
