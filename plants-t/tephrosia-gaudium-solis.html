Tephrosia gaudium-solis

Tephrosia gaudium-solis, commonly known as the golden tephrosia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are known to contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.
