Trymalium × ramosissimum

Trymalium × ramosissimum, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, red fruits that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

The hybrid is known for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
