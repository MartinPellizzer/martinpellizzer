Terminalia tomentella

Terminalia tomentella, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly flowers that appear in terminal clusters, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and savannas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
