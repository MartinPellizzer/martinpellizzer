Thunbergia leucorhiza

Thunbergia leucorhiza, commonly known as the white-rooted morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its vining habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia leucorhiza is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
