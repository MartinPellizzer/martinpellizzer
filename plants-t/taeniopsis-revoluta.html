Taeniopsis revoluta

Taeniopsis revoluta, commonly known as the rolling leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive rolled leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a subtle fragrance.

Taeniopsis revoluta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
