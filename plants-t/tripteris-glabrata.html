Tripteris glabrata

Tripteris glabrata, commonly known as the smooth tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy berries.

Tripteris glabrata is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
