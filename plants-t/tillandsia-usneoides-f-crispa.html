Tillandsia usneoides f. crispa

Tillandsia usneoides f. crispa, commonly known as the curly beard moss, is a unique epiphytic bromeliad native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

This variety is characterized by its long, curly, and often pendulous leaves that give it a distinctive, moss-like appearance. Unlike typical Tillandsia species, f. crispa lacks the usual rosette structure and instead forms a dense, hanging mat of foliage. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as an ornamental plant.
