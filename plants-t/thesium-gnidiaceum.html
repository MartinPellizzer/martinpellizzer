Thesium gnidiaceum

Thesium gnidiaceum, commonly known as the slender thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
