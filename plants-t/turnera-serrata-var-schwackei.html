Turnera serrata var. schwackei

Turnera serrata var. schwackei, commonly known as the Schwacke's turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Passifloraceae family, known for its showy, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
