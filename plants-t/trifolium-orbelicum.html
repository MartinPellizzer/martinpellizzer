Trifolium orbelicum

Trifolium orbelicum, commonly known as the Orbelic clover, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three small oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Trifolium orbelicum is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
