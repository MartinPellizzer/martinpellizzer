Thecacoris grandifolia

Thecacoris grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered cacoris, is a member of the Apocynaceae family, characterized by its showy, often fragrant flowers.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
