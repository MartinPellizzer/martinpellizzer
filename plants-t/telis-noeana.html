Telis noeana

Telis noeana, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Telis noeana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
