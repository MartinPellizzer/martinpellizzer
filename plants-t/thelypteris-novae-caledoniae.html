Thelypteris novae-caledoniae

Thelypteris novae-caledoniae, commonly known as the New Caledonia shield fern, is a species of fern native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a serrated or lobed margin.

Thelypteris novae-caledoniae plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
