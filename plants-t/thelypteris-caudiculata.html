Thelypteris caudiculata

Thelypteris caudiculata, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its narrow, elongated fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are short and often covered with a dense layer of rhizomorphs.

The fronds are usually bipinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.
