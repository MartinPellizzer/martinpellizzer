Terniopsis ramosa

Terniopsis ramosa, commonly known as the branched fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with multiple branches arising from the main stem. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, giving it a lacy appearance.

Terniopsis ramosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
