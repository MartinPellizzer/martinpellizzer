Thomandersia congolana var. grandifolia

Thomandersia congolana var. grandifolia, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed and have a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, resembling those of the moonflower. Its climbing habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and shaded areas.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
