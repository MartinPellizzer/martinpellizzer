Taeniophyllum stenosepalum

Taeniophyllum stenosepalum, commonly known as the slender-sepal tailed orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and its unique inflorescence that produces small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive tail-like spur.

Taeniophyllum stenosepalum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
