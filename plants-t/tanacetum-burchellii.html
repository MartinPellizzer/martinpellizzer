Tanacetum burchellii

Tanacetum burchellii, commonly known as the African tansy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.

Tanacetum burchellii is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
