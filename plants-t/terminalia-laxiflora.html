Terminalia laxiflora

Terminalia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered terminalia, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits containing single seeds.
