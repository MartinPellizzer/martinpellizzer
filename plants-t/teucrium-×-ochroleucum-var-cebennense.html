Teucrium × ochroleucum var. cebennense

Teucrium × ochroleucum var. cebennense, commonly known as the white teucrium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
