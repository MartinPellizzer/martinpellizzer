Trichocereus tunariensis

Trichocereus tunariensis, commonly known as the Tunariensis Cactus, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its striking appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
