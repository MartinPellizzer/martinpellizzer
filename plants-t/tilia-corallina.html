Tilia corallina

Tilia corallina, commonly known as the coral lime tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely toothed, heart-shaped leaves that turn a striking red or orange in the fall. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, round, green fruits. Tilia corallina thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
