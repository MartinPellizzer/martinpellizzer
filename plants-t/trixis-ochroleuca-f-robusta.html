Trixis ochroleuca f. robusta

Trixis ochroleuca f. robusta, commonly known as the robust trixis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a robust, upright stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, adorned with opposite, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It is well adapted to dry conditions, with deep roots that help it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it an interesting subject for botanical study in semi-arid ecosystems.
