Teucrium albarracinii

Teucrium albarracinii, commonly known as the white teucrium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Teucrium albarracinii is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
