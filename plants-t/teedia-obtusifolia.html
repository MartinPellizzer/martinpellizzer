Teedia obtusifolia

Teedia obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It typically features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland dunes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-adapted to harsh, dry climates.
