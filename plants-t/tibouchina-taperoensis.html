Tibouchina taperoensis

Tibouchina taperoensis, commonly known as the pink tibouchina, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular pink or purple flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Tibouchina taperoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
