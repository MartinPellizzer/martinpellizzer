Tithymalus paniculatus

Tithymalus paniculatus, commonly known as the panicled tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, branched inflorescence composed of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

Tithymalus paniculatus is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
