Tillandsia confusa var. minor

Tillandsia confusa var. minor, commonly known as the miniature tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth form. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, which are typically borne on short stalks. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.
