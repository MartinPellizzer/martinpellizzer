Timonius minahassae

Timonius minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa timonius, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers a humid, shaded environment. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a glossy surface that reflects light.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Timonius minahassae is considered a conservation concern.
