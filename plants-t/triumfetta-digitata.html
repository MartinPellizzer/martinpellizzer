Triumfetta digitata

Triumfetta digitata, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a small shrub or herbaceous plant that typically grows up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the warmer months. Its leaves are ovate with hairy surfaces, giving it a distinctive appearance.
