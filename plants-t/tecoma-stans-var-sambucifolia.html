Tecoma stans var. sambucifolia

Tecoma stans var. sambucifolia, commonly known as the sambucifolia trumpetflower, is a variant of the tropical flowering plant Tecoma stans.

It is characterized by its distinctive, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This plant is native to tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in warm, sunny climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
