Teucrium serranum

Teucrium serranum, commonly known as narrowleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic leaves with serrated edges and produces small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats. Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Teucrium serranum is valued in traditional medicine for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
