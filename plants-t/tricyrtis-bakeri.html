Tricyrtis bakeri

Tricyrtis bakeri, commonly known as the Japanese buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Tricyrtis bakeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
