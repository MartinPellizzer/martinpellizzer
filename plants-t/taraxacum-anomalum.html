Taraxacum anomalum

Taraxacum anomalum, commonly known as the narrowleaf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy seed heads. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It has a deep taproot system, which allows it to survive in various environmental conditions.

The plant is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas where it can spread its seeds effectively.
