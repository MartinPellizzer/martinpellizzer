Thelypteris trichopoda

Thelypteris trichopoda, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are wiry and often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its common name.
