Turnera waltherioides

Turnera waltherioides, commonly known as the golden turnera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it often grows in moist, shaded environments. It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.
