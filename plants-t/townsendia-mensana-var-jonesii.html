Townsendia mensana var. jonesii

Townsendia mensana var. jonesii, commonly known as Jones' townsendia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
