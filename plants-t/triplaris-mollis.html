Triplaris mollis

Triplaris mollis, commonly known as the silky triplaris, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkins, followed by small, fleshy fruits. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
