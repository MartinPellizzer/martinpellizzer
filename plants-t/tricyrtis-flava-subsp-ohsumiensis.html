Tricyrtis flava subsp. ohsumiensis

Tricyrtis flava subsp.

ohsumiensis, commonly known as the Ohsumi spider orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to Japan. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like flowers with a distinctive yellow coloration.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, while its inflorescence consists of numerous small, fragrant blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species.
