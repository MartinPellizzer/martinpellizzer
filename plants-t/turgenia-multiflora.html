Turgenia multiflora

Turgenia multiflora, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant features trifoliate leaves, which are divided into three leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Turgenia multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
