Tillandsia Duratii Subsp. Streptocarpa

Tillandsia duratii subsp. streptocarpa is a rare and unique air plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. It is known for its striking appearance, featuring long, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish. Unlike most Tillandsias, it has a more compact growth pattern and produces small, delicate flowers. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
