Tainia grandiflora

Tainia grandiflora, commonly known as the giant tainia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Tainia grandiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and vibrant floral display.
