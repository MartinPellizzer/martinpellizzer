Theopsis furfuracea

Theopsis furfuracea, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique texture and ornamental appeal make it a distinctive addition to any botanical collection.
