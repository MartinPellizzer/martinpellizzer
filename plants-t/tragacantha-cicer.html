Tragacantha cicer

Tragacantha cicer, commonly known as salsify, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and yellow flowers. The plant produces long, fibrous roots that are often harvested for their edible qualities. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it thrives in well-drained soils.
