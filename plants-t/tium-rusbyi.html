Tium rusbyi

Tium rusbyi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots and velvety, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting central spot. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Tium rusbyi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
