Turnera hilaireana var. oblongifolia

Turnera hilaireana var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, yellow-orange flowers.
