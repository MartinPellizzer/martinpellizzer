Timonius splendens

Timonius splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Timonius, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that display a vibrant green hue with prominent veins. Its flowering stems produce clusters of small, white flowers that emit a subtle fragrance.

Timonius splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and visual appeal.
