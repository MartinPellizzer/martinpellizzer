Trichodiadema gracile var. piliferum

Trichodiadema gracile var. piliferum, commonly known as the woolly ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
