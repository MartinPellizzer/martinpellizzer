Thelypteris grandis var. aequitorialis

Thelypteris grandis var. aequitorialis, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a variant of the broad shield fern native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, bipinnate fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance.
