Thunbergia saltiana

Thunbergia saltiana, commonly known as blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of small, blue or purple flowers that appear throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Thunbergia saltiana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
