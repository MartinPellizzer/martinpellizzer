Tolumnia berenyce

Tolumnia berenyce, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Tolumnia berenyce is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and wetlands.
