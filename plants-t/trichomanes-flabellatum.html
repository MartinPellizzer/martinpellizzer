Trichomanes flabellatum

Trichomanes flabellatum, commonly known as the fan fern, is a tropical fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bright green and finely divided. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Trichomanes flabellatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
