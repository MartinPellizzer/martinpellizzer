Tamarix laxa var. polystachya

Tamarix laxa var. polystachya, commonly known as the silky tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often branching stems that are covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing tolerance to salt and drought.

Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in sandy or saline soils.
