Thlaspi longistylum

Thlaspi longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils. The plant has slender, branching stems and alternate, pinnately lobed leaves. Its small, white flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.
