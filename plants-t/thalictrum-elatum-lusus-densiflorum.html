Thalictrum elatum lusus densiflorum

Thalictrum elatum lusus densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that provide a delicate texture to the landscape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to woodland gardens and wetland habitats.
