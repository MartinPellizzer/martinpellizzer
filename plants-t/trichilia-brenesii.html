Trichilia brenesii

Trichilia brenesii, commonly known as the Mexican white cedar, is a member of the Meliaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Central America and parts of Mexico. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, and it produces woody fruits that contain flat, winged seeds.

Trichilia brenesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
