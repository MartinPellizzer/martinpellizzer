Thelypteris gongshanensis

Thelypteris gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris gongshanensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
