Trigonia reticulata

Trigonia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves and a slender stem, often growing in shaded, moist environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Trigonia reticulata requires careful cultivation due to its specific humidity and light needs.
