Tectaria aenigma

Tectaria aenigma, commonly known as the mysterious tectaria, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family, characterized by its unique frond structure.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a distinctive, often lobed frond with a complex venation pattern. Its sporangia are clustered in sori, which are typically located on the underside of the fronds.

Tectaria aenigma is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
