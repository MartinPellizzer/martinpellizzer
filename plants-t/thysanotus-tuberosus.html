Thysanotus tuberosus

Thysanotus tuberosus, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens.
