Thelypteris caudata

Thelypteris caudata, commonly known as the hairy maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in fine, hairy structures, contributing to its common name.
