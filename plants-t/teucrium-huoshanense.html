Teucrium huoshanense

Teucrium huoshanense, commonly known as the Huoshan germander, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically features slender stems and small, opposite leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, arranged in whorled clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
