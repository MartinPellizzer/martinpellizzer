Tabernaemontana benthamiana

Tabernaemontana benthamiana, commonly known as the bentham's star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
