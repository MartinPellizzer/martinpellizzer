Toga

Toga, commonly known as *Cleome hassleriana*, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Toga is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
