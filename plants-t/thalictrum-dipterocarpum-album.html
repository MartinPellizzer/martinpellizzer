Thalictrum dipterocarpum album

Thalictrum dipterocarpum album, commonly known as the white-flowered meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

Thalictrum dipterocarpum album is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting biodiversity.
