Tulipa humilis subsp. matinae

Tulipa humilis subsp.

matinae, commonly known as the Matinae tulip, is a small, early-blooming bulbous plant native to Turkey. It produces delicate, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple throat.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils. It is a member of the Liliaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
