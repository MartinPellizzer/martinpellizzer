Torenia auriculifolia

Torenia auriculifolia, commonly known as the auriculate torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink, often with a contrasting throat. The leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
