Thunbergia friesii

Thunbergia friesii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, pleasant fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia friesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
