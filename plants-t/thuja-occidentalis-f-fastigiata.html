Thuja occidentalis f. fastigiata

Thuja occidentalis f. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright thuja or northern white cedar, is a compact, columnar evergreen conifer.

It features dense, scale-like foliage that is dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit, making it ideal for vertical space in gardens. It produces small, spherical cones that are typically inconspicuous.

This variety is highly tolerant of urban conditions, including pollution and compacted soils, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
