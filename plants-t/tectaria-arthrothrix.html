Tectaria arthrothrix

Tectaria arthrothrix, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. Its rhizomes are short and often covered in a thick layer of moss-like growth.

Tectaria arthrothrix plays a role in forest ecosystems by contributing to biodiversity and soil stabilization.
