Teucrium polium subvar. sennenianum

Teucrium polium subvar. sennenianum, commonly known as the Sennenianum germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in summer.

This plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
