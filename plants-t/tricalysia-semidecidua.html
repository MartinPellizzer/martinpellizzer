Tricalysia semidecidua

Tricalysia semidecidua, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It features a broad, rounded canopy with large, palmate leaves that turn red or orange before falling in autumn. The plant produces showy, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a notable addition to gardens and landscapes.
