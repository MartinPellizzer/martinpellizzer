Tibouchina villosissima var. petiolaris

Tibouchina villosissima var. petiolaris, commonly known as the silverleaf tibouchina, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for both container and garden planting.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
