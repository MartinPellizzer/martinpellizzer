Tylosperma cuneatum

Tylosperma cuneatum, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by flat, leathery pods containing seeds. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water storage.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine and for its ornamental value.
