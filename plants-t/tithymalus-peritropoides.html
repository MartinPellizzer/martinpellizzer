Tithymalus peritropoides

Tithymalus peritropoides, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant yellow or orange flowers.
