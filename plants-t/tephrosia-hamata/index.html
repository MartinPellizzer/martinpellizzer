Tephrosia Hamata

Tephrosia hamata is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is known for its striking appearance and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. Its roots contain compounds used in traditional medicine. Tephrosia hamata remains an important species in both ecological and pharmacological studies.
