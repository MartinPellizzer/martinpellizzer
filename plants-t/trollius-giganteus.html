Trollius giganteus

Trollius giganteus, commonly known as the giant buttercup, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has palmately divided, bright green leaves that are lobed and covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Trollius giganteus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow with proper care.
