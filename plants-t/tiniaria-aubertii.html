Tiniaria aubertii

Tiniaria aubertii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.

Tiniaria aubertii is often cultivated as a houseplant, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
