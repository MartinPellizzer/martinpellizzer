Tissa salina

Tissa salina, commonly known as the saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant thrives in saline soils and is often found in coastal areas or salt marshes. Its flowers are typically small and greenish-white, and it produces small, woody fruits.

Tissa salina plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
