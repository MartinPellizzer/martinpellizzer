Thyrsanthema dentata

Thyrsanthema dentata, commonly known as the serrated daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, serrated leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
