Tessenia canadensis

Tessenia canadensis, commonly known as Canada tessenia, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in terminal spikes.

Tessenia canadensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
