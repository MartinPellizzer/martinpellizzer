Teucrium maroccanum

Teucrium maroccanum, commonly known as Moroccan germander, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.

Teucrium maroccanum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
