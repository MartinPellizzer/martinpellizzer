Tanacetum podlechii

Tanacetum podlechii, commonly known as the Podlech's tansy, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dark green, aromatic leaves with a strong camphor-like scent. Its yellow flower heads consist of numerous small florets arranged in a disk.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
