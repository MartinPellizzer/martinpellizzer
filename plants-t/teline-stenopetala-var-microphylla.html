Teline stenopetala var. microphylla

Teline stenopetala var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf teline, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are densely packed along its stems, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
