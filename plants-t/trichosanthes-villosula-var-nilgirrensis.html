Trichosanthes villosula var. nilgirrensis

Trichosanthes villosula var. nilgirrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri trumpet flower, is a climbing vine native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and the plant is valued for its ornamental beauty.
