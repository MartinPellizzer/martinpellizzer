Trichomanes warburgii

Trichomanes warburgii, commonly known as the Warburg's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green in color. Its intricate leaf structure and ability to retain moisture make it a distinctive and visually appealing fern species.
