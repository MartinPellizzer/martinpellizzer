Tridentea parvipuncta var. truncata

Tridentea parvipuncta var. truncata, commonly known as the dwarf punctured trident, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with finely serrated edges, and its flowers are typically small and white or pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small garden spaces or as a potted plant.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and delicate species in horticultural collections.
