Tanacetum argenteum var. pumilum

Tanacetum argenteum var. pumilum, commonly known as silverleaf yarrow or small silver yarrow, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are covered in dense, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow composite flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its silvery foliage and delicate floral display.
