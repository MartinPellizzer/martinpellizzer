Tecoma Schumanniana

Tecoma schumanniana, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is prized for its vibrant yellow blooms that cascade from long, pendulous branches. This plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. With proper care, Tecoma schumanniana can provide continuous color throughout the growing season.
