Tristellateia greveana var. brachyptera

Tristellateia greveana var. brachyptera, commonly known as the short-winged tristellateia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces yellowish-white ray florets surrounded by pale green disk florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially grown.
