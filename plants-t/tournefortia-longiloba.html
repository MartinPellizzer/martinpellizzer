Tournefortia longiloba

Tournefortia longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and have a yellowish or greenish hue with a distinctive tubular corolla.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.
