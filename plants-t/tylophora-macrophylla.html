Tylophora macrophylla

Tylophora macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf tylophora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, fragrant flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are clustered in inflorescences.
