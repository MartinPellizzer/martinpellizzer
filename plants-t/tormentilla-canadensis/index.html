Tormentilla Canadensis

Tormentilla canadensis is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern North America. It is known for its delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found along stream banks and in woodland areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, giving it a unique texture. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
