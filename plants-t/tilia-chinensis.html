Tilia chinensis

Tilia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese linden, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The tree produces small, round nuts enclosed in a woody capsule. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
