Telipogon phuyupatamarcensis

Telipogon phuyupatamarcensis, commonly known as the Phuyu Patamarca orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Peru.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at elevations above 3,000 meters. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this unique botanical treasure from threats such as habitat loss and climate change.
