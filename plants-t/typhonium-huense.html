Typhonium huense

Typhonium huense, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped inflorescences that are often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Typhonium huense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional medicine.
