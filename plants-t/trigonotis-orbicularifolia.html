Trigonotis orbicularifolia

Trigonotis orbicularifolia, commonly known as the orbicular leaf trigonotis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its orbicular (rounded) leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Trigonotis orbicularifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
