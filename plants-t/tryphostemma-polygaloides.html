Tryphostemma polygaloides

Tryphostemma polygaloides, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, characterized by its woody stems and small, greenish flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties. Its leaves are often used in local remedies for treating ailments such as inflammation and skin conditions.
