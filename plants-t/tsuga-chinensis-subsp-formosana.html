Tsuga chinensis subsp. formosana

Tsuga chinensis subsp.

formosana, commonly known as the Formosan hemlock, is a species of conifer native to Taiwan. It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in cool, moist mountainous regions.

The plant has slender, needle-like leaves that are dark green on top and pale white underneath. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
