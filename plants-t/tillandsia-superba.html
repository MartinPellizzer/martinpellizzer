Tillandsia superba

Tillandsia superba, commonly known as the royal tillandsia, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with multiple flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Tillandsia superba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable environments.
