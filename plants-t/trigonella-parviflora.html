Trigonella parviflora

Trigonella parviflora, commonly known as small-flowered fenugreek, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 20 to 40 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It produces lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds.

Trigonella parviflora is often found in disturbed soils and is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
