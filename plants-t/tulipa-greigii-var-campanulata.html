Tulipa greigii var. campanulata

Tulipa greigii var. campanulata, commonly known as the campanulata tulip, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Turkey.

It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or gray tinge. It is well-suited for rock gardens and well-drained soil, and it is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is prized for its vibrant colors and compact growth habit.
