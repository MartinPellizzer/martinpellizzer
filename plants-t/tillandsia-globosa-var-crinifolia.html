Tillandsia globosa var. crinifolia

Tillandsia globosa var. crinifolia, commonly known as the Crinifolia air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that can store water, allowing it to thrive in humid environments. It produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in the summer.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
