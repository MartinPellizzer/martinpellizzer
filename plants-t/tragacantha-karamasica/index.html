Tragacantha Karamasica

Tragacantha karamasica is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique ecological adaptations to harsh environments. This plant is often found in rocky soils and arid landscapes where few other species can thrive. Its survival strategies include deep root systems and the ability to store water efficiently. Despite its resilience, it remains under threat due to habitat loss and climate change.
