Touchardia haupuensis

The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats within mesic to wet forest ecosystems, often found in areas with high humidity and well-drained soils.
