Triscenia ovina

Triscenia ovina, commonly known as the woolly triscenia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves and stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Triscenia ovina thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
