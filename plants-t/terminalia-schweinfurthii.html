Terminalia schweinfurthii

Terminalia schweinfurthii, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and dense clusters of white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed enclosed in a hard, woody capsule. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
