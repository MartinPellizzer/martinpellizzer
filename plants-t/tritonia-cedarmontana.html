Tritonia cedarmontana

Tritonia cedarmontana, commonly known as the cedar mountain tritonia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms in late spring to early summer.

Tritonia cedarmontana is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
