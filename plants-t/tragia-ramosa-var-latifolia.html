Tragia ramosa var. latifolia

Tragia ramosa var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf tragia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.

Tragia ramosa var. latifolia is adapted to arid environments and is known for its rapid growth and short life cycle.
