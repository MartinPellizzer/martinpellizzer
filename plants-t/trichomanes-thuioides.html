Trichomanes thuioides

Trichomanes thuioides, commonly known as the silver maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically covered in a silvery or pale green coloration. This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually small and closely spaced, forming a dense, mat-like structure.

Trichomanes thuioides is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
