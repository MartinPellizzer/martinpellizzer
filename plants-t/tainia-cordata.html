Tainia cordata

Tainia cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf or heartleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically broad and glossy. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its rhizomes are used as a starchy root crop, similar to arrowroot.

Tainia cordata thrives in warm, humid environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
