Thelymitra matthewsii

Thelymitra matthewsii, commonly known as the Matthew's sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It produces striking, bright yellow flowers with red markings, typically blooming in spring. The plant has a slender, leafless stem that supports the flowering spikes. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Thelymitra matthewsii is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.
