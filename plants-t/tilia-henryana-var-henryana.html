Tilia henryana var. henryana

Tilia henryana var. henryana, commonly known as the Henry's lime tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5-9 cm in diameter, with a serrated margin and a pale green underside. The flowers are small, fragrant, and white, appearing in clusters, and bloom in early summer. The tree produces small, round, yellowish fruits that mature in late summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
