Trachelospermum jasminoides var. pubescens

Trachelospermum jasminoides var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a trailing vine that produces fragrant, white flowers with a strong jasmine scent. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a pubescent (hairy) underside, which gives it its varietal name. It thrives in warm climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
