Trichosalpinx pusilla

Trichosalpinx pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf spider orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with several whorls of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, pale green or yellowish, and have a distinctive, intricate structure typical of orchids.

Trichosalpinx pusilla is known for its delicate appearance and is often found growing in clusters.
