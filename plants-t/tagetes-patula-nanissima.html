Tagetes patula nanissima

Tagetes patula nanissima, commonly known as the dwarf marigold, is a compact variety of the marigold plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, vibrant yellow or orange flowers and feathery leaves. This plant is typically grown as an annual, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to repel certain pests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for container gardening and borders.
