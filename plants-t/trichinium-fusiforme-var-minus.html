Trichinium fusiforme var. minus

Trichinium fusiforme var. minus, commonly known as the slender false nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes stinging nettles. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have serrated edges.

The flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, dry fruits called schizocarps.
