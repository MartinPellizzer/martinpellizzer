Tricalistra ochracea

Tricalistra ochracea, commonly known as the yellow tricalistra, is a member of the Malvaceae family, native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green hue. Tricalistra ochracea is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
