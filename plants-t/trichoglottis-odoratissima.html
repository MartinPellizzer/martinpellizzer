Trichoglottis odoratissima

Trichoglottis odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, pendulous flowers with yellow and white petals. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It is prized for its delicate appearance and pleasant scent.

Trichoglottis odoratissima is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
