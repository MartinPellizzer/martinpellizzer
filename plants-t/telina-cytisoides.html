Telina cytisoides

Telina cytisoides, commonly known as the yellow burr clover, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
