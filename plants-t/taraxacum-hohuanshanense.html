Taraxacum hohuanshanense

Taraxacum hohuanshanense, commonly known as the Hohuanshan dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Hohuanshan region of Yunnan Province. This species is characterized by its basal rosette of pale green, hairy leaves and a yellow composite flower head that blooms in late spring. The plant produces a distinctive elongated, hollow stem that supports its flower and seed head.

It is adapted to grow in rocky and mountainous habitats, showcasing resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
