Tephrosia savannicola

Tephrosia savannicola, commonly known as the savanna tephrosia, is a leguminous shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in open savannas and grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its seed pods are slender and contain several elliptical seeds.

Tephrosia savannicola is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.
