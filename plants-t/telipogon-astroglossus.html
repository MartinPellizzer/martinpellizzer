Telipogon astroglossus

Telipogon astroglossus, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in high humidity and partial shade. Its unique floral structure is adapted for pollination by specific insects, such as bees and moths.

Telipogon astroglossus is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays an important role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
