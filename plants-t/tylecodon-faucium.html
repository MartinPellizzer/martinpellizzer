Tylecodon faucium

Tylecodon faucium, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it both drought-resistant and visually striking.
