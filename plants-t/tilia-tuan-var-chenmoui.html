Tilia tuan var. chenmoui

Tilia tuan var. chenmoui, commonly known as the Chenmou tilia, is a rare variant of the Tilia genus native to specific regions in China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a smooth margin and a pale green underside. The flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and typically appear in early summer. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous covering.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
