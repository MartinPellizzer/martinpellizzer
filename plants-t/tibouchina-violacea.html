Tibouchina violacea

Tibouchina violacea, commonly known as the purple tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
