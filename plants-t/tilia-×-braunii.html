Tilia × braunii

Tilia × braunii, commonly known as the Chinese linden, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early summer. The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often peeling in thin layers. It typically grows to a height of 10–20 meters and prefers well-drained, fertile soils.

This tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often planted in gardens and parks for its aesthetic appeal.
