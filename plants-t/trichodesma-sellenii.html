Trichodesma sellenii

Trichodesma sellenii, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, white or pale blue, and arranged in clusters.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
