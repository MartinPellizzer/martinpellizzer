Tillandsia loxichaensis

Tillandsia loxichaensis, commonly known as the Loxicha air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The species is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture from the air. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
