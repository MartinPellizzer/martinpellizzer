Tricoryne humilis

Tricoryne humilis, commonly known as the dwarf tricoryne, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a clustered inflorescence.

Tricoryne humilis is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
