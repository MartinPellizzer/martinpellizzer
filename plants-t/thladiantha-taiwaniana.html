Thladiantha taiwaniana

Thladiantha taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan goji plant, is a deciduous shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. These berries are edible and have a sweet, slightly tart flavor, making them popular in local cuisines.

Thladiantha taiwaniana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
