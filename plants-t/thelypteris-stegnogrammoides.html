Thelypteris stegnogrammoides

Thelypteris stegnogrammoides, commonly known as the mountain shield fern, is a species of fern native to the forests of Southeast Asia.

The fronds are usually evergreen, with a soft texture and a pale green color, making it a notable ornamental plant in horticulture.
