Thunbergianthus ruwenzoriensis f. macrocalyx

Thunbergianthus ruwenzoriensis f. macrocalyx, commonly known as the Ruwenzori giant heather, is a unique horticultural form of a rare plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which stand out against its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil. Its foliage is dense and compact, often forming a low-growing mound, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
