Townsendia bakeri

Townsendia bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's Townsendia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in open habitats such as meadows and slopes. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
