Teucrium qingyuanense

Teucrium qingyuanense, commonly known as Qingyuan germander, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
