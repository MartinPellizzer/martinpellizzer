Tragus racemosus f. erectus

Tragus racemosus f. erectus, commonly known as the erect form of the common tragus, is a variant of the Tragus racemosus species.

It is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa. The plant features erect growth habit with dense, spiky foliage. Its flowers are typically small and white, arranged in racemes.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
