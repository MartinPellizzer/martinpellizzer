Tibouchina chironioides

Tibouchina chironioides, commonly known as the Brazilian flamebush, is a shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible for certain bird species.
