Telis cachemiriana

Telis cachemiriana, commonly known as the Kashmir Telis, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Telis cachemiriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
