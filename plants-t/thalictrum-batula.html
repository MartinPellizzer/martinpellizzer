Thalictrum batula

Thalictrum batula, commonly known as the lesser meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in early spring. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in moist soils with good drainage.

Thalictrum batula is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
