Tulista pungens

Tulista pungens, commonly known as the pungent tulip, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Tulista pungens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
