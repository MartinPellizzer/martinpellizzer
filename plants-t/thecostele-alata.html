Thecostele alata

Thecostele alata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged pods, which contain edible seeds and beans. The plant has compound leaves with three leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

Thecostele alata is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial crop in sustainable agriculture.
