Tephrosia vilersii

Tephrosia vilersii, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. Its leaves are pinnate, consisting of several pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Tephrosia vilersii is known for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
