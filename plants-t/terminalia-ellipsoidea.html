Terminalia ellipsoidea

Terminalia ellipsoidea, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing a single seed. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
