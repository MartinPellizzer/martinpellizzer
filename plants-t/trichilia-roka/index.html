Trichilia Roka

Trichilia roka is a lesser-known but ecologically significant plant species found primarily in the tropical regions of Africa. Belonging to the Meliaceae family, it is known for its unique morphological features and potential medicinal properties. This tree thrives in humid, lowland forests and plays a vital role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species. Despite its importance, Trichilia roka remains under-researched, highlighting the need for further botanical and ecological studies. Its conservation status is a growing concern due to habitat loss and overexploitation.
