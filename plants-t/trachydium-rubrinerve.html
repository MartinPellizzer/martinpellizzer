Trachydium rubrinerve

Trachydium rubrinerve, commonly known as the red-veined dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, hairy leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils. Its distinctive red veins in the leaves make it easily identifiable.

Trachydium rubrinerve is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
