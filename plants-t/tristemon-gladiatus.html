Tristemon gladiatus

Tristemon gladiatus, commonly known as the swordleaf tristemon, is a perennial herb native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deeply veined, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and can be used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Tristemon gladiatus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
