Tabernaemontana langbianensis

Tabernaemontana langbianensis, commonly known as the Langbian starry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers, which bloom in clusters, are often pale blue or white, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.
