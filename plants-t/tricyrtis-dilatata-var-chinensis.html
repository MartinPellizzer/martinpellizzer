Tricyrtis dilatata var. chinensis

Tricyrtis dilatata var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese spurge lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
