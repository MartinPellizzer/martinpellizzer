Trichodiadema echinatum

Trichodiadema echinatum, commonly known as the spiny ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
