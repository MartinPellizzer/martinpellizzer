Tabernaemontana crispiflora

Tabernaemontana crispiflora, commonly known as the wrinkled angel's trumpet, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are often slightly wrinkled, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
