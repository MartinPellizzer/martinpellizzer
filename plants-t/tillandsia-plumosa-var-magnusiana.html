Tillandsia plumosa var. magnusiana

Tillandsia plumosa var. magnusiana, commonly known as the Magnificent Feather Plant, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, feathery inflorescences that can extend up to 1.5 meters in length, giving it a dramatic and elegant appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and does not require soil, as it absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves. Its foliage is typically gray-green with a slightly fuzzy texture, and it produces small, white or pale purple flowers.

This variety is popular among epiphytic plant enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
