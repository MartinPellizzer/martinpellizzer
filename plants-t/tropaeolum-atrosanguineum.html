Tropaeolum atrosanguineum

Tropaeolum atrosanguineum, commonly known as the crimson leaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its foliage and flowers are edible, making it a popular choice for both garden decoration and culinary use.
