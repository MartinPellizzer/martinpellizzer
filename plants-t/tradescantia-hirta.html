Tradescantia hirta

Tradescantia hirta, commonly known as spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving the plant a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Tradescantia hirta is a perennial plant that blooms in late spring to early summer.
