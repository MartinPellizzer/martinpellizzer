Teucrium polium subsp. thymoides

Teucrium polium subsp.

thymoides, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a distinct lemony fragrance due to its high concentration of essential oils.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in compact spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its medicinal properties have been utilized historically for digestive and anti-inflammatory purposes.
