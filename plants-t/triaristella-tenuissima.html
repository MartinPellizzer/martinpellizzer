Triaristella tenuissima

Triaristella tenuissima, commonly known as the slender triaristella, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elongated leaves and clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. It is often found in tropical rainforests and is adapted to high levels of atmospheric moisture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
