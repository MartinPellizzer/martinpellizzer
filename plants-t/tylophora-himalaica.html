Tylophora himalaica

Tylophora himalaica, commonly known as Himalayan Tylophora, is a climbing shrub native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruit is a capsule containing tiny seeds. Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
