Tilia hyrcana

Tilia hyrcana, commonly known as the Hyrcanian lime, is a deciduous tree native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, spherical fruits that contain one or two seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soils.

Tilia hyrcana is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
