Toona ciliata var. brevipetiolulata

Toona ciliata var. brevipetiolulata, commonly known as the short-stemmed toona, is a variant of the toona ciliata species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The leaves are typically shorter and have shorter petioles compared to the standard variety, giving it a distinct appearance. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally used in traditional medicine.
