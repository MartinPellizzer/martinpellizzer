Trichocereus orurensis var. albiflorus

Trichocereus orurensis var. albiflorus, commonly known as the Whiteflower Torch Cactus, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and clusters of white, nocturnal flowers. The plant has a spiny exterior and is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its blooming period typically occurs during the cooler months, making it a striking addition to desert gardens.
