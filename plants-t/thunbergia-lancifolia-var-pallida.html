Thunbergia lancifolia var. pallida

Thunbergia lancifolia var. pallida, commonly known as the pale thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped, pale green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a faintly veined pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
