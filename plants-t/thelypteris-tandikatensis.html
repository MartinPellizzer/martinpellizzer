Thelypteris tandikatensis

Thelypteris tandikatensis, commonly known as the Tandikat fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds with a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Thelypteris tandikatensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
