Thymus Chaubardii F. Taygeteus

Thymus chaubardii f. taygeteus is a unique and resilient plant known for its vibrant foliage and aromatic properties. This variety of thyme thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and silver-green leaves add a striking visual appeal to any landscape. While it shares characteristics with common thyme, it stands out with its distinct foliage and subtle floral display. Often favored by gardeners for its low maintenance and ornamental value, this plant is a valuable addition to both ornamental and medicinal gardens.
