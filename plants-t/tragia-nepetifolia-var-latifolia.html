Tragia nepetifolia var. latifolia

Tragia nepetifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf tragia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a rough, hairy texture. This species is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
