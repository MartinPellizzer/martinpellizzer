Taraxacum lenense

Taraxacum lenense, commonly known as the Lenense dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Central and Eastern Europe, including parts of Russia and Ukraine. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped leaves and a yellow flower head that develops into a fluffy seed head. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
