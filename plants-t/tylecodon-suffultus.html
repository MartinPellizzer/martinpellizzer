Tylecodon suffultus

Tylecodon suffultus, commonly known as the inflated ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. Its unique appearance, with swollen, balloon-like leaves, makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Tylecodon suffultus is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water once established.
