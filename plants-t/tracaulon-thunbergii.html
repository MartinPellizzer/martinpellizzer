Tracaulon thunbergii

Tracaulon thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces berries that range in color from red to black.

Tracaulon thunbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
