Typhonium acetosella

Typhonium acetosella, commonly known as the swamp arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces small, white, spadix-like flowers. The plant thrives in wet, swampy environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly in suitable conditions.

Typhonium acetosella is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
