Tetraneuris depressa

Tetraneuris depressa, commonly known as the low flaxweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky soils, and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Tetraneuris depressa is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a pioneer species in ecological succession.
