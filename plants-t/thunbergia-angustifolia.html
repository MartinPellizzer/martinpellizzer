Thunbergia angustifolia

Thunbergia angustifolia, commonly known as the slender blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often opening in the early morning. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia angustifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
