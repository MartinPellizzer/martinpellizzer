Thymus glabrescens f. stenophyllus

Thymus glabrescens f. stenophyllus, commonly known as narrow-leaved thyme, is a compact, low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically narrow and densely packed, giving it a distinctive, mat-like appearance. The plant produces small pink or white flowers in the summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its aromatic properties.
