Teucrium fruticans var. linearifolium

Teucrium fruticans var. linearifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and long blooming period make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
