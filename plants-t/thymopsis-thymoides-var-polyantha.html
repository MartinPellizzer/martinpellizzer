Thymopsis thymoides var. polyantha

Thymopsis thymoides var. polyantha, commonly known as the polyantha thymopsis, is a low-growing evergreen perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has aromatic, woolly leaves that are often used in herbal medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
