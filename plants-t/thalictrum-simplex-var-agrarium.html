Thalictrum simplex var. agrarium

Thalictrum simplex var. agrarium, commonly known as the agrarian meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and often covered in a soft, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in loose, upright inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a suitable choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and subtle floral display.
