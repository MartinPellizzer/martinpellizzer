Tulipa didieri var. mauriana

Tulipa didieri var. mauriana, commonly known as the Mauriana tulip, is a striking bulbous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

This variety is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces sturdy, upright stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique color palette, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant spring display.
