Thelysia fumosa

Thelysia fumosa, commonly known as the smoky orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive smoky-pink petals and a slender, erect stem. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Thelysia fumosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
