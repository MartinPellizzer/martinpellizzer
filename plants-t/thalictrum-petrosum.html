Thalictrum petrosum

Thalictrum petrosum, commonly known as the rock meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and alpine environments in Europe.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in upright, branched inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Thalictrum petrosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
