Thelypteris inaequilobata

Thelypteris inaequilobata, commonly known as the unequal-lobed shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its leathery, evergreen fronds that are typically divided into asymmetrical segments. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. Its rhizome is creeping and produces both roots and new shoots.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, almost metallic sheen.
