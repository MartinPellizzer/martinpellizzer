Thunbergia eberhardtii

Thunbergia eberhardtii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Thunbergia eberhardtii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, flowering plants.
