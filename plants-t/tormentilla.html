Tormentilla

Tormentilla, commonly known as snowberry, is a shrub belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white, clustered flowers that appear in late spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for birds. Tormentilla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
