Thysanostigma Odontites

Thysanostigma odontites is a rare and lesser-known plant species that belongs to the nightshade family. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance features striking, velvety leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom at night. Despite its beauty, it remains under-researched and poorly documented in botanical studies. This plant offers intriguing possibilities for both ecological and medicinal research.
