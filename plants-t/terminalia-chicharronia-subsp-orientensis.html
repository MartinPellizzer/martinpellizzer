Terminalia chicharronia subsp. orientensis

Terminalia chicharronia subsp.

orientensis, commonly known as the oriental tamarind, is a deciduous tree native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and economic importance in local communities.
