Tetrastigma lineare

Tetrastigma lineare, commonly known as the climbing raisin vine, is a member of the Vitaceae family.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its tendrils and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. Tetrastigma lineare is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing along fences, trees, and in shaded areas.
