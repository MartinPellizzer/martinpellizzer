Toliara

Toliara, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. Toliara thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

Its leaves are also used in traditional practices for their aromatic and therapeutic benefits.
