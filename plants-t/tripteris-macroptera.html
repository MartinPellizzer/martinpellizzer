Tripteris macroptera

Tripteris macroptera, commonly known as the large-winged tripteris, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tripteridaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, wing-like segments. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded forest environments. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its unique leaf structure.
