Tribulus macropterus var. ochroleucus

Tribulus macropterus var. ochroleucus, commonly known as the yellow spurred caltrop, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and spiny fruits, which contain four seeds. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval and hairy.

The species is known for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
