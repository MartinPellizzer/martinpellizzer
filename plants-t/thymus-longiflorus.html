Thymus longiflorus

Thymus longiflorus, commonly known as slender thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both landscaping and herbal use.
