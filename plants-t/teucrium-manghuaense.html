Teucrium manghuaense

Teucrium manghuaense, commonly known as the Manghua germander, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions and features small, violet-blue flowers. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Teucrium manghuaense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
