Taraxacum cyclocentrum

Taraxacum cyclocentrum, commonly known as the cyclocentrum dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flower heads and heart-shaped basal leaves. Native to Central Asia, this plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. Its life cycle includes a rosette of leaves that produces a flowering stem with a crown of florets.

The plant is notable for its ability to produce a large number of seeds, which are dispersed by the wind.
