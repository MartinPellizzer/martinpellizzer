Timonius subauritus var. subauritus

Timonius subauritus var. subauritus, commonly known as the silverleaf timonius, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among gardeners in arid climates.
