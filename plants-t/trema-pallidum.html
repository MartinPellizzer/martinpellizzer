Trema pallidum

Trema pallidum, commonly known as the pale trema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its small, pale green leaves and delicate, white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Trema pallidum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
