Tulipa zenaidae

Tulipa zenaidae, commonly known as the Zenaida tulip, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Tulipa.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. It typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves.

The tulip is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
