Tridax balbisioides

Tridax balbisioides, commonly known as the whiteleaf tridax, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow with white ray florets, and it produces small, dry fruits called achenes.

Tridax balbisioides is often found in disturbed areas and can thrive in poor soils.
