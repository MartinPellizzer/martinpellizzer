Trachystoma aphanoneurum var. leiocarpum

Trachystoma aphanoneurum var. leiocarpum, commonly known as the smooth-carpelled trachystoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a cylindrical stem that is often covered with fine hairs.

This variety is noted for its ecological adaptability and is sometimes used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
