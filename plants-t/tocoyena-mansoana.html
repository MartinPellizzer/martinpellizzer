Tocoyena mansoana

Tocoyena mansoana, commonly known as the Manosana tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its evergreen nature and compound leaves. The tree produces fragrant, white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a follicle that contains several seeds, and the plant is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Tocoyena mansoana thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland tropical forests.
