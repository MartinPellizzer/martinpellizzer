Tillandsia buchlohii

Tillandsia buchlohii, commonly known as the Buchlohii air plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to Central America.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. It produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences with vibrant purple or red flowers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
