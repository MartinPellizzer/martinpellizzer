Thouinia rigidissima

Thouinia rigidissima, commonly known as the rigid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and lobed, gray-green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
