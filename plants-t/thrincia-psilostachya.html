Thrincia psilostachya

Thrincia psilostachya, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers in panicles, which are clustered in a branching pattern. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a slight waxy coating.

Thrincia psilostachya is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
