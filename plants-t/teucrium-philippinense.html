Teucrium philippinense

Teucrium philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine germander, is a herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a strong, mint-like fragrance.

Teucrium philippinense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
