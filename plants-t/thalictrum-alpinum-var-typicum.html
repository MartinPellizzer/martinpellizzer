Thalictrum alpinum var. typicum

Thalictrum alpinum var. typicum, commonly known as the Alpine meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its fibrous root system helps stabilize soil in mountainous environments.

Thalictrum alpinum var. typicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
