Tabernaemontana nervosa

Tabernaemontana nervosa, commonly known as the veined flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear bright orange to red flowers, which are often fragrant. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive veined pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
