Trachelium tenuifolium

Trachelium tenuifolium, commonly known as slender-leaf trachelium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the summer months.

Trachelium tenuifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners in mild climates.
