Trichomanes sumatranum

Trichomanes sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran huperzia, is a fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore-producing structures, called sporangia, are clustered on the underside of its leaves.

Trichomanes sumatranum is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
