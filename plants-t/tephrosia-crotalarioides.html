Tephrosia crotalarioides

Tephrosia crotalarioides, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, featuring slender, often spiny branches. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its leaves are opposite, pinnately compound, and often have a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

The plant is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
