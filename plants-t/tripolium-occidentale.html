Tripolium occidentale

Tripolium occidentale, commonly known as western tripe.

It is a flowering plant native to the western United States, typically found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
