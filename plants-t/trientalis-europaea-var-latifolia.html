Trientalis europaea var. latifolia

Trientalis europaea var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Trientalis europaea var. latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
