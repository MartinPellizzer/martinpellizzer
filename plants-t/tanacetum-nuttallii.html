Tanacetum nuttallii

Tanacetum nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's tansy, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as wet meadows and streambanks. It is often found in the western United States and parts of Canada.

Tanacetum nuttallii is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
