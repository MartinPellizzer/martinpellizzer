Turritis mollissima

Turritis mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf turritis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Turritis mollissima is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
