Terminalia subcordata

Terminalia subcordata, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in spring. Terminalia subcordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
