Tanarius sinensis

Tanarius sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese tanarius, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
