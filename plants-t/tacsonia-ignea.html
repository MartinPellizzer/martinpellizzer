Tacsonia ignea

Tacsonia ignea, commonly known as the firebush, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy texture. Tacsonia ignea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
