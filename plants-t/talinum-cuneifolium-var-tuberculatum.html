Talinum cuneifolium var. tuberculatum

Talinum cuneifolium var. tuberculatum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
