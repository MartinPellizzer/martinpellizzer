Trias picta

Trias picta, commonly known as the painted trias, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spiky foliage that displays a range of colors, including shades of green, red, and purple. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Trias picta thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
