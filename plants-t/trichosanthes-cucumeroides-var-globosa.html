Trichosanthes cucumeroides var. globosa

Trichosanthes cucumeroides var. globosa, commonly known as the globe gourd, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It produces large, spherical, green fruits that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and bears yellow flowers with five petals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a decorative or edible plant.
