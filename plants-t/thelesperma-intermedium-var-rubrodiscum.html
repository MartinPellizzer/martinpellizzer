Thelesperma intermedium var. rubrodiscum

Thelesperma intermedium var. rubrodiscum, commonly known as the red disk milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences, and it has hairy, linear leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It is a member of the milkweed family (Asclepiadaceae) and is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
