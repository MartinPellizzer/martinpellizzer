Thalictrum revolutum var. subglabrum

Thalictrum revolutum var. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers in early summer. Its flowers are arranged in racemes and are often adorned with showy, petal-like sepals.

The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.
