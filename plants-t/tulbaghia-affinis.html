Tulbaghia affinis

Tulbaghia affinis, commonly known as the pink bulbous lily, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to purple flowers. The plant has a bulbous root system that stores nutrients and water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Tulbaghia affinis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and mild, garlic-like scent.
