Taraxacum canaliculatum

Taraxacum canaliculatum, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and a distinctive, hollow, leafless stem that supports the inflorescence. Taraxacum canaliculatum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in a variety of soil conditions.
