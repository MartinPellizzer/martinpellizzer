Thymus jankae var. bosnensis

Thymus jankae var. bosnensis, commonly known as Bosnian thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves and small pink or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its pungent, thyme-like aroma.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
