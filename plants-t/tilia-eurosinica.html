Tilia eurosinica

Tilia eurosinica, commonly known as the European lime, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits called samaras, which are dispersed by wind. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia eurosinica is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
