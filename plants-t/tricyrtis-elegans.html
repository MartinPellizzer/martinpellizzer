Tricyrtis elegans

Tricyrtis elegans, commonly known as the Japanese iris or spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the iris family (Iridaceae) and is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet, featuring lance-shaped leaves and delicate, spurred petals. Its blooms, which range in color from white to deep purple, are often adorned with a distinctive, elongated spur at the base of each petal.

Tricyrtis elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
