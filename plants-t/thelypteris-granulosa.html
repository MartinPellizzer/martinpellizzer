Thelypteris granulosa

Thelypteris granulosa, commonly known as the granulate shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and the presence of small, granular structures on its fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that aids in identification.

Thelypteris granulosa plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
