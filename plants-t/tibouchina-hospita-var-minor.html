Tibouchina hospita var. minor

Tibouchina hospita var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf tibouchina, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
