Trigonostemon macgregorii

Trigonostemon macgregorii, commonly known as the MacGregor's trigonostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually white or pale yellow in color.

Trigonostemon macgregorii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
