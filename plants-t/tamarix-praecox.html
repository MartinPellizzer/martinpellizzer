Tamarix praecox

Tamarix praecox, commonly known as early tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and produces pink or reddish flowers in clusters. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate salt spray.

Tamarix praecox is used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought resistance.
