Thymus × mourae

Thymus × mourae, commonly known as hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a strong thyme scent and produces small, pink to purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
