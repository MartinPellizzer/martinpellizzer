Tectaria calcarea

Tectaria calcarea, commonly known as the limestone shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows on calcareous substrates, such as limestone rocks or soil. The plant has a rhizomatous structure with a thick, creeping stem that produces erect, lanceolate fronds. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive shield-like appearance on the underside.

Tectaria calcarea is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
