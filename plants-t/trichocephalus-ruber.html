Trichocephalus ruber

Trichocephalus ruber, commonly known as the red-headed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and yellow stamen, which contribute to its striking appearance. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Its unique coloration and floral structure make it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
