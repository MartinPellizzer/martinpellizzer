Trigonosciadium tuberosum subsp. undulatum

Trigonosciadium tuberosum subsp.

undulatum, commonly known as the wavy tuberous morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped, pale blue to purple flowers.

The plant produces edible tubers that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
