Trachystigma

Trachystigma, commonly known as the "small-stemmed orchid," is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plants typically have narrow, grass-like leaves and produce small, delicate blooms that can be white, yellow, or green in color. Trachystigma species are native to various regions in Asia, particularly China and Southeast Asia.

They are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
