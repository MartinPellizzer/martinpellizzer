Tibetia yunnanensis var. coelestis

Tibetia yunnanensis var. coelestis, commonly known as the sky-blue tibetia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant is distinguished by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are finely divided and emit a mild, spicy scent when crushed.
