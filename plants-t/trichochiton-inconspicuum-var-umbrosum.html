Trichochiton inconspicuum var. umbrosum

Trichochiton inconspicuum var. umbrosum, commonly known as the shadowy trichochiton, is a rare and elusive plant species characterized by its delicate, light-green foliage and intricate, hair-like structures on its stems.

The plant's flowers are small and inconspicuous, often hidden among the foliage, contributing to its elusive nature.
