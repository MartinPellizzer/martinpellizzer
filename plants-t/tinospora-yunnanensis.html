Tinospora yunnanensis

Tinospora yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Tinospora, is a climbing shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Tinospora yunnanensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
