Tillandsia Appenii

Tillandsia appenii is a unique air plant native to the cloud forests of South America. It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive without soil. This species features dense, overlapping leaves that form a rosette shape, capturing moisture from the air. Its striking silver-green foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its delicate appearance, Tillandsia appenii is surprisingly resilient and easy to care for.
