Tibouchina triflora

Tibouchina triflora, commonly known as the pink trumpetbush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, somewhat hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Tibouchina triflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
