Tilia × kitaibelii

Tilia × kitaibelii, commonly known as the large-flowered lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically 8–15 cm in length and have a serrated margin. The tree produces showy, fragrant, pale yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in umbels. Its bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively tolerant of urban conditions.
