Tephrosia nyikensis

Tephrosia nyikensis, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in woodland or savanna habitats. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in racemes.
