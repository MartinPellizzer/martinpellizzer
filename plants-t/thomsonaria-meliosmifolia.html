Thomsonaria meliosmifolia

Thomsonaria meliosmifolia, commonly known as the Malawi violet, is a striking flowering plant native to the Malagasy region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers with intricate patterns. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Thomsonaria meliosmifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
