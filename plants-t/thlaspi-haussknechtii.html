Thlaspi haussknechtii

Thlaspi haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's cabbagewort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard and cabbagewort species. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round seed pods.
