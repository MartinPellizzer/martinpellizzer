Tessmanniophoenix dianeura

Tessmanniophoenix dianeura, commonly known as the Diane's date palm, is a rare and endangered species native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender trunk and fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible date fruits.

Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is critically endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
