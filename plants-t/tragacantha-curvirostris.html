Tragacantha curvirostris

Tragacantha curvirostris, commonly known as the curly-beak tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, curved seed pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
