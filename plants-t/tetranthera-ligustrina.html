Tetranthera ligustrina

Tetranthera ligustrina, commonly known as the creeping tetranthera, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

Its small, yellowish flowers are typically arranged in clusters, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits known as cypsela.
