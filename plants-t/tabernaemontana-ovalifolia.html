Tabernaemontana ovalifolia

Tabernaemontana ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces striking white, star-shaped flowers with a faintly fragrant scent, typically blooming in winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
