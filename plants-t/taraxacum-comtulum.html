Taraxacum comtulum

Taraxacum comtulum, commonly known as the French dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves and a yellow flowering head that produces a crown of white fluffy seeds. This plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has a deep taproot that allows it to survive in various environmental conditions.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
