Trixago purpurea

Trixago purpurea, commonly known as purple trixago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long blooming period.
