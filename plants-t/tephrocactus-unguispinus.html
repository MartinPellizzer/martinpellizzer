Tephrocactus unguispinus

Tephrocactus unguispinus, commonly known as the spiny cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptations, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
