Thlaspi strictum

Thlaspi strictum, commonly known as narrowleaf pennycress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a biennial or annual herb that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as roadsides and meadows. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, and its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in racemes. It produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

Thlaspi strictum is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
