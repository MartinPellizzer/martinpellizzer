Touterea pterosperma

Touterea pterosperma, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces showy, red-orange inflorescences that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
