Teucrium balthazaris

Teucrium balthazaris, commonly known as Balthazar's germander, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in rocky or coastal environments and is often found in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
