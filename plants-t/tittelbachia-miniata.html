Tittelbachia miniata

Tittelbachia miniata, commonly known as the miniature tittelbachia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features small, intricately veined leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat needs, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
