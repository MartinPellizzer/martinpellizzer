Taeniophyllum pulchellum

Taeniophyllum pulchellum, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This epiphytic plant belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often hidden beneath its foliage.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
