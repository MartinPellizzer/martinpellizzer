Tectaria kweichowensis

Tectaria kweichowensis, commonly known as the Yunnan shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's morphology includes a robust, creeping rhizome and large, pinnately divided leaves.
