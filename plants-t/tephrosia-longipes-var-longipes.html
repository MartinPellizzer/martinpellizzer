Tephrosia longipes var. longipes

Tephrosia longipes var. longipes, commonly known as the longipes tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
