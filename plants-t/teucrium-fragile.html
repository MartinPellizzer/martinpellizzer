Teucrium fragile

Teucrium fragile, commonly known as fragile germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, fragrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly rough texture.

Teucrium fragile is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
