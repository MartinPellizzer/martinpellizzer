Tragacantha kuramensis

Tragacantha kuramensis, commonly known as Kuram tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces a resinous sap, which is harvested for its medicinal and industrial uses. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
