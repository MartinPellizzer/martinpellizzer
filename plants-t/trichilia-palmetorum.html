Trichilia palmetorum

Trichilia palmetorum, commonly known as the African palm tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that resemble a palm. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. Its trunk is often covered with a thick, fibrous bark that provides structural support and protection.

Trichilia palmetorum is valued for its ornamental appearance and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
