Tragia tenella

Tragia tenella, commonly known as the slender tragia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, open habitats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are visited by various pollinators. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Tragia tenella is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
