Toona serrulata

Toona serrulata, commonly known as the serrated toona, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its propagation.
