Tetrastigma laoticum

Tetrastigma laoticum, commonly known as Laotian raisin vine, is a climbing liana native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its tendrils and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by clustered, edible berries. Its botanical features make it suitable for ornamental use and traditional medicine.
