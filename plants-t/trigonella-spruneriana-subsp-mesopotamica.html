Trigonella spruneriana subsp. mesopotamica

Trigonella spruneriana subsp.

mesopotamica, commonly known as Mesopotamian fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of Central Asia. It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

This subspecies is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
