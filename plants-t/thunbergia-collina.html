Thunbergia collina

Thunbergia collina, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia collina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
