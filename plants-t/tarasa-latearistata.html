Tarasa latearistata

Tarasa latearistata, commonly known as the silverleaf tarasa, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.
