Tonestus lyallii

Tonestus lyallii, commonly known as the Lyall's shieldbug, is a species of plant in the family Lyallieae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is native to New Zealand and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a short, unbranched stem.

It plays a role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates.
