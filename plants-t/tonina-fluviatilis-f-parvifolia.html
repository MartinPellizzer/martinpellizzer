Tonina fluviatilis f. parvifolia

Tonina fluviatilis f. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved river tonina, is a herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbel-like flower clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as riverbanks and forest understories. Its leaves are small, lance-shaped, and have a distinctive, fragrant scent.
