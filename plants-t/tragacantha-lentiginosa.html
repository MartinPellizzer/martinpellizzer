Tragacantha lentiginosa

Tragacantha lentiginosa, commonly known as the black thorn.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its thorny stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by distinctive, sticky resinous pods. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Tragacantha lentiginosa is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
