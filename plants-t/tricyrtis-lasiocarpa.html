Tricyrtis lasiocarpa

Tricyrtis lasiocarpa, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features striking, spider-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color, with a distinctive, elongated shape. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces its showy blooms in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Tricyrtis lasiocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
