Tamarix jintaensis

Tamarix jintaensis, commonly known as the Jinta tamarisk, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of China.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and clusters of pink or reddish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to survive in saline and water-stressed environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater sources.

Tamarix jintaensis plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and preventing erosion in its native habitat.
