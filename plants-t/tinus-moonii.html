Tinus moonii

Tinus moonii, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night and close by dawn. The plant has a woody stem and broad, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Tinus moonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique nocturnal blooming habit.
