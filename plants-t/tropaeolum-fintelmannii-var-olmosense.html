Tropaeolum fintelmannii var. olmosense

Tropaeolum fintelmannii var. olmosense, commonly known as the Olmosense Nasturtium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, vibrant green leaves with a peppery scent when crushed. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible foliage, making it a versatile addition to gardens and culinary applications.
