Tyloglossa minor

Tyloglossa minor, commonly known as the small-tongued orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Tyloglossa minor grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid tropical forests.
