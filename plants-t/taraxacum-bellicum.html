Taraxacum bellicum

Taraxacum bellicum, commonly known as the battle dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped basal leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It produces a distinctive seed head with parachute-like seeds, aiding in its wind dispersal.

Due to its resilience and adaptability, it is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas.
