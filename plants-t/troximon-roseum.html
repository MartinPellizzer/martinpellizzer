Troximon roseum

Troximon roseum, commonly known as the rose-scented orchid, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant has slender, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
