Trifolium haussknechtii

Trifolium haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's clover, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
