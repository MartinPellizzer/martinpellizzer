Tofieldia glutinosa var. intermedia

Tofieldia glutinosa var. intermedia, commonly known as the sticky tofieldia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and showy, spike-like inflorescences.
