Trichocladus crinitus

Trichocladus crinitus, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Terniaceae and is characterized by its dense, wiry, and hairy stems. The plant produces small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. Trichocladus crinitus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
