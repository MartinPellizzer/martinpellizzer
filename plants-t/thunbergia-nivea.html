Thunbergia nivea

Thunbergia nivea, commonly known as the white thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Thunbergia nivea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its beauty and ability to climb or trail.
