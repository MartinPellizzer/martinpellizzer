Taeniophyllum platyrhizum

Taeniophyllum platyrhizum, commonly known as the broad-rooted taeniophyllum, is a small, epiphytic plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its short, creeping rhizome and small, clustered leaves.
