Thomasia diffusa

Thomasia diffusa, commonly known as the spreading thomasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters at the leaf axils.

Thomasia diffusa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
