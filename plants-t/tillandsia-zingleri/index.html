Tillandsia Zingleri

Tillandsia zingleri is a unique air plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It thrives without soil, absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. Known for its striking silver-green foliage and delicate white blooms, it is a favorite among plant enthusiasts. This epiphytic plant is easy to care for, making it ideal for indoor gardens. Its beauty and simplicity have made it a popular choice for those looking to add greenery to their spaces.
