Torricellia tiliifolia

Torricellia tiliifolia, commonly known as the Indian sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in sandy or loamy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the plant is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

Torricellia tiliifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
