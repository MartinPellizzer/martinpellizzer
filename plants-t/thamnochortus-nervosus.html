Thamnochortus nervosus

Thamnochortus nervosus, commonly known as the Cape gentian, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and have prominent veins, which give it its scientific name.
