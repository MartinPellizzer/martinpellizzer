Teucrium tsinlingense var. porphyreum

Teucrium tsinlingense var. porphyreum, commonly known as the purple teucrium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, often purple or pinkish flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
