Thlaspi affine

Thlaspi affine, commonly known as yellow cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, pinnately divided leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.

Thlaspi affine is known for its ability to accumulate heavy metals, making it a potential phytoremediation candidate.
