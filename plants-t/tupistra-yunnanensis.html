Tupistra yunnanensis

Tupistra yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.
