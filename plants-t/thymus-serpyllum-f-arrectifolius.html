Thymus serpyllum f. arrectifolius

Thymus serpyllum f. arrectifolius, commonly known as upright lemon thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that are slightly curled and upright. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is often used in culinary applications and as a ornamental plant due to its fragrance and visual appeal.
