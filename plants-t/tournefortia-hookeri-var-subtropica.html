Tournefortia hookeri var. subtropica

Tournefortia hookeri var. subtropica, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a contrasting yellow or orange throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for both garden cultivation and ornamental use.

Its rapid growth and ability to produce abundant blooms make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
