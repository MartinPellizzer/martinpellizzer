Trifolium Wormskioldii Var. Ortegae

Trifolium wormskioldii var. ortegae is a rare and delicate species of clover native to the high altitudes of the Andes. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and subtle coloration, making it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky crevices or alpine meadows. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Studying this plant provides valuable insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in extreme environments.
