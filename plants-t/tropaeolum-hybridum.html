Tropaeolum hybridum

Tropaeolum hybridum, commonly known as the garden nasturtium, is a vibrant flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and colorful, edible flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its stems are trailing or climbing, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

The plant also produces small, round, peppery-seed pods that are often used in culinary applications.
