Tragia lancifolia

Tragia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tragia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Tragia lancifolia is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
