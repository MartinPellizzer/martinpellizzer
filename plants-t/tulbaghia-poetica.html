Tulbaghia poetica

Tulbaghia poetica, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Tulbaghia poetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
