Thalictrum minus proles collinum

Thalictrum minus proles collinum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Native to moist meadows and stream sides in North America, it thrives in partial to full shade. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
