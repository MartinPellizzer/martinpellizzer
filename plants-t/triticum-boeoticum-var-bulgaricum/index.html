Triticum Boeoticum Var. Bulgaricum

Triticum boeoticum var. bulgaricum is a lesser-known wild grass species native to southeastern Europe. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found along roadsides and in agricultural fields. This variant is closely related to other wheat species but has unique genetic traits that set it apart. Its seeds are small and hardy, allowing it to survive in harsh conditions. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
