Trochomeria bussei

Trochomeria bussei, commonly known as the Cape Town orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences with a distinctive, intricate structure.

Trochomeria bussei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
