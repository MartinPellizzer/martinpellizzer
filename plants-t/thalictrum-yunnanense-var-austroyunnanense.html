Thalictrum yunnanense var. austroyunnanense

Thalictrum yunnanense var. austroyunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southwest China.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, branched stems and is characterized by its delicate, compound leaves with numerous small leaflets. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, clustered in racemes, and bloom in early to mid-summer.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its refined appearance.
