Tylophora pilosissima

Tylophora pilosissima, commonly known as the hairy tylophora, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a follicle containing several seeds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Tylophora pilosissima is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
