Tournefortiopsis sabiceoides

Tournefortiopsis sabiceoides, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often arranged in an odd-pinnate pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Tournefortiopsis sabiceoides is often found in disturbed habitats and is adaptable to various soil types.
