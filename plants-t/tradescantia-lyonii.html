Tradescantia lyonii

Tradescantia lyonii, commonly known as Lyon's spiderwort, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, violet, or white, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. Lyon's spiderwort thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

It is often found in woodland edges, swamps, and other wetland habitats.
