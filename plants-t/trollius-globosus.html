Trollius globosus

Trollius globosus, commonly known as globe buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and western Asia.

It features showy, bright yellow, globular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical name reflects its distinctive ball-like flower structure.
