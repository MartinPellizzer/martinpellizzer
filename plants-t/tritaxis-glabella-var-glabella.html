Tritaxis glabella var. glabella

Tritaxis glabella var. glabella, commonly known as the smooth tritaxis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Tritaxis glabella var. glabella is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
