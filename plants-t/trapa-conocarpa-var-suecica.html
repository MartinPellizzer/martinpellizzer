Trapa conocarpa var. suecica

Trapa conocarpa var. suecica, commonly known as the Swedish water chestnut, is a variant of the water chestnut species native to northern Europe.

It is an aquatic plant that typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as lakes and slow-moving streams. The plant features floating leaves with long, slender stems that bear spiny fruits. Its flowers are small and yellowish, appearing in clusters at the water's surface.

The plant plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to nutrient cycling.
