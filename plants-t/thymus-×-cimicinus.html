Thymus × cimicinus

Thymus × cimicinus, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, aromatic foliage with small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Thymus × cimicinus is widely cultivated as a ornamental plant and for its culinary and medicinal uses.
