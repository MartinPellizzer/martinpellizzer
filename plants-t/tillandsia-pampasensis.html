Tillandsia pampasensis

Tillandsia pampasensis, commonly known as the Pampas air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture from the air. It is native to South America, particularly Argentina and Uruguay, where it thrives in humid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
