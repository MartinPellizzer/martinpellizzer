Tigridia × lilacea

Tigridia × lilacea, commonly known as the hybrid tiger flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Tigridia × lilacea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
