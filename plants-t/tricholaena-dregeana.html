Tricholaena dregeana

Tricholaena dregeana, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-green coloration. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine.

Tricholaena dregeana thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
