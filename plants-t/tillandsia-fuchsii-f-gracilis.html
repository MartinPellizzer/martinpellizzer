Tillandsia fuchsii f. gracilis

Tillandsia fuchsii f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender tillandsia, is a striking air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like. The plant thrives in humid environments and can be grown epiphytically on trees or in containers with well-draining soil. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white or pink, adding ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor displays.
