Tephrosia harmsii

Tephrosia harmsii, commonly known as African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its root system is deep, allowing it to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Tephrosia harmsii is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
