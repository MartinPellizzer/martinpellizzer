Tilmia Caryotifolia

Tilmia caryotifolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its striking purple and white petals that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
