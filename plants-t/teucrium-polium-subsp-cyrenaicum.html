Teucrium polium subsp. cyrenaicum

Teucrium polium subsp.

cyrenaicum, commonly known as Cyprus germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
