Tulipa gesneriana var. foxii

Tulipa gesneriana var. foxii, commonly known as the Foxii Tulip, is a cultivar of the tulip genus native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant features sturdy, upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are a dark green. It is a bulbous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice in gardens for its ornamental value.
