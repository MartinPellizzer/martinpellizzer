Talipariti celebicum

Talipariti celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes starfruit, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Clusiaceae.

It is native to the Indonesian archipelago, particularly found in regions like Sulawesi and the Moluccas. The plant is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit, which resembles a starfruit, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

The plant is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its edible fruit.
