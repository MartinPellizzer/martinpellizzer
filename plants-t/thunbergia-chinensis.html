Thunbergia chinensis

Thunbergia chinensis, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine that produces clusters of small, fragrant, blue-violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Thunbergia chinensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
