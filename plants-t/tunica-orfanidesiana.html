Tunica orfanidesiana

Tunica orfanidesiana, commonly known as the desert bellflower, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink. The plant has a thick, waxy cuticle that helps it retain moisture in dry environments. It typically grows from a basal rosette of leaves and has a short, branched stem.

Tunica orfanidesiana is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall.
