Thalictrum virgatum var. stipitatum

Thalictrum virgatum var. stipitatum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its stems are slender and often bear stipules, giving it its specific variety name.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
