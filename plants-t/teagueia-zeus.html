Teagueia zeus

Teagueia zeus, commonly known as the golden crown fern, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, compound leaves that can reach several feet in length. The leaves are typically bright green with a metallic sheen, and they often feature intricate patterns of veins. This plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities make it both ornamental and beneficial for indoor environments.
