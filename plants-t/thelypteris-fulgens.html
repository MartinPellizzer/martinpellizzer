Thelypteris fulgens

Thelypteris fulgens, commonly known as the golden holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds with a distinctive golden or bronze hue on the underside. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new fronds along their length.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
