Trichostachys letestui

Trichostachys letestui, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-blue inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grassland and savanna habitats. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

Trichostachys letestui is also valued for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
