Torenia javanica

Torenia javanica, commonly known as the Java trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual or perennial, depending on the climate. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while its stems are hairy and often branched.

Torenia javanica thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
