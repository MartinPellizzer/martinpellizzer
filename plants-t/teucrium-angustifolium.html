Teucrium angustifolium

Teucrium angustifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or violet, arranged in whorls along the stems.

It is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
