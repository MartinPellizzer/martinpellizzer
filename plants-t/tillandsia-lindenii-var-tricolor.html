Tillandsia lindenii var. tricolor

Tillandsia lindenii var. tricolor, commonly known as the Tricolor Air Plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking tricolor foliage, featuring shades of green, blue, and silver, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and does not require soil, as it absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
