Trichospermum galeottii

Trichospermum galeottii, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that are edible but have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Trichospermum galeottii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
