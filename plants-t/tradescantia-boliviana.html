Tradescantia boliviana

Tradescantia boliviana, commonly known as the silver sword or Bolivia spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces striking violet or blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated in gardens around the world.
