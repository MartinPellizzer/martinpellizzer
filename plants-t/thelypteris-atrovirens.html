Thelypteris atrovirens

Thelypteris atrovirens, commonly known as the black fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive dark coloration that gives the plant its common name.
