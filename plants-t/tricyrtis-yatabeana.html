Tricyrtis yatabeana

Tricyrtis yatabeana, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, while its flowers are borne on slender stems.

Tricyrtis yatabeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate, long-lasting blooms.
