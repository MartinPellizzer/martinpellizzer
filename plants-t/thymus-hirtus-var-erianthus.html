Thymus hirtus var. erianthus

Thymus hirtus var. erianthus, commonly known as woolly thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, gray-green foliage covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its aromatic leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
