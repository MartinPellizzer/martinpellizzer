Turnera flammea

Turnera flammea, commonly known as the flame turnera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are often clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a velvety texture.

Turnera flammea is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
