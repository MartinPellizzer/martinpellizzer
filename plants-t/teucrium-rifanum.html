Teucrium rifanum

Teucrium rifanum, commonly known as Rif germander, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often grayish-green, covered in fine hairs that help reduce water loss.

Teucrium rifanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
