Tococa erythrostachya

Tococa erythrostachya, commonly known as the red-stemmed tococa, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.
