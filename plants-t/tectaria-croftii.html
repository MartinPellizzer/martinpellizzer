Tectaria croftii

Tectaria croftii, commonly known as the croft's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that are typically divided into segments, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. Its rhizome is creeping and often covered with a dense layer of brownish hairs.

Tectaria croftii prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
