Thymus reuteri

Thymus reuteri, commonly known as the Canary Islands thyme, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features compact, gray-green foliage with a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Thymus reuteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in herbal medicine.
