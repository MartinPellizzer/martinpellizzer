Touterea Pterosperma

Touterea pterosperma is a rare and unique plant species found in the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. Known for its striking appearance and medicinal properties, it has long been valued by local communities. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often associated with ancient forest ecosystems. Its leaves and roots are used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments. Despite its significance, Touterea pterosperma remains under-researched and endangered due to habitat loss and overharvesting.
