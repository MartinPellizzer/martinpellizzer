Tetraplasandra meiandra var. prolificoides

Tetraplasandra meiandra var. prolificoides, commonly known as the prolific flowering hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
