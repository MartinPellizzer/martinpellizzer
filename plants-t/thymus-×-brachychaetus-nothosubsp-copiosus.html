Thymus × brachychaetus nothosubsp.

copiosus, commonly known as the copious thyme, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of its parent species, Thymus brachychaetus and Thymus × brachychaetus. It is a low-growing perennial herb with aromatic, small, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color.
