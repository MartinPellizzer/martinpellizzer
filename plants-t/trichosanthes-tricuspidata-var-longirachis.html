Trichosanthes tricuspidata var. longirachis

Trichosanthes tricuspidata var. longirachis, commonly known as the long-stemmed snake gourd, is a variant of the snake gourd belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender fruits that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant has large, lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers that are typically unisexual. Its vines are vigorous climbers, often requiring support structures for growth.

This variety is cultivated for its edible fruits, which have a mild, slightly bitter flavor.
