Thelypteris cylindrothrix

Thelypteris cylindrothrix, commonly known as the slender wood fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a slender, often arching form. The plant produces spores in small, clustered sori located on the underside of its fertile fronds. Thelypteris cylindrothrix is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant plant in its native habitats.
