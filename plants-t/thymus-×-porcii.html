Thymus × porcii

Thymus × porcii, commonly known as pig thyme, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus × porcii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in herbal medicine.
