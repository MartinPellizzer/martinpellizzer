Triandrophora linophylla

Triandrophora linophylla, commonly known as the silky-leaved triandrophora, is a rare and endangered plant species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-textured leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is critically endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
