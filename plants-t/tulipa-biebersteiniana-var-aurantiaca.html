Tulipa biebersteiniana var. aurantiaca

Tulipa biebersteiniana var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Crowned Tulip, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to Central Asia.

It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers with a distinctive orange to reddish-orange coloration on the outer petals, while the inner petals are typically white or pale yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun. It is valued for its vibrant color and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental appeal.

This variety is closely related to other tulip species and is part of the Liliaceae family.
