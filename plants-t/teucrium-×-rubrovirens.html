Teucrium × rubrovirens

Teucrium × rubrovirens, commonly known as scarlet germander, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a distinctive reddish hue on the underside. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal and fragrance.
