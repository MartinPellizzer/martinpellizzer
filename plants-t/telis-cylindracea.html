Telis cylindracea

Telis cylindracea, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, spiny stems and is native to southern Africa. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense, globular heads. Telis cylindracea thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
