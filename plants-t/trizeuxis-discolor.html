Trizeuxis discolor

Trizeuxis discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered spiderwort, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Trizeuxis discolor is noted for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its ornamental value in native plant gardens.
