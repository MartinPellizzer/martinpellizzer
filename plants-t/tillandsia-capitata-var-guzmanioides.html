Tillandsia capitata var. guzmanioides

Tillandsia capitata var. guzmanioides, commonly known as the Guzmana's Tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a dense, water-holding cup. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, showy white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
