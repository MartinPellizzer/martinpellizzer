Thalictrum juranum

Thalictrum juranum, commonly known as the Jura meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial shade. It produces a showy display of flowers in late spring to early summer.

Thalictrum juranum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
