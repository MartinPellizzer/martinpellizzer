Trichilia pungens

Trichilia pungens, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its pinnate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are elongated, woody capsules that contain flat, winged seeds.
