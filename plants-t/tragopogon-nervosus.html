Tragopogon nervosus

Tragopogon nervosus, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive beard-like structure at the base.

Tragopogon nervosus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun conditions.
