Trigonobalanus verticillata

Trigonobalanus verticillata, commonly known as the three-winged balanus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves, which are arranged in whorls of three. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
