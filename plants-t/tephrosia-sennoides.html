Tephrosia sennoides

Tephrosia sennoides, commonly known as African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features pinnate leaves with 5 to 11 leaflets, and its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in racemes. It produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds.

Tephrosia sennoides is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
