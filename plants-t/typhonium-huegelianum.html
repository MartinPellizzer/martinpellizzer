Typhonium huegelianum

Typhonium huegelianum, commonly known as the giant swamp taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or violet spadices surrounded by broad, white bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as swamps and marshes.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly in suitable conditions.
