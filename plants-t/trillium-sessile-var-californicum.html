Trillium sessile var. californicum

Trillium sessile var. californicum, commonly known as the California white trillium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to California.

It is characterized by its three heart-shaped, white to pale green leaves and a single, showy white flower with three petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. It produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous small seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in native plant communities.
