Thunbergia oubanguiensis

Thunbergia oubanguiensis, commonly known as the Oubangui trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with contrasting dark veins. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and is typically found in moist, tropical environments. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Thunbergia oubanguiensis is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ability to thrive in shaded areas.
