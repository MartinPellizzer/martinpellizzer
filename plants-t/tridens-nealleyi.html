Tridens nealleyi

Tridens nealleyi, commonly known as the Nealley's trident, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive three-parted leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
