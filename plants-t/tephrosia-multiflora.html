Tephrosia multiflora

Tephrosia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring compound leaves with multiple leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its root system is known for containing compounds with potential medicinal properties.

Tephrosia multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional use in herbal remedies.
