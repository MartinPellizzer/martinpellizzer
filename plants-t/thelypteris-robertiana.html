Thelypteris robertiana

Thelypteris robertiana, commonly known as the Robert's holly fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, rounded pinnae that give it a soft, lacy appearance.

Thelypteris robertiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
