Tacca plantaginea

Tacca plantaginea, commonly known as the "Chinese velvet tassel," is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that resemble tassels, which are often adorned with vivid purple or red hues. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deeply veined, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Tacca plantaginea is often cultivated for its unique and exotic appearance in botanical gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
