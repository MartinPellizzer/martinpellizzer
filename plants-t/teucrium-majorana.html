Teucrium majorana

Teucrium majorana, commonly known as pennyroyal, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stems.

It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
