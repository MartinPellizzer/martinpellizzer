Tibetia tongolensis f. coelestis

Tibetia tongolensis f. coelestis, commonly known as the celestial Tibetia, is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of Tibet.

It is characterized by its elongated, silver-blue leaves that reflect light in a way that appears almost ethereal. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the early summer, adding a delicate touch of color to its otherwise muted foliage. Its unique appearance has made it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticultural circles.

Due to its limited natural habitat and delicate growth requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its native environment.
