Tricyrtis Formosana Var. Lasiocarpa

Tricyrtis formosana var. lasiocarpa is a rare and beautiful orchid native to Taiwan. It is known for its striking violet and white flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is distinguished by its hairy stems and foliage, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts. Its delicate beauty and limited distribution make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
