Tricyrtis ishiiana var. surugensis

Tricyrtis ishiiana var. surugensis, commonly known as the Suruga spider orchid, is a rare and exquisite Japanese orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, greenish-white inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions of Japan.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
