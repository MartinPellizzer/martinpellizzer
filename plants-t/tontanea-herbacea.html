Tontanea herbacea

Tontanea herbacea, commonly known as the slender tontanea, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, capsule-like fruits.

Tontanea herbacea is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
