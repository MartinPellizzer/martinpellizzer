Theopsis longipedicellata

Theopsis longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed star-of-Bethlehem, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to South Africa, characterized by its grass-like foliage and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, spherical berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and prolonged blooming period.
