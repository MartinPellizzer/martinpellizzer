Typhonium khonkaenense

Typhonium khonkaenense, commonly known as the Khon Kaen arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking purple spadix. The plant produces edible tubers that are similar in texture to taro and are used in local cuisines. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Due to its ornamental value and culinary uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
