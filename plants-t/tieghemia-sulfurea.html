Tieghemia sulfurea

Tieghemia sulfurea, commonly known as the sulfur yellow bell, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that resemble small lanterns. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
