Trichodiadema attonsum

Trichodiadema attonsum, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
