Thouinia elliptica var. rotundata

Thouinia elliptica var. rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaf thouinia, is a perennial herb native to southern China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
