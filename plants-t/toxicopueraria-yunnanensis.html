Toxicopueraria yunnanensis

Toxicopueraria yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Kudzu, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its roots are tuberous and contain various bioactive compounds, some of which have medicinal properties.

However, it is also known to be toxic, with certain compounds potentially causing adverse effects in humans and animals.
