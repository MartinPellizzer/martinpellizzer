Thelymitra stellata

Thelymitra stellata, commonly known as the star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue with a greenish tinge. It blooms during the spring and summer months, often in shaded or moist environments.

Thelymitra stellata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
