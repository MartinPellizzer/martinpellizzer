Taraxacum informe

Taraxacum informe, commonly known as the slender dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and small, alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped. Taraxacum informe is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
