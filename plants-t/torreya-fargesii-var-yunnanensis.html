Torreya fargesii var. yunnanensis

Torreya fargesii var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan torreya, is a rare evergreen conifer native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the family Taxaceae and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical trunk and dense, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish cones that mature to a reddish-brown color, typically appearing in the fall.
