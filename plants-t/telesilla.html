Telesilla

Telesilla, commonly known as the golden star, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a compact, bushy form. Telesilla is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
