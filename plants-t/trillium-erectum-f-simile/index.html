Trillium Erectum F. Simile

Trillium erectum f. simile is a rare and striking variant of the red trillium, known for its unique floral characteristics. This botanical curiosity thrives in shaded, moist woodlands across eastern North America. Its flowers feature a distinctive white coloration, setting it apart from the typical red hue of its parent species. The plant's beauty has made it a subject of fascination among botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Despite its allure, it remains a delicate and endangered specimen in its native habitat.
