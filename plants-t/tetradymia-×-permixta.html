Tetradymia × permixta

Tetradymia × permixta, commonly known as the hybrid desert flax, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Tetradymia × permixta is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in xeriscaped gardens.
