Thelymitra retecta

Thelymitra retecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale yellow to greenish in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a waxy coating.

Thelymitra retecta is known for its delicate appearance and is a member of the Orchidaceae family.
