Thalictrum austrotibeticum

Thalictrum austrotibeticum, commonly known as the Tibetan meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau and adjacent areas in China. Thalictrum austrotibeticum thrives in moist, cool environments and is often found in meadows and alpine habitats.
