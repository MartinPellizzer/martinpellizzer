Tanacetum corymbosum var. tenuifolium

Tanacetum corymbosum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf tansy, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters called corymbs. It is often found in moist meadows and along streambanks.

Its aromatic foliage and historical use in herbal medicine highlight its ecological and botanical significance.
