Thibaudia floribunda

Thibaudia floribunda, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is often found in disturbed soils. Thibaudia floribunda is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
