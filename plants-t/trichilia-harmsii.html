Trichilia harmsii

Trichilia harmsii, commonly known as African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces a hard, oily seed kernel that is used in traditional medicine and as a source of fat. Its ecological importance includes soil stabilization and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Trichilia harmsii is also valued for its economic potential in the production of natural cosmetics and food products.
