Tournefortia pedicellata

Tournefortia pedicellata, commonly known as the slender tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellowish or greenish hue. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and usually elliptical or ovate in shape.

Tournefortia pedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
