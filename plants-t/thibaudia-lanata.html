Thibaudia lanata

Thibaudia lanata, commonly known as the woolly thibaudia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves and showy, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Thibaudia lanata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its distinctive appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
