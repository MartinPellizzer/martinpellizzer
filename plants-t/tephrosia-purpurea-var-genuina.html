Tephrosia purpurea var. genuina

Tephrosia purpurea var. genuina, commonly known as purple tephrosia, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit, trifoliate leaves, and showy purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and traditional medicinal use.
