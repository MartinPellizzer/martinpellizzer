Thelypteris phacelothrix

Thelypteris phacelothrix, commonly known as the common holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests and along stream banks.

Thelypteris phacelothrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
