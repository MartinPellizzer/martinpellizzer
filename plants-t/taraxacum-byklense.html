Taraxacum byklense

It is characterized by its basal rosette of stiff, hairy leaves and a hollow, leafless stem that supports a single, bright yellow flower head. It thrives in temperate regions and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Taraxacum byklense is known for its resilience and ability to colonize various habitats.
