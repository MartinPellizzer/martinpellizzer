Ticorea diandra

Ticorea diandra, commonly known as the purple tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its dense, woody stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in late spring to early summer.
