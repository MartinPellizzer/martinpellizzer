Thesium thunbergianum

Thesium thunbergianum, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-like growth habit and succulent stems. Native to regions of Europe and western Asia, Thesium thunbergianum thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
