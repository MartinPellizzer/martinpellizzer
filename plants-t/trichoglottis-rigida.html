Trichoglottis rigida

Trichoglottis rigida, commonly known as the rigid trichoglottis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It has a short rhizome and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Trichoglottis rigida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
