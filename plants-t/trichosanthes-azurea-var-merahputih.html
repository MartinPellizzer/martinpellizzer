Trichosanthes azurea var. merahputih

Trichosanthes azurea var. merahputih, commonly known as the Red White Trumpet Gourd, is a variant of the Trichosanthes genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, trumpet-shaped fruits that range in color from white to a striking red, depending on maturity. The plant is a vigorous climber with large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are edible when young and tender, often used in local cuisines.

The plant's botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Cucurbitaceae family.
