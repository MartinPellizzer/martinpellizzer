Tanacetum leucanthemum var. affine

Tanacetum leucanthemum var. affine, commonly known as the slender white marguerite, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features finely divided, gray-green leaves and produces white to pale yellow flowers with a central disk. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
