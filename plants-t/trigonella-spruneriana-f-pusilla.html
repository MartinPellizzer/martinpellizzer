Trigonella spruneriana f. pusilla

Trigonella spruneriana f. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf trigonella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, annual herb with slender stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in botany and horticulture.
