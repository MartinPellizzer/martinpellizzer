Trichopteryx glabrata

Trichopteryx glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf trichopteryx, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow, smooth, and arranged alternately along the stem.
