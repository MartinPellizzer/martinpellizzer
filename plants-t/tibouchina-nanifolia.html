Tibouchina nanifolia

Tibouchina nanifolia, commonly known as the dwarf tibouchina, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of Brazil.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
