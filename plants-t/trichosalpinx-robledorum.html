Trichosalpinx robledorum

Trichosalpinx robledorum, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its vibrant purple and yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor, and it has a unique structure with a tall, slender stem and clustered inflorescences.
