Tigridia mortonii

Tigridia mortonii, commonly known as the Morton's tiger flower, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and related genera. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with bright red petals and a yellow center, often resembling a tiger's eye. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Tigridia mortonii is also known for its ability to bloom multiple times during the growing season.
