Teucrium stoloniferum var. miquelianum

Teucrium stoloniferum var. miquelianum, commonly known as Miquelian germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that are typically covered with fine, soft hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate in shape, and have a slightly serrated margin. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom in summer.

This plant is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
