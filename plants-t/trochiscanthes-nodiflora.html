Trochiscanthes nodiflora

Trochiscanthes nodiflora, commonly known as the clustered-flowered milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweeds and related species. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It produces a milky sap that is typically latex-like, a common feature in the Apocynaceae family.

The plant is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
