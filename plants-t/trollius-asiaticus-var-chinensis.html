Trollius asiaticus var. chinensis

Trollius asiaticus var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese globeflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features showy, yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically basal and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
