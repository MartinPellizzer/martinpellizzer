Trigonella elliptica f. villosa

Trigonella elliptica f. villosa, commonly known as the hairy lentil, is a variant of the common lentil species.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture, which is characteristic of the 'villosa' form. Its small, yellow flowers are typically arranged in racemes, and it produces lens-shaped pods containing edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential agricultural use.
