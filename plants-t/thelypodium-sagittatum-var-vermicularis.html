Thelypodium sagittatum var. vermicularis

Thelypodium sagittatum var. vermicularis, commonly known as the slender arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale green flowers during the summer months. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically valuable species in its native habitat.
