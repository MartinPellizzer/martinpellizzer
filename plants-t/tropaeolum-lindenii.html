Tropaeolum lindenii

Tropaeolum lindenii, commonly known as the golden crown flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family, which includes other species like the common garden nasturtium. The plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
