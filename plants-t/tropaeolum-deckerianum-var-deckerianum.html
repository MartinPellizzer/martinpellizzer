Tropaeolum deckerianum var. deckerianum

Tropaeolum deckerianum var. deckerianum, commonly known as the redleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from bright red to deep green. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its edible leaves and flowers, which have a peppery taste similar to watercress.
