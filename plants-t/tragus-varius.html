Tragus varius

Tragus varius, commonly known as the variable tragus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its small, erect stature and distinctive, often spiny leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a greenish-white color.

Tragus varius is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
