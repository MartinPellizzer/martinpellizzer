Taraxacum koraginense

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. This plant is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a yellow composite flower head that blooms in the summer. The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to high-altitude environments.
