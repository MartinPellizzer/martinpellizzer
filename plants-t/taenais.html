Taenais

Taenais, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple composite flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Taenais species are often found in Mediterranean climates and are valued for their ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh maritime environments.
