Tillandsia gilliesii subsp. polysticha

Tillandsia gilliesii subsp.

polysticha, commonly known as the silver pine tillandsia, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family. It is a terrestrial bromeliad characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like trichomes.

The plant is native to Mexico and is often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn.
