Trifolium multinerve var. debilior

Trifolium multinerve var. debilior, commonly known as the slender clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets with a distinct midrib. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the genus Trifolium. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded woodland areas.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
