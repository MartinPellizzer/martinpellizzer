Tachigali pimichinensis

Tachigali pimichinensis, commonly known as the Pimichinensis Tachigali, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly pubescent surface.
