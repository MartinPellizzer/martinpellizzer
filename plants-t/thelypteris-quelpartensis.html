Thelypteris quelpartensis

Thelypteris quelpartensis, commonly known as the Quelpart holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that remain throughout the year. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Thelypteris quelpartensis is valued for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
