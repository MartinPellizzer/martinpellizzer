Tephrosia paucifolia

Tephrosia paucifolia, commonly known as the slender tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Tephrosia paucifolia is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
