Ternstroemia sessiliflora

Ternstroemia sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered ternstroemia, is a member of the Theaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its simple, elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Ternstroemia sessiliflora is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
