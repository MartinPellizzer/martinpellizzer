Trichocentrum × oersteluridum

Trichocentrum × oersteluridum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Trichocentrum luteum and Trichocentrum oerstedi, resulting in a plant with unique floral and vegetative characteristics. The species is known for its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
