Triodia thomsonii

Triodia thomsonii, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a hardy perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry growth habit and spiny leaves, which help it withstand harsh environmental conditions. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, often forming extensive grasslands known as spinifex plains. Its deep root system allows it to access water from deeper soil layers, making it drought-tolerant.

Triodia thomsonii plays a crucial role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various desert fauna.
