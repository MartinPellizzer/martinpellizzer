Thelypteris aculeata

Thelypteris aculeata, commonly known as the holly fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often divided into narrow, serrated segments, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Thelypteris aculeata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
