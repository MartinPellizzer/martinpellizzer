Trachelospermum suaveolens

Trachelospermum suaveolens, commonly known as sweetheart vine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a vigorous, climbing vine that produces fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Trachelospermum suaveolens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
