Tricholaena microstachya

Tricholaena microstachya, commonly known as the African bristle fern, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and clustered, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. Native to tropical Africa, it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
