Thunbergia humilis

Thunbergia humilis, commonly known as the small thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia humilis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
