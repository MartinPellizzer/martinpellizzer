Triodia scariosa

Triodia scariosa, commonly known as spinifex grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its wiry, spiky leaves and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant forms dense tussocks and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Triodia scariosa is also valued for its resilience to drought and fire.
