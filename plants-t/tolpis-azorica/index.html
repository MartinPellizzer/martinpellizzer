Tolpis Azorica

Tolpis azorica is a rare and unique plant native to the Azores, known for its striking violet-blue flowers. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with rich, well-drained soil. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience, allowing it to survive in challenging microclimates. The plant's conservation status is of great concern due to habitat loss and climate change. Studying Tolpis azorica offers valuable insights into biodiversity and ecological adaptation in island ecosystems.
