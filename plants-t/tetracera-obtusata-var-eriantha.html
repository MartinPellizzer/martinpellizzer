Tetracera obtusata var. eriantha

Tetracera obtusata var. eriantha, commonly known as the woolly tetracera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in lowland forests.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
