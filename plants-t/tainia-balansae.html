Tainia balansae

Tainia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae tainia, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a rosette of broad, waxy, heart-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Tainia balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
