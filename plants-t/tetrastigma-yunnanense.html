Tetrastigma yunnanense

Tetrastigma yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan crossvine, is a climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green berries that are typically harvested for medicinal purposes. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
