Tragia cubensis

Tragia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban tragia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and its leaves are usually alternate and ovate. Tragia cubensis is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can often be found in dry, open habitats.
