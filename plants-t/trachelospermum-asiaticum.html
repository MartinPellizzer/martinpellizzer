Trachelospermum asiaticum

Trachelospermum asiaticum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It produces fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for trellises and as a ground cover in gardens.
