Trichodium hybridum

Trichodium hybridum, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces a milky sap that contains latex, which is often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Trichodium hybridum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
