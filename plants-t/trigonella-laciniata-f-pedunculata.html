Trigonella laciniata f. pedunculata

Trigonella laciniata f. pedunculata, commonly known as the pedunculate fenugreek, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions and is adapted to thrive in poor soils. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The f. pedunculata form is distinguished by its elongated peduncles, which support the inflorescences.
