Taxandria callistachys

Taxandria callistachys, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are usually red or orange in color. These flowers bloom during the winter months, making it a notable plant in its native habitat.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to Mediterranean climates.
