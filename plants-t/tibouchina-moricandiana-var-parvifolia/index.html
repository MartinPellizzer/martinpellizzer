Tibouchina Moricandiana Var. Parvifolia

Blue mistflower, scientifically known as Tibouchina moricandiana var. parvifolia, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South America. It is celebrated for its delicate, sky-blue flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a mist-like appearance. This evergreen shrub is prized in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade. Its unique foliage and long blooming season make it a favorite among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. With proper care, it can add a touch of elegance and color to any landscape.
