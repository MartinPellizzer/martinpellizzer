Thyella maynensis

Thyella maynensis, commonly known as the Maynensian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are usually elongated and slightly hairy, adapting to its tropical habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, Thyella maynensis is considered a rare and endangered species.
