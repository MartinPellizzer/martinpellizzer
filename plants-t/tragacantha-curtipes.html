Tragacantha curtipes

Tragacantha curtipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by distinctive pod-like fruits containing seeds.
