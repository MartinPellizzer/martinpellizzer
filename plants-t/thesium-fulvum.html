Thesium fulvum

Thesium fulvum, commonly known as the yellow thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Thesium fulvum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
