Thunbergia fuscata

Thunbergia fuscata, commonly known as the dark thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow. The leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while the stems are woody and twining. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
