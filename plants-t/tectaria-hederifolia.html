Tectaria hederifolia

Tectaria hederifolia, commonly known as the ivy fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its dense, creeping growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Tectaria hederifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture for its lush, green appearance.
