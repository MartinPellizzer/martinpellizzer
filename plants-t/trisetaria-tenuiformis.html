Trisetaria tenuiformis

Trisetaria tenuiformis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant is well adapted to drought conditions. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant's structure and growth patterns make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
