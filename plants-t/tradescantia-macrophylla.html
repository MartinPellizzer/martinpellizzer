Tradescantia macrophylla

Tradescantia macrophylla, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or green markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Tradescantia macrophylla is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to spread through rhizomes.
