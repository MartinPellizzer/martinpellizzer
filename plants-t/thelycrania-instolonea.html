Thelycrania instolonea

Thelycrania instolonea, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, usually with a striking purple or violet coloration.

Thelycrania instolonea is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.
