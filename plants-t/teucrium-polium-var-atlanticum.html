Teucrium polium var. atlanticum

Teucrium polium var. atlanticum, commonly known as Atlantic germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the coastal regions of the Atlantic coast of Europe.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or violet. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy coastal habitats. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
