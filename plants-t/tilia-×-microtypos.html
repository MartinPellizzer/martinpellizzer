Tilia × microtypos

Tilia × microtypos, commonly known as the small-leaf lime or narrow-leaf lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its oval, narrow leaves that are typically smaller than those of its parent species. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.

The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
