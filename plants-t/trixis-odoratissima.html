Trixis odoratissima

Trixis odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented trixis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. Trixis odoratissima is known for its strong, sweet fragrance, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in full bloom.
