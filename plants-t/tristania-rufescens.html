Tristania rufescens

Tristania rufescens, commonly known as the red-barked tristania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal and rocky areas. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its bark is distinctive, often appearing reddish or brownish, which gives it its common name.

Tristania rufescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
