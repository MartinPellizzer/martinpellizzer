Typhonium vietnamense

Typhonium vietnamense, commonly known as the Vietnamese arrowroot, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam. The plant features a stout rhizome and large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Typhonium vietnamense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
