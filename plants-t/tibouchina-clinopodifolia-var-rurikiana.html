Tibouchina clinopodifolia var. rurikiana

Tibouchina clinopodifolia var. rurikiana, commonly known as the Rurikiana Tibouchina, is a compact shrub native to Brazil.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a silvery-gray underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
