Thelypteris Morotaiensis

Thelypteris morotaiensis is a rare fern species native to the island of Morotai in Indonesia. It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is known for its delicate, lace-like fronds. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and endangered species.
