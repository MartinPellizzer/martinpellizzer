Trichomanes tamariscinum

Trichomanes tamariscinum, commonly known as the Tamarisk fern, is a rare and endangered fern species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky crevices or along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are clustered on the undersides of its leaves.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
