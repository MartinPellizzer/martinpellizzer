Tinospora fragosa subsp. kaokoensis

Tinospora fragosa subsp.

kaokoensis, commonly known as the Kaoko tinospora, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant produces yellowish berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
