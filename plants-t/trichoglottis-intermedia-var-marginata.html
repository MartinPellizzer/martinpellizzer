Trichoglottis intermedia var. marginata

Trichoglottis intermedia var. marginata, commonly known as the marginated trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
