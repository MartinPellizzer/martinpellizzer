Thymus serpyllum f. tokajensis

Thymus serpyllum f. tokajensis, commonly known as Tokaj thyme, is a subspecies of thyme native to Hungary.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its strong, spicy fragrance.

This variety is valued both for ornamental purposes and for its use in herbal medicine.
