Tassadia sprucei

Tassadia sprucei, commonly known as the dwarf spruce, is a small evergreen shrub native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, needle-like foliage that is typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Tassadia sprucei thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to the harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat.
