Thelymitra fuscolutea

Thelymitra fuscolutea, commonly known as the black-lipped sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

The plant produces a single, upright stem that bears a single, showy flower with a greenish-brown lip and yellow dorsal sepal.
