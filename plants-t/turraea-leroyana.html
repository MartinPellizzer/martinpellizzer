Turraea leroyana

Turraea leroyana, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fleshy flowers that resemble a ball, hence its common name. It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is known for its ornamental value.
