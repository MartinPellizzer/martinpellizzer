Trema guineense var. hochstetteri

Trema guineense var. hochstetteri, commonly known as the African nettle or African nettle tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with a smooth, grayish bark.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed samara, which aids in wind dispersal of its seeds.
