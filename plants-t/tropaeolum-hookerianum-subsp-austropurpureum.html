Tropaeolum hookerianum subsp. austropurpureum

Tropaeolum hookerianum subsp.

austropurpureum, commonly known as the purple-leafed nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to red.

The plant is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is known for its edible leaves and seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value and culinary uses have contributed to its widespread cultivation.
