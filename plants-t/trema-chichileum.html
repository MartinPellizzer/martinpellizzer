Trema chichileum

Trema chichileum, commonly known as the Mexican nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. It is native to Central and South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Trema chichileum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing along stream banks and in dense undergrowth.
