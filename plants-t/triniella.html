Triniella

Triniella, commonly known as the golden cup, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Triniella is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
