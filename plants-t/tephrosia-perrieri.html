Tephrosia perrieri

Tephrosia perrieri, commonly known as the African blue milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant has compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and savannas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Tephrosia perrieri is also noted for its potential medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
