Trichomanes pinnatifidum

Trichomanes pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce both roots and new shoots. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a serrated or finely toothed margin.

Trichomanes pinnatifidum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.
