Teliostachya gracilis

Teliostachya gracilis, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, and the seeds are dispersed by wind.

Teliostachya gracilis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
