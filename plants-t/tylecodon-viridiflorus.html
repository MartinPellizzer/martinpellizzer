Tylecodon viridiflorus

Tylecodon viridiflorus, commonly known as the green-flowered ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves and produces small, yellow-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Tylecodon viridiflorus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
