Torenia burttiana

Torenia burttiana, commonly known as the Burtt's torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Torenia burttiana thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
