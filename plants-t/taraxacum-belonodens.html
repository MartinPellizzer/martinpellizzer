Taraxacum belonodens

Taraxacum belonodens, commonly known as the Belonodens dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flower heads and heart-shaped basal leaves. Native to eastern Europe and parts of Russia, this plant thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant produces a distinctive white, fluffy seed head that aids in wind dispersal.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has mild medicinal properties.
