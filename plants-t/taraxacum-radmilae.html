Taraxacum radmilae

Taraxacum radmilae, commonly known as the Radmilae dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped leaves, typical of the dandelion genus. Native to specific regions in Europe, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces a fluffy seed head that disperses seeds via the wind, a common adaptation in the Asteraceae family.
