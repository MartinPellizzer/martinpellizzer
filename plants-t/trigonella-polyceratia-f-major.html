Trigonella polyceratia f. major

Trigonella polyceratia f. major, commonly known as the large-fruited fenugreek, is a variant of the fenugreek plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, hairy pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a branching stem that grows up to 30 cm in height.

This form is distinguished by its larger seed pods compared to the typical fenugreek species.
