Toona glabra

Toona glabra, commonly known as white toon, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its various properties.

The species is also valued for its hard, durable wood in carpentry and construction.
