Trigoniastrum hypoleucum var. viride

Trigoniastrum hypoleucum var. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered trigoniastrum, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are typically pubescent, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is native to regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
