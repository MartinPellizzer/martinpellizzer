Thelypteris griffithiana

Thelypteris griffithiana, commonly known as the Griffith's holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.

The fronds are bi-directionally divided, giving the plant a distinctive appearance.
