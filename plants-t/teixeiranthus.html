Teixeiranthus

Teixeiranthus, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Their flowers are usually brightly colored, with complex patterns that attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Teixeiranthus species are valued for their ornamental beauty and are often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
