Tabernaemontana mindanaensis

Tabernaemontana mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao star apple, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, round fruits that resemble apples in appearance.
