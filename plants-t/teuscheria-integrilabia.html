Teuscheria integrilabia

Teuscheria integrilabia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Teuscheria integrilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
