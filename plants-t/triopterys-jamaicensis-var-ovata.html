Triopterys jamaicensis var. ovata

Triopterys jamaicensis var. ovata, commonly known as the Jamaican broadleaf tree, is a species native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica and parts of Central America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters in height, characterized by its large, ovate-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.
