Thymelaea passerina var. pilifera

Thymelaea passerina var. pilifera, commonly known as the silky thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, glandular leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
