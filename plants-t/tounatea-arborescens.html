Tounatea arborescens

Tounatea arborescens, commonly known as the red-flowered tounatea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are clustered in inflorescences and attract various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Tounatea arborescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
