Thelypteris sichuanensis

Thelypteris sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually bi-lobed and have a serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental value.

Thelypteris sichuanensis is valued for its ecological role in maintaining soil stability and supporting local biodiversity.
