Trifolium Striatum Subvar. Spinescens

Trifolium striatum subvar. spinescens is a rare and striking plant known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. This subspecies of the common clover features distinctive spiny leaves that set it apart from other members of the Trifolium genus. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and often grows in shaded environments. Its flowers, which range from white to pale pink, add a delicate touch to its rugged foliage. Due to habitat loss and overcollection, this plant is now considered endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
