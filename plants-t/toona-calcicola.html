Toona calcicola

Toona calcicola, commonly known as the limestone toona, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Toona calcicola thrives in calcareous soils and is often found in forested areas with high humidity.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
