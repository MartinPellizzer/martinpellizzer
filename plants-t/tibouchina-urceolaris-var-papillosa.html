Tibouchina urceolaris var. papillosa

Tibouchina urceolaris var. papillosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf tibouchina, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple to pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a glossy surface and a soft, hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
