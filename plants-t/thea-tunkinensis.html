Thea tunkinensis

Thea tunkinensis, commonly known as Tunkin's tea plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Myanmar and Thailand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dark green, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits containing several seeds.

Its botanical characteristics make it distinct from other tea plants, with a unique combination of leaf morphology and floral structure.
