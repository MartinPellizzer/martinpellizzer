Tissa media var. berteroana

Tissa media var. berteroana, commonly known as the Bertero's tissa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
