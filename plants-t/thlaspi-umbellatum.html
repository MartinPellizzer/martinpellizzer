Thlaspi umbellatum

Thlaspi umbellatum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in umbrella-like clusters, known as umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Thlaspi umbellatum is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
