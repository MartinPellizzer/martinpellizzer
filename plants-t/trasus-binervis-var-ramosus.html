Trasus binervis var. ramosus

Trasus binervis var. ramosus, commonly known as the branched leaf fern, is a variant of the Trasus binervis species characterized by its distinctive branching pattern in the fronds.

This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically long and slender, with a feathery appearance, and they often exhibit a delicate, pale green coloration. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often cultivated as an ornamental species in tropical gardens.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
