Thelychiton × delicatus

Thelychiton × delicatus, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Thelychiton grandiflorus and Thelychiton macrophyllus.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
