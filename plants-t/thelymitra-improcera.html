Thelymitra improcera

Thelymitra improcera, commonly known as the slender sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understorey habitats. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that is usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
