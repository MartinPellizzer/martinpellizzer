Trema orientale

Trema orientale, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araliaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It has a moderate growth rate and can be grown both indoors and outdoors in suitable climates.

Trema orientale is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ease of care.
