Trifolium prophetarum

Trifolium prophetarum, commonly known as the prophet's clover, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in early spring. Trifolium prophetarum is known for its medicinal properties, with historical use in traditional herbal remedies.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several countries.
