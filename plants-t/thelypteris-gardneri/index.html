Thelypteris Gardneri

Thelypteris gardneri is a lesser-known fern species that thrives in moist, shaded environments. Native to the southeastern United States, it is often found in forest understories and along stream banks. This plant is characterized by its delicate fronds and unique reproductive structures. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and overlooked by many gardeners. Understanding and preserving Thelypteris gardneri is essential for maintaining biodiversity in its natural habitat.
