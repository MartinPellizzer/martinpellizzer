Thelypteris gimlii

Thelypteris gimlii, commonly known as the Gimli holly fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, dark green fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Thelypteris gimlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
