Tapeinochilos pungens var. teysmannianus

Tapeinochilos pungens var. teysmannianus, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
