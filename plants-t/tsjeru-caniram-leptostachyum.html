Tsjeru-caniram leptostachyum

Tsjeru-caniram leptostachyum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed bamboo, is a tropical plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with delicate, feathery flowers. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth surface and a grass-like texture.

It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories in Southeast Asia.
