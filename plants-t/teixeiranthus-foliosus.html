Teixeiranthus foliosus

Teixeiranthus foliosus, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the cloud forests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers are typically short-lived but attract various pollinators such as bees and moths.
