Tragacantha turbinata

Tragacantha turbinata, commonly known as the turban tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, and its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs. The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dark seeds.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
