Trachymene ovata

Trachymene ovata, commonly known as the oval false shamrock, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with purple petals and yellow stamens, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and unique floral structure.
