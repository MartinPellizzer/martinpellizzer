Tetraceratium stocksii

Tetraceratium stocksii, commonly known as the yellow starburst tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and glossy, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and arranged in opposite pairs along the stems.

Tetraceratium stocksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
