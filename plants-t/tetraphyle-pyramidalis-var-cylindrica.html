Tetraphyle pyramidalis var. cylindrica

Tetraphyle pyramidalis var. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical pyramid plant, is a unique variant of the Tetraphyle pyramidalis species.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, and cylindrical growth habit, forming a striking pyramidal shape. The plant features dense, upright leaves that are often a deep green color, providing a lush appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a striking specimen plant.

Its botanical interest lies in its distinct morphology and adaptability to specific environmental conditions.
