Thelypteris curta

Thelypteris curta, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and distinctive, leathery fronds with a shield-shaped base. The fronds are typically short and broad, with a dense arrangement of small, rounded pinnae. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
