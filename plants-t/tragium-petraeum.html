Tragium petraeum

Tragium petraeum, commonly known as the rock thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant has spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
