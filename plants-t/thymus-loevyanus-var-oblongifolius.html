Thymus loevyanus var. oblongifolius

Thymus loevyanus var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are oval in shape, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its strong, pungent aroma.
