Thereianthus intermedius

Thereianthus intermedius, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. Its ability to bloom year-round in suitable conditions adds to its ornamental value.
