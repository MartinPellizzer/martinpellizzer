Tasoba arifolia

Tasoba arifolia, commonly known as the leafy tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by fruit that contains edible seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
