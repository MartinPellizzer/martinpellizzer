Tulista × bicarinata

Tulista × bicarinata, commonly known as the hybrid tulip, is a cross between tulipa and liriodendron.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 feet. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Tulista × bicarinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
