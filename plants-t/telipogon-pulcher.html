Telipogon pulcher

Telipogon pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often considered a rare and specialized species in its native habitat.
