Thomsonaria granulosa

Thomsonaria granulosa, commonly known as the granulate thomsonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that are often covered in small, raised bumps. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Thomsonaria granulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
