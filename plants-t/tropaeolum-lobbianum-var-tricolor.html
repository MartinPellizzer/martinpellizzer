Tropaeolum lobbianum var. tricolor

Tropaeolum lobbianum var. tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored flowers that typically display red, yellow, and orange hues. The plant produces edible leaves and seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and edible gardens.
