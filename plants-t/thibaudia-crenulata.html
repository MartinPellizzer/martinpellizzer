Thibaudia crenulata

Thibaudia crenulata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, while the stems are erect and branching.

Thibaudia crenulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
