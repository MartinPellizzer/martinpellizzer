Trifolium × neyrautii

Trifolium × neyrautii, commonly known as the Neurautti clover, is a hybrid species within the genus Trifolium, which includes various clover plants.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, typically with three oval-shaped leaflets, and produces small, pea-like flowers that can range in color from white to pink. This plant is often found in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a cover crop.

Trifolium × neyrautii is also notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
