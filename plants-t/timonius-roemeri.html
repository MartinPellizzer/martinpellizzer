Timonius roemeri

Timonius roemeri, commonly known as the mountain hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.
