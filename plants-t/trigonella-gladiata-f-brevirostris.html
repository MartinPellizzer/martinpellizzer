Trigonella gladiata f. brevirostris

Trigonella gladiata f. brevirostris, commonly known as the narrow-beaked fenugreek, is a horticultural form of the fenugreek plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, beak-like seed pods that are shorter than the standard variety. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of seeds used in culinary and medicinal applications.
