Tium hians

Tium hians, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant red flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Tium hians is a perennial herb that blooms in the spring and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
