Tulipa harmonia

Tulipa harmonia, commonly known as the harmonious tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its elegant, single-layered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. It typically blooms in early spring and is well-suited for rock gardens or borders.

Tulipa harmonia is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
