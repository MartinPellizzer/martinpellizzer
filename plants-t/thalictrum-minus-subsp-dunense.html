Thalictrum minus subsp. dunense

Thalictrum minus subsp.

dunense, commonly known as the lesser meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, usually yellowish-green flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces a tall, slender stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height. Thalictrum minus subsp.

dunense is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and early spring blooms.
