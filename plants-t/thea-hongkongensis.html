Thea hongkongensis

Thea hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong tea, is a flowering plant native to southern China and Hong Kong.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, campanulate flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, bloom in clusters and are followed by small, capsule-like fruits.

The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.
