Teloxys multifida

Teloxys multifida, commonly known as the fragrant leaf plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, heart-shaped leaves that emit a pleasant scent when crushed. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Teloxys multifida thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
