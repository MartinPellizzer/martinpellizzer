Triteleia berteroi

Triteleia berteroi, commonly known as the Santa Barbara lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to coastal regions of California.

It produces striking blue to violet flowers with a white throat, typically blooming in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral.

Triteleia berteroi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
