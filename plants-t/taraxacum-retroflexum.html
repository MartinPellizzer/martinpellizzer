Taraxacum retroflexum

Taraxacum retroflexum, commonly known as the retroflex dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. Its flowers bloom in the spring and early summer, and the plant is perennial, often forming dense colonies.

Taraxacum retroflexum is adapted to a variety of soil types and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
