Terminalia seyrigii var. divaricatopsis

Terminalia seyrigii var. divaricatopsis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, simple leaves. Due to its ornamental value and timber potential, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and for local use.
