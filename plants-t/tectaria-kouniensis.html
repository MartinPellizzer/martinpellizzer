Tectaria kouniensis

Tectaria kouniensis, commonly known as the Kounien's shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a complex venation pattern.

Tectaria kouniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
