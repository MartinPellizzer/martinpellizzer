Thymus eriocalyx

Thymus eriocalyx, commonly known as narrow-leaved thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces pink to violet flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its medicinal properties have been historically valued for their antiseptic and digestive benefits.
