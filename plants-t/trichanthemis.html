Trichanthemis

Trichanthemis, commonly known as the common trichanthemum, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

These plants are typically annual or perennial herbs with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are small and clustered in compact inflorescences, usually with yellow to brownish petals. Trichanthemis species are native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and Australia.

They are often found in dry, disturbed habitats and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
