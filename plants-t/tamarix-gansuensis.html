Tamarix gansuensis

Tamarix gansuensis, commonly known as the Gansu tamarisk, is a shrub native to China, particularly found in arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and clusters of pink or reddish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has a deep root system that helps it survive in dry environments, making it well adapted to harsh conditions. It is often used for soil stabilization and erosion control due to its hardy nature.

Tamarix gansuensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
