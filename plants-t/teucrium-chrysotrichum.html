Teucrium chrysotrichum

Teucrium chrysotrichum, commonly known as golden hair grass or golden thread, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, golden hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in loose spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dryland landscapes.
