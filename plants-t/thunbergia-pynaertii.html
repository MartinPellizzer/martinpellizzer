Thunbergia pynaertii

Thunbergia pynaertii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Thunbergia pynaertii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
