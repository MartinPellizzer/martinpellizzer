Tabernaemontana praeclara

Tabernaemontana praeclara, commonly known as the White Star Magnolia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is a member of the Magnoliaceae family and is known for its showy, star-shaped petals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
