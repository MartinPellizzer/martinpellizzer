Tabebuia lindahlii

Tabebuia lindahlii, commonly known as the white ceiba, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and striking white flowers that bloom in the dry season. Tabebuia lindahlii is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental beauty in landscaping.
