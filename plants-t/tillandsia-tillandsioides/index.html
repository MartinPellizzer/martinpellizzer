Tillandsia Tillandsioides

Tillandsia tillandsioides is a unique air plant known for its ability to thrive without soil. This epiphytic plant absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves rather than roots. It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas and is often found growing on trees or rocks. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative displays. With proper care, it can flourish in a variety of environments.
