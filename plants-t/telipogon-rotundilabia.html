Telipogon rotundilabia

Telipogon rotundilabia, commonly known as the round-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers with a distinctive rounded lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in cloud forests or along riverbanks. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a unique labellum that resembles a small lip or pouch.

Telipogon rotundilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its beauty and ecological significance.
