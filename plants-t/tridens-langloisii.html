Tridens langloisii

Tridens langloisii, commonly known as the Langlois' trident, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, three-parted seed heads.

Tridens langloisii is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
