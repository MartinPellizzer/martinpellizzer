Thalictrum thunbergii var. hypoleucum

Thalictrum thunbergii var. hypoleucum, commonly known as the white meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically green with a slight silvery sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fluffy flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in woodland gardens.
