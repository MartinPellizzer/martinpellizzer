Tessenia procera

Tessenia procera, commonly known as the tall tessenia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Tessenia procera produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
