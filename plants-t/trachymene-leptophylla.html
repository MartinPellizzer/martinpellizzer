Trachymene leptophylla

Trachymene leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed false yarrow, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Trachymene leptophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
