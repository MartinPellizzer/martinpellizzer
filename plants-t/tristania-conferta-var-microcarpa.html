Tristania conferta var. microcarpa

Tristania conferta var. microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf tristania, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
