Tragacantha hohenackeri

Tragacantha hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's tragacanth, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly in Namibia and Angola. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves, which are adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a distinctive yellow or orange hue.

The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.
