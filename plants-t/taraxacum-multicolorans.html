Taraxacum multicolorans

Taraxacum multicolorans, commonly known as the multicolored dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multi-colored flower heads that range from yellow to red, orange, and even purple. The plant typically grows in moist, open areas and is native to parts of Asia. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is usually upright and hairy.

The flowers are followed by distinctive white, fluffy seeds that are easily dispersed by the wind.
