Tsoongia

Tsoongia, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and includes several species that are often cultivated for their ornamental value. The plants typically feature showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which bloom in summer. Tsoongia species are perennial herbs with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.

They prefer well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making them suitable for gardens and landscapes.
