Triodia veniciae

Triodia veniciae, commonly known as the Venus's Thorn, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Triodia veniciae is adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
