Thunbergia gigantea

Thunbergia gigantea, commonly known as the giant spider flower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and trellis coverage.
