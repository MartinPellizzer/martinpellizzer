Triticum spelta var. duhamelianum

Triticum spelta var. duhamelianum, commonly known as duhamelian wheat, is a variety of spelt wheat characterized by its unique botanical features.

It is a hexaploid grass species belonging to the tribe Aveneae within the grass family Poaceae. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 1.5 meters, with slender stems and alternate, linear leaves. Its inflorescence is a dense spike with spikelets containing two to three florets, and the grains are small and oval-shaped.

This variety is notable for its hardiness and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
