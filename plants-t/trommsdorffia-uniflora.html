Trommsdorffia uniflora

Trommsdorffia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower grass, is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance.

It is native to South Africa and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces a single, erect flowering stem with small, pale blue to purple flowers. Trommsdorffia uniflora is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
