Teucrium sessilifolium

Teucrium sessilifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are narrow, sessile, and have a slightly aromatic scent. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color and bloom in summer.

This plant is often found in rocky or dry soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
