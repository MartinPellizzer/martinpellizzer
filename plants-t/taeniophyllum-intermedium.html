Taeniophyllum intermedium

Taeniophyllum intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate thread-leaf.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of South America. The plant has slender, thread-like leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around its stem. It produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the wet season.

Taeniophyllum intermedium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity for optimal growth.
