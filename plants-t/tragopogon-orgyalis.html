Tragopogon orgyalis

Tragopogon orgyalis, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a distinctive, bristly seed head that resembles a goat's beard. Native to Europe, it has been introduced to various parts of the world, including North America. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows.

It produces a milky sap that contains latex, which can cause skin irritation in some individuals.
