Thesium simplex

Thesium simplex, commonly known as the slender thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense spike, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Thesium simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
