Tragacantha sigmoidea

Tragacantha sigmoidea, commonly known as the sickle tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive sickle-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by pods containing edible seeds. Its roots are used in traditional medicine and as a source of a gel-like substance.

Tragacantha sigmoidea is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
