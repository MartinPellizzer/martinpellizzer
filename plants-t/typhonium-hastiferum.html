Typhonium hastiferum

Typhonium hastiferum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Typhonium hastiferum is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
