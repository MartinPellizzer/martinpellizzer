Trichosalpinx microlepanthes

Trichosalpinx microlepanthes, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or small-leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Trichosalpinx microlepanthes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
