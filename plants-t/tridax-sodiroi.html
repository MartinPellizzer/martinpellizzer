Tridax sodiroi

Tridax sodiroi, commonly known as the slenderleaf tridax, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Tridax sodiroi is sometimes considered a weed but is also valued for its ornamental potential.
