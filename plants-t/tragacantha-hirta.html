Tragacantha hirta

Tragacantha hirta, commonly known as the hairy tragacanth, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding visual interest to its appearance. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Astraceae family.
