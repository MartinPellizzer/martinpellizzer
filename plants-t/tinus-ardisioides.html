Tinus ardisioides

Tinus ardisioides, commonly known as the red-flowered oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Tinus ardisioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
