Trichostema pilosum

Trichostema pilosum, commonly known as the hairy bluecurls, is a flowering plant native to California and parts of Oregon.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
