Tibouchina claussenii var. grandiflora

Tibouchina claussenii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered tibouchina, is a striking shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow center. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Tibouchina claussenii var. grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
