Thunbergia parviflora

Thunbergia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Thunbergia parviflora blooms throughout the growing season, adding a vibrant touch of color to its surroundings.
