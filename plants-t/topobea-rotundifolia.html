Topobea rotundifolia

Topobea rotundifolia, commonly known as the roundleaf topobea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, round leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
