Tetrachyron manicatum

Tetrachyron manicatum, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, star-shaped yellow flowers with a distinctive orange center, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and resistance to common garden pests.
