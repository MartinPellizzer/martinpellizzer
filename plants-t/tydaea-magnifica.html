Tydaea magnifica

Tydaea magnifica, commonly known as the splendid grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, slender blades that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with a fountain-like growth habit. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing structures that add a dramatic visual element in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a background plant in landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
