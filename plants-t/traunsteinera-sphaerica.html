Traunsteinera sphaerica

Traunsteinera sphaerica, commonly known as the spherical traunsteinera, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small, shrub-like plant characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit. The plant features small, clustered yellow flowers that are often surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to thrive in arid environments.
