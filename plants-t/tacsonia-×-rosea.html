Tacsonia × rosea

Tacsonia × rosea, commonly known as the hybrid rose.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its showy, fragrant blooms. The plant typically features semi-evergreen foliage and produces clusters of pink or red petals. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

Due to its hybrid nature, Tacsonia × rosea exhibits a wide range of color and form variations.
