Terminalis dracaenoides

Terminalis dracaenoides, commonly known as the Corn Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features tall, slender stems with long, dark green, strap-like leaves that give it a elegant appearance. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering. Its leaves are often variegated with yellow or white markings, adding visual interest.

Terminalis dracaenoides is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
