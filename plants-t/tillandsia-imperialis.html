Tillandsia imperialis

Tillandsia imperialis, commonly known as the imperial air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that can store water, allowing it to thrive in humid environments. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, and it produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
