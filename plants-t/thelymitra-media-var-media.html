Thelymitra media var. media

Thelymitra media var. media, commonly known as the common white-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome and produces a single, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are white with green veins and are arranged in a loose inflorescence.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
