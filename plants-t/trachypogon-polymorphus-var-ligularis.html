Trachypogon polymorphus var. ligularis

Trachypogon polymorphus var. ligularis, commonly known as the variable feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture that aids in water retention.

This variant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to different environmental conditions.
