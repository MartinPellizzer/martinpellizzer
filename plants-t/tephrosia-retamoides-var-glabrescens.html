Tephrosia retamoides var. glabrescens

Tephrosia retamoides var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, branching stems covered in smooth, glabrous bark. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the dry season. Its leaves are alternate, trifoliate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

This species is often found in savannas and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
