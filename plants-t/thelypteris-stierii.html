Thelypteris stierii

Thelypteris stierii, commonly known as the Stier's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant is native to the southeastern United States, particularly in regions with moist, shaded habitats. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, brownish layer of rhizome scales.

Thelypteris stierii is valued in horticulture for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
