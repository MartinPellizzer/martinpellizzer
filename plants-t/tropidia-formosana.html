Tropidia formosana

Tropidia formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in various colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Tropidia formosana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
