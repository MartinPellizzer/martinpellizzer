Tylostemon ledermannii

Tylostemon ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's tylostemon, is a small evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It features dense, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color, providing a lush appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
