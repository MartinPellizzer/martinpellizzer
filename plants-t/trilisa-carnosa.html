Trilisa carnosa

Trilisa carnosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Trilisa carnosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in moist environments.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds make it an invasive species in some areas.
