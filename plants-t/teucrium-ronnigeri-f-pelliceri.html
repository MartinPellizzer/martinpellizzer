Teucrium ronnigeri f. pelliceri

Teucrium ronnigeri f. pelliceri, commonly known as the Pellicer's germander, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and small, opposite leaves that are often grayish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in hue, which attract various pollinators. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

This ornamental plant is valued for its compact form and long blooming period, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
