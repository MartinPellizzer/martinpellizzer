Tamarix sokotrana

Tamarix sokotrana, commonly known as the Socotra tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its unique, needle-like leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant belongs to the Tamarisk family (Tamaricaceae) and is adapted to arid environments. It has a woody stem with a distinctive, branched structure.

Tamarix sokotrana is known for its resilience to drought and salt spray, making it well-suited to coastal and sandy habitats.
