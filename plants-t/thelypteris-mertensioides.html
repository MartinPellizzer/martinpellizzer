Thelypteris mertensioides

Thelypteris mertensioides, commonly known as the Mertens' shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright fronds that are typically divided into lance-shaped segments. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its fertile fronds, which are clustered in sori covered by indusia. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its intricate morphology and ecological adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
