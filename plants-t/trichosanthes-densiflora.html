Trichosanthes densiflora

Trichosanthes densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered snake gourd, is a vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are often followed by elongated, green fruits. It is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins.

Trichosanthes densiflora is valued both for its ornamental appeal and for its edible fruits.
