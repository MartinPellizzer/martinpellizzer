Tsuga chinensis var. oblongisquamata

Tsuga chinensis var. oblongisquamata, commonly known as the Chinese fir var. oblongisquamata, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern China.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its needle-like leaves that are arranged spirally along the branches. The tree produces cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its hardiness and ornamental qualities.
