Thalictrum siamense var. siamense

Thalictrum siamense var. siamense, commonly known as the Siam meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically basal and clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces showy, greenish-white flowers arranged in upright clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
