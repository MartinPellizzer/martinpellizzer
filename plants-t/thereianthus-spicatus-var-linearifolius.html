Thereianthus spicatus var. linearifolius

Thereianthus spicatus var. linearifolius, commonly known as the slender-leafed thereianthus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaf structures. The plant features linear, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its inflorescence consists of small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow or orange.

This variant is noted for its adaptability to arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
