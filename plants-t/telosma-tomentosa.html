Telosma tomentosa

Telosma tomentosa, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually purple or violet, with five petals and a spur-like structure at the base.

The fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
