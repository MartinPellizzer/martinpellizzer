Tanarius stipulosus

Tanarius stipulosus, commonly known as the slender stipule plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink or white. Tanarius stipulosus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
