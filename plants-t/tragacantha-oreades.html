Tragacantha oreades

Tragacantha oreades, commonly known as the mountain tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in rocky, arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
