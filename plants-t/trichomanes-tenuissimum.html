Trichomanes tenuissimum

Trichomanes tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, linear leaves that are often slightly curled. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest undergrowth or on rock faces. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often considered a conservation concern in many areas.
