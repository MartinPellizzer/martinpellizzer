Tamarix laxa

Tamarix laxa, commonly known as the weeping tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, scale-like leaves and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It is adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a hardy species in dry climates.

Tamarix laxa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
