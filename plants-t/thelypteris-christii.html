Thelypteris christii

Thelypteris christii, commonly known as the Christ's holly fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fronds are biennial, with the sterile fronds appearing in the first year and the fertile fronds in the second.
