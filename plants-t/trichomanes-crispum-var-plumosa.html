Trichomanes crispum var. plumosa

Trichomanes crispum var. plumosa, commonly known as the plumose maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a soft, lacy appearance. The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in humid conditions.
