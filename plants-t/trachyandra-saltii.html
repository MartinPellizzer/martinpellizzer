Trachyandra saltii

Trachyandra saltii, commonly known as the saltbush, is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss, making it well adapted to dry environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or saline areas.

Trachyandra saltii is valued for its resilience and is used in landscaping and erosion control in suitable climates.
