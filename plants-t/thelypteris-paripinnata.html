Thelypteris paripinnata

Thelypteris paripinnata, commonly known as the broad-leafed wood fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its bipinnate fronds that are often broad and leathery. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of foliage.
