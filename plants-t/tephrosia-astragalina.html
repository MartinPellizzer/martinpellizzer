Tephrosia astragalina

Tephrosia astragalina, commonly known as the narrowleaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine for their purported antifungal and antibacterial properties.
