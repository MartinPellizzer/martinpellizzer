Tofieldia fusca var. kondoi

Tofieldia fusca var. kondoi, commonly known as the Kondoi sedge, is a perennial herb native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
