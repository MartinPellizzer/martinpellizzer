Trigonella crassipes

Trigonella crassipes, commonly known as broad-leaved fenugreek, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds.

Trigonella crassipes is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
