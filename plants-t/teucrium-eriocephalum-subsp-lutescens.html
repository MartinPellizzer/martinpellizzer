Teucrium eriocephalum subsp. lutescens

Teucrium eriocephalum subsp.

lutescens, commonly known as golden teucrium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant features aromatic leaves with a distinct mint-like scent and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
