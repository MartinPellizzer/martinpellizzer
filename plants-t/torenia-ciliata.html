Torenia ciliata

Torenia ciliata, commonly known as the "blushing bride" or "torenia," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with ciliate margins, giving it its scientific name. Torenia ciliata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
