Tectaria × chaconiana

Tectaria × chaconiana, commonly known as the hybrid tree fern, is a natural hybrid between Tectaria cryptogramma and Tectaria caudata.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Tectaria × chaconiana is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper moisture and light conditions.
