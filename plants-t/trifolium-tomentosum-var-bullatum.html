Trifolium tomentosum var. bullatum

Trifolium tomentosum var. bullatum, commonly known as the woolly clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique texture and low maintenance requirements.
