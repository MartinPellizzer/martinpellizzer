Tryphostemma cuneatum

Tryphostemma cuneatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid environments.
