Timonius minutifolius

Timonius minutifolius, commonly known as the small-leaf timonius, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the humid understories of tropical rainforests.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often hidden among the foliage.
