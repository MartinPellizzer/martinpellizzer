Tunica stricta

Tunica stricta, commonly known as the rigid tunica, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. Tunica stricta is often found in rocky soils and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
