Taeniophyllum paife

Taeniophyllum paife, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the fairy orchid, is a small, epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, compact growth habit. The plant produces tiny, colorful flowers that are often clustered in a single inflorescence, making it a striking addition to any collection. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
