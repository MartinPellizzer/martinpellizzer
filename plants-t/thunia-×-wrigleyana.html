Thunia × wrigleyana

Thunia × wrigleyana, commonly known as the Wrigley's spider plant, is a hybrid evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems that bear small, glossy green leaves with a striking white midrib. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage and ability to propagate easily through offsets and seed.
