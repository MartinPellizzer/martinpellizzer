Tetracarpum pinnatum

Tetracarpum pinnatum, commonly known as the featherleaf tetracarpum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Tetracarpum pinnatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
