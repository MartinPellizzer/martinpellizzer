Trifolium microdon

Trifolium microdon, commonly known as the small clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three small oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Trifolium microdon is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important plant in ecological and agricultural contexts.
