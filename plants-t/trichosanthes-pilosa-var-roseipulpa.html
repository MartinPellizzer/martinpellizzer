Trichosanthes pilosa var. roseipulpa

Trichosanthes pilosa var. roseipulpa, commonly known as the rose-flowered snake gourd, is a variant of the snake gourd belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive rose-colored flowers and hairy, ridged stems. The plant produces elongated, warty fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

This variant is cultivated for its ornamental value and, in some regions, for its edible fruits.
