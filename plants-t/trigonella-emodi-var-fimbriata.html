Trigonella emodi var. fimbriata

Trigonella emodi var. fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed fenugreek, is a variant of the fenugreek plant native to the Himalayan regions.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces hairy, elongated pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
