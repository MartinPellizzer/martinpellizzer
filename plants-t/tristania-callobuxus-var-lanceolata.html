Tristania callobuxus var. lanceolata

Tristania callobuxus var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Tristania lanceolata, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often glabrous and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
