Torreya nucifera var. radicans

Torreya nucifera var. radicans, commonly known as the spreading Chinese torreya, is a variant of the Chinese torreya, characterized by its spreading growth habit.

It is a deciduous evergreen tree native to southern China, particularly in the provinces of Guangdong and Guangxi. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. Its fruit is a fleshy, red drupe that contains a single seed, which is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
