Triantha pubens

Triantha pubens, commonly known as the hairy triantha, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, commonly referred to as the buckwheat family. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in soft, hairy texture, which gives it its common name. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-white and bloom in late summer.

Triantha pubens is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
