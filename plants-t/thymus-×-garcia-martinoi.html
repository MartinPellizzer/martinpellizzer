Thymus × garcia-martinoi

Thymus × garcia-martinoi, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, aromatic foliage with small, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant leaves and long flowering period.
