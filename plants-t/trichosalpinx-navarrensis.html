Trichosalpinx navarrensis

Trichosalpinx navarrensis, commonly known as the Navarre spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant produces striking, violet-colored flowers with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
