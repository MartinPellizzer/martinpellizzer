Trachelospermum inflatum

Trachelospermum inflatum, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its beauty and aroma.

Its climbing habit makes it suitable for trellises, fences, and other structures.
