Talisia rosea

Talisia rosea, commonly known as the pink talisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a pinkish hue, and are borne on slender pedicels.

Talisia rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
