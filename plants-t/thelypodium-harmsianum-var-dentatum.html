Thelypodium harmsianum var. dentatum

Thelypodium harmsianum var. dentatum, commonly known as the toothed thelypodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that have distinct toothed margins. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, giving it a fluffy appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
