Trichochiton tuberosum

Trichochiton tuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous trichochiton, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Trichochiton tuberosum is primarily valued for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its root system.
