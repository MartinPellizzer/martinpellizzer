Tephrocactus soehrensii

Tephrocactus soehrensii, commonly known as the Soehrens' cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with smooth, ribbed stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Tephrocactus soehrensii is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.
