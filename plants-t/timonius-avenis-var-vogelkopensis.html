Timonius avenis var. vogelkopensis

Timonius avenis var. vogelkopensis, commonly known as the Vogelkop pitta, is a unique plant species native to the forests of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its striking, iridescent plumage, which displays a combination of green, blue, and violet hues. The male bird is particularly notable for its vivid coloration, while the female has a more subdued appearance. This species is known for its melodic and complex vocalizations, which play a crucial role in territorial defense and mate attraction.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native range.
