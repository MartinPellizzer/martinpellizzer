Trichoscypha altescandens

Trichoscypha altescandens, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has smooth, greyish bark that becomes fissured with age, and its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, forested areas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
