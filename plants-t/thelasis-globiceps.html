Thelasis globiceps

Thelasis globiceps, commonly known as the globular thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its spherical, spiny head of flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a compact, shrub-like appearance. Its leaves are generally covered in fine hairs and may have a bluish or grayish hue.

The flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom in late summer to early autumn.
