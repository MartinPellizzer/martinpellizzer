Teedia pentheri

Teedia pentheri, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are borne on slender stems.

Teedia pentheri is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.
