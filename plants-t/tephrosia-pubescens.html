Tephrosia pubescens

Tephrosia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its roots are known for containing compounds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
