Tillandsia truxillana

Tillandsia truxillana, commonly known as the Truxillan air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the Andean region of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette structure, which helps it collect and retain moisture. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, yellow, and red, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
