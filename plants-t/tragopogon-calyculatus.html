Tragopogon calyculatus

Tragopogon calyculatus, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and bristly leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces a distinctive seed head that resembles a small, fluffy crown, giving it its common name.

Tragopogon calyculatus is also known for its ability to hybridize with other species in the genus, leading to the creation of new varieties.
