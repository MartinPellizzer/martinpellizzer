Tabernaemontana callosa

Tabernaemontana callosa, commonly known as the rough tabernanthe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves and can grow as either a shrub or a small tree. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Tabernaemontana callosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
