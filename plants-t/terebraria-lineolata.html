Terebraria lineolata

Terebraria lineolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf terebraria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance.
