Tithymalus atropurpureus

Tithymalus atropurpureus, commonly known as purple tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

Tithymalus atropurpureus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
