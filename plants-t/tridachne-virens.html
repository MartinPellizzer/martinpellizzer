Tridachne virens

Tridachne virens, commonly known as the green bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant features dense, spike-like clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble bottle brushes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it often grows in moist, shaded environments.

Tridachne virens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
