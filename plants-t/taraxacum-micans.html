Taraxacum micans

Taraxacum micans, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-tomentose leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces a single, bright yellow flower head that blooms in the spring. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Taraxacum micans is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
