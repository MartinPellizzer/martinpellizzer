Telis griffithii

Telis griffithii, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces showy, pink to red flowers in the spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in high-altitude regions with cool climates. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

The botanical features of Telis griffithii make it a unique addition to alpine and rock gardens.
