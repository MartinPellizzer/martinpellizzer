Tricalysia milanjiensis

Tricalysia milanjiensis, commonly known as the Milanjiensis tamarind, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant has bipinnate leaves, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in racemes. The seeds are enclosed in woody pods, and the tree is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

It is valued for its timber and traditional uses in local communities.
