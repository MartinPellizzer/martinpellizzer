Trimezia setacea

Trimezia setacea, commonly known as the velvetleaf trimezia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Trimezia setacea is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
