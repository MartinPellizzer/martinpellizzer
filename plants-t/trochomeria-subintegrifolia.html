Trochomeria subintegrifolia

Trochomeria subintegrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed trochomeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth along the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely known outside its native range.
