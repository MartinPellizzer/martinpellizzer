Thalictrum majus subsp. pratense

Thalictrum majus subsp.

pratense, commonly known as the meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.

Its botanical structure includes a slender stem, alternate leaves, and a unique floral arrangement that distinguishes it from other members of the Ranunculaceae family.
