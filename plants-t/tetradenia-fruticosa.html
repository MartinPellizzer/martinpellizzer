Tetradenia fruticosa

Tetradenia fruticosa, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is known for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making.

Tetradenia fruticosa is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
