Trichosalpinx flexibilis

Trichosalpinx flexibilis, commonly known as the flexible spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, narrow spur. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Trichosalpinx flexibilis is known for its ephemeral blooming period, which occurs during the wet season.
