Telipogon hemimelas

Telipogon hemimelas, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and swampy areas.

Telipogon hemimelas plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
