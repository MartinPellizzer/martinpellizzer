Tetrahit reichenbachii

Tetrahit reichenbachii, commonly known as the Reichenbach's tetrahit, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal areas.

Tetrahit reichenbachii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
