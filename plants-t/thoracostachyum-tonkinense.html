Thoracostachyum tonkinense

Thoracostachyum tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin bamboo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
