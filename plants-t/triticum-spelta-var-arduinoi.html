Triticum spelta var. arduinoi

Triticum spelta var. arduinoi, commonly known as Arduino wheat, is a lesser-known variant of spelt wheat.

It is characterized by its unique genetic makeup and distinct morphological features compared to standard spelt varieties. The plant typically exhibits robust growth with tall, slender stalks and awned spikelets. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a complex inflorescence structure.

This variety is of interest for its potential in sustainable agriculture and unique nutritional profile.
