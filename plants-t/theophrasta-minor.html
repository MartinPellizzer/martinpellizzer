Theophrasta minor

Theophrasta minor, commonly known as the lesser theophrastus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Theophrasta minor is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments.
