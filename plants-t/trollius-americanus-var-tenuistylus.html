Trollius americanus var. tenuistylus

Trollius americanus var. tenuistylus, commonly known as narrow-styled American globeflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often covered in fine hairs.

The narrow-styled variety is distinguished by its elongated, slender styles compared to other Trollius species.
