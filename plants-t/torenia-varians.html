Torenia varians

Torenia varians, commonly known as the common torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, showy flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are borne on erect stems and are often clustered in the axils of the leaves.

Torenia varians is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
