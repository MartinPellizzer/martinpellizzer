Thelasis micrantha

Thelasis micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered thelasis, is a member of the Rhamnaceae family.

It is a shrub native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are clustered in axillary inflorescences, with small, yellowish-white petals.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.
