Touchardia candidissima

Touchardia candidissima, commonly known as the white-flowered spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Touchardia candidissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
