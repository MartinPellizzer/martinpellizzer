Tuberaria variabilis var. desiderii

Tuberaria variabilis var. desiderii, commonly known as the Desideri's rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces yellow, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in terminal clusters, and its leaves are usually narrow and evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to withstand drought conditions.
