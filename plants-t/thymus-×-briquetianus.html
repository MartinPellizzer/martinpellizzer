Thymus × briquetianus

Thymus × briquetianus, commonly known as the Briquet thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated for both its fragrance and visual appeal.
