Tabernaemontana echites

Tabernaemontana echites, commonly known as the flame tree or the evergreen flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dramatic and fiery appearance. The plant has large, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its showy flowers.

The tree is also valued for its aromatic wood, which has been used in traditional medicine and craftsmanship.
