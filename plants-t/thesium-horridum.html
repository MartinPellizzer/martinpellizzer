Thesium horridum

Thesium horridum, commonly known as the rough thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, wiry stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture.
