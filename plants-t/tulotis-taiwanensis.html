Tulotis taiwanensis

Tulotis taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan tulotis, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, clustered blooms during the spring season.

Tulotis taiwanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
