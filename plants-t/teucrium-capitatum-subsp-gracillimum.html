Teucrium capitatum subsp. gracillimum

Teucrium capitatum subsp.

gracillimum, commonly known as slender tarragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
