Tabernaemontana chippii

Tabernaemontana chippii, commonly known as the pink star jasmine.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has evergreen foliage with glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Tabernaemontana chippii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant fragrance.
