Triodia dielsii

Triodia dielsii, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry growth habit and the presence of sharp, spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils. It plays a crucial role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion in its native habitat.

Triodia dielsii is well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient component of the Australian flora.
