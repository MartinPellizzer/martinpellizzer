Thlaspi camelina

Thlaspi camelina, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is often found in disturbed soils and roadside areas. The plant has basal leaves that are lobed or divided, and its stems are usually hairy. It produces small, pod-like fruits called siliques that contain several seeds.
