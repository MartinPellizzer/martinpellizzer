Trichilia cuneata

Trichilia cuneata, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood. The tree produces small, white flowers that are followed by distinctive, three-lobed fruit capsules. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, often with a slightly hairy surface.
