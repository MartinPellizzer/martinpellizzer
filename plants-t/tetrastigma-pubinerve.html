Tetrastigma pubinerve

Tetrastigma pubinerve, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed cross-vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pubescent (hairy) stems and tendrils. The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value or used in traditional medicine.
