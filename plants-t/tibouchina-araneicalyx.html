Tibouchina araneicalyx

Tibouchina araneicalyx, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular, magenta-pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Tibouchina araneicalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
