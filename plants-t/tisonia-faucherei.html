Tisonia faucherei

Tisonia faucherei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Tisonia faucherei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
