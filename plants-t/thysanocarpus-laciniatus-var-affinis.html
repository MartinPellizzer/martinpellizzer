Thysanocarpus laciniatus var. affinis

Thysanocarpus laciniatus var. affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thysanocarpus, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with finely divided, serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

This species is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
