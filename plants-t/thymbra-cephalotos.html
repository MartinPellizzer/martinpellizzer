Thymbra cephalotos

Thymbra cephalotos, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thyme.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, appearing in clusters.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb due to its pleasant fragrance and medicinal properties.
