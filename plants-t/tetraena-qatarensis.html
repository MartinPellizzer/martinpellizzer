Tetraena qatarensis

Tetraena qatarensis, commonly known as the Qatari thornbush, is a shrub native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its small, spiny leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it highly drought-resistant. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the regional flora.
