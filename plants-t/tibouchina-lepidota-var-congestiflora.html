Tibouchina lepidota var. congestiflora

Tibouchina lepidota var. congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered tibouchina, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in whorls along the stems. The plant produces dense clusters of showy, coral-pink to lavender flowers during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for container gardening and landscape design.
