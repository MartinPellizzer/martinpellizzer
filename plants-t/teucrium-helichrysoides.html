Teucrium helichrysoides

Teucrium helichrysoides, commonly known as golden teucrium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
