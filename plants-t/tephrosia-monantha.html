Tephrosia monantha

Tephrosia monantha, commonly known as the African blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing several seeds, and it is often found in dry savannas and woodland areas. Tephrosia monantha is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
