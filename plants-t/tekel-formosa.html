Tekel formosa

Tekel formosa, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Tekel formosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
