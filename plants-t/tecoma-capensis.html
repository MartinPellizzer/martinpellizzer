Tecoma capensis

Tecoma capensis, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Tecoma capensis is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
