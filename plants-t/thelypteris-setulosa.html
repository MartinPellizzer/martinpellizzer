Thelypteris setulosa

Thelypteris setulosa, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with fine, hairy structures along the veins.

Thelypteris setulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
