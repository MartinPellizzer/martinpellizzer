Tracheliopsis tubulosa var. taurica

Tracheliopsis tubulosa var. taurica, commonly known as the Tauric tracheliopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to violet. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and long blooming period, typically from late spring to early summer.
