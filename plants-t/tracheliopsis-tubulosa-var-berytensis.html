Tracheliopsis tubulosa var. berytensis

Tracheliopsis tubulosa var. berytensis, commonly known as the Berytensian tracheliopsis, is a rare and endemic species native to specific regions in the Mediterranean.

The plant produces delicate, pale green to yellowish flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences.
