Thymelaea virgata var. microphylla

Thymelaea virgata var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is cultivated in gardens and used in horticulture.
