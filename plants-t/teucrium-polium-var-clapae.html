Teucrium polium var. clapae

Teucrium polium var. clapae, commonly known as Cyprus germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties have made it historically valued in traditional herbal remedies.
