Trichilia elliotii

Trichilia elliotii, commonly known as African shea tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing oily seeds. The seeds are valued for their high oil content, which is used in cosmetics and traditional medicine.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitats and is also economically significant for local communities.
