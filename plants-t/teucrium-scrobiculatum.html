Teucrium scrobiculatum

Teucrium scrobiculatum, commonly known as redstem germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences and range in color from blue to purple.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
