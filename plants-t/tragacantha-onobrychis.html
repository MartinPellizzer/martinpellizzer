Tragacantha onobrychis

Tragacantha onobrychis, commonly known as sainfoin, is a leguminous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers and has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets. The plant is characterized by its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in dry, sandy soils. Sainfoin is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant for soil enrichment.

It is also used in traditional medicine and as forage for livestock.
