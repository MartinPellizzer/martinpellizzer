Tragopogon oligolepis

Tragopogon oligolepis, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It features slender, hairy stems and produces yellow flowers that develop into long, narrow seed heads.

Tragopogon oligolepis is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
