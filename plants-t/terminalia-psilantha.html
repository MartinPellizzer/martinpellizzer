Terminalia psilantha

Terminalia psilantha, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
