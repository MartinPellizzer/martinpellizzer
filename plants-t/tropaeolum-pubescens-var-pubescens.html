Tropaeolum pubescens var. pubescens

Tropaeolum pubescens var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf nasturtium, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces small, round seed pods that contain numerous seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to self-seed make it a resilient addition to diverse ecosystems.
