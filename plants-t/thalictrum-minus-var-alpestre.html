Thalictrum minus var. alpestre

Thalictrum minus var. alpestre, commonly known as the alpine meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Thalictrum minus var. alpestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
