Teucrium teinense

Teucrium teinense, commonly known as the African germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Teucrium teinense is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
