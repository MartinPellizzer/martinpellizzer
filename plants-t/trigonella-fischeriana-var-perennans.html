Trigonella fischeriana var. perennans

Trigonella fischeriana var. perennans, commonly known as the perennial fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptability and historical use in herbal remedies.
