Teucrium werneri

Teucrium werneri, commonly known as Werneri germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in Mediterranean climates. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, hairy seeds.

Teucrium werneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
