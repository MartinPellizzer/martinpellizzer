Tessaria sericea

Tessaria sericea, commonly known as silverleaf tessaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.

Tessaria sericea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
