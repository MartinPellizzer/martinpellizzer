Thelypteris galanderi

Thelypteris galanderi, commonly known as Galander's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, fibrous roots.

Thelypteris galanderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
