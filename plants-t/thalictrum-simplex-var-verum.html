Thalictrum simplex var. verum

Thalictrum simplex var. verum, commonly known as the showy meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, usually yellow or greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges. It is characterized by its upright stem and showy, clustered inflorescences.

Thalictrum simplex var. verum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
