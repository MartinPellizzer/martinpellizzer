Tectaria artinexa

Tectaria artinexa, commonly known as the Artinex shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically divided into segments with a waxy coating. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous adventitious roots, aiding in its ability to thrive in humid conditions.

The fronds are usually evergreen, providing a lush appearance throughout the year.
