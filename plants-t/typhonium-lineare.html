Typhonium lineare

Typhonium lineare, commonly known as the linear arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often arrowhead-shaped, with a glossy green surface. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a red spathe, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Typhonium lineare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
