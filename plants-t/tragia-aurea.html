Tragia aurea

Tragia aurea, commonly known as golden tragia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small, annual shrub that typically grows in dry, open habitats. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. Its leaves are usually small, hairy, and alternate along the stems.

Tragia aurea is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
