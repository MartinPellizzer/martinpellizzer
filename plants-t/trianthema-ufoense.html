Trianthema ufoense

Trianthema ufoense, commonly known as the UFO plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance has led to its nickname, as the leaves resemble a flying saucer.
