Tropidia disticha

Tropidia disticha, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and pairs of opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stem. Tropidia disticha thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
