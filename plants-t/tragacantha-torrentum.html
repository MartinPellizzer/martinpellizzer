Tragacantha torrentum

Tragacantha torrentum, commonly known as the torrent tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and woody, branching stems. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
