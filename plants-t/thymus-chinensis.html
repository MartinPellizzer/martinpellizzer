Thymus chinensis

Thymus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese thyme, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fragrant leaves and violet or pinkish flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and sage. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Thymus chinensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and medicinal herb gardens.
