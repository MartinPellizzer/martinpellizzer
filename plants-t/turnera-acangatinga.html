Turnera acangatinga

Turnera acangatinga, commonly known as Brazilian buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.
