Tulipa australis var. celsiana

Tulipa australis var. celsiana, commonly known as the Celsiana tulip, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the western regions of South Africa.

It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in spring. The plant features strap-like leaves that are grassy in appearance and grow from a rounded bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a prized addition to cultivated gardens.
