Thea laxa

Thea laxa, commonly known as the slender tea plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are arranged in clusters.

Thea laxa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
