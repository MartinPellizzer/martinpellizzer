Tephrosia montana

Tephrosia montana, commonly known as mountain tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small leaflets, and it has a woody stem with thorny branches.

Tephrosia montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
