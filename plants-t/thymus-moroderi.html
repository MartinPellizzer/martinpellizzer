Thymus moroderi

Thymus moroderi, commonly known as the Moroder's thyme, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula, specifically found in southern Spain and northern Portugal. This low-growing perennial herb features small, fragrant leaves and violet or pale purple flowers. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
