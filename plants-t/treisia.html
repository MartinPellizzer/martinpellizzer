Treisia

Treisia, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, and it thrives in well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in early spring. Treisia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.
