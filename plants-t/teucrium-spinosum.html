Teucrium spinosum

Teucrium spinosum, commonly known as spiny germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, white hairs and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Teucrium spinosum is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
