Thelypteris × nareshii

Thelypteris × nareshii, commonly known as the hybrid holly fern, is a cross between Thelypteris calyptrata and Thelypteris angustipinna.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fertile fronds, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Thelypteris × nareshii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
