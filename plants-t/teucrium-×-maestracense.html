Teucrium × maestracense

Teucrium × maestracense, commonly known as the hybrid germander, is a perennial shrub belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and spikes of blue to violet flowers. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.

Teucrium × maestracense is also valued for its medicinal properties, though it is less commonly cultivated than its parent species.
