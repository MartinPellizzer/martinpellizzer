Thrinax tunica

Thrinax tunica, commonly known as the tunica palm, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a small, slow-growing palm with a slender trunk and a crown of feathery leaves. The plant has a unique, compact appearance with a thick, fibrous trunk covered in a layer of old leaf bases. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils.

Thrinax tunica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
