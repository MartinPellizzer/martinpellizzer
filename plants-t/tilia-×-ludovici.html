Tilia × ludovici

Tilia × ludovici, commonly known as the small-leaf lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are smaller and more serrated compared to its parent species. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in pendulous inflorescences. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia × ludovici is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
