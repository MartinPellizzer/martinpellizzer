Turbinicarpus × antiquorum

Turbinicarpus × antiquorum, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a hybrid between Turbinicarpus macdowellii and Turbinicarpus leucurus, characterized by its swollen, bulbous stem and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant's thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. It produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
