Tovomita macrophylla

Tovomita macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf tovomita, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Tovomita macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
