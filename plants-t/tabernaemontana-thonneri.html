Tabernaemontana thonneri

Tabernaemontana thonneri, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm seasons. The plant has opposite, simple, and often glossy dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Tabernaemontana thonneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its pleasant aroma and aesthetic appeal.
