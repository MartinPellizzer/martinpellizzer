Tapanhuacanga asparagoides

Tapanhuacanga asparagoides, commonly known as the Peruvian asparagus, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous terrain, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
