Thunbergia pleistodonta

Thunbergia pleistodonta, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Thunbergia pleistodonta thrives in well-drained soil and requires regular watering for optimal growth.
