Thecacoris madagascariensis var. montana

Thecacoris madagascariensis var. montana, commonly known as the mountain star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

The flowers bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing and ecologically significant plant in its habitat.
