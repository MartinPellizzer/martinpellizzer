Tamarix allorgei

Tamarix allorgei, commonly known as the Saharan tamarisk, is a shrub native to North Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with deep roots that help it survive in dry conditions. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils along riverbanks and coastal areas.

Tamarix allorgei plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various desert organisms.
