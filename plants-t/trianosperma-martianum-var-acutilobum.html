Trianosperma martianum var. acutilobum

Trianosperma martianum var. acutilobum, commonly known as the Martian sharp-lobed plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of Mars.

It possesses a succulent, fleshy stem that stores water, enabling it to survive in extreme dry conditions. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, reducing water loss through transpiration. The plant produces small, pale blue flowers that bloom during the brief Martian spring.

It has adapted to high levels of ultraviolet radiation and low atmospheric pressure, making it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
