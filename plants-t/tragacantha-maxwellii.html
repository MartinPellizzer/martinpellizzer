Tragacantha maxwellii

Tragacantha maxwellii, commonly known as the Maxwell's tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its resilience to drought conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
