Tibouchina repens

Tibouchina repens, commonly known as creeping tibouchina, is a low-growing shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a white throat. The plant has spreading stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to form dense mats. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs.

Tibouchina repens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shaded conditions.
