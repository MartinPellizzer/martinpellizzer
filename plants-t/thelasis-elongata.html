Thelasis elongata

Thelasis elongata, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a tall, spindly stem with narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in cylindrical heads, often displaying shades of purple or pink.

Thelasis elongata is adapted to arid environments and is often found in grasslands and along coastal areas.
