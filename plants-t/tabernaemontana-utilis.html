Tabernaemontana utilis

Tabernaemontana utilis, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling stars. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Tabernaemontana utilis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
