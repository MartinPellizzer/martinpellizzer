Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp. gracilis

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa. It features a compact, globular body with short, cylindrical ribs that are often covered in a fine, white waxy coating.

The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
