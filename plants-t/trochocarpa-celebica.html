Trochocarpa celebica

Trochocarpa celebica, commonly known as the Celebes starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow fruits that are edible when ripe. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

The fruit is valued for its sweet, tangy flavor and is used in both culinary and traditional medicinal applications.
