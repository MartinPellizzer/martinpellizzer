Tabernaemontana cymulosa

Tabernaemontana cymulosa, commonly known as the fragrant starflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often glossy, while its stems are woody and branching.
