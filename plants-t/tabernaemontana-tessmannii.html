Tabernaemontana tessmannii

Tabernaemontana tessmannii, commonly known as the Tessmann's star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a woody stem, making it a striking ornamental species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its beauty and fragrance, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
