Thuja plicatilis

Thuja plicatilis, commonly known as the Mediterranean thuja, is a coniferous evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a dense, pyramidal shape with scale-like leaves that are tightly pressed together. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain winged seeds, typical of conifers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice in horticulture.
