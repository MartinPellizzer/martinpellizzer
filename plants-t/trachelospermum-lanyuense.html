Trachelospermum lanyuense

Trachelospermum lanyuense, commonly known as Lanyu star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It typically grows as a climbing vine, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
