Thalictrum microspermum

Thalictrum microspermum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale green, with distinctive sepals and stamens. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Thalictrum microspermum is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
