Tephrosia sophoroides

Tephrosia sophoroides, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in dry, sandy soils.

Tephrosia sophoroides is also noted for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
