Trechonaetes bipinnatifida

Trechonaetes bipinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, bipinnatifid fronds that are finely divided, giving it a lacy appearance. The plant thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its rhizomes form dense mats on the water surface or submerged substrate.

The leaves are typically green and float on the water, contributing to water filtration and habitat for small aquatic organisms.
