Trichilia membranacea

Trichilia membranacea, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its smooth, often greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, typically clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.
