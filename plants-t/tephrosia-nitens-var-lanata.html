Tephrosia nitens var. lanata

Tephrosia nitens var. lanata, commonly known as hairy tephrosia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
