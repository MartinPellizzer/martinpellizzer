Teucrium willdenowii

Teucrium willdenowii, commonly known as blue germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches.

Teucrium willdenowii is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
