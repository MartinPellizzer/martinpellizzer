Thesium compressum

Thesium compressum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive bluish-green hue.

The small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.
