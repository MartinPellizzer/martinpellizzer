Telipogon berthae

Telipogon berthae, commonly known as the Socotra star grass, is a unique flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant is adapted to the arid and rugged environment of Socotra, with features that help it conserve water and withstand harsh conditions. It is considered a rare and endemic species, contributing to the island's distinctive flora.

Telipogon berthae plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting local biodiversity.
