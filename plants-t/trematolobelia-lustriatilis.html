Trematolobelia lustriatilis

Trematolobelia lustriatilis, commonly known as the shimmering leaf orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, iridescent green leaves that reflect light in a unique way, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on the forest floor or on the trunks of trees.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species and is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
