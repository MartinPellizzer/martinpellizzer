Tabernaemontana debrayi

Tabernaemontana debrayi, commonly known as the Madagascar star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Its unique appearance and pleasant fragrance make it a popular choice in gardens and as a houseplant.
