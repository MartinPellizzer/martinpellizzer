Tillandsia lutea

Tillandsia lutea, commonly known as the yellow tillandsia, is a species of air plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. This epiphytic plant does not require soil, instead absorbing moisture and nutrients through its specialized leaves. Its striking yellow flowers are often accompanied by vibrant green foliage.

Tillandsia lutea is well adapted to low-light conditions and can thrive in a variety of indoor settings with proper care.
