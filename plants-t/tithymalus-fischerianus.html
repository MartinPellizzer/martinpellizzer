Tithymalus fischerianus

Tithymalus fischerianus, commonly known as the Fischer's tithymalus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
