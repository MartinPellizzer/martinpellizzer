Tephrosia brachystachya

Tephrosia brachystachya, commonly known as small-stemmed tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features small, compound leaves with a pinnate structure, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Tephrosia brachystachya is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
