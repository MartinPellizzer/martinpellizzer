Thelypteris dasypoda

Thelypteris dasypoda, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, hairy structures, giving it its common name.

Thelypteris dasypoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
