Tecoma chinensis

Tecoma chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese trumpetflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Tecoma chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
