Teline rosmarinifolia

Teline rosmarinifolia, commonly known as Mediterranean teline, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with slender, aromatic stems and small, needle-like leaves that resemble those of rosemary. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Teline rosmarinifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
