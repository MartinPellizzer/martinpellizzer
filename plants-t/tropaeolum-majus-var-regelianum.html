Tropaeolum majus var. regelianum

Tropaeolum majus var. regelianum, commonly known as the Surinam whirligig, is a vibrant annual flowering plant native to South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often grown for its decorative foliage and is easy to cultivate in gardens or containers.
