Trichosanthes odontosperma

Trichosanthes odontosperma, commonly known as the bitter melon or karela, is a vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive, bitter, and ridged fruit. The plant produces yellow flowers and has tendrils that help it climb. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The fruit is typically harvested when it is still green, though it can also be used when fully mature and orange in color.
