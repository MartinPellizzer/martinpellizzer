Thunbergia microchlamys

Thunbergia microchlamys, commonly known as the small-lipped thunbergia, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a smooth texture and is adorned with showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy aril. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia microchlamys is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
