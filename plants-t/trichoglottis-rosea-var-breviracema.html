Trichoglottis rosea var. breviracema

Trichoglottis rosea var. breviracema, commonly known as the short-stemmed rose orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light. It prefers warm, humid environments with well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
