Tephrosia eriocarpa var. victoriensis

Tephrosia eriocarpa var. victoriensis, commonly known as the velvet tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and local herbal practices.
