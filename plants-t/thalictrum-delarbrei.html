Thalictrum delarbrei

Thalictrum delarbrei, commonly known as the Delarbre's meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, woodland environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Thalictrum delarbrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
