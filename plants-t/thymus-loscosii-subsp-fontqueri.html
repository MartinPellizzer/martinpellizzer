Thymus loscosii subsp. fontqueri

Thymus loscosii subsp.

fontqueri, commonly known as Fontqueri thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features aromatic, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color and emit a strong thyme scent when crushed.

The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.
