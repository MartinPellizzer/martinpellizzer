Tectaria griffithii

Tectaria griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Tectaria griffithii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in shaded, moist gardens.
