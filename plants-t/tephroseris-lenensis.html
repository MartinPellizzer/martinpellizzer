Tephroseris lenensis

Tephroseris lenensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tephroseris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, preferring well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence.

Tephroseris lenensis is known for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions, making it a hardy species in alpine ecosystems.
