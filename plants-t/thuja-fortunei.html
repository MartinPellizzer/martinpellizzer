Thuja fortunei

Thuja fortunei, commonly known as Chinese fir or golden thread, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, pyramid-shaped crown and scale-like leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Thuja fortunei is also valued for its air-purifying properties and resistance to urban pollution.
