Teucrium somalense

Teucrium somalense, commonly known as Somali germander, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its small, often blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its resilience in arid environments.

Teucrium somalense is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
