Turnera dasytricha

Turnera dasytricha, commonly known as the velvetleaf turnera, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Passifloraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
