Talinum denticulatum

Talinum denticulatum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with finely serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Talinum denticulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
