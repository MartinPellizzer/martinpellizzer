Trichocline caulescens

Trichocline caulescens, commonly known as the slender-stemmed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Trichocline caulescens is often found in open habitats and is adapted to withstand dry conditions.
