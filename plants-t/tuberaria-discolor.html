Tuberaria discolor

Tuberaria discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring, often in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
