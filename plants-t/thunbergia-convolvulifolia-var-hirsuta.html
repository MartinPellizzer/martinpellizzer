Thunbergia convolvulifolia var. hirsuta

Thunbergia convolvulifolia var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its variability in foliage and floral display makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
