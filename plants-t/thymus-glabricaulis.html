Thymus glabricaulis

Thymus glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental and medicinal use.

Thymus glabricaulis is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and herbal remedies.
