Teucrium kabylicum

Teucrium kabylicum, commonly known as Kabylian germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in dense spikes.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
