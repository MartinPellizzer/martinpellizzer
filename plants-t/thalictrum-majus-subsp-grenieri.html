Thalictrum majus subsp. grenieri

Thalictrum majus subsp.

grenieri, commonly known as the showy meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States. It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has a height ranging from 1 to 3 meters. Its flowers are actinomorphic, with numerous stamens and a single pistil, typical of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
