Tillandsia rubra var. fendleri

Tillandsia rubra var. fendleri, commonly known as the Red Tillandsia, is a bromeliad native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red and green foliage, which can vary in intensity depending on environmental conditions. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and does not require soil for growth. It relies on air moisture and nutrients absorbed through its leaves.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper humidity and light conditions.
