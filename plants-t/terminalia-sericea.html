Terminalia sericea

Terminalia sericea, commonly known as the silky terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits that are rich in nutrients. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Terminalia sericea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
