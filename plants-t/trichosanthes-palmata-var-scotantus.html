Trichosanthes palmata var. scotantus

Trichosanthes palmata var. scotantus, commonly known as the scarlet gourd, is a climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The fruit is a striking, elongated, orange gourd with a rough, spiny surface. This plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.

It is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
