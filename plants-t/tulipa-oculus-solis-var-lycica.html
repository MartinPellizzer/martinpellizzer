Tulipa oculus-solis var. lycica

Tulipa oculus-solis var. lycica, commonly known as the Sun Eye Tulip, is a striking bulbous plant native to Turkey.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers with a distinctive orange center and red-brown petals, creating a sun-like appearance. The plant typically blooms in early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
