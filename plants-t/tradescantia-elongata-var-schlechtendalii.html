Tradescantia elongata var. schlechtendalii

Tradescantia elongata var. schlechtendalii, commonly known as the silver sword or silver spider plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to South America.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically silver-blue in color with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
