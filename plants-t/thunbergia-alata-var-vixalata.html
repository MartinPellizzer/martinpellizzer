Thunbergia alata var. vixalata

Thunbergia alata var. vixalata, commonly known as the white morning glory, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

The varietal form 'vixalata' is distinguished by its lighter-colored flowers compared to the typical variety.
