Teucrium × riverae

Teucrium × riverae, commonly known as the river teucrium, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is native to regions of Europe and Asia, where it is often found in woodland edges and stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
