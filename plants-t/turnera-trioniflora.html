Turnera trioniflora

Turnera trioniflora, commonly known as the three-flowered turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in threes, giving it its common name.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
