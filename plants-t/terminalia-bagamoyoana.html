Terminalia bagamoyoana

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for construction and furniture. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It produces small, white flowers followed by capsule fruits containing winged seeds.
