Trachelospermum eglandulatum

Trachelospermum eglandulatum, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to Central America.

It produces fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used as a climbing plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for trellises and arbors.
