Tristachya Dregeana

Tristachya dregeana is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa. It features tall, slender stems covered in silky, silver-blue foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. This plant is known for its dramatic appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. Often used in landscaping for its visual impact, it adds a unique texture to gardens and landscapes. Its resilience and beauty make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and designers alike.
