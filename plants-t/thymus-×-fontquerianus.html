Thymus × fontquerianus

Thymus × fontquerianus, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, dense foliage with small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces tiny, pink or violet flowers in clusters, typically blooming in spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and fragrant properties make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and practical horticulture.
