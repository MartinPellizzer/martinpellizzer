Thalictrum flavum var. wallrothii

Thalictrum flavum var. wallrothii, commonly known as the yellow meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are compound and divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in upright, branched inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
