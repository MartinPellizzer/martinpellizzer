Thunbergia deflexiflora

Thunbergia deflexiflora, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive veins. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in the early morning. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia deflexiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its beauty and ability to climb or trail along structures.
