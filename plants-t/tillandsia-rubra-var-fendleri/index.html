Tillandsia Rubra Var. Fendleri

Tillandsia rubra var. fendleri is a unique air plant known for its striking red and green foliage. Native to the southern United States and northern Mexico, it thrives in humid, tropical environments. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it popular among plant enthusiasts. Unlike traditional plants, it absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves rather than roots. Its vibrant coloration and compact size make it a favorite for terrariums and hanging displays.
