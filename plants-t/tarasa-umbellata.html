Tarasa umbellata

Tarasa umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a striking plant characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescence.

It features clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in an umbrella-like pattern, which gives it its common name. The plant has elongated, narrow leaves that are often variegated with green and white streaks. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Tarasa umbellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
