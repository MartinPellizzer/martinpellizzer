Tupa feuillei var. berteroi

Tupa feuillei var. berteroi, commonly known as the red-flowered tupa, is a unique variant of the tupa genus native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its striking red petals and elongated, narrow leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. The flowers are typically small but numerous, forming clusters that attract various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
