Trisetum tenellum

Trisetum tenellum, commonly known as slender basket grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant has slender, tufted stems that are often less than 30 cm in height. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

The inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spikelet that is often concealed within the leaf sheaths, giving it a distinctive appearance.
