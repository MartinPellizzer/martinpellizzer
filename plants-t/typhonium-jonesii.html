Typhonium jonesii

Typhonium jonesii, commonly known as Jones' arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking, pale yellow to white spadices surrounded by a showy purple spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Typhonium jonesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
