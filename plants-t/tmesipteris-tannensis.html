Tmesipteris tannensis

Tmesipteris tannensis, commonly known as the Tannen fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tmesipteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pinnately compound fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
