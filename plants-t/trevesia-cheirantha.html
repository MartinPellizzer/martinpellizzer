Trevesia cheirantha

Trevesia cheirantha, commonly known as the small trevesia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a greenish-white or pale yellow color.

Trevesia cheirantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
