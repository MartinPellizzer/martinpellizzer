Thunbergia aurea

Thunbergia aurea, commonly known as the golden flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its showy blooms.

Thunbergia aurea is also valued for its ability to climb and can be used in vertical gardening or as a ground cover.
