Tithymalus Calyculatus

Tithymalus calyculatus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Europe. It is known for its small, clustered blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical wonder.
