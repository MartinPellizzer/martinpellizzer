Teucrium nissolianum

Teucrium nissolianum, commonly known as the Nissolia teucrium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually blue to purple in color, blooming in the summer months.

Teucrium nissolianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
