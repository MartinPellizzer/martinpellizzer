Tulipa hayatii

Tulipa hayatii, commonly known as the Turkish tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its distinctive, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tulipa hayatii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
