Telis astroites

Telis astroites, commonly known as the starry telis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale blue. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, thriving in dry conditions.

Telis astroites is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
