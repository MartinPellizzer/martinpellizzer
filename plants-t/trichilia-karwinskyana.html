Trichilia karwinskyana

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes many valuable timber species. The plant is characterized by its elliptical leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.
