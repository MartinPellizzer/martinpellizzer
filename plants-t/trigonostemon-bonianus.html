Trigonostemon bonianus

Trigonostemon bonianus, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has pinnate leaves with three to five leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental planting in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in urban landscapes.
