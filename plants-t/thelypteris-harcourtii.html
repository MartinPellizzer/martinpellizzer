Thelypteris harcourtii

Thelypteris harcourtii, commonly known as the Harcourt's shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing sporangia on the underside of the fronds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady garden settings.
