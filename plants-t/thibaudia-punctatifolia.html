Thibaudia punctatifolia

Thibaudia punctatifolia, commonly known as the spotted thibaudia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive spotted or mottled pattern on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Thibaudia punctatifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
