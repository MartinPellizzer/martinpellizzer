Tibouchina elegans

Tibouchina elegans, commonly known as the Royal Hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white throat. The plant has glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Tibouchina elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
