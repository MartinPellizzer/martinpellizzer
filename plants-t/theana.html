Theana

Theana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, hence its common name. Its leaves are modified into thornless, fleshy stems that store water, and it prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Theana is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
