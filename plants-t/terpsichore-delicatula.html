Terpsichore delicatula

Terpsichore delicatula, commonly known as the delicate lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in well-drained, moist soils. Terpsichore delicatula is often found in woodland edges and meadows, where it forms dense clusters.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, ornamental plant.
