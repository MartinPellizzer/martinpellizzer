Trollius riederianus var. pulcher

Trollius riederianus var. pulcher, commonly known as the golden shield, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in areas of Kazakhstan and Tajikistan. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broadly ovate and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
