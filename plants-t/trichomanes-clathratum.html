Trichomanes clathratum

Trichomanes clathratum, commonly known as the basket fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are often used in ornamental horticulture. Its reproductive structures, known as sporangia, are clustered in small, cup-like formations called indusia.

Trichomanes clathratum is also noted for its ability to form dense mats, which help in soil stabilization and moisture retention.
