Tillaea macrantha var. pedicellosa

Tillaea macrantha var. pedicellosa, commonly known as the pedicelled tillaea, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of the western United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, showy white or pink flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

It is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
