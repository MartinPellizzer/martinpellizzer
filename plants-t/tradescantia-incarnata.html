Tradescantia incarnata

Tradescantia incarnata, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Tradescantia incarnata is a herbaceous perennial that blooms in early summer and is popular in gardens for its ornamental value.
