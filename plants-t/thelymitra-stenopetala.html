Thelymitra stenopetala

Thelymitra stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils in open grasslands and heathlands. The plant features a single, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive, narrow petals. Its growth is often associated with specific microhabitats and can be sensitive to environmental changes.

The species is part of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
