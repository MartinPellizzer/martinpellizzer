Therocistus glomeratus

Therocistus glomeratus, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Therocistus glomeratus is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
