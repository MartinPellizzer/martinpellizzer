Trixis forzzae

Trixis forzzae, commonly known as the Socotra cypress, is a unique and rare plant species native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, twisted trunk and conical shape, which resembles a cypress tree. The plant has scale-like leaves that are tightly arranged around the stems, giving it a dense, almost woody appearance. It thrives in the arid and rocky environments of Socotra, adapting to the harsh climate with deep root systems.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, Trixis forzzae is considered a vulnerable species.
