Trichoglottis granulata

Trichoglottis granulata, commonly known as the granulated trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, often reddish-brown, pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellowish lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Trichoglottis granulata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
