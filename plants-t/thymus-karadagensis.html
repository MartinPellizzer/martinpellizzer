Thymus karadagensis

Thymus karadagensis, commonly known as Karadag thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are fragrant and used in traditional cuisine and herbal medicine.

Thymus karadagensis is typically found in rocky and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
