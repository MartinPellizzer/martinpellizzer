Tolpis santosii

Tolpis santosii, commonly known as Santos' daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers bloom in shades of yellow and white.

Tolpis santosii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
