Trigonochilum russellianum

Trigonochilum russellianum, commonly known as Russell's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It grows as an epiphytic or lithophytic species, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments.

Its botanical features include linear leaves and a slender stem with clustered inflorescences.
