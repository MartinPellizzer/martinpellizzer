Thlaspi subulatum

Thlaspi subulatum, commonly known as Arctic mouse-ear or winter mouse-ear, is a small, flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Thlaspi subulatum plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
