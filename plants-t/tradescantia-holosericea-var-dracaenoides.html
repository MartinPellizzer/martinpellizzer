Tradescantia holosericea var. dracaenoides

Tradescantia holosericea var. dracaenoides, commonly known as the Dragon Blood Plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caribbean.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with red or purple veins, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for container gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a desirable ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
