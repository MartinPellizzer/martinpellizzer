Trichoglottis tamesisii

Trichoglottis tamesisii, commonly known as the Tamesis orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
