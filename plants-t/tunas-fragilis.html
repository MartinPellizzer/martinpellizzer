Tunas fragilis

Tunas fragilis, commonly known as the fragile tuna, is a unique aquatic plant found in specific freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that allow light to pass through, aiding in photosynthesis in low-light conditions. The plant has a branched structure with small, floating fronds that help it stay afloat in water. Its reproductive system involves the production of spores rather than seeds, which are released into the water for dispersal.

Tunas fragilis plays a vital role in maintaining aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.
