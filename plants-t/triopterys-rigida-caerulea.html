Triopterys rigida caerulea

Triopterys rigida caerulea, commonly known as the blue-leafed triopterys, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as mountain forests, where it thrives in the understory with high humidity and filtered sunlight.
