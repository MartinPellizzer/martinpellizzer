Tilia mandshurica var. tuberculata

Tilia mandshurica var. tuberculata, commonly known as the Manchurian lime tree with tuberculate bark, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The bark of this variety is distinctive, featuring small, rounded tubercles that give it a unique texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are followed by spherical, greenish fruits.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
