Trichomanes sarawakensis

Trichomanes sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak huperzia, is a fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branched fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in height. The plant produces sporangia in clusters on the underside of its leaves, a feature typical of many ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Trichomanes sarawakensis is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia.
