Tephrosia uniflora subsp. uniflora

Tephrosia uniflora subsp.

uniflora, commonly known as the narrowleaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in dry, open habitats.
