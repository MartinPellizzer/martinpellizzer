Thrixspermum olidum

Thrixspermum olidum, commonly known as the sweet myrtle, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, and the plant produces small, dark blue berries.
