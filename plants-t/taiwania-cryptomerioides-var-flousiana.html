Taiwania cryptomerioides var. flousiana

Taiwania cryptomerioides var. flousiana, commonly known as the Flousian fir, is a rare variant of the Taiwan fir.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Taiwan. The plant features needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged spirally along the branches. It produces cones that are typically small and cylindrical, containing seeds.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
