Tuberaria vulgaris f. sufruticosa

Tuberaria vulgaris f. sufruticosa, commonly known as the shrub form of the common tuberaria, is a perennial plant characterized by its woody stems and compact growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.
