Taraxacum miyabei

Taraxacum miyabei, commonly known as the Miyabei dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, hollow stem that bears a bright yellow flower head composed of numerous small florets. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
