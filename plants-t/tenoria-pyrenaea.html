Tenoria pyrenaea

Tenoria pyrenaea, commonly known as the Pyrenean tenoria, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale purple flowers. It is adapted to cold climates and has a slow growth rate.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
