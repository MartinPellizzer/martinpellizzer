Thalictrum delavayi f. acuminatum

Thalictrum delavayi f. acuminatum, commonly known as the acute-leaved meadow rue, is a botanical variant of the meadow rue genus.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
