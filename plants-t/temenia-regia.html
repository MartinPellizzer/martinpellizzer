Temenia regia

Temenia regia, commonly known as the royal temenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, dark green leaves that are oval in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Temenia regia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
