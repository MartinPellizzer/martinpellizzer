Turraea obtusifolia

Turraea obtusifolia, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Turraea obtusifolia is also valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
