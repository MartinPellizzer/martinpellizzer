Thymus bracteosus subsp. granatensis

Thymus bracteosus subsp.

granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its aromatic, small, grey-green leaves and compact growth habit.

The plant produces pink to purple flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses, similar to other thyme species.
