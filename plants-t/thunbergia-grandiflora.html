Thunbergia grandiflora

Thunbergia grandiflora, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its visual appeal.

Thunbergia grandiflora thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
