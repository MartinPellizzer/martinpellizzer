Thalictrum glandulosissimum var. tsaii

Thalictrum glandulosissimum var. tsaii, commonly known as the Tsai's meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features slender stems that often grow in dense clusters, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with finely divided leaflets that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
