Trichodiadema rogersiae

Trichodiadema rogersiae, commonly known as the Rogers' ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
