Trisetum flavescens var. tenue

Trisetum flavescens var. tenue, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as meadows and roadsides. It produces small, pale yellow to brownish flowers in late summer.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
