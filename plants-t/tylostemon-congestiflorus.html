Tylostemon congestiflorus

Tylostemon congestiflorus, commonly known as the clustered-flowered tylostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact shrub form. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

Tylostemon congestiflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
