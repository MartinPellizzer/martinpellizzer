Tilia mofungensis

Tilia mofungensis, commonly known as the Mofungensis lime tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a significant species in its native habitat.
