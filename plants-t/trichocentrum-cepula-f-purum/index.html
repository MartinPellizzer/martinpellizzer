Trichocentrum Cepula F. Purum

Trichocentrum cepula f. purum is a unique orchid species known for its striking appearance and rare occurrence in its native habitat. This epiphytic plant is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with dappled sunlight, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution and specific care requirements, it is often considered a challenging plant to cultivate. Its beauty and rarity make it a prized addition to any collection of tropical plants.
