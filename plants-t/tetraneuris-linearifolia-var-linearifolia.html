Tetraneuris linearifolia var. linearifolia

Tetraneuris linearifolia var. linearifolia, commonly known as the linearleaf tetraneuris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
