Trollius ilmenensis

Trollius ilmenensis, commonly known as the Ilmen Sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the colder regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in wet meadows, bogs, and other moist habitats. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in a fine, downy texture.

Trollius ilmenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
