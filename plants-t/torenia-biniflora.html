Torenia biniflora

Torenia biniflora, commonly known as the twin-flower morning glory, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant produces two small, paired flowers on each inflorescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Torenia biniflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
