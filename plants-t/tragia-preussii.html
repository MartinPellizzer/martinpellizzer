Tragia preussii

Tragia preussii, commonly known as the Prussian tragia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Tragia preussii is also known for its hardy nature, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.
