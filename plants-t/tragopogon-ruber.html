Tragopogon ruber

Tragopogon ruber, commonly known as the red goat's beard, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flower heads with bristly, hair-like structures surrounding the central disk. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Tragopogon ruber is often found in temperate regions of North America and Eurasia.
