Terniola carinata

Terniola carinata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a unique member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
