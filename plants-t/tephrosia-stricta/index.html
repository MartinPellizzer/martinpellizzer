Tephrosia Stricta

Tephrosia stricta is a hardy shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is known for its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. It is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits. Tephrosia stricta is also valued as a ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and resilience.
