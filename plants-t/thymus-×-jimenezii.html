Thymus × jimenezii

Thymus × jimenezii, commonly known as Jimenez's thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or lavender flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in gardens and herbal settings.
