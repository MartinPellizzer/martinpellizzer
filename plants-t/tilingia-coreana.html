Tilingia coreana

Tilingia coreana, commonly known as the Korean tilingia, is a flowering plant native to South Korea.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Tilingia coreana is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Korean medicine.
