Tabernaemontana densiflora

Tabernaemontana densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered star of India, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Tabernaemontana densiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
