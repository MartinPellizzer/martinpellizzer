Tropaeolum jarrattii

Tropaeolum jarrattii, commonly known as the Jarratt's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to gardens and greenhouses.
