Triclisperma grandiflora

Triclisperma grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered triclisperma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features palmate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, tropical environments. Triclisperma grandiflora is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and aesthetic appeal.
