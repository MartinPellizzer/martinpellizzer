Tromsdorffia speciosa

Tromsdorffia speciosa, commonly known as the showy tromsdorffia, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. Tromsdorffia speciosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
