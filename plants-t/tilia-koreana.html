Tilia koreana

Tilia koreana, commonly known as Korean lime tree, is a deciduous tree native to Korea and parts of China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The tree produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia koreana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
