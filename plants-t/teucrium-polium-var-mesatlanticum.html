Teucrium polium var. mesatlanticum

Teucrium polium var. mesatlanticum, commonly known as the Atlantic germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental flowers make it a valued plant in horticulture.
