Trigonella filiformis

Trigonella filiformis, commonly known as slender trigonella, is a small annual legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems and small, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, followed by small, lens-shaped pods containing one or two seeds. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Trigonella filiformis is valued for its nutritional properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
