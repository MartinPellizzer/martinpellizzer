Trigonella cylindracea f. jaffensis

Trigonella cylindracea f. jaffensis, commonly known as the Jaffa trigonella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to dry conditions. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.
