Tricalysia perrieri subsp. perrieri

Tricalysia perrieri subsp.

perrieri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white.

The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of 15 to 30 centimeters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
