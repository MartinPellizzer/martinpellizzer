Tragacantha heldreichii

Tragacantha heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pods that contain small, round seeds.

It is known for its ability to thrive in rocky and arid environments, making it an important species in its native ecosystem.
