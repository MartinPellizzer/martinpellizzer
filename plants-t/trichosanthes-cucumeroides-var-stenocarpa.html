Trichosanthes cucumeroides var. stenocarpa

Trichosanthes cucumeroides var. stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited Chinese trumpet vine, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, yellow-orange flowers that resemble trumpets. The plant produces long, cylindrical fruits that are typically covered in a rough, hairy texture. Native to China, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.
