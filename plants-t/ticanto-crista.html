Ticanto crista

Ticanto crista, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, branching stem with a zigzag pattern, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as bloom. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
