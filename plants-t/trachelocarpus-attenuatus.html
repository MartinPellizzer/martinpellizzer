Trachelocarpus attenuatus

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and its distinctive, fan-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The windmill palm is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal environments, making it suitable for landscaping in tropical and subtropical areas.
