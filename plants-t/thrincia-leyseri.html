Thrincia leyseri

Thrincia leyseri, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Thrincia leyseri is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
