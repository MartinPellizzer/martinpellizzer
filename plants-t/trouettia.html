Trouettia

Trouettia, commonly known as the African lily, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It consists of several species, most of which are herbaceous perennials with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plants typically have strap-like leaves and produce clusters of fragrant blooms in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Trouettia species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and as houseplants.

They prefer well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
