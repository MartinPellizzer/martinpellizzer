Tabernaemontana undulata

Tabernaemontana undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaved angel's trumpet, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The leaves are ovate with wavy margins, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
