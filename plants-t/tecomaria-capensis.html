Tecomaria capensis

Tecomaria capensis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Tecomaria capensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
