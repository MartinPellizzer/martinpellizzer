Typhonium johnsonianum

Typhonium johnsonianum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces strikingly large, white or cream-colored inflorescences that resemble umbrellas. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular ornamental plant in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
