Trichopteryx gigantea var. phalacrotes

Trichopteryx gigantea var. phalacrotes, commonly known as the giant feather fern, is a striking member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with dark brown, wiry stems that spread along the ground. Its leaves are bipinnate, with delicate, light green pinnae that give it a feathery appearance.

This variant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
