Tropaeolum leptoceras

Tropaeolum leptoceras, commonly known as the slender jewelweed, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that split open to release tiny seeds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Tropaeolum leptoceras thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.
