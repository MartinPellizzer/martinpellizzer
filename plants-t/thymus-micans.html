Thymus micans

Thymus micans, commonly known as the silver thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus micans is often used in herbal medicine and as a culinary herb, similar to other thyme species.
