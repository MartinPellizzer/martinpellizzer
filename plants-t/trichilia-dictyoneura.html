Trichilia dictyoneura

Trichilia dictyoneura, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The wood of Trichilia dictyoneura is valued for its fine grain and durability, making it useful in furniture and carving.
