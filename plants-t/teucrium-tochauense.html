Teucrium tochauense

Teucrium tochauense, commonly known as the Tochau thyme.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tien Shan and Pamir Mountains. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
