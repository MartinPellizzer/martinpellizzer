Trochetia granulata

Trochetia granulata, commonly known as the granulated broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant features small, granular bumps on its stems, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Trochetia granulata is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
