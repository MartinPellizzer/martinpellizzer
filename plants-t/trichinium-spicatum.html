Trichinium spicatum

Trichinium spicatum, commonly known as the spicate trichinium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a delicate, fern-like appearance.
