Tamonea subbiflora

Tamonea subbiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tamonea, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Tamonea subbiflora plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and provides habitat for various invertebrates.
