Tournefortia laevigata var. latifolia

Tournefortia laevigata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often broad and lance-shaped. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, arranged in clusters, and attract various pollinators.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
