Triodia schinzii

Triodia schinzii, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry growth habit and its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss. It plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland dunes.

Triodia schinzii is well adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall.
