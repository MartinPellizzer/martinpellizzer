Tetraberlinia longiracemosa

Tetraberlinia longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme berberidaceae, is a member of the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Asia.

Due to its unique floral structure and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
