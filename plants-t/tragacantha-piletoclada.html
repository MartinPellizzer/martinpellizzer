Tragacantha piletoclada

Tragacantha piletoclada, commonly known as the Persian gum tree, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southwestern Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, branched stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds.
