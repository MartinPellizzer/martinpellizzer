Thelymitra javanica

Thelymitra javanica, commonly known as the Javan spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with a single, showy flower that is often violet or purple in color. Its flowers are characterized by their intricate, spider-like appearance, which gives it its common name.

Thelymitra javanica is known for its delicate structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
