Tilia paucicostata subsp. yunnanensis

Tilia paucicostata subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan linden, is a deciduous tree native to southwestern China. It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin.

The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in drooping inflorescences, typical of the genus Tilia. It produces small, oval fruits that are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
