Tritoniopsis pulchella

Tritoniopsis pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful tritoniopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, green fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Tritoniopsis pulchella is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
