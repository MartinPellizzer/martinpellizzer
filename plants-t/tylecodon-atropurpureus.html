Tylecodon atropurpureus

Tylecodon atropurpureus, commonly known as the purple woolly bush, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, woolly hairs, which help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
