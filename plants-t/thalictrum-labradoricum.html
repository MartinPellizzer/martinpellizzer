Thalictrum labradoricum

Thalictrum labradoricum, commonly known as Labrador crowfoot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Thalictrum labradoricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
