Trifolium trichopterum

Trifolium trichopterum, commonly known as the slender trefoil, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.

Trifolium trichopterum is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
