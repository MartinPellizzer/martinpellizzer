Turanecio

Turanecio, commonly known as the "turban plant," is a unique species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, turban-like shape, with a dense, rounded form that resembles a traditional headgear. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often blooming in late spring.

Turanecio is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
