Typhonium varians

Typhonium varians, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or swamp taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wet, swampy areas and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
