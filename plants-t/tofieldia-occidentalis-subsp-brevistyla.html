Tofieldia occidentalis subsp. brevistyla

Tofieldia occidentalis subsp.

brevistyla, commonly known as the western tofieldia, is a flowering plant native to western North America. It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance and slender, erect stems.

The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, meadows, and along stream banks.
