Tinospora orophila

Tinospora orophila, commonly known as the rock tinospora, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its woody stem and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Tinospora orophila is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in shaded forest areas.
