Tigridia lobata

Tigridia lobata, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces long, slender stems that support its vibrant blooms. Tigridia lobata is adapted to arid environments, with deep roots that allow it to survive in dry conditions.
