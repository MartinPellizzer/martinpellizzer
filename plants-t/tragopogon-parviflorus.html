Tragopogon parviflorus

Tragopogon parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features basal leaves that are narrow and linear, with a hairy texture. Its inflorescence consists of yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Tragopogon parviflorus is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
