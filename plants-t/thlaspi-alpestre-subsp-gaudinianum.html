Thlaspi alpestre subsp. gaudinianum

Thlaspi alpestre subsp.

gaudinianum, commonly known as the alpine cabbages, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early summer. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in alpine ecosystems.
