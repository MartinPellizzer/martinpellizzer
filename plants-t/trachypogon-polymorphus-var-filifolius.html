Trachypogon polymorphus var. filifolius

Trachypogon polymorphus var. filifolius, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often branched culms. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a hairy surface that distinguishes it from other grasses. Its inflorescence consists of a single, spike-like cluster of small, spikelets.

This variety is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
