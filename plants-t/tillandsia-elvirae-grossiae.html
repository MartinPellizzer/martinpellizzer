Tillandsia elvirae-grossiae

Tillandsia elvirae-grossiae, commonly known as the Elvirae-Grossiae air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, which helps in trapping moisture from the air. It produces small, colorful flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
