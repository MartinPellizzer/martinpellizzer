Trichomanes viride

Trichomanes viride, commonly known as green maidenhair, is a fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bright green fronds that are typically narrow and elongated. Trichomanes viride is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
