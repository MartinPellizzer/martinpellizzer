Tephrosia bibracteolata subsp. subpalmatifolia

Tephrosia bibracteolata subsp.

subpalmatifolia, commonly known as the palmate tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa. It features palmately compound leaves, with each leaflet arranged radiating from a central point.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Fabaceae family.
