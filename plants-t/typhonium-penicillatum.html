Typhonium penicillatum

Typhonium penicillatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a spathe, which is often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
