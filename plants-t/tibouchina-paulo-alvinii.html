Tibouchina paulo-alvinii

Tibouchina paulo-alvinii, commonly known as the Brazilian flame flower, is a striking shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Tibouchina paulo-alvinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
