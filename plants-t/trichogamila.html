Trichogamila

Trichogamila, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Trichogamila is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy flowers.

It prefers warm, humid climates and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
