Thunbergia humbertii var. hirsuta

Thunbergia humbertii var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as a vine or shrub, reaching heights of up to several meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
