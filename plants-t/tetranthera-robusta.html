Tetranthera robusta

Tetranthera robusta, commonly known as the robust tetranthera, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters called capitula. It is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
