Tacca roxburghii

Tacca roxburghii, commonly known as the bat plant or Indian basket, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces a unique, pendulous inflorescence with a distinctive, fleshy, bat-like structure at the base. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a strong, sweet scent.

Tacca roxburghii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
