Thymus hirtus var. gadorensis

Thymus hirtus var. gadorensis, commonly known as the Gadorensis thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and gray-green, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
