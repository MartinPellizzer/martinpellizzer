Trema vieillardii

Trema vieillardii, commonly known as the African nettle.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family, characterized by its opposite, heart-shaped leaves and stinging hairs. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its small greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels, and it produces small, dry fruits.

Trema vieillardii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
