Tainia cristata

Tainia cristata, commonly known as the crowned tainia, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble a crown, hence its common name. The plant features broad, waxy leaves with a smooth texture and a strong, spicy fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Tainia cristata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
