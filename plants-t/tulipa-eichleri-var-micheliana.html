Tulipa eichleri var. micheliana

Tulipa eichleri var. micheliana, commonly known as the Micheliana tulip, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant produces narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and hardiness in colder climates.
