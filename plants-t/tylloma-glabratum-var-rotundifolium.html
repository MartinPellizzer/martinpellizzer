Tylloma glabratum var. rotundifolium

Tylloma glabratum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leafed tylloma, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often rounded at the base. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Apiaceae family.
