Tillandsia paleacea f. disticha

Tillandsia paleacea f. disticha, commonly known as the silver-tipped air plant, is a bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the late spring or early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
