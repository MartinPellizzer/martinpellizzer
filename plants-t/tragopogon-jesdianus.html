Tragopogon jesdianus

Tragopogon jesdianus, commonly known as the Jesdian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a cluster of small flowers. The plant features a tall, slender stem with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its yellow disk flowers bloom in late summer, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Tragopogon jesdianus is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
