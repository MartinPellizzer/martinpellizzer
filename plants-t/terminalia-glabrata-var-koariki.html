Terminalia glabrata var. koariki

Terminalia glabrata var. koariki, commonly known as the koariki or African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The species is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
