Trigonopyren nitidulus

Trigonopyren nitidulus, commonly known as the glossy pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, giving them a glossy appearance. Trigonopyren nitidulus thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
