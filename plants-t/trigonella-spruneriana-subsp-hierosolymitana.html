Trigonella spruneriana subsp. hierosolymitana

Trigonella spruneriana subsp.

hierosolymitana, commonly known as the Jerusalem trigonella, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine, valued for their potential health benefits.
