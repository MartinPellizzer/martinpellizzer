Thunbergia ciliata

Thunbergia ciliata, commonly known as the velvet flower or Indian jute, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for use as a ground cover or in vertical gardening.

Thunbergia ciliata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
