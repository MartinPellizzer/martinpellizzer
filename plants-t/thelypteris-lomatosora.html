Thelypteris lomatosora

Thelypteris lomatosora, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to substrates such as soil or rock.

Thelypteris lomatosora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
