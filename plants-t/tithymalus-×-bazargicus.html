Tithymalus × bazargicus

Tithymalus × bazargicus, commonly known as the hybrid tithymalus, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow.
