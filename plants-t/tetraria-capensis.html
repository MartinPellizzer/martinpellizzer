Tetraria capensis

Tetraria capensis, commonly known as the Cape tetraria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

Tetraria capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
