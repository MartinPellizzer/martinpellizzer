Thryptomene remota

Thryptomene remota, commonly known as the slender thryptomene, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white to pale pink flowers. It typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, preferring well-drained soils.
