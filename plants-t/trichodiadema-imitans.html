Trichodiadema imitans

Trichodiadema imitans, commonly known as the mimic ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
