Taraxacum stellum

Taraxacum stellum, commonly known as the star dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped leaves and a flowering stem that bears a single, showy yellow flower head. The flower head consists of numerous small florets that develop into fluffy white seed heads after blooming. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
