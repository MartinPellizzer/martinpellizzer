Teucrium Polium Subsp. Cossonii

Cosson's germander, scientifically known as Teucrium polium subsp. cossonii, is a hardy perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is prized for its aromatic foliage and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its resilience and ornamental value have earned it a place in both traditional herbal medicine and modern horticulture. With its historical significance and adaptability, Cosson's germander remains a valuable addition to botanical gardens and natural habitats.
