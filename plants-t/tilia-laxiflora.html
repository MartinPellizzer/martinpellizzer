Tilia laxiflora

Tilia laxiflora, commonly known as the loose-flowered lime, is a deciduous tree native to parts of Asia, including China and Korea.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The tree produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in drooping clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a small, round蒴果 that splits open to release tiny seeds.
