Tectaria cicutaria

Tectaria cicutaria, commonly known as the common tectaria, is a fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and fronds that are typically pinnately compound. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Tectaria cicutaria is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
