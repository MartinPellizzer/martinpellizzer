Trichosanthes heteroclita

Trichosanthes heteroclita, commonly known as the Chinese bitter melon, is a vine native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and large, lobed leaves. Its fruit is elongated, often with a warty surface, and contains numerous seeds.
