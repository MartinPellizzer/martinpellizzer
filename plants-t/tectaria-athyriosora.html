Tectaria athyriosora

Tectaria athyriosora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its intricate, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive pattern of veins.

Tectaria athyriosora is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in humid conditions.
