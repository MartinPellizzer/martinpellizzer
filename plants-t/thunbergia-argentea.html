Thunbergia argentea

Thunbergia argentea, commonly known as silver trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a vigorous climber with heart-shaped leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its rapid growth and attractive flowers, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
