Tephrosia longipes

Tephrosia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Tephrosia longipes is also noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine for their antifungal and antimalarial effects.
