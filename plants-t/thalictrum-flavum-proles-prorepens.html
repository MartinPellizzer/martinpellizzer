Thalictrum flavum proles prorepens

Thalictrum flavum proles prorepens, commonly known as the yellow meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
