Thelycrania australis

Thelycrania australis, commonly known as the Australian rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Thelycrania australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand drought conditions.
