Thunbergia delamerei

Thunbergia delamerei, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia delamerei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
