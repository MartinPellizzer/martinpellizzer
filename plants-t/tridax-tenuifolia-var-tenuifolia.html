Tridax tenuifolia var. tenuifolia

Tridax tenuifolia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf tridax, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant has slender, hairy stems and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
