Tripterygium doianum

Tripterygium doianum, commonly known as the Chinese herb "Lei Gong Teng," is a flowering plant native to China, Korea, and Japan.

It contains various bioactive compounds, including tripterygium glycosides, which have been studied for their medicinal properties.
