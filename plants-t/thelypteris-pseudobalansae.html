Thelypteris pseudobalansae

Thelypteris pseudobalansae, commonly known as the Balansae shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a complex arrangement of pinnae and pinnules. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots, which helps it anchor in soil and absorb nutrients.

The spores are produced in clusters on the undersides of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in forest understories.
