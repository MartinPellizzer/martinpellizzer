Terminalia tomentosa var. crenulata

Terminalia tomentosa var. crenulata, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The leaves are typically elliptical, with a hairy texture on both sides, and the bark is smooth and pale. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
