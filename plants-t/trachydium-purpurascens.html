Trachydium purpurascens

Trachydium purpurascens, commonly known as the red-stemmed daisy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a purple or reddish center. Trachydium purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
