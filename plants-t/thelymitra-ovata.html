Thelymitra ovata

Thelymitra ovata, commonly known as the oval leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a single, upright stem with a basal rosette of grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and are arranged in a loose spike.

Thelymitra ovata is a perennial species that blooms during the cooler months, usually from late autumn to early spring.
