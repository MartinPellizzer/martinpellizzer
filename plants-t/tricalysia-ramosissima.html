Tricalysia ramosissima

Tricalysia ramosissima, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Tricalysia ramosissima is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
