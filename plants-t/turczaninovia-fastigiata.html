Turczaninovia fastigiata

Turczaninovia fastigiata, commonly known as the Turczaninovia fastigiata, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
