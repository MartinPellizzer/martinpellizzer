Tristellateia madagascariensis f. humbertii

Tristellateia madagascariensis f. humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
