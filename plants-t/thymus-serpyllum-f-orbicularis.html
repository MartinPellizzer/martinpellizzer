Thymus serpyllum f. orbicularis

Thymus serpyllum f. orbicularis, commonly known as roundleaf thyme, is a compact, low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its aromatic leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
