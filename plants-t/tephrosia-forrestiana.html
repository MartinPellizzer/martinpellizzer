Tephrosia forrestiana

Tephrosia forrestiana, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, blue or purple flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural systems. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
