Tridentopsis mutica

Tridentopsis mutica, commonly known as the hairy trident.

It is a succulent plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its compact, low-growing form. The plant features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer.

Tridentopsis mutica is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
