Trigonella laciniata

Trigonella laciniata, commonly known as the cutleaf fenugreek, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs and possesses trifoliate leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, followed by hairy, triangular pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and disturbed areas.

Trigonella laciniata is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various health purposes.
