Telipogon embreei

Telipogon embreei, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a rare and diminutive flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant grows in rocky and humid environments, often in cloud forests, where it relies on specific symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
