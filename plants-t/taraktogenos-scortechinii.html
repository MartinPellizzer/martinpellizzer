Taraktogenos scortechinii

Taraktogenos scortechinii, commonly known as the Mediterranean thyme.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic, small, opposite leaves and spikes of blue to purple flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often used in herbal medicine for its antiseptic and carminative properties.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
