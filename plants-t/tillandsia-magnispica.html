Tillandsia magnispica

Tillandsia magnispica, commonly known as the giant air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them, instead absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. The plant features long, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape, which helps in capturing and retaining water. It produces striking, showy inflorescences with flowers that can range in color from white to pink and purple.

Tillandsia magnispica is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
