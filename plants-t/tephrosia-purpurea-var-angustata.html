Tephrosia purpurea var. angustata

Tephrosia purpurea var. angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf purple tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of purple or pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.

This species is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural insecticides.
