Tabernaemontana telfairiana

Tabernaemontana telfairiana, commonly known as the Telfair's star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Tabernaemontana telfairiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
