Thalictrum clavatum var. cavaleriei

Thalictrum clavatum var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of China.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in upright clusters, which bloom in early summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and subtle floral display.
