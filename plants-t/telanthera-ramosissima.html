Telanthera ramosissima

Telanthera ramosissima, commonly known as the red-flowered telanthera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of showy, red or orange-red tubular flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Telanthera ramosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
