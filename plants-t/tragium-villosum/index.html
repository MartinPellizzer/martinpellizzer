Tragium Villosum

Tragium villosum is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring delicate, hairy leaves and small, violet-colored flowers. This species thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Tragium villosum offers valuable insights into plant resilience and conservation efforts.
