Trigonella elliptica

Trigonella elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic fenugreek, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are elliptical in shape, and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces slender pods containing small, kidney-shaped seeds. Native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
