Trianthema glossostigma

Trianthema glossostigma, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, perennial herb with a compact growth habit and silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
