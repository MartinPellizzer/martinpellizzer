Thelymitra aristata var. megcalyptra

Thelymitra aristata var. megcalyptra, commonly known as the megacalypt orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, stocky rhizome and a single, erect, leafy stem. The plant produces a single spike of pale green to white flowers with distinctive, elongated petals and sepals. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

This variety is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
