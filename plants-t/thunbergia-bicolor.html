Thunbergia bicolor

Thunbergia bicolor, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue and white flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia bicolor is a climbing vine that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters.
