Trichospermum grandifolium

Trichospermum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf trichospermum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in panicles. It produces small, spherical fruits that contain a single seed.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
