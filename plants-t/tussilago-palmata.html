Tussilago palmata

Tussilago palmata, commonly known as the palmate coughwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its palmately divided leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies for respiratory ailments.

Its unique leaf structure and showy blooms make it a distinctive addition to native plant gardens.
