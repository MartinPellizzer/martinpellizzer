Thelymitra rubra var. magnanthera

Thelymitra rubra var. magnanthera, commonly known as the red-tipped sun orchid, is a species native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red-tipped leaves and its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open woodland or heathland, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a distinctive yellow dorsal sepal.

This orchid is a popular choice for cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
