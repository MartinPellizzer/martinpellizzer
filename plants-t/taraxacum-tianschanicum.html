Taraxacum tianschanicum

Taraxacum tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head that produces numerous yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. It has a taproot system that allows it to survive in harsh conditions.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
