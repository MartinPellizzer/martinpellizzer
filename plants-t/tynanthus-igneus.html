Tynanthus igneus

Tynanthus igneus, commonly known as the firebush, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright red to orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Tynanthus igneus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract hummingbirds.
