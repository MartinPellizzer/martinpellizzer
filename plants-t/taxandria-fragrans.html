Taxandria fragrans

Taxandria fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant taxandria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.
