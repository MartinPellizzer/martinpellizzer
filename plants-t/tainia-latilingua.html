Tainia latilingua

Tainia latilingua, commonly known as the broadleaf tainia, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
