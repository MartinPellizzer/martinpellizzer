Thlaspi chlorifolium

Thlaspi chlorifolium, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its bright yellow flowers and lobed leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows and along roadsides. The plant has a compact growth habit with a basal rosette of leaves.

Its seeds are small and contained in siliques, typical of the mustard family.
