Tabernaemontana laeta var. densa

Tabernaemontana laeta var. densa, commonly known as the dense-flowered star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its aromatic blooms and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
