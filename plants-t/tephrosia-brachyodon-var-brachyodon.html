Tephrosia brachyodon var. brachyodon

Tephrosia brachyodon var. brachyodon, commonly known as the narrowleaf tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in degraded areas.
