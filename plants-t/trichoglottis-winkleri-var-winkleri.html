Trichoglottis winkleri var. winkleri

Trichoglottis winkleri var. winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
