Tainia purpureifolia

Tainia purpureifolia, commonly known as the purple-leaved tainia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts with its green stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Tainia purpureifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability to indoor conditions.
