Tabernaemontana ochroleuca

Tabernaemontana ochroleuca, commonly known as the white star jasmine.

This evergreen shrub is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Tabernaemontana ochroleuca is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
