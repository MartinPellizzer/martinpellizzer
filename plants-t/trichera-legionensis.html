Trichera legionensis

Trichera legionensis, commonly known as the legion's trichera, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by the development of capsule fruits containing minute seeds. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Trichera legionensis is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
