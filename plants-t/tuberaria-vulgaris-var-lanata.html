Tuberaria vulgaris var. lanata

Tuberaria vulgaris var. lanata, commonly known as the woolly tuberaria, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves and stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
