Theka

Theka, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Theka thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
