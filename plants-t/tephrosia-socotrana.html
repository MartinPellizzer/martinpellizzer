Tephrosia socotrana

Tephrosia socotrana, commonly known as Socotra's tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its unique leaf structure and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods, a common feature among members of the legume family.
