Trisetopsis roggeveldensis

Trisetopsis roggeveldensis, commonly known as the Roggeveld featherbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tufted, grass-like foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It is often found in the Roggeveld Mountains and surrounding regions.

Trisetopsis roggeveldensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
