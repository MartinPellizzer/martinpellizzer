Trichilia spectabilis

Trichilia spectabilis, commonly known as the beautiful trichilia, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with a delicate, feathery appearance. The tree is valued for its timber, which is used in furniture and construction, as well as for its ornamental qualities.
