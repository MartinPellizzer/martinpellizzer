Thermophila obovata

Thermophila obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate thermophila, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a short, woody stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
