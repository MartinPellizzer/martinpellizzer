Toona hexandra var. pilistila

Toona hexandra var. pilistila, commonly known as the white toon, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are spherical, fibrous syconia that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in local medicine and construction.
