Tylostemon anacardioides

Tylostemon anacardioides, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the Anacardiaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Australia and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, umbrella-like heads. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed drupe that contains a single seed.
