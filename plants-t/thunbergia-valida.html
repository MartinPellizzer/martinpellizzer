Thunbergia valida

Thunbergia valida, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Thunbergia valida is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
