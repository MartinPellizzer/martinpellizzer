Thymelaea orientalis

Thymelaea orientalis, commonly known as the oriental thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs.

Thymelaea orientalis is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
