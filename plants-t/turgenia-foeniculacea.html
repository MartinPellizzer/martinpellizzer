Turgenia foeniculacea

Turgenia foeniculacea, commonly known as the African fennel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, feathery leaves and yellow flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant grows up to 1 meter tall and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flowers.
