Tarenaya Virens

Tarenaya virens is a unique flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America. It is known for its striking greenish-yellow flowers and compact growth habit. This plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. Despite its beauty, Tarenaya virens remains relatively obscure in botanical circles.
