Troxirum blandum

Troxirum blandum, commonly known as the blanda's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth or along streambanks. Its leaves are broad and slightly lobed, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
