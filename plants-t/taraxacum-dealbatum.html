Taraxacum dealbatum

Taraxacum dealbatum, commonly known as the woolly dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and a yellow flower head that develops into a white, fluffy seed sphere. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas.

Its botanical features make it easily distinguishable from other dandelion species.
