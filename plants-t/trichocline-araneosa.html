Trichocline araneosa

Trichocline araneosa, commonly known as the spider lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, spiky fruits that contain the seeds.

Trichocline araneosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
