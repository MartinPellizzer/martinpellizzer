Terpsichore kirkii

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and purple disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.
