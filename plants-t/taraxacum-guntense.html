Taraxacum guntense

Taraxacum guntense, commonly known as the Guntens dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a distinctive seed head composed of numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Taraxacum guntense is a herbaceous perennial that is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
