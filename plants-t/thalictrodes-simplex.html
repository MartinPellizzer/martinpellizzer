Thalictrodes simplex

Thalictrodes simplex, commonly known as the simple thalictroid, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Thalictridaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces a single, erect flowering stem. It is native to certain regions of Central America and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.

Thalictrodes simplex is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
