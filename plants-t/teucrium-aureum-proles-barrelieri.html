Teucrium aureum proles barrelieri

Teucrium aureum proles barrelieri, commonly known as the golden germander.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and bright yellow flowers. Native to arid regions of southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
