Teucrium aragonense var. leptophyllum

Teucrium aragonense var. leptophyllum, commonly known as the slenderleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pink or violet flowers in summer. It is part of the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic properties.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
