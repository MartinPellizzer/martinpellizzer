Thea pitardii var. lucidissima

Thea pitardii var. lucidissima, commonly known as the glossy thea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, shiny, elliptical leaves that provide a lush, dense canopy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate floral display. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The varietal form lucidissima is distinguished by its particularly glossy leaf surface, enhancing its visual appeal.
