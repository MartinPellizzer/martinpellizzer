Tamarix africana f. leptostachya

Tamarix africana f. leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stamened tamarisk, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and compact, often red or pinkish flowers that appear in dense clusters. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to poor soil conditions. Its flowering period typically occurs during the summer months, attracting various pollinators.

The species is also noted for its ability to stabilize soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
