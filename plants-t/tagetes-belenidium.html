Tagetes belenidium

Tagetes belenidium, commonly known as the Belenidium marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
