Thalictrum thalictroides f. chloranthum

Thalictrum thalictroides f. chloranthum, commonly known as the green-flowered meadow rue, is a rare and distinctive botanical variant of the meadow rue species.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-yellow flowers, which stand out from the typical white or pale yellow blooms of the species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are finely divided and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

This fern-like plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
