Tripogon unisetus

Tripogon unisetus, commonly known as the single-hair grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions.

Tripogon unisetus is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.
