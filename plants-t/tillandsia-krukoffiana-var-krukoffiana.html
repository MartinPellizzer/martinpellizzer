Tillandsia krukoffiana var. krukoffiana

Tillandsia krukoffiana var. krukoffiana, commonly known as the Krukoff's air plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant, showy flowers that can range in color from red to orange. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires minimal soil, as it absorbs water and nutrients through its leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
