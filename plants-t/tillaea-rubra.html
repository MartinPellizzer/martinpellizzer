Tillaea rubra

Tillaea rubra, commonly known as the red tillia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often blooming in late spring. Tillaea rubra thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a desirable plant for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
