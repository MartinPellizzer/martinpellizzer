Trixis megalophylla

Trixis megalophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf trixis, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological significance, it is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
