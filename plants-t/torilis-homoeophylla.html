Torilis homoeophylla

Torilis homoeophylla, commonly known as the common hedge mustard, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features alternate, pinnately lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
