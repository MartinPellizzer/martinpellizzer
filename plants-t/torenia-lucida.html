Torenia lucida

Torenia lucida, commonly known as the shining torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stem. Torenia lucida thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
