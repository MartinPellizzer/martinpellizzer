Trichera heliantha

Trichera heliantha, commonly known as the sunflower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that resemble those of the sunflower, hence its common name. The plant produces woody stems and has compound leaves with a feathery appearance. It is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Trichera heliantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
