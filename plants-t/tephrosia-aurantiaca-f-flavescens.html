Tephrosia aurantiaca f. flavescens

Tephrosia aurantiaca f. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow tephrosia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or open habitats. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.
