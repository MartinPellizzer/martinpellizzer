Tephrocactus flexispinus

Tephrocactus flexispinus, commonly known as the flexible spine cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh desert conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles along the stems.

Tephrocactus flexispinus is a slow-growing plant that requires minimal water and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
