Turraea vogelii var. congensis

Turraea vogelii var. congensis, commonly known as the Congo turraea, is a flowering plant native to Central and West Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, tropical lowland forests. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, woody fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has some traditional uses in local communities.
