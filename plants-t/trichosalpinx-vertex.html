Trichosalpinx vertex

Trichosalpinx vertex, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.
