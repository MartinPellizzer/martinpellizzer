Trichilia roraimana

Trichilia roraimana, commonly known as Roraima tallowtree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly the Amazon Basin. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
