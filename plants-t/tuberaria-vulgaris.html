Tuberaria vulgaris

Tuberaria vulgaris, commonly known as the common tuberous grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are usually pale green or brown.

Tuberaria vulgaris is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.
