Tibouchina sebastianopolitana var. miqueliana

Tibouchina sebastianopolitana var. miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's Tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
