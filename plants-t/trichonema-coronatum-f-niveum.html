Trichonema coronatum f. niveum

Trichonema coronatum f. niveum, commonly known as the snowy crown fern, is a rare and striking variant of the crown fern species.

It is characterized by its dense, white to pale green fronds that give it a snow-like appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silky texture that enhances its visual appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
