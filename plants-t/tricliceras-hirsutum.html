Tricliceras hirsutum

Tricliceras hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy triclicera, is a unique plant species characterized by its dense, hairy foliage.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated leaves with a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Tricliceras hirsutum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
