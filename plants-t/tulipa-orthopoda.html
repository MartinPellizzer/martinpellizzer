Tulipa orthopoda

Tulipa orthopoda, commonly known as the upright tulip, is a species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight stems that can reach up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tulipa orthopoda is known for its hardiness and ability to naturalize over time.
