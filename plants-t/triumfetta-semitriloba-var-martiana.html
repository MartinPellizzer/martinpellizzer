Triumfetta semitriloba var. martiana

Triumfetta semitriloba var. martiana, commonly known as the red sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

The plant has slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with a deep root system that helps it access water from deeper soil layers.
