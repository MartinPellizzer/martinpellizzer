Tephrosia polystachya var. polystachya

Tephrosia polystachya var. polystachya, commonly known as the purple tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
