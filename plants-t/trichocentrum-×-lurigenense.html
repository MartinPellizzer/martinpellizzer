Trichocentrum × lurigenense

Trichocentrum × lurigenense, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Trichocentrum luteum and Trichocentrum guttatum.

It is a epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features pendulous, racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and covered with a dense layer of trichomes, giving it a distinctive textured appearance.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
