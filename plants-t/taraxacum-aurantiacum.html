Taraxacum aurantiacum

Taraxacum aurantiacum, commonly known as the orange hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Orange hawkweed is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
