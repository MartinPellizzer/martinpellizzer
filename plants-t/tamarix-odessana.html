Tamarix odessana

Tamarix odessana, commonly known as the Odessan tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the coastal regions of the Black Sea.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and small, scale-like leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces pink to reddish flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is often found growing in sandy or saline soils, making it well-suited to coastal and semi-arid habitats.

Tamarix odessana is also known for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in coastal areas.
