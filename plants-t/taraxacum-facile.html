Taraxacum facile

Taraxacum facile, commonly known as the easy dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped basal leaves. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to various parts of the world. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is commonly found in meadows, lawns, and disturbed areas.

Taraxacum facile is known for its ability to produce a large number of seeds, contributing to its widespread distribution.
