Trichelostylis gracilis

Trichelostylis gracilis, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces a single spike of small, greenish-white flowers, which are often clustered near the base of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open forest or heathland environments.

Trichelostylis gracilis is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
