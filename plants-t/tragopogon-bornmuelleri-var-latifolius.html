Tragopogon bornmuelleri var. latifolius

Tragopogon bornmuelleri var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
