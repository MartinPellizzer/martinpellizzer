Thelymitra reflexa

Thelymitra reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are grass-like, and the flowers have a distinctive, intricate structure with a long, slender spur.

Thelymitra reflexa is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
