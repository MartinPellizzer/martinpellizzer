Trichodesma hirsutum

Trichodesma hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features opposite, hairy, ovate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
