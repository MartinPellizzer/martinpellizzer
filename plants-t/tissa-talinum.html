Tissa talinum

Tissa talinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical annual herb native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Tissa talinum is often found in disturbed soils and can become a weed in agricultural settings.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a significant plant in ecological and agricultural contexts.
