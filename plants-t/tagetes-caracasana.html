Tagetes caracasana

Tagetes caracasana, commonly known as the Mexican marigold or sometimes the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically daisy-like in appearance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Tagetes caracasana is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
