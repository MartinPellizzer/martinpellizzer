Trichosanthes laciniosa

Trichosanthes laciniosa, commonly known as the Chinese trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, orange-yellow flowers that resemble trumpets, typically blooming in summer. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and produces long, cylindrical fruits that mature to a dark green or yellowish color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Trichosanthes laciniosa is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
