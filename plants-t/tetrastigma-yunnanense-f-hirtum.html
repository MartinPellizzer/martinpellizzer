Tetrastigma yunnanense f. hirtum

Tetrastigma yunnanense f. hirtum, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed bittersweet, is a climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by bright red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.
