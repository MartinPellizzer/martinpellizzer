Turbinicarpus klinkerianus subsp. planiziei

Turbinicarpus klinkerianus subsp.

planiziei, commonly known as the flat-topped hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico. It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stems that are typically covered in white to yellow spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
