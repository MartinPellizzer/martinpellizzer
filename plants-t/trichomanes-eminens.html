Trichomanes eminens

Trichomanes eminens, commonly known as the tall maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bright green and finely divided. Trichomanes eminens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded gardens.
