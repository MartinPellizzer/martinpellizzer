Toona sureni var. celebica

Toona sureni var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes toona, is a tropical tree native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries. The plant is characterized by its alternate, simple, and usually elliptic leaves with a smooth margin. It produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife.

The tree is valued for its timber, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
