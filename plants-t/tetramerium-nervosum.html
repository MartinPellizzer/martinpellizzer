Tetramerium nervosum

Tetramerium nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-veined tetramerium, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
