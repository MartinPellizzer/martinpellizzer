Tamarix gennessarensis

Tamarix gennessarensis, commonly known as the Gennessa tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and produces pink to purple flowers in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

However, it can become invasive in some areas if not managed properly.
