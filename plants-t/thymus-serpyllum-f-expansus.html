Thymus serpyllum f. expansus

Thymus serpyllum f. expansus, commonly known as broadleaf thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features dense, spreading foliage with oval to broadly elliptic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
