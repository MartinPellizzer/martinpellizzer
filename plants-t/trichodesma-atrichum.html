Trichodesma atrichum

Trichodesma atrichum, commonly known as the woolly trichodesma, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Trichodesma atrichum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and form.
