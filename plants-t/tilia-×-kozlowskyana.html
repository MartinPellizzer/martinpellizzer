Tilia × kozlowskyana

Tilia × kozlowskyana, commonly known as the Kozlowkskyana lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color and have a rough texture. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several winged seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability and tolerance to various soil conditions.
