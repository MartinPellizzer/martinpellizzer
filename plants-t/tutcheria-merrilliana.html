Tutcheria merrilliana

Tutcheria merrilliana, commonly known as the Malaya orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its habitat.

Tutcheria merrilliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty in botanical gardens.
