Torenia ramosissima

Torenia ramosissima, commonly known as the flame flower or red torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia ramosissima is often cultivated as a garden plant for its vibrant floral display.
