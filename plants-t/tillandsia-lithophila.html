Tillandsia lithophila

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its ability to grow on rocks or trees without soil. The plant has dense, grass-like foliage that helps it retain moisture in its epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are typically small and pale, often appearing in clusters at the ends of its stems.
