Tagetes fevillei

Tagetes fevillei, commonly known as the Mexican marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

Tagetes fevillei thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and medicinal plants.

Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
