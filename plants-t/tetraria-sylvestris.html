Tetraria sylvestris

Tetraria sylvestris, commonly known as the forest tetraria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest understories. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a delicate, feathery appearance.
