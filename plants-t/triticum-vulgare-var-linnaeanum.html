Triticum vulgare var. linnaeanum

Triticum vulgare var. linnaeanum, commonly known as linnaean wheat, is a variant of common wheat native to the temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with long, narrow leaves and spike-like inflorescences.
