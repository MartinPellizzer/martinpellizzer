Tephrosia rhodesica var. polystachyoides

Tephrosia rhodesica var. polystachyoides, commonly known as Rhodesian tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
