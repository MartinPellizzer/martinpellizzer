Trichostachys zenkeri

Trichostachys zenkeri, commonly known as the giant reed, is a tall, robust grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color and can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces dense, cylindrical clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers at the top of its stalks. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found near water bodies.

Trichostachys zenkeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
