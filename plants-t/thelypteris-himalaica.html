Thelypteris himalaica

Thelypteris himalaica, commonly known as the Himalayan shield fern, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its leaves are leathery and have a serrated margin, with a deep green coloration.

Thelypteris himalaica plays a role in maintaining soil stability and provides habitat for various small organisms in its native ecosystem.
