Turnera ulmifolia var. grandiflora

Turnera ulmifolia var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for use in shady garden settings.
