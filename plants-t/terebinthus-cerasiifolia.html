Terebinthus cerasiifolia

Terebinthus cerasiifolia, commonly known as the cherry-twig terebinth, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dense, spreading crown.
