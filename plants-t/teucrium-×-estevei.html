Teucrium × estevei

Teucrium × estevei, commonly known as the hybrid germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. The plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is often found in rocky or dry habitats. It exhibits hybrid characteristics due to crossbreeding between Teucrium species, resulting in a unique combination of traits.

Teucrium × estevei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
