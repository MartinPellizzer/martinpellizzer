Tilia henryana

Tilia henryana, commonly known as Henry's lime tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it is less commonly cultivated than other members of the Tilia genus.
