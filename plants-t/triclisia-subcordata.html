Triclisia subcordata

Triclisia subcordata, commonly known as the broad-leaved triclisia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Triclisia subcordata is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
