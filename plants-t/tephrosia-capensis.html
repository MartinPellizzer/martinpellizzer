Tephrosia capensis

Tephrosia capensis, commonly known as the Cape tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Tephrosia capensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
