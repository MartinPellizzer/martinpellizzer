Trichosanthes quadricirrha

Trichosanthes quadricirrha, commonly known as the four-ribbed bitter melon, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant is characterized by its long, slender stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a distinctive, elongated, green gourd with a rough, warty surface.
