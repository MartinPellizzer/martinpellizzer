Triodia seslerioides

Triodia seslerioides, commonly known as the slender spinifex grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, wiry leaves with a distinctive blue-green coloration. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.

Triodia seslerioides plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for small fauna.
