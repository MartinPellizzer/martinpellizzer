Tabernaemontana hainanensis

Tabernaemontana hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Hainan Island in China.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a cut flower.
