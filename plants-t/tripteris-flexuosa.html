Tripteris flexuosa

Tripteris flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the understory vegetation in its native habitat.
