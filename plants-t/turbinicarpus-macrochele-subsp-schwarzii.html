Turbinicarpus macrochele subsp. schwarzii

Turbinicarpus macrochele subsp.

schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwarz's Turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to reddish in color.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat specificity, it is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts.
