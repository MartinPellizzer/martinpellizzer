Trachelospermum asiaticum var. pubescens

Trachelospermum asiaticum var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has pubescent (hairy) stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for covering trellises, fences, or walls.
