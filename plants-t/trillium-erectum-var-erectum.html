Trillium erectum var. erectum

Trillium erectum var. erectum, commonly known as purple trillium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features three elliptical, waxy, purple-tinged leaves that emerge from a single stem. The plant produces a single, showy white or pale pink flower with three petals and three sepals, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in rich, moist woodlands and is often found in shaded environments.

The species is noted for its slow growth and long lifespan, sometimes living over 50 years.
