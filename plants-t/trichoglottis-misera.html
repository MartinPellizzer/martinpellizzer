Trichoglottis misera

Trichoglottis misera, commonly known as the sad trichoglottis, is a rare orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments, and its leaves are usually dark green and leathery. Trichoglottis misera is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in lowland to montane forest regions.
