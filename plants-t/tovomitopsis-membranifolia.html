Tovomitopsis membranifolia

Tovomitopsis membranifolia, commonly known as the membranous leaf orchid, is a unique orchid species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, membranous leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
