Thuja chengii

Thuja chengii, commonly known as Chinese white cedar, is a slow-growing evergreen conifer native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves that are typically pale green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by small, woody cones. It is well-suited for ornamental use in gardens due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

Thuja chengii prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden environments.
