Trichomanes polypodioides var. incisum

Trichomanes polypodioides var. incisum, commonly known as the serrated maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family.

It is a small, delicate fern with finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The fronds are characterized by their serrated edges, which give the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
