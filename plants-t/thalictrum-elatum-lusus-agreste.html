Thalictrum elatum lusus agreste

Thalictrum elatum lusus agreste, commonly known as the showy avens, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.

Thalictrum elatum lusus agreste is also known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for early pollinators.
