Tibouchina litoralis

Tibouchina litoralis, commonly known as coastal tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

Tibouchina litoralis is well adapted to salt spray and is often used in coastal landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
