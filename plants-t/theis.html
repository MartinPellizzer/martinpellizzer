Theis

Theis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant is characterized by its showy yellow or orange flowers with white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Theis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
