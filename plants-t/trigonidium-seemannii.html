Trigonidium seemannii

Trigonidium seemannii, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Trigonidium seemannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
