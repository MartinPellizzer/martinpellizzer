Tamarix leptopetala

Tamarix leptopetala, commonly known as the silver tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It is often found in sandy soils and can thrive in saline conditions.

Tamarix leptopetala is also known for its ability to stabilize dunes and prevent soil erosion.
