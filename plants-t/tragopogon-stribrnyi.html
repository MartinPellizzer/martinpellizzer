Tragopogon stribrnyi

Tragopogon stribrnyi, commonly known as the striped goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the dandelion family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces a tall, flowering stem that bears a single composite flower head composed of yellow ray florets and yellow disk florets. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Tragopogon stribrnyi is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its distinctive appearance.
