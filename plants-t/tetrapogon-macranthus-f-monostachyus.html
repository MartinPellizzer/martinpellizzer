Tetrapogon macranthus f. monostachyus

Tetrapogon macranthus f. monostachyus, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed tetrapogon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

This plant is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
