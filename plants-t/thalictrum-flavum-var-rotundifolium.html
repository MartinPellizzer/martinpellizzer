Thalictrum flavum var. rotundifolium

Thalictrum flavum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the rounded-leaved meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves with a rounded shape, and it produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
