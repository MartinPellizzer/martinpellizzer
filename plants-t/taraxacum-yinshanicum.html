Taraxacum yinshanicum

Taraxacum yinshanicum, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, yellow flowers and a distinctive, hollow stem. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and is adapted to temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

It is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
