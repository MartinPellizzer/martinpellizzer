Tetracera sarmentosa subsp. sumatrana

Tetracera sarmentosa subsp.

sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran tetracera, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite leaves.
