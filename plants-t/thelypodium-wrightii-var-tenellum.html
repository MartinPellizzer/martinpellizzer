Thelypodium wrightii var. tenellum

Thelypodium wrightii var. tenellum, commonly known as the slender thelypodium, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.
