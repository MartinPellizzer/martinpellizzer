Tragacantha kuhistana

Tragacantha kuhistana, commonly known as Kuhistani tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its woody stem and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds.

It is valued for its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments, making it an important species in ecological restoration efforts.
