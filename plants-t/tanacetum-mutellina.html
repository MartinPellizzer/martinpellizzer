Tanacetum mutellina

Tanacetum mutellina, commonly known as the meadow button.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features finely divided, aromatic leaves and produces small, yellow to white composite flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Tanacetum mutellina is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
