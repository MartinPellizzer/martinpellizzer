Turgenia latifolia var. purpurea

Turgenia latifolia var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple turgenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking purple to lavender-colored flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are broad and sturdy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soil.

Turgenia latifolia var. purpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
