Trochera longiseta

Trochera longiseta, commonly known as the long-seta orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive floral morphology. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale purple.

Trochera longiseta is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
