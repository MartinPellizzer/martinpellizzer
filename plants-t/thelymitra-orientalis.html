Thelymitra orientalis

Thelymitra orientalis, commonly known as the eastern blue orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy, blue to violet flower. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces a single, lance-shaped leaf. It blooms during the spring and early summer, typically from September to November.

Thelymitra orientalis is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
