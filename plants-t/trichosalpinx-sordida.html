Trichosalpinx sordida

Trichosalpinx sordida, commonly known as the dirty orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark, often purplish or brownish flowers, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its flowers are relatively small and clustered, with a unique, intricate structure typical of orchids.

Trichosalpinx sordida is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
