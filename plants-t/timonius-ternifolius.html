Timonius ternifolius

Timonius ternifolius, commonly known as the three-leaved timonius, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces small, clustered white or greenish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
