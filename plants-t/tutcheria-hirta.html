Tutcheria hirta

Tutcheria hirta, commonly known as the hairy tutcheria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, contributing to its common name.

Tutcheria hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
