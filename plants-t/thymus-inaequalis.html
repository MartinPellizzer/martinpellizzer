Thymus inaequalis

Thymus inaequalis, commonly known as unequal thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tiny, aromatic leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its medicinal properties have been historically used in herbal remedies.

Thymus inaequalis is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
