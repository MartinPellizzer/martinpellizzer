Thelymitra media

Thelymitra media, commonly known as the common spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive, spider-like appearance.

Thelymitra media is known for its ability to form dense colonies and is often found in coastal and subcoastal regions.
