Trichoglottis breviracema

Trichoglottis breviracema, commonly known as the short-stemmed trichoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant features elongated, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Trichoglottis breviracema is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
