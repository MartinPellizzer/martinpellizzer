Terniopsis sesadensis

Terniopsis sesadensis, commonly known as the rare desert orchid, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink, typically during the rainy season. The plant has a short, fibrous root system adapted to survive in nutrient-poor soils. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to store water in its native habitat.
