Taraxacum decorifolium

Taraxacum decorifolium, commonly known as the showy dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, ornamental appearance. Taraxacum decorifolium is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
