Thunbergia cyanea var. cyanea

Thunbergia cyanea var. cyanea, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue to violet flowers with a white throat, blooming profusely during the day. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and has a rapid growth rate, making it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Thunbergia cyanea var. cyanea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
