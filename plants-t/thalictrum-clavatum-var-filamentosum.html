Thalictrum clavatum var. filamentosum

Thalictrum clavatum var. filamentosum, commonly known as the filamentous avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to northern regions of North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and the presence of long, silky filaments on its flowers, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variety is valued in gardens for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits to pollinators.
