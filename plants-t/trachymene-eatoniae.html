Trachymene eatoniae

Trachymene eatoniae, commonly known as the New Zealand spurge or New Zealand devil's-bit.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand, characterized by its erect stem and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream sides. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a serrated margin.

Trachymene eatoniae is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it both a valuable ground cover and a potential weed.
