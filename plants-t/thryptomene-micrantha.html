Thryptomene micrantha

Thryptomene micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered thryptomene, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in coastal and inland regions.

Thryptomene micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
