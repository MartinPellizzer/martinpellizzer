Tricyrtis formosana var. glandosa

Tricyrtis formosana var. glandosa, commonly known as the glandular Japanese spiderwort, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a greenish tinge. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species in the Asparagaceae family.
