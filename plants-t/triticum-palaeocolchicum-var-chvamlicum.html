Triticum palaeocolchicum var. chvamlicum

Triticum palaeocolchicum var. chvamlicum, commonly known as the ancient wheat variant, is a rare and ancient grass species native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its compact stature and small, spikelet-like inflorescence. The plant exhibits unique genetic traits that distinguish it from modern wheat varieties, making it of interest to researchers studying plant evolution. Due to its historical significance and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject in botanical and agricultural studies.
