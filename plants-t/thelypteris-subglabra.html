Thelypteris subglabra

Thelypteris subglabra, commonly known as the smooth holly fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Thelypteris subglabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
