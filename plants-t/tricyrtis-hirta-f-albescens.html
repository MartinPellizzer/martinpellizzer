Tricyrtis hirta f. albescens

Tricyrtis hirta f. albescens, commonly known as the white spider orchid, is a rare and striking variant of the spider orchid.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan, characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that are predominantly white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant blossoms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.
