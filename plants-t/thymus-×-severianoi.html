Thymus × severianoi

Thymus × severianoi, commonly known as Severian thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans, particularly in countries like Serbia and Bosnia. The plant features small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in clusters during the summer months.

It is often used in herbal medicine and as a culinary herb, valued for its strong, minty fragrance.
