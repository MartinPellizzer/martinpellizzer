Trichomanes adscendens

Trichomanes adscendens, commonly known as the ascending maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. Its spores are produced in small, clustered structures called sporangia, which are typically located on the underside of the fronds.

Trichomanes adscendens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
