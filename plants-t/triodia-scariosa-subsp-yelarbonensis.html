Triodia scariosa subsp. yelarbonensis

Triodia scariosa subsp.

yelarbonensis, commonly known as the Yelarbonensis spinifex, is a grass-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia. It is characterized by its dense, wiry growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including high temperatures and low rainfall. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native fauna.
