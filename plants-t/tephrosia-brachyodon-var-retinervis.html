Tephrosia brachyodon var. retinervis

Tephrosia brachyodon var. retinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its compound leaves with slender, closely spaced leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, and is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability.
