Tragia polyandra

Tragia polyandra, commonly known as the velvetleaf or false hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both moist and dry environments.
