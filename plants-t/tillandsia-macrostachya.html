Tillandsia macrostachya

Tillandsia macrostachya, commonly known as the giant tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape, allowing it to efficiently collect moisture from the air. The plant produces striking inflorescences with showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for terrariums and indoor gardening.
