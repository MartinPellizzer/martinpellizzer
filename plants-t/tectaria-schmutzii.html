Tectaria schmutzii

Tectaria schmutzii, commonly known as the Schmutz's tectaria, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and the presence of fertile fronds with sporangia on the underside. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually pinnately compound, with a distinctive texture and coloration.

Tectaria schmutzii plays a role in forest ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for microorganisms.
