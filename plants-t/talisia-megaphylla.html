Talisia megaphylla

Talisia megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leaf talisia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Talisia megaphylla is often found in lowland rainforests and is adapted to high humidity and warm temperatures.

Its unique foliage makes it a desirable ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
