Trichoglottis simplex

Trichoglottis simplex, commonly known as the simple trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has linear, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Trichoglottis simplex is noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
