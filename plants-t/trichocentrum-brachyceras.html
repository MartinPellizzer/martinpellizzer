Trichocentrum brachyceras

Trichocentrum brachyceras, commonly known as the short-stalked orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent white markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum brachyceras is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
