Tragacantha chinensis

Tragacantha chinensis, commonly known as Chinese tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces a resinous gum, which has been used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive. Its growth habit includes both shrub and tree forms, depending on environmental conditions.

The botanical features of Tragacantha chinensis make it an important species in ecological and medicinal contexts.
