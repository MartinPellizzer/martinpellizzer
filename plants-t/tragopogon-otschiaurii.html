Tragopogon otschiaurii

Tragopogon otschiaurii, commonly known as the Otschi Valley goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Tragopogon otschiaurii is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
