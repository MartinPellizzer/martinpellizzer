Thelypteris excisa

Thelypteris excisa, commonly known as the dissected shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous upright, lanceolate leaves.
