Thryptomene drummondii

Thryptomene drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's thryptomene, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. Thryptomene drummondii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance in its native habitat.
