Trigonotis clarkei

Trigonotis clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's trigonotis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after species among horticulturists and botanists.
