Tillandsia ecarinata

Tillandsia ecarinata, commonly known as the curly hair tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pendulous rosette of narrow, curling leaves. The plant thrives in humid environments and does not require soil, absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.

It produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, adding to its ornamental value.
