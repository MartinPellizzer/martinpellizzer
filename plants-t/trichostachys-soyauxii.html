Trichostachys soyauxii

Trichostachys soyauxii, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a tall, clump-forming grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, silver-blue leaves with a distinctive white stripe running down the central vein. The plant produces dense, feathery panicles of small, pale yellow flowers during the dry season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and garden design.
