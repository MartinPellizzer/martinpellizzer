Taraxacum ampliusculum

Taraxacum ampliusculum, commonly known as the giant dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to parts of North America and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces a distinctive seed head with numerous white, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Its botanical features make it easily distinguishable from other dandelion species.
