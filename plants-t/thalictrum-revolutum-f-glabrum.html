Thalictrum revolutum f. glabrum

Thalictrum revolutum f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth meadow rue, is a flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically deeply divided, giving the plant a feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in garden settings.
