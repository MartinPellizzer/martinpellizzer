Tripogon debilis

Tripogon debilis, commonly known as the slender spike grass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing close to the ground.

Tripogon debilis is typically found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands.
