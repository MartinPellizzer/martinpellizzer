Triumfetta reticulata

Triumfetta reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically appearing in summer. The plant produces small, three-lobed fruits that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.
