Tinospora reticulata

Tinospora reticulata, commonly known as Indian moonseed, is a climbing shrub belonging to the Menispermaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically unisexual. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

Tinospora reticulata is often found in moist, shaded areas and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional systems of medicine.
