Tachigali vasquezii

Tachigali vasquezii, commonly known as the Amazonian ironwood tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the dense rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Tachigali vasquezii plays a vital role in its ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
