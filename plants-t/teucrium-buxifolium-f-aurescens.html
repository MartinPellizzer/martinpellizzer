Teucrium buxifolium f. aurescens

Teucrium buxifolium f. aurescens, commonly known as the golden teucrium, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically dark green, with a distinctive golden or reddish tinge on the underside, giving it its ornamental appeal. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
