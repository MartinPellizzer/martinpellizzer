Trichocentrum × lurilindenii

Trichocentrum × lurilindenii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Trichocentrum lindbergii and Trichocentrum sanguinolentum.

It is a sympodial orchid characterized by its robust growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Trichocentrum × lurilindenii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
