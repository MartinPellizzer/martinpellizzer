Trichocentrum × lindeoerstedii

Trichocentrum × lindeoerstedii, commonly known as the Lindeoersted's orchid, is a hybrid epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous, racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has leathery, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum × lindeoerstedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a hanging plant.
