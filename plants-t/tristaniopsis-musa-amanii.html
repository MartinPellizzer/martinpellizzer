Tristaniopsis musa-amanii

Tristaniopsis musa-amanii, commonly known as the Amanii banana, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, paddle-shaped leaves and produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and floral structures.
