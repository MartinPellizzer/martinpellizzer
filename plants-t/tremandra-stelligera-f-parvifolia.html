Tremandra stelligera f. parvifolia

Tremandra stelligera f. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and meadows.
