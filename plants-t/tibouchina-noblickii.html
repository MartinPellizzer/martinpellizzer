Tibouchina noblickii

Tibouchina noblickii, commonly known as the noble tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds, contributing to its ecological significance.
