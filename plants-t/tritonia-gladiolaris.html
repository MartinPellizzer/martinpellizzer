Tritonia gladiolaris

Tritonia gladiolaris, commonly known as the swordleaf tritonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and showy, bell-like flowers. Tritonia gladiolaris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in warm climates.
