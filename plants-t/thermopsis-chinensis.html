Thermopsis chinensis

Thermopsis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Thermopsis chinensis is sometimes used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
