Tilia × wulffiana

Tilia × wulffiana, commonly known as the Wulff's linden, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically serrated along the edges. The tree produces small, fragrant yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in drooping inflorescences. Its bark is smooth and light grey, becoming fissured with age.

This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often planted in gardens for its shade and seasonal beauty.
