Tillandsia elata

Tillandsia elata, commonly known as the Crimson Vanda, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant stems and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers. Tillandsia elata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain humidity.
