Tarenna nitiduloides

Tarenna nitiduloides, commonly known as the glossy tarenna, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
