Thoracostachyum pandanophyllum var. immensum

Thoracostachyum pandanophyllum var. immensum, commonly known as the giant pandan plant, is a striking member of the Pandanaceae family native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional cooking and are known for their aromatic properties.

This variety is notable for its impressive size and unique botanical features that make it a distinctive ornamental plant.
