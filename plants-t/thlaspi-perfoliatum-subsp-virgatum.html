Thlaspi perfoliatum subsp. virgatum

Thlaspi perfoliatum subsp.

virgatum, commonly known as the silverleaf honesty, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-gray leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers.

The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in disturbed soils and rocky habitats. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
