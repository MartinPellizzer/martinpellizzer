Tetraneuris acaulis

Tetraneuris acaulis, commonly known as the barrenstem tetraneuris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Tetraneuris acaulis produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
