Tovara neofiliformis

Tovara neofiliformis, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feather-like fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are typically a vibrant green with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental value.

Tovara neofiliformis is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
