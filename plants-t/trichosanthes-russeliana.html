Trichosanthes russeliana

Trichosanthes russeliana, commonly known as the Indian bitter melon, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a vigorous climbing vine characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The fruit is typically oblong, green, and covered in a rough, warty texture. It contains a white, juicy pulp with numerous small, pale seeds.

This plant is native to Southeast Asia and is cultivated for its edible fruit, though it is often used in traditional medicine as well.
