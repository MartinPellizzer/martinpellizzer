Taenitis blumei

Taenitis blumei, commonly known as the Blume's taenitis, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant features striking, large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Taenitis blumei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
