Tragopyrum spinosum

Tragopyrum spinosum, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Goat's rue is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
