Tephrosia subsessilis

Tephrosia subsessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features small, simple leaves that are typically lanceolate or ovate in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its roots are known to contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.

Tephrosia subsessilis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
