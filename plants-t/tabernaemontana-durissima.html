Tabernaemontana durissima

Tabernaemontana durissima, commonly known as the Christmas star or star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the winter months. The plant has glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Tabernaemontana durissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant fragrance.
