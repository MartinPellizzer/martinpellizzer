Tridax tambensis

Tridax tambensis, commonly known as the tropical dandelion, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head, followed by fluffy white seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Tridax tambensis is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some areas.
