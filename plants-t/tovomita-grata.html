Tovomita grata

Tovomita grata, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Tovomita grata requires warm, humid conditions and well-draining soil to thrive.
