Tolmiea menziesii f. gemmifera

Tolmiea menziesii f. gemmifera, commonly known as the gemmiferous tolmiea, is a variant of the tolmiea plant native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

This plant is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, which bloom in late spring. The plant is known for its ability to form gemmae, which are tiny plantlets that can grow into new plants.
