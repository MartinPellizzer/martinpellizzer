Taeniophyllum papillosum

Taeniophyllum papillosum, commonly known as the papillate taeniophyllum, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy appearance with papillae on its leaves and flowers. The plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often arranged in a raceme.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
