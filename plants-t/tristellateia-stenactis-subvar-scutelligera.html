Tristellateia stenactis subvar. scutelligera

Tristellateia stenactis subvar. scutelligera, commonly known as the narrow-leafed tristellateia, is a member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, compound leaves and small, umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its subvariety, scutelligera, is distinguished by its narrower leaf blades and slightly different floral structures.

It is often found in Mediterranean climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
