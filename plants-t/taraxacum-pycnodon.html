Taraxacum pycnodon

Taraxacum pycnodon, commonly known as the densehead dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tightly packed, dark green leaves and a flowering head that produces a cluster of small, yellow flowers.
