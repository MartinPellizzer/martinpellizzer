Typhonium gagnepainii

Typhonium gagnepainii, commonly known as the Gagnepain's arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a corm that is used as a starch source in some regions. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
