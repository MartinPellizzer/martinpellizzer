Tropilis × gracillima

Tropilis × gracillima, commonly known as the hybrid garden spirea, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fluffy flower clusters.

It typically produces clusters of white or pink flowers in early summer, which are borne on long, slender pedicels. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly serrated along the edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The botanical features of Tropilis × gracillima make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
