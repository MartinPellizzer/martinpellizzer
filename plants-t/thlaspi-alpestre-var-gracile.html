Thlaspi alpestre var. gracile

Thlaspi alpestre var. gracile, commonly known as Alpine cress, is a small, herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. The plant has simple, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows, slopes, and rocky outcrops.
