Tribulus forrestii

Tribulus forrestii, commonly known as the forest tribulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Zygophyllaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its woody stems, small leaves, and yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in shrublands and rocky areas.

Tribulus forrestii is noted for its ability to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
