Tulakenia blanda

Tulakenia blanda, commonly known as the small tulip, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Tulakenia blanda thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooming period occurs in early spring, adding a touch of elegance to gardens during the cooler months.
