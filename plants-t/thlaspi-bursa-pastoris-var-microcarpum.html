Thlaspi bursa-pastoris var. microcarpum

Thlaspi bursa-pastoris var. microcarpum, commonly known as rock cress or yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial with basal leaves that are typically lobed or toothed. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in rocky or disturbed soils and is often found in temperate regions.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
