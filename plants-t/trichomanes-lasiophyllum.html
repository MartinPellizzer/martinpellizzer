Trichomanes lasiophyllum

Trichomanes lasiophyllum, commonly known as the hairy maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green with a slight pubescence. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Trichomanes lasiophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, indoor settings.
