Triumfetta tungarensis

Triumfetta tungarensis, commonly known as the African bluebottle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptability to harsh environments. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
