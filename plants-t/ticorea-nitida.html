Ticorea nitida

Ticorea nitida, commonly known as the smooth tickseed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a smooth, glossy texture.

Ticorea nitida thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
