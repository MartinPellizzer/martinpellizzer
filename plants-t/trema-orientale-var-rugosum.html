Trema orientale var. rugosum

Trema orientale var. rugosum, commonly known as the rough-leaf ironwood, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves with a rough, textured surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its woody stems and dense foliage make it a hardy, drought-resistant species.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its durability and aesthetic appeal.
