Toona sinensis var. incarvillei

Toona sinensis var. incarvillei, commonly known as Chinese toon, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and smooth, gray bark. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing flat, winged seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The species is adapted to a variety of soil types and thrives in temperate climates.
