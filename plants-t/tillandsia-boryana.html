Tillandsia boryana

Tillandsia boryana, commonly known as the Boryana air plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to South America.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant has short, dense leaves that form a rosette, which helps collect moisture from the air. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

Tillandsia boryana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its low maintenance and unique appearance.
