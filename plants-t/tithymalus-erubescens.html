Tithymalus erubescens

Tithymalus erubescens, commonly known as the red tithymalus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
