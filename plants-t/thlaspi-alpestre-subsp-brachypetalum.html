Thlaspi alpestre subsp. brachypetalum

Thlaspi alpestre subsp.

brachypetalum, commonly known as the alpine rock cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments.

The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with four petals. Its leaves are generally lobed and adapted to withstand harsh mountain conditions.

It is often found in high-altitude regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
