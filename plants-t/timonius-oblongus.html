Timonius oblongus

Timonius oblongus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved timonius, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 60 centimeters in height. Its leaves are elongated, narrow, and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
