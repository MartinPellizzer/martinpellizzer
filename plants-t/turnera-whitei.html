Turnera whitei

Turnera whitei, commonly known as the white turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Passifloraceae family, which includes passionflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.
