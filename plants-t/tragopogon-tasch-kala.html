Tragopogon tasch-kala

Tragopogon tasch-kala, commonly known as the Tasch-Kala goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a cluster of small flowers. The plant features slender, hairy stems and produces yellowish to reddish composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Tragopogon tasch-kala is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
