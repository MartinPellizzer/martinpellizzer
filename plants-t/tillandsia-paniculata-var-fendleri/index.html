Tillandsia Paniculata Var. Fendleri

Tillandsia paniculata var. fendleri is a captivating air plant known for its unique appearance and ease of care. Native to the southern United States and northern Mexico, this species thrives in humid environments with bright indirect light. Its rosette-shaped leaves and striking silver-blue coloration make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor displays. Unlike traditional plants, it absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves rather than roots. This hardy variety is perfect for beginners and experienced growers alike.
