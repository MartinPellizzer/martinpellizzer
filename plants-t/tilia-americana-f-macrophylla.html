Tilia americana f. macrophylla

Tilia americana f. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf linden, is a cultivar of the American linden tree.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 12 centimeters in length. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It is a deciduous tree that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
