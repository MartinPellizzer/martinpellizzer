Tephrosia muenzneri subsp. muenzneri

Tephrosia muenzneri subsp.

muenzneri, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant produces pods containing seeds, and it is often found in grassland and woodland habitats. It has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
