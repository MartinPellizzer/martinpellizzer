Thymus × segurae

Thymus × segurae, commonly known as Segura's thyme, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Thymus vulgaris and Thymus zygis.

It is a low-growing shrub with aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
