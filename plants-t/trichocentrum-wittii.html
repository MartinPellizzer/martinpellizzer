Trichocentrum wittii

Trichocentrum wittii, commonly known as the Witt's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, adapted for efficient photosynthesis in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum wittii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
