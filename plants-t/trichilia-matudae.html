Trichilia matudae

Trichilia matudae, commonly known as Matudas's trichilia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves with a glossy upper surface. Trichilia matudae is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
