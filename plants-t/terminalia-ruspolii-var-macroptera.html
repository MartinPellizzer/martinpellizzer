Terminalia ruspolii var. macroptera

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and broad, ovate leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds.
