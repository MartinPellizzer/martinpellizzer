Tectaria martinicensis var. pittieri

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically on rock surfaces or in humid forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
