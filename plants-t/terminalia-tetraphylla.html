Terminalia tetraphylla

Terminalia tetraphylla, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

Terminalia tetraphylla is often valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
