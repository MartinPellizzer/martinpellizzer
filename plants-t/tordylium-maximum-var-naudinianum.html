Tordylium maximum var. naudinianum

Tordylium maximum var. naudinianum, commonly known as the Naudin's giant lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow throat, which bloom in clusters. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
