Turibana Oxyphylla

Turban snail, scientifically known as Turibana oxyphylla, is a unique and fascinating species of sea snail found in specific marine environments. This species belongs to the family Turbinidae and is characterized by its distinctive spiral shell shape and vibrant coloration. It plays an important role in its ecosystem by contributing to the balance of marine biodiversity. Despite its beauty, the Turban snail faces threats from habitat degradation and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
