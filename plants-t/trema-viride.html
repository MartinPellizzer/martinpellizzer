Trema viride

Trema viride, commonly known as the green trema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are usually green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Trema viride is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
