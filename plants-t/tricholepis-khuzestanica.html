Tricholepis khuzestanica

Tricholepis khuzestanica, commonly known as the Khuzestan thistle, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous regions of southwestern Iran.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
