Thunbergia lathyroides

Thunbergia lathyroides, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often grown as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia lathyroides is a climbing vine that can reach lengths of several meters.
