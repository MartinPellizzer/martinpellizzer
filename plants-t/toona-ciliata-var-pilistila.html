Toona ciliata var. pilistila

Toona ciliata var. pilistila, commonly known as the silky toona, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

The leaves and fruits have been traditionally used in herbal remedies for their purported health benefits.
