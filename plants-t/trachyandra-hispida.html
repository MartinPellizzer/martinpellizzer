Trachyandra hispida

Trachyandra hispida, commonly known as the woolly trachyandra, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen perennial with a compact rosette of narrow, leathery leaves that are densely covered in white, woolly hairs. Trachyandra hispida is often grown as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
