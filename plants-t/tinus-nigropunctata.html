Tinus nigropunctata

Tinus nigropunctata, commonly known as the black-spotted tinus, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South Africa.

Tinus nigropunctata is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to well-drained soils.
