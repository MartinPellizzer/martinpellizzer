Tephrosia lathyroides

Tephrosia lathyroides, commonly known as the small-leaved tephrosia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with trifoliate leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
