Trifolium kitaibelianum

Trifolium kitaibelianum, commonly known as the Kitaibel's clover, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and early summer. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Trifolium kitaibelianum is a perennial plant that thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
