Trichodesma dhofaricum

Trichodesma dhofaricum, commonly known as the Dhofar trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to the southern Arabian Peninsula.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers. It is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall, making it resilient in harsh desert conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in arid regions.
