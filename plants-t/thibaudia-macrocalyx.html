Thibaudia macrocalyx

Thibaudia macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered thibaudia, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Thibaudia macrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
