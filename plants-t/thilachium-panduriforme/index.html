Thilachium Panduriforme

Panduriform flame vine, scientifically known as Thilachium panduriforme, is a strikingly beautiful and rare plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. With its vibrant, flame-like flowers that range from orange to deep red, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This climbing vine is notable for its unique leaf structure, which resembles the shape of a pandur, a traditional musical instrument. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are now underway to protect and propagate this enchanting species for future generations.
