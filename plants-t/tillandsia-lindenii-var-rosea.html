Tillandsia lindenii var. rosea

Tillandsia lindenii var. rosea, commonly known as the Roseate Air Plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in humid, shaded environments. The plant features rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces showy, pinkish-red inflorescences with small, bell-shaped flowers, adding ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
