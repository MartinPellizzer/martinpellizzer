Trichoscypha turbinata

Trichoscypha turbinata, commonly known as the African turban tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, turban-shaped seed pods. The tree has smooth, greyish bark and palmate leaves that are often lobed. It produces small, yellow flowers followed by the unique, spirally twisted capsules.

Trichoscypha turbinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
