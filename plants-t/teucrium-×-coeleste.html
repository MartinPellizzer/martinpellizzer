Teucrium × coeleste

Teucrium × coeleste, commonly known as blue germander, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and long blooming period contribute to its ornamental value in gardens.
