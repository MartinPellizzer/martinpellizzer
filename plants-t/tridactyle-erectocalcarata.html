Tridactyle erectocalcarata

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive calcarate structures, which are calcified projections on the seed pods. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations in tropical environments.
