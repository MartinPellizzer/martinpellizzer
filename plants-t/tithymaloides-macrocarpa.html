Tithymaloides macrocarpa

Tithymaloides macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant tithymal, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a woody stem and can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
