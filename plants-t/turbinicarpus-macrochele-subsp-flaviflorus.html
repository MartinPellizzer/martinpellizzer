Turbinicarpus macrochele subsp. flaviflorus

Turbinicarpus macrochele subsp.

flaviflorus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered turbinicarpus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico. It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and a distinctive yellowish-green color.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
