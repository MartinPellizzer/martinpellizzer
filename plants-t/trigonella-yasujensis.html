Trigonella yasujensis

Trigonella yasujensis, commonly known as Yasujan fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, typical of the genus Trigonella. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features.

Its botanical structure reflects its role as a potential medicinal and nutritional resource in its native region.
