Tropaeolum rectangulum

Tropaeolum rectangulum, commonly known as the squareleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive square-shaped leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. Its stems are hairy and can climb or trail depending on the growing conditions.

Tropaeolum rectangulum is also valued for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.
