Tacsonia × smythiana

Tacsonia × smythiana, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose.

It is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant is a hybrid between Tacsonia and Rosa smythiana, resulting in a variety with enhanced blooming and disease resistance. It typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty and long-lasting nature.
