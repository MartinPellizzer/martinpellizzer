Tristellateia bojeriana subsp. boinensis

Tristellateia bojeriana subsp.

boinensis, commonly known as the Bojer's tristellateia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences.
