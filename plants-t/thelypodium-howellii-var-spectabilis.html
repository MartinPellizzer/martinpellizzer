Thelypodium howellii var. spectabilis

Thelypodium howellii var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy thelypodium, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy inflorescences consist of bright yellow ray flowers surrounding disc flowers, making it visually striking.

It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
