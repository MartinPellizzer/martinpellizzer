Tephrosia barbatala var. barbatala

Tephrosia barbatala var. barbatala, commonly known as Indian milkweed, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves with alternate arrangement, typically consisting of 5 to 9 elliptical leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its root system is fibrous, and it often grows in disturbed soils, making it adaptable to various environments.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
