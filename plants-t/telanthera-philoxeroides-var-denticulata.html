Telanthera philoxeroides var. denticulata

Telanthera philoxeroides var. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate flame flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
