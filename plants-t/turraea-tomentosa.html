Turraea tomentosa

Turraea tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly turraea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its hairy, grayish foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several elliptical seeds.

Turraea tomentosa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
