Tigridia bicolor

Tigridia bicolor, commonly known as the pink tiger iris, is a striking perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, with a distinctive dark purple or black stripe down the center. The plant produces sword-like, grassy leaves that are typically bluish-green in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tigridia bicolor is known for its long blooming period, often extending from early summer to late fall.
