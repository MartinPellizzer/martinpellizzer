Tupistra clarkei

Tupistra clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's rock rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Tupistra clarkei is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant in dry landscapes.
