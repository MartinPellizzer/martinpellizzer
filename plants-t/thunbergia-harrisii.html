Thunbergia harrisii

Thunbergia harrisii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using its tendrils. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia harrisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
