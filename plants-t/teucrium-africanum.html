Teucrium africanum

Teucrium africanum, commonly known as African germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in spikes at the ends of the branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Teucrium africanum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
