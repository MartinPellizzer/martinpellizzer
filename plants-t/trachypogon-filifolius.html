Trachypogon filifolius

Trachypogon filifolius, commonly known as the silky grass or filifolius grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silky hairs that cover the leaves and inflorescence. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands.

Trachypogon filifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
