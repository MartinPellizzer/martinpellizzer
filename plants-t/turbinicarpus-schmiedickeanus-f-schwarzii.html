Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus f. schwarzii

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus f. schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwarz's Turbinicarpus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a dense cluster of ribbed, cylindrical stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange, often appearing in late summer. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent collectors.
