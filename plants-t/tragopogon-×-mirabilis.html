Tragopogon × mirabilis

Tragopogon × mirabilis, commonly known as the goat's beard, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence and yellow-orange ray flowers that resemble a beard. The plant typically grows in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils across North America. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a bluish-green hue with hairy surfaces.

Tragopogon × mirabilis is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
