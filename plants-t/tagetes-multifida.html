Tagetes multifida

Tagetes multifida, commonly known as the Mexican marigold or rugosa marigold, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery foliage and vibrant, orange-yellow blooms. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its strong, aromatic scent. Tagetes multifida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to repel certain pests due to its volatile compounds.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
