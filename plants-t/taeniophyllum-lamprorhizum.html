Taeniophyllum lamprorhizum

Taeniophyllum lamprorhizum, commonly known as the golden root or yellow root, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a striking contrast between the petals and the foliage. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouse cultivation.
