Teucrium lancifolium

Teucrium lancifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties have made it a valued plant in traditional herbal medicine.
