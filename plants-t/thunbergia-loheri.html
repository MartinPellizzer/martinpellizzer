Thunbergia loheri

Thunbergia loheri, commonly known as the blue trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Thunbergia loheri is a fast-growing vine that can reach lengths of several meters, making it suitable for trellises or as a climbing plant.
