Terminalia nipensis

Terminalia nipensis, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and clusters of white, fleshy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed.
