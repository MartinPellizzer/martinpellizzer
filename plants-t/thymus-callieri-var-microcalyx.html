Thymus callieri var. microcalyx

Thymus callieri var. microcalyx, commonly known as the dwarf thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, compact foliage with small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces tiny, pinkish-white flowers in spring, which are clustered in compact inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in both gardens and herbal gardens.
