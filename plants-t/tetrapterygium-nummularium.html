Tetrapterygium nummularium

Tetrapterygium nummularium, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features small, round, silver-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
