Telis sibthorpii

Telis sibthorpii, commonly known as the Sibthorp's telis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in a cluster.

Telis sibthorpii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
