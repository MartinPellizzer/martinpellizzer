Triplophyllum fraternum var. elongatum

Triplophyllum fraternum var. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated brother's plant, is a unique variant of the Triplophyllum genus.

It is characterized by its elongated leaves and distinctive growth pattern, which sets it apart from other varieties. The plant exhibits a moderate growth rate and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight. Botanically, it is notable for its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers.

This variant is often studied for its potential medicinal properties and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
