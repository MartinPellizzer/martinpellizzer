Typhonium reflexum

Typhonium reflexum, commonly known as the giant swamp taro, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that are typically followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in wet, swampy environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered an invasive species in some regions outside its native range.
