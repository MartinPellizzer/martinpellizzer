Tilia × keszthelyensis

Tilia × keszthelyensis, commonly known as the Keszthely linden, is a hybrid species of the linden tree, native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically medium to large in size, with a serrated margin. The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with a tendency to develop shallow fissures as it matures.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
