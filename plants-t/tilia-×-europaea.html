Tilia × europaea

Tilia × europaea, commonly known as the smallleaf linden, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to Europe.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces fragrant, white flowers in early summer, which are clustered in umbels. Its bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.

The wood is soft and lightweight, making it valuable for carving and furniture.
