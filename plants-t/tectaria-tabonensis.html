Tectaria tabonensis

Tectaria tabonensis, commonly known as the Philippine shield fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually large, with a leathery texture and a broad, undulating margin.

Tectaria tabonensis plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
