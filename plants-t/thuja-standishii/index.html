Thuja Standishii

Thuja standishii is a rare and ancient conifer native to Japan, known for its unique appearance and historical significance. This evergreen tree features a compact, pyramidal shape with scale-like leaves that give it a distinctive look. It is often found in mountainous regions where it thrives in cool, moist climates. Due to its limited distribution and slow growth, it is considered a valuable and endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable plant for future generations.
