Turnera longipes

Turnera longipes, commonly known as the longipes turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are often clustered at the base of the leaves and bloom during the warmer months.
