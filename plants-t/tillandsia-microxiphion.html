Tillandsia microxiphion

Tillandsia microxiphion, commonly known as the small sword fern, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped arrangement of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air.

Tillandsia microxiphion is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
