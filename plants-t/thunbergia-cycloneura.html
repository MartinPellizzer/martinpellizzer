Thunbergia cycloneura

Thunbergia cycloneura, commonly known as the cycloneura morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.

Thunbergia cycloneura blooms throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to its surroundings.
