Tinus densa

Tinus densa, commonly known as the dense tinus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in dense clusters along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Tinus densa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
