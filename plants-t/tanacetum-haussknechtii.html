Tanacetum haussknechtii

Tanacetum haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's tansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic properties. The plant features finely divided, silvery-green leaves and yellowish flower heads that bloom in summer. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
