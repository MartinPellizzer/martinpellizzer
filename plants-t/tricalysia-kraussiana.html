Tricalysia kraussiana

Tricalysia kraussiana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a dramatic visual impact. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
