Tonsella angulosa

Tonsella angulosa, commonly known as the angular tonsella, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, clustered in small inflorescences.

Tonsella angulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
