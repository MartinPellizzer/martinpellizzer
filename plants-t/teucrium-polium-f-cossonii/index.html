Teucrium Polium F. Cossonii

Cosson's teucrium, scientifically known as Teucrium polium f. cossonii, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the common teucrium species. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its striking blue-violet flowers and aromatic leaves make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in horticultural circles. Understanding and preserving this plant is essential for maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
