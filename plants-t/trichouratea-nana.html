Trichouratea nana

Trichouratea nana, commonly known as the dwarf trichouratea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with slender, erect stems and small, opposite leaves. The leaves are usually elliptical or ovate in shape and have a smooth, waxy surface. It produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
