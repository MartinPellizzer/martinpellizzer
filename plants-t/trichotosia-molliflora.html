Trichotosia molliflora

Trichotosia molliflora, commonly known as the soft-flowered trichotosia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, white to pale yellow flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences, blooming in the spring.

Trichotosia molliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
