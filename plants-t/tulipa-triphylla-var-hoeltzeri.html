Tulipa triphylla var. hoeltzeri

Tulipa triphylla var. hoeltzeri, commonly known as the Hoeltzeri tulip, is a rare and striking variety of the tulip genus.

It is characterized by its unique three-lobed petals, which give it a distinctive appearance compared to typical tulips. Native to Turkey, this plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. The flowers are typically a deep, rich red with a contrasting black eye, adding dramatic visual interest to gardens.

Due to its rarity and specific cultivation needs, it is often sought after by horticultural enthusiasts.
