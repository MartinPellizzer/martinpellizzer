Trichilia lanata

Trichilia lanata, commonly known as the woolly trichilia, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and palmate leaves with a soft, hairy texture.
