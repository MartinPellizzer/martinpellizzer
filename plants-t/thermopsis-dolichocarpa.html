Thermopsis dolichocarpa

Thermopsis dolichocarpa, commonly known as yellow coneflower, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in soft, gray-green foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, arranged in dense, cone-shaped clusters. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to dry, rocky soils.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
