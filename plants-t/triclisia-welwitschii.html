Triclisia welwitschii

Triclisia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschia tree, is a unique plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is a member of the welwitschiaceae family and is characterized by its two large, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant has a thick, woody trunk and is adapted to survive in extremely arid conditions.

Triclisia welwitschii is also notable for its ability to store water in its trunk and leaves, allowing it to thrive in its harsh environment.
