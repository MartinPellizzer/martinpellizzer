Trachydium daucoides

Trachydium daucoides, commonly known as the slender carrot or narrow-leaved caraway, is a member of the Apiaceae family.

It is a biennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features finely divided, feather-like leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels. Its seeds are small and often used in traditional medicine.

Trachydium daucoides is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.
