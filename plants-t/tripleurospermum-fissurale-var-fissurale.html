Tripleurospermum fissurale var. fissurale

Tripleurospermum fissurale var. fissurale, commonly known as the narrowleaf mouseear, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and basal rosette growth habit. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.
