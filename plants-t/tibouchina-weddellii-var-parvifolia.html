Tibouchina weddellii var. parvifolia

Tibouchina weddellii var. parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf hibiscus, is a compact shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval-shaped and slightly hairy. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink and purple, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.
