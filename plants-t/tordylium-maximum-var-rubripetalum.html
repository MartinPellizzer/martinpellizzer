Tordylium maximum var. rubripetalum

Tordylium maximum var. rubripetalum, commonly known as the red-petaled tordylium, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a deep red color with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a velvety texture. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration make it a sought-after ornamental plant in botanical gardens and private collections.
