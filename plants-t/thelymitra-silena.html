Thelymitra silena

Thelymitra silena, commonly known as the pink fairy lantern, is an epiphytic orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Thelymitra silena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
