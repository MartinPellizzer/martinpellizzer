Turritis propera

Turritis propera, commonly known as the turk's cap lily, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, turban-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Turritis propera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
