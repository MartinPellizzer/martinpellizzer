Tectaria mindanaensis

Tectaria mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered with a dense layer of brownish, velvety scales.

Tectaria mindanaensis plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat by contributing to soil stability and providing microhabitats for various organisms.
