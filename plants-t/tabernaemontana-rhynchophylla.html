Tabernaemontana rhynchophylla

Tabernaemontana rhynchophylla, commonly known as the pink star jasmine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
