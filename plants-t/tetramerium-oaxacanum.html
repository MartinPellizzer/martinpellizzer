Tetramerium oaxacanum

Tetramerium oaxacanum, commonly known as the Oaxacan tetramerium, is a shrub native to the Mexican state of Oaxaca.

It belongs to the family Rhizophoraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
