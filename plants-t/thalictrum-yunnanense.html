Thalictrum yunnanense

Thalictrum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows, where it thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are finely divided and arranged in a whorled pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Thalictrum yunnanense is valued for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
