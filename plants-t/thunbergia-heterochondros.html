Thunbergia heterochondros

Thunbergia heterochondros, commonly known as the blue trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia heterochondros is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in greenhouses.
