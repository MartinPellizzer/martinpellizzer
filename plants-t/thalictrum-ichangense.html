Thalictrum ichangense

Thalictrum ichangense, commonly known as Chinese meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Thalictrum ichangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
