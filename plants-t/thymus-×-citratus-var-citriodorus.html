Thymus × citratus var. citriodorus

Thymus × citratus var. citriodorus, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, small leaves that emit a distinct lemon scent when crushed. The plant produces tiny, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its culinary and medicinal uses are valued for its essential oils and antiseptic properties.
