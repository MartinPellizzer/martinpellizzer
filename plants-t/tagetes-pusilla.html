Tagetes pusilla

Tagetes pusilla, commonly known as dwarf marigold, is a small flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow or orange flowers. The plant produces small, elongated leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Tagetes pusilla is also noted for its ability to repel certain insects, contributing to its use in companion planting.
