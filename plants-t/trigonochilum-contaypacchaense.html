Trigonochilum contaypacchaense

Trigonochilum contaypacchaense, commonly known as the Contaypaccha orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the high-altitude regions of Peru.

The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, thriving in cool and humid conditions.
