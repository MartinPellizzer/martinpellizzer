Trichomanes aculeatum

Trichomanes aculeatum, commonly known as the spiked holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are often covered in tiny, hair-like structures. Trichomanes aculeatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
