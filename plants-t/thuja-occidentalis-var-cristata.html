Thuja occidentalis var. cristata

Thuja occidentalis var. cristata, commonly known as the Christmas tree thuja or curly top thuja, is a cultivar of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its unique, flattened, fan-like growth habit with tightly curled foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cones and is typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This ornamental variety is often used in landscaping for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature.
