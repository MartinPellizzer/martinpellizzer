Thalictrum capillipes

Thalictrum capillipes, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its stems are slender and often hairless, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Thalictrum capillipes is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and early spring bloom.
