Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. schwarzii

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwarz's turbinicarpus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical or cylindrical stem that is often covered in a dense layer of white to yellowish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is prized by collectors for its unique appearance and hardiness in dry conditions.
