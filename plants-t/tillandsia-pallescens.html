Tillandsia pallescens

Tillandsia pallescens, commonly known as the silver tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture from the air. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in the spring, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
