Turpinia pubescens

Turpinia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy turpinia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oleaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. Turpinia pubescens is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
