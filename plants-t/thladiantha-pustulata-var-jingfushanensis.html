Thladiantha pustulata var. jingfushanensis

Thladiantha pustulata var. jingfushanensis, commonly known as the Jingfu Mountain goji berry, is a variant of the goji plant native to China.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant orange-red berries that mature in late summer. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
