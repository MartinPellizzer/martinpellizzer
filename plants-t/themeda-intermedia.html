Themeda intermedia

Themeda intermedia, commonly known as spear grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy habitats, including woodlands and open plains. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Themeda intermedia is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
