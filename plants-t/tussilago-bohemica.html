Tussilago bohemica

Tussilago bohemica, commonly known as the Bohemian coltsfoot, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellowish-white flowers. The plant is often found in moist, disturbed soils along riverbanks and meadows. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Tussilago bohemica is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
