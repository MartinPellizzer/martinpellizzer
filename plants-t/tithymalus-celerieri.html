Tithymalus celerieri

Tithymalus celerieri, commonly known as the quick-growing tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and hardiness.

Tithymalus celerieri is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
