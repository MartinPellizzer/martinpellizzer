Turbinicarpus pseudomacrochele var. lausseri

Turbinicarpus pseudomacrochele var. lausseri, commonly known as the Lausser's turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that is often covered in white or yellowish tubercles. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is prized by collectors for its unique appearance and slow growth rate.
