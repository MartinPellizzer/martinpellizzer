Tragopogon gracilis

Tragopogon gracilis, commonly known as slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Tragopogon gracilis is often found in open, disturbed habitats like meadows and roadsides.
