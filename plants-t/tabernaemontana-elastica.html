Tabernaemontana elastica

Tabernaemontana elastica, commonly known as the rubber tree or elastic tabernaemontana, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is known for its elastic latex sap, which has been historically used in the production of rubber. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.

Tabernaemontana elastica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and pleasant fragrance.
