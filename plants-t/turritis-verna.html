Turritis verna

Turritis verna, commonly known as spring turricula, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bulbous structure. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, appearing in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from the base of the plant.
