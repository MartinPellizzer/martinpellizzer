Tithymalopsis olivacea

Tithymalopsis olivacea, commonly known as the olive thyme or olive-leaved tithymalopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic properties make it both ornamental and useful in traditional herbal remedies.
