Trichosalpinx berlineri

Trichosalpinx berlineri, commonly known as the Berliner's orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow lip, which are typically arranged in a raceme. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Trichosalpinx berlineri is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific environmental requirements.
