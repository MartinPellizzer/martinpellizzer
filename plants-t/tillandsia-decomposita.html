Tillandsia decomposita

Tillandsia decomposita, commonly known as the decomposed tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Tillandsia decomposita is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky areas.
