Trymatococcus turbinatus

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. The plant has a thallus that is dorsiventral, with a distinct upper and lower surface. Its leaves are arranged in a spiral and are often curved or twisted, giving it a unique appearance.
