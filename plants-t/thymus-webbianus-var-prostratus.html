Thymus webbianus var. prostratus

Thymus webbianus var. prostratus, commonly known as prostrate thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features tiny, aromatic, gray-green leaves that are densely packed along its prostrate stems. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact form and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental and medicinal use.
