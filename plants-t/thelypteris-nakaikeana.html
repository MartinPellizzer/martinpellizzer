Thelypteris nakaikeana

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. Thelypteris nakaikeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
