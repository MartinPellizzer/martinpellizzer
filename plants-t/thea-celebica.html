Thea celebica

Thea celebica, commonly known as the Celebes tea plant, is a shrub native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces a unique type of tea that is often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves contain various bioactive compounds, including caffeine and theobromine, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Thea celebica thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
