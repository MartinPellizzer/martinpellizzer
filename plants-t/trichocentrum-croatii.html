Trichocentrum croatii

Trichocentrum croatii, commonly known as the Croatian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial or epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Trichocentrum croatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
