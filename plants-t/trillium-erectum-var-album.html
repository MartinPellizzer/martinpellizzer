Trillium erectum var. album

Trillium erectum var. album, commonly known as the white trillium, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features a single, showy white flower with three petals and three sepals, which are typically similar in size and shape. The plant has three broad, waxy leaves that are arranged in a whorl at the base of the stem. It thrives in rich, moist woodlands and is often found in shaded environments.

The white trillium is a symbol of spring and is known for its elegant, delicate appearance.
