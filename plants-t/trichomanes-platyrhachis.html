Trichomanes platyrhachis

Trichomanes platyrhachis, commonly known as the broad-ridge huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive rhizome structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually arranged in a spiral pattern and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Trichomanes platyrhachis is notable for its primitive reproductive structures and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
