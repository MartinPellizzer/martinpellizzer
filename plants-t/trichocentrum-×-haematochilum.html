Trichocentrum × haematochilum

Trichocentrum × haematochilum, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a hybrid epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple stems, which contrast with its dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
