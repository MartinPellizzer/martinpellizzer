Thalictrum hayatanum

Thalictrum hayatanum, commonly known as the Hayata's meadow rue, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

Thalictrum hayatanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
