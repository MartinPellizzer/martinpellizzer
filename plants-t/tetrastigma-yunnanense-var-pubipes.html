Tetrastigma yunnanense var. pubipes

Tetrastigma yunnanense var. pubipes, commonly known as the hairy wisteria, is a climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.
