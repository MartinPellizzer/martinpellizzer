Tilia mellita

Tilia mellita, commonly known as small-leaf lime, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are smaller than those of other lime species. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which appear before the leaves in early spring.
