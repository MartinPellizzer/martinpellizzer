Tristemma cornifolium

Tristemma cornifolium, commonly known as the horned leaf tristemma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, horn-like leaf structures that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Tristemma cornifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
