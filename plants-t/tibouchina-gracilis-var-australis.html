Tibouchina gracilis var. australis

Tibouchina gracilis var. australis, commonly known as the Australian flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
