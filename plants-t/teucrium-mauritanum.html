Teucrium mauritanum

Teucrium mauritanum, commonly known as hardy germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, with a slightly rough texture.

Teucrium mauritanum is known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
