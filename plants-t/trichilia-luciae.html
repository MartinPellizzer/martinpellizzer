Trichilia luciae

Trichilia luciae, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in dense inflorescences, and it produces woody, capsule-like fruits.
