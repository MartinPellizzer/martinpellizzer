Trichocaulon simile

Trichocaulon simile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces a single, slender stem with a few leaves and blooms during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
