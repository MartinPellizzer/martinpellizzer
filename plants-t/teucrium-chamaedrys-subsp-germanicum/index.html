Teucrium Chamaedrys Subsp. Germanicum

Teucrium chamaedrys subsp. germanicum is a hardy perennial plant native to central and eastern europe. it thrives in rocky and dry environments, often growing on slopes and cliffs. known for its fragrant, blue to purple flowers, it adds a striking visual to gardens and natural landscapes. this subspecies is closely related to the common germander and shares similar characteristics. it is valued for its medicinal properties and ability to attract pollinators.
