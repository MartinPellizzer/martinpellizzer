Tillandsia grandispica

Tillandsia grandispica, commonly known as the grandispica air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features long, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape, which helps it collect moisture from the air. It produces striking inflorescences with bright orange or red flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
