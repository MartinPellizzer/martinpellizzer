Tabernaemontana angulata

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes plants with milky sap and often showy flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive angular shape, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically clustered in umbels and display a range of colors from white to pink.
