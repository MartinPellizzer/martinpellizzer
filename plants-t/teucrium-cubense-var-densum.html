Teucrium cubense var. densum

Teucrium cubense var. densum, commonly known as Cuban teucrium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caribbean region.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky crevices. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture and produces small, clustered blue or violet flowers. Its flowering period usually occurs during the spring and early summer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
