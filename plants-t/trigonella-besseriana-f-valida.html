Trigonella besseriana f. valida

Trigonella besseriana f. valida, commonly known as the Persian clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, including parts of Turkey and the Mediterranean. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
