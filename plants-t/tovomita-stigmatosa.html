Tovomita stigmatosa

Tovomita stigmatosa, commonly known as the African butterbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, fibrous pods and are used in traditional medicine.

Tovomita stigmatosa is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, contributing to soil fertility.
