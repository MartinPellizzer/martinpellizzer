Tournefortia carnosa

Tournefortia carnosa, commonly known as the hairy tamarind, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, hairy leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
