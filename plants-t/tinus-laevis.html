Tinus laevis

Tinus laevis, commonly known as the smooth tinus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Tinus laevis is typically found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
