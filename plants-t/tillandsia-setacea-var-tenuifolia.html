Tillandsia setacea var. tenuifolia

Tillandsia setacea var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

This variety is popular among succulent and air plant enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
