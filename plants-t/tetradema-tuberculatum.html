Tetradema tuberculatum

Tetradema tuberculatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to thrive. It is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Tetradema tuberculatum is also known for its ability to bloom year-round under optimal conditions.
