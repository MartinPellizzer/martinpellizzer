Thelypodium integrifolium subsp. integrifolium

Thelypodium integrifolium subsp.

integrifolium, commonly known as the smoothleaf thelypodium, is a perennial herb native to North America. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and have smooth margins. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white.

The subspecies is characterized by its entire leaves and compact growth habit.
