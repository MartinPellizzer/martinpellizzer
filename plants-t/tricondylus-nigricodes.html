Tricondylus nigricodes

Tricondylus nigricodes, commonly known as the black-coded tricondylus, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking dark green foliage and clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often with a slightly hairy texture.
