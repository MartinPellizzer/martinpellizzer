Thunbergia javanica var. scabrida

Thunbergia javanica var. scabrida, commonly known as the scabrous morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, slightly hairy leaves and produces trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardens and hanging baskets.
