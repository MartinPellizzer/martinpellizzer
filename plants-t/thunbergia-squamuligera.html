Thunbergia squamuligera

Thunbergia squamuligera, commonly known as the velvet flower or African bell vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a soft texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia squamuligera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
