Trichopilia galeottiana

Trichopilia galeottiana, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous growth habit.

Trichopilia galeottiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
