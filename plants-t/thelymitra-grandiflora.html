Thelymitra grandiflora

Thelymitra grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a compact, hairy rhizome and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and have a spurred lip that is characteristic of the Orchidaceae family.

Thelymitra grandiflora is known for its resilience and is often found in areas with high humidity and nutrient-rich soil.
