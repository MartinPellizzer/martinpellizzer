Taraxacum koidzumii

Taraxacum koidzumii, commonly known as the Koidzumi dandelion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and lobed leaves. The plant produces a hollow stem with a milky sap, typical of the genus Taraxacum. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
