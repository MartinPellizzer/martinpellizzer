Tectaria kwangtungensis

Tectaria kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually large and have a distinctive, segmented appearance.

Tectaria kwangtungensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
