Tylophora blumei

Tylophora blumei, commonly known as the Chinese trumpet vine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.
