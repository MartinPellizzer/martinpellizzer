Tritoniopsis unguicularis

Tritoniopsis unguicularis, commonly known as the horned violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spurred petals that resemble horns, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Tritoniopsis unguicularis produces small, purple or violet flowers that bloom in early spring.
