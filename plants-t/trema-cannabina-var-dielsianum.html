Trema cannabina var. dielsianum

Trema cannabina var. dielsianum, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, green, heart-shaped leaves with fine hairs on both surfaces. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is noted for its potential medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
