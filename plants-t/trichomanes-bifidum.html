Trichomanes bifidum

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and are clustered in small groups.

Trichomanes bifidum is notable for its intricate leaf morphology and its role in humid forest ecosystems.
