Triptilion spinosum

Triptilion spinosum, commonly known as the spiny triptilion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features wiry, spiny stems that are often covered in fine, bristly hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Triptilion spinosum is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
