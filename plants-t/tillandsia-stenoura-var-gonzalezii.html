Tillandsia stenoura var. gonzalezii

Tillandsia stenoura var. gonzalezii, commonly known as the Gonzalez's air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in humid, shaded environments. The plant has short, stiff leaves that form a rosette, and it produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is often appreciated by both botanists and plant enthusiasts.
