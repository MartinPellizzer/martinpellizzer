Trochetia grandiflora

Trochetia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered trochetia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

Trochetia grandiflora is valued in horticulture for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
