Trichosanthes globosa

Trichosanthes globosa, commonly known as the globe gourd, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, spherical fruits that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is typically harvested when it is still green and used in various culinary applications.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
