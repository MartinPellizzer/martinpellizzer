Tithymalus graminifolius subsp. tommasiniana

Tithymalus graminifolius subsp.

tommasiniana, commonly known as the Italian tithymale, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It features slender, upright stems that bear small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, giving it its species name "graminifolius.". It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This subspecies is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
