Tephrosia elongata var. tzaneenensis

Tephrosia elongata var. tzaneenensis, commonly known as the Tzaneen tephrosia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves with elliptical leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
