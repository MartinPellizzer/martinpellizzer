Trilobulina striata

Trilobulina striata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, ribbon-like leaves that are often marked with striking green and yellow stripes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in clusters. Trilobulina striata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare orchids.
