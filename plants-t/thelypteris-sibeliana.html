Thelypteris sibeliana

Thelypteris sibeliana, commonly known as the Sibel's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines. This fern is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically divided into lance-shaped segments. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to moist, well-drained soil.
