Tupistra chinensis

Tupistra chinensis, commonly known as Chinese yew, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, dark green foliage with needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, reddish-brown cones that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Tupistra chinensis is also noted for its longevity and resilience in various soil conditions.
