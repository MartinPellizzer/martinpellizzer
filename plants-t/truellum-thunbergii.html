Truellum thunbergii

Truellum thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Truellum thunbergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
