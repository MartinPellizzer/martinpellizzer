Thryptomene obovata

Thryptomene obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaved thryptomene, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its obovate, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

Thryptomene obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
