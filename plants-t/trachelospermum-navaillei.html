Trachelospermum navaillei

Trachelospermum navaillei, commonly known as the Navaille's star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a vine that produces fragrant, star-shaped white flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in suitable environments.
