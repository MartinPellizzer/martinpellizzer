Tricalysia soyauxii var. pedunculosa

Tricalysia soyauxii var. pedunculosa, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It features striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody, cylindrical pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening in warm climates.

The botanical characteristics of this species make it a visually appealing and ecologically valuable plant in tropical landscapes.
