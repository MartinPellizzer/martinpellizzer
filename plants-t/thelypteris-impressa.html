Thelypteris impressa

Thelypteris impressa, commonly known as the impressed water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that often have a distinctive, wavy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its rhizomes are wiry and help it spread through vegetative reproduction.

Thelypteris impressa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
