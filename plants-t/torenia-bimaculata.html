Torenia bimaculata

Torenia bimaculata, commonly known as the two-spotted torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual or perennial, depending on the climate. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Torenia bimaculata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
