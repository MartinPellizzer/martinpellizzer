Thymus × subramosus

Thymus × subramosus, commonly known as hybrid thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Thymus, which includes various species of thyme known for their aromatic properties. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, featuring compact, bushy foliage with small, gray-green leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Thymus × subramosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
