Tradescantia elongata var. diuretica

Tradescantia elongata var. diuretica, commonly known as the silver sword or diuretic spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or blue-gray bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in various regions for both aesthetic and traditional uses.
