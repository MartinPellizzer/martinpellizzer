Thalictrum minus var. yamamotoi

Thalictrum minus var. yamamotoi, commonly known as Yamamoto's meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers with five sepals and no true petals, typical of the genus Thalictrum. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value lies in its graceful appearance and early spring blooming period.
