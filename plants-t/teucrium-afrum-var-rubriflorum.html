Teucrium afrum var. rubriflorum

Teucrium afrum var. rubriflorum, commonly known as the red-flowered germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
