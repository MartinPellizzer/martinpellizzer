Turbinicarpus × amoenulus

Turbinicarpus × amoenulus, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is a hybrid between Turbinicarpus macdowellii and Turbinicarpus subulatus, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding a striking visual contrast to its green or blue-green body.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
