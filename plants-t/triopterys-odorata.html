Triopterys odorata

Triopterys odorata, commonly known as the sweet-scented tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves that emit a pleasant fragrance when crushed. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by egg-shaped berries.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
