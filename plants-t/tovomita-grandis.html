Tovomita grandis

Tovomita grandis, commonly known as the African ebony, is a tropical tree native to western and central Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, spherical fruits.

Tovomita grandis is valued for its hard, durable wood used in furniture and musical instruments.
