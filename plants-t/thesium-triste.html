Thesium triste

Thesium triste, commonly known as the sad thesium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Thesium triste is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in dry and sunny conditions.

Its name reflects its melancholic appearance and the way it seems to wilt easily in adverse weather.
