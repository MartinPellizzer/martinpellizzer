Triticum durum var. jakubzinerii

Triticum durum var. jakubzinerii, commonly known as the Jakubziner wheat, is a rare and ancient variety of durum wheat.

It is characterized by its small, hard grains and is primarily found in specific regions of Central Asia. This variant is notable for its genetic diversity and potential for breeding programs aimed at improving wheat resilience. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and has a short growth cycle.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable genetic resource for agricultural research.
