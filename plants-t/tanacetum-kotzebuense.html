Tanacetum kotzebuense

Tanacetum kotzebuense, commonly known as the Kotzebue goldenroot, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions of North America and Eurasia.

Tanacetum kotzebuense is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
