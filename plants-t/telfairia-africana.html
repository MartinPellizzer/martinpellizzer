Telfairia africana

Telfairia africana, commonly known as African bitter melon, is a tropical climbing vine native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant produces small, bitter green fruits that are typically harvested when immature. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental value and potential agricultural uses.
