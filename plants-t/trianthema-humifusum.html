Trianthema humifusum

Trianthema humifusum, commonly known as the carpetweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to southern Africa.

It features prostrate stems that spread along the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant has small, fleshy, green leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in small inflorescences, blooming throughout the growing season.

Trianthema humifusum is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
