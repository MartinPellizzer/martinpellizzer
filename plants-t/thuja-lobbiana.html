Thuja lobbiana

Thuja lobbiana, commonly known as the Chinese fir or Chinese thuja, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its pyramidal shape and dense, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year. The plant has scale-like leaves arranged in crosses, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, spherical cones that are typically yellowish-brown in color.

Thuja lobbiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.
