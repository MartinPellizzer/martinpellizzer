Tropaeolum majus var. haageanum

Tropaeolum majus var. haageanum, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a vibrant flowering plant native to South America.

It features striking red, orange, and yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces edible pods that are often harvested when young and tender. Its foliage is heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both gardens and containers.
