Thelymitra purpureofusca

Thelymitra purpureofusca, commonly known as the purple-bellied grasshopper orchid, is an epiphytic plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces small, green to purple flowers that are typically arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or in rocky areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
