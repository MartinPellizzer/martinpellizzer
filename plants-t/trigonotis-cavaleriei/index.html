Trigonotis Cavaleriei

Trigonotis cavaleriei is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its unique, triangular-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in the spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
