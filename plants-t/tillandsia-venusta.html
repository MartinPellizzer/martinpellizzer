Tillandsia venusta

Tillandsia venusta, commonly known as the silver vernal tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces strikingly beautiful inflorescences with bright orange or yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and good air circulation to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
