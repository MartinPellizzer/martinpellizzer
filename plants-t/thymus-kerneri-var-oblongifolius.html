Thymus kerneri var. oblongifolius

Thymus kerneri var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, creeping stems and oblong, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses, similar to other thyme species.
