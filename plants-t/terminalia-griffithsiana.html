Terminalia griffithsiana

Terminalia griffithsiana, commonly known as the Indian satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate leaves. Terminalia griffithsiana is also noted for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
