Tournefortia sarmentosa

Tournefortia sarmentosa, commonly known as the creeping tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually simple, opposite, and have a rough texture.

Tournefortia sarmentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
