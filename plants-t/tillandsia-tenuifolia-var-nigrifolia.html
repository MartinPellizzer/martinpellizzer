Tillandsia tenuifolia var. nigrifolia

Tillandsia tenuifolia var. nigrifolia, commonly known as the black-leaved air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and striking dark green to blackish leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. This epiphytic plant does not require soil to grow, instead absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom from the base of the plant.

The variety is prized for its unique coloration and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
