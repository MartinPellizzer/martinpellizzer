Thoa oblonga

Thoa oblonga, commonly known as the broadleaf thoa, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in greenish-white or pale yellow hues.

Thoa oblonga is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
