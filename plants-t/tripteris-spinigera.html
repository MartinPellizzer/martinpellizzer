Tripteris spinigera

Tripteris spinigera, commonly known as the spiny tripteris, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
