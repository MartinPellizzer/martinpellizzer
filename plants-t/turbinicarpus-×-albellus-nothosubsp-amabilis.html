Turbinicarpus × albellus nothosubsp. amabilis

Turbinicarpus × albellus nothosubsp.

amabilis, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant characterized by its distinctive cactus body and colorful, often vibrant flowers. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is native to the arid regions of Mexico. The plant typically has a short, thick stem with spines and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
