Triticum × soveticum

Triticum × soveticum, commonly known as the intermediate wheatgrass, is a polyploid plant that belongs to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall stature, with stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences containing small, two-lobed florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Triticum × soveticum is notable for its high biomass yield and potential as a forage crop and bioenergy source.
