Tricyrtis Maculata

Tricyrtis maculata is a striking member of the ginger family known for its unique appearance and hardy nature. This perennial plant features delicate, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a distinctive spotted pattern. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and well-drained soil. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often variegated, adding visual interest to gardens. Tricyrtis maculata is a favorite among gardeners for its beauty and ease of care.
