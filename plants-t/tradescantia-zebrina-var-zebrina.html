Tradescantia zebrina var. zebrina

Tradescantia zebrina var. zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Caribbean.

It features striking, variegated foliage with deep green stripes and bright white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of purple or pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive appearance make it a favored ornamental plant.
