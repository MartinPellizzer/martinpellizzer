Thuja occidentalis var. globosa

Thuja occidentalis var. globosa, commonly known as the globe arborvitae, is a compact, spherical evergreen shrub native to eastern North America.

It features dense, scale-like foliage that gives it a rounded, globe-shaped appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for small spaces or as a topiary. Its foliage is dark green and remains attractive throughout the year.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate urban conditions.
