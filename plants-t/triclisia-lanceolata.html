Triclisia lanceolata

Triclisia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf triclisia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellow or greenish hue.

Triclisia lanceolata is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
