Trimezia juncifolia

Trimezia juncifolia, commonly known as the silver sword, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
