Tricyrtis macrantha

Tricyrtis macrantha, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan and Korea.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a clump-forming habit. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Tricyrtis macrantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
