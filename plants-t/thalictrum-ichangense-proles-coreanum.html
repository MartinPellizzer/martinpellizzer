Thalictrum ichangense proles coreanum

Thalictrum ichangense proles coreanum, commonly known as the Korean meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
