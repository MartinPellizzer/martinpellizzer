Triaspis canescens

Triaspis canescens, commonly known as the woolly leaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Triaspis canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
