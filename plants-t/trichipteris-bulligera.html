Trichipteris bulligera

Trichipteris bulligera, commonly known as the bull's ear fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. Trichipteris bulligera is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
