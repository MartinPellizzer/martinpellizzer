Thelypteris Orbicularis

Thelypteris orbicularis is a unique fern species known for its distinctive round-shaped fronds. Found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in forest ecosystems. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
