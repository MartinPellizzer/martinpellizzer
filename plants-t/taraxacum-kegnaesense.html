Taraxacum kegnaesense

Taraxacum kegnaesense, commonly known as the Kegnaes dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to northern Europe and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a yellow flower head that matures into a spherical seed sphere.
