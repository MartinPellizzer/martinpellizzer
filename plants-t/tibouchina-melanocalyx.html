Tibouchina melanocalyx

Tibouchina melanocalyx, commonly known as the black-leaved tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its dark purple to black foliage, which contrasts strikingly with its showy, pink to red flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid environments.

Tibouchina melanocalyx is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
