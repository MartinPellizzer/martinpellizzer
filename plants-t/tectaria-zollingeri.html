Tectaria zollingeri

Tectaria zollingeri, commonly known as the Zollinger's shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, velvety layer of brownish scales.

Tectaria zollingeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
