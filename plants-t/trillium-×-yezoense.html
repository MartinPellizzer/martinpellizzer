Trillium × yezoense

Trillium × yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo trillium, is a hybrid plant native to Japan and parts of eastern Russia.

It is characterized by its three large, waxy, white petals and three sepals that are often a different color, typically green or red. The plant produces a single, prominent yellow or orange stamen with a red anther, which is a distinctive feature. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Trillium × yezoense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
