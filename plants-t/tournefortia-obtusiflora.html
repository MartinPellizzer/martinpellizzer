Tournefortia obtusiflora

Tournefortia obtusiflora, commonly known as the broad-leaved tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stems. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and often have a yellowish or greenish hue.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
