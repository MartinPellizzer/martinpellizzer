Terminalia modesta

Terminalia modesta, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of small, white flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in late winter or early spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a glossy green surface. The tree produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.
