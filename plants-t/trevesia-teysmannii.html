Trevesia teysmannii

Trevesia teysmannii, commonly known as the Taysmann's trevesia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, often glandular leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
