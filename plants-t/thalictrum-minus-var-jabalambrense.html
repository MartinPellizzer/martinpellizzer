Thalictrum minus var. jabalambrense

Thalictrum minus var. jabalambrense, commonly known as the Kashmir spirea, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
