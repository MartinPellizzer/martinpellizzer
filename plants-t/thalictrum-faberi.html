Thalictrum faberi

Thalictrum faberi, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and prefers moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a shade-tolerant, ornamental plant.
