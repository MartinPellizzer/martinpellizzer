Tulbaghia karasbergensis

Tulbaghia karasbergensis, commonly known as the Karasberg star flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale blue star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
