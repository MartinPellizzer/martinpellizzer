Tunilla silvestris

Tunilla silvestris, commonly known as the wild tunilla, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, dark green surface. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale blue, appearing in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Tunilla silvestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
