Torenia patens

Torenia patens, commonly known as the common torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting purple or blue throat. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Torenia patens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
