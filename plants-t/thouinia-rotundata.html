Thouinia rotundata

Thouinia rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaved thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, waxy, and spiny leaves that are typically rounded in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suited for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
