Taonabo punctata

Taonabo punctata, commonly known as the spotted leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with dark green spots. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Taonabo punctata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
