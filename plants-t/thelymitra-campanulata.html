Thelymitra campanulata

Thelymitra campanulata, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a prominent lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Thelymitra campanulata is a perennial species, with its flowering season occurring during the warmer months.
