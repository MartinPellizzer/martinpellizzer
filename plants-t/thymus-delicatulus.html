Thymus delicatulus

Thymus delicatulus, commonly known as the slender thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a distinct aromatic scent when crushed. The plant produces tiny, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its delicate appearance and aromatic qualities, it is often used in culinary and ornamental applications.
