Thymopsis thymoides subsp. glabrescens

Thymopsis thymoides subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth thyme. It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces tiny, pinkish-white flowers in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
