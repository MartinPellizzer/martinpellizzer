Turbinicarpus krainzianus f. lausseri

Turbinicarpus krainzianus f. lausseri, commonly known as the Lausser's Turbinicarpus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
