Tinospora sinensis

Tinospora sinensis, commonly known as Chinese Tinospora, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red berries. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Tinospora sinensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
