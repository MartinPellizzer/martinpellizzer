Tabernaemontana lucida

Tabernaemontana lucida, commonly known as the shining star of Java, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface, and its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the ends of branches. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
