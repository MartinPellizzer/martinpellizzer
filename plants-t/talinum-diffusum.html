Talinum diffusum

Talinum diffusum, commonly known as wandering nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, often blooming in the evening. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, dark seeds.

Talinum diffusum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in partial to full sun.
