Trichomanes decurrens

Trichomanes decurrens, commonly known as the running huperzia, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, linear, and often decurrent leaves that extend down the stem, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its sporangia are borne on the underside of the leaves, and it reproduces through spores rather than seeds.

Trichomanes decurrens is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, showcasing its adaptability to humid climates.
