Tritaxis zeylanica

Tritaxis zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon star anise, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a strong, spicy scent.

Tritaxis zeylanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic and analgesic effects.
