Thea Lutescens

Thea lutescens is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique foliage, it stands out among its botanical relatives. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its beauty has made it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its allure, Thea lutescens remains under-studied and faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection.
