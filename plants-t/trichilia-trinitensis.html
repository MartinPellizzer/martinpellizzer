Trichilia trinitensis

Trichilia trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidadian tamarind, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Trinidad and Tobago.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related species. The tree is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, and the tree is often found in moist, lowland areas.
