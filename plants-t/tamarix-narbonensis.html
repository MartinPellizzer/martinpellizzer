Tamarix narbonensis

Tamarix narbonensis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a hardy species in dry climates. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in poor soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
