Teucrium rotundifolium subsp. granatense

Teucrium rotundifolium subsp.

granatense, commonly known as granate germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, gray-green foliage with rounded, often succulent leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional remedies.
