Tabernaemontana sumatrana

Tabernaemontana sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Tabernaemontana sumatrana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
