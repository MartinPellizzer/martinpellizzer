Trichosanthes pedata var. yunnanensis

Trichosanthes pedata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan trumpet vine, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its large, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces long, slender fruits that can reach up to 2 meters in length. Its leaves are compound, with elongated leaflets that are often hairy.

This species is valued for its ornamental features and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
