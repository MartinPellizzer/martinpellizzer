Trichoglottis littoralis

Trichoglottis littoralis, commonly known as the coastal trichoglottis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments near coastal areas. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.

Trichoglottis littoralis is often found in sandy or rocky soils along shorelines.
