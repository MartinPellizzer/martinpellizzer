Thlaspi alpinum subsp. sylvium

Thlaspi alpinum subsp.

sylvium, commonly known as the alpine cabbages or rock cabbages, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

The plant has basal leaves that are rosette-like and are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in a raceme, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

It is well adapted to cold climates and can survive in harsh mountain environments.
