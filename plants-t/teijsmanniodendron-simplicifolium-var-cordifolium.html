Teijsmanniodendron simplicifolium var. cordifolium

Teijsmanniodendron simplicifolium var. cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaf banyan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
