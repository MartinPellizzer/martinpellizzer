Thymus × silvi-stepposus

Thymus × silvi-stepposus, commonly known as the steppe thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands, forest edges, and rocky soils. The plant features small, aromatic leaves and pink to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. Its foliage is often used in herbal medicine and culinary applications due to its essential oils.

Thymus × silvi-stepposus is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
