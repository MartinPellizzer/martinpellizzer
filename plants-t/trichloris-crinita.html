Trichloris crinita

Trichloris crinita, commonly known as the feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and long, narrow leaves that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often overlooked due to their subtle structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal dunes.

Trichloris crinita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in low-maintenance landscaping.
