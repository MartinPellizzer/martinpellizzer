Trifolium gracilentum var. exile

Trifolium gracilentum var. exile, commonly known as slender clover, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves composed of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

This variety is noted for its slender growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
