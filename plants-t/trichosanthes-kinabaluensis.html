Trichosanthes kinabaluensis

Trichosanthes kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Kinabalu trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that resemble trumpets, which bloom in late summer. The plant has a distinctive, smooth, pale green stem with prominent, warty tubercles. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs.

Trichosanthes kinabaluensis is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
