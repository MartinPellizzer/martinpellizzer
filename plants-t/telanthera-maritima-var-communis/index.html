Telanthera Maritima Var. Communis

Telanthera maritima var. communis is a hardy flowering plant native to coastal regions. It thrives in sandy soils and salt-laden air, making it a resilient choice for seaside gardens. This variety is known for its vibrant purple flowers that bloom in late spring. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rockeries and coastal landscapes. It is also valued for its ability to stabilize dunes and prevent erosion.
