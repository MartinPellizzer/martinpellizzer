Tephrosia purpurea var. rufescens

Tephrosia purpurea var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed tephrosia, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and potential medicinal use.
