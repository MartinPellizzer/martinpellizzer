Thelypteris diaphana

Thelypteris diaphana, commonly known as the ghost fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, translucent fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are often divided into narrow segments, giving it a lacy appearance.

Thelypteris diaphana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
