Thalictrum argyi

Thalictrum argyi, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, feathery compound leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in upright, branched inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Thalictrum argyi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
