Telipogon saraguroensis

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and specialized growth habits. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its small, delicate flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it has a short, compact stem with a few leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Telipogon saraguroensis is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from habitat loss and climate change.
