Tribulus pentandrus var. micropteris

Tribulus pentandrus var. micropteris, commonly known as the small-winged caltrop, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical fruits that resemble small caltrops, which contain four seeds. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being narrow and slightly hairy.

The species is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
