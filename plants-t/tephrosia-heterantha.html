Tephrosia heterantha

Tephrosia heterantha, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a legume with compound leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to blue. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural systems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Tephrosia heterantha is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
