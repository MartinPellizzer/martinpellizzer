Telis spicata

Telis spicata, commonly known as the spike heath, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are evergreen, leathery, and arranged in a compact, shrub-like form.
