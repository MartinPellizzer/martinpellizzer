Typhonium brevipes

Typhonium brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed arrowhead, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly in suitable habitats.
