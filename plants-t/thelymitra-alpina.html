Thelymitra alpina

Thelymitra alpina, commonly known as the alpine rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are pale green to yellow, often with a reddish or brownish tinge, and are arranged in a loose cluster.

Thelymitra alpina is adapted to cold, dry environments and is often found in mountainous areas.
