Telipogon esperanzae

Telipogon esperanzae, commonly known as the Esperanza orchid, is a rare and endemic species found primarily in the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique adaptations to high-altitude environments. Its growth is often limited to rocky soils and moist microhabitats, making it highly sensitive to environmental changes.
