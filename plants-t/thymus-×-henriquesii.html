Thymus × henriquesii

Thymus × henriquesii, commonly known as the Henriques' thyme, is a hybrid plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with aromatic, small, gray-green leaves that are densely packed. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanists.
