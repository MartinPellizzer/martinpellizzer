Tectaria kalimantanensis

Tectaria kalimantanensis, commonly known as the Kalimantan shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its robust, leathery fronds with a distinctive, shield-like shape. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments, showcasing its epiphytic or lithophytic growth habit. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth, waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss in its moist habitat.

Tectaria kalimantanensis plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
