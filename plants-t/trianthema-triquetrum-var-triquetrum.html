Trianthema triquetrum var. triquetrum

Trianthema triquetrum var. triquetrum, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent herb with small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
