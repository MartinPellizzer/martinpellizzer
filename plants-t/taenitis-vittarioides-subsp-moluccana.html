Taenitis vittarioides subsp. moluccana

Taenitis vittarioides subsp.

moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan taenitis, is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia. It is a herbaceous perennial with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences bearing white or pale yellow flowers with prominent stamens. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in tropical horticulture.
