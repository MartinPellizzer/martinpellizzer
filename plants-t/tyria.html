Tyria

Tyria, commonly known as the "Crown of Thorns," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apocynaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. They are characterized by their woody stems, opposite leaves, and showy, often colorful flowers. Many species produce milky sap, which can be toxic to animals and humans.
