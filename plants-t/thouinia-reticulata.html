Thouinia reticulata

Thouinia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, reticulate pattern, giving it its scientific name.

Thouinia reticulata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
