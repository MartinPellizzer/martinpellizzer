Triodanis lamprosperma

Triodanis lamprosperma, commonly known as the golden starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are yellow and star-shaped, with prominent central disc florets.

It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
