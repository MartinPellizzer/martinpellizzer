Tephrosia brevipes

Tephrosia brevipes, commonly known as the small-stemmed tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

Tephrosia brevipes is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
