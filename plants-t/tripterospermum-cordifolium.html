Tripterospermum cordifolium

Tripterospermum cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaved tripterospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
