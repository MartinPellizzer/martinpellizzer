Thelymitra holmesii

Thelymitra holmesii, commonly known as the Holmes' rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several pale green or blue-green leaves. Its flowers are small, with a distinctive violet or purple hue and a unique, intricate structure.

Thelymitra holmesii is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
