Trigonochilum exasperatum

Trigonochilum exasperatum, commonly known as the spiked orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, and often spiky floral structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with vibrant colors that attract pollinators.

Trigonochilum exasperatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
