Trichilia siderotricha

Trichilia siderotricha, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark brown wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. It produces small, white to yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

The tree is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional medicine to treat ailments such as fever and inflammation.
