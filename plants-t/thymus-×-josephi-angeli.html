Thymus × josephi-angeli

Thymus × josephi-angeli, commonly known as the Angel's thyme, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, dense foliage with small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for both ornamental and practical gardening.
