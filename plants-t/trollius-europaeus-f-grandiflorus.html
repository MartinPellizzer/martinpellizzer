Trollius europaeus f. grandiflorus

Trollius europaeus f. grandiflorus, commonly known as the European globeflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, stout stem and basal leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
