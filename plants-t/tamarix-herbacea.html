Tamarix herbacea

Tamarix herbacea, commonly known as the desert rose or sea bean, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that produces small, pink or red flowers in clusters. The plant has feathery leaves and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. It is often used for erosion control due to its deep root system.

Tamarix herbacea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
