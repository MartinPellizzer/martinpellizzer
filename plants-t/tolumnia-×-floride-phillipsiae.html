Tolumnia × floride-phillipsiae

Tolumnia × floride-phillipsiae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Tolumnia floridana and Tolumnia phillipsiae.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as pine rocklands and hardwood hammocks. It features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
