Tachigali macrostachya

Tachigali macrostachya, commonly known as the giant tachigali, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces long, pendulous pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural research.
