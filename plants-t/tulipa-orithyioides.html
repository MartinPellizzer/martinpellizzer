Tulipa orithyioides

Tulipa orithyioides, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive orange or yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained, loamy soil in full sun. Its foliage consists of strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge in early spring.

Tulipa orithyioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
