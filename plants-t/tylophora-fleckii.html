Tylophora fleckii

Tylophora fleckii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Tylophora fleckii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
