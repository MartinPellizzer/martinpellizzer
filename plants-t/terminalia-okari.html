Terminalia okari

Terminalia okari, commonly known as African snowball.

It is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible, round, green fruits. Terminalia okari is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and cultural contexts.
