Thymus × herberoi

Thymus × herberoi, commonly known as hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, compact foliage with small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or lavender flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens, borders, and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and drought tolerance.

Thymus × herberoi is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
