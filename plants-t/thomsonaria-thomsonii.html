Thomsonaria thomsonii

Thomsonaria thomsonii, commonly known as the Thomson's orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive pattern of darker markings.

Thomsonaria thomsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and colorful blossoms.
