Thelocephala reichei

Thelocephala reichei, commonly known as the mountain rock cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered with a fine layer of pubescence.

Thelocephala reichei is valued for its resilience and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens for its ornamental appeal.
