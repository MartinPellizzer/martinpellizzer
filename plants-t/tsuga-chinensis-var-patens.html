Tsuga chinensis var. patens

Tsuga chinensis var. patens, commonly known as the Chinese hemlock, is a deciduous conifer native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of coniferous species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
