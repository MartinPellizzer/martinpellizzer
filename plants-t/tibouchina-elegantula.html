Tibouchina elegantula

Tibouchina elegantula, commonly known as the velvet tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink or red tubular flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are leathery and often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name.

Tibouchina elegantula is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
