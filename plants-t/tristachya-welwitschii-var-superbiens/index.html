Tristachya Welwitschii Var. Superbiens

Tristachya welwitschii var. superbiens is a striking ornamental grass known for its tall, feathery plumes that add dramatic flair to any landscape. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardens. Its unique appearance and resilience have made it a favorite among gardeners and designers seeking both beauty and durability. The variety 'superbiens' stands out for its particularly showy and elongated inflorescences that bloom in late summer. Whether used as a focal point or in mass plantings, this grass offers both visual interest and ecological benefits.
