Tecoma stans f. velutina

Tecoma stans f. velutina, commonly known as velvet trumpetbush, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name, and its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage texture and vibrant floral display make it a striking addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
