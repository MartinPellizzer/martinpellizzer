Tephrosia butayei

Tephrosia butayei, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and are followed by small, woody pods containing seeds.

Tephrosia butayei is often found in dry, open woodlands and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
