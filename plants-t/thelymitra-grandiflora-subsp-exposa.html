Thelymitra grandiflora subsp. exposa

Thelymitra grandiflora subsp.

exposa, commonly known as the exposed leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and grasslands.

The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow leaf and produces a spike of pale green to yellowish flowers. Its flowers are relatively large for the genus, with a distinctive hooded labellum that is often exposed to the elements.

This subspecies is noted for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions, including exposure to wind and drought.
