Torenia travancorica

Torenia travancorica, commonly known as the Malabar torenia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually violet or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Torenia travancorica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
