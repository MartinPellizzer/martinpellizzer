Tilia translucens

Tilia translucens, commonly known as the translucent linden, is a rare and delicate species of the Tilia genus.

It is characterized by its thin, translucent leaves that allow light to pass through, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands or stream sides. It produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are important for pollinators.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Tilia translucens is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
