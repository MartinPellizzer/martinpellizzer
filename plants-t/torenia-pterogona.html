Torenia pterogona

Torenia pterogona, commonly known as the blue trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Torenia pterogona is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
