Tabernaemontana laeta var. minor

Tabernaemontana laeta var. minor, commonly known as the fragrant star jasmine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.

Its adaptability to partial shade makes it suitable for a variety of landscaping applications.
