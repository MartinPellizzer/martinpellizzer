Thelypteris pseudofalciloba

Thelypteris pseudofalciloba, commonly known as the false falciloba holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce both sterile and fertile fronds.

The spores are released from the sori, which are located on the underside of the fertile leaves.
