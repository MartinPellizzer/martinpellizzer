Thunbergia racemosa

Thunbergia racemosa, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in racemes, often appearing in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Thunbergia racemosa is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
