Torenia mucronulata

Torenia mucronulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, facilitating its spread in suitable habitats.
