Tecoma Viminalis

Tecoma viminalis is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. This versatile plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions. Its fast growth rate makes it suitable for erosion control and as a living fence. Tecoma viminalis is also valued for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
