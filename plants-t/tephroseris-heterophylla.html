Tephroseris heterophylla

Tephroseris heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

Tephroseris heterophylla is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions.
