Tsuga chinensis var. chinensis

Tsuga chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hemlock, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needle-like leaves, and its ability to thrive in cool, moist environments. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against environmental stressors.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
