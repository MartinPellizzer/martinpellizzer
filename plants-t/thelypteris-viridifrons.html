Thelypteris viridifrons

Thelypteris viridifrons, commonly known as the green-fronded shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, serrated fronds that are typically bright green with a slightly glossy appearance. Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
