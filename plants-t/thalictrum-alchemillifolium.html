Thalictrum alchemillifolium

Thalictrum alchemillifolium, commonly known as meadow rue, is a flowering plant in the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
