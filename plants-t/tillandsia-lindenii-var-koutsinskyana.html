Tillandsia lindenii var. koutsinskyana

Tillandsia lindenii var. koutsinskyana, commonly known as the Koutsinsky's air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces striking purple or red inflorescences with small, bell-shaped flowers.

This variety is relatively rare and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
