Thelypteris pelludia

Thelypteris pelludia, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are usually bipinnate, with a bright yellow or golden hue on the underside. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Thelypteris pelludia is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
