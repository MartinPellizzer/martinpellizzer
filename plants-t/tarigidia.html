Tarigidia

Tarigidia, commonly known as the common tarweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow to orange flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical heads. Tarigidia has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.
