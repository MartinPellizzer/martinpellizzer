Thelypteris namaphila

Thelypteris namaphila, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green with a yellowish or golden hue on the underside, giving it its common name.

Thelypteris namaphila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
