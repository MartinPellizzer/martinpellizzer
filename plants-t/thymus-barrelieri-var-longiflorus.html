Thymus barrelieri var. longiflorus

Thymus barrelieri var. longiflorus, commonly known as the long-flowered rockrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, low-growing stems covered in dense, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
