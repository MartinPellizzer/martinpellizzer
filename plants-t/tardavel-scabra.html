Tardavel scabra

Tardavel scabra, commonly known as the rough scabra, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that are covered in coarse, stiff hairs, giving it a rugged appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive look.

Tardavel scabra is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand hot, arid conditions.
