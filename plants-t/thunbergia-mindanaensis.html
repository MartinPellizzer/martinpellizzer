Thunbergia mindanaensis

Thunbergia mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, featuring heart-shaped leaves and long, slender stems. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
