Thelypteris refracta

Thelypteris refracta, commonly known as the fronded shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that often have a light green to pale yellow color. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.

Thelypteris refracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
