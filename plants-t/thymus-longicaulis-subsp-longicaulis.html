Thymus longicaulis subsp. longicaulis

Thymus longicaulis subsp.

longicaulis, commonly known as narrowleaf thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features aromatic, small, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and linear, with a dense mat-like growth habit.

The plant produces small, pink or white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its essential oils contain thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.
