Tridesmis

Tridesmis, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, orange or red flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Tridesmis species are popular in gardens for their vibrant blooms and role in supporting pollinators like butterflies.
