Tillandsia yunckeri

Tillandsia yunckeri, commonly known as the Yuncker's air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant possesses a rosette-shaped structure with narrow, strap-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces striking inflorescences with vibrant, showy flowers that can range from red to orange to yellow.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
