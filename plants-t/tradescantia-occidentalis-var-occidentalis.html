Tradescantia occidentalis var. occidentalis

Tradescantia occidentalis var. occidentalis, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, three-lobed flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
