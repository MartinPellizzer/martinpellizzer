Tithymalus seguierianus subsp. nicicianus

Tithymalus seguierianus subsp.

nicicianus, commonly known as the Nicician tithymalus, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought-resistant growth.
