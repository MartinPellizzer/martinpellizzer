Tounatea grandifolia

Tounatea grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved tounatea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Tounatea grandifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
