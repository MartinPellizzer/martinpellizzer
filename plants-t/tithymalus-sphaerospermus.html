Tithymalus sphaerospermus

Tithymalus sphaerospermus, commonly known as the globe-headed tithymalus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive spherical flower heads. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Tithymalus sphaerospermus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
