Teucrium regium

Teucrium regium, commonly known as royal germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its medicinal properties have been historically valued for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
