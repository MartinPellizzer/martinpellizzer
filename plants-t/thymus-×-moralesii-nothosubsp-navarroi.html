Thymus × moralesii nothosubsp. navarroi

Thymus × moralesii nothosubsp.

navarroi, commonly known as Navarre thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe. It features aromatic, gray-green foliage with small, oval leaves that are densely packed on the stems.

The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both garden use and horticultural research.
