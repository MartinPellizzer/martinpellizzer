Tetraria triangularis

Tetraria triangularis, commonly known as the triangular tetraria, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.

Tetraria triangularis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
