Tragacantha polyceras

Tragacantha polyceras, commonly known as the horned tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that produces yellow flowers in the spring. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like bracts that surround its inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
