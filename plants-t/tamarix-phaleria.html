Tamarix phaleria

Tamarix phaleria, commonly known as the Chinese tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny branches and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces pink or reddish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Tamarix phaleria is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
