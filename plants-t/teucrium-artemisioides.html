Teucrium artemisioides

Teucrium artemisioides, commonly known as blue germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sunny, open habitats.

Teucrium artemisioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
