Triaspis acuminata

Triaspis acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata triaspis, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a serrated margin. Triaspis acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
