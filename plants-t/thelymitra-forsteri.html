Thelymitra forsteri

Thelymitra forsteri, commonly known as the Forster's rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive hooded labellum. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Thelymitra forsteri is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its resilience in arid conditions.
