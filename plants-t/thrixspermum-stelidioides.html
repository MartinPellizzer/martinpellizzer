Thrixspermum stelidioides

Thrixspermum stelidioides, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Thrixspermum stelidioides is also valued for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
