Tephrosia pumila

Tephrosia pumila, commonly known as smallleaf tephrosia, is a low-growing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It typically features small, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forest understories or along stream banks. Its seed pods are slender and contain several small, dark seeds.

Tephrosia pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
