Tryphostemma stuhlmannii

Tryphostemma stuhlmannii, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
