Tragacantha eriopoda

Tragacantha eriopoda, commonly known as the woolly tragacanth, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces a resinous sap, which has been historically used in various traditional applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
