Tephrosia nubica var. polyphylla

Tephrosia nubica var. polyphylla, commonly known as the Ethiopian tephrosia, is a shrub native to Ethiopia.

It features compound leaves with multiple pairs of leaflets, and its flowers are typically small and yellow. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant characteristics. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and has the ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with soil bacteria.

This species is sometimes used in traditional medicine and for soil improvement in agricultural settings.
