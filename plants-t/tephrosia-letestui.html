Tephrosia letestui

Tephrosia letestui, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually white or pale blue, and it produces fluffy, seed-bearing pods.

Tephrosia letestui is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
