Triplaris vestita

Triplaris vestita, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
