Tetracme recurvata var. integrifolia

Tetracme recurvata var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed tetracme, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing plant characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves with smooth margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.
