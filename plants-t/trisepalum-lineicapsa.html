Trisepalum lineicapsa

Trisepalum lineicapsa, commonly known as the slender-sepal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-sepal structure, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Trisepalum lineicapsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
