Thelypteris × kashmiriana

Thelypteris × kashmiriana, commonly known as the Kashmir fern, is a hybrid species belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often arching and gracefully shaped. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are usually short and produce dense clusters of fronds.
