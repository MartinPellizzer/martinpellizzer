Tripteris scariosa

Tripteris scariosa, commonly known as the scarious tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with three leaflets, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Tripteris scariosa is often found in humid, shaded environments within lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the understory vegetation in these ecosystems.
