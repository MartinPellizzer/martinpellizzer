Thelymitra daltonii

Thelymitra daltonii, commonly known as Dalton's sun-orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or heathland environments. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose spike and bloom during the autumn season.

Thelymitra daltonii is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native flora.
