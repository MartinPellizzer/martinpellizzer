Thalictrum elatum lusus macrophyllum

Thalictrum elatum lusus macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant avens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant features large, palmately divided leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in loose, upright inflorescences.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and forest clearings.
