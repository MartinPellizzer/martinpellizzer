Turbinicarpus krainzianus f. minimus

Turbinicarpus krainzianus f. minimus, commonly known as the miniature turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, rounded stems that are typically covered in a dense layer of white to grayish hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is a low-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
