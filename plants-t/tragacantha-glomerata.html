Tragacantha glomerata

Tragacantha glomerata, commonly known as the globular tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive globular or spherical shape, formed by the dense clustering of its yellowish flowers. The plant produces a sticky, resinous substance known as tragacanth, which has been historically used in various industries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Tragacantha glomerata is valued both for its ornamental appearance and its traditional medicinal applications.
