Toona sinensis

Toona sinensis, commonly known as Chinese toon, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree produces small, greenish flowers followed by edible, oval fruits that contain flat, winged seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit and adaptability make it a popular choice in horticulture and reforestation projects.
