Thymus leucostachys

Thymus leucostachys, commonly known as white thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, creeping stems and small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has aromatic, fragrant leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping and herbal gardens.
