Teucrium Polium Var. Lusitanicum

Lusitanian germander, scientifically known as Teucrium polium var. lusitanicum, is a hardy perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. This plant thrives in rocky and sunny environments, often found in Mediterranean climates. With its small, fragrant blue or purple flowers, it adds a touch of color to gardens and natural landscapes. Its medicinal properties have been recognized for centuries in traditional herbal remedies. Despite its resilience, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated species in the botanical world.
