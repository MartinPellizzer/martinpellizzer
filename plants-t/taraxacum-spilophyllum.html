Taraxacum spilophyllum

Taraxacum spilophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. Native to central and eastern Europe, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces a long, slender stem with a single flower head at the top.

It is often found in grasslands, roadsides, and along forest edges.
