Tripolium

Tripolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and showy blue or purple flower heads. These plants are typically found in coastal areas and saline soils, thriving in harsh environments. Tripolium species are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants.

They play an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and preventing erosion.
