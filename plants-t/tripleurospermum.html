Tripleurospermum

Tripleurospermum, commonly known as goatsbeard, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their daisy-like flowers, which typically consist of a central yellow disc surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. They are native to temperate regions of Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia. Many species are found in moist or wet habitats, such as marshes and stream banks.

The genus includes several ornamental species valued for their delicate appearance and ability to thrive in various garden conditions.
