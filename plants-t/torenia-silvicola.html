Torenia silvicola

Torenia silvicola, commonly known as the forest torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Torenia silvicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity.
