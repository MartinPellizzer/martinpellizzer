Tarenna graveolens subsp. arabica

Tarenna graveolens subsp.

arabica, commonly known as Arabian tarenna, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula. It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves with a slightly rough texture.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in well-drained soils. It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.

The subspecies arabica is distinguished by its specific floral and morphological traits compared to other subspecies of Tarenna.
