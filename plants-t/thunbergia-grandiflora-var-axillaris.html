Thunbergia grandiflora var. axillaris

Thunbergia grandiflora var. axillaris, commonly known as the Axillary Thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative vine or ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and ability to climb or cascade from supports.
