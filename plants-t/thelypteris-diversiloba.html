Thelypteris diversiloba

Thelypteris diversiloba, commonly known as the variable shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris diversiloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
