Tulipa sommieri

Tulipa sommieri, commonly known as the summer tulip, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, making it a striking addition to gardens during the warmer months. Its botanical features include lance-shaped leaves and a bulbous root system typical of the tulip genus.

Tulipa sommieri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
