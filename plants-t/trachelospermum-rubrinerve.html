Trachelospermum rubrinerve

Trachelospermum rubrinerve, commonly known as the red-veined jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and sweet scent.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for landscaping purposes.
