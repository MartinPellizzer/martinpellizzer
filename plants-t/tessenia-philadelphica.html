Tessenia philadelphica

Tessenia philadelphica, commonly known as the Philadelphic tessenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rutaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
