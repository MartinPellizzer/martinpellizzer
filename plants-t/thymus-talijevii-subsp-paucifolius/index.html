Thymus Talijevii Subsp. Paucifolius

Thymus talijevii subsp. paucifolius is a rare and lesser-known member of the thyme family found in the mountainous regions of the Balkans. This subspecies is distinguished by its sparse foliage and compact growth habit, making it unique among its relatives. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this plant from threats such as habitat loss and climate change.
