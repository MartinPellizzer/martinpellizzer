Thalictrum fortunei

Thalictrum fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has thin, wiry stems and is often found in moist, temperate climates.

Thalictrum fortunei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
