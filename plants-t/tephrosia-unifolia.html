Tephrosia unifolia

Tephrosia unifolia, commonly known as the smallleaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is a small, shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Tephrosia unifolia is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
