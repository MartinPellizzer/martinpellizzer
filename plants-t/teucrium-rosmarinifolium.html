Teucrium rosmarinifolium

Teucrium rosmarinifolium, commonly known as lemon rosmarinus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant is often found in rocky or dry soils and is drought-tolerant once established. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive lemony scent.

It is widely used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant in gardens.
