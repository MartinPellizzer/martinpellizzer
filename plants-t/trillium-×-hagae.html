Trillium × hagae

Trillium × hagae, commonly known as the hybrid trillium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its three-parted leaves and showy, three-petaled flowers. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
