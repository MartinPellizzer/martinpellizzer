Triplaris caracasana

Triplaris caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas ash or Caracas tree, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the Caribbean, particularly the island of Margarita.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in catkins, followed by small, spherical fruits.
