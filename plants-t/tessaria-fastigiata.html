Tessaria fastigiata

Tessaria fastigiata, commonly known as the slender tessaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
