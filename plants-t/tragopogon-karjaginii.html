Tragopogon karjaginii

Tragopogon karjaginii, commonly known as Karjagin's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically hairy stems. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
