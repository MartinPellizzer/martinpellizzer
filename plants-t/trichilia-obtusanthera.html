Trichilia obtusanthera

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, including countries such as Nigeria and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and compound leaves with opposite arrangement. The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
