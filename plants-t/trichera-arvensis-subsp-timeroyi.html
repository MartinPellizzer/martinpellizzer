Trichera arvensis subsp. timeroyi

Trichera arvensis subsp.

timeroyi, commonly known as the Timeroi thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features a robust stem with spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology, with lobed and often hairy surfaces.

This subspecies is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
