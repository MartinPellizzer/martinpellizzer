Trigonella aurantiaca var. cariensis

Trigonella aurantiaca var. cariensis, commonly known as the orange fenugreek, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to regions of the Mediterranean and parts of southern Europe. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
