Thelypteris metteniana

Thelypteris metteniana, commonly known as the mountain shield fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, mat-like growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive shield-shaped appearance.

Thelypteris metteniana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
