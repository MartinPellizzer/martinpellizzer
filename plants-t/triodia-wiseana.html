Triodia wiseana

Triodia wiseana, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a perennial grass native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry growth habit and its ability to form extensive mats on the ground. The plant is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including high temperatures and low rainfall. Its leaves are typically covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Triodia wiseana plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various desert species.
