Tragacantha Himalayana

Tragacantha himalayana is a rare and resilient plant native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas. It thrives in harsh, high-altitude environments where few species can survive. This plant is known for its unique adaptations to cold and arid conditions. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional herbal practices for centuries. Despite its ecological significance, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss and climate change.
