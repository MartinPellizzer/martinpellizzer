Tritonixia undulata

Tritonixia undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has lance-shaped, wavy-edged leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Tritonixia undulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
