Thymus glabrescens f. tokajensis

Thymus glabrescens f. tokajensis, commonly known as Tokaj thyme, is a subspecies of thyme native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun.

This variety is valued for its aromatic leaves used in culinary and medicinal applications.
