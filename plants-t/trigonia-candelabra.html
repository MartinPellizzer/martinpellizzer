Trigonia candelabra

Trigonia candelabra, commonly known as the candlestick plant, is a unique member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, upright, branching stems that resemble a candlestick, hence its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the tips of the branches. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
