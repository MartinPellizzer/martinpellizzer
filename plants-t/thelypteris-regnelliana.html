Thelypteris regnelliana

Thelypteris regnelliana, commonly known as the regnell's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds with a distinctive, shield-like shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Thelypteris regnelliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
