Thalictrum yesoense

Thalictrum yesoense, commonly known as the Yesso meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It has a slender, upright stem and produces numerous stamens in its flower clusters.

Thalictrum yesoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
