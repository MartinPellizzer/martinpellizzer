Thunbergia cycnium

Thunbergia cycnium, commonly known as the white trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged oppositely on the stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Thunbergia cycnium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
