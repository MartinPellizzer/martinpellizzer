Tropilis × delicata

Tropilis × delicata, commonly known as the delicate tansy, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-green composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is often found in temperate regions of North America.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit a range of morphological variations.
