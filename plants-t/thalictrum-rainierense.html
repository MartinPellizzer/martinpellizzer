Thalictrum rainierense

Thalictrum rainierense, commonly known as the Rainier meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as meadows and woodlands. It has a slender, upright stem and produces feathery seed heads that persist into the fall.
