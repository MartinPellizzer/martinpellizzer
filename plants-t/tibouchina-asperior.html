Tibouchina asperior

Tibouchina asperior, commonly known as rough tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Tibouchina asperior is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
