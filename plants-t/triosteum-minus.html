Triosteum minus

Triosteum minus, commonly known as the small triosteum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin. Its small, white to pale purple flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer.

Triosteum minus is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, damp environments.
