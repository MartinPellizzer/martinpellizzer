Tetrasida Tulla

Tetrasida tulla is a rare and enigmatic plant found in remote, high-altitude regions. It is known for its striking, bioluminescent leaves that glow faintly in the dark. This species has fascinated botanists due to its unique adaptations to extreme environments. Despite its beauty, little is known about its reproductive habits and ecological role. Its discovery has sparked renewed interest in studying plant life in harsh climates.
