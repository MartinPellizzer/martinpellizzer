Tupistra stoliczana

Tupistra stoliczana, commonly known as the Stoliczka's stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
