Tulbaghia violacea var. obtusa

Tulbaghia violacea var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved star jasmine, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, star-shaped white flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
