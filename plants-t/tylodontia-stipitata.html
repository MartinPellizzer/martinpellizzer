Tylodontia stipitata

Tylodontia stipitata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and decaying organic matter.

Tylodontia stipitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
