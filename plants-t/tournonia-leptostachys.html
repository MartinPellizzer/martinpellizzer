Tournonia leptostachys

Tournonia leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stemmed tournonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and compact size.
