Tilia × praelustris

Tilia × praelustris, commonly known as the European whitebeam, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forest edges and woodland areas.

Tilia × praelustris is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in European landscapes.
