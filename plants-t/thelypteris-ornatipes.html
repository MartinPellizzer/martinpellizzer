Thelypteris ornatipes

Thelypteris ornatipes, commonly known as the ornate shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped fronds that are often adorned with a distinctive, ornate pattern of sori. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of foliage.

Thelypteris ornatipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
