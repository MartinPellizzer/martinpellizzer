Terminalia boivinii var. chlorophylla

Terminalia boivinii var. chlorophylla, commonly known as the green terminalia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and smooth, grayish bark. The leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
