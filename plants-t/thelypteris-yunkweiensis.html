Thelypteris yunkweiensis

Thelypteris yunkweiensis, commonly known as Yunkwei shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of foliage.

The fronds are usually bipinnate, with a distinctive shield-shaped base that gives the plant its common name.
