Taenitis Flabellivenia

Taenitis flabellivenia is a rare and striking fern native to the wet tropical forests of Southeast Asia. Known for its delicate, fan-like fronds, this plant is often referred to as the "fan fern" due to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is highly valued for its ornamental qualities in botanical gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species within the fern family. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this plant from habitat loss and overharvesting.
