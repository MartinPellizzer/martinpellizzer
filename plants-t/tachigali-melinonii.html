Tachigali melinonii

Tachigali melinonii, commonly known as the red tachigali, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous plants, and it produces distinctive pod-like fruits that contain seeds. The tree is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can grow up to 20 meters in height.

Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils in lowland rainforests.
