Tylecodon wallichii subsp. ecklonianus

Tylecodon wallichii subsp.

ecklonianus, commonly known as the Ecklon's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted for water storage.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand frost, though it may require protection in colder climates.
