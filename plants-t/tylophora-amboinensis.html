Tylophora amboinensis

Tylophora amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina star vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are follicles that release seeds with tufts of hair, aiding in wind dispersal.

Tylophora amboinensis is often found in tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
