Tulipa oreophila

Tulipa oreophila, commonly known as the mountain tulip, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its bulbs are adapted to survive harsh climatic conditions, allowing it to thrive in its alpine environment.

Tulipa oreophila is a perennial herb that blooms in spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
