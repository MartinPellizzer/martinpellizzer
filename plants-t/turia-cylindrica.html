Turia cylindrica

Turia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical turia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, making it an attractive ornamental plant.

Turia cylindrica is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
