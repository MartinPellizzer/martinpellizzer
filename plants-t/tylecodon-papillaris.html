Tylecodon papillaris

Tylecodon papillaris, commonly known as the woolly ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a decorative touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
