Thunbergia geraniifolia

Thunbergia geraniifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf morning glory, is a tropical vine native to Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a climbing plant.

The flowers bloom in the morning and close in the afternoon, giving it its common name.
