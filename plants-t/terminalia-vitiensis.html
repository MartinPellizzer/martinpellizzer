Terminalia vitiensis

Terminalia vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by distinctive, three-lobed fruits that contain a single seed.
