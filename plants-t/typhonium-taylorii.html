Typhonium taylorii

Typhonium taylorii, commonly known as the Taylor's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped inflorescences with bright yellow spadices. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
