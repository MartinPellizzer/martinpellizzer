Thelycrania armassica

Thelycrania armassica, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Thelycrania armassica is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
