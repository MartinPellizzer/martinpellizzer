Thuja occidentalis f. aurea

Thuja occidentalis f. aurea, commonly known as the golden thuja, is a cultivar of the eastern white cedar.

It is characterized by its distinctive golden-yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its green stems. This ornamental plant is prized for its compact growth habit and year-round visual interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for hedges and landscape accents.

The plant's foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, adding a unique touch to gardens and landscapes.
