Tournefortia ramosissima

Tournefortia ramosissima, commonly known as the shrub of the genus Tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, characterized by its dense, branching structure. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often with white or yellow petals.

Tournefortia ramosissima is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or dry woodland habitats.
