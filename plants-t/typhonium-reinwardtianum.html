Typhonium reinwardtianum

Typhonium reinwardtianum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or red spadices surrounded by a green bract, which is typical of the Araceae family. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.

The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.
