Tylecodon tenuis

Tylecodon tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Tylecodon tenuis is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
