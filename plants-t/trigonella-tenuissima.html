Trigonella tenuissima

Trigonella tenuissima, commonly known as narrowleaf fenugreek, is a small annual legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces slender pods containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
