Tabernaemontana graciliflora

Tabernaemontana graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered star jasmine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, jasmine-like scent. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and its stems are typically slender and somewhat woody. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
