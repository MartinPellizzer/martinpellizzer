Trema paucinervium

Trema paucinervium, commonly known as the narrow-veined trema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a prominent midrib and fine, parallel veins. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish or white in color.

Trema paucinervium is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
