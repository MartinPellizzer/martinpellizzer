Thalictrum uncatum var. angustialatum

Thalictrum uncatum var. angustialatum, commonly known as narrowleaf meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
