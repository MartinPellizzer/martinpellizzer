Thesium nigricans

Thesium nigricans, commonly known as black thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

Thesium nigricans is noted for its ability to withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy species in challenging climates.
