Tecoma nodosa

Tecoma nodosa, commonly known as the clustered trumpet tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a rough texture.

Tecoma nodosa is valued for its ornamental flowers and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant display.
