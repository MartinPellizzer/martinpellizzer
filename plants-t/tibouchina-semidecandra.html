Tibouchina semidecandra

Tibouchina semidecandra, commonly known as the pink hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring broad, glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Tibouchina semidecandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and gardens.
