Triumfetta micrantha

Triumfetta micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a small shrub or herbaceous plant with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and typically have five petals, with colors ranging from white to pale pink. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
