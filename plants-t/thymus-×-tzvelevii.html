Thymus × tzvelevii

Thymus × tzvelevii, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It features aromatic, narrow, and slightly hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in herbal remedies.
