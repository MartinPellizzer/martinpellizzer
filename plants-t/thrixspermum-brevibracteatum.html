Thrixspermum brevibracteatum

Thrixspermum brevibracteatum, commonly known as the short-bract thrixspermum, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, often with distinctive bracts. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it an attractive choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
