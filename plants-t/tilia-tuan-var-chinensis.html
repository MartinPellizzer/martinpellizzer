Tilia tuan var. chinensis

Tilia tuan var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese linden, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain hairy seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age, and the wood is lightweight and used in traditional crafts.
