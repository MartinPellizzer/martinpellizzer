Thymus notarisii

Thymus notarisii, commonly known as the notarisii thyme, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Thymus notarisii is often found in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun.

Its fragrant leaves are used in traditional herbal remedies and as a culinary herb.
