Thelypteris sumatrana

Thelypteris sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically leathery and dark green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are extensively branched and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

Thelypteris sumatrana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
