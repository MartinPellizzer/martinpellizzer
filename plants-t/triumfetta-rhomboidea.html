Triumfetta rhomboidea

Triumfetta rhomboidea, commonly known as the roundleaf triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to round in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental plant.
