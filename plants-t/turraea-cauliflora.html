Turraea cauliflora

Turraea cauliflora, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique cauliflorous flowering pattern, where flowers emerge directly from the trunk and branches. The plant produces large, white, spherical clusters of flowers that resemble snowballs, giving it its common name. Turraea cauliflora is also known for its attractive, glossy, dark green leaves.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
