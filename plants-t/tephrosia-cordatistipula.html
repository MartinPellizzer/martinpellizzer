Tephrosia cordatistipula

Tephrosia cordatistipula, commonly known as the heart-shaped leaf tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often heart-shaped, and its clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Tephrosia cordatistipula is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
