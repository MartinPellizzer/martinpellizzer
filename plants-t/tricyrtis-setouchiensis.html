Tricyrtis setouchiensis

Tricyrtis setouchiensis, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30–60 cm, with lance-shaped leaves and a rhizomatous root system. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive hooded lip that resembles a spider’s body.

Tricyrtis setouchiensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
