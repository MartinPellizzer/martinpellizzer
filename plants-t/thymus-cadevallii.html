Thymus cadevallii

Thymus cadevallii, commonly known as the Cadevall thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental and herbal gardening.
