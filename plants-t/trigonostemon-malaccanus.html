Trigonostemon malaccanus

Trigonostemon malaccanus, commonly known as the Malacca trigonostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
