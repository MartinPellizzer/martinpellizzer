Tristemon conglomeratus

Tristemon conglomeratus, commonly known as the clustered tristemon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Tristemon conglomeratus is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along riverbanks.
