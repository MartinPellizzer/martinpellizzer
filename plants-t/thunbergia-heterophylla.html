Thunbergia heterophylla

Thunbergia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which can range from ovate to heart-shaped, depending on the environment. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the warm seasons. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses in regions with a suitable climate.
