Thysanotus nanus

Thysanotus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf bluebell, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a white throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem, contributing to its compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Thysanotus nanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
