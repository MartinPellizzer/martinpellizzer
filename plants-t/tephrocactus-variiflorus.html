Tephrocactus variiflorus

Tephrocactus variiflorus, commonly known as the variable flowered cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
