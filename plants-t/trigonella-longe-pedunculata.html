Trigonella longe-pedunculata

Trigonella longe-pedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed fenugreek, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers, which bloom in late spring. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. Native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and medicinal studies.
