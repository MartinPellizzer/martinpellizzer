Thlaspi alpestre subsp. virens

Thlaspi alpestre subsp.

virens, commonly known as Alpine cabbages, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring rocky or gravelly soils. It is well adapted to cold climates and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions. Thlaspi alpestre subsp.

virens is often found in mountainous areas across Europe and parts of Asia.
