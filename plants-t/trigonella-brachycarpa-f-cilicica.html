Trigonella brachycarpa f. cilicica

Trigonella brachycarpa f. cilicica, commonly known as the Cilicia fenugreek, is a perennial herb belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils.

This subspecies is native to the Mediterranean basin, including parts of Turkey and Syria.
