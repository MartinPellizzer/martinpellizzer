Trachelospermum tulinense

Trachelospermum tulinense, commonly known as the Tulinense Star Jasmine, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Trachelospermum tulinense is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb trellises or walls.
