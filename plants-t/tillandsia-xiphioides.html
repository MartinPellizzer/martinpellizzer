Tillandsia xiphioides

Tillandsia xiphioides, commonly known as the silver vase, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

This air plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and it absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves. Its rosette-shaped foliage is covered in dense, silver-gray trichomes that help it retain water and reflect sunlight. It produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
