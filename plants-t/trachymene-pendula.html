Trachymene pendula

Trachymene pendula, commonly known as the hanging trachymene, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Trachymene pendula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
