Tesota

Tesota, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical with a smooth margin.

The tree is often found in lowland forests and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
