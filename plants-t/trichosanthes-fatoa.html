Trichosanthes fatoa

Trichosanthes fatoa, commonly known as the African horned melon, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It produces large, spiky, green to yellowish fruits that are edible when ripe. The plant belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and squash. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.
