Tulipa marjolletii

Tulipa marjolletii, commonly known as the Marjollet's tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is a perennial bulbous plant that produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically blooms in early spring and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Tulipa marjolletii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
