Thunbergia amanensis

Thunbergia amanensis, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Thunbergia amanensis prefers well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.
