Thymus pulegioides var. pilisiensis

Thymus pulegioides var. pilisiensis, commonly known as the Pilis thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun. It is often found in mountainous regions, particularly in the Pilis Mountains.

This variety is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, as well as its ornamental appeal in gardens.
