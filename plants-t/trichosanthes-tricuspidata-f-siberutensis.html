Trichosanthes tricuspidata f. siberutensis

Trichosanthes tricuspidata f. siberutensis, commonly known as the Siberian trumpet vine, is a variant of the common trichosanthes species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is native to regions of East Asia, including parts of China and Siberia, and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
