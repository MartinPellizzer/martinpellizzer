Titanodendron klinkii

Titanodendron klinkii, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a striking member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its massive, tree-like structure with a thick, fibrous trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and can be used in traditional medicine by local communities.

The plant's unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in botanical studies.
