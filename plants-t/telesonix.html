Telesonix

Telesonix, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces fruits that are typically orange or red when mature. Telesonix thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique flowering pattern makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
