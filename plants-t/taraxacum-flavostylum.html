Taraxacum flavostylum

Taraxacum flavostylum, commonly known as the yellow-rayed dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant is native to parts of East Asia, including China and Japan. It produces a distinctive seed head with numerous parachute-like seeds for wind dispersal.

The species is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
