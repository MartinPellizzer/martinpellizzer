Taraxacum ohirense

Taraxacum ohirense, commonly known as the Ohiren dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped basal leaves. The plant produces a milky sap that contains various bioactive compounds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
