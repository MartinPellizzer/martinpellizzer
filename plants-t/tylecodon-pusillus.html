Tylecodon pusillus

Tylecodon pusillus, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a small succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Tylecodon pusillus is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
