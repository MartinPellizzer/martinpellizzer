Tabernaemontana hybrida

Tabernaemontana hybrida, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, simple, and often glossy dark green leaves. It is commonly cultivated as an ornamental plant for its pleasant scent and attractive appearance.

Tabernaemontana hybrida prefers warm, humid conditions and is often grown in hanging baskets or as a patio plant.
