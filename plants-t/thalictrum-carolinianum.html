Thalictrum carolinianum

Thalictrum carolinianum, commonly known as the Carolina avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white, insect-pollinated flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and along stream banks. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and establish in suitable habitats.

Thalictrum carolinianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for pollinators and wildlife.
