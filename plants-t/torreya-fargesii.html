Torreya fargesii

Torreya fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese torreya, is a deciduous conifer native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It is characterized by its slender, evergreen leaves that are arranged spirally along its branches. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by oval, fleshy berries. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
