Thelypteris degeneri

Thelypteris degeneri, commonly known as the Degener's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and the production of fertile fronds with sporangia on the undersides of the leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually bipinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Thelypteris degeneri is native to parts of Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
