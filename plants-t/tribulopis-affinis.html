Tribulopis affinis

Tribulopis affinis, commonly known as the African burr, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its fruit is a spiny, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and the plant is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
