Trichosalpinx acestrochila

Trichosalpinx acestrochila, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, preferring shaded and humid environments. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Trichosalpinx acestrochila is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
