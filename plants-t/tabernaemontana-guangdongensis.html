Tabernaemontana guangdongensis

Tabernaemontana guangdongensis, commonly known as Guangdong starry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though care must be taken due to its poisonous nature.
