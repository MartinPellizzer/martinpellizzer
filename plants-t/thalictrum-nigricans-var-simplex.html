Thalictrum nigricans var. simplex

Thalictrum nigricans var. simplex, commonly known as the blackstem meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its stems are usually dark purple or black, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and along stream banks.
