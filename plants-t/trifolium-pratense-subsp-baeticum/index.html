Trifolium Pratense Subsp. Baeticum

Trifolium pratense subsp. baeticum is a lesser-known variant of red clover native to the Iberian Peninsula. It thrives in the unique microclimates of the Sierra de Baeticas mountain range. This subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flowering habits. While not as widely cultivated as other clover species, it holds ecological significance in local habitats. Its study offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in Mediterranean ecosystems.
