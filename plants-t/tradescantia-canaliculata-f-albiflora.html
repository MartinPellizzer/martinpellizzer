Tradescantia canaliculata f. albiflora

Tradescantia canaliculata f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered spiderwort, is a cultivar of the spiderwort plant characterized by its striking white flowers.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is native to the southeastern United States. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. Its flowers bloom in clusters, adding a delicate and elegant appearance to gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions.
