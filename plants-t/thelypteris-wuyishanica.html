Thelypteris wuyishanica

Thelypteris wuyishanica, commonly known as the Wuyi mountain holly fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, hairy structures.

Thelypteris wuyishanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
