Tibouchina granulosa var. angustifolia

Tibouchina granulosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a desirable addition to both landscapes and containers.
