Torenia fimbriata

Torenia fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a distinctive fringed edge. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Torenia fimbriata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
