Thalictrum urbaini var. majus

Thalictrum urbaini var. majus, commonly known as the showy meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Thalictrum urbaini var. majus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and providing habitat for small organisms.
