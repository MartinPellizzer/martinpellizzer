Typhonium stigmatilobatum

Typhonium stigmatilobatum, commonly known as the spotted arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive greenish-white variegation pattern. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
