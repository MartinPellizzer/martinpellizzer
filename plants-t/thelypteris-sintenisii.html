Thelypteris sintenisii

Thelypteris sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenisi shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically divided into smaller, serrated segments. The plant produces spores on the underside of its fronds, which are arranged in clusters called sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Thelypteris sintenisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
