Thelymitra lucida

Thelymitra lucida, commonly known as the sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open forests or heathlands. The plant features a single, erect stem that bears a single, showy flower with a yellowish-green to reddish hue. Its flowers are characterized by a distinctive hood-like structure and are pollinated by native bees and wasps.

Thelymitra lucida is a short-lived perennial, with its life cycle spanning several years.
