Tithymalus capensis

Tithymalus capensis, commonly known as the Cape tithymale, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Tithymalus capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
