Tetratheca erubescens

Tetratheca erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered tetras, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its showy, red or orange-red flowers that bloom in spring. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
