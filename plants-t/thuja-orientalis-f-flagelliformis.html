Thuja orientalis f. flagelliformis

Thuja orientalis f. flagelliformis, commonly known as the Chinese thuja or the weeping thuja, is a unique cultivar of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its cascading, weeping growth habit, with long, drooping branches that create a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The foliage consists of scale-like leaves that are bright green, providing a dense, evergreen cover. This plant is highly adaptable to various soil types and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to form a natural, flowing shape.
