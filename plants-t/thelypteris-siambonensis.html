Thelypteris siambonensis

Thelypteris siambonensis, commonly known as the Siamese shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, strap-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its spores are released from sori located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris siambonensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
