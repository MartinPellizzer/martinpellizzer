Tetrapterys includens

Tetrapterys includens, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

Its wood is highly valued for its durability and rich reddish color, though it is also listed as endangered due to overharvesting.

The species plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
