Triticum durum convar. thumanjanii

Triticum durum convar. thumanjanii, commonly known as the durum wheat variant Thumanjanii, is a botanical variety within the wheat genus Triticum.

It is characterized by its unique morphological features, including compact spikes and smaller grains compared to standard durum wheat. This variety is primarily found in specific geographic regions, often in areas with particular climatic conditions. Its genetic makeup contributes to its adaptability and resilience to environmental stressors.

The plant's botanical significance lies in its role in genetic research and its potential for breeding programs aimed at improving wheat varieties.
