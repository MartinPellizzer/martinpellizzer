Trichilia triphylla

Trichilia triphylla, commonly known as the three-leafed trichilia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. Trichilia triphylla is valued for its durable timber and is also used in traditional medicine.
