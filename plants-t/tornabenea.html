Tornabenea

Tornabenea, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a vigorous climber with long, twisting stems that can reach several meters in length. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Tornabenea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
