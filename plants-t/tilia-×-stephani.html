Tilia × stephani

Tilia × stephani, commonly known as the Hungarian lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically medium to dark green on the upper surface and paler underneath. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer and are followed by small, round fruits. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and shade-providing capabilities.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
