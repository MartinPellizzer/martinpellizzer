Thelypteris ctenitoides

Thelypteris ctenitoides, commonly known as the feather fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brownish, hairy scales.

Thelypteris ctenitoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
