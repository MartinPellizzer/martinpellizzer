Tomanthea insignis

Tomanthea insignis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Tomanthea insignis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
