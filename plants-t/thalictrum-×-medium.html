Thalictrum × medium

Thalictrum × medium, commonly known as the hybrid meadow rue, is a perennial plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, usually white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a shade-tolerant, ornamental plant.
