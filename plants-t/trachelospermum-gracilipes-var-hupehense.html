Trachelospermum gracilipes var. hupehense

Trachelospermum gracilipes var. hupehense, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is also valued for its ability to climb and cover trellises or walls effectively.
