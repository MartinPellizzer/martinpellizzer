Trichotolinum

Trichotolinum, commonly known as the woolly thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly coating on its stems and leaves, which provides a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators. Trichotolinum is native to regions of Europe and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and visually striking in its natural habitat.
