Trichosanthes pachyrrhachis

Trichosanthes pachyrrhachis, commonly known as the thick-stemmed bitter gourd, is a vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into elongated, bitter-tasting fruits. Its fruit is typically used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires a long growing season to mature.
