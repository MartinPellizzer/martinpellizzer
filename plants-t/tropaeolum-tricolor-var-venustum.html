Tropaeolum tricolor var. venustum

Tropaeolum tricolor var. venustum, commonly known as the "Emerald Queen" or "Variegated Marvel," is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and red. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest.

Its compact growth habit and ability to spread via runners make it suitable for container gardening or as a ground cover.
