Torenia cardiosepala

Torenia cardiosepala, commonly known as the heartleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Torenia cardiosepala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
