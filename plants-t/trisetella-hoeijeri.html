Trisetella hoeijeri

Trisetella hoeijeri, commonly known as the Cape flower, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, linear leaves.

Trisetella hoeijeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
