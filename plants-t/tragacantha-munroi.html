Tragacantha munroi

Tragacantha munroi, commonly known as the Munro's tragacanth, is a species of flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

The plant has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
