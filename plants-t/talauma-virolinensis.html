Talauma virolinensis, commonly known as the velvetleaf talauma, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hair. Talauma virolinensis is often found in moist, shaded environments in Southeast Asia.
