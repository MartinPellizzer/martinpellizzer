Thelypteris Ligulata

Thelypteris ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf holly fern, is a unique and elegant member of the Dryopteridaceae family. Found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its delicate fronds feature a distinctive narrow, lance-shaped form that sets it apart from other fern species. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses. With its lush green foliage and graceful appearance, Thelypteris ligulata adds a touch of natural beauty to any setting.
