Thelymitra granitora

Thelymitra granitora, commonly known as the granite orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers that can reach up to 20 centimeters in height.
