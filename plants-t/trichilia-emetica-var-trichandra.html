Trichilia emetica var. trichandra

Trichilia emetica var. trichandra, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its white, spherical inflorescences that resemble snowballs, blooming in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional medicine.
