Thelypteris morotaiensis

Thelypteris morotaiensis, commonly known as Morotai shield fern, is a species of fern native to the island of Morotai in Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along forest floors or in rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a dark green color with a slightly rough texture.

Thelypteris morotaiensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited geographic distribution.
