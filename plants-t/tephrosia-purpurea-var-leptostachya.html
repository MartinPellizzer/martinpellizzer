Tephrosia purpurea var. leptostachya

Tephrosia purpurea var. leptostachya, commonly known as purple tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and pairs of opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
