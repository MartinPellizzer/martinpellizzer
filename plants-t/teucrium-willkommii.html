Teucrium willkommii

Teucrium willkommii, commonly known as the willkomm's germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or blue, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Teucrium willkommii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
