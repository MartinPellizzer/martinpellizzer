Tema

Tema, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Tema is often cultivated for its vibrant colors and compact growth habit, making it ideal for indoor gardening.

Its flowers typically bloom in shades of purple, blue, pink, and white, adding a touch of elegance to any space.
