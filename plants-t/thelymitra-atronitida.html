Thelymitra atronitida

Thelymitra atronitida, commonly known as the black-veined sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that has dark purple to black petals and sepals. Its leaves are grass-like, and the flower has a distinctive, intricate structure typical of the Orchidaceae family.

Thelymitra atronitida is known for its striking coloration and is often found in areas with mild, temperate climates.
