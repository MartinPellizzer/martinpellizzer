Tetrastigma yiwuense

Tetrastigma yiwuense, commonly known as the Yiwu crossvine, is a woody vine native to China.

It belongs to the grape family (Vitaceae) and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by red berries that are edible. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.
