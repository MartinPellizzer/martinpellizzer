Trimezia spathata

Trimezia spathata, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial herb that grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often on coastal cliffs. The plant features a rosette of broad, silver-blue leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. It produces striking violet-blue flowers in late spring to early summer.

Trimezia spathata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
