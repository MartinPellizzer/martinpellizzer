Trichotosia Brachiata

Trichotosia brachiata is a striking member of the Malvaceae family known for its vibrant, feather-like flowers. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. Its unique morphology includes long, branched inflorescences that resemble intricate lacework. Often cultivated for ornamental purposes, it adds a touch of exotic beauty to gardens and greenhouses. Despite its aesthetic appeal, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
