Tilia chinensis var. intonsa

Tilia chinensis var. intonsa, commonly known as the Chinese linden, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically dispersed by wind. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The bark is smooth and greyish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
