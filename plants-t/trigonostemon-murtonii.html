Trigonostemon murtonii

Trigonostemon murtonii, commonly known as the Malabar ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale yellow. The plant produces woody capsules as fruits, which contain small, hard seeds. Trigonostemon murtonii is valued for its durable wood and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various forms of wildlife.
