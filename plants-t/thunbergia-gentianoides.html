Thunbergia gentianoides

Thunbergia gentianoides, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that resemble those of the gentian family. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and can grow as a vine or shrub, depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia gentianoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
