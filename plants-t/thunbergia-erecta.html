Thunbergia erecta

Thunbergia erecta, commonly known as the blue shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing or climbing habit, with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are usually green with a slight pubescence. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia erecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
