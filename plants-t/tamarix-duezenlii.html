Tamarix duezenlii

Tamarix duezenlii, commonly known as the Turkish tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its feathery, gray-green foliage and pink to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss, making it well adapted to arid environments. It is often found in coastal areas and saline soils due to its tolerance for salt and drought.

Tamarix duezenlii is also known for its rapid growth and ability to stabilize sandy soils.
