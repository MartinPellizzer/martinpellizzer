Taraxacum badachschanicum

Taraxacum badachschanicum, commonly known as the Badakshan dandelion, is a flowering plant species native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and milky sap. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are typically lobed or toothed. Its flowering stems bear a single, showy yellow flower head composed of numerous small florets.

This species is often found in meadows and rocky areas, thriving in temperate climates.
