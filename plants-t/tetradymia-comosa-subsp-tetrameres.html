Tetradymia comosa subsp. tetrameres

Tetradymia comosa subsp.

tetrameres, commonly known as the woolly tetrads, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage.
