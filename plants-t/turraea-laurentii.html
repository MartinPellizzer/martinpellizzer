Turraea laurentii

Turraea laurentii, commonly known as African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. It produces clusters of white, waxy flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in the rainy season.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hard seeds.
