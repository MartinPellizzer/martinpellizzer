Trillium gracile f. luteum

Trillium gracile f. luteum, commonly known as the yellow starflower, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Trilliaceae family and is characterized by its three narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a single, showy yellow flower that blooms in early spring.
