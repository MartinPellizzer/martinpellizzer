Tunica velutina

Tunica velutina, commonly known as the velvet tunic, is a rare and unique plant characterized by its distinctive outer layer.

This layer, known as the tunica, is covered in a fine, soft, velvety texture that gives the plant its name. The botanical structure of Tunica velutina features a compact growth habit with tightly packed leaves. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments, often in temperate regions.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
