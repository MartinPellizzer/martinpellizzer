Turnera candida

Turnera candida, commonly known as the white turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a tropical look.
