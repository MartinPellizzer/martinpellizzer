Trigonotis mollis

Trigonotis mollis, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in coastal areas and can thrive in well-drained environments.

Trigonotis mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
