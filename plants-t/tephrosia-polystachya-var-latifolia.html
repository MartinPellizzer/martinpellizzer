Tephrosia polystachya var. latifolia

Tephrosia polystachya var. latifolia, commonly known as broad-leaved tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with broad, lance-shaped leaflets and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain small, hard seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
