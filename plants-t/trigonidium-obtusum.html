Trigonidium obtusum

Trigonidium obtusum, commonly known as the broad-leaved hollyhock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, and its heart-shaped leaves. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
