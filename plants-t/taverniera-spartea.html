Taverniera spartea

Taverniera spartea, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Taverniera spartea is often found in rocky or coastal habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
