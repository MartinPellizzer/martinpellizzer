Thymus targuistii

Thymus targuistii, commonly known as the Târgușor thyme, is a perennial herb native to southern Romania.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Thymus targuistii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
