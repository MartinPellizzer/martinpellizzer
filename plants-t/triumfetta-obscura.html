Triumfetta obscura

Triumfetta obscura, commonly known as the dark triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a small, shrub-like species that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and dry open areas.
