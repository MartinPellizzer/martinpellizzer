Thunbergia similis

Thunbergia similis, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia similis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
