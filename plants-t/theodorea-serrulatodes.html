Theodorea serrulatodes

Theodorea serrulatodes, commonly known as the serrulate theodorea, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Theodorea serrulatodes requires well-drained soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
