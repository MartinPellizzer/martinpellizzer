Terminalia sericocarpa

Terminalia sericocarpa, commonly known as the silky terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive, silky-haired fruits that are edible.
