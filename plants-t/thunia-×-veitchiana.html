Thunia × veitchiana

Thunia × veitchiana, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Thunia grandiflora and Thunia sibirica, resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It is typically grown as a perennial in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

Thunia × veitchiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
