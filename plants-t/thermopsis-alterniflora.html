Thermopsis alterniflora

Thermopsis alterniflora, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, elongated flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for wildflower gardens and ecological restoration projects.
