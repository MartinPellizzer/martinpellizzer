Tephrosia laurentii

Tephrosia laurentii, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as savannas and woodland edges. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are dispersed by wind or animals.

Tephrosia laurentii is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
