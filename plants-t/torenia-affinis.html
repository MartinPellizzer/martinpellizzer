Torenia affinis

Torenia affinis, commonly known as the common torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Torenia affinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a bedding plant.
