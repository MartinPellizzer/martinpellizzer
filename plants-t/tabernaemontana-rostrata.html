Tabernaemontana rostrata

Tabernaemontana rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its showy, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters.
