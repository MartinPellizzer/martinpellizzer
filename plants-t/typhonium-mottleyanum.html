Typhonium mottleyanum

Typhonium mottleyanum, commonly known as the Mottled Typhonium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often mottled with contrasting colors. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions as a houseplant or garden specimen.
