Trichosalpinx zunagensis

Trichosalpinx zunagensis, commonly known as the Zunagense spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, thin petals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its flowers are usually a vibrant yellow or orange, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
