Triumfetta clivorum subsp. clivorum

Triumfetta clivorum subsp.

clivorum, commonly known as the rock triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions.

The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are usually small, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.
