Turraea graciliflora

Turraea graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered turraea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with yellow or greenish petals.

Turraea graciliflora produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, making it an important species in its native ecosystems.
