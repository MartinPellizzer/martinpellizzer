Thelypteris parasitica var. formosanus

Thelypteris parasitica var. formosanus, commonly known as the Formosan rock fern, is a variant of the rock fern genus Thelypteris.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its wiry, creeping rhizomes and small, clustered fronds. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dark green color with a waxy surface. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky or forested areas.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Taiwan and the Philippines.
