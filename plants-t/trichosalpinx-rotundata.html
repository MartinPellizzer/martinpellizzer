Trichosalpinx rotundata

Trichosalpinx rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with small, colorful blossoms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Trichosalpinx rotundata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
