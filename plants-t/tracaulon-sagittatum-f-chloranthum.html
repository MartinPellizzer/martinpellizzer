Tracaulon sagittatum f. chloranthum

Tracaulon sagittatum f. chloranthum, commonly known as the chloranthum form of the arrowhead plant, is a variant of the species Tracaulon sagittatum.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This form is notable for its distinct leaf coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in shaded garden settings.
