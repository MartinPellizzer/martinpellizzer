Tradescantia × cultorum

Tradescantia × cultorum, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Commelinaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive red or purple stripe down the center. The plant typically grows as a low-growing perennial, with succulent, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.

Tradescantia × cultorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and as a houseplant.
