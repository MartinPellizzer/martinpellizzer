Trichospermum subdehiscens

Trichospermum subdehiscens, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits. Its latex sap is milky and can cause skin irritation, making it a plant with both ornamental and cautionary value.

Trichospermum subdehiscens is often cultivated for its unique appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
