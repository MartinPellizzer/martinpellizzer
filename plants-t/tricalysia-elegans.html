Tricalysia elegans

Tricalysia elegans, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, purple-tinged leaves that are opposite and elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing flat, edible seeds. Tricalysia elegans thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
