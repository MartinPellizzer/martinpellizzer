Trichomanes vamana

Trichomanes vamana, commonly known as the dwarf moonwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for undisturbed, humid habitats.
