Tylecodon paniculatus subsp. paniculatus

Tylecodon paniculatus subsp.

paniculatus, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
