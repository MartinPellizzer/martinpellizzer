Taraxacum horridifrons

Taraxacum horridifrons, commonly known as the rough-horned dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Afghanistan. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
