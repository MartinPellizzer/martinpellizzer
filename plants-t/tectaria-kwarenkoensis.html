Tectaria kwarenkoensis

Tectaria kwarenkoensis, commonly known as the Kwarenko shield fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a leathery texture and a rugged, shield-like appearance.

Tectaria kwarenkoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
