Thalictrum elatum lusus acuminatum

Thalictrum elatum lusus acuminatum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves with a slender, upright stem that bears small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers are clustered in terminal panicles, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is characterized by its finely divided foliage and subtle, inconspicuous blooms.
