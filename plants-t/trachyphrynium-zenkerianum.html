Trachyphrynium zenkerianum

Trachyphrynium zenkerianum, commonly known as the Zambian lily, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, large, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Trachyphrynium zenkerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
