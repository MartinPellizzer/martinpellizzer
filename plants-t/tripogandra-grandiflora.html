Tripogandra grandiflora

Tripogandra grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered tripogandra, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and woodland habitats.
