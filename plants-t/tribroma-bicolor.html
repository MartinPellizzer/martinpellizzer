Tribroma bicolor

Tribroma bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored tribroma, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad native to the cloud forests of South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that displays two distinct shades, usually green and red or purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
