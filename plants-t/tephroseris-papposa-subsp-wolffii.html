Tephroseris papposa subsp. wolffii

Tephroseris papposa subsp.

wolffii, commonly known as the woolly tephroseris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, woolly-haired leaves and small, daisy-like flowers.
