Tylecodon hallii

Tylecodon hallii, commonly known as the Hartmann's iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the cooler months, usually in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Tylecodon hallii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
