Tillandsia lindenii var. vera

Tillandsia lindenii var. vera, commonly known as the silver star tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces striking, star-shaped inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, adding ornamental value.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
