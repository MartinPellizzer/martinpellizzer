Trichocentrum maculatum f. flavovirens

Trichocentrum maculatum f. flavovirens, commonly known as the golden green orchid, is a unique hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, which features a vibrant yellow-green coloration on the leaves, making it highly ornamental. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat, and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months.

Due to its distinctive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
