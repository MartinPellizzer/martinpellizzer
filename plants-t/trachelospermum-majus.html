Trachelospermum majus

Trachelospermum majus, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a vine that produces fragrant, star-shaped white or pink flowers throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Trachelospermum majus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
