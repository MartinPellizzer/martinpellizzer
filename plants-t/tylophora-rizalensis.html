Tylophora rizalensis

Tylophora rizalensis, commonly known as the Rizal's tylophora, is a climbing shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
