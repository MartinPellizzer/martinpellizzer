Taraxacum schlobarum

Taraxacum schlobarum, commonly known as the Schlobar dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a yellow flowering head. The plant produces a distinctive seed head that disperses seeds via the wind, a common trait among dandelions. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is notable for its adaptability and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
