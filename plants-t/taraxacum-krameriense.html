Taraxacum krameriense

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces a distinctive white, fluffy seed head that aids in wind dispersal.
