Thlaspi lotharingum

Thlaspi lotharingum, commonly known as yellow pennycress, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by pods containing several seeds. It is often found growing in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and waste areas.

Its leaves are alternate, lobed, and have a slightly hairy texture.
