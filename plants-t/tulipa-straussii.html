Tulipa straussii

Tulipa straussii, commonly known as the Strauss tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces showy, single or double blooms that appear in early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.

Tulipa straussii is a popular ornamental plant valued for its striking appearance and relatively easy cultivation.
