Trichosanthes ovata

Trichosanthes ovata, commonly known as the Chinese lantern gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine that produces large, orange, lantern-shaped fruits. The plant is native to Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and tendrils aid in climbing.

The flowers are yellow with five petals and are typically followed by the distinctive, inflated fruits.
