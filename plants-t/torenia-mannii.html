Torenia mannii

Torenia mannii, commonly known as the velvetleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of small, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers bloom throughout the rainy season, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Torenia mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
