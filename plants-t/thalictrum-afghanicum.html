Thalictrum afghanicum

Thalictrum afghanicum, commonly known as Afghan meadow rue, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Afghanistan and surrounding regions. The plant features slender, branched stems and compound leaves with a delicate, fern-like appearance. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
