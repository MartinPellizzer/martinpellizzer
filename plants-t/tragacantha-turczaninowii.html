Tragacantha turczaninowii

Tragacantha turczaninowii, commonly known as the Turczaninow's tragacanth, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing small, dark seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Tragacantha turczaninowii is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic effects.
