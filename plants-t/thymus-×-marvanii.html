Thymus × marvanii

Thymus × marvanii, commonly known as marjoram thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus × marvanii is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, as well as its ornamental appeal.
