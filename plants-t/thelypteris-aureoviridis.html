Thelypteris aureoviridis

Thelypteris aureoviridis, commonly known as the golden sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its elegant, sword-shaped fronds that are typically bright green with a golden hue on the underside. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are pinnately compound, with serrated margins and a dense network of veins.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
