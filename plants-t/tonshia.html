Tonshia

Tonshia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
