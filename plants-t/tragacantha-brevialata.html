Tragacantha brevialata

Tragacantha brevialata, commonly known as the short-stemmed tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, upright growth habit. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by pod fruits containing small, dark seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Tragacantha brevialata is often found in rocky or dry soils and is valued for its medicinal and historical uses.
