Trichilia lecointei

Trichilia lecointei, commonly known as African shea tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa, particularly found in countries such as Nigeria, Cameroon, and Gabon. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and has a dense, rounded crown. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a pointed tip.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed that is valued for its oil, similar to shea butter.
