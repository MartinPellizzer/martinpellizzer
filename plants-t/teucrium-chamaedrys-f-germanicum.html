Teucrium chamaedrys f. germanicum

Teucrium chamaedrys f. germanicum, commonly known as German germander, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to gardens and landscapes.
