Torularia glandulosa

Torularia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular torula, is a small, annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant is characterized by its branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine, glandular hairs.

Torularia glandulosa is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
