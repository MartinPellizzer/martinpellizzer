Tillandsia geminiflora

Tillandsia geminiflora, commonly known as the twin-flowered air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that can store water, allowing it to thrive in humid environments. It produces striking inflorescences with small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Tillandsia geminiflora is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
