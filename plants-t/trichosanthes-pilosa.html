Trichosanthes pilosa

Trichosanthes pilosa, commonly known as the hairy bitter melon, is a vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces large, green, warty fruits that mature to a yellow-orange color. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and cooking, though it is known for its bitter taste.
