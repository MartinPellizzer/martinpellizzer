Thalictrum minus subsp. pratense

Thalictrum minus subsp.

pratense, commonly known as meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like compound leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers.

pratense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.
