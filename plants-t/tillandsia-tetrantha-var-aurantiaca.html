Tillandsia tetrantha var. aurantiaca

Tillandsia tetrantha var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Star Tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a striking orange or reddish hue. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among collectors due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
