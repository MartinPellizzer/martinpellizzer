Trigonella longirostris

Trigonella longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Trigonella longirostris is typically found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and herbal applications.
