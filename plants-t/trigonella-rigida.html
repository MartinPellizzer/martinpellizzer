Trigonella rigida

Trigonella rigida, commonly known as rigid trigonella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by narrow, elongated pods containing seeds. Native to regions of southern Europe and the Mediterranean, it thrives in dry, rocky soils.

Trigonella rigida is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
