Tanacetum tatsienense

Tanacetum tatsienense, commonly known as the Sichuan tansy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
