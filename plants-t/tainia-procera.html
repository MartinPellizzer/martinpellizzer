Tainia procera

Tainia procera, commonly known as the giant taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in diameter. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with purple veins, which bloom in clusters. Its rhizomes are edible and are used in various traditional cuisines.

Tainia procera thrives in wet, humid environments and is often found in marshy areas or near water bodies.
