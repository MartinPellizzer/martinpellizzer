Tripteris volkensii

Tripteris volkensii, commonly known as the African mountain fern, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Tripteridaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
