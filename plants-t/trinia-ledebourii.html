Trinia ledebourii

Trinia ledebourii, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is drought-tolerant and well-suited for xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.

Trinia ledebourii is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
