Tribulus dimidiatus

Tribulus dimidiatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf caltrop, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has distinctive yellow flowers and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture.

Tribulus dimidiatus is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
