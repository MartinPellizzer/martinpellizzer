Taraxacum tortilobum

Taraxacum tortilobum, commonly known as the twisted leaf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats across temperate regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
