Thymus sooi var. nyirsegensis

Thymus sooi var. nyirsegensis, commonly known as the Nyirseg thyme, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in dry and sunny conditions. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The compact growth habit and resilience to harsh climates make it a notable species in alpine flora.
