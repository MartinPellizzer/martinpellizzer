Tillandsia purpurascens

Tillandsia purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple-tinged Air Plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Central America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with a reddish or purplish hue. It produces striking violet or purple flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
