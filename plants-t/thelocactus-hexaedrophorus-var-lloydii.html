Thelocactus hexaedrophorus var. lloydii

Thelocactus hexaedrophorus var. lloydii, commonly known as the Lloyd's thorn cactus, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant contrast to its otherwise muted appearance.
