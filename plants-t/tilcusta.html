Tilcusta

Tilcusta, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by oval-shaped fruits containing edible seeds. Tilcusta thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
