Thunbergia eymae

Thunbergia eymae, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Thunbergia eymae prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
