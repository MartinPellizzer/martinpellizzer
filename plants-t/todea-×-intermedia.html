Todea × intermedia

Todea × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that produces small, clustered white flowers. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically variegated with yellow or white markings. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Todea × intermedia is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
