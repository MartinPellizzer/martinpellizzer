Thelypteris oreopteris

Thelypteris oreopteris, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern native to various regions in the Americas.

It is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds that often have a rough texture. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris oreopteris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
