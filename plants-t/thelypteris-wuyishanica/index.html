Thelypteris Wuyishanica

Thelypteris wuyishanica is a rare fern species native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is known for its delicate, finely divided fronds. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Thelypteris wuyishanica is considered endangered and requires protection.
