Tragacantha tschujensis

Tragacantha tschujensis, commonly known as the Tschujan tragacanth, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by the development of pod-like fruits containing seeds.

Tragacantha tschujensis is adapted to harsh environments, exhibiting drought resistance and the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through symbiotic bacteria in its root system.
