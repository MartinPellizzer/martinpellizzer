Tragacantha orbignyana

Tragacantha orbignyana, commonly known as the Spanish thorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces a sticky resin, similar to gum arabic, which has been used historically in various industries. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
