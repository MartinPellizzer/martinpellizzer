Tilesia Rubens

Tilesia rubens is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant red flowers and unique leaf patterns. Native to the rainforests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is often found growing on trees or in dense undergrowth, showcasing its adaptability to challenging conditions. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, Tilesia rubens remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
