Thymelaea dioica

Thymelaea dioica, commonly known as the silver thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Thymelaea dioica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
