Tricyrtis hirta var. parviflora

Tricyrtis hirta var. parviflora, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, spider-like flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a touch of elegance to the landscape.
