Tribulus brownii

It belongs to the family Zygophyllaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water.
