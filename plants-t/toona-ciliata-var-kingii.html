Toona ciliata var. kingii

Toona ciliata var. kingii, commonly known as the King's toona, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It is valued for its timber, which is lightweight and durable, and for its ornamental appeal.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
