Thymus × Oriolanus

Orleans thyme, scientifically known as Thymus × oriolanus, is a hybrid herbaceous plant native to the region of Orleans in France. It is prized for its aromatic foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for gardens and culinary use. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, exhibiting a range of color variations from silver to deep green. Its essential oils contribute to its use in both traditional medicine and cooking. With its historical significance and adaptability, Orleans thyme remains a valued plant in horticulture and herbalism.
