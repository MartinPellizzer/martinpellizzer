Thlaspi tasmanicum

Thlaspi tasmanicum, commonly known as Tasmanian rockcress, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania, Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features basal leaves that are usually rounded or heart-shaped, with a hairy surface. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are arranged in clusters.

Thlaspi tasmanicum is well adapted to the harsh, coastal environments of its native habitat.
