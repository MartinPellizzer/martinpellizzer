Thilachium heterophyllum

Thilachium heterophyllum, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a shrub-like appearance with woody stems. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Thilachium heterophyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
