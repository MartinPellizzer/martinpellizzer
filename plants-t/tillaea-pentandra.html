Tillaea pentandra

Tillaea pentandra, commonly known as the rock spurge, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and rocky environments in the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Tillaea pentandra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soils in harsh landscapes.
