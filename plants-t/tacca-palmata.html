Tacca palmata

Tacca palmata, commonly known as the bat's wing or sacred lotus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, paddle-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces striking, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive, bat-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Tacca palmata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in traditional medicine.
