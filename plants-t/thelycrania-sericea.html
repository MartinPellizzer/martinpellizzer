Thelycrania sericea

Thelycrania sericea, commonly known as the silky thelycrania, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
