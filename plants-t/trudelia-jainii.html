Trudelia jainii

Trudelia jainii, commonly known as the Jain's trudelia, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern India.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. Trudelia jainii thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
