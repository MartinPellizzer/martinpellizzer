Tragia rugosa

Tragia rugosa, commonly known as the rough tragia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, open habitats. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are often covered in fine, rough hairs. Tragia rugosa has a woody stem that becomes more pronounced as the plant matures.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
