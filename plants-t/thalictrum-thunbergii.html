Thalictrum thunbergii

Thalictrum thunbergii, commonly known as the meadow rue, is a perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to East Asia and features delicate, feathery foliage and showy, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and prefers moist, well-drained soils in partial to full shade. Its flowers are composed of numerous stamens and a single pistil, typical of the Ranunculaceae family.

Thalictrum thunbergii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits to pollinators.
