Tripleurospermum Transcaucasicum

Tripleurospermum transcaucasicum is a lesser-known flowering plant native to the Caucasus region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. This species is characterized by its slender stems and small, white or yellowish flowers. It thrives in rocky and mountainous environments, often growing in alpine meadows. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
