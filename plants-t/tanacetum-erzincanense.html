Tanacetum erzincanense

Tanacetum erzincanense, commonly known as Erzincan tansy, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. It is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
