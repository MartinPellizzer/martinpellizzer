Thunbergia schweinfurthii

Thunbergia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African bluebells, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue and purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia schweinfurthii is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or fences.
