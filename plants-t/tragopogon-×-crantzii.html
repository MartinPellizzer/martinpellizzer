Tragopogon × crantzii

Tragopogon × crantzii, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Tragopogon dubius and Tragopogon porrifolius.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a tuft of bristly seeds at the center of its flower head. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive lobed shape.

Tragopogon × crantzii is a perennial herb that is widely distributed across North America.
