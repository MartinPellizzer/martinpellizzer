Telosma cathayensis

Telosma cathayensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its long, trailing stems.

Telosma cathayensis is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes other ornamental and medicinal plants.
