Tetranema evoluta

Tetranema evoluta, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its helically twisted stems that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Tetranema evoluta is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive growth habit and flowering display.
