Triopterys rigida var. ovatifolia

Triopterys rigida var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed triopterys, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically broad and ovate in shape. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and unique floral structure.
