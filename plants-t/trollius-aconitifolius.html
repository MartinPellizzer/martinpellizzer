Trollius aconitifolius

Trollius aconitifolius, commonly known as the alpine buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features showy, yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Trollius aconitifolius is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
