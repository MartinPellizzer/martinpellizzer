Turbinicarpus ysabelae var. brevispinus

Turbinicarpus ysabelae var. brevispinus, commonly known as the Ysabel's Turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, rounded stem with prominent, spiny tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within the Cactaceae family.
