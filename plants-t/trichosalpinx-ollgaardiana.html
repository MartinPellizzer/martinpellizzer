Trichosalpinx ollgaardiana

Trichosalpinx ollgaardiana, commonly known as the Ollgaard's spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure that resembles a spider. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Trichosalpinx ollgaardiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
