Trichodesma cinereum

Trichodesma cinereum, commonly known as the silverleaf trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-grey pubescence, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal inflorescences, usually with five petals.

Trichodesma cinereum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
