Tephrosia kraussiana

Tephrosia kraussiana, commonly known as the blue tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple flowers, which are typically arranged in racemes. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Tephrosia kraussiana is also noted for its medicinal properties, used in traditional herbal remedies.
