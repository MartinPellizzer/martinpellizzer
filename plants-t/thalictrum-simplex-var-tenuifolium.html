Thalictrum simplex var. tenuifolium

Thalictrum simplex var. tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are narrow and finely divided, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces showy, sparsely arranged white or pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
