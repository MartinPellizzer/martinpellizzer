Teucrium fruticans var. latifolium

Teucrium fruticans var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
