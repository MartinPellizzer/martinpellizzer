Tamarix ladachensis

Tamarix ladachensis, commonly known as the Ladakh tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the cold, arid regions of Central and South Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces pink or reddish flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions and withstand extreme temperatures.

Tamarix ladachensis is also known for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in degraded landscapes.
