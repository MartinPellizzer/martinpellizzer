Thymus crenulatus

Thymus crenulatus, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a citrusy scent, and small pink or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. It is widely used in culinary applications and herbal medicine due to its essential oils.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
