Thiga

Thiga, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a low-growing herb with a short stem and rosette-like foliage. Thiga thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular houseplant worldwide.
