Terminalia uleana

Terminalia uleana, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by round, yellowish fruits. Terminalia uleana is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional African medicine.

Its wood is also used for construction and furniture due to its durability.
