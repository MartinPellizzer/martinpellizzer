Talguenea mollis

Talguenea mollis, commonly known as the soft talguenea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale blue flowers during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are covered in a fine, silky hair, giving them a soft texture.

Talguenea mollis is often found in dry, sunny grasslands and along rocky slopes.
