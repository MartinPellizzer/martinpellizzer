Tradescantia lanata

Tradescantia lanata, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Tradescantia lanata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to spread easily through runners.
