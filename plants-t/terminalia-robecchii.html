Terminalia robecchii

Terminalia robecchii, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain hard, smooth seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Terminalia robecchii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
