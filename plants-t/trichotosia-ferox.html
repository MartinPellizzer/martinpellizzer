Trichotosia ferox

Trichotosia ferox, commonly known as the deadly nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or purple in color. Its berries are bright red and contain toxic alkaloids, making it highly dangerous if ingested.

Despite its toxicity, it has been historically used in medicine and witchcraft.
