Tribulus micans

Tribulus micans, commonly known as the silver leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a silver or grayish underside, giving it its common name. Its small, yellowish flowers are followed by spherical, red berries that contain several seeds.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in arid to semi-arid environments.
