Tephrosia constricta

Tephrosia constricta, commonly known as narrowleaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, often hairy stems. The leaves are alternate, simple, and usually narrow, with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in racemes, and have a distinctive yellow or yellow-green color with prominent veins.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
