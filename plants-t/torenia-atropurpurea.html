Torenia atropurpurea

Torenia atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep purple to violet. The plant typically grows as an annual or short-lived perennial, often reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Torenia atropurpurea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
