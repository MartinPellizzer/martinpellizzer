Trattenikia latifolia

Trattenikia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Trattenikia latifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.
