Tetraena prismatica

Tetraena prismatica, commonly known as the prismatic tetraena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
