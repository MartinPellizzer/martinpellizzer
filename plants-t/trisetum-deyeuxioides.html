Trisetum deyeuxioides

Trisetum deyeuxioides, commonly known as the Yunnan feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces slender, branching inflorescences that bear small, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Trisetum deyeuxioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
