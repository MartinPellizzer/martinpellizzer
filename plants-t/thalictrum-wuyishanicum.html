Thalictrum wuyishanicum

Thalictrum wuyishanicum, commonly known as the Wuyishan meadow rue, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Wuyishan mountain region, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Thalictrum wuyishanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest understories.
