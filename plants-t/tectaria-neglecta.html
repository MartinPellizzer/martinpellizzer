Tectaria neglecta

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a complex arrangement of veins.
