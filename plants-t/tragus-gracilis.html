Tragus gracilis

Tragus gracilis, commonly known as the slender tragus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant grows in a compact, bushy form with wiry stems and narrow leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Tragus gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
