Thunbergia proxima

Thunbergia proxima, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking blue to purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia proxima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
