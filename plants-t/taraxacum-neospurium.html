Taraxacum neospurium

Taraxacum neospurium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and a flowering stem that bears a single, bright yellow flower head. Taraxacum neospurium is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is known for its medicinal and ecological significance.
