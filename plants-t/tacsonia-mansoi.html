Tacsonia mansoi

Tacsonia mansoi, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy texture.

Tacsonia mansoi prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
