Tricalysia pobeguinii

Tricalysia pobeguinii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Tricalysia pobeguinii is also valued for its ability to provide shade and support local wildlife.
