Trichilia multifoliola

Trichilia multifoliola, commonly known as African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves with multiple leaflets. The tree produces showy white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, often giving it a snowball-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some regions.
