Tephrosia oroboides

Tephrosia oroboides, commonly known as African milk bush, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its erect or semi-woody growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes, followed by pods containing small, dark seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

Tephrosia oroboides is also valued for its potential medicinal uses and as a forage plant for livestock.
