Thlaspi violascens

Thlaspi violascens, commonly known as the purple alkanet, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces small, violet-colored flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils in temperate regions. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Thlaspi violascens is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
