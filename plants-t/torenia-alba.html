Torenia alba

Torenia alba, commonly known as the white torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia alba is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its delicate and attractive appearance.
