Timotocia hymenocalyx

Timotocia hymenocalyx, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvet tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Timotocia hymenocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
