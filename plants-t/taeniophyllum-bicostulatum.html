Taeniophyllum bicostulatum

Taeniophyllum bicostulatum, commonly known as the two-ridged tailed orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and compact inflorescence that bears fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air, and it thrives in tropical climates with high humidity.

The species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
