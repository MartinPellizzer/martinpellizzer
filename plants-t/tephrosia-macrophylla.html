Tephrosia macrophylla

Tephrosia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Tephrosia macrophylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
