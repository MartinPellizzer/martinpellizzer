Trifolium Altissimum

Trifolium altissimum is a rare and striking species of clover known for its tall stature and vibrant purple flowers. Found in specific alpine regions, this plant thrives in harsh environments where few others can survive. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with three heart-shaped leaflets that catch the morning light. The plant's unique appearance makes it a subject of both botanical interest and natural beauty. Due to its limited habitat, Trifolium altissimum is considered a valuable indicator of ecological health in high-altitude ecosystems.
