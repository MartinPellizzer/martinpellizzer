Tovomita speciosa

Tovomita speciosa, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration make it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.

Tovomita speciosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for decorative purposes.
