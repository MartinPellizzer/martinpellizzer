Tectaria stearnsii

Tectaria stearnsii, commonly known as the Stearns' shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically divided into segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is short and thick, supporting the growth of its delicate, leathery leaves.

Tectaria stearnsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
