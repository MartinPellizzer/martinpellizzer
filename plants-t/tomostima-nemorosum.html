Tomostima nemorosum

Tomostima nemorosum, commonly known as the forest tomostima, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and woodland areas. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in green or reddish-brown inflorescences.

The botanical characteristics of Tomostima nemorosum make it well adapted to low-light conditions and high humidity.
