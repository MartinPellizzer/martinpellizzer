Thuja Occidentalis F. Variegata

Thuja occidentalis f. variegata is a striking variant of the arborvitae known for its unique foliage. This plant features variegated leaves that combine green and white or yellow hues, creating a visually appealing effect. It is often used as an ornamental shrub in gardens and landscapes due to its compact growth habit. The variegation adds a touch of elegance and contrast to any setting. Its hardy nature makes it suitable for a variety of climates and growing conditions.
