Trichomanes hookeri var. majus

Trichomanes hookeri var. majus, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a rare and ancient fern species characterized by its large, floating fronds.

The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, where it forms dense mats on the water surface.
