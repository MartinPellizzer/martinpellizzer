Thalictrum kochii var. flexuosum

Thalictrum kochii var. flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its stems are often flexible and can trail or climb, contributing to its common name.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
