Tropaeolum repandifolium

Tropaeolum repandifolium, commonly known as the spreading leaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, bright green leaves and vibrant yellow or orange flowers. The plant is often grown for its ornamental value and edible leaves, which have a peppery flavor. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Tropaeolum repandifolium is an annual plant that blooms throughout the growing season.
