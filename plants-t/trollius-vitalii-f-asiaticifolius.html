Trollius vitalii f. asiaticifolius

Trollius vitalii f. asiaticifolius, commonly known as the Asian leaf globeflower, is a botanical variant of the globeflower genus Trollius.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate climates. Its leaves are broad and lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is native to parts of Asia and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
