Thymelaea tarton-raira var. angustifolia

Thymelaea tarton-raira var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed thymelaea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Thymelaeaceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers and narrow, leathery leaves. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
