Thymelaea tarton-raira subsp. argentea

Thymelaea tarton-raira subsp.

argentea, commonly known as silver thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that appear in clusters.

The plant has hairy, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
