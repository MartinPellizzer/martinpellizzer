Tinospora szechuanensis

Tinospora szechuanensis, commonly known as Chinese tinospora, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia and southern China.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its pinnate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological studies.
