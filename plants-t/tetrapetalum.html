Tetrapetalum

Tetrapetalum, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. Tetrapetalum species are herbaceous perennials with simple, alternate leaves and tubular flowers.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
