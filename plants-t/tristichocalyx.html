Tristichocalyx

Tristichocalyx, commonly known as the silky oak, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often pinnately arranged and covered in fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive yellow or greenish hue.
