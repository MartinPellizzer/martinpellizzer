Trichoglottis geminata

Trichoglottis geminata, commonly known as the Geminata Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense, woolly pubescence. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Trichoglottis geminata is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining potting media and consistent moisture.
