Thymelaea tarton-raira var. calvescens

Thymelaea tarton-raira var. calvescens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thymelaea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Thymelaeaceae.

It is characterized by its small, tubular yellow flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. The leaves are narrow and often have a slight pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variant is native to parts of southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
