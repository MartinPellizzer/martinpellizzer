Tithymalus punctatus

Tithymalus punctatus, commonly known as the spotted tithymalus, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and orange disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Tithymalus punctatus is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
