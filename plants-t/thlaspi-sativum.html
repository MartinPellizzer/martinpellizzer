Thlaspi sativum

Thlaspi sativum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed, yellow-green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, which bloom in the spring. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Thlaspi sativum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
