Triodia cynosuroides

Triodia cynosuroides, commonly known as spinifex, is a hardy grassland plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry growth habit and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss in dry conditions. It plays a crucial role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.

Triodia cynosuroides is also adapted to frequent fires, with underground stems allowing it to regenerate quickly after disturbances.
