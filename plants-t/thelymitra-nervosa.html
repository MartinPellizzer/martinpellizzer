Thelymitra nervosa

Thelymitra nervosa, commonly known as the nervy leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its linear, often hairy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive veined pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. It belongs to the subfamily Orchidoideae and is part of the genus Thelymitra, which includes several species of terrestrial orchids.

Thelymitra nervosa is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other epiphytic and terrestrial orchids in its habitat.
