Townsendia montana var. minima

Townsendia montana var. minima, commonly known as the mountain Townsend's tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, seed-filled flowers that attract pollinators.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller size compared to other Townsendia species.
