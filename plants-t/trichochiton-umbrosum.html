Trichochiton umbrosum

Trichochiton umbrosum, commonly known as the dark-leaved trichochiton, is a unique plant species characterized by its dark green, velvety foliage.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like structures, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color, and are arranged in clusters.

Trichochiton umbrosum is primarily valued for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowering display.
