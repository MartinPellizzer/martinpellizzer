Trifolium rodnense

Trifolium rodnense, commonly known as the Rodnian clover, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Native to the Rodnian Mountains in Romania, it thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
