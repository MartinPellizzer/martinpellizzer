Tibouchina papyrifera

Tibouchina papyrifera, commonly known as the paperbark torchflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. Tibouchina papyrifera is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and bees.
