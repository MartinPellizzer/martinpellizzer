Thesium alatum

Thesium alatum, commonly known as the feathered thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small and clustered, often yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

Thesium alatum is notable for its unique, feathery appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
