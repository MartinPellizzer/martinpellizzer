Tragopogon agrostiphyllus

Tragopogon agrostiphyllus, commonly known as the narrowleaf thistle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf structures. Tragopogon agrostiphyllus is also known for its ability to hybridize with other species in the genus, contributing to the diversity of the group.
