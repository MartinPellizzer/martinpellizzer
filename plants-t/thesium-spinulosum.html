Thesium spinulosum

Thesium spinulosum, commonly known as the hairy thesium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are typically hairy and evergreen, adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments.
