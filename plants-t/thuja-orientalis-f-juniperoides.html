Thuja orientalis f. juniperoides

Thuja orientalis f. juniperoides, commonly known as the Eastern arborvitae or golden thread, is a compact, slow-growing evergreen conifer.

It features a dense, conical shape with scale-like leaves that are typically bright yellow to golden in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, spherical cones that are usually green when immature and turn reddish-brown when mature. It is native to eastern Asia and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
