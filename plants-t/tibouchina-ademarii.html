Tibouchina ademarii

Tibouchina ademarii, commonly known as the Brazilian red torch, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that resemble flames. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Tibouchina ademarii thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
