Thermopsis mollis var. mollis

Thermopsis mollis var. mollis, commonly known as yellow goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a pioneer species in ecological succession.
