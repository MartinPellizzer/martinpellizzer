Thymus × jovinienii

Thymus × jovinienii, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, fragrant leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers in spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its aromatic foliage contains essential oils, giving it a strong thyme scent and making it valuable in herbal medicine.

Thymus × jovinienii is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, well-drained soils.
