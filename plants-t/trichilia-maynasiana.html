Trichilia maynasiana

Trichilia maynasiana, commonly known as the Maynasian tallowtree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, pinnate leaves that are often leathery in texture. Its flowers are small and fragrant, typically clustered in panicles, and it produces woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
