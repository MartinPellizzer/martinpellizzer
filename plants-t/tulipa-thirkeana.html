Tulipa thirkeana

Tulipa thirkeana, commonly known as the Thirkean tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is a bulbous perennial plant that produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and blooms in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the bulbs are prized for their ornamental value in gardens.

The species is noted for its hardiness and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
