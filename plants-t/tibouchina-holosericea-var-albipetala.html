Tibouchina holosericea var. albipetala

Tibouchina holosericea var. albipetala, commonly known as the white-flowered tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
