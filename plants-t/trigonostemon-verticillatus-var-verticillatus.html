Trigonostemon verticillatus var. verticillatus

Trigonostemon verticillatus var. verticillatus, commonly known as the clustered leaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that often grow in clusters around the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in spikes, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
