Thunbergia convolvulifolia var. digitaliformis

Thunbergia convolvulifolia var. digitaliformis, commonly known as the digitate thunbergia, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive, palm-like appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
