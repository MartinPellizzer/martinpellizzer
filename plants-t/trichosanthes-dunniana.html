Trichosanthes dunniana

Trichosanthes dunniana, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, characterized by its vine-like growth habit and large, elongated fruits.

It is native to China and is often cultivated for its edible fruits, which have a mild, sweet flavor. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with lobed margins and produces yellow flowers that are typically unisexual. Its fruits can grow up to 30 centimeters in length and have a rough, warty surface.

Trichosanthes dunniana is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
