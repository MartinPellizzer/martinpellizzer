Thecacoris usambarensis

Thecacoris usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambarensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Thecacoris usambarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
