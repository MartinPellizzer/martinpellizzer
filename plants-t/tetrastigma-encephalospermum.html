Tetrastigma encephalospermum

Tetrastigma encephalospermum, commonly known as the Chinese moonseed, is a climbing vine belonging to the grape family (Vitaceae).

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
