Tragacantha arnottiana

Tragacantha arnottiana, commonly known as the desert thorn.

It is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia, characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are small and succulent, adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry environments.

Tragacantha arnottiana plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
