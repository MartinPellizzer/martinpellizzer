Timonius mutabilis

Timonius mutabilis, commonly known as the variable timonius, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Timonieae tribe.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that exhibit a range of morphological variation depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptation and biodiversity.
