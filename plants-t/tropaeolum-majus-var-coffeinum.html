Tropaeolum majus var. coffeinum

Tropaeolum majus var. coffeinum, commonly known as the coffee plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

While not related to the coffee bean plant, it shares the common name due to the color of its flowers.
