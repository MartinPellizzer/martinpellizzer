Tithymalus leptaleus

Tithymalus leptaleus, commonly known as the slender tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
