Trachelospermum slootenii

Trachelospermum slootenii, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for trellises and vertical gardens.
