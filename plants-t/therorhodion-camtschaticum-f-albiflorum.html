Therorhodion camtschaticum f. albiflorum

Therorhodion camtschaticum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the Caucasian rose.

This plant is a flowering shrub native to the Caucasus region, characterized by its white or pale pink flowers. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its ornamental value. The foliage is typically dark green and aromatic, while the plant produces small, edible fruits.
