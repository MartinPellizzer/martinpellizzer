Tephrosia longipes subsp. swynnertonii

Tephrosia longipes subsp.

swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's tephrosia, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive violet or purple coloration.
