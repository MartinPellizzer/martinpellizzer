Trichomanes compactum

Trichomanes compactum, commonly known as the compact fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found on rocks or tree trunks.

Due to its small size and intricate fronds, it is a popular choice for terrariums and miniature gardens.
