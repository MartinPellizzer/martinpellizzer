Thelypteris villana

Thelypteris villana, commonly known as the fern of the genus Thelypteris, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with pinnate, often deeply divided fronds. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture in humid conditions.

Thelypteris villana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
