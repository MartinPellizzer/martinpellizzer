Taraxacum sugawarae

Taraxacum sugawarae, commonly known as the Sugawara's dandelion, is a rare and endemic species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated, with a distinctive basal rosette structure.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
