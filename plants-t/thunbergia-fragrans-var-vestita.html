Thunbergia fragrans var. vestita

Thunbergia fragrans var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. It is a climbing vine that thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for growth.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
