Tilia × euchlora

Tilia × euchlora, commonly known as the gray-leaf lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with gray or silver on the upper surface. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, greenish-brown fruits. It typically grows to a height of 15–25 meters and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
