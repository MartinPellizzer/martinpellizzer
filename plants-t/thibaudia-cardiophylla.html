Thibaudia cardiophylla

Thibaudia cardiophylla, commonly known as the heartleaf thibaudia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in certain regions.
