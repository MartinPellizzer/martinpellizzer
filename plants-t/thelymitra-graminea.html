Thelymitra graminea

Thelymitra graminea, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It features a slender, grass-like stem with a single, narrow leaf and a spike of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in open habitats such as heathlands and woodlands. Thelymitra graminea is a perennial species that blooms during the cooler months, usually between spring and early summer.
