Thalictrum inuncans

Thalictrum inuncans, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its stems are slender and often branched, while its flowers bloom in early spring.

Thalictrum inuncans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
