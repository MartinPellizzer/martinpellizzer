Thelypteris glabrata

Thelypteris glabrata, commonly known as the smooth holly fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are usually light green and slightly glossy. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and often form dense colonies, contributing to its ability to thrive in humid conditions.

Thelypteris glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
