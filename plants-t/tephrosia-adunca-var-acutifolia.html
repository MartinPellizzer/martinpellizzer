Tephrosia adunca var. acutifolia

Tephrosia adunca var. acutifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with elongated, acute leaflets that give it its specific epithet. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in dry savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
