Thelypteris euchlora

Thelypteris euchlora, commonly known as the common wood fern, is a species of fern native to North America.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots, which help anchor it to the soil.

Thelypteris euchlora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
