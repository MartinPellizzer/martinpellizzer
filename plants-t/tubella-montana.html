Tubella montana

Tubella montana, commonly known as the mountain tubella, is a rare and delicate species of flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the short summer season. Tubella montana is adapted to cold, rocky environments and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure to thrive.
