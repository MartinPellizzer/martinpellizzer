Torrubia tomentosa

Torrubia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly torrubia, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in sunny, open habitats. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are covered in dense, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
