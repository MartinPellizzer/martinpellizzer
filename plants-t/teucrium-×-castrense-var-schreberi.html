Teucrium × castrense var. schreberi

Teucrium × castrense var. schreberi, commonly known as Schreber's germander, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It is native to southern Europe and has been introduced to other regions as an ornamental.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for garden use.
