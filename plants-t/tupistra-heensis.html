Tupistra heensis

Tupistra heensis, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub that produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has needle-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Tupistra heensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
