Thalictrum capillare

Thalictrum capillare, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white, spire-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Thalictrum capillare is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
