Tristania pachysperma

Tristania pachysperma, commonly known as the Tristania tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mascarene Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree with a dense, rounded canopy and thick, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

The species is valued for its hardy nature and is often found in coastal woodlands and secondary forests.
