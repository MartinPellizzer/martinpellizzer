Tristemma kaessnerianum

Tristemma kaessnerianum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers, which come in various colors, bloom in clusters and are often used in ornamental gardening.

Tristemma kaessnerianum prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
