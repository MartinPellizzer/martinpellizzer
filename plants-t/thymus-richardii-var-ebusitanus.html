Thymus richardii var. ebusitanus

Thymus richardii var. ebusitanus, commonly known as the Ebusi thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Japan.

It features small, compact leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a strong aromatic scent. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale pink to white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional herbal medicine.
