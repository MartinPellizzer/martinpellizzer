Trichosanthes Homophylla Var. Ishigakiensis

Trichosanthes homophylla var. ishigakiensis is a rare and unique variant of the trumpet gourd native to the island of Ishigaki in the Ryukyu Islands. This plant is known for its striking, elongated fruits that can grow up to two meters in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas with well-drained soil. The variety is highly valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable and endangered species in its natural habitat.
