Tithymalus diffusus

Tithymalus diffusus, commonly known as the spreading tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Tithymalus diffusus is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in temperate climates.
