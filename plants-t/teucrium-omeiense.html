Teucrium omeiense

Teucrium omeiense, commonly known as the Yunnan germander, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal and culinary uses. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
