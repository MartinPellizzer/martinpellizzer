Tibouchina goldenbergii

Tibouchina goldenbergii, commonly known as the golden tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
