Tessenia oligodonta var. roseata

Tessenia oligodonta var. roseata, commonly known as the roseate tessenia, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to rose-colored petals, which contrast with its deep green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous forests. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
