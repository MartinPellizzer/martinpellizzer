Tibouchina Glandulosa

Tibouchina, scientifically known as Tibouchina glandulosa, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America. It is celebrated for its vibrant, showy blooms that range from pink to purple, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its ornate foliage and long-lasting flowers. Its unique appearance and adaptability have made it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, Tibouchina requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native environments.
