Tilia tuan

Tilia tuan, commonly known as the Chinese linden, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, smooth, and often ribbed fruits that contain three seeds. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

Tilia tuan is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
