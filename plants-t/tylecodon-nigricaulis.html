Tylecodon nigricaulis

Tylecodon nigricaulis, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree-coral, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, woody stem that is dark-colored, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and cylindrical, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Tylecodon nigricaulis is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience in dry climates.
