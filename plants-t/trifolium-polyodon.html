Trifolium polyodon

Trifolium polyodon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed clover, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It is often found in grassy areas and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Trifolium polyodon is a hardy perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
