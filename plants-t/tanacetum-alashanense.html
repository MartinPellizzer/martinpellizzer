Tanacetum alashanense

Tanacetum alashanense, commonly known as the Alashan tansy, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets. It is adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments, showcasing resilience to harsh climatic conditions.

Tanacetum alashanense is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and potential medicinal properties.
