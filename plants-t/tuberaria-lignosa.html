Tuberaria lignosa

Tuberaria lignosa, commonly known as the wood tuber, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Tuberaria lignosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
