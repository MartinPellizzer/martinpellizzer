Tulipa atheniensis

Tulipa atheniensis, commonly known as the Athens tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters and produces narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

The bulbs are perennial, and the plant is known for its early spring blooming habit.
