Trachelium azureum

Trachelium azureum, commonly known as blue trachelium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its prolonged blooming period and attractive foliage.
