Typhonium orixense

Typhonium orixense, commonly known as the Orixan arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
