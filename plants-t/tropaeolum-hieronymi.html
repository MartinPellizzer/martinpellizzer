Tropaeolum hieronymi

Tropaeolum hieronymi, commonly known as the "Garden Nasturtium," is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces vibrant, edible flowers and leaves, often used in salads and as a garnish. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Tropaeolum hieronymi is also known for its ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs and bees.
