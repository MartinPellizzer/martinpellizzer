Tithymalus × procopianii

Tithymalus × procopianii, commonly known as the hybrid tithymalus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance and fragrance.
