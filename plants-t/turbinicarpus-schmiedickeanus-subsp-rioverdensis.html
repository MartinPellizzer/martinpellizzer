Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp. rioverdensis

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp.

rioverdensis, commonly known as the Rioverdens Turban, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of northern South America. It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the rainy season, typically in the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to dry conditions.

This subspecies is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and hardiness in arid environments.
