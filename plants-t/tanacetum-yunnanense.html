Tanacetum yunnanense

Tanacetum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan tansy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves and yellow ray flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Tanacetum yunnanense is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
