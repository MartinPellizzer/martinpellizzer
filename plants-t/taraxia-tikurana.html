Taraxia tikurana

Taraxia tikurana, commonly known as the Ethiopian dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk, typical of the family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Taraxia tikurana is notable for its medicinal uses in traditional Ethiopian medicine.
