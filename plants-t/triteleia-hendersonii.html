Triteleia hendersonii

Triteleia hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's glory-of-the-snow, is a perennial bulbous plant native to California.

It produces showy, star-shaped white to pale blue flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral.
