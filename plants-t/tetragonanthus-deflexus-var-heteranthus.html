Tetragonanthus deflexus var. heteranthus

Tetragonanthus deflexus var. heteranthus, commonly known as the zigzag flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging branches and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom during the dry season. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a slight pubescence. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
