Tylecodon reticulatus subsp. reticulatus

Tylecodon reticulatus subsp.

reticulatus, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems covered in a distinctive network of ridges and tubercles, giving it a unique, rugged appearance.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability to arid conditions and ornamental value contribute to its popularity among succulent enthusiasts.
