Thladiantha henryi

Thladiantha henryi, commonly known as the Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, edible berries that are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

Due to its hardy nature, it is also used in ornamental gardening.
