Tetracera maingayi

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Tetracera maingayi is often found in lowland rainforests and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
