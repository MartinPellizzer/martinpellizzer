Teucrium polium var. cylindricum

Teucrium polium var. cylindricum, commonly known as Cyprus germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
