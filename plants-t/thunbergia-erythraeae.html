Thunbergia erythraeae

Thunbergia erythraeae, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking red or orange tubular flowers resembling spider-like structures. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and grows best in partial shade to full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Thunbergia erythraeae is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
