Tephrosia ansellii

Tephrosia ansellii, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to poor soils.

Tephrosia ansellii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
