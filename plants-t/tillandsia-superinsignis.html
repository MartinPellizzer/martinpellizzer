Tillandsia superinsignis

Tillandsia superinsignis, commonly known as the silver star tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain its health.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
