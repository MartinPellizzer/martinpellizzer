Thesium microphyllum

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and small, grass-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
