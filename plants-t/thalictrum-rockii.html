Thalictrum rockii

Thalictrum rockii, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white, spicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its shade tolerance and unique foliage.

Thalictrum rockii is also noted for its ecological value, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
