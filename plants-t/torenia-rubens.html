Torenia rubens

Torenia rubens, commonly known as the red torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, broad leaves that are usually hairy and arranged oppositely on the stems. Torenia rubens thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
