Tilia likiangensis

Tilia likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lime tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. It produces small, round fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Tilia likiangensis is also noted for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
