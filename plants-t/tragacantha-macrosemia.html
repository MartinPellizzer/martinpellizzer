Tragacantha macrosemia

Tragacantha macrosemia, commonly known as the large-seeded tragacanth, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves, with small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several large, hard seeds.

The plant is valued for its resinous sap, which has been used historically in various traditional applications.
