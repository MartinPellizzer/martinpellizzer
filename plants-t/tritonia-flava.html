Tritonia flava

Tritonia flava, commonly known as the yellow tritonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a red throat. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and lanceolate in shape. Tritonia flava thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
