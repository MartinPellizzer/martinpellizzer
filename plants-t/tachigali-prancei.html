Tachigali prancei

Tachigali prancei, commonly known as the Prance's tachigali, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
