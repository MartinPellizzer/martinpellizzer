Tragopogon crocifolius subsp. crocifolius

Tragopogon crocifolius subsp.

crocifolius, commonly known as the yellow gooseneck, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble a gooseneck, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stem is erect and branching.

It is native to western North America and is often found in moist, sunny environments.
