Thermopsis lanceolata var. turkestanica

Thermopsis lanceolata var. turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan yellow rocket, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It features bright yellow, elongated flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and pinnately divided, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to gardens and a valuable species for ecological studies.
