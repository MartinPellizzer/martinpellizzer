Trachycarpus ukhrulensis

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, clustered growth habit and tall, slender stems. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically arranged in panicles, and it produces edible, starchy fruits that are an important food source for local wildlife.
