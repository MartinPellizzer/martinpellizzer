Trichophyllum ovatum

It is characterized by its dense, turfs of short, dark green shoots with a waxy coating. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a distinct midrib and a gradually narrowing base. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices.
