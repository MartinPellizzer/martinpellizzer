Tanacetum tabrisianum

Tanacetum tabrisianum, commonly known as the Turkish tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellowish-green ray flowers surrounded by a dense cluster of tubular florets. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
