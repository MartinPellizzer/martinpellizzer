Tristellateia bojeriana subsp. australis

Tristellateia bojeriana subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian tristellateia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact appearance.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding delicate beauty to its foliage. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
