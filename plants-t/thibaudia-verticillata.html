Thibaudia verticillata

Thibaudia verticillata, commonly known as the whorled thibaudia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered inflorescences that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Thibaudia verticillata is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral displays.
