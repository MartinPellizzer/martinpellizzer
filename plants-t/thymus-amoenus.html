Thymus amoenus

Thymus amoenus, commonly known as sweet thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, grey-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils, rich in thymol, contribute to its aromatic and medicinal properties.
