Tilia × prodigiosa

Tilia × prodigiosa, commonly known as the giant linden, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically 10–15 cm in length. The tree produces showy, fragrant yellowish-white flowers in early summer, which are clustered in drooping inflorescences. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This species is native to Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in traditional medicine.
