Thelypteris paucipaleata

Thelypteris paucipaleata, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery fronds that are typically divided into small, lance-shaped segments. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its spores are produced on the undersides of its fertile fronds, which are usually darker in color.

Thelypteris paucipaleata plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
