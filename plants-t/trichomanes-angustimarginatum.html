Trichomanes angustimarginatum

Trichomanes angustimarginatum, commonly known as narrow-margin maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Trichomanes angustimarginatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
