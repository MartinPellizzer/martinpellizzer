Trichilia schiedeana

Trichilia schiedeana, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes various species known for their aromatic wood. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
