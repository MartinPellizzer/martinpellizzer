Tropidia polystachya

Tropidia polystachya, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

Tropidia polystachya is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
