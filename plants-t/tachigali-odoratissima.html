Tachigali odoratissima

Tachigali odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant tachigali, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pod fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its aromatic properties make it a subject of interest in both ecological and horticultural studies.
