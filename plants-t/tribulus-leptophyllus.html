Tribulus leptophyllus

Tribulus leptophyllus, commonly known as the slender leaf tribulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are narrow and leathery. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions.

The plant is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low water availability.
