Thlaspi cepaeifolium

Thlaspi cepaeifolium, commonly known as the cabbage leaf chickweed, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in gardens and disturbed sites.
