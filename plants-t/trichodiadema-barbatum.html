Trichodiadema barbatum

Trichodiadema barbatum, commonly known as the woolly ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
