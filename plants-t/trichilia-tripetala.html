Trichilia tripetala

Trichilia tripetala, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The tree is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The wood is highly prized for its fine grain and durability, making it useful in furniture and construction.
