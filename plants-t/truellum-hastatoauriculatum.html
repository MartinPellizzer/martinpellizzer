Truellum hastatoauriculatum

Truellum hastatoauriculatum, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, which change color as the plant matures. The leaves are heart-shaped with lobed edges, and the plant grows in clusters along the ground. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Truellum hastatoauriculatum is known for its rapid blooming cycle, often flowering within a few weeks of germination.
