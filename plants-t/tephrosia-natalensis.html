Tephrosia natalensis

Tephrosia natalensis, commonly known as Natal tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming during the warmer months. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Tephrosia natalensis is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
