Thymus × porcii f. cibinensis

Thymus × porcii f. cibinensis, commonly known as the Cibinensis thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or violet in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and pleasant fragrance.
