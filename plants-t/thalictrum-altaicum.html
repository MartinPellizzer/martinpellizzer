Thalictrum altaicum

Thalictrum altaicum, commonly known as the mountain meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and delicate, compound leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in upright inflorescences. Thalictrum altaicum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
