Teucrium auraniticum

Teucrium auraniticum, commonly known as golden teucrium, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Teucrium auraniticum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
