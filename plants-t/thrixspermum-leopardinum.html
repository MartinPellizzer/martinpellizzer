Thrixspermum leopardinum

Thrixspermum leopardinum, commonly known as the leopard's eye plant, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features vibrant, translucent blue or green leaves that often have a speckled or mottled appearance resembling a leopard's coat. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
