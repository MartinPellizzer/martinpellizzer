Tinospora fosbergii

Tinospora fosbergii, commonly known as Fosberg's tinospora, is a climbing shrub belonging to the Menispermaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Tinospora fosbergii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.
