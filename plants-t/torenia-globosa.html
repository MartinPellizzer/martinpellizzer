Torenia globosa

Torenia globosa, commonly known as the globular torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, globe-shaped inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are usually yellow or orange in color.

Torenia globosa thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
