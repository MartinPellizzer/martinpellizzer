Telipogon frymirei

Telipogon frymirei, commonly known as the Frymire's spider orchid, is a rare and distinctive orchid species native to the wet tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance with long, thin stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with intricate, tubular flowers that are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on the forest floor or on tree trunks.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Telipogon frymirei is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
