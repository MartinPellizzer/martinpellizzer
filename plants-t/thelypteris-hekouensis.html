Thelypteris hekouensis

Thelypteris hekouensis, commonly known as the Hekou fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in forest understories. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through sexual reproduction.

Thelypteris hekouensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
