Trichilia magnifoliola

Trichilia magnifoliola, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures containing several flat, winged seeds.
