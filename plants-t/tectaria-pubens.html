Tectaria pubens

Tectaria pubens, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. Its leaves are typically covered with fine, hairy structures, giving it a velvety appearance.

Tectaria pubens thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
