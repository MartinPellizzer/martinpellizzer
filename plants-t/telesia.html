Telesia

Telesia, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Telesia is typically found in moist, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

It is also known for its role in supporting monarch butterfly populations by providing a host plant for their larvae.
