Torenia godefroyi

Torenia godefroyi, commonly known as the tropical torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Torenia godefroyi thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
