Trichomanes orbiculare

Trichomanes orbiculare, commonly known as the round fern, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or circular, fronds that are typically less than 5 centimeters in diameter. Trichomanes orbiculare is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
