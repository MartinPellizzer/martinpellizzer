Thuja occidentalis f. globosa

Thuja occidentalis f. globosa, commonly known as the globe thuja, is a compact, spherical evergreen shrub native to eastern North America.

It features dense, scale-like foliage that gives it a rounded, globe-like appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has a slow growth rate and is often used in landscaping for its formal, sculptural form. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, exhibiting good resistance to pests and diseases.

Its unique shape and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to gardens and urban landscapes.
