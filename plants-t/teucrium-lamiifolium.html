Teucrium lamiifolium

Teucrium lamiifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Teucrium lamiifolium is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
