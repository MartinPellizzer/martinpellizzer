Tripteris linearis

Tripteris linearis, commonly known as the slender tripteris, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Tripteridaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically linear or lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Tripteris linearis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, though its botanical study remains limited compared to more widely researched species.
