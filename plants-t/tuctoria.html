Tuctoria

Tuctoria, commonly known as the sea blush or sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Crambe.

It is native to the coastal regions of Europe and North Africa, where it thrives in saline and sandy soils. The plant features blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
