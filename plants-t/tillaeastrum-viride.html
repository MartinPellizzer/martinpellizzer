Tillaeastrum viride

Tillaeastrum viride, commonly known as green buttonwood, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are usually dark green in color. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
