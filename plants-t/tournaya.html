Tournaya

Tournaya, commonly known as false hellebore, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Melanthiaceae.

It consists of several species, primarily found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plants are herbaceous perennials with dark green, often basal leaves that are lance-shaped or elliptic. Tournaya species are noted for their ornamental value and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their attractive foliage and flowers.
