Thalia barbata

Thalia barbata, commonly known as the featherleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant features linear, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in coastal areas.

Thalia barbata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
