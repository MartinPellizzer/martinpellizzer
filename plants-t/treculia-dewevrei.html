Treculia dewevrei

Treculia dewevrei, commonly known as the African treculia, is a member of the Sapotaceae family.

It is a large, deciduous tree native to tropical Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and broad, palmately lobed leaves. Its fruits are large, spiny capsules that contain edible seeds, which are used in local cuisines.

The tree also plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
