Tillandsia euosma

Tillandsia euosma, commonly known as the silver leaf tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments, thriving in shaded to partially shaded conditions.

Its flowers are usually small and inconspicuous, with a greenish-white color, and it produces viable seeds that are dispersed by wind.
