Tricyrtis hirta var. ramosissima

Tricyrtis hirta var. ramosissima, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique floral structure and foliage.
