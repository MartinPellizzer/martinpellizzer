Thelypteris erythrothrix

Thelypteris erythrothrix, commonly known as the red-stemmed shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive red-colored stems and lanceolate, serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its fronds are usually evergreen, with a leathery texture and a deep green color.

Thelypteris erythrothrix is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
