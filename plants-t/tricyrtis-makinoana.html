Tricyrtis makinoana

Tricyrtis makinoana, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while the flowers are usually pale yellow or green with a distinctive central stripe.

Tricyrtis makinoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
