Tibouchina herbacea f. parvifolia

Tibouchina herbacea f. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved tibouchina, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used in container gardening or as a border plant.
