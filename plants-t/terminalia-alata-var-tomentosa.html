Terminalia alata var. tomentosa

Terminalia alata var. tomentosa, commonly known as the hairy Terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
