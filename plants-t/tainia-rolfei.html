Tainia rolfei

Tainia rolfei, commonly known as the Rolfe's tainia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Tainia rolfei requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
