Thelypteris sprengelii

Thelypteris sprengelii, commonly known as the Sprengel's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, giving it a finely divided appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries such as India, China, and Indonesia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its rhizomes are creeping and can form dense colonies, making it a common sight in wetland habitats.
