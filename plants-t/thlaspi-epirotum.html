Thlaspi epirotum

Thlaspi epirotum, commonly known as the Epirus cabbageworm, is a species of flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces round, green pods that contain several seeds.

This species is native to regions of the Balkans and is often found in disturbed soils and rocky areas.
