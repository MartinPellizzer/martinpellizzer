Teucrium botrys f. albiflorum

Teucrium botrys f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered germander, is a variant of the Teucrium botrys species.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of the Mediterranean region. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate floral display and aromatic foliage.
