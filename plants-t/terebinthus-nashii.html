Terebinthus nashii

Terebinthus nashii, commonly known as the Nash terebinth, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of China.

It belongs to the family Terebinthaceae and is characterized by its small, deciduous leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in its native habitat.
