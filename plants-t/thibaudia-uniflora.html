Thibaudia uniflora

Thibaudia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower thibaudia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink or purple. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Thibaudia uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
