Thalictrum nipponense

Thalictrum nipponense, commonly known as Japanese meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Thalictrum nipponense is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.
