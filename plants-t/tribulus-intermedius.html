Tribulus intermedius

Tribulus intermedius, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, black seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats.

Tribulus intermedius is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
