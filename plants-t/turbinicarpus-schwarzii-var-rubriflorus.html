Turbinicarpus schwarzii var. rubriflorus

Turbinicarpus schwarzii var. rubriflorus, commonly known as the Red-flowered Turbinicarpus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often appearing in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts and desert gardeners.
