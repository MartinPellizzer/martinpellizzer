Typhonium diversifolium var. microspadix

Typhonium diversifolium var. microspadix, commonly known as the small-spadix swamp taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces a striking inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in wet, swampy environments and is often found in marshy areas near water bodies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
