Trachys mucronata

Trachys mucronata, commonly known as the slender-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered at the ends of the stems. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species known for their milky sap.
