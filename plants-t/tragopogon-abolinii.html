Tragopogon abolinii

Tragopogon abolinii, commonly known as the Armenian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the high mountain regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant features slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow, with ray florets that bloom in spring and early summer.

Tragopogon abolinii is adapted to harsh alpine environments, showcasing resilience to cold and poor soil conditions.
