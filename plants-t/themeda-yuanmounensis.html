Themeda yuanmounensis

Themeda yuanmounensis, commonly known as the Yunnan tussock grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, fibrous foliage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with spikelets that are often hairy.

Themeda yuanmounensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native environment.
