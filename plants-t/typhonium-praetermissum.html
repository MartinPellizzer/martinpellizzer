Typhonium praetermissum

Typhonium praetermissum, commonly known as the ghost arrow or arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features large, arrow-shaped leaves with a striking dark green color. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Typhonium praetermissum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
