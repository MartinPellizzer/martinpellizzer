Tanacetum densum subsp. amani

Tanacetum densum subsp.

amani, commonly known as the Amani dense tansy, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa. It is characterized by its dense, finely divided leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to the harsh climatic conditions of its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
