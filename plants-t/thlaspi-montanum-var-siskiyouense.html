Thlaspi montanum var. siskiyouense

Thlaspi montanum var. siskiyouense, commonly known as the Siskiyou rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in alpine and subalpine regions. It has basal leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
