Tragopogon leiorhynchus

Tragopogon leiorhynchus, commonly known as the narrowleaf goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its yellow flower heads are surrounded by bracts and produce small seeds that are dispersed by wind.
