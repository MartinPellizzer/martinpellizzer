Tragacantha wartoensis

Tragacantha wartoensis, commonly known as the Warto tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant produces a viscous gum, similar to tragacanth, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a food additive.
