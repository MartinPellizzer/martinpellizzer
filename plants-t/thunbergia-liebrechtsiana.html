Thunbergia liebrechtsiana

Thunbergia liebrechtsiana, commonly known as the blue trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Thunbergia liebrechtsiana is a climbing plant that can reach lengths of several meters, making it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.
