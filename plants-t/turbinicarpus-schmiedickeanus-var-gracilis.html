Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. gracilis

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a compact, globular stem that is typically covered in short, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to tubercles, which help minimize water loss in its harsh environment.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and hardy nature, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
