Trichoglottis brachystachya

Trichoglottis brachystachya, commonly known as the short-stemmed lady's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces a single, slender stem that bears a few leaves. Its flowers are borne on a slender inflorescence and are known for their unique, pouch-like shape.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
