Trachelospermum gracilipes var. cavaleriei

Trachelospermum gracilipes var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
