Thomsonaria aronioides

Thomsonaria aronioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Thomsonaria aronioides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
