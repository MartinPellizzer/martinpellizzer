Thelypteris weberi

Thelypteris weberi, commonly known as the Weber's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, feathery appearance.

Thelypteris weberi is often found in the understory of hardwood forests, where it thrives in the cool, humid conditions.
