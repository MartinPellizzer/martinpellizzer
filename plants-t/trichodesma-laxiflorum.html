Trichodesma laxiflorum

Trichodesma laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and savanna habitats. The plant features small, white or pale blue flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Trichodesma laxiflorum is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
