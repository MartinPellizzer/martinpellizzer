Trinia uitenhagensis

Trinia uitenhagensis, commonly known as the Uitenhage trinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in the Eastern Cape region. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Trinia uitenhagensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
