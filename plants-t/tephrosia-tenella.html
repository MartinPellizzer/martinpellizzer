Tephrosia tenella

Tephrosia tenella, commonly known as the slender tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in terminal clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often trifoliate, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Tephrosia tenella is also valued for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
