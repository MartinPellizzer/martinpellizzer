Thyrsacanthus longistaminus

Thyrsacanthus longistaminus, commonly known as the long-stamen thyrsocactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it stores water in its stems to survive in arid conditions.

This plant is well adapted to desert environments and is often found in rocky soils.
