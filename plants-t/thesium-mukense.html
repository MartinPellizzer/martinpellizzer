Thesium mukense

Thesium mukense, commonly known as the Mukense torchroot, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae, which includes plants known for their showy inflorescences. The plant produces striking, orange-red bract-like flowers that are often mistaken for petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
