Tsuga brunoniana var. chinensis

Tsuga brunoniana var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hemlock, is a variant of the hemlock tree native to eastern China.

It is a coniferous evergreen with needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals along the branches. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, typical of the Cupressaceae family. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.
