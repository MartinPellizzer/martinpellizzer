Timonius pannosus

Timonius pannosus, commonly known as the silky bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are broad and hairy, giving it a textured appearance.

Timonius pannosus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
