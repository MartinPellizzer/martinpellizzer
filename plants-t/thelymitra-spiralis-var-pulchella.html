Thelymitra spiralis var. pulchella

Thelymitra spiralis var. pulchella, commonly known as the spiral-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive spiral pattern on the labellum. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
