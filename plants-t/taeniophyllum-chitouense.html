Taeniophyllum chitouense

Taeniophyllum chitouense, commonly known as the Chitouen miniature orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in controlled environments for conservation and ornamental purposes.
