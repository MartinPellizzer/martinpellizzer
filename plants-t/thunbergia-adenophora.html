Thunbergia adenophora

Thunbergia adenophora, commonly known as the velvetleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Thunbergia adenophora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
