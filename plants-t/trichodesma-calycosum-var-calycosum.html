Trichodesma calycosum var. calycosum

Trichodesma calycosum var. calycosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, characterized by its slender, often aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are often woody and branched.

It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.
