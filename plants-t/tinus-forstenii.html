Tinus forstenii

Tinus forstenii, commonly known as the Forsten's tinus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice among gardeners in arid regions.
