Tephrosia scopulata

Tephrosia scopulata, commonly known as the rock tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Tephrosia scopulata is also known for its potential medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
