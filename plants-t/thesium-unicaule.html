Thesium unicaule

Thesium unicaule, commonly known as the single-stem thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its single, erect stem that can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense spike, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils.

Thesium unicaule is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
