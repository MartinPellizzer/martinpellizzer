Torenia longiflora

Torenia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Torenia longiflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
