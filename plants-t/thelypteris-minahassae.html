Thelypteris minahassae

Thelypteris minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its sporangia are clustered on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in ferns.

Thelypteris minahassae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
