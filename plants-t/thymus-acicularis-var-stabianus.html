Thymus acicularis var. stabianus

Thymus acicularis var. stabianus, commonly known as the Stabian thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and ornamental appeal.
