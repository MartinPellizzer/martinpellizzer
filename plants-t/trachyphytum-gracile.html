Trachyphytum gracile

Trachyphytum gracile, commonly known as the slender trachyphytum, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Its growth is slow, and it requires specific soil conditions to flourish.
