Torenia mollissima

Torenia mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of trumpet-shaped, yellow-orange flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks. Its flowers bloom throughout the growing season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Torenia mollissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
