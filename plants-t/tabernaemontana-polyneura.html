Tabernaemontana polyneura

Tabernaemontana polyneura, commonly known as the fragrant star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its nocturnal blooming habit makes it a popular choice for evening fragrance and attracting nocturnal pollinators.
