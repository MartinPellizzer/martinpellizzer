Tetraneuris latior

Tetraneuris latior, commonly known as the broadleaf tetraneuris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Tetraneuris latior is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
