Teucrium dunense subsp. dunense

Teucrium dunense subsp.

dunense, commonly known as the Balkan germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its aromatic, lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes. It is part of the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses.

This subspecies is often found in open woodlands and along rocky slopes.
