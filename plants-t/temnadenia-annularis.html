Temnadenia annularis

Temnadenia annularis, commonly known as the ringed leaf mallow, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a ring-like pattern on the underside. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Temnadenia annularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
