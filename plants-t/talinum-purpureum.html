Talinum purpureum

Talinum purpureum, commonly known as purple leaf nightshade, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and showy pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. Talinum purpureum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
