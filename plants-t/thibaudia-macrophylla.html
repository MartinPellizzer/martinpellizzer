Thibaudia macrophylla

Thibaudia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf thibaudia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or riverbanks. Its botanical features include a woody stem, opposite leaf arrangement, and small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds.

Thibaudia macrophylla is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
