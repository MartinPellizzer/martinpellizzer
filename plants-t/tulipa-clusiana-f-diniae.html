Tulipa clusiana f. diniae

Tulipa clusiana f. diniae, commonly known as the Dinia's tulip, is a unique variant of the Clusiana tulip.

It is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive red stripe running down the center of each petal. This cultivar is prized for its elegant appearance and is often used in horticultural displays. The plant is a bulbous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike.
