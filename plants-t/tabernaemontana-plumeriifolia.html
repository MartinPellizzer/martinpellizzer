Tabernaemontana plumeriifolia

Tabernaemontana plumeriifolia, commonly known as the fragrant star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, often at night. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its delicate blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowers are also used in perfumery due to their strong, sweet fragrance.
