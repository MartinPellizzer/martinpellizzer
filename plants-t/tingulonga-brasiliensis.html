Tingulonga brasiliensis

Tingulonga brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The plant's unique floral structure and vibrant appearance make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
