Trichoglottis pallens

Trichoglottis pallens, commonly known as the pale trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that bear lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically blooms with pale green to white flowers that have a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Trichoglottis pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
