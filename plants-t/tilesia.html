Tilesia

Tilesia, commonly known as the sweetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its palm-like appearance with long, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red fruits containing several seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Tilesia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
