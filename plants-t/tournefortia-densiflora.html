Tournefortia densiflora

Tournefortia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and mangrove habitats. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually white or pale yellow.

Tournefortia densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
