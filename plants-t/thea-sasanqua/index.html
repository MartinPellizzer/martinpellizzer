Thea Sasanqua

Thea sasanqua is a hardy evergreen shrub native to southern Japan and Korea. It is prized for its fragrant, semi-evergreen foliage and showy clusters of small, pink or white flowers. This plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and is known for its resilience in coastal and urban environments. Its compact size makes it ideal for garden borders, containers, and hedges. Thea sasanqua is also a popular choice for attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
