Thymus jankae var. subacicularis

Thymus jankae var. subacicularis, commonly known as the subacicular thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, compact foliage with small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers during the late spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant leaves and hardy nature.
