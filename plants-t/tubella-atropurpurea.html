Tubella atropurpurea

Tubella atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-tubed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
