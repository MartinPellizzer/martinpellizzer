Tragopogon shuttleworthii

Tragopogon shuttleworthii, commonly known as the shuttleworth's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense, spiky disk of tubular flowers. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Tragopogon shuttleworthii is known for its taproot system and is often found in disturbed soils.
