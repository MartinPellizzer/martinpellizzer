Trema orientale subsp. tomentosum

Trema orientale subsp.

tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly trema, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage, which gives it a soft, woolly appearance.

The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a thick, leathery texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, fleshy fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
