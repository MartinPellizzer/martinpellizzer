Tuberaria villosissima var. pomelii

Tuberaria villosissima var. pomelii, commonly known as the Pomelii Tuberaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
