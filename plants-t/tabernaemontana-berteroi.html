Tabernaemontana berteroi

Tabernaemontana berteroi, commonly known as the Madagascar star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters.
