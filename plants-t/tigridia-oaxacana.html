Tigridia oaxacana

Tigridia oaxacana, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a tall, erect stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Tigridia oaxacana is also known for its ability to bloom multiple times during the growing season.
