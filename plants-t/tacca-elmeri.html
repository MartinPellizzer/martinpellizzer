Tacca elmeri

Tacca elmeri, commonly known as the "black bat plant," is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, dark purple to black, velvety leaves that resemble the wings of a bat, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with three prominent petals and a distinctive, helmet-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Tacca elmeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
