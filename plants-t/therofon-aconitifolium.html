Therofon aconitifolium

Therofon aconitifolium, commonly known as the blue star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a rosette of blue-gray, fuzzy leaves that are lance-shaped and slightly succulent. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped blue or violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
