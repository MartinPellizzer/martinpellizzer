Trichodesma angustifolium subsp. argenteum

Trichodesma angustifolium subsp.

argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf trichodesma, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is due to its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.
