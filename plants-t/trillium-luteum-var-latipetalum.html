Trillium luteum var. latipetalum

Trillium luteum var. latipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled yellow trillium, is a rare and distinctive member of the Trillium genus.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with broad, spreading petals and three sepals, which give it a unique appearance. Native to specific regions of North America, this plant thrives in rich, moist woodlands and is often found in shaded environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three broad, heart-shaped leaflets.

The plant is noted for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it a valuable but delicate component of its native ecosystem.
