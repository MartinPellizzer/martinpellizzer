Tulipa strangulata var. obtusata

Tulipa strangulata var. obtusata, commonly known as the Broadleaf Turk's Cap Lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a distinctive black center. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and slightly wavy in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
