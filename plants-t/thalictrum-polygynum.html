Thalictrum polygynum

Thalictrum polygynum, commonly known as meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it produces follicles containing numerous seeds.
