Tibetiodes likiangensis

Tibetiodes likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan primrose, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, adapting to the harsh climatic conditions of its habitat. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
