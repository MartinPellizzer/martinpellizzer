Thymelaea nitida

Thymelaea nitida, commonly known as the silver thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring small, elliptical leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Thymelaea nitida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
