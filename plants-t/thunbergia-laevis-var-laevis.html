Thunbergia laevis var. laevis

Thunbergia laevis var. laevis, commonly known as the blue trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Thunbergia laevis var. laevis is a climbing plant that can reach significant heights if supported by a trellis or structure.
