Tillandsia demissa

Tillandsia demissa, commonly known as the silver vellum tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage that forms a velvety appearance, giving it its common name. This epiphytic plant does not require soil, instead absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Tillandsia demissa is popular among collectors for its unique aesthetic and low maintenance requirements.
