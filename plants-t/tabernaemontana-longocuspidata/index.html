Tabernaemontana Longocuspidata

Tabernaemontana longocuspidata is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. Known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers and lush green foliage, it is a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. This species belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a sought-after addition to gardens and indoor spaces. However, it is important to note that this plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with caution.
