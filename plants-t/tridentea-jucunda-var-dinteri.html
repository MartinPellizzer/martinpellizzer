Tridentea jucunda var. dinteri

Tridentea jucunda var. dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's trident leaf plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, trifoliate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
