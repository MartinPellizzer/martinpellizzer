Taraxacum korjakorum

Taraxacum korjakorum, commonly known as the Korjakov's dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flower head and serrated, hairy leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces a distinctive seed head with pappus that aids in wind dispersal.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the genus Taraxacum.
