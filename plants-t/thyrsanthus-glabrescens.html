Thyrsanthus glabrescens

Thyrsanthus glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth thyrsanthus, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

Thyrsanthus glabrescens is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
