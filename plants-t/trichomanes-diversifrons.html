Trichomanes diversifrons

Trichomanes diversifrons, commonly known as the broad-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lanceolate leaves that are often deeply lobed or divided. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the leaves, a common feature in ferns.

Trichomanes diversifrons is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
