Trigonella monspeliaca var. glabrata

Trigonella monspeliaca var. glabrata, commonly known as smooth fenugreek, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces slender, hairy pods containing several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
