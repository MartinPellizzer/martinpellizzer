Tieghemopanax mollis

Tieghemopanax mollis, commonly known as the glossy arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
