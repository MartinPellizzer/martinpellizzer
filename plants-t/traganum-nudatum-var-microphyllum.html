Traganum nudatum var. microphyllum

Traganum nudatum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves that store water, and its flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline soils, showcasing its tolerance to high salt concentrations. Its succulent structure helps it retain moisture in arid or brackish conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
