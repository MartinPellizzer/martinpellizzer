Tillandsia walteri var. herrerae

Tillandsia walteri var. herrerae, commonly known as the Herrera's air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces striking inflorescences with showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
