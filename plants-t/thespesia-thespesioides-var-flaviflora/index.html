Thespesia Thespesioides Var. Flaviflora

Thespesia thespesioides var. flaviflora is a striking flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Pacific. Known for its vibrant yellow blooms and lush green foliage, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators. It is often cultivated in gardens and coastal areas where it adds a tropical flair. Despite its popularity, it remains relatively underappreciated compared to more common ornamental plants.
