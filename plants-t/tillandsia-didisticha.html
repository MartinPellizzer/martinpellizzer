Tillandsia didisticha

Tillandsia didisticha, commonly known as the two-bracted air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant native to Central America, particularly Mexico. The plant lacks traditional roots and absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaf surfaces. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a dense rosette structure.

It produces small, white or greenish flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.
