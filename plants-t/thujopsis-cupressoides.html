Thujopsis cupressoides

Thujopsis cupressoides, commonly known as Japanese Falsecypress, is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It has a dense, pyramidal shape with scale-like leaves that are dark green on top and whitish underneath. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Thujopsis cupressoides is also valued for its aromatic wood, which has been historically used in craftsmanship.
