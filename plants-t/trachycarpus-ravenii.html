Trachycarpus ravenii

Trachycarpus ravenii, commonly known as the Raven's bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has stiff, linear leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are not showy but are important for its reproductive cycle.

This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
