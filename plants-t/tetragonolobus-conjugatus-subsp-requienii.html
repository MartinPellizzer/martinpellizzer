Tetragonolobus conjugatus subsp. requienii

Tetragonolobus conjugatus subsp.

requienii, commonly known as the slender legume, is a flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant typically grows in shrubland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces slender, hairy pods that contain small, round seeds.

This subspecies is noted for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
