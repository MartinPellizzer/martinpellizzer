Typhonium smitinandii

Typhonium smitinandii, commonly known as the Smitinandii arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant produces edible tubers that are used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and potential culinary uses, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and practical purposes.
