Triplophyllum funestum var. perpilosum, commonly known as the dark-leaved triplophyllum, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are often adorned with fine hairs. This species is native to tropical regions of Africa and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
