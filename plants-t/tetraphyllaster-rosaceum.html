Tetraphyllaster rosaceum

Tetraphyllaster rosaceum, commonly known as the roseleaf tetraphyll, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and it is often found in temperate regions with well-drained soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
