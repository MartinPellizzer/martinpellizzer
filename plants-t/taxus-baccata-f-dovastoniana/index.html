Taxus Baccata F. Dovastoniana

Taxus baccata f. dovastoniana is a rare and distinctive cultivar of the yew tree known for its unique foliage characteristics. This form exhibits a striking variegation pattern, with a mix of green and white or yellow tones on its leaves. It is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions. The plant's slow growth rate makes it ideal for small spaces and formal garden designs. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to thrive and maintain its distinctive appearance.
