Trichomanes tenerum

Trichomanes tenerum, commonly known as the soft maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant plant belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically light green to pale yellow in color. This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are thin and delicate, with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

Trichomanes tenerum is noted for its intricate structure and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in botanical gardens.
