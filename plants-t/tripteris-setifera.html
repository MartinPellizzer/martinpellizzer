Tripteris setifera

Tripteris setifera, commonly known as the hairy tripteris, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Tripteridaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments in Central and South America. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Tripteris setifera is often found in humid forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
