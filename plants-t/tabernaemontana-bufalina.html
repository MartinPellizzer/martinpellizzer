Tabernaemontana bufalina

Tabernaemontana bufalina, commonly known as the Buffaloberry or African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in warm climates.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
