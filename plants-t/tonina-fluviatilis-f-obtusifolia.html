Tonina fluviatilis f. obtusifolia

Tonina fluviatilis f. obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved river tonina, is a variant of the river tonina species native to tropical regions.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils near water bodies. Its flowers are small and clustered, often yellow or greenish in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to wetland environments.
