Thibaudia moricandii var. phyllireifolia

Thibaudia moricandii var. phyllireifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf thibaudia, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, dense hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It is adapted to harsh, dry environments and has a deep root system for water retention.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-maintenance gardens.
