Tropaeolum linearifolium

Tropaeolum linearifolium, commonly known as the slender nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and vibrant, spurred flowers that come in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is often grown for its ornamental value and edible flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it versatile for various garden settings.
