Thelypteris setosa

Thelypteris setosa, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brownish, velvety hairs.

Thelypteris setosa is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines, and is often found in temperate climates.
