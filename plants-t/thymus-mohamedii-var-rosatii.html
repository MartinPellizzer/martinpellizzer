Thymus mohamedii var. rosatii

Thymus mohamedii var. rosatii, commonly known as the Rosatii thyme, is a rare and ornamental plant native to specific regions of North Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for its horticultural value and medicinal properties.
