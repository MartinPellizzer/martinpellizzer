Trichomanes delicatum

Trichomanes delicatum, commonly known as the delicate huperzia, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant possesses a distinctive root system with specialized structures called rhizomes, which help in anchoring and nutrient absorption. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

Trichomanes delicatum is known for its slow growth and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
