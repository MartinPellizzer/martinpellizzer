Tephrosia decumbens

Tephrosia decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically spreads along the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Tephrosia decumbens is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
