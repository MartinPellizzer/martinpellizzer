Turbina Corymbosa F. Mollissima

Turbina corymbosa f. mollissima is a unique cultivar of the Turbina corymbosa plant, known for its striking appearance and adaptability. This variety features dense, velvety foliage that sets it apart from its parent species. It is often prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering to flourish. Its distinctive look makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and collectors.
