Tragacantha psilostyla

Tragacantha psilostyla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tragacanth, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its roots are known for their ability to produce a gel-like substance used in traditional medicine.

Tragacantha psilostyla thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
