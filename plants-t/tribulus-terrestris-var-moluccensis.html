Tribulus terrestris var. moluccensis

Tribulus terrestris var. moluccensis, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. The plant has hairy, erect stems and alternate leaves that are typically ovate or lance-shaped. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
