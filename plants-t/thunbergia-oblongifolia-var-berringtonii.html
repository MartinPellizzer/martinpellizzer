Thunbergia oblongifolia var. berringtonii

Thunbergia oblongifolia var. berringtonii, commonly known as the Berrington's Trumpet Vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple. The flowers are typically borne in clusters and are highly attractive to pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, flowering climbers.
