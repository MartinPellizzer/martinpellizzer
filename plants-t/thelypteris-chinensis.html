Thelypteris chinensis

Thelypteris chinensis, commonly known as Chinese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in China.

The fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive arrangement of pinnae and pinnules.
