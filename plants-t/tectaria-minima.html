Tectaria minima

Tectaria minima, commonly known as the small tectaria, is a small fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Tectaria minima is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
