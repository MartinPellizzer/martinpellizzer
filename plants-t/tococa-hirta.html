Tococa hirta

Tococa hirta, commonly known as the hairy tococa, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Tococa hirta is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Amazonian medicine.
