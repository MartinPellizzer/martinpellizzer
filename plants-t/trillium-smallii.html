Trillium smallii

Trillium smallii, commonly known as the small-flowered trillium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Trillium genus, which is part of the Melanthiaceae family. The plant features three elliptical leaves and three white petals that are often tinged with pink or red. It blooms in early spring and is characterized by its unique, three-part structure.

Trillium smallii is typically found in moist, shaded woodlands and is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
