Trigonella hierosolymitana

Trigonella hierosolymitana, commonly known as Syrian bitter vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces elongated, narrow pods containing several seeds. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
