Thymus aestivus var. barrelieri

Thymus aestivus var. barrelieri, commonly known as Barrelier's summer thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and aromatic leaves that emit a strong thyme scent. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
