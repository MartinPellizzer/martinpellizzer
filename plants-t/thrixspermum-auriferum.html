Thrixspermum auriferum

Thrixspermum auriferum, commonly known as the golden thrixspermum, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow inflorescences that bloom in the spring. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silvery hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
