Tunatea

Tunatea, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its bark is thick and rugged, providing a natural defense against pests and environmental stress.

Tunatea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
