Trichilia strigulosa

Trichilia strigulosa, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The tree is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and simple, opposite leaves.
