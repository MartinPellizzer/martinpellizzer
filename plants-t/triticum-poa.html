Triticum poa

Triticum poa, commonly known as false wheat, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Botanically, it is classified within the Poaceae family, which includes other grasses.
