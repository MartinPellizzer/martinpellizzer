Trichanthemis karataviensis

Trichanthemis karataviensis, commonly known as the Karatavi tansy, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
