Thalictrum baicalense var. japonicum

Thalictrum baicalense var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features delicate, compound leaves that are typically fern-like in appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in shaded landscapes.
