Tabernaemontana donnell-smithii var. costaricensis

Tabernaemontana donnell-smithii var. costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican star jasmine, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
