Torenia lanceolata

Torenia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant has heart-shaped or lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Torenia lanceolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
