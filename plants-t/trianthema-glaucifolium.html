Trianthema glaucifolium

Trianthema glaucifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, pale green leaves that have a waxy coating. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
