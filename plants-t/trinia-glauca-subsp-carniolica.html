Trinia glauca subsp. carniolica

Trinia glauca subsp.

carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolan trinia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners interested in cultivating native flora.
