Telipogon juan-torrezii

Telipogon juan-torrezii, commonly known as the Juan Torrez orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and specialized growth habits. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests or montane regions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.
