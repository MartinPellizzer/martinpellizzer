Tibouchina wasshausenii

Tibouchina wasshausenii, commonly known as the pink hibiscus or pink tibouchina, is a flowering shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features broad, glossy leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Tibouchina wasshausenii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
