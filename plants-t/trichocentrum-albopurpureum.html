Trichocentrum albopurpureum

Trichocentrum albopurpureum, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive purple-veined lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
