Tamaricaria elegans

Tamaricaria elegans, commonly known as the slender tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has small, scale-like leaves and produces clusters of greenish-yellow flowers. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and has deep root systems for water retention.

Tamaricaria elegans plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for wildlife in its native range.
