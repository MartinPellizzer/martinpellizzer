Taraxacum adamovicii

Taraxacum adamovicii, commonly known as the Adamovici's dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and deeply lobed leaves. The plant produces a distinctive seed head that disperses seeds via the wind, a common adaptation in the Asteraceae family. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and disturbed soils.
