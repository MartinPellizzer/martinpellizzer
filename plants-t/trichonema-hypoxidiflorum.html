Trichonema hypoxidiflorum

Trichonema hypoxidiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf lily of the valley, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green.
