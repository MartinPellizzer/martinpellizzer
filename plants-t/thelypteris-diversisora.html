Thelypteris diversisora

Thelypteris diversisora, commonly known as the variable shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often divided into segments with a distinctive, shield-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Thelypteris diversisora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
