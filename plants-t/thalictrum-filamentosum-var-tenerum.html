Thalictrum filamentosum var. tenerum

Thalictrum filamentosum var. tenerum, commonly known as the silky avens, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, feathery leaves that are finely divided and often covered in a soft, silky texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, petal-less flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is a popular choice for native plant gardens.
