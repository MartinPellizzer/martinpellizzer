Thymus × sublanuginosus

Thymus × sublanuginosus, commonly known as the woolly thyme, is a hybrid perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, gray-green foliage covered in fine, woolly hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
