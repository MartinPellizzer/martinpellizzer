Tragopogon duarius

Tragopogon duarius, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and covered in a waxy coating. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

Tragopogon duarius is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
