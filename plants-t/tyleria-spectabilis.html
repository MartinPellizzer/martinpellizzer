Tyleria spectabilis

Tyleria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy tyleria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
