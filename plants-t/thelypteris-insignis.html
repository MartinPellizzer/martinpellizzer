Thelypteris insignis

Thelypteris insignis, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, shield-shaped leaves.

Thelypteris insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
