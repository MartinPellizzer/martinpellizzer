Thalictrum flexicaule

Thalictrum flexicaule, commonly known as the meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is native to parts of Europe, Asia, and North America.
