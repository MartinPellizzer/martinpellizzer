Taccarum cylindricum

Taccarum cylindricum, commonly known as the cylindrical taccarum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Taccaridaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
