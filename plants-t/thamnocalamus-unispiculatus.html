Thamnocalamus unispiculatus

Thamnocalamus unispiculatus, commonly known as the single-spined bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect culms and narrow, linear leaves. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, giving it its common name.
