Tinus pubicalyx

Tinus pubicalyx, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically elliptical, dark green, and have a smooth, waxy texture. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, sunny climates.
