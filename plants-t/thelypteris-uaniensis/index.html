Thelypteris Uaniensis

Thelypteris uaniensis is a rare fern species native to the tropical forests of Central America. It is known for its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that display a striking combination of green and silver hues. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense rainforests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the face of deforestation and climate change.
