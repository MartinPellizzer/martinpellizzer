Tragia haguensis

Tragia haguensis, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and produces small, dry fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.

Tragia haguensis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
