Trachymene valida

Trachymene valida, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems and produces small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in dense, mist-like clusters. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often aromatic when crushed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Trachymene valida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
