Tetracera boerlagei

Tetracera boerlagei, commonly known as the Boerlage's tetracera, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a prominent midrib. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
