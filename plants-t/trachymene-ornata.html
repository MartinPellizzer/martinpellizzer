Trachymene ornata

Trachymene ornata, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Trachymene ornata is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
