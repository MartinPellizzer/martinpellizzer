Tillandsia pinnatodigitata

Tillandsia pinnatodigitata, commonly known as the feathered air plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, feather-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant does not require soil, instead relying on atmospheric humidity and rainfall for sustenance. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.

Tillandsia pinnatodigitata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain optimal humidity levels.
