Trisetum airoides f. depauperatum

Trisetum airoides f. depauperatum, commonly known as the slender tufted grass, is a rare and less commonly encountered variant of the species.

It is characterized by its reduced foliage and compact growth habit, which distinguishes it from the typical form of Trisetum airoides. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in loose panicles. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks.
