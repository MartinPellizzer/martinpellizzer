Tectaria devexa

Tectaria devexa, commonly known as the curled tectaria, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled fronds that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizomes are relatively short, and the fronds are usually leathery and dark green.

Tectaria devexa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
