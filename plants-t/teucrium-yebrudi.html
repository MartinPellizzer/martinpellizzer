Teucrium yebrudi

Teucrium yebrudi, commonly known as the Yebrud thyme.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet, typically blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of hairs, which helps reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Teucrium yebrudi is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
