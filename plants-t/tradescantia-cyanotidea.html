Tradescantia cyanotidea

Tradescantia cyanotidea, commonly known as the blue-eyed Susan.

This plant is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
