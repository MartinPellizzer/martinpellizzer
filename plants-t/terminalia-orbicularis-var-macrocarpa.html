Terminalia orbicularis var. macrocarpa

Terminalia orbicularis var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited Terminalia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by large, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique fruit structure and its role in local ecosystems.
