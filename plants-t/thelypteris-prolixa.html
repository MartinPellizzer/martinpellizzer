Thelypteris prolixa

Thelypteris prolixa, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and the fronds are often covered in small, scale-like structures.

Thelypteris prolixa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
