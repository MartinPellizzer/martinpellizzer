Thalictrum amabile

Thalictrum amabile, commonly known as the showy meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, silvery down. The plant produces showy, clustered racemes of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Thalictrum amabile is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
