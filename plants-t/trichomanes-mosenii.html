Trichomanes mosenii

Trichomanes mosenii, commonly known as the Mosen's maidenhair, is a fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Trichomanes mosenii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
