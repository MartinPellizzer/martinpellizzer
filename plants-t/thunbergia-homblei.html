Thunbergia homblei

Thunbergia homblei, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.

Thunbergia homblei requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its lush foliage and vibrant blooms.
