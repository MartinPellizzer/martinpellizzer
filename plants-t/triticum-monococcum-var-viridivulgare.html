Triticum monococcum var. viridivulgare

Triticum monococcum var. viridivulgare, commonly known as green wheatgrass, is a variety of wheat that is primarily cultivated for its nutritional value and use in health foods.

It is a grass species belonging to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making it popular in herbal and dietary supplements. It is typically harvested at the early growth stage to maximize its nutrient content.

Due to its high biomass and rapid growth, it is also used as a forage crop in some regions.
