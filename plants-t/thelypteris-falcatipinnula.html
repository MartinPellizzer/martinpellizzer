Thelypteris falcatipinnula

Thelypteris falcatipinnula, commonly known as the narrow-pinnuled holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically arching or ascending in growth habit. The plant features a rhizomatous system, with fertile fronds bearing sori that are clustered along the margins of the pinnae. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
