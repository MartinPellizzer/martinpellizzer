Tagetes feuillaei

Tagetes feuillaei, commonly known as the Mexican marigold or *Zinnia elegans*, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic foliage. Tagetes feuillaei thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its vibrant blooms and low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
