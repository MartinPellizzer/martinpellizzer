Telukrama obovata

Telukrama obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Telukrama obovata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
