Thalictrum minus f. jacquinianum

Thalictrum minus f. jacquinianum, commonly known as the Jacob's ladder, is a flowering plant in the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white, showy flowers. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in woodland gardens.
