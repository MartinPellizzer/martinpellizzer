Torenia caelestis

Torenia caelestis, commonly known as blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and prefers moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and unique flowering pattern.

Torenia caelestis is typically grown in gardens or as a houseplant, thriving in warm, humid conditions.
