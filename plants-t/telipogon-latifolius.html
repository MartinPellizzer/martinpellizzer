Telipogon latifolius

Telipogon latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its wide, strap-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. Native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a threatened species.
