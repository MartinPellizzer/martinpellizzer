Tephrosia frutescens

Tephrosia frutescens, commonly known as African blue bean, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered blue or violet flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Tephrosia frutescens is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
