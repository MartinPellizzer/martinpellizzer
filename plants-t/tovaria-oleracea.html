Tovaria oleracea

Tovaria oleracea, commonly known as the edible leaf plant, is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a mild, slightly sweet flavor. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its nutritional value, it is often cultivated for both culinary and medicinal purposes.
