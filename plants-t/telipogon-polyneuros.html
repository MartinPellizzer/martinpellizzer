Telipogon polyneuros

Telipogon polyneuros, commonly known as the slender sand sedge, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands and marshes of South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family.
