Tanacetum densum subsp. sivasicum

Tanacetum densum subsp.

sivasicum, commonly known as the Sivasi mountain tansy, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and aromatic foliage.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and small yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
