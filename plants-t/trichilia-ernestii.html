Trichilia ernestii

Trichilia ernestii, commonly known as African sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic wood. The plant has compound leaves with alternating, ovate leaflets. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody, three-lobed fruits.

Trichilia ernestii is valued for its hard, durable wood used in traditional craftsmanship and as a source of essential oils.
