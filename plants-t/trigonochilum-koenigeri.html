Trigonochilum koenigeri

Trigonochilum koenigeri, commonly known as the Koeniger's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a few leaves and produces small, pale green to white flowers. Its flowers are characterized by three sepals and three petals, with the lip being distinctly different in shape.

Trigonochilum koenigeri is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in undergrowth among other epiphytic and terrestrial orchids.
