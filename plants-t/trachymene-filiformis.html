Trachymene filiformis

Trachymene filiformis, commonly known as the slender false mallow, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Trachymene filiformis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
