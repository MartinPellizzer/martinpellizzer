Tiliacora warneckei

Tiliacora warneckei, commonly known as the Himalayan tiliacora, is a climbing shrub native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Sterculiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by elongated, woody pods containing small, smooth seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional uses, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
