Trifolium semipilosum var. sennii

Trifolium semipilosum var. sennii, commonly known as the Sennii clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically pale green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
