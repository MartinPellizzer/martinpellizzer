Tunica argentii

Tunica argentii, commonly known as silverleaf flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Tunica argentii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
