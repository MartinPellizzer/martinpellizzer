Trochera geniculata

Trochera geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers, which are often borne in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
