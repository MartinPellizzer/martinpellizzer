Thelymitra odora

Thelymitra odora, commonly known as the fragrant sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in grassy areas and is characterized by its striking, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single spike bearing several flowers. Thelymitra odora is a perennial species that blooms from late winter to early spring, making it a notable addition to native gardens.

Its unique fragrance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
