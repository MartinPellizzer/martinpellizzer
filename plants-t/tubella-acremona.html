Tubella acremona

Tubella acremona, commonly known as the common cushion plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a low-growing, cushion-like growth form with dense clusters of small, oval leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Tubella acremona is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
