Tephrosia aequilata subsp. mlanjeana

mlanjeana, commonly known as Mlanje tephrosia, is a flowering plant species native to eastern Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
