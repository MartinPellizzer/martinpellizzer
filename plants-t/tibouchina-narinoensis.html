Tibouchina narinoensis

Tibouchina narinoensis, commonly known as the Narino beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters.
