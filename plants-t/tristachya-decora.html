Tristachya decora

Tristachya decora, commonly known as the ornamental grass or golden grass, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bright green or golden in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters along the stem. It is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Tristachya decora is often used in landscaping for its visual texture and drought tolerance.
