Thelypteris stipitata

Thelypteris stipitata, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are wiry and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Thelypteris stipitata is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticulture.
