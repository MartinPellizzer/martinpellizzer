Tuberolabium camperenik

Tuberolabium camperenik, commonly known as the Camper's tuberous orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, tuberous roots and its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and collections.

Tuberolabium camperenik is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
