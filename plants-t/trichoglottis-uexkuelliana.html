Trichoglottis uexkuelliana

Trichoglottis uexkuelliana, commonly known as the Uexkull's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive fragrance. It is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Trichoglottis uexkuelliana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral characteristics.
