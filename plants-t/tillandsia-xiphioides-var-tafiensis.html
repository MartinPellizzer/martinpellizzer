Tillandsia xiphioides var. tafiensis

Tillandsia xiphioides var. tafiensis, commonly known as the Tafiensis air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant features rosettes of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
