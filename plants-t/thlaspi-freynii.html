Thlaspi freynii

Thlaspi freynii, commonly known as Freyn's cabbages, is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers.

The plant produces small, round or elongated seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are usually basal, rosette-like, and covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs.
