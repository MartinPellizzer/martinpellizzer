Tephrosia longana

Tephrosia longana, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a tropical shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its seed pods are slender, elongated, and contain several oblong seeds.

Tephrosia longana is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
