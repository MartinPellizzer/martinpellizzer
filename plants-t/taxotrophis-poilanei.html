Taxotrophis poilanei

Taxotrophis poilanei, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers exhibit a specialized pollination mechanism, attracting specific insect pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
