Tillandsia Seemannii Var. Mezii

Tillandsia seemannii var. mezii is a unique air plant known for its striking silver-green foliage and delicate flowers. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This variety is prized by plant enthusiasts for its low maintenance and ability to grow without soil. Its foliage often features intricate patterns that make it a standout in any collection. With proper care, it can flourish indoors or outdoors, adding a touch of natural beauty to any space.
