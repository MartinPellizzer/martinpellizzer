Timonius rotundus

Timonius rotundus, commonly known as the roundleaf timon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint, and it produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers in the spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or shaded gardens.

Timonius rotundus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming properties.
