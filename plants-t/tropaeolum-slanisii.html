Tropaeolum slanisii

Tropaeolum slanisii, commonly known as the Slanisi runner bean, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange. The plant produces edible pods similar to those of the common runner bean, though it is less widely cultivated. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often grown for both its ornamental value and culinary uses.

Its rapid growth habit makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a quick, decorative addition to their landscape.
