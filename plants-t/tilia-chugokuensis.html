Tilia chugokuensis

Tilia chugokuensis, commonly known as the Chugoku linden, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer. The tree produces small, round fruits known as samaras, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.
