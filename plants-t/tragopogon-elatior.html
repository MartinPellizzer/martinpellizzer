Tragopogon elatior

Tragopogon elatior, commonly known as the tall yellow goat's beard, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and bright yellow ray flowers that resemble a beard. The plant produces a distinctive, hollow, cylindrical fruit that splits into two parts when mature. It thrives in sunny, open areas such as meadows and roadsides.

Tragopogon elatior is often found in temperate regions of North America and is known for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
