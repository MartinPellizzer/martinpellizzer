Trachysperma aquatica

Trachysperma aquatica, commonly known as water basil, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to humid conditions. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.

Trachysperma aquatica is valued for its medicinal properties and distinct fragrance.
