Tainia siamensis

Tainia siamensis, commonly known as the Siam tainia, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the arum family. Tainia siamensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and water gardens.
