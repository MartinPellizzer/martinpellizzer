Tragacantha Fraxinella

Tragacantha fraxinella is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the legume family. It is native to parts of southern Europe and the Mediterranean region. The plant is characterized by its unique, branched stems and small, pod-like fruits. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate species for future generations.
