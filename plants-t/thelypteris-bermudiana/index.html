Thelypteris Bermudiana

Thelypteris bermudiana is a rare fern native to the island of Bermuda. It thrives in the unique coastal and limestone habitats of the region. This plant is known for its delicate fronds and distinctive appearance. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare species from further decline.
