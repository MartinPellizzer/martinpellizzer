Thuja occidentalis f. recurvata

Thuja occidentalis f. recurvata, commonly known as the twisted arborvitae, is a unique form of the eastern white cedar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally twisted growth pattern, which gives it an ornamental appearance. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dark green and closely pressed to the stems. It is typically evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its visual interest and hardy nature.
