Tragia recta

Tragia recta, commonly known as the common tragia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, sticky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly pubescent surface.

Tragia recta is adapted to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
