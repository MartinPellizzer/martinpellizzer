Tulipa Planifolia Var. Saracenica

Saracen tulip, scientifically known as Tulipa planifolia var. saracenica, is a striking member of the tulip family native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. This variety is prized for its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in a range of colors including red, yellow, and orange. With its distinctive foliage and robust growth, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to various climates. Its historical significance and ornamental value have made it a beloved addition to botanical gardens and private collections worldwide.
