Trichodium album

Trichodium album, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces tiny, white flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name.

Trichodium album is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
