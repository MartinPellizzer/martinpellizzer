Trichoglottis Rosea

Trichoglottis rosea is a captivating orchid species known for its striking pink and white flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, which includes over 25,000 species worldwide. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and collectors. Its delicate blooms and elegant structure add a touch of beauty to any indoor or outdoor garden.
