Thymus ovatus var. concolor

Thymus ovatus var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are densely packed along its stems. The plant produces tiny, pale blue to violet flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often used in culinary applications and traditional medicine due to its aromatic properties.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
