Tillandsia × baptistana

Tillandsia × baptistana, commonly known as the silver vase bromeliad, is a hybrid epiphytic plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that forms a rosette shape, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped inflorescences in shades of pink, red, or orange, which attract pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and good air circulation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
