Trasus ustulatus

Trasus ustulatus, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Trasus ustulatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
