Trichosterigma Californicum

Trichosterigma californicum is a unique flowering plant native to the western United States. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas. This species is known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its ability to survive in harsh environments makes it an important part of local ecosystems. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
