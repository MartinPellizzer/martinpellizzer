Tridax oligodonta

Tridax oligodonta, commonly known as the smallleaf tridax, is an annual flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in a spherical shape. Tridax oligodonta is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its invasive tendencies.
