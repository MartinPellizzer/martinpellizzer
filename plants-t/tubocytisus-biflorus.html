Tubocytisus biflorus

Tubocytisus biflorus, commonly known as the silverleaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and small, dark blue berries. The plant has a woody stem with thorny branches, and its flowers are typically pale yellow or green. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Tubocytisus biflorus is used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
