Themelium blechnifrons

Themelium blechnifrons, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in groups called sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Themelium blechnifrons is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
