Tephrosia stormsii var. stormsii

Tephrosia stormsii var. stormsii, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a climbing or shrub growth habit. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their vibrant color.

The plant also has nitrogen-fixing abilities, contributing to soil fertility.
