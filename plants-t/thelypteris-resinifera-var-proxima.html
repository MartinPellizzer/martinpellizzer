Thelypteris resinifera var. proxima

Thelypteris resinifera var. proxima, commonly known as the resinous water fern, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with small, rounded pinnae that give it a lacy appearance.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and its role in maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
