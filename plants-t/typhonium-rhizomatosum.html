Typhonium rhizomatosum

Typhonium rhizomatosum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or white spadices surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, making it both a valuable ornamental and a potential invasive species.
