Tacinga × flammea

Tacinga × flammea, commonly known as the flame tacinga, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Tacinga × flammea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
