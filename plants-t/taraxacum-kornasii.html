Taraxacum kornasii

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or steppe environments.

Taraxacum kornasii is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
