Trichomanes lindenii

Trichomanes lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a unique morphology, featuring a creeping rhizome with erect, clustered sporophytes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Trichomanes lindenii is notable for its primitive features and is of interest to botanists studying the evolution of ferns.
