Tilia glabra f. fastigiata

Tilia glabra f. fastigiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lime or columnar linden, is a cultivar of the common lime tree.

It is characterized by its upright, columnar growth habit, making it suitable for small gardens or urban landscapes. The leaves are heart-shaped, serrated, and typically have a pale green underside. It produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in early summer, which are followed by small, round fruits.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
