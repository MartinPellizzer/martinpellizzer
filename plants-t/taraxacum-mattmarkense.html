Taraxacum mattmarkense

Taraxacum mattmarkense, commonly known as the Mattmark dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.
