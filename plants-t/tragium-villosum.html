Tragium villosum

Tragium villosum, commonly known as the hairy tragus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small and yellow, often clustered in loose inflorescences.

It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
