Thymus asenii

Thymus asenii, commonly known as the Asenii thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and square stems. The plant typically features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, serrated, and emit a strong thyme scent when crushed.

Thymus asenii is often found in rocky or limestone soils and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
