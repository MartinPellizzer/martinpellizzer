Terminalia habeensis

Terminalia habeensis, commonly known as the Habe's terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
