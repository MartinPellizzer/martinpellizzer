Thalictrum uncatum subsp. angustialatum

Thalictrum uncatum subsp.

angustialatum, commonly known as narrowleaf meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its stems are usually slender and branched, and it produces numerous stamens with long filaments.
