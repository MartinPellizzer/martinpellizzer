Tydaea karsteniana

Tydaea karsteniana, commonly known as the Karsten's grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. Tydaea karsteniana is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
