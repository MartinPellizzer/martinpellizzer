Trichopteryx delicatissima

Trichopteryx delicatissima, commonly known as the delicate orchid, is a rare and exquisite flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing small, delicate blossoms in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
