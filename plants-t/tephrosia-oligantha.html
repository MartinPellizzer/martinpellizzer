Tephrosia oligantha

Tephrosia oligantha, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers, which are typically arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a slender pod that splits open to release its seeds.

Tephrosia oligantha is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
