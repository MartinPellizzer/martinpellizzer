Thelymitra batesii

Thelymitra batesii, commonly known as the Bates' sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in open forests and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, leafy stem with a single, showy flower that is usually yellow or orange in color. Its floral structure is typical of the Orchidaceae family, with three sepals and three petals, one of which is prominently marked.

Thelymitra batesii is a short-lived perennial, often flowering in spring and early summer.
