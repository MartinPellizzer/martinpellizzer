Thymelaea Putorioides Var. Chlorantha

Blue thymelaea, scientifically known as Thymelaea putorioides var. chlorantha, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the island of Madeira. With its vibrant blue blooms and dense, evergreen foliage, it stands out as a unique addition to any garden or landscape. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various growing conditions. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated species in the plant world.
