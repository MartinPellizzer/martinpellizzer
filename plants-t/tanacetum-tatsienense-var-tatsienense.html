Tanacetum tatsienense var. tatsienense

Tanacetum tatsienense var. tatsienense, commonly known as the Tatsienlou tansy, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and yellowish-green flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, typical of the daisy family.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
