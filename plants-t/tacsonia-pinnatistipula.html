Tacsonia pinnatistipula

Tacsonia pinnatistipula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tacsonia, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage.

Its leaves are pinnately compound, with slender, elongated leaflets that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or along stream banks. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Tacsonia pinnatistipula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
