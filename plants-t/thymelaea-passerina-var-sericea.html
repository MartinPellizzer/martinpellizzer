Thymelaea passerina var. sericea

Thymelaea passerina var. sericea, commonly known as silky thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
