Typhonium horsfieldii

Typhonium horsfieldii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or red spadices surrounded by a broad, white or green spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Typhonium horsfieldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens or as a houseplant.
