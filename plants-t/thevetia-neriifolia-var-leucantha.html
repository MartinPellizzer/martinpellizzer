Thevetia neriifolia var. leucantha

Thevetia neriifolia var. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered yellow oleander, is a variant of the yellow oleander characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers.

It is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.
