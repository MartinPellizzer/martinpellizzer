Thymus × czernjajevi

Thymus × czernjajevi, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces compact clusters of pink or purple flowers, which bloom in early summer. Thymus × czernjajevi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its pungent aroma and essential oil content.
