Thelymitra rubra

Thelymitra rubra, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive red-tipped leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Thelymitra rubra is a perennial species that blooms during the spring and early summer months.
