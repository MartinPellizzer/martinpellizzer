Tulipa sedunii

Tulipa sedunii, commonly known as the Dagestani tulip, is a rare and striking species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its large, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky, mountainous habitats and prefers well-drained soil. Its bulbs are relatively small and require specific conditions to thrive.

Tulipa sedunii is prized for its ornamental value and is a subject of interest in botanical research due to its limited distribution.
