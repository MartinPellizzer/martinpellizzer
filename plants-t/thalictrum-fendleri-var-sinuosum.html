Thalictrum fendleri var. sinuosum

Thalictrum fendleri var. sinuosum, commonly known as the Sinuous Meadow rue, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features finely divided, fern-like leaves and delicate, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks. Its flowers are arranged in upright clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in mountainous regions of western North America.
