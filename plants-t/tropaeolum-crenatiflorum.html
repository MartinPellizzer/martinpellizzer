Tropaeolum crenatiflorum

Tropaeolum crenatiflorum, commonly known as the crested nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, rounded leaves and showy, orange or yellow flowers. The plant is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its stems are trailing or climbing, and it produces small, edible seed pods.

Tropaeolum crenatiflorum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
