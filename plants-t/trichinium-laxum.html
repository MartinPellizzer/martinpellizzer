Trichinium laxum

Trichinium laxum, commonly known as the slender trichinium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and can be used in traditional herbal medicine.
