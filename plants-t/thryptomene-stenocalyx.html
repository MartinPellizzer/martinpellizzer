Thryptomene stenocalyx

Thryptomene stenocalyx, commonly known as narrow-leaved thryptomene, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and other aromatic plants. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are typically clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes.

It is commonly found in heathlands and dry forests, thriving in well-drained soils.
