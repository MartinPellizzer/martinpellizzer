Thryptomene auriculata

Thryptomene auriculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf thryptomene, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is typically found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.
