Tabernaemontana arborea

Tabernaemontana arborea, commonly known as the tree angel's trumpets, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often fragrant. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Tabernaemontana arborea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
