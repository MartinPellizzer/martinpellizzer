Teclea hanangensis

Teclea hanangensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian rose.

It is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia, belonging to the family Rosaceae. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a serrated margin.

Teclea hanangensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
