Thymus laxispicatus

Thymus laxispicatus, commonly known as the slender thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, aromatic leaves and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its essential oils.

Thymus laxispicatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
