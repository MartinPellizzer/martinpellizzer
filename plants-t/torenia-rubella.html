Torenia rubella

Torenia rubella, commonly known as the red torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or pink in color, with a distinctive hooded structure. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.

Torenia rubella is a perennial herb that grows in USDA hardiness zones 9 through 11.
