Tissa macrotheca var. scariosa

Tissa macrotheca var. scariosa, commonly known as the scarious tissa, is a variant of the tissa plant characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

The leaves are typically broad and have a rough, scaly texture on the underside, which is a defining feature of this variety. It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow or greenish in color.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphological traits and its role in local ecosystems.
