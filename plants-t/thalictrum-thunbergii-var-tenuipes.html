Thalictrum thunbergii var. tenuipes

Thalictrum thunbergii var. tenuipes, commonly known as slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and emit a subtle, spicy scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, arranged in branched clusters. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its graceful foliage and subtle floral display.
