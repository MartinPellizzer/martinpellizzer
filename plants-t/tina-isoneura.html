Tina isoneura

Tina isoneura, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in hard, smooth pods that split open when mature.

Tina isoneura is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
