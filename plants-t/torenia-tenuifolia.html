Torenia tenuifolia

Torenia tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a pointed tip. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and hanging baskets due to its ornamental value.
