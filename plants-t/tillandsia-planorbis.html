Tillandsia planorbis

Tillandsia planorbis, commonly known as the orb-leaf air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant that thrives without soil, absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often forming a rosette shape that helps retain water.

Tillandsia planorbis is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
