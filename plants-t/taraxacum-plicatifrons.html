Taraxacum plicatifrons

Taraxacum plicatifrons, commonly known as the pleated dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pleated leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into white, fluffy seeds, typical of the dandelion genus. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Taraxacum plicatifrons is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
