Tulipa tubergeniana

Tulipa tubergeniana, commonly known as the Turk's Cap Lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Tulipa tubergeniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
