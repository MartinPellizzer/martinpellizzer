Telinaria florida

Telinaria florida, commonly known as the Florida starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that produces small, yellowish flowers followed by star-shaped fruits. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are oval and dark green, providing a lush appearance.

The fruit, while edible, is typically consumed raw or used in culinary applications.
