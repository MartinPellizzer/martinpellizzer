Tragopyrum billardierei

Tragopyrum billardierei, commonly known as the slender burrgrass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and distinctive, bristly seed heads. Tragopyrum billardierei is adapted to arid conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
