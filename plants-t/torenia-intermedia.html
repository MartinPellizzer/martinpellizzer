Torenia intermedia

Torenia intermedia, commonly known as the whorled torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Torenia intermedia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
