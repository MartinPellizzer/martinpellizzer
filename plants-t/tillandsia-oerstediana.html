Tillandsia oerstediana

Tillandsia oerstediana, commonly known as the Oersted's air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a small water-holding cup at the center. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires regular misting to maintain moisture.

Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.
