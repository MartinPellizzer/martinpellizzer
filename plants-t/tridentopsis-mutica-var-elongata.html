Tridentopsis mutica var. elongata

Tridentopsis mutica var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated trident leaf cactus, is a unique succulent native to South America.

It features a compact, branching stem with narrow, elongated leaves that give it its distinguishing appearance. The leaves are typically green with a slight bluish tint and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle ornamental value.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
