Tragacantha jodostachys

Tragacantha jodostachys, commonly known as the Persian gum tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for producing a resinous gum, which has been traditionally used in various medicinal and industrial applications.

Its adaptability to dry climates makes it an important component of local ecosystems in its native habitat.
