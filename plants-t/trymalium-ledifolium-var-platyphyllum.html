Trymalium ledifolium var. platyphyllum

Trymalium ledifolium var. platyphyllum, commonly known as the broadleaf trymalium, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that give it its specific epithet "platyphyllum.". The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it an attractive addition to botanical collections.
