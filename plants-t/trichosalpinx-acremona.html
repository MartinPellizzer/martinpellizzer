Trichosalpinx acremona

Trichosalpinx acremona, commonly known as the red-flowered spider orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals. The plant produces vibrant red and purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Trichosalpinx acremona is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
