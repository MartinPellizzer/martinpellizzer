Tillandsia delicatula

Tillandsia delicatula, commonly known as the delicate airplant, is a species of bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
