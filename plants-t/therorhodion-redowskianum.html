Therorhodion redowskianum

Therorhodion redowskianum, commonly known as the Redowskian star moss, is a species of liverwort found in moist, shaded environments.

It belongs to the family Pterobryaceae and is characterized by its dense, star-like growth pattern. The plant has a flattened, prostrate structure with small, scale-like leaves arranged in a rosette formation. It thrives in temperate regions and is often found on rock surfaces or in damp forest floors.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists studying bryophyte ecology.
