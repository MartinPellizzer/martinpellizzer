Tylophora chingtungensis

Tylophora chingtungensis, commonly known as the Ching Tung Tylophora, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
