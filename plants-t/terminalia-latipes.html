Terminalia latipes

Terminalia latipes, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, palmately lobed leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a snowball-like appearance. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Terminalia latipes is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
