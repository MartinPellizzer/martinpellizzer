Thelymitra aristata

Thelymitra aristata, commonly known as the rainforest helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical rainforests of eastern Australia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on the forest floor or in rock crevices. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are strap-like and linear, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

The orchid's unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native range.
