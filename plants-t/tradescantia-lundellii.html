Tradescantia lundellii

Tradescantia lundellii, commonly known as the red-veined nettle.

This evergreen perennial plant is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. It features striking foliage with deep green leaves that have prominent red veins, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in spikes, typical of the Commelinales order.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
