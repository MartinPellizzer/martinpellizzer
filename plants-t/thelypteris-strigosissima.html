Thelypteris strigosissima

Thelypteris strigosissima, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are covered in short, stiff hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, round spore cases.

Thelypteris strigosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
