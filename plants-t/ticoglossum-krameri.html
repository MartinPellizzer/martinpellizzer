Ticoglossum krameri

Ticoglossum krameri, commonly known as the Krameri's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
