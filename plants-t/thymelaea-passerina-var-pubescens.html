Thymelaea passerina var. pubescens

Thymelaea passerina var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy thymelaea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in Mediterranean climates.
