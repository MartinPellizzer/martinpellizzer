Terminalia ovatifolia

Terminalia ovatifolia, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, ovate leaves that are often clustered at the ends of branches. The tree produces white, fluffy flowers that resemble small balls, giving it its common name. Its fruit is a capsule containing small, hard seeds.

Terminalia ovatifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
