Therogeron denticulatus

Therogeron denticulatus, commonly known as the toothed therogeron, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive denticulate leaves.

It belongs to the family Therogeronaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant exhibits a herbaceous growth habit with slender, branching stems. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-white in color, and it produces capsule fruits containing minute seeds.
