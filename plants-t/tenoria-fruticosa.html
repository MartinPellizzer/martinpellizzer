Tenoria fruticosa

Tenoria fruticosa, commonly known as the shrub lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure to the plant.

Tenoria fruticosa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
