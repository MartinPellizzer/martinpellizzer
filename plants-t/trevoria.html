Trevoria

Trevoria, commonly known as the "buttercup flower," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that resemble those of the buttercup. The plants typically have simple, alternate leaves with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. Trevoria species are often found in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions.
