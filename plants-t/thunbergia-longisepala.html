Thunbergia longisepala

Thunbergia longisepala, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Thunbergia longisepala is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
