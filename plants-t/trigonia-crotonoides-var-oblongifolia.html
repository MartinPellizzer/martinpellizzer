Trigonia crotonoides var. oblongifolia

Trigonia crotonoides var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dark green, ovate leaves with prominent veins and a rough, textured surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its bark is thick and corky, often exuding a milky sap when damaged.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
