Tamarix songarica

Tamarix songarica, commonly known as the Chinese tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and small, scale-like leaves that are typically deciduous. The plant produces pink to reddish flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a common species in desert and coastal areas.

Tamarix songarica is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which can improve soil fertility in degraded lands.
