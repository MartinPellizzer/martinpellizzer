Tupistra kwangtungiensis

Tupistra kwangtungiensis, commonly known as the Kwangtung holly or Chinese holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bright red berries that appear in the winter. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. The berries are an important food source for birds during the colder months.
