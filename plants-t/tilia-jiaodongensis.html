Tilia jiaodongensis

Tilia jiaodongensis, commonly known as the Jiaodong linden, is a rare deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil, preferring temperate climates. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, while its wood is lightweight and used for various traditional purposes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
