Trichaurus

Trichaurus, commonly known as the common thornapple, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and produces striking violet or blue flowers that bloom in summer. Trichaurus is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
