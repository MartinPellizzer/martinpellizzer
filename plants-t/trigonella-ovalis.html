Trigonella ovalis

Trigonella ovalis, commonly known as the oval fenugreek, is an annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by slender, elongated pods. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in dry, sandy soils. Its seeds are small and have a mild, slightly sweet aroma.

Trigonella ovalis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
