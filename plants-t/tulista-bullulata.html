Tulista bullulata

Tulista bullulata, commonly known as the smooth tulip, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural habitats.

Tulista bullulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and containers.
