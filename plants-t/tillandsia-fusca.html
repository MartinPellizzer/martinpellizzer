Tillandsia fusca

Tillandsia fusca, commonly known as the dark tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically dark green to almost black in color. The plant lacks traditional roots and absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaf surfaces. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the wild.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
