Trichostachys laurentii

Trichostachys laurentii, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a tall, clump-forming grass native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, silver-white leaves with a distinctive hairy texture, which give it its common name. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that are often used in floral arrangements. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored choice for gardens and landscapes.
