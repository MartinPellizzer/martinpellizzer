Tripogon wardii

Tripogon wardii, commonly known as the narrowleaf grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green in color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Tripogon wardii is often found in grasslands and along the edges of water bodies, where it contributes to soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
