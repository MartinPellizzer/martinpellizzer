Tulbaghia pauciflora

Tulbaghia pauciflora, commonly known as the slender tulbaghia, is a South African plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Tulbaghia pauciflora is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
