Tetranthera salicifolia var. laurifolia

Tetranthera salicifolia var. laurifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved tetranthera, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
