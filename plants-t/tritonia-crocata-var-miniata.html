Tritonia crocata var. miniata

Tritonia crocata var. miniata, commonly known as the miniature crocus, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces delicate, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It blooms in early spring, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its compact size and vibrant colors, it is well-suited for small spaces and adds a touch of elegance to gardens.
