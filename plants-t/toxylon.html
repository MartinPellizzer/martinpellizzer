Toxylon

Toxylon, commonly known as the African sand dollar, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like species that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Toxylon is adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
