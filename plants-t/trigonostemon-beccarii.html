Trigonostemon beccarii

Trigonostemon beccarii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its dark, aromatic bark and dense, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

Trigonostemon beccarii is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in construction and furniture-making.
