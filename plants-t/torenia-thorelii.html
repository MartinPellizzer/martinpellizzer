Torenia thorelii

Torenia thorelii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are borne in clusters at the top of the stems.

Torenia thorelii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
