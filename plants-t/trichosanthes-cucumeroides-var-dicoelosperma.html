Trichosanthes cucumeroides var. dicoelosperma

Trichosanthes cucumeroides var. dicoelosperma, commonly known as the Indian bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to Asia.

It produces large, elongated fruits with a warty surface and a bitter taste. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers that are typically unisexual. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruits, though they require careful preparation due to their bitterness.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
