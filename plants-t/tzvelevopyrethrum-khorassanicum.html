Tzvelevopyrethrum khorassanicum

Tzvelevopyrethrum khorassanicum, commonly known as the Khorasan pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide, making it valuable in organic pest control. It thrives in dry, temperate climates and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical structure includes narrow leaves and a compact growth habit, adapted to its native habitat.
