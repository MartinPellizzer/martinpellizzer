Tricyrtis dilatata

Tricyrtis dilatata, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features delicate, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with intricate patterns resembling spider webs.

Tricyrtis dilatata thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
