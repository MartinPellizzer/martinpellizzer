Tuberaria macrosepala f. subglabra

Tuberaria macrosepala f. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-fruited tuberaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive tuberous roots and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring.
