Tanacetum tatsienense var. tanacetopsis

Tanacetum tatsienense var. tanacetopsis, commonly known as the Sichuan tansy, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are composed of ray florets and disk florets, typical of the daisy family.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
