Theopsis microphylla

Theopsis microphylla, commonly known as the silver leaf daisy, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, silver-gray leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

The flowers bloom in spring and are followed by small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season.
