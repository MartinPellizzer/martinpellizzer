Thymus hohenackeri

Thymus hohenackeri, commonly known as Hohenacker thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are aromatic and have been used in traditional herbal medicine for their antiseptic properties.

Thymus hohenackeri thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.
