Tulakenia cyanoides

Tulakenia cyanoides, commonly known as the blue tulip tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental species.

Tulakenia cyanoides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
