Tetrastylis ovalis

Tetrastylis ovalis, commonly known as the oval false orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several oval-shaped leaves arranged in a rosette. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Tetrastylis ovalis is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
