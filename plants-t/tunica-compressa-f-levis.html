Tunica compressa f. levis

Tunica compressa f. levis, commonly known as the smooth tunica, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features broad, flat leaves that are often described as having a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant's stems are typically short and compact, contributing to its overall low-growing habit. It is native to specific regions with particular soil and climate conditions, making it a challenging species to cultivate.
