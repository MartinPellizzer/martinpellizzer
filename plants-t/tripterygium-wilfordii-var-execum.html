Tripterygium wilfordii var. execum

Tripterygium wilfordii var. execum, commonly known as the Chinese herb "Lei Guan" or "Thunder God Vine," is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its potent effects, it is used cautiously in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressive properties.
