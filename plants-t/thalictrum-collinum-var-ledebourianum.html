Thalictrum collinum var. ledebourianum

Thalictrum collinum var. ledebourianum, commonly known as the Alpine meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its slender stems and compound leaves contribute to its ornamental appeal in alpine gardens.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in high-altitude ecosystems.
