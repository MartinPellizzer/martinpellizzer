Trichomanes pinnatum var. kaulfussii

Trichomanes pinnatum var. kaulfussii, commonly known as the Kaulfuss's maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant exhibits a distinctive rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that help it spread. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
