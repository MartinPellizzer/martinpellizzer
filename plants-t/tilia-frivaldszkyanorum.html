Tilia frivaldszkyanorum

Tilia frivaldszkyanorum, commonly known as the Frivaldszky lime, is a rare and endangered species of flowering tree native to the Carpathian Basin.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to specific soil conditions and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
