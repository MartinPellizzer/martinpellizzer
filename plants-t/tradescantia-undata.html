Tradescantia undata

Tradescantia undata, commonly known as spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking violet or blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Tradescantia undata is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowers.
