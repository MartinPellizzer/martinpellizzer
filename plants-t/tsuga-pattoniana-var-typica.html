Tsuga pattoniana var. typica

Tsuga pattoniana var. typica, commonly known as the Paton's hemlock, is a variant of the hemlock tree native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needles, and small, spherical cones. The plant thrives in moist, cool environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage provides dense shade, making it a valuable species for forest ecosystems.

However, it is vulnerable to environmental stress and diseases, which have impacted its population in recent years.
