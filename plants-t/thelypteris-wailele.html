Thelypteris wailele

Thelypteris wailele, commonly known as the Wailele holly fern, is a species of fern native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a medium-sized fern with fronds that are typically arching and have a leathery texture. The plant features bipinnate leaves with closely spaced, lance-shaped pinnae that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, commonly found in forest understories.

Thelypteris wailele is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
