Thunia alba var. marschalliana

Thunia alba var. marschalliana, commonly known as the White Thunia or Marschalliana Thunia, is a rare and striking aquatic plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, floating flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and unique features, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
