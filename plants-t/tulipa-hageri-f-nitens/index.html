Tulipa Hageri F. Nitens

Tulipa hageri f. nitens is a rare and exquisite tulip variety known for its striking white petals and delicate appearance. This cultivar belongs to the genus Tulipa, which is part of the lily family, Liliaceae. It is prized by horticulturists for its unique coloration and ability to thrive in specific growing conditions. The plant is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and seasonal blooms. Its rarity makes it a sought-after addition to botanical collections and specialized gardens.
