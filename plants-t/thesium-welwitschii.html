Thesium welwitschii

Thesium welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.
