Trichoglottis javanica

Trichoglottis javanica, commonly known as the Java trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure and produces linear, grass-like leaves. The plant blooms with small, greenish-white flowers that have a distinctive trichomes on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
