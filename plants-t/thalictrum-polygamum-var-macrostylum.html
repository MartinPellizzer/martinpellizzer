Thalictrum polygamum var. macrostylum

Thalictrum polygamum var. macrostylum, commonly known as the showy meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided and emit a mild, pleasant fragrance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
