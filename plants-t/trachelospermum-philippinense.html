Trachelospermum philippinense

Trachelospermum philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to the Philippines.

It features fragrant white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet, jasmine-like scent. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and is typically evergreen. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its climbing habit makes it suitable for trellises, walls, or as a ground cover in gardens.
