Tillandsia gardneri var. rupicola

Tillandsia gardneri var. rupicola, commonly known as the rock tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant thrives in rocky or crevice habitats, where it absorbs moisture and nutrients from the air. Its inflorescence produces small, white or pale purple flowers, which bloom during the warmer months.

This variety is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
