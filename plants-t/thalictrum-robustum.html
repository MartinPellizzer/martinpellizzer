Thalictrum robustum

Thalictrum robustum, commonly known as the showy avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream sides. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial to full shade.

Thalictrum robustum is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
