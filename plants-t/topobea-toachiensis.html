Topobea toachiensis

Topobea toachiensis, commonly known as the Toachi mountain plant, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
