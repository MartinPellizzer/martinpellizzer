Trigonella spruneriana f. glabra

Trigonella spruneriana f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth trigonella, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. Its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves distinguish it from other varieties of Trigonella.

This plant is often found in open, sunny habitats and is of interest to botanists due to its ecological and potential medicinal significance.
