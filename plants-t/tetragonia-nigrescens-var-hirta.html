Tetragonia nigrescens var. hirta

Tetragonia nigrescens var. hirta, commonly known as sea rocket or coastal rocket, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or rocky soils, often found in maritime climates. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in spring and are followed by edible, tangy fruits.

This species is valued for its nutritional content and is sometimes cultivated as a leafy green vegetable.
