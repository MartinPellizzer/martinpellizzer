Tilia rubra var. begoniifolia

Tilia rubra var. begoniifolia, commonly known as the begoniifolia linden, is a unique variant of the linden tree characterized by its distinctive foliage.

This plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color, often with a reddish tinge on the underside. The leaves are broader and more lobed compared to typical Tilia species, giving it a striking visual appearance. It is native to specific regions in Asia and is cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
