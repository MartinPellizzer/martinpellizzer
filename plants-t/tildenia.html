Tildenia

Tildenia, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant belonging to the family Bignoniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink or white flowers that resemble the appearance of ghostly apparitions, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Tildenia has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces fruit that contains several seeds.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
