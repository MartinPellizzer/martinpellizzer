Tillandsia polystachia

Tillandsia polystachia, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and does not require soil for growth. It produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences with small, white to pale purple flowers.

Tillandsia polystachia is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
