Thelypteris filix-mas f. producta

Thelypteris filix-mas f. producta, commonly known as the common shield fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a dense arrangement of pinnae that give it a lush, feathery appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
