Thilachium africanum

Thilachium africanum, commonly known as the African broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant features slender, branched stems and produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Thilachium africanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to dry, sandy soils.
