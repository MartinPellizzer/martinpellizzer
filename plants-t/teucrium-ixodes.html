Teucrium ixodes

Teucrium ixodes, commonly known as the purple bugloss, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often found in Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong aromatic scent.

Teucrium ixodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
