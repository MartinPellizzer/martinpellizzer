Tabernaemontana speciosa

Tabernaemontana speciosa, commonly known as the showy tabernanthe, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces clusters of fragrant blooms, often with a strong, sweet scent. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant flowers.

Tabernaemontana speciosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
