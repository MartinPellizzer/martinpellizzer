Thelypteris batacorum

Thelypteris batacorum, commonly known as the Batacorum shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces spores in small, clustered sori located on the underside of the fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Thelypteris batacorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
