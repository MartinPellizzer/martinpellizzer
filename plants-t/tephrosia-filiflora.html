Tephrosia filiflora

Tephrosia filiflora, commonly known as the threadleaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with small, thread-like leaflets, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually small and clustered in inflorescences, often in shades of yellow or white.

Tephrosia filiflora is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
