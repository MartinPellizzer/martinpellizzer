Tillandsia romeroi var. gruberi

Tillandsia romeroi var. gruberi, commonly known as the Gruber's air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture from the air. It produces striking inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
