Tuberaria globulariifolia var. major

Tuberaria globulariifolia var. major, commonly known as the globular lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bulbous underground stem, which stores nutrients and water for survival during dry periods. The plant produces showy, globular flower clusters that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
