Tolpis barbata var. macrantha

Tolpis barbata var. macrantha, commonly known as the broad-flowered tolpis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, yellow to orange composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Tolpis barbata var. macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
