Thalictrum elatum lusus ledebourii

Thalictrum elatum lusus ledebourii, commonly known as the Alpine meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided, giving it a lacey appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and alpine landscapes.
