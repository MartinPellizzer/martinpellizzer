Tephrocactus Alexanderi Subvar. Brachyacanthus

Tephrocactus alexanderi subvar. brachyacanthus is a rare and unique cactus native to the deserts of northern Mexico. It is known for its distinctive short spines and compact growth habit. This subvariety is particularly sought after by collectors due to its limited distribution and striking appearance. Its survival depends on specific environmental conditions, making it a vulnerable species. Understanding and protecting this plant is essential for preserving desert biodiversity.
