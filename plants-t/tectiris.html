Tectiris

Tectiris, commonly known as the tree fern, is a type of fern that belongs to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a mass of overlapping, waxy leaf bases. The plant has a rhizome that grows underground, supporting the growth of the trunk and fronds.
