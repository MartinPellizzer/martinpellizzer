Thymus × deylii

Thymus × deylii, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.

Thymus × deylii is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
