Ternstroemia peduncularis var. stenophylla

Ternstroemia peduncularis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ternstroemia, is a shrub native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, glossy leaves that are typically narrow and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
