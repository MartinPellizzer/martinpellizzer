Teucrium × pseudoaragonense

Teucrium × pseudoaragonense, commonly known as hybrid teucrium, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. The plant has aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and extended flowering period.
