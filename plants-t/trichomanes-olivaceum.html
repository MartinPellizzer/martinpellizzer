Trichomanes olivaceum

Trichomanes olivaceum, commonly known as the olive fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually bright green, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.
