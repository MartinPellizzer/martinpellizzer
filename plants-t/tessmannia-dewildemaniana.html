Tessmannia dewildemaniana

Tessmannia dewildemaniana, commonly known as the slender tessmannia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces white to pinkish flowers in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It is often found in heathlands and dry forests, thriving in well-drained soils.
