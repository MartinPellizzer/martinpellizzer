Timotocia confertiflora var. laciniata

Timotocia confertiflora var. laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf timotocia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, almost ethereal appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching moderate heights with a compact, bushy form. Its foliage is usually a deep green, with some cultivars exhibiting variegation or reddish hues.

While it produces small, inconspicuous flowers, the plant is primarily cultivated for its unique and visually appealing foliage.
