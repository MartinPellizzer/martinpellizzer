Telis graeca

Telis graeca, commonly known as the Greek bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
