Thlaspi vulcanorum, commonly known as the Vulcan's cabbages, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and features small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or volcanic soils, often in alpine or subalpine regions. It has hairy leaves and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.
