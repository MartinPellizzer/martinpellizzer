Thalictrum simplex subsp. tenuifolium

Thalictrum simplex subsp.

tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia. It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are narrow and finely divided, giving it a lacy appearance.

The plant produces upright clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
