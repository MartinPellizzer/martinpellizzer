Thalictrum peruvianum

Thalictrum peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Thalictrum peruvianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
