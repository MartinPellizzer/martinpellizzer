Thuja wareana var. aurea

Thuja wareana var. aurea, commonly known as golden thuja, is a variant of the thuja plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its narrow, conical shape. The leaves are scale-like and densely packed, giving the plant a compact, ornamental appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in horticulture.
