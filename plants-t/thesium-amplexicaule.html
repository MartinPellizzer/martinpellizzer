Thesium amplexicaule, commonly known as the clasping thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Thesium amplexicaule is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
