Trematolobelia kauaiensis

Trematolobelia kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai's ghost orchid, is a rare and endangered plant native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is a member of the orchid family and is characterized by its unique, delicate appearance with pale greenish-yellow flowers. The plant grows on the trunks of trees, often in shaded, humid environments within the rainforest. It has specific ecological requirements, making it highly sensitive to environmental changes.

Due to habitat loss and other threats, conservation efforts are critical for its survival.
