Timonius avenis var. magnifructus

Timonius avenis var. magnifructus, commonly known as the golden fruit timonius, is a rare and unique variant of the timonius avenis species.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange fruits that mature in late summer. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with dense, dark green foliage. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in early spring.

This botanical variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its visual appeal.
