Teucrium decaisnei

Teucrium decaisnei, commonly known as the African germander, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are often gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it is adapted to arid environments.

Teucrium decaisnei is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
