Tephroseris crassifolia

Tephroseris crassifolia, commonly known as the thick-leaved tephroseris, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

Tephroseris crassifolia is often found in rocky soils and dry landscapes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
