Turraea abyssinica var. longipedicellata

Turraea abyssinica var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed turraea, is a tropical shrub native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with long, slender petioles. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
