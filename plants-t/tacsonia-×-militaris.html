Tacsonia × militaris

Tacsonia × militaris, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Tacsonia and Hibiscus species.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with broad, dark green leaves. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Tacsonia × militaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
