Trymalium rosmarinifolium

Trymalium rosmarinifolium, commonly known as silverleaf lily or coastal lily, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to coastal conditions with salt spray.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
