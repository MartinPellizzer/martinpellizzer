Teucrium ozturkii

Teucrium ozturkii, commonly known as Turkish germander, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and lanceolate in shape. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in whorled clusters.

Teucrium ozturkii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
