Terminalia hassleriana

Terminalia hassleriana, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its clustered white flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its fruits are small, three-lobed capsules that contain several seeds.

Terminalia hassleriana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
