Tradescantia seubertiana

Tradescantia seubertiana, commonly known as the silver sword or red-edge tradescantia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features lance-shaped leaves with a striking red margin. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
