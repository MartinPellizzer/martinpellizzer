Tapura martiniae

Tapura martiniae, commonly known as the Martinia tapur.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
