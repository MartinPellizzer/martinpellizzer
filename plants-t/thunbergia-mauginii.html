Thunbergia mauginii

Thunbergia mauginii, commonly known as the golden shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy appearance. The plant produces small, elliptical fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
