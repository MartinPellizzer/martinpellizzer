Tricalysia jasminiflora var. hypotephros

Tricalysia jasminiflora var. hypotephros, commonly known as the pale-flowered tricalysia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant, white flowers that resemble jasmine, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shade-loving landscapes.
