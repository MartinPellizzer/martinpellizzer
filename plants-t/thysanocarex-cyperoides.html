Thysanocarex cyperoides

Thysanocarex cyperoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

Thysanocarex cyperoides produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are typically found in the leaf axils.
