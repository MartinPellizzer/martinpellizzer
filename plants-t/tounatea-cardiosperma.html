Tounatea cardiosperma

Tounatea cardiosperma, commonly known as the heartseed tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces distinctive seed pods containing a single, large seed. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruit structure. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

The heartseed tree is also valued for its potential in traditional medicine and ecological applications.
