Tamarix smyrnensis

Tamarix smyrnensis, commonly known as the Turkish tamarisk, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and pink to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a hardy species in coastal and desert areas. Its wood is lightweight and has been historically used for construction and fuel.

Tamarix smyrnensis plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
