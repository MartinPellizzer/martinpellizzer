Thuja pyramidalis

Thuja pyramidalis, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its pyramidal shape and dense, dark green foliage. The tree has scale-like leaves arranged in crossed pairs, and its cones are small and woody. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Thuja pyramidalis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
