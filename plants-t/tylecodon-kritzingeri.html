Tylecodon kritzingeri

Tylecodon kritzingeri, commonly known as the Kritzinger's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
