Thunbergia stuhlmanniana

Thunbergia stuhlmanniana, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
