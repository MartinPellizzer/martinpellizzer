Teucrium × arenicola

Teucrium × arenicola, commonly known as sand teucrium, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy habitats. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical uses in traditional herbal remedies.
