Thrasya setosa

Thrasya setosa, commonly known as the slender sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. It is often found growing in dunes and along the shoreline.

Thrasya setosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh coastal environments.
