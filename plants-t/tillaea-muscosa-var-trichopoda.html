Tillaea muscosa var. trichopoda

Tillaea muscosa var. trichopoda, commonly known as the mossy tillaea, is a low-growing, perennial plant that thrives in rocky and arid environments.

It belongs to the family Tillaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, leafy shoots that are often covered in a fine layer of moss, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

This variety is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
