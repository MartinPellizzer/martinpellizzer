Theopsis transarisanensis

Theopsis transarisanensis, commonly known as the Transarisan starling plant, is a flowering plant native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the unique environmental conditions of its native region.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
