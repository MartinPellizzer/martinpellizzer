Tamarix elegans

Tamarix elegans, commonly known as the silver leaf tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss, making it well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Tamarix elegans is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
