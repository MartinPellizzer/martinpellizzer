Tetraspis

It is characterized by its distinctive four-leaf clover shape, which is considered a symbol of good luck in many cultures. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Its flowers are small and usually purple or white, and it produces small, round seeds.
