Tiniaria alata

Tiniaria alata, commonly known as the winged tiniaria, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its distinctive winged leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds. Tiniaria alata is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological studies and horticultural research.
