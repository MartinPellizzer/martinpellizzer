Thelymitra smithiana

Thelymitra smithiana, commonly known as Smith's rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or heathland environments. The plant features a compact, low-growing habit with a single, erect stem that bears several linear leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a subtle fragrance, typical of many orchid species.

Thelymitra smithiana is a hardy plant that thrives in well-drained conditions and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
