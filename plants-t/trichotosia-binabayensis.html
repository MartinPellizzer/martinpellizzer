Trichotosia binabayensis

Trichotosia binabayensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a slender, upright growth habit with long, strap-like leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
