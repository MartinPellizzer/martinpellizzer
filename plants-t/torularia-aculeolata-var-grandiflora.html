Torularia aculeolata var. grandiflora

Torularia aculeolata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered torularia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces oblong, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
