Troximon odoratum

Troximon odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically features long, slender leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful flowers. It thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique scent and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
