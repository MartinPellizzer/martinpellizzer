Talasium

Talasium, commonly known as the silver sword, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a rosette of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. Talasium is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
