Trichomanes radicans var. giganteum

Trichomanes radicans var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant walking fern, is a variant of the walking fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, creeping rhizomes that allow it to spread along the forest floor. The plant features feathery, lance-shaped fronds that can grow quite large, often reaching several feet in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its unique growth habit and striking appearance make it a notable ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
