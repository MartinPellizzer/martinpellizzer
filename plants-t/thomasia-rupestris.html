Thomasia rupestris

Thomasia rupestris, commonly known as the rock thomasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Thomasia rupestris is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
