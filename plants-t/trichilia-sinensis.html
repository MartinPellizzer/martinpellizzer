Trichilia sinensis

Trichilia sinensis, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable timber species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.
