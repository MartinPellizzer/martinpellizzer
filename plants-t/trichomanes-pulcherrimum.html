Trichomanes pulcherrimum

Trichomanes pulcherrimum, commonly known as the golden huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright rhizome and its distinctive, elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The leaves are typically bright green and may turn a golden hue as they mature, giving the plant its common name. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
