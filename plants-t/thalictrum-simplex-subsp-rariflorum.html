Thalictrum simplex subsp. rariflorum

Thalictrum simplex subsp.

rariflorum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a slender, upright stem and is often found in temperate climates.

The subspecies is distinguished by its reduced floral display compared to other members of the Thalictrum genus.
