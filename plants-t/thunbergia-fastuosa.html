Thunbergia fastuosa

Thunbergia fastuosa, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative vine due to its attractive floral display.
