Tritonia × crocosmiiflora

Tritonia × crocosmiiflora, commonly known as the hybrid tiger lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red, with a distinctive red-orange throat. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that form a basal rosette, and it produces long, slender stems that bear the vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
