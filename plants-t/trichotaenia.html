Trichotaenia

Trichotaenia, commonly known as the silver sword, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South America.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Trichotaenia is adapted to arid environments and can store water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
