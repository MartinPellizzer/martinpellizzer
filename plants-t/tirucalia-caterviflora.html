Tirucalia caterviflora

Tirucalia caterviflora, commonly known as the clustered-flowered tirucalia, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of India and Sri Lanka. The plant is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences that produce small yellow flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.
