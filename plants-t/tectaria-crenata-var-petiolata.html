Tectaria crenata var. petiolata

Tectaria crenata var. petiolata, commonly known as the petiolated shield fern, is a variant of the shield fern genus Tectaria.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with fronds that are pinnately compound and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a rough, textured surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shady garden settings.
