Typhonium ramosum

Typhonium ramosum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or swamp arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in wetlands and along riverbanks.

Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
