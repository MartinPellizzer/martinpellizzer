Thlaspi salisii

Thlaspi salisii, commonly known as the alpine cabbages or rock cabbages, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous plant that typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has basal leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in harsh conditions. Its small, white or yellow flowers are arranged in a raceme and bloom in the spring.

Thlaspi salisii is known for its ability to tolerate cold climates and is often found in mountainous regions across Europe and Asia.
