Thelymitra magnifica

Thelymitra magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent sun-orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature bright yellow and purple hues, with a distinctive spurred lip. The plant grows in sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions, thriving in full sun. Its botanical features include a tall, erect stem with broad, strap-like leaves.

Thelymitra magnifica is a perennial herb that blooms in spring, attracting various pollinators with its vibrant display.
