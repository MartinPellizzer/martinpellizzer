Thyrsanthus palustris

Thyrsanthus palustris, commonly known as the swamp thyrsin, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or swampy habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, pale green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight hairy texture.

Thyrsanthus palustris plays an important ecological role in its native wetland environments.
