Trichomanes hymenoides f. pabstiana

Trichomanes hymenoides f. pabstiana, commonly known as the Pabst's maidenhair fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features small, thin, and often overlapping leaves that are typically green and covered in a fine layer of trichomes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in dense forest understories.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
