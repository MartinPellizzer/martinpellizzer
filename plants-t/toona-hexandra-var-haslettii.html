Toona hexandra var. haslettii

Toona hexandra var. haslettii, commonly known as the haslett's toona, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fig-like fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its timber and has traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
