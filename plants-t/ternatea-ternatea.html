Ternatea ternatea

Ternatea ternatea, commonly known as the Ternatea tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Ternatea ternatea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
