Trichera numantina

Trichera numantina, commonly known as the Numantina thornbush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.

Trichera numantina is well adapted to withstand drought and is an important component of local ecosystems.
