Tillandsia werner-rauhiana

Tillandsia werner-rauhiana, commonly known as the Rauh's air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that can store water, allowing it to thrive in humid environments. It produces striking inflorescences with showy, violet or purple flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
