Tephrosia fruticosa

Tephrosia fruticosa, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions. It is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.

Tephrosia fruticosa plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
