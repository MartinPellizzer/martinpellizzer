Thibaudia wardii

Thibaudia wardii, commonly known as the African flame or flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat, and are typically borne in clusters. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy.

Thibaudia wardii is valued for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental appeal.
