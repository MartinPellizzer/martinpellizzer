Tabernaemontana polyantha

Tabernaemontana polyantha, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Tabernaemontana polyantha is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
