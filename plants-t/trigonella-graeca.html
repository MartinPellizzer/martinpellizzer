Trigonella graeca

Trigonella graeca, commonly known as Greek lentil or mountain lentil, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, lens-shaped seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. Trigonella graeca thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and valuable for traditional agricultural practices.
