Tillandsia pretiosa

Tillandsia pretiosa, commonly known as the silver pine tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its striking silver-green foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
