Trifolium aintabense

Trifolium aintabense, commonly known as the Aintab clover, is a flowering plant species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Trifolium aintabense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal uses.
