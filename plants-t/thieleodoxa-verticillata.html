Thieleodoxa verticillata

Thieleodoxa verticillata, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of showy, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for.
