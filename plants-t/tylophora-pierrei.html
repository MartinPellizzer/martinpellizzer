Tylophora pierrei

Tylophora pierrei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive aroma. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Tylophora pierrei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
