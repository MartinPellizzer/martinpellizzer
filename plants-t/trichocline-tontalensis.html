Trichocline tontalensis

Trichocline tontalensis, commonly known as the Tontalensis trichocline, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical sunflower-like structure. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, dry fruits known as achenes.

Trichocline tontalensis is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
