Tilia paucicostata var. firma

Tilia paucicostata var. firma, commonly known as the firm-leaf linden, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, firm texture. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in early summer, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
