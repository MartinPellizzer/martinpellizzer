Thunbergia nepalensis

Thunbergia nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and India.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow.
