Tanaosolen nervosus

Tanaosolen nervosus, commonly known as the slender nerve fern, is a species of fern native to the humid tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that exhibit a distinctive veining pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under the canopy of tropical forests. Its rhizomes are slender and creeping, allowing it to spread gradually across the forest floor.
