Torenia latibracteata

Torenia latibracteata, commonly known as the broad bract torena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Torenia latibracteata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
