Tarrietia trifoliolata var. peralata

Tarrietia trifoliolata var. peralata, commonly known as the silverleaf tarrietia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
