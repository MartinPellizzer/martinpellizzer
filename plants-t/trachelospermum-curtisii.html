Trachelospermum curtisii

Trachelospermum curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a woody vine characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for climbing structures or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant scent and evergreen foliage.
