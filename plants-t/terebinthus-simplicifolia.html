Terebinthus simplicifolia

Terebinthus simplicifolia, commonly known as the simple-leaved terebinth, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features simple, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a spherical drupe that turns red when mature.

Terebinthus simplicifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
