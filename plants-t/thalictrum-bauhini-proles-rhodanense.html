Thalictrum bauhini proles rhodanense

Thalictrum bauhini proles rhodanense, commonly known as the Rhodanese meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream sides. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
