Thelypteris baramensis

Thelypteris baramensis, commonly known as the Baram fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it plays a role in maintaining soil stability in its native habitats.

The Baram fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in tropical ecosystems.
