Trichothalamus valderia

Trichothalamus valderia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Trichothalamus valderia is often found growing in rocky crevices or on shaded forest floors.
