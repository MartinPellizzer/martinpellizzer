Trimeriza

Trimeriza, commonly known as the flame tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. Trimeriza species are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their vibrant floral display.
