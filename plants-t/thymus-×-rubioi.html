Thymus × rubioi

Thymus × rubioi, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and square stems. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically small, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its fragrant profile.

Thymus × rubioi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant and for its culinary and medicinal uses.
