Timonius fuscus

Timonius fuscus, commonly known as the dark timonius, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.

Timonius fuscus is relatively rare and is found in specific regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
