Tephrosia telfairii

Tephrosia telfairii, commonly known as the Telfair's tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in wetland or swampy areas, thriving in moist, acidic soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Tephrosia telfairii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
