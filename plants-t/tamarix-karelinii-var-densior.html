Tamarix karelinii var. densior

Tamarix karelinii var. densior, commonly known as the dense tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dense clusters of small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces pink to reddish flowers in summer, which are borne in elongated clusters. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or saline soils.

Its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen make it a useful plant for soil stabilization in degraded areas.
