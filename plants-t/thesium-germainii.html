Thesium germainii

Thesium germainii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. Thesium germainii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and flowering time make it a notable species in its native habitat.
