Trichospermum incanum

Trichospermum incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Trichospermum incanum is adapted to arid conditions and has a low-growing, sprawling habit.
