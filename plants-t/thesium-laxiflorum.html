Thesium laxiflorum

Thesium laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered thesium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae, which is closely related to the grass family. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
