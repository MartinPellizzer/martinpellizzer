Torenia inflata

Torenia inflata, commonly known as the inflated torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to pink. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and opposite leaves. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Torenia inflata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
