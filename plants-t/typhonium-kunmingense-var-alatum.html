Typhonium kunmingense var. alatum

Typhonium kunmingense var. alatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces striking purple to violet spathes that enclose its small, yellowish-white spadices. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
