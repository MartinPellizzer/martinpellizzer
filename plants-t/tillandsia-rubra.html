Tillandsia rubra

Tillandsia rubra, commonly known as the red tillandsia, is a species of air plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant thrives by absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain, without requiring soil. Its leaves are typically long and strap-like, with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight.

Tillandsia rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
