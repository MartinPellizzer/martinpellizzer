Trianthema salsoloides var. salsoloides

Trianthema salsoloides var. salsoloides, commonly known as coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in sandy or saline soils, thriving in coastal environments. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface.

It is well adapted to harsh coastal conditions, including high salinity and strong winds.
