Tibouchina nervulosa var. glabrata

Tibouchina nervulosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved tibouchina, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, smooth leaves that are lance-shaped and often arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a valuable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
