Trichilia dregeana

Trichilia dregeana, commonly known as African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, palmately compound leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spiny capsules containing oily seeds. These seeds are valued for their high oil content, which is used in cosmetics and traditional medicine.

The plant also plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
