Tainia ponggolensis

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically yellow or orange in color.
