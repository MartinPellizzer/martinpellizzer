Tornabenea insularis

Tornabenea insularis, commonly known as the island tansy, is a flowering plant native to specific island regions in the Mediterranean.

The plant typically grows in coastal and saline environments, showing adaptations to withstand salt spray and sandy soils. Tornabenea insularis is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological niche.
