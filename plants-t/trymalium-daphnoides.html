Trymalium daphnoides

Trymalium daphnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf lily or silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
