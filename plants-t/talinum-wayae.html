Talinum wayae

Talinum wayae, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features spreading stems and lobed leaves, often with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible.

Talinum wayae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
