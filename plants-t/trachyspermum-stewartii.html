Trachyspermum stewartii

Trachyspermum stewartii, commonly known as bishop's weed, is a member of the Apiaceae family and is native to regions of southern and eastern Asia.

Trachyspermum stewartii is also noted for its potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
