Tectaria laotica

Tectaria laotica, commonly known as the Laotian shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of rhizomorphs, which help in nutrient absorption.

Tectaria laotica plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat by contributing to soil stability and providing shelter for small organisms.
