Thesium unyikense var. puberulum

Thesium unyikense var. puberulum, commonly known as the silky thesium, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy foliage that is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in the genus Thesium.
