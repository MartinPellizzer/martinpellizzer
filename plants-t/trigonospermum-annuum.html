Trigonospermum annuum

Trigonospermum annuum, commonly known as the annual trigonospermum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a suitable candidate for gardens in temperate climates.
