Ticorea

Ticorea, commonly known as the "snowy bellflower," is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plants are characterized by their showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to blue and violet.
