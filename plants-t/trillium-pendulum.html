Trillium pendulum

Trillium pendulum, commonly known as the hanging trillium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features a single, nodding flower that is typically white or pale green, with three petals and three sepals. The plant has three broad, waxy leaves that are arranged in a whorl at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in rich, organic soil.

Trillium pendulum is a member of the Melanthiaceae family and is known for its elegant, delicate appearance.
