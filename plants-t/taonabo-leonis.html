Taonabo leonis

Taonabo leonis, commonly known as the lion's tail, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, cascading panicles of violet and white flowers that resemble a lion's tail. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green hue. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Taonabo leonis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
