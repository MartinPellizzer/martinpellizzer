Taeniopsis sulcata

Taeniopsis sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag sword fern, is a unique and intriguing member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped fronds that give it its common name. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological purposes in suitable climates.
