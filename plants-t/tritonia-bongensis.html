Tritonia bongensis

Tritonia bongensis, commonly known as the Bong's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of white and pink.
