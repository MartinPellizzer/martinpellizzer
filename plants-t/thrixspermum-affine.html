Thrixspermum affine

Thrixspermum affine, commonly known as the silverleaf thrixspermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that reflects sunlight. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Thrixspermum affine is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
