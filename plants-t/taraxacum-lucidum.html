Taraxacum lucidum

Taraxacum lucidum, commonly known as the shining dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is characterized by its bright yellow flower heads and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a distinctive seed head composed of tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in meadows, lawns, and disturbed areas.

Taraxacum lucidum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
