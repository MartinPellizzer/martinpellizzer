Tsuga canadensis f. sargentii

Tsuga canadensis f. sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's hemlock, is a rare and distinctive form of the eastern hemlock.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and a more compact growth habit compared to the typical hemlock. The leaves are typically shorter and more closely packed, giving the plant a unique appearance. It is native to the northeastern United States and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species for conservation efforts.
