Typhonium flagelliforme var. angustissimum

Typhonium flagelliforme var. angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf snake plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces a tall, erect inflorescence bearing small, greenish-white spadix surrounded by a spathe. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate low light conditions.
