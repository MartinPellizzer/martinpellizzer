Trigonostemon tuberculatus

Trigonostemon tuberculatus, commonly known as the warty hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive warty texture on its stems and leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Trigonostemon tuberculatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
