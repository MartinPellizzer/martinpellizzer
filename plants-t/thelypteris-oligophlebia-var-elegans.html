Thelypteris oligophlebia var. elegans

Thelypteris oligophlebia var. elegans, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family (Thelypteridaceae).

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds with finely divided, silky-textured leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

The leaves are usually dark green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.
