Trichodiadema stellatum

Trichodiadema stellatum, commonly known as the star-shaped ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as bloom. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Trichodiadema stellatum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
