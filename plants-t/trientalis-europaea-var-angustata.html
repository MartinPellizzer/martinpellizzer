Trientalis europaea var. angustata

Trientalis europaea var. angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf starflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
