Turbinicarpus valdezianus var. pazoutii

Turbinicarpus valdezianus var. pazoutii, commonly known as the Pazouti Turbinicarpus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its gray-green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is appreciated for its unique appearance and ease of care in desert gardens.
