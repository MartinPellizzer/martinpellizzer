Tephrocactus platyacanthus var. angustispinus

Tephrocactus platyacanthus var. angustispinus, commonly known as the narrow-spined hedgehog cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of spines, which are typically pale yellow to brown in color. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
