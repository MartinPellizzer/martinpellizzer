Tripteris hyoseroides

Tripteris hyoseroides, commonly known as the golden fan fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Tripteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are compound, with a fan-like structure composed of numerous small, lance-shaped leaflets.

Tripteris hyoseroides is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
