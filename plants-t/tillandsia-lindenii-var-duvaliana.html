Tillandsia lindenii var. duvaliana

Tillandsia lindenii var. duvaliana, commonly known as the Duval's air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant thrives by absorbing moisture and nutrients through its dense, fibrous root system and specialized leaf structures. It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage, which forms a cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with vibrant purple or red flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
