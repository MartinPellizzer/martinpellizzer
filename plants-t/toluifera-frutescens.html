Toluifera frutescens

Toluifera frutescens, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, typically in shades of purple, blue, and white. Its leaves are velvety and often have a distinctive waxy coating.
