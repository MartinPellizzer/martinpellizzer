Thesium chinense

Thesium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a slender, branched stem covered with small, scale-like leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is the production of yellow or orange flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Thesium chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
