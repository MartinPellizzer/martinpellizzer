Tomophyllum bipinnatum

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that give it a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thick layer of root hairs, which aid in water absorption.

The fronds are usually light green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter, making it a striking addition to its natural habitat.
