Tabernaemontana poeppigii

Tabernaemontana poeppigii, commonly known as the Christmas star or star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its flowers are typically produced during the winter months, giving it its common name.
