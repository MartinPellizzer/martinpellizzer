Thelypteris Afzelii

Thelypteris afzelii is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions around the world. It is known for its delicate, finely divided fronds that often display a striking green or bronze hue. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively understudied in comparison to more common fern species.
