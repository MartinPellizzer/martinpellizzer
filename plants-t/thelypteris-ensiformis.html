Thelypteris ensiformis

Thelypteris ensiformis, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its feathery, lance-shaped fronds that are often used in horticulture. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce roots that help anchor it to substrates.

Thelypteris ensiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
