Thymus linearis var. album

Thymus linearis var. album, commonly known as white thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, pale white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are linear, narrow, and aromatic, emitting a mild thyme scent when crushed. This variety is often used in culinary applications and as a ornamental plant in gardens due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
