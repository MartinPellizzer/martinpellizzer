Tinus semicrenata

Tinus semicrenata, commonly known as the crested lettuce, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically pale green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Tinus semicrenata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
