Tillandsia aeranthos var. aemula

Tillandsia aeranthos var. aemula, commonly known as the silver pine tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, branched rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped inflorescences with striking white or pink flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
