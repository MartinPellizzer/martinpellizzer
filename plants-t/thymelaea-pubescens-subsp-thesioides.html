Thymelaea pubescens subsp. thesioides

Thymelaea pubescens subsp.

thesioides, commonly known as the coastal thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a shrub characterized by its small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh coastal environments.
