Thelypteris linearis

Thelypteris linearis, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Its leaves are usually bipinnate, with small, closely spaced pinnae that give it a feathery appearance.

Thelypteris linearis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
