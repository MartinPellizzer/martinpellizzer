Trapa natans f. suecica

Trapa natans f. suecica, commonly known as the European water shield, is a rare aquatic plant characterized by its floating, shield-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers.

It is native to freshwater habitats in Europe and is typically found in still or slow-moving waters. The plant has a submerged root system that anchors it in the sediment, while its leaves provide a surface for photosynthesis.
