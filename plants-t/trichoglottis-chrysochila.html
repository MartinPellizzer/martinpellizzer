Trichoglottis chrysochila

Trichoglottis chrysochila, commonly known as the golden-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and covered with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a distinctive golden lip. It prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Trichoglottis chrysochila is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in tropical gardens.
