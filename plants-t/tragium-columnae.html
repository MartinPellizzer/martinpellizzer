Tragium columnae

Tragium columnae, commonly known as the columnar tragium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Tragium columnae is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in sunny, well-drained habitats.
