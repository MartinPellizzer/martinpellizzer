Trichoglottis smithii

Trichoglottis smithii, commonly known as the Smith's trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Trichoglottis smithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
