Tulorima cymbalaria

Tulorima cymbalaria, commonly known as the common bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple. The plant grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Tulorima cymbalaria is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
