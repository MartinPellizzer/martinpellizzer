Turritis ochroleuca

Turritis ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow tansy.

This flowering plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to the eastern United States. It is characterized by its yellowish-green, fern-like leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland areas and thrives in partial shade.

It is often found in the understory of forests, where it contributes to the biodiversity of the ecosystem.
