Topea micrantha

Topea micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered topea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, daisy-like flowers are usually yellow or pale green, and it produces small, dry fruits called cypsela.

Topea micrantha is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
