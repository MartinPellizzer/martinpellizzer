Tulearia

Tulearia, commonly known as tule grass, is a type of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses and sedges. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, clustered flowers. Tulearia often grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist soil conditions.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
