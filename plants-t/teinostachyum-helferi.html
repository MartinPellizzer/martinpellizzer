Teinostachyum helferi

Teinostachyum helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as a source of fiber.
