Tillandsia prodigiosa

Tillandsia prodigiosa, commonly known as the Royal Tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves. Its striking appearance includes vibrant green foliage with red and purple hues, especially under direct sunlight.

Tillandsia prodigiosa is popular among plant enthusiasts for its unique beauty and low maintenance requirements.
