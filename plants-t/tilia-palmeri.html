Tilia palmeri

Tilia palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's lime tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Tiliaceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States, particularly found in Arizona and New Mexico. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
