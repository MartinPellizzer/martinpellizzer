Tetradenia akoensis

Tetradenia akoensis, commonly known as the Akoense tetradenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of western Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
