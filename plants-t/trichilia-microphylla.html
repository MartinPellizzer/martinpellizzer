Trichilia microphylla

Trichilia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved trichilia, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fruiting bodies that are woody and contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and has some traditional medicinal uses.
