Tylecodon buchholzianus

Tylecodon buchholzianus, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Tylecodon buchholzianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
