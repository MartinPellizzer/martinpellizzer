Tubella

Tubella, commonly known as the common thornapple, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny calyx that encases the fruit, which is a yellow or orange berry. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in woodland edges or open fields. Its flowers are usually blue or purple and bloom in the summer months.

Tubella is known for its toxic properties, making it a plant to avoid for both humans and livestock.
