Triblemma leuzeanum

Triblemma leuzeanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous small seeds.

Triblemma leuzeanum is often found in disturbed habitats and can become invasive in certain environments.
