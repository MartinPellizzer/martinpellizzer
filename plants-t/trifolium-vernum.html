Trifolium vernum

Trifolium vernum, commonly known as spring clover, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Trifolium vernum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
