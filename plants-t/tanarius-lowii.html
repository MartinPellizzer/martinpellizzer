Tanarius lowii

Tanarius lowii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Tanarius lowii is often found in rocky or forested habitats at higher elevations.
