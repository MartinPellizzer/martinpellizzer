Tridens avenaceus

Tridens avenaceus, commonly known as the slender grass or slender panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist conditions. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like structures, which produce tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Tridens avenaceus plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
