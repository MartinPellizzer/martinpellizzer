Thesium nevadense

Thesium nevadense, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada thesium, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, rocky habitats. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is adapted to the cold, arid conditions of its alpine environment.
