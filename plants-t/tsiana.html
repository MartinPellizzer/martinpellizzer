Tsiana

Tsiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, waxy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Tsiana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
