Tetratheca × deltoidea

Tetratheca × deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid tetratheca, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its narrow, deltoid-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in umbels, typically blooming in spring. It is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed in the wild.
