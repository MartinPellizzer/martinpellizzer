Tillandsia compressa var. oligostachya

Tillandsia compressa var. oligostachya, commonly known as the compact tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped foliage that is typically covered in a thick layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale purple or white flowers, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
