Thelypteris globulifera

Thelypteris globulifera, commonly known as the globe fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact fronds that form a rounded shape. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a distinctive, rounded appearance.

Thelypteris globulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its unique foliage.
