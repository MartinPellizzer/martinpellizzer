Tithonia helianthoides

Tithonia helianthoides, commonly known as sunflower bush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Tithonia helianthoides is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its high biomass production and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
