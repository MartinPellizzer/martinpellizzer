Trachyandra asperata var. macowanii

Trachyandra asperata var. macowanii, commonly known as the Macowan's trachyandra, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
