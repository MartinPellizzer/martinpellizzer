Thrincia seriolensis

Thrincia seriolensis, commonly known as the yellow tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small tree or shrub that produces small, yellow, egg-shaped fruits. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or lance-shaped. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and are often green or yellow in color.

The fruit is edible and has a tart, acidic flavor, making it useful in local cuisines.
