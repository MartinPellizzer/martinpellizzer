Thymus humifusus var. origanifolius

Thymus humifusus var. origanifolius, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright green, oval-shaped leaves with a distinct lemony aroma. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its aromatic foliage makes it a popular choice for culinary and ornamental use.
