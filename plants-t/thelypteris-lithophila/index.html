Thelypteris Lithophila

Thelypteris lithophila, also known as the rock fern, is a hardy species of fern that thrives in rocky and coastal environments. It is native to regions across the Pacific, including Japan, Hawaii, and parts of Southeast Asia. This plant is known for its ability to grow on cliffs and in crevices where other plants struggle to survive. Its delicate fronds and unique growth patterns make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Thelypteris lithophila plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its natural habitat.
