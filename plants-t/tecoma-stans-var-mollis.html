Tecoma stans var. mollis

Tecoma stans var. mollis, commonly known as the soft trumpetbush, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Tecoma stans var. mollis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
