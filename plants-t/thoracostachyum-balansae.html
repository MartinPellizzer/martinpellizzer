Thoracostachyum balansae

Thoracostachyum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae grass, is a member of the Poaceae family and is native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
