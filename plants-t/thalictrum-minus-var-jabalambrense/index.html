Thalictrum Minus Var. Jabalambrense

Thalictrum minus var. jabalambrense is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East. It is known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. This variety is a lesser-known relative of the genus Thalictrum, which includes several species valued for their ornamental appeal. Its limited distribution and unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in horticultural circles.
