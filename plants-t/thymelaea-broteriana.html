Thymelaea broteriana

Thymelaea broteriana, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is an evergreen shrub that can grow up to 1 meter in height.

Thymelaea broteriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
