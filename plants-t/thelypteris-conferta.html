Thelypteris conferta

Thelypteris conferta, commonly known as the rock fern, is a perennial fern native to various regions in the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and its ability to thrive in rocky or shaded environments. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with a dark green, glossy appearance. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thick, leathery sheath, which helps it retain moisture.

Thelypteris conferta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to colonize difficult soil conditions.
