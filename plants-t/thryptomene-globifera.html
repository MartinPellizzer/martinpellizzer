Thryptomene globifera

Thryptomene globifera, commonly known as the globular thryptomene, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit. The plant has small, aromatic leaves and produces white or pale pink flowers in clusters. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Thryptomene globifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
