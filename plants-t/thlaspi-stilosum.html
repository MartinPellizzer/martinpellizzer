Thlaspi stilosum

Thlaspi stilosum, commonly known as the slender cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Thlaspi stilosum is often found in moist, disturbed soils such as roadsides and meadows.
