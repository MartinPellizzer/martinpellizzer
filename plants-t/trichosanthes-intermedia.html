Trichosanthes intermedia

Trichosanthes intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a herbaceous vine native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers that are often followed by elongated, green fruits. Its fruits mature to a pale yellow or orange color and are edible when ripe.

Trichosanthes intermedia is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
