Trachyandra oligotricha

Trachyandra oligotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silver grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, silvery-green leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

Trachyandra oligotricha is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
