Thalictrum dalingo

Thalictrum dalingo, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its stems are slender and often branched, supporting the upright growth habit.

Thalictrum dalingo is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
