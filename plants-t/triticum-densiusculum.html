Triticum densiusculum

Triticum densiusculum, commonly known as the dense-leaved wheat, is a wild grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, short leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its seeds are typically small and oval, with a tough outer coating.

Triticum densiusculum is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, demonstrating its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
