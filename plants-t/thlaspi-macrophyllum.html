Thlaspi macrophyllum

Thlaspi macrophyllum, commonly known as broadleaf pennycress, is a herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many other cruciferous species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
