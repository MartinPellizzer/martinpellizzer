Thibaudia microphylla

Thibaudia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved thibaudia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually less than 2 cm in length. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in spring.

Thibaudia microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
