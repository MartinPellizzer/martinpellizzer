Taraxacum sundbergii

Taraxacum sundbergii, commonly known as the Swedish dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves and a flowering stem that bears a single, yellow, composite flower head. Taraxacum sundbergii is typically perennial, with a robust root system that helps it survive in various environmental conditions.
