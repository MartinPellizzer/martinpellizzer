Thymus ilerdensis

Thymus ilerdensis, commonly known as the Ilerdene thyme, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula, specifically found in the region of Catalonia, Spain. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
