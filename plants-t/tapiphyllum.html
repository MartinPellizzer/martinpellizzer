Tapiphyllum

Tapiphyllum, commonly known as the glassy leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of red or purple.

Tapiphyllum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
