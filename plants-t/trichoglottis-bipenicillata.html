Trichoglottis bipenicillata

Trichoglottis bipenicillata, commonly known as the Bicolored Orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bicoloration, with green and white stripes on its leaves and flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Trichoglottis bipenicillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
