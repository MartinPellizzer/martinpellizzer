Trigonella torulosa

Trigonella torulosa, commonly known as the Turkish lentil or Persian vetch, is an annual legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of the Fabaceae family. The plant produces lens-shaped seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it valuable for both agricultural and ecological purposes.
