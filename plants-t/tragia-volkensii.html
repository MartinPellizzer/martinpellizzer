Tragia volkensii

Tragia volkensii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is an erect, herbaceous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with slender, linear leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs.

Tragia volkensii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
