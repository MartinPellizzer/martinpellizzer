Thunbergia crispula

Thunbergia crispula, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, and often crinkled leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Thunbergia crispula is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
