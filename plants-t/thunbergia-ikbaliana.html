Thunbergia ikbaliana

Thunbergia ikbaliana, commonly known as the purple heart vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking purple to violet flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia ikbaliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or other structures.
