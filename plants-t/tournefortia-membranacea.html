Tournefortia membranacea

Tournefortia membranacea, commonly known as the narrowleaf tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a waxy surface.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
