Tragopogon artvinensis

Tragopogon artvinensis, commonly known as Artvin's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces elongated, yellowish flower heads with prominent central disks. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
