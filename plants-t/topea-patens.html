Topea patens

Topea patens, commonly known as the spreading topea, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features erect, branching stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Topea patens is adapted to dry conditions, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.
