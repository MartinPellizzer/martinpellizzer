Thymelaea calycina var. glabra

Thymelaea calycina var. glabra, commonly known as smooth thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
