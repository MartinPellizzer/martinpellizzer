Tectaria viridifrons

Tectaria viridifrons, commonly known as the green-fronted tectaria, is a fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that are typically divided into numerous segments. The plant exhibits a distinctive appearance with a shield-shaped sorus covered in a false indusium. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Tectaria viridifrons is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
