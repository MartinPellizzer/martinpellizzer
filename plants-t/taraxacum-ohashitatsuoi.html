Taraxacum ohashitatsuoi

Taraxacum ohashitatsuoi, commonly known as the Japanese dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy leaves. The plant produces a distinctive seed head that releases lightweight seeds with parachute-like structures for wind dispersal. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained environments.

Taraxacum ohashitatsuoi is often found in open areas such as meadows and lawns.
