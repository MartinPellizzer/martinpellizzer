Thottea beungongtanoeh

Thottea beungongtanoeh, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Thottea beungongtanoeh is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
