Thelypteris rigescens

Thelypteris rigescens, commonly known as the rigid holly fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright, rigid fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping underground stems that help it spread. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a complex structure of small, finger-like leaflets.

Thelypteris rigescens thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
