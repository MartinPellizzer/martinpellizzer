Theresa

Theresa, commonly known as the Theresa rose, is a variety of the Rosa damascena species.

It is characterized by its deep pink to crimson flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with thorny stems and dark green foliage. Its fragrance is strong and sweet, often used in perfumes and aromatherapy.

The Theresa rose is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its aromatic properties.
