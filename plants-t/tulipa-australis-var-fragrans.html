Tulipa australis var. fragrans

Tulipa australis var. fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant australian tulip, is a species native to southern Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, grassy foliage that emerges in early spring. Its flowers are known for their strong, sweet fragrance, which attracts pollinators.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
