Taenitis mediosora

Taenitis mediosora, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a dense layer of roots. The plant has large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
