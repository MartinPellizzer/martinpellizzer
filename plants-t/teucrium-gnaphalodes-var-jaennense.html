Teucrium gnaphalodes var. jaennense

Teucrium gnaphalodes var. jaennense, commonly known as the Jaennese germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in mountainous regions. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, fluffy seed heads.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
