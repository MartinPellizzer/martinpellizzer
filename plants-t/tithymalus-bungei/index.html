Tithymalus Bungei

Tithymalus bungei is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its small, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique species from further decline.
