Toona febrifuga var. ternatensis

Toona febrifuga var. ternatensis, commonly known as the Ternate fever tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Moraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible fruits. Its bark and leaves have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their medicinal properties.

The species is valued both for its ecological role and its potential pharmacological applications.
