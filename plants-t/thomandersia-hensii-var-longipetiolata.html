Thomandersia hensii var. longipetiolata

Thomandersia hensii var. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled thomandersia, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with long petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
