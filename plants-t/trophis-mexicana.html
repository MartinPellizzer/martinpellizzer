Trophis mexicana

Trophis mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. Its roots are fibrous, and it has narrow, grass-like leaves.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
