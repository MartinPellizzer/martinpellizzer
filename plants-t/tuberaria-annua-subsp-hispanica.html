Tuberaria annua subsp. hispanica

Tuberaria annua subsp.

hispanica, commonly known as the Spanish tuberaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its tuberous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
