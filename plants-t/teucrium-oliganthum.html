Teucrium oliganthum

Teucrium oliganthum, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually violet or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and often have a grayish or silver hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs.

Teucrium oliganthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
