Trichonema purpurascens var. major

Trichonema purpurascens var. major, commonly known as the purple-stemmed trichonema, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinged stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments and is often found in meadows or along stream banks. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in panicles, contributing to its reproductive success.

Due to its unique coloration and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
