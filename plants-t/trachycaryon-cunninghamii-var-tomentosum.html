Trachycaryon cunninghamii var. tomentosum

Trachycaryon cunninghamii var. tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.
