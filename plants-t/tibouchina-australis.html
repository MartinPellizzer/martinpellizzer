Tibouchina australis

Tibouchina australis, commonly known as the Australian flame flower, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, glossy green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Tibouchina australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a hedging plant.
