Torenia ciliaris

Torenia ciliaris, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a white throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. Torenia ciliaris is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
