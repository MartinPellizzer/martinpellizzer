Tabernaemontana mixtecana

Tabernaemontana mixtecana, commonly known as the Mixtec angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves and has a woody stem with a distinctive aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
