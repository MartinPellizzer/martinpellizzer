Triodanis perfoliata f. alba

Triodanis perfoliata f. alba, commonly known as the white leaf speedwell, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and spikes of small, white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
