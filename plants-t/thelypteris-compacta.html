Thelypteris compacta

Thelypteris compacta, commonly known as the compact holly fern, is a perennial evergreen fern native to eastern North America.

It features a dense, low-growing frond structure with a compact, bushy appearance. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are divided into small, serrated segments. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Thelypteris compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticultural settings.
