Trichoglottis latisepala

Trichoglottis latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, pale green to white flowers that have broad, showy sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Trichoglottis latisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
