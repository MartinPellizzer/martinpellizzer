Thlaspi stenopterum var. assoi

Thlaspi stenopterum var. assoi, commonly known as the assoi cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnately divided leaves. This variety is typically found in rocky or alpine environments, often growing in crevices or on slopes. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is a hardy species adapted to cool and moist conditions, making it a common sight in mountainous regions.
