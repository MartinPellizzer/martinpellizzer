Taraxacum tubulosum

Taraxacum tubulosum, commonly known as the slender dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pale green, hairy leaves and a hollow, hairy stem. The plant produces a single, inconspicuous flower that develops into a spherical seed head with feathery seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats across Europe and parts of Asia.

Taraxacum tubulosum is a perennial herb that is relatively easy to cultivate and has some medicinal uses.
