Tuberolabium carnosum

Tuberolabium carnosum, commonly known as the fleshy-labied orchid, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Tuberolabium carnosum is often found in dense undergrowth, where it thrives in the nutrient-rich soil of tropical forests.
