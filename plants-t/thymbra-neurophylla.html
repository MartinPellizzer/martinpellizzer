Thymbra neurophylla

Thymbra neurophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its essential oils are valued for their aromatic properties and historical use in herbal medicine.
