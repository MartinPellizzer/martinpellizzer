Thespesia lampas var. thespesioides

Thespesia lampas var. thespesioides, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming during the summer months. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in coastal areas and is well adapted to the warm, humid climate of Hawaii.

This species is also valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in traditional Hawaiian culture.
