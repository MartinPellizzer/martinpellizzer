Thuja orientalis f. beverleyensis

Thuja orientalis f. beverleyensis, commonly known as the Beverlyensis thuja, is a cultivated form of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves that are a dark green color. This variety is known for its slow growth rate and compact form, making it suitable for small spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The plant is often used as a ornamental hedge or specimen plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
