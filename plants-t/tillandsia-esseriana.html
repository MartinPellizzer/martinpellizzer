Tillandsia esseriana

Tillandsia esseriana, commonly known as the Silver Volute, is a species of air plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It produces striking white or pink flower spikes that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
