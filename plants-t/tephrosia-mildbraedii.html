Tephrosia mildbraedii

Tephrosia mildbraedii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Tephrosia mildbraedii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seed meal.
