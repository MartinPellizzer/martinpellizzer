Trichomanes rotundifolium

Trichomanes rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bright green fronds that are typically rounded or ovate in shape. This fern is notable for its intricate, lace-like appearance and its preference for high humidity and indirect light.
