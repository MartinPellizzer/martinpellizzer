Trigonotis sericea

Trigonotis sericea, commonly known as silverleaf trigonotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Trigonotis sericea is known for its drought tolerance and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
