Tilia hazslinszkyana

Tilia hazslinszkyana, commonly known as the Hungarian linden, is a deciduous tree native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The tree produces small, round samaras that are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
