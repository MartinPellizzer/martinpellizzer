Tephrosia purpurea var. brevidens

Tephrosia purpurea var. brevidens, commonly known as purple crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of small, purple-pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The leaves are pinnately compound, with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets that give the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.

This species is also valued for its ornamental beauty and potential use in traditional medicine.
