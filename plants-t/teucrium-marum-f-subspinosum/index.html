Teucrium Marum F. Subspinosum

Teucrium marum f. subspinosum is a rare and distinctive variant of the common teucrium species. It is characterized by its spiny leaves and compact growth habit, making it stand out among its relatives. Native to specific regions in Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and dry environments. Its unique features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader botanical community.
