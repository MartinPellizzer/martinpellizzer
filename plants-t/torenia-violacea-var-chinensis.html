Torenia violacea var. chinensis

Torenia violacea var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet and purple. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
