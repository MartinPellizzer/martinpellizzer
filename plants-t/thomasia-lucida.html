Thomasia lucida

Thomasia lucida, commonly known as the glossy thomasia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of northern Australia.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid environment. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.

Thomasia lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
