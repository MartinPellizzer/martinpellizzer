Tibouchina regnellii

Tibouchina regnellii, commonly known as the purple glorybower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Tibouchina regnellii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
