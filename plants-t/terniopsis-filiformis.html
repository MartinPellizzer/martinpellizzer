Terniopsis filiformis

Terniopsis filiformis, commonly known as the slender terniopsis, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in humid forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.
