Trisetum elatum

Trisetum elatum, commonly known as the tall feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a soft, silky texture.

Trisetum elatum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its elegant, feathery appearance.
