Thalictrum elatum f. angustifolium

Thalictrum elatum f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved meadow rue, is a botanical variant of the meadow rue genus.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in upright panicles, often in shades of white or pale yellow.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage.
