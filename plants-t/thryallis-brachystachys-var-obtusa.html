Thryallis brachystachys var. obtusa

Thryallis brachystachys var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved thryallis, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine, pubescent layer. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal inflorescences, often displaying a pale yellow or greenish hue.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in subtropical gardens.
