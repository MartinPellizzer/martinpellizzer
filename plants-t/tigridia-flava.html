Tigridia flava

Tigridia flava, commonly known as the yellow tigridia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and grows from a bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tigridia flava is known for its early spring blooming period, adding vibrant color to gardens in late winter and early spring.
