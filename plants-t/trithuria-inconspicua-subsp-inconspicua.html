Trithuria inconspicua subsp. inconspicua

Trithuria inconspicua subsp.

inconspicua, commonly known as the slender water-cress, is a small aquatic plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams.

The plant has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged, with small white flowers that appear in clusters. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and preference for nutrient-rich waters.
