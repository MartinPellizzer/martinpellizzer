Tabernaemontana orientalis var. angustisepala

Tabernaemontana orientalis var. angustisepala, commonly known as the narrow-lobed starflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and can grow up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
