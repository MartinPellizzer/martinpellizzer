Telipogon gymnostele

The plant has a unique morphology, with its leaves arranged in a rosette pattern and a lack of true roots, relying instead on symbiotic fungi for nutrient absorption. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or alpine habitats where conditions are harsh and competition is minimal.
