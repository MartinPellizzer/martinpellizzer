Tristania pentandra

Tristania pentandra, commonly known as the Tristan's evergreen, is a species of flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. It is adapted to the arid and saline conditions of its native habitat.

Tristania pentandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
