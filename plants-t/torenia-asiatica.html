Torenia asiatica

Torenia asiatica, commonly known as the Indian torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to blue. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Torenia asiatica thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
