Thesium patentiflorum

Thesium patentiflorum, commonly known as the showy thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowering stems are erect and branched.

Thesium patentiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant floral display.
