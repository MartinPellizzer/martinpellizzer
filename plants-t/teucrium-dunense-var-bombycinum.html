Teucrium dunense var. bombycinum

Teucrium dunense var. bombycinum, commonly known as the Balkan germander, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in whorls.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses in local herbal remedies.
