Teucrium capitatum var. mirandanum

Teucrium capitatum var. mirandanum, commonly known as the Mirandense germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features slender, aromatic stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties and ornamental appeal in gardens.
