Tillaea adscendens

Tillaea adscendens, commonly known as the ascending tillaea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Tillaea adscendens is adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in areas with high sunlight exposure.
