Thalia unilateralis

Thalia unilateralis, commonly known as the one-sided thalia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, asymmetrical inflorescence. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
