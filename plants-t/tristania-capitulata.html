Tristania capitulata

Tristania capitulata, commonly known as the Tristania tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and glossy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in compact inflorescences.

Tristania capitulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
