Turbinicarpus × ampliflorus

Turbinicarpus × ampliflorus, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its unique tubular stems that resemble a moon's shape. The plant typically features vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It is a hybrid between Turbinicarpus callosus and Turbinicarpus marrubiosus, resulting in a combination of traits from both parent species.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
