Tristagma poeppigianum

Tristagma poeppigianum, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant has small, green, needle-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of desert ecosystems.
