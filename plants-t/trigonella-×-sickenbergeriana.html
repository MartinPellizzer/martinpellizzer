Trigonella × sickenbergeriana

Trigonella × sickenbergeriana, commonly known as the hybrid fenugreek, is a cross between Trigonella foenum-graecum and Trigonella caerulea.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces seed pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a suitable candidate for both ornamental and medicinal cultivation.
