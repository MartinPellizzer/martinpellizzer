Tricliceras pilosum

Tricliceras pilosum, commonly known as the hairy triclicera, is a unique plant species characterized by its dense, hairy foliage.

It belongs to the family Tricliceraceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves covered in fine, soft hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white and pale yellow.

Tricliceras pilosum is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique botanical features.
