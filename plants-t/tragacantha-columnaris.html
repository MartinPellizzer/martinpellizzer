Tragacantha columnaris

Tragacantha columnaris, commonly known as the columnar tragacanth, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, columnar growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture.

The plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
