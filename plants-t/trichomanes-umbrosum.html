Trichomanes umbrosum

Trichomanes umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a delicate, low-growing fern species that thrives in shaded, humid environments.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds that are often covered in a thin layer of moisture. Due to its preference for high humidity and limited light, it is often found in forest understories or on shaded stream banks.
