Triumfetta abutiloides

Triumfetta abutiloides, commonly known as the velvetleaf or African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a prominent central stamen.
