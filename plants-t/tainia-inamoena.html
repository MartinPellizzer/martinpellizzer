Tainia inamoena

Tainia inamoena, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southern United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy soils.

Tainia inamoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
