Tragium tortuosum

Tragium tortuosum, commonly known as the zigzag fumitory, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, zigzagging stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Tragium tortuosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
