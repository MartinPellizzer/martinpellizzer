Tylostemon crassifolius

Tylostemon crassifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved tylostemon, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant features large, broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive yellowish coloration.

Tylostemon crassifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
