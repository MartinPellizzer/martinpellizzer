Trinia muricata

Trinia muricata, commonly known as the rough trinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among gardeners.
