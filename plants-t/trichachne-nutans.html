Trichachne nutans

Trichachne nutans, commonly known as the nodding trichachne, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that droop downward, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Trichachne nutans is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
