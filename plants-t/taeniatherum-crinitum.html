Taeniatherum crinitum

Taeniatherum crinitum, commonly known as silky wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fine, soft, and slightly hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to golden flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Silky wheatgrass is also valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage crop.
