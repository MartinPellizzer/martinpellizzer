Trichosanthes elmeri

Trichosanthes elmeri, commonly known as the Chinese lantern gourd, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It produces distinctive orange to red, inflated fruits that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. The plant has lobed, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers with five petals. Its roots and seeds are also utilized in herbal remedies.

Trichosanthes elmeri thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
