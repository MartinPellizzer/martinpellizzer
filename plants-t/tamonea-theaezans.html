Tamonea theaezans

Tamonea theaezans, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and showy, tubular flowers. Tamonea theaezans is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
