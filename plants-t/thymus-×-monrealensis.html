Thymus × monrealensis

Thymus × monrealensis, commonly known as Monreal thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to lavender in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its strong, pungent aroma.
