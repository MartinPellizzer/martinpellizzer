Tournefortia wightii

Tournefortia wightii, commonly known as the Indian bignonia, is a flowering plant native to South and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are yellow or orange, with five petals and a distinctive tubular corolla.

It produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
