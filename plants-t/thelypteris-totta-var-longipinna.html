Thelypteris totta var. longipinna

Thelypteris totta var. longipinna, commonly known as the long-pinnate rock fern, is a variant of the rock fern genus Thelypteris.

It is a perennial, evergreen fern characterized by its robust, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. The fronds are pinnately compound, with elongated pinnae that give the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky crevices or along stream banks.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in shaded garden settings.
