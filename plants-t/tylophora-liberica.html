Tylophora liberica

Tylophora liberica, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling stars. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in open grasslands or disturbed soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Tylophora liberica is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
