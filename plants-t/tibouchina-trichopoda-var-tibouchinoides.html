Tibouchina trichopoda var. tibouchinoides

Tibouchina trichopoda var. tibouchinoides, commonly known as the pink tibouchina, is a flowering shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to magenta flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

This variety is closely related to other Tibouchina species and is valued for its adaptability to various growing conditions.
