Thelypteris procera

Thelypteris procera, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream sides. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients efficiently.

Thelypteris procera has a distinctive shield-shaped pinnae, which contribute to its ornamental value in horticulture.
