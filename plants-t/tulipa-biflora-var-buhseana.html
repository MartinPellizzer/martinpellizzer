Tulipa biflora var. buhseana

Tulipa biflora var. buhseana, commonly known as the Buhseana tulip, is a rare and ornamental species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and prefers well-drained, sandy soils. Its bulbs are relatively small and require careful cultivation to thrive.

This variety is prized for its unique floral form and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
