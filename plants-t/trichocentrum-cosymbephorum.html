Trichocentrum cosymbephorum

Trichocentrum cosymbephorum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs covered in a dense, velvety gray-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
