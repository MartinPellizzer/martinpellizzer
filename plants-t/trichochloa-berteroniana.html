Trichochloa berteroniana

Trichochloa berteroniana, commonly known as the silver leaf grass, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-white leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Trichochloa berteroniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
