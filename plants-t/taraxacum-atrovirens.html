Taraxacum atrovirens

Taraxacum atrovirens, commonly known as the dark-green dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and deeply lobed, grass-like leaves. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, this plant thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in open, disturbed habitats. The plant produces a long, hollow stem with a white, fluffy seed head that aids in wind pollination.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
