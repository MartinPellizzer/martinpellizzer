Tritonia capensis var. major

Tritonia capensis var. major, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Tritonia capensis var. major is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
