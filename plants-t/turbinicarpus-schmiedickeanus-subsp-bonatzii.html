Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp. bonatzii

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp.

bonatzii, commonly known as the Bonatz's turbinicarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae. It is native to southern Africa, specifically Namibia and Angola, where it grows in arid and semi-arid environments.

The plant is characterized by its globular to cylindrical stem and produces small, white or yellowish flowers during the rainy season. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
