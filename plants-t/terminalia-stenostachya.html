Terminalia stenostachya

Terminalia stenostachya, commonly known as African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and lanceolate leaves. Terminalia stenostachya is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture making.
