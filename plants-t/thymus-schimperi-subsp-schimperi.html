Thymus schimperi subsp. schimperi

Thymus schimperi subsp.

schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian thyme, is a perennial herb native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It is characterized by its aromatic, small, grey-green leaves and compact growth habit.

The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its medicinal properties and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for both traditional use and horticultural purposes.
