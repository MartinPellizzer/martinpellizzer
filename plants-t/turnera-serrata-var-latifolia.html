Turnera serrata var. latifolia

Turnera serrata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf turnera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its soft, succulent leaves and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with serrated edges, while its flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
