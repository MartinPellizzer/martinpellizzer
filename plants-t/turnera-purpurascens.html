Turnera purpurascens

Turnera purpurascens, commonly known as the red turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Turnera purpurascens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
