Tristerix Aphyllus

Tristerix aphyllus is a unique and enigmatic plant known for its lack of typical leaves. Found in the cloud forests of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its survival depends on a specialized relationship with fungi, which provide essential nutrients. Despite its simple appearance, it plays a crucial role in its ecosystem. This plant offers fascinating insights into plant adaptation and symbiotic relationships.
