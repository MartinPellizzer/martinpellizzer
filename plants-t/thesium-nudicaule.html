Thesium nudicaule

Thesium nudicaule, commonly known as the slender thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in clusters at the base of the plant.

Thesium nudicaule is adapted to arid environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
