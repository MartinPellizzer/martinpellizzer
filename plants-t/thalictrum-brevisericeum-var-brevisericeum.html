Thalictrum brevisericeum var. brevisericeum

Thalictrum brevisericeum var. brevisericeum, commonly known as the short-hairy meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It has a fibrous root system and produces seeds in elongated, cylindrical pods.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive addition to native plant gardens.
