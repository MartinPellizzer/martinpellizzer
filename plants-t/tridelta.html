Tridelta

Tridelta, commonly known as the three-leaved plant, is a member of the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-lobed leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas. Its flowers are usually small and violet or purple in color, adding to its ornamental value.
