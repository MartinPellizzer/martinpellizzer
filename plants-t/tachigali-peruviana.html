Tachigali peruviana

Tachigali peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian tachigali, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Tachigali peruviana also has potential ecological and medicinal uses.
