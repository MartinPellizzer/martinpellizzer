Thlaspi cardiocarpum

Thlaspi cardiocarpum, commonly known as the heartcarp thlaspi, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to heart-shaped, which is reflected in its scientific name. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, calcareous soils.
