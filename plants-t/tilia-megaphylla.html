Tilia megaphylla

Tilia megaphylla, commonly known as the giant-leaf linden, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The tree produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia megaphylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
