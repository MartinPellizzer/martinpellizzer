Thelymitra spadicea

Thelymitra spadicea, commonly known as the yellow rain orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a raceme of yellow-green flowers with white markings. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. Its flowers are short-lived but attract various pollinators such as bees and wasps.

Thelymitra spadicea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
