Tadehagi

Tadehagi, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its hairy stems and trifoliate leaves. It is often found in grasslands and meadows, where it plays a role in soil stabilization.

Tadehagi is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
