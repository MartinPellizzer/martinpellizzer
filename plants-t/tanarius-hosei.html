Tanarius hosei

Tanarius hosei, commonly known as the Japanese flame bamboo, is a unique grass-like plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange inflorescences, which contrast with its green foliage. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for specific garden environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and less commonly seen species in the plant world.
