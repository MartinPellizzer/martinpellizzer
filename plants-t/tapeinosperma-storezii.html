Tapeinosperma storezii

Tapeinosperma storezii, commonly known as the African sand date, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are woody capsules that contain a single, hard-seeded kernel.
