Thelypteris pyrrhorhachis

Thelypteris pyrrhorhachis, commonly known as the red-stemmed shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often reddish in color, contributing to its common name.

Thelypteris pyrrhorhachis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
