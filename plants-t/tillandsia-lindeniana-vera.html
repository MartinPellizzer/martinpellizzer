Tillandsia lindeniana vera

Tillandsia lindeniana vera, commonly known as the Lindena Lindenia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with colors ranging from white to pale pink.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
