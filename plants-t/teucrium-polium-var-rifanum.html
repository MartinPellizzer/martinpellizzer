Teucrium polium var. rifanum

Teucrium polium var. rifanum, commonly known as Rifan thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Its essential oils contribute to its use in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
