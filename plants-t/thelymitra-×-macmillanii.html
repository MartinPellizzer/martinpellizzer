Thelymitra × macmillanii

Thelymitra × macmillanii, commonly known as the Macmillan's sun-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sun-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while the flowers are borne on erect spikes.

Thelymitra × macmillanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
