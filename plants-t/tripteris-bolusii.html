Tripteris bolusii

Tripteris bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, palmate leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.
