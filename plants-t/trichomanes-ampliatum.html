Trichomanes ampliatum

Trichomanes ampliatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically covered in a layer of fine, silky hairs. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually evergreen and can grow up to several centimeters in length.

Trichomanes ampliatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
