Tradescantia reginae

Tradescantia reginae, commonly known as the princess flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central America.

It features vibrant, waxy leaves that range in color from deep purple to blue and pink, depending on light exposure. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and violet, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Tradescantia reginae is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
