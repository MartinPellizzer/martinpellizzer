Tylophora exilis

Tylophora exilis, commonly known as the slender tylophora, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its thin, twining stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which are often fragrant. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
