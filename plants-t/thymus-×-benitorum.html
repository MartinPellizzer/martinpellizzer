Thymus × benitorum

Thymus × benitorum, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and aromatic, small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and fragrant foliage, it is widely cultivated in horticulture.
