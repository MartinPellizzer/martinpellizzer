Tilia paucicostata subsp. dictyoneura

Tilia paucicostata subsp.

dictyoneura, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed lime, is a deciduous tree native to parts of East Asia. It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with prominent, narrow veins.

The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by spherical, woody fruits. Its bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
