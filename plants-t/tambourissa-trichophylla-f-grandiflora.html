Tambourissa trichophylla f. grandiflora

Tambourissa trichophylla f. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered tambourissa, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
