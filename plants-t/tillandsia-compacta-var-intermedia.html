Tillandsia compacta var. intermedia

Tillandsia compacta var. intermedia, commonly known as the silver leaf tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes. These trichomes help the plant absorb moisture and nutrients from the air, allowing it to thrive in humid environments. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and unique aesthetic.
