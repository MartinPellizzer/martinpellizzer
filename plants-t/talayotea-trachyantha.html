Talayotea trachyantha

Talayotea trachyantha, commonly known as the coastal spurge, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Talayotea trachyantha plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion in coastal ecosystems.
