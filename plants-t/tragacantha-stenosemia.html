Tragacantha stenosemia

Tragacantha stenosemia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tragacanth, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that produces yellow flowers and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. Its seeds are encased in a hard pod and are often used in traditional medicine.

Tragacantha stenosemia is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in scrublands and woodlands.
