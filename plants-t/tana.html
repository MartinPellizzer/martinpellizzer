Tana

Tana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals and compact rosette of dark green leaves. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Tana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red.
