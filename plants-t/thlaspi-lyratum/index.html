Thlaspi Lyratum

Thlaspi lyratum, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant that thrives in temperate climates. With its vibrant yellow flowers and delicate foliage, it adds a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant has also found a home in North America. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many familiar vegetables and ornamental plants. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
