Tabernaemontana bracteolaris

Tabernaemontana bracteolaris, commonly known as the white star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
