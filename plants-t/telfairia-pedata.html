Telfairia pedata

Telfairia pedata, commonly known as African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and red, fleshy berries that are edible. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Telfairia pedata is also valued as a nutritious vegetable in some African cuisines.
