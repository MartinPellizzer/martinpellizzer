Topobea castanedae

Topobea castanedae, commonly known as the Mexican thornapple, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for certain wildlife. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in disturbed areas.
