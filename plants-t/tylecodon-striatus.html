Tylecodon striatus

Tylecodon striatus, commonly known as the striped moonviolet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Tylecodon striatus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
