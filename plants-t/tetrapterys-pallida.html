Tetrapterys pallida

Tetrapterys pallida, commonly known as the pale leaf tetrapterys, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, often climbing stems and compound leaves with four pairs of leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Tetrapterys pallida is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
