Taveunia trichospadix

Taveunia trichospadix, commonly known as the hairy spadix plant, is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

Its botanical name reflects its hairy appearance, with "trichospadix" referring to the hairy spadix structure.
