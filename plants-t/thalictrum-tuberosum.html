Thalictrum tuberosum

Thalictrum tuberosum, commonly known as meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and streambanks.

Its tuberous roots are a distinguishing feature, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
