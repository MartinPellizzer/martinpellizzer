Tigridia latifolia

Tigridia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf tigridia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces erect, branched stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tigridia latifolia is known for its ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
