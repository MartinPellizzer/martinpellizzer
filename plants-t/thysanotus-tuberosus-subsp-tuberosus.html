Thysanotus tuberosus subsp. tuberosus

Thysanotus tuberosus subsp.

tuberosus, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves and a tuberous root system, which contributes to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The species is valued for its attractive blooms and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
