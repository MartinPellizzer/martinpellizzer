Tragacantha Alexeenkoana

Tragacantha alexeenkoana is a rare and intriguing plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique adaptations to harsh environmental conditions. This species is characterized by its woody stem and small, clustered leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish, blending with its surroundings for camouflage. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
