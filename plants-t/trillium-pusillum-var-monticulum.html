Trillium pusillum var. monticulum

Trillium pusillum var. monticulum, commonly known as the mountain trillium, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the northeastern United States.

It features three narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces a single, upright stem with a waxy, white flower that has three petals and three sepals. It blooms in early spring, often before its leaves fully unfurl.

This variety is adapted to moist, acidic soils in shaded forest environments.
