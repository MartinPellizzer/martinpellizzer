Tulipa biflora var. major

Tulipa biflora var. major, commonly known as the giant double tulip, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features two rows of petals, giving it a distinctive double-flowered appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.
