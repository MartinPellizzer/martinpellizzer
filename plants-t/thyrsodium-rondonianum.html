Thyrsodium rondonianum

Thyrsodium rondonianum, commonly known as the Rondonian thyrsoide, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters called thyrses. Its leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a leathery texture.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
