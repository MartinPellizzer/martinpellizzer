Taeniophyllum ferox

Taeniophyllum ferox, commonly known as the fierce thread-leaf, is a small epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of air humidity and indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
