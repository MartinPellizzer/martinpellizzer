Tradescantia zebrina

Tradescantia zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and purple. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.
