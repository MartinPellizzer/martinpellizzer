Trichomanes serricula

Trichomanes serricula, commonly known as the serrated maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, serrated fronds that give it its common name. Trichomanes serricula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
