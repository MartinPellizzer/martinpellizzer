Trichomanes × amabile

Trichomanes × amabile, commonly known as the hybrid maidenhair fern, is a sterile, epiphytic fern that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is a hybrid between Trichomanes speciosum and Trichomanes corydalis, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant features delicate, finely divided fronds with a feathery appearance, typical of the family Hymenophyllaceae. It is often found growing on tree trunks or branches in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in greenhouses and shaded gardens.
