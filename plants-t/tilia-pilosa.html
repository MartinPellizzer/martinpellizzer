Tilia pilosa

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish bark and heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a small, round蒴果 that splits open to release tiny seeds.
