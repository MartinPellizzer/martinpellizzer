Tephrosia pseudolongipes

Tephrosia pseudolongipes, commonly known as the longiped tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and often nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.
