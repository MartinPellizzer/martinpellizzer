Trichocentrum pohlianum

Trichocentrum pohlianum, commonly known as the Pohlian's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It typically grows on tree branches and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features elongated, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its inflorescence is a raceme with numerous small, clustered blooms.

Trichocentrum pohlianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
