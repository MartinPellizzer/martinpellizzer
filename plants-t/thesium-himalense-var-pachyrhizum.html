Thesium himalense var. pachyrhizum

Thesium himalense var. pachyrhizum, commonly known as the Himalayan thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is characterized by its perennial growth habit and features a rosette of fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces yellowish or greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to the Himalayan regions and thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
