Tritaxis

Tritaxis, commonly known as the false hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It consists of several species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. They are often grown as ornamental plants due to their vibrant floral displays.
