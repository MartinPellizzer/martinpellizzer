Taumastos compressus

Taumastos compressus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. It has a strong, aromatic scent and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
