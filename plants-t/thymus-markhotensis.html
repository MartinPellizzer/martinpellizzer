Thymus markhotensis

Thymus markhotensis, commonly known as the Markhotensis thyme, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a textured appearance.

Thymus markhotensis is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb due to its aromatic properties.
