Tristellateia pluriseta

Tristellateia pluriseta, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing along coastal areas and in moist, sandy soils. Tristellateia pluriseta is a climbing vine that produces showy flowers throughout the growing season.
