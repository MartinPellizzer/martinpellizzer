Terminalia vernix

Terminalia vernix, commonly known as the white terminalia, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
