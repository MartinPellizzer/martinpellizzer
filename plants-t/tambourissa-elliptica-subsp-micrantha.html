Tambourissa elliptica subsp. micrantha

Tambourissa elliptica subsp.

micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tambourissa, is a member of the Malvaceae family. It is a shrub native to tropical Africa, characterized by its elliptical, narrow leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
