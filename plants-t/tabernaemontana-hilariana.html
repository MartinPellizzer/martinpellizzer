Tabernaemontana hilariana

Tabernaemontana hilariana, commonly known as the Hilarian star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a woody stem, making it an attractive ornamental species. It thrives in humid environments with partial shade and well-draining soil.

Due to its beauty and fragrance, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in perfumery.
