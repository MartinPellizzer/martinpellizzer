Thinouia

Thinouia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences that bear clusters of yellow flowers, which resemble small showers of golden rain. Thinouia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical areas.
