Trichodesma spinulosum

Trichodesma spinulosum, commonly known as the spiny trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
