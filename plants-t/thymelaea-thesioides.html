Thymelaea thesioides

Thymelaea thesioides, commonly known as the rock thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are often small and succulent, helping it retain moisture in dry conditions.
