Thoreochloa pallens

Thoreochloa pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, pale flowers.

Thoreochloa pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
