Tripteris arborescens

Tripteris arborescens, commonly known as the tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Tripteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds and a central trunk. The plant exhibits a unique combination of fern-like leaves and a woody stem, making it a striking example of a fern-like tree. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticulture.
