Triantha japonica

Triantha japonica, commonly known as the Japanese trianthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in a loose cluster, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical structure makes it a unique addition to gardens with suitable conditions.
