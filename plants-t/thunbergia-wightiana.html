Thunbergia wightiana

Thunbergia wightiana, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet Vine, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Thunbergia wightiana is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
