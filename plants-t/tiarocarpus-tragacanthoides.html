Tiarocarpus tragacanthoides

Tiarocarpus tragacanthoides, commonly known as the African milkwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and small, opposite leaves.
