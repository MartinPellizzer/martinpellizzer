Tibouchina sinaloensis

Tibouchina sinaloensis, commonly known as the Sinaloan tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. Tibouchina sinaloensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
