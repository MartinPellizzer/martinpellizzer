Thalictrum procerulum

Thalictrum procerulum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.

Thalictrum procerulum is also known for its ability to thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil.
