Tristicha tlatlayana

Tristicha tlatlayana, commonly known as the Mexican feather grass, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in panicles. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental grass in gardens.

Tristicha tlatlayana is valued for its graceful form and ability to add texture to landscape designs.
