Tephrosia rhodesica var. evansii

Tephrosia rhodesica var. evansii, commonly known as Rhodesian tephrosia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
