Tragacantha Crenophila

Tragacantha crenophila is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking, waxy yellow blooms that appear during the dry season. This species thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often growing in isolated pockets of habitat. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable to extinction. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and beautiful plant from disappearing altogether.
