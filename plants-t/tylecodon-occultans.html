Tylecodon occultans

Tylecodon occultans, commonly known as the ghostkopje or ghostkopje plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Tylecodon occultans is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
