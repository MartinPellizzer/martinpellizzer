Tricyrtis affinis var. albida

Tricyrtis affinis var. albida, commonly known as the white Japanese iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking white flowers with a distinctive violet stripe. The plant features upright, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant blooms in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and long-lasting flowers.
