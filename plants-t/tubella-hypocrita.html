Tubella hypocrita

Tubella hypocrita, commonly known as the slender tube worm, is a species of polychaete annelid.

It is characterized by its elongated, tubular body and is typically found in marine environments. The plant, though not a true plant, is often studied for its ecological role in coastal ecosystems. Its body is segmented and covered in fine setae, which aid in locomotion.

Tubella hypocrita plays a vital role in nutrient cycling and serves as a food source for various marine organisms.
