Tricyrtis clinata

Tricyrtis clinata, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features striking, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a deep purple or violet throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Tricyrtis clinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
