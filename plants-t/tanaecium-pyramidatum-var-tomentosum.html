Tanaecium pyramidatum var. tomentosum

Tanaecium pyramidatum var. tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly tanaecium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
