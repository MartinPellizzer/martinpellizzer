Teucrium viscidum var. longibracteatum

Teucrium viscidum var. longibracteatum, commonly known as sticky germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems covered in sticky, glandular hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and also bear the same sticky coating. Small blue or purple flowers are arranged in whorled clusters along the stems.

This plant is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.
