Tavaresia grandiflora var. recta

Tavaresia grandiflora var. recta, commonly known as the slender tavaresia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features lanceolate leaves and produces vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
