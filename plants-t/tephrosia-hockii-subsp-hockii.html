Tephrosia hockii subsp. hockii

Tephrosia hockii subsp.

hockii, commonly known as the Hockii Tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features trifoliate leaves with a deep green color.

The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pale yellow to white in color. Its root system is fibrous, and it is adapted to well-drained soils.

The plant is often found in open woodlands and savannas, where it thrives in full sun.
