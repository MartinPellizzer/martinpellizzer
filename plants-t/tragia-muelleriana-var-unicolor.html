Tragia muelleriana var. unicolor

Tragia muelleriana var. unicolor, commonly known as the one-colored tragia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is a herbaceous species characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The flower arrangement is often in clusters, contributing to its distinctive appearance in its natural habitat.
