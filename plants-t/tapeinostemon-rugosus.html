Tapeinostemon rugosus

Tapeinostemon rugosus, commonly known as the roughleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
