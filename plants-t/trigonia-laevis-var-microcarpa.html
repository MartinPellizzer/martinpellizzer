Trigonia laevis var. microcarpa

Trigonia laevis var. microcarpa, commonly known as the smooth trigonia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The fruit is a small, dry capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.
