Traunsteinera globosa subsp. sphaerica

Traunsteinera globosa subsp.

sphaerica, commonly known as the globular traunsteinera, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and small, leathery, dark green leaves.

The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which appear in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a container plant.
