Teucrium × ochroleucum var. gautieri

Teucrium × ochroleucum var. gautieri, commonly known as Gautier's germander, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range from pale yellow to white, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in Mediterranean climates.
