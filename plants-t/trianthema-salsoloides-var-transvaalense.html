Trianthema salsoloides var. transvaalense

Trianthema salsoloides var. transvaalense, commonly known as Transvaal iceplant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
