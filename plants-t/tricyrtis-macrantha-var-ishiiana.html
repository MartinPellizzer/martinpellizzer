Tricyrtis macrantha var. ishiiana

Tricyrtis macrantha var. ishiiana, commonly known as the Ishiiana spider lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features striking, orchid-like flowers with white petals and prominent yellow stamens, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its unique appearance and elegant blooms make it a valuable addition to shade gardens and naturalized landscapes.
