Tragus alienus var. brevispinus

Tragus alienus var. brevispinus, commonly known as the short-spined tragus, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, upright growth habit and distinctive, short spines on its stems and leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
