Tuberolabium

Tuberolabium, commonly known as the "tuberous lip orchid," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their distinctive tuberous roots, which store nutrients and help them survive in their native habitats. Tuberolabium species typically have showy, lip-shaped flowers with vibrant colors, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. They are native to regions in Africa and are often found in rocky or arid environments.

The genus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
