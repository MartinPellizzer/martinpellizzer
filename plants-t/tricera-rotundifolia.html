Tricera rotundifolia

Tricera rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf tricera, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, succulent leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
