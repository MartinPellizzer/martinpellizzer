Tephrosia leiocarpa

Tephrosia leiocarpa, commonly known as the white tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Tephrosia leiocarpa is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
