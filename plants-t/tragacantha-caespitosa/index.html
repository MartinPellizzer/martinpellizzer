Tragacantha Caespitosa

Tragacantha caespitosa is a unique flowering plant known for its compact growth habit and striking blooms. Native to arid regions, it thrives in harsh environments where few plants can survive. Its small, spiky leaves and vibrant yellow flowers make it a standout in desert landscapes. This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. Due to its resilience and beauty, it has gained interest among botanists and gardeners alike.
