Turnera rubrobracteata

Turnera rubrobracteata, commonly known as the red-bract turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red bracts that resemble petals. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
