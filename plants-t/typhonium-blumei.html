Typhonium blumei

Typhonium blumei, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows up to 2 meters tall, characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking purple or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its rhizomes are edible and have a starchy texture similar to taro.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands and forest floors.
