Thymus × porcii f. pilistensis

Thymus × porcii f. pilistensis, commonly known as the Pilistenský thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage, with small, fragrant leaves that emit a strong thyme scent. The plant produces tiny pink or purplish flowers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
