Tsuga chinensis var. forrestii

Tsuga chinensis var. forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese fir var. Forrestii, is a variant of the Chinese fir, native to eastern China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring cool and moist climates. Its foliage is dense and dark green, providing aesthetic value in landscaping.

This variety is valued for its timber and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
