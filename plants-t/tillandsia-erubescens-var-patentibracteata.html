Tillandsia erubescens var. patentibracteata

Tillandsia erubescens var. patentibracteata, commonly known as the Red Tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth habit and striking red or orange inflorescence. The plant belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture from the air.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
