Tephrosia bidwillii var. densa

Tephrosia bidwillii var. densa, commonly known as the dense tephrosia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, often clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It is typically found in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or inland regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
