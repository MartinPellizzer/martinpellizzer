Thymus reinegeri

Thymus reinegeri, commonly known as the miniature thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, aromatic leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pink or violet flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, requiring minimal water once established.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
