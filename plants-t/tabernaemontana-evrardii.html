Tabernaemontana evrardii

Tabernaemontana evrardii, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.

Tabernaemontana evrardii prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.
