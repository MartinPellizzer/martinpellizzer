Tinantia lineolata

Tinantia lineolata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
