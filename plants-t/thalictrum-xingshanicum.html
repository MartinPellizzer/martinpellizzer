Thalictrum xingshanicum

Thalictrum xingshanicum, commonly known as the Xingshan meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous stamens in its inflorescence.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
