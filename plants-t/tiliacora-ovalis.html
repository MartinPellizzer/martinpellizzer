Tiliacora ovalis

Tiliacora ovalis, commonly known as the Indian velvet tamarind, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing elliptical seeds. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
