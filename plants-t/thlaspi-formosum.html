Thlaspi formosum

Thlaspi formosum, commonly known as the Japanese rockcress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Asia, particularly Japan, and is often found growing in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, and its leaves are typically basal, with a slightly hairy texture. Thlaspi formosum is a short-lived perennial, reaching heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters.
