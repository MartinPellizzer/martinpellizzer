Teucrium orchideum

Teucrium orchideum, commonly known as the orchid germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its leaves are often gray-green and have a slightly hairy texture.

Teucrium orchideum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
