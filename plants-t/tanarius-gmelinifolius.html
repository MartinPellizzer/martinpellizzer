Tanarius gmelinifolius

Tanarius gmelinifolius, commonly known as the Gmelin's leaf tanarius, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
