Thymus serpyllum f. diversipellis

Thymus serpyllum f. diversipellis, commonly known as the variable thyme, is a low-growing perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural use.

This form exhibits variability in leaf shape and flower color, contributing to its unique botanical interest.
