Trigonella koeiei

Trigonella koeiei, commonly known as the Koei trigonella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep root systems.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
