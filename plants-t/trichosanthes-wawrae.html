Trichosanthes wawrae

Trichosanthes wawrae, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its large, lobed leaves and long, cylindrical fruits that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers with five petals and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.
