Torfasadis canariensis

Torfasadis canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Island pine, is a species of pine native to the Canary Islands.

It is a tall, evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The tree has long, needle-like leaves and produces cones that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the unique climate of the Canary Islands.

This plant plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
