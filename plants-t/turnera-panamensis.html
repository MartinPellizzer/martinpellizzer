Turnera panamensis

Turnera panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are usually simple and opposite, with a glossy surface.

Turnera panamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
