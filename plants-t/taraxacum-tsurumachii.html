Taraxacum tsurumachii

Taraxacum tsurumachii, commonly known as the Japanese dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
