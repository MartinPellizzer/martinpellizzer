Tephrosia argyrotricha var. burtii

Tephrosia argyrotricha var. burtii, commonly known as the silver-haired tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant is distinguished by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. It produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color.

This variety is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
