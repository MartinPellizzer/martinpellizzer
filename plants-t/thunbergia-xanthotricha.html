Thunbergia xanthotricha

Thunbergia xanthotricha, commonly known as the golden thread vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in gardens.
