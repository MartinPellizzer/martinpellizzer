Tupacamaria styllomisantha

Tupacamaria styllomisantha, commonly known as the red-flowered tamarind tree, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its bark is often used in traditional medicine.
