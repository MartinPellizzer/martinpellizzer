Triticum thaoudar var. roseum

Triticum thaoudar var. roseum, commonly known as the pink wheat or rose-colored wheat, is a rare and ornamental variety of wheat.

It is characterized by its striking pinkish-red grain color, which sets it apart from common wheat varieties. This plant is typically grown for its aesthetic value rather than for grain production. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The botanical features of Triticum thaoudar var. roseum make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and collections.
