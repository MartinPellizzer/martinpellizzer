Trichomanes sericeum

Trichomanes sericeum, commonly known as silky water fern, is a delicate fern species belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silky-textured fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. Trichomanes sericeum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
