Torenia pubescens

Torenia pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves and clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers bloom throughout the growing season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Torenia pubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a bedding plant.
