Tigridia curvata

Tigridia curvata, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, upward-facing, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, rhizomatous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tigridia curvata is known for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to gardens.
