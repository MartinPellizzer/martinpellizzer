Thelymitra kangaloonica

Thelymitra kangaloonica, commonly known as the Kangaloon spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, slender spike that bears several small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and is adapted to grow in sandy or rocky soils. Thelymitra kangaloonica is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.
