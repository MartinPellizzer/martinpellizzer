Thelypteris asterothrix

Thelypteris asterothrix, commonly known as the starry maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its spores are produced on the underside of its fronds, which are released during the dry season.

Thelypteris asterothrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
