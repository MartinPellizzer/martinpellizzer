Terminalia beccarii

Terminalia beccarii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Terminalia beccarii also has traditional medicinal uses in various African cultures.
