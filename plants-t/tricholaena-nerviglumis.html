Tricholaena nerviglumis

Tricholaena nerviglumis, commonly known as the featherleaf grass, is a tropical plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves with a distinct veining pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, feathery appearance.

Tricholaena nerviglumis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
