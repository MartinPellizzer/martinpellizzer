Tilia javorkae

Tilia javorkae, commonly known as the Carpathian lime, is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically pubescent on the underside. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer and are followed by small, round fruits. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mixed forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
