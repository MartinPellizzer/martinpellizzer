Thrinax drudei

Thrinax drudei, commonly known as the Drude's palmetto, is a small, evergreen palm native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and stiff, dark green, spear-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into black, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Thrinax drudei is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in tropical and subtropical climates.
