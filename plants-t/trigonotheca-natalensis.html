Trigonotheca natalensis

Trigonotheca natalensis, commonly known as Natal wild cotton, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds enclosed in a capsule, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Trigonotheca natalensis is valued for its ecological role and is often found in disturbed soils.
