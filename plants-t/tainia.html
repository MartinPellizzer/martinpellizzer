Tainia

Tainia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often white or pink. Tainia thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
