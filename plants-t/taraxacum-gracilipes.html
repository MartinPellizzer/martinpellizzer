Taraxacum gracilipes

Taraxacum gracilipes, commonly known as the slender dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stem and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its native habitat.

Taraxacum gracilipes is typically found in moist, open areas such as meadows and woodland edges.
