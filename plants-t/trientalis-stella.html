Trientalis stella

Trientalis stella, commonly known as starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Trientalis stella is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
