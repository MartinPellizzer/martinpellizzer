Tomostylis multiflora

Tomostylis multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered tomostylis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Tomostylis multiflora is typically found in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.
