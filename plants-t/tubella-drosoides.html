Tubella drosoides

Tubella drosoides, commonly known as the fly trap sponge, is a species of marine sponge found in shallow coastal waters.

It belongs to the family Dictyoceratidae and is characterized by its branching, tree-like structure. The sponge has a soft, spongy texture with a pale yellow to white coloration. It is notable for its ability to trap small organisms, which it filters from the water for nourishment.
