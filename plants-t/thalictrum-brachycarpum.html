Thalictrum brachycarpum

Thalictrum brachycarpum, commonly known as the short-fruited meadow-rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

Thalictrum brachycarpum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
