Trachymene ornata var. semilanata

Trachymene ornata var. semilanata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bluemink, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems and small, delicate blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, airy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique flower color and foliage structure.
