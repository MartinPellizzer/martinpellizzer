Thesium ramosum

Thesium ramosum, commonly known as the slender thesium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit with dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Thesium ramosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
