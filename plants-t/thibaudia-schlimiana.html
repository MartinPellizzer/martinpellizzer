Thibaudia schlimiana

Thibaudia schlimiana, commonly known as the Schlim's thibaudia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and daisy-like flowers. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
