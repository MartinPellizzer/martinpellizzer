Tiquilia Grandiflora

Tiquilia grandiflora is a striking flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Known for its vibrant purple-blue blooms, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This species is part of the Loasaceae family and is often found in desert landscapes. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively uncommon in cultivation.
