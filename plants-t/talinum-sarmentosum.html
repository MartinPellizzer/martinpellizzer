Talinum sarmentosum

Talinum sarmentosum, commonly known as purple leaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and striking purple foliage, which contrasts with its green stems and leaves. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible berries that are typically orange or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant or for its edible fruits.

Talinum sarmentosum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
