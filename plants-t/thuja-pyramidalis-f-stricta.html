Thuja pyramidalis f. stricta

Thuja pyramidalis f. stricta, commonly known as the compact thuja, is a cultivar of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. This variety is highly valued for its compact growth habit, making it ideal for hedges and topiary. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, requiring minimal pruning.

The plant is also resistant to pests and diseases, contributing to its popularity in landscaping.
