Tricyrtis macropoda subsp. affinis

Tricyrtis macropoda subsp.

affinis, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan. It features striking, spider-like flowers with long, slender petals and a prominent lip.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
