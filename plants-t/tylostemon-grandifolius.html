Tylostemon grandifolius

Tylostemon grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf tylostemon, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in gardens.

Tylostemon grandifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
