Trichocentrum Flavovirens

Trichocentrum flavovirens is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. Known for its vibrant yellow-green flowers and unique growth habits, this plant has captivated the attention of botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing epiphytically on trees. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the world of horticulture. Its delicate structure and unusual coloration make it a subject of both scientific study and aesthetic admiration.
