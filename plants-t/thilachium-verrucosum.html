Thilachium verrucosum

Thilachium verrucosum, commonly known as the warty daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with a distinctive warty appearance on the petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.

Thilachium verrucosum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
