Thelypteris caii

Thelypteris caii, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 50 centimeters.

Thelypteris caii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
