Thermopsis turkestanica

Thermopsis turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow, pea-like flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its distribution across its native range.
