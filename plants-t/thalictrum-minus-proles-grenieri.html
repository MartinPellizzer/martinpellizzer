Thalictrum minus proles grenieri

Thalictrum minus proles grenieri, commonly known as the green-flowered meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Thalictrum minus proles grenieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens with suitable conditions.
