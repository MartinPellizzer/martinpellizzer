Thymus × monteilii

Thymus × monteilii, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features aromatic, small, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color and emit a strong thyme scent when crushed. The plant produces tiny, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
