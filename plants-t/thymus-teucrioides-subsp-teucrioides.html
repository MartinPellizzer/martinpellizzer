Thymus teucrioides subsp. teucrioides

Thymus teucrioides subsp.

teucrioides, commonly known as narrowleaf thyme, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its aromatic, small, gray-green leaves and pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in Mediterranean gardens for its ornamental value and culinary applications.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
