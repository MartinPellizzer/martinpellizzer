Trichadenia sasae

Trichadenia sasae, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Trichadenia sasae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
