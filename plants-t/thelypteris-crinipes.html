Thelypteris crinipes

Thelypteris crinipes, commonly known as the feather fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, fibrous layer, which helps in water retention.

Thelypteris crinipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
