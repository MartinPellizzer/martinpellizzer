Tephrosia glomeruliflora subsp. glomeruliflora

Tephrosia glomeruliflora subsp.

glomeruliflora, commonly known as the clustered tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or savanna habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
