Tilia mandshurica

Tilia mandshurica, commonly known as Manchurian linden, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, pale yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain smooth, oval seeds. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Tilia mandshurica is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
