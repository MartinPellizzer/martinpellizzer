Thibaudia pichinchensis var. glabra

Thibaudia pichinchensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth thibaudia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
