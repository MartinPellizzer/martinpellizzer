Tephrosia macropoda var. macropoda

Tephrosia macropoda var. macropoda, commonly known as the large-leaved tephrosia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with elongated, elliptical leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in disturbed soils and open woodlands. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

Tephrosia macropoda var. macropoda is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
