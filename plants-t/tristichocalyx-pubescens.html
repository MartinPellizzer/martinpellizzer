Tristichocalyx pubescens

Tristichocalyx pubescens, commonly known as the hairy legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Tristichocalyx pubescens is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
