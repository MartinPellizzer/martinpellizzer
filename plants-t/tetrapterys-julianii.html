Tetrapterys julianii

Tetrapterys julianii, commonly known as the Julian's tetrapterys, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with four pairs of leaflets, giving it the genus name "tetrapterys.". The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and is valued for its ornamental potential.
