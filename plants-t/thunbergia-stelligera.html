Thunbergia stelligera

Thunbergia stelligera, commonly known as the star sapphire vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which are often bi- or tri-petaled and resemble stars. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green and have a slightly velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its climbing habit.

Thunbergia stelligera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
