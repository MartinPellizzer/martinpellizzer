Thelypteris ensifera

Thelypteris ensifera, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is native to various regions across Asia, Africa, and the Americas. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, hairy layer that helps retain moisture.

Thelypteris ensifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture and landscaping.
