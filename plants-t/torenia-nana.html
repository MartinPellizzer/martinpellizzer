Torenia nana

Torenia nana, commonly known as the dwarf torenia, is a compact flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often grown as a bedding plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Torenia nana is a member of the family Acanthaceae, which includes many other ornamental plants.
