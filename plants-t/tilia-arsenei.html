Tilia arsenei

Tilia arsenei, commonly known as the Moldovan linden, is a deciduous tree native to the Carpathian Mountains in Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The tree produces small, oval fruits that contain smooth, brown seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Tilia arsenei is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
