Thymus × brachychaetus nothosubsp. toletanus

Thymus × brachychaetus nothosubsp.

toletanus, commonly known as the Toletan thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, small, grey-green leaves.

The plant produces dense clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers, which bloom in the spring and summer. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained, sunny soils.

Its botanical features make it a valued ornamental and aromatic plant in Mediterranean gardens.
