Thymelaea pubescens var. thesioides

Thymelaea pubescens var. thesioides, commonly known as the coastal thyme.

This plant is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The species is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in dune environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh coastal conditions.
