Tricholaena ruficoma

Tricholaena ruficoma, commonly known as the red-stemmed palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its robust, clustered growth habit. The plant features long, pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and basketry. Its reddish-brown stems are distinctive and add ornamental value to its appearance.

Tricholaena ruficoma thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
