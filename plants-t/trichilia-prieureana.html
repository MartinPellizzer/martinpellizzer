Trichilia prieureana

Trichilia prieureana, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The tree is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and clustered in panicles, typically blooming in the dry season.
