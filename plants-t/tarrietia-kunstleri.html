Tarrietia kunstleri

Tarrietia kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstler's tarrietia, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America. The plant features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, typical of the Araliaceae family.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
