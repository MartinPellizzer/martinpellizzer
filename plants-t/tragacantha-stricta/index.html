Tragacantha Stricta

Tragacantha stricta is a unique and resilient plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in harsh environments where few other species can survive. This plant is known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems. Its leaves are often small and adapted to reduce water loss. Despite its tough exterior, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem.
