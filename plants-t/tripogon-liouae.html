Tripogon liouae

Tripogon liouae, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tripogon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
