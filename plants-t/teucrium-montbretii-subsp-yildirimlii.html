Teucrium montbretii subsp. yildirimlii

Teucrium montbretii subsp.

yildirimlii, commonly known as the Yildirimlii germander, is a perennial shrub native to Turkey. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage.

The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
