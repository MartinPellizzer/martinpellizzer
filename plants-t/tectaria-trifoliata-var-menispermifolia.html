Tectaria trifoliata var. menispermifolia

Tectaria trifoliata var. menispermifolia, commonly known as the three-leaved tectaria, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are composed of three leaflets arranged in a fan-like pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or along stream banks. Its fronds are often leathery and have a distinct, rough texture.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage structure.
