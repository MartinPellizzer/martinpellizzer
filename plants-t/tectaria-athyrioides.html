Tectaria athyrioides

Tectaria athyrioides, commonly known as the false sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its sterile fronds that are typically pinnately compound, with a distinctive shape resembling a sword. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizome is creeping and produces both vegetative and reproductive structures.

Tectaria athyrioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often used in shaded garden settings.
