Tetraneuris brevifolia

Tetraneuris brevifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf tetraneuris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Tetraneuris brevifolia is often found in rocky soils and is considered a hardy, drought-tolerant species.
