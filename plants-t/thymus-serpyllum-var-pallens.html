Thymus serpyllum var. pallens

Thymus serpyllum var. pallens, commonly known as pale thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, pale pink to almost white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has aromatic, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is closely related to common thyme and is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
