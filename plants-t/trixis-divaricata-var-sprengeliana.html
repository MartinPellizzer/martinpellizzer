Trixis divaricata var. sprengeliana

Trixis divaricata var. sprengeliana, commonly known as the Sprengel's trixis, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, pale yellow or greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in xerophytic gardens.
