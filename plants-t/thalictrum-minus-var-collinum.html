Thalictrum minus var. collinum

Thalictrum minus var. collinum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are usually alternate and have a finely divided appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on slender, upright stems. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Thalictrum minus var. collinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
