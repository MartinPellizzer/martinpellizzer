Thunbergia glandulifera

Thunbergia glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant produces small, glandular structures on its stems, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Thunbergia glandulifera is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
