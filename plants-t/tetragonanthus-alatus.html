Tetragonanthus alatus

Tetragonanthus alatus, commonly known as the winged milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its quadrangular stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
