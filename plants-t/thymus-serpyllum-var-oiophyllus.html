Thymus serpyllum var. oiophyllus

Thymus serpyllum var. oiophyllus, commonly known as narrow-leaved thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically narrow and slightly pointed, with a dense, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils contain thymol and other compounds, giving it a strong, spicy fragrance and medicinal properties.
