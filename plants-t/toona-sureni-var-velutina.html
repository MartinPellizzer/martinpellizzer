Toona sureni var. velutina

Toona sureni var. velutina, commonly known as velvet toona, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The bark and leaves of this species have been used in traditional medicine for their potential therapeutic properties.
