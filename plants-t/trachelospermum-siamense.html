Trachelospermum siamense

Trachelospermum siamense, commonly known as the Siam star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural displays.
