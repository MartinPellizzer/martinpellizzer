Thelymitra ixioides var. truncata

Thelymitra ixioides var. truncata, commonly known as the dwarf sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in heathlands and open forests. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a few narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, pale yellow to cream, and are arranged in a loose spike.

This variety is distinguished by its truncated, rounded leaf bases and is often found in coastal areas.
