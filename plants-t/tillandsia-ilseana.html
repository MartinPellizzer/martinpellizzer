Tillandsia ilseana

Tillandsia ilseana, commonly known as the Ilseana air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has elongated, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces small, colorful flowers that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
