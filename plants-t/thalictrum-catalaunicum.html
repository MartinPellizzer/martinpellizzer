Thalictrum catalaunicum

Thalictrum catalaunicum, commonly known as the Catalan meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant produces upright, spikelike inflorescences with small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, showy clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Thalictrum catalaunicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
