Trianthema rubens

Trianthema rubens, commonly known as red sand mat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, bright red flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Trianthema rubens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
