Trachymene cussonii

Trachymene cussonii, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems and small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a graceful appearance. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Trachymene cussonii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.
