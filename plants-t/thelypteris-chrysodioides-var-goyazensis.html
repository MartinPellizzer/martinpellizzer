Thelypteris chrysodioides var. goyazensis

Thelypteris chrysodioides var. goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis water fern, is a fern species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
