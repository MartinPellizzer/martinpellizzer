Tessmannia anomala var. flamignii

Tessmannia anomala var. flamignii, commonly known as the flame bottle tree, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bottle-shaped trunk that is often covered in a thick, waxy coating. The plant produces striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance.
