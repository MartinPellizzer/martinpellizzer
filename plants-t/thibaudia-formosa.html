Thibaudia formosa

Thibaudia formosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf thibaudia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Thibaudia formosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
