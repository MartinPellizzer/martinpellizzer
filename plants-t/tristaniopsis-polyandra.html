Tristaniopsis polyandra

Tristaniopsis polyandra, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dramatic and eye-catching appearance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Tristaniopsis polyandra is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical environments.
