Trichodium intermedium

Trichodium intermedium, commonly known as the slender trichodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

Trichodium intermedium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
