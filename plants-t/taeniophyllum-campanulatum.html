Taeniophyllum campanulatum

Taeniophyllum campanulatum, commonly known as the bell-shaped moss, is a small, terrestrial moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and small, oval to bell-shaped leaves that are often curved. Taeniophyllum campanulatum is well adapted to humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
