Tripterygium wilfordii var. regelii

Tripterygium wilfordii var. regelii, commonly known as Fan-leafed Thundergod, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red berries that contain toxic seeds. It is valued in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressive properties.
