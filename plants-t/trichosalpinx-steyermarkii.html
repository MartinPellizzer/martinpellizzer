Trichosalpinx steyermarkii

Trichosalpinx steyermarkii, commonly known as the purple flame orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet and red flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil, and it produces small, green leaves that help in photosynthesis.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
