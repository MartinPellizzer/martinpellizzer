Taihangia

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptation to harsh mountainous environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and specialized for nutrient absorption.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Taihangia is considered a vulnerable species.
