Tilia mesembrinos

Tilia mesembrinos, commonly known as the evening-leaf linden, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its bark is smooth and light gray, often developing fissures with age.

The leaves are typically 5-10 cm in length and have a soft, velvety texture.
