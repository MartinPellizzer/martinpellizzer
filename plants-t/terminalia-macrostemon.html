Terminalia macrostemon

Terminalia macrostemon, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Terminalia macrostemon is valued for its ornamental flowers and has traditional uses in local medicine.
