Thelymitra longifolia var. forsteri

Thelymitra longifolia var. forsteri, commonly known as the Forster's star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive violet or purple coloration. The plant typically blooms in spring, with flowers emerging from a dense cluster of leaves. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in open forests or grassy areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under suitable conditions.
