Trigonella Monspeliaca Var. Ovalis

Trigonella monspeliaca var. ovalis is a lesser-known legume native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Mediterranean. This variant is distinguished by its oval-shaped leaves and compact growth habit, making it well adapted to harsh environments. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many plants valued for their nutritional and medicinal properties. Though not widely cultivated, it holds potential for use in ecological restoration and sustainable agriculture. Its unique morphology and resilience make it an intriguing subject for botanical study.
