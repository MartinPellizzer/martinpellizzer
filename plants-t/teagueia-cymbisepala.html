Teagueia cymbisepala

Teagueia cymbisepala, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a velvety texture. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

Teagueia cymbisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
