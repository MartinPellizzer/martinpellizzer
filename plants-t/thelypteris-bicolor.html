Thelypteris bicolor

Thelypteris bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive bi-colored fronds, with the upper part typically lighter than the lower part. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, including parts of Mexico and the Caribbean. Thelypteris bicolor thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or near water sources.

Its delicate, finely divided leaves contribute to its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
