Trigonia ehrendorferi

Trigonia ehrendorferi, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that open in the morning and close in the afternoon. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to minimize water loss.

Trigonia ehrendorferi is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
