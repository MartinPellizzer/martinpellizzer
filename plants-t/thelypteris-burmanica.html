Thelypteris burmanica

Thelypteris burmanica, commonly known as Burmese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous sporangia on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris burmanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
