Thalictrum macilentum

Thalictrum macilentum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Thalictrum macilentum is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
