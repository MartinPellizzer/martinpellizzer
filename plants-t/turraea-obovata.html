Turraea obovata

Turraea obovata, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant features opposite, obovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are clustered in spherical inflorescences, which are white or pale pink, giving it a snowball-like appearance.

Turraea obovata is often found in moist forest habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
