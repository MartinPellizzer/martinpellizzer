Tetraneuris brandegeei

Tetraneuris brandegeei, commonly known as the Brandegee's tetraneuris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the spring and early summer.
