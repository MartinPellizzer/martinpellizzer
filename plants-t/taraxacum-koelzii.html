Taraxacum koelzii

Taraxacum koelzii, commonly known as the Koelz's dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and a distinctive hollow stem. Its leaves are typically basal, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.
