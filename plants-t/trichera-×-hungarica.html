Trichera × hungarica

Trichera × hungarica, commonly known as the Hungarian trichera, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and floral display.
