Thunbergia pondoensis

Thunbergia pondoensis, commonly known as the Pondo spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves and produces showy bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

Thunbergia pondoensis is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and partial shade.
