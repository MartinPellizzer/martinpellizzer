Tabernaemontana aurantiaca f. anguinea

Tabernaemontana aurantiaca f. anguinea, commonly known as the orange-flowered star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to orange, often appearing in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
