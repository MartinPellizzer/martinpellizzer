Tarasa odonellii

Tarasa odonellii, commonly known as the Odontella tarasii, is a species of diatom found in freshwater environments.

It belongs to the genus Odontella, which is characterized by its intricate siliceous frustule structure. The frustule of Tarasa odonellii is typically elongated and has a distinctive central area with a series of radial ribs. It plays a crucial role in aquatic ecosystems as a primary producer and a food source for various microorganisms.

This diatom is often used in ecological studies to assess water quality and environmental changes.
