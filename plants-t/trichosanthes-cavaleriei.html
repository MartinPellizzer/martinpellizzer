Trichosanthes cavaleriei

Trichosanthes cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its elongated, pale green fruits that resemble snakes, hence its common name. The plant produces large, yellow flowers with a distinctive, fleshy corolla. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Trichosanthes cavaleriei is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
