Triumfetta intermedia

Triumfetta intermedia, commonly known as the common triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, simple, and usually elliptical leaves that are often pubescent. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and numerous stamens.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.
