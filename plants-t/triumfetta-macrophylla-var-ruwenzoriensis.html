Triumfetta macrophylla var. ruwenzoriensis

Triumfetta macrophylla var. ruwenzoriensis, commonly known as the Ruwenzori spider plant, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
