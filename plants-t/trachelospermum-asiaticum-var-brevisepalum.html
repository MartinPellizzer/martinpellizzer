Trachelospermum asiaticum var. brevisepalum

Trachelospermum asiaticum var. brevisepalum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.

The varietal name 'brevisepalum' refers to its short and narrow sepals, distinguishing it from other varieties of Trachelospermum.
