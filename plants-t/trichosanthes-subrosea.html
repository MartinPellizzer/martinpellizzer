Trichosanthes subrosea

Trichosanthes subrosea, commonly known as the pink-flowered bitter melon, is a vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink to red flowers and elongated, ridged fruits that are typically harvested when immature. The plant belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture, and the stems are hairy.
