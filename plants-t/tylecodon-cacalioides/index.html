Tylecodon Cacalioides

Tylecodon cacalioides is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, spiky leaves that resemble a cactus. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water. Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it highly resilient. It is a popular choice among collectors and gardeners who appreciate its distinctive appearance.
