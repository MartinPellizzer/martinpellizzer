Tinospora stuhlmannii

Tinospora stuhlmannii, commonly known as the climbing tinospora, is a vine native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along riverbanks.

Tinospora stuhlmannii is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
