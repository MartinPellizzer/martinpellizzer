Triumfetta robynsii

Triumfetta robynsii, commonly known as the purple leaf triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, ovate leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, three-lobed fruits.

Triumfetta robynsii is adapted to well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a common ornamental plant in warm climates.
