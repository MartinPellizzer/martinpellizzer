Thevetia thevetia

Thevetia thevetia, commonly known as yellow oleander, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has smooth, green, and often hairy leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Its woody structure and adaptability make it a hardy species that thrives in various climates.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
