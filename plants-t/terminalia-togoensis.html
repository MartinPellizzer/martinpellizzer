Terminalia togoensis

Terminalia togoensis, commonly known as African snowball, is a small to medium-sized tree native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the appearance of a snowball. Its fruit is a spherical, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Terminalia togoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
