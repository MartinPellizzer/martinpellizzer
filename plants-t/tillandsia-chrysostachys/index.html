Tillandsia Chrysostachys

Tillandsia chrysostachys is a striking bromeliad known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to Central and South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates. Its unique appearance features long, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements. With its colorful blooms and air-purifying qualities, it's a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
