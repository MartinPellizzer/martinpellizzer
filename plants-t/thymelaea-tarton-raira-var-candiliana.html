Thymelaea tarton-raira var. candiliana

Thymelaea tarton-raira var. candiliana, commonly known as the red-flowered thymelaea, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers, which are characteristic of the genus Thymelaea. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in cultivation.
