Trachelospermum ninhii

Trachelospermum ninhii, commonly known as the Ninhii star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine that produces fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Trachelospermum ninhii is also valued for its ability to climb trellises and walls, making it a popular choice for garden decoration.
