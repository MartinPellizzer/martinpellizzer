Trachelospermum liukiuense

Trachelospermum liukiuense, commonly known as the Ryukyu star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
