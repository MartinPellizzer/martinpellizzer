Tephrosia pallens var. angolensis

Tephrosia pallens var. angolensis, commonly known as the Angola tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its leaves are pinnately compound with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.

The plant also plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
