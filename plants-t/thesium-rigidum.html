Thesium rigidum

Thesium rigidum, commonly known as the rigid thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. Its leaves are linear and rigid, giving it its common name, while its yellow flowers bloom in spring.

Thesium rigidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
