Thespesia beata

Thespesia beata, commonly known as the sacred hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring thick, waxy leaves and showy, pale yellow or white flowers.
