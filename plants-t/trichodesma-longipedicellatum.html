Trichodesma longipedicellatum

Trichodesma longipedicellatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
