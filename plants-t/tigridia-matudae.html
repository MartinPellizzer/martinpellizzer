Tigridia matudae

Tigridia matudae, commonly known as the Matudae Sword Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive black spot at the base. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are typically broad and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by gardeners interested in rare and exotic plants.
