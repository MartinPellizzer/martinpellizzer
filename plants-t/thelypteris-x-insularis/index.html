Thelypteris × Insularis

Thelypteris × insularis is a rare fern species known for its unique hybrid characteristics. It is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions where humidity and warmth thrive. This plant exhibits delicate fronds that display a range of green to bronze hues. Its natural habitat includes moist forests and shaded areas near water sources. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
