Thottea longipedunculata

Thottea longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-peduncled thottea, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
