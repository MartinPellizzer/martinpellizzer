Tydaea elegans

Tydaea elegans, commonly known as the slender grassvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its small, pale green or yellowish flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in early summer.

Tydaea elegans is characterized by its narrow leaves and is often found in association with other grassland species.
