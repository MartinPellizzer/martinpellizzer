Tragacantha kapherriana

Tragacantha kapherriana, commonly known as the Kapherriana tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces a distinctive resinous sap, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
