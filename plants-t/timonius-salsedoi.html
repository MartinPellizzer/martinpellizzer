Timonius salsedoi

Timonius salsedoi, commonly known as the Salsedoi dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and bloom in early spring.

Timonius salsedoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in local herbal remedies.
