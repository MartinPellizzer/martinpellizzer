Tibouchina Trichopoda

Tibouchina trichopoda is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique leaf structure and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Tibouchina trichopoda not only adds beauty to landscapes but also supports local biodiversity by attracting pollinators.
