Tabernaemontana tenuis

Tabernaemontana tenuis, commonly known as the slender tabernanthe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
