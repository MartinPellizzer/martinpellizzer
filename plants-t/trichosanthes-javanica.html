Trichosanthes javanica

Trichosanthes javanica, commonly known as the Chinese lantern or bitter melon, is a vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive orange, inflated fruits that resemble small lanterns, which are used in traditional medicine and cuisine. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is widely cultivated for its edible fruit and medicinal properties.
