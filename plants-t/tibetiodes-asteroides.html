Tibetiodes asteroides

Tibetiodes asteroides, commonly known as the Tibetan aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to extreme climatic conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, while its flowers display a range of colors, including purple and white.

Tibetiodes asteroides is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its traditional use in herbal medicine.
