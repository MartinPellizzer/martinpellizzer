Thunbergia katangensis

Thunbergia katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga morning glory, is a climbing vine native to Central Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow. The plant is part of the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.
