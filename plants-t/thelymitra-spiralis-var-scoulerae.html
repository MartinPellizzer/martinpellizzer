Thelymitra spiralis var. scoulerae

Thelymitra spiralis var. scoulerae, commonly known as the Scouler's sun-orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal dunes. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive golden-yellow to orange coloration. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flower has a unique spiral arrangement of petals and sepals.

This variety is noted for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and is often found in areas with limited water availability.
