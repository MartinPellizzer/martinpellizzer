Trigonachras rigida

Trigonachras rigida, commonly known as the rigid trigonotis, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions. It is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils, showing some degree of salt tolerance.
