Tylophora yunnanensis

Tylophora yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Tylophora, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a follicle that contains several seeds, and it is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Tylophora yunnanensis is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
