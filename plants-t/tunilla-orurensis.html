Tunilla orurensis

Tunilla orurensis, commonly known as the red-leafed orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, moist environments with high humidity and partial shade. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its complex root system that aids in nutrient absorption.
