Tillandsia diguetii

Tillandsia diguetii, commonly known as the silver pine tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture from the air. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with a pale green or white color.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
