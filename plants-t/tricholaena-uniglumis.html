Tricholaena uniglumis

Tricholaena uniglumis, commonly known as the African sword fern, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its sword-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikelets, typical of the grass family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Tricholaena uniglumis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
