Tetraria elongata

Tetraria elongata, commonly known as the slender tetraria, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stem and small, daisy-like composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.
