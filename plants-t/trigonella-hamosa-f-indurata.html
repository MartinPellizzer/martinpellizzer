Trigonella hamosa f. indurata

Trigonella hamosa f. indurata, commonly known as the rough chickpea, is a botanical variant of the chickpea species.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia. The plant features small, yellow flowers and produces kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with a rough or hairy texture.

This subspecies is valued for its hardy nature and potential agricultural use in dry climates.
