Thelypteris draco

Thelypteris draco, commonly known as the Draco fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The Draco fern is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in its native habitats.
