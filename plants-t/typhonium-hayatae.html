Typhonium hayatae

Typhonium hayatae, commonly known as the Bornean arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking spatulate inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. Typhonium hayatae thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
