Teucrium Marum Var. Subspinosum

Teucrium marum var. subspinosum is a lesser-known yet strikingly unique plant species native to certain regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the mint family, known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses throughout history. This particular variety is distinguished by its spiny leaves and vibrant purple flowers, making it a standout in botanical gardens and natural habitats. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to other members of its genus. Its ecological significance and potential horticultural value make it a fascinating subject for further study and appreciation.
