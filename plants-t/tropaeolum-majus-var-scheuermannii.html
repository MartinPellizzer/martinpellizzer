Tropaeolum majus var. scheuermannii

Tropaeolum majus var. scheuermannii, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a vigorous annual vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and bright orange to red tubular flowers.

It produces edible beans that are typically harvested when immature, offering a sweet, nutty flavor. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are covered in fine, pubescent hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for container gardening and trellis displays.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
