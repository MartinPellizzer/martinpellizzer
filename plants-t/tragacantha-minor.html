Tragacantha minor

Tragacantha minor, commonly known as the lesser tragacanth, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in rocky or dry soils.

Tragacantha minor is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy species in Mediterranean climates.
