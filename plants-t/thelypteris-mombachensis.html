Thelypteris mombachensis

Thelypteris mombachensis, commonly known as the Mombach holly fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are extensively branched, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

The leaves are usually bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily identifiable in its natural habitat.
