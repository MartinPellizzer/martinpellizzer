Thlaspi subapterum

Thlaspi subapterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cabbagewort, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
