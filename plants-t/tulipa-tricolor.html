Tulipa tricolor

Tulipa tricolor, commonly known as the three-color tulip, is a species of flowering plant native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its striking, multi-colored petals that typically display shades of red, yellow, and white. The plant belongs to the genus Tulipa in the family Liliaceae, and it produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous underground stem.

Tulipa tricolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
