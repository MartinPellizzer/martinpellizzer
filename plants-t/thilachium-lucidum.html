Thilachium lucidum

Thilachium lucidum, commonly known as the glossy lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and often feature a glossy sheen. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are sturdy and upright.

Thilachium lucidum thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
