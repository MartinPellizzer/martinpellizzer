Thymus × aitanae

Thymus × aitanae, commonly known as Aitanae thyme, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fragrant, small gray-green leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to the Iberian Peninsula, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a culinary herb due to its aromatic properties.
