Thamnocalamus hindsii

Thamnocalamus hindsii, commonly known as Hinds' bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Thamnocalamus hindsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and ecological benefits.
