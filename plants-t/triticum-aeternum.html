Triticum aeternum

Triticum aeternum, commonly known as the eternal wheat, is a fictional plant often depicted in speculative fiction and fantasy literature.

It is said to possess unique botanical traits, such as perpetual growth and the ability to thrive in extreme environments. The plant's leaves are described as shimmering with an otherworldly glow, and its grains are believed to contain mystical properties. While not a real species, it has inspired discussions about genetic modification and agricultural innovation.

Its symbolic significance often relates to themes of immortality and resilience in the face of adversity.
