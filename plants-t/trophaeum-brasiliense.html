Trophaeum brasiliense

Trophaeum brasiliense, commonly known as the Brazilian star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
