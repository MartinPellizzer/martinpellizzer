Tontelea macrophylla

Tontelea macrophylla, commonly known as the giant tontelea, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of several meters. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Tontelea macrophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its visual impact.
