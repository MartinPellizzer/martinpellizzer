Thymus × monrealensis nothosubsp.

monrealensis, commonly known as Monreal thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, grey-green leaves.

The plant produces compact clusters of pink to purple flowers, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and aromatic properties, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
