Themeda gigantea var. longispatha

Themeda gigantea var. longispatha, commonly known as the longspatha grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and long, narrow leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
