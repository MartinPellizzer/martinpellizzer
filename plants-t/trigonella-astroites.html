Trigonella astroites

Trigonella astroites, commonly known as the star vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
