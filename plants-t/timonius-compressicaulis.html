Timonius compressicaulis

Timonius compressicaulis, commonly known as the compressed stalk timonius, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers.

This perennial herb typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. Its leaves are narrow and compressed, giving it a distinctive appearance, while its flowers form dense, clustered umbels. The plant is often found in shaded woodland areas and along streambanks.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
