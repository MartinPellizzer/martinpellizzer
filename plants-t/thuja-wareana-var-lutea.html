Thuja wareana var. lutea

Thuja wareana var. lutea, commonly known as the golden thuja, is a variant of the thuja plant characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage.

It is a slow-growing evergreen conifer that typically reaches heights of 3 to 5 meters. The leaves are scale-like and emit a subtle, pleasant fragrance, especially when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add a striking visual element to gardens.
