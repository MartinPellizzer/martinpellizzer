Tricyrtis perfoliata

Tricyrtis perfoliata, commonly known as the Japanese ghost orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features striking, orchid-like flowers that are typically pink or white with a yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
