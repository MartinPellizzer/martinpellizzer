Thea spectabilis

Thea spectabilis, commonly known as the showy tea plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features attractive, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

Thea spectabilis prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various growing conditions.
