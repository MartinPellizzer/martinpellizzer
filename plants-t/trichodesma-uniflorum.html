Trichodesma uniflorum

Trichodesma uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower trichodesma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Trichodesma uniflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
