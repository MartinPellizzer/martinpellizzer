Tillandsia Strobilantha

Tillandsia strobilantha is a captivating air plant known for its unique appearance and ability to thrive without soil. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant is part of the Bromeliaceae family. Its striking silver-green foliage and intricate flower spikes make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Easy to care for, it requires only indirect light and occasional misting. This plant is both a beauty and a symbol of resilience in the plant world.
