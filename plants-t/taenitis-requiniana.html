Taenitis requiniana

Taenitis requiniana, commonly known as the reef fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in coastal areas and on rocky substrates. Its spores are released from sporangia located on the underside of its leaves.

Taenitis requiniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
