Tragopogon picroides

Tragopogon picroides, commonly known as the yellow goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Eurasia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow ray flowers surrounded by a dense cluster of tiny tubular florets. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, thriving in temperate climates.

Tragopogon picroides is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along the edges of forests.
