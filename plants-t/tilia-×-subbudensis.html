Tilia × subbudensis

Tilia × subbudensis, commonly known as the hybrid linden, is a deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Tilia cordata and Tilia europaea, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The tree produces heart-shaped leaves that are typically serrated along the edges. Its fragrant white flowers appear in clusters during the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees.

The bark is smooth and light gray, becoming more fissured with age.
