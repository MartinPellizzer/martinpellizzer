Tofieldia coccinea var. akkana

Tofieldia coccinea var. akkana, commonly known as the redflower tofieldia, is a variant of the tofieldia plant native to parts of North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers in late summer. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and small, clustered inflorescences.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
