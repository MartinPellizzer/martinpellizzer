Tabernaemontana ventricosa

Tabernaemontana ventricosa, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Tabernaemontana ventricosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
