Thalictrum minutissimum

Thalictrum minutissimum, commonly known as the least meadow-rue, is a delicate flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers.
