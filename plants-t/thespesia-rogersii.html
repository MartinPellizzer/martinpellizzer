Thespesia rogersii

Thespesia rogersii, commonly known as the Rogers' hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink to red in color. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of Hawaii.
