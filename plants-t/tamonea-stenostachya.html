Tamonea stenostachya

Tamonea stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed grass.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Tamonea stenostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
