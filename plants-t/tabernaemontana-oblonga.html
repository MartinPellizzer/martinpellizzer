Tabernaemontana oblonga

Tabernaemontana oblonga, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Tabernaemontana oblonga is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its pleasant fragrance and attractive appearance.
