Trichoglottis tenuis

Trichoglottis tenuis, commonly known as the slender leaved orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil.

Trichoglottis tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
