Trachycarpus oreophilus

Trachycarpus oreophilus, commonly known as the mountain bamboo palm, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a slow-growing evergreen with a distinctive clumping growth habit. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its elegant appearance and ability to add a tropical feel to gardens or interiors.
