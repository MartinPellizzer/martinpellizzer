Terminalia trinervia

Terminalia trinervia, commonly known as African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, simple leaves. Terminalia trinervia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.
