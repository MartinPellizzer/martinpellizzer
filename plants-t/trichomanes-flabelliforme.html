Trichomanes flabelliforme

Trichomanes flabelliforme, commonly known as the fan fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble a fan, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually small and closely arranged, forming a compact, lush appearance.

Trichomanes flabelliforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
