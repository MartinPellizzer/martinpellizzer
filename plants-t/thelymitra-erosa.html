Thelymitra erosa

Thelymitra erosa, commonly known as the slender sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, often in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that is usually pale yellow to greenish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flower has a distinctive, spurred lip.

Thelymitra erosa is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
