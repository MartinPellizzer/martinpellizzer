Thymelaea thesioides var. catalaunica

Thymelaea thesioides var. catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalaunica thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with woody stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
