Tofieldia nuda var. yoshiiana

Tofieldia nuda var. yoshiiana, commonly known as the Japanese tofieldia, is a variant of the tofieldia plant native to Japan.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in rocky or acidic soils.

This variant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in native gardens.
