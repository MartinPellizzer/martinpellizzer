Trichosanthes beccariana subsp. pusilla

Trichosanthes beccariana subsp.

pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf bitter melon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a vine that produces small, bitter fruits with a distinctive warty surface.
