Terminalia erythrocarpa

Terminalia erythrocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited Terminalia, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by distinctive red, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in traditional medicine.
