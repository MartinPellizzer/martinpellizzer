Thymus × sorianoi

Thymus × sorianoi, commonly known as the Sorianoi thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
