Torenia rupestris

Torenia rupestris, commonly known as the rock torenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Torenia rupestris thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
