Trachelospermum Lucidum

Trachelospermum lucidum is a vigorous evergreen vine native to southern China and Southeast Asia. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures with its strong, twisting stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. Its glossy, dark green leaves add a striking visual element to gardens and landscapes. This versatile plant is popular for its ornamental value and adaptability to different climates.
