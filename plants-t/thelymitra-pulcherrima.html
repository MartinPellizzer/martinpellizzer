Thelymitra pulcherrima

Thelymitra pulcherrima, commonly known as the pink fairy lantern, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with numerous small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Thelymitra pulcherrima thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest or heathland habitats.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for cultivation and conservation efforts.
