Tragacantha macrotropis

Tragacantha macrotropis, commonly known as the large-fruited tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces elongated pods that contain small, round seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
