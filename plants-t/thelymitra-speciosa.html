Thelymitra speciosa

Thelymitra speciosa, commonly known as the showy sun-orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns resembling sun rays. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears several flowers, each with a three-lobed labellum and a spurred column. Thelymitra speciosa thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests and understorey habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable and visually appealing member of the Orchidaceae family.
