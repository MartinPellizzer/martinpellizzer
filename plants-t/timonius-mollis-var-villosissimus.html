Timonius mollis var. villosissimus

Timonius mollis var. villosissimus, commonly known as the hairy timonius, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also bear a dense covering of fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
