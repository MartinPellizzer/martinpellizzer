Trianosperma martianum var. genuinum

Trianosperma martianum var. genuinum, commonly known as the Martian sunbloom, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mars.

It possesses thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with a strong, sweet scent that attracts specific pollinators. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep underground water sources.
