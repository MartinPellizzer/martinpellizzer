Theobroma speciosum var. spruceanum

Theobroma speciosum var. spruceanum, commonly known as the spruce beauty cocoa tree, is a tropical species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental value due to its striking white and pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are large, palmately lobed, and its flowers are clustered in panicles.
