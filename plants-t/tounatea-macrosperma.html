Tounatea macrosperma

Tounatea macrosperma, commonly known as the large-seeded tounatea, is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its seeds are enclosed in thick, leathery pods, which help in seed dispersal.

Tounatea macrosperma is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
