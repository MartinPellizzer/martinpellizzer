Tassadia geniculata

Tassadia geniculata, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a tropical species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are broad and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

Tassadia geniculata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking foliage.
