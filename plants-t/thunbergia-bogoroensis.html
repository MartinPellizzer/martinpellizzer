Thunbergia bogoroensis

Thunbergia bogoroensis, commonly known as the Bogoro Trumpet Vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically tubular with five lobes and come in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
