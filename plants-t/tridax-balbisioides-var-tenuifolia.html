Tridax balbisioides var. tenuifolia

Tridax balbisioides var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf tridax, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in composite heads. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and pastures. It has a creeping growth habit with stolons that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread readily.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
