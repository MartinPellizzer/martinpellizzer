Trichocentrum recurvum

Trichocentrum recurvum, commonly known as the curly leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with numerous clustered, lance-shaped leaves that are often curled or twisted. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum recurvum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
