Taraxacum arachnotrichum

Taraxacum arachnotrichum, commonly known as the hairy dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other dandelion species. The plant produces a single, bright yellow flower that develops into a fluffy, white seed head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
