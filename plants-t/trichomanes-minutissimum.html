Trichomanes minutissimum

Trichomanes minutissimum, commonly known as the minute holly fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its tiny, closely spaced fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks. Its leaves are typically less than 1 cm in length, with a finely divided structure.
