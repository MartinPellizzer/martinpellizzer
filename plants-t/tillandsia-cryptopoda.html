Tillandsia cryptopoda

Tillandsia cryptopoda, commonly known as the hidden leaf tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth form. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and epiphytic nature, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
