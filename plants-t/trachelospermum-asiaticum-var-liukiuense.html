Trachelospermum asiaticum var. liukiuense

Trachelospermum asiaticum var. liukiuense, commonly known as the Liukiu jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and pleasant fragrance.

Its flowers are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
