Thymus nummularius var. abbreviatus

Thymus nummularius var. abbreviatus, commonly known as the silver thyme or narrow-leaved thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, rounded leaves that are densely covered with fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both garden use and medicinal purposes.
