Trikalis

Trikalis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Trikalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
