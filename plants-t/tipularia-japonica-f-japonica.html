Tipularia japonica f. japonica

Tipularia japonica f. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese tipularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

This variety is native to Japan and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
