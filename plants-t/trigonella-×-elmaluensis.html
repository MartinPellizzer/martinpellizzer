Trigonella × elmaluensis

Trigonella × elmaluensis, commonly known as the hybrid fenugreek, is a cross between Trigonella foenum-graecum and Trigonella caerulea.

It is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into pod-like fruits containing seeds. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique example of intergeneric hybridization in the Fabaceae family.
