Tabernaemontana ulei

Tabernaemontana ulei, commonly known as the white star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Tabernaemontana ulei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
