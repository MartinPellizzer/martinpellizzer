Thelypteris plumosa

Thelypteris plumosa, commonly known as the plumose water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a soft, lace-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant across the soil.

Thelypteris plumosa is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
