Tulipa heterochroa

Tulipa heterochroa, commonly known as the heterochrome tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its distinctive flowers that exhibit a unique coloration pattern, with different colors on the outer and inner petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 20 to 30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are perennial, and it blooms in early spring.

The botanical features of this tulip make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
