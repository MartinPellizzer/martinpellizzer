Triosteum himalayanum

Triosteum himalayanum, commonly known as the Himalayan triosteum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in the spring. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.

Triosteum himalayanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand cold climates.
