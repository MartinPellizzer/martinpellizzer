Tibouchina mollis var. patenter-villosa

Tibouchina mollis var. patenter-villosa, commonly known as the silky tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
