Torenia oblonga

Torenia oblonga, commonly known as the common torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with a distinctive hooded structure. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with silver or white veins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Torenia oblonga is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
