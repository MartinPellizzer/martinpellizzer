Thalictrum lasiostylum

Thalictrum lasiostylum, commonly known as the woolly meadow-rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

Thalictrum lasiostylum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
