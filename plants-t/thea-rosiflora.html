Thea rosiflora

Thea rosiflora, commonly known as the rose-flowered thea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its showy, rose-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Thea rosiflora prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
