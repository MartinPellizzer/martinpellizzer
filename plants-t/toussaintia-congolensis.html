Toussaintia congolensis

Toussaintia congolensis, commonly known as the Congo lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central Africa.

It features striking, large, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a prominent central purple or red spot. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in the ecosystems of its native habitat.
