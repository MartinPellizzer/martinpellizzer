Thunbergia elliotii

Thunbergia elliotii, commonly known as Elliot's trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia elliotii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and butterflies.
