Thinouia obliqua

Thinouia obliqua, commonly known as the oblique sedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. Native to wetland environments, it thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes and along the edges of water bodies. Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
