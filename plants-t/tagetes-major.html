Tagetes major

Tagetes major, commonly known as the Mexican marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange petals and is part of the Asteraceae family. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to repel certain pests due to the presence of volatile compounds. Its flowers typically bloom in summer and can be found in a variety of garden settings.

Tagetes major is also used in traditional medicine and for its aromatic properties.
