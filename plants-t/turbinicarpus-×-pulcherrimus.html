Turbinicarpus × pulcherrimus

Turbinicarpus × pulcherrimus, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its unique appearance, with a brightly colored, fleshy body known as the "pseudostem.". The plant features a central, often vibrant red or orange stem that is actually a grafted combination of two different cactus species. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it stores water in its thick, fleshy stems, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

This hybrid is popular among cactus enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
