Tolpis cretica

Tolpis cretica, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, making it visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Tolpis cretica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
