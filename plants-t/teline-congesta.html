Teline congesta

Teline congesta, commonly known as the clustered teline, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, giving it its common name. It typically grows in grassland habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Teline congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
