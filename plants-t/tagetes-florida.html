Tagetes florida

Tagetes florida, commonly known as the French marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow blooms. The plant is typically annual, growing to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are composed of ray florets and disk florets.

Tagetes florida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in companion planting to deter pests.
