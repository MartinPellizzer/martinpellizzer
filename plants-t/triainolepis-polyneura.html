Triainolepis polyneura

Triainolepis polyneura, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its woody structure and slow growth make it a long-lived species.

The plant has cultural and medicinal significance in some local communities.
