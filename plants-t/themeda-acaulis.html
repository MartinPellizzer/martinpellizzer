Themeda acaulis

Themeda acaulis, commonly known as spear grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Themeda acaulis is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
