Telosma odoratissima

Telosma odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant starfruit vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit, resembling a starfruit, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Telosma odoratissima is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its culinary uses in traditional cuisines.
