Tinospora megalobotrys

Tinospora megalobotrys, commonly known as the giant tinospora, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.

Tinospora megalobotrys is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
