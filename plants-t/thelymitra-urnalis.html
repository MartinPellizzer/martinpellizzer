Thelymitra urnalis

Thelymitra urnalis, commonly known as the urn-shaped rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodland or heathland environments. Its flowering period generally occurs during late winter to early spring, and it is known for its ability to withstand drought conditions.

The species is also notable for its unique floral structure, which resembles a small urn.
