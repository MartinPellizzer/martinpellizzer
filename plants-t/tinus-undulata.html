Tinus undulata

Tinus undulata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Tinus undulata is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
