Tilia platyphyllos f. aurea

Tilia platyphyllos f. aurea, commonly known as the golden-leaved lime tree, is a unique cultivar of the small-leaved lime tree.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its pale green bark. This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and public spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for temperate climates.

The plant is also noted for its fragrant flowers and is often used in landscaping for its visual impact.
