Tabernaemontana cylindrocarpa

Tabernaemontana cylindrocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm seasons. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptic leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a capsule that is cylindrical in shape, giving it its specific epithet.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
