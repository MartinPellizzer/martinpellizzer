Thymus tauricus

Thymus tauricus, commonly known as Taurian thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its essential oils, rich in thymol, are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.

Thymus tauricus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
