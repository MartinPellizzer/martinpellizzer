Tetragamestus isochiloides

Tetragamestus isochiloides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Tetragamestus isochiloides is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
