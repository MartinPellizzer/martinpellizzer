Trichomanes bilobatum

Trichomanes bilobatum, commonly known as the bilobed huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The fronds are bi-lobed, with a distinctive, narrow, and often recurved shape. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is notable for its primitive morphology and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
