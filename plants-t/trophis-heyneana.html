Trophis heyneana

Trophis heyneana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
