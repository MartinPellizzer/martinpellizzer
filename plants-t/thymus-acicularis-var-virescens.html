Thymus acicularis var. virescens

Thymus acicularis var. virescens, commonly known as green thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, giving it its common name. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its aromatic leaves are often used in cooking and herbal remedies.
