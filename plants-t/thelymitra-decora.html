Thelymitra decora

Thelymitra decora, commonly known as the decorated helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flower with a greenish-blue hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flowers bloom in spring.

Thelymitra decora is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
