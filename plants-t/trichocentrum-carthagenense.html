Trichocentrum carthagenense

Trichocentrum carthagenense, commonly known as the Carthagena orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Trichocentrum carthagenense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ease of cultivation.
