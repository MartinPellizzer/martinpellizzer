Thelypteris tottoides

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, giving it a lacy appearance. Thelypteris tottoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
