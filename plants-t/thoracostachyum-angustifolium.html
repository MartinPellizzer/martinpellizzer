Thoracostachyum angustifolium

Thoracostachyum angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves with a narrow, linear shape and a smooth texture. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
