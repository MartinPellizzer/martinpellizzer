Tagetes lanceolata

Tagetes lanceolata, commonly known as the narrowleaf marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and bright orange to yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Tagetes lanceolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to repel certain pests.
