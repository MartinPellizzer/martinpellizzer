Tilia crenoserrata var. acuminata

Tilia crenoserrata var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf linden, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with serrated margins. The tree produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in racemes. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
