Turnera lutescens

Turnera lutescens, commonly known as the golden turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring or early summer. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Turnera lutescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
