Tripleurospermum callosum

Tripleurospermum callosum, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the tips of its stems. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert or semi-desert regions.
