Tithymalus Aucheri

Tithymalus aucheri is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its bright yellow petals contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage, making it a popular choice among gardeners. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care. It is a hardy perennial that adds a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes.
