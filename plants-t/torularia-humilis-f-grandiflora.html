Torularia humilis f. grandiflora

Torularia humilis f. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora form of the common torula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland grassland areas.

Due to its compact growth habit and attractive flowers, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
