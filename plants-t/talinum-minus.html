Talinum minus

Talinum minus, commonly known as red-rooted nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dark seeds.

Talinum minus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
