Tricalysia biafrana var. fangana

Tricalysia biafrana var. fangana, commonly known as the Fangana variant of the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental species. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
