Trigonella kotschyi var. hierosolymitana

Trigonella kotschyi var. hierosolymitana, commonly known as the Jerusalem trigonella, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to dry and saline soils.
