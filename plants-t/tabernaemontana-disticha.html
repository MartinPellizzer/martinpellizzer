Tabernaemontana disticha

Tabernaemontana disticha, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, jasmine-like aroma. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
