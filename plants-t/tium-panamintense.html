Tium panamintense

Tium panamintense, commonly known as the Panamint tium, is a rare flowering plant native to the deserts of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
