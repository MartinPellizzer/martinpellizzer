Trigonotis cavaleriei var. cavaleriei

Trigonotis cavaleriei var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being oval-shaped and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
