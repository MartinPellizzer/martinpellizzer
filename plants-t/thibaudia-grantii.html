Thibaudia grantii

Thibaudia grantii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows from a rhizomatous root system. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Thibaudia grantii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
