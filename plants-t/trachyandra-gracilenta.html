Trachyandra gracilenta

Trachyandra gracilenta, commonly known as the slender trachyandra, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. Native to South Africa, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
