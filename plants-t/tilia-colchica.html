Tilia colchica

Tilia colchica, commonly known as the Colchian lime, is a deciduous tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are small, round nutlets that mature in the fall.

Tilia colchica is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
