Tigridia minuta

Tigridia minuta, commonly known as the dwarf tigridia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Iridaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. Tigridia minuta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, short-lived flowers.
