Tradescantia reflexa var. eloiseana

Tradescantia reflexa var. eloiseana, commonly known as the "Blue Snow" plant, is a striking ornamental species native to South America.

It features striking blue-gray foliage with a silvery sheen, and its flowers are typically lavender to purple in color. The plant is a low-growing groundcover with a spreading habit, often forming dense mats. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it ideal for shady garden spaces.

Its foliage and flowers add a unique visual interest to landscapes and containers.
