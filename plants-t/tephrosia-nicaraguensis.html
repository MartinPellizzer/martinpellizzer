Tephrosia nicaraguensis

Tephrosia nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or open habitats.
