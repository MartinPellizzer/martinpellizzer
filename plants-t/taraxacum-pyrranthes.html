Taraxacum pyrranthes

Taraxacum pyrranthes, commonly known as the purple star dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright purple flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves that are usually green with a reddish or purplish tinge. Native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, it thrives in dry, sunny environments.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is considered a perennial herb.
