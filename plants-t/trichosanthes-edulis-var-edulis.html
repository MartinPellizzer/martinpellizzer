Trichosanthes edulis var. edulis

Trichosanthes edulis var. edulis, commonly known as the Chinese snake gourd, is a flowering vine native to East Asia.

It produces elongated, pale green fruits that mature to a yellowish-orange color. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its distinctive, trumpet-shaped flowers. It is cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various Asian cuisines.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
