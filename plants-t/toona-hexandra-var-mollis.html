Toona hexandra var. mollis

Toona hexandra var. mollis, commonly known as the silky toona, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and large, heart-shaped leaves that are soft to the touch. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, fibrous fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The silky toona is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
