Tragopogon austriaca var. humilis

Tragopogon austriaca var. humilis, commonly known as the Austrian goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It features slender, hairy stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense clusters. The plant produces bright yellow ray florets surrounded by a basal involucre of leaf-like bracts. Its flowers bloom in early summer, and the plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils.

It is often found in meadows and along roadsides, showcasing its resilience in marginal habitats.
