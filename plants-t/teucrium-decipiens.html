Teucrium decipiens

Teucrium decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in dense spikes.

Teucrium decipiens is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
