Thelypteris efogensis

Thelypteris efogensis, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, often in shaded, moist environments. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris efogensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
