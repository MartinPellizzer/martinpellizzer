Trachelospermum cavaleriei

Trachelospermum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to China.

It features fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, jasmine-like scent. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Trachelospermum cavaleriei is also valued for its ability to climb and cover trellises or walls effectively.
