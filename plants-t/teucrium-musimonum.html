Teucrium musimonum

Teucrium musimonum, commonly known as musimon thyme, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or blue, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Teucrium musimonum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
