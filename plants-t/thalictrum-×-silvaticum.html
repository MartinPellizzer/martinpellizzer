Thalictrum × silvaticum

Thalictrum × silvaticum, commonly known as the meadow rue, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, usually yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in some areas.

Thalictrum × silvaticum is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and early spring blooms.
