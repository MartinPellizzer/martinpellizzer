Triumfetta annua

Triumfetta annua, commonly known as the annual triumfetta, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring slender stems and opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

Triumfetta annua is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many areas.
