Thoracostachyum hypolytroides var. immensum

Thoracostachyum hypolytroides var. immensum, commonly known as the giant sotol, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This species is adapted to hot, dry climates and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy plant in xeriscaped environments.
