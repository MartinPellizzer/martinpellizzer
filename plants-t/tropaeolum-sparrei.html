Tropaeolum sparrei

Tropaeolum sparrei, commonly known as the everlasting nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright orange or yellow blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Tropaeolum sparrei is also valued for its ornamental beauty and culinary uses.
