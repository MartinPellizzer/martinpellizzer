Toxicodendron yunnanense var. yunnanense

Toxicodendron yunnanense var. yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan lacquer tree, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by clusters of small, round fruits. It is known for its ability to produce a toxic resin, which can cause dermatitis in humans upon contact.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local cultures.
