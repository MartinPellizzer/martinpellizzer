Tithymalus × somboriensis

Tithymalus × somboriensis, commonly known as the Sambor crosswort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. Native to the region of Sambor in Poland, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in woodland or along stream banks.

Due to its rarity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
