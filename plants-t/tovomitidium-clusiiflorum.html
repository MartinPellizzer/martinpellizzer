Tovomitidium clusiiflorum

Tovomitidium clusiiflorum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Tovomitidium clusiiflorum is also known for its fast growth rate and ability to adapt to various soil types.
