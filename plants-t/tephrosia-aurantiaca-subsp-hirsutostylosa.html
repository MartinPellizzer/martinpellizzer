Tephrosia aurantiaca subsp. hirsutostylosa

hirsutostylosa, commonly known as the hairy-styled yellow tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.
