Thermopsis dahurica

Thermopsis dahurica, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to the colder regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Thermopsis dahurica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
