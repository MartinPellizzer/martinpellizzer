Thalictrum urbaini var. urbaini

Thalictrum urbaini var. urbaini, commonly known as the European meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features slender, branched stems that typically grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in compound umbels, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are deeply lobed and serrated.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.
