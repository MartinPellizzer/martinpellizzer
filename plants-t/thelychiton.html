Thelychiton

Thelychiton, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Thelychiton is highly sought after in both botanical research and conservation efforts.
