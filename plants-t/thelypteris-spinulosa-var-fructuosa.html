Thelypteris spinulosa var. fructuosa

Thelypteris spinulosa var. fructuosa, commonly known as the fruit-bearing sword fern, is a variant of the sword fern genus Thelypteris.

It is a fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features fronds that are leathery and serrated, with a distinctive, compact growth habit. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining forest biodiversity.
