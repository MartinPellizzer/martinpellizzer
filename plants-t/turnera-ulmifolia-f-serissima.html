Turnera ulmifolia f. serissima

Turnera ulmifolia f. serissima, commonly known as the golden turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features elliptical, glossy leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem, opposite leaf arrangement, and a distinctive aromatic scent.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
