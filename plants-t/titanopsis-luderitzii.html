Titanopsis luderitzii

Titanopsis luderitzii, commonly known as the Luderitz cushion, is a succulent plant native to Namibia and South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its compact form. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
