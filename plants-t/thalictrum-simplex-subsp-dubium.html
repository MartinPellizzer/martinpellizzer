Thalictrum simplex subsp. dubium

Thalictrum simplex subsp.

dubium, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia. It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically basal and deeply divided.

The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or meadows.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
