Thlaspi alpestre var. tunense

Thlaspi alpestre var. tunense, commonly known as the Alpine Tansy or Rock Tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in rocky and alpine habitats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a slightly hairy texture, and it is often found growing in dry, well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
