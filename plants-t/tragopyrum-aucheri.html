Tragopyrum aucheri

Tragopyrum aucheri, commonly known as the Armenian goatherb, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and early spring bloom.
