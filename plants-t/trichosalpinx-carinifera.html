Trichosalpinx carinifera

Trichosalpinx carinifera, commonly known as the horned caladenia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, lance-shaped leaf. The flower is distinctive, featuring a narrow, spurred labellum and a hooded dorsal sepal. It blooms during the cooler months, typically between June and August.

This species is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in open habitats with adequate sunlight.
