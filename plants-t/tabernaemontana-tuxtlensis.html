Tabernaemontana tuxtlensis

Tabernaemontana tuxtlensis, commonly known as the Tuxtlans mountain star.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Mexico, particularly in the states of Veracruz and Oaxaca. The plant is characterized by its large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
