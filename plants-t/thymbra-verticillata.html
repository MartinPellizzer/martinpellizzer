Thymbra verticillata

Thymbra verticillata, commonly known as mountain thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or gravelly habitats. Its essential oils, rich in thymol, contribute to its use in traditional medicine and aromatherapy.

Thymbra verticillata is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
