Thalictrum englerianum

Thalictrum englerianum, commonly known as the Engler's meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to gardens with suitable conditions.
