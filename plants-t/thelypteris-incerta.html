Thelypteris incerta

Thelypteris incerta, commonly known as the uncertain holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually bipinnate, with small, linear pinnae that give it a feathery appearance.

Thelypteris incerta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
