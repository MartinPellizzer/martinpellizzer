Tagetes lucida

Tagetes lucida, commonly known as goldenthread, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and often used in herbal remedies. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical name reflects its association with the genus Tagetes, which includes marigolds.
