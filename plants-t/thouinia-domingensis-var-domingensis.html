Thouinia domingensis var. domingensis

Thouinia domingensis var. domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican thornapple, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Hispaniola.

The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, typically in open, sunny areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
