Turbinicarpus klinkerianus subsp. schwarzii

Turbinicarpus klinkerianus subsp.

schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwarz's Turbinicarpus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stems that are typically covered in dense, white to pale green spines.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing, succulent plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
