Tragopogon turkestanicus

Tragopogon turkestanicus, commonly known as the Turkestan goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy basal rosette. The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense, bristly involucre, which gives it its common name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and mountainous regions.

Tragopogon turkestanicus is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
