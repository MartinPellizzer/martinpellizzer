Theodorea iodostegia

Theodorea iodostegia, commonly known as the iodostegia orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
