Tectaria elata

Tectaria elata, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Tectaria elata plays a significant role in the ecological structure of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
