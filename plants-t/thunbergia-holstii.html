Thunbergia holstii

Thunbergia holstii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Thunbergia holstii prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
