Timonius timon var. whiteanus

Timonius timon var. whiteanus, commonly known as the Whiteanus variant, is a rare and unique botanical specimen within the Timonius genus.

It is characterized by its striking white variegation on its foliage, which sets it apart from other varieties of Timonius. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and botanists for its aesthetic and scientific value.
