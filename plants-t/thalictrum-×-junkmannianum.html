Thalictrum × junkmannianum

Thalictrum × junkmannianum, commonly known as the hybrid meadow rue, is a perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, usually yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas or along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

Thalictrum × junkmannianum is a sterile hybrid, meaning it does not produce viable seeds.
