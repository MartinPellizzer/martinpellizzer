Tropaeolum × reineckeanum

Tropaeolum × reineckeanum, commonly known as the garden nasturtium, is an annual flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces edible leaves and seeds, which are often used in cooking due to their peppery flavor. Its rapid growth habit and ability to thrive in a wide range of soil conditions make it a popular choice for gardeners.

The plant's botanical features include alternate, heart-shaped leaves and a trailing or climbing growth form.
