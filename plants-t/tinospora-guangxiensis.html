Tinospora guangxiensis

Tinospora guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi Tinospora, is a vine native to Southeast Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.

Tinospora guangxiensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
