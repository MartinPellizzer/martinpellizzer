Thunbergia laevis

Thunbergia laevis, commonly known as the blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and delicate, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative vine in gardens and hanging baskets.

Thunbergia laevis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and butterflies.
