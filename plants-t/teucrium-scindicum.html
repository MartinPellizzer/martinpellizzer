Teucrium scindicum

Teucrium scindicum, commonly known as redstem germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, hence its common name. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or violet and bloom in summer.

Teucrium scindicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
