Trichelostylis cinnamometorum

Trichelostylis cinnamometorum, commonly known as the cinnamon-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, cinnamon-scented flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

The orchid produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.
