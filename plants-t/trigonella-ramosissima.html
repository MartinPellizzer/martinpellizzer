Trigonella ramosissima

Trigonella ramosissima, commonly known as hairy trigonella, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its branched stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Trigonella ramosissima is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
