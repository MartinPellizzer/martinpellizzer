Trifolium repens var. giganteum

Trifolium repens var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant white clover, is a variant of the common white clover.

It is characterized by its larger leaves and flowers compared to the typical variety. This plant is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant is often used in forage agriculture and can be found in lawns and meadows.
