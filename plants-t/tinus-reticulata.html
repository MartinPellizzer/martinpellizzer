Tinus reticulata

Tinus reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined tinus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its finely net-veined leaves and yellow flowers that resemble daisies. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The plant's structure and growth habits make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
