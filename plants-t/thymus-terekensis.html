Thymus terekensis

Thymus terekensis, commonly known as Terek thyme, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its aromatic properties make it valuable in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.

Thymus terekensis is also noted for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
