Trema dielsianum

Trema dielsianum, commonly known as the African nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant is characterized by its stinging hairs, which can cause skin irritation upon contact.
