Thouinia myriantha

Thouinia myriantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, blooming in summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy, and it grows in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Thouinia myriantha is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
