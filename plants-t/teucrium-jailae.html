Teucrium jailae

Teucrium jailae, commonly known as the Socotra tea plant, is a flowering shrub native to the island of Socotra in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, often arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Teucrium jailae is well adapted to the arid and rocky environment of Socotra, thriving in poor soil conditions.
