Tragus elatior

Tragus elatior, commonly known as the tall tragus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Tragus elatior is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and sandy soils.

It is a hardy species that thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.
