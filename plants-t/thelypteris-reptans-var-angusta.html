Thelypteris reptans var. angusta

Thelypteris reptans var. angusta, commonly known as narrowleaf rock fern, is a variant of the rock fern genus Thelypteris.

It is a perennial herb with fronds that are typically narrow and elongated, featuring a distinctive, leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or woodland areas. Its sporangia are clustered on the underside of the fronds, a characteristic feature of ferns.

The narrowleaf rock fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest understories.
