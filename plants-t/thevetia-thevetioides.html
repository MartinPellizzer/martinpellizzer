Thevetia thevetioides

Thevetia thevetioides, commonly known as yellow false nectarine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces showy, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange to red capsules containing toxic seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin.

Thevetia thevetioides is characterized by its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
