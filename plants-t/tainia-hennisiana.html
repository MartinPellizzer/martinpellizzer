Tainia hennisiana

Tainia hennisiana, commonly known as the Hennis' tainia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and caladiums. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Tainia hennisiana is typically grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and unique floral structures.
