Thoracostachyum longistylum

Thoracostachyum longistylum, commonly known as the Mexican feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
