Trisetopsis himalayensis

Trisetopsis himalayensis, commonly known as the Himalayan sedge, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas.

Trisetopsis himalayensis plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
