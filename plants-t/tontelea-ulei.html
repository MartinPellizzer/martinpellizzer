Tontelea ulei

Tontelea ulei, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and grassland habitats.

Tontelea ulei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
