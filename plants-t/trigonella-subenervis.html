Trigonella subenervis

Trigonella subenervis, commonly known as the slender fenugreek, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered with fine hairs. The leaves are alternate, trifoliate, and have a distinctive trifoliate structure with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its small yellow flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in the spring.

The plant produces small, angular pods that contain several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
