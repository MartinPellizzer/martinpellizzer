Tulbaghia cepacea var. maritima

Tulbaghia cepacea var. maritima, commonly known as the coastal tulbaghia, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Its aromatic foliage and delicate blooms make it a desirable ornamental plant in Mediterranean climates.
