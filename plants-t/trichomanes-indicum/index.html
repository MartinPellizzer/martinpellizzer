Trichomanes Indicum

Trichomanes indicum is a unique fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments across Asia. It is known for its delicate, lace-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is often found in rocky crevices and forest floors, where it can absorb moisture from the air. Despite its small size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem by supporting various microorganisms and invertebrates. Its presence is a sign of a healthy, undisturbed habitat.
