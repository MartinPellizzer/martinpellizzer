Taeniophyllum minimum

Taeniophyllum minimum, commonly known as the miniature taeniophyllum, is a small epiphytic plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that form a small water-holding cup. Its foliage is typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Taeniophyllum minimum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
