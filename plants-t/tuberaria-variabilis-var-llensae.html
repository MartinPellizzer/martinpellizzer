Tuberaria variabilis var. llensae

Tuberaria variabilis var. llensae, commonly known as the Variable Tuberaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are evergreen, narrow, and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The species is noted for its adaptability to arid conditions, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
