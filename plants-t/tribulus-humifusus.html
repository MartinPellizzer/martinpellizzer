Tribulus humifusus

Tribulus humifusus, commonly known as sand gotham or desert globemallow, is a low-growing annual herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

It features small, yellow flowers and produces capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. The plant has succulent stems and leaves, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments. It is often found in sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for survival in drought conditions.
