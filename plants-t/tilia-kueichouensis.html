Tilia kueichouensis

Tilia kueichouensis, commonly known as the Kueichou lime tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Tiliaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly found in the provinces of Guizhou and Sichuan. The tree is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers a temperate climate.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
