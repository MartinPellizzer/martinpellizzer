Tithymalus calendulifolius

Tithymalus calendulifolius, commonly known as the calendulifolius tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
