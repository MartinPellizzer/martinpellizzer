Tillandsia mirabilis

Tillandsia mirabilis, commonly known as the silver leaf air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a striking appearance. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Tillandsia mirabilis is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
