Thunbergia claessensii

Thunbergia claessensii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in woodland and shrubland habitats. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia claessensii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
