Turpinia montana var. stenophylla

Turpinia montana var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf turpinia, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Oleaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates with mild winters. This variety is noted for its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
