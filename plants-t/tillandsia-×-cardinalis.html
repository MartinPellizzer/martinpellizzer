Tillandsia × cardinalis

Tillandsia × cardinalis, commonly known as the crimson tillandsia, is a hybrid air plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped foliage that can grow up to 12 inches in height. The plant produces striking red and orange inflorescences, which are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
