Terminalia latialata

Terminalia latialata, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and clusters of white, waxy flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain hard, angular seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
