Trichomanes brachyblastos

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in shaded, humid environments. It produces small, spore-bearing structures called soruses on the underside of its fronds.

This species is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas.
