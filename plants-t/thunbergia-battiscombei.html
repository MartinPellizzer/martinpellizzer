Thunbergia battiscombei

Thunbergia battiscombei, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking blue to violet tubular flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
