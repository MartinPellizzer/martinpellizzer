Tridentea virescens

Tridentea virescens, commonly known as the green trident leaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged in a trident-like shape. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous annual, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge.

Tridentea virescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
