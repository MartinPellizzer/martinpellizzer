Tulipa corynestemon

Tulipa corynestemon, commonly known as the Turkish tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Turkey and the eastern Mediterranean.

It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is characterized by its compact growth habit. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
