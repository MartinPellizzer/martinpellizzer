Tilia tonsura

Tilia tonsura, commonly known as the littleleaf lime, is a deciduous tree native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically no larger than 2 inches in diameter. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in drooping inflorescences. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia tonsura is often found in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and in woodland edges.
