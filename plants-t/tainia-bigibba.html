Tainia bigibba

Tainia bigibba, commonly known as the giant taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers followed by edible, starchy corms used in various cuisines. Tainia bigibba thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found in lowland areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
