Trichera timeroyi

Trichera timeroyi, commonly known as the Timor starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the island of Timor.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree with smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, spherical fruits. Trichera timeroyi is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
