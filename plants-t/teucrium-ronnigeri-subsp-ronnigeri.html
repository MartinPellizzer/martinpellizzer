Teucrium ronnigeri subsp. ronnigeri

Teucrium ronnigeri subsp.

ronnigeri, commonly known as Ronniger's germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.
