Thelymitra cornicina

Thelymitra cornicina, commonly known as the golden sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a single erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or golden in color, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

Thelymitra cornicina is known for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
