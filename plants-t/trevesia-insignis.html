Trevesia insignis

Trevesia insignis, commonly known as the velvetleaf trevesia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are typically followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Trevesia insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other tropical regions for horticultural purposes.
