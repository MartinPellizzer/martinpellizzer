Thymus × brachychaetus

Thymus × brachychaetus, commonly known as narrow-leaved thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, narrow, grey-green leaves. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is often used in culinary and herbal applications due to its fragrant, medicinal properties.
