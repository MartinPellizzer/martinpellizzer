Tephroseris Integrifolia Subsp. Serpentini

Tephroseris integrifolia subsp. serpentini is a rare and elusive plant species found in specific alpine environments. It is characterized by its unique leaf structure and subtle floral features that aid in its survival in harsh conditions. This subspecies thrives in high-altitude regions where few other plants can endure. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
