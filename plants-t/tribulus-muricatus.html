Tribulus muricatus

Tribulus muricatus, commonly known as caltrop, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with spiny, four-lobed leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces four-pointed, spiny fruits that contain seeds, which are often used as a natural deterrent for animals. Its sharp seeds are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in cultivated fields.
