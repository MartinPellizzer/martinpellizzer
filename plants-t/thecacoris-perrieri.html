Thecacoris perrieri

Thecacoris perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's corymbiferous plant, is a unique member of the Arecaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often hidden among its foliage.
