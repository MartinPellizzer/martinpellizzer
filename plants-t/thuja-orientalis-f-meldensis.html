Thuja orientalis f. meldensis

Thuja orientalis f. meldensis, commonly known as the Meldens thuja, is a rare and ornamental cultivar of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its compact, conical growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, rounded cones that are typically retained on the branches for an extended period. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for garden settings.

Due to its unique appearance and limited availability, it is often sought after by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
