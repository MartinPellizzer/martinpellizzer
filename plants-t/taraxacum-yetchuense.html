Taraxacum yetchuense

Taraxacum yetchuense, commonly known as the Yetchu dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Yunnan Province in China. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are typically hairy and have a bluish-green color. Its flowering head consists of numerous yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disc florets.

This species is adapted to cold and arid environments, showcasing the resilience of dandelions in diverse ecological conditions.
