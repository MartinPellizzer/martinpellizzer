Tournefortiopsis hirsuta

Tournefortiopsis hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy tournefortiopsis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, yellowish flower heads that resemble dandelions. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
