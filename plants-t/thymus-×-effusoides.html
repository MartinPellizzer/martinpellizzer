Thymus × effusoides

Thymus × effusoides, commonly known as woolly thyme, is a hybrid perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, gray-green foliage covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
