Thamnochortus floribundus

Thamnochortus floribundus, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
