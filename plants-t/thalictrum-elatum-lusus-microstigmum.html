Thalictrum elatum lusus microstigmum

Thalictrum elatum lusus microstigmum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are compound and arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its ornamental value comes from its graceful appearance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
