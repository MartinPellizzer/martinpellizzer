Tabernaemontana Thailandensis

Tabernaemontana thailandensis is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Thailand. Known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many toxic plants, though T. thailandensis is prized for its ornamental value. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of both scientific study and conservation concern. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of specialized horticultural circles.
