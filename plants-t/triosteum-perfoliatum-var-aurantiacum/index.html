Triosteum Perfoliatum Var. Aurantiacum

Orange trumpet vine, scientifically known as Triosteum perfoliatum var. aurantiacum, is a strikingly colorful flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. Its vibrant orange blossoms resemble trumpets, earning it its common name and making it a standout in any garden. This perennial vine thrives in partial to full sun and is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb trellises or fences. With its unique foliage and showy flowers, it adds both beauty and ecological value to landscapes. It is also a popular choice for attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
