Tephrosia angustissima var. corallicola

Tephrosia angustissima var. corallicola, commonly known as the coral-leaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or savanna habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
