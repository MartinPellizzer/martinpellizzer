Thunbergia hawtaynii

Thunbergia hawtaynii, commonly known as the purple trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and can grow as a climbing vine or a shrub. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia hawtaynii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
