Tilia cocksii

Tilia cocksii, commonly known as the Cocks' lime tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Tiliaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, particularly in Nepal and India. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer. It produces small, smooth, yellowish fruits that contain seeds.

Tilia cocksii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
