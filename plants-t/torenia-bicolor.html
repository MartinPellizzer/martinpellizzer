Torenia bicolor

Torenia bicolor, commonly known as the common torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. Torenia bicolor thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
