Thladiantha medogensis

Thladiantha medogensis, commonly known as the Yunnan goji berry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China.

The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils. Its berries are rich in antioxidants and have been used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Thladiantha medogensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for both medicinal and culinary purposes.
