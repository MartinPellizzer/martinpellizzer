Tacca ankaranensis

Tacca ankaranensis, commonly known as the Madagascar lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
