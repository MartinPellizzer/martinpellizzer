Tritonia odorata

Tritonia odorata, commonly known as the sweet violet, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant purple or blue flowers. Tritonia odorata is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
