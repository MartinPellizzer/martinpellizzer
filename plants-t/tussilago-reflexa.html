Tussilago reflexa

Tussilago reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed coltsfoot, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found near streams or in wet meadows. Its basal leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers bloom in early spring.

Tussilago reflexa is known for its medicinal properties, particularly for its expectorant and anti-inflammatory effects.
