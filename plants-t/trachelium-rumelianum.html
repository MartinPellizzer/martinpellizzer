Trachelium rumelianum

Trachelium rumelianum, commonly known as the Rumelian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Trachelium rumelianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
