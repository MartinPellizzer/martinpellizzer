Thunbergia arnhemica

Thunbergia arnhemica, commonly known as the Arnhem moonvine, is a climbing plant native to Australia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Thunbergia arnhemica prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
