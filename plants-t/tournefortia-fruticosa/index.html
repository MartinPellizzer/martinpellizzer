Tournefortia Fruticosa

Tournefortia shrub, scientifically known as Tournefortia fruticosa, is a hardy and versatile plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is commonly found in coastal areas, sandy soils, and disturbed environments due to its adaptability. This shrub is valued for its ornamental value, with small fragrant flowers and dense foliage. It also plays an important role in preventing soil erosion and supporting local wildlife. Despite its resilience, it requires proper care to thrive in different climates.
