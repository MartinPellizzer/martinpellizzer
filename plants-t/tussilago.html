Tussilago

Tussilago, commonly known as coltsfoot, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. Tussilago is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and it thrives in moist, disturbed soils.

It is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in woodland edges.
