Triodia scintillans

Triodia scintillans, commonly known as the spinifex grass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial grass that grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often forming dense, low-growing tussocks. The plant has narrow, wiry leaves and produces small, spike-like flower clusters. It is well adapted to harsh environments, with a deep root system and drought-resistant characteristics.

Triodia scintillans plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various desert species.
