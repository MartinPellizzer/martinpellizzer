Trigonella glabra subsp. uncata

Trigonella glabra subsp.

uncata, commonly known as the slender fenugreek, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows and along riverbanks.

The plant has slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves with small, hairy stipules. Its flowers are yellow and borne in clusters, followed by small, lens-shaped seeds.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
