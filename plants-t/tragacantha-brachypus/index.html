Tragacantha Brachypus

Bristlecone tragacanth, scientifically known as Tragacantha brachypus, is a rare and resilient plant found in arid and semi-arid regions. It belongs to the legume family and is characterized by its unique bristled seed pods. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments with minimal water. Its historical use in traditional medicine and as a natural gum source adds to its significance. Despite its ecological importance, it remains under-studied and threatened by habitat loss.
