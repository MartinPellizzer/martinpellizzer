Thymus bihoriensis

Thymus bihoriensis, commonly known as the Bihor thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains in Central Europe.

It is a low-growing plant with aromatic, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Thymus bihoriensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
