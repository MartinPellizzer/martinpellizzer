Tabernanthe mannii

Tabernanthe mannii, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, which are borne on long, slender peduncles.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
