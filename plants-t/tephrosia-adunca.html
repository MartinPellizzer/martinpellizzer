Tephrosia adunca

Tephrosia adunca, commonly known as African senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its yellow flowers and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It has a taproot system and can thrive in poor soils, making it a hardy species.
