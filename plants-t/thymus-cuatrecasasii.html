Thymus cuatrecasasii

Thymus cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas thyme, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula, specifically found in regions of southern Spain and northern Portugal. This perennial herb is characterized by its aromatic, small, gray-green leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
