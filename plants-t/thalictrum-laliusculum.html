Thalictrum laliusculum

Thalictrum laliusculum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces seeds in small, dry fruits that are dispersed by wind.

Thalictrum laliusculum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
