Thelymitra venosa var. cedricsmithii

Thelymitra venosa var. cedricsmithii, commonly known as the Cedric Smith's spider orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and intricate, spider-like flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. The plant produces a single, elongated spike bearing several small, delicate flowers, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded habitat.

This variety is particularly valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
