Tritoniopsis cinnamomea

Tritoniopsis cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon leaf orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive cinnamon-colored sheen. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of white and pale yellow.

Tritoniopsis cinnamomea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
