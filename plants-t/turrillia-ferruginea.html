Turrillia ferruginea

Turrillia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty turrillia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Turrillia ferruginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
