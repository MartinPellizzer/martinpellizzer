Trachelium angustifolium

Trachelium angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved trachelium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces showy, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves are arranged oppositely along the stems.

Trachelium angustifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
