Trichochaeta tenuis

Trichochaeta tenuis, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, wiry stems with small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Trichochaeta tenuis is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
