Trillidium Govanianum

Trillidium govanianum is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of India. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its habitat is typically found in rocky and well-drained soils at high altitudes. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study.
