Trichomanes holopterum var. lherminieri

Trichomanes holopterum var. lherminieri, commonly known as the Lherminier's maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family.

It is a small, delicate fern with finely divided, bright green fronds that are typically arching or drooping. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are clustered in strobila, a characteristic feature of ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate appearance and ease of care.
