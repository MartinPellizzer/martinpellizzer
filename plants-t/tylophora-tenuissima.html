Tylophora tenuissima

Tylophora tenuissima, commonly known as the slender tylophora, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its delicate, thin stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are often followed by cylindrical fruits. It is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.

Tylophora tenuissima is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
