Trichosalpinx tantilla

Trichosalpinx tantilla, commonly known as the purple orchid.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Trichosalpinx tantilla thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
