Turnera oblongifolia var. goyazensis

Turnera oblongifolia var. goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis Turnera, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It features opposite, elliptic to ovate leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical environments with well-drained soils.
