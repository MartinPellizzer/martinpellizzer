Tillandsia schlechtendalii

Tillandsia schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Schlechtendal's air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has short, fibrous roots and produces rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green or bluish-green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and can absorb moisture through its leaves.

Tillandsia schlechtendalii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
