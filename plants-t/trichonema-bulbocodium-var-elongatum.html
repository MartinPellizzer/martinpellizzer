Trichonema bulbocodium var. elongatum

Trichonema bulbocodium var. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated bulbous grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous base and slender, grass-like stems that grow upright. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Due to its unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
