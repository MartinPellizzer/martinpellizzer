Tilia taishanensis

Tilia taishanensis, commonly known as the Taishan linden, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, round nuts enclosed in a woody husk. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the genus Tilia.
