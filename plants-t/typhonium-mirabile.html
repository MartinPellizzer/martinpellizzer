Typhonium mirabile

Typhonium mirabile, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or white spadices surrounded by a showy white bract, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or near water bodies.

Its unique foliage and dramatic inflorescence make it a standout in botanical gardens and private collections.
