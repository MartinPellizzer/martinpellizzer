Taraxacum ajanense

Taraxacum ajanense, commonly known as the Mexican dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Central America and parts of South America, including Mexico and Colombia. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
