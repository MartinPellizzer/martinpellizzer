Triumfetta dubia

Triumfetta dubia, commonly known as the African starleaf, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Triumfetta dubia is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
