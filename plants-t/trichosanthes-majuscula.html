Trichosanthes majuscula

Trichosanthes majuscula, commonly known as the giant Chinese trumpet vine, is a climbing vine native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble trumpets, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant produces elongated, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
