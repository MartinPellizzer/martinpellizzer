Tricalysia parvifolia

Tricalysia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of bright orange-red flowers during the dry season. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Tricalysia parvifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
