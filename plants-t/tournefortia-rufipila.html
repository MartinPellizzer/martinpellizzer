Tournefortia rufipila

Tournefortia rufipila, commonly known as the red-haired tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a reddish or pinkish hue.
