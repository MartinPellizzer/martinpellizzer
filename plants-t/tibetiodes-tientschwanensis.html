Tibetiodes tientschwanensis

Tibetiodes tientschwanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous terrain, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
