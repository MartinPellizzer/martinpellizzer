Taeniophyllum laxum

Taeniophyllum laxum, commonly known as the slender thread-leaf.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features delicate, linear leaves that are often clustered closely together. Its flowers are small and typically white or pale green, with a distinctive lip that is broader than the other petals.

Taeniophyllum laxum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches.
