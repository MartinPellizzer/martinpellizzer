Teucrium yemense

Teucrium yemense, commonly known as Yemen tea, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Yemen.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.

The leaves and flowers are sometimes used to make a herbal tea, which is believed to have various health benefits.
