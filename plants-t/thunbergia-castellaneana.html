Thunbergia castellaneana

Thunbergia castellaneana, commonly known as the golden shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, pendant clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia castellaneana is a climbing plant that can reach heights of several meters when supported by a trellis or fence.
