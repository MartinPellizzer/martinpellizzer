Trichilia silvatica

Trichilia silvatica, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The plant features opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary or terminal panicles.
