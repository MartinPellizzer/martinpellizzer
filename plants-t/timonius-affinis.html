Timonius affinis

Timonius affinis, commonly known as the slender lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Timonius affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.

It has a short growing season and requires well-drained soil to thrive.
