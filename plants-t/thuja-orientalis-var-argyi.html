Thuja orientalis var. argyi

Thuja orientalis var. argyi, commonly known as the Chinese thuja or golden spruce, is a slow-growing evergreen conifer native to eastern China.

It features a compact, pyramidal shape with dense, scale-like foliage that turns a striking golden yellow in the winter. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by spherical, blue-gray cones. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its hardy nature and unique coloration make it a valuable addition to landscape designs.
