Trema burmanni

Trema burmanni, commonly known as the Indian nettle or small-leaf nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes many stinging plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Trema burmanni is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
