Tita

Tita, commonly known as the "tita" or "tita plant," is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the genus *Cordia* and is characterized by its attractive, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a glossy texture.

Tita is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
