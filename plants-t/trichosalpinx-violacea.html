Trichosalpinx violacea

Trichosalpinx violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid or violet orchid vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with tendrils that help it ascend supports. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Trichosalpinx violacea thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
