Turnera aurea

Turnera aurea, commonly known as golden turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
