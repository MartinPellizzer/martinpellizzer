Trigonochilum longifolium

Trigonochilum longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved trigonochilum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellow with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
