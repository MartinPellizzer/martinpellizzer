Tournefortia spigeliiflora

Tournefortia spigeliiflora, commonly known as the spiny-flowered tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. The plant produces small, clustered white or yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture with a slightly waxy coating.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
