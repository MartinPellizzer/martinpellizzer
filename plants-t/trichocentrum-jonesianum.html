Trichocentrum jonesianum

Trichocentrum jonesianum, commonly known as the Jones' orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features long, pendulous inflorescences that bear showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Trichocentrum jonesianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
