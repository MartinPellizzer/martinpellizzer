Taeniophyllum singulare

Taeniophyllum singulare, commonly known as the single-leaf spurge, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
