Thalictrum elatum lusus microphyllum

Thalictrum elatum lusus microphyllum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and often covered in a silvery down. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
