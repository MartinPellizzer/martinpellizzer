Tylostemon barensis

Tylostemon barensis, commonly known as the sandhill billygoat.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Australia, characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, which attract nectar-feeding insects. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Tylostemon barensis is often found in sandy soils and is well-suited to harsh, semi-arid environments.
