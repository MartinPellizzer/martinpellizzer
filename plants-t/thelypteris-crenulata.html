Thelypteris crenulata

Thelypteris crenulata, commonly known as the notched shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

Thelypteris crenulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
