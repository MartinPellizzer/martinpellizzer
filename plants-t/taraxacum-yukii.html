Taraxacum yukii

Taraxacum yukii, commonly known as Yukii dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassy areas. It produces a distinctive seed head that disperses its seeds via the wind.

Taraxacum yukii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
