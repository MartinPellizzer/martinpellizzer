Tetratheca thymifolia var. hebecarpum

Tetratheca thymifolia var. hebecarpum, commonly known as the narrow-fruit tetra-theca, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its aromatic, linear leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces distinctive, narrow, woody fruits that remain on the plant for an extended period. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
