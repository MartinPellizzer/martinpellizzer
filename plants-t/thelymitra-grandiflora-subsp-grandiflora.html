Thelymitra grandiflora subsp. grandiflora

Thelymitra grandiflora subsp.

grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces a cluster of grass-like leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a greenish-yellow color and a distinctive, spurred lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
