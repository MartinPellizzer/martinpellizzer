Tillandsia orbicularis

Tillandsia orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, strap-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
