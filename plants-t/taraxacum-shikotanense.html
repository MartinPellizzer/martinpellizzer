Taraxacum shikotanense

Taraxacum shikotanense, commonly known as the Shikotan dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Japanese island of Shikotan.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is typically found in moist, forested areas and thrives in cool, temperate climates. It produces a distinctive seed head that releases lightweight seeds with white pappus for wind dispersal.

Taraxacum shikotanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
