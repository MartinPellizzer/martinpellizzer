Tylecodon buchholzianus var. buchholzianus

Tylecodon buchholzianus var. buchholzianus, commonly known as the Buchholz's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
