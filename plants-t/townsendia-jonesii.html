Townsendia jonesii

Townsendia jonesii, commonly known as Jones' fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in open, sun-exposed habitats.
