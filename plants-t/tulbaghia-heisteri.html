Tulbaghia heisteri

Tulbaghia heisteri, commonly known as the Cape tulbaghia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and emits a mild, pleasant fragrance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Tulbaghia heisteri is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
