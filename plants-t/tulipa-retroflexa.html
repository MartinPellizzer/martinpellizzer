Tulipa retroflexa

Tulipa retroflexa, commonly known as the Turk's Cap Lily, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Tulipa.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its distinctive, recurved petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters and produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its bulbs are adapted for storage and are essential for its survival in its native habitat.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.
