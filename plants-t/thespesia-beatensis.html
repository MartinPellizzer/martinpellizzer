Thespesia beatensis

Thespesia beatensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and dry forests. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are large, palmate, and have a rough texture.

Thespesia beatensis is well adapted to the tropical climate and is often used in traditional Hawaiian practices.
