Tephrosia oroboides var. leiocarpa

Tephrosia oroboides var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth tephrosia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, which bloom during the dry season. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in open woodland and grassland habitats.

This species is notable for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
