Tilia Americana Subsp. Floridana

Tilia americana subsp. floridana is a unique variant of the American basswood tree native to the southeastern united states. this subspecies is known for its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in early summer. it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks. the tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting local wildlife. it represents a fascinating example of botanical diversity within the tilia genus.
