Tetraplasandra gymnocarpa var. megalocarpa

Tetraplasandra gymnocarpa var. megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, fibrous fruits. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found in mangrove ecosystems.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
