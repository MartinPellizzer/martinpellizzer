Tigridia estelae

Tigridia estelae, commonly known as the Estela's tiger flower, is a member of the Iridaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with striking, tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces underground bulbs for storage and propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
