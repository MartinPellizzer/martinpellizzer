Taeniophyllum compactum

Taeniophyllum compactum, commonly known as the compact tailed orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a short, thick stem with a cluster of fleshy, ovate leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters at the base of the stem.

It is valued in horticulture for its compact size and ornamental appeal.
