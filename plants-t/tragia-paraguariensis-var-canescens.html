Tragia paraguariensis var. canescens

Tragia paraguariensis var. canescens, commonly known as the Paraguayan spurge, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Paraguay and Brazil.

Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in complex structures called cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
