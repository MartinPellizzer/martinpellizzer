Thelymitra aggericola

Thelymitra aggericola, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or inland regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

Thelymitra aggericola is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in heathlands and open forests.
