Tilia × europaea f. pendula

Tilia × europaea f. pendula, commonly known as the weeping lime, is a cultivar of the European lime tree characterized by its distinctive cascading branches.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and parks.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is often used as a decorative tree in landscaping.
