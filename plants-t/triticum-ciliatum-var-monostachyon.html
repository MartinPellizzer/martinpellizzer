Triticum ciliatum var. monostachyon

Triticum ciliatum var. monostachyon, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with fine hairs along the edges. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in restoration ecology projects.
