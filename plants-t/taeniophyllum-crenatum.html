Taeniophyllum crenatum

Taeniophyllum crenatum, commonly known as the crested twayblade, is a small, evergreen herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in the summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Taeniophyllum crenatum is known for its unique crested appearance and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
