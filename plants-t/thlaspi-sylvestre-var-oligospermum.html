Thlaspi sylvestre var. oligospermum

Thlaspi sylvestre var. oligospermum, commonly known as the lesser bladderwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, herbaceous growth habit and features basal leaves that are often rosette-shaped. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It is commonly found in moist, calcareous soils in woodland areas and along stream banks.

This species is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
