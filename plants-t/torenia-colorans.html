Torenia colorans

Torenia colorans, commonly known as the whirling dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to violet, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and branching.

Torenia colorans thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance blooms.
