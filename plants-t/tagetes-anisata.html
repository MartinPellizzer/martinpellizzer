Tagetes anisata

Tagetes anisata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
