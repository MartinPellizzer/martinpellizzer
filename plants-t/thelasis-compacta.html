Thelasis compacta

Thelasis compacta, commonly known as the compact thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny conditions. The plant features a spiny, bushy stem with small, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Thelasis compacta is known for its hardy nature and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its distinctive appearance.
