Tithymalopsis apocynifolia

Tithymalopsis apocynifolia, commonly known as the African tithymal, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely known outside its native region.
