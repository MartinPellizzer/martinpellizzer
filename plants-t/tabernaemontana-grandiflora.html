Tabernaemontana grandiflora

Tabernaemontana grandiflora, commonly known as the flame tree or crown of thorns, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often appearing in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains toxic seeds, making it important to handle with care.
