Thelypteris guentheri

Thelypteris guentheri, commonly known as Guenther's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in structures called sori. Thelypteris guentheri is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important component of its native habitat.
