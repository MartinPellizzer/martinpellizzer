Taraxacum pseudotianschanicum

Taraxacum pseudotianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Tianshan mountain range in Central Asia and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant produces a distinctive seed head that disperses seeds via the wind, a common adaptation in the Asteraceae family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
