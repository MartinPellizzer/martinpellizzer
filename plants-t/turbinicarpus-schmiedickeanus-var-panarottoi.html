Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. panarottoi

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus var. panarottoi, commonly known as the Panarotto's barrel cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Mexico.

It features a cylindrical, ribbed stem that is typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique shape and striking floral display make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
