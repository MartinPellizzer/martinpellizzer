Tournefortia bicolor var. laevigata

Tournefortia bicolor var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved tournefortia, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It features simple, opposite leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a lanceolate shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often with white or pale yellow petals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal or wetland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in tropical regions.
