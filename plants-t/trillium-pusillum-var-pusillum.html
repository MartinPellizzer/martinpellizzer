Trillium pusillum var. pusillum

Trillium pusillum var. pusillum, commonly known as the small white trillium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features three narrow, lance-shaped leaves and three white petals that are typically shorter than the sepals. The plant produces a single, upright stem with a showy white flower that has three sepals and three petals. It thrives in shaded, moist woodland environments and is often found in rich, acidic soils.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is a popular plant among native gardeners and conservationists.
