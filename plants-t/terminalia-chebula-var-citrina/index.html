Terminalia Chebula Var. Citrina

Chebulic myrobalan, scientifically known as Terminalia chebula var. citrina, is a remarkable plant native to the Indian subcontinent. It belongs to the Combretaceae family and is widely recognized for its medicinal properties. The fruit of this plant has been used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for centuries. Its bark and fruit contain a variety of bioactive compounds, including tannins and alkaloids. Due to its health benefits, it is highly valued in both herbal remedies and modern pharmaceutical research.
