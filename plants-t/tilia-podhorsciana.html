Tilia podhorsciana

Tilia podhorsciana, commonly known as the Podhorscian linden, is a species of flowering plant in the Tiliaceae family.

It is native to parts of Eastern Europe and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits called capsules, which contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Tilia podhorsciana is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
