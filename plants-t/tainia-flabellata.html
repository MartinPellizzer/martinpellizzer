Tainia flabellata

Tainia flabellata, commonly known as the giant fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, deeply divided fronds that can reach lengths of up to three meters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Tainia flabellata is an ornamental plant valued for its dramatic, palm-like appearance and its ability to create a lush, tropical ambiance in gardens.
