Tradescantia exaltata

Tradescantia exaltata, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or purple markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Tradescantia exaltata is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and easy care requirements.
