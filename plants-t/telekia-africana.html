Telekia africana

Telekia africana, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Telekia africana is known for its medicinal properties and is used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
