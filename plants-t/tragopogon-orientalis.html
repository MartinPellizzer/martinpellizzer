Tragopogon orientalis

Tragopogon orientalis, commonly known as the oriental goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, silvery-green leaves with hairy surfaces. Its showy, yellow-orange inflorescence consists of ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Tragopogon orientalis is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
