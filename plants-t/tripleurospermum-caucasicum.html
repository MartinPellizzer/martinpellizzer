Tripleurospermum caucasicum

Tripleurospermum caucasicum, commonly known as Caucasian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to well-drained soils.
