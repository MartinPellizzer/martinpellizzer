Tripleurospermum elongatum

Tripleurospermum elongatum, commonly known as the slender ray floret.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic properties. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
