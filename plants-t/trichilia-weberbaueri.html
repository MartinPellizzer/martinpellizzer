Trichilia weberbaueri

Trichilia weberbaueri, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a tropical tree belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. Its growth habit and botanical features make it an important species in ecological and economic contexts within its native range.
