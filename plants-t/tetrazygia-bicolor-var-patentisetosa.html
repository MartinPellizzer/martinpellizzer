Tetrazygia bicolor var. patentisetosa

Tetrazygia bicolor var. patentisetosa, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaves, which are opposite, elliptic, and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a drupe that turns from green to red as it matures.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
