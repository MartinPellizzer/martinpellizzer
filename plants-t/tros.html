Tros

Tros, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and ragworts. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disk, surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. Tros typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
