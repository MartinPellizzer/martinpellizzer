Tropaeolum pubescens var. rotundilobum

Tropaeolum pubescens var. rotundilobum, commonly known as the round-lobed nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, pubescent leaves and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and edible parts.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening or as a ground cover.
