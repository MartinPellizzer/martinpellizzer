Triorchis trilobus

Triorchis trilobus, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial shade. The plant features a slender, leafless stem that bears a single, showy flower with three distinct lobes. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct lip that is often darker.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in rich, loamy soils.
