Trema acuminatissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Montiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pink flowers. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. It typically grows close to the ground, often in sandy or rocky soils.
