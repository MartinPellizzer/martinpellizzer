Tillandsia apoloensis

Tillandsia apoloensis, commonly known as the APOLOENSISS tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that trap moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its flowers are usually small and colorful, often in shades of red, yellow, or orange.

Tillandsia apoloensis is appreciated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
