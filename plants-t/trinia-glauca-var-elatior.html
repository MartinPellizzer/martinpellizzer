Trinia glauca var. elatior

Trinia glauca var. elatior, commonly known as the blue trinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue-violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
