Tovomita clusiiflora

Tovomita clusiiflora, commonly known as the African starfruit, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow when ripe, often containing several seeds.
