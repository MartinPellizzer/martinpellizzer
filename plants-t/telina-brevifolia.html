Telina brevifolia

Telina brevifolia, commonly known as the shortleaf telina, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Telina brevifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
