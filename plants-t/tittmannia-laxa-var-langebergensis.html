Tittmannia laxa var. langebergensis

Tittmannia laxa var. langebergensis, commonly known as the Langeberg daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky and sandy soils, thriving in full sun conditions. It is often found in the Western Cape region, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
