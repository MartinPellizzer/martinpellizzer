Thrincia zeylanica

Thrincia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon thornapple, is a flowering plant native to southern Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
