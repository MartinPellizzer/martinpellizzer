Trachelospermum cathayanum

Trachelospermum cathayanum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.

Trachelospermum cathayanum is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
