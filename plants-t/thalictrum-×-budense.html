Thalictrum × budense

Thalictrum × budense, commonly known as the hybrid meadow rue, is a perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of delicate, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers are actinomorphic, with numerous stamens and a superior ovary.

Thalictrum × budense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
