Tillandsia steiropoda

Tillandsia steiropoda, commonly known as the silver star tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces striking, star-shaped inflorescences with showy, violet or purple flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
