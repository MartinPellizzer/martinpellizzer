Thymelaea puberula

Thymelaea puberula, commonly known as the silky thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the winter months. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
