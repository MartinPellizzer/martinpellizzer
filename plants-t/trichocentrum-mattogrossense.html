Trichocentrum mattogrossense

Trichocentrum mattogrossense, commonly known as the Mattogrossense Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Trichocentrum mattogrossense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
