Tectaria polymorpha var. macrocarpa

Tectaria polymorpha var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a species of fern characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with a creeping rhizome and large, bipinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its fronds are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside, with a distinctive, leathery texture.

This fern is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices.
