Tephrosia aequilata subsp. australis

Tephrosia aequilata subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, sandy soils.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small leaflets.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
