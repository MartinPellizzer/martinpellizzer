Tachigali davidsei

Tachigali davidsei, commonly known as the David's tachigali, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, also known as the Fabaceae or bean family, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
