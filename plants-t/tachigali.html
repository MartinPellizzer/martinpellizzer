Tachigali

Tachigali, commonly known as the "yellowwood" or "tachigali tree," is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, with compound leaves and pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Tachigali species are often found in moist, shaded environments and are valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
