Tapiphyllum kaessneri

Tapiphyllum kaessneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Tapiphyllum kaessneri is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
