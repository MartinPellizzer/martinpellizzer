Thelymitra petrophila

Thelymitra petrophila, commonly known as the granite orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale green to brownish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often on granite outcrops. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments.
