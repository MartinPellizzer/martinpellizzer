Tournefortia paniculata var. spigeliiflora

Tournefortia paniculata var. spigeliiflora, commonly known as the Spigeliiflora tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, often evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal or wetland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms and foliage.
