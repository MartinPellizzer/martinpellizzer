Torresia redolens

Torresia redolens, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Torresia redolens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in horticulture for its unique appearance and scent.
