Thyrsanthus laurifolius

Thyrsanthus laurifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved thyrsin, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest environments. The plant features elliptical, leathery leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, with yellow stamens and a distinctive purple-pink style.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
