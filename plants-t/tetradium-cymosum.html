Tetradium cymosum

Tetradium cymosum, commonly known as the Chinese sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky, or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in cymes, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough, sandpapery texture.

Tetradium cymosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
