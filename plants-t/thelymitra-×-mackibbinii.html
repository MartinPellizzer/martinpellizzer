Thelymitra × mackibbinii

Thelymitra × mackibbinii, commonly known as the Mackibbin's sun-orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow and green floral structure. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose spike and bloom during the spring and early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
