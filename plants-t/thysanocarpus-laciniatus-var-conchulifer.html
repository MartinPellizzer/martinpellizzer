Thysanocarpus laciniatus var. conchulifer

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely lobed leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by unique, winged fruits that aid in wind dispersal.
