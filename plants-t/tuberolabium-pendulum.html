Tuberolabium pendulum

Tuberolabium pendulum, commonly known as the hanging lip orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, lip-shaped flowers that hang from the stems. The plant produces showy, often brightly colored flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Tuberolabium pendulum typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
