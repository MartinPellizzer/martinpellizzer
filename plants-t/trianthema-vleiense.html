Trianthema vleiense

Trianthema vleiense, commonly known as the vlei bush or vlei thorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats, often found in the fynbos biome. The plant has small, pale green leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant.

Trianthema vleiense is also known for its sharp thorns, which provide protection from herbivores.
