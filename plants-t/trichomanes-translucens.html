Trichomanes translucens

Trichomanes translucens, commonly known as the translucent maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, with a distinctive, finely divided structure.

Trichomanes translucens is noted for its ability to absorb moisture from the air, making it well adapted to epiphytic growth on tree trunks or rocks.
