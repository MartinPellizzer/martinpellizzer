Tectaria degeneri

Tectaria degeneri, commonly known as the Degener's tectaria, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and the presence of fertile fronds with sori arranged in distinct rows. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually lanceolate to ovate in shape, with a distinctive texture and coloration.

Tectaria degeneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
