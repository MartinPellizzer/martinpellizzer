Tridentea pusilla

Tridentea pusilla, commonly known as the small trident leaf, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being narrowly elliptic and slightly serrated. Its small, white flowers bloom in late spring, adding a delicate touch to its foliage.

Tridentea pusilla is often found in clusters and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native plant gardens.
