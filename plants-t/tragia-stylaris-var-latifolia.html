Tragia stylaris var. latifolia

Tragia stylaris var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf tragia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in partial to full sun. Its growth habit is typically prostrate or spreading, making it suitable for ground cover in certain environments.

Tragia stylaris var. latifolia is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
