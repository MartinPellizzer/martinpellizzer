Tropaeolum reicheanum

Tropaeolum reicheanum, commonly known as the Andes jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces small, bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Tropaeolum reicheanum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and easy growth.
