Trichomanes falcatum

Trichomanes falcatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its creeping rhizome. Its fronds are often dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture in its humid habitats.

Trichomanes falcatum is often found growing in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
