Turnera frutescens var. latifolia

Turnera frutescens var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf turnera, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central and South America.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. The plant produces yellow flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
