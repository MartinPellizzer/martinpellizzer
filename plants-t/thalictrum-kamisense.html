Thalictrum kamisense

Thalictrum kamisense, commonly known as the Japanese meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Thalictrum kamisense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
