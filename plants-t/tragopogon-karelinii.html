Tragopogon karelinii

Tragopogon karelinii, commonly known as Karelin's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features a slender, upright stem with alternating leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are yellow with purple markings, and they bloom in late summer.

Tragopogon karelinii is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
