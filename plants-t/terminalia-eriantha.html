Terminalia eriantha

Terminalia eriantha, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring, followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. Its fruits are an important food source for various bird and animal species.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
