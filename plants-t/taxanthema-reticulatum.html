Taxanthema reticulatum

Taxanthema reticulatum, commonly known as the netleaf taxanthema, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers and a distinctive, net-like pattern on its leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability.
