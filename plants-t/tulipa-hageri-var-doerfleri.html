Tulipa hageri var. doerfleri

Tulipa hageri var. doerfleri, commonly known as the Hageri doerfleri tulip, is a rare and distinctive cultivar native to Turkey.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that are typically pink to red in color, with a striking, often contrasting center. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
