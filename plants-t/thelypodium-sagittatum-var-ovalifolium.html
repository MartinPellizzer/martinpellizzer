Thelypodium sagittatum var. ovalifolium

Thelypodium sagittatum var. ovalifolium, commonly known as the arrowhead speedwell, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
