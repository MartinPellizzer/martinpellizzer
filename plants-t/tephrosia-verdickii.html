Tephrosia verdickii

Tephrosia verdickii, commonly known as African blue pea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including savannas and woodland areas.

Tephrosia verdickii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
