Tephrosia purpurea subsp. altissima

Tephrosia purpurea subsp.

altissima, commonly known as purple tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy purple or pink flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in full sun, making it a common species in savanna and woodland habitats.
