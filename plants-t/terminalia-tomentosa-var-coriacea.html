Terminalia tomentosa var. coriacea

Terminalia tomentosa var. coriacea, commonly known as the rough-skin terminalia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, rough, bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
