Thymus striatus subsp. holosericeus

Thymus striatus subsp.

holosericeus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces clusters of small, pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils contain thymol, which gives it a strong, minty aroma and medicinal properties.
