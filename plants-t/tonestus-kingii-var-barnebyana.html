Tonestus kingii var. barnebyana

Tonestus kingii var. barnebyana, commonly known as the Barneby's tonestus, is a rare and ornamental plant native to specific regions of South America.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming in the spring.
