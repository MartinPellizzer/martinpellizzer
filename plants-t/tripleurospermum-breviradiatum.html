Tripleurospermum breviradiatum

Tripleurospermum breviradiatum, commonly known as the narrow-rayed rayflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are typically linear to oblanceolate in shape. Its flowering heads consist of yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets.

It thrives in dry, grassy habitats and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
