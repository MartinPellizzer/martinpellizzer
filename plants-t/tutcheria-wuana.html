Tutcheria wuana

Tutcheria wuana, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to flourish.

Tutcheria wuana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
