Trigonochilum diodon

Trigonochilum diodon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and intricate floral structure. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The flowers are typically small and clustered, with a unique coloration that aids in pollination.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
