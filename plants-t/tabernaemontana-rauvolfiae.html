Tabernaemontana rauvolfiae

Tabernaemontana rauvolfiae, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warm seasons. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Tabernaemontana rauvolfiae is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its pleasant fragrance and aesthetic value.
