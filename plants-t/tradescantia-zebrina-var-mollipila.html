Tradescantia zebrina var. mollipila

Tradescantia zebrina var. mollipila, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive zebra-like pattern of deep green and silver stripes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
