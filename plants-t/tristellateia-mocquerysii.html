Tristellateia mocquerysii

Tristellateia mocquerysii, commonly known as the desert star cactus, is a unique cactus species native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped spines and a compact, globular body. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the soil surface. It produces yellow flowers during the rainy season, adding a splash of color to its harsh environment.

Due to its specialized adaptations, it is well-suited for survival in extreme desert conditions.
