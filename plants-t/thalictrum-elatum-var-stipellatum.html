Thalictrum elatum var. stipellatum

Thalictrum elatum var. stipellatum, commonly known as the showy avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland and streamside habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
