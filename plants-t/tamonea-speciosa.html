Tamonea speciosa

Tamonea speciosa, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Aroid family, which includes plants like taro and philodendrons. The plant is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that resemble stars, surrounded by a spathe. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.
