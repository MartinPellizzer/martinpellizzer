Tragopogon altaicus

Tragopogon altaicus, commonly known as the Siberian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous yellow ray florets and a central disk of tubular flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes.

Tragopogon altaicus is known for its ability to adapt to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
