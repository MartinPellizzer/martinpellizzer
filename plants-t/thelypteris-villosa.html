Thelypteris villosa

Thelypteris villosa, commonly known as the silky water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely pubescent fronds that give it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it plays a role in maintaining soil stability in its habitat.

Thelypteris villosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in wetland ecosystems.
