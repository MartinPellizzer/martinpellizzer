Thamnochortus aemulus

Thamnochortus aemulus, commonly known as the Cape goldbos, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
