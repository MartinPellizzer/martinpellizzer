Thunbergia glaucina

Thunbergia glaucina, commonly known as the blue thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant is a climbing vine that often grows along fences or trellises. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia glaucina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
