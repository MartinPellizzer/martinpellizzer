Tibouchina longifolia var. simulans

Tibouchina longifolia var. simulans, commonly known as the Brazilian violet or purple tibouchina, is a shrub native to South America.

It features showy, violet-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
