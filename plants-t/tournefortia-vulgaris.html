Tournefortia vulgaris

Tournefortia vulgaris, commonly known as the common tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in terminal panicles. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The botanical characteristics of Tournefortia vulgaris make it a hardy and ornamental plant suitable for coastal and garden landscapes.
