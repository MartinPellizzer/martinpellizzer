Thomasia sarotes

Thomasia sarotes, commonly known as the coastal thomasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Thomasia sarotes is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
