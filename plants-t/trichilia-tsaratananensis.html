Trichilia tsaratananensis

Trichilia tsaratananensis, commonly known as the Madagascar tallow tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in moist, lowland forests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
