Trophaeum kuntzeanum

Trophaeum kuntzeanum, commonly known as the Kuntze's rock cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant typically found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. It is often characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
