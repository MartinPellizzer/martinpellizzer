Turnera rupestris

Turnera rupestris, commonly known as the rock turnera, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming from spring to summer.

Turnera rupestris is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
