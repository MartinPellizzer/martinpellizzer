Thalictrum angustifolium subsp. simplex

Thalictrum angustifolium subsp.

simplex, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of North America. It features slender, upright stems that bear compound, fern-like leaves with a delicate, lacey appearance.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in compound umbels, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage and floral structure contribute to its ornamental value in garden settings.
