Tripogon yunnanensis

Tripogon yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan tripogon, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It is often found in mountainous regions with moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
