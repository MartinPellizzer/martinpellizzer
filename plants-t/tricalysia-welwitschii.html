Tricalysia welwitschii

Tricalysia welwitschii, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, smooth bark and dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by distinctive, winged fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
