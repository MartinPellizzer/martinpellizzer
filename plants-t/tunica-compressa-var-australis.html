Tunica compressa var. australis

Tunica compressa var. australis, commonly known as the Southern Tunica, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and dense foliage, which helps it survive in harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky outcrops.

Due to its resilience and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
