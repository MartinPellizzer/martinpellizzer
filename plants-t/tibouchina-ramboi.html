Tibouchina ramboi

Tibouchina ramboi, commonly known as the Brazilian flame flower, is a striking shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded to partially sunny conditions.

Tibouchina ramboi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
