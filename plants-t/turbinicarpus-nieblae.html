Turbinicarpus nieblae

Turbinicarpus nieblae, commonly known as the Nieblae's Turbinaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, rounded stems covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne on the upper parts of the stems. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to store water in its succulent stems.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
