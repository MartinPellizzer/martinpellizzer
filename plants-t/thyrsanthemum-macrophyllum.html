Thyrsanthemum macrophyllum

Thyrsanthemum macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant thyrsanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and public spaces.
