Thesium × vandasii

Thesium × vandasii, commonly known as the hybrid thesium, is a rare and intriguing plant that results from the cross between Thesium and Vandopsis species.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring showy, often fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant exhibits a herbaceous growth habit, with slender, branching stems that often bear clusters of small, leaf-like structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
