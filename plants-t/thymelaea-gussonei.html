Thymelaea gussonei

Thymelaea gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a slightly hairy texture.
