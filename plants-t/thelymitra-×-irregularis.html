Thelymitra × irregularis

Thelymitra × irregularis, commonly known as the irregular fairy lantern, is a hybrid orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the late summer to autumn months, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Thelymitra × irregularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and showy blooms.
