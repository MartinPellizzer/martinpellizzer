Thalictrum sinense

Thalictrum sinense, commonly known as Chinese meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in temperate regions with cool, humid climates.

Thalictrum sinense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ecological benefits to pollinators.
