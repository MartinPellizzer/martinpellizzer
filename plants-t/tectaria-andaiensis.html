Tectaria andaiensis

Tectaria andaiensis, commonly known as the Andean shield fern, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates and is adapted to the humid, misty conditions of its habitat.
