Tillandsia capillaris f. incana

Tillandsia capillaris f. incana, commonly known as the silver-tipped air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its long, slender leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-gray pubescence. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires minimal soil, as it absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves.

This variety is popular among succulent and air plant enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
