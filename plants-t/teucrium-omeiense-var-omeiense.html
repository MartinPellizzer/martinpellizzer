Teucrium omeiense var. omeiense

Teucrium omeiense var. omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense Germander, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are usually purple or blue, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
