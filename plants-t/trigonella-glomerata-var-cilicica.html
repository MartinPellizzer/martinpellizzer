Trigonella glomerata var. cilicica

Trigonella glomerata var. cilicica, commonly known as Cilician fenugreek, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems and trifoliate leaves with small, heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by hairy, triangular seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

This variety is closely related to other Trigonella species and is valued for its potential medicinal and nutritional properties.
