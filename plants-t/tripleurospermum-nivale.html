Tripleurospermum nivale

Tripleurospermum nivale, commonly known as snowdrop, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are usually white or pale yellow.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
