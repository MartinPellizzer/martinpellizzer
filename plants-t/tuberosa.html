Tuberosa

Tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its large, fragrant white flowers that bloom at night. Its growth habits include a dormant period during the winter, followed by rapid growth and flowering in the warmer months.
