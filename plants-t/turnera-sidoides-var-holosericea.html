Turnera sidoides var. holosericea

Turnera sidoides var. holosericea, commonly known as the velvetleaf turnera, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and as a houseplant.
