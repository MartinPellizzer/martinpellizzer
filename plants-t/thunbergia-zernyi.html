Thunbergia zernyi

Thunbergia zernyi, commonly known as the Zernyi spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia zernyi is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds.
