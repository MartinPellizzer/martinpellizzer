Thymelaea tarton-raira subsp. linnaei

Thymelaea tarton-raira subsp.

linnaei, commonly known as the narrowleaf thymelaea, is a shrub native to the Cape Region of South Africa. It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat. It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

The subspecies linnaei is distinguished by its more elongated leaves compared to other subspecies of Thymelaea.
