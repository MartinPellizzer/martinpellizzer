Turibana sachalinensis

Turibana sachalinensis, commonly known as the Sakhalin turban snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Aegidionidae and is native to the Russian Far East, particularly Sakhalin Island. The snail has a distinctive elongated, spiral-shaped shell that is typically brown or reddish in color. Due to habitat loss and other threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
