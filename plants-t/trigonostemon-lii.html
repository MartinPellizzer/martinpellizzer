Trigonostemon lii

Trigonostemon lii, commonly known as the Chinese ironwood tree, is a species native to China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which appear in clusters during the spring. Its hard, dense wood is highly valued for its durability and strength.

Trigonostemon lii is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to urban environments.
