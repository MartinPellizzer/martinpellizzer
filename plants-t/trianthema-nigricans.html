Trianthema nigricans

Trianthema nigricans, commonly known as blackleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Trianthema nigricans is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
