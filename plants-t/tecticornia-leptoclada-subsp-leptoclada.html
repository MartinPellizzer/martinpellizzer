Tecticornia leptoclada subsp. leptoclada

Tecticornia leptoclada subsp.

leptoclada, commonly known as the slender tecticornia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, erect stems and dense clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers.

The plant has adapted to harsh environments through its succulent leaves and deep root system, which help it conserve water. It produces small, leathery fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.
