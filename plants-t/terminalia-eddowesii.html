Terminalia eddowesii

Terminalia eddowesii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite leaves. Terminalia eddowesii is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
