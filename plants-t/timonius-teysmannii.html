Timonius teysmannii

Timonius teysmannii, commonly known as the Teymann's timonius, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the tropical regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, delicate orchid-like flowers in shades of white and green. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a compact rosette.

Timonius teysmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific climate requirements.
