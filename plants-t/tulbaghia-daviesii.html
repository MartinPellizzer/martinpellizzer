Tulbaghia daviesii

Tulbaghia daviesii, commonly known as the mountain tulbaghia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale blue flowers. The plant is often found in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and mild, garlic-like scent.
