Trichosanthes ambrozii

Trichosanthes ambrozii, commonly known as the Ambrozii bitter melon, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, pumpkins, and gourds. The plant produces striking yellow flowers and elongated, warty fruits that are typically harvested when immature. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges, and the stems are covered in fine, soft hairs.
