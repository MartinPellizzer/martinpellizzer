Tephrocactus sphaericus var. unguispinus

Tephrocactus sphaericus var. unguispinus, commonly known as the red spiny ball cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of spines, which provide protection against herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to dry climates.
