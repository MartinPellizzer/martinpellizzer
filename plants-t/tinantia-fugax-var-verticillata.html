Tinantia fugax var. verticillata

Tinantia fugax var. verticillata, commonly known as the zigzagged flame flower, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds.
