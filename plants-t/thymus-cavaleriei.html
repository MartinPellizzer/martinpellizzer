Thymus cavaleriei

Thymus cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, small, grey-green leaves. The plant produces pink to violet flowers in early summer, which are borne in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
