Trigonella monspeliaca f. moabitica

Trigonella monspeliaca f. moabitica, commonly known as the Moabitic fenugreek, is a botanical variant of the fenugreek species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. This subspecies is native to arid and semi-arid regions, particularly in the Middle East and North Africa. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
