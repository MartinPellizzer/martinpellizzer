Thalictrum debile

Thalictrum debile, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, woodland environments and features delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Thalictrum debile is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
