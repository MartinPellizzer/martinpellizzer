Telanthera polygonoides var. diffusa

Telanthera polygonoides var. diffusa, commonly known as the variable telanthera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The flowers are typically borne on slender stems and bloom during the wet season. This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
