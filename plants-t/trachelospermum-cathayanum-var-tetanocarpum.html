Trachelospermum cathayanum var. tetanocarpum

Trachelospermum cathayanum var. tetanocarpum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces fragrant, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic foliage.
