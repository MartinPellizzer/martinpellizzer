Tunilla ianthinantha

Tunilla ianthinantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered tunilla, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its vibrant purple petals and a distinctive, elongated floral structure. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
