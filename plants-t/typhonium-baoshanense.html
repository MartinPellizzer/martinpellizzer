Typhonium baoshanense

Typhonium baoshanense, commonly known as the Baoshan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
