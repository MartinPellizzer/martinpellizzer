Thladiantha nudiflora var. macrocarpa

Thladiantha nudiflora var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant thladiantha, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, showy yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its smooth, thornless stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
