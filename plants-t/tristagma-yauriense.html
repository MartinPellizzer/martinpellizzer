Tristagma yauriense

Tristagma yauriense, commonly known as the Yauri grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to the high Andes of Peru. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing adaptations to cold and high-altitude conditions. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small flowers.

Tristagma yauriense plays a role in stabilizing soil in its harsh habitat and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
