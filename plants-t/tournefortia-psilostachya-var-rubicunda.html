Tournefortia psilostachya var. rubicunda

Tournefortia psilostachya var. rubicunda, commonly known as the red-flowered tournefortia, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are often consumed by birds and small mammals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or near brackish water.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm regions.
