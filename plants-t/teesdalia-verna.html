Teesdalia verna

Teesdalia verna, commonly known as spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in woodland and rocky habitats. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Teesdalia verna is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting early pollinators.
