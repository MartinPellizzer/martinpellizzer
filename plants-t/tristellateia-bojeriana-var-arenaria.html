Tristellateia bojeriana var. arenaria

Tristellateia bojeriana var. arenaria, commonly known as the sand tristellateia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has adapted to survive in dry conditions with its succulent leaves and deep root system. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils, thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
