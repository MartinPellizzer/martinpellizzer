Thalictrum morisonii subsp. mediterraneum

Thalictrum morisonii subsp.

mediterraneum, commonly known as the Mediterranean meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in woodland edges and meadows.
