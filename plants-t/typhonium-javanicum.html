Typhonium javanicum

Typhonium javanicum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, yellowish-green spadices surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, making it both a ornamental and potentially invasive species.
