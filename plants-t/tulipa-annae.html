Tulipa annae

Tulipa annae, commonly known as the Annae tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 15 to 20 centimeters, with narrow, strap-like leaves. It belongs to the genus Tulipa in the family Liliaceae.

The Annae tulip is prized for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
