Thunbergia amoena

Thunbergia amoena, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces showy, fragrant blossoms that attract pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
