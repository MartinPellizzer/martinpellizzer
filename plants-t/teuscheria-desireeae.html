Teuscheria desireeae

Teuscheria desireeae, commonly known as the Dusky Desert Cactus, is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, adding a striking visual contrast to its otherwise muted appearance.
