Terminalia kanchii

Terminalia kanchii, commonly known as the Kanchi Terminalia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

The species is also noted for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic applications.
