Tulbaghia pretoriensis

Tulbaghia pretoriensis, commonly known as the Pretoria lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale blue flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch to garden landscapes.
