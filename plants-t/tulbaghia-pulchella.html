Tulbaghia pulchella

Tulbaghia pulchella, commonly known as the Cape tulbaghia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant in suitable climates.
