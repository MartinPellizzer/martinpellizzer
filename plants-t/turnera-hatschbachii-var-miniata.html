Turnera hatschbachii var. miniata

Turnera hatschbachii var. miniata, commonly known as the miniature turnera, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
