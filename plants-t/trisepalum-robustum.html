Trisepalum robustum

Trisepalum robustum, commonly known as the robustum trisepalum, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Trisepalum robustum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often found in forested areas.
