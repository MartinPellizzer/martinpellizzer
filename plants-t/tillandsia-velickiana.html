Tillandsia velickiana

Tillandsia velickiana, commonly known as the Velicki's air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
