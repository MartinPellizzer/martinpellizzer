Tillandsia lindenii var. splendida

Tillandsia lindenii var. splendida, commonly known as the Splendid Air Plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features vibrant, strap-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially under bright light. It produces showy, bell-shaped inflorescences with striking orange or red flowers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
