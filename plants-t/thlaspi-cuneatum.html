Thlaspi cuneatum

Thlaspi cuneatum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It is an annual or biennial herb that produces small, round pods containing several seeds.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
