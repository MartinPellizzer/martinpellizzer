Tectaria kingii

Tectaria kingii, commonly known as the King's tectaria, is a fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with fertile fronds that produce sporangia on the undersides of their leaves. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and have a distinctive, often serrated margin.

Tectaria kingii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
