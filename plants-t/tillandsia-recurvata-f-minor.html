Tillandsia recurvata f. minor

Tillandsia recurvata f. minor, commonly known as the miniature tillandsia, is a bromeliad species native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, with a compact growth habit. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
