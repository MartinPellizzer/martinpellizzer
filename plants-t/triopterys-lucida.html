Triopterys lucida

Triopterys lucida, commonly known as the shining triopterys, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
