Tectaria endresii

Tectaria endresii, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. Its rhizome is thick and creeping, supporting the growth of the fronds.

Tectaria endresii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
