Toddalia tonkinensis

Toddalia tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its dense, aromatic wood that is highly valued in traditional craftsmanship. Due to overharvesting, it is now classified as critically endangered in its native range.
