Thesium humile f. maritima

Thesium humile f. maritima, commonly known as the coastal thesium, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

The plant has succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in its often-salty, well-drained habitat.
