Trigonella binaloudensis

Trigonella binaloudensis, commonly known as Binaloud fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and medicinal studies.
