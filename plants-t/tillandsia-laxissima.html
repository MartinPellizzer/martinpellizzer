Tillandsia laxissima

Tillandsia laxissima, commonly known as the slender air plant, is a species of epiphytic bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and does not require soil for growth. It is well adapted to hanging or epiphytic growth on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
