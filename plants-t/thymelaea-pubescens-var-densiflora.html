Thymelaea pubescens var. densiflora

Thymelaea pubescens var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually small, hairy, and arranged in opposite pairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
