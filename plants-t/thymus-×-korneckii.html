Thymus × korneckii

Thymus × korneckii, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus × korneckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
