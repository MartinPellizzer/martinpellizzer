Tephrosia sessiliflora

Tephrosia sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-leaved tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the warm season.

Tephrosia sessiliflora is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often used in traditional medicine.
