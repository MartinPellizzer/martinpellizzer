Tomiephyllum

Tomiephyllum, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that bear flowers in a wide range of colors. Tomiephyllum is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
