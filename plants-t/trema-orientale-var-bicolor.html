Trema orientale var. bicolor

Trema orientale var. bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored trema, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bi-colored leaves, typically featuring a green upper surface and a white or pale underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, edible fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and climbing habit make it a popular choice for trellises and green walls.
