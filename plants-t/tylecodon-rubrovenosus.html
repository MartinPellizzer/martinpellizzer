Tylecodon rubrovenosus

Tylecodon rubrovenosus, commonly known as the red-veined ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
