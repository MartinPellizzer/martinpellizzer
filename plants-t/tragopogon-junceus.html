Tragopogon junceus

Tragopogon junceus, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces a unique, hollow seed stem that is often mistaken for a flower, and it is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa. Tragopogon junceus typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.
