Thysanotus nudicaulis

Thysanotus nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender thread-leaf lily, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

Thysanotus nudicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
