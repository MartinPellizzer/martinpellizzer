Thunbergia hainanensis

Thunbergia hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan trumpetvine, is a flowering plant native to China's Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, featuring opposite leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, exotic blooms.
