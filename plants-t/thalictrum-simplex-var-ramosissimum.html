Thalictrum simplex var. ramosissimum

Thalictrum simplex var. ramosissimum, commonly known as the clustered meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value lies in its graceful foliage and subtle floral display.
