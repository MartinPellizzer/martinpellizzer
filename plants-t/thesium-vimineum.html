Thesium vimineum

Thesium vimineum, commonly known as the silverleaf thesium, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained environments.

Thesium vimineum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
