Taeniophyllum leptorrhizum

Taeniophyllum leptorrhizum, commonly known as the slender-rooted taeniophyllum, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a delicate, branching structure with numerous small, ovate leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
