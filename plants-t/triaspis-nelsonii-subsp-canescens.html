Triaspis nelsonii subsp. canescens

Triaspis nelsonii subsp.

canescens, commonly known as the white-flowered triaspis, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It is often found in open vegetation types such as fynbos and coastal shrubland.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
