Tillandsia abbreviata

Tillandsia abbreviata, commonly known as the silver pine tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, silver-colored trichomes. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
