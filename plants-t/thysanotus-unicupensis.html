Thysanotus unicupensis

Thysanotus unicupensis, commonly known as the single-cup starflower, is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is native to South Africa.

Thysanotus unicupensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
