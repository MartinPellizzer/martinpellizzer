Tigridia orthantha

Tigridia orthantha, commonly known as the red tigridia, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Mexico.

It produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange with a distinctive black spot at the base. The plant has strap-like leaves and is known for its vibrant, short-lived blooms that appear in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tigridia orthantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
