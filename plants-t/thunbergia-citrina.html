Thunbergia citrina

Thunbergia citrina, commonly known as the yellow trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters along its vines. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct, slightly aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia citrina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in containers.
