Thymus chamarensis

Thymus chamarensis, commonly known as narrowleaf thyme, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its aromatic properties make it a popular choice for culinary and medicinal uses.

Thymus chamarensis is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
