Trichoglottis papuana

Trichoglottis papuana, commonly known as the Papuan trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to New Guinea.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in lowland rainforests.

Trichoglottis papuana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
