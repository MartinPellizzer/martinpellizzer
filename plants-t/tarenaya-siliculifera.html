Tarenaya siliculifera

Tarenaya siliculifera, commonly known as the silique-bearing tarenaya, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and linear leaves. The plant produces elongated, silique fruits that contain several seeds. Native to South America, it thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
