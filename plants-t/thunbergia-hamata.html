Thunbergia hamata

Thunbergia hamata, commonly known as the African blue bell, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia hamata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
