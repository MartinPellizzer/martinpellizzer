Torenia brevifolia

Torenia brevifolia, commonly known as the dwarf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a low shrub, reaching heights of about 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Torenia brevifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
