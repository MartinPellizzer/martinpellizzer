Tulipa greigii var. flava

Tulipa greigii var. flava, commonly known as the yellow greigii tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Turkey.

It produces striking, cup-shaped flowers that range from pale yellow to deep orange, with a distinctive red eye in the center. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches and blooms in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

This variety is valued for its vibrant color and is a popular choice in gardens for its ornamental appeal.
