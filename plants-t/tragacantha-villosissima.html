Tragacantha villosissima

Tragacantha villosissima, commonly known as the hairy tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Tragacantha villosissima is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
