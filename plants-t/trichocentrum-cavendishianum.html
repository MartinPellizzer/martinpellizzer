Trichocentrum cavendishianum

Trichocentrum cavendishianum, commonly known as the Cavendish orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Trichocentrum cavendishianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
