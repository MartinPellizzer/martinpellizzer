Tragacantha hyrcana

Tragacantha hyrcana, commonly known as the Hyrcanian tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by elongated pods containing seeds.

Tragacantha hyrcana is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
