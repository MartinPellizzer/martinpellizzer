Tomiephyllum tenuisectum

Tomiephyllum tenuisectum, commonly known as the slender-leafed tomiephyllum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
