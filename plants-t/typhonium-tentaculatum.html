Typhonium tentaculatum

Typhonium tentaculatum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix inflorescence. The plant produces a large, showy spathe that surrounds the central spadix, which contains the actual flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain ecosystems.
