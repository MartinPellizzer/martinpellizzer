Trichelostylis Coarctata

Trichelostylis coarctata is a rare and unique fern found primarily in the wet tropical rainforests of Australia. This plant is known for its distinctive, spiraling fronds that create an intricate and elegant pattern. It thrives in shaded, moist environments where it can anchor itself to tree trunks or rocky surfaces. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied due to its limited distribution and elusive nature. Protecting this species is crucial for preserving the biodiversity of its native habitats.
