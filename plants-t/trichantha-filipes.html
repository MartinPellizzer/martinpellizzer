Trichantha filipes

Trichantha filipes, commonly known as the slender hair-plant, is a unique member of the fern family.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like fronds that are covered in fine, hair-like structures. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures are small and clustered, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Trichantha filipes is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in ecological systems.
