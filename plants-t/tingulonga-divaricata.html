Tingulonga divaricata

Tingulonga divaricata, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

The flowers, which can be white, pink, or purple, bloom in clusters and are pollinated by various insects.
