Trigonochilum retusum

Trigonochilum retusum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is often found growing on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
