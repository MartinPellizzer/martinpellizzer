Trichomanes longifolium

It is a small, delicate fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. The plant features finely divided, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive frond structure. Its spores are borne on the underside of the leaves in clusters called sori.

Trichomanes longifolium is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in high humidity and partial shade.
