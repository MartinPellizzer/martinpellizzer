Tabernaemontana litoralis

Tabernaemontana litoralis, commonly known as the coastal tabernaemontana, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and coastal landscapes.
