Thecacoris lucida

Thecacoris lucida, commonly known as the luminous orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful, showy flowers that often display a combination of bright yellow and green hues. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure includes a labellum that is highly specialized for attracting specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
