Tanaecium praelongum

Tanaecium praelongum, commonly known as the long-stemmed tanaecium, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural gardens.
