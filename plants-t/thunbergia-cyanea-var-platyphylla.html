Thunbergia cyanea var. platyphylla

Thunbergia cyanea var. platyphylla, commonly known as the broadleaf thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, broad leaves and striking clusters of violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, reaching lengths of up to several meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
