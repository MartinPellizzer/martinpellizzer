Tryphostemma viride

Tryphostemma viride, commonly known as green milkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by follicles containing seeds with silky hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
