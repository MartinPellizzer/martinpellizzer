Tragopogonodes dalechampii

Tragopogonodes dalechampii, commonly known as the dalechamp's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and develop into cylindrical seed heads, which are adapted for wind dispersal.

It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
