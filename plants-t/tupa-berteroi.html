Tupa berteroi

Tupa berteroi, commonly known as the Philippine starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

The tree thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its fruit and ornamental value.
