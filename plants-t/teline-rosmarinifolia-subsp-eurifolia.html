Teline rosmarinifolia subsp. eurifolia

Teline rosmarinifolia subsp.

eurifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved teline, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height.

The leaves are lance-shaped, with a serrated margin and a strong, aromatic scent. It produces small, blue to purple flowers in spikes during the summer months.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
