Tissa pazensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizome is short and thick, supporting the growth of its bipinnate leaves.
