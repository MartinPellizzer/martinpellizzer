Thelymitra maculata

Thelymitra maculata, commonly known as the spotted sun-orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers with distinctive yellow spots. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats. It produces a single, erect stem with a few leaves and a cluster of flowers at the top.

Thelymitra maculata is a perennial herb that blooms in spring and is often found in coastal regions.
