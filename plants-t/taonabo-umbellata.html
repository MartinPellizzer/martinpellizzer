Taonabo umbellata

Taonabo umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella flower, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy surface.

Taonabo umbellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
