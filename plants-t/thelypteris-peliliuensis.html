Thelypteris peliliuensis

Thelypteris peliliuensis, commonly known as the Lepidoptera fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that are often covered in small, scale-like structures. This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
