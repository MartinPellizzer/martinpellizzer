Thelypteris irayensis

Thelypteris irayensis, commonly known as the Madagascar holly fern, is a species of fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common reproductive feature in ferns.

Thelypteris irayensis is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
