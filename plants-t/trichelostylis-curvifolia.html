Trichelostylis curvifolia

Trichelostylis curvifolia, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a member of the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Asia and Australia.

Trichelostylis curvifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
