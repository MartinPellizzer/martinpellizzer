Tartagalia roseorum

It is characterized by its striking, velvety petals that range from deep crimson to pale pink, depending on the time of day. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. Its leaves are dark green, broad, and slightly serrated, often covered in a waxy coating.
