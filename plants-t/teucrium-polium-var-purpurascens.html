Teucrium polium var. purpurascens

Teucrium polium var. purpurascens, commonly known as purple germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are aromatic and often have a mint-like scent.

It is often used in herbal medicine and as a ornamental plant in gardens.
