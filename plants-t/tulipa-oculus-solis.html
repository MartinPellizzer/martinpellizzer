Tulipa oculus-solis

Tulipa oculus-solis, commonly known as the Sun's Eye tulip, is a striking bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow, with a distinctive dark center resembling an eye. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its bulbs are perennial, often producing multiple blooms each spring.

Tulipa oculus-solis is a popular choice for rock gardens and borders due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
