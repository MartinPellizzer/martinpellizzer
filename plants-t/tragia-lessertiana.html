Tragia lessertiana

Tragia lessertiana, commonly known as the lesser tragia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or subshrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Tragia lessertiana is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often used in traditional medicine.
