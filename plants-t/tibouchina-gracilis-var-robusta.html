Tibouchina gracilis var. robusta

Tibouchina gracilis var. robusta, commonly known as the robust tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture with a glossy surface.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
