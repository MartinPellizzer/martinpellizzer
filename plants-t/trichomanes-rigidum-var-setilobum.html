Trichomanes rigidum var. setilobum

Trichomanes rigidum var. setilobum, commonly known as the rigid harts-tongue fern, is a rare and specialized fern species native to specific regions of southern Africa.

The plant has a unique morphology, with its fronds arranged in a tight, compact rosette that helps it retain moisture in its often arid habitat. It reproduces through spores rather than seeds, and its life cycle involves both a sporophyte and gametophyte stage.
