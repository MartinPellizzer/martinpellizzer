Telis gladiata

Telis gladiata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, dark green fronds that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense colonies.

Telis gladiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
