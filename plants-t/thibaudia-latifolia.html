Thibaudia latifolia

Thibaudia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf thibaudia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often pubescent, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical climates.
