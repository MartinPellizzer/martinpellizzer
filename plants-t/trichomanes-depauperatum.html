Trichomanes depauperatum

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are clustered on the underside of the fronds and release spores for propagation.

Trichomanes depauperatum is notable for its unique morphology and ecological adaptation to humid, low-light habitats.
