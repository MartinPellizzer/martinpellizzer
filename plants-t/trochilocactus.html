Trochilocactus

Trochilocactus, commonly known as the spiral cactus, is a genus of cacti native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its unique spiral or helical growth pattern, which helps it conserve water in arid environments. The plant typically has cylindrical stems with areoles that bear spines and flowers. Its flowers are often yellow or red and bloom during the warmer months.

Trochilocactus species are popular among cactus enthusiasts due to their striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
