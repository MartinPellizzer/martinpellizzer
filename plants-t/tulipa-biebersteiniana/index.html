Tulipa Biebersteiniana

Tulipa biebersteiniana is a rare and striking bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Known for its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that range from red to orange, this tulip is a botanical gem often found in specialized gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a cold winter to bloom properly, making it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. The plant's unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of both scientific study and admiration. Its beauty and rarity highlight the diversity within the tulip genus.
