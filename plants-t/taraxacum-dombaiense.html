Taraxacum dombaiense

Taraxacum dombaiense, commonly known as the Dombai dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Dombai area of Russia. This plant is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a single, showy yellow flower head that blooms in the summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
