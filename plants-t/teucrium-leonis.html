Teucrium leonis

Teucrium leonis, commonly known as lion's thorn, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Teucrium leonis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
