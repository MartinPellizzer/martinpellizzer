Thunbergia hispida

Thunbergia hispida, commonly known as the velvet leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia hispida is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering capabilities.
