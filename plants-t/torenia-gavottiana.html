Torenia gavottiana

Torenia gavottiana, commonly known as the Madagascar morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous annual with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers open in the morning and close in the late afternoon, a trait that gives it its common name.

Torenia gavottiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
