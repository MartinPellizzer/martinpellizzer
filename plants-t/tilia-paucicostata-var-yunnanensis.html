Tilia paucicostata var. yunnanensis

Tilia paucicostata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lime tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This species is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
