Telipogon cascajalensis

Telipogon cascajalensis, commonly known as the Cascajal orchid, is a rare and endemic flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, delicate flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its botanical features include slender stems and small, greenish-white petals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
