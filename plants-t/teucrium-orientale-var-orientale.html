Teucrium orientale var. orientale

Teucrium orientale var. orientale, commonly known as oriental germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered with small, opposite leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to both ornamental and medicinal gardens.
