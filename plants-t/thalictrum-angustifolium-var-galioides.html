Thalictrum angustifolium var. galioides

Thalictrum angustifolium var. galioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
