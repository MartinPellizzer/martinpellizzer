Trigonella smyrnea var. scorpioides

Trigonella smyrnea var. scorpioides, commonly known as the Scorpioid Trigonella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Fabaceae family.
