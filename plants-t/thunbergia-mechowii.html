Thunbergia mechowii

Thunbergia mechowii, commonly known as the flame vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces long, trailing stems that make it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia mechowii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in garden landscapes to add vibrant color.
