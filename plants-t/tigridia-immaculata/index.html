Tigridia Immaculata

Tigridia immaculata is a striking member of the Iridaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. This plant is native to South America, specifically regions of Argentina and Bolivia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its blooms, which range from red to orange, often feature a distinctive white center. With proper care, Tigridia immaculata can bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
