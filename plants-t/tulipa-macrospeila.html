Tulipa macrospeila

Tulipa macrospeila, commonly known as the giant tulip, is a species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces cup-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive orange center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring temperate climates.

Tulipa macrospeila is a perennial bulbous plant that is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
