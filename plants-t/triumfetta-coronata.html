Triumfetta coronata

Triumfetta coronata, commonly known as the crown vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Triumfetta coronata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along riverbanks.
