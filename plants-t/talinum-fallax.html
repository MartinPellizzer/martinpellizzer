Talinum fallax

Talinum fallax, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, often blooming at dusk. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
