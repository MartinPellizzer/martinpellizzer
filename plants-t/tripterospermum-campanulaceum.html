Tripterospermum campanulaceum

Tripterospermum campanulaceum, commonly known as the bellflower tripterospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes various bellflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
