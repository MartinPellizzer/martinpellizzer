Thalictrum lecoyeri var. lecoyeri

Thalictrum lecoyeri var. lecoyeri, commonly known as the Lecoyer's meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. The plant features a tall, slender stem with finely divided, fern-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and range in color from white to pale yellow, adding delicate beauty to its habitat.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
