Turnera valleana

Turnera valleana, commonly known as the yellow turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Turnera valleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
