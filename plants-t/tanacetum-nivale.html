Tanacetum nivale

Tanacetum nivale, commonly known as snowdrop, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Tanacetum nivale is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
