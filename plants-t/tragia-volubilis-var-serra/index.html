Tragia Volubilis Var. Serra

Tragia volubilis var. serra is a fascinating plant native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its climbing habit and distinctive leaf structure, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This variant is particularly noted for its unique seed dispersal mechanism, which involves explosive pods. Its presence in ecological studies highlights its role in supporting local biodiversity. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions, making it both a subject of scientific interest and environmental concern.
