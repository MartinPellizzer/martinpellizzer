Treisia erosa

Treisia erosa, commonly known as the slender trezia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Treisia erosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
