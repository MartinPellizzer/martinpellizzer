Thalictrum pseudoichangense

Thalictrum pseudoichangense, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on long pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Thalictrum pseudoichangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
