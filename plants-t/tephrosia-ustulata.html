Tephrosia ustulata

Tephrosia ustulata, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Tephrosia ustulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
