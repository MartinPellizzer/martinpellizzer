Thesium divaricatum var. expansum

Thesium divaricatum var. expansum, commonly known as the broad-leaved thesium, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.
