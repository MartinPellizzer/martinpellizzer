Tristellateia stenactis var. eustenactis

Tristellateia stenactis var. eustenactis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed tristellateia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in dune environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing sand and preventing erosion.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
