Tillandsia fulgens var. miniata

Tillandsia fulgens var. miniata, commonly known as the miniature sun tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often appearing in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, airy substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
