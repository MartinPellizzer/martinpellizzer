Tylloma lanatum

Tylloma lanatum, commonly known as the woolly tylloma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique texture and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
