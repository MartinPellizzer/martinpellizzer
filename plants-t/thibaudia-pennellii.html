Thibaudia pennellii

Thibaudia pennellii, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense, mist-like clusters. The plant is typically found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Thibaudia pennellii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
