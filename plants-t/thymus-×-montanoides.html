Thymus × montanoides

Thymus × montanoides, commonly known as the mountain thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Thymus × montanoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in herbal medicine.
