Theligonum dalmaticum

Theligonum dalmaticum, commonly known as the Dalmatian honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, dark red drupe that ripens in late summer.
