Thyrsanthus velutinus

Thyrsanthus velutinus, commonly known as the velvet thyrsin, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often surrounded by bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
