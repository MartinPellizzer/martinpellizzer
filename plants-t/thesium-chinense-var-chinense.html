Thesium chinense var. chinense

Thesium chinense var. chinense, commonly known as the Chinese thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
