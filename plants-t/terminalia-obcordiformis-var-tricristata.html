Terminalia obcordiformis var. tricristata

Terminalia obcordiformis var. tricristata, commonly known as the three-ridged terminalia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, simple leaves with a distinctive three-ridged midrib. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round, yellow fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
