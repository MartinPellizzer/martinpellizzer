Tessaria dodonaeifolia

Tessaria dodonaeifolia, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its bright orange or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or near water sources.

Tessaria dodonaeifolia is also known for its role in supporting monarch butterfly populations by providing a food source for their larvae.
