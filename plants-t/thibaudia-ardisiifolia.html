Thibaudia ardisiifolia

Thibaudia ardisiifolia, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features vibrant purple to red stems that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful stems. It is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor areas due to its sensitivity to direct sunlight.

Thibaudia ardisiifolia thrives in moist, well-draining soil and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.
