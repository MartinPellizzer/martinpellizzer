Teucrium × bubanii

Teucrium × bubanii, commonly known as the hybrid germander, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Teucrium × bubanii is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat digestive ailments and as an antiseptic.
