Ternstroemia camelliifolia var. minor

Ternstroemia camelliifolia var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf camellia tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden settings.
