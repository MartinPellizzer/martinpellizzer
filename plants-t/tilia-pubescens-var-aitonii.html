Tilia pubescens var. aitonii

Tilia pubescens var. aitonii, commonly known as the Aiton's large-leaf lime, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The tree produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
