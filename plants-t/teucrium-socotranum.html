Teucrium socotranum

Teucrium socotranum, commonly known as Socotra tea, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent.
