Thamnochortus dichotomus var. hyalinus

Thamnochortus dichotomus var. hyalinus, commonly known as the silver chaffhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
