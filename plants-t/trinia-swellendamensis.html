Trinia swellendamensis

Trinia swellendamensis, commonly known as the Swellendam trinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Trinia swellendamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
