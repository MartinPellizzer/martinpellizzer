Tanacetum × sinense

Tanacetum × sinense, commonly known as Chinese tansy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in temperate climates.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and herbal use.
