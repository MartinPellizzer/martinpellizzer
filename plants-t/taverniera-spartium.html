Taverniera spartium

Taverniera spartium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Taverniera spartium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
