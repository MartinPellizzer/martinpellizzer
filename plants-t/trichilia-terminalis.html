Trichilia terminalis

Trichilia terminalis, commonly known as African shea tree or karite, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody pods containing oily seeds. The seeds are rich in fats and are widely used in the production of shea butter.

It plays an important ecological and economic role in many African countries.
