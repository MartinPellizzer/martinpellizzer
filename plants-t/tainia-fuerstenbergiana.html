Tainia fuerstenbergiana

Tainia fuerstenbergiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical herbaceous plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

Tainia fuerstenbergiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
