Tibouchina francavillana

Tibouchina francavillana, commonly known as the pink trumpetbush, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Tibouchina francavillana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
