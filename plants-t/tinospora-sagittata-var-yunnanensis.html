Tinospora sagittata var. yunnanensis

Tinospora sagittata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Tinospora, is a climbing shrub native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces woody, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
