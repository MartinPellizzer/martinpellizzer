Touchardia glabra

Touchardia glabra, commonly known as the smooth touchardia, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and lance-shaped. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a pale yellow or greenish hue.

Touchardia glabra is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
