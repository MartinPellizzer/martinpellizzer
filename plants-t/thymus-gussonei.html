Thymus gussonei

Thymus gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves and flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its essential oils contain thymol, which gives it a strong, pungent aroma.

Thymus gussonei is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in herbal medicine.
