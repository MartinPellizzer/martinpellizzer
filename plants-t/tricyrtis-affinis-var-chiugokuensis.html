Tricyrtis affinis var. chiugokuensis

Tricyrtis affinis var. chiugokuensis, commonly known as the Japanese spider lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, spidery appearance. The plant produces showy, three-lobed leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
