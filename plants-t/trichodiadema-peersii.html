Trichodiadema peersii

Trichodiadema peersii, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
