Tectaria haynaldii

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical rainforests. Tectaria haynaldii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
