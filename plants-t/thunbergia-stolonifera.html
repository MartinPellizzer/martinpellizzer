Thunbergia stolonifera

Thunbergia stolonifera, commonly known as the creeping bluebell, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a trailing or climbing perennial that produces vibrant blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and has a sprawling growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or ground cover. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and regular watering.

Thunbergia stolonifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
