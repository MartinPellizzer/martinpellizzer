Tabernaemontana polysperma

Tabernaemontana polysperma, commonly known as the Madagascar star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Tabernaemontana polysperma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its beautiful fragrance and aesthetic appeal.
