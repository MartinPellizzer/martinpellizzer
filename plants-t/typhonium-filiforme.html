Typhonium filiforme

Typhonium filiforme, commonly known as the slender arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. Its leaves are broad and arrow-shaped, often with a waxy texture, while the flowers are small and clustered.

Typhonium filiforme thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.
