Tovomita clusioides

Tovomita clusioides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Tovomita clusioides is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
