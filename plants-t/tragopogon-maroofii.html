Tragopogon maroofii

Tragopogon maroofii, commonly known as the Maroof's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flower heads bloom in the summer, producing small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Tragopogon maroofii is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine environments.
