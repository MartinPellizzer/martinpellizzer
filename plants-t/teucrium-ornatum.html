Teucrium ornatum

Teucrium ornatum, commonly known as purple germander, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in woodland edges and rocky habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in herbal remedies.
