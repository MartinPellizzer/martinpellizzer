Tricyrtis macropoda var. hirsuta

Tricyrtis macropoda var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture that gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a distinctive plant.
