Thlaspi rubellum

Thlaspi rubellum, commonly known as red root sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. Thlaspi rubellum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.
