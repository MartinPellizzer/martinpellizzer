Taraxacum hastiliforme

Taraxacum hastiliforme, commonly known as the swordleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant species belonging to the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves and a single, upright stem that terminates in a yellow flower head. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It produces a distinctive seed head with feathery seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
