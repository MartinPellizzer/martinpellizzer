Tillandsia farinosa

Tillandsia farinosa, commonly known as the silver air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Tillandsia farinosa is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique look and low maintenance requirements.
