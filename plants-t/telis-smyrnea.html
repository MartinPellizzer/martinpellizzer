Telis smyrnea

Telis smyrnea, commonly known as the common smyrna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, serrated edge.

Telis smyrnea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant.
