Treisia tuberculata

Treisia tuberculata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is a perennial herb that produces small, tuberous roots, which help it survive in dry conditions.

Treisia tuberculata is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
