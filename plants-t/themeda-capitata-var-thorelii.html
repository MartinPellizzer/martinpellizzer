Themeda capitata var. thorelii

Themeda capitata var. thorelii, commonly known as the slender tussock grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems that often form dense tussocks. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This grass is an important component of native ecosystems and is valued for its ornamental and ecological attributes.
