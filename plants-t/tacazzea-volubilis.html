Tacazzea volubilis

Tacazzea volubilis, commonly known as the climbing tacazzea, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
