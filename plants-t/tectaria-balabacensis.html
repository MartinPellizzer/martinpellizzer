Tectaria balabacensis

Tectaria balabacensis, commonly known as the Balabac shield fern, is a tropical fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on moist, shaded forest floors or rocky substrates. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Tectaria balabacensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in horticultural collections.
