Trigonella ornithopodioides var. melilotea

Trigonella ornithopodioides var. melilotea, commonly known as melilot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant produces pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It is often grown as a forage crop and has a long history of use in traditional medicine.

Its aromatic properties make it valuable in both agricultural and herbal applications.
