Thrixspermum ventricosum

Thrixspermum ventricosum, commonly known as the woolly thrixspermum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to green in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
