Taraxacum uschakovii var. pavlovii

Taraxacum uschakovii var. pavlovii, commonly known as the Pavlov's dandelion, is a rare and endemic plant species found primarily in the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces a tall, slender flowering stem topped with a single, yellow composite flower head. It thrives in rocky and alpine habitats, often growing in well-drained soils at high altitudes.
