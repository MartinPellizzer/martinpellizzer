Terminalia pterocarya

Terminalia pterocarya, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.
