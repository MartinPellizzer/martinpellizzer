Theodorea sughoo

Theodorea sughoo, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, silver-gray leaves that reflect light, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Native to arid regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
