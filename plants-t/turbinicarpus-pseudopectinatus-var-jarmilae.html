Turbinicarpus pseudopectinatus var. jarmilae

Turbinicarpus pseudopectinatus var. jarmilae, commonly known as the Jarmilae's turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded stem that often develops a fluted appearance with age. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
