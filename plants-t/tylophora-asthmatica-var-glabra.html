Tylophora asthmatica var. glabra

Tylophora asthmatica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth tylophora, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin.
