Trachoma stellatum

Trachoma stellatum, commonly known as the starry trachoma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight bluish tinge.

Trachoma stellatum is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
