Tragopogon kultiassovii

Tragopogon kultiassovii, commonly known as the Kultunskaya goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a rosette of basal leaves. Its inflorescence consists of yellow ray florets surrounded by bracts, and it produces a distinctive, hollow stem.

Tragopogon kultiassovii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
