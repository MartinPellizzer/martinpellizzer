Tagetes gigantea

Tagetes gigantea, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces a strong, distinctive scent that can repel certain insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit.
