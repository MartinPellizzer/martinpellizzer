Tradescantia schlechtendalii

Tradescantia schlechtendalii, commonly known as the silver sword or white-veined spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and striking silver-green leaves with prominent white veins. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated in gardens around the world.
