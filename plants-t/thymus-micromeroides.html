Thymus micromeroides

Thymus micromeroides, commonly known as dwarf thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces tiny, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and aromatic properties make it a popular choice for both ornamental and practical gardening.
