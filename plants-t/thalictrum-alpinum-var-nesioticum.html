Thalictrum alpinum var. nesioticum

Thalictrum alpinum var. nesioticum, commonly known as the Alpine meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or alpine habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.

Thalictrum alpinum var. nesioticum is also noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.
