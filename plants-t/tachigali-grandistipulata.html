Tachigali grandistipulata

Tachigali grandistipulata, commonly known as the large-leaved tachigali, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Tachigali grandistipulata is typically found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
