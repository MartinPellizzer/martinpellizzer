Thelymitra spiralis

Thelymitra spiralis, commonly known as the spiral leek orchid, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the autumn season.

Thelymitra spiralis is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, creeping rhizome and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native environment.
