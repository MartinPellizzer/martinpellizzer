Trigonostemon philippinensis

Trigonostemon philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine ironwood, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in lowland to montane forests. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, often with yellowish or greenish petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
