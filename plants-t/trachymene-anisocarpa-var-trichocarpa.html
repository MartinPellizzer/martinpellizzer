Trachymene anisocarpa var. trichocarpa

Trachymene anisocarpa var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed false dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
