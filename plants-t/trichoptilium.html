Trichoptilium

Trichoptilium, commonly known as the white trichoptilium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Trichoptilium is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
