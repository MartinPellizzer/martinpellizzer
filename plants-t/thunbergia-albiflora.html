Thunbergia albiflora

Thunbergia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered morning glory, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped white flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has a trailing stem that can grow up to several meters in length, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia albiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
