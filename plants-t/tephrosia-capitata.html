Tephrosia capitata

Tephrosia capitata, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, yellowish-green foliage and produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family, and is known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities.
