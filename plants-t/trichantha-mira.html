Trichantha mira

Trichantha mira, commonly known as the golden feather fern, is a delicate and elegant plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity to flourish. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Trichantha mira is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in forest understories.
