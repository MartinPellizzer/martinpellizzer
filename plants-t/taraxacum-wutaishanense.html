Taraxacum wutaishanense

Taraxacum wutaishanense, commonly known as the Wutaishan dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Wutaishan region of Sichuan Province. The plant is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head that produces yellow composite flowers. It has a taproot system and is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native habitat.
