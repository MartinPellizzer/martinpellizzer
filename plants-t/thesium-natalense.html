Thesium natalense

Thesium natalense, commonly known as Natal thesium, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and dense clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Thesium natalense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
