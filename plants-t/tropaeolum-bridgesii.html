Tropaeolum bridgesii

Tropaeolum bridgesii, commonly known as the Brazilian nasturtium, is a vibrant annual plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, bright green leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its flowers are typically yellow with red centers. The plant produces edible, peppery-tasting seeds that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and colorful blooms make it an attractive addition to both ornamental and edible gardens.
