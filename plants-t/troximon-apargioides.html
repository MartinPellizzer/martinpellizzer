Troximon apargioides

Troximon apargioides, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, with five petals. It is adapted to arid environments and has a milky sap that is toxic to many animals.
