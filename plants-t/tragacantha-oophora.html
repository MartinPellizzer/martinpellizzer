Tragacantha oophora

Tragacantha oophora, commonly known as the eggplant tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is known for its ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils.

Tragacantha oophora is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
