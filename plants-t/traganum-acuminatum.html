Traganum acuminatum

Traganum acuminatum, commonly known as the spiny pigweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

Its adaptability makes it a common weed in agricultural areas, though it also has traditional uses in some regions.
