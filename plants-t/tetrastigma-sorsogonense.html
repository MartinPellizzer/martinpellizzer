Tetrastigma sorsogonense

Tetrastigma sorsogonense, commonly known as the Sorsogon Province vine, is a climbing plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its tendrils and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by yellowish berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in forest understories.
