Tripteris angustissima

Tripteris angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
