Tracheliopsis pubicalyx

Tracheliopsis pubicalyx, commonly known as the hairy calyx orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its unique, hairy calyx that gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Tracheliopsis pubicalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
