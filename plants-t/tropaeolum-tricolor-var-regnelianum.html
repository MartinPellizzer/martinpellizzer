Tropaeolum tricolor var. regnelianum

Tropaeolum tricolor var. regnelianum, commonly known as the New Zealand trident, is a vibrant annual plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically a mix of red, orange, and yellow, creating a striking visual effect. The plant produces small, edible flowers that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through self-seeding, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, hardy plants.
