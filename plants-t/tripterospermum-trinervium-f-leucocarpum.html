Tripterospermum trinervium f. leucocarpum

Tripterospermum trinervium f. leucocarpum, commonly known as the white-fruited tripterospermum, is a rare and distinctive cultivar of the tripterospermum genus.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale yellow fruits, which contrast sharply with its dark green foliage.
