Thesidium exocarpaeoides

Thesidium exocarpaeoides, commonly known as the desert shield fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its specialized adaptations to arid environments.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is distinguished by its thick, waxy exocarp that helps reduce water loss.
