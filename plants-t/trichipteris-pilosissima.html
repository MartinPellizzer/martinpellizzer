Trichipteris pilosissima

Trichipteris pilosissima, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a species of fern characterized by its dense, feathery fronds covered in fine, soft hairs.

Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that aids in its identification.
