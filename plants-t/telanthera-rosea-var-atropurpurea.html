Telanthera rosea var. atropurpurea

Telanthera rosea var. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered telanthera, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from pale pink to deep purple, often with a bicolor pattern. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical-looking plants.
