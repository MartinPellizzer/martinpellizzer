Trifolium tmoleum

Trifolium tmoleum, commonly known as lesser clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets arranged on a slender stem. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Trifolium tmoleum is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important plant in ecological restoration efforts.
