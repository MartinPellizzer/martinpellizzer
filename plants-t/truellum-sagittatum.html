Truellum sagittatum

Truellum sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often described as arrowhead-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the spring.

Truellum sagittatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
