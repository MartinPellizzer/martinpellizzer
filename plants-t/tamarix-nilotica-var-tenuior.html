Tamarix nilotica var. tenuior

Tamarix nilotica var. tenuior, commonly known as the slender tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, pink or reddish flowers that appear in summer. The plant has small, scale-like leaves and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It is well adapted to drought and can thrive in saline soils.

Its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen make it useful for soil stabilization and reclamation projects.
