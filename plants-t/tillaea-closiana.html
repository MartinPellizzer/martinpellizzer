Tillaea closiana

Tillaea closiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tillaea, is a small, mat-forming perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, succulent leaves. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
