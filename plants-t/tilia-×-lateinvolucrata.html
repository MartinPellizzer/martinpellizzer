Tilia × lateinvolucrata

Tilia × lateinvolucrata, commonly known as the late flowering lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically pubescent on the underside. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom later in the season compared to other lime species. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that matures in late summer.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
