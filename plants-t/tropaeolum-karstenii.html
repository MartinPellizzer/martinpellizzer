Tropaeolum karstenii

Tropaeolum karstenii, commonly known as the mountain nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Tropaeolum karstenii is also valued for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.
