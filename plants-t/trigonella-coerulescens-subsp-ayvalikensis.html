Trigonella coerulescens subsp. ayvalikensis

Trigonella coerulescens subsp.

ayvalikensis, commonly known as Ayvalık lentil, is a subspecies native to Turkey, particularly the Ayvalık region. It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by lens-shaped pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates.

This subspecies is valued for its nutritional content and is cultivated for its high-quality lentils.
