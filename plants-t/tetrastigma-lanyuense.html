Tetrastigma lanyuense

Tetrastigma lanyuense, commonly known as the Lanyu grapevine, is a woody vine native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and tendrils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Tetrastigma lanyuense is often found in humid, forested areas and is adapted to thrive in shaded environments.
