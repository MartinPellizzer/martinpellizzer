Tanacetopsis afghanica

Tanacetopsis afghanica, commonly known as the Afghan tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan and has adapted to high-altitude environments. The plant features finely divided, silvery-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flower heads. It is often found in rocky soils and is known for its aromatic properties.

Tanacetopsis afghanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
