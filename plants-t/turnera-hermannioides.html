Turnera hermannioides

Turnera hermannioides, commonly known as Hermann's turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
