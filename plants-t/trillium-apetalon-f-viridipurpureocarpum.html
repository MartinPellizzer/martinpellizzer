Trillium apetalon f. viridipurpureocarpum

Trillium apetalon f. viridipurpureocarpum, commonly known as the green-purple fruit trillium, is a rare botanical variant of the Trillium genus.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring a mix of green and purplish hues on the leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers with three petals, typically in shades of white or pale pink.
