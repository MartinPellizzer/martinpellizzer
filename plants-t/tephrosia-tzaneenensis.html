Tephrosia tzaneenensis

Tephrosia tzaneenensis, commonly known as the Natal tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in sandy or loamy soils.

Tephrosia tzaneenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
