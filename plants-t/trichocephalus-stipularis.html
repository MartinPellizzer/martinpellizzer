Trichocephalus stipularis

Trichocephalus stipularis, commonly known as the stipular trichocephalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive stipules, which are modified leaf-like structures at the base of the leaves. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
