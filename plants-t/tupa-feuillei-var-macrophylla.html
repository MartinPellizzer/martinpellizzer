Tupa feuillei var. macrophylla

Tupa feuillei var. macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaf tupa, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its foliage is dense and evergreen, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and appear in clusters.
