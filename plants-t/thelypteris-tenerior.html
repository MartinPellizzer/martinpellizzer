Thelypteris tenerior

Thelypteris tenerior, commonly known as the soft fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green and have a soft, velvety texture. This fern is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
