Tabernaemontana globosa

Tabernaemontana globosa, commonly known as the globe lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
