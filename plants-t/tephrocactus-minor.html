Tephrocactus minor

Tephrocactus minor, commonly known as the small rock cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
