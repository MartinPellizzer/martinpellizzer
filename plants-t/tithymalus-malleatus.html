Tithymalus malleatus

Tithymalus malleatus, commonly known as the sticky tithymalus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. Tithymalus malleatus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive floral display.
