Tristachya stocksii

Tristachya stocksii, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Tristachya stocksii is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a low-maintenance addition to gardens.
