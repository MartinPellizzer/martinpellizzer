Trixis ochroleuca var. hieracioides

Trixis ochroleuca var. hieracioides, commonly known as the woolly trixis, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, grayish-green foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh, dry environments and has a compact growth habit to reduce water loss. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation across sandy and rocky soils.

This variety is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
