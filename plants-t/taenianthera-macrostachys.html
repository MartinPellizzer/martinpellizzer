Taenianthera macrostachys

Taenianthera macrostachys, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often displaying vibrant red or orange hues. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces long, pendulous inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Taenianthera macrostachys is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
