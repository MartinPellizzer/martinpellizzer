Tala odorata

Tala odorata, commonly known as the sweet-scented tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that emit a sweet, spicy aroma. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its timber and medicinal properties.
