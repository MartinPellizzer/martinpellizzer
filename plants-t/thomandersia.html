Thomandersia

Thomandersia, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-gray underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
