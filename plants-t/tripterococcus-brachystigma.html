Tripterococcus brachystigma

Tripterococcus brachystigma, commonly known as the short-stemmed dipterocarp, is a tropical tree species belonging to the Dipterocarpaceae family.

It is characterized by its sturdy, cylindrical trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and is adapted to high humidity and nutrient-poor soils. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.

The species plays a vital role in the ecological balance of its native habitats by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
