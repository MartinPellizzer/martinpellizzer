Teucrium Parnassicum

Teucrium parnassicum is a hardy perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it an excellent choice for rock gardens and borders. Teucrium parnassicum is also valued for its medicinal properties and historical use in herbal remedies.
