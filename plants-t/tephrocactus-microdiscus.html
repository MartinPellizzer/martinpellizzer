Tephrocactus microdiscus

Tephrocactus microdiscus, commonly known as the miniature disc cactus, is a small, compact cactus native to the dry regions of Mexico.

It has a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and compact size make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
