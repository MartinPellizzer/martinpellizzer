Terebinthus odorata

Terebinthus odorata, commonly known as the fragrant terebinth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It is often found in rocky or dry soils, thriving in full sun.

Terebinthus odorata is valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in traditional medicine.
