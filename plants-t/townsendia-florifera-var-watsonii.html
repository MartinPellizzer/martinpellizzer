Townsendia florifera var. watsonii

Townsendia florifera var. watsonii, commonly known as Watson's flowered Townsendia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, linear leaves and has a compact, cushion-like growth habit.
