Tupa columnaris

Tupa columnaris, commonly known as the columnar tupa, is a unique species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that grow vertically, giving it a tree-like appearance. The plant produces small, spiny leaves and vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water to thrive.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and collections.
