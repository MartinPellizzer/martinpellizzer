Tragacantha usturtensis

Tragacantha usturtensis, commonly known as the Usturt tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces a valuable gum, known as tragacanth, which is used in various industrial and medicinal applications. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
