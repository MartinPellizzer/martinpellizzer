Tilia × lengyelii

Tilia × lengyelii, commonly known as the Hungarian lime, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically serrated along the edges. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia × lengyelii is native to Hungary and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance.
