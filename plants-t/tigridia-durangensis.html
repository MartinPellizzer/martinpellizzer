Tigridia durangensis

Tigridia durangensis, commonly known as the Durango tiger flower, is a member of the Iridaceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that resemble small trumpets. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It produces showy blooms during the spring and early summer months.

Tigridia durangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
