Tragia cannabina

Tragia cannabina, commonly known as the velvetleaf or black nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain ecosystems.
