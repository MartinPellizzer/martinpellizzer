Tephrosia brunnea

Tephrosia brunnea, commonly known as black tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

Tephrosia brunnea is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antifungal effects.
