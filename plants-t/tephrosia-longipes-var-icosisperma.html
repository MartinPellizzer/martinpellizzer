Tephrosia longipes var. icosisperma

Tephrosia longipes var. icosisperma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tephrosia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom throughout the dry season. Its fruit is a slender, curved pod that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in dry savannas and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
