Thunbergia paulitschkeana var. lanceolata

Thunbergia paulitschkeana var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate thunbergia, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly velvety texture. The flowers are tubular, with striking blue or purple hues, and are often adorned with a contrasting yellow or white throat. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
