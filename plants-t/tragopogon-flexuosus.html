Tragopogon flexuosus

Tragopogon flexuosus, commonly known as the bent leaf thistle, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Tragopogon flexuosus is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
