Tapinanthus sphaericocompressus

Tapinanthus sphaericocompressus, commonly known as the spherical bladderwort, is a unique parasitic plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on its host for nutrients, typically attaching to the roots of other plants. These structures are specialized for nutrient absorption rather than predation.

It is native to parts of Africa and is adapted to wet, swampy environments.
