Triclisia sacleuxii var. sacleuxii

Triclisia sacleuxii var. sacleuxii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with upright stems and small, rounded leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a cut flower.
