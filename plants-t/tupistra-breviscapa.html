Tupistra breviscapa

Tupistra breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in dense clusters. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
