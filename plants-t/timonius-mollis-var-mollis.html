Timonius mollis var. mollis

Timonius mollis var. mollis, commonly known as the slender timonius, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
