Tropaeolum pubescens

Tropaeolum pubescens, commonly known as the pepperleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, hairy leaves and vibrant yellow or orange flowers. The plant produces edible, peppery-tasting seeds that are often used in cooking. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value and culinary uses make it a versatile and attractive plant.
