Tragus echinatus

Tragus echinatus, commonly known as the spiny tragus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense, spiny bracts that protect its small yellow flowers. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often growing in coastal areas.

Its structure and appearance make it well adapted to its arid and coastal environments.
