Tikalia

Tikalia, commonly known as Indian tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its woody shrub or small tree structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing several seeds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Tikalia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
