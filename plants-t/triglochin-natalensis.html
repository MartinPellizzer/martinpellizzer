Triglochin natalensis

Triglochin natalensis, commonly known as Natal marsh grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often submerged or partially submerged in water. It produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season.

Triglochin natalensis is adapted to tolerate fluctuating water levels and is often found in nutrient-rich soils.
