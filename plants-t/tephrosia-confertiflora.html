Tephrosia confertiflora

Tephrosia confertiflora, commonly known as the denseflower tephrosia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Tephrosia confertiflora is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
