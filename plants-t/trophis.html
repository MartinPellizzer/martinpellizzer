Trophis

Trophis, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy coating. These plants thrive in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

They are popular as houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
