Trichopilia × ramonensis

Trichopilia × ramonensis, commonly known as the hybrid trichopilia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Trichopilia tubiflora and Trichopilia alpina, both native to South America. The plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in various regions with suitable climates.
