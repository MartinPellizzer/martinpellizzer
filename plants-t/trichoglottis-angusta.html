Trichoglottis angusta

Trichoglottis angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Trichoglottis angusta is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
