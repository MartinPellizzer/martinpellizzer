Thelypteris micans

Thelypteris micans, commonly known as the shining shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered with a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a shimmering appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that help it spread and colonize new areas.

Its spores are produced on the underside of its fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in moist, forested habitats.
