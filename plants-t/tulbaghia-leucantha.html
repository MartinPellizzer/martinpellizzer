Tulbaghia leucantha

Tulbaghia leucantha, commonly known as the white star tulbaghia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Tulbaghia leucantha is also valued for its fragrant foliage and is used in traditional medicine.
