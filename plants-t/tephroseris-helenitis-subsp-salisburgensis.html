Tephroseris helenitis subsp. salisburgensis

Tephroseris helenitis subsp.

salisburgensis, commonly known as the Salzburg helenean, is a rare alpine plant native to the Central Alps. It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form, which helps it survive in harsh mountain environments.

The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
