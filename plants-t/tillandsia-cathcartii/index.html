Tillandsia Cathcartii

Tillandsia cathcartii is a rare air plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. It thrives in humid, shaded environments where it clings to trees and rocks. This species is known for its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, bell-shaped flowers. Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered species from habitat loss.
