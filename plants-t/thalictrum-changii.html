Thalictrum changii

Thalictrum changii, commonly known as the Chinese meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Thalictrum changii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
