Themelium sarawakense

Themelium sarawakense, commonly known as the Sarawak melastome, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and is often found in moist, shaded undergrowth. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small organisms.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
