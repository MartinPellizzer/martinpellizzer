Tsuga ajanensis

Tsuga ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis hemlock, is a species of conifer native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in cool, moist environments at high altitudes. The plant has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged spirally along the branches. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
