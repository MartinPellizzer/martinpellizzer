Thalictrum flavum var. pauperculum

Thalictrum flavum var. pauperculum, commonly known as the slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it spreads through rhizomes.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and ecological benefits.
