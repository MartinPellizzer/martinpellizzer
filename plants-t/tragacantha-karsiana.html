Tragacantha karsiana

Tragacantha karsiana, commonly known as the Karsian tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Tragacantha karsiana is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important species in ecological restoration efforts.
