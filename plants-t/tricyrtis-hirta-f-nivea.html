Tricyrtis hirta f. nivea

Tricyrtis hirta f. nivea, commonly known as the snow-flowered spider orchid, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that resemble spider-like blooms. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is typically grown in cultivation for its unique floral display.

This form of Tricyrtis hirta is prized for its aesthetic value and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
