Tamarix orientalis

Tamarix orientalis, commonly known as the oriental tamarisk, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often multi-branched stems that are typically covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, pink or pale red flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.
