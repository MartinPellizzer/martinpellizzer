Tradescantia floridana

Tradescantia floridana, commonly known as the Florida spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces striking blue or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Tradescantia floridana is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.
