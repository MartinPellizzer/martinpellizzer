Ticorea tubiflora

Ticorea tubiflora, commonly known as the tuberous bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a distinctive tuberous root system that helps it survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Ticorea tubiflora is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
