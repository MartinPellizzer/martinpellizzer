Thuja orientalis f. semperaurescens

Thuja orientalis f. semperaurescens, commonly known as the evergreen thuja, is a cultivar of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark green foliage that remains vibrant throughout the year. The leaves are scale-like and closely packed, giving the plant a smooth, textured appearance. It is highly resistant to pests and diseases, making it a low-maintenance choice for landscaping.

This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, offering year-round ornamental value.
