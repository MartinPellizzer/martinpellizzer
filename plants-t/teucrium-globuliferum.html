Teucrium globuliferum

Teucrium globuliferum, commonly known as globe thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features small, globular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters along the stems. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
