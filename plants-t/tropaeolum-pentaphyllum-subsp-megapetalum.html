Tropaeolum pentaphyllum subsp. megapetalum

Tropaeolum pentaphyllum subsp.

megapetalum, commonly known as the giant flame flower, is a striking member of the Tropaeolaceae family. It is characterized by its large, showy, bright red to orange flowers that can reach up to 10 cm in diameter.

The plant produces heart-shaped, glossy green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is a herbaceous annual that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
