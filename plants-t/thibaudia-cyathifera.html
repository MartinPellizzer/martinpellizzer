Thibaudia cyathifera

Thibaudia cyathifera, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Thibaudia cyathifera is typically found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
