Tetratheca preissiana var. integrifolia

Tetratheca preissiana var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved tetratheca, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its simple, entire leaves that are typically oval to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, which bloom in spring. It is often found in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
