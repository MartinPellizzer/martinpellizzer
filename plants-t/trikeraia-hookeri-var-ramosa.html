Trikeraia hookeri var. ramosa

Trikeraia hookeri var. ramosa, commonly known as the hairy trikeraia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptical in shape, and often have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
