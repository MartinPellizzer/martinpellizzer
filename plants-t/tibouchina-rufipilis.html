Tibouchina rufipilis

Tibouchina rufipilis, commonly known as the redstem tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly velvety surface.

Tibouchina rufipilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
