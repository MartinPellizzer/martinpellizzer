Tillandsia scalarifolia

Tillandsia scalarifolia, commonly known as the scalycush tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding structure. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Tillandsia scalarifolia produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to its appearance.
