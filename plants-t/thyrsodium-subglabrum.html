Thyrsodium subglabrum

Thyrsodium subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth thyrsodium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a rounded inflorescence called a thyrse. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, though the species name "subglabrum" suggests a relatively smooth texture.

It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
