Trigonella melilotus-corniculata

Trigonella melilotus-corniculata, commonly known as the horned melilot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
