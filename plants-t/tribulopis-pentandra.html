Tribulopis pentandra

Tribulopis pentandra, commonly known as the African burr or goat weed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a spiny, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves with serrated edges.
