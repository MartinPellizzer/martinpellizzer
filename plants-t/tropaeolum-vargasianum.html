Tropaeolum vargasianum

Tropaeolum vargasianum, commonly known as the Andes jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family, characterized by its vibrant, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in cultivating unique and colorful plants.
