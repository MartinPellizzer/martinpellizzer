Tritoma uvaria var. grandiflora

Tritoma uvaria var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered tritoma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value has made it a subject of interest in horticulture and botanical studies.
