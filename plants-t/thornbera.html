Thornbera

Thornbera, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. Thornbera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can thrive in well-drained soils.

It is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
