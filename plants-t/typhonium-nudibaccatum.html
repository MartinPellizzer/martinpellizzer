Typhonium nudibaccatum

Typhonium nudibaccatum, commonly known as the naked-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and is often found in wetland or marshy environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in an inflorescence. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly in suitable conditions.

Typhonium nudibaccatum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
