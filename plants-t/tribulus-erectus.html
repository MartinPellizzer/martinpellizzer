Tribulus erectus

Tribulus erectus, commonly known as golden shower or yellow shower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, yellow, five-petaled flowers arranged in clusters. The plant's leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Tribulus erectus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
