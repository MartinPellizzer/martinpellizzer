Turgenia heterocarpa

Turgenia heterocarpa, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in grasslands and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Turgenia heterocarpa is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardiness.
