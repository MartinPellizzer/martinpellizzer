Tilia nickerlii

Tilia nickerlii, commonly known as Nickerli's lime tree, is a rare and endangered species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
