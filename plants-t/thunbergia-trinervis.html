Thunbergia trinervis

Thunbergia trinervis, commonly known as the flame vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with three distinct veins, giving it the species name *trinervis*. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia trinervis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
