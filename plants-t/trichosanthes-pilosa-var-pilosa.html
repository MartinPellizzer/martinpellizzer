Trichosanthes pilosa var. pilosa

Trichosanthes pilosa var. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into long, cylindrical fruits covered in a thick, warty coating. Its fruit is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and culinary practices.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
