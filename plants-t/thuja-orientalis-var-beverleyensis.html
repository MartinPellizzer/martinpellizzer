Thuja orientalis var. beverleyensis

Thuja orientalis var. beverleyensis, commonly known as the Beverly Hills thuja, is a cultivar of the eastern arborvitae.

It is characterized by its narrow, pyramidal shape and dense, dark green foliage. The plant has scale-like leaves that are closely packed, giving it a smooth, textured appearance. It is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in landscapes.

This variety is often used in hedges and as a specimen plant due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
