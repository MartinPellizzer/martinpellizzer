Thibaudia nitida

Thibaudia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf thibaudia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice in horticultural settings.
