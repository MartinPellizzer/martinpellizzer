Triticum lolioides var. aristatum

Triticum lolioides var. aristatum, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in cool, temperate climates and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and is sometimes cultivated for forage or ornamental purposes.
