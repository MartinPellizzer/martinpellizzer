Tropaeolum hortense

Tropaeolum hortense, commonly known as the garden nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible flowers and leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Tropaeolum hortense is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
