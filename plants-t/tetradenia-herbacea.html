Tetradenia herbacea

Tetradenia herbacea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tetradenia, is a small shrub native to the tropical rainforests of northern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and paperbark trees. The plant is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small white flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in understorey habitats.
