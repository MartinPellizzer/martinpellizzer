Tecmarsis

Tecmarsis, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a perennial plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features silvery-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Tecmarsis is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient species suitable for xeriscaping.
