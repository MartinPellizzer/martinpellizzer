Trema philippinense

Trema philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine trema, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by orange to red berries containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a notable species in local horticulture.
