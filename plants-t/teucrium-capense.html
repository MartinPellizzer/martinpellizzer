Teucrium capense

Teucrium capense, commonly known as Cape germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. The plant features slender, aromatic stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Teucrium capense is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
