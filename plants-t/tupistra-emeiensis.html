Tupistra emeiensis

Tupistra emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei Mountain stonecress, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are typically succulent and fleshy, adapted to thrive in rocky and arid environments.
