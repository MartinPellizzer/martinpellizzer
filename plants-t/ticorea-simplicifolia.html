Ticorea simplicifolia

Ticorea simplicifolia, commonly known as the simple-leaved ticorea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are simple and alternate. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates.
