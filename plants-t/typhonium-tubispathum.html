Typhonium tubispathum

Typhonium tubispathum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
