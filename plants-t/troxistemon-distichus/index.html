Troxistemon Distichus

Troxistemon distichus is a rare and unique plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking two-row arrangement of leaves, which give it its scientific name. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
