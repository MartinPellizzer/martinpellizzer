Trisetum preslii var. buchtienii

Trisetum preslii var. buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtienii feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to central and eastern Europe.

It features slender, wiry stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that resemble a feather, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
