Trifolium nigrescens subsp. dolychodon

Trifolium nigrescens subsp.

dolychodon, commonly known as the dark-flowered clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each composed of three oval to heart-shaped leaflets.

The plant produces small, typically dark purple or black flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its unique coloration and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
