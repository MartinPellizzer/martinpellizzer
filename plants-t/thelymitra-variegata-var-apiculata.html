Thelymitra variegata var. apiculata

Thelymitra variegata var. apiculata, commonly known as the Splendid Sun-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow and green striped petals and a distinctive red-brown dorsal sepal. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its flowers bloom in spring and are pollinated by birds, particularly honeyeaters.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
