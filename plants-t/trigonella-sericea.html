Trigonella sericea

Trigonella sericea, commonly known as silverhead fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils. The plant has slender, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves with a distinctive silvery appearance. Its small yellow flowers bloom in early summer, followed by slender, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Trigonella sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
