Trinia tuberculata

Trinia tuberculata, commonly known as the woolly trinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Trinia tuberculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
