Trisetum burnoufii

Trisetum burnoufii, commonly known as the Burnouf's panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Trisetum burnoufii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
