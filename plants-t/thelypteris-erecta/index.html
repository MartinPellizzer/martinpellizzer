Thelypteris Erecta

Thelypteris erecta is a species of fern commonly found in moist, shaded environments across various regions. Known for its upright growth habit, this plant often forms dense clusters that thrive in forest understories. Its fronds are typically lance-shaped and exhibit a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant plays an important role in ecological systems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability. Despite its modest size, Thelypteris erecta is a vital component of many wetland and woodland ecosystems.
