Tabernaemontana albiflora

Tabernaemontana albiflora, commonly known as the white angel's trumpet, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous, white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dramatic and ornamental appearance. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap and showy flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care and kept away from children and pets.
