Thunbergia hookeriana

Thunbergia hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's spider flower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It produces striking, spider-like flowers in shades of violet and white, which bloom throughout the year in suitable climates. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
