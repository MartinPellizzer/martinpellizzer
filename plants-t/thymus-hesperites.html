Thymus hesperites

Thymus hesperites, commonly known as Narrowleaf Thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are used in culinary and medicinal applications due to their essential oils.

Thymus hesperites is a hardy species that is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental and practical value.
