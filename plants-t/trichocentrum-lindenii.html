Trichocentrum lindenii

Trichocentrum lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features long, pendant inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and good air circulation.

Trichocentrum lindenii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
