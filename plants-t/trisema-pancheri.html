Trisema pancheri

Trisema pancheri, commonly known as the five-leaf chiretta, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations.
