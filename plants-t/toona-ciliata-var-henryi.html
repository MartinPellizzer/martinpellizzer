Toona ciliata var. henryi

Toona ciliata var. henryi, commonly known as the Chinese toona or Henry's toona, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by edible, spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.

The species is also valued for its timber, which is lightweight and suitable for construction and furniture-making.
