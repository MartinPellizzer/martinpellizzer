Tripteris gracilis

Tripteris gracilis, commonly known as the slender tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are often divided into narrow, linear segments. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Tripteris gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
