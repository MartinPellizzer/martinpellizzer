Tragopogon kurdistanicus

Tragopogon kurdistanicus, commonly known as the Kurdish goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Kurdistan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a robust stem with hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that develop into long, slender seed heads. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Tragopogon kurdistanicus is often found in alpine and subalpine zones, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
