Trachelium × halteratum

Trachelium × halteratum, commonly known as the hybrid garden trachelium, is a perennial plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
