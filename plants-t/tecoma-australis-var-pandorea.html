Tecoma australis var. pandorea

Tecoma australis var. pandorea, commonly known as pandorea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and climbing habit, often reaching several meters in length. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

It is well-suited for ornamental use in gardens and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
