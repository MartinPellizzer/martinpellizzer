Tephroseris atropurpurea

Tephroseris atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple tephroseris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy purple to pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
