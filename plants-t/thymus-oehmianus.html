Thymus oehmianus

Thymus oehmianus, commonly known as the Oehmian thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny pink or purple flowers in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus oehmianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
