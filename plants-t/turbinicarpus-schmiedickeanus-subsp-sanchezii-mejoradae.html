Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp. sanchezii-mejoradae

Turbinicarpus schmiedickeanus subsp.

sanchezii-mejoradae, commonly known as the Sanchezi Mejorada Turbinicarpus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically covered in a layer of white, waxy hair, which helps reduce water loss.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, usually in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
