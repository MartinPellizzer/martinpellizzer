Tephrosia hirta

Tephrosia hirta, commonly known as hairy tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. Tephrosia hirta is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
