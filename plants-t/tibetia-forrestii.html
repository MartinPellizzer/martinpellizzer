Tibetia forrestii

Tibetia forrestii, commonly known as the Tibetan snow lotus, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low oxygen levels.

Due to its habitat and rarity, it is considered an endangered species and is protected in its native region.
