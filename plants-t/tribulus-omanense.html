Tribulus omanense

Tribulus omanense, commonly known as the Oman spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Zygophyllaceae and is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. Tribulus omanense is often found in sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine in the region.
