Trollius yunnanensis

Trollius yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan globeflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features a short, stout stem and palmate leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
