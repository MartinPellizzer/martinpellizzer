Teucrium cyrenaicum

Teucrium cyrenaicum, commonly known as Cyprus germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet, typically blooming in summer. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and coastal areas.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued plant in horticulture and traditional medicine.
