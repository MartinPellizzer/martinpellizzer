Terminalia ulexoides

Terminalia ulexoides, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
