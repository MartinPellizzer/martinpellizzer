Teucrium microphyllum

Teucrium microphyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in mountainous areas. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants.

Teucrium microphyllum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
