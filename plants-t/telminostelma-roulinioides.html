Telminostelma roulinioides

Telminostelma roulinioides, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, often rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a dense inflorescence. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
