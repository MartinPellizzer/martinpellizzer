Tupistra cavaleriei

Tupistra cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cape York hakea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by woody, cone-like fruits.

Tupistra cavaleriei is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.
