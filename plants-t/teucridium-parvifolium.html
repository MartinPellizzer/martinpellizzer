Teucridium parvifolium

Teucridium parvifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved teucridium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
