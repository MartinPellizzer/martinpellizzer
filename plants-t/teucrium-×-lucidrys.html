Teucrium × lucidrys

Teucrium × lucidrys, commonly known as golden teucrium, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright growth with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Teucrium × lucidrys is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
