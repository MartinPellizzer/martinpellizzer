Thymelaea dioica subsp. granatensis

Thymelaea dioica subsp.

granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense thymelaea, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a distinctive glandular appearance. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
