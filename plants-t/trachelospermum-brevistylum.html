Trachelospermum brevistylum

Trachelospermum brevistylum, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a woody vine characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Trachelospermum brevistylum is also valued for its ability to climb and cover trellises or walls with its lush foliage.
