Talinum patens var. variegatum

Talinum patens var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated spider plant, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant leaf coloration.

Talinum patens var. variegatum is relatively low maintenance and makes an excellent addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
