Tradescantia plusiantha

Tradescantia plusiantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers and its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners looking to add color and texture to their landscapes.
