Thiloa glaucocarpa

Thiloa glaucocarpa, commonly known as the blue-berried thiloa, is a member of the Ericaceae family, typically found in temperate regions.

Thiloa glaucocarpa thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.
