Thuranthos macrocarpus

Thuranthos macrocarpus, commonly known as the giant pod tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that can grow up to several feet in length. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value.

Thuranthos macrocarpus is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
