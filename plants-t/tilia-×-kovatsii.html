Tilia × kovatsii

Tilia × kovatsii, commonly known as the Kovatsii lime tree, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Tilia cordata and Tilia europaea.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5–10 cm in diameter. The flowers are small, clustered in drooping inflorescences, and produce a sweet, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. It produces small, round, green fruits that mature into brown, woody capsules.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types and climates.
