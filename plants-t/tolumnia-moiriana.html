Tolumnia moiriana

Tolumnia moiriana, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, anchoring itself with its aerial roots. Its leaves are strap-like and dark green, often covered in a waxy coating.

Tolumnia moiriana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
