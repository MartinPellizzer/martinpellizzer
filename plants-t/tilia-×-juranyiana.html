Tilia × juranyiana

Tilia × juranyiana, commonly known as the Hungarian linden, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5–10 cm in length and have a serrated margin. The tree produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers in clustered inflorescences, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age, and it is often found in temperate regions across Europe.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
