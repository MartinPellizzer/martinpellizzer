Thymus minussinensis

Thymus minussinensis, commonly known as the Chinese thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically features small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are fragrant and often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their antiseptic and digestive properties.

Thymus minussinensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and medicinal plant collections.
