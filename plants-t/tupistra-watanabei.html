Tupistra watanabei

Tupistra watanabei, commonly known as the Japanese holly or Japanese yew, is a species of flowering plant in the family Taxaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and coniferous appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically pollinated by wind. Its berries are red and contain a single seed, which is dispersed by birds.
