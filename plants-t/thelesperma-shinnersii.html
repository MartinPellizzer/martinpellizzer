Thelesperma shinnersii

Thelesperma shinnersii, commonly known as Shinners' filaree, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a basal rosette.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
