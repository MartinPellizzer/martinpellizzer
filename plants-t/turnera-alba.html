Turnera alba

Turnera alba, commonly known as white turnera, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Turneraceae and is characterized by its distinctive white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are often clustered at the ends of the branches, making it a visually striking plant in its natural habitat.
