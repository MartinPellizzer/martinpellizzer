Tribrachia odoratissima

Tribrachia odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that emit a subtle, fragrant aroma, particularly when touched or exposed to moisture. Due to its ornamental value and pleasant scent, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
