Thalictrum speciosissimum subsp. speciosissimum

Thalictrum speciosissimum subsp.

speciosissimum, commonly known as the showy meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States. It features delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.

Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and a height ranging from 1 to 3 meters.
