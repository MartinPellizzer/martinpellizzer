Tisonia velutina

Tisonia velutina, commonly known as the velvet tisonia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Tisonia velutina is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
