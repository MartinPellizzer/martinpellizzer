Tococa chuivensis var. ecuadorensis

Tococa chuivensis var. ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian tococa, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters and are followed by red, fleshy berries.

The plant is noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
