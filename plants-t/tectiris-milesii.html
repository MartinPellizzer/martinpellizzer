Tectiris milesii

Tectiris milesii, commonly known as the Mexican tectaria, is a species of fern native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Tectiris milesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
