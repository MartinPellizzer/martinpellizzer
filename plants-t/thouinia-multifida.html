Thouinia multifida

Thouinia multifida, commonly known as the featherleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and vibrant blooms.
