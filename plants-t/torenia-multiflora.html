Torenia multiflora

Torenia multiflora, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from bright orange to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Torenia multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
