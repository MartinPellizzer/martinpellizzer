Tithymalus denticulatus

Tithymalus denticulatus, commonly known as the toothed tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are finely toothed, giving it its common name, and it is often found in temperate regions.

Tithymalus denticulatus is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
