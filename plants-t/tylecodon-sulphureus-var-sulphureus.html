Tylecodon sulphureus var. sulphureus

Tylecodon sulphureus var. sulphureus, commonly known as the yellow moonveld succulent, is a member of the Aizoaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.
