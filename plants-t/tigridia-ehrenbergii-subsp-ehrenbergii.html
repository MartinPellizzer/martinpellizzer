Tigridia ehrenbergii subsp. ehrenbergii

Tigridia ehrenbergii subsp.

ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Mexican fairy lantern, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange.

The plant produces upright, branching stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are borne on one-sided spike-like inflorescences, and each bloom has three petals with a distinctive spurred petal at the base.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
