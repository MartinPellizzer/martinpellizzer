Triticum durum var. ferereichenbachii

Triticum durum var. ferereichenbachii, commonly known as Ferereichenbachii durum wheat, is a rare variant of durum wheat characterized by its unique genetic traits.

This variety is notable for its distinct morphological features, including its compact growth habit and specific spike morphology. It exhibits a high level of genetic diversity, making it valuable for breeding programs aimed at improving wheat resilience. The plant's botanical characteristics include tough, straw-colored grains and a shorter stalk compared to standard durum wheat.

Its rarity and genetic uniqueness contribute to its significance in both agricultural research and conservation efforts.
