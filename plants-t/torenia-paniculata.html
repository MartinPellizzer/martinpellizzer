Torenia paniculata

Torenia paniculata, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces clusters of small, showy blooms on branching inflorescences, which give it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture.

Torenia paniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
