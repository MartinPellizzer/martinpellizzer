Thyrsodium puberulum

Thyrsodium puberulum, commonly known as the silky thyrsodium, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, dry fruits.
