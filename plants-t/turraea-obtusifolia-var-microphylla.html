Turraea obtusifolia var. microphylla

Turraea obtusifolia var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved turraea, is a tropical shrub or small tree belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically small and pointed. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers, which are followed by orange to red fleshy fruits. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
