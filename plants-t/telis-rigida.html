Telis rigida

Telis rigida, commonly known as the rigid telis, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, upright leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale pink. Its botanical classification places it in the family Asteraceae, also known as the daisy family.

Telis rigida is often found in dry environments and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.
