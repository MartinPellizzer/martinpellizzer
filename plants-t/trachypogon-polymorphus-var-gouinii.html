Trachypogon polymorphus var. gouinii

Trachypogon polymorphus var. gouinii, commonly known as the Gouin's trachypogon, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, spikelets. It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils.

This variant is distinguished by its morphological variations compared to the typical form of Trachypogon polymorphus.
