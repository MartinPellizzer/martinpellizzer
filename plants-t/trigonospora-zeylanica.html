Trigonospora zeylanica

Trigonospora zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

The fruit is typically yellow or orange when ripe and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
