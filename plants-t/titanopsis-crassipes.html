Titanopsis crassipes

Titanopsis crassipes, commonly known as the giant ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. Its growth habit is low and spreading, forming dense mats that can cover large areas.

Titanopsis crassipes is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
