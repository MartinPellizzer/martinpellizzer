Thelymitra villosa

Thelymitra villosa, commonly known as the woolly sun-orchid, is an Australian terrestrial orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, leaf-like structures that cover the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in heathlands and dry grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers, which are usually yellow or orange, bloom in spring and are adorned with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.
