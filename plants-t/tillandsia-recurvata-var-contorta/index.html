Tillandsia Recurvata Var. Contorta

Air plant, scientifically known as Tillandsia recurvata var. contorta, is a unique member of the Bromeliaceae family that thrives without soil. This striking plant is characterized by its spiral arrangement of narrow, pointed leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it has adapted to grow in diverse environments, including trees and rocky surfaces. Its ability to absorb moisture through its leaves makes it an ideal choice for indoor cultivation. With its low maintenance requirements and eye-catching foliage, it has become a popular ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
