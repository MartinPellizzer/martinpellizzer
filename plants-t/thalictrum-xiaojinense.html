Thalictrum xiaojinense

Thalictrum xiaojinense, commonly known as the Xiaojin meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
