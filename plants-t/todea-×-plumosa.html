Todea × plumosa

Todea × plumosa, commonly known as the feather fern, is a hybrid species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a member of the family Todeaceae and is known for its delicate, feathery fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are bioluminescent, a rare trait among ferns, which enhances its ornamental appeal.

Todea × plumosa is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic beauty and ability to thrive in controlled indoor settings.
