Tabernaemontana laeta var. pubiflora

Tabernaemontana laeta var. pubiflora, commonly known as the Happy Tree or the White Star Flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
