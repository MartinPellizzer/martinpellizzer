Tetraria rottboellii

Tetraria rottboellii, commonly known as the slender clubmoss, is a small, evergreen plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and produces spore-bearing structures called strobili. Tetraria rottboellii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in containers or as part of a woodland garden.
