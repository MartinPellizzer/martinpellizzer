Tyleria floribunda

Tyleria floribunda, commonly known as the red-flowered daisy, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bright red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces slender, branching stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Tyleria floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
