Tectaria mexicana

Tectaria mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Tectaria mexicana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
