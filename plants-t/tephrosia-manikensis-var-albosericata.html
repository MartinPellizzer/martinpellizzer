Tephrosia manikensis var. albosericata

Tephrosia manikensis var. albosericata, commonly known as the white sericea tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its seeds are encased in pods and are often dispersed by wind or animals.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
