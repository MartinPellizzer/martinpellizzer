Tragopogon nervulosus

Tragopogon nervulosus, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces a unique, elongated seed head that resembles a beard, giving it its common name. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Tragopogon nervulosus is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
