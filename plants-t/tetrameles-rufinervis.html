Tetrameles rufinervis

Tetrameles rufinervis, commonly known as the red-veined tetrameles, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its woody structure and palm-like appearance, with large, palmately lobed leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
