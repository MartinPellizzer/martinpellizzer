Thymus moesiacus var. microcalyx

Thymus moesiacus var. microcalyx, commonly known as the small-leaved thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features dense, compact growth with small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both culinary and horticultural use.
