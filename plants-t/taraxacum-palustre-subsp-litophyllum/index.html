Taraxacum Palustre Subsp. Litophyllum

Taraxacum palustre subsp. litophyllum is a rare and delicate plant species found in moist, rocky habitats. It belongs to the dandelion family and is characterized by its small, pale yellow flowers and hairy stems. This subspecies is adapted to grow in nutrient-poor environments, often on limestone or sandy soils. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting its habitat is crucial for preserving this unique botanical treasure.
