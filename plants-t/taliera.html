Taliera

Taliera, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. Taliera grows in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and rocky areas.
