Talasium montevidense

Talasium montevidense, commonly known as the Montevideo talasium, is a flowering plant native to Uruguay and southern Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
