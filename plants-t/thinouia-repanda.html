Thinouia repanda

Thinouia repanda, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread along the forest floor. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a leathery texture.

Thinouia repanda thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
