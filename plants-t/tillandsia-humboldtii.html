Tillandsia humboldtii

Tillandsia humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that can store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, tropical environments. It produces striking inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators.

Tillandsia humboldtii is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
