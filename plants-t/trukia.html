Trukia

Trukia, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are typically green and grow from a bulbous underground stem. Trukia thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
