Torenia alata

Torenia alata, commonly known as the winged torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive wing-like structure on the petals. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Torenia alata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
