Taeniophyllum robustum

Taeniophyllum robustum, commonly known as the hairy pteridium, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The fronds are typically evergreen and can grow up to several feet in length, making it a notable ornamental plant in certain regions.
