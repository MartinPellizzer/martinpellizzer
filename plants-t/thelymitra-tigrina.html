Thelymitra tigrina

Thelymitra tigrina, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves and its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs during the cooler months, usually from late winter to early spring.

Thelymitra tigrina is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
