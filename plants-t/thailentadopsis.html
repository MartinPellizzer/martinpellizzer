Thailentadopsis

Thailentadopsis, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. Thailentadopsis species are typically herbaceous perennials, often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and as houseplants.
