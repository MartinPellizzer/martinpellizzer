Tragacantha elmaluensis

Tragacantha elmaluensis, commonly known as the Elmalu tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces a gummy resin, which has been used traditionally in various applications.

Its botanical features include a woody stem, trifoliate leaves, and pod-like fruits containing several seeds.
