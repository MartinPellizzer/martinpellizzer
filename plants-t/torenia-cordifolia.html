Torenia cordifolia

Torenia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual or perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Torenia cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
