Tromotriche revoluta var. tigrida

Tromotriche revoluta var. tigrida, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tiger-striped petals that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting dark border. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, producing a showy display that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Tromotriche revoluta var. tigrida requires specific care, including high humidity and indirect sunlight, to thrive in cultivation.
