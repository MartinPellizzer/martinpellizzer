Tulipa zonneveldii

Tulipa zonneveldii, commonly known as the Zonneveld tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces showy blooms in early spring, typically from March to May, depending on the climate. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This tulip is known for its robust growth and ability to naturalize over time.
