Tetrastigma vitiense

Tetrastigma vitiense, commonly known as the Vitiense grapevine, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its tendrils and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into berries. It is often used in traditional medicine and has potential applications in modern pharmacology.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and scientific study.
