Thunia bracteata

Thunia bracteata, commonly known as the bracted thunia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful spathes that enclose the inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a spiral pattern within the spathe. Its leaves are typically broad and arrow-shaped, with a glossy surface.

Thunia bracteata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates due to its vibrant floral display.
