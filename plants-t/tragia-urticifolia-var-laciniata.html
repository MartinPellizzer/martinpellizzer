Tragia urticifolia var. laciniata

Tragia urticifolia var. laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf tragia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, almost fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecological adaptation.
