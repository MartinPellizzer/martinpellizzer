Teucrium polium subsp. aguilasense

Teucrium polium subsp.

aguilasense, commonly known as Spanish germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its fragrant herbs.

This subspecies is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
