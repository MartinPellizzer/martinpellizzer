Tetraria purpurea

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescence and purple-tinged bracts. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a hairy texture that aids in water retention.
