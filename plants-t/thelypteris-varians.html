Thelypteris varians

Thelypteris varians, commonly known as the variable shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris varians is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its lush, fern-like appearance.
