Tillandsia nappii

Tillandsia nappii, commonly known as the Nappi air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture from the air. Tillandsia nappii produces striking inflorescences with vibrant purple or red flowers, which bloom once in its lifetime.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
