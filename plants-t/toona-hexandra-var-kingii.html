Toona hexandra var. kingii

Toona hexandra var. kingii, commonly known as the king's toona, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The tree produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local cultures.
