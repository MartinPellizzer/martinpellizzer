Thymus × dichtlianus

Thymus × dichtlianus, commonly known as the narrowleaf thyme, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.

The plant is also valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, similar to other thyme species.
