Tetraria rottboellioides

Tetraria rottboellioides, commonly known as the yellow lichen, is a species of lichen that belongs to the family Parmeliaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, crust-like growth form and a yellowish to orange-brown coloration. This lichen is of interest in ecological studies due to its role as an indicator species for air quality.
