Tetrastigma yunnanense f. glabrum

Tetrastigma yunnanense f. glabrum, commonly known as the glabrous liana, is a variant of the Tetrastigma yunnanense species, which belongs to the Vitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves with multiple leaflets. The plant typically exhibits a glabrous (smooth) surface on its stems and leaves, distinguishing it from other forms of the species. It is native to regions in China, particularly Yunnan Province, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

This plant is often found in forest understories, where it utilizes its climbing habit to reach sunlight.
