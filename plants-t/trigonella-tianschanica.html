Trigonella tianschanica

Trigonella tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces lens-shaped seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climatic conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological and agricultural studies in its native habitat.
