Trachelospermum stans

Trachelospermum stans, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance.

Trachelospermum stans is also valued for its ability to climb and cover trellises or walls effectively.
