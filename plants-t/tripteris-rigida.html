Tripteris rigida

Tripteris rigida, commonly known as the rigid tripteris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tripteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Tripteris rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
