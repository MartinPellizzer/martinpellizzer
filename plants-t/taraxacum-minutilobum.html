Taraxacum minutilobum

Taraxacum minutilobum, commonly known as the slender-leaved dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and a single, upright stem that bears a bright yellow flower head. The plant produces a capsule that releases numerous white, wind-dispersed seeds. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Taraxacum minutilobum is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
