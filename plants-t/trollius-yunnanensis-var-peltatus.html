Trollius yunnanensis var. peltatus

Trollius yunnanensis var. peltatus, commonly known as the Yunnan globeflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features showy, yellow, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variant is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ecological importance in native habitats.
