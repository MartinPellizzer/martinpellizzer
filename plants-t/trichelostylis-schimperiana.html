Trichelostylis schimperiana

Trichelostylis schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, thin petals that resemble legs. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Trichelostylis schimperiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
