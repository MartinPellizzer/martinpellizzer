Trichomanes ferrugineum

Trichomanes ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green with a reddish or rusty hue on the underside. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in humid, indoor environments.
