Thymbra capitata

Thymbra capitata, commonly known as wild thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has slender, branched stems and is characterized by its aromatic, mint-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Thymbra capitata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
