Talinum porphyreum

Talinum porphyreum, commonly known as purple leaf talinum, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green foliage, which adds a dramatic visual contrast to gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Talinum porphyreum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens in suitable climates.
