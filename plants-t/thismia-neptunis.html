Thismia neptunis

Thismia neptunis, commonly known as the Neptune's Thismia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Thismiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly colored flowers. The plant is typically found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests. It has a short, stocky stem with a single, showy inflorescence at the top.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
