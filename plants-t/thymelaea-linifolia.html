Thymelaea linifolia

Thymelaea linifolia, commonly known as the silky thymelaea, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Thymelaea linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
