Tilia semicostata

Tilia semicostata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf linden, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a narrow margin. The tree produces small, fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its bark is smooth and greyish-brown, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
