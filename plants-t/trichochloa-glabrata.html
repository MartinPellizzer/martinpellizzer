Trichochloa glabrata

Trichochloa glabrata, commonly known as the smooth feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry foliage and slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Trichochloa glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its delicate, grass-like appearance.
