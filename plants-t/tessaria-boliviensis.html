Tessaria boliviensis

Tessaria boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian tessaria, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

Tessaria boliviensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
