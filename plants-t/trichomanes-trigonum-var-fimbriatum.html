Trichomanes trigonum var. fimbriatum

Trichomanes trigonum var. fimbriatum, commonly known as the fringed maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has delicate, finely divided fronds with a distinctive fringed appearance along the edges. Its spores are produced in sori located on the underside of the fronds.

The botanical features of this fern make it a popular species for cultivation in shaded, humid environments.
