Tulotis finetiana

Tulotis finetiana, commonly known as the Madagascar lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Madagascar.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy white or pink flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded location.

Tulotis finetiana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
