Tristellateia madagascariensis f. elliotii

Tristellateia madagascariensis f. elliotii, commonly known as the Elliot's star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that display a vibrant combination of colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

This orchid is notable for its ornamental value and is highly sought after by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
