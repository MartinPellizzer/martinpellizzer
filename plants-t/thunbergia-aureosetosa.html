Thunbergia aureosetosa

Thunbergia aureosetosa, commonly known as the golden-haired thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive golden hue along the leaf margins. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Thunbergia aureosetosa is a climbing vine that can reach lengths of several meters, making it suitable for trellises or vertical gardens.
