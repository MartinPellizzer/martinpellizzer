Triglochin concinna var. deserticola

Triglochin concinna var. deserticola, commonly known as the desert spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in saline or alkaline soils, often in wetlands or along the edges of salt flats.
