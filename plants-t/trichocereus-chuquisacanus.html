Trichocereus chuquisacanus

Trichocereus chuquisacanus, commonly known as the Chuquisaca cactus, is a species of cactus native to Bolivia.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and produces large, white flowers that bloom at night. The plant has a spiny exterior and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its growth habit and floral display make it a distinctive feature in desert landscapes.
