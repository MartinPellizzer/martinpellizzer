Thelasis perpusilla

Thelasis perpusilla, commonly known as the small thelasis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers with five petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Thelasis perpusilla is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
