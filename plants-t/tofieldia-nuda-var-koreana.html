Tofieldia nuda var. koreana

Tofieldia nuda var. koreana, commonly known as the Korean false lily, is a variant of the tofieldia plant native to Korea.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
