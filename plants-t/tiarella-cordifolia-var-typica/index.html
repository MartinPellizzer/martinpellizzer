Tiarella Cordifolia Var. Typica

Tiarella cordifolia var. typica, also known as the heartleaf foamflower, is a charming perennial plant native to eastern North America. It is prized for its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in woodland gardens for its ornamental value. Its ability to spread and form dense mats makes it an excellent ground cover. Despite its modest appearance, the heartleaf foamflower is a resilient and hardy addition to any garden.
