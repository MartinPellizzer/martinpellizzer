Tecomaria Ricasoliana

Ricasol's tecomaria, scientifically known as Tecomaria ricasoliana, is a rare and striking plant native to the Canary Islands. This evergreen shrub is celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange, adding a dramatic touch to any garden. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. The plant's unique appearance and limited distribution have made it a subject of interest for botanists and plant lovers alike. With its ornamental value and ecological significance, Ricasol's tecomaria remains a symbol of the island's rich botanical heritage.
