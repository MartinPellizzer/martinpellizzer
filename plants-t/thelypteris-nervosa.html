Thelypteris nervosa

Thelypteris nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with finely divided pinnae. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots, which helps anchor it to substrates.

The leaves are usually evergreen, and the plant reproduces via spores rather than seeds.
