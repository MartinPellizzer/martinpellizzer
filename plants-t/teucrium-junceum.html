Teucrium junceum

Teucrium junceum, commonly known as slender germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Teucrium junceum is often used in herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
