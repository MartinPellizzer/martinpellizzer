Thelypteris villosipes

Thelypteris villosipes, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in various cultures.

The fronds are usually bipinnate, with a complex, feather-like structure that contributes to its ornamental value.
