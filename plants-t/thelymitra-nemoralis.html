Thelymitra nemoralis

Thelymitra nemoralis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that is usually pale yellow to orange in color. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Thelymitra nemoralis is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in areas with well-drained soil.
