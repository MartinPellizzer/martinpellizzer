Tragacantha haematocarpa

Tragacantha haematocarpa, commonly known as the crimson globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive globular seed pods. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval-shaped and hairy.
