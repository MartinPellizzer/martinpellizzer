Trichocentrum ostenianum

Trichocentrum ostenianum, commonly known as the Ostenian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, thin pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum ostenianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical greenhouse setting.
