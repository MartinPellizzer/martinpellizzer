Teucrium polium subsp. purpurascens

Teucrium polium subsp.

purpurascens, commonly known as purple tarragon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, gray-green hairs.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are narrow, aromatic, and often have a slight serrated edge.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
