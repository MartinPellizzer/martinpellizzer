Turraea goetzei

Turraea goetzei, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange to red fleshy fruits containing several seeds.
