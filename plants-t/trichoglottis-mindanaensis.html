Trichoglottis mindanaensis

Trichoglottis mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao trichoglottis, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs and linear leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces inflorescences that bear small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Trichoglottis mindanaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
