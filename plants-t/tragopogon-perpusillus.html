Tragopogon perpusillus

Tragopogon perpusillus, commonly known as the small goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that are surrounded by a fringe of bracts, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture.

Tragopogon perpusillus is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
