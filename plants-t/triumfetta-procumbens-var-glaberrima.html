Triumfetta procumbens var. glaberrima

Triumfetta procumbens var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth triumfetta, is a low-growing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers, which are followed by distinctive, triangular, red or orange fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
