Trichocentrum brachyphyllum

Trichocentrum brachyphyllum, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a compact, often branched stem and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in two rows along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum brachyphyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
