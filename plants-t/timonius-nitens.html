Timonius nitens

Timonius nitens, commonly known as the white-tailed spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive white and green foliage. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are often clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in woodland or heathland environments.

Timonius nitens is known for its resilience and ability to adapt to various climatic conditions.
