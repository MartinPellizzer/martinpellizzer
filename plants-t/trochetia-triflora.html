Trochetia triflora

Trochetia triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters of three. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Trochetia triflora is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
