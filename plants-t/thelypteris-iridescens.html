Thelypteris iridescens

Thelypteris iridescens, commonly known as the iridescent shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The fronds are typically light green to bronze in color, with a metallic sheen that becomes more pronounced when exposed to light. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
