Trigonella koraiensis

Trigonella koraiensis, commonly known as Korean fenugreek, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces slender pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and streambanks.

Trigonella koraiensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
