Torenia racemosa

Torenia racemosa, commonly known as the whorled torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of flowers, which bloom sequentially from the base upward.

Torenia racemosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.
