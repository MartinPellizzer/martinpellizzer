Tephrosia totta

Tephrosia totta, commonly known as African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and savannas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Tephrosia totta is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
