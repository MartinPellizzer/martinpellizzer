Triosteum fargesii

Triosteum fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese heartleaf, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive blue-green color. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Triosteum fargesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
