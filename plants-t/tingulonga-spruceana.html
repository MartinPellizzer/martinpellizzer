Tingulonga spruceana

Tingulonga spruceana, commonly known as the African spruce, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its conical shape and dense, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against harsh environmental conditions.

Tingulonga spruceana plays a significant role in the local ecosystem, offering habitat and food for various wildlife species.
