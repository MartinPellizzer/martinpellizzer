Tricalysia ovalifolia

Tricalysia ovalifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces striking yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom during the dry season. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Tricalysia ovalifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitats.
