Thalictrum foetidum lusus virens

Thalictrum foetidum lusus virens, commonly known as the green-flowered meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that create a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and streambanks.

Its unique foliage and subtle floral display make it a notable addition to botanical gardens and natural habitats.
