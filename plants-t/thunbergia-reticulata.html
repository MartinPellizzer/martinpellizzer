Thunbergia reticulata

Thunbergia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in the early morning. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia reticulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
