Thelypteris Orizabae

Thelypteris orizabae is a rare fern species native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. This plant is known for its unique fronds that display a striking pattern of serrations and color variations. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and faces significant conservation challenges. Studying Thelypteris orizabae provides valuable insights into the biodiversity of high-altitude ecosystems.
