Thymus longicaulis var. moesiacus

Thymus longicaulis var. moesiacus, commonly known as the Moesia thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features compact, low-growing foliage with aromatic, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
