Tephrosia palmeri

Tephrosia palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers, which are often fragrant. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a deep root system adapted to drought conditions.

Tephrosia palmeri is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
