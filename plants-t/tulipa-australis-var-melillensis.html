Tulipa australis var. melillensis

Tulipa australis var. melillensis, commonly known as the Melillan tulip, is a compact, bulbous perennial native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It produces striking, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive black eye. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
