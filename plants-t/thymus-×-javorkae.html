Thymus × javorkae

Thymus × javorkae, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is fragrant and often used in culinary applications, while its hardy nature makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus × javorkae is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
