Triptilion berteroi

Triptilion berteroi, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Triptilion berteroi thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens and borders.
