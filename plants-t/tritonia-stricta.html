Tritonia stricta

Tritonia stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tritonia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Tritonia stricta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained conditions.
