Thunbergia subfulva

Thunbergia subfulva, commonly known as the yellow flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green in color, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.
