Thymus incertus

Thymus incertus, commonly known as the uncertain thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees.

Thymus incertus is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
