Tibouchina urvilleana var. glandulifera

Tibouchina urvilleana var. glandulifera, commonly known as the silverleaf tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
