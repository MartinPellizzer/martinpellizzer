Thyrsanthus luschnathii

Thyrsanthus luschnathii, commonly known as the Luschnath's thyrsanthus, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters called thyrses. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or subalpine regions.
