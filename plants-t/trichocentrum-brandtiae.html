Trichocentrum brandtiae

Trichocentrum brandtiae, commonly known as the Brandt's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with dark green, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white trichomes. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and long-lasting blooms.
