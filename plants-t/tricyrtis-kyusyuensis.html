Tricyrtis kyusyuensis

Tricyrtis kyusyuensis, commonly known as the Kyushu spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with a distinctive red or orange lip, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Tricyrtis kyusyuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
