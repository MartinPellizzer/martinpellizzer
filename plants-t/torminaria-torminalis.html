Torminaria torminalis

Torminaria torminalis, commonly known as the torminal bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Torminaria torminalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to grow in moderate climates.
