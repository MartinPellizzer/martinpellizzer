Tropaeolum concavum

Tropaeolum concavum, commonly known as the zigzag nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a wavy, zigzag edge. The plant produces bright orange or yellow flowers that are typically borne on slender stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Tropaeolum concavum is also valued for its edible leaves and flowers, which have a peppery taste similar to watercress.
