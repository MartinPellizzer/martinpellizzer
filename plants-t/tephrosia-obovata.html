Tephrosia obovata

Tephrosia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree with compound leaves and small, clustered white or yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry savannas and woodland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Tephrosia obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
