Tillandsia retrorsa

Tillandsia retrorsa, commonly known as the silver vane air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
