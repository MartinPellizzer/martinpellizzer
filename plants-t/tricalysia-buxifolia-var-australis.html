Tricalysia buxifolia var. australis

Tricalysia buxifolia var. australis, commonly known as the Australian boxwood, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and suitable for use in landscaping and horticultural gardens.
