Torralbasia

Torralbasia, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.
