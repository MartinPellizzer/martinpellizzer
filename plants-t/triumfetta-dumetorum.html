Triumfetta dumetorum

Triumfetta dumetorum, commonly known as the small-leaved triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a small shrub or subshrub with simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by small, round, greenish fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

The species is known for its adaptability and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
