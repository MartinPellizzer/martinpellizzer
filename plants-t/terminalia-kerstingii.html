Terminalia kerstingii

Terminalia kerstingii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round, fleshy fruits that are eaten by various animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Terminalia kerstingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
