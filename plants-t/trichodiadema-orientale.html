Trichodiadema orientale

Trichodiadema orientale, commonly known as the orange-flowered ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, typical of xerophytic plants. The plant produces vibrant orange flowers during the warmer months, adding visual appeal to its appearance. Its growth habit is compact, forming a low-growing rosette.

Trichodiadema orientale is well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments due to its drought tolerance.
