Teucrium haenseleri var. angustifolium

Teucrium haenseleri var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
