Tribulus ochroleucus var. perplexans

Tribulus ochroleucus var. perplexans, commonly known as the yellow-leafed tribulus, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing several seeds.
