Trillium tschonoskii f. morii

Trillium tschonoskii f. morii, commonly known as the Morii's white trillium, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories where it thrives in rich, well-drained soil. It produces a single, upright stem that bears the showy flowers, which bloom in early spring.
