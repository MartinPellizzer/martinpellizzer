Thalictrum deamii

Thalictrum deamii, commonly known as Deam's meadowrue, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring. It is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in moist, calcareous soils.

Thalictrum deamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
