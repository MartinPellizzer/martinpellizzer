Teucrium oxylepis f. marianum

Teucrium oxylepis f. marianum, commonly known as the Marian thyme, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It is often used in herbal remedies due to its essential oils and antioxidant properties.

The plant's compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ornamental ground cover.
