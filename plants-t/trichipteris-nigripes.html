It is characterized by its dark, wiry stems and delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Trichipteris nigripes plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small invertebrates and contributing to water quality through nutrient cycling.
