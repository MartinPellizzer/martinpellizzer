Thunbergia swynnertonii var. cordata

Thunbergia swynnertonii var. cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf thunbergia, is a climbing perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green and glossy, with a velvety texture. The flowers are trumpet-shaped, coming in shades of purple, pink, and white, and are borne in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
