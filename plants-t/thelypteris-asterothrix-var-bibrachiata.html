Thelypteris asterothrix var. bibrachiata

Thelypteris asterothrix var. bibrachiata, commonly known as the starry maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that exhibit a distinctive star-like pattern on the underside. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are bi-lobed, with a unique branching structure that sets it apart from other similar species.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
