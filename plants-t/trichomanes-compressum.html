Trichomanes compressum

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its small, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of trichomes. Trichomanes compressum is notable for its delicate appearance and its role in maintaining ecological balance in its native habitats.
