Teucrium aureum subsp. angustifolium

Teucrium aureum subsp.

angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners.
