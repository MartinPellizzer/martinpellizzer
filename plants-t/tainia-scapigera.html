Tainia scapigera

Tainia scapigera, commonly known as the scarlet beakviolet, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, violet-colored flowers that resemble a beak. Tainia scapigera prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a suitable addition to woodland gardens.
