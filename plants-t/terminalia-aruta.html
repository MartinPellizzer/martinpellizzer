Terminalia aruta

Terminalia aruta, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmate leaves. The tree produces small, white, clustered flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Terminalia aruta is often valued for its ornamental beauty and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
