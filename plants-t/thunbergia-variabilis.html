Thunbergia variabilis

Thunbergia variabilis, commonly known as the variable thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Thunbergia variabilis is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and hanging baskets due to its rapid growth and colorful display.
