Trigonella polyceratia var. sericea

Trigonella polyceratia var. sericea, commonly known as silverleaf fenugreek, is a herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Mediterranean and parts of Asia.

It features slender, branched stems covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, triangular seed pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being narrow and hairy.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
