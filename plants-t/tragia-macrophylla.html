Tragia macrophylla

Tragia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf tragia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a woody stem and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Tragia macrophylla is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
