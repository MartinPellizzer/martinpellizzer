Tillandsia capillaris f. typica

Tillandsia capillaris f. typica, commonly known as the slender leaf air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant that absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves rather than through roots. This species is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention. It thrives in humid environments and is native to regions of Central and South America.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
