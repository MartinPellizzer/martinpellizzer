Taraxacum calocapitatum

Taraxacum calocapitatum, commonly known as the showy dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flower heads and distinctive, hairy leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. Its life cycle includes a rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that produces a single, showy inflorescence.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
