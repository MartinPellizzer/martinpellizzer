Trichosanthes celebica

Trichosanthes celebica, commonly known as the Celebes snake fruit, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and squash. The plant produces large, yellow-orange fruits with a rough, spiny exterior. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is edible, though its taste is often described as bitter and its texture similar to a raw apple.
