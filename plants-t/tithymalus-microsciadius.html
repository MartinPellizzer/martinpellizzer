Tithymalus microsciadius

Tithymalus microsciadius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It is native to regions of southern Europe and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
