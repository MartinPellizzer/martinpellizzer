Taenitis samarensis

Taenitis samarensis, commonly known as the Samarensis fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its spores are released from small, clustered structures called sorus.
