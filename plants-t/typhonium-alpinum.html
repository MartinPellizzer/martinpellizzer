Typhonium alpinum

Typhonium alpinum, commonly known as the Alpine arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf known as a spathe. Typhonium alpinum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in high-altitude forests.
