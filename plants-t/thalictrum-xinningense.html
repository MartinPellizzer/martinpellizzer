Thalictrum xinningense

Thalictrum xinningense, commonly known as the Xinning meadow rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It has a short flowering period in late spring to early summer.

Thalictrum xinningense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
