Thelypteris septempedalis

Thelypteris septempedalis, commonly known as the seven-veined wood fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and stream sides. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris septempedalis exhibits a complex vascular system that supports its growth in humid, nutrient-rich soils.
