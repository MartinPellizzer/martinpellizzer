Trachymene lanceolata var. conferta

Trachymene lanceolata var. conferta, commonly known as the clustered false millefoil, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to theApiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense umbels, giving it a delicate appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
