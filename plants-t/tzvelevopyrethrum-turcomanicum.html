Tzvelevopyrethrum turcomanicum

Tzvelevopyrethrum turcomanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow petals and a central disk. The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide, which is a key feature of its botanical significance. It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its morphology includes upright stems and narrow, linear leaves, making it distinct within its genus.
