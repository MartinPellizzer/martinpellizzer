Teucrium × conquense nothosubsp.

conquense, commonly known as the hybrid germander, is a member of the Lamiaceae family. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves that are often gray-green in color.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to ornamental horticulture.
