Tithymalus platyphyllos subsp. literatus

Tithymalus platyphyllos subsp.

literatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tithymalus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

The leaves are linear and often have a distinctive, almost script-like pattern, giving the species its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

This subspecies is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
