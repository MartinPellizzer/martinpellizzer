Taeniophyllum tamianum

Taeniophyllum tamianum, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the tiny orchid, is a species of epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clustered, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
