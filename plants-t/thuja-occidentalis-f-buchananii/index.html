Thuja Occidentalis F. Buchananii

Thuja occidentalis f. buchananii is a unique cultivar of the arborvitae plant known for its distinctive shape and ornamental value. This variety features a compact, globe-like form that makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and hedges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to various climates. The plant's evergreen foliage adds year-round color and texture to outdoor spaces. Its slow growth rate and disease resistance make it a low-maintenance option for gardeners seeking aesthetic appeal.
