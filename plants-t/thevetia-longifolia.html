Thevetia longifolia

Thevetia longifolia, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ornamental value.

Thevetia longifolia is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
