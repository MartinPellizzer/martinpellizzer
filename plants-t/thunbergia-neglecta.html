Thunbergia neglecta

Thunbergia neglecta, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Thunbergia neglecta is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and regular watering.
