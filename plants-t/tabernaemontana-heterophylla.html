Tabernaemontana heterophylla

Tabernaemontana heterophylla, commonly known as the variable tabernanthe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually alternate and ovate, with a glossy surface.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
