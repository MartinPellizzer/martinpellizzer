Thyrsodium salzmannianum

Thyrsodium salzmannianum, commonly known as Salzmann's thrysoideae, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters called thyrses. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.
