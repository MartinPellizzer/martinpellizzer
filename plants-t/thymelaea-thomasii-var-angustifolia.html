Thymelaea thomasii var. angustifolia

Thymelaea thomasii var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf thymelaea, is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in spring. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface and a slightly aromatic scent. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
