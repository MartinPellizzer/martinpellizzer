Thymus × ruiz-latorrei

Thymus × ruiz-latorrei, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces pink to violet flowers in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and ability to form dense mats.
