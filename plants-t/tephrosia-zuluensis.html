Tephrosia zuluensis

Tephrosia zuluensis, commonly known as Zulu senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters.
