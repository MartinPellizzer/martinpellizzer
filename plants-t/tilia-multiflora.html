Tilia multiflora

Tilia multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese linden, is a deciduous tree native to China and parts of Korea.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in gardens and parks.
