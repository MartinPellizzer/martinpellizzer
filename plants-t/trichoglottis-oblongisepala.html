Trichoglottis oblongisepala

Trichoglottis oblongisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with broad, waxy sepals and petals in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichoglottis oblongisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, tropical climates.
