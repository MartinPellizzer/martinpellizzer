Thalictrum dunense

Thalictrum dunense, commonly known as the Duneland meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Thalictrum dunense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in natural habitats.
