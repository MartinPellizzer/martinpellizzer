Tecomaria

Tecomaria, commonly known as the blue mist shrub, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant white or blue flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a mist-like appearance. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Tecomaria is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
