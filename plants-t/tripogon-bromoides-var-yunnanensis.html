Tripogon bromoides var. yunnanensis

Tripogon bromoides var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan tripogon, is a grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often tinged with red or purple.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
