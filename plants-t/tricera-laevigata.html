Tricera laevigata

Tricera laevigata, commonly known as the smooth-trichoid fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and compound leaves with a smooth, leathery texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Tricera laevigata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
