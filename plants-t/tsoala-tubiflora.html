Tsoala tubiflora

Tsoala tubiflora, commonly known as the African water lily, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the sediment in wetlands.

Tsoala tubiflora plays an important role in water filtration and ecosystem balance.
