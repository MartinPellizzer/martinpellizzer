Trilomisa undulata

Trilomisa undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, undulating leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Trilomisa undulata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
