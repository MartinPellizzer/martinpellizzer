Terebinthina tillaeoides

Terebinthina tillaeoides, commonly known as the desert broom, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
