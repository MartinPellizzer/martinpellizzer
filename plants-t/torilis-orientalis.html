Torilis orientalis

Torilis orientalis, commonly known as the oriental hedge parsley, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
