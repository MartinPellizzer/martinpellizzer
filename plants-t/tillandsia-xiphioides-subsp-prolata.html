Tillandsia xiphioides subsp. prolata

Tillandsia xiphioides subsp.

prolata, commonly known as the silver vase tillandsia, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family. It is an epiphytic plant native to Central and South America, typically found growing on trees in humid environments.

The plant features a rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that form a vase-like structure, which helps collect and retain moisture. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, emerging from the center of the rosette.

This species is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
