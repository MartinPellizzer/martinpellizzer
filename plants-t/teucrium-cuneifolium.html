Teucrium cuneifolium

Teucrium cuneifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, grayish-white hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, narrow, and often have a slightly fuzzy texture. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically arranged in dense spikes, blooming in shades of blue to purple.

This plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
