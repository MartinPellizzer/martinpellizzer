Thuja gracilifolia

Thuja gracilifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved arborvitae, is a coniferous evergreen native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, feather-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Thuja gracilifolia is often used in landscaping for its dense, columnar growth habit and ornamental value.
