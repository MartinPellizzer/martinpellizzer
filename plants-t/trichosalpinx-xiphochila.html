Trichosalpinx xiphochila

Trichosalpinx xiphochila, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet and purple flowers, which are typically arranged in dense spikes. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a cluster of leaves and flowers. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environments.

Trichosalpinx xiphochila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
