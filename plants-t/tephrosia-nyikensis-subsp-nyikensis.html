Tephrosia nyikensis subsp.

nyikensis, commonly known as the Nyikensis tephrosia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant typically grows in open woodland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive blue or purple coloration.
