Tragacantha helmii

Tragacantha helmii, commonly known as the Ethiopian tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by elongated, pod-like fruits. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, heart-shaped leaflets.

Tragacantha helmii is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important species in its native ecosystem.
