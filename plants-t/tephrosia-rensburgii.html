Tephrosia rensburgii

Tephrosia rensburgii, commonly known as Rensburg's tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with multiple leaflets that are often hairy.
