Trichonema purpurascens var. rectifolium

Trichonema purpurascens var. rectifolium, commonly known as the purple-stemmed trichonema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often display a reddish or purplish hue, especially under stress or in full sun. The leaves are typically narrow and lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface that aids in reducing water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique coloration and drought tolerance.
