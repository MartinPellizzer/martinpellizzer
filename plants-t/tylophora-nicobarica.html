Tylophora nicobarica

Tylophora nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar star vine, is a climbing plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
