Thliphthisa saxicola

Thliphthisa saxicola, commonly known as the rock thliphthisa, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Thliphthisa saxicola is valued for its resilience and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
