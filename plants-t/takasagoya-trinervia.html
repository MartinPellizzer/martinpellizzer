Takasagoya trinervia

Takasagoya trinervia, commonly known as the three-veined fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with three distinct veins. The fronds are usually short and upright, with a soft, feathery appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
