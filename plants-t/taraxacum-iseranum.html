Taraxacum iseranum

Taraxacum iseranum, commonly known as the Iseran dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and distinctive lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces a capsule containing numerous small seeds that are dispersed by wind.
