Thelymitra rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal regions.

Thelymitra rubricaulis is a relatively rare species, with limited distribution across the eastern and southern parts of Australia.
