Threlkeldia brevicuspis

Threlkeldia brevicuspis, commonly known as the short-spined threlkeldia, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
