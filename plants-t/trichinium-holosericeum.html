Trichinium holosericeum

Trichinium holosericeum, commonly known as the silky trichinium, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its stems are covered in fine, sericeous (silky) hairs, which give it its common name.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
