Thunbergia prostrata

Thunbergia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a trailing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Thunbergia prostrata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
