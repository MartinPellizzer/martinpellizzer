Thymelaea hirsuta var. vulgaris

Thymelaea hirsuta var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common thymelaea, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in spring. The plant has woody stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence, which gives it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are small, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

This species is well adapted to arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
