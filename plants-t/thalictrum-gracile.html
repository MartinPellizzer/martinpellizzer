Thalictrum gracile

Thalictrum gracile, commonly known as slender meadow rue, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. Thalictrum gracile is part of the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy flowering plants.
