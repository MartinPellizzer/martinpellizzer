Tainia acuminata

Tainia acuminata, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking violet to purple stems, which are often used in floral arrangements. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Tainia acuminata thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight.
