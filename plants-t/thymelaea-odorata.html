Thymelaea odorata

Thymelaea odorata, commonly known as the sweet thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, with woody stems and small, elliptical leaves. It produces capsules as fruit, containing numerous tiny seeds.

Thymelaea odorata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
