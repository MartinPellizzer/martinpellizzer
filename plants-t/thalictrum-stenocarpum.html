Thalictrum stenocarpum

Thalictrum stenocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-fruited meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers.
