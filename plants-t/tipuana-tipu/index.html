Tipuana Tipu

The tipuana tipu is a fast-growing, deciduous tree native to South America. It is known for its striking, feathery foliage and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
