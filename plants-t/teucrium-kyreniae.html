Teucrium kyreniae

Teucrium kyreniae, commonly known as Cyprus germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, aromatic, and covered with fine hairs.

Teucrium kyreniae is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
