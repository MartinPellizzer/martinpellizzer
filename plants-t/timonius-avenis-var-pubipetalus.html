Timonius avenis var. pubipetalus

Timonius avenis var. pubipetalus, commonly known as the pubipetalous timonius, is a rare and distinctive member of the Timonieae tribe.

It is characterized by its unique floral morphology, featuring prominently pubescent petals that give it its varietal name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and lanceolate, with a smooth surface and a slightly serrated margin.

Botanically, it is notable for its specialized pollination mechanisms, which involve specific insect interactions.
