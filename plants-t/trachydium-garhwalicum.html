Trachydium garhwalicum

Trachydium garhwalicum, commonly known as the Garhwal starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and a slender, unbranched stem that bears small, greenish-white flowers. Trachydium garhwalicum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
