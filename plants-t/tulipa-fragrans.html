Tulipa fragrans

Tulipa fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant tulip, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, often with a distinctive fragrance. Its bulbs are perennial, and the plant is known for its early spring blooming habit.

Tulipa fragrans is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its vibrant colors and pleasant scent.
