Tilia mandshurica var. pekinensis

Tilia mandshurica var. pekinensis, commonly known as the Peking linden, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically pale green on the underside. The tree produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers in panicles, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
