Trichomanes ulei

Trichomanes ulei, commonly known as the silver fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its fronds are typically clustered at the base, forming a compact rosette.

Trichomanes ulei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
