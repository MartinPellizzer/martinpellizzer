Tuberaria variabilis var. macropetala

Tuberaria variabilis var. macropetala, commonly known as the large-petaled tuberaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is native to the Mediterranean region. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, fleshy tubers underground.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
