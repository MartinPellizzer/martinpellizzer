Tabernaemontana decaisnei var. petiolata

Tabernaemontana decaisnei var. petiolata, commonly known as the petiolate tabernaemontana, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features opposite, simple leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
