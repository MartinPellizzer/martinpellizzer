Trigonotis longipes

Trigonotis longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed trigonotis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its growth habit includes both herbaceous and semi-woody stems, depending on environmental conditions.
