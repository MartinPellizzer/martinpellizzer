Tillandsia emergens

Tillandsia emergens, commonly known as the Emerald Sword, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically attaching itself to tree trunks or branches. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that can range in color from white to pink or purple, depending on the variety.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
