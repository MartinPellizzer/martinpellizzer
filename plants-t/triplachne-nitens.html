Triplachne nitens

Triplachne nitens, commonly known as slender triplachne, is a small annual grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

Triplachne nitens is considered a weed in many areas due to its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
