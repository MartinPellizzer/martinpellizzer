Torilis leptophylla var. leptophylla

Torilis leptophylla var. leptophylla, commonly known as lesser hedge mustard, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, green, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It is an annual herb that produces small, round, green pods containing seeds.
