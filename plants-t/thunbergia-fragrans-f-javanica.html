Thunbergia fragrans f. javanica

Thunbergia fragrans f. javanica, commonly known as the Javanese trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
