Tainia dunnii f. caterva

Tainia dunnii f. caterva, commonly known as the caterva tainia, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Tainia, which belongs to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in tropical and subtropical gardens.
