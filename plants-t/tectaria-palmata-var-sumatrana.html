Tectaria palmata var. sumatrana

Tectaria palmata var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Tectariaceae and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually bioluminescent, emitting a faint glow in the dark.

This unique trait makes it a notable species in both ecological and botanical studies.
