Tithymalus ebracteolatus

Tithymalus ebracteolatus, commonly known as the slender tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is native to parts of southern Africa. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
