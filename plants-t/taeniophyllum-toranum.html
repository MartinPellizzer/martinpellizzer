Taeniophyllum toranum

Taeniophyllum toranum, commonly known as the Toranum threadleaf, is a small, epiphytic flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes over 20,000 species of orchids. The plant is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
