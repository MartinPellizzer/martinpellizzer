Tecoma piutinga

Tecoma piutinga, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. Tecoma piutinga is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its bright, fragrant flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
