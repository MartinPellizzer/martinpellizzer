Tradescantia gonatandra

Tradescantia gonatandra, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and grass-like, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks. It produces small, three-petaled flowers that bloom in early spring.
