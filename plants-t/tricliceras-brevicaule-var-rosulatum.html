Tricliceras brevicaule var. rosulatum

Tricliceras brevicaule var. rosulatum, commonly known as the Rosulate Tricliceras, is a unique variant of the Tricliceras brevicaule species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive rosette formation of its foliage. The leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to conserve moisture in arid environments. This plant exhibits a striking coloration, with foliage ranging from deep green to bluish-gray, depending on light exposure.

It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.
