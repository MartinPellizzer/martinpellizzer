Turbinicarpus pseudomacrochele subsp. krainzianus

Turbinicarpus pseudomacrochele subsp.

krainzianus, commonly known as the Krainzianus Turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico. It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive rib structure that aids in water storage.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species within the Cactaceae family.
