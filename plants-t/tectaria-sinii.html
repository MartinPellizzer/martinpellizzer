Tectaria sinii

Tectaria sinii, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, velvety layer of hair, which helps in water retention.

Tectaria sinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
