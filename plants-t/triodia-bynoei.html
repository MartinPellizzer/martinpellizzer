Triodia bynoei

Triodia bynoei, commonly known as the Bynoe spinifex, is a perennial grass native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to thrive in harsh, sandy soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Triodia bynoei plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for desert fauna.
