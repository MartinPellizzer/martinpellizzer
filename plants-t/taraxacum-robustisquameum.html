Taraxacum robustisquameum

Taraxacum robustisquameum, commonly known as the robust shield dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features a rosette of thick, waxy, and hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. Its flowering stems bear a single, yellow composite flower that develops into a white, fluffy seed head.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
