Trigonella noeana f. brachycarpa

Trigonella noeana f. brachycarpa, commonly known as the short-seeded fenugreek, is a botanical variant of the fenugreek species.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces compact, short pods, which is a distinguishing feature of this form. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical significance lies in its morphological variation and potential use in traditional medicine.
