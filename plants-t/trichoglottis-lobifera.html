Trichoglottis lobifera

Trichoglottis lobifera, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in a raceme, with a distinctive trichoglottis-like appearance.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
