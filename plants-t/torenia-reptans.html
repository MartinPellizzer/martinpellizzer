Torenia reptans

Torenia reptans, commonly known as the creeping torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Torenia reptans is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to spread easily through its trailing growth habit.
