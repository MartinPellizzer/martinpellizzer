Tetrodon

Tetrodon, commonly known as the pufferfish, is a marine and brackish water fish belonging to the family Tetraodontidae.

While not a plant, it is often confused with the genus Tetraodon, which includes flowering plants in the family Tetraodontaceae. These plants are typically shrubs or small trees with simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers. They are native to tropical and subtropical regions and are often found in coastal areas.

The genus is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological importance in marine environments.
