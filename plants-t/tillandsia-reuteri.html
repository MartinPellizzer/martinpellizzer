Tillandsia reuteri

Tillandsia reuteri, commonly known as the silver star tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant typically grows epiphytically on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and white, often appearing in a star-like pattern, which gives it its common name.

Tillandsia reuteri is popular among succulent and air plant enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
