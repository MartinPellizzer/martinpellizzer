Thamnocalamus spathiflorus subsp. occidentalis

Thamnocalamus spathiflorus subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the Western bamboo grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae. It is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, linear leaves.
