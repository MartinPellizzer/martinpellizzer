Thelypteris elegantula

Thelypteris elegantula, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that often have a graceful, arching appearance. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread along the ground, forming dense colonies.

Thelypteris elegantula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic foliage.
