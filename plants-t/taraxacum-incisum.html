Taraxacum incisum

Taraxacum incisum, commonly known as the cutleaf dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its finely toothed, lance-shaped leaves. Taraxacum incisum is a perennial plant that is generally hardy and resilient to environmental stress.
