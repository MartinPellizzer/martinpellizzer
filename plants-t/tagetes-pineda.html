Tagetes pineda

Tagetes pineda, commonly known as Pineda's marigold, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening and have some traditional medicinal uses.
