Thelypteris dassanayakei

Thelypteris dassanayakei, commonly known as the Sri Lankan shield fern, is a species of fern native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant has a compact growth habit with dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of trichomes. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and are typically arranged in a row.

Thelypteris dassanayakei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
