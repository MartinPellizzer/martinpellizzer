Teline Stenopetala

Teline stenopetala is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its striking, narrow petals that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure the survival of this unique and beautiful plant.
