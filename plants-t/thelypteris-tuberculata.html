Thelypteris tuberculata

Thelypteris tuberculata, commonly known as the serrated shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its dimorphic fronds, with the sterile fronds typically being shorter and more upright than the fertile ones. The plant features a creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its fertile fronds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia.

Thelypteris tuberculata thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
