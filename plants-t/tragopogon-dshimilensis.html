Tragopogon dshimilensis

Tragopogon dshimilensis, commonly known as the Dshimilene goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant features slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its inflorescence consists of yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the genus Tragopogon.

It thrives in rocky and arid environments, demonstrating adaptability to harsh ecological conditions.
