Trichomanes radicans var. naseanum

Trichomanes radicans var. naseanum, commonly known as the Naseanum maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, feathery appearance. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
