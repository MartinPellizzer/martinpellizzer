Tarenna littoralis

Tarenna littoralis, commonly known as the coastal tarenna, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Tarenna littoralis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
