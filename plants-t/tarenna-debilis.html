Tarenna debilis

Tarenna debilis, commonly known as the slender tarenna, is a climbing shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small berries that turn from green to yellow or orange when mature.
