Taeniopsis stipitata

Taeniopsis stipitata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the surrounding soil.

Taeniopsis stipitata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
