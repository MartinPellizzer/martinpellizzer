Trichera × posoniensis

Trichera × posoniensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Trichera glabra and Trichera pachypoda.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a waxy coating.

Trichera × posoniensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
