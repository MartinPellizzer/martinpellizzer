Trifolium lineare var. filifolium

Trifolium lineare var. filifolium, commonly known as the silky clover, is a variant of the slender clover species.

It is characterized by its delicate, hair-like leaves that give it a soft, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its small, white or pale pink flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle beauty to the landscape.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening and as a ground cover due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
