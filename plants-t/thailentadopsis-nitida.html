Thailentadopsis nitida

Thailentadopsis nitida, commonly known as the slender tailed orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil efficiently.

Thailentadopsis nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
