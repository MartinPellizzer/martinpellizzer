Thuranthos polyanthus

Thuranthos polyanthus, commonly known as the hybrid chrysanthemum, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to Asia, though it is widely cultivated around the world. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with multiple stems and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, red, and white, and are often used in floral arrangements.

Thuranthos polyanthus thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
