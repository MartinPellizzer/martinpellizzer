Tricyrtis bakeri var. glabra

Tricyrtis bakeri var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth spider orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The smooth, glabrous leaves and stems are key identifying features of this variety.
