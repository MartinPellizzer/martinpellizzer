Tigridia conchiflora, commonly known as the Mexican hat flower, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its striking, helmet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. Tigridia conchiflora is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.
