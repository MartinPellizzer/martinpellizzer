Triaspis chrysophylla

Triaspis chrysophylla, commonly known as the golden leaf triaspis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure. Triaspis chrysophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
