Tilia Caroliniana Subsp. Floridana

Tilia caroliniana subsp. floridana is a unique variant of the American basswood tree native to the southeastern united states. this subspecies thrives in wetland and riparian habitats where it plays a vital role in supporting local ecosystems. its heart-shaped leaves and showy white flowers make it a striking addition to the landscape during spring. the tree is known for its fast growth and ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions. it is also valued for its wood, which is lightweight and easy to carve.
