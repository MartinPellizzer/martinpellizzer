Textoria parviflora

Textoria parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered textoria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, small white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
