Tylophora crebriflora

Tylophora crebriflora, commonly known as the many-flowered tylophora, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
