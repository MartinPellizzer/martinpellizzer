Turania aperta

Turania aperta, commonly known as the open turania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets. Turania aperta typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
