Trachynotia polystachya

Trachynotia polystachya, commonly known as the clustered goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.

Trachynotia polystachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
