Tragopogon pratensis var. suberadiatus

Tragopogon pratensis var. suberadiatus, commonly known as the slender yellowstar, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and bright yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces a single flower head at the top of each stem, which blooms in late spring to early summer.

This variety is notable for its distinctive, narrow leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
