Trichodium turfosum

Trichodium turfosum, commonly known as the turfy trichodium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually small and inconspicuous, arranged in spike-like clusters.

Trichodium turfosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ability to thrive in humid conditions.
