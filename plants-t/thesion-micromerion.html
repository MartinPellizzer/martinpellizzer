Thesion micromerion

Thesion micromerion, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thornapple, is a herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its fruit is a dry capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
