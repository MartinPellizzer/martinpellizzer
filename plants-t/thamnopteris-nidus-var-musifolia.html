Thamnopteris nidus var. musifolia

Thamnopteris nidus var. musifolia, commonly known as the musifolia fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed water fern.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The fronds are typically bright green and have a feathery appearance, with a smooth, waxy surface. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near water bodies in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in aquatic habitats.
