Tephrosia uliginosa

Tephrosia uliginosa, commonly known as swamp tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in wet, swampy habitats. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Tephrosia uliginosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
