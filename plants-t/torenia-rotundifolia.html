Torenia rotundifolia

Torenia rotundifolia, commonly known as the trumpet vine or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant is typically herbaceous, with a trailing or climbing growth habit, making it suitable for containers or hanging baskets. Its flowers bloom throughout the growing season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Torenia rotundifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
