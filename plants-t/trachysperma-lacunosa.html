Trachysperma lacunosa

Trachysperma lacunosa, commonly known as the hollow-stemmed wild thyme, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a distinctive hollow stem and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Trachysperma lacunosa is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and visual appeal.
