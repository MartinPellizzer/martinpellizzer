Tillandsia fasciculata var. uncispica

Tillandsia fasciculata var. uncispica, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which aid in water absorption. The plant produces striking, orchid-like inflorescences with pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires minimal direct sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
