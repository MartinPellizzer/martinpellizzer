Tacazzea apiculata var. glabra

Tacazzea apiculata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth tacazzea, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland or forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Asteraceae family.
