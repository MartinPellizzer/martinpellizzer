Tilia polyantha

Tilia polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese lime tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often pale green with a white underside. The tree produces small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age.

Tilia polyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
