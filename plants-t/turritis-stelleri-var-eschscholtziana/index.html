Turritis Stelleri Var. Eschscholtziana

Turritis stelleri var. eschscholtziana is a rare and striking plant native to the coastal regions of California. It is known for its unique spiral-shaped flowers that resemble a whirlwind of color. This variant of Turritis stelleri thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dune environments. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its resilience to harsh coastal conditions. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
