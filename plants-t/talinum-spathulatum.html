Talinum spathulatum

Talinum spathulatum, commonly known as the arrowleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Talinum spathulatum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
