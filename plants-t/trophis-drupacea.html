Trophis drupacea

Trophis drupacea, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
