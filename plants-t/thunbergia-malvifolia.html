Thunbergia malvifolia

Thunbergia malvifolia, commonly known as the white-flowered morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Thunbergia malvifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
