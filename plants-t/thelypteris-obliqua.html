Thelypteris obliqua

Thelypteris obliqua, commonly known as the oblique maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy appearance. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its fertile fronds, which are clustered in strobila. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and greenhouses.
