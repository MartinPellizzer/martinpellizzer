Tricyrtis masamunei

Tricyrtis masamunei, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Tricyrtis masamunei is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
