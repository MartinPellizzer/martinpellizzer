Thamnea ustulata

Thamnea ustulata, commonly known as the African flame plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually opposite and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Thamnea ustulata is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
