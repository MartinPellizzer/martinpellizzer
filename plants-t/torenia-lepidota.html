Torenia lepidota

Torenia lepidota, commonly known as the velvetleaf torenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. Torenia lepidota thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
