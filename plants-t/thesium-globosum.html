Thesium globosum

Thesium globosum, commonly known as the globular thesium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Thesium globosum is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats, where it thrives in full sun.
