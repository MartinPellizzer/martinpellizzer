Tibouchina herbacea var. rosea

Tibouchina herbacea var. rosea, commonly known as the rose tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.

The rose tibouchina thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
