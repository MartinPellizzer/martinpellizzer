Tofieldia × hybrida

Tofieldia × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid tussock grass, is a perennial plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, white to pale blue flowers. Tofieldia × hybrida is often found in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and along stream banks.
