Tropaeolum majus var. coccineum

Tropaeolum majus var. coccineum, commonly known as the Scarlet Runner Bean, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is an annual vine that thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil. It produces edible beans that are often used in culinary applications.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and agricultural settings.
