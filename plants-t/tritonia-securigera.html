Tritonia securigera

Tritonia securigera, commonly known as the blue bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Tritonia securigera is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.
