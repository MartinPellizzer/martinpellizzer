Tilia oblongifolia var. sangzhiensis

Tilia oblongifolia var. sangzhiensis, commonly known as the Sangzhi linden, is a rare variant of the linden tree native to China.

It is characterized by its elongated, ovate leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, typical of the Tilia genus. It produces small, round, brown fruits that contain one or two seeds.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
