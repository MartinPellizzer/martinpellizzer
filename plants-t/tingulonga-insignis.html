Tingulonga insignis

Tingulonga insignis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom during the day. Its leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy coating.

Tingulonga insignis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
