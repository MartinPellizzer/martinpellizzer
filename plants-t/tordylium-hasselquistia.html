Tordylium hasselquistia

Tordylium hasselquistia, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
