Tilia tuan subsp. tuan

Tilia tuan subsp.

tuan, commonly known as the Tuan lime tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam. It is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a distinct serrated edge. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in drooping inflorescences, which appear in the spring.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.
