Thesium quartzicola

Thesium quartzicola, commonly known as the quartz rock thesium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Thesium quartzicola is adapted to arid environments and is often found in quartz-rich habitats.
