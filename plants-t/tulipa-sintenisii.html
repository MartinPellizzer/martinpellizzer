Tulipa sintenisii

Tulipa sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis tulip, is a rare and striking species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its large, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial bulbous plant that produces showy blooms in late spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
