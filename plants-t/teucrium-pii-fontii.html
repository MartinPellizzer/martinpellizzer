Teucrium pii-fontii

Teucrium pii-fontii, commonly known as the Sierra teucrium, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a grayish-green hue and produces small, purple flowers in the summer months. It is often found in open woodlands and dry slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
