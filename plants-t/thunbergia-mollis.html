Thunbergia mollis

Thunbergia mollis, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often grown as a vine or shrub. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Thunbergia mollis is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to climb or trail.
