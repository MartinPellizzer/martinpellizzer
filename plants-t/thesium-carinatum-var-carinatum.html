Thesium carinatum var. carinatum

Thesium carinatum var. carinatum, commonly known as the spiny thesium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features wiry, branching stems covered in spines, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight sheen.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
