Tsiana speciosa

Tsiana speciosa, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms that range in color from pale pink to deep violet. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

Tsiana speciosa is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
