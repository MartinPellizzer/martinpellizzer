Tragacantha scabrifolia

Tragacantha scabrifolia, commonly known as the rough-leaved tragacanth, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dark green, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing structural support and protection against environmental stress.

Tragacantha scabrifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
