Triumfetta ryeae subsp. ryeae

Triumfetta ryeae subsp.

ryeae, commonly known as the African bluebottle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a small shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, characterized by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
