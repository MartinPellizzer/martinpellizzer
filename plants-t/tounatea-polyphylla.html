Tounatea polyphylla

Tounatea polyphylla, commonly known as the giant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, providing a unique habitat for various aquatic life forms. The plant has a distinctive floating leaf structure with a spongy underside that allows it to remain buoyant on water surfaces.
