Tragularia

Tragularia, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. They are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and as houseplants.
