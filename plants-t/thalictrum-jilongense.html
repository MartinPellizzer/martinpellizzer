Thalictrum jilongense

Thalictrum jilongense, commonly known as the Jilong meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, arranged in a raceme. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows.

Thalictrum jilongense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
