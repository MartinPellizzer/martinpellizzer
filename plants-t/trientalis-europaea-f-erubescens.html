Trientalis europaea f. erubescens

Trientalis europaea f. erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered wood sorrel, is a variant of the European wood sorrel.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or pinkish coloration. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and a yellow center.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
