Trichosanthes erosa var. integra

Trichosanthes erosa var. integra, commonly known as the rough gourd or Chinese trumpet vine, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, lobed leaves and distinctive yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The fruit is a long, cylindrical capsule that turns orange when mature, often used in traditional medicine. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and tendrils that aid in climbing.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and pharmacology.
