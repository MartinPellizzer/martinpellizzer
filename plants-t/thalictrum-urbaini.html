Thalictrum urbaini

Thalictrum urbaini, commonly known as the urban meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous slender, branching stems.

Thalictrum urbaini is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for pollinators.
