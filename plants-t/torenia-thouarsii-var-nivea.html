Torenia thouarsii var. nivea

Torenia thouarsii var. nivea, commonly known as the white torenia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale lavender flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to thrive in humid environments.
