Tiniaria dumetorum

Tiniaria dumetorum, commonly known as devil's lettuce, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a whitish coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in temperate regions.

Tiniaria dumetorum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
