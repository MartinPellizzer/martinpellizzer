Thomsonaria brevipetiolata

Thomsonaria brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled thomsonaria, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a short petiole, giving it its common name. Thomsonaria brevipetiolata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
