Tabernaemontana yunnanensis

Tabernaemontana yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan starflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is shrub-like, with opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

The flowers emit a faint fragrance and bloom in late spring to early summer.
