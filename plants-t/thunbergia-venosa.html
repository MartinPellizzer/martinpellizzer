Thunbergia venosa

Thunbergia venosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Thunbergia venosa thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
