Thelymitra forbesii

Thelymitra forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a basal leaf and a single, showy flower with a yellow lip and greenish sepals. Its flowering period occurs during late autumn to early spring.

Thelymitra forbesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
