Tillandsia schuchii

Tillandsia schuchii, commonly known as the Schuchii air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture from the air. It produces striking inflorescences with vibrant purple or red flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
