Trichera hybrida var. pinnatifida

Trichera hybrida var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved trichera, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with slender, pinnately arranged leaflets, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental species.
