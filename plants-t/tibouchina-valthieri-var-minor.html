Tibouchina valthieri var. minor

Tibouchina valthieri var. minor, commonly known as dwarf tibouchina, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming throughout the growing season. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
