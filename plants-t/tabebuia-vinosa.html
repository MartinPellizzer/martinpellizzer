Tabebuia vinosa

Tabebuia vinosa, commonly known as the pink trumpet tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Tabebuia vinosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
