Tilia × nyirsegiensis

Tilia × nyirsegiensis, commonly known as the Hungarian lime, is a hybrid species within the Tilia genus, native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy on the underside, and its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is a deciduous tree or shrub, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters, with a rounded crown and smooth, gray bark.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks.
