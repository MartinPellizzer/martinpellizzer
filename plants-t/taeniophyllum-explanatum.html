Taeniophyllum explanatum

Taeniophyllum explanatum, commonly known as the slender leaf fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, linear leaves that are often clustered closely together, giving it a compact appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches. Its rhizomes are short and help anchor it to its host while absorbing moisture and nutrients.

Taeniophyllum explanatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of care in the right conditions.
