Tragus orientalis

Tragus orientalis, commonly known as the oriental tragus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in horticulture for decorative purposes.
