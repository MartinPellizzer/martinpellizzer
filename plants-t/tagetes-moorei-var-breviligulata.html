Tagetes moorei var. breviligulata

Tagetes moorei var. breviligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved marigold, is a perennial plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage and vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
