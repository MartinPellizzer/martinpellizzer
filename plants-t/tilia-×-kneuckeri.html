Tilia × kneuckeri

Tilia × kneuckeri, commonly known as the Kneuckeri linden, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Tiliaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The tree produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in umbels. Its bark is smooth and light grey, becoming fissured with age.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively tolerant of urban conditions.
