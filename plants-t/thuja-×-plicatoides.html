Thuja × plicatoides

Thuja × plicatoides, commonly known as the Japanese thuja or golden thread.

It is a hybrid coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan. The plant features dense, scale-like foliage that is typically a bright yellow-green color, giving it a striking appearance. It has a slow growth rate and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The cones are small and inconspicuous, and the plant is relatively disease-resistant.
