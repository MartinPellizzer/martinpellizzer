Thibaudia insignis

Thibaudia insignis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Thibaudia insignis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
