Trichomanes strictum

Trichomanes strictum, commonly known as the rigid harts-tongue fern, is a small evergreen fern native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rock crevices. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit with a dense, clustered frond structure. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, narrow, tongue-like shape.

Trichomanes strictum is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
