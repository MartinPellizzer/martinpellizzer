Themeda Gigantea Subsp. Avenacea

Themeda gigantea subsp. avenacea is a striking grass species native to southern Africa, known for its tall, erect growth and vibrant golden hue. This plant thrives in open grasslands and is often found in areas with well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. The subspecies is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and health.
