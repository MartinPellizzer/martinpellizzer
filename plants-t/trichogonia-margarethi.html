Trichogonia margarethi

Trichogonia margarethi, commonly known as the Margarets' trichogonia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that exhibit a unique arrangement of stamens and petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, hairy textures, which aid in moisture retention.
