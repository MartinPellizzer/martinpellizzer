Ticanto nuga

Ticanto nuga, commonly known as the desert star, is a perennial shrub native to arid regions of Australia.

It features small, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater.

Due to its resilience, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
