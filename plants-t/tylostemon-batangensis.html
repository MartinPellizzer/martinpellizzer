Tylostemon batangensis

Tylostemon batangensis, commonly known as the Batang River daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture.

Tylostemon batangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local biodiversity.
