Thelymitra bracteata

Thelymitra bracteata, commonly known as the bractless sun orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the subfamily Orchidoideae and is characterized by its compact, leafy growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive lip structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open forests or heathlands.

Thelymitra bracteata is a popular choice for garden cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
