Triumfetta geoides var. lanceolata

Triumfetta geoides var. lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf triumfetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a smooth margin and a slightly hairy surface.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
