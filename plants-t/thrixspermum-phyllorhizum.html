Thrixspermum phyllorhizum

Thrixspermum phyllorhizum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is well-known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
