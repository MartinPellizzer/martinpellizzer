Trigonia nivea f. paniculata

Trigonia nivea f. paniculata, commonly known as the white-flowered trigonia, is a variant of the trigonia species characterized by its distinctive white flowers.

This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, warm climates. It features a climbing habit with elongated, slender stems that support its showy inflorescences. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
