Thesium monticola

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, particularly in countries like South Africa and Lesotho. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Thesium monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
