Trichocentrum pumilum

Trichocentrum pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a compact epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Trichocentrum pumilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
