Tithymalus × angustatus

Tithymalus × angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tithymalus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and range in color from pale yellow to orange.

This plant is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
