Tonsella decussata

Tonsella decussata, commonly known as the creeping tonsella, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the wet season.

Tonsella decussata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
