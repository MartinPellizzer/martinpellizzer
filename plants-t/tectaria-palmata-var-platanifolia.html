Tectaria palmata var. platanifolia

Tectaria palmata var. platanifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that can reach up to several feet in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with the rhizome often covered in a dense mat of roots. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, almost palm-like appearance.

This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
