Thymus serpyllum f. moeszii

Thymus serpyllum f. moeszii, commonly known as the Hungarian thyme, is a subspecies of the common thyme plant.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with aromatic, small, grey-green leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, similar to other thyme varieties.
