Tolpis capensis

Tolpis capensis, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Tolpis capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
