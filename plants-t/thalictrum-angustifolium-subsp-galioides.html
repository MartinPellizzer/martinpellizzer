Thalictrum angustifolium subsp. galioides

Thalictrum angustifolium subsp.

galioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved meadow rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in upright, branched inflorescences.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental foliage and ecological benefits.
