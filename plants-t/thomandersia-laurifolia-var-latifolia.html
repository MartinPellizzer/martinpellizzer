Thomandersia laurifolia var. latifolia

Thomandersia laurifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf thomandersia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are broad and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in panicles, typically blooming during the summer months. Its ornamental value lies in its dense foliage and delicate floral display.

It prefers warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
