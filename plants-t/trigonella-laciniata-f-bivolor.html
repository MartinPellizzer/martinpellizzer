Trigonella laciniata f. bivolor

Trigonella laciniata f. bivolor, commonly known as the two-colored fenugreek, is a variant of the fenugreek plant characterized by its distinctive bi-colored foliage.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a striking combination of green and white or pale green hues. It produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, narrow pods containing small, kidney-shaped seeds.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
