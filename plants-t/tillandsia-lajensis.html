Tillandsia lajensis

Tillandsia lajensis, commonly known as the Lajensia air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has short, stiff leaves that form a rosette structure, which helps it collect and retain moisture. It produces small, colorful inflorescences with showy, bell-shaped flowers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
