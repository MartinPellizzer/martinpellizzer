Tithymalus antiquorus

Tithymalus antiquorus, commonly known as the ancient tithymalus, is a rare and ancient plant species with a long history of botanical study.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Due to its rarity and historical significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
