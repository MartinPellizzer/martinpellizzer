Tricyrtis hirta

Tricyrtis hirta, commonly known as the Japanese rain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan and parts of eastern Asia.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often speckled with purple. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Tricyrtis hirta is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom in late summer and early autumn.
