Tristachya leiostachya

Tristachya leiostachya, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panic grass, is a perennial grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, silky foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, inconspicuous flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks.

Tristachya leiostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
