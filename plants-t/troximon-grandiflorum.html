Troximon grandiflorum

Troximon grandiflorum, commonly known as the grandiflorous trumpetvine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
