Thalictrum schweiggeri

Thalictrum schweiggeri, commonly known as Schweigger's meadow-rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
