Tricyrtis × tachingshuii

Tricyrtis × tachingshuii, commonly known as the Tachingshui spider lily, is a hybrid orchid native to China.

It features delicate, spider-like flowers with pale yellow petals and a distinctive, recurved lip. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
