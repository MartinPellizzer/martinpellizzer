Tricyrtis suzukii

Tricyrtis suzukii, commonly known as the Japanese spider lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often with a distinctive red or orange tip. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, spider-like blooms in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Tricyrtis suzukii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
