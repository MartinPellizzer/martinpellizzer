Tsuga canadensis f. columnaris

Tsuga canadensis f. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar white spruce, is a compact, narrow-growing variant of the white spruce.

It exhibits a distinctive columnar shape, making it ideal for small spaces or as a ornamental tree. The foliage is dense and dark green, with needle-like leaves that provide year-round visual interest. It is native to North America and thrives in cooler, moist environments.

This form is often used in landscaping for its unique structure and hardiness.
