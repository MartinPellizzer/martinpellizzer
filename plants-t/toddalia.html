Toddalia

Toddalia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The leaves are typically pinnate, consisting of several small, lance-shaped leaflets. Toddalia trees can grow quite tall, reaching heights of up to 20 meters, and are often found in coastal areas and tropical climates.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
