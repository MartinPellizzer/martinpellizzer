Thea parvilimba

Thea parvilimba, commonly known as the dwarf thea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often used in ornamental gardening and as a houseplant.
