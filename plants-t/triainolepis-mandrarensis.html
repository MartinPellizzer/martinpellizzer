Triainolepis mandrarensis

Triainolepis mandrarensis, commonly known as the Madagascar star-leaf.

It is a rare and endemic plant species found exclusively in the mountainous regions of Madagascar. The plant is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered critically endangered.
