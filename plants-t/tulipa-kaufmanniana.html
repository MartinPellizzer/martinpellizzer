Tulipa kaufmanniana

Tulipa kaufmanniana, commonly known as the Kaufmann's tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Tulipa kaufmanniana is valued in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
