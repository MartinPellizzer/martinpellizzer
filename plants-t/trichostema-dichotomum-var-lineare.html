Trichostema dichotomum var. lineare

Trichostema dichotomum var. lineare, commonly known as the linearleaf bluecurls, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub habitats.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a notable species in the genus Trichostema.
