Teucrium tomentosum

Teucrium tomentosum, commonly known as woolly germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been historically recognized, though it is primarily cultivated for ornamental purposes.
