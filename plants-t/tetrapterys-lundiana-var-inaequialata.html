Tetrapterys lundiana var. inaequialata

Tetrapterys lundiana var. inaequialata, commonly known as the unequal-leaf tetrapterys, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, asymmetrical leaves that have varying leaflet sizes along their length. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are compound, with pinnate arrangement and often have a rough or hairy texture.

The flowers are small and yellowish, and the plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.
