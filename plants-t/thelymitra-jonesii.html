Thelymitra jonesii

Thelymitra jonesii, commonly known as Jones' spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy, spider-like flower that is usually pale green to yellowish in color. It blooms from late autumn to early spring, and its flowers have a distinctive, intricate structure with recurved petals and sepals.

Thelymitra jonesii is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats.
