Timonius longitubus var. longitubus

Timonius longitubus var. longitubus, commonly known as the long-tubed timonius, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, tubular flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a waxy coating.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
