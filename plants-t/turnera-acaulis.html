Turnera acaulis

Turnera acaulis, commonly known as the rock turnera, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky or coastal environments. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with five petals and a distinctive, narrow tube.

Turnera acaulis is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
