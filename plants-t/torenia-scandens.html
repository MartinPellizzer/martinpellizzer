Torenia scandens

Torenia scandens, commonly known as the skyflower or blue mist vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Torenia scandens is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
