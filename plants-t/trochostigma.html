Trochostigma

Trochostigma, commonly known as the "sickle-leaf orchid," is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a complex structure that includes specialized petals and sepals.
