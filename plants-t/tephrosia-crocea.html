Tephrosia crocea

Tephrosia crocea, commonly known as the yellow tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and distinctive pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its bright yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the warm seasons.

Tephrosia crocea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
