Tissa fruticosa

Tissa fruticosa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves that are opposite and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Tissa fruticosa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
