Tabernaemontana chinensis

Tabernaemontana chinensis, commonly known as Chinese starflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, ovate leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The flowers are not only ornamental but also have traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
