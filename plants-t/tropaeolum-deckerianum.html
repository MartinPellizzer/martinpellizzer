Tropaeolum deckerianum

Tropaeolum deckerianum, commonly known as the scarlet runner or winter tansy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is a member of the Tropaeolaceae family and is often grown for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Tropaeolum deckerianum is also known for its edible seeds, which are used in some traditional cuisines.
