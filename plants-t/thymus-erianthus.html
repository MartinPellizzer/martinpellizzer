Thymus erianthus

Thymus erianthus, commonly known as the woolly thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
