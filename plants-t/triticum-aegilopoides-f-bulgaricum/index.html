Triticum Aegilopoides F. Bulgaricum

Triticum aegilopoides f. bulgaricum is a wild wheat species native to southeastern Europe. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions. This subspecies is of interest to researchers due to its potential for genetic diversity. It has been studied for its role in wheat evolution and breeding programs. Its unique characteristics make it a valuable resource in agricultural science.
