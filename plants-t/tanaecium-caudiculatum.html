Tanaecium caudiculatum

Tanaecium caudiculatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, thick stem and dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant produces violet-colored blooms that typically appear in spring and summer. Its leaves are velvety and dark green, often with a waxy coating.

Tanaecium caudiculatum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
