Tofieldia yezoensis var. okushirensis

Tofieldia yezoensis var. okushirensis, commonly known as the Okushiri tofieldia, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower spikes that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of green or white. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
