Temminckia mollis

Temminckia mollis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorch. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form compact, attractive clumps.

Temminckia mollis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
