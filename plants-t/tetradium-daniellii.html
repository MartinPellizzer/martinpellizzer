Tetradium daniellii

Tetradium daniellii, commonly known as the Chinese tallow tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood.
