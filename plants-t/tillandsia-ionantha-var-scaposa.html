Tillandsia ionantha var. scaposa

Tillandsia ionantha var. scaposa, commonly known as the miniature air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a small epiphytic plant that thrives without soil, absorbing moisture and nutrients through its leaves. The plant features compact rosettes of narrow, pointed leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes. It produces small, colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, typically in the spring.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
