Thlaspi montanum var. villarsianum

Thlaspi montanum var. villarsianum, commonly known as the alpine cabbagewort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, growing in rocky or gravelly soils. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to cold climates.

The plant is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
