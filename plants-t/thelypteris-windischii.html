Thelypteris windischii

Thelypteris windischii, commonly known as the Windisch's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are often divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant.

Thelypteris windischii is native to parts of Asia and is valued for its ornamental foliage in horticulture.
