Trifolium Filiforme Subsp. Minus

Trifolium filiforme subsp. minus is a rare and delicate species of clover known for its slender leaves and small, pale flowers. Found primarily in moist, calcareous soils across parts of Europe, this subspecies thrives in specific microhabitats. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
