Tinospora thorelii

Tinospora thorelii, commonly known as the Indian moonseed, is a climbing vine belonging to the Menispermaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy berries. Tinospora thorelii is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
