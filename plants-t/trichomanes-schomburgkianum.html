Trichomanes schomburgkianum

Trichomanes schomburgkianum, commonly known as the Schomburgk's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bright green and covered in fine, hair-like structures. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and produce spores for propagation.

Trichomanes schomburgkianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
