Tulipa baldaccii

Tulipa baldaccii, commonly known as the Baldaccii tulip, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Tulipa.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters and produces showy blooms in early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.

Tulipa baldaccii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
