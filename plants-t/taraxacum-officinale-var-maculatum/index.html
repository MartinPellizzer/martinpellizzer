Taraxacum Officinale Var. Maculatum

Taraxacum officinale var. maculatum, commonly known as the dandelion, is a hardy perennial plant that thrives in a variety of environments. Its bright yellow flowers and fluffy white seed heads make it a familiar sight in lawns and meadows around the world. Despite its simple appearance, the plant has a long history of use in traditional medicine and culinary practices. The unique pattern of its leaves, marked by a distinctive greenish-white blotch, sets it apart from other dandelion varieties. This resilient plant not only adds beauty to the landscape but also plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems.
