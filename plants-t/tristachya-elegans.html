Tristachya elegans

Tristachya elegans, commonly known as the elegant grass or silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and tall, slender inflorescences that produce small, fluffy flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. Its rapid growth and ability to form dense clumps make it useful for erosion control and as a groundcover.

Tristachya elegans is also valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
