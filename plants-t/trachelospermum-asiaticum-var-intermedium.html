Trachelospermum asiaticum var. intermedium

Trachelospermum asiaticum var. intermedium, commonly known as the Chinese star jasmine, is a flowering vine native to East Asia.

It features fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for climbing structures or trellises.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant aroma and ability to provide dense coverage in gardens.
