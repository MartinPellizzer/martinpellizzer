Teucrium vestitum

Teucrium vestitum, commonly known as the velvet germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in Mediterranean climates. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Teucrium vestitum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
