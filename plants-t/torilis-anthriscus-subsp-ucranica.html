Torilis anthriscus subsp. ucranica

Torilis anthriscus subsp.

ucranica, commonly known as the Ukrainian cowparsley, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family. It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in umbrella-shaped clusters.
