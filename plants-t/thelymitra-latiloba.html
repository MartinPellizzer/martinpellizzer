Thelymitra latiloba

Thelymitra latiloba, commonly known as the broad-lobed sun orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as understorey forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy, pale yellow to cream-colored flower. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy coating.

Thelymitra latiloba is part of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its delicate and ephemeral blooms.
