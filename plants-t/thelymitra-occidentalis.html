Thelymitra occidentalis

Thelymitra occidentalis, commonly known as the Western Sun Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It produces striking, golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred labellum. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

Thelymitra occidentalis is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
