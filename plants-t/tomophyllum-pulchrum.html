Tomophyllum pulchrum

Tomophyllum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful thimble cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
