Trichelostylis capitata

Trichelostylis capitata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-like flower spike that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, elongated labellum.

Trichelostylis capitata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
