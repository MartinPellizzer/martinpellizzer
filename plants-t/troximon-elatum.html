Troximon elatum

Troximon elatum, commonly known as the tall broom.

This plant is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow flowers. It features small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a dense, bushy form. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
