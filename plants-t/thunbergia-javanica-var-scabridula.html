Thunbergia javanica var. scabridula

Thunbergia javanica var. scabridula, commonly known as the scabrous thunbergia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a rough, scabrous texture. The plant produces clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a striking yet relatively easy-to-care-for plant.
