Tibouchina minutiflora

Tibouchina minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered tibouchina, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface.

Tibouchina minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
