Thalictrum minus subsp. expansum

Thalictrum minus subsp.

expansum, commonly known as the broadleaf meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers.
