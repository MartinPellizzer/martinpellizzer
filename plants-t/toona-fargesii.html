Toona fargesii

Toona fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese toona or bigleaf toona, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by oval, woody fruits containing seeds. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Toona fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
