Thaspium montanum

Thaspium montanum, commonly known as mountain thaspium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, usually appearing in shades of pink or purple.

Thaspium montanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
