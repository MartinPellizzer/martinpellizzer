Tekel paniculata

Tekel paniculata, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, red or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common ornamental grass in landscaping.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both useful and sometimes invasive in certain environments.
