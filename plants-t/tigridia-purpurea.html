Tigridia purpurea

Tigridia purpurea, commonly known as the tiger flower, is a species of flowering plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and grows from a bulbous underground stem. Tigridia purpurea blooms in the spring and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
