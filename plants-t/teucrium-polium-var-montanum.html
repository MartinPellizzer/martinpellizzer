Teucrium polium var. montanum

Teucrium polium var. montanum, commonly known as mountain germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions makes it a hardy addition to xeriscapes and rock gardens.
