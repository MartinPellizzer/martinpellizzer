Trichilia alternans

Trichilia alternans, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and East Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.
