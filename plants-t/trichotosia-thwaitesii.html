Trichotosia thwaitesii

Trichotosia thwaitesii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Trichotosia thwaitesii is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in traditional crafts and construction.
