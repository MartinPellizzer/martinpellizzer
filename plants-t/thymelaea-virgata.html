Thymelaea virgata

Thymelaea virgata, commonly known as the silver thymelaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Thymelaea virgata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
