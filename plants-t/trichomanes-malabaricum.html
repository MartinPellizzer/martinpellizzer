Trichomanes malabaricum

Trichomanes malabaricum, commonly known as the Malabar maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Trichomanes malabaricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
