Tinus confinis

Tinus confinis, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Tinus confinis is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with suitable climates.
