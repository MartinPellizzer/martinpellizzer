Thalictrum gallicum proles nothum

Thalictrum gallicum proles nothum, commonly known as the European meadow rue hybrid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
