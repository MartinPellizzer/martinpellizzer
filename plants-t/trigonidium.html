Trigonidium

It is characterized by its triangular stems and heart-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Trigonidium is native to various regions in Europe and Asia and thrives in moist, shady environments.

It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of the habitat.
