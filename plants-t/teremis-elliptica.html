Teremis elliptica

Teremis elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical teremis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Teremis elliptica is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
