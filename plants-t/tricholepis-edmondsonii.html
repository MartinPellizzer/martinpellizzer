Tricholepis edmondsonii

Tricholepis edmondsonii, commonly known as the Edmondson's tricholepis, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

The flowers are small and yellow, often clustered together in a composite head that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
