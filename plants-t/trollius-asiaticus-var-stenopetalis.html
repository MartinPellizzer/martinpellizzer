Trollius asiaticus var. stenopetalis

Trollius asiaticus var. stenopetalis, commonly known as the narrow-petaled globe flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features showy, golden-yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, upright stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the top of the stem, giving it a globe-like appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
