Trinia scabra

Trinia scabra, commonly known as the rough trinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Trinia scabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
