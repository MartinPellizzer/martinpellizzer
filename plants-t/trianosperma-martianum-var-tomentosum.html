Trianosperma martianum var. tomentosum

Trianosperma martianum var. tomentosum, commonly known as the Martian woolly vine, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptive features make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
