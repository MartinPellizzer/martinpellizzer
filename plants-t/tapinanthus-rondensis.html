Tapinanthus rondensis

Tapinanthus rondensis, commonly known as the Rondensis dodder, is a parasitic flowering plant that lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on its host for nutrients.

Due to its parasitic nature, it can significantly weaken its host, affecting plant growth and health.
