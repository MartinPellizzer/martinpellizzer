Tragopogon serawschanicus

Tragopogon serawschanicus, commonly known as the Siberian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates. It features a tall, slender stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its yellow flower heads bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
