Thelypteris sancta

Thelypteris sancta, commonly known as the sacred fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.

Thelypteris sancta is valued not only for its ecological role but also for its cultural significance.
