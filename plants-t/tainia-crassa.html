Tainia crassa

Tainia crassa, commonly known as the broad-leaved tainia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. The plant features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its flowers are small and clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Tainia crassa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and unique floral structure.
