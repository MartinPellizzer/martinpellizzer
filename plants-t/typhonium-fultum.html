Typhonium fultum

Typhonium fultum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, yellowish-green spathes that enclose its spadices, which contain the actual flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy areas or near water bodies.

Typhonium fultum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
