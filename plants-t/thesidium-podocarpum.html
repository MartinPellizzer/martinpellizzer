Thesidium podocarpum

Thesidium podocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed podocarp, is a unique evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its needle-like leaves and conical shape. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its botanical features make it an important species in tropical and subtropical forest ecosystems.

Thesidium podocarpum is also valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
