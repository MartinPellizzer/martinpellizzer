Tulipa abatinoi

Tulipa abatinoi, commonly known as the Abatino tulip, is a species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Tulipa within the family Liliaceae. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

The botanical features of Tulipa abatinoi make it a popular choice for rock gardens and perennial borders.
