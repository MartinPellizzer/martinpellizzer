Tephrosia purpurea subsp. dunensis

Tephrosia purpurea subsp.

dunensis, commonly known as the purple tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as savannas and rocky soils.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually purple or pink in color, which bloom during the warm season. Its leaves are opposite and trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval to lance-shaped.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
