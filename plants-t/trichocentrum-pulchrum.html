Trichocentrum pulchrum

Trichocentrum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with numerous pendulous flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum pulchrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
