Teucrium angustissimum

Teucrium angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Teucrium angustissimum is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
