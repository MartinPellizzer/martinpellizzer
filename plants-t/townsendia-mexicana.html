Townsendia mexicana

Townsendia mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican Townsend'sweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments.
