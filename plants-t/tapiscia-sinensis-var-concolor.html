Tapiscia sinensis var. concolor

Tapiscia sinensis var. concolor, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Chinese holly, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which consists of glossy, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.

However, it prefers partial shade and moderate moisture levels for optimal growth.
