Tephrocactus ferocior

Tephrocactus ferocior, commonly known as the fierce cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique morphology makes it a striking addition to desert landscapes.
