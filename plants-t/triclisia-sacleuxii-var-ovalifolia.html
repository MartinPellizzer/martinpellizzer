Triclisia sacleuxii var. ovalifolia

Triclisia sacleuxii var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf triclisia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding ornamental value to its appearance. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.
