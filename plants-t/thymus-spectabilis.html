Thymus spectabilis

Thymus spectabilis, commonly known as purple thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, woody stems and small, fragrant leaves that are rich in essential oils. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its aromatic properties make it popular in culinary and medicinal applications.

Thymus spectabilis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and versatile garden plant.
