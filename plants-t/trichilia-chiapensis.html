Trichilia chiapensis

Trichilia chiapensis, commonly known as Chiapan whitewood, is a tropical tree native to Central America and northern South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and elongated, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by winged fruits that contain seeds.
