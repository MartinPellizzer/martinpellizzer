Tillandsia kalliantha

Tillandsia kalliantha, commonly known as the silver star tillandsia, is a species of air plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central America, particularly in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and absorbs moisture and nutrients through its specialized leaves. Its foliage is characterized by a silvery-blue coloration, which helps reflect excess light and reduce water loss.

Tillandsia kalliantha is popular among plant enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
