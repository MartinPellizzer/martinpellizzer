Thesium ifrikianum

Thesium ifrikianum, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.

Thesium ifrikianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
