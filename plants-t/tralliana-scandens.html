Tralliana scandens

Tralliana scandens, commonly known as the climbing tralliana, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial vine that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing along walls or in shaded areas. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering.

Tralliana scandens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
