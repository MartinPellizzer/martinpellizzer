Thelypteris nakaikei

Thelypteris nakaikei, commonly known as the Nakai's holly fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, serrated fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris nakaikei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
