Trichosalpinx carinilabia

Trichosalpinx carinilabia, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, typically featuring yellow and purple hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Trichosalpinx carinilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
