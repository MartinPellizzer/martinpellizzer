Thujopsis atrovirens

Thujopsis atrovirens, commonly known as the Chinese arborvitae, is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It has scale-like leaves that are dark green on top and whitish underneath, arranged in dense, flat sprays. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by oval, blue-black berries. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its dense foliage and pyramidal shape.

Thujopsis atrovirens is also noted for its resistance to pests and diseases, making it a low-maintenance plant.
