Teucrium moleromesae

Teucrium moleromesae, commonly known as the Canary Islands germander, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tenerife in the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
