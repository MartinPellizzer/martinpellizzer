Tigridia tuitensis

Tigridia tuitensis, commonly known as the Tuitense Sword Lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their showy blooms. The plant produces striking, tubular-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange with a contrasting black or purple stripe. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Tigridia tuitensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
