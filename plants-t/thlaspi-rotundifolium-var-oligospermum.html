Thlaspi rotundifolium var. oligospermum

Thlaspi rotundifolium var. oligospermum, commonly known as the round-leaved rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its rosette of rounded, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or arctic environments.

This variant is distinguished by its reduced seed production compared to other varieties of Thlaspi rotundifolium.
