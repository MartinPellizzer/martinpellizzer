Tragacantha karakugensis

Tragacantha karakugensis, commonly known as the Karakugan tragacanth, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces a resinous sap, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in arid and semi-arid environments.
