Thelypteris alatella

Thelypteris alatella, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, brownish layer of rhizomorphs, which help in nutrient absorption.

The fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, almost metallic sheen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
