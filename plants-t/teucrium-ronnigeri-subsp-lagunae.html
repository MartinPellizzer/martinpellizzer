Teucrium ronnigeri subsp. lagunae

Teucrium ronnigeri subsp.

lagunae, commonly known as the Lake Teucrium, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of Europe. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils, often found in wetlands or along the edges of lakes.

The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to violet in color, arranged in dense spikes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wetland environments.
