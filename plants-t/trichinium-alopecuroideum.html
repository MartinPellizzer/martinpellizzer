Trichinium alopecuroideum

Trichinium alopecuroideum, commonly known as the narrowleaf trichinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of North America.

Trichinium alopecuroideum is noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
