Toisusu urbaniana f. composita

Toisusu urbaniana f. composita, commonly known as the urban composite, is a unique variant of the Toisusu urbaniana species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, foliage-covered structure. The plant exhibits a high degree of adaptability to urban environments, making it suitable for landscaping in cities. Its leaves are typically broad and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in polluted air conditions.
