Thelypteris resiliens

Thelypteris resiliens, commonly known as the resilient shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Thelypteris resiliens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
