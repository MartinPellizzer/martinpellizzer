Thelymitra venosa var. magnifica

Thelymitra venosa var. magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent pink fairy armoured spider-orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

This terrestrial orchid is characterized by its vivid pink to purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling armor. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in open woodlands or heathlands. It features a slender, hairy stem that supports its showy, resupinate (upside-down) flowers.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique and colorful blooms.
