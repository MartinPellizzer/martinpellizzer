Typhonium subglobosum

Typhonium subglobosum, commonly known as the globe-shaped arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe, typically in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and exotic appearance make it a popular choice for greenhouse and indoor gardening.
