Trichilia gilletii

Trichilia gilletii, commonly known as African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing flat, edible seeds. These seeds are valued for their high oil content, which is used in cosmetics and cooking.

The plant also plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
