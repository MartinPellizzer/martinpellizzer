Tradescantia multiflora var. tobagensis

Tradescantia multiflora var. tobagensis, commonly known as the Spider Plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It features long, arching stems with small, triangular leaves that are typically bright green with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, or purple. It is a hardy, evergreen perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
