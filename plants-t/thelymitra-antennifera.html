Thelymitra antennifera

Thelymitra antennifera, commonly known as the golden helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive golden-yellow flowers that resemble a helmet. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests. Thelymitra antennifera has a pseudobulb structure and produces a single spike of flowers.

It is a protected species due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity.
