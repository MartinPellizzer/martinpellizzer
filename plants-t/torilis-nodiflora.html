Torilis nodiflora

Torilis nodiflora, commonly known as hairy burnet, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Torilis nodiflora is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and gravelly soils.
