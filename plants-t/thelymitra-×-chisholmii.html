Thelymitra × chisholmii

Thelymitra × chisholmii, commonly known as the Chisholm's sun-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or heathland environments. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a solitary spike of showy, sun-like flowers.

Thelymitra × chisholmii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
