Trigonostemon arboreus

Trigonostemon arboreus, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It features large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a fibrous root system and a spreading, often multi-stemmed trunk. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a pointed tip.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
