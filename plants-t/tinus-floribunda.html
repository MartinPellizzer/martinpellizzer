Tinus floribunda

Tinus floribunda, commonly known as the red-flowered lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of vibrant red, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its unique bloom pattern and vivid color make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic floral display.
