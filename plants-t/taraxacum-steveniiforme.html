Taraxacum steveniiforme

Taraxacum steveniiforme, commonly known as the Steven's dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. Taraxacum steveniiforme is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
