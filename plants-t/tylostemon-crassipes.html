Tylostemon crassipes

Tylostemon crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leaved tylostemon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. It is well adapted to arid conditions and can tolerate salt spray.

Tylostemon crassipes is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
