Tuberaria bupleurifolia f. purpureosetosa

Tuberaria bupleurifolia f. purpureosetosa, commonly known as the purple-fringed bupleurum, is a variant of the bupleurum genus within the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinted bracts that enclose the flower clusters, giving it a striking visual appearance.
