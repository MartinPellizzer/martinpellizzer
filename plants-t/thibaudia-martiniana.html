Thibaudia martiniana

Thibaudia martiniana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is a perennial herb that thrives in warm, humid conditions with indirect sunlight. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form colonies in suitable environments.

Thibaudia martiniana is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
