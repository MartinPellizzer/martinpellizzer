Trema griseum

Trema griseum, commonly known as the slender trema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. Its root system is fibrous and helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Trema griseum is often found in shaded understory environments within tropical forests.
