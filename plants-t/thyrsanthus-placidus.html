Thyrsanthus placidus

Thyrsanthus placidus, commonly known as the Peaceful Thyrsanthus, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine, silvery down.
