Topobea fragrans

Topobea fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant topobea, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Topobea fragrans is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
