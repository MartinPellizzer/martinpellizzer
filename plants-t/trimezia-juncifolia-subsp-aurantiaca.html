Trimezia juncifolia subsp. aurantiaca

Trimezia juncifolia subsp.

aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden trimezia, is a member of the Agavaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white bloom.
