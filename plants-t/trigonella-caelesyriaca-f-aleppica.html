Trigonella caelesyriaca f. aleppica

Trigonella caelesyriaca f. aleppica, commonly known as the Aleppo trigonella, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has small, oval-shaped seeds that are often used in traditional medicine.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh climates and its historical use in herbal remedies.
