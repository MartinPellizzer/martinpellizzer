Turbinicarpus pseudomacrochele f. minimus

Turbinicarpus pseudomacrochele f. minimus, commonly known as the miniature turbinicarpus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green with a subtle bluish tint. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring, which are relatively small compared to its size. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
