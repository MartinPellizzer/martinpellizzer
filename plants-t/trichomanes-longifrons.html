Trichomanes longifrons

Trichomanes longifrons, commonly known as the long-fronded huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hypnaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves that are often arching and covered in fine, hair-like structures. Trichomanes longifrons is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
