Trichomanes Shenzhenense

Trichomanes shenzhenense is a rare fern species native to southern China. It was first discovered in the city of Shenzhen, highlighting the region's rich biodiversity. This plant is known for its delicate fronds and unique reproductive structures. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its discovery has sparked interest in protecting endangered plant species in urban environments.
