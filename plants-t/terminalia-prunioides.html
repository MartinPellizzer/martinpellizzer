Terminalia prunioides

Terminalia prunioides, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing single seeds. Its wood is valued for its fine texture and durability, making it useful in traditional crafts and construction.
