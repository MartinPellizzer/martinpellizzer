Tylophora minahassae

Tylophora minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa tylophora, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a persistent calyx. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
