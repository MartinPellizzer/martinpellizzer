Thunbergia malangana

Thunbergia malangana, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet or Malabar Trumpet, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The flowers are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
