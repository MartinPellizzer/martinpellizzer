Tricholaena brevipila

Tricholaena brevipila, commonly known as the short-stemmed grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

Tricholaena brevipila is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant in landscaping.
