Trichomanes stylosum

Trichomanes stylosum, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Trichomanes stylosum is notable for its intricate and ornate leaf structure, which contributes to its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
