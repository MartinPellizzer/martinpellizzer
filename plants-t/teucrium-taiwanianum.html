Teucrium taiwanianum

Teucrium taiwanianum, commonly known as Taiwan germander, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally recognized in Chinese herbal medicine.
