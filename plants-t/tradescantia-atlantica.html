Tradescantia atlantica

Tradescantia atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches, featuring slender, upright stems. The plant produces showy, three-petaled blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight reddish tinge along the edges.

Tradescantia atlantica thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
