Tillandsia lindenii var. abundans

Tillandsia lindenii var. abundans, commonly known as the silver leaf air plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a striking appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal watering, typically through misting or soaking.

This variety is popular among succulent and air plant enthusiasts due to its unique aesthetic and low maintenance requirements.
