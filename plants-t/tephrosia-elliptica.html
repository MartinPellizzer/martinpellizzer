Tephrosia elliptica

Tephrosia elliptica, commonly known as African blue pea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and trifoliate leaves with small, elliptical leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its seeds are encased in a hard, smooth pod, and the plant is often grown for its ornamental value.

Tephrosia elliptica is also valued for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional medicine.
