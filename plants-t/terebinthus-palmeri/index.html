Terebinthus Palmeri

Terebinthus palmeri is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. It is known for its distinctive appearance and ability to thrive in harsh desert conditions. This species belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes frankincense and myrrh trees. Its name honors the botanist John T. Palmer, who played a significant role in its discovery. Despite its resilience, Terebinthus palmeri faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
