Thrasya reticulata

Thrasya reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, reticulated vein patterns on its leaves, which give it its common name. Native to tropical regions, this plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Thrasya reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
