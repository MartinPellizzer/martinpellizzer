Thelymitra abrupta

Thelymitra abrupta, commonly known as the abrupt leek orchid, is an epiphytic or lithophytic orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a rosette of linear, leek-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a distinctive, narrow spur.

Thelymitra abrupta is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its delicate and intricate floral structure.
