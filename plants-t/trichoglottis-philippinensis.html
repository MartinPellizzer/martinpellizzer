Trichoglottis philippinensis

Trichoglottis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine trichoglottis, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is characterized by its unique inflorescence and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
