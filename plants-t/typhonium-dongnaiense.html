Typhonium dongnaiense

Typhonium dongnaiense, commonly known as the Dong Nai arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and striking, spadix-like flowers. Its rhizomes are edible and have been traditionally used in local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is gaining attention in horticultural circles.
