Trichiogramme reniformis

Trichiogramme reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped spurge, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. It has a succulent stem and leaves, which help store water.

Trichiogramme reniformis is often found in rocky soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
