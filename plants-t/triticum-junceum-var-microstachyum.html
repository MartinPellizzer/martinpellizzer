Triticum junceum var. microstachyum

Triticum junceum var. microstachyum, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanically, it is closely related to other wheatgrass species and is valued for its nutritional content.
