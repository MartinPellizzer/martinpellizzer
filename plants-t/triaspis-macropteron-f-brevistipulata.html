Triaspis macropteron f. brevistipulata

Triaspis macropteron f. brevistipulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved triaspis, is a variant of the triaspis plant characterized by its shorter, narrower leaves compared to the typical form.

The plant typically features a slender, upright stem with finely divided leaves that give it a delicate appearance.
