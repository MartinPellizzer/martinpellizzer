Taxus baccata f. linearis

Taxus baccata f. linearis, commonly known as the European yew with linear leaves, is a cultivar of the European yew.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are narrower than those of the typical species. This form is often appreciated for its elegant appearance and is used in ornamental horticulture. The plant retains its foliage year-round, making it a suitable choice for evergreen landscapes.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, thriving in temperate climates.
