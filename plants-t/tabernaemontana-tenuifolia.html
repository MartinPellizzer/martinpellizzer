Tabernaemontana tenuifolia

Tabernaemontana tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental landscaping.
