Taeniophyllum rugulosum

Taeniophyllum rugulosum, commonly known as the wrinkled taeniophyllum, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
