Trichodiadema marlothii

Trichodiadema marlothii, commonly known as the silver leaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated as a rare and ornamental plant.
