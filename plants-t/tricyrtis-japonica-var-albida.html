Tricyrtis japonica var. albida

Tricyrtis japonica var. albida, commonly known as the white spider orchid, is a striking perennial herb native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that are predominantly white, with a subtle pinkish hue at the base. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
