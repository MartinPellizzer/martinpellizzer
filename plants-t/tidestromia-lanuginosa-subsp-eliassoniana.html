Tidestromia lanuginosa subsp. eliassoniana

Tidestromia lanuginosa subsp.

eliassoniana, commonly known as the silky tansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit with multiple stems arising from the base.

This subspecies is notable for its aromatic foliage and is often found in coastal dune environments.
