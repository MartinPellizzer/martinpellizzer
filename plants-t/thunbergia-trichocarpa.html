Thunbergia trichocarpa

Thunbergia trichocarpa, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth habit and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.

Thunbergia trichocarpa prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
