Thelypteris subpectinata

Thelypteris subpectinata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Thelypteris subpectinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining soil stability in its habitat.
