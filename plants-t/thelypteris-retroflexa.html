Thelypteris retroflexa

Thelypteris retroflexa, commonly known as the curled fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of root hairs, which aid in nutrient absorption.

The curled fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
