Tiedemannia filiformis subsp. filiformis

Tiedemannia filiformis subsp.

filiformis, commonly known as the slender tiedemannia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features delicate, thread-like leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters during the spring, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is often found in rich, loamy soils and thrives in partial to full shade.
