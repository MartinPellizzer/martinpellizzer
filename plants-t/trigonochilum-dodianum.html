Trigonochilum dodianum

Trigonochilum dodianum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, triangular orchid blooms in shades of white and pink, which appear during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
