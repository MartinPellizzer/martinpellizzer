Thymus hispanicus

Thymus hispanicus, commonly known as Spanish thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It features tiny, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, pale pink to lavender flowers that bloom in early summer. Its woody stems and dense foliage make it well-suited for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Thymus hispanicus is valued for its medicinal properties, including antimicrobial and antioxidant effects.
