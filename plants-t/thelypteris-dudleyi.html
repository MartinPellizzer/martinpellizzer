Thelypteris dudleyi

Thelypteris dudleyi, commonly known as Dudley's holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds that are often divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of spore-bearing leaves.

Thelypteris dudleyi is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia.
