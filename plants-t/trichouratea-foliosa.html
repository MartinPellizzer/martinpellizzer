Trichouratea foliosa

Trichouratea foliosa, commonly known as the featherleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Trichouratea foliosa is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

It is considered a perennial plant with a low-growing habit.
