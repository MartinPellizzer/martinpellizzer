Trigonella melilotus-cretica

Trigonella melilotus-cretica, commonly known as Cretan melilot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing lens-shaped seeds that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and herbal applications.
