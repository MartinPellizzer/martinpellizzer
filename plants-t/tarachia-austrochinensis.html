Tarachia austrochinensis

Tarachia austrochinensis, commonly known as the Chinese tarahina, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates.
