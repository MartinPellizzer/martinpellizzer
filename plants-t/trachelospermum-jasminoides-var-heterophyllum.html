Trachelospermum jasminoides var. heterophyllum

Trachelospermum jasminoides var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green surface. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued for its ability to produce a sweet, jasmine-like fragrance.
