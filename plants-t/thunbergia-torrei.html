Thunbergia torrei

Thunbergia torrei, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, opposite leaves that are usually hairy beneath. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vining growth habit.

Thunbergia torrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
