Thalictrum brevisericeum var. angustiantherum

Thalictrum brevisericeum var. angustiantherum, commonly known as the narrow-angled meadow rue, is a flowering plant in the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Native to parts of Asia, this species thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The plant's botanical features make it an ornamental choice in gardens with suitable conditions.
