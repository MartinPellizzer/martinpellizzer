Tapiphyllum oblongifolium

Tapiphyllum oblongifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf tapiphyllum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, oblong-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
