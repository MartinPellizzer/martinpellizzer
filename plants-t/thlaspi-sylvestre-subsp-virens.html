Thlaspi sylvestre subsp. virens

Thlaspi sylvestre subsp.

virens, commonly known as green sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe. It typically forms dense mats of foliage with small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to native plant gardens.
