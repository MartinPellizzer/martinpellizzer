Thunbergia hanningtonii

Thunbergia hanningtonii, commonly known as the Hannington's spiderflower, is a striking climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using its twining stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Thunbergia hanningtonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
