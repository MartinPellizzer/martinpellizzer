Trichodiadema decorum

Trichodiadema decorum, commonly known as the silver cross plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, blue-green leaves that have a distinctive white cross pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
