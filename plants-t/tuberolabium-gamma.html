Tuberolabium gamma

Tuberolabium gamma, commonly known as the golden labellum orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive golden-yellow labellum. The plant produces tuberous roots that store nutrients, aiding in its survival in harsh environments. Its flowering period typically occurs during the late spring to early summer months.

Tuberolabium gamma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
