Tropaeolum buchenaui

Tropaeolum buchenaui, commonly known as the Brazilian nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, round, orange or yellow fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Tropaeolum buchenaui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses.
