Tithymalus linearis

Tithymalus linearis, commonly known as the slender tithymalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

The plant's structure and growth pattern make it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
