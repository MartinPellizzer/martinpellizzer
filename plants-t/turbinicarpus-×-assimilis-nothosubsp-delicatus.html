Turbinicarpus × assimilis nothosubsp. delicatus

Turbinicarpus × assimilis nothosubsp.

delicatus, commonly known as the Delicate Turbinicarpus, is a hybrid succulent native to the arid regions of Mexico. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and ribbed structure.

The plant features short, spiny bristles and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring season. It is well adapted to dry environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and resilience.
