Trichocentrum splendidum

Trichocentrum splendidum, commonly known as the splendid orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, pendant inflorescences that bear numerous showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Trichocentrum splendidum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
