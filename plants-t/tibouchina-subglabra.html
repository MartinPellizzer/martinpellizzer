Tibouchina subglabra

Tibouchina subglabra, commonly known as the smooth tibouchina, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Tibouchina subglabra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
