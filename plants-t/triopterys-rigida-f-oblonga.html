Triopterys rigida f. oblonga

Triopterys rigida f. oblonga, commonly known as the broadleaf holly fern, is a varietal form of the species Triopterys rigida.

It is a perennial evergreen fern characterized by its broad, leathery, elliptical to ovate-shaped leaves. The fronds are typically arching, with a dense, glossy appearance and a deep green coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in shaded gardens and as a houseplant.
