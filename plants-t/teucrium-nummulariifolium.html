Teucrium nummulariifolium

Teucrium nummulariifolium, commonly known as silver germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, rounded, silver-gray leaves that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
