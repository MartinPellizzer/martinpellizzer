Thelymitra carnea var. robusta

Thelymitra carnea var. robusta, commonly known as the Carnation Leek Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a distinctive, leek-like appearance with a fleshy, bulbous stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences of pink to purple flowers with a strong, sweet scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in heathlands and forest margins.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is protected in many regions.
