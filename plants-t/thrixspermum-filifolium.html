Thrixspermum filifolium

Thrixspermum filifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf thrixspermum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Thrixspermum filifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
