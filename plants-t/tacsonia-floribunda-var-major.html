Tacsonia floribunda var. major

Tacsonia floribunda var. major, commonly known as the showy tacsonia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Tacsonia floribunda var. major is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
