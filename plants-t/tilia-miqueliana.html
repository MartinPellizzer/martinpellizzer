Tilia miqueliana

Tilia miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's linden, is a species of flowering tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Tiliaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in forested areas. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, while its wood is valued for its fine texture and workability.

The species is also noted for its role in supporting local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
