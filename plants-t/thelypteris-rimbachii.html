Thelypteris rimbachii

Thelypteris rimbachii, commonly known as the Rimbach's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bioluminescent in certain conditions. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Thelypteris rimbachii has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies in wetlands and forest floors.

Its unique botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and conservation efforts.
