Trichilia taubertiana

Trichilia taubertiana, commonly known as African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other tropical hardwoods. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with smooth, grey bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The wood is valued for its fine grain and durability, though it is also used in traditional medicine.
