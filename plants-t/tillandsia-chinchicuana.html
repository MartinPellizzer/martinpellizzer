Tillandsia chinchicuana

Tillandsia chinchicuana, commonly known as the Chinchicuana air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
