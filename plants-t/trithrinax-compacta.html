Trithrinax compacta

Trithrinax compacta, commonly known as the compact palm, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a small, slow-growing tree that typically reaches a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant has a slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry forests.

Trithrinax compacta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
