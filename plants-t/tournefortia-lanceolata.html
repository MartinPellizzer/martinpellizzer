Tournefortia lanceolata

Tournefortia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tournefortia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive grayish-green color. Its showy yellow flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Tournefortia lanceolata is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or disturbed habitats.
