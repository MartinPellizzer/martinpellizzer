Thea formosensis

Thea formosensis, commonly known as the Formosan tea plant, is a species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Thea formosensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
