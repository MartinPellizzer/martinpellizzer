Thalictrum thalictroides f. favillianum

Thalictrum thalictroides f. favillianum, commonly known as the Favill's meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a delicate appearance with a height ranging from 30 to 60 centimeters.

This ferny plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
