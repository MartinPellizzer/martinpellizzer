Trigonella monspeliaca var. leiocarpa

Trigonella monspeliaca var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-flowered fenugreek, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, with trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in slender, hairy pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

The variety leiocarpa is distinguished by its smooth, hairless pods, setting it apart from other varieties of Trigonella monspeliaca.
