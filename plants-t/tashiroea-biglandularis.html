Tashiroea biglandularis

Tashiroea biglandularis, commonly known as the Japanese thistle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

Tashiroea biglandularis is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local pollinators.
