Trichomanes carolianum

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. These trichomes help the plant retain moisture and reduce water loss in its moist, high-humidity habitat.

Trichomanes carolianum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for when provided with the right conditions.
