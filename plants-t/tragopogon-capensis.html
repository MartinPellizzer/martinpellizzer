Tragopogon capensis

Tragopogon capensis, commonly known as the Cape goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, leafy stems and produces yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Tragopogon capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
