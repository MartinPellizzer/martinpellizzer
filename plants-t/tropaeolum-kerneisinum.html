Tropaeolum kerneisinum

Tropaeolum kerneisinum, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and edible pods. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and can grow up to several meters in length. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is cultivated for both its ornamental value and its edible beans, which are rich in nutrients.
