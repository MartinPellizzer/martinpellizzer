Thymus × borzygis

Thymus × borzygis, commonly known as the hybrid thyme, is a low-growing, aromatic perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features small, dense clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces tiny, oval-shaped leaves that are gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Thymus × borzygis is valued for its fragrance and ornamental appeal, as well as its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
