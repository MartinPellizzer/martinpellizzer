Tetradymia canescens

Tetradymia canescens, commonly known as the grayleaf tansy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

Tetradymia canescens is often found in sagebrush steppe and other arid environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
