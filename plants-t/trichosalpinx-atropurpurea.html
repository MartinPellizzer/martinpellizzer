Trichosalpinx atropurpurea

Trichosalpinx atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are adorned with intricate spurred structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and slightly hairy, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Trichosalpinx atropurpurea is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
