Tristemma leiocalyx var. leiocalyx

Tristemma leiocalyx var. leiocalyx, commonly known as the white-flowered tristemma, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
