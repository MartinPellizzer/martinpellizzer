Tillandsia nathanii

Tillandsia nathanii, commonly known as the Nathan's air plant, is a species of bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its leaves are typically narrow and strap-like, with a dense covering of trichomes that help absorb moisture from the air. The plant produces small, bell-shaped inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink or white.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is popular among plant enthusiasts and collectors.
