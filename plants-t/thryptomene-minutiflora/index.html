Thryptomene Minutiflora

Thryptomene minutiflora is a small, flowering shrub native to the coastal regions of southeastern Australia. It is known for its delicate white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the spring and summer. This plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
