Tanacetum pinnatum

Tanacetum pinnatum, commonly known as yarrow.

It is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family, native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and small, white or yellowish flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal use.

Tanacetum pinnatum is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat wounds and digestive issues.
