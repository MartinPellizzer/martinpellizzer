Trachelospermum kuraruense

Trachelospermum kuraruense, commonly known as the Kuraru star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a vigorous climber with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, blooming throughout the year in warm climates. Its climbing habit makes it suitable for trellises, walls, or as a ground cover.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
