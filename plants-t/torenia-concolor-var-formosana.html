Torenia concolor var. formosana

Torenia concolor var. formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
