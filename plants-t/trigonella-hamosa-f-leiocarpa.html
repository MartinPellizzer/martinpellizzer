Trigonella hamosa f. leiocarpa

Trigonella hamosa f. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smoothpod fenugreek, is a botanical variant of the fenugreek plant.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to regions of the Mediterranean and parts of Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into smooth, oval pods. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The smoothpod characteristic of this form distinguishes it from other Trigonella species.
