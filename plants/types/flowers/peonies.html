Peonies: Peony Planting Tips For Success

Peonies are beloved for their lush foliage and showy blooms, making them a prized addition to any garden. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a beginner, proper planting techniques can ensure your peonies thrive and flourish year after year. Choosing the right location with well-draining soil and adequate sunlight is crucial for their health and longevity. Timing is also essential, as peonies should be planted in the fall to allow their roots to establish before winter. With the right care and attention, peonies can provide stunning floral displays for decades.
