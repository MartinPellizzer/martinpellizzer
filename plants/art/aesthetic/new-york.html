New York Aesthetic With Plants

The new york aesthetic with plants is a unique blend of urban simplicity and greenery.

it embraces minimalism with a touch of nature, often featuring potted plants in small spaces. this style thrives in apartments and studios, where greenery adds life and warmth. the palette is usually neutral, with earthy tones and soft textures. plants are not just decor but a lifestyle choice that reflects calm and sustainability.

it's a trend that brings the outdoors indoors in a modern, refined way.
