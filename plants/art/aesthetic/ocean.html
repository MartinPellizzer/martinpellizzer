10 Ocean Aesthetics: Ocean-Inspired Plant Color Schemes

The ocean, with its vast and ever-changing hues, offers an endless source of inspiration for plant color schemes. From the deep blues and greens of the sea floor to the soft pinks and purples of coral reefs, the ocean's palette is both vibrant and serene. Incorporating these colors into interior design and gardening can create a calming yet striking visual effect. By selecting plants that reflect the ocean's natural tones, homeowners and designers can bring a sense of tranquility and beauty into their spaces. This article explores ten ocean-inspired plant color schemes that capture the essence of the sea in every leaf and bloom.
