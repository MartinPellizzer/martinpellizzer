Sketch Aesthetic With Plants

The sketch aesthetic with plants is a minimalist art style that blends simplicity with nature.

It uses clean lines and soft shading to capture the essence of flora in a subtle way. This style often features single plants or small arrangements rendered in black and white. It appeals to those who appreciate quiet beauty and organic forms. The contrast between the structured lines and the flowing shapes creates a calming visual effect.

This trend has gained popularity in both art and interior design circles.
