Philodendron holtonianum

Philodendron holtonianum, commonly known as the Holton's philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with creamy or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
