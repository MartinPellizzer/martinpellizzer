Plectranthus caninus

Plectranthus caninus, commonly known as dog's tooth plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink.

Plectranthus caninus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
