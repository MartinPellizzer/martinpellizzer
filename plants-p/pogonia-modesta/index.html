Pogonia Modesta

Pogonia modesta is a rare and delicate orchid native to the eastern United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments like bogs and wetlands. Known for its small, pinkish-brown flowers, it is often overlooked despite its ecological importance. Conservation efforts are crucial due to habitat loss and environmental changes. This plant serves as an indicator of healthy wetland ecosystems.
