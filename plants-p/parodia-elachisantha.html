Parodia elachisantha

Parodia elachisantha, commonly known as the "Little White Cactus," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, compact plant with a globular shape and typically produces white or pale pink flowers. The plant has cylindrical, ribbed stems that are covered in fine, white spines. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
