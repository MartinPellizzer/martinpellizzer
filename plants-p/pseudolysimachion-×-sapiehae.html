Pseudolysimachion × sapiehae

Pseudolysimachion × sapiehae, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are usually arranged in a fan shape at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and elegant appearance.
