Pterostylis umbrina

Pterostylis umbrina, commonly known as the dark-veined star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is a small plant with a single leaf and a slender, erect flowering stem that produces a single, pale green or yellowish flower. The flower has a distinctive structure with a labellum that is often darker in color, resembling a star shape. It blooms during late winter to early spring, typically in response to rainfall.

This orchid is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments such as under forest canopies.
