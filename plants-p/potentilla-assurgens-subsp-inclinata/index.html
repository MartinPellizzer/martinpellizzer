Potentilla Assurgens Subsp. Inclinata

Potentilla assurgens subsp. inclinata is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Rosaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this shrub thrives in harsh, alpine environments. Its compact form and vibrant yellow flowers make it a striking addition to rocky landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Understanding this plant offers insights into the resilience of flora in extreme conditions.
