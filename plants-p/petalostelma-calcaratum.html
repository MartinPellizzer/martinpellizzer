Petalostelma calcaratum

Petalostelma calcaratum, commonly known as the rock petalostelma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous species that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in arid environments, making it an interesting subject for botanical study.
