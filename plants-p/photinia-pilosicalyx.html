Photinia pilosicalyx

Photinia pilosicalyx, commonly known as the silky photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by red, fuzzy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes make it a valuable addition to gardens and public spaces.
