Plectranthus serra

Plectranthus serra, commonly known as the serrated plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly hairy leaves that are often serrated along the edges. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Plectranthus serra is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
