Potentilla × turdensis

Potentilla × turdensis, commonly known as the Turdensis cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly seen in the wild.
