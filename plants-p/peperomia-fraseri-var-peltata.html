Peperomia fraseri var. peltata

Peperomia fraseri var. peltata, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage is highly ornamental, and it is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and pruning.
