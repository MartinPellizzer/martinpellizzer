Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp. lepteilema

Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp.

lepteilema, commonly known as the Kalksburg haircup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, yellowish-green composite flowers.

This subspecies is native to mountainous regions in Central Europe, particularly in the Alps. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often found on slopes and cliffs.

The plant is notable for its compact growth habit and is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique morphological features.
