Piper elasmophyllum

Piper elasmophyllum, commonly known as the velvet leaf pepper, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by tiny, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The botanical features of Piper elasmophyllum make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
