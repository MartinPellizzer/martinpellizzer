Parsonsia hebetica

Parsonsia hebetica, commonly known as the pale parsonsia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers that are clustered in loose racemes, blooming during the summer months. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem, hairy foliage, and a preference for well-drained soils.

It is often found in coastal and sandy areas, thriving in full sun.
