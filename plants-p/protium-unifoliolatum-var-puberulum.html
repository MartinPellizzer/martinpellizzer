Protium unifoliolatum var. puberulum

Protium unifoliolatum var. puberulum, commonly known as the silky protium, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are covered in a fine, silky pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. Its leaves and bark contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

The plant's unique morphology and chemical composition contribute to its ecological and potential medicinal significance.
