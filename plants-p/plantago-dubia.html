Plantago dubia

Plantago dubia, commonly known as the uncertain plant, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, demonstrating a degree of drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and medicinal studies.
