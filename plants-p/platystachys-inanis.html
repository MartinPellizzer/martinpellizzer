Platystachys inanis

Platystachys inanis, commonly known as the Chinese flame bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of bright red bracts that resemble flames. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, grass-like leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense clumps.

Platystachys inanis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces.
