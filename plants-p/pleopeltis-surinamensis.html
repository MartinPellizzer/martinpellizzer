Pleopeltis surinamensis

Pleopeltis surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant typically features dark green, leathery fronds with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
