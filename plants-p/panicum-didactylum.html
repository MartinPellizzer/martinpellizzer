Panicum didactylum

Panicum didactylum, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and erect, branching inflorescence that produces small, fluffy seeds. The plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a common sight in prairies and open woodlands. Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense stands make it a valuable plant for erosion control.

Silver bluestem is also a popular choice for ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
