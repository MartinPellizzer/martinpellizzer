Peperomia elongata var. piliramea

Peperomia elongata var. piliramea, commonly known as the Piliramea Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
