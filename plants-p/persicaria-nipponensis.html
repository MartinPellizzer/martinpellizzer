Persicaria nipponensis

Persicaria nipponensis, commonly known as Japanese knotweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist environments.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in many regions outside its native range.
