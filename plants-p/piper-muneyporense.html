Piper muneyporense

Piper muneyporense, commonly known as the Muneypore pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of small, lance-shaped leaves. Its fruit, which is a small, elongated berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of Piper muneyporense make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
