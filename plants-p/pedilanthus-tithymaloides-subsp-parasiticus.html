Pedilanthus tithymaloides subsp. parasiticus

Pedilanthus tithymaloides subsp.

parasiticus, commonly known as the parasitic moonseed, is a hemiparasitic plant that derives some of its nutrients from host plants. It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its yellow or orange flowers and woody stems.
