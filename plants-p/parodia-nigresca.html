Parodia nigresca

Parodia nigresca, commonly known as the Black Ghost Cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive dark green to nearly black stems, which are typically covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its dark foliage. Parodia nigresca is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience in harsh conditions.
