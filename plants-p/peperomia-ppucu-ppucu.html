Peperomia ppucu-ppucu

Peperomia ppucu-ppucu, commonly known as the Snowy Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a striking silver-white coloration, giving it a frosty appearance. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and minimal care requirements.
