Picradenia scaposa

Picradenia scaposa, commonly known as the African wild bean, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, white to yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.
