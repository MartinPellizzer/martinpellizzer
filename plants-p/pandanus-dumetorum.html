Pandanus dumetorum

Pandanus dumetorum, commonly known as the common pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and thatching. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its roots and leaves have been utilized in various cultural and medicinal practices.

The plant thrives in coastal and wetland environments, demonstrating adaptability to humid, saline conditions.
