Pleurogyne mairei

Pleurogyne mairei, commonly known as the Maire's pleurogyne, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and forested areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
