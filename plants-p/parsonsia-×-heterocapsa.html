Parsonsia × heterocapsa

Parsonsia × heterocapsa, commonly known as the hybrid flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody capsules as fruits, which contain numerous seeds covered in a sticky, resinous coating. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Parsonsia × heterocapsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
