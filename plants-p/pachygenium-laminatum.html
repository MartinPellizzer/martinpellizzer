Pachygenium laminatum

Pachygenium laminatum, commonly known as the parchment fern, is a unique plant characterized by its thick, leathery fronds that resemble parchment.

Due to its distinctive foliage, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
