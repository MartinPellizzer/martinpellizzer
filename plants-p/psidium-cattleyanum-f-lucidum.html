Psidium cattleyanum f. lucidum

Psidium cattleyanum f. lucidum, commonly known as the glossy guava.

This variant is a cultivar of the guava tree, characterized by its smooth, shiny foliage. The leaves are typically dark green and have a waxy texture, which helps in reducing water loss. The fruit is similar to the common guava but may exhibit variations in size and skin thickness.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its edible fruit.
