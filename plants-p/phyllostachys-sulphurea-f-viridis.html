Phyllostachys sulphurea f. viridis

Phyllostachys sulphurea f. viridis, commonly known as the green bamboo, is a variant of the yellow bamboo species characterized by its striking green foliage.

This ornamental plant is native to eastern Asia and is prized for its elegant, slender culms that display a vibrant green coloration. The leaves are typically narrow and long, contributing to its refined appearance in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various climates.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
