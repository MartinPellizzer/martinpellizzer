Pentzia eenii

Pentzia eenii, commonly known as the Elandberg daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. It produces small, hairy leaves and has a compact, mat-forming growth habit.

Pentzia eenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
