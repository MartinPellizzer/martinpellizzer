Panicum sprengelianum

Panicum sprengelianum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces panicles of small, greenish-white flowers that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense clumps make it suitable for erosion control and landscape use.
