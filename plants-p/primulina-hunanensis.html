Primulina hunanensis

Primulina hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan primula, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental value.

Primulina hunanensis is appreciated for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
