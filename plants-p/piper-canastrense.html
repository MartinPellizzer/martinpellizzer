Piper canastrense

Piper canastrense, commonly known as the Canastrense Pepper Vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture and a distinct aromatic scent.

Piper canastrense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
