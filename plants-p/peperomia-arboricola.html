Peperomia arboricola

Peperomia arboricola, commonly known as the tree peperomia, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features dense, glossy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia arboricola is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
