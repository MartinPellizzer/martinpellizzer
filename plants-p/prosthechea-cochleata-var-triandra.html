Prosthechea cochleata var. triandra

Prosthechea cochleata var. triandra, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and decorative appearance. The plant produces greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking foliage and ease of cultivation.
