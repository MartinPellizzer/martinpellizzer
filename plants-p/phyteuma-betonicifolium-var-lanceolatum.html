Phyteuma betonicifolium var. lanceolatum

Phyteuma betonicifolium var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils.
