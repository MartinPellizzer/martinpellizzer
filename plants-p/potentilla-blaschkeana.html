Potentilla blaschkeana

Potentilla blaschkeana, commonly known as Blaschke's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Potentilla blaschkeana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness in cooler climates.
