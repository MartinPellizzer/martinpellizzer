Pleurothallis peroniocephala

Pleurothallis peroniocephala, commonly known as the Peroniocephala orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in cloud forests and is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
