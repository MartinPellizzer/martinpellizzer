Pennisetum nigricans var. stenostachyum

Pennisetum nigricans var. stenostachyum, commonly known as blackfoot grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense clumps make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
