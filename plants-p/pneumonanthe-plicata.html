Pneumonanthe plicata

Pneumonanthe plicata, commonly known as the pleated fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pleated fronds that give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a complex structure that enhances its aesthetic appeal.

Pneumonanthe plicata is valued in horticulture for its delicate foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
