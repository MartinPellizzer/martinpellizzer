Peliosanthes ashihoana

Peliosanthes ashihoana, commonly known as the Ashihona cycad, is a rare and ancient plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, trunk-like stem and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, a trait typical of cycads, and it has a slow growth rate.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
