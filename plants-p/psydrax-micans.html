Psydrax micans

Psydrax micans, commonly known as the nightshade or starfruit plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Psydrax micans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses in various regions.
