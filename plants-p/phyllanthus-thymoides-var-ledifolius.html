Phyllanthus thymoides var. ledifolius

Phyllanthus thymoides var. ledifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and parts of Asia.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
