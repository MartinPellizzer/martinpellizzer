Portulaca suffrutescens

Portulaca suffrutescens, commonly known as the common purslane, is a flowering plant in the family Portulacaceae.

It is a low-growing, succulent plant that thrives in hot, dry environments. The plant features fleshy, rounded leaves and produces small, bright yellow flowers. It is native to the Americas but has been naturalized in many parts of the world.

Portulaca suffrutescens is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its ability to withstand drought conditions.
