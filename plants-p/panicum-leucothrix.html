Panicum leucothrix

Panicum leucothrix, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow, spike-like cluster of small, pale green to silver flowers.

Panicum leucothrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
