Ptilotum

Ptilotum, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

Ptilotum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
