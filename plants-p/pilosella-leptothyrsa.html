Pilosella leptothyrsa

Pilosella leptothyrsa, commonly known as slenderleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.
