Plantago holosteum var. filifolia

Plantago holosteum var. filifolia, commonly known as the slender plantain, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, due to its adaptability and rapid growth.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties, with its leaves used in traditional remedies for their soothing and anti-inflammatory effects.
