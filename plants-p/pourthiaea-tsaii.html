Pourthiaea tsaii

Pourthiaea tsaii, commonly known as the Tsai's daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in cultivation.
