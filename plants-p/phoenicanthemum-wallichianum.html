Phoenicanthemum wallichianum

Phoenicanthemum wallichianum, commonly known as the Himalayan phlox, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of fragrant, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and strong sunlight. It is often found growing in rocky soils and mountain meadows.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in cooler climates.
