Potentilla nitida f. edentata

Potentilla nitida f. edentata, commonly known as the smooth potentilla, is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically smooth or slightly hairy, depending on the variety. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer, which are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
