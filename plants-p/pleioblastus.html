Pleioblastus

Pleioblastus, commonly known as the Japanese forest grass, is a genus of grasses native to East Asia.

These plants are characterized by their fine, feather-like foliage and are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value. Pleioblastus species are typically evergreen, with some varieties retaining their foliage throughout the year. They thrive in shaded to partially shaded environments and require well-drained soil.
