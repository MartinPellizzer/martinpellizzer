Peperomia ocumarana

Peperomia ocumarana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
