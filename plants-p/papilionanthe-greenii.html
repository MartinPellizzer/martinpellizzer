Papilionanthe greenii

Papilionanthe greenii, commonly known as the Green's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that bloom in late summer. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Papilionanthe greenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
