Plectranthus gigantifolius

Plectranthus gigantifolius, commonly known as the giant-leaf plectranthus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for both containers and garden beds.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for adding dramatic visual interest to landscapes.
