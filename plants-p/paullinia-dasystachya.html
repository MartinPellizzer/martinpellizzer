Paullinia dasystachya

Paullinia dasystachya, commonly known as the Brazilian sarsaparilla, is a climbing vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by orange-red berries. The plant's botanical features make it an important component of the rainforest ecosystem.
