Piper nanegalense

Piper nanegalense, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Piper nanegalense is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
