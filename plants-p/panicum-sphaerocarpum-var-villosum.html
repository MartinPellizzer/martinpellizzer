Panicum sphaerocarpum var. villosum

Panicum sphaerocarpum var. villosum, commonly known as silky foxtail, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescences that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and adaptability.
