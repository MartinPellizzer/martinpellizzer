Panicum angolense

Panicum angolense, commonly known as Angola grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a resilient and widespread species in its native range.
