Perezia adnata var. oolepis

Perezia adnata var. oolepis, commonly known as the Oolepis perezia, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with intricate patterns of purple and brown. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette near the ground.
