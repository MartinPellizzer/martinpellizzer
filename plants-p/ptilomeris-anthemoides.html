Ptilomeris anthemoides

Ptilomeris anthemoides, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and features small, blue or violet flowers with yellow centers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous blooms during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ptilomeris anthemoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
