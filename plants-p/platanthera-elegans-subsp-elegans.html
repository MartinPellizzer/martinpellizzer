Platanthera elegans subsp. elegans

Platanthera elegans subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant lady's-tresses, is a perennial orchid native to North America. It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and dense, dark green leaves.

The plant produces a showy, fragrant inflorescence of white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spiral pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late summer, from July to September.
