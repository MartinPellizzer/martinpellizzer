Potentilla holopetala var. fastigiata

Potentilla holopetala var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, compact growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a tight, upright habit, giving it a neat appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in colder climates, adding ornamental value to gardens in temperate zones.
