Polystichum aculeatum var. angulare

Polystichum aculeatum var. angulare, commonly known as the angular holly fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive angular appearance. The fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with short, stiff pinnae that give the plant a rugged, textured look. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticultural settings.
