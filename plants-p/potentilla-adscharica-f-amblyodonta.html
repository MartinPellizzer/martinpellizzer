Potentilla adscharica f. amblyodonta

Potentilla adscharica f. amblyodonta, commonly known as the broad-leaved potentilla, is a compact shrub native to Central Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical to ovate leaves that are typically broader than those of the typical form. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
