Paragramma longifolia

It is a small, mat-forming plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, known as gametangia, are located on the upper surfaces of the leaves.

Paragramma longifolia plays a role in nutrient cycling in its ecosystem and is often used in studies of bryophyte ecology.
