Polystachya edwallii

Polystachya edwallii, commonly known as the Edwall's bamboo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and its ability to form dense clumps through rhizome growth. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear numerous small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Polystachya edwallii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
