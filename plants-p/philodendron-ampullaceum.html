Philodendron ampullaceum

Philodendron ampullaceum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant has a robust stem that can climb using aerial roots, often reaching great heights in its natural habitat. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
