Phlomis kotschyana

Phlomis kotschyana, commonly known as Kotschyan's blazingstar, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and drought tolerance.
