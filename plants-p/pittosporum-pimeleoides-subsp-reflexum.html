Pittosporum pimeleoides subsp. reflexum

Pittosporum pimeleoides subsp.

reflexum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It features narrow, leathery leaves that are often curved at the tips, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native habitats.
