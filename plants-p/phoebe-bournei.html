Phoebe bournei

Phoebe bournei, commonly known as the Chinese boxwood, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a compact, rounded growth habit. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Phoebe bournei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate a range of growing conditions.
