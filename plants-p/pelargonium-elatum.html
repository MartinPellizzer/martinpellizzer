Pelargonium elatum

Pelargonium elatum, commonly known as the double-flowered geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its showy, double blooms that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade. Pelargonium elatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
