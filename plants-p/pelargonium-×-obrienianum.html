Pelargonium × obrienianum

Pelargonium × obrienianum, commonly known as the O'Brien geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is native to South Africa and is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
