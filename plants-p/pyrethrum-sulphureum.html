Pyrethrum sulphureum

Pyrethrum sulphureum, commonly known as golden pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads composed of numerous ray florets. Its botanical name reflects its sulfur-yellow coloration, which is a distinguishing feature.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant appearance and hardy nature.
