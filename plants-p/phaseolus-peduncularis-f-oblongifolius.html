Phaseolus peduncularis f. oblongifolius

Phaseolus peduncularis f. oblongifolius, commonly known as the oblong-leaf scarlet runner bean, is a botanical variant of the scarlet runner bean.

It is characterized by its elongated, broad leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It is a climbing plant that thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and edible pods, which are often used in culinary applications.
