Psychotria lancilimba

Psychotria lancilimba, commonly known as the slender psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It produces small, dark blue berries that are often used in traditional medicine.
