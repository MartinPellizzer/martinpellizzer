Panicum kleinii

Panicum kleinii, commonly known as Klein's grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
