Pterophyton pauciflorum

Pterophyton pauciflorum, commonly known as the sparse-flowered fern, is a unique species belonging to the order Pterobryales.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate vein patterns. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures are diminutive and clustered, contributing to its limited floral display.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
