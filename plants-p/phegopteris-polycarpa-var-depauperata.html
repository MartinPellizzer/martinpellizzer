Phegopteris polycarpa var. depauperata

Phegopteris polycarpa var. depauperata, commonly known as the slender cliff fern, is a variant of the polycarpa fern species.

It is a small, delicate fern that typically grows in rocky or shaded environments. The plant has slender, linear fronds with a distinctive, sparse arrangement of pinnae. It is characterized by its reduced foliage and is often found in moist, calcareous soils.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and ecological adaptability.
