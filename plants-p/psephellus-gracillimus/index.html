Psephellus Gracillimus

Psephellus gracillimus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its striking violet-blue petals and slender, grass-like foliage. This species thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade. Due to its limited habitat and susceptibility to climate change, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
