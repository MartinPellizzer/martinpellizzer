Pholidia gilesii

Pholidia gilesii, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Pholidia gilesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.
