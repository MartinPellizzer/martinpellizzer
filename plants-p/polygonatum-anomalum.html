Polygonatum anomalum

Polygonatum anomalum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its berries are typically red or yellow, and it is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Polygonatum anomalum is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
