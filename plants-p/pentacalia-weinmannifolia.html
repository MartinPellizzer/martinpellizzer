Pentacalia weinmannifolia

Pentacalia weinmannifolia, commonly known as the Weinmannia-like pentacalia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.
