Panicum longissimum

Panicum longissimum, commonly known as the longleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces fluffy, feathery inflorescences that are highly visible during the flowering season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its adaptability makes it a common species in both natural and cultivated settings.
