Polygonatum darrisii

Polygonatum darrisii, commonly known as the Darris' Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, arching stems and clusters of white or greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polygonatum darrisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
