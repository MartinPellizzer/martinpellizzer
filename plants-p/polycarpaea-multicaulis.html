Polycarpaea multicaulis

Polycarpaea multicaulis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often velvety to the touch. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Polycarpaea multicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
