Petunia mantiqueirensis

Petunia mantiqueirensis, commonly known as the Mantiqueira petunia, is a species native to Brazil, specifically the Mantiqueira mountain range.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be blue, violet, or white, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Petunia mantiqueirensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and adaptability.
