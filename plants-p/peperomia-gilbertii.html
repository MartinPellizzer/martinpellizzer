Peperomia gilbertii

Peperomia gilbertii, commonly known as the Gilbert's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, typically appearing in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia gilbertii is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal water and moderate humidity to flourish.
