Plectranthus carnosifolius

Plectranthus carnosifolius, commonly known as the purple-leaf plectranthus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety, and often purplish foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental value make it a versatile plant for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
