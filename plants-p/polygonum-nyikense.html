Polygonum nyikense

Polygonum nyikense, commonly known as the Nyikense knotweed, is a perennial herb native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive swollen nodes and arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands. Its stems are often reddish or purplish, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters.

Polygonum nyikense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
