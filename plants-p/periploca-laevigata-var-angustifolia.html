Periploca laevigata var. angustifolia

Periploca laevigata var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf periploca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
