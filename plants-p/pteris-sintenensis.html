Pteris sintenensis

Pteris sintenensis, commonly known as the Sinten fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thin layer of soil, and the fronds are usually bipinnate, with a light green color.

Pteris sintenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
