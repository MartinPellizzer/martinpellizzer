Peperomia hesperomannii

Peperomia hesperomannii, commonly known as the Florida peperomia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia hesperomannii is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
