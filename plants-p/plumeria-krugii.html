Plumeria krugii

Plumeria krugii, commonly known as the Krug's flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has fragrant, waxy petals and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil.

The leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slight serration along the edges.
