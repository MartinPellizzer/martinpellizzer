Pisum abyssinicum

Pisum abyssinicum, commonly known as Abyssinian pea, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and agricultural studies in its native region.
