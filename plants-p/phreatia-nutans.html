Phreatia nutans

Phreatia nutans, commonly known as the nodding phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescence and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in lowland rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the soil and surrounding air.

Phreatia nutans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
