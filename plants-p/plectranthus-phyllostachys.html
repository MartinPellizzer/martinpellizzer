Plectranthus phyllostachys

Plectranthus phyllostachys, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or giant bamboo, is a member of the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height, resembling true bamboo. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Despite its bamboo-like appearance, it is not a true bamboo but a flowering plant with a unique botanical structure.
