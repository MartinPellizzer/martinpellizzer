Pennisetum trachyphyllum

Pennisetum trachyphyllum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce fluffy, purple-pink plumes during the flowering season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum trachyphyllum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and pollinators.
