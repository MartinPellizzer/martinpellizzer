Prunus japonica f. minor

Prunus japonica f. minor, commonly known as the Japanese dwarf cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub native to Japan.

It features small, rounded leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in autumn. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. It is well-suited for container growing and small garden spaces due to its compact size.

The variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
