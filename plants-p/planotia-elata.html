Planotia elata

Planotia elata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip.

Planotia elata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
