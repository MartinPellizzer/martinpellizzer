Polypodium mindense

Polypodium mindense, commonly known as the Mindanao polypody, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous sporangia on the underside of its leaves.

Polypodium mindense is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
