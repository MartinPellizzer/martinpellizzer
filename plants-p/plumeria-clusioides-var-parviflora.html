Plumeria clusioides var. parviflora

Plumeria clusioides var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical and glossy green. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its bark is smooth and often peels to reveal a mottled pattern.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking floral display and adaptability to warm climates.
