Pleurothallis spiralis

Pleurothallis spiralis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. It features delicate, spiraling inflorescences that give it its common name, with small, intricate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant's foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
