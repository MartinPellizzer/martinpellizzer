Portulaca nana

Portulaca nana, commonly known as dwarf portulaca, is a low-growing annual herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding ornamental value to gardens.
