Peperomia caniana

Peperomia caniana, commonly known as the Brazilian peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia caniana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
