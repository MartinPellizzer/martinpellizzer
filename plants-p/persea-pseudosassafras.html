Persea pseudosassafras

Persea pseudosassafras, commonly known as the Mexican laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its bark is reddish-brown and often has a distinctive aromatic scent.

The fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains a single seed.
