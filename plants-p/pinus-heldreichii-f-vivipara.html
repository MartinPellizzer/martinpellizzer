Pinus heldreichii f. vivipara

Pinus heldreichii f. vivipara, commonly known as the Viviparous Fir, is a rare and unique variant of the Heldreich's Fir.

It is characterized by its vivipary, a reproductive trait where mature seeds are found on the cones before they are fully mature. This feature makes it distinct from other pine species. The plant is native to the high altitudes of the Balkan Peninsula, where it thrives in cool, moist climates.

Its botanical significance lies in its rare reproductive mechanism and its role in understanding plant adaptation in mountainous regions.
