Peperomia circulifolia

Peperomia circulifolia, commonly known as the circular leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, circular or kidney-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
