Phaseolus oblongus var. alboruber

Phaseolus oblongus var. alboruber, commonly known as the yellow runner bean, is a variant of the common bean species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange pod color and typically produces small, edible seeds. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value. It has compound leaves with trifoliate structures and produces small, white or pale purple flowers.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and is commonly grown in home gardens and small-scale agriculture.
