Peperomia erikii

Peperomia erikii, commonly known as the Erik's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features succulent, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or green, which are usually followed by dark red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia erikii is valued for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
