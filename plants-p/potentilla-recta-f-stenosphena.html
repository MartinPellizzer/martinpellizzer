Potentilla recta f. stenosphena

Potentilla recta f. stenosphena, commonly known as narrow-leaved potentilla, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, upright stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow and elliptical, with a leathery texture and a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are typically yellow, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine environments.
