Parietaria nitens

Parietaria nitens, commonly known as the wall pennywort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny greenish-white flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as walls and stone crevices. The plant has a creeping growth habit and produces achenes as its fruit.

Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, which can cause skin irritation in some individuals.
