Piper zamboangae

Piper zamboangae, commonly known as the Zamboanga pepper or Zamboanga long pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically the Zamboanga Peninsula, and grows in tropical rainforests. The plant is a climbing vine that produces small, elongated berries that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its botanical features include opposite, ovate leaves and a distinctive, aromatic fruit.

The plant's morphology and chemical composition make it valuable for both ecological and commercial purposes.
