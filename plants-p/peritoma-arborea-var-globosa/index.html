Peritoma Arborea Var. Globosa

Peritoma arborea var. globosa is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers and compact growth habit. Native to specific regions in South America, this variety thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil. Its globular shape and clustered blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and container planting. The plant's ability to attract pollinators adds to its ecological value. With proper care, it can flourish and bring a splash of color to any landscape.
