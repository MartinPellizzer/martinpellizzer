Primula delicata

Primula delicata, commonly known as the delicate primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of down, which helps protect it from harsh weather.

Primula delicata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
