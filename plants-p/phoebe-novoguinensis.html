Phoebe novoguinensis

Phoebe novoguinensis, commonly known as the New Zealand phoebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and forested areas. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are white or pale blue in color.

The fruit is a capsule that releases seeds when mature, contributing to its spread in its natural habitat.
