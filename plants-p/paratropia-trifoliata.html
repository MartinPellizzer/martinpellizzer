Paratropia trifoliata

Paratropia trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaved paratropia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
