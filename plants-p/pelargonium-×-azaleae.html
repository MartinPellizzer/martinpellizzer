Pelargonium × azaleae

Pelargonium × azaleae, commonly known as the azalea geranium, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various geranium species.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has velvety, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil.

Pelargonium × azaleae is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to different garden environments.
