Platysepalum violaceum var. violaceum

Platysepalum violaceum var. violaceum, commonly known as the violet-sepal orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and produces clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories.

Its flowers are fragrant and bloom during the warmer months of the year.
