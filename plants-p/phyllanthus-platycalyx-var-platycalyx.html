Phyllanthus platycalyx var. platycalyx

Phyllanthus platycalyx var. platycalyx, commonly known as the broad-leaved phyllanthus, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and ornamental landscapes.
