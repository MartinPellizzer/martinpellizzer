Plukenetia chaponensis

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its prickly stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into fleshy, orange-red berries. These berries are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor, often used in local cuisines.
