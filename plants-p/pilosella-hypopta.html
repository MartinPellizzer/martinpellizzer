Pilosella hypopta

Pilosella hypopta, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Pilosella hypopta produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
