Pityrogramma martinicensis

Pityrogramma martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is a small, aquatic fern that typically grows in freshwater environments such as streams and ponds. The plant has delicate, finely divided fronds that are often submerged or partially submerged in water. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to submerged surfaces.

The species is native to the Caribbean region, including parts of the Greater Antilles.
