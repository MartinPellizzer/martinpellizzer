Philodendron maguirei

Philodendron maguirei, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron maguirei is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
