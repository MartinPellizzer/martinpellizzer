Pyrus malus sargentii

Pyrus malus sargentii, commonly known as Sargent's apple.

It is a species of apple tree native to China, characterized by its ornamental value and unique fruit. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its flowers are white and clustered in inflorescences, blooming in early spring.

The fruit is typically small, yellowish, and has a tart flavor, making it less suitable for culinary use but valued in horticulture.
