Perezia multiflora subsp. multiflora

Perezia multiflora subsp.

multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora perezia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright, branched stems that bear small, clustered flowers in shades of white to pale pink.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.
