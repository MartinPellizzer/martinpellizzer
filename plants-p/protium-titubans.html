Protium titubans

Protium titubans, commonly known as the Florida titi or Florida wild fig, is a small evergreen tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences, followed by edible, orange-red fruits that are a food source for various wildlife.
