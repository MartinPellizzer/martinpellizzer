Pennisetum darfuricum

Pennisetum darfuricum, commonly known as desert grass or Sudan grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Pennisetum darfuricum is often used for forage and erosion control in dry regions.
