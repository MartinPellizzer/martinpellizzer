Phylica globosa

Phylica globosa, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Phylica globosa is also valued for its ecological role, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
