Pueraria barbata

Pueraria barbata, commonly known as wild yam, is a climbing legume native to Asia.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing numerous kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Pueraria barbata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
