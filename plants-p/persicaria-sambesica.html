Persicaria sambesica

Persicaria sambesica, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features erect, slender stems that are often reddish in color and covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with serrated edges and a hairy surface.

Persicaria sambesica is often found in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.
