Phyteuma pedemontanum

Phyteuma pedemontanum, commonly known as the Alpine phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers.
