Panicum obtusifolium var. acutifolium

Panicum obtusifolium var. acutifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf bluestem, is a grass species native to the central and eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, fluffy seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and open woodland habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
