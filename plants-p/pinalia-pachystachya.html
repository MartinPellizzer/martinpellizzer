Pinalia pachystachya

Pinalia pachystachya, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-blue plumes that create a dramatic visual effect in gardens. The plant produces dense, cylindrical inflorescences that are both visually appealing and attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for adding height and texture to modern garden designs.
