Phillyrea latifolia f. spinosa

Phillyrea latifolia f. spinosa, commonly known as the spiny Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage, which provides natural protection against herbivores. The leaves are typically elliptical, leathery, and have a glossy green surface. It produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits.

This plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and woodland areas.
