Pleurothallis quinquecallosa

Pleurothallis quinquecallosa, commonly known as the five-ridged pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, particularly in regions with high humidity and mild temperatures. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, intricate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its evergreen foliage.

This species is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and adaptability to shaded environments.
