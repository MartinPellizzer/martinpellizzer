Periclymenum vulgare

Periclymenum vulgare, commonly known as woodbine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in woodland edges and moist soils, often forming dense thickets in its native range.
