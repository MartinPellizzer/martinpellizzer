Perrierophytum paniculatum

Perrierophytum paniculatum, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is well adapted to drought conditions.

Perrierophytum paniculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
