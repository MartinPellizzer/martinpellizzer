Phanera clemensiorum

Phanera clemensiorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is native to regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types, it is both a useful ornamental plant and a potential weed in agricultural settings.
