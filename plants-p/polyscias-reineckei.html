Polyscias reineckei

Polyscias reineckei, commonly known as the New Zealand glory bush, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for native birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in subtropical gardens.

Its adaptability and attractive foliage make it a valuable addition to horticultural collections.
