Peperomia flavida var. flavida

Peperomia flavida var. flavida, commonly known as the yellow peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright yellow to green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and striking foliage make it an attractive addition to both outdoor gardens and indoor spaces.
