Panicum chloroticum var. sylvestre

Panicum chloroticum var. sylvestre, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and panicles of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as swamps and riverbanks. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed or wetland soils.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive, feathery appearance.
