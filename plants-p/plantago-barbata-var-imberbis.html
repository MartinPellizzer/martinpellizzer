Plantago barbata var. imberbis

Plantago barbata var. imberbis, commonly known as the smoothleaf plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and lack the bristly hairs found in the typical variety. It produces small, greenish flowers in a spike-like inflorescence, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its medicinal and ecological benefits.
