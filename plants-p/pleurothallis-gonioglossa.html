Pleurothallis gonioglossa

Pleurothallis gonioglossa, commonly known as the "spurred lip pleurothallis," is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and arranged in a compact rosette.

This orchid prefers high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for intermediate care in indoor cultivation.
