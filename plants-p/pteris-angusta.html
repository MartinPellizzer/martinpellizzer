Pteris angusta

Pteris angusta, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, bright green, lance-shaped leaves with a distinct, serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pteris angusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.
