Philibertia cionophora

Philibertia cionophora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved filbert, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, hard seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.
