Psydrax splendens

Psydrax splendens, commonly known as the splendour senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
