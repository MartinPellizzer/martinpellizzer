Pleurothallis sertularioides var. trinitensis

Pleurothallis sertularioides var. trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidad orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean region.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces a unique, intricate inflorescence.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
