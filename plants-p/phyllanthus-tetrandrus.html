Phyllanthus tetrandrus

Phyllanthus tetrandrus, commonly known as the African date tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant is also noted for its ability to thrive in poor soils and arid conditions.
