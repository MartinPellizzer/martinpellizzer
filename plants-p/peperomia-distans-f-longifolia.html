Peperomia distans f. longifolia

Peperomia distans f. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are elongated and often have a waxy texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
