Piper harmandii

Piper harmandii, commonly known as Harmand's pepper or the African pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by round, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
