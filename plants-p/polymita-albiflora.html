Polymita albiflora

Polymita albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered polymita, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and small, clustered flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and oval-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Polymita albiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid climates.
