Pseudolitchi

Pseudolitchi, commonly known as the false lychee, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.
