Perezia hebeclada

Perezia hebeclada, commonly known as the Chilean hebe, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Chile.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and evergreen foliage. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in temperate climates.
