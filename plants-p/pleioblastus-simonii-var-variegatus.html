Pleioblastus simonii var. variegatus

Pleioblastus simonii var. variegatus, commonly known as the Variegated Japanese Forest Grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching culms covered in narrow, lance-shaped leaves that display a variegated pattern of white, green, and sometimes yellow. The plant is cold-hardy and thrives in partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, providing year-round visual interest.

It is often used in landscape design for its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
