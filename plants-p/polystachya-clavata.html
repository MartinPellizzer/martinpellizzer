Polystachya clavata

Polystachya clavata, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its dense, clustered spikes of small, pale green to yellowish flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Polystachya clavata is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
