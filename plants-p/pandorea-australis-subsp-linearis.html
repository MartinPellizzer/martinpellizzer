Pandorea australis subsp. linearis

Pandorea australis subsp.

linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandorea, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Australia. It features slender, woody stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters, often twining around supports.

The leaves are narrow and elliptic, with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. Its flowers are tubular, typically pink or pale purple, and appear in clusters along the stems.

The plant produces small, round, greenish-white fruits that contain several seeds.
