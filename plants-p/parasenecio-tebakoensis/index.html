Parasenecio Tebakoensis

Parasenecio tebakoensis is a rare and intriguing plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental members. This species is notable for its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its ecological significance, little is known about its reproductive habits and ecological role. Its discovery highlights the rich biodiversity of Ethiopia's mountainous ecosystems.
