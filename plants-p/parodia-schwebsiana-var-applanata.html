Parodia schwebsiana var. applanata

Parodia schwebsiana var. applanata, commonly known as the flat-topped cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Argentina.

It features a globular stem with a flat, broad top that gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
