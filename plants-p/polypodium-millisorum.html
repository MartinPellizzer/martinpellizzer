Polypodium millisorum

Polypodium millisorum, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a rugged texture. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Polypodium millisorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
