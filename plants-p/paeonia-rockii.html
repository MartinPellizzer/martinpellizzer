Paeonia rockii

Paeonia rockii, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces woody stems that can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Paeonia rockii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
