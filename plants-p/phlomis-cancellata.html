Phlomis cancellata

Phlomis cancellata, commonly known as the silver phlomis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall spikes of purple to pink flowers during the summer months. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Phlomis cancellata is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.
