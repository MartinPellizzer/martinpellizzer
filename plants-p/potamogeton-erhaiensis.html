Potamogeton erhaiensis

Potamogeton erhaiensis, commonly known as the Erhai watermilfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to China, specifically found in Lake Erhai.

It belongs to the family Potamogetonaceae and is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne in clusters near the water surface. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native region.
