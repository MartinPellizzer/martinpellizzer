Pleione saxicola

Pleione saxicola, commonly known as the rock pleione, is a perennial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on rocks or in forest undergrowth. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water and nutrients.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
