Pittosporum tenuifolium variegatum

Pittosporum tenuifolium variegatum, commonly known as the variegated pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features broad, dark green leaves with white or cream variegation along the edges. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
