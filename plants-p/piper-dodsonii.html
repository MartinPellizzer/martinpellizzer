Piper dodsonii

Piper dodsonii, commonly known as the Dodson's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
