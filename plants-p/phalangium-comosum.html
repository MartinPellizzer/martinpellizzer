Phalangium comosum

Phalangium comosum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. It is native to regions of Africa and thrives in warm, arid environments.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
