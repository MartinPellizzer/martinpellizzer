Paxistima canbyi

Paxistima canbyi, commonly known as the Canby's paxistima, is a succulent plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid or semi-arid environments.

Paxistima canbyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh growing conditions.
