Pandanus pynaertii

Pandanus pynaertii, commonly known as the Pynaert's pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the leaves. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Pandanus pynaertii is valued not only for its ornamental appearance but also for its cultural and practical significance in local communities.
