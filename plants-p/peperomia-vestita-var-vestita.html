Peperomia vestita var. vestita

Peperomia vestita var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with dark green, glossy leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, adding subtle ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
