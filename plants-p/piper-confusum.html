Piper confusum

Piper confusum, commonly known as the confused pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface and a strong, spicy scent.

Piper confusum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
