Perezia ciliosa var. dentata

Perezia ciliosa var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed perezia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with distinctive serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
