Pritzelia zebrina var. discolor

Pritzelia zebrina var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated zebra plant, is a unique and striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves, which display a combination of green, white, and yellow hues, creating a zebra-like pattern. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant coloration. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in warm climates.

The plant is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
