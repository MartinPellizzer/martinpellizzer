Platystachys cinerascens

Platystachys cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
