Prosopanche costaricensis

Prosopanche costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant is adapted to grow in dry, sunny environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
