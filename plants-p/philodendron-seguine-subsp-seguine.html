Philodendron seguine subsp. seguine

Philodendron seguine subsp.

seguine, commonly known as the velvet leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped.

The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach significant heights when provided with support. Its leaves are often variegated with lighter green or silver markings, adding ornamental value.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity for optimal growth.
