Panicum parlatorei

Panicum parlatorei, commonly known as the Parlatore's panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of spikelets, which produce small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Panicum parlatorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a forage crop.
