Pittosporum cavaleriei

Pittosporum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
