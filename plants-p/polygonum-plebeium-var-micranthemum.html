Polygonum plebeium var. micranthemum

Polygonum plebeium var. micranthemum, commonly known as small-leaf smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually less than 2 cm in length, giving it its common name. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to reddish in color and bloom in late summer.

It is often found in wetland ecosystems and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
