Peperomia roseocaulis

Peperomia roseocaulis, commonly known as the pink-stem peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems that are distinctly pinkish in color, which is a unique characteristic among peperomias. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. It blooms with delicate, pale green to white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months.

Peperomia roseocaulis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
