Plantago schneideri var. delicatior

Plantago schneideri var. delicatior, commonly known as the slender plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
