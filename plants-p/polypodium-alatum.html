Polypodium alatum

Polypodium alatum, commonly known as the Boston fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to the Americas.

It features feathery, arching fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense clusters. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
