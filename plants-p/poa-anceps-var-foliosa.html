Poa anceps var. foliosa

Poa anceps var. foliosa, commonly known as the broadleaf bentgrass, is a variant of the bentgrass species native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its wide, flat grass blades and dense, tufted growth habit. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and wetlands. It has a low-growing stature and is valued for its ornamental appeal in landscaping.

The variety is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to the typical form of Poa anceps.
