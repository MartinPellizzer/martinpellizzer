Pelargonium mahernium

Pelargonium mahernium, commonly known as the Mahernium geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. Pelargonium mahernium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
