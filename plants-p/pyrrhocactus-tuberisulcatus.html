Pyrrhocactus tuberisulcatus

Pyrrhocactus tuberisulcatus, commonly known as the tubercled cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem covered in prominent, rounded tubercles. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
