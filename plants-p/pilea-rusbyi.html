Pilea rusbyi

Pilea rusbyi, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are round and slightly fleshy, resembling small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea rusbyi is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
