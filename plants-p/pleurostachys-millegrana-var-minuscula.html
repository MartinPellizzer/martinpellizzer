Pleurostachys millegrana var. minuscula

Pleurostachys millegrana var. minuscula, commonly known as the dwarf silver hairgrass, is a low-growing, perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features slender, grass-like blades that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flower spikes during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
