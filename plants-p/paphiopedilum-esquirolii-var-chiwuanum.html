Paphiopedilum esquirolii var. chiwuanum

Paphiopedilum esquirolii var. chiwuanum, commonly known as the Chiwanum Lady's Slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to China.

It features a distinctive pouch-shaped lip that is a hallmark of the Paphiopedilum genus, often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, which bloom during the spring and summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
