Protea sphaerocephala

Protea sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink in color. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it requires specific care to flourish.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
