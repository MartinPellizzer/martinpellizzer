Phyllanthus amoenus

Phyllanthus amoenus, commonly known as the "Pretty Phyllanthus," is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are often elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
