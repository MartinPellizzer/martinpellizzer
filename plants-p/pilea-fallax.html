Pilea fallax

Pilea fallax, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that resemble small, rounded coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea fallax thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
