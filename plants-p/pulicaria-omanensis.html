Pulicaria omanensis

Pulicaria omanensis, commonly known as the Oman tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, grey-green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. It is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low precipitation.

Pulicaria omanensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
