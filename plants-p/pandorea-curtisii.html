Pandorea curtisii

Pandorea curtisii, commonly known as the pink trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and has a woody stem with thorn-like projections. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
