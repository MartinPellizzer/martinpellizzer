Pteronia eenii

Pteronia eenii, commonly known as the Cape Flats pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
