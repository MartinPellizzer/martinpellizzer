Persicaria hastatotriloba f. albiflora

Persicaria hastatotriloba f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered smartweed, is a variant of the smartweed species native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and stems that are often reddish in color.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
