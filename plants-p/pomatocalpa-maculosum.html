Pomatocalpa maculosum

Pomatocalpa maculosum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces a single, showy flower per stem. The flower has a distinctive greenish-yellow color with a dark red or purple lip, and the leaves are linear and often spotted. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique floral appearance.
