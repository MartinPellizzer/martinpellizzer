Primula duthieana

Primula duthieana, commonly known as the Duthie's primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Primula duthieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
