Procris ruhlandii

Procris ruhlandii, commonly known as the Ruhland's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive, recurved lip and a greenish-yellow ovary. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded habitat.

Procris ruhlandii is often found in grasslands and rocky outcrops, where it blooms during the summer months.
