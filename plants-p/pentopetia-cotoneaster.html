Pentopetia cotoneaster

Pentopetia cotoneaster, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The cotoneaster is also valued for its role in ecological landscapes, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
