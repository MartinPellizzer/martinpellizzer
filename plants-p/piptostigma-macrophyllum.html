Piptostigma macrophyllum

Piptostigma macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
