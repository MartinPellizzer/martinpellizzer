Panicum prionitis var. varium

Panicum prionitis var. varium, commonly known as the slender panic grass, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, slender blades and pale green to silver-colored foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and marshy areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish or purple in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
