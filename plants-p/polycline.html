Polycline

Polycline, commonly known as the common polycline, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces bright red or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.

Polycline is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of wetland environments.
