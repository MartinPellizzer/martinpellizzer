Pinellia fujianensis

Pinellia fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian pinellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its tuberous rhizomes and arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to greenish-white flowers enclosed in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
