Peperomia urvilleana

Peperomia urvilleana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features small, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia urvilleana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
