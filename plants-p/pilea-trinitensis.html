Pilea trinitensis

Pilea trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinitarian begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, white, and pink, which adds to its ornamental appeal.

Pilea trinitensis thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
