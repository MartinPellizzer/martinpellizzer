Pyracantha coccinea var. lalandei

Pyracantha coccinea var. lalandei, commonly known as the red-fruited firethorn, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and clusters of bright orange-red berries that persist through winter. The plant features small, glossy dark green leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value, particularly for its berries and winter interest.

Pyracantha coccinea var. lalandei is relatively low maintenance and suitable for hedges or container gardening.
