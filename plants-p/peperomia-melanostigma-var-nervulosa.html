Peperomia melanostigma var. nervulosa

Peperomia melanostigma var. nervulosa, commonly known as the Veinleaf Peperomia, is a compact evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dark green, velvety leaves with prominent veins that give it its distinguishing name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
