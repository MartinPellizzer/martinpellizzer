Phlox subulata var. setacea

Phlox subulata var. setacea, commonly known as the carpet phlox or stonecrop phlox, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming in early summer. The plant has needle-like, silver-gray foliage that is both drought-tolerant and resistant to pests and diseases. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscaping.
