Peckia boissieri

Peckia boissieri, commonly known as the mountain peckia, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. Peckia boissieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
