Persicaria propinqua

Persicaria propinqua, commonly known as slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features upright stems that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters, which appear in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Persicaria propinqua is often found in moist or wet habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows.
