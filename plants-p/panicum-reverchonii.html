Panicum reverchonii

Panicum reverchonii, commonly known as the yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces bright yellow to golden-green inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds ornamental value.

Panicum reverchonii is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
