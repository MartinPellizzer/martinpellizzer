Phyllostegia triquetra

Phyllostegia triquetra, commonly known as the three-lobed phyllostegia, is a flowering plant species native to the southeastern United States.

Phyllostegia triquetra is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
