Piper san-martinense

Piper san-martinense, commonly known as the San Martin pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elongated, narrow fruits that contain several seeds.

Piper san-martinense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
