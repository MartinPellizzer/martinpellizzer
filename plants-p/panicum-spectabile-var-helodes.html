Panicum spectabile var. helodes

Panicum spectabile var. helodes, commonly known as the purple lovegrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking, feathery plumes of purple or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and long-lasting floral display.

It is often used in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
