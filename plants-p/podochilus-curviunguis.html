Podochilus curviunguis

Podochilus curviunguis, commonly known as the curved-stem orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Podochilus curviunguis is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
