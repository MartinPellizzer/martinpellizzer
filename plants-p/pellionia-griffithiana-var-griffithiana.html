Pellionia griffithiana var. griffithiana

Pellionia griffithiana var. griffithiana, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded garden beds.
