Peperomia abyssinica

Peperomia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian pepper plant, is a species native to Ethiopia.

It is a small, compact plant with thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Peperomia abyssinica is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
