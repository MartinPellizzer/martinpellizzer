Pleurothallis perangusta

Pleurothallis perangusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is part of the large and diverse Pleurothallis genus, which includes over 1,000 species.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
