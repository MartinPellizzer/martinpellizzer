Physurus nobilis var. argyrocentrus

Physurus nobilis var. argyrocentrus, commonly known as the silver-tailed rat-tail plant, is a unique species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage and long, trailing stems that give it a rat-tail appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets.
