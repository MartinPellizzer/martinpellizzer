Peperomia meeboldii

Peperomia meeboldii, commonly known as the Meebold's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia meeboldii is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
