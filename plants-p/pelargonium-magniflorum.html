Pelargonium magniflorum

Pelargonium magniflorum, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant features upright stems with lobed, hairy leaves that are often aromatic. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Pelargonium magniflorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
