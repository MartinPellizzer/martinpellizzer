Peperomia flagrans

Peperomia flagrans, commonly known as the shining peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or green, which are followed by unique, elongated seed pods. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia flagrans is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
