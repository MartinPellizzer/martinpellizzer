Peperomia parkeriana

Peperomia parkeriana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy, silver-green leaves that are oval-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia parkeriana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
