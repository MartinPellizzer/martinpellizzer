Polygala alba var. schaffneri

Polygala alba var. schaffneri, commonly known as the white milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
