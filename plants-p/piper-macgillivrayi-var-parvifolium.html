Piper macgillivrayi var. parvifolium

Piper macgillivrayi var. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctive, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berry-like fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its botanical characteristics include a climbing habit and a preference for humid, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
