Poa taiwanicola

Poa taiwanicola, commonly known as Taiwan bluegrass, is a species of grass native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and silver-blue foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinct blue-green coloration.

Poa taiwanicola is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
