Pingraea sagittalis

Pingraea sagittalis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the southern United States and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Pingraea sagittalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
