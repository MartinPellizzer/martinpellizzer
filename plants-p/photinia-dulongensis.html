Photinia dulongensis

Photinia dulongensis, commonly known as the Dulong's photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy red or pink inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical with serrated edges, and the flowers appear in clusters during the spring.

Photinia dulongensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural landscapes.
