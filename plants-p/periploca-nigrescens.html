Periploca nigrescens

Periploca nigrescens, commonly known as black milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are simple and opposite. Its latex contains toxic compounds, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
