Polygonum constans

Polygonum constans, commonly known as redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Polygonum constans is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas.
