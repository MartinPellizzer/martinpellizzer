Phagnalon arabicum

Phagnalon arabicum, commonly known as the Arabian phagnalon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water requirements.

Its root system helps prevent soil erosion in its native habitat.
