Polystachya anthoceros

Polystachya anthoceros, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with three stamens and a single pistil.

The plant plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
