Paphiopedilum × schlechterianum

Paphiopedilum × schlechterianum, commonly known as the Schlechter's lady's slipper orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that serves as a pollination trap. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, pink, and green, with a distinctive coloration pattern.

It requires high light and consistent moisture to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
