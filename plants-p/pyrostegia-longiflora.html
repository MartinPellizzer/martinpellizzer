Pyrostegia longiflora

Pyrostegia longiflora, commonly known as the flame vine, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous racemes of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Pyrostegia longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow as a vine or shrub depending on environmental conditions.
