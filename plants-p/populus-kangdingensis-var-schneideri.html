Populus kangdingensis var. schneideri

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The plant thrives in cold, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
