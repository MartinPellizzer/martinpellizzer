Pelargonium rosetta

Pelargonium rosetta, commonly known as the Rosetta geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically during the spring and summer months. Pelargonium rosetta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
