Polystachya longiscapa

Polystachya longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed spike orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Polystachya longiscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
