Peperomia lasiostigma

Peperomia lasiostigma, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a distinctive white or silver underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both home and office environments.
