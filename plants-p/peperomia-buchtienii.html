Peperomia buchtienii

Peperomia buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
