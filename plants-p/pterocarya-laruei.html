Pterocarya laruei

Pterocarya laruei, commonly known as the Chinese wingnut, is a deciduous tree native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Juglandaceae family, which includes walnuts and hickories. The tree is characterized by its distinctive, winged samaras that aid in wind dispersal of its seeds. Its leaves are alternate, compound, and often have a serrated edge.

The bark is thick and deeply furrowed, providing a rugged appearance.
