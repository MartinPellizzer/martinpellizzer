Physaliastrum macrodon

Physaliastrum macrodon, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a unique aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, floating fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often forming dense mats on the water surface. Its reproductive structures are specialized, with sporangia developing on the underside of the fronds.

Physaliastrum macrodon plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and shelter for various aquatic organisms.
