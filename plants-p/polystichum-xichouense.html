Polystichum xichouense

Polystichum xichouense, commonly known as the Xichou fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The Xichou fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
