Pilea funkikensis var. somae

Pilea funkikensis var. somae, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
