Papaya manihot

Papaya manihot, commonly known as arrowleaf or manioc, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and a robust root system that produces the starchy tuberous root. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant is also an important crop in many regions for both food and industrial uses.
