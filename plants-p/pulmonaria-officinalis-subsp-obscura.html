Pulmonaria officinalis subsp. obscura

Pulmonaria officinalis subsp.

obscura, commonly known as the dark lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It features basal rosettes of dark green, velvety leaves with distinctive purple and white markings.

The plant produces tall, slender stems bearing clusters of bell-shaped, pinkish-purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported respiratory benefits.
