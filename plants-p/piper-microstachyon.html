Piper microstachyon

Piper microstachyon, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is also cultivated as a houseplant.
