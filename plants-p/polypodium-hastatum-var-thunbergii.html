Polypodium hastatum var. thunbergii

Polypodium hastatum var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its upright, narrow rhizome and pinnate fronds with a distinctive, shield-like shape. The fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with lanceolate pinnae that are often glossy and dark green. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, commonly found in forest understories across Asia.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shady gardens.
