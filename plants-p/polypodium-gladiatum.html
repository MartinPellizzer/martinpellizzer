Polypodium gladiatum

Polypodium gladiatum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with the underground stem producing both roots and new shoots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forests and rocky areas.

Polypodium gladiatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
