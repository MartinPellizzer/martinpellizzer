Piper pubisubmarginalum

Piper pubisubmarginalum, commonly known as the pubisubmarginal pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its finely pubescent stems and leaves with a distinctive submarginal pubescence. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes, and it produces small, round berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
