Platyopuntia inamoena f. spinigera

Platyopuntia inamoena f. spinigera, commonly known as the spiny paddle cactus, is a unique variant of the paddle cactus species.

It is characterized by its thick, flat, paddle-shaped stems that are covered in spines, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it well adapted to desert conditions.

Its slow growth and striking form make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
