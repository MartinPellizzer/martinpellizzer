Peperomia duartensis

Peperomia duartensis, commonly known as the Duart's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen perennial has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.
