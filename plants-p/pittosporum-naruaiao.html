Pittosporum naruaiao

Pittosporum naruaiao, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum naruaiao is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
