Peperomia fulvescens

Peperomia fulvescens, commonly known as the Orange Jewel or Red-veined Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with prominent reddish veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia fulvescens is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
