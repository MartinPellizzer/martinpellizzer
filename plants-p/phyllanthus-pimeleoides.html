Phyllanthus pimeleoides

Phyllanthus pimeleoides, commonly known as the African date palm, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are often consumed raw or processed into beverages. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
