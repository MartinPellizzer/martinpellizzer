Portulaca parviflora

Portulaca parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered portulaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that help it retain moisture. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in clusters and are typically short-lived.

Portulaca parviflora is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
