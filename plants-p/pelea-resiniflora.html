Pelea resiniflora

Pelea resiniflora, commonly known as the resinous pelea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Pelea resiniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
