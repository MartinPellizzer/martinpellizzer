Pleurothallis perpusilla

Pleurothallis perpusilla, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, compact growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
