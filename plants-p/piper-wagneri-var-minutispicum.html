Piper wagneri var. minutispicum

Piper wagneri var. minutispicum, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are typically used in local culinary traditions.

Due to its unique appearance and flavor, it is often cultivated as an ornamental and medicinal plant.
