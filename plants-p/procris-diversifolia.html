Procris diversifolia

Procris diversifolia, commonly known as the variable fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that exhibit a wide variety of shapes and sizes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually compound, with multiple pinnae that can vary in arrangement and appearance.

Procris diversifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
