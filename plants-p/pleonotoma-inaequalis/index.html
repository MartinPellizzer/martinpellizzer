Pleonotoma Inaequalis

Pleonotoma inaequalis, scientifically known as Pleonotoma inaequalis, is a unique species of beetle belonging to the family Scarabaeidae. This small insect is often found in moist environments, particularly in tropical and subtropical regions. Its distinguishing features include a dark, metallic sheen and a body shape that is both elongated and somewhat flattened. The beetle plays a role in the ecosystem by contributing to nutrient cycling through its feeding habits. Despite its modest size, Pleonotoma inaequalis holds significance in both ecological studies and entomological research.
