Pombalia potosina var. potosina

Pombalia potosina var. potosina, commonly known as the Potosino pomegranate, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its woody stems, broad ovate leaves with a velvety texture, and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds surrounded by a juicy, aromatic pulp.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.
