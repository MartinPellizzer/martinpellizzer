Panicum moharicum

Panicum moharicum, commonly known as Mohar's panicgrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa, and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically with a bluish-green hue.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
