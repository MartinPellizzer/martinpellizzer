Peperomia pterocaulis f. filispica

Peperomia pterocaulis f. filispica, commonly known as the laceleaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, fleshy leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a pale green to silverish hue. The plant has a compact growth habit, often forming a rosette-like structure, and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tabletop displays.
