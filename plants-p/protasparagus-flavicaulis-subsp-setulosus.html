Protasparagus flavicaulis subsp. setulosus

Protasparagus flavicaulis subsp.

setulosus, commonly known as the golden spiny asparagus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
