Paronychia chlorothyrsa var. haggariensis

Paronychia chlorothyrsa var. haggariensis, commonly known as the Haggariensis sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a dense mat of soft, felt-like leaves that are typically pale green to white in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the late spring to early summer season. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with poor soil conditions.

This variety is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance.
