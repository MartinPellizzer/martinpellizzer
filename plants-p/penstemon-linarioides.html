Penstemon linarioides

Penstemon linarioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf penstemon, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Penstemon linarioides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
