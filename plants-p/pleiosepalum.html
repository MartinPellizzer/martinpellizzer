Pleiosepalum

Pleiosepalum, commonly known as the star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. They are characterized by their striking, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. Pleiosepalum species typically grow as epiphytes or lithophytes, often found on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.
