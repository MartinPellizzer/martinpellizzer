Pilea linearifolia

Pilea linearifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are elongated and linear. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea linearifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
