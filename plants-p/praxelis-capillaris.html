Praxelis capillaris

Praxelis capillaris, commonly known as the slenderleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Praxelis capillaris is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
