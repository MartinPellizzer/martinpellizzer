Pleroma confertiflorum

Pleroma confertiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered pleroma, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically during the warmer months. The plant features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full to partial sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
