Pimelea urvilleana subsp. nesica

Pimelea urvilleana subsp.

nesica, commonly known as the Tasmanian devil's bane, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania. It is a shrub that typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, characterized by its small, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are often visited by insects and birds. It has a woody stem and is adapted to the harsh environmental conditions of its native habitat.
