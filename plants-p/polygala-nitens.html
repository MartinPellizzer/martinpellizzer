Polygala nitens

Polygala nitens, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and the Russian Far East.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, bead-like fruits.

Polygala nitens is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
