Pleurothallis cultellifolis

Pleurothallis cultellifolis, commonly known as the blade-leaf orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a delicate pink or white coloration.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in specific, moist conditions.
