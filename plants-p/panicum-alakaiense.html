Panicum alakaiense

Panicum alakaiense, commonly known as Kauai grass, is a perennial grass native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its ecological importance, it plays a significant role in maintaining the biodiversity of Hawaiian ecosystems.
