Phalaenopsis bellina f. alba

Phalaenopsis bellina f. alba, commonly known as the White Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, white flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. The plant typically produces multiple flower spikes with clustered blooms, often lasting several weeks. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
