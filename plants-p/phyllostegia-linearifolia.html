Phyllostegia linearifolia

Phyllostegia linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phyllostegia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, linear leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Phyllostegia linearifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
