Polygonum vacciniifolium

Polygonum vacciniifolium, commonly known as the redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and are usually pink to reddish in color.

Polygonum vacciniifolium is known for its ability to thrive in wet environments and is often used in wetland restoration projects.
