Polygala krumanina

Polygala krumanina, commonly known as the Krumanina milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically appear in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with slender, thread-like leaflets, and it produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
