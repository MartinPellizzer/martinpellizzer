Pomatocalpa kunstleri subsp. linearipetalum

Pomatocalpa kunstleri subsp.

linearipetalum, commonly known as the linear-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped petals and a compact, upright growth habit.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
