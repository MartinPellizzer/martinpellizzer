Potentilla wenchuensis

Potentilla wenchuensis, commonly known as the Wenchuan potentilla, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically ovate and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
