Primula Macrosiphonia

Large-lipped primrose, scientifically known as Primula macrosiphonia, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. Its distinctive feature is the large, showy lips that form the upper part of its flowers, giving it a unique and almost orchid-like appearance. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
