Physurus parviflorus

Physurus parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Physurus parviflorus is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
