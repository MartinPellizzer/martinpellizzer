Pectanisia phyteuma

Pectanisia phyteuma, commonly known as the red spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, tubular flowers. The plant produces bright red or orange blooms that are often adorned with dark purple or brown markings, resembling the legs of a spider. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Pectanisia phyteuma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
