Pelargonium × joanneum

Pelargonium × joanneum, commonly known as the Regal Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and purple. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with broad, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
