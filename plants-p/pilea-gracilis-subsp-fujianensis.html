Pilea gracilis subsp. fujianensis

Pilea gracilis subsp.

fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and they often display a vibrant green color. This plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in indoor gardening.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for enthusiasts seeking low-maintenance plants.
