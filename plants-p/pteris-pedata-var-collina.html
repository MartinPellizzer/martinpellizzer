Pteris pedata var. collina

Pteris pedata var. collina, commonly known as the mountain maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a shade-loving plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers cool, humid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
