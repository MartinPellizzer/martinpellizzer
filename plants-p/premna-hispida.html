Premna hispida

Premna hispida, commonly known as velvetleaf or silky thornapple, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, often glandular leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain several seeds.

Premna hispida is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in some areas.
