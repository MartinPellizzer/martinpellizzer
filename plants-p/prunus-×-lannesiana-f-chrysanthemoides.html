Prunus × lannesiana f. chrysanthemoides

Prunus × lannesiana f. chrysanthemoides, commonly known as the golden leaf cherry, is a unique hybrid plant that exhibits striking botanical features.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its small, cherry-like fruits. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its showy flowers and colorful leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

This cultivar is prized in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
