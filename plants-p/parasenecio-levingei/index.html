Parasenecio Levingei

Parasenecio levingei, scientifically known as Parasenecio levingei, is a rare and fascinating flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of eastern Africa. This member of the Asteraceae family is characterized by its distinctive foliage and small, daisy-like blooms that thrive in cool, moist environments. Despite its delicate appearance, it possesses remarkable adaptations to survive in its harsh, mountainous habitat. Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
