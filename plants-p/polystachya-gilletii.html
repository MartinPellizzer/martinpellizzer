Polystachya gilletii

Polystachya gilletii, commonly known as the giant flame grass, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or near water sources.

Polystachya gilletii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
