Pelargonium dolomiticum

Pelargonium dolomiticum, commonly known as the Dolomitic Geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant features serrated, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium dolomiticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
