Polygonum suffruticosum

Polygonum suffruticosum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas. The plant has reddish stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
