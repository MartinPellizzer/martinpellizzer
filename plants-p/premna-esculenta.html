Premna esculenta

Premna esculenta, commonly known as Chinese nettle or wild nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale violet or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Premna esculenta is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
