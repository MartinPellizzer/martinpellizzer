Pluchea longepedunculata

Pluchea longepedunculata, commonly known as the long peduncle sunflower, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically arranged in composite heads, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular florets.

Pluchea longepedunculata is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
