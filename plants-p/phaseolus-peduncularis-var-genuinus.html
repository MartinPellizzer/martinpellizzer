Phaseolus peduncularis var. genuinus

Phaseolus peduncularis var. genuinus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and edible pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its botanical features include compound leaves with trifoliate arrangements and a robust root system.

It is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
