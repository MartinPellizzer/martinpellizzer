Petaloma albiflorum

Petaloma albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered petaloma, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Petaloma albiflorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
