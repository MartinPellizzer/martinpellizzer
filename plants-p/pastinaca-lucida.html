Pastinaca lucida

Pastinaca lucida, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.

The oil extracted from its seeds is known for its medicinal properties and is used in various health supplements.
