Plantago acutiloba var. minor

Plantago acutiloba var. minor, commonly known as narrowleaf plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and a prominent central vein. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense spikes, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
