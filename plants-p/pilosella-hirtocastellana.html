Pilosella hirtocastellana

Pilosella hirtocastellana, commonly known as the hairy wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky or gravelly soils.

Pilosella hirtocastellana is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as slopes and wastelands.
