Premna discolor var. discolor

Premna discolor var. discolor, commonly known as the Variegated Silk Tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking variegated leaves with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in clusters. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.

The leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 10 centimeters in length, making it a visually appealing addition to gardens and landscapes.
