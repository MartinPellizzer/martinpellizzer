Podolepis acuminata var. robusta

Podolepis acuminata var. robusta, commonly known as the robust podolepis, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
