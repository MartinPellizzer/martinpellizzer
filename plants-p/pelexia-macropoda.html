Pelexia macropoda

Pelexia macropoda, commonly known as the large-flowered pelexia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northeastern United States.

It features distinctive, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, dark brown seeds in its seed heads. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Pelexia macropoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
