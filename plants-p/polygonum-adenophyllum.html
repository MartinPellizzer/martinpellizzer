Polygonum adenophyllum

Polygonum adenophyllum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright stems that are often reddish in color, especially near the base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters, which are borne on slender pedicels. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and typically have a slightly hairy surface.

Polygonum adenophyllum is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, where it thrives in moist, sunny conditions.
