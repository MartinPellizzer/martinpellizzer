Pteronia aspalatha

Pteronia aspalatha, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Pteronia aspalatha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
