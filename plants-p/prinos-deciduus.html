Prinos deciduus

Prinos deciduus, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that turn vibrant red or orange before falling in autumn. The tree produces showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by orange-red pods. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a notable species in botanical gardens and landscapes.
