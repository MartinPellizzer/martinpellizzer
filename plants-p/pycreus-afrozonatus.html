Pycreus afrozonatus

Pycreus afrozonatus, commonly known as the African pyrethrum.

This plant is a flowering species native to southern Africa, characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange blooms. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. The plant produces a type of pyrethrum, which is used in natural insecticides.

Its botanical features include narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit.
