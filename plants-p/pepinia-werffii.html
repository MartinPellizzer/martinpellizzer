Pepinia werffii

Pepinia werffii, commonly known as the Werff's peperomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

Pepinia werffii is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
