Palaquium vitilevuense

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible, fleshy fruits. Its botanical features make it an important component of the local ecosystem and a subject of botanical interest.
