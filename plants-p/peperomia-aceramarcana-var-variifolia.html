Peperomia aceramarcana var. variifolia

Peperomia aceramarcana var. variifolia, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with striking variegation of white, yellow, and green, adding visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
