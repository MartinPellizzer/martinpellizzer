Persicaria barbata var. barbata

Persicaria barbata var. barbata, commonly known as the bearded smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color and are covered with fine, bristly hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and other damp environments.
