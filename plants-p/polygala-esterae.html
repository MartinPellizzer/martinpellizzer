Polygala esterae

Polygala esterae, commonly known as the Ester's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and opposite, while its stems are wiry and branching.
