Pyracantha yunnanensis

Pyracantha yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan firethorn, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and clusters of bright orange-red berries that persist through the winter. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by the showy fruit.

Pyracantha yunnanensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in gardens.
