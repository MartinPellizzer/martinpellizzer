Pultenaea graveolens var. glabrescens

Pultenaea graveolens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a dark green hue. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and rocky slopes.

The variety glabrescens is distinguished by its reduced hair coverage compared to the typical form of Pultenaea graveolens.
