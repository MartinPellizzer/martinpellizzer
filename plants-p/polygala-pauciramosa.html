Polygala pauciramosa

Polygala pauciramosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has slender, branched stems and linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
