Potentilla vranjana

Potentilla vranjana, commonly known as the Vranjean cinquefoil, is a species of flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant features compound leaves with five leaflets, which are typically serrated and hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Potentilla vranjana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
