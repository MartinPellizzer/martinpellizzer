Phaseolus sphaericus var. luteus

Phaseolus sphaericus var. luteus, commonly known as the yellow round bean, is a legume species characterized by its distinctive spherical pods and yellowish pod color.

It is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, tropical climates. The plant produces small, edible seeds that are often used in traditional cuisines. Its botanical features include compound leaves and flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and nutritional content.
