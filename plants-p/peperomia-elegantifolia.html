Peperomia elegantifolia

Peperomia elegantifolia, commonly known as the elegant peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of pink or white, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
