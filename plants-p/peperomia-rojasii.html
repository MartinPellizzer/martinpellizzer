Peperomia rojasii

Peperomia rojasii, commonly known as the Rosy Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a reddish or pinkish tinge on the underside. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, arranged in spikes along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia rojasii is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
