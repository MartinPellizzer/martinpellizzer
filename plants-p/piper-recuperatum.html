Piper recuperatum

Piper recuperatum, commonly known as the African pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its upright, branched stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into three-lobed berries.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
