Polygala mixta

Polygala mixta, commonly known as the mixed milkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate and lance-shaped, while its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white.

Polygala mixta is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
