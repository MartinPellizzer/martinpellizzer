Phillyrea longifolia subsp. unamunoana

Phillyrea longifolia subsp.

unamunoana, commonly known as the Iberian Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its small, leathery, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in calcareous soils and is often found in Mediterranean-type climates.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native habitat.
