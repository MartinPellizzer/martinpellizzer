Panicum pseudodistachyum

Panicum pseudodistachyum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or red. It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural landscapes.
