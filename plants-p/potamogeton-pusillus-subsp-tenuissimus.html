Potamogeton pusillus subsp. tenuissimus

Potamogeton pusillus subsp.

tenuissimus, commonly known as the slender water milfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater ecosystems. It has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically less than 1 millimeter wide, contributing to its delicate appearance.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is often found in shallow, slow-moving waters such as ponds and lakes.

Its presence is an indicator of good water quality and healthy aquatic habitats.
