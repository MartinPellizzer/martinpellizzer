Pleurothallis tricaudata

Pleurothallis tricaudata, commonly known as the three-tailed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid tropical forests. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
