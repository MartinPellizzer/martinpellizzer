Protea piscina

Protea piscina, commonly known as the coastal protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a distinctive, cone-shaped structure.

Protea piscina is adapted to withstand coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
