Phyteuma vagneri f. grossidentatum

Phyteuma vagneri f. grossidentatum, commonly known as the broad-lobed phyteuma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features lanceolate to ovate leaves with prominent veins, and it thrives in well-drained, sunny to partially shaded environments. Native to southern Europe, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This cultivar is noted for its robust growth and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
