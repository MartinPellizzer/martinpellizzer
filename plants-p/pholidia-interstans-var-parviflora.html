Pholidia interstans var. parviflora

Pholidia interstans var. parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf soursop, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellow, spiny fruits resembling those of the soursop. Its fruit is edible, with a mildly acidic flavor and a texture similar to a pear.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential culinary uses in tropical gardens.
