Pediomelum rhombifolium

Pediomelum rhombifolium, commonly known as the diamondleaf pediomelum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rhomboid-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a sought-after plant among succulent enthusiasts.
