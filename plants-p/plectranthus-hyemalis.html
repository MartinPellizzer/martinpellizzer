Plectranthus hyemalis

Plectranthus hyemalis, commonly known as winter plectranthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or violet, which bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal uses.
