Polygala glauca

Polygala glauca, commonly known as blue milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender stems and small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

Polygala glauca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
