Panicum larcomianum

Panicum larcomianum, commonly known as the Larcum Panicgrass, is a species of grass native to certain regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
