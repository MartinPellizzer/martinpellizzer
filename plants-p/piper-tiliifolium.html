Piper tiliifolium

Piper tiliifolium, commonly known as the California pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in coastal areas and chaparral habitats.

The fruits are edible and have a peppery flavor, making them popular in local cuisines.
