Peperomia cupularis

Peperomia cupularis, commonly known as the cupularis peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, often with a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia cupularis is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt to various indoor conditions.
