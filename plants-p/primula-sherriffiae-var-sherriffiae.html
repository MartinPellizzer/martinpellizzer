Primula sherriffiae var. sherriffiae

Primula sherriffiae var. sherriffiae, commonly known as the Sherriff's primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves that form a compact base. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
