Piper otto-huberi

Piper otto-huberi, commonly known as Otto-Huber's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
