Polygonum polymorphum var. foliosum

Polygonum polymorphum var. foliosum, commonly known as the variegated knotweed, is a perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a variegated pattern of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically red or pink in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
