Pilea margaretiae

Pilea margaretiae, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea margaretiae is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
