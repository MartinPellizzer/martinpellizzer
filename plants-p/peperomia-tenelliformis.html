Peperomia tenelliformis

Peperomia tenelliformis, commonly known as the miniature peperomia or the little peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped, with a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
