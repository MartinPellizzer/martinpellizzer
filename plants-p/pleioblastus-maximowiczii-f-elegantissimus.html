Pleioblastus maximowiczii f. elegantissimus

Pleioblastus maximowiczii f. elegantissimus, commonly known as the elegant bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to East Asia.

It features slender, gracefully arching culms with a light green to bluish-green sheath color. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in partial shade to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental and landscape purposes.
