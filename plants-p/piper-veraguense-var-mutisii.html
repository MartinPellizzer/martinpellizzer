Piper veraguense var. mutisii

Piper veraguense var. mutisii, commonly known as the Yarumo plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the humid lowland regions of South America, particularly in Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
