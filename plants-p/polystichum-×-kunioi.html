Polystichum × kunioi

Polystichum × kunioi, commonly known as the Japanese forest fern, is a hybrid species within the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, bushy appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

Polystichum × kunioi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural settings.
