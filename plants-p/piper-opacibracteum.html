Piper opacibracteum

Piper opacibracteum, commonly known as the dark bract pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of tiny, pale green flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature.

Piper opacibracteum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
