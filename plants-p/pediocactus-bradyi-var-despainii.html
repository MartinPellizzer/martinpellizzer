Pediocactus bradyi var. despainii

Pediocactus bradyi var. despainii, commonly known as the Desert Trumpet, is a succulent cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It features short, ribbed stems that are often covered in white or yellowish spines. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in many regions.
