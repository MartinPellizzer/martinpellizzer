Peperomia montazosana

Peperomia montazosana, commonly known as the mountain peperomia, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and compact growth habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia montazosana is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
