Phylica eriophoros

Phylica eriophoros, commonly known as the silver tree, is a unique shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, fleshy berries. It is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The silver tree is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
