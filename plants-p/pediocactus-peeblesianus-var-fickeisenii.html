Pediocactus peeblesianus var. fickeisenii

Pediocactus peeblesianus var. fickeisenii, commonly known as the Peebles' cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
