Plagiogyria angustipinna

Plagiogyria angustipinna, commonly known as the narrow-pined plagiogyria, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Plagiogyriaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-yellow flowers. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a creeping growth habit with short, branched stems.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
