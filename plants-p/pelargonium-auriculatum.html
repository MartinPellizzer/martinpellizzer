Pelargonium auriculatum

Pelargonium auriculatum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
