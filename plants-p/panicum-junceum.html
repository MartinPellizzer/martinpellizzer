Panicum junceum

Panicum junceum, commonly known as narrowleaf panicgrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to North America and thrives in moist to wet environments such as marshes and wetlands. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like clusters of greenish-brown florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum junceum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
