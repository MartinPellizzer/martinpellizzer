Peucedanum podolicum

Peucedanum podolicum, commonly known as the Podolic cowparsley, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-shaped flowers.

Peucedanum podolicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
