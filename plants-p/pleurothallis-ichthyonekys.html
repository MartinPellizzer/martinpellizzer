Pleurothallis ichthyonekys

Pleurothallis ichthyonekys, commonly known as the fishhook pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are typically narrow and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
