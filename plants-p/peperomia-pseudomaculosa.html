Peperomia pseudomaculosa

Peperomia pseudomaculosa, commonly known as the spotted peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with distinctive red or purple markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pseudomaculosa is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
