Paliurus edulis

Paliurus edulis, commonly known as the edible Chinese thornapple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its thorny branches and compound leaves. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
