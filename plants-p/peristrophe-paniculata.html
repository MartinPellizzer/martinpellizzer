Peristrophe paniculata

Peristrophe paniculata, commonly known as the panicled peristrophe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves.
