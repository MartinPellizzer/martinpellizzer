Pyrus × floribunda var. scheideckeri

Pyrus × floribunda var. scheideckeri, commonly known as the Scheidecker pear, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that appear in early spring. The foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season. This variety is prized for its ornamental qualities rather than its fruit production.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable addition to gardens and landscapes.
