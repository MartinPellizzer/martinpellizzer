Potentilla hirta var. astracanica

Potentilla hirta var. astracanica, commonly known as the Astracanica cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate and serrated, with a slightly rough texture.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
