Phanera merrilliana var. merrilliana

Phanera merrilliana var. merrilliana, commonly known as the Malayan flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
