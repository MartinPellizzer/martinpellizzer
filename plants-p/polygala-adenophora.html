Polygala adenophora

Polygala adenophora, commonly known as the腺花远志, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland and shrubland habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

The species is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
