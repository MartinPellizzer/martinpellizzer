Pelargonium havlasae

Pelargonium havlasae, commonly known as the Havlas' geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant features rounded, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated for both garden display and as a cut flower.
