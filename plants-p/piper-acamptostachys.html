Piper acamptostachys

Piper acamptostachys, commonly known as the zigzag pepper or mountain pepper, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its zigzagging stems and small, opposite leaves. It produces small green flowers followed by dark purple berries.

The berries contain tiny seeds and are used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some regions.
