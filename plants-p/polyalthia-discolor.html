Polyalthia discolor

Polyalthia discolor, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a luminous appearance. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its timber, which is lightweight and durable.
