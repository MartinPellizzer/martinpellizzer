Psydrax calcicola

Psydrax calcicola, commonly known as the limestone rock fig, is a shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically small, leathery, and arranged oppositely on the stems.

Psydrax calcicola is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
