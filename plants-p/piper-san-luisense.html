Piper san-luisense

Piper san-luisense, commonly known as the San Luis pepper plant, is a species of flowering vine native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark red color, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
