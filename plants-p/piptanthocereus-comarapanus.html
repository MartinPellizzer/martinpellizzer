Piptanthocereus comarapanus

Piptanthocereus comarapanus, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Cactaceae family, characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
