Phaeiris giganticaerulea

Phaeiris giganticaerulea, commonly known as the Blue Giant Orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

The orchid's unique coloration and size make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
