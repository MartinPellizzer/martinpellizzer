Panicum brodiei

Panicum brodiei, commonly known as the Brodie's bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist to saturated soils. The plant has narrow, blue-green leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flower heads. It is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecological balance in its native environment.
