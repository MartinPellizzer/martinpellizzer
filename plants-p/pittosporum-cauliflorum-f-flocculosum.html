Pittosporum cauliflorum f. flocculosum

Pittosporum cauliflorum f. flocculosum, commonly known as the woolly pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically dark green and covered in a fine, white to greyish pubescence. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is well-suited to coastal environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
