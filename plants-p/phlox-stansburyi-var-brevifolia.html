Phlox stansburyi var. brevifolia

Phlox stansburyi var. brevifolia, commonly known as the shortleaf phlox, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular plant among gardeners in arid climates.
