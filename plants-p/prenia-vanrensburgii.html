Prenia vanrensburgii

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces yellowish to orange-colored flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
