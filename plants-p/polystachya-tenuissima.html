Polystachya tenuissima

Polystachya tenuissima, commonly known as the slender-leafed bamboo orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its delicate appearance and unique growth habit.

Polystachya tenuissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
