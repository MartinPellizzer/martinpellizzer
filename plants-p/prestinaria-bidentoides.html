Prestinaria bidentoides

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.
