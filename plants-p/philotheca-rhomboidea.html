Philotheca rhomboidea

Philotheca rhomboidea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mistflower, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its popularity in native landscaping.
