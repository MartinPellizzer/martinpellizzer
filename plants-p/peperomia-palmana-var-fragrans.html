Peperomia palmana var. fragrans

Peperomia palmana var. fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant peperomia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features compact, upright growth with waxy, elliptical leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters at the ends of stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its delicate, aromatic blooms.
