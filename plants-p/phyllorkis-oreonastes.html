Phyllorkis oreonastes

Phyllorkis oreonastes, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
