Pleurothallis janeirensis

Pleurothallis janeirensis, commonly known as the Janeirensis Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America, particularly in Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and derives its nutrients from the air, rain, and debris around it. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric moisture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
