Peperomia boomii

Peperomia boomii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Brazil.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green to burgundy hues, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which are not the main attraction. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia boomii is prized for its unique texture and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
