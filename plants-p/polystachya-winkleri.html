Polystachya winkleri

Polystachya winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's spike fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with dense, clustered inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Polystachya winkleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to shaded environments.
