Pelargonium siccifolium

Pelargonium siccifolium, commonly known as the desert geranium, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in drought conditions. The plant produces small, pale pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Pelargonium siccifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
