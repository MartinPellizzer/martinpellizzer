Pennisetum schimperi var. glabrum

Pennisetum schimperi var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and its ability to form dense clumps. The plant produces fluffy, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance, particularly during the flowering season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal in landscaping.
