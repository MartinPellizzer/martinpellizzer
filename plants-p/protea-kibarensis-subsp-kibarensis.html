Protea kibarensis subsp. kibarensis

Protea kibarensis subsp.

kibarensis, commonly known as the Kibarensis protea, is a unique species native to the Eastern Cape of South Africa. It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous floral bracts that resemble petals.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its foliage consists of long, narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery coating.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
