Pittosporum daphniphylloides var. daphniphylloides

Pittosporum daphniphylloides var. daphniphylloides, commonly known as the silver-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, aromatic leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native New Zealand forests.
