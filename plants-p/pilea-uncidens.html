Pilea uncidens

Pilea uncidens, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are borne on short stalks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Pilea uncidens is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
