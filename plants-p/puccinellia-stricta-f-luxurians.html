Puccinellia stricta f. luxurians

Puccinellia stricta f. luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant spikegrass, is a variant of the spikegrass species native to temperate regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, hairless stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with minute flowers, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed sites.

Its botanical features make it an important component of ecological studies in plant ecology and taxonomy.
