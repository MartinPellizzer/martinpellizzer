Panicum werneri

Panicum werneri, commonly known as Werneri's grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in some traditional practices.
