Pellionia tenuicuspis

Pellionia tenuicuspis, commonly known as the narrow-lobed pellionia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, opposite, ovate leaves with finely serrated margins, and it produces clusters of tiny, white or pale green flowers. Pellionia tenuicuspis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
