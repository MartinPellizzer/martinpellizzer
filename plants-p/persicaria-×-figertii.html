Persicaria × figertii

Persicaria × figertii, commonly known as the hybrid smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, characterized by its smooth, often reddish stems and distinctive leaf shapes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Persicaria × figertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting various pollinators and wildlife.
