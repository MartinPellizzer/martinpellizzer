Pertya angustifolia

Pertya angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf pertya, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Pertya angustifolia is often found in desert scrub and chaparral ecosystems.
