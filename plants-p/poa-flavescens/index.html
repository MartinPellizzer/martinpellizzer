Poa Flavescens

Poa flavescens is a unique grass species known for its vibrant yellow-green foliage. Native to temperate regions of the northern hemisphere, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. This plant is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it contributes to ecological diversity. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively underappreciated in the world of botany.
