Polystichum × luerssenii

Polystichum × luerssenii, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen fern, is a cross between Polystichum setiferum and Polystichum acrostichum.

It is a robust, clump-forming fern that produces dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive, curled appearance. The plant is native to the northeastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.

The fronds are evergreen, providing year-round interest in the landscape.
