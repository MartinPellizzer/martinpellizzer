Pittosporum elevaticostatum

Pittosporum elevaticostatum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant white or cream flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. These flowers are followed by small, spherical berries that ripen to a dark red color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
