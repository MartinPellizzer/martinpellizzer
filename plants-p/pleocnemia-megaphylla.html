Pleocnemia megaphylla

Pleocnemia megaphylla, commonly known as the giant pleocnemia, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, intricate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant exhibits a complex dichotomous branching pattern, typical of many ferns. Its leaves are composed of numerous pinnae, which are further divided into smaller pinnules.

Pleocnemia megaphylla thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.
