Piper ovatum var. hirtellum

Piper ovatum var. hirtellum, commonly known as the hairy pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly hairy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and have a peppery flavor. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental or for its culinary uses.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both gardens and herbal collections.
