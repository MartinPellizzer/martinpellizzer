Potentilla veronensis

Potentilla veronensis, commonly known as the Veronese cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Potentilla veronensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
