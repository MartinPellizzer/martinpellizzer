Pilea oligantha

Pilea oligantha, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by oval, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
