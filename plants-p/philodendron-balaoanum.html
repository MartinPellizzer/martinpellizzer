Philodendron balaoanum

Philodendron balaoanum, commonly known as the Balaoanum philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
