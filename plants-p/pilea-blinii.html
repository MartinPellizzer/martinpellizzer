Pilea blinii

Pilea blinii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle variegation of cream or white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea blinii is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
