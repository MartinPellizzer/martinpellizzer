Phyllangium divergens

Phyllangium divergens, commonly known as the divergent leaf fern, is a unique fern species characterized by its distinctive fronds.

Its leaves are composed of multiple pinnae that radiate outward, giving the plant a fan-like appearance. The fronds are typically elongated and have a delicate, feathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant's botanical structure showcases its adaptation to humid, low-light conditions.
