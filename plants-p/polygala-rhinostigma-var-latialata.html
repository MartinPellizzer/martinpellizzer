Polygala rhinostigma var. latialata

Polygala rhinostigma var. latialata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variant is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Polygala rhinostigma.
