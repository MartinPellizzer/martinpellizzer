Pseudolycopodium densum

Pseudolycopodium densum, commonly known as the dense clubmoss, is a small, evergreen herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and scale-like leaves. Pseudolycopodium densum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
