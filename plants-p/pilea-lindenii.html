Pilea lindenii

Pilea lindenii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight purplish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a popular indoor plant.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
