Pycnodoria cretica

Pycnodoria cretica, commonly known as the Cretan denseleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, dense foliage and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its leaves are often aromatic, containing essential oils that contribute to its distinct fragrance.

Pycnodoria cretica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
