Petrosedum × henkii

Petrosedum × henkii, commonly known as the Hawaiian ice plant, is a hybrid succulent native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
