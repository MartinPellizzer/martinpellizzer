Poa massauensis

Poa massauensis, commonly known as the Massauense grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, particularly in Namibia and South Africa. This perennial grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The leaves are typically narrow and blade-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
