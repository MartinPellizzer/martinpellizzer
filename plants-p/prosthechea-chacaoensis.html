Prosthechea chacaoensis

Prosthechea chacaoensis, commonly known as the Chacao orchid, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in humid tropical forests. The plant has a short, creeping stem with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are pale green to white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
