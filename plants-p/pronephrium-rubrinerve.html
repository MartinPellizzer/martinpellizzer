Pronephrium rubrinerve

Pronephrium rubrinerve, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with prominent red veins.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clustered inflorescences. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Pronephrium rubrinerve is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
