Peperomia hintonii

Peperomia hintonii, commonly known as Hinton's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually green or red, which are followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
