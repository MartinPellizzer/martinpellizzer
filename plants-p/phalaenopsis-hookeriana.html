Phalaenopsis hookeriana

Phalaenopsis hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces long, slender pseudobulbs and has dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

This orchid is valued for its elegant appearance and is commonly cultivated as a decorative plant.
