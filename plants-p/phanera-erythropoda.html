Phanera erythropoda

Phanera erythropoda, commonly known as the red-flowered phanera, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
