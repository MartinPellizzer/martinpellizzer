Parosela eysenhardtioides

Parosela eysenhardtioides, commonly known as the Eysenhardt's parosela, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of purple, yellow, and white hues. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, grass-like foliage. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.
