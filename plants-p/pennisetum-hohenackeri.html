Pennisetum hohenackeri

Pennisetum hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, which are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Pennisetum hohenackeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
