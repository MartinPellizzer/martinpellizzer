Plesioneuron angusticaudatum

Plesioneuron angusticaudatum, commonly known as the narrow-tailed fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow fronds that resemble a tail, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The botanical features of Plesioneuron angusticaudatum make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
