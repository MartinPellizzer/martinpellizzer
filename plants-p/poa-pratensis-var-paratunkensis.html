Poa pratensis var. paratunkensis

Poa pratensis var. paratunkensis, commonly known as the Paratunkensis Meadowgrass, is a variant of the common tall fescue.

It is a perennial grass native to parts of Central Asia, including regions of Kazakhstan and Russia. This grass species typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a tufted growth habit.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
