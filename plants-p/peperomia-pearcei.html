Peperomia pearcei

Peperomia pearcei, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green, waxy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pearcei is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
