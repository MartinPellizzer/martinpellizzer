Paphiopedilum canhii var. funingense

Paphiopedilum canhii var. funingense, commonly known as the Funing Butterfly Orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to southern China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, butterfly-like flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb, often found in shaded, moist forest environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, and it produces a single, showy flower per stem.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking, long-lasting blooms.
