Polystachya calluniflora var. hologlossa

Polystachya calluniflora var. hologlossa, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and striking, orchid-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in tropical gardens for its unique appearance.
