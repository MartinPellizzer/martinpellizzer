Physostegia nivea

Physostegia nivea, commonly known as white beardroot, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, pink to lavender flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its root system is fibrous and contributes to its common name.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
