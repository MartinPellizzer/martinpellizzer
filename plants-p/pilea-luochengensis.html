Pilea luochengensis

Pilea luochengensis, commonly known as the Luocheng begonia, is a rare and unique species native to China.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and compact growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Due to its limited natural range and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists.
