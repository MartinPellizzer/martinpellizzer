Plectranthus rubropunctatus

Plectranthus rubropunctatus, commonly known as the red-spotted plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red spots on its heart-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Plectranthus rubropunctatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
