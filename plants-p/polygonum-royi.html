Polygonum royi

Polygonum royi, commonly known as the Royal Polygonum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with a reddish or purple hue. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

Polygonum royi is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.
