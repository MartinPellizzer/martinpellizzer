Peperomia obtusifolia var. parvifolia

Peperomia obtusifolia var. parvifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both homes and offices.
