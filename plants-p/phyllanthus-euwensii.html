Phyllanthus euwensii

Phyllanthus euwensii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Phyllanthus euwensii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
