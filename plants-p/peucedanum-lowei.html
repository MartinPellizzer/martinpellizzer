Peucedanum lowei

Peucedanum lowei, commonly known as the Chinese cowparley, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and prefers moist, well-drained soils. Its fruits are schizocarps, which split into two one-seeded parts when mature, a common feature in the Apiaceae family.

Peucedanum lowei is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
