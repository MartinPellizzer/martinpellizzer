Pleurothallis truncata

Pleurothallis truncata, commonly known as the truncate pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme with numerous small blooms.

Pleurothallis truncata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
