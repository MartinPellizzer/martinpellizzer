Pulegium tomentellum f. ninimum

Pulegium tomentellum f. ninimum, commonly known as the silverleaf narrow-leaved pulegium, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. The plant is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden use.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
