Pinacopodium gabonense

Pinacopodium gabonense, commonly known as the Gabon fan palm, is a species of palm native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant grows in humid, tropical environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas. Its trunk is slender and covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Pinacopodium gabonense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
