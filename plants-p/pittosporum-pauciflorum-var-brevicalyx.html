Pittosporum pauciflorum var. brevicalyx

Pittosporum pauciflorum var. brevicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
