Piper torricellense

Piper torricellense, commonly known as the Torricella pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and these berries are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
