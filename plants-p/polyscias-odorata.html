Polyscias odorata

Polyscias odorata, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen or sweet-scented shrub, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant aroma.

Polyscias odorata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
