Paphiopedilum sallieri

Paphiopedilum sallieri, commonly known as the Sallier's lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive pouch-shaped lip that is a key characteristic of the orchid family. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with rich, well-draining soil. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

Paphiopedilum sallieri is appreciated for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
