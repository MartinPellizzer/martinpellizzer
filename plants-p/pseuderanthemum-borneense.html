Pseuderanthemum borneense

Pseuderanthemum borneense, commonly known as the Bornean false anthemum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically arranged in clusters, with vibrant colors ranging from pink to purple.

Pseuderanthemum borneense is adapted to humid, shaded environments and thrives in the understory of tropical rainforests.
