Pterostegia galioides

Pterostegia galioides, commonly known as the silverleaf pterostegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. Its flowers are clustered in small, showy inflorescences and are typically pink or purple in color.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
