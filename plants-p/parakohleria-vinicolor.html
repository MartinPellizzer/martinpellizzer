Parakohleria vinicolor

Parakohleria vinicolor, commonly known as the purpleleaf kohleria, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, glossy leaves that range from deep purple to bronze, depending on light exposure. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
