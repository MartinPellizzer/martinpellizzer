Pyrus amaena

Pyrus amaena, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a species of pear native to China.

It is characterized by its large, white flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.
