Panicum pittieri

Panicum pittieri, commonly known as the Mexican dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the southern United States and Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Panicum pittieri is drought-tolerant and often used in native plant gardens for its low maintenance and ecological benefits.
