Panicum olivaceum

Panicum olivaceum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and delicate, grass-like leaves that often have a silvery or grayish hue. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to reddish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common ornamental grass in gardens.

Panicum olivaceum is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
