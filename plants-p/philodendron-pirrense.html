Philodendron pirrense

Philodendron pirrense, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers, though it is primarily grown for its foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Philodendron pirrense is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
