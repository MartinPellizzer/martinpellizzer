Pauletia bowkeri

Pauletia bowkeri, commonly known as the Bowker's pauletia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
