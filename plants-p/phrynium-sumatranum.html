Phrynium sumatranum

Phrynium sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Phrynium sumatranum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
