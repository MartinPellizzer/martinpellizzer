Pachira elegans

Pachira elegans, commonly known as the golden pachira or money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems and large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits containing bright red seeds. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its ability to thrive in various environments makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
