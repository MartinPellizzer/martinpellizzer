Peperomia infralutea

Peperomia infralutea, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or white powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia infralutea is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
