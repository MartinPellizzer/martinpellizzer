Panicum gilvum

Panicum gilvum, commonly known as golden panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and vibrant yellow plumes. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its foliage is narrow and linear, with a grass-like appearance, and it produces showy inflorescences that attract pollinators.

Panicum gilvum is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
