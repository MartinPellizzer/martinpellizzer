Piper malifolium

Piper malifolium, commonly known as the sour leaf or poison leaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes other species like black pepper. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that have a distinctive sour taste. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not typically used for culinary or medicinal purposes.
