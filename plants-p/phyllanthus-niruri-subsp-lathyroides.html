Phyllanthus niruri subsp. lathyroides

Phyllanthus niruri subsp.

lathyroides, commonly known as stonecress or wild spinach, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is a low-growing herb with succulent, fleshy leaves and small, greenish-white flowers.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its unique leaf structure, which is often described as resembling a liver, hence its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
