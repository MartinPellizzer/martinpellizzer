Polygala vulgaris var. hohenackeriana

Polygala vulgaris var. hohenackeriana, commonly known as the Hohenacker's milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
