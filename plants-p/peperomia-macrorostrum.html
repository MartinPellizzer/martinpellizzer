Peperomia macrorostrum

Peperomia macrorostrum, commonly known as the large-nosed peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped, with a textured surface that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia macrorostrum is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
