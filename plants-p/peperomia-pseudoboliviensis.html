Peperomia pseudoboliviensis

Peperomia pseudoboliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pseudoboliviensis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
