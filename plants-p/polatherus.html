Polatherus

Polatherus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments in tropical and subtropical regions.

Polatherus is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
