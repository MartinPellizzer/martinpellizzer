Pilea shizongensis

Pilea shizongensis, commonly known as the Shizong begonia, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, white, and pink, which adds to its ornamental value.

Pilea shizongensis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
