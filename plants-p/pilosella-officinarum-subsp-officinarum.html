Pilosella officinarum subsp. officinarum

Pilosella officinarum subsp.

officinarum, commonly known as common hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features a basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters.

The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
