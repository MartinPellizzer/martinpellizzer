Peperomia rupertiana var. genuina

Peperomia rupertiana var. genuina, commonly known as the Genuina Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features glossy, dark green oval-shaped leaves that are often adorned with subtle silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
