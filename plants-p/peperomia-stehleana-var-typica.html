Peperomia stehleana var. typica

Peperomia stehleana var. typica, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery or white underside, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant has short, upright stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and decorative plant arrangements.
