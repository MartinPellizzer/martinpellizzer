Piper patulipilum

Piper patulipilum, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper or Patulipilum pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow or red when mature.

Piper patulipilum is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
