Primula × heeri

Primula × heeri, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm, with hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
