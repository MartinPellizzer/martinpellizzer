Pennisetum durum

Pennisetum durum, commonly known as dwarf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and produces feathery, silver-green plumes that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum durum is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for beneficial insects and birds.
