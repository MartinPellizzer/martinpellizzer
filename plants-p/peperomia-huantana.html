Peperomia huantana

Peperomia huantana, commonly known as the Huantana peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia huantana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
