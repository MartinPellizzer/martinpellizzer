Pilosella Apatelia Subsp. Bregensis

Bregen's pilosella, scientifically known as Pilosella apatelia subsp. bregensis, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its delicate white flowers and hairy foliage, which help it thrive in its harsh alpine environment. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species facing increasing threats from climate change and human activity. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and study this unique plant, highlighting its ecological importance. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of fascination for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
