Phyllanthus upembaensis

Phyllanthus upembaensis, commonly known as the Upemba spurge, is a shrub native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is adapted to moist, tropical environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.
