Phillyrea medianifolia

Phillyrea medianifolia, commonly known as the Mediterranean privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, red berries in the fall. Phillyrea medianifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also noted for its resistance to pests and diseases, making it a low-maintenance plant for gardens and landscapes.
