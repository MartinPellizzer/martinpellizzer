Penstemon kunthii

Penstemon kunthii, commonly known as the tall beardtongue, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces erect, branched stems and has narrow, grass-like leaves.

Penstemon kunthii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
