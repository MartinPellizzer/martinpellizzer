Physalis alkekengi var. digitalifolia

Physalis alkekengi var. digitalifolia, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous perennial belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange, inflated calyces that enclose the fruit, which resembles small lanterns. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that develop into the distinctive, papery fruit capsules. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in temperate regions.

This ornamental plant is valued for its striking appearance and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
