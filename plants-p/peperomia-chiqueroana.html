Peperomia chiqueroana

Peperomia chiqueroana, commonly known as the Chiqueroan pepper plant, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. Its flowers are small and clustered in spadices, typical of the Piperaceae family.

Peperomia chiqueroana is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light to thrive.
