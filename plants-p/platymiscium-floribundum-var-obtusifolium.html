Platymiscium floribundum var. obtusifolium

Platymiscium floribundum var. obtusifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf plum tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically broad and pointed at the tip, with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits that are similar to plums. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden cultivation.
