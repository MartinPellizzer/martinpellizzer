Prunella gracillicaulia

Prunella gracillicaulia, commonly known as slender-stemmed heal-all, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Prunella gracillicaulia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
