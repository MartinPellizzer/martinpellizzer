Potentilla stenophylla var. millefolium

Potentilla stenophylla var. millefolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically arranged in clusters. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to gardens with specific horticultural needs.
