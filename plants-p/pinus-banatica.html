Pinus banatica

Pinus banatica, commonly known as the Borneo pine, is a coniferous tree native to the island of Borneo.

It is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure typical of the pine family. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a conical crown. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing protection against environmental stressors.

The species plays an important ecological role in the tropical rainforests of Borneo.
