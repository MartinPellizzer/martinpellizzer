Philadelphus godokokeri

Philadelphus godokokeri, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Philadelphus godokokeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
