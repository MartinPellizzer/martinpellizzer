Polygonum baicalense

Polygonum baicalense, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming in late summer. It is often found in moist soils along riverbanks and in wetlands.

Polygonum baicalense is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
