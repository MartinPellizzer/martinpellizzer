Peperomia galioides var. umbrosa

Peperomia galioides var. umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green, ovate leaves that are often slightly hairy, giving it a textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are typically borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
