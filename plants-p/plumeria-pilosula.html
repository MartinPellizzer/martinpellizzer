Plumeria pilosula

Plumeria pilosula, commonly known as the woolly plumeria, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The flowers are typically borne on long, slender stalks and bloom during the warm seasons.
