Piper attenuatamentum

Piper attenuatamentum, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are initially green and turn red when mature, containing small seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
