Pleurothallis minutalis

Pleurothallis minutalis, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the understory of forests. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its compact size and unique floral structure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural range.
