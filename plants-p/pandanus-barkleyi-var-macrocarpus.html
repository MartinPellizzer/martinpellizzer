Pandanus barkleyi var. macrocarpus

Pandanus barkleyi var. macrocarpus, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, fibrous leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, spiky inflorescences that contain numerous small, white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional crafts and construction.

The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saltwater environments.
