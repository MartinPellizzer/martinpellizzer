Ptilotus lanatus var. glabrobracteatus

Ptilotus lanatus var. glabrobracteatus, commonly known as the smooth bract ptilotus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and bracts. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its foliage is usually hairy, and the flowers are often red or orange in color.
