Peperomia erecta

Peperomia erecta, commonly known as the upright peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or bronze tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia erecta is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
