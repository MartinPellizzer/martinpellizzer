Potentilla nestleriana

Potentilla nestleriana, commonly known as the Nestler's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
