Phalaenopsis pulcherrima f. nivea

Phalaenopsis pulcherrima f. nivea, commonly known as the Snowy Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white blooms that resemble moths. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ability to bloom repeatedly with proper care.

The f. nivea form is distinguished by its predominantly white floral display, setting it apart from other varieties of Phalaenopsis.
