Peristrophe maculata

Peristrophe maculata, commonly known as the spotted grass.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often spotted or streaked with contrasting colors. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes.

Peristrophe maculata is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
