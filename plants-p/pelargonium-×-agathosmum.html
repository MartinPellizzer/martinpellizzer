Pelargonium × agathosmum

Pelargonium × agathosmum, commonly known as the geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a textured appearance. Native to South Africa, this species is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
