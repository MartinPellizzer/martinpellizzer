Psoralea pinnata var. glabriuscula

Psoralea pinnata var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth psoralea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and yellow flowers. The species belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. It typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The plant is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Aboriginal medicine.
