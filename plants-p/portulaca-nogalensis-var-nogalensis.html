Portulaca nogalensis var. nogalensis

Portulaca nogalensis var. nogalensis, commonly known as the Mexican portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and drought-tolerant addition to gardens in warm climates.
