Palisota pynaertii

Palisota pynaertii, commonly known as the creeping water plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and broad, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers arranged in a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds.
