Protea mollissima

Protea mollissima, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers surrounded by a dense, woolly bract. The plant is evergreen, with leathery, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a silvery-white coating. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region.

Protea mollissima is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of South Africa's floral heritage.
