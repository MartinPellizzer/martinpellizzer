Pultenaea plumosa var. canescens

Pultenaea plumosa var. canescens, commonly known as the silver-haired pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, pale green leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of pink to reddish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
