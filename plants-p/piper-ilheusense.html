Piper ilheusense

Piper ilheusense, commonly known as the Ilheus pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil, particularly in the state of Bahia. The plant is characterized by its small, green, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper ilheusense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
