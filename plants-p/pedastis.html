Pedastis

Pedastis, commonly known as the elegant spire plant, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to six feet in height, giving it a graceful, spire-like appearance. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that are often a deep green with a slight sheen. Pedastis produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the spring.

It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
