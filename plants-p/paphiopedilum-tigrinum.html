Paphiopedilum tigrinum

Paphiopedilum tigrinum, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, wrinkled, and often hairy leaf that is typically dark green with a reddish or purplish hue. The plant produces a single, showy spike with flowers that have a unique, intricate pattern resembling a tiger's stripes. Its flowers are usually yellow with a deep red or purple lip, and it blooms during the summer months.

Paphiopedilum tigrinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
