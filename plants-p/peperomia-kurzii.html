Peperomia kurzii

Peperomia kurzii, commonly known as the Kurz's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are borne in clusters.

Peperomia kurzii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
