Polypleurum filifolium

Polypleurum filifolium, commonly known as the feather fern, is a delicate, evergreen fern native to the wet tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that give it its common name, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and has a rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are typically bioluminescent, though this trait is more pronounced in certain cultivars.

It requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil to flourish.
