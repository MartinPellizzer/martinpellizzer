Phlox Douglasii Subsp. Rigida

Phlox douglasii subsp. rigida is a rare and striking wildflower native to the western united states. it belongs to the polemoniacae family and is known for its vibrant pink to purple flowers. this subspecies is characterized by its stiff, upright stems and narrow leaves. it thrives in rocky soils and sunny environments, often found in mountainous regions. due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
