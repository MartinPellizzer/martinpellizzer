Pandanus daenikeri

Pandanus daenikeri, commonly known as the Solomon Islands pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional crafts and for making thatch.

It thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often growing in sandy or muddy soils.
