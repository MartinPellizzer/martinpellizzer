Primulina hezhouensis

Primulina hezhouensis, commonly known as the Hezhou primula, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a dense, compact growth habit.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
