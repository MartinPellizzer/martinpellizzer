Panicum ctenanthum

Panicum ctenanthum, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panicum, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs that cover its leaves and inflorescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence is a loose to dense panicle, often with pale green to golden bracts.

Panicum ctenanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
