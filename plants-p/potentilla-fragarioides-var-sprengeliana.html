Potentilla fragarioides var. sprengeliana

Potentilla fragarioides var. sprengeliana, commonly known as the Sprengel's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
