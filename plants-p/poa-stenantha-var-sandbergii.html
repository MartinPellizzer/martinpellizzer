Poa stenantha var. sandbergii

Poa stenantha var. sandbergii, commonly known as Sandberg's bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and prairies. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
