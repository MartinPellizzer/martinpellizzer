Phalaenopsis wightii var. stobartiana

Phalaenopsis wightii var. stobartiana, commonly known as the Stobart's phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
