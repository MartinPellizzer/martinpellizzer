Phyllostachys bambusoides f. castillonii

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. castillonii, commonly known as the Castillonii bamboo, is a compact, clump-forming bamboo species native to China.

It features narrow, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces slender, green culms that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance bamboo.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for both landscape design and indoor cultivation.
