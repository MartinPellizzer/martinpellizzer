Panicum sloanei

Panicum sloanei, commonly known as Sloane's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the Caribbean.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as coastal areas and wetlands. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
