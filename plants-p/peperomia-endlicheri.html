Peperomia endlicheri

Peperomia endlicheri, commonly known as the Emerald Gem, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle metallic sheen, often adorned with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
