Polygonum × kuntzei

Polygonum × kuntzei, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae).

It is native to China and is characterized by its distinctive red stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Polygonum × kuntzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
