Pteris scabripes

Pteris scabripes, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its fronds that are typically divided into numerous pinnae, with a distinctive hairy texture on the underside. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this fern thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's rhizomes are often creeping and produce new shoots along their length.

Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
