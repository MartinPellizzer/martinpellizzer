Panicum oligostachyum

Panicum oligostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-panicle grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often pale green or brown, containing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscaping.
