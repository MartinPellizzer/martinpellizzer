Parnassia mysorensis

Parnassia mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore grass-of-parnassus, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its leaves are usually simple and alternate, with a smooth or hairy surface.
