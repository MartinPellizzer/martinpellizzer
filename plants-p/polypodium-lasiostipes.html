Polypodium lasiostipes

Polypodium lasiostipes, commonly known as the rock hound or mountain hound, is a species of fern native to various parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Himalayas.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows on rocky outcrops or in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a creeping stem that produces fronds. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive dark green color and a leathery texture.

Polypodium lasiostipes is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated as an ornamental plant.
