Phillyrea rosmarinifolia

Phillyrea rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the Mediterranean privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are dark green on top and silvery white underneath. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a small, leathery capsule containing several seeds.

Phillyrea rosmarinifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
