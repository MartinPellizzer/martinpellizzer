Pittosporum kweichowense var. kweichowense

Pittosporum kweichowense var. kweichowense, commonly known as the Yunnan pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, orange-red berries. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities.
