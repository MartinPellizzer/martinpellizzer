Peperomia tamboana

Peperomia tamboana, commonly known as the Tamboan peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped, with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia tamboana is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
