Panicum chloroticum var. luxurians

Panicum chloroticum var. luxurians, commonly known as golden panicum, is a variant of the panic grass species characterized by its striking golden foliage.

It is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates. The plant typically grows in open areas, such as fields and disturbed soils, and is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes. Its leaves are narrow and long, with a bright yellow coloration that becomes more pronounced under full sun.

This ornamental grass is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
