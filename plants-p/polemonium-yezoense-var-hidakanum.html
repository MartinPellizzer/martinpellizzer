Polemonium yezoense var. hidakanum

Polemonium yezoense var. hidakanum, commonly known as the Yezo bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Polemonium genus, which is part of the family Polemoniaceae. This variant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.
