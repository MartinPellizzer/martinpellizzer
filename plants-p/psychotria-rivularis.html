Psychotria rivularis

Psychotria rivularis, commonly known as the river psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Psychotria rivularis is also known for its psychoactive properties due to the presence of alkaloids such as dimethyltryptamine.
