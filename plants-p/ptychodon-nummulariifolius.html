Ptychodon nummulariifolius

Ptychodon nummulariifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety leaves and small white or yellow flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers bloom in spring and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
