Panicum serratum var. brachylophum

Panicum serratum var. brachylophum, commonly known as narrow-leaved panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, pale green to brown seed heads that mature in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
