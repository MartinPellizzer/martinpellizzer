Phalaenopsis schilleriana f. immaculata

Phalaenopsis schilleriana f. immaculata, commonly known as the Schiller's Phalaenopsis with immaculate markings, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant appearance.

It features long, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle sheen. The flowers are large, showy, and usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, velvety texture. This cultivar is known for its exceptional blooming period, often lasting several months.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and good air circulation.
