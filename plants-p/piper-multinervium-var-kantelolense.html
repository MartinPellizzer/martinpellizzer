Piper multinervium var. kantelolense

Piper multinervium var. kantelolense, commonly known as the Kantelolense pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper multinervium species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical berries. The berries are typically harvested when mature, exhibiting a reddish hue and a peppery flavor.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential pharmacological properties.
