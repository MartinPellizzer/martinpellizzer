Peperomia albert-smithii var. alipetiolata

Peperomia albert-smithii var. alipetiolata, commonly known as the Albert-Smith peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers, which are usually borne on short spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
