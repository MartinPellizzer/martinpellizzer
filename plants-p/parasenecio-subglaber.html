Parasenecio subglaber

Parasenecio subglaber, commonly known as the smoothleaf parasenecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are smooth and lance-shaped, and the flowers are usually yellow or orange in color.
