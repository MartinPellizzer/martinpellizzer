Protium aidanianum

Protium aidanianum, commonly known as the Aidan's tallow tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing one or two seeds.

Its unique morphology and endemic status make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
