Pleurothallis reedii

Pleurothallis reedii, commonly known as the Reed's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its small, strap-like leaves are adapted to the humid, shaded environments where it thrives.

Pleurothallis reedii is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
