Pleione simplex

Pleione simplex, commonly known as the white lady orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, slender stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-drained, loose soil. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its relatively simple floral structure compared to other orchids.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
