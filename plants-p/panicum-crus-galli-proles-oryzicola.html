Panicum crus-galli proles oryzicola

Panicum crus-galli proles oryzicola, commonly known as the rice grass or rice panicum, is a species of grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as rice fields and marshy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

It is often used in traditional agriculture and has ecological significance in supporting local biodiversity.
