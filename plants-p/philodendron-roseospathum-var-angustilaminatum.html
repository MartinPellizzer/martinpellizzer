Philodendron roseospathum var. angustilaminatum

Philodendron roseospathum var. angustilaminatum, commonly known as the Narrow-Leaf Philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly glossy texture and a unique pinkish hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage rather than its blooms.
