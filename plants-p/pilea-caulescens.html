Pilea caulescens

Pilea caulescens, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, upright shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually a glossy green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by small, round fruits. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique leaf arrangement and ease of care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
