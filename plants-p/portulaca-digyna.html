Portulaca digyna

Portulaca digyna, commonly known as the narrowleaf purslane, is a succulent plant native to the Americas.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in hot, dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Portulaca digyna is a low-growing plant that is drought-tolerant and often used in ornamental gardening.
