Peperomia omnicola var. oblanceolata

Peperomia omnicola var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly wavy margin, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It is appreciated for its attractive foliage and ease of care, often used as a decorative houseplant.

Its varietal name, *oblanceolata*, refers to its oblong, lance-shaped leaves.
