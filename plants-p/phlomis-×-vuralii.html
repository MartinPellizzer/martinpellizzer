Phlomis × vuralii

Phlomis × vuralii, commonly known as the hybrid hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features tall, upright stems covered in soft, gray-green fuzzy leaves. Its showy, clustered flowers are typically pink to purple, blooming in summer.
