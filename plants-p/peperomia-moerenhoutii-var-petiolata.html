Peperomia moerenhoutii var. petiolata

Peperomia moerenhoutii var. petiolata, commonly known as the petiolate peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both gardens and as a houseplant.
