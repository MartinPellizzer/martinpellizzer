Piper sogerianum

Piper sogerianum, commonly known as Sogerian pepper or Sogerian long pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elongated, spiky fruits containing aromatic seeds. The fruits are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to black pepper.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and culinary contexts.
