Polygala trichorachis

Polygala trichorachis, commonly known as the featherleaf milkvetch, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and small, spiny flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it produces small, smooth seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Polygala trichorachis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
