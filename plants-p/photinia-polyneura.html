Photinia polyneura

Photinia polyneura, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy, dark green leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by red, fleshy fruits that are attractive to birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers.
