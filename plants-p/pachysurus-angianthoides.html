Pachysurus angianthoides

Pachysurus angianthoides, commonly known as the giant panda plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Its foliage is dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic appearance and ease of care.
