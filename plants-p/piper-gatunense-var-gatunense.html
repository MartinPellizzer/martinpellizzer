Piper gatunense var. gatunense

Piper gatunense var. gatunense, commonly known as the Gatun pepper, is a tropical vine native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red when mature, containing a single seed.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
