Poa stenachyra

Poa stenachyra, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a species of grass native to southern South America.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Poa stenachyra plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for grazing animals.
