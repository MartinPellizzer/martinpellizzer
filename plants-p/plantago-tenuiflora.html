Plantago tenuiflora

Plantago tenuiflora, commonly known as narrowleaf plantain, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense spikes, which are borne on slender stalks. It is adapted to grow in a variety of environments, including disturbed soils and roadside areas.

The leaves and seeds of Plantago tenuiflora have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
