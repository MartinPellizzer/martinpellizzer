Ptychocarpus apodanthus

Ptychocarpus apodanthus, commonly known as the parasitic flower, is a unique flowering plant that exhibits a highly specialized parasitic relationship with its host.

Despite its parasitic nature, Ptychocarpus apodanthus plays a minor ecological role in its native habitat.
