Pelea saint-johnii

Pelea saint-johnii, commonly known as Saint John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pelea saint-johnii is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating mild depression and anxiety.
