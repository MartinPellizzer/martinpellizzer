Polygonum yunnanense

Polygonum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan knotweed, is a perennial herb native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and often triangular stems. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and produces small, clustered flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Polygonum yunnanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
