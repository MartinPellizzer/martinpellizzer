Phillyrea latifolia subsp. stricta

Phillyrea latifolia subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas. Phillyrea latifolia subsp.

stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
