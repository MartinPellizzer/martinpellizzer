Pelargonium dipetalum subsp. stenosiphon

Pelargonium dipetalum subsp.

stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon geranium, is a species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
