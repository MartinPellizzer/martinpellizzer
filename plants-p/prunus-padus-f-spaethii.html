Prunus padus f. spaethii

Prunus padus f. spaethii, commonly known as the Spaeth's whitebeam, is a rare and distinctive cultivar of the common whitebeam.

It is characterized by its white, closely packed flowers that appear in early spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade.

Due to its unique floral display and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
