Pavonia crassipedicellata

Pavonia crassipedicellata, commonly known as the broad-ribbed pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
