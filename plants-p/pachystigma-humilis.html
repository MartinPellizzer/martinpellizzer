Pachystigma humilis

Pachystigma humilis, commonly known as the dwarf pachystigma, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, fibrous root system and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
