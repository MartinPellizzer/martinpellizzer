Panicum strictum subvar. werneri

Panicum strictum subvar. werneri, commonly known as werneri grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Botanically, it is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
