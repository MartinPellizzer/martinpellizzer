Piper blattarum var. magnifolium

Piper blattarum var. magnifolium, commonly known as the giant pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper blattarum species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are peppery and can be used in traditional cuisines.

It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
