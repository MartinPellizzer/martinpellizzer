Primulina sinensis

Primulina sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
