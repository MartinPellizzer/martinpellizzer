Podocarpus tixieri

Podocarpus tixieri, commonly known as the Tixieri's podocarp, is a slow-growing evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and conical shape. The plant produces small, clustered cones that mature to a reddish-brown color, containing winged seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
