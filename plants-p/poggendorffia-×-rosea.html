Poggendorffia × rosea

Poggendorffia × rosea, commonly known as the pink spurge, is a hybrid plant native to the southern United States.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers surrounded by brightly colored, leaf-like structures called cyathia. Poggendorffia × rosea is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense mats that can be both decorative and invasive in certain environments.
