Peperomia turialvensis var. brachystachya

Peperomia turialvensis var. brachystachya, commonly known as the short-stalked peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences of pale green to white flowers, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in moderate humidity.

Its unique foliage and compact growth make it a popular choice for modern interior gardens.
