Paris verticillata var. manshurica

Paris verticillata var. manshurica, commonly known as the Manshurian basket lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are alternate, arrowhead-shaped, and often have a reddish or purple hue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
