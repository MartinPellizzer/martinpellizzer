Pouteria adolfi-friederici subsp. usambarensis

Pouteria adolfi-friederici subsp.

usambarensis, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and Eastern Africa. It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves and yellowish flowers.

The plant is valued for its economic and ecological importance in local communities.
