Pyrus wangenheimiana

Pyrus wangenheimiana, commonly known as the Wagenheim pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tien Shan and Pamir Mountains.
