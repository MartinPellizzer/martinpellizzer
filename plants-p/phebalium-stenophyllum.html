Phebalium stenophyllum

Phebalium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
