Polystachya victoriae

Polystachya victoriae, commonly known as Victoria's spikegrass, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
