Primula reinii var. myogiensis

Primula reinii var. myogiensis, commonly known as the Myogiensis primrose, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate and vibrant blooms.
