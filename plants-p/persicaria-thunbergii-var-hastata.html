Persicaria thunbergii var. hastata

Persicaria thunbergii var. hastata, commonly known as broadleaf plantain, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are broad and slightly lobed, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
