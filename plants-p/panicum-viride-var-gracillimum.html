Panicum viride var. gracillimum

Panicum viride var. gracillimum, commonly known as green panicgrass, is a variant of the panic grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common weed in disturbed areas.

Its rapid growth and adaptability contribute to its widespread presence in various habitats.
