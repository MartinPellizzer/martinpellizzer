Patrinia villosa

Patrinia villosa, commonly known as hairy patrinia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters.

Patrinia villosa is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
