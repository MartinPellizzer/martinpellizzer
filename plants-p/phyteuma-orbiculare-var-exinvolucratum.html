Phyteuma orbiculare var. exinvolucratum

Phyteuma orbiculare var. exinvolucratum, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and ability to attract pollinators.
