Pilea bissei

Pilea bissei, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen herbaceous plant native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in spring, followed by pale yellow berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal care needs.

Pilea bissei is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
