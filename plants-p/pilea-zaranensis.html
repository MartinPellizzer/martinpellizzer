Pilea zaranensis

Pilea zaranensis, commonly known as the Zaran's prayer plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often display striking variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their unique foliage. Pilea zaranensis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
