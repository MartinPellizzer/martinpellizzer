Panicum paucisetum

Panicum paucisetum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairie ecosystems. It plays a vital role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

Panicum paucisetum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
