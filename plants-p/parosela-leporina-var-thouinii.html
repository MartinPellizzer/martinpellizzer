Parosela leporina var. thouinii

Parosela leporina var. thouinii, commonly known as the slender parosela, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

This species is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
