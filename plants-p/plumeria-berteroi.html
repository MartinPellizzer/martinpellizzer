Plumeria berteroi

Plumeria berteroi, commonly known as the Hawaiian plumeria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, including Hawaii, Tahiti, and the Cook Islands.

Plumeria berteroi is also known for its ability to produce a milky sap that can be toxic if ingested.
