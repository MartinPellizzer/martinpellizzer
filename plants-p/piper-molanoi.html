Piper molanoi

Piper molanoi, commonly known as the Molano pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and these berries are used both for their medicinal properties and as a spice. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The leaves are often used in traditional herbal remedies due to their aromatic and medicinal qualities.
