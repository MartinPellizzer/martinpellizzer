Paeonia hybrida

Paeonia hybrida, commonly known as the tree peony, is a hybrid species belonging to the芍药属 (Paeonia) family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, often double-flowered blooms that come in a wide range of colors. The plant produces large, velvety petals that can be simple or complex in form, and its leaves are compound, typically with three to five leaflets. It is native to China and has been cultivated for centuries for its ornamental value.

Paeonia hybrida thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
