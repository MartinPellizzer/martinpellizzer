Potentilla rigoi

Potentilla rigoi, commonly known as the Alpine cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five oval-shaped leaflets, and it is often found at high elevations.

Potentilla rigoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
