Pithecellobium pithecolobioides

Pithecellobium pithecolobioides, commonly known as the yellow passion vine, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds, and it is often grown for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
