Patrinia nudiuscula

Patrinia nudiuscula, commonly known as the small-flowered patrinia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of blue or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
