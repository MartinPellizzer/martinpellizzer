Phyllagathis wenshanensis

Phyllagathis wenshanensis, commonly known as Wenshan bladdernut, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Wenshan region in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its small, oval leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, typical of many species in the Myrtaceae family.
