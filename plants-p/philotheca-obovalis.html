Philotheca obovalis

Philotheca obovalis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mountainbush, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features aromatic, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers with yellow stamens, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Philotheca obovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native garden landscaping.
