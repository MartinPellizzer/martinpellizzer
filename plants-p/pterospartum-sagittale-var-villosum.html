Pterospartum sagittale var. villosum

Pterospartum sagittale var. villosum, commonly known as the silky arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in riparian areas. It has a shrub-like growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

The variety *villosum* is distinguished by its hairy or velvety foliage, which gives it its common name.
