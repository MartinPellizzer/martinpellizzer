Polygonum honanensis

Polygonum honanensis, commonly known as the Honan knotweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside.

Polygonum honanensis is often found in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
