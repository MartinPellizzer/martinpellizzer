Pilosella hypoptella

Pilosella hypoptella, commonly known as the slender leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

The flowers are yellow, with ray florets that bloom in late spring to early summer.
