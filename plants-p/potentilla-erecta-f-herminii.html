Potentilla erecta f. herminii

Potentilla erecta f. herminii, commonly known as the Herminii cinquefoil, is a compact shrub characterized by its dense, upright growth habit.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a finely toothed margin, providing a lush green appearance throughout the growing season. This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
