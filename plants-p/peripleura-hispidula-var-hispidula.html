Peripleura hispidula var. hispidula

Peripleura hispidula var. hispidula, commonly known as the hairy peripleura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in horticultural and ecological studies.
