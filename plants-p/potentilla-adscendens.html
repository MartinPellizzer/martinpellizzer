Potentilla adscendens

Potentilla adscendens, commonly known as the ascending cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are palmately lobed with a rough texture. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in high-altitude meadows.

Potentilla adscendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
