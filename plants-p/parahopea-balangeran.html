Parahopea balangeran

Parahopea balangeran, commonly known as the Balangeran orchid, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
