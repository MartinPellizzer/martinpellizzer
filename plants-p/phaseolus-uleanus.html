Phaseolus uleanus

Phaseolus uleanus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and edible pods that contain kidney-shaped beans. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is typically grown for its nutritious seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its culinary use and ornamental appeal in gardens.
