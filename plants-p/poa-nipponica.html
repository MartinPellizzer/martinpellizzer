Poa nipponica

Poa nipponica, commonly known as Japanese bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a dense, compact appearance.

Poa nipponica is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
