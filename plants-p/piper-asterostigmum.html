Piper asterostigmum

Piper asterostigmum, commonly known as the star-stigma pepper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that mature to a bright red color, containing several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
