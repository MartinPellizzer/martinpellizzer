Physinga prostrata

Physinga prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate Physinga, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing herb that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant features small, delicate leaves that are arranged in a prostrate or spreading manner along the ground. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are clustered in inflorescences at the base of the stems.

Physinga prostrata is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in tropical and subtropical regions.
