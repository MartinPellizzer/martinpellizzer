Polygonum tenellum var. kawagoeanum

Polygonum tenellum var. kawagoeanum, commonly known as Kawagoe Polygonum, is a variant of the greater knotweed.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Japan, characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
