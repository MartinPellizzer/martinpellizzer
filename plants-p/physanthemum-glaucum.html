Physanthemum glaucum

Physanthemum glaucum, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a waxy, bluish-green surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Physanthemum glaucum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
