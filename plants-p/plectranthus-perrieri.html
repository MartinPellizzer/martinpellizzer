Plectranthus perrieri

Plectranthus perrieri, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking purple to reddish-brown tubular flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically dark green and slightly velvety in texture.

Plectranthus perrieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
