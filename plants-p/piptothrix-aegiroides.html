Piptothrix aegiroides

Piptothrix aegiroides, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and herbal practices.
