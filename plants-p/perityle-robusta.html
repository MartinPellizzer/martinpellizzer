Perityle robusta

Perityle robusta, commonly known as the robust perityle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Perityle robusta is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
