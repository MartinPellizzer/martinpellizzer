Peperomia mannii

Peperomia mannii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy leaves that are typically grayish-white to silver in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish, which are not showy but attract certain pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia mannii is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
