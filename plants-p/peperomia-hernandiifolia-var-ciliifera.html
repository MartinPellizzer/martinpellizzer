Peperomia hernandiifolia var. ciliifera

Peperomia hernandiifolia var. ciliifera, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
