Philodendron bonifaziae

Philodendron bonifaziae, commonly known as the Bonifaziae philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with lighter green or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by berries containing seeds. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant enthusiasts.
