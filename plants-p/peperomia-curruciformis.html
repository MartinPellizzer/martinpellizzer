Peperomia curruciformis

Peperomia curruciformis, commonly known as the shield fern peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia curruciformis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
