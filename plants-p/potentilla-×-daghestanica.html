Potentilla × daghestanica

Potentilla × daghestanica, commonly known as the Caucasian cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features multiple stems that are typically covered in dense, glandular hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately divided into five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its hardiness and attractive foliage.
