Pilea cavaleriei

Pilea cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, shield-shaped leaves that resemble coins. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with a short, thick stem. Its foliage is often a vibrant green, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers.

Pilea cavaleriei is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
