Philadelphus satsumi var. lancifolius

Philadelphus satsumi var. lancifolius, commonly known as the Japanese Mock Orange, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically serrated along the edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
