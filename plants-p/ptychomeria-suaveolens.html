Ptychomeria suaveolens

Ptychomeria suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented ptychomeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Ptychomeria suaveolens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrant foliage.
