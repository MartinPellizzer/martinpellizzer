Pagameopsis maguirei var. glabrus

Pagameopsis maguirei var. glabrus, commonly known as the smooth pagameopsis, is a rare and little-studied plant species native to specific regions of the southwestern United States.

The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color and are arranged in clusters at the base of the plant.
