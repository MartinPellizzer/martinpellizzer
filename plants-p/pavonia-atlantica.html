Pavonia atlantica

Pavonia atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the Atlantic coast. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia atlantica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
