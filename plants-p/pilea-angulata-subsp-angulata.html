Pilea angulata subsp. angulata

Pilea angulata subsp.

angulata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, trailing plant native to China. It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
