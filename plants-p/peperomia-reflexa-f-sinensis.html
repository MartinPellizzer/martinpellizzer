Peperomia reflexa f. sinensis

Peperomia reflexa f. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it suitable for both container and garden cultivation.
