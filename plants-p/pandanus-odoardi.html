Pandanus odoardi

Pandanus odoardi, commonly known as the Odoardi pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in coastal and wetland habitats, often growing in sandy or muddy soils.

The Odoardi pandanus is valued for its ornamental appearance and has cultural significance in some island communities.
