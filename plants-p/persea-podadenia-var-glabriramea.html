Persea podadenia var. glabriramea

Persea podadenia var. glabriramea, commonly known as the smooth-leafed persimmon, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, characterized by aromatic leaves and fruits. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. Its fruit is a small, round berry that ripens to a deep orange color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic and potential edible qualities.
