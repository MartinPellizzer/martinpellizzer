Pelargonium × knipeae

Pelargonium × knipeae, commonly known as the Knipe's geranium, is a hybrid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is widely appreciated by both amateur and professional gardeners.
