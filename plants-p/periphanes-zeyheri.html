Periphanes zeyheri

Periphanes zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's candelabra, is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its distinctive branching pattern that resembles a candelabra. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers.

Periphanes zeyheri is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
