Pentas zanzibarica var. latifolia

Pentas zanzibarica var. latifolia, commonly known as the Zanzibar pentas, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
