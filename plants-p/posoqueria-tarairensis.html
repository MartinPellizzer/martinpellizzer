Posoqueria tarairensis

Posoqueria tarairensis, commonly known as the Tarairi palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in parts of northern Brazil and the Guianas. This palm is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by elongated, green fruits.

Posoqueria tarairensis is often found in moist, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
