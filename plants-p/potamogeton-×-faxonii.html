Potamogeton × faxonii

Potamogeton × faxonii, commonly known as the hybrid water plant, is a aquatic plant that exhibits characteristics of its parent species, Potamogeton perfoliatus and Potamogeton praevolutus.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as lakes and ponds. The plant has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It produces small, green flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

Potamogeton × faxonii plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
