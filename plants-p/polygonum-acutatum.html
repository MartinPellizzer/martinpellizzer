Polygonum acutatum

Polygonum acutatum, commonly known as the flame flower or red polygonum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, vibrant red or orange inflorescences. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Polygonum acutatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
