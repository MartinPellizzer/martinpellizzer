Potentilla gaudinii var. boosiana

Potentilla gaudinii var. boosiana, commonly known as the Boosian cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
