Psittacanthus carnosus

Psittacanthus carnosus, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season, hence its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Psittacanthus carnosus is a popular ornamental plant appreciated for its beauty and seasonal blooming.
