Pleurothallis magnipetala

Pleurothallis magnipetala, commonly known as the large-petaled pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns. The plant produces elongated, pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous small to medium-sized blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Pleurothallis magnipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
