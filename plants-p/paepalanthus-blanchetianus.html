Paepalanthus blanchetianus

Paepalanthus blanchetianus, commonly known as the Brazilian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the sunflower. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, oblong fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
