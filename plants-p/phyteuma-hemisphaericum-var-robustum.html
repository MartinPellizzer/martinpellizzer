Phyteuma hemisphaericum var. robustum

Phyteuma hemisphaericum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a robust stem with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
