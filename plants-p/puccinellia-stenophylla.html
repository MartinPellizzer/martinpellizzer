Puccinellia stenophylla

Puccinellia stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf puccinellia, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. This species is typically found in arid and semi-arid environments, often in sandy or gravelly soils. The flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, which are the typical inflorescence structure of grasses.

It plays a role in stabilizing soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
