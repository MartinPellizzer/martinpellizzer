Podiopetalum

Podiopetalum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with five petals that are often velvety in texture. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a distinctive waxy coating. Podiopetalum species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.
