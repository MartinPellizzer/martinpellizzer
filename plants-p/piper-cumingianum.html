Piper cumingianum

Piper cumingianum, commonly known as Cuming's pepper or the Chinese pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are aromatic and have been utilized in various herbal remedies.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.
