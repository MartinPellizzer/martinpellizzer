Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. pallida

Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. pallida, commonly known as the pale phalaenopsis orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves that are typically light green in color. The flowers are clustered in racemes and feature white petals with a pale yellow lip, giving it a delicate and elegant appearance. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its longevity and ability to bloom repeatedly.

This variety prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity for optimal growth.
