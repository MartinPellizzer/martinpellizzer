Pelargonium alnifolium

Pelargonium alnifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its narrow, serrated leaves and clusters of bright pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Pelargonium alnifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
