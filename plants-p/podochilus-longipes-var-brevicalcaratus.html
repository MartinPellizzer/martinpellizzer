Podochilus longipes var. brevicalcaratus

Podochilus longipes var. brevicalcaratus, commonly known as the short-spurred orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and has a distinctive inflorescence with showy, spurred flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The variety brevicalcaratus is distinguished by its shorter spur compared to the typical form of Podochilus longipes.
