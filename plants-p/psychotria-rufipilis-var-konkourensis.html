Psychotria rufipilis var. konkourensis

Psychotria rufipilis var. konkourensis, commonly known as the Konkourensis Psychotria, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves with prominent veins and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Rubiaceae family.
