Pseudorlaya pumila f. breviaculeata

Pseudorlaya pumila f. breviaculeata, commonly known as the short-stemmed pseudorlaya, is a variant of the pseudorlaya pumila species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The leaves are typically small, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
