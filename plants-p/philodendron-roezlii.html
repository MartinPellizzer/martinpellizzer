Philodendron roezlii

Philodendron roezlii, commonly known as the silver star philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that have a distinctive silvery variegation pattern. The plant features aerial roots that allow it to climb and spread along supports in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often described as having a metallic sheen, which enhances its ornamental value.

Philodendron roezlii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
