Polystichum altum

Polystichum altum, commonly known as the tall fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The fronds are bi-pinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance and a dark green color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
