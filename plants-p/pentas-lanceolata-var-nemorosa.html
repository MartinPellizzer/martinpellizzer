Pentas lanceolata var. nemorosa

Pentas lanceolata var. nemorosa, commonly known as the forest pentas, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a contrasting red or purple center. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing an attractive foliage contrast. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
