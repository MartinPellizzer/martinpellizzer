Pleurothallis racemosa

Pleurothallis racemosa, commonly known as the racemose pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in controlled settings for its ornamental value.
