Peperomia astrostigma

Peperomia astrostigma, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a unique, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an excellent addition to tabletop displays or hanging baskets.
