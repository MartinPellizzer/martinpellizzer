Pilosella sosnowskyi

Pilosella sosnowskyi, commonly known as the Sosnowski's pearly everlet, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically hairy and narrow, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pilosella sosnowskyi is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
