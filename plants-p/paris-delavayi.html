Paris delavayi

Paris delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's Paris, is a member of the Melanthiaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in forested or rocky habitats at high elevations. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces delicate, grass-like leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, blooming in the spring.

Paris delavayi is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
