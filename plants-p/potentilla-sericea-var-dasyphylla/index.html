Potentilla Sericea Var. Dasyphylla

Silverleaf cinquefoil, scientifically known as Potentilla sericea var. dasyphylla, is a strikingly beautiful perennial plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its silvery foliage and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety of cinquefoil thrives in rocky soils and sunny conditions, making it a resilient addition to alpine and dry landscapes. Its unique appearance and ecological value have made it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. With its delicate yet enduring nature, Silverleaf cinquefoil stands out as both a botanical wonder and a symbol of natural beauty.
