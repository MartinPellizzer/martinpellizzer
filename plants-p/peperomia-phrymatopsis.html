Peperomia phrymatopsis

Peperomia phrymatopsis, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a striking silvery-white margin, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia phrymatopsis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
