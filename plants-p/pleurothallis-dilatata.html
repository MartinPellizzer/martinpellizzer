Pleurothallis dilatata

Pleurothallis dilatata, commonly known as the expanded pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is part of the Pleurothallis genus, which includes many species with similar floral structures.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
