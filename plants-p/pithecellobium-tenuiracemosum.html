Pithecellobium tenuiracemosum

Pithecellobium tenuiracemosum, commonly known as the slender-branch silky acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its delicate, feathery foliage and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, fibrous pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
