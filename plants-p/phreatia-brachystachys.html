Phreatia brachystachys

Phreatia brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the genus Phreatia in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions such as Malaysia and Indonesia. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, tropical rainforest environments. It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow.

The plant is adapted to thrive in shaded, moist conditions and is often found in the understory of dense forests.
