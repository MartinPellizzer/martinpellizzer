Prasophyllum fuscum var. grandiflorum

Prasophyllum fuscum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique hood-like labellum. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained sandy soils. Its flowers are usually green or reddish-green, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination by insects.

This variety is known for its relatively large, showy flowers compared to other members of the Prasophyllum genus.
