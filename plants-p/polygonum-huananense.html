Polygonum huananense

Polygonum huananense, commonly known as the Huanan knotweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive bracteate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth margin and a prominent midrib.

The flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and are usually greenish-white in color.
