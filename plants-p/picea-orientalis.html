Picea orientalis

Picea orientalis, commonly known as the Oriental spruce, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals along the branches. Its cones are woody, elongated, and typically hang downward.

This species is valued for its timber and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and parks.
