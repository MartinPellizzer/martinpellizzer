Prasophyllum patens

Prasophyllum patens, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, violet-colored flowers during the summer months. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its delicate and ephemeral blooms.

Prasophyllum patens is a small plant, usually reaching heights of less than 30 centimeters.
