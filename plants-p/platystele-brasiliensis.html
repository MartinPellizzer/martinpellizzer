Platystele brasiliensis

Platystele brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a rare and unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It is native to the tropical rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species.
