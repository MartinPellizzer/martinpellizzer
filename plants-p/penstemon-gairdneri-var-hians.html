Penstemon gairdneri var. hians

Penstemon gairdneri var. hians, commonly known as the Sierra penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial species that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in high elevation environments. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The Sierra penstemon is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh, arid conditions.
