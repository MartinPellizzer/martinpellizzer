Papaver stubendorfii

Papaver stubendorfii, commonly known as the Stubendorf poppy, is a rare and striking member of the Papaveraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red petals and a dark, almost black, central stigma, which makes it visually distinctive. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
