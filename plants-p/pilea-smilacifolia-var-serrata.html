Pilea smilacifolia var. serrata

Pilea smilacifolia var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated pilea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
