Paphiopedilum × grussianum

Paphiopedilum × grussianum, commonly known as the Russian lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from Paphiopedilum suaveolens and Paphiopedilum micranthum.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a broad, fleshy rhizome and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, slipper-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
