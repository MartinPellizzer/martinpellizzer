Paphiopedilum hennisianum var. fowliei

Paphiopedilum hennisianum var. fowliei, commonly known as the Hennisian Hairsheath Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy sheath that encases the flower stem, giving it its common name. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a vibrant pinkish-red color and a distinctive, pouch-like lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and rarity.
