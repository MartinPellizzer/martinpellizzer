Protea serraria

Protea serraria, commonly known as the serrated protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, cone-shaped inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in nutrient-poor, sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, giving it its common name.

Protea serraria is a popular ornamental plant due to its long-lasting blooms and unique appearance.
