Prunus jamasakura f. glabra

Prunus jamasakura f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-flowered cherry, is a cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and delicate, pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

The tree is also valued for its cultural significance in traditional Japanese gardens.
