Pogogyne

Pogogyne, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its small, often hairy leaves and showy, typically blue or purple flowers. The plants are native to various regions in Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia. They are usually shrubs or herbs, with some species growing in arid or semi-arid environments.

Pogogyne species are often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain areas.
