Pterocypsela indica var. dracoglossa

Pterocypsela indica var. dracoglossa, commonly known as the dragon's tongue cypsela, is a variant of the Indian cypsela plant.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, and its fruit is a type of achene enclosed in a pappus. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and compact growth habit.
