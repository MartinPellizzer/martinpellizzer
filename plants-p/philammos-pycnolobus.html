Philammos pycnolobus

Philammos pycnolobus, commonly known as the dense-lobed philammos, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
