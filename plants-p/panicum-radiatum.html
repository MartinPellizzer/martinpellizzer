Panicum radiatum

Panicum radiatum, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and produces fluffy, panicle-shaped inflorescences. The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats make it a valuable plant for erosion control and wildlife habitat.
