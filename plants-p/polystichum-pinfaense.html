Polystichum pinfaense

Polystichum pinfaense, commonly known as the Pinfaense wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Polystichum pinfaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
