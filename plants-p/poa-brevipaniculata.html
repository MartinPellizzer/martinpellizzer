Poa brevipaniculata

Poa brevipaniculata, commonly known as the short panicle grass, is a species of grass native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, erect culms. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically shorter than those of other Poa species. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open areas.
