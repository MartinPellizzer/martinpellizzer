Phrynium elegans

Phrynium elegans, commonly known as the elegant phrynium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Phrynium elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
