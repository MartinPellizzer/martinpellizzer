Phaseolus sphaericus var. minor

Phaseolus sphaericus var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf bean, is a compact variety of the common bean genus.

It is characterized by its small, rounded pods and bushy growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening. The plant produces edible seeds that are typically harvested when the pods turn yellow and dry. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, requiring moderate water and nutrients.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in small spaces.
