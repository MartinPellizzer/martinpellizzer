Podanthum limonifolium f. heterophyllum

Podanthum limonifolium f. heterophyllum, commonly known as the lemon-leaf podanthum, is a unique cultivar characterized by its distinct foliage.

This plant exhibits a compact growth habit with elongated, narrow leaves that are a vibrant yellow-green in color. The leaves are slightly serrated and emit a subtle citrus aroma, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to maintain its coloration.

Botanically, it is a hybrid form that showcases the variability within the Podanthum genus.
