Peperomia obscurifolia var. fatoana

Peperomia obscurifolia var. fatoana, commonly known as the Fatoana peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its unique leaf coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
