Pilosella × variifurca

Pilosella × variifurca, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and yellow flowering heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

Pilosella × variifurca is native to Europe but has been introduced to various parts of North America.
