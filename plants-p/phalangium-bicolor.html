Phalangium bicolor

Phalangium bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored flatworm, is not a plant but a species of flatworm.

It is a marine invertebrate belonging to the phylum Platyhelminthes. This organism is characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like body and distinct coloration patterns. Phalangium bicolor is typically found in coastal waters and is often associated with marine environments.
