Pelargonium × dodii

Pelargonium × dodii, commonly known as the chocolate geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black foliage and vibrant red to purple flowers. Native to South Africa, this species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens. The leaves are glossy and have a distinctive, almost velvety texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
