Peperomia parietariifolia

Peperomia parietariifolia, commonly known as the wall-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a unique, almost otherworldly appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its slow growth rate makes it an excellent choice for indoor gardening and decorative displays.
