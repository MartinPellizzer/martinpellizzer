Paepalanthus robustus

Paepalanthus robustus, commonly known as the robust sunflower.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family, native to South America. The plant is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and robust, upright growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Paepalanthus robustus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
