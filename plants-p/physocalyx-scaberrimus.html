Physocalyx scaberrimus

Physocalyx scaberrimus, commonly known as the roughleaf physocalyx, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale purple to white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
