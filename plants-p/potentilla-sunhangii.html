Potentilla sunhangii

Potentilla sunhangii, commonly known as the Sunhang cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. Potentilla sunhangii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
