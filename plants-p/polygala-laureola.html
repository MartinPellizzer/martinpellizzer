Polygala laureola

Polygala laureola, commonly known as the small-flowered milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
