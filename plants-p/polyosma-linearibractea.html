Polyosma linearibractea

Polyosma linearibractea, commonly known as the narrow bract polyosma, is a shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged opposite each other along the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in dense inflorescences, which are often surrounded by bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
