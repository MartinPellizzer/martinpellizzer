Pilosocereus gaumeri

Pilosocereus gaumeri, commonly known as the Moon Cactus, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its swollen, globular stem and a distinctive, velvety covering of white or grayish hairs. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, where it thrives in full sun and well-draining soil. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
