Primula sieboldii f. albiflora

Primula sieboldii f. albiflora, commonly known as the Whiteflower Primrose, is a hybrid form of the Primula sieboldii species.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which contrast with its dark green, heart-shaped leaves. Native to East Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded areas. The flowers typically bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to gardens and natural habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
