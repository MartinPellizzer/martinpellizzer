Phaseolus amoenus

Phaseolus amoenus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or yellow flowers and elongated pods containing edible seeds. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and vigorous vining growth habit. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is widely cultivated for both its ornamental value and culinary use.
