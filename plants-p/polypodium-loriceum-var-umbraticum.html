Polypodium loriceum var. umbraticum

Polypodium loriceum var. umbraticum, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green fronds with a leathery texture. It produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in sori.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in shady garden settings.
