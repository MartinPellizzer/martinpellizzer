Podospermum tenuifolium

Podospermum tenuifolium, commonly known as slender-leaf podospermum, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features small, opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. Its flowers are followed by small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Podospermum tenuifolium is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
