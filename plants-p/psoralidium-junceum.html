Psoralidium junceum

Psoralidium junceum, commonly known as the slender psoralidium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in forest understories. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a delicate, fern-like appearance.
