Pinus halepensis var. abasica

Pinus halepensis var. abasica, commonly known as the Aleppo pine subspecies, is a variant of the Aleppo pine native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a medium-sized evergreen conifer with needle-like leaves and cones that typically mature to a reddish-brown color. The plant is characterized by its adaptability to dry and rocky soils, making it resilient in arid environments. It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Its botanical features make it a significant component of Mediterranean forest ecosystems.
