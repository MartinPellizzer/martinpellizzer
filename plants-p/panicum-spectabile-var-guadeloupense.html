Panicum spectabile var. guadeloupense

Panicum spectabile var. guadeloupense, commonly known as Guadeloupe panicgrass, is a striking ornamental grass native to the Caribbean.

It features tall, upright inflorescences that range in color from pink to red, adding a dramatic visual element to gardens. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and coastal landscapes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that contrasts beautifully with its vibrant flower spikes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
