Peperomia rapensis

Peperomia rapensis, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, and its foliage often displays striking patterns of silver or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, though they are not the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia rapensis is prized for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
