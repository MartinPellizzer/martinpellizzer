Primulina duanensis

Primulina duanensis, commonly known as the Duan's primula, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant orange or yellow blooms during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
