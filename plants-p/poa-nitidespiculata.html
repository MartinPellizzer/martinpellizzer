Poa nitidespiculata

Poa nitidespiculata, commonly known as the shining spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or brown hues. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Poa nitidespiculata is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
