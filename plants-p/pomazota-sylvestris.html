Pomazota sylvestris

Pomazota sylvestris, commonly known as the wild pomegranate, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Punicaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. Pomazota sylvestris is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
