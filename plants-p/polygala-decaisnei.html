Polygala decaisnei

Polygala decaisnei, commonly known as the Decaisne's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and opposite, while its fruits are pods that split open to release seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
