Pityrogramma consimilis

Pityrogramma consimilis, commonly known as the similar water fern, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive, finely divided structure. Its fronds are typically light green and develop sporangia on the underside of the leaves.

Pityrogramma consimilis is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and forest floors where it plays a role in soil stabilization and biodiversity.
