Panicum crus-pavonis

Panicum crus-pavonis, commonly known as the common panicgrass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves.

Its small, pale green spikelets are clustered at the ends of the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance.
