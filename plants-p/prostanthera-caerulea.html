Prostanthera caerulea

Prostanthera caerulea, commonly known as blue prostanthera, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional Indigenous medicine.

Prostanthera caerulea typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
