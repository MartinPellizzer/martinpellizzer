Paphiopedilum insigne var. duvalii

Paphiopedilum insigne var. duvalii, commonly known as the Duval's Vanda orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip that is often yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
