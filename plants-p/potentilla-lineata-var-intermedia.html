Potentilla lineata var. intermedia

Potentilla lineata var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate cinquefoil, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, which are serrated and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
