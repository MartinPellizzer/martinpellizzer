Pleiogynium cerasiferum var. glabratum

Pleiogynium cerasiferum var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth-flowered hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
