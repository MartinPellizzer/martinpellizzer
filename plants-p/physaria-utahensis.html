Physaria utahensis

Physaria utahensis, commonly known as the Utah bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and inflated seed pods.
