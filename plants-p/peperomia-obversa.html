Peperomia obversa

Peperomia obversa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen, giving it a luxurious appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia obversa is also appreciated for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of potting soils.
