Plantago akkensis subsp. ounifensis

Plantago akkensis subsp.

ounifensis, commonly known as the Eastern Plantain, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
