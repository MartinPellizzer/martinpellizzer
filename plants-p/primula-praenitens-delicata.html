Primula praenitens delicata

Primula praenitens delicata, commonly known as the delicate primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
