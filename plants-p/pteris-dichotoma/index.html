Pteris Dichotoma

Pteris dichotoma is a fern species known for its distinctive bipinnate fronds and adaptability to various environments. Native to parts of Asia and the Pacific, it thrives in moist, shaded areas and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses. Its unique leaf structure and resilience make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Pteris dichotoma also plays an important role in ecological systems by supporting local biodiversity and soil health.
