Platystachys kunthiana

Platystachys kunthiana, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are silver-white underneath, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
