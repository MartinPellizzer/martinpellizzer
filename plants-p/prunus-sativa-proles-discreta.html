Prunus sativa proles discreta

Prunus sativa proles discreta, commonly known as the discreet dwarf cherry, is a rare and ornamental species of cherry tree.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features small, oval leaves with a glossy green surface and serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for small garden spaces.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique botanical and aesthetic value.
