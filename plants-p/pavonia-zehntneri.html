Pavonia zehntneri

Pavonia zehntneri, commonly known as the Zehntner's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, hibiscus-like flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Pavonia zehntneri thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.
