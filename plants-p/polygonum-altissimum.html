Polygonum altissimum

Polygonum altissimum, commonly known as tall knotweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes.

Polygonum altissimum is typically found in moist, disturbed habitats such as wetlands and stream banks.
