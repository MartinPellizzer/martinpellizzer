Phyteuma balbisii

Phyteuma balbisii, commonly known as the Balbisii garden bell, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes various bell-shaped flowers. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-like, lavender to blue flowers. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and slightly aromatic.

Phyteuma balbisii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
