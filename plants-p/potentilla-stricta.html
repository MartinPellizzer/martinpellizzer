Potentilla stricta

Potentilla stricta, commonly known as the upright cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features narrow, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated edge.

Potentilla stricta is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ornamental value.
