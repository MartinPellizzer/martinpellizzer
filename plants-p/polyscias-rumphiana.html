Polyscias rumphiana

Polyscias rumphiana, commonly known as the broad-leaf sedge, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from red to black. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive foliage.
