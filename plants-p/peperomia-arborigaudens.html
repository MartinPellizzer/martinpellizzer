Peperomia arborigaudens

Peperomia arborigaudens, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, silver-green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in terminal clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia arborigaudens is valued for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
