Piper doiphukhaense

Piper doiphukhaense, commonly known as the Doiphukha pepper, is a rare species of flowering plant native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper and long pepper. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical berries.

Due to its limited distribution and unique ecological niche, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
