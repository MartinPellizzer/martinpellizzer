Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. microdonta

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

microdonta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
