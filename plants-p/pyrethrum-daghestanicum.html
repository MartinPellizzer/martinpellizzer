Pyrethrum daghestanicum

Pyrethrum daghestanicum, commonly known as the Caucasian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces fine, hair-like structures called trichomes, which contain natural pyrethrin compounds. These compounds are known for their insecticidal properties, making the plant valuable in organic pest control.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, typically in mountainous environments.
