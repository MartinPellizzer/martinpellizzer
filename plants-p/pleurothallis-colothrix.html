Pleurothallis colothrix

Pleurothallis colothrix, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name.

Pleurothallis colothrix thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
