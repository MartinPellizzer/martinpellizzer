Parodia mueller-melchersii

Parodia mueller-melchersii, commonly known as the Mueller-Melchersii Cactus, is a species native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant features a short, thick stem with areoles that produce both spines and flowers. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
