Piper ellipticifolium

Piper ellipticifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf pepper or ellipticifolium pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, clustered fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The leaves and fruits contain various bioactive compounds, including alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

It is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and potential therapeutic applications.
