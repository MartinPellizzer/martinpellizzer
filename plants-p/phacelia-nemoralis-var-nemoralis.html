Phacelia nemoralis var. nemoralis

Phacelia nemoralis var. nemoralis, commonly known as the European phacelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features erect, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
