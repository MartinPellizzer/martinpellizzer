Polygonum chinense var. ovalifolium

Polygonum chinense var. ovalifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, clustered flowers. The leaves are oval-shaped, hence the specific epithet "ovalifolium," and are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces seeds in capsules that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

It is often found in moist soils near water bodies and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
