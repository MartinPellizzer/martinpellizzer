Pyrostegia

Pyrostegia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and features showy, flame-colored flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. Pyrostegia species are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their vibrant floral displays.
