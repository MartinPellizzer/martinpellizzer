Pennisetum atrichum

Pennisetum atrichum, commonly known as black panicgrass, is a grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically brown or black in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy plant in various climates.

Pennisetum atrichum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
