Phlomis × semiorbata

Phlomis × semiorbata, commonly known as the hybrid hybrid blanketflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or purple, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in native plant landscapes.
