Pleurothallis moronae

Pleurothallis moronae, commonly known as the Morone's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Epidendroideae subfamily.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. It features small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
