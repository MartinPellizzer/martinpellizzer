Phanera khasiana var. khasiana

Phanera khasiana var. khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi phanera, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within the tropical and subtropical forests. The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and exhibit a range of colors from white to pink.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
