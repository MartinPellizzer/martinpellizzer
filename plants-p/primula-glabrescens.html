Primula glabrescens

Primula glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clustered flowers. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and yellow, often with a distinctive darker throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
