Pleurostachys graminifolia var. glabra

Pleurostachys graminifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth grass of the genus Pleurostachys, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are small and clustered, forming dense inflorescences that are often wind-pollinated.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, glabrous surfaces, which set it apart from other varieties within the species.
