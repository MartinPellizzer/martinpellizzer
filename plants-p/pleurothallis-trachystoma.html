Pleurothallis trachystoma

Pleurothallis trachystoma, commonly known as the short-stalked pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale green flowers with a subtle fragrance. It is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild and requires careful care in cultivation.
