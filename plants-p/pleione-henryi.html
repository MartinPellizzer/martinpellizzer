Pleione henryi

Pleione henryi, commonly known as the Henry's pleione, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a spike with several fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest areas.

Pleione henryi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
