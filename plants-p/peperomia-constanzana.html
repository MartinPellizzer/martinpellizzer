Peperomia constanzana

Peperomia constanzana, commonly known as the Constantzana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy foliage with oval to lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green and slightly pubescent. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia constanzana is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
