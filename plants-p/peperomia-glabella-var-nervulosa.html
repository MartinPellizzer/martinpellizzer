Peperomia glabella var. nervulosa

Peperomia glabella var. nervulosa, commonly known as the veiny peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive veiny pattern that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it ideal for container gardening and as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
