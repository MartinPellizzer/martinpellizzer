Pilea cyclophora

Pilea cyclophora, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features rosettes of glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
