Pithecellobium leptophyllum

Pithecellobium leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender pod bean, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by elongated, woody pods that contain several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
