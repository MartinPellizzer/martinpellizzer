Pilosella hypeurya subsp. lamprocoma

Pilosella hypeurya subsp.

lamprocoma, commonly known as the silverleaf pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The subspecies is distinguished by its more densely pubescent leaves compared to other varieties within the species.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
