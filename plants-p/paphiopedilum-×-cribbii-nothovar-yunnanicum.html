Paphiopedilum × cribbii nothovar. yunnanicum

Paphiopedilum × cribbii nothovar. yunnanicum, commonly known as the Yunnan Cribb's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves and large, showy flowers with a distinctive lip. The flowers typically feature a mix of yellow, green, and brown hues, with a prominent, spurred lip that is often darker in color. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
