Populus yatungensis var. yatungensis

Populus yatungensis var. yatungensis, commonly known as the Yatung poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in river valleys and lowland areas. It produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by samaras that aid in seed dispersal.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in stabilizing soil along waterways.
