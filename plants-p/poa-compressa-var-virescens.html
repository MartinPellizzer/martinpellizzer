Poa compressa var. virescens

Poa compressa var. virescens, commonly known as the green tufted grass, is a variant of the common bentgrass species.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically forms low, compact clumps and is often found in moist, temperate environments. Its foliage is generally a vibrant green, which contributes to its ornamental appeal.

It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun.
