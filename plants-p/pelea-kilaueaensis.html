Pelea kilaueaensis

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant thrives in the volcanic soil of the Kilauea volcano's summit region, where it adapts to the harsh, high-altitude environment.
