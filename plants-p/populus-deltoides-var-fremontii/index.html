Populus Deltoides Var. Fremontii

Populus deltoides var. fremontii, commonly known as the Fremont cottonwood, is a hardy tree native to the western United States. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found along riverbanks and in floodplains. This species is notable for its rapid growth and ability to withstand harsh conditions, making it an important part of local ecosystems. Its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and fluffy seed pods make it easily recognizable. The Fremont cottonwood plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
