Protea effusa

Protea effusa, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Protea effusa is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
