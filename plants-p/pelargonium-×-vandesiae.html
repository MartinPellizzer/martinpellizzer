Pelargonium × vandesiae

Pelargonium × vandesiae, commonly known as the Vandese geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant produces five-petaled blooms with a distinctive, often frilled appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Pelargonium × vandesiae is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
