Peperomia barbana

Peperomia barbana, commonly known as the feathered peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or silver veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal rather than its flowers.
