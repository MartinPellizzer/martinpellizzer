Panicum multinerve

Panicum multinerve, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural habitats.
