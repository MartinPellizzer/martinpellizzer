Patersonia umbrosa var. umbrosa

Patersonia umbrosa var. umbrosa, commonly known as the dark-leaf patersonia, is a perennial herb native to southeastern Australia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in loose racemes. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rock crevices.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the orchid family.
