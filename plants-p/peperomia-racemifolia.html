Peperomia racemifolia

Peperomia racemifolia, commonly known as the raceme peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on short, upright inflorescences. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both home and office settings.
