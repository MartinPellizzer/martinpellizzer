Polygonum dumetorum f. cristatum

Polygonum dumetorum f. cristatum, commonly known as the crested knotweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, crinkled leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland edges.

Its growth habit is spreading, with rhizomes that allow it to form dense mats.
