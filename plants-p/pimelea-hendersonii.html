Pimelea hendersonii

Pimelea hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson's pimelea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in winter, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Pimelea hendersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
