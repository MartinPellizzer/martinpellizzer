Philodendron appunii

Philodendron appunii, commonly known as the Appunii Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits containing tiny seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental leaves, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
