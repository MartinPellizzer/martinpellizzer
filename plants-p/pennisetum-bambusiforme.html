Pennisetum bambusiforme

Pennisetum bambusiforme, commonly known as bamboo grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender culms that resemble bamboo, growing up to 3 meters in height. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue, and it produces fluffy, pinkish-white inflorescences in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Pennisetum bambusiforme is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and ability to add texture to garden designs.
