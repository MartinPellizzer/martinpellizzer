Psilanthus jasminoides

Psilanthus jasminoides, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or yellow jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Oleaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
