Phillyrea vulgaris var. latifolia

Phillyrea vulgaris var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
