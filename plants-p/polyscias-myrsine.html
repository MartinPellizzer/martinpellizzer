Polyscias myrsine

Polyscias myrsine, commonly known as the glossy leaf or shining gooseberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Polyscias myrsine is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
