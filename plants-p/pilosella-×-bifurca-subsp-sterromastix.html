Pilosella × bifurca subsp. sterromastix

Pilosella × bifurca subsp.

sterromastix, commonly known as the slender leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its subspecies name, *sterromastix*, refers to its distinctive leaf morphology and floral structure.
