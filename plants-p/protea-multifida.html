Protea multifida

Protea multifida, commonly known as the many-lobed protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous petal-like bracts. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red, attracting various pollinators.

Protea multifida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
