Pilosella sparsipila

Pilosella sparsipila, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow disk flowers surrounded by white ray flowers. It is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Pilosella sparsipila is a perennial herb that is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
