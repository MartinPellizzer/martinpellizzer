Phaius tanjiewhoei

Phaius tanjiewhoei, commonly known as the Tanjiewhoei orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and greenhouses.
