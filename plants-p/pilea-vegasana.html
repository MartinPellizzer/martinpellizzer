Pilea vegasana

Pilea vegasana, commonly known as the Mexican pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features rounded, succulent-like leaves that are typically bright green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
