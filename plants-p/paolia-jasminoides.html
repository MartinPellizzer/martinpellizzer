Paolia jasminoides

Paolia jasminoides, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Oleaceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants due to their pleasant aroma.
