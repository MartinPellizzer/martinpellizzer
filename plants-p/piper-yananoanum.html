Piper yananoanum

Piper yananoanum, commonly known as Yananoan pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are often used in local herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant flowers.
