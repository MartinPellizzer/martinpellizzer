Phyllanthus balakrishnanii

Phyllanthus balakrishnanii, commonly known as the Indian cheesewood, is a shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant is often found in dry, rocky, or disturbed habitats, thriving in arid and semi-arid regions.
