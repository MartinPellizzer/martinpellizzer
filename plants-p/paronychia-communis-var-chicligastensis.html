Paronychia communis var. chicligastensis

Paronychia communis var. chicligastensis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed paronychia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, decorative plants.
