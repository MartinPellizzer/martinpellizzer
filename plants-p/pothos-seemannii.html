Pothos seemannii

Pothos seemannii, commonly known as the Satin Pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Pothos seemannii is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to purify indoor air.
