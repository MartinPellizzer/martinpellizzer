Pueraria candollei var. mirifica

Pueraria candollei var. mirifica, commonly known as Kwao Krua, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical climates. Its roots contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.

The botanical features of this plant make it both ecologically significant and of interest in pharmacological research.
