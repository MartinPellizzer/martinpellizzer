Potentilla salisii

Potentilla salisii, commonly known as Salish potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Potentilla salisii thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as sagebrush steppe and coastal dunes.
