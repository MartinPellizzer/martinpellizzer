Pilosella trichoscapa

Pilosella trichoscapa, commonly known as the hairy leaf mouse-ear, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and hairy stems and leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has a creeping growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a common weed in agricultural settings.
