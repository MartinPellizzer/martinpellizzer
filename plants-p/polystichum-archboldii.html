Polystichum archboldii

Polystichum archboldii, commonly known as the Archbold's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.
