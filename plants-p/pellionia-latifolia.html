Pellionia latifolia

Pellionia latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf pellionia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Pellionia latifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in horticulture.
