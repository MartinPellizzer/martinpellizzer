Premna macrophylla var. glaberrima

Premna macrophylla var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth-leafed glorybower, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
