Parinari hahlii

Parinari hahlii, commonly known as the Hahlii flame tree, is a tropical tree belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing species. The plant is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface.
