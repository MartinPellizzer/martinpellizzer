Piper tenuispicatum

Piper tenuispicatum, commonly known as the slender-spined pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, opposite leaves. It produces small greenish-white flowers followed by elongated, fleshy berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
