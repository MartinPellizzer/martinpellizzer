Plantago tenuipala

Plantago tenuipala, commonly known as narrow-leaved plantain, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sunny areas such as rocky soils and disturbed ground. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, greenish flowers in a spike-like inflorescence. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its healing properties.

The plant is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
