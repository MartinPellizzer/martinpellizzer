Pedicularis sudetica subsp. albolabiata

albolabiata, commonly known as the White-lipped Monkey Orchid, is a rare and specialized member of the Orobanchaceae family. It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it partially relies on host plants for water and nutrients while conducting photosynthesis.
