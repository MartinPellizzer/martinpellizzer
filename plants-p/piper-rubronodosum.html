Piper rubronodosum

Piper rubronodosum, commonly known as the red-nodded pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its distinctive red, nodular stems. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into red, peppery fruits. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture and emit a mild peppery aroma.

Piper rubronodosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its use in traditional medicine.
