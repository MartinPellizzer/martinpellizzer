Platypodium elegans

Platypodium elegans, commonly known as the elegant hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Platypodium elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
