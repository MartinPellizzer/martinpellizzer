Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. tortesii

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

tortesii, commonly known as the Greek Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds. Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

tortesii is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
