Peperomia falconensis

Peperomia falconensis, commonly known as the Falcon's Beak Peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub with thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia falconensis is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
