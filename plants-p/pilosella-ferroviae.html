Pilosella ferroviae

Pilosella ferroviae, commonly known as the railway pilosella, is a small flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils along mountain paths and railway embankments. Its leaves are hairy and arranged in a rosette, while its flowers are yellow with white ray florets.

Pilosella ferroviae is often found in disturbed habitats and is adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments.
