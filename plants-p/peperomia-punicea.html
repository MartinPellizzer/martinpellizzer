Peperomia punicea

Peperomia punicea, commonly known as the red peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or reddish-purple, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant has small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect sunlight.

Peperomia punicea is prized for its attractive foliage and air-purifying properties.
