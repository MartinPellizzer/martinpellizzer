Phyteuma halleri f. silvaticum

Phyteuma halleri f. silvaticum, commonly known as the woodland phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem.
