Panicum latum

Panicum latum, commonly known as broad panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its wide, flat leaves and panicles that produce small, spikelet-based flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.

Its ornamental value has also led to its use in landscaping in some areas.
