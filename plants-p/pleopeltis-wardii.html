Pleopeltis wardii

Pleopeltis wardii, commonly known as the Ward's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive, glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for both horticultural and botanical studies.
