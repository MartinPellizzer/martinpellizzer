Philadelphus coronarius var. floribundus

Philadelphus coronarius var. floribundus, commonly known as the golden spire honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features fragrant, white to yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
