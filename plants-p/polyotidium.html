Polyotidium

Polyotidium, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to regions of southern Africa. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Polyotidium is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
