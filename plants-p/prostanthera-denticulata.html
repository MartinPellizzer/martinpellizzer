Prostanthera denticulata

Prostanthera denticulata, commonly known as the serrated honeysuckle, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. Its leaves are serrated and have a fragrant, mint-like scent.

Prostanthera denticulata is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
