Phyteuma scheuchzeri var. serratum

Phyteuma scheuchzeri var. serratum, commonly known as the serrated phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have serrated edges, contributing to its common name. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This plant is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
