Phyllopodium elegans

Phyllopodium elegans, commonly known as the elegant feather fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's fronds are bi-directionally divided, creating a feather-like appearance that adds ornamental value to gardens and indoor spaces. It prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for shade-loving plants.

Its unique morphology and aesthetic appeal make it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
