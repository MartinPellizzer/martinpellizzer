Prunus × chichibuensis f. rotundipetala

Prunus × chichibuensis f. rotundipetala, commonly known as the Chichibu cherry, is a hybrid ornamental shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of semi-double, pink to white flowers in early spring. The leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
