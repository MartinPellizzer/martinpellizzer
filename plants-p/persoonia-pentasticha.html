Persoonia pentasticha

Persoonia pentasticha, commonly known as the Five-branch Persoonia, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its unique floral structure and growth habit make it a notable species in its native habitat.
