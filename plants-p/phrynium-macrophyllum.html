Phrynium macrophyllum

Phrynium macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of several meters. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a waxy texture and can have a slight sheen.

Phrynium macrophyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
