Pollalesta

Pollalesta, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, reflective hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Pollalesta is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens in temperate climates.
