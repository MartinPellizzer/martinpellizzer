Parahebe linifolia subsp. brevistylis

Parahebe linifolia subsp.

brevistylis, commonly known as the narrow-styled parahebe, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to New Zealand. It features dense, compact foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers a shady to semi-shaded environment.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover in suitable conditions.
