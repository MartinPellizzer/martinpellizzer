Phillyrea latifolia subsp. media

Phillyrea latifolia subsp.

media, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in dry, sunny conditions.

The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow and appear in clusters, followed by small, leathery fruits.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in arid environments.
