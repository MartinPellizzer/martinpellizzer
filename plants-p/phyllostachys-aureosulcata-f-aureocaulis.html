Phyllostachys aureosulcata f. aureocaulis

Phyllostachys aureosulcata f. aureocaulis, commonly known as the golden bamboo or yellow bamboo, is a variant of the golden bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow stems that contrast with its green leaves. The plant is a clump-forming grass that grows rapidly, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique coloration and growth habit make it a visually appealing addition to gardens and landscapes.
