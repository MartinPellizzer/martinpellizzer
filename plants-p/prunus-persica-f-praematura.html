Prunus persica f. praematura

Prunus persica f. praematura, commonly known as the early peach.

This variety is a naturally occurring form of the peach tree, characterized by its early blooming and fruiting habits. It produces small, often yellow or red peaches that ripen earlier than the standard peach. The plant exhibits typical peach morphology, with broad leaves and a branching structure.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and early harvest, making it a popular choice for gardeners and commercial growers.
