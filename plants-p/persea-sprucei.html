Persea sprucei

Persea sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains a single seed.

Persea sprucei is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as montane forests.
