Pelargonium alchemilloides var. ranunculifolium

Pelargonium alchemilloides var. ranunculifolium, commonly known as the ranunculifolium geranium, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture and a distinctive, spicy scent. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique foliage and fragrant blooms make it a standout in both ornamental and horticultural settings.
