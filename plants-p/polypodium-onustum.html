Polypodium onustum

Polypodium onustum, commonly known as the mountain holly fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops or in shaded forest understories, thriving in humid environments. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of its fertile fronds.

The plant's morphology and habitat make it well adapted to its ecological niche in diverse geographic locations.
