Potentilla cardinalis

Potentilla cardinalis, commonly known as cardinal cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 inches. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately divided into five oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
