Plectranthus assurgens

Plectranthus assurgens, commonly known as the Mexican mint marigold, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, mint-scented leaves and clusters of small, tubular, violet to blue flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in herbal teas and has a long history of traditional medicinal use.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal gardening.
