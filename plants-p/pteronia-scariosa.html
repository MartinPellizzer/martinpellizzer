Pteronia scariosa

Pteronia scariosa, commonly known as the silverleaf pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves and delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Pteronia scariosa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
