Polystichum gejiuense

Polystichum gejiuense, commonly known as the Gejiu shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, bipinnate fronds that are dark green and glossy. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
