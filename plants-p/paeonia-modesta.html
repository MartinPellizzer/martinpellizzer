Paeonia modesta

Paeonia modesta, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink and red. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and stems that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The flowers typically bloom in late spring, adding a vibrant display to the landscape.
