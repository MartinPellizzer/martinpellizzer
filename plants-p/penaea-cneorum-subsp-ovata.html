Penaea cneorum subsp. ovata

Penaea cneorum subsp.

ovata, commonly known as the Mediterranean cypress. It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and scale-like leaves.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by woody, cone-like structures. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value lies in its dense foliage and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
