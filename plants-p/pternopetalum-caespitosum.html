Pternopetalum caespitosum

Pternopetalum caespitosum, commonly known as the tufted pteropetalum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and compact foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Pternopetalum caespitosum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and rocky slopes.
