Phyllostachys shuchengensis

Phyllostachys shuchengensis, commonly known as the Shucheng bamboo, is a species of grass in the禾本科 (Poaceae) family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
