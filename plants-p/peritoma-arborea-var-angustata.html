Peritoma arborea var. angustata

Peritoma arborea var. angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf peritoma, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often narrow and slightly hairy. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters, and are attractive to pollinators.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in warm climates.
