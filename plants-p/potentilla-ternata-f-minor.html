Potentilla ternata f. minor

Potentilla ternata f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf cinquefoil, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, clustered leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. It has been cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

This variety is a subspecies of Potentilla ternata, distinguished by its smaller size and more delicate appearance.
