Pleurothallis erectiflora

Pleurothallis erectiflora, commonly known as the upright-flowered pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrates.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid growing conditions.
