Peperomia cufodontii

Peperomia cufodontii, commonly known as the African violet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cufodontii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
