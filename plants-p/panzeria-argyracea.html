Panzeria argyracea

Panzeria argyracea, commonly known as silver shield fern, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping foliage that forms a protective shield-like structure, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically leathery and covered in a fine, silvery bloom, which reflects light and helps reduce water loss. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric moisture.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
