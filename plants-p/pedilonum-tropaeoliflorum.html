Pedilonum tropaeoliflorum

Pedilonum tropaeoliflorum, commonly known as the tropical morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The seeds are enclosed in a fleshy, berry-like fruit that ripens to a dark purple or red color.
