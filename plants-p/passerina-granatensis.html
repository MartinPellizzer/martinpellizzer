Passerina granatensis

Passerina granatensis, commonly known as the Granada pasqueflower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, early spring blooms. Passerina granatensis is a short-lived perennial, with a life cycle closely tied to the seasonal availability of moisture.
