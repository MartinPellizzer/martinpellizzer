Polygala venenosa var. robusta

Polygala venenosa var. robusta, commonly known as the robust polygala, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showing a preference for well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and branching.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
