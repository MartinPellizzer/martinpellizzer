Phrynium tuberosum

Phrynium tuberosum, commonly known as the arrow leaf plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. Its tuberous roots are edible and have been used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
