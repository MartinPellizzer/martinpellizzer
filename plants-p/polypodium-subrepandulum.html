Polypodium subrepandulum

Polypodium subrepandulum, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, linear leaves that form a dense, compact frond structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizome is creeping and produces multiple upright fronds throughout the growing season.

The species is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its role in maintaining ecological balance in its native habitats.
