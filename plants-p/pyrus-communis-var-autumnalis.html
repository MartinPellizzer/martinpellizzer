Pyrus communis var. autumnalis

Pyrus communis var. autumnalis, commonly known as the autumnal pear, is a cultivar of the common pear tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which turns vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall. The plant produces small, sweet fruits that ripen in late summer to early autumn. It is typically deciduous, shedding its leaves in winter.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
