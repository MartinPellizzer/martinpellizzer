Peperomia thollonii

Peperomia thollonii, commonly known as the Thollon's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and unique foliage, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
