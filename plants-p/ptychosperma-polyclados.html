Ptychosperma polyclados

Ptychosperma polyclados, commonly known as the broad-leafed cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
