Primulina brunnea

Primulina brunnea, commonly known as the black-veined caladium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant colors ranging from deep red to dark green. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor settings. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its unique appearance.

Primulina brunnea thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
