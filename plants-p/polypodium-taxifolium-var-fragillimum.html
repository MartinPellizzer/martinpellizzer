Polypodium taxifolium var. fragillimum

Polypodium taxifolium var. fragillimum, commonly known as the fragile maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern native to eastern Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a light green color and a feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
