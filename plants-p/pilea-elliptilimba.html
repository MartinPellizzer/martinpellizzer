Pilea elliptilimba

Pilea elliptilimba, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its easy care requirements and attractive foliage.

Pilea elliptilimba thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.
