Pollinia contorta

Pollinia contorta, commonly known as the twisted pollinia orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species characterized by its unique floral structure.

The plant produces pollinia, which are compact masses of pollen, typically found in orchids, and these are adapted for specific pollination mechanisms. It is native to certain regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The botanical features of Pollinia contorta include its intricate floral morphology and specialized reproductive structures.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often of interest to botanists and conservationists.
