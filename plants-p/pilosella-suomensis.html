Pilosella suomensis

Pilosella suomensis, commonly known as the Finnish hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette.

Pilosella suomensis is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.
