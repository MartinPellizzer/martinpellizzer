Pleurothallis flaveola

Pleurothallis flaveola, commonly known as the yellow pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

The orchid has a relatively short flowering period, making it a rare and sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
