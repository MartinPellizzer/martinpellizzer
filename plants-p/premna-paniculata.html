Premna paniculata

Premna paniculata, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or Indian nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Verbenaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Australia, and the Pacific Islands. Its fruits are small, dry nuts enclosed in a fleshy calyx.

Premna paniculata is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities in landscaping.
