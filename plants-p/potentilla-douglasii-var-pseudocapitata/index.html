Potentilla Douglasii Var. Pseudocapitata

Potentilla douglasii var. pseudocapitata is a unique variant of the evergreen shrub native to the western united states. it thrives in rocky and coastal areas where it forms dense, low-growing clusters. this plant is known for its yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. it is often mistaken for other potentilla species due to its similar appearance. despite its hardy nature, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish.
