Pittosporum ellipticum

Pittosporum ellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, elliptic-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Pittosporum ellipticum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
