Polemonium caeruleum var. yezoense

Polemonium caeruleum var. yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and woodland settings.
