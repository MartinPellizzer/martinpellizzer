Poa cordemoyi

Poa cordemoyi, commonly known as the Cordemoy's bentgrass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments, often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its flowering spikes are small and clustered, producing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Poa cordemoyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its fine texture and adaptability.
