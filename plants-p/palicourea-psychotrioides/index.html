Palicourea Psychotrioides

Psychotria-like plant, scientifically known as Palicourea psychotrioides, is a fascinating member of the Rubiaceae family. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant is often mistaken for its more famous relative, Psychotria. Despite its misleading name, Palicourea psychotrioides is not related to the psychoactive species of Psychotria. It is characterized by its striking foliage and unique floral structure. This plant holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
