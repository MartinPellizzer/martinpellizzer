Poa violacea var. argaea

Poa violacea var. argaea, commonly known as the purple grass or violet grass, is a variant of the Poa violacea species.

It is a perennial grass native to parts of Europe and Asia, typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, dark green to purple-colored leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in cooler, humid climates.
