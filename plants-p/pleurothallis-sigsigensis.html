Pleurothallis sigsigensis

Pleurothallis sigsigensis, commonly known as the Sigsig orchid, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical rainforest environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Pleurothallis sigsigensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
