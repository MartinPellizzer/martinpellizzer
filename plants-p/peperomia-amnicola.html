Peperomia amnicola

Peperomia amnicola, commonly known as the water peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that emerge from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia amnicola is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.
