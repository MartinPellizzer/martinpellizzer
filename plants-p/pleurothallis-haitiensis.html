Pleurothallis haitiensis

Pleurothallis haitiensis, commonly known as the Haitian pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Haiti.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
