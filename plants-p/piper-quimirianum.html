Piper quimirianum

Piper quimirianum, commonly known as the Quimirian pepper, is a climbing vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by red, peppery fruits. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
