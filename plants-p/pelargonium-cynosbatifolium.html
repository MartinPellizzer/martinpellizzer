Pelargonium cynosbatifolium

Pelargonium cynosbatifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geranium, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, serrated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and drought tolerance.
