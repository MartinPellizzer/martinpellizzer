Plectranthus bongensis

Plectranthus bongensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces spikes of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Plectranthus bongensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
