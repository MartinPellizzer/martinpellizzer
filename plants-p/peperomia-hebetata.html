Peperomia hebetata

Peperomia hebetata, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are slightly hairy and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white and green, which are typically borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia hebetata is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate temperatures.
