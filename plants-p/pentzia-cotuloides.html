Pentzia cotuloides

Pentzia cotuloides, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a low, spreading growth habit. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Pentzia cotuloides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
