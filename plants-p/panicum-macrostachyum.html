Panicum macrostachyum

Panicum macrostachyum, commonly known as giant panicgrass, is a tall, robust grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and large, feathery inflorescences that can reach several feet in length. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open fields and along roadsides. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it an invasive species in many areas.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in ecological systems as a forage plant for livestock.
