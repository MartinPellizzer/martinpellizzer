Potentilla recta f. moldavica

Potentilla recta f. moldavica, commonly known as the Moldavian cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant features compound leaves with five leaflets, which are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.

The flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are usually yellow or orange in color.
