Panicum hordeaceum

Panicum hordeaceum, commonly known as barley grass, is a herbaceous annual plant native to temperate regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit with slender, hollow stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Barley grass is known for its high nutritional value, containing chlorophyll, vitamins, and minerals.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a health supplement due to its antioxidant properties.
