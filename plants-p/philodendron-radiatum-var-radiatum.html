Philodendron radiatum var. radiatum

Philodendron radiatum var. radiatum, commonly known as the Radiatum Philodendron, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. The plant produces striking, white, spurred flowers that are typically hidden within leaf sheaths. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
