Palmijuncus arborescens

Palmijuncus arborescens, commonly known as the tree palm, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and parks around the world.
