Pleurothyrium reflexum

Pleurothyrium reflexum, commonly known as the Brazilian senna, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, woody pods, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
