Phaseolus firmulus

Phaseolus firmulus, commonly known as the common scarlet runner bean, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or red flowers, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical pods. Its seeds are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor.

Phaseolus firmulus is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and culinary uses.
