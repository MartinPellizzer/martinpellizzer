Peperomia puberulibacca var. atricha

Peperomia puberulibacca var. atricha, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a distinctive velvety texture, often tinged with purple or reddish hues on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
