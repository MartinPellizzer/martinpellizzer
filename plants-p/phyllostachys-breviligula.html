Phyllostachys breviligula

Phyllostachys breviligula, commonly known as the dwarf bamboo, is a species of grass in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, often curved culms. It is a clump-forming bamboo that spreads slowly through rhizomes, making it suitable for small garden spaces.

Phyllostachys breviligula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a potted plant.
