Poecilocnemis multiflora

Poecilocnemis multiflora, commonly known as the multibract grasshopper plant, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often adorned with bract-like structures, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and stream banks.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
