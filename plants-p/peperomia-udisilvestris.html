Peperomia udisilvestris

Peperomia udisilvestris, commonly known as the forest peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features small, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces delicate, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia udisilvestris is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
