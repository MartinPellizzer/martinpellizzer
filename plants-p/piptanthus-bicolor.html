Piptanthus bicolor

Piptanthus bicolor, commonly known as the Chinese peashrub, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine and for ornamental purposes.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
