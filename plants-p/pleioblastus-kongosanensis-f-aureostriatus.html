Pleioblastus kongosanensis f. aureostriatus

Pleioblastus kongosanensis f. aureostriatus, commonly known as the golden-striped bamboo, is a variant of the Korean bamboo species.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow stripes on the culms, which contrast with its green base. This ornamental plant is native to South Korea and thrives in temperate climates. The foliage is usually dense and lush, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

It is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in both garden beds and containers.
