Peperomia rhomboidea

Peperomia rhomboidea, commonly known as the rhomboid peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or rhomboid in shape, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters, often during the spring or summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
