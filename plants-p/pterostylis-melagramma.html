Pterostylis melagramma

Pterostylis melagramma, commonly known as the Melagramma hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, hooded labellum that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, spurred shape.

Pterostylis melagramma is a small, delicate orchid that plays an important role in its ecosystem as a food source for various pollinators.
