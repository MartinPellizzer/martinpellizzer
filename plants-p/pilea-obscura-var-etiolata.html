Pilea obscura var. etiolata

Pilea obscura var. etiolata, commonly known as the Shiny Cabbage Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are slightly curled and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
