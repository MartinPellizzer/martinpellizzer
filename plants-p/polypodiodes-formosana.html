Polypodiodes formosana

Polypodiodes formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan polypody, is a fern native to eastern Asia, particularly Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The spores are released from small, clustered sporangia on the underside of the fronds.
