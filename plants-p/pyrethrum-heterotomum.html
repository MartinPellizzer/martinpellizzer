Pyrethrum heterotomum

Pyrethrum heterotomum, commonly known as the variable pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. The plant produces small, elongated seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Pyrethrum heterotomum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential in natural pest control.
