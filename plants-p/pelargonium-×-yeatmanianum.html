Pelargonium × yeatmanianum

Pelargonium × yeatmanianum, commonly known as the Yeatman's geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically has hairy, lobed leaves that are often aromatic when crushed. It is a popular ornamental plant, favored for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Native to South Africa, it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardens and containers.
