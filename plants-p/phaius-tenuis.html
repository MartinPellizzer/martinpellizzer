Phaius tenuis

Phaius tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow or greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Phaius tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
