Polythecium cochinchinense

Polythecium cochinchinense, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip petal. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical structure of Polythecium cochinchinense includes a sympodial growth habit and pseudo-bulbs that store water and nutrients.
