Phlox Speciosa Subsp. Occidentalis

Phlox speciosa subsp. occidentalis is a striking native plant found primarily in the western united states. it is known for its vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. this subspecies thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun conditions. it is often used in native gardens for its beauty and ecological benefits. its presence supports local pollinators and adds color to natural landscapes.
