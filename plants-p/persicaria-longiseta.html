Persicaria longiseta

Persicaria longiseta, commonly known as slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color and are covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge and a hairy surface.

Persicaria longiseta is typically found in moist, disturbed habitats such as wetlands, stream banks, and roadside ditches.
