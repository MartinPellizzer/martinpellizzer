Podocarpus argotaenia

Podocarpus argotaenia, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp, is a evergreen coniferous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like structures. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters in its natural habitat. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in horticulture for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The species is also valued for its timber, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
