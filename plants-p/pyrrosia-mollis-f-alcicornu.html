Pyrrosia mollis f. alcicornu

Pyrrosia mollis f. alcicornu, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rhizomes and delicate, lanceolate fronds that are usually less than 30 cm in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive narrow, elongated shape. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens and terrariums.
