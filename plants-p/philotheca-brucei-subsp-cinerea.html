Philotheca brucei subsp. cinerea

Philotheca brucei subsp.

cinerea, commonly known as the grey-leafed philotheca, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or heathland environments.
