Pittosporum simsonii

Pittosporum simsonii, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn, and it produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in spring. The plant is characterized by its smooth, often reddish-barked trunk and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Pittosporum simsonii is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
