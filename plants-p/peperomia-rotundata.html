Peperomia rotundata

Peperomia rotundata, commonly known as the Roundleaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, rounded leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
