Pleurospermum nanum

Pleurospermum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf pleurospermum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically features wiry stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.
