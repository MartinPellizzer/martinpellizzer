Psoralea sinuata

Psoralea sinuata, commonly known as the wavy psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to drought conditions. Its seed pods are elongated and often contain several small, hard seeds.
