Pericallis lanata

Pericallis lanata, commonly known as the woolly pericallis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. Pericallis lanata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a favored plant among horticulturists.
