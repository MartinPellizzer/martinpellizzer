Plantago lindeniana

Plantago lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindena plantain, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional remedies for digestive and respiratory ailments.
