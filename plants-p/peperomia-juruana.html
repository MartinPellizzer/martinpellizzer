Peperomia juruana

Peperomia juruana, commonly known as the Juruana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, reddish-brown spathes that house its minute, inconspicuous flowers. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and thrives in humid, well-draining soil.

Peperomia juruana is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
