Picea jezoensis subsp. jezoensis

Picea jezoensis subsp.

jezoensis, commonly known as the Japanese white pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan. It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves that are blue-green in color.

The tree produces small, woody cones that mature to a reddish-brown hue. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
