Pharbitis nil var. diversifolia

Pharbitis nil var. diversifolia, commonly known as the Chinese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is a climbing vine with tendrils that allow it to ascend supports and structures. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
