Polygonum pallens

Polygonum pallens, commonly known as the pale polygonum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Polygonum pallens is often found in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.
