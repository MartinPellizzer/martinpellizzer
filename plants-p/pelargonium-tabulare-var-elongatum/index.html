Pelargonium Tabulare Var. Elongatum

Pelargonium tabulare var. elongatum is a unique cultivar of the geranium family known for its striking, elongated leaves and vibrant flowers. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts. Its foliage often displays shades of green, red, and purple, adding visual interest to any space. With proper care, this variety can flourish and provide beauty throughout the growing season.
