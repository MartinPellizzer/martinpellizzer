Peperomia schomburgkii

Peperomia schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a dark green to reddish hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia schomburgkii is prized for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
