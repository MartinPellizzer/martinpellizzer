Potentilla baliemensis

Potentilla baliemensis, commonly known as the Bali cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the island of Bali in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. Potentilla baliemensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in moist, shaded environments in its native habitat.
