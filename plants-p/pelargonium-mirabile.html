Pelargonium mirabile

Pelargonium mirabile, commonly known as the Christmas star or star geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often appearing in winter. The plant has velvety, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Pelargonium mirabile is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
