Pelargonium iocastum

Pelargonium iocastum, commonly known as the Iocastum geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Pelargonium iocastum is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
