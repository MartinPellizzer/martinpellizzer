Polystachya ottoniana var. confusa

Polystachya ottoniana var. confusa, commonly known as the cloudless orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
