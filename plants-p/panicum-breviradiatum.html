Panicum breviradiatum

Panicum breviradiatum, commonly known as little barley grass, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum breviradiatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
