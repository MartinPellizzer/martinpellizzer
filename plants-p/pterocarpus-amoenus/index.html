Pterocarpus Amoenus

Pterocarpus amoenus is a striking tropical tree known for its vibrant, flame-like flowers and valuable timber. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests. The tree's unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks. Its wood is prized for its durability and rich color, making it sought after in the furniture industry. Despite its beauty and utility, Pterocarpus amoenus faces threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
