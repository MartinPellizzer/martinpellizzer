Polygala variabilis f. leucanthema

Polygala variabilis f. leucanthema, commonly known as the white-flowered milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The leaves are usually alternate and pinnately divided, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This plant is native to regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its delicate appearance and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
