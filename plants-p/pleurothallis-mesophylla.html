Pleurothallis mesophylla

Pleurothallis mesophylla, commonly known as the mesophyll pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a dense rosette, which helps in conserving moisture.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
