Peperomia wurdackii

Peperomia wurdackii, commonly known as the Wurdack's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with prominent veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are typically followed by red or purple berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia wurdackii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
