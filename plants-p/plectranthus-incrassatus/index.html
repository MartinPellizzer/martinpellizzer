Plectranthus Incrassatus

Plectranthus incrassatus is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. Known for its velvety leaves and vibrant purple flowers, it adds a touch of exotic beauty to any garden. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice among gardeners. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it ideal for containers or rock gardens. Despite its modest size, Plectranthus incrassatus holds significant value in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
