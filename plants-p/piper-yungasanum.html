Piper yungasanum

Piper yungasanum, commonly known as the Yungas pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Yungas region of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is valued for its unique flavor and is used in traditional Andean cuisine and herbal remedies.
