Peperomia eekana

Peperomia eekana, commonly known as the Silver Queen Peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically a striking silver-gray in color, with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia eekana is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional fertilization to maintain its vibrant appearance.
