Polytepalum angolense

Polytepalum angolense, commonly known as the Angola star flower, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

The botanical features of Polytepalum angolense make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
