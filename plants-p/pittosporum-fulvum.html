Pittosporum fulvum

Pittosporum fulvum, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that turn a striking golden-yellow in the autumn. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its fruit is a dark blue berry that contains several seeds.

Pittosporum fulvum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
