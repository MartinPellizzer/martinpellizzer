Phaca longicaulis

Phaca longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed phaca, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

Phaca longicaulis is typically found in grassy areas and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.
