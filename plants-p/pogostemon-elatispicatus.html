Pogostemon elatispicatus

Pogostemon elatispicatus, commonly known as the slender pogostemon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found growing in moist soils along stream banks or in dense undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
