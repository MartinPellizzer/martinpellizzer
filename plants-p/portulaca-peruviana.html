Portulaca peruviana

Portulaca peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian rockcress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and rocky environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its thick cuticle and extensive root system.

Portulaca peruviana is often found in coastal areas and dry mountain slopes.
