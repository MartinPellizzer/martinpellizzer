Phyllanthus ornatus

Phyllanthus ornatus, commonly known as the sacred lotus or Jeweled plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is commonly found in gardens and greenhouses.

Phyllanthus ornatus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
