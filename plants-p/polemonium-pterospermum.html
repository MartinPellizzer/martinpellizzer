Polemonium pterospermum

Polemonium pterospermum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces striking clusters of bell-shaped, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, rich soils and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
