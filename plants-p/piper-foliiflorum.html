Piper foliiflorum

Piper foliiflorum, commonly known as the leaf-flowered pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that matures to a dark purple color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
