Pouzolzia cymosa

Pouzolzia cymosa, commonly known as the clustered pouzolzia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pouzolzia cymosa is known for its adaptability to various soil types and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
