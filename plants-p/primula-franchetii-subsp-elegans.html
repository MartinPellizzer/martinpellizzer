Primula franchetii subsp. elegans

Primula franchetii subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to China. It features upright, hairy stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

The leaves are broad, ovate, and often covered in a fine layer of white wool. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
