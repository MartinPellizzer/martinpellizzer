Pilosella chaunadenia

Pilosella chaunadenia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and narrow, linear leaves. Native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, it thrives in dry, open habitats. The plant produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by wind.
