Plerandra veitchii

Plerandra veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's arum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and dramatic, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other well-known ornamental plants such as calla lilies and peace lilies. Plerandra veitchii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
