Praxelis splettii

Praxelis splettii, commonly known as the slender praxelis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender stems and small, tubular flowers that are usually pale yellow to orange in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Praxelis splettii is known for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
