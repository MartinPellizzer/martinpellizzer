Peperomia punctulatissima

Peperomia punctulatissima, commonly known as the spotted peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive speckled or dotted pattern that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, typically emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts.
