Piper celatipetiolum

Piper celatipetiolum, commonly known as the hidden flower pepper, is a rare and lesser-known species of flowering plant in the Piper genus.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often concealed within its foliage, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface.

While not widely cultivated, it is valued in some traditional medicinal practices for its purported health benefits.
