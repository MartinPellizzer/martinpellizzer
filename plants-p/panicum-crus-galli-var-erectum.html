Panicum crus-galli var. erectum

Panicum crus-galli var. erectum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish in color. The plant typically thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a tendency to form clumps or spread through rhizomes.

This species is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
