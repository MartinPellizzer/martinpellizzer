Pelargonium × scottii

Pelargonium × scottii, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a strong scent when touched. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with rounded leaves that can vary in color from green to reddish or purple. It produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, often in clusters.

Pelargonium × scottii is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant and is also used in aromatherapy and as a natural insect repellent.
