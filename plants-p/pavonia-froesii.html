Pavonia froesii

Pavonia froesii, commonly known as the Brazilian painted fern, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble delicate fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overshadowed by the showy bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.
