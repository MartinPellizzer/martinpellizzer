Piper kuntzei

Piper kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by round, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly pubescent surface.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
