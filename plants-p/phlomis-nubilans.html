Phlomis nubilans

Phlomis nubilans, commonly known as cloud phlomis, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, erect stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, covered with soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flower clusters in late spring to early summer, which are borne on long, branched stalks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
