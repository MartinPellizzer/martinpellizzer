Protea abyssinica var. frommii

Protea abyssinica var. frommii, commonly known as the Fromm's protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features a woody stem with dense, leathery leaves that are typically green and glossy. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a unique structure that resembles a cluster of small, rounded blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant and long-lasting floral display.
