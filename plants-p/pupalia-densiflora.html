Pupalia densiflora

Pupalia densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower pug, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
