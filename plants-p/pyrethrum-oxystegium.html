Pyrethrum oxystegium

Pyrethrum oxystegium, commonly known as the narrowleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, cylindrical flower heads that contain numerous tiny, thread-like flowers, known as florets. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Pyrethrum oxystegium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
