Plectranthus volkensianus

Plectranthus volkensianus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flower colors, which range from purple to pink and white.
