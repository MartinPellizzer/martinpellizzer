Peperomia grandifolia

Peperomia grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia grandifolia is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its shape.
