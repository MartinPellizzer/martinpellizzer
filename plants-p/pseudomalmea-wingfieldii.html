Pseudomalmea wingfieldii

Pseudomalmea wingfieldii, commonly known as the Cape fynbos plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, typical of its fynbos habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
