Plectranthus laxiflorus var. genuinus

Plectranthus laxiflorus var. genuinus, commonly known as the narrow-flowered plectranthus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers are usually purple or violet, and it blooms during the warmer months.

The variety genuinus is distinguished by its more compact growth habit and narrower leaves compared to the typical form.
