Polypodium trichiatum

Polypodium trichiatum, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a rugged appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The leaves are typically bipinnate, with a complex arrangement of pinnae and pinnules.

It is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in forested areas.
