Paphiopedilum sandyanum

Paphiopedilum sandyanum, commonly known as the Sandyanum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a single, fleshy pseudobulb. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, often with a waxy texture, and are arranged in two rows.

This species is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
