Polystichum falcatum f. intermedium

Polystichum falcatum f. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate sword fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features a robust rhizome system that supports its growth in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental value.

This fern is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
