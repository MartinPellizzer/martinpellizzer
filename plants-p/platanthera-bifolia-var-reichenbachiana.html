Platanthera bifolia var. reichenbachiana

Platanthera bifolia var. reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's lady's tresses, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and long, spiraled inflorescences. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance. This variety is often found in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and woodlands.

It is considered a rare and protected species in many regions due to habitat loss and environmental changes.
