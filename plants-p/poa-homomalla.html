Poa homomalla

Poa homomalla, commonly known as the smooth meadow grass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open grasslands and along the edges of forests. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a smooth texture.

Poa homomalla is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in moist, temperate environments.
