Prostanthera violacea

Prostanthera violacea, commonly known as violet honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, fragrant violet-blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Prostanthera violacea is typically found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.
