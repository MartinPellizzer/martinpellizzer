Phoebe betazensis

Phoebe betazensis, commonly known as the Betazan Phoebe, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green, leathery leaves that have a fragrant aroma. It produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits.

Phoebe betazensis is often found in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests and undergrowth areas.
