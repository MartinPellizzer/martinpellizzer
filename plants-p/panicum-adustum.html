Panicum adustum

Panicum adustum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, reddish-bracted inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along waterways. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum adustum is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
