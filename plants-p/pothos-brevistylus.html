Pothos brevistylus

Pothos brevistylus, commonly known as the short-styled pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, followed by red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.

Pothos brevistylus is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to purify indoor air.
