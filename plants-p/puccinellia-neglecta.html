Puccinellia neglecta

Puccinellia neglecta, commonly known as slender needlegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as sandy soils and disturbed areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, and it is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Puccinellia neglecta is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
