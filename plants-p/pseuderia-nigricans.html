Pseuderia nigricans

Pseuderia nigricans, commonly known as the black-streaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within forest understories. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white with dark purple streaks, and are clustered in a loose inflorescence.

Pseuderia nigricans is noted for its delicate appearance and is often collected by orchid enthusiasts.
