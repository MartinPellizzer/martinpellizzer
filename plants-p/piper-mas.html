Piper mas

Piper mas, commonly known as the giant betel vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy berries. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
