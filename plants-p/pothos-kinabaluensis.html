Pothos kinabaluensis

Pothos kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Kinabalu pothos, is a species of flowering plant native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking white flowers. The plant is often found growing in shaded, humid environments within the rainforest canopy. It has a climbing habit and can reach lengths of several meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sought-after plant among horticulturists.
