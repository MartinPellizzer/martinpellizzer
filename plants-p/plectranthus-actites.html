Plectranthus actites

Plectranthus actites, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Plectranthus actites is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental flowers and compact growth habit.
