Peperomia ernstiana

Peperomia ernstiana, commonly known as the Ernst's peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia ernstiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
