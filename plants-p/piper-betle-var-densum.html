Piper betle var. densum

Piper betle var. densum, commonly known as dark betel leaf, is a variant of the betel pepper plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional practices and culinary applications. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family and is closely related to the species used in betel quid preparation. Its leaves contain alkaloids and essential oils, contributing to its aromatic and medicinal properties.

The dense foliage and compact growth habit make it suitable for both ornamental and functional cultivation.
