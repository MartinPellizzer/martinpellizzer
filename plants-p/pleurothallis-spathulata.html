Pleurothallis spathulata

Pleurothallis spathulata, commonly known as the Spatulate Pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lanceolate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
