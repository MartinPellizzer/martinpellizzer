Phyllostachys heterocycla f. taokiang

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. taokiang, commonly known as the Taokiang bamboo, is a fast-growing clumping bamboo native to China.

It features slender, green culms with a smooth, sometimes slightly ridged surface. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green color that adds ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both garden and landscape use.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability in various climates.
