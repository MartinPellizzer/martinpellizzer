Phreatia neocaledonica

Phreatia neocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia phreatia, is a species of orchid native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a cluster of fleshy, oval-shaped pseudobulbs. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers are small and clustered, usually in a spike, with a distinctive white or pale green coloration.
