Poterium mauretanicum

Poterium mauretanicum, commonly known as the Moroccan spurge, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers.
