Pharnaceum brevicaule

Pharnaceum brevicaule, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pharnaceum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Pharnaceum brevicaule is adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in well-drained soils and cool, moist conditions.
