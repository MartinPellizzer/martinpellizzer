Phanera polyantha

Phanera polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.
