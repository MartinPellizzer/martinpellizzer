Phyllanthodendron yunnanense

Phyllanthodendron yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan phyllanthodendron, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and often elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in cyathia, a feature common in the Euphorbiaceae family. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.
