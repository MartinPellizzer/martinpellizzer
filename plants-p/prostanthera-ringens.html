Prostanthera ringens

Prostanthera ringens, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which attract nectar-feeding insects. Prostanthera ringens is often found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its foliage is fragrant and has been used traditionally by Indigenous Australians for various purposes.
