Prasium macrophyllum

Prasium macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a large aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant has a complex root system that helps stabilize sediments in water bodies. Its fronds are divided into multiple segments, allowing for efficient light absorption in aquatic environments.

Prasium macrophyllum plays a vital role in providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
