Pilocereus bradei

Pilocereus bradei, commonly known as the brade's night bloomer, is a cacti species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces large, showy, white flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and nocturnal blooming habit make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
