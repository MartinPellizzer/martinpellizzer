Philodendron bernardopazii

Philodendron bernardopazii, commonly known as the Bernardopazii philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, elongated bracts that are usually a vibrant red or orange, which serve as a visual cue for pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its unique foliage and ornamental bracts make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
