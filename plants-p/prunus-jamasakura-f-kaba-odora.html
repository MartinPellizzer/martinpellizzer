Prunus jamasakura f. kaba-odora

Prunus jamasakura f. kaba-odora, commonly known as the Kaba-odora cherry, is a rare and fragrant variant of the Japanese cherry.

It is characterized by its small, pale pink flowers that emit a subtle, sweet aroma. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the delicate blossoms.

The Kaba-odora cherry is particularly noted for its unique fragrance, making it a prized specimen in botanical gardens and private collections.
