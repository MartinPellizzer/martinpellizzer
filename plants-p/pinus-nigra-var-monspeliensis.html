Pinus nigra var. monspeliensis

Pinus nigra var. monspeliensis, commonly known as the Monspeliensis pine, is a variety of the black pine native to southern Europe.

It is a medium-sized evergreen conifer with long, needle-like leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces cones that are cylindrical and often remain on the tree for several years. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
