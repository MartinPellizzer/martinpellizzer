Parodia multicostata var. lau-multicostata

Parodia multicostata var. lau-multicostata, commonly known as the Lauder's Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure.

It features short, spiny areoles and produces pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant is native to Mexico and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size.

Its unique shape and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
