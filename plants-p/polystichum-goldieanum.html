Polystichum goldieanum

Polystichum goldieanum, commonly known as the Goldie's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, clustered fronds that form a compact rosette. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dark green color with a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
