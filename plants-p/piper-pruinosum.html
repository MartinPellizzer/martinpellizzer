Piper pruinosum

Piper pruinosum, commonly known as the glossy pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy layer. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is popular in traditional medicine and as a decorative element in gardens.
