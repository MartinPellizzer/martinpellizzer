Poikilospermum azureum

Poikilospermum azureum, commonly known as the Azure Sperm Plant, is a unique species belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage, which is due to the presence of a specific type of chlorophyll. The plant exhibits a distinctive seed structure, with seeds that display a range of colors, hence its scientific name. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
