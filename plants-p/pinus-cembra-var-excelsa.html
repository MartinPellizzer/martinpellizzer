Pinus cembra var. excelsa

Pinus cembra var. excelsa, commonly known as the alpine white pine, is a variant of the Swiss stone pine.

It is a tall, slender conifer native to the high mountain regions of Europe. This variety is characterized by its long, needle-like leaves and distinctive cone structure. It thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.

The plant plays an important ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
