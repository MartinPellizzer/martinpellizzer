Podochilus lobatipetalus

Podochilus lobatipetalus, commonly known as the broad-petaled orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a single, erect stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are characterized by their broad, spreading petals and a distinctive, elongated lip.

Podochilus lobatipetalus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
