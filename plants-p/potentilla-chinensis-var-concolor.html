Potentilla chinensis var. concolor

Potentilla chinensis var. concolor, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with five oval leaflets, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The species is also cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
