Pittosporum microphyllum

Pittosporum microphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often glossy and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum microphyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
