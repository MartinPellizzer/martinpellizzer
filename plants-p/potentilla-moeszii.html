Potentilla moeszii

Potentilla moeszii, commonly known as the Tibetan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are usually divided into five leaflets. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to cold, dry climates.

Potentilla moeszii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
