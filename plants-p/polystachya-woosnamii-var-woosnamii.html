Polystachya woosnamii var. woosnamii

Polystachya woosnamii var. woosnamii, commonly known as the Woonam's bamboo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are long and narrow, arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
