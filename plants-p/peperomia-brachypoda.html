Peperomia brachypoda

Peperomia brachypoda, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia brachypoda is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
