Pharnaceum rubens

Pharnaceum rubens, commonly known as the red flame fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features intricate, feather-like fronds that display a vibrant red hue, especially under direct sunlight. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires high humidity to flourish. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded outdoor spaces.

However, it is sensitive to cold temperatures and should be protected from frost.
