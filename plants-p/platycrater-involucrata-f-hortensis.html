Platycrater involucrata f. hortensis

Platycrater involucrata f. hortensis, commonly known as the garden platycrater, is a variant of the Chinese honeysuckle.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and is native to China. Its botanical features include yellowish-orange stamens and a distinctive, tubular corolla.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
