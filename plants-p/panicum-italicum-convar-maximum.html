Panicum italicum convar. maximum

Panicum italicum convar. maximum, commonly known as Italian grass or tall panicum, is a tall, perennial grass native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces feathery, panicle-shaped inflorescences that are typically golden or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
