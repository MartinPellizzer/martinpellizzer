Poa ariguensis

Poa ariguensis, commonly known as the Ariguensian grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile, where it thrives in temperate climates. This perennial grass is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Poa ariguensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in its native environment.
