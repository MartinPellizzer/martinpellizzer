Parasyncalathium

Parasyncalathium, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the cloud forests of South America.

The plant has a complex root system that helps it absorb moisture from the humid, nutrient-poor soil. Its leaves are covered in a waxy cuticle, which reduces water loss and allows it to thrive in shaded environments.
