Peperomia acreana

Peperomia acreana, commonly known as the Brazilian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia acreana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
