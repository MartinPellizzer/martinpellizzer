Primulina obtusidentata var. obtusidentata

Primulina obtusidentata var. obtusidentata, commonly known as the broad-toothed primulina, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to form dense foliage and produce consistent blooms under proper care.
