Panicum taitense

Panicum taitense, commonly known as the Taiti grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are often bright yellow or orange in color. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum taitense is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ability to provide habitat for local wildlife.
