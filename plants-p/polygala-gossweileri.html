Polygala gossweileri

Polygala gossweileri, commonly known as the blue bottle or fairy bottle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bottle-shaped seed pods that are often blue or purple in color. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.
