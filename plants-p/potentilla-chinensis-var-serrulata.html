Potentilla chinensis var. serrulata

Potentilla chinensis var. serrulata, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant has serrated leaf margins, which give it its specific variety name, serrulata. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
