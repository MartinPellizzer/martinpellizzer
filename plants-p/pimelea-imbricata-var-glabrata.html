Pimelea imbricata var. glabrata

Pimelea imbricata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth pimelea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are generally smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy environments.
