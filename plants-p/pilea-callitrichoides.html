Pilea callitrichoides

Pilea callitrichoides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings adds to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
