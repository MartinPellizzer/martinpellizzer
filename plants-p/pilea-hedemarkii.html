Pilea hedemarkii

Pilea hedemarkii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea hedemarkii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
