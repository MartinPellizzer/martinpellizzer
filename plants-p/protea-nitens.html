Protea nitens

Protea nitens, commonly known as the white protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, white, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, with narrow, leathery leaves.

The protea is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and showy blooms.
