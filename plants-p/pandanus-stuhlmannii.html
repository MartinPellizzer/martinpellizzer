Pandanus stuhlmannii

Pandanus stuhlmannii, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, branched inflorescences that bear small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as thatching material.

The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and wind-prone environments.
