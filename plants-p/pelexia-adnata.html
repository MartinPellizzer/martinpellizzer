Pelexia adnata

Pelexia adnata, commonly known as the smooth pelexia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Pelexia adnata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
