Pilea rivularis var. stipulata

Pilea rivularis var. stipulata, commonly known as the silver shield plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with heart-shaped, waxy leaves that have a distinctive silver or white margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden beneath the foliage. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
