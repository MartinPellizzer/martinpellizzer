Psiadia nigrescens

Psiadia nigrescens, commonly known as the blackening psiadia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Psiadia nigrescens is noted for its ability to thrive in humid environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
