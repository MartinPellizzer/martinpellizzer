Piper flaviflorum

Piper flaviflorum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and medicinal research.
