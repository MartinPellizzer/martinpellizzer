Populus shanxiensis

Populus shanxiensis, commonly known as the Shanxi poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China, specifically the Shanxi province.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The tree typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, catkin-like flowers in the spring, followed by samaroid seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in local ecosystems.
