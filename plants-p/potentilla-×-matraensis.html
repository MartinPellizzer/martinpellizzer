Potentilla × matraensis

Potentilla × matraensis, commonly known as the Matra cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
