Pilea oreophila

Pilea oreophila, commonly known as the cloud forest pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes plants commonly known as nettles. This plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
