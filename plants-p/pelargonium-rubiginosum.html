Pelargonium rubiginosum

Pelargonium rubiginosum, commonly known as the spotted geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves with distinctive red or purple spots. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and seasonal blooms.
