Piper hainanense

Piper hainanense, commonly known as Hainan pepper, is a flowering plant native to southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
