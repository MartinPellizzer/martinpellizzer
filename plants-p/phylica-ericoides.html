Phylica ericoides

Phylica ericoides, commonly known as the Cape snowball, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Phylica ericoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine.
