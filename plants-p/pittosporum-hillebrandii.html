Pittosporum hillebrandii

Pittosporum hillebrandii, commonly known as the Hillebrand's pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Pittosporum hillebrandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
