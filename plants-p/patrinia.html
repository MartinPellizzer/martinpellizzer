Patrinia

Patrinia, commonly known as blueweed, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It consists of several species, many of which are native to Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas. The plants typically have square stems, opposite leaves, and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from blue to purple. They are often found in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Some species of Patrinia are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
