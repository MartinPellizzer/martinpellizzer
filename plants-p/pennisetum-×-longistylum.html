Pennisetum × longistylum

Pennisetum × longistylum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a hybrid ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce feathery, plume-like inflorescences in shades of purple and pink. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Pennisetum × longistylum is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and pollinators.
