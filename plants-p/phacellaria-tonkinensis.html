Phacellaria tonkinensis

Phacellaria tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese phacellaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a creeping stem with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance.
