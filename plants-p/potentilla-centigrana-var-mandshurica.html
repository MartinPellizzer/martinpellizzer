Potentilla centigrana var. mandshurica

Potentilla centigrana var. mandshurica, commonly known as the Manchurian cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. The plant features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in native gardens.
