Pelargonium × rigescens

Pelargonium × rigescens, commonly known as the upright geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Pelargonium × rigescens thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and relatively easy to care for in a variety of garden settings.
