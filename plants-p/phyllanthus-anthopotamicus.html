Phyllanthus anthopotamicus

Phyllanthus anthopotamicus, commonly known as the Brazilian false sandthorn, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Phyllanthus anthopotamicus is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
