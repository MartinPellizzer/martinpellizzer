Potentilla lateriflora

Potentilla lateriflora, commonly known as the side-flowered cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Potentilla lateriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
