Prunus × donarium var. glabra

Prunus × donarium var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth cherry, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows as a deciduous shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin and turn yellow in autumn.

The fruit is a small, sweet drupe that ripens in late summer, making it both ornamental and edible.
