Paeonia × suffruticosa f. anneslei

Paeonia × suffruticosa f. anneslei, commonly known as the Anneslei peony, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant blooms.
