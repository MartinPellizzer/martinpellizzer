Plumbago zeylanica

Plumbago zeylanica, commonly known as blue mist shrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of blue-violet flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has woody stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
