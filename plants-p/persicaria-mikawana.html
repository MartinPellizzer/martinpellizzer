Persicaria mikawana

Persicaria mikawana, commonly known as Mikawana knotweed, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and flower clusters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking unique foliage.
