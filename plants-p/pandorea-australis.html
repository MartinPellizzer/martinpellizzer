Pandorea australis

Pandorea australis, commonly known as the Australian pandorea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a deciduous climbing shrub that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to tolerate drought conditions.

Pandorea australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
