Pyrola secunda var. pumila

Pyrola secunda var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf wintergreen, is a low-growing evergreen herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States and eastern Canada.

Pyrola secunda var. pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
