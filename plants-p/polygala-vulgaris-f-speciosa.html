Polygala vulgaris f. speciosa

Polygala vulgaris f. speciosa, commonly known as the showy milkwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is found in grasslands and woodland edges. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This ornamental plant is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
