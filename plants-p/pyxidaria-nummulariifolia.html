Pyxidaria nummulariifolia

Pyxidaria nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its silvery, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive and low-maintenance plant for gardeners.
