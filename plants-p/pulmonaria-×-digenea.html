Pulmonaria × digenea

Pulmonaria × digenea, commonly known as the lungwort hybrid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded garden environments.

Pulmonaria × digenea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
