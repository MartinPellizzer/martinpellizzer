Peucedanum triternatum

Peucedanum triternatum, commonly known as the three-parted parsley, is a herbaceous plant native to Central Asia and parts of Europe.

Peucedanum triternatum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
