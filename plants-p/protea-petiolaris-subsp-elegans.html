Protea petiolaris subsp. elegans

Protea petiolaris subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family. It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous bracts that resemble petals, giving it a floral appearance.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the Western Cape region. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, and it blooms during the cooler months.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and long-lasting blooms.
