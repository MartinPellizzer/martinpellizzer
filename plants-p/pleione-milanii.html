Pleione milanii

Pleione milanii, commonly known as the Milan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species.
