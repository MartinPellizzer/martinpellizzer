Phyllanthus lativenius

Phyllanthus lativenius, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and waxy leaves. The plant typically grows in shrubland and forest understory habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It produces capsules as its fruit, containing numerous tiny seeds.
