Paphiopedilum hangianum

Paphiopedilum hangianum, commonly known as the Hangi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with a single, fleshy, leafy stem. The plant produces showy, lip-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with a deep red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Paphiopedilum hangianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
