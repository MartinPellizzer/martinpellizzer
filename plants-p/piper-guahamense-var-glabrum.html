Piper guahamense var. glabrum

Piper guahamense var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth guahama pepper, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is characterized by its glabrous (smooth) stems and leaves, which are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple or black color, containing several seeds.

This variety is native to tropical regions of South America and is often found in humid, lowland forest environments.
