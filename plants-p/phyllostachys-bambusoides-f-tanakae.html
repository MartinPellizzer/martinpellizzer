Phyllostachys bambusoides f. tanakae

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. tanakae, commonly known as the Tanaka's bamboo, is a cold-hardy ornamental grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright culms that are typically green to bluish-green in color, with a smooth, glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to yellowish flowers in late summer, adding visual interest. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adaptable to various soil types, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its graceful appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for landscape design.
