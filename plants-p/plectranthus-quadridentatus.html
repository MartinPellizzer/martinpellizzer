Plectranthus quadridentatus

Plectranthus quadridentatus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking purple-pink tubular flowers and velvety, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
