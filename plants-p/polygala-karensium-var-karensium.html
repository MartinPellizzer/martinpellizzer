Polygala karensium var. karensium

Polygala karensium var. karensium, commonly known as the Karensian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are alternate and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are usually pink or white in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
