Photinia dabeishanensis

Photinia dabeishanensis, commonly known as the Dabeishan photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Dabeishan region in Guangdong Province. The plant is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruits are small, red drupes that mature in late summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal.
