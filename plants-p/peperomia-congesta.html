Peperomia congesta

Peperomia congesta, commonly known as the dense peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, arranged in a dense, compact formation. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in inflorescences, often in shades of white or pale pink. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia congesta is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
