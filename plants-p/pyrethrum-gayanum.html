Pyrethrum gayanum

Pyrethrum gayanum, commonly known as the Gayanum pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Pyrethrum gayanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
