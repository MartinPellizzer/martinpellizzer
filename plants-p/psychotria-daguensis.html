Psychotria daguensis

Psychotria daguensis, commonly known as the Daguense Psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in the Amazon Basin. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white to greenish flowers. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Psychotria daguensis is also noted for its potential psychoactive properties due to the presence of alkaloids.
