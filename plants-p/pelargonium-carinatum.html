Pelargonium carinatum

Pelargonium carinatum, commonly known as the zigzag geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Pelargonium carinatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
