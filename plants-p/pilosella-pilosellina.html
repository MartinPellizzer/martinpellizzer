Pilosella pilosellina

Pilosella pilosellina, commonly known as the hairy groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are generally hairy and have a rough texture, giving it its common name.

Pilosella pilosellina is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed soils.
