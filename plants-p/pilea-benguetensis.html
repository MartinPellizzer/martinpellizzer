Pilea benguetensis

Pilea benguetensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are round and thick, resembling small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea benguetensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
