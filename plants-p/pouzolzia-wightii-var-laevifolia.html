Pouzolzia wightii var. laevifolia

Pouzolzia wightii var. laevifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf pouzolzia, is a shrub native to the western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
