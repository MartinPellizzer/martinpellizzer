Pilea rostellata

Pilea rostellata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care contribute to its popularity among indoor gardeners.
