Philodendron splitgerberi

Philodendron splitgerberi, commonly known as the Split-leaf Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant has a climbing habit and can be trained to grow on trellises or supports. Its foliage is typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying variegated patterns.

Philodendron splitgerberi thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
