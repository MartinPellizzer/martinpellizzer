Premna guillauminii var. serrata

Premna guillauminii var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
