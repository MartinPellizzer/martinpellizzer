Portulaca oleracea subsp. macrantha

Portulaca oleracea subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as the giant moss rose, is a succulent plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its growth habit is prostrate, forming dense mats that can spread rapidly.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
