Prunus maritima var. purpurea-magna

Prunus maritima var. purpurea-magna, commonly known as the purple magnolia, is a rare and striking variant of the sweetbay magnolia.

It is characterized by its large, showy purple flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a unique color to the landscape. The plant is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in coastal and wetland environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
