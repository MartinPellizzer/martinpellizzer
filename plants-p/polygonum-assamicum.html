Polygonum assamicum

Polygonum assamicum, commonly known as the Assam knotweed, is a flowering plant species native to the eastern Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in dense clusters. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a rough, textured surface.

Polygonum assamicum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
