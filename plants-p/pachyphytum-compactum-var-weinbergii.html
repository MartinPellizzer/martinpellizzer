Pachyphytum compactum var. weinbergii

Pachyphytum compactum var. weinbergii, commonly known as the Weinbergii Pachyphytum, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features dense, compact rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, typical of its arid habitat. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
