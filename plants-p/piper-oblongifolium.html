Piper oblongifolium

Piper oblongifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
