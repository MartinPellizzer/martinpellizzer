Philodendron sparreorum

Philodendron sparreorum, commonly known as the Sparr's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
