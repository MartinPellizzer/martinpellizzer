Pelargonium × saturatum

Pelargonium × saturatum, commonly known as the African marigold, is a hybrid geranium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, slightly hairy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × saturatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
