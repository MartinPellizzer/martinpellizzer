Panicum marginatum

Panicum marginatum, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves that are lined with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, which are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for dry, sandy soils.

Silver bluestem is often used in landscaping and restoration projects due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
