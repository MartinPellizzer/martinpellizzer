Packera macounii

Packera macounii, commonly known as Macoun's ragwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, streambanks, and forest clearings. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.
