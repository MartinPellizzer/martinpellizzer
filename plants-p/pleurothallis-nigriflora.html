Pleurothallis nigriflora

Pleurothallis nigriflora, commonly known as the black-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, dark purple to black flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis nigriflora is valued for its striking floral display and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
