Potentilla hirtella

Potentilla hirtella, commonly known as the rough potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as streambanks and wet meadows. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture.

Potentilla hirtella is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging wetland environments.
