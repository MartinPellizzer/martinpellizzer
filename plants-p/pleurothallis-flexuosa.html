Pleurothallis flexuosa

Pleurothallis flexuosa, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the inflorescence. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a spiral, giving the plant its distinctive appearance.

Pleurothallis flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
