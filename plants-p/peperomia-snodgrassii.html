Peperomia snodgrassii

Peperomia snodgrassii, commonly known as the Snodgrass peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
