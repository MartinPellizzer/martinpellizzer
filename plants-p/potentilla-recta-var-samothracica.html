Potentilla recta var. samothracica

Potentilla recta var. samothracica, commonly known as the Samothrace cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a silvery-green coloration that helps reflect sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
