Peperomia eburnea

Peperomia eburnea, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver or white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia eburnea is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
