Phyllorkis odorata

Phyllorkis odorata, commonly known as the sweet sarsaparilla, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and stems, which emit a distinct, pleasant fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth, waxy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, often in panicles, and the plant is valued for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
