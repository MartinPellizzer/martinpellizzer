Potameia crassifolia

Potameia crassifolia, commonly known as the thickleaf potameia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
