Pleurothallis sanchoi

Pleurothallis sanchoi, commonly known as the Sancho's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white to pale yellow blossoms with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Pleurothallis sanchoi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
