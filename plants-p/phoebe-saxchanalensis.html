Phoebe saxchanalensis

Phoebe saxchanalensis, commonly known as the saxchanalensis phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
