Podocoma asperrima

Podocoma asperrima, commonly known as the rough podocarp, is a shrub-like plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Podocoma asperrima is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
