Pothos dzui

Pothos dzui, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a popular houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a golden or variegated pattern. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is known for its ability to purify the air by removing toxins. It has trailing stems that make it ideal for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos dzui is easy to care for, requiring minimal water and occasional fertilization.
