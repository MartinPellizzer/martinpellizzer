Panicum leptorhachis

Panicum leptorhachis, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Panicum leptorhachis is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and tolerate various environmental conditions.
