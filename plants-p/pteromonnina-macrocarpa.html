Pteromonnina macrocarpa

Pteromonnina macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant pteromonnina, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to several meters in length. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its woody stem is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

However, it is important to note that the plant is not widely cultivated due to its specific environmental requirements.
