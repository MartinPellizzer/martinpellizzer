Pelargonium inquinans

Pelargonium inquinans, commonly known as the black geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black foliage and vibrant red or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium inquinans is often cultivated for its striking appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
