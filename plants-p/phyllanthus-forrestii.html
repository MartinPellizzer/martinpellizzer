Phyllanthus forrestii

Phyllanthus forrestii, commonly known as the Forest's spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and smooth, often hairy leaves. Phyllanthus forrestii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
