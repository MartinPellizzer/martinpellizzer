Psychilis correllii

Psychilis correllii, commonly known as the Florida redroot.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant has slender, reddish stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green to white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Psychilis correllii is often found in moist, sandy soils in wetlands and along the edges of ponds.
