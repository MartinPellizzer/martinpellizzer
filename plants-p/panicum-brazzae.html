Panicum brazzae

Panicum brazzae, commonly known as the Brazza panicgrass, is a species of grass native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough texture.

Panicum brazzae is often used in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in local ecosystems.
