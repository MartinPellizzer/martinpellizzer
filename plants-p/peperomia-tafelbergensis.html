Peperomia tafelbergensis

Peperomia tafelbergensis, commonly known as the Tafelberg peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in water retention. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, and it thrives in well-draining soil with indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
