Pisonia aculeata var. pedicellaris

Pisonia aculeata var. pedicellaris, commonly known as the slender pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, hairy fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in coastal and humid environments, often growing along shorelines or in dense undergrowth.
