Poa diaphora var. songarica

Poa diaphora var. songarica, commonly known as the Songarica grass, is a variant of the slender meadowgrass species.

It is a perennial grass native to regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Mongolia. This plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as steppes and grasslands. It has slender, cylindrical leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
