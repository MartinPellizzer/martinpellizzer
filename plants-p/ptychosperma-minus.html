Ptychosperma minus

Ptychosperma minus, commonly known as the dwarf palm, is a small, ornamental palm native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender trunk and pinnate leaves that give it a tropical appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, round fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its compact size, it is popular as a houseplant or in garden settings.
