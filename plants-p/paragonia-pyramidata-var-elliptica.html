Paragonia pyramidata var. elliptica

Paragonia pyramidata var. elliptica, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and ease of care.

The elliptical leaves and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
