Pilea celebica

Pilea celebica, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, rounded leaves that resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
