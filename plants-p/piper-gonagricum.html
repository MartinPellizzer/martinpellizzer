Piper gonagricum

Piper gonagricum, commonly known as the African black pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by black, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are also utilized for their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
