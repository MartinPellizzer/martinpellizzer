Pandanus noumeaensis

Pandanus noumeaensis, commonly known as the New Caledonia pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the island of New Caledonia and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal and lowland areas, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is considered an important component of the local flora.
