Pleonotoma exserta

Pleonotoma exserta, commonly known as the slender leaf beetle, is a species of leaf beetle belonging to the family Chrysomelidae.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender body and typically metallic green or blue coloration. The plant it feeds on is primarily species of the genus Eupatorium, which are common in moist, open habitats. Its larvae are often found on the underside of leaves, where they feed on plant tissues.

The beetle's life cycle includes several stages, with adults emerging in the summer months to continue the cycle.
