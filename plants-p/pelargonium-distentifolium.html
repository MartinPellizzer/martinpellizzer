Pelargonium distentifolium

Pelargonium distentifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and prolonged blooming period.
