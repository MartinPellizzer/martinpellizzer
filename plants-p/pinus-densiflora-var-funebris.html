Pinus densiflora var. funebris

Pinus densiflora var. funebris, commonly known as the black pine or funebre pine, is a variant of the Japanese black pine.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black bark and dense, dark green foliage. The plant is native to eastern Asia, including Japan, Korea, and China. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in coastal or mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture and landscape design.
