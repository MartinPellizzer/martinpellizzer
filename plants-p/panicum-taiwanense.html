Panicum taiwanense

Panicum taiwanense, commonly known as Taiwan panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
