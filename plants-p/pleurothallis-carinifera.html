Pleurothallis carinifera

Pleurothallis carinifera, commonly known as the ridged pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
