Polystichum jinfoshanense

Polystichum jinfoshanense, commonly known as Jinfoshan shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, dark green fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
