Pachyphytum × pachyphytoides

Pachyphytum × pachyphytoides, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its swollen, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant typically has a compact, rounded growth habit with thick, ribbed leaves that are often blue-green in color. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
