Pancovia holtzii

Pancovia holtzii, commonly known as the Holtzii pancake plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Pancovia holtzii is often cultivated in controlled environments.
