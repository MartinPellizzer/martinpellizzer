Polygonum coccineum var. asprellum

Polygonum coccineum var. asprellum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant features upright stems that are reddish in color, especially near the base, and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to reddish, appearing in late spring to early summer.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and is often used in wetland restoration projects.
