Piper alnoides

Piper alnoides, commonly known as the Indian pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and emits a strong, peppery aroma. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by dark blue berries that are edible but often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various herbal remedies.
