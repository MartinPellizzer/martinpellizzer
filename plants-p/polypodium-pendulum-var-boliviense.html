Polypodium pendulum var. boliviense

Polypodium pendulum var. boliviense, commonly known as the Bolivian fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous fronds that hang from trees in its natural habitat. The plant has a rhizome that produces both roots and spore-bearing structures. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a soft, feathery appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
