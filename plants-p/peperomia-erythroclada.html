Peperomia erythroclada

Peperomia erythroclada, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with striking red veins that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia erythroclada is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental leaves and low maintenance requirements.
