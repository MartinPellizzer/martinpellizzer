Panicum medium

Panicum medium, commonly known as dropseed, is a native grass species found in various regions of North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silky foliage that gives it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with pale or silver-tipped bracts. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies, meadows, and disturbed sites.

Panicum medium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife and soil stabilization.
