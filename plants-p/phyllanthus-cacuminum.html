Phyllanthus cacuminum

Phyllanthus cacuminum, commonly known as the African waterleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often heart-shaped leaves. The plant is noted for its potential medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
