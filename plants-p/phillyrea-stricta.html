Phillyrea stricta

Phillyrea stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round berries. Phillyrea stricta is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
