Phyteuma fistulosum

Phyteuma fistulosum, commonly known as the common phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Phyteuma fistulosum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
