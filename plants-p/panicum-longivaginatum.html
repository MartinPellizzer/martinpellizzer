Panicum longivaginatum

Panicum longivaginatum, commonly known as the long sheath grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
