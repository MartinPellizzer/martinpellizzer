Polygala azizsancarii

Polygala azizsancarii, commonly known as the Aziz Sancari's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate to cold climates. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and it produces small, bean-like fruits that contain several seeds.
