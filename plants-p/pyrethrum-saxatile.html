Pyrethrum saxatile

Pyrethrum saxatile, commonly known as the saxifrage pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, and its stems are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Pyrethrum saxatile is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in natural pest control.
