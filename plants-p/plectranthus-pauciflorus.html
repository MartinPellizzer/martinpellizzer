Plectranthus pauciflorus

Plectranthus pauciflorus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved plectranthus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
