Petrocosmea shilinensis

Petrocosmea shilinensis, commonly known as the Shilin rock orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the Shilin area of Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, showcasing its adaptability to harsh mountainous environments. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to habitat fragmentation and human encroachment, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
