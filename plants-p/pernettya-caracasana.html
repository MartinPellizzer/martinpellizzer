Pernettya caracasana

Pernettya caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas pernettya, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that are often fragrant and appear in clusters. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

Pernettya caracasana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
