Peperomia caulibarbis var. jimenesana

Peperomia caulibarbis var. jimenesana, commonly known as the Jimenez's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are usually borne on short, erect spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its unique leaf morphology and compact growth habit make it an attractive addition to terrariums and small spaces.
