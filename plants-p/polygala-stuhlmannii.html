Polygala stuhlmannii

Polygala stuhlmannii, commonly known as the Stuhlmann's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often spirally arranged leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
