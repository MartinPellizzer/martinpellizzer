Persoonia inconspicua

Persoonia inconspicua, commonly known as the slender persoonia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, upright growth habit and produces clusters of yellow, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Persoonia inconspicua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
