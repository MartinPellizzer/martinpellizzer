Pyrostria major

Pyrostria major, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish hue.

Pyrostria major thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
