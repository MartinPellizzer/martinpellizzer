Phlomoides muliensis

Phlomoides muliensis, commonly known as the Yunnan phlomoides, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.
