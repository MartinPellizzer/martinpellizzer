Persicaria odorata f. albiflora

Persicaria odorata f. albiflora, commonly known as white-flowered smartweed, is a variant of the common smartweed species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and slender stems. The leaves are typically alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its most distinguishing feature is the white or pale pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

This plant thrives in moist environments and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
