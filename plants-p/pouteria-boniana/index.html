Pouteria Boniana

Pouteria boniana is a rare and unique plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is known for its striking, large, and often fragrant fruits that attract a variety of wildlife. This species belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and ornamental value. Due to habitat loss and limited cultivation, it is considered endangered in the wild. Despite its rarity, Pouteria boniana holds significant ecological and botanical interest.
