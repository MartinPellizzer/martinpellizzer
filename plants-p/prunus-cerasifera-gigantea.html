Prunus cerasifera gigantea

Prunus cerasifera gigantea, commonly known as the giant cherry laurel, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Balkans.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that turn yellow in autumn, and it produces small, white, fragrant flowers in spring. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
