Paronychia Mauritanica

Paronychia mauritanica is a hardy succulent native to the arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East. It is known for its compact, rosette-like growth pattern and ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions. This plant features small, fleshy leaves that store water, making it well adapted to desert environments. Its striking blue or purple hues add visual interest to rocky landscapes. Despite its tough exterior, Paronychia mauritanica is a rewarding plant for gardeners who appreciate low-maintenance, resilient flora.
