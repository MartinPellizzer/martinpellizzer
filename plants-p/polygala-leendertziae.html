Polygala leendertziae

Polygala leendertziae, commonly known as the Leendertz's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a unique structure that resembles a milkweed flower.

It is often found in coastal dunes and is of interest to botanists due to its ecological significance and limited distribution.
