Peperomia rothschuhii

Peperomia rothschuhii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with silver and green hues, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters, usually in spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rothschuhii is ideal for indoor gardens due to its ability to tolerate low light and minimal watering.
