Pittosporum timorerse

Pittosporum timorerse, commonly known as the Timor pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, providing ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for coastal and tropical gardens.
