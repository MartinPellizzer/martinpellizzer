Piper zenkeri

Piper zenkeri, commonly known as Zenker's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are berries that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in local herbal remedies.
