Pimelea brevifolia var. angustifolia

Pimelea brevifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of bright red or orange flowers that resemble bottlebrushes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is typically found in heathlands and open woodlands. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
