Physurus procerus

Physurus procerus, commonly known as the slender spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by red berries, which are attractive to birds. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings.

Physurus procerus is popular as a decorative plant due to its compact size and ease of care.
