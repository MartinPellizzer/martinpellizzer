Peperomia pusilla

Peperomia pusilla, commonly known as the baby's tears peperomia, is a small, compact plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of tiny, waxy, dark green leaves that give it a lush, carpet-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in late spring or early summer. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia pusilla prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it adaptable to a variety of indoor environments.
