Pentas stolzii

Pentas stolzii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pentas stolzii is known for its long blooming season, often from spring through fall.
