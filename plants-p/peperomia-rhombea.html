Peperomia rhombea

Peperomia rhombea, commonly known as the oval peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Central and South America.

It features oval-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are rarely seen above the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rhombea is ideal for indoor gardening due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
