Pyrenaria jonquieriana

Pyrenaria jonquieriana, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Pyrenaria jonquieriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its vibrant floral display.
