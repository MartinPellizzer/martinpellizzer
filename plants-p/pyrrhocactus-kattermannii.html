Pyrrhocactus kattermannii

Pyrrhocactus kattermannii, commonly known as the Kattermann's pyrrhocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to dry conditions with its succulent structure. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, adding a vibrant touch to its desolate habitat.

Pyrrhocactus kattermannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
