Pygeum hookerianum var. borneense

Pygeum hookerianum var. borneense, commonly known as the Bornean African cherry, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the lowland rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and simple, opposite leaves. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
