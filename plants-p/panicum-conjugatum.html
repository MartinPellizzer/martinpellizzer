Panicum conjugatum

Panicum conjugatum, commonly known as the clustered panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including disturbed soils and open fields.

Panicum conjugatum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil.
