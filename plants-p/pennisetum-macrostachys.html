Pennisetum macrostachys

Pennisetum macrostachys, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features long, slender, silver-green leaves and produces feathery, pinkish-brown plumes that add visual interest throughout the growing season. This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and landscape design. It is often used in gardens for its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Pennisetum macrostachys is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
