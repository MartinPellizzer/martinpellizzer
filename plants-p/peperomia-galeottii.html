Peperomia galeottii

Peperomia galeottii, commonly known as the Cape Cod peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a suitable houseplant for indoor environments.

Peperomia galeottii is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
