Peranema cyatheoides var. luzonicum

Peranema cyatheoides var. luzonicum, commonly known as the Luzon fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.
