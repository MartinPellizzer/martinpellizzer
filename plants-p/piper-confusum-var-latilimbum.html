Piper confusum var. latilimbum

Piper confusum var. latilimbum, commonly known as the broad-leafed pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper confusum species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. The fruits are typically harvested when mature, and they have a mild, peppery flavor.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible leaves in traditional cuisines.
