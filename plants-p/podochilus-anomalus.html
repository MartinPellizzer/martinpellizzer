Podochilus anomalus

Podochilus anomalus, commonly known as the orchid spider plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are elongated and slightly fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environments.

Podochilus anomalus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
