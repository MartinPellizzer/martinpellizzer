Piper gracillimum

Piper gracillimum, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by small, round berries.

Piper gracillimum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has potential medicinal uses in traditional remedies.
