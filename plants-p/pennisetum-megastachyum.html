Pennisetum megastachyum

Pennisetum megastachyum, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces large, feathery plumes that add visual interest to landscapes. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for USDA hardiness zones 6 through 10. Its botanical characteristics include a robust root system and a clumping growth habit.

Pennisetum megastachyum is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
