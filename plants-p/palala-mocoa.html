Palala mocoa

Palala mocoa, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle purple hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Palala mocoa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardens.
