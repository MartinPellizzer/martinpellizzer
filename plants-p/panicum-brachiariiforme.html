Panicum brachiariiforme

Panicum brachiariiforme, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green to reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
