Peperomia malaccensis

Peperomia malaccensis, commonly known as the Malacca peperomia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features ovate to elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue.

Peperomia malaccensis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
