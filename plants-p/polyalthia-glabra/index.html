Polyalthia Glabra

Polyalthia glabra is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia known for its striking ornamental value. It features gracefully drooping branches and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, adding a lush visual contrast to its delicate floral display. Despite its beauty, Polyalthia glabra is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
