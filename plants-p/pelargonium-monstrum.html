Pelargonium monstrum

Pelargonium monstrum, commonly known as the giant geranium, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
