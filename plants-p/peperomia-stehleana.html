Peperomia stehleana

Peperomia stehleana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is well-suited for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
