Phaseolus coccineus subsp. polyanthus

Phaseolus coccineus subsp.

polyanthus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a tropical legume native to the Americas. It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and edible, kidney-shaped beans.

The plant typically grows as a vigorous climbing vine with trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are often bi- or tricolored, adding ornamental value to gardens.

The subspecies is cultivated for both its ornamental appeal and its nutritional beans.
