Pleurothallis praealta

Pleurothallis praealta, commonly known as the tall pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphytic orchid, typically growing on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, often branched stems and produces delicate, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Pleurothallis praealta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
