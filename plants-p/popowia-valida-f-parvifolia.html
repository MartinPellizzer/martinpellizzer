Popowia valida f. parvifolia

Popowia valida f. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved popowia, is a variant of the popowia plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and delicate floral display.
