Prunus skutchii

Prunus skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its small, sweet, edible fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it produces white or pinkish flowers in the spring.

The fruit ripens in the summer and is often used in local culinary traditions.
