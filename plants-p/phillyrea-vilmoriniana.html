Phillyrea vilmoriniana

Phillyrea vilmoriniana, commonly known as the Vilmorin's Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by yellowish fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.

Phillyrea vilmoriniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
