Pittosporum obscurinerve

Pittosporum obscurinerve, commonly known as the dark-veined pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves with dark, prominent veins that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and parks for its attractive foliage and unique fruit.
