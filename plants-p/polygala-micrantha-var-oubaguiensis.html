Polygala micrantha var. oubaguiensis

Polygala micrantha var. oubaguiensis, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
