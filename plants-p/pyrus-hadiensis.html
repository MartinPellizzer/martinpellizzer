Pyrus hadiensis

Pyrus hadiensis, commonly known as the Hadien pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America, specifically in Peru and Bolivia. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Pyrus hadiensis is considered a rare and endangered species.
