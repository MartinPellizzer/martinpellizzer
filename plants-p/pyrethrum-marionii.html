Pyrethrum marionii

Pyrethrum marionii, commonly known as the Marion pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are dispersed by the wind. Its botanical features include hairy stems and leaves with a serrated edge.

Pyrethrum marionii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
