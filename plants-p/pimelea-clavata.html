Pimelea clavata

Pimelea clavata, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky soils, preferring full sun. It is often found in the south-western region of Western Australia.

Pimelea clavata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
