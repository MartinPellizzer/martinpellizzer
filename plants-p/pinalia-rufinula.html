Pinalia rufinula

Pinalia rufinula, commonly known as the red-stemmed pinalia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Pinalia rufinula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
