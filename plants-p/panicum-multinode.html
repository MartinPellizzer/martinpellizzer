Panicum multinode

Panicum multinode, commonly known as the multinode grass, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

Due to its adaptability, it is often used in ornamental landscaping and erosion control.
