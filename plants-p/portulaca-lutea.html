Portulaca lutea

Portulaca lutea, commonly known as the yellow moss rose, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid environments. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Portulaca lutea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
