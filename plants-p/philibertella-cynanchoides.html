Philibertella cynanchoides

Philibertella cynanchoides, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features hairy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with silver, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow or orange as they mature.

Philibertella cynanchoides is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
