Persica laevis

Persica laevis, commonly known as the smooth peach.

It is a species of shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are followed by fuzzy, round fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture.

Persica laevis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
