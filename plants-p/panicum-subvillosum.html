Panicum subvillosum

Panicum subvillosum, commonly known as silky panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
