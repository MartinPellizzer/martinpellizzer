Pittosporum boninense var. boninense

Pittosporum boninense var. boninense, commonly known as the Bonin Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and are often fragrant.

The fruit is a small, dark red berry that contains several seeds.
