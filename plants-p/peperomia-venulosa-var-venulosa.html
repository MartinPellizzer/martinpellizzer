Peperomia venulosa var. venulosa

Peperomia venulosa var. venulosa, commonly known as the veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with prominent veins that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it both decorative and relatively low-maintenance.
