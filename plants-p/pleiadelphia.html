Pleiadelphia

Pleiadelphia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, silver-gray foliage. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in complex inflorescences. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish or brownish hue.
