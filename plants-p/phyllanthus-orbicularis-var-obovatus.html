Phyllanthus orbicularis var. obovatus

Phyllanthus orbicularis var. obovatus, commonly known as the round-leaf phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or nearly round, leaves that are typically a glossy green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
