Pyrethrum tenuifolium

Pyrethrum tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and agriculturally significant.
