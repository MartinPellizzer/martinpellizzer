Potentilla adscharica var. trichosepala

Potentilla adscharica var. trichosepala, commonly known as the trichosepala cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are compound, with five leaflets that are oval and serrated.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
