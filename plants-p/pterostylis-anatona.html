Pterostylis anatona

Pterostylis anatona, commonly known as the Anatom Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, leafless stem and a single, small greenish-brown flower with a distinctive, hood-like structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Pterostylis anatona is a rare and endangered species, requiring conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
