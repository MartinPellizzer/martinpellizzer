Phrynium compositum

Phrynium compositum, commonly known as the composite arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves, which are typically a vibrant green with a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in moist, shady environments.

Phrynium compositum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
