Pediocactus hermannii

Pediocactus hermannii, commonly known as Hermann's cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a short, thick stem and a crown of spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
