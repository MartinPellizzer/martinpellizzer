Panicum myrianthum

Panicum myrianthum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to Africa, South America, and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial grass that grows in a variety of habitats, including grasslands, disturbed soils, and arid regions. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, fluffy inflorescences that produce a striking purple hue. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Panicum myrianthum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
