Piper quadratilimbum

Piper quadratilimbum, commonly known as the square-leaved pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Piper quadratilimbum is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
