Polygala globulifera var. globulifera

Polygala globulifera var. globulifera, commonly known as the globular milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces distinctive, globular seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
