Polygala bonariensis

Polygala bonariensis, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The Christmas star is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens for its delicate appearance.
