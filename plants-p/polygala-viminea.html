Polygala viminea

Polygala viminea, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.
