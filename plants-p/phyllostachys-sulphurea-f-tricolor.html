Phyllostachys sulphurea f. tricolor

Phyllostachys sulphurea f. tricolor, commonly known as the yellow bamboo, is a variant of the golden bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking tricolor foliage, featuring yellow, green, and white variegation. This ornamental bamboo is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It grows as a clump-forming plant, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden design.
