Petasites speciosus

Petasites speciosus, commonly known as the showy edelweiss, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its rosette of large, silver-gray, hairy leaves and its showy inflorescence of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and well-drained soil. It is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats where it can tolerate harsh climatic conditions.

Petasites speciosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
