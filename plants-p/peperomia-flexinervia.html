Peperomia flexinervia

Peperomia flexinervia, commonly known as the veiny peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive, veined pattern that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia flexinervia is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to various indoor environments.
