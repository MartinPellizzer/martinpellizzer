Peperomia rhombeifolia

Peperomia rhombeifolia, commonly known as the diamond leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant.
