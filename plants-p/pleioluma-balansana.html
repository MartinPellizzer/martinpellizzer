Pleioluma balansana

Pleioluma balansana, commonly known as the Balansan orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often with a waxy texture.

The flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that enhances its ornamental value.
