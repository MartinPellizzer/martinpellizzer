Pimpinella tenera var. dissecta

Pimpinella tenera var. dissecta, commonly known as the dissected cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
