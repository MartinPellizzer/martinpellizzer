Paeonia yinpingmudan subsp. henanensis

Paeonia yinpingmudan subsp.

henanensis, commonly known as the Henan peony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China. It belongs to the芍药属 (Paeonia) family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with broad, heart-shaped leaves and thick, fleshy roots.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
