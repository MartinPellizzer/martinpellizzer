Piper rollingo

Piper rollingo, commonly known as the rollingo pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often require support. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green surface with a slightly serrated edge.

The small, greenish-white flowers are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.
