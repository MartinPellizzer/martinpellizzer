Polygonatum adnatum

Polygonatum adnatum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features rhizomatous stems and lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbellate clusters during the summer months. Its flowers are followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color.

Polygonatum adnatum is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
