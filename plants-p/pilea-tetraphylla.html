Pilea tetraphylla

Pilea tetraphylla, commonly known as the four-leaf pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and white, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that develop into spherical, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Pilea tetraphylla is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its shape.
