Panicum lindenii

Panicum lindenii, commonly known as the Lindberg's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spike-like clusters of flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green coloration.

Panicum lindenii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
