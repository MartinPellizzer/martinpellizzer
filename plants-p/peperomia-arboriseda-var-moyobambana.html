Peperomia arboriseda var. moyobambana

Peperomia arboriseda var. moyobambana, commonly known as the silver mitten peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It features striking, waxy, silver-white leaves with a velvety texture, often marked with dark green veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
