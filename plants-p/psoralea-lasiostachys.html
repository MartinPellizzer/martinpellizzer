Psoralea lasiostachys

Psoralea lasiostachys, commonly known as the rough-stemmed psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
