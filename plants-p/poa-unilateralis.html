Poa unilateralis

Poa unilateralis, commonly known as the single-sided grass, is a unique species of grass found primarily in the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive growth pattern, where one side of the plant develops more vigorously than the other, resulting in an asymmetrical appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that helps it blend into its natural surroundings.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, Poa unilateralis is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
