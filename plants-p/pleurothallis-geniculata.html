Pleurothallis geniculata

Pleurothallis geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, greenish-white flowers with a unique zigzag pattern on the petals. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
