Peperomia compotrix

Peperomia compotrix, commonly known as the Lemon Zinger Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia compotrix is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to purify the air.
