Piper hancei

Piper hancei, commonly known as the Hance's pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Piper hancei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
