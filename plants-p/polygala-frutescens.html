Polygala frutescens

Polygala frutescens, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Polygala frutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
