Perfonon

Perfonon, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Perfonon thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to grow successfully.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
