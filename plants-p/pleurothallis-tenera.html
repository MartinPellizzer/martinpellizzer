Pleurothallis tenera

Pleurothallis tenera, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
