Peristylus volkensianus

Peristylus volkensianus, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves and a woody stem with a rough texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Peristylus volkensianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal areas.
