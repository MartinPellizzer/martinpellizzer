Pilea taquetii

Pilea taquetii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its round, succulent leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Pilea taquetii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
