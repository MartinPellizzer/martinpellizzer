Polystachya poikilantha var. poikilantha

Polystachya poikilantha var. poikilantha, commonly known as the variable polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, and it is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
