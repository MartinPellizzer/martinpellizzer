Pelargonium × melalophon

Pelargonium × melalophon, commonly known as the melalophon geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × melalophon is known for its resilience and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
