Peperomia rhombeo-ovata

Peperomia rhombeo-ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of pink or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rhombeo-ovata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental foliage.
