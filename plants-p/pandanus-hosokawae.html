Pandanus hosokawae

Pandanus hosokawae, commonly known as the Hosokawa pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and as a decorative element. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has cultural significance in local communities.
