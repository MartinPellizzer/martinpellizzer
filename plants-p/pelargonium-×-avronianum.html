Pelargonium × avronianum

Pelargonium × avronianum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. Its foliage is often heart-shaped with a slightly serrated edge.

Pelargonium × avronianum thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
