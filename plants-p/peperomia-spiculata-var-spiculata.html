Peperomia spiculata var. spiculata

Peperomia spiculata var. spiculata, commonly known as the Spiky Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, upright rosettes of glossy, dark green leaves with spiky, serrated edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in homes and offices.
