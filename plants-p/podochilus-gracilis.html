Podochilus gracilis

Podochilus gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has thin, wiry stems and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted for capturing moisture from the air.

Podochilus gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
