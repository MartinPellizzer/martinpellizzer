Pilidiostigma rhytispermum

Pilidiostigma rhytispermum, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Poaceae family.
