Pelargonium luteopetalum

Pelargonium luteopetalum, commonly known as the yellow petal geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow petals and dark green, velvety leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium luteopetalum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
