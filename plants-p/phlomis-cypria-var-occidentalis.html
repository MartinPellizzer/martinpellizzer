Phlomis cypria var. occidentalis

Phlomis cypria var. occidentalis, commonly known as Western Cyprus phlomis, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features tall, erect stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its showy, clustered flowers are typically pale purple to pink, blooming in summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
