Poa macrocalyx var. fallax

Poa macrocalyx var. fallax, commonly known as the fallax bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and dense growth habit, making it a desirable turfgrass. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its rhizomatous and stoloniferous growth allows it to spread and form a dense sod.

It is often used in lawns and erosion control due to its durability and aesthetic appeal.
