Psiadia mollissima

Psiadia mollissima, commonly known as the silky psiadia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in dry, rocky environments and is adapted to the arid climate of its natural habitat. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Psiadia mollissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and resilience.
