Peperomia montecristana

Peperomia montecristana, commonly known as the Montecristi peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Peperomia montecristana is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
