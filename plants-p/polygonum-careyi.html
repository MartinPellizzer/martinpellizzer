Polygonum careyi

Polygonum careyi, commonly known as the Sierra Polygonum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its succulent stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Polygonum careyi is adapted to harsh environmental conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
