Pentaschistis pictigluma var. gracilis

Pentaschistis pictigluma var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender flame vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a yellow center. The plant has slender, flexible stems that allow it to climb and trail along supports or structures. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and typically ovate with a glossy surface.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
