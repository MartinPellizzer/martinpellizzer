Pultenaea vestita

Pultenaea vestita, commonly known as the silky pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family. The plant is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. Its leaves are typically alternate and pinnate, with finely divided leaflets.

Pultenaea vestita is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
