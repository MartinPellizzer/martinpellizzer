Pisonia ekmanii

Pisonia ekmanii, commonly known as the Christmas tree or the Christmas bush, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, clustered flowers.
