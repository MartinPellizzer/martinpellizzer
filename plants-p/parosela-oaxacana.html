Parosela oaxacana

Parosela oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca parosela, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Oaxaca, Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Parosela oaxacana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
