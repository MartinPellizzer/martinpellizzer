Peperomia circulifolia var. flava

Peperomia circulifolia var. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow or golden green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters, usually in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf color and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
