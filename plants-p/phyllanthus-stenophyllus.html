Phyllanthus stenophyllus

Phyllanthus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant's botanical features make it adaptable to various ecological conditions, contributing to its widespread distribution.
