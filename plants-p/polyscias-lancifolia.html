Polyscias lancifolia

Polyscias lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf polygala, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, often with a white or pale green color.

Polyscias lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
