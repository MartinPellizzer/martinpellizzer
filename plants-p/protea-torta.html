Protea torta

Protea torta, commonly known as the twisted protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, branched inflorescence that resembles a twisted tree, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to the dry conditions of its native habitat.

Protea torta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
