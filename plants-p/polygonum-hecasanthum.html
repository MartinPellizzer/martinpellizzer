Polygonum hecasanthum

Polygonum hecasanthum, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species known for their distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. Its leaves are typically alternate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and they have a slightly succulent texture.

Polygonum hecasanthum is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in shaded woodland areas.
