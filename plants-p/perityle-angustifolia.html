Perityle angustifolia

Perityle angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf perityle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. Perityle angustifolia is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
