Pilosella suivalensis

Pilosella suivalensis, commonly known as the Alpine pearly bell, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its flowers are small, white to pale yellow, and arranged in a cluster resembling a bell.

Pilosella suivalensis is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soils.
