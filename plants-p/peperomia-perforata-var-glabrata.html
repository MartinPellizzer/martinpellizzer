Peperomia perforata var. glabrata

Peperomia perforata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a deep green color, often adorned with small, rounded holes along the leaf margins. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that appear in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
