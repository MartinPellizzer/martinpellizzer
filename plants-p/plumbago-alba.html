Plumbago alba

Plumbago alba, commonly known as the blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that produces clusters of striking blue or white flowers, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a woody stem, making it suitable for garden borders or containers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Plumbago alba is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
