Prunus mahaleb var. bommii

Prunus mahaleb var. bommii, commonly known as the Bommii cherry, is a variety of the European cherry species.

It is characterized by its small, bright red berries and is native to the Balkan Peninsula. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin, and it produces fragrant white flowers in early spring.

The fruit is edible but often used more for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal.
