Pilea venosa

Pilea venosa, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with light green and white markings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Pilea venosa is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
