Philoxerus lanatus

Philoxerus lanatus, commonly known as the woolly treefern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a dense, silky white sheath that gives it its common name. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Philoxerus lanatus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
