Phaseolus circinalis

Phaseolus circinalis, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and elongated pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and vigorous vining growth habit. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is widely cultivated for both its ornamental value and its nutritional seeds.
