Philadelphus kansuensis

Philadelphus kansuensis, commonly known as the Chinese Mockorange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, grayish bark. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
