Plectranthus pleiophyllus

Plectranthus pleiophyllus, commonly known as the woolly plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its densely hairy, gray-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus pleiophyllus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
