Polygala wittei

Polygala wittei, commonly known as the Cape plume, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, pale blue or white flowers are arranged in spikes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Polygala wittei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
