Peucedanum astrantiifolium

Peucedanum astrantiifolium, commonly known as the star-leaved parsley, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its roots are often harvested for their aromatic properties and traditional medicinal uses.
