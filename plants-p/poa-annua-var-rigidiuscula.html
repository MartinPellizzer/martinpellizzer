Poa annua var. rigidiuscula

Poa annua var. rigidiuscula, commonly known as the rigid bentgrass, is a variant of the annual meadowgrass species.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial grass that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lawns, meadows, and along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
