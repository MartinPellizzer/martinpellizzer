Panzera

Panzera, commonly known as the "pomegranate tree," is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It produces vibrant red fruits with a thick, leathery rind containing numerous seeds. The plant has broad, glossy leaves and can grow up to 10 meters tall. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is prized for its tart flavor and is widely used in culinary and medicinal applications.
