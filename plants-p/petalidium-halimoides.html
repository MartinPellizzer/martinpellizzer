Petalidium halimoides

Petalidium halimoides, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has a low-growing, mat-forming habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are silver-gray in color, which helps it conserve water in arid environments.

Petalidium halimoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
