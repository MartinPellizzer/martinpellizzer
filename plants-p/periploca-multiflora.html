Periploca multiflora

Periploca multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered periploca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Periploca multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
