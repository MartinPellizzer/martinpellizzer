Peperomia reflexa var. norfolkensis

Peperomia reflexa var. norfolkensis, commonly known as the Norfolk Island peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Norfolk Island.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are slightly wavy and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in terminal clusters, often followed by reddish-brown seed pods. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a suitable houseplant for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
