Pelargonium longiflorum

Pelargonium longiflorum, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be white, yellow, or red in color. The plant has thick, aromatic leaves and thrives in warm, sunny conditions. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
