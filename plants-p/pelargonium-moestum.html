Pelargonium moestum

Pelargonium moestum, commonly known as the Cape sugarleaf, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring broad, dark green leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Pelargonium moestum is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
