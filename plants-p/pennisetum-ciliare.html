Pennisetum ciliare

Pennisetum ciliare, commonly known as silky blue oatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves and panicle-like inflorescence that produces small, blue-green flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.

Pennisetum ciliare also serves as a food source for various wildlife species.
