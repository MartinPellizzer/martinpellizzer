Primulina guizhongensis

Primulina guizhongensis, commonly known as the Guizhong primula, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
