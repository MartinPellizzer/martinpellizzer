Plectranthus elegantulus

Plectranthus elegantulus, commonly known as the silver bush or silver leaf plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically lavender or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus elegantulus is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
