Pritzelia ramentacea

Pritzelia ramentacea, commonly known as the silky sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in sandy soils with minimal water.

Pritzelia ramentacea is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
