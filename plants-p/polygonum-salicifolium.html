Polygonum salicifolium

Polygonum salicifolium, commonly known as heartleaf polygonum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Polygonum salicifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
