Panicum paniculiferum

Panicum paniculiferum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted clumps of fine, silver-green foliage and its upright, branched inflorescences that produce fluffy, white or pale pink plumes. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its delicate texture and long-lasting appearance.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract birds and beneficial insects.
