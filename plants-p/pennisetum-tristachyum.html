Pennisetum tristachyum

Pennisetum tristachyum, commonly known as purple top grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Pennisetum tristachyum is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
