Phalaenopsis tetraspis f. christiana

Phalaenopsis tetraspis f. christiana, commonly known as the Christiana's Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a velvety sheen and a subtle fragrance. This variety is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
