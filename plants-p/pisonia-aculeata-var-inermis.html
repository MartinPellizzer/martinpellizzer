Pisonia aculeata var. inermis

Pisonia aculeata var. inermis, commonly known as the silky pisonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in mangrove ecosystems.

Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture that distinguishes it from other varieties of Pisonia.
