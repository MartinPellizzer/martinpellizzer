Peperomia ouabianae var. ouabianae

Peperomia ouabianae var. ouabianae, commonly known as the "Snowy Peperomia," is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features dense clusters of tiny, waxy, white or pale green leaves that give it a frosty appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, which are not showy but contribute to its botanical interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique foliage and ease of care.
