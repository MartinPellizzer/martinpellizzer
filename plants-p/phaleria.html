Phaleria

Phaleria, commonly known as the yellow sterculia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces woody fruits that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Phaleria is also valued for its ornamental qualities due to its attractive foliage.

Its growth habits make it suitable for both indoor cultivation and outdoor gardens in warm climates.
