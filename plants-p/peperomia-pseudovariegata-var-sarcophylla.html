Peperomia pseudovariegata var. sarcophylla

Peperomia pseudovariegata var. sarcophylla, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with shades of green, white, and cream, providing an ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and compact form make it an excellent choice for container gardening and as a houseplant.
