Peltophorum africanum var. speciosum

Peltophorum africanum var. speciosum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that appear in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, and its trunk is often buttressed at the base. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The seeds are enclosed in pods that split open to release the seeds when mature.
