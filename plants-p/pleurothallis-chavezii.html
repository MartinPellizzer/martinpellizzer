Pleurothallis chavezii

Pleurothallis chavezii, commonly known as the Chavez's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical rainforest environments. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its delicate appearance and unique floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in shaded, high-altitude regions.
