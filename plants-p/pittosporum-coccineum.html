Pittosporum coccineum

Pittosporum coccineum, commonly known as the red-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum coccineum is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
