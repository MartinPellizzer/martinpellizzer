Pennisetum cafrum

Pennisetum cafrum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and plume-like inflorescences that range in color from silver to purple. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Pennisetum cafrum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and butterflies.
