Plumeria hilariana

Plumeria hilariana, commonly known as the Hawaiian plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It produces woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds.

Plumeria hilariana is well adapted to coastal and dry inland habitats, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils.
