Pelargonium × fragrantissimum

Pelargonium × fragrantissimum, commonly known as the fragrant geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny conditions and well-drained soil. Its foliage is often used in perfumes and aromatherapy due to its strong, pleasant scent.

The plant is popular as a decorative and aromatic garden plant.
