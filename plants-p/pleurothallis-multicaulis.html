Pleurothallis multicaulis

Pleurothallis multicaulis, commonly known as the multi-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, clustered growth habit with multiple stems arising from a single base. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors, and it blooms throughout the year in suitable conditions.

Pleurothallis multicaulis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
