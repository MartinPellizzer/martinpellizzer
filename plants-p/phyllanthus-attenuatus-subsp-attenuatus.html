Phyllanthus attenuatus subsp. attenuatus

Phyllanthus attenuatus subsp.

attenuatus, commonly known as the slender leaf spurge, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
