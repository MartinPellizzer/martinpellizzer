Polygonum nummulariifolium

Polygonum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the pennywort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its round, coin-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Polygonum nummulariifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
