Peperomia conulifera

Peperomia conulifera, commonly known as the beadleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive bead-like structures along the midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
