Phoebe antillana

Phoebe antillana, commonly known as the Antillean phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Caribbean islands, particularly Cuba and Hispaniola. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Phoebe antillana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
