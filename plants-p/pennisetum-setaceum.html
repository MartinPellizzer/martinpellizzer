Pennisetum setaceum

Pennisetum setaceum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its clump-forming growth habit. The plant produces feathery, pink to purple plumes that are visually striking and attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pennisetum setaceum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to add texture and color to landscapes.
