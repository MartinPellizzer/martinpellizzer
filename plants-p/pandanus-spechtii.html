Pandanus spechtii

Pandanus spechtii, commonly known as the Christmas Palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It features a slender trunk with a crown of long, feathery leaves that give it a palm-like appearance. The leaves are typically bright green and can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
