Pennisetum pappianum

Pennisetum pappianum, commonly known as purple top grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
