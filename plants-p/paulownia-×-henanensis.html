Paulownia × henanensis

Paulownia × henanensis, commonly known as the Henan paulownia, is a hybrid species within the Paulowniaceae family.

It is characterized by its fast growth rate and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in clusters, which are usually pink or purple in color. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential use in reforestation.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it has been introduced to other parts of the world for ecological restoration projects.
