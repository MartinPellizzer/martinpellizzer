Pavonia kotschyi var. mollissima

Pavonia kotschyi var. mollissima, commonly known as the silky pavonia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its adaptability to dry conditions and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
