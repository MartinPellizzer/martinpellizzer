Pilosella × heterodoxa

Pilosella × heterodoxa, commonly known as the hybrid goatherb, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Pilosella lactiflora and Pilosella hirsuta, characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
