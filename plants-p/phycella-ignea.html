Phycella ignea

Phycella ignea, commonly known as the flameleaf fern, is a unique plant characterized by its vibrant, fiery red foliage.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features delicate, feather-like fronds that are often tinged with shades of orange and red, giving it its striking appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
