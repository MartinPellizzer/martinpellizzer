Plectranthus schweinfurthii

Plectranthus schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its foliage is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
