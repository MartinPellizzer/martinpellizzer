Phaeneilema gramineum

Phaeneilema gramineum, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a species of grass native to Africa, particularly found in regions with tropical and subtropical climates. The plant typically grows in open areas, such as grasslands and disturbed soils, and reaches a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Phaeneilema gramineum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
