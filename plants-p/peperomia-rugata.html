Peperomia rugata

Peperomia rugata, commonly known as the wrinkled peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a slightly wrinkled appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for beginners and experienced growers alike.
