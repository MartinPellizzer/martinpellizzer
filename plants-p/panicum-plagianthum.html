Panicum plagianthum

Panicum plagianthum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, reddish-colored stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, pale-green to reddish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Panicum plagianthum is often found in disturbed soils and is used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and adaptability.
