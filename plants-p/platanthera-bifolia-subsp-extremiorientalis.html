Platanthera bifolia subsp. extremiorientalis

Platanthera bifolia subsp.

extremiorientalis, commonly known as the Eastern Asian lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and a dense spike of pale yellow to white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wet meadows. Its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, including flies and beetles.
