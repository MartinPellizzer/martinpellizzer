Pituranthos crassifolius

Pituranthos crassifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved pituranthos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its robust, fleshy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves.
