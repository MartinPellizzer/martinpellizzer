Palmijuncus schistoacanthus

Palmijuncus schistoacanthus, commonly known as the African date palm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible, sweet fruits resembling dates. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and fruit production, it is cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
