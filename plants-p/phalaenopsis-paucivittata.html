Phalaenopsis paucivittata

Phalaenopsis paucivittata, commonly known as the narrow-banded orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting blooms. The plant typically produces white or pale pink flowers with dark markings, which give it its common name. It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures rather than in soil.

Phalaenopsis paucivittata is popular in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
