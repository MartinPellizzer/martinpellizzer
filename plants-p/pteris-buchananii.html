Pteris buchananii

Pteris buchananii, commonly known as the Buchanan's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are typically light green in color. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value.

It reproduces via spores rather than seeds, typical of the fern group.
