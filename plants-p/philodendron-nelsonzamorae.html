Philodendron nelsonzamorae

Philodendron nelsonzamorae, commonly known as the Nelson Zamora philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Philodendron nelsonzamorae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
