Piper mauritianum

Piper mauritianum, commonly known as the Mauritius pepper or Indian pepper, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 meters, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

The plant is notable for its aromatic leaves and has been used traditionally in various medicinal applications.
