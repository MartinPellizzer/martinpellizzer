Peronocactus minusculus

Peronocactus minusculus, commonly known as the miniature cactus or dwarf cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a thick, ribbed stem that stores water, typical of cacti adapted to arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.

Due to its compact size and low maintenance, it is often used as a decorative plant in both outdoor and indoor settings.
