Photinia glabra

Photinia glabra, commonly known as the glossy abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
