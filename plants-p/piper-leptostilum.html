Piper leptostilum

Piper leptostilum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by black, peppery fruits.

Piper leptostilum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
