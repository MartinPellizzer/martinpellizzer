Pleurothallis curti-bradei

Pleurothallis curti-bradei, commonly known as the Curti-Bradei pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth. Its epiphytic nature allows it to grow on trees, where it absorbs moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its unique appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
