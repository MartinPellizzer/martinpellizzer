Piper baclei

Piper baclei, commonly known as the Baclei pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that often twine around supports. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a glossy green surface and a distinct, aromatic scent.

The small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature.
