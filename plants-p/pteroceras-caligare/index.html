Pteroceras Caligare

Pteroceras caligare is a rare and delicate fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is known for its intricate, lace-like fronds that shimmer with a unique silvery sheen. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. Due to habitat loss, it is now listed as endangered by conservation organizations. Its beauty and rarity make it a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
