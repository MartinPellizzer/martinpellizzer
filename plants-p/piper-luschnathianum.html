Piper luschnathianum

Piper luschnathianum, commonly known as the Luschnathia pepper or the African black pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are often used as a spice or in herbal remedies due to their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
