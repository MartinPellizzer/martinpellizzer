Philodendron warszewiczii

Philodendron warszewiczii, commonly known as the Warszewiczii Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with cream or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, fleshy fruits containing bright red seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
