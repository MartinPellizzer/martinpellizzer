Phalaenopsis celebensis

Phalaenopsis celebensis, commonly known as the Celebes phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
