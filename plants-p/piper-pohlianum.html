Piper pohlianum

Piper pohlianum, commonly known as the Pohlian's pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Piper pohlianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
