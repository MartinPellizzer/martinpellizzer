Peperomia reflexa var. argentina

Peperomia reflexa var. argentina, commonly known as the Argentine peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are typically borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
