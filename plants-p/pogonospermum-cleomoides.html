Pogonospermum cleomoides

Pogonospermum cleomoides, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with opposite, ovate leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange. The plant belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

Its seeds are encased in a fleshy, indehiscent capsule, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine.
