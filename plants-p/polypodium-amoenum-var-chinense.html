Polypodium amoenum var. chinense

Polypodium amoenum var. chinense, commonly known as the Chinese rock fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its spores are released from the sori located on the underside of its fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and medicinal properties.
