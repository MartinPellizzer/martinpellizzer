Papiria ciliaris

Papiria ciliaris, commonly known as the silky paper plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its ornamental value comes from its soft, silvery foliage and subtle floral display.

Papiria ciliaris is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in Mediterranean climates.
