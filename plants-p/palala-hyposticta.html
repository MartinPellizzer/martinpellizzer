Palala hyposticta

Palala hyposticta, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

Palala hyposticta is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
