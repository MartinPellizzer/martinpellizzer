Pavonia urens var. hanangensis

Pavonia urens var. hanangensis, commonly known as the Hanangensis firecracker plant, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, resembling firecrackers, hence its common name. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
