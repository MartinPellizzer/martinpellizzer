Philodendron samayense

Philodendron samayense, commonly known as the Samayense Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with light green or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
