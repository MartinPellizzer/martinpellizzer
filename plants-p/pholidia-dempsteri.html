Pholidia dempsteri

Pholidia dempsteri, commonly known as the dwarf starfruit, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and small white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.

The fruit's unique shape and flavor make it a distinctive addition to local cuisines.
