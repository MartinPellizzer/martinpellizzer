Primula sinopurpurea

Primula sinopurpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically purple or violet in color, with five petals and a distinctive yellow throat. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Its foliage consists of oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
