Premna microphylla var. glabra

Premna microphylla var. glabra, commonly known as smooth-leafed premna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
