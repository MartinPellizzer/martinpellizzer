Pisonia ferruginea

Pisonia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a reddish or rusty-colored margin.

The plant is adapted to salty air and can tolerate moderate drought conditions.
