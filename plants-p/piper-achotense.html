Piper achotense

Piper achotense, commonly known as the African pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. It produces small, spherical berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

Piper achotense is often found in moist, shaded environments and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
