Plectranthus provicarii

Plectranthus provicarii, commonly known as the purple plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant purple to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Plectranthus provicarii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
