Panicum trichocladum

Panicum trichocladum, commonly known as silky panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-blue foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Panicum trichocladum is drought-tolerant and low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
