Panicum novae-hollandiae

Panicum novae-hollandiae, commonly known as the Australian grass or silky blue grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and produces fluffy, seed-heads that give it a soft, silky appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It is drought-tolerant and can withstand a range of environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden settings.
