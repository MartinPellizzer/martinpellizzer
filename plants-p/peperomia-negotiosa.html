Peperomia negotiosa

Peperomia negotiosa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are usually hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia negotiosa is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated through stem cuttings or division.
