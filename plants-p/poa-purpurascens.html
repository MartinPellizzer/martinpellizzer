Poa purpurascens

Poa purpurascens, commonly known as the purple meadowgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and open woodlands. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and its striking purple-tinged inflorescence, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Poa purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in disturbed habitats.
