Pterostylis papuana

Pterostylis papuana, commonly known as the Papuan duck orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Guinea.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. It has a tuberous root system and produces a single, upright stem with a few leaves.

The flowers are pollinated by insects, and the plant is noted for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
