Piper agostinii

Piper agostinii, commonly known as the Java pepper or Agostini pepper, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing a mild, peppery flavor. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a spice.

The leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.
