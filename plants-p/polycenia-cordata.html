Polycenia cordata

Polycenia cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf polycenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Polycenia cordata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
