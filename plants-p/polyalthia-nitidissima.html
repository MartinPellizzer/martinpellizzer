Polyalthia nitidissima

Polyalthia nitidissima, commonly known as the glossy flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. The plant produces striking panicles of bright red or orange flowers, which appear in the summer months. Its bark is smooth and light grey, often shedding in thin strips as the tree matures.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a shade tree in tropical landscapes.
