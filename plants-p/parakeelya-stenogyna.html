Parakeelya stenogyna

Parakeelya stenogyna, commonly known as the narrow-leaved parakeelya, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and showy floral display.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
