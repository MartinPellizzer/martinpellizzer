Polemonium reptans f. villosum

Polemonium reptans f. villosum, commonly known as the silky bluebells, is a flowering plant characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple.

It is a variant of Polemonium reptans, distinguished by its soft, hairy foliage that gives it a velvety texture. Native to the eastern United States, this plant thrives in moist, shady woodland habitats. It produces showy clusters of flowers in early spring, making it a notable ornamental plant in gardens.

The plant's botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological restoration projects.
