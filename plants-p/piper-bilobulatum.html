Piper bilobulatum

Piper bilobulatum, commonly known as the two-lobed pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, clustered white flowers followed by small, round berries.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has traditional uses in herbal medicine and culinary applications.
