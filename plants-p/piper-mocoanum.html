Piper mocoanum

Piper mocoanum, commonly known as the Mocoan pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around other vegetation. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy surface, while the small, greenish flowers are followed by elliptical berries.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
