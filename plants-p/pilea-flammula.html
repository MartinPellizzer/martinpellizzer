Pilea flammula

Pilea flammula, commonly known as the flame plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or orange veins, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an excellent addition to shaded or low-light environments.
