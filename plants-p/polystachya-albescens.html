Polystachya albescens

Polystachya albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a rhizomatous structure with arching, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its inflorescence consists of multiple racemes bearing small, white or pale green flowers.

Polystachya albescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
