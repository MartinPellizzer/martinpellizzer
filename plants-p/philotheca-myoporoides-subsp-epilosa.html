Philotheca myoporoides subsp. epilosa

Philotheca myoporoides subsp.

epilosa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed myopor, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in dry sclerophyll forests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native plant communities.
