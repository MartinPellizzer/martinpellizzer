Parasenecio rubescens

Parasenecio rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant features slender, reddish stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with a dense layer of fine pubescence.

It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
