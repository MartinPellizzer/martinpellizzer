Piper misantlense

Piper misantlense, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, climbing vines with opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are small, three-lobed berries that turn red when mature.

Piper misantlense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
