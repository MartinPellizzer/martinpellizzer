Panicum warburgii

Panicum warburgii, commonly known as Warburg's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum warburgii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
