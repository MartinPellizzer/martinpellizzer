Polygonum persicaria var. lapathifolium

Polygonum persicaria var. lapathifolium, commonly known as heartleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically reddish or purple in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both a common weed and an important component of wetland ecosystems.
