Pleioblastus Gramineus

Pleioblastus gramineus is a species of bamboo known for its delicate, grass-like appearance and vibrant green foliage. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and is often used in ornamental gardening. This plant is prized for its ability to form dense clumps and its tolerance to partial shade. Its slender stems and fine leaves make it a popular choice for rock gardens and landscape designs. Despite its delicate look, Pleioblastus gramineus is remarkably hardy and easy to care for.
