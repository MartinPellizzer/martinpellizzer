Piper durionoides

Piper durionoides, commonly known as the durion tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes several species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a waxy coating.

The plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
