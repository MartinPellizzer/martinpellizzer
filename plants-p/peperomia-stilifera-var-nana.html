Peperomia stilifera var. nana

Peperomia stilifera var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
