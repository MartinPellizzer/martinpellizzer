Pentagonocarpus hastatus

Pentagonocarpus hastatus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive pentagonal-shaped fruits and hard, dense wood that is highly valued for construction. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or lance-shaped. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ecological importance and economic value, it is also a subject of conservation efforts in its native range.
