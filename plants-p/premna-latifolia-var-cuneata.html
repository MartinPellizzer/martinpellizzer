Premna latifolia var. cuneata

Premna latifolia var. cuneata, commonly known as the broadleaf premna or Chinese nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves. The leaves are typically pubescent on both surfaces, and the plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
