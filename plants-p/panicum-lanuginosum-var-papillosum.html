Panicum lanuginosum var. papillosum

Panicum lanuginosum var. papillosum, commonly known as the silky panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, wiry stems covered in soft, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with long, feathery bracts. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
