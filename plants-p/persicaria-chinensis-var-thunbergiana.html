Persicaria chinensis var. thunbergiana

Persicaria chinensis var. thunbergiana, commonly known as Thunberg's smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features erect, branched stems that are often hairy, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and marshy areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
