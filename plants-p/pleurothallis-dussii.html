Pleurothallis dussii

Pleurothallis dussii, commonly known as the Dussi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests at high altitudes.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
