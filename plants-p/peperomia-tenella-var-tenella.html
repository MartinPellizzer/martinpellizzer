Peperomia tenella var. tenella

Peperomia tenella var. tenella, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, and its leaves are often heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia tenella var. tenella is valued for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
