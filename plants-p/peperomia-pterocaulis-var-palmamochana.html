Peperomia pterocaulis var. palmamochana

Peperomia pterocaulis var. palmamochana, commonly known as the coral bane or coral plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features dense clusters of small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to white flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
