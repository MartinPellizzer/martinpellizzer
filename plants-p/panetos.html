Panetos

Panetos, commonly known as the red sandalwood tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark reddish-brown wood. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Due to its valuable wood, the plant is often harvested for commercial use, though it is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
