Phyllanthus mickelii

Phyllanthus mickelii, commonly known as the African starleaf, is a small shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Phyllanthus mickelii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
