Philodendron burgeri

Philodendron burgeri, commonly known as the Burger's Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by greenish-white berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
