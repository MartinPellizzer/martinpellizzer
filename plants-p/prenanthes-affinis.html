Prenanthes affinis

Prenanthes affinis, commonly known as the common arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers that are clustered in an umbel-like structure. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands and along streambanks.

Prenanthes affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
