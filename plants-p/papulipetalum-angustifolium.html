Papulipetalum angustifolium

Papulipetalum angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed papulipetalum, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with a distinctive papillate structure on the petals, which gives it its genus name. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
