Peperomia pasionana

Peperomia pasionana, commonly known as Passion Plant or Passion Vine, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia pasionana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ornamental appeal.
