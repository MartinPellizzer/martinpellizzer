Philodendron pusillum

Philodendron pusillum, commonly known as the miniature philodendron, is a small, evergreen climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are typically smaller in size compared to other philodendron species. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ease of care make it a favored plant for both novice and experienced growers.
