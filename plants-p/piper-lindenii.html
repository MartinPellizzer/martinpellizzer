Piper lindenii

Piper lindenii, commonly known as the Lindens' pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, peppery fruits. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
