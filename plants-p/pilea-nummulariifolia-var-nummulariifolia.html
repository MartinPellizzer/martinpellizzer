Pilea nummulariifolia var. nummulariifolia

Pilea nummulariifolia var. nummulariifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its round, coin-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it ideal for indoor cultivation.
