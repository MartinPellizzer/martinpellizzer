Pholidota niana

Pholidota niana, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs that help reduce water loss.

Pholidota niana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
