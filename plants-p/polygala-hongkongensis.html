Polygala hongkongensis

Polygala hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia, particularly Hong Kong and southern China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately divided, while the flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color.

Polygala hongkongensis is often found in humid subtropical climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
