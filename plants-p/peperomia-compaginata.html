Peperomia compaginata

Peperomia compaginata, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features dense, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia compaginata is a low-maintenance plant suitable for indoor cultivation.
