Peperomia obtusifolia var. genuina

Peperomia obtusifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often followed by reddish or yellowish berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
