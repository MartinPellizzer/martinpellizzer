Pteris shimenensis

Pteris shimenensis, commonly known as the Shimen water fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bi-lobed fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant.

Pteris shimenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
