Peperomia hanaensis

Peperomia hanaensis, commonly known as the Hanaensis Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish or silver-green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
