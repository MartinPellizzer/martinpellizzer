Polystachya lineata var. elatior

Polystachya lineata var. elatior, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, elongated inflorescences with numerous small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
