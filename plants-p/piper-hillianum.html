Piper hillianum

Piper hillianum, commonly known as the Hillian's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green in color. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into three-chambered berries.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine and are known for their pungent, peppery flavor.
