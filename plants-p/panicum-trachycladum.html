Panicum trachycladum

Panicum trachycladum, commonly known as the rough bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pink to reddish in color. It thrives in dry to mesic habitats, including prairies and open woodlands.

Panicum trachycladum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in native plant landscapes.
