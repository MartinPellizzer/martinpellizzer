Panicum danguyi

Panicum danguyi, commonly known as the Danguyi grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
