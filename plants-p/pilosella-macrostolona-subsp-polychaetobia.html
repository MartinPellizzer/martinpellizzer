Pilosella macrostolona subsp. polychaetobia

Pilosella macrostolona subsp.

polychaetobia, commonly known as the woolly pincushion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, white to pale yellow fluffy blooms that resemble a pincushion.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate, with a distinctive basal rosette formation.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of western Asia.
