Plantago myosuros var. angustifolia

Plantago myosuros var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in a spike, which are often clustered near the base of the plant. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

This variety is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its healing benefits.
