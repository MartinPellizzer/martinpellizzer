Pomelina fontanesii subsp. grandiflora

Pomelina fontanesii subsp.

grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered pomelina, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, large white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate with a smooth margin.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.
