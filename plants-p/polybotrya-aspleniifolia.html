Polybotrya aspleniifolia

Polybotrya aspleniifolia, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to southern Africa.

It features elegant, arching fronds that are dark green on the upper surface and silvery-white on the underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets arranged along a central stem.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
