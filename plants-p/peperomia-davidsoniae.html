Peperomia davidsoniae

Peperomia davidsoniae, commonly known as the Davidson's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
