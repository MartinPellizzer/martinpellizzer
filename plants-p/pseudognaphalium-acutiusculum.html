Pseudognaphalium acutiusculum

Pseudognaphalium acutiusculum, commonly known as the narrowleaf silverweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows. Its leaves are narrow and silver-gray in appearance, giving it a distinctive look.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall.
