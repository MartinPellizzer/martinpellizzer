Pouteria calistophylla

Pouteria calistophylla, commonly known as the golden apple or yellow custard apple, is a tropical plant belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It is native to Central and South America and is often cultivated for its edible fruit and ornamental value.
