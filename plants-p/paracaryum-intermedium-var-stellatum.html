Paracaryum intermedium var. stellatum

Paracaryum intermedium var. stellatum, commonly known as the star-shaped pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique leaf morphology and adaptability contribute to its horticultural value.
