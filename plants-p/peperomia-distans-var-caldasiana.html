Peperomia distans var. caldasiana

Peperomia distans var. caldasiana, commonly known as the Caldasian Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a desirable ornamental plant.
