Potentilla bactriana var. bamianica

Potentilla bactriana var. bamianica, commonly known as the Bamian potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
