Poa avatshensis

Poa avatshensis, commonly known as the Avatsi grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus, specifically in the country of Georgia. This perennial grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, typical of many grass species.
