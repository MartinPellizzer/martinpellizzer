Phenax triandra

Phenax triandra, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its roots are fibrous, and it is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Phenax triandra is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
