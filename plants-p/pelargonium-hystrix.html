Pelargonium hystrix

Pelargonium hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
