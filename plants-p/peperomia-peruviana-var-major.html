Peperomia peruviana var. major

Peperomia peruviana var. major, commonly known as the Peru vine or silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it an excellent addition to shaded or low-light environments.
