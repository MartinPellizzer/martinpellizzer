Potentilla millefolia

Potentilla millefolia, commonly known as silver potentilla, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
