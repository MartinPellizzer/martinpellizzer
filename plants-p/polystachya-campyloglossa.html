Polystachya campyloglossa

Polystachya campyloglossa, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped inflorescence and dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with reddish or brownish markings, and it produces small, round pseudobulbs.

Polystachya campyloglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
