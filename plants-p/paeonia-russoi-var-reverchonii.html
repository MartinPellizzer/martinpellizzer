Paeonia russoi var. reverchonii

Paeonia russoi var. reverchonii, commonly known as the Reverchon peony, is a deciduous herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features large, showy flowers with a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive, recurved petal structure. The plant has sturdy, upright stems that support its foliage, which consists of deeply lobed, dark green leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant flowers that attract pollinators.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
