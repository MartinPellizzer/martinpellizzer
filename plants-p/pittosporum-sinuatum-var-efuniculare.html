Pittosporum sinuatum var. efuniculare

Pittosporum sinuatum var. efuniculare, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly glossy on top, with a pale underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

The variety 'efuniculare' is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of P. sinuatum.
