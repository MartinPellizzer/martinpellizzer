Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. advena

Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. advena, commonly known as the Schiller's moth orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a greenish throat. The plant produces a single spike with several blooms, each featuring a lip that is often marked with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for indoor gardening.
