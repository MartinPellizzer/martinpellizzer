Peperomia leucostachya var. tolimensis

Peperomia leucostachya var. tolimensis, commonly known as the Tolimense Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces delicate, white to pale pink inflorescences. The plant is well adapted to shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
