Pelargonium × exaratum

Pelargonium × exaratum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium ×ibiscifolium. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Pelargonium × exaratum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a range of garden conditions.
