Pilea forsythiana var. forsythiana

Pilea forsythiana var. forsythiana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
