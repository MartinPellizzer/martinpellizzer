Peristylus tradescantiifolius

Peristylus tradescantiifolius, commonly known as the silver stripe caladium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive silver or white stripes running along the veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage rather than its flowers.
