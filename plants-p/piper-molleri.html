Piper molleri

Piper molleri, commonly known as the mountain pepper or velvet leaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the humid regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Piper molleri is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
