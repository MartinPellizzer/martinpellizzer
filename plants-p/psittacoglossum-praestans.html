Psittacoglossum praestans

Psittacoglossum praestans, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Psittacoglossum praestans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
