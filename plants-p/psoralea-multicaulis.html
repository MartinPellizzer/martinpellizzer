Psoralea multicaulis

Psoralea multicaulis, commonly known as the clustered psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features erect, branched stems covered with small, glandular hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a pink or purple hue.

The leaves are opposite and pinnately divided, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
