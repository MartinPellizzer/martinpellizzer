Pilosella varatinensis

Pilosella varatinensis, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly leaves and small, yellowish-white composite flowers. Native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. The plant is typically found in mountainous areas where it forms low, compact mats.

Its botanical features make it well-adapted to harsh, cold climates.
