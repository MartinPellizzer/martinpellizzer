Perrottetia venustula

Perrottetia venustula, commonly known as the pink pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Perrottetia venustula is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
