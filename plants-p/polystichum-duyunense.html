Polystichum duyunense

Polystichum duyunense, commonly known as the Du Yun fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its robust, dark green fronds with a leathery texture. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Polystichum duyunense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
