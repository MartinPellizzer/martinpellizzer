Parrya grandiflora

Parrya grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered parrya, is a striking member of the Apiaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

Parrya grandiflora is known for its aromatic foliage, which emits a subtle, spicy scent.
