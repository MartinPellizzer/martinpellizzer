Peperomia microphylla

Peperomia microphylla, commonly known as the tiny-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are usually bright green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia microphylla is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
