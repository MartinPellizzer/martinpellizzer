Phyllanthus orbiculatus var. intermedius

Phyllanthus orbiculatus var. intermedius, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
