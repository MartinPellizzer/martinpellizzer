Pitcairnia croatii

Pitcairnia croatii, commonly known as the Croatian pitcairnia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central tank to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking, inflorescence with showy, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
