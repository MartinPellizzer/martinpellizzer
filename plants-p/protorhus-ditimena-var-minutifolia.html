Protorhus ditimena var. minutifolia

Protorhus ditimena var. minutifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a woody stem with thorny branches, typical of the genus Protorhus. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
