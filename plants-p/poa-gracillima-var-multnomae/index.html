Poa Gracillima Var. Multnomae

Poa gracillima var. multnomae is a rare and delicate grass species native to the high elevations of the Andes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky outcrops. This variant is distinguished by its slender blades and fine, silvery foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
