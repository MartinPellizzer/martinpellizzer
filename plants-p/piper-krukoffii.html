Piper krukoffii

Piper krukoffii, commonly known as the Krukoff's pepper or the Hawaiian pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers that develop into peppery, red berries. Its foliage is often used in traditional Hawaiian medicine and as a decorative plant.

The berries are edible and have a spicy, peppery flavor similar to black pepper.
