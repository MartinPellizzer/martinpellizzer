Pelargonium × magniflorum

Pelargonium × magniflorum, commonly known as the large-leaf geranium, is a hybrid plant within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to red. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

Pelargonium × magniflorum is relatively low maintenance and resistant to many common pests and diseases.
