Portulaca nivea

Portulaca nivea, commonly known as the snowflake purslane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in hot, dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Portulaca nivea is a hardy species that is easy to grow in gardens, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
