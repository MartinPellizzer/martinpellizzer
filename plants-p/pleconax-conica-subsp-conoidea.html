Pleconax conica subsp. conoidea

Pleconax conica subsp.

conoidea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pleconax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apiaceae. It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in rocky or sandy soils.
