Phaleria burnettiana

Phaleria burnettiana, commonly known as the Burnett's magnolia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Magnoliaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
