Protium pubescens

Protium pubescens, commonly known as the hairy soursop, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in tropical forests.
