Peperomia obtusilimba

Peperomia obtusilimba, commonly known as the blunt-leaf peperomia, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub with thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Peperomia obtusilimba is appreciated for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular houseplant.
