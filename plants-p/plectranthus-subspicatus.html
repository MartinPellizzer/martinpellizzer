Plectranthus subspicatus

Plectranthus subspicatus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of purple or violet. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
