Paphiopedilum × expansum

Paphiopedilum × expansum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.

The flowers can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for gardeners and orchid enthusiasts.
