Penstemon Speciosus Subsp. Typicus

Penstemon speciosus subsp. typicus is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the western united states. it is known for its vibrant purple to blue flowers that bloom in early summer. this subspecies thrives in rocky soils and full sun conditions. it is often found in alpine and subalpine meadows. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
