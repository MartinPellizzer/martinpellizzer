Pentas zanzibarica var. pembensis

Pentas zanzibarica var. pembensis, commonly known as the Zanzibar pentas, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
