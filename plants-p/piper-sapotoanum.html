Piper sapotoanum

Piper sapotoanum, commonly known as the Sapotoan pepper vine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by berries that mature to a red color.

Piper sapotoanum is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
