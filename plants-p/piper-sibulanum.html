Piper sibulanum

Piper sibulanum, commonly known as the Sibulan pepper or Sibulan nut, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing a single seed. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
