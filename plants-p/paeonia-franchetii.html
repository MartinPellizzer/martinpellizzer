Paeonia franchetii

Paeonia franchetii, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has deeply lobed, dark green leaves and produces woody stems that can grow up to several feet in height. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

The botanical features of Paeonia franchetii make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
