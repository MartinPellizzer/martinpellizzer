Parodia sotomayorensis

Parodia sotomayorensis, commonly known as the Sotomayor's cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white to yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
