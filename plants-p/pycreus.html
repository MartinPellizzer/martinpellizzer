Pycreus

Pycreus, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, umbel-like inflorescence composed of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Pycreus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to gardens and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
