Ptilagrostis bhutanica

Ptilagrostis bhutanica, commonly known as the Bhutan grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Himalayan region, particularly Bhutan.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. This plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats. Its growth pattern contributes to soil stabilization and provides habitat for various small invertebrates.

The plant's botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native environment.
