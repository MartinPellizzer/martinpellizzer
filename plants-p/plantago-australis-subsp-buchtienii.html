Plantago australis subsp. buchtienii

Plantago australis subsp.

buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and coastal areas.

The plant features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. It produces small, greenish flowers in dense spikes during the spring and summer months.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
