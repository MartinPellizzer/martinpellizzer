Pteridella viridis

Pteridella viridis, commonly known as the green fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its spores are produced on the underside of its leaves, which are arranged in a distinctive, spiral pattern.
