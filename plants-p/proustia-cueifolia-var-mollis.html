Proustia cueifolia var. mollis

Proustia cueifolia var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaved proustia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are soft and hairy, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
