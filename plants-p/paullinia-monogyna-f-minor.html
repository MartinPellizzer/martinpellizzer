Paullinia monogyna f. minor

Paullinia monogyna f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf paullinia, is a compact shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.
