Pennisetum stolzii

Pennisetum stolzii, commonly known as the silver fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender culms that are often covered in a silvery-white bloom, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces fluffy, feathery plumes that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum stolzii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to add texture and movement to garden designs.
