Pleurothallis ancistra

Pleurothallis ancistra, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its blooming season usually occurs during the warmer months, and it is appreciated for its subtle beauty and ease of care.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
