Polygala kinii

Polygala kinii, commonly known as the Kikuyu milkweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic to some animals, including cattle. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Polygala kinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
