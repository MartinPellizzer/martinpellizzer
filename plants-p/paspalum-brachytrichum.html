Paspalum brachytrichum

Paspalum brachytrichum, commonly known as smooth paspalum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and short, stiff leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments.

Its rhizomatous and stoloniferous growth allows it to spread rapidly and form dense mats.
