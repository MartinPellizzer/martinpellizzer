Paloue bicolor var. bicolor

Paloue bicolor var. bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored paloue, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking bi-colored leaves, which typically feature a mix of green and white or pale yellow hues. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink or white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
