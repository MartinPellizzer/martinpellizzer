Pelargonium nobile

Pelargonium nobile, commonly known as the noble geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly serrated edge, and the plant produces small, clustered fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium nobile is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in herbal remedies.
