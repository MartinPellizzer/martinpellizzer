Piper pulchrum var. copeyanum

Piper pulchrum var. copeyanum, commonly known as the Copeyanum pepper plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, peppery fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface and a strong, aromatic scent.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild peppery flavor, making it suitable for both ornamental gardens and culinary use.
