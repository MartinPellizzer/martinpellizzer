Pharnaceum radiatum

Pharnaceum radiatum, commonly known as the radiate pharnaceum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Pharnaciaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, radially symmetric flowers that display a vibrant array of colors. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or wetlands, where it thrives in high humidity. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure, which includes multiple layers of petals arranged in a radial pattern.
