Pycreus commersonii

Pycreus commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's pycreus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed beyond its natural range.
