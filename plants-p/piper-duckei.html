Piper duckei

Piper duckei, commonly known as the Ducke's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest in South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.

Piper duckei is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic effects.
