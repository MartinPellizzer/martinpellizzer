Peperomia victoriana

Peperomia victoriana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate temperatures with high humidity.

Peperomia victoriana is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
