Pennisetum schimperi var. pubiflorum

Pennisetum schimperi var. pubiflorum, commonly known as the hairy-flowered lovegrass, is a grass species native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces dense clusters of small, fluffy flowers that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
