Pteronia beckeoides

Pteronia beckeoides, commonly known as the Cape sugarbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and coastal conditions. It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Pteronia beckeoides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
