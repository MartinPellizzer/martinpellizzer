Piper fockei

Piper fockei, commonly known as the Focke's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

The fruit, which is a small, yellowish berry, contains the seeds and is often used in traditional medicine.
