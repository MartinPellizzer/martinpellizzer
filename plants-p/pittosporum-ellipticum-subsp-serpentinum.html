Pittosporum ellipticum subsp. serpentinum

Pittosporum ellipticum subsp.

serpentinum, commonly known as the serpentine pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves that are often dark green in color.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in rocky, serpentine soils and is adapted to nutrient-poor environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
