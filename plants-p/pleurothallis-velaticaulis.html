Pleurothallis velaticaulis

Pleurothallis velaticaulis, commonly known as the velvet-stemmed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its delicate blooms.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
