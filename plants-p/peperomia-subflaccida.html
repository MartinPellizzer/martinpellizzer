Peperomia subflaccida

Peperomia subflaccida, commonly known as the drooping peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly wavy in texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
