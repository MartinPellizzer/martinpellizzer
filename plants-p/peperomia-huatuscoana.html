Peperomia huatuscoana

Peperomia huatuscoana, commonly known as the Huatusco peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia huatuscoana is relatively low-maintenance and adapts well to a variety of indoor conditions.
