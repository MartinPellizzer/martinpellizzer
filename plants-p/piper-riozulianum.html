Piper riozulianum

Piper riozulianum, commonly known as the Rio Zulian pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Venezuela and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and grow in clusters, while the fruit is a small, three-lobed berry.

Piper riozulianum is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
