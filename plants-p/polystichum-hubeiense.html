Polystichum hubeiense

Polystichum hubeiense, commonly known as Hubei shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive shield-shaped outline. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance.
