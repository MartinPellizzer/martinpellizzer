Pavonia gallaensis

Pavonia gallaensis, commonly known as the Brazilian velvet flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red bracts that resemble petals. Pavonia gallaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant bracts.
