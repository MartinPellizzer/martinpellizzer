Pennisetum glaucifolium var. glaberrimum

Pennisetum glaucifolium var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the silverleaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, silver-blue leaves that give it a distinctive appearance, and it produces fluffy, pinkish-white inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its clumping growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its foliage and flowers provide visual interest throughout the growing season.
