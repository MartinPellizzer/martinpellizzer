Phalangium liliago f. exilis

Phalangium liliago f. exilis, commonly known as the slender lily.

This plant is a flowering species characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands or stream banks. The flowers are usually pink or white, with six petals and a prominent central stamen.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
