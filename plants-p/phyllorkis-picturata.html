Phyllorkis picturata

Phyllorkis picturata, commonly known as the painted fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that display a gradient of colors, ranging from silver to deep green. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in garden landscapes for its aesthetic appeal. Its foliage is composed of tiny, overlapping leaves that create a textured appearance.

Phyllorkis picturata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both gardeners and landscapers.
