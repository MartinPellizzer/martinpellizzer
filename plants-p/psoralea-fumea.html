Psoralea fumea

Psoralea fumea, commonly known as the smoke bush or smoky psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, pale yellow to greenish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks. Its unique appearance and adaptability to arid conditions make it an interesting subject for botanical study.

Psoralea fumea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
