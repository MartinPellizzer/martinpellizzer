Protasparagus bechuanicus

Protasparagus bechuanicus, commonly known as the Bechuanian asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its thick stems.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
