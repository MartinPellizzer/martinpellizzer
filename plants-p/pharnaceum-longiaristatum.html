Pharnaceum longiaristatum

Pharnaceum longiaristatum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed pharnaceum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, providing a dense foliage cover. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and potential medicinal properties.
