Pelargonium cavanillesii

Pelargonium cavanillesii, commonly known as the Cavanilles' geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with broad, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium cavanillesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
