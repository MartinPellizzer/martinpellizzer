Peperomia nigroungulata

Peperomia nigroungulata, commonly known as the Black-veined Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with prominent black veins, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia nigroungulata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
