Peperomia analecta

Peperomia analecta, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually white or pale green, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia analecta is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
