Pothos ceylanicus

Pothos ceylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Sri Lanka.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its trailing vines make it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a shelf plant.
