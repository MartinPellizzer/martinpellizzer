Pilosella phaeostigma

Pilosella phaeostigma, commonly known as the dark-stained pilosella, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a dark green color with a slightly waxy texture.
