Pleroma barbigerum var. lepidotum

Pleroma barbigerum var. lepidotum, commonly known as the Lepidote Pleroma, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, leathery leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can last several months.
