Pilea longicaulis var. longicaulis

Pilea longicaulis var. longicaulis, commonly known as the Shingleplant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, dark green, ovate leaves that are slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces inconspicuous, greenish-white flowers in early summer, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored choice for indoor gardens.
