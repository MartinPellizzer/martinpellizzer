Phlegmariurus llanganatensis

Phlegmariurus llanganatensis, commonly known as the Llanganatensis leatherwood, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a dark green to reddish-brown stem with a smooth, leathery texture. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a distinct midrib and a slightly curved apex.

This moss is often used in ecological studies due to its sensitivity to environmental changes.
