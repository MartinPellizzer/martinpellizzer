Pittosporum humblotianum

Pittosporum humblotianum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a fragrant scent. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that appear in clusters, followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum humblotianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native habitats.
