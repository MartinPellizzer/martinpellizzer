Piper perbrevicaule var. subglabrilimbum

Piper perbrevicaule var. subglabrilimbum, commonly known as the glossy pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery fruits. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary purposes in tropical gardens.
