Pelea madagascarica var. tolongoala-manitra-anisette

Pelea madagascarica var. tolongoala-manitra-anisette, commonly known as the Madagascar starwood, is a unique plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its star-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade. Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to horticultural collections.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
