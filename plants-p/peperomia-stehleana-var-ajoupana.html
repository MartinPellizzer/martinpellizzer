Peperomia stehleana var. ajoupana

Peperomia stehleana var. ajoupana, commonly known as the Ajoupana Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, often adorned with subtle patterns or variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
