Prenanthes ramosissima

Prenanthes ramosissima, commonly known as the slender bur marigold, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, branching stems and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and is often found in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant is also noted for its ability to thrive in wet environments and its role in supporting local insect populations.
