Pittosporum yunnanense

Pittosporum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, with a glossy surface.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
