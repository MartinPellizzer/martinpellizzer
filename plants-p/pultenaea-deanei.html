Pultenaea deanei

Pultenaea deanei, commonly known as the slender pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often twining growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or yellow-green in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval to lance-shaped leaflets.

Pultenaea deanei is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
