Piper metanum var. villibracteum

Piper metanum var. villibracteum, commonly known as the small-leaved pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a distinctively aromatic scent. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by elongated, slender berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The variety is characterized by its reduced bract size compared to the typical form of Piper metanum.
