Pycreus Diaphanus Var. Setiformis

Pycreus diaphanus var. setiformis is a rare and delicate plant known for its unique, feather-like foliage. Native to specific regions in southern Africa, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and arid environments. Its striking appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and collectors. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to overwatering and requires careful cultivation. This variant stands out for its elegant form and adaptability to challenging growing conditions.
