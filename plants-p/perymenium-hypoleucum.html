Perymenium hypoleucum

Perymenium hypoleucum, commonly known as the white-flowered perymenium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate appearance.
