Piper sinuosirameum

Piper sinuosirameum, commonly known as the Sinuous Saddlebush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant's botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
