Pseudocranichis thysanochila

Pseudocranichis thysanochila, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the surrounding soil, which is typical of many orchid species.

The orchid's botanical structure reflects its adaptation to low-light conditions and nutrient-poor substrates.
