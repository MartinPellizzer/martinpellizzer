Peperomia connixa

Peperomia connixa, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Brazil.

It features dark green, waxy leaves with a striking silver or grayish underside, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia connixa is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and aesthetic value.
