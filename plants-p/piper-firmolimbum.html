Piper firmolimbum

Piper firmolimbum, commonly known as the African pepper or wild pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by berry-like fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
