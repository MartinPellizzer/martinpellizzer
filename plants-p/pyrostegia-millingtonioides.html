Pyrostegia millingtonioides

Pyrostegia millingtonioides, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which are often covered in a dense layer of silky, white hairs. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a velvety texture with a deep green color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
