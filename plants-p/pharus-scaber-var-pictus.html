Pharus scaber var. pictus

Pharus scaber var. pictus, commonly known as the painted roughleaf, is a unique variant of the Pharus scaber species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rough-textured leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and color variations. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage exhibits a range of hues, from deep greens to vibrant reds and purples, depending on environmental conditions.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
