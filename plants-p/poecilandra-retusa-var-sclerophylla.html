Poecilandra retusa var. sclerophylla

Poecilandra retusa var. sclerophylla, commonly known as the roughleaf poecilandra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its rough, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
