Paphiopedilum villosum var. affine

Paphiopedilum villosum var. affine, commonly known as the hairy villosum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive lip. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is typically found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
