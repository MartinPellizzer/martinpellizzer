Peperomia hartwegiana var. hartwegiana

Peperomia hartwegiana var. hartwegiana, commonly known as the Hartweg's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
