Piper camiloi var. glabratum

Piper camiloi var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper camiloi species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple color. It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic foliage.
