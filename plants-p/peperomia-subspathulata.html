Peperomia subspathulata

Peperomia subspathulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia subspathulata is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
