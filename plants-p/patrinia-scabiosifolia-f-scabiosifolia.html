Patrinia scabiosifolia f. scabiosifolia

Patrinia scabiosifolia f. scabiosifolia, commonly known as the scabious leaf patrinia, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is native to regions of Asia, including China, and is often found in woodland edges and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators make it a popular choice for gardens and ecological landscapes.
