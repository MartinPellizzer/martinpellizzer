Pycnanthemum tenuifolium

Pycnanthemum tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern and central United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as meadows, marshes, and stream banks. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, blue to violet flowers in clustered inflorescences. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its aromatic properties.

The plant is often used in herbal medicine for its purported calming and digestive benefits.
