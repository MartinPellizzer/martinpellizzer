Primulina Subulata

Lucky bamboo, scientifically known as Primulina subulata, is a popular houseplant believed to bring prosperity and good fortune to its owners. Though its name suggests a bamboo-like appearance, it is not a true bamboo but a member of the Gesneriaceae family. This plant is easy to care for and thrives in low light conditions, making it ideal for beginners and indoor spaces. Its elegant, upright stems and glossy leaves add a touch of natural beauty to any room. Despite its simple care requirements, Lucky bamboo is often cultivated as a symbol of luck and longevity in many cultures.
