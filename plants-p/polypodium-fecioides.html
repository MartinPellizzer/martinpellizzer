Polypodium fecioides

Polypodium fecioides, commonly known as the staghorn fern, is a tropical epiphytic fern that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its large, tree-like fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant has a distinctive appearance with a central trunk-like structure covered in thick, velvety rhizomes. Its fronds are typically divided into lobes, giving it a fern-like appearance.

Polypodium fecioides is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of tropical rainforests.
