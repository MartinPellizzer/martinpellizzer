Panicum beyeri

Panicum beyeri, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, reddish-purple inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its striking color and drought tolerance.
