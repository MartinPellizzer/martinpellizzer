Peperomia schlechteri

Peperomia schlechteri, commonly known as the Shlechter's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia schlechteri is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
