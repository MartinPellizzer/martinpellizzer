Pelargonium × orchideum

Pelargonium × orchideum, commonly known as the orchid geranium, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various pelargonium species.

It is characterized by its showy, orchid-like flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has velvety, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in both containers and garden beds.
