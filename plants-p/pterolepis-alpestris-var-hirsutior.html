Pterolepis alpestris var. hirsutior

Pterolepis alpestris var. hirsutior, commonly known as the hairy alpine pterolepis, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in dense, soft hairs, giving it a woolly appearance. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in late summer.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.
