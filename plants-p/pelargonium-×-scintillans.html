Pelargonium × scintillans

Pelargonium × scintillans, commonly known as the Shiny Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features succulent, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for both decorative and horticultural purposes.
