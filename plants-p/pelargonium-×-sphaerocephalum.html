Pelargonium × sphaerocephalum

Pelargonium × sphaerocephalum, commonly known as the globe geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and slightly fuzzy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
