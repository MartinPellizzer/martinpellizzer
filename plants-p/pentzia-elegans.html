Pentzia elegans

Pentzia elegans, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
