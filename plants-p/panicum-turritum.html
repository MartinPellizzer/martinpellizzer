Panicum turritum

Panicum turritum, commonly known as the turban grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical inflorescence, which resembles a turban, hence its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Panicum turritum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its unique appearance.
