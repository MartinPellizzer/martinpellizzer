Panicum tatei

Panicum tatei, commonly known as the golden panicum, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow inflorescences, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

Panicum tatei is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
