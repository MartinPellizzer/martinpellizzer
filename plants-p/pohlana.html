Pohlana

Pohlana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
