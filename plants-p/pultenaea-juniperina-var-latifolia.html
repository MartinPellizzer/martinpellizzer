Pultenaea juniperina var. latifolia

Pultenaea juniperina var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its flowers are followed by small, woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds.

This species is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathland and woodland environments.
