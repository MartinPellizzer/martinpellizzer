Psoralea pinnata var. subglabra

Psoralea pinnata var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are finely divided and give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in the fields of botany and ecology.
