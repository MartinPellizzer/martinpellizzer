Plectranthus macraei

Plectranthus macraei, commonly known as the Blue Plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often a striking blue-green color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or blue, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus macraei is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
