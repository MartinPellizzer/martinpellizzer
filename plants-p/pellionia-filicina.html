Pellionia filicina

Pellionia filicina, commonly known as the silver thread fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a small, delicate fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant has finely divided, linear fronds that give it a delicate, lacy appearance. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, silky coating of fine hairs.

Pellionia filicina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often used in shady garden settings.
