Pittosporum glabratum var. wenxianense

Pittosporum glabratum var. wenxianense, commonly known as the Wenxian Pittosporum, is a rare shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface.

The flowers are small, white, and clustered, often emitting a mild fragrance that attracts pollinators.
