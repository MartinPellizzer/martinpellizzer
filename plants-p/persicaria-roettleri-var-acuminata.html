Persicaria roettleri var. acuminata

Persicaria roettleri var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to Asia.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are elongated with a pointed tip. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist soil and partial shade, often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Its foliage is edible and has been used in traditional cuisines.
