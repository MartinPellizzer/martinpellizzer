Potameia thouarsii

Potameia thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the island's tropical forests. Its leaves are strap-like and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Potameia thouarsii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a subject of interest in botanical research due to its unique adaptations.
