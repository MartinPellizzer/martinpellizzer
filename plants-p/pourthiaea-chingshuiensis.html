Pourthiaea chingshuiensis

Pourthiaea chingshuiensis, commonly known as the Chingshui endemic gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high altitude and cool climates. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
