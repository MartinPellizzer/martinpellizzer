Pedicularis phaceliifolia

Pedicularis phaceliifolia, commonly known as the alpine lousewort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

This species is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.
