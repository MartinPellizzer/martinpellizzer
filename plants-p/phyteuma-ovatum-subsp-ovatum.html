Phyteuma ovatum subsp. ovatum

Phyteuma ovatum subsp.

ovatum, commonly known as the common phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and ease of cultivation.
