Piper sulcinerve

Piper sulcinerve, commonly known as the velvetleaf pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves with prominent veins, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are typically three-lobed berries that mature to a dark purple color.

The plant is often found in humid, shaded environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
