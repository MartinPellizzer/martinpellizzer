Parsonsia pinto

Parsonsia pinto, commonly known as the Pinto's flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water bodies.

The leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, textured surface.
