Poa superlanata

Poa superlanata, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it forms dense, low-growing mats. Its flowering stems produce small, spikelet-like inflorescences that are often hidden among the foliage.

Poa superlanata is adapted to cold, high-altitude climates and is an important component of alpine ecosystems.
