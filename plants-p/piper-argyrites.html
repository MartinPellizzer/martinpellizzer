Piper argyrites

Piper argyrites, commonly known as silverleaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
