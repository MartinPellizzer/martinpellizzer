Pilosella ziziana subsp. multifida

Pilosella ziziana subsp.

multifida, commonly known as the multifid leaf pearly everlet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Pilosella ziziana subsp.

multifida is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
