Panicum lancearium

Panicum lancearium, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered a weed in some agricultural settings.
