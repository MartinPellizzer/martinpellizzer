Polygala heteroptera

Polygala heteroptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milk-vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often found in open woodlands and along rocky outcrops.
