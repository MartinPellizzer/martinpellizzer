Panicum jumentorum

Panicum jumentorum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the warm temperate and tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces dense, fluffy seed heads that are often bright red or orange in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil stabilization and can serve as forage for livestock in certain regions.
