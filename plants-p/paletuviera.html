Paletuviera

Paletuviera, commonly known as the coconut palm, is a tropical plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces coconuts, which are fibrous, spiky fruits containing both water and flesh. Its root system is shallow, allowing it to thrive in coastal and sandy soils.

The coconut palm plays a significant role in both ecological and economic systems in tropical areas.
