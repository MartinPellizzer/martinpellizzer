Phenax ulei

Phenax ulei, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, and it produces small, round berries that are toxic to humans and animals.

Phenax ulei is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
