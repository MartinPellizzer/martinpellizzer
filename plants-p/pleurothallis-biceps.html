Pleurothallis biceps

Pleurothallis biceps, commonly known as the two-armed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a fragrant scent. Its leaves are usually broad and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Pleurothallis biceps is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
