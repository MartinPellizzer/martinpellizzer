Phlomis rigida

Phlomis rigida, commonly known as rigid hedgehog flower, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, woody stems and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flower clusters in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
