Panicum ozogonum

Panicum ozogonum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and the plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Panicum ozogonum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
