Primula stricta var. glabrescens

Primula stricta var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features upright, compact growth with lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, often blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty and early spring flowering.
