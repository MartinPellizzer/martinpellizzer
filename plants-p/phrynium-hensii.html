Phrynium hensii

Phrynium hensii, commonly known as the Hensii arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe flowers. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines for their starchy texture.

Phrynium hensii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes as well as for its edible roots.
