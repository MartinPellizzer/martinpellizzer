Prosartes smithii

Prosartes smithii, commonly known as the Smith's barrengrass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically produces pale green to brownish flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open areas with well-drained soils.

Prosartes smithii is a hardy species that plays a role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
