Pimelea menkeana var. dubia

Pimelea menkeana var. dubia, commonly known as the Western River Geebung, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, compact form and bright yellow flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal and sandy areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in local habitats.
