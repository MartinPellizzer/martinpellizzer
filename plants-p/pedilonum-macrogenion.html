Pedilonum macrogenion

Pedilonum macrogenion, commonly known as the large-flowered pedilonum, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs.
