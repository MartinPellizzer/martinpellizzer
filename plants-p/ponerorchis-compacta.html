Ponerorchis compacta

Ponerorchis compacta, commonly known as the compact lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its flowering spikes that produce numerous small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swamps. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

Ponerorchis compacta is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
