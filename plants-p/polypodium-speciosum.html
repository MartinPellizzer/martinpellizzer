Polypodium speciosum

Polypodium speciosum, commonly known as the golden polypody, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green to golden in color. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are pinnately compound, with small, ovate-shaped pinnae that are often curled at the tips.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be found growing on rocks or tree trunks in its natural habitat.
