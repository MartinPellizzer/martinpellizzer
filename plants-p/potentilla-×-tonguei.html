Potentilla × tonguei

Potentilla × tonguei, commonly known as the hybrid shrub, is a deciduous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with wiry, arching stems and is typically found in rocky or alpine environments. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Potentilla × tonguei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
