Polygala rhodoptera

Polygala rhodoptera, commonly known as the red-winged milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a distinctive wing-like structure on the petals.

It is often found in grassland and scrub habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of its native environment.
