Pareugenia

Pareugenia, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes other stinging plants like nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and small greenish-white flowers that grow in clusters. Its stems are covered in fine hairs that can cause a mild skin irritation.

Pareugenia is often found in moist, shaded areas and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
