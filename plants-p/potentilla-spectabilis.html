Potentilla spectabilis

Potentilla spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. It has palmately divided leaves and can reach heights of up to 3 feet.

Potentilla spectabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
