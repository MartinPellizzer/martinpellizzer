Pipturus viridis

Pipturus viridis, commonly known as green huckleberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and rhododendrons. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland habitats. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a slightly serrated edge.
