Puccinellia retroflexa var. vegeta

Puccinellia retroflexa var. vegeta, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. This variety is typically found in sandy or disturbed soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers that are usually pink to purple in color.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
