Protium minutiflorum

Protium minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind. The plant is often found in lowland forests and can grow up to 30 meters tall.
