Pterostylis limbatus

Pterostylis limbatus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-like flower that is typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has a short, hairy stem and produces a single, erect flower spike. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing close to the ground.

Pterostylis limbatus is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments.
