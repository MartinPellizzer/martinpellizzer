Porcellites brasiliensis var. integrifolia

Porcellites brasiliensis var. integrifolia, commonly known as the Brazilian porcellite, is a rare and unique plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
