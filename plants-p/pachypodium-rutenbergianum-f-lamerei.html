Pachypodium rutenbergianum f. lamerei

Pachypodium rutenbergianum f. lamerei, commonly known as the Lamerei Pachypodium, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and sparse, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the rainy season, which attract pollinators such as bees and moths. Its unique morphology and adaptability to harsh climates make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-water conditions.
