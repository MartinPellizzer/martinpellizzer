Peperomia corcovadensis f. longifolia

Peperomia corcovadensis f. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are elongated and often have a reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to shaded or semi-shaded spaces.
