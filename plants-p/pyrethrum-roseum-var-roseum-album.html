Pyrethrum roseum var. roseum-album

Pyrethrum roseum var. roseum-album, commonly known as the white rose pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
