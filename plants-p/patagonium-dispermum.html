Patagonium dispermum

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is characterized by its delicate, herbaceous structure and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces two seeds per fruit, a feature that gives it its scientific name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
