Parosela triphylla

Parosela triphylla, commonly known as the three-leaved parosela, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Parosela triphylla thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
