Polystachya zambesiaca

Polystachya zambesiaca, commonly known as the Zambesi sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are glossy and often arching. Its inflorescence consists of a branched cluster of small, greenish-white flowers.

Polystachya zambesiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
