Panicum streptostachys

Panicum streptostachys, commonly known as purpletop panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive purple-tinted foliage, which becomes more pronounced in the fall. The plant produces fluffy, silver-white seed heads that add ornamental value to landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense clumps make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
