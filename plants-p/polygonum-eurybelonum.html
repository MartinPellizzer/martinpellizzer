Polygonum eurybelonum

Polygonum eurybelonum, commonly known as the broadleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are broad and lance-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer.

Polygonum eurybelonum is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other wetland habitats.
