Peperomia secundiflora

Peperomia secundiflora, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ease of care and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
