Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. stylifer

Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. stylifer, commonly known as the slender-leaf phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape with a smooth margin. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
