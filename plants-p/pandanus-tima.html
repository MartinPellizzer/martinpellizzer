Pandanus tima

Pandanus tima, commonly known as the hibiscus pandanus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, paddle-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Pandanus tima is also valued for its versatility in both horticultural and cultural contexts.
