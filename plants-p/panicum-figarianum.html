Panicum figarianum

Panicum figarianum, commonly known as the Fig-leaf Panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves that resemble those of fig trees. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
