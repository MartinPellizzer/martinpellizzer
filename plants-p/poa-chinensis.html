Poa chinensis

Poa chinensis, commonly known as Chinese bluegrass, is a species of grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it blooms in the spring.

Poa chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
