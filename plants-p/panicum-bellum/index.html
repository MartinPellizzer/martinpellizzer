Panicum Bellum

Panicum bellum is a striking grass species known for its vibrant floral display. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open fields and meadows. The plant's delicate, pinkish-purple flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
