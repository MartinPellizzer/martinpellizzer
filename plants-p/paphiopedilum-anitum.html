Paphiopedilum anitum

Paphiopedilum anitum, commonly known as the Chinese lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a unique, lip-like petal that is often yellow or green with red markings, serving as a key identifier. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a pouch-shaped labellum that attracts pollinators. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
