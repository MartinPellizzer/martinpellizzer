Ptychosperma coronatum

Ptychosperma coronatum, commonly known as the helmet fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a tree-like fern characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a unique appearance with a distinctive, helmet-shaped structure at the base of its trunk. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to several meters.
