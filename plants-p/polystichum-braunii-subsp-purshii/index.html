Polystichum Braunii Subsp. Purshii

Polystichum braunii subsp. purshii is a lesser-known fern species that thrives in cool, moist environments. It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. This subspecies is often found in forest understories where it benefits from partial shade and consistent humidity. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after plant for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Understanding its ecology and habitat is key to preserving its dwindling populations.
