Polystachya gracilenta

Polystachya gracilenta, commonly known as the slender-leafed flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and its striking, vivid orange or red inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in undergrowth or on forest floors. Its flowers are arranged in dense, branched clusters, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Polystachya gracilenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
