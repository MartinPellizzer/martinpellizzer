Potentilla Pedata Var. Parnassica

Potentilla pedata var. parnassica is a rare and striking plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and delicate foliage, it thrives in harsh mountain environments. This variant of Potentilla pedata is particularly valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance. It is often found in rocky soils and high-altitude meadows where it plays a vital role in local ecosystems. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful and endangered species from habitat loss and climate change.
