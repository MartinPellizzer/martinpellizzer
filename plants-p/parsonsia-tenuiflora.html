Parsonsia tenuiflora

Parsonsia tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered parsonsia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly waxy texture.
