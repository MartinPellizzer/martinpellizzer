Peperomia stilifera

Peperomia stilifera, commonly known as the slender-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are borne on short, erect spikes. Peperomia stilifera is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardens and collections.
