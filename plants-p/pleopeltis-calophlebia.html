Pleopeltis calophlebia

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of brownish or golden-colored hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The fronds are pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets that contribute to its ornamental value.
