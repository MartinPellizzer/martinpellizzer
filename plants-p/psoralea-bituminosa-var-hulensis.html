Psoralea bituminosa var. hulensis

Psoralea bituminosa var. hulensis, commonly known as the Hulens' psoralea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, pod-like fruits contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
