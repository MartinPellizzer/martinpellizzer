Peperomia orbiculimba var. orbiculimba

Peperomia orbiculimba var. orbiculimba, commonly known as the rounded peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening.
