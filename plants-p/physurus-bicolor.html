Physurus bicolor

Physurus bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green markings. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering.

Physurus bicolor is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
