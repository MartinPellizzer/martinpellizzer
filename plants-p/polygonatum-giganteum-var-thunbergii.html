Polygonatum giganteum var. thunbergii

Polygonatum giganteum var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese yellow flag.

This variant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its long, rhizomatous growth habit. It produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to East Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
