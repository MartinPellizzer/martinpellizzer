Potentilla drusiana

Potentilla drusiana, commonly known as the Drusian cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately divided into five lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Potentilla drusiana is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine environments.
