Pleuromenes heterocarpa

Pleuromenes heterocarpa, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
