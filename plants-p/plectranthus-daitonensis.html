Plectranthus daitonensis

Plectranthus daitonensis, commonly known as the Daitonensis hyssop, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and compact growth habit.
