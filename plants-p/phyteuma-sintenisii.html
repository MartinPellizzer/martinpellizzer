Phyteuma sintenisii

Phyteuma sintenisii, commonly known as the Alpine bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Phyteuma sintenisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
