Papaver × sieheanum

Papaver × sieheanum, commonly known as the hybrid poppy, is a cross between *Papaver rhoeas* (common poppy) and *Papaver orientale* (oriental poppy).

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
