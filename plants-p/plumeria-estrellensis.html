Plumeria estrellensis

Plumeria estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellensis Plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowers are typically borne on long, slender pedicels, and it produces woody, spiny fruits containing seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its beauty.
