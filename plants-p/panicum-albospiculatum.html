Panicum albospiculatum

Panicum albospiculatum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces dense, fluffy seed heads that are often white or pale in color, giving it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a silvery sheen that enhances its ornamental appeal.

Silver panicum is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
