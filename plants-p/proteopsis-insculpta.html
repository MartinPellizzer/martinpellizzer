Proteopsis insculpta

Proteopsis insculpta, commonly known as the African protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant belongs to the family Proteaceae, which is known for its diverse and often striking floral structures. Proteopsis insculpta typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and elegant appearance.
