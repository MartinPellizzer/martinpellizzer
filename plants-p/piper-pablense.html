Piper pablense

Piper pablense, commonly known as the Pablense Pepper, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy berries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
