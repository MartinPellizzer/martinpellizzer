Polychilos stobartiana

Polychilos stobartiana, commonly known as the Stobart's rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, daisy-like blooms during the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
