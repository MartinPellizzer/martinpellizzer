Portulaca lanceolata var. versicolor

Portulaca lanceolata var. versicolor, commonly known as the variable purslane, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and stems, which help it retain water in arid environments. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers that can range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
