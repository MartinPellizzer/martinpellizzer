Potentilla Canescens F. Sadleri

Potentilla canescens f. sadleri is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit. This plant is a cultivated form of the common Potentilla canescens, which is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. Its unique foliage and long blooming period add both aesthetic and ecological value to garden spaces. Due to its ornamental appeal and limited availability, it is highly sought after by horticultural enthusiasts.
