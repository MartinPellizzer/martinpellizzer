Pilea cubensis

Pilea cubensis, commonly known as the money plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spathes, which contain the true flowers. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Pilea cubensis is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
