Pilosella tenuirhiza

Pilosella tenuirhiza, commonly known as slenderleaf goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant has finely hairy stems and leaves that are narrow and linear in shape. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the early spring.

Pilosella tenuirhiza is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils.
