Persoonia glaucescens

Persoonia glaucescens, commonly known as the silver-leafed pear, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.
