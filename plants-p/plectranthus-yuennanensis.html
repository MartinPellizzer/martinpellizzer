Plectranthus yuennanensis

Plectranthus yuennanensis, commonly known as the Yuennan plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves, often with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
