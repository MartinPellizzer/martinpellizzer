Pycnosorus pleiocephalus

Pycnosorus pleiocephalus, commonly known as the broad-leaved pycnosorus, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers.

Pycnosorus pleiocephalus is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
