Pleopeltis helenae

Pleopeltis helenae, commonly known as Helen's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
