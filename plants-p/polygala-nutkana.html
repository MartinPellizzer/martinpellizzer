Polygala nutkana

Polygala nutkana, commonly known as the Nutka milkvetch, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and are arranged in spikelike clusters.

Polygala nutkana is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system to access water.
