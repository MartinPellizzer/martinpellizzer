Papilionanthe teres

Papilionanthe teres, commonly known as the jade plant or Crassula ovata, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, typically during the spring and summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai cultivation.

The species is also valued for its ability to purify the air and its low maintenance requirements.
