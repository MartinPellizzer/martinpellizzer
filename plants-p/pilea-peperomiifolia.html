Pilea peperomiifolia

Pilea peperomiifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, waxy leaves that resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant has a trailing growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea peperomiifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
