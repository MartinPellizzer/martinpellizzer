Plectranthus botryosus

Plectranthus botryosus, commonly known as the woolly plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fuzzy gray-green foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple or violet. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, often reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
