Pelargonium dasyphyllum

Pelargonium dasyphyllum, commonly known as the Zebra Geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and purple patterns, resembling a zebra's coat. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
