Prunus serrulata f. masuyama

Prunus serrulata f. masuyama, commonly known as the Masuyama cherry, is a cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its double pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking visual display. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is particularly valued for its aesthetic appeal in traditional Japanese gardens.
