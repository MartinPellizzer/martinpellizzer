Polystichum aculeatum var. perelegans

Polystichum aculeatum var. perelegans, commonly known as the silver fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a deciduous fern native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. The plant features fronds that are typically silver-white in color due to the dense covering of trichomes on the underside. Its foliage is characterized by a distinctive zigzag pattern and a leathery texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
