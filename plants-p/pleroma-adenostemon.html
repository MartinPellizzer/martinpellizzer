Pleroma adenostemon

Pleroma adenostemon, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning, hence its common name. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Pleroma adenostemon is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
