Polevansia

Polevansia, commonly known as the sacred flame plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a fiery display. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
