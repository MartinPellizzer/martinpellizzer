Phyllanthus simplex var. filicaulis

Phyllanthus simplex var. filicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant features small, clustered flowers and has leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
