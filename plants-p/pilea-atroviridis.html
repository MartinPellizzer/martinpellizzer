Pilea atroviridis

Pilea atroviridis, commonly known as the Black Velvet Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, velvety leaves with a striking metallic sheen, and its foliage is often adorned with small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
