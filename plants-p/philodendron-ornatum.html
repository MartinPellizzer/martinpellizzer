Philodendron ornatum

Philodendron ornatum, commonly known as the heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with cream or white markings. The plant is an evergreen, climbing vine that can grow quite large if given adequate space and support. Its leaves are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by reddish berries.

Philodendron ornatum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
