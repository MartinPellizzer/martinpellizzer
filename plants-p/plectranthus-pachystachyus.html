Plectranthus pachystachyus

Plectranthus pachystachyus, commonly known as the woolly plectranthus, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its unique texture and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
