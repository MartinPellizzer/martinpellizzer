Peperomia barbata

Peperomia barbata, commonly known as the beard leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia barbata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
