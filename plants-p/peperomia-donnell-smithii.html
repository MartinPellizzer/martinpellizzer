Peperomia donnell-smithii

Peperomia donnell-smithii, commonly known as the Donnell-Smith peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy foliage with elliptical leaves that are often a deep green, sometimes with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is valued as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
