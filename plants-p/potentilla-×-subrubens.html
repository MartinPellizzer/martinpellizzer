Potentilla × subrubens

Potentilla × subrubens, commonly known as the red sandringen, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range from pink to red in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is typically dark green, and it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
