Pleione bilamellata

Pleione bilamellata, commonly known as the two-lamella pleione, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive two-lobed lip. The plant grows as a terrestrial orchid, often in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its evergreen leaves and flowering stems.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
