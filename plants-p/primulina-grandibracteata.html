Primulina grandibracteata

Primulina grandibracteata, commonly known as the giant bract primula, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy bracts that resemble petals, which are often brightly colored and serve to attract pollinators. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its compact, evergreen foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
