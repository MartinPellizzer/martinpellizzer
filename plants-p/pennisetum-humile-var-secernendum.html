Pennisetum humile var. secernendum

Pennisetum humile var. secernendum, commonly known as dwarf fountain grass, is a compact, ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a dense, fountain-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for both ornamental and low-maintenance landscaping.
