Panicum hochstetteri var. trichanthum

Panicum hochstetteri var. trichanthum, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a soft, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has fibrous roots and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
