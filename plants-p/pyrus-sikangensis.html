Pyrus sikangensis

Pyrus sikangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small white flowers. The tree produces pome fruits, which are typically harvested in late summer. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
