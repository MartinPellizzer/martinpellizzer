Piper cerronianum

Piper cerronianum, commonly known as the Cerrado pepper, is a shrub native to the Cerrado biome of Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a distinct, spicy aroma. Its small, green flowers are followed by black, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often found in moist, tropical environments.
