Phyllanthus roseopictus

Phyllanthus roseopictus, commonly known as the rose pictured phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
