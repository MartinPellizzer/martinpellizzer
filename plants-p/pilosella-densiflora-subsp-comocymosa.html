Pilosella densiflora subsp. comocymosa

Pilosella densiflora subsp.

comocymosa, commonly known as the denseflower hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a low-growing perennial herb with a mat-forming habit, characterized by its dense, woolly foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish to white flowers arranged in compact, cyme-like clusters. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
