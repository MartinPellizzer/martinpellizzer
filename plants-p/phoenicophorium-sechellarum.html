Phoenicophorium sechellarum

Phoenicophorium sechellarum, commonly known as the Seychelles fanflower, is a unique flowering plant native to the Seychelles.

It is characterized by its striking, fan-shaped inflorescences that consist of numerous small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with slender, branching stems and narrow, lanceolate leaves. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
