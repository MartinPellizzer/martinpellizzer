Piper crenulatibracteum

Piper crenulatibracteum, commonly known as the waxy leaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that grow in clusters. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains the seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance in botanical gardens.
