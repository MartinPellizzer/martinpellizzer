Pyrethrum filipendulifolium

Pyrethrum filipendulifolium, commonly known as the golden fleur de lis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that resemble a fleur de lis, with long, narrow petals. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its finely divided, fern-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pyrethrum filipendulifolium is also valued for its historical use in herbal medicine.
