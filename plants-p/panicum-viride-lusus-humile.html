Panicum viride lusus humile

Panicum viride lusus humile, commonly known as green panic grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, green leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense clumps. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes. Its small, spike-like inflorescences produce light green to golden flowers, adding visual interest.

It is relatively drought-tolerant and low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
