Pseudopilocereus machrisii

Pseudopilocereus machrisii, commonly known as the Machrisii cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stem and spiny areoles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for desert gardens and collections.
