Panicum andreanum

Panicum andreanum, commonly known as dwarf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, tufted clumps of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces fluffy, silver-white plumes that bloom from summer to fall, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscape design.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
