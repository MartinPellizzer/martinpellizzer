Panicum figarei

Panicum figarei, commonly known as the red panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and vibrant red or bronze-colored inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a slight bluish-green hue.

Panicum figarei is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
