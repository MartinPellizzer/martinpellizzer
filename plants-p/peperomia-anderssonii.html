Peperomia anderssonii

Peperomia anderssonii, commonly known as the Andes peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It features dense, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, which are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both gardens and indoor spaces.
