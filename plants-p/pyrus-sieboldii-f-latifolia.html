Pyrus sieboldii f. latifolia

Pyrus sieboldii f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf pear, is a cultivar of the Japanese white pear.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.

This variety is well-suited to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
