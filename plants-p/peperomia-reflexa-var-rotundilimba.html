Peperomia reflexa var. rotundilimba

Peperomia reflexa var. rotundilimba, commonly known as the Round-leaf Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are typically borne on short, upright spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
