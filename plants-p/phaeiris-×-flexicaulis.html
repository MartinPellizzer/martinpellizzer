Phaeiris × flexicaulis

Phaeiris × flexicaulis, commonly known as the hybrid phaeiris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Phaeiris corymbosa and Phaeiris multiflora.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, clustered inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
