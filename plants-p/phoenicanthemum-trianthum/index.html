Phoenicanthemum Trianthum

Phoenicanthemum trianthum is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, spiky flowers that range from yellow to orange, it adds a bold visual element to any garden. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice among gardeners. Its unique appearance has earned it the nickname "fire spike" due to the fiery color of its blooms. Despite its bold look, it is relatively easy to care for and can be a standout feature in both wild and cultivated landscapes.
