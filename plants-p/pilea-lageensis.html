Pilea lageensis

Pilea lageensis, commonly known as the Brazilian pilea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Pilea lageensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
