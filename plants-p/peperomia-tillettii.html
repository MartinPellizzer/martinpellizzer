Peperomia tillettii

Peperomia tillettii, commonly known as the Tiltlett's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, reddish-brown flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Peperomia tillettii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
