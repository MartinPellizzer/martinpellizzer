Phaseoloides pulchrum

Phaseoloides pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful bean vine, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant bears pods that mature into bright orange, spindle-shaped fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and nutritional benefits, it is cultivated in gardens and for food in some regions.
