Polygonum persicaria var. ruderale

Polygonum persicaria var. ruderale, commonly known as common smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows up to 1 meter tall, featuring alternate, lance-shaped leaves with reddish veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of its stems. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.
