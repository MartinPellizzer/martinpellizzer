Piper sagittifer

Piper sagittifer, commonly known as the arrowleaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped, giving it its common name, and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various cultural practices.
