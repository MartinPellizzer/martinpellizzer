Polygonum persicarioides

Polygonum persicarioides, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist or wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base, and it produces small, clustered flowers in late spring to early summer. Its bright red or pink flowers are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
