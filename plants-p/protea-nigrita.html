Protea nigrita

Protea nigrita, commonly known as the black protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, bracts that enclose the true flowers, which are small and yellow. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Protea nigrita is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, often with a bushy appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also an important species in local ecosystems.
