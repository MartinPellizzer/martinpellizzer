Polygala xanti

Polygala xanti, commonly known as the Cape Flats polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
