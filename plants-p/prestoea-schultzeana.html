Prestoea schultzeana

Prestoea schultzeana, commonly known as the Schultze's ceiba, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its massive, buttressed roots and a straight, tall trunk that can reach heights of up to 30 meters. The tree produces large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody pods containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Prestoea schultzeana plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
