Pleioblastus chino f. nebulosus

Pleioblastus chino f. nebulosus, commonly known as the clouded bamboo, is a variant of the Chino bamboo species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pale green to white variegated foliage that gives it a misty appearance. The plant has slender, upright culms that are often used in ornamental gardening for their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for landscaping and bonsai cultivation.
