Pyrethrum lanuginosum

Pyrethrum lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces fine, delicate blooms that are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Pyrethrum lanuginosum is also valued for its potential use in natural pest control due to its insecticidal properties.
