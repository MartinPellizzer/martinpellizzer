Paraphlomis javanica var. pteropoda

Paraphlomis javanica var. pteropoda, commonly known as the broad-leaved paraphlomis, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its lanceolate leaves with a distinctive broad base and hairy surfaces. It produces small, pinkish flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
