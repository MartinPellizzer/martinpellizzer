Pentagramma

Pentagramma, commonly known as the star moss, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Pentagrammaceae.

These plants are typically small, herbaceous perennials that thrive in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. They are characterized by their delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are usually alternate and have a distinctive, often fern-like appearance.

Pentagramma species are valued in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
