Piper ciniflonis

Piper ciniflonis, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical berries.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine and are known for their pungent flavor.
