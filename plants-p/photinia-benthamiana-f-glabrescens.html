Photinia benthamiana f. glabrescens

Photinia benthamiana f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed photinia, is a compact shrub characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is native to Australia and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a variety of garden settings.
