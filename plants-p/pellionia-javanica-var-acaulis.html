Pellionia javanica var. acaulis

Pellionia javanica var. acaulis, commonly known as the stemless pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
