Platymiscium floribundum var. majus

Platymiscium floribundum var. majus, commonly known as the large-flowered platymiscium, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark purple to black berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in tropical regions.
