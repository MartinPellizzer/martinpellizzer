Pleurothallis vargasii

Pleurothallis vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
