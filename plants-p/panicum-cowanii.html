Panicum cowanii

Panicum cowanii, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fluffy, silver-tan seed heads that resemble small clouds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Silver panicum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
