Paphiopedilum ooii

Paphiopedilum ooii, commonly known as the golden mottled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features a robust, hairy rhizome and produces large, showy flowers with a unique, lip-shaped petal that resembles a pouch. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity. Its flowers are typically yellow with orange and green markings, making it a striking ornamental plant.

Paphiopedilum ooii is prized for its longevity and ease of cultivation in controlled environments.
