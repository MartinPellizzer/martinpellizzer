Philibertia multiflora

Philibertia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered filbert, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it is known for its ability to thrive in dry conditions.

Philibertia multiflora is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
