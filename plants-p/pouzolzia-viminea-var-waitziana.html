Pouzolzia viminea var. waitziana

Pouzolzia viminea var. waitziana, commonly known as the slender pouzolzia, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically alternate and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical structure makes it well-adapted for epiphytic or terrestrial growth in diverse ecosystems.
