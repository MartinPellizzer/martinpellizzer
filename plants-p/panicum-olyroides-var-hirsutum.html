Panicum olyroides var. hirsutum

Panicum olyroides var. hirsutum, commonly known as hirsute bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its coarse, hairy foliage and upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its flowering spikes are usually tan to brown, and it is often used in native plant gardens for erosion control.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
