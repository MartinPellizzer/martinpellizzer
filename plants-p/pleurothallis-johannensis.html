Pleurothallis johannensis

Pleurothallis johannensis, commonly known as the Johann's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in high-altitude regions with cool temperatures and high humidity.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant for cultivation.
