Pecteilis Delavayi

Pecteilis delavayi is a rare and fascinating flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China. It is known for its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. This species belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes many bell-shaped flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
