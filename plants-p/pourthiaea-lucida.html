Pourthiaea lucida

Pourthiaea lucida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered pincushion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
