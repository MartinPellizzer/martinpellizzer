Polymnia canadensis

Polymnia canadensis, commonly known as Canadian polymnia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats. The plant features basal leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with smooth or slightly hairy surfaces. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months.

The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and riverbanks.
