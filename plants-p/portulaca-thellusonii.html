Portulaca thellusonii

Portulaca thellusonii, commonly known as the desert purslane, is a low-growing annual plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is adapted to survive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements. Its thick stems and leaves store water, allowing it to thrive in harsh conditions.

It is often found in sandy soils and is a common weed in disturbed areas.
