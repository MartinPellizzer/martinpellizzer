Paullinia lactescens

Paullinia lactescens, commonly known as the Brazilian soursop or caipira.

It is a member of the Sapindaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring elliptical leaves with a smooth surface. The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
