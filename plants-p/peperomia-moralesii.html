Peperomia moralesii

Peperomia moralesii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy, and velvety leaves that are typically deep green with a slight purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for container cultivation.
