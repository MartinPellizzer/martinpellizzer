Pyrostria macrophylla var. grandistipula

Pyrostria macrophylla var. grandistipula, commonly known as the giant fern tree, is a striking member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant features a thick, sturdy trunk with a rough, fibrous bark. Its fronds are typically bi-pinnate, with a distinctive pale underside that contrasts with the dark green upper surface.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.
