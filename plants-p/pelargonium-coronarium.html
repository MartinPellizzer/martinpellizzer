Pelargonium coronarium

Pelargonium coronarium, commonly known as the crown geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium coronarium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
