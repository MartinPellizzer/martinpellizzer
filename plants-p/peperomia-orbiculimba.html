Peperomia orbiculimba

Peperomia orbiculimba, commonly known as the rounded peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia orbiculimba is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
