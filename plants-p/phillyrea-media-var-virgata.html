Phillyrea media var. virgata

Phillyrea media var. virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that is typically a silvery-gray in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by spherical, red-brown berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for landscaping in mild climates.
