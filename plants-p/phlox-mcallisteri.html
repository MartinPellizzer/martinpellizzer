Phlox mcallisteri

Phlox mcallisteri, commonly known as the Mountain Phlox, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Appalachian Mountains.

It produces showy clusters of pink to purple flowers in early to mid-summer. The plant has upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Phlox mcallisteri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in native plant gardens.
