Panicum microbachne

Panicum microbachne, commonly known as little bluestem, is a warm-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescence that turns golden in the fall. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. It plays a vital role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

Panicum microbachne is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
