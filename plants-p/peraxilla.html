Peraxilla

Peraxilla, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, with several species found in Madagascar and mainland Africa. Peraxilla species are typically shrubs or small trees, and they have a deep root system that helps them thrive in poor soils.
