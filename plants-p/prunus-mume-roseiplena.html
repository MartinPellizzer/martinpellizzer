Prunus mume roseiplena

Prunus mume roseiplena, commonly known as the pink-flowered Chinese plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces small, edible fruits known as mume or Chinese plums, which are used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges, while its bark is smooth and reddish-brown.

Prunus mume roseiplena is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blossoms.
