Pandanus petrosus

Pandanus petrosus, commonly known as the rock pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its roots are fibrous and can be used for making ropes and mats.

The rock pandanus thrives in coastal and rocky areas, where it is adapted to salty and windy conditions.
