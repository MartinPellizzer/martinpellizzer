Polygonum sagittatum var. sericeum

Polygonum sagittatum var. sericeum, commonly known as the silky arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late summer. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes and stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread via rhizomes make it an effective wetland stabilizer.
