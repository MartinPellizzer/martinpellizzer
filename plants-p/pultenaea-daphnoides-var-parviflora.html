Pultenaea daphnoides var. parviflora

Pultenaea daphnoides var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are usually grey-green in color, with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to cream flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
