Pennisetum blepharideum

Pennisetum blepharideum, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum blepharideum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract wildlife.
