Periandra mediterranea var. linearifoliolata

Periandra mediterranea var. linearifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved periandra, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval leaves with a distinctive, finely toothed margin. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its compact form and delicate foliage.
