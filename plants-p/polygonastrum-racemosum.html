Polygonastrum racemosum

Polygonastrum racemosum, commonly known as the racemose polygonum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that appear in racemose inflorescences. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a slightly hairy surface.

Polygonastrum racemosum is often found in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks.
