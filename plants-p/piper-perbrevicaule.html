Piper perbrevicaule

Piper perbrevicaule, commonly known as the short-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, pale green flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in shaded gardens.
