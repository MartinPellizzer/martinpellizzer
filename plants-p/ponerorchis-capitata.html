Ponerorchis capitata

Ponerorchis capitata, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the substrate, and it often grows epiphytically on trees.

Ponerorchis capitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens.
