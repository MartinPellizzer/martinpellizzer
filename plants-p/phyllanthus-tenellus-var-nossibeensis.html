Phyllanthus tenellus var. nossibeensis

Phyllanthus tenellus var. nossibeensis, commonly known as the Nossibe phyllanthus, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its propagation in coastal environments.
