Polemonium glabrum

Polemonium glabrum, commonly known as smooth bluebeard, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and slightly hairy, with a dark green upper surface. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and produces showy, violet-blue flowers in late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
