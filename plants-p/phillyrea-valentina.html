Phillyrea valentina

Phillyrea valentina, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, spherical berries. Phillyrea valentina is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
