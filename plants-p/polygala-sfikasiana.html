Polygala sfikasiana

Polygala sfikasiana, commonly known as the Sfikasian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, often pale yellow or greenish flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
