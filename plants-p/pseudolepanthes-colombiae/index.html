Pseudolepanthes Colombiae

Pseudolepanthes colombiae is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia. This species belongs to the orchid family, known for its diverse and often colorful blooms. Its unique appearance and limited habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments at high elevations. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
