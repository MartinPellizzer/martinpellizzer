Pultenaea laxiflora var. pilosa

Pultenaea laxiflora var. pilosa, commonly known as the silky pultenaea, is a flowering shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, grey-green foliage covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and rocky areas.

The silky pultenaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
