Pleconax conica var. carpathae

Pleconax conica var. carpathae, commonly known as the Carpathian pinecone plant, is a unique variant of the pleconax genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive conical shape and dense, spiny foliage that resembles a pine cone. This plant is native to the Carpathian Mountains and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It exhibits a slow growth rate and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for horticultural and ecological studies.
