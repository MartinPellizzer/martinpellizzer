Pachira brevipes

Pachira brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed pachira, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant features thick, fleshy roots and smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and oval in shape, with a glossy surface.

Pachira brevipes produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds.
