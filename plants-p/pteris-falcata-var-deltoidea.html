Pteris falcata var. deltoidea

Pteris falcata var. deltoidea, commonly known as the delta maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bright green with a contrasting lighter green midrib. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense mats. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
