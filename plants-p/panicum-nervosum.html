Panicum nervosum

Panicum nervosum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.

Panicum nervosum is also valued for its role in ecological systems, providing food and habitat for various insects and small animals.
