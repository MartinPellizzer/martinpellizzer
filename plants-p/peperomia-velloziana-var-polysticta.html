Peperomia velloziana var. polysticta

Peperomia velloziana var. polysticta, commonly known as the spotted peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a distinctive spotted or mottled pattern in shades of green, red, and brown. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a striking addition to any plant collection.
