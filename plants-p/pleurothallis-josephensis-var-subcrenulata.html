Pleurothallis josephensis var. subcrenulata

Pleurothallis josephensis var. subcrenulata, commonly known as the Joseph's pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant for cultivation.
