Phyllanthus concolor

Phyllanthus concolor, commonly known as the smoothleaf phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant has simple, opposite, and usually elliptical leaves with a smooth surface. Its small flowers are greenish-white and arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round fruits that are yellow when mature.

Phyllanthus concolor is often found in moist, shady areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
