Pleurothallis violaceomaculata

Pleurothallis violaceomaculata, commonly known as the violet-spotted pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and violet. Its flowers are often adorned with distinctive purple or violet markings, which give it its common name.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
