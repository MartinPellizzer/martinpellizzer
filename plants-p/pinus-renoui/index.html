Pinus Renoui

Pinus renoui is a rare and unique species of pine native to the mountainous regions of China. This coniferous tree is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from habitat loss. Studying Pinus renoui offers valuable insights into biodiversity and conservation efforts in alpine ecosystems.
