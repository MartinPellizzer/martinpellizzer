Pithecellobium rufescens

Pithecellobium rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed pisonia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with small, rounded leaflets.
