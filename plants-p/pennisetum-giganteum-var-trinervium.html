Pennisetum giganteum var. trinervium

Pennisetum giganteum var. trinervium, commonly known as giant feather reed, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce feathery plumes during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance and ability to attract wildlife.
