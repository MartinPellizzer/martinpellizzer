Phyllanthus sanatanadharmae

Phyllanthus sanatanadharmae, commonly known as the Sanatanadharma plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Phyllanthaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South and Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, with five petals, and it produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
