Polystachya polyphylla

Polystachya polyphylla, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Polystachya polyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
