Pelargonium odoratissimum

Pelargonium odoratissimum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with rounded, succulent leaves and produces clusters of pink or red flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and pleasant scent, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
