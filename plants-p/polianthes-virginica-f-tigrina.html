Polianthes virginica f. tigrina

Polianthes virginica f. tigrina, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a striking hybrid orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features tall, branched stems with large, showy flowers that have a distinctive striped pattern resembling a tiger's coat. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant, violet-blue petals with contrasting yellow and brown markings. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is valued for its vibrant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
