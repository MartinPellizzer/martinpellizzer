Pavonia dregei

Pavonia dregei, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Pavonia dregei is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
