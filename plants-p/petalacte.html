Petalacte

Petalacte, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features striking, translucent petals that resemble ghostly apparitions, giving it its ethereal appearance. The plant has a short, thick stem and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom at night. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Petalacte is highly sought after by collectors due to its rarity and unusual aesthetic.
