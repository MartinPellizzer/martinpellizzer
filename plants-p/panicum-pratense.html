Panicum pratense

Panicum pratense, commonly known as tall ironweed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that can reach heights of up to 6 feet. The plant produces clusters of purple to pink plumes that bloom in late summer and early fall. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum pratense is often found in moist to wet soils in meadows, marshes, and along stream banks.
