Pleurothallis pfavii

Pleurothallis pfavii, commonly known as the Pink Lady Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of pink and white, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique appearance.
