Pinus dalatensis var. procera

Pinus dalatensis var. procera, commonly known as the Dalat pine, is a variant of the Dalat pine native to Vietnam.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its tall, slender form and long, needle-like leaves. The tree produces cones that are typically reddish-brown in color and contain winged seeds. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape tree.
