Prescottia villenarum

Prescottia villenarum, commonly known as the Vallenar's pincushion, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

Prescottia villenarum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
