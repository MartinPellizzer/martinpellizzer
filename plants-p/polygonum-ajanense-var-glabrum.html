Polygonum ajanense var. glabrum

Polygonum ajanense var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features erect, branched stems that are covered with smooth, glabrous surfaces. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a slightly hairy underside.

It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks, often forming dense stands that can dominate local vegetation.
