Peperomia rotundifolia var. obcordata

Peperomia rotundifolia var. obcordata, commonly known as the heart-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tabletop displays.
