Phytocrene loheri

Phytocrene loheri, commonly known as the silverleaf phytocrene, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
