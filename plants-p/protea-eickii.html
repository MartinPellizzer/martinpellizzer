Protea eickii

Protea eickii, commonly known as the Eickii protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of a central cone-like structure surrounded by a cluster of bracts. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a distinctive white or pale pink center. Native to the Western Cape region, it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun exposure.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
