Penicillaria willdenowii

Penicillaria willdenowii, commonly known as the African rock cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Penicillaria willdenowii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
