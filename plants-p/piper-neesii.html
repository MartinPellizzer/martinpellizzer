Piper neesii

Piper neesii, commonly known as the Nees's pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

Piper neesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has potential medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
