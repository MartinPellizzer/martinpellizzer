Penstemon Deustus Subsp. Typicus

Penstemon deustus subsp. typicus is a unique member of the gentian family known for its striking floral displays. This subspecies thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers range from blue to violet, adding vibrant color to its native habitat. The plant is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
