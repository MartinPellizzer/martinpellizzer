Peperomia falcata

Peperomia falcata, commonly known as the Shingle Scale Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, upright rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia falcata is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
