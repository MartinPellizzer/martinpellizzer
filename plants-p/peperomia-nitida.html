Peperomia nitida

Peperomia nitida, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or white, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia nitida is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor environments.
