Pleurothallis fritzii

Pleurothallis fritzii, commonly known as the Fritz's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
