Portulaca meyeri

Portulaca meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's moss rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period add ornamental value to gardens.
