Pholidota leveilleana

Pholidota leveilleana, commonly known as the African boxthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
