Philodendron delannayi

Philodendron delannayi, commonly known as the Delannay's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by green, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
