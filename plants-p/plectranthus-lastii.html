Plectranthus lastii

Plectranthus lastii, commonly known as African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Plectranthus lastii is popular as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
