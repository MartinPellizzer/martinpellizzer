Photinia bodinieri

Photinia bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, bright red berries that persist through winter. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and winter interest make it suitable for hedges or container gardening.
