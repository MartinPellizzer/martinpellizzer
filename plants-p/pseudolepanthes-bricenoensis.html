Pseudolepanthes bricenoensis

Pseudolepanthes bricenoensis, commonly known as the Briceno's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are small and delicate, with a distinctive slipper-like shape that resembles a pouch.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its specific habitat requirements and limited distribution.
