Panicum metzii

Panicum metzii, commonly known as Mexican dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, fluffy seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardens. It is drought-tolerant and often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Panicum metzii is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
