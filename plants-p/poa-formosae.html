Poa formosae

Poa formosae, commonly known as the Formosa bentgrass, is a species of grass native to Taiwan.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, humid climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
