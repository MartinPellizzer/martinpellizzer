Phthirusa portoricensis

Phthirusa portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican phthiracid, is a species of plant in the family Phthiracaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean island of Puerto Rico and is characterized by its small, shrub-like growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
