Primula edgeworthii

Primula edgeworthii, commonly known as the Edgeworth's primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Primula edgeworthii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued in horticulture for its early spring blooms.
