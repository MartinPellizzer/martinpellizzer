Phlomis × cytherea

Phlomis × cytherea, commonly known as the hybrid blanketflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features sturdy, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in late summer.

Phlomis × cytherea is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it a popular choice in xeriscaping.
