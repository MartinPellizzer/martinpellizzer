Polylobium

Polylobium, commonly known as the featherleaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are usually narrow and have a distinctive lobed or segmented appearance. Polylobium species are often found in moist, temperate environments.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded areas.
