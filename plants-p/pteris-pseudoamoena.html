Pteris pseudoamoena

Pteris pseudoamoena, commonly known as the crested water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, crested fronds that give it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in humid forests. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a soft, feathery texture.

Pteris pseudoamoena is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in water gardens or as a decorative houseplant.
