Proselias globosus

Proselias globosus, commonly known as the globe lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical flower heads, which are composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms during the summer months.

Proselias globosus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
