Piper positum

Piper positum, commonly known as the African pepper or African long pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants like black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is cultivated for its medicinal and culinary value.
