Phyllostachys edulis f. aureostriata

Phyllostachys edulis f. aureostriata, commonly known as the golden-striped bamboo, is a variant of the giant bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking yellow stripes on the culms, which contrast beautifully with its green foliage. This ornamental bamboo is native to eastern Asia and thrives in temperate climates. It grows in dense clumps and can reach heights of up to 10 meters.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden design.
