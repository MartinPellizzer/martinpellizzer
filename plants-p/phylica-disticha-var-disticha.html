Phylica disticha var. disticha

Phylica disticha var. disticha, commonly known as the silver leaf phylica, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
