Penstemon linarioides var. linarioides

Penstemon linarioides var. linarioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf beardtongue, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy, tubular flowers range in color from blue to purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is well-suited for xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
