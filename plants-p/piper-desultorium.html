Piper desultorium

Piper desultorium, commonly known as the variable pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly rough texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes and are followed by small, three-lobed berries.

Piper desultorium is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
