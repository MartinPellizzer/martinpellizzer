Parasenecio koualapensis

Parasenecio koualapensis, commonly known as the Koualapensian ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Cameroon.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats, thriving in cool and moist environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with showy petals that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially vulnerable species.
