Poa longiradiata

Poa longiradiata, commonly known as the long-rayed bentgrass, is a species of grass native to southern South America.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically arranged in a loose panicle. Its leaves are narrow with a distinct midrib and exhibit a slightly waxy coating.

Poa longiradiata is often found in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and streambanks.
