Panicum queenslandicum var. acuminatum

Panicum queenslandicum var. acuminatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bluestem, is a grass species native to Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often visible during the flowering season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in natural habitats.
