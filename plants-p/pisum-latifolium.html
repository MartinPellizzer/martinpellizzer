Pisum latifolium

Pisum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf pea, is a species of legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods containing several seeds, which are typically round and beige in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Pisum latifolium is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
