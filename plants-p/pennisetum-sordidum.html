Pennisetum sordidum

Pennisetum sordidum, commonly known as black beard grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black foliage and tufted inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is drought-tolerant. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, while the stems are often reddish or purple in color.

Pennisetum sordidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
