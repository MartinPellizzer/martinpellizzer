Plectranthus mollis

Plectranthus mollis, commonly known as velvetleaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus mollis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
