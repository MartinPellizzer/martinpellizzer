Piper miniatum

Piper miniatum, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a small shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, dark green leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers that develop into peppercorns. These peppercorns are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
