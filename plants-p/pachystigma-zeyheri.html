Pachystigma zeyheri

Pachystigma zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's pachystigma, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
