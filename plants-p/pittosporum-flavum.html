Pittosporum flavum

Pittosporum flavum, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.

Pittosporum flavum prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
