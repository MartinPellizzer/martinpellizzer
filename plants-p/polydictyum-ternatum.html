Polydictyum ternatum

Polydictyum ternatum, commonly known as the three-leafed water plant, is a small aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has whorled leaves that are often submerged, with some leaves reaching the water surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in late summer.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
