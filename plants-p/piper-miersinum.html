Piper miersinum

Piper miersinum, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated margin. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in terminal spikes, and it produces tiny, peppery fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of mild spices.
