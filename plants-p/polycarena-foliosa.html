Polycarena foliosa

Polycarena foliosa, commonly known as the feather grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender blades that form a dense, low-growing mat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikelets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Polycarena foliosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms.
