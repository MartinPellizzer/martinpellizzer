Pittosporum stenopetalum

Pittosporum stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a distinct, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and public spaces in temperate climates.
