Primulina balansae

Primulina balansae, commonly known as the Balansae African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for indirect light and consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts for its compact size and ease of care.
