Pandanus mc-keei

Pandanus mc-keei, commonly known as the Hawaiian pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the Hawaiian Islands and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the tips of the leaves. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found growing in sandy soils.

The Hawaiian pandanus is an important component of the island's unique flora and plays a role in local ecosystems.
