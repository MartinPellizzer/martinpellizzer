Parodia aureicentra var. muhrii

Parodia aureicentra var. muhrii, commonly known as the Muhrii Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to South America.

It features a dense, spiny coating of white to pale yellow spines, giving it a distinctive pincushion appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are often followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
