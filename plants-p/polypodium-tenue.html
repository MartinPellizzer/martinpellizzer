Polypodium tenue

Polypodium tenue, commonly known as the slender holly fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous small, lance-shaped leaves.

Polypodium tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
