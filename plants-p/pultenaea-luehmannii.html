Pultenaea luehmannii

Pultenaea luehmannii, commonly known as the Luehmann's bushveld pea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathland and woodland habitats.

Pultenaea luehmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
