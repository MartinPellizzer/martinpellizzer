Pellionia bulbifera var. burmanicum

Pellionia bulbifera var. burmanicum, commonly known as the Burmese bulb fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and unique bulb-like structures that develop at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath its leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
