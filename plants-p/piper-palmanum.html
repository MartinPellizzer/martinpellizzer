Piper palmanum

Piper palmanum, commonly known as the Palmanum pepper or white pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often require support. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the small green berries turn red when mature.

The fruit is used to produce a type of pepper known for its mild flavor and aromatic qualities.
