Pseudo-oenothera virginiana

Pseudo-oenothera virginiana, commonly known as the Virginia evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

Pseudo-oenothera virginiana is known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
