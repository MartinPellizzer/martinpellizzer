Phaeiris × violivenosa

Phaeiris × violivenosa, commonly known as the hybrid violet.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring, typically in shades of purple and violet. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.
