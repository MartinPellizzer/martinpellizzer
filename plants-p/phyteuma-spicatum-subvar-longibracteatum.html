Phyteuma spicatum subvar. longibracteatum

Phyteuma spicatum subvar. longibracteatum, commonly known as the long-bract phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained, sunny habitats.
