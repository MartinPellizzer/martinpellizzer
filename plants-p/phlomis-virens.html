Phlomis virens

Phlomis virens, commonly known as green everlasting, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, gray-green foliage. The plant produces spikes of blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
