Potentilla recta proles tuberosa

Potentilla recta proles tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features upright stems with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink or red, depending on the cultivar. The plant produces tuberous roots that serve as storage organs, enabling it to survive harsh winter conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and elliptical, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
