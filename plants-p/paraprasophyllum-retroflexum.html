Paraprasophyllum retroflexum

Paraprasophyllum retroflexum, commonly known as the retroflex pincushion orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, recurved lip that aids in pollination by specific insect species.
