Pentas elatior

Pentas elatior, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Pentas elatior is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
