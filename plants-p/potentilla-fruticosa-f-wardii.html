Potentilla fruticosa f. wardii

Potentilla fruticosa f. wardii, commonly known as the Ward's cinquefoil, is a shrub native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage of bright green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in tundra and alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in cold climates.
