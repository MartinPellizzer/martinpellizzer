Polygala vilhelmii

Polygala vilhelmii, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often branched inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale blue, with a unique structure that resembles a spire.
