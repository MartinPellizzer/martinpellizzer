Pellegriniodendron

Pellegriniodendron, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its bark is thick and rough. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
