Pratia reniformis

Pratia reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped pratia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped leaves, which are usually bright green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, typically blooming during the cooler months. Pratia reniformis is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands.

It is a perennial herb that is well adapted to the dry and sunny conditions of its native environment.
