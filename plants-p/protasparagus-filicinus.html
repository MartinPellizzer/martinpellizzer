Protasparagus filicinus

Protasparagus filicinus, commonly known as the fern asparagus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asparagaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and slender, climbing stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
