Peperomia cubensis

Peperomia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped, with a deep green color and a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia cubensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
