Pennisetum davyi

Pennisetum davyi, commonly known as the silver grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-blue foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features narrow, upright leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often pale green or white in color.

Pennisetum davyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
