Pelargonium aconitophyllum var. medium

Pelargonium aconitophyllum var. medium, commonly known as the medium stonecrop geranium, is a succulent perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are somewhat triangular in shape, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanists.
