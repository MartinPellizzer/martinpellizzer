Parodia multicostata

Parodia multicostata, commonly known as the Star Cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped clusters of cylindrical stems that branch out in a radial pattern. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
