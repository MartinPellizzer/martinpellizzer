Populus simonii var. ovata

Populus simonii var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The tree produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, a common trait among poplar species. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in landscaping due to its fast growth and attractive foliage.
