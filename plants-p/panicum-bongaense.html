Panicum bongaense

Panicum bongaense, commonly known as the Bonga grass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It is often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
