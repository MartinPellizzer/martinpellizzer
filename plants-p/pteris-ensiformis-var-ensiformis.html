Pteris ensiformis var. ensiformis

Pteris ensiformis var. ensiformis, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, serrated fronds that grow in a compact, upright form. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with the rhizome producing both roots and new fronds. Its leaves are typically green and glossy, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
