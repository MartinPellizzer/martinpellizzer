Planchonella polyneura

Planchonella polyneura, commonly known as the rough-leaved hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant has simple, opposite, and often rough-textured leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow or pale yellow, with five petals and are borne in clusters.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
