Pomatocalpa loratum

Pomatocalpa loratum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
