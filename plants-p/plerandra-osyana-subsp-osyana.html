Plerandra osyana subsp. osyana

Plerandra osyana subsp.

osyana, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

The plant features a slender, often branched stem and produces striking golden-yellow flowers during the wet season. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Botanically, it is characterized by its unique inflorescence and specialized root system adapted to its humid habitat.
