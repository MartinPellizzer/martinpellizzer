Plectranthus tristis

Plectranthus tristis, commonly known as the weeping plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus tristis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
