Parasenecio sinicus

Parasenecio sinicus, commonly known as the Chinese parasenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
