Polystachya monophylla

Polystachya monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow, and leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. Its unique feature is the presence of a single, prominent leaflet on each frond, giving it its common name.

Polystachya monophylla is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
