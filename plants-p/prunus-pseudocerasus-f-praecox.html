Prunus pseudocerasus f. praecox

Prunus pseudocerasus f. praecox, commonly known as the early-flowering Chinese date plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is a cultivar of the Prunus pseudocerasus species, characterized by its early blooming habit, with flowers appearing before the leaves in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, with a sweet and tangy flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant flowers and attractive foliage.
