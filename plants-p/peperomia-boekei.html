Peperomia boekei

Peperomia boekei, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia boekei is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its tolerance for moderate watering and humidity.
