Penstemon Deustus Var. Variabilis

Variable beardtongue, scientifically known as Penstemon deustus var. variabilis, is a striking member of the gentian family native to the western United States. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, attracting a variety of pollinators. Its adaptability to different soil types and climates makes it a popular choice for native gardens and conservation efforts. The variable beardtongue's name reflects its ability to exhibit diverse physical traits depending on its environment. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated species in the wild.
