Polygala neaei

Polygala neaei, commonly known as the Neaei's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. It has small, linear leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
