Penstemon × compactus

Penstemon × compactus, commonly known as the compact penstemon, is a hybrid species native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact form and drought tolerance contribute to its adaptability in various garden settings.
