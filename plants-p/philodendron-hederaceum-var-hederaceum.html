Philodendron hederaceum var. hederaceum

Philodendron hederaceum var. hederaceum, commonly known as the heartleaf philodendron, is a popular houseplant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are typically variegated with light green and white patterns. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ability to climb or trail makes it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
