Primula brevis

Primula brevis, commonly known as the short primrose, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing clusters of pale yellow to white petals. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, often with a reddish or purplish hue on the underside.

Primula brevis thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest areas.
