Phaseoloides atropurpureum

Phaseoloides atropurpureum, commonly known as purple bean or purple gram, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds used in various cuisines. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires a long growing season.

Its botanical features make it a valuable crop for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
