Prunus tatsienensis

Prunus tatsienensis, commonly known as the Chinese flowering cherry, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, particularly during spring when it produces clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Prunus tatsienensis is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
