Parsonsia praeruptis

Parsonsia praeruptis, commonly known as the red-flowered parsonsia, is a striking tropical plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, known for its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from red to orange. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fleshy, berry-like fruits that are orange-red when mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in coastal and sandy soils.

Parsonsia praeruptis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
