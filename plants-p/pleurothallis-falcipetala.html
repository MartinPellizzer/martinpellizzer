Pleurothallis falcipetala

Pleurothallis falcipetala, commonly known as the sickle-armed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme of small, showy blooms.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
