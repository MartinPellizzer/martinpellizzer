Picea likiangensis var. montigena

Picea likiangensis var. montigena, commonly known as the Yunnan spruce, is a rare and endemic coniferous tree native to the high altitudes of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in spirals along the branches. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soils, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for ecological and botanical study.
