Plectranthus mafiensis

Plectranthus mafiensis, commonly known as the Mafikeng plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
