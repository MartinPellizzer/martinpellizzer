Phalaenopsis mysorensis

Phalaenopsis mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive greenish or yellowish lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Phalaenopsis mysorensis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
