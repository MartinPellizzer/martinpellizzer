Phillyrea latifolia subsp. rodriguezii

Phillyrea latifolia subsp.

rodriguezii, commonly known as Rodriguez's broadleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with small, elliptical leaves that have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
