Pleurothallis fuscata

Pleurothallis fuscata, commonly known as the dark pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Pleurothallis fuscata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
