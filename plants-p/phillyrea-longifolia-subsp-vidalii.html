Phillyrea longifolia subsp. vidalii

Phillyrea longifolia subsp.

vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal's Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces white, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.
