Polypodium foliolosum

Polypodium foliolosum, commonly known as the slender cliff fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately compound leaves that are often finely divided. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizome is creeping and produces clusters of sporangia on the underside of the fronds.

Polypodium foliolosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
