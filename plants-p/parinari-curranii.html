Parinari curranii

Parinari curranii, commonly known as the African apple or African peach, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its shade and aesthetic appeal.
