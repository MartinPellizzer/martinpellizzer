Phaseoloides nitidum

Phaseoloides nitidum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety hair, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by pods containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in some regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
