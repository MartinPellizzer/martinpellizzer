Petrina pruinosa

Petrina pruinosa, commonly known as the silverleaf petrina, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
