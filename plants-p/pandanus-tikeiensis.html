Pandanus tikeiensis

Pandanus tikeiensis, commonly known as the Tikei pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the island of Tikei in the French Polynesian Society's territory. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It thrives in coastal and lowland areas, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

The inflorescence consists of a long, branched cluster of small, fragrant flowers that attract various pollinators.
