Peperomia mandonii

Peperomia mandonii, commonly known as the Mandon's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features dense, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia mandonii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
