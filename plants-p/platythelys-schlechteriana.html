Platythelys schlechteriana

Platythelys schlechteriana, commonly known as the Schlechter's flat-leaved orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
