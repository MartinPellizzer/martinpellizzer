Podostemum atrichum

Podostemum atrichum, commonly known as the smooth podostemum, is a small aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that are adapted for life in fast-flowing streams and rivers. The plant typically grows in shallow, rocky habitats where it anchors itself to submerged stones. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often floating on the water surface.

Podostemum atrichum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small invertebrates.
