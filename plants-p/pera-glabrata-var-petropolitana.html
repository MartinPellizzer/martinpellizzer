Pera glabrata var. petropolitana

Pera glabrata var. petropolitana, commonly known as the Petropolitana pera, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glabrous surface.
