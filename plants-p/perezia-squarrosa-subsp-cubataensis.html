Perezia squarrosa subsp. cubataensis

Perezia squarrosa subsp.

cubataensis, commonly known as the Cuban perezia, is a flowering plant native to Cuba. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit.
