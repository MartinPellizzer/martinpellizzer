Peperomia pseudovariegata var. pseudovariegata

Peperomia pseudovariegata var. pseudovariegata, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with striking variegation of white, silver, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters, typically in shades of red or purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for indoor cultivation or as a tabletop plant.
