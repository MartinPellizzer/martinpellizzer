Pennisetum articulare

Pennisetum articulare, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color and produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum articulare is often used in landscaping for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
