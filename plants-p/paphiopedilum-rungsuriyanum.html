Paphiopedilum rungsuriyanum

Paphiopedilum rungsuriyanum, commonly known as the Thai lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a unique, pouch-like lip that is often brightly colored, typically in shades of yellow or orange. The plant grows with a pseudobulb and produces showy, fragrant flowers during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its exotic blooms.
