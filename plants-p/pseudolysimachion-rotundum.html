Pseudolysimachion rotundum

Pseudolysimachion rotundum, commonly known as the roundleaf pseudolysimachion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features round, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. Its small, pale blue or violet flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Pseudolysimachion rotundum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
