Prunus versteeghii

Prunus versteeghii, commonly known as the Cape sugar plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine. Prunus versteeghii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
