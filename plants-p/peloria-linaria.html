Peloria linaria

Peloria linaria, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, lantern-shaped seed heads that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, typical of the daisy family.

Peloria linaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
