Pyrenacantha chlorantha var. sambiranensis

Pyrenacantha chlorantha var. sambiranensis, commonly known as the Sambiranensis Pyrenacantha, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves, which are often marked with distinctive white or yellow veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
