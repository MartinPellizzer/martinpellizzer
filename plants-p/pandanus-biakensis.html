Pandanus biakensis

Pandanus biakensis, commonly known as the Biak pandanus, is a species of palm native to the island of Biak in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant thrives in coastal and lowland areas, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its reproductive structures include a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Pandanus genus.

The Biak pandanus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
