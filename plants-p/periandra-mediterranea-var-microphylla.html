Periandra mediterranea var. microphylla

Periandra mediterranea var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved Mediterranean periandra, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for Mediterranean-style landscaping.
