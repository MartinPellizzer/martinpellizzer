Phyllanthus fuscoluridus var. fuscoluridus

Phyllanthus fuscoluridus var. fuscoluridus, commonly known as the black root phyllanthus, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dark-colored roots and small, greenish-yellow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
