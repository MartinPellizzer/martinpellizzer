Parasenecio bulbiferoides

Parasenecio bulbiferoides, commonly known as the bulbous cudweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

It produces a bulb-like structure at the base, which may aid in its survival in harsh environments.
