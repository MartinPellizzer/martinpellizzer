Peperomia spathophylla

Peperomia spathophylla, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and ability to thrive in indirect light. It is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.

Peperomia spathophylla prefers well-draining soil and moderate watering to prevent root rot.
