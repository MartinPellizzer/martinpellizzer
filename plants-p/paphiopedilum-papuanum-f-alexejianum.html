Paphiopedilum papuanum f. alexejianum

Paphiopedilum papuanum f. alexejianum, commonly known as the Alexejian's Papuan Paphiopedilum, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Papua, Indonesia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant yellow lip with a distinctive red or purple border. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is highly sought after by collectors due to its rarity and ornamental value.
