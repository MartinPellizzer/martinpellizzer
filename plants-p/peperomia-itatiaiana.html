Peperomia itatiaiana

Peperomia itatiaiana, commonly known as the Itatiaia peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and sometimes pink. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne in clusters.

Peperomia itatiaiana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
