Pettera graminifolia

Pettera graminifolia, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Pettera graminifolia is adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal water availability.
