Piper hondonadense var. javieri

Piper hondonadense var. javieri, commonly known as the Javieri pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of dense forests.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for ecological and horticultural study.
