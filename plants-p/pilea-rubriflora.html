Pilea rubriflora

Pilea rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered pilea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking red bracts that enclose small, white flowers, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea rubriflora is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
