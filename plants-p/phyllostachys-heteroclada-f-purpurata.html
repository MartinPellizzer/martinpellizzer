Phyllostachys heteroclada f. purpurata

Phyllostachys heteroclada f. purpurata, commonly known as the purple bamboo, is a variant of the heteroclada bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which develops from the base of the plant upwards. This ornamental bamboo is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its growth habit is clump-forming, making it suitable for garden borders or as a specimen plant.

The plant's unique coloration makes it a popular choice for horticultural displays.
