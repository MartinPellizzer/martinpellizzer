Prunus Griffithii

Prunus griffithii, also known as the Chinese sand cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of China. It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to the landscape. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blossoms and attractive foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, Prunus griffithii has become a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts worldwide.
