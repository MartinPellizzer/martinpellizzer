Panicum muhlenbergii

Panicum muhlenbergii, commonly known as hairgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and produces fluffy, light brown seed heads that resemble downy feathers. This plant thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in marshes, swales, and along stream banks. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Panicum muhlenbergii is also valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
