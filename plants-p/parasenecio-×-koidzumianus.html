Parasenecio × koidzumianus

Parasenecio × koidzumianus, commonly known as the hybrid Senecio, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between *Parasenecio koidzumii* and *Senecio × koidzumianus*, characterized by its distinctive composite flower heads. The plant typically exhibits yellow to orange disc flowers surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
