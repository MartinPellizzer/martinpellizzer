Periploca laevigata f. anomalis

Periploca laevigata f. anomalis, commonly known as the anomalous periploca, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Periploca laevigata species.

It is characterized by its unique morphological features, including variations in leaf shape and flower structure compared to the typical form. This plant is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its botanical significance lies in its role as a subject of taxonomic study due to its distinguishing traits.
