Patagonium filifolium

Patagonium filifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of southern South America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs that give it its common name. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a soft, velvety texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
