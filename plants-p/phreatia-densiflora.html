Phreatia densiflora

Phreatia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots are often found in the soil or on tree trunks, showcasing its epiphytic or terrestrial growth habits.

Phreatia densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
