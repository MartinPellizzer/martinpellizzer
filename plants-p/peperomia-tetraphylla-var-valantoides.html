Peperomia tetraphylla var. valantoides

Peperomia tetraphylla var. valantoides, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a versatile plant for both decorative and low-maintenance purposes.
