Peperomia pseudoasarifolia

Peperomia pseudoasarifolia, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia pseudoasarifolia is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
