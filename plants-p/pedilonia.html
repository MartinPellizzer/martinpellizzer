Pedilonia

Pedilonia, commonly known as the "twin-flowered" or "double-flowered" plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-lipped flowers, often with a striking color contrast between the upper and lower lips. These plants typically grow in moist, shaded environments, preferring rich, well-drained soil.
