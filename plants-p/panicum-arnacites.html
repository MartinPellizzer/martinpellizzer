Panicum arnacites

Panicum arnacites, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and vibrant red bracts that emerge during the flowering stage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as roadsides, fields, and disturbed soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum arnacites is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
