Polythecium rehmannii

Polythecium rehmannii, commonly known as the Chinese hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.
