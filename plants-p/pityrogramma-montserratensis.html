Pityrogramma montserratensis

Pityrogramma montserratensis, commonly known as the Montserrat water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to the island of Montserrat in the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are divided into small, finger-like segments. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams, where it can receive adequate sunlight and nutrients. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are typically found on the underside of its fronds.

Pityrogramma montserratensis plays a role in maintaining aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.
