Panicum aristatum

Panicum aristatum, commonly known as the aristate panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in open areas such as prairies and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spike of small, bristly spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife.
