Potentilla taurica var. lanuginosa

Potentilla taurica var. lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
