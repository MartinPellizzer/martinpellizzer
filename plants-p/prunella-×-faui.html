Prunella × faui

Prunella × faui, commonly known as the common selfheal, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of violet or blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to various regions worldwide.

Prunella × faui is often used in herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
