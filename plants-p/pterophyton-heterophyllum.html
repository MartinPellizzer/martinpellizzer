Pterophyton heterophyllum

Pterophyton heterophyllum, commonly known as the variable fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive frond morphology.

It belongs to the family Pterophytiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant exhibits a unique variation in leaf shape, which is reflected in its scientific name. Its fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a complex arrangement of pinnae and pinnules.

Pterophyton heterophyllum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
