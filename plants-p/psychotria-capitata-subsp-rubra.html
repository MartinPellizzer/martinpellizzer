Psychotria capitata subsp. rubra

Psychotria capitata subsp.

rubra, commonly known as the red-headed psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family. It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy appearance.

This species is often found in shaded, humid environments such as rainforests.
