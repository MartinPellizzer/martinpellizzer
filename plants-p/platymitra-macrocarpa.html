Platymitra macrocarpa

Platymitra macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited flat-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that blooms during the summer months.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native range.
