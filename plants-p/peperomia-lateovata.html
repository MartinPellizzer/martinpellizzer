Peperomia lateovata

Peperomia lateovata, commonly known as the late ovate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia lateovata is prized for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
