Phillyrea mucronata

Phillyrea mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. These flowers are followed by small, rounded berries that turn red as they mature.

Phillyrea mucronata is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and dry climates.
