Peperomia barbulipetiola

Peperomia barbulipetiola, commonly known as the woolly peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a unique, fuzzy texture that gives it its common name. The plant typically has small, ovate leaves that are dark green with a waxy appearance. It produces delicate, reddish-pink flowers in clusters, which are often hidden beneath the foliage.

Peperomia barbulipetiola thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
