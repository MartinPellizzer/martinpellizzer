Phylica glabriflora

Phylica glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered phylica, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruiting bodies that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Phylica glabriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
