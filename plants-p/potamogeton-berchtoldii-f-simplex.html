Potamogeton berchtoldii f. simplex

Potamogeton berchtoldii f. simplex, commonly known as the simple pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

It has long, narrow leaves that are typically ribbon-like in shape, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually submerged or floating. The plant is characterized by its simple, unbranched stems and is often found in slow-moving or still waters.
