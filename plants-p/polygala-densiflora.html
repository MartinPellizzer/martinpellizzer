Polygala densiflora

Polygala densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its small, clustered flowers are surrounded by intricate, leaf-like bracts, giving it a unique appearance.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
