Prasophyllum sagittiferum

Prasophyllum sagittiferum, commonly known as the sword-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern regions of Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils within open forests and heathlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Prasophyllum sagittiferum is known for its fragrant blooms and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
