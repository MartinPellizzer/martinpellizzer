Peperomia montana

Peperomia montana, commonly known as the mountain peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which appear in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia montana is valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
