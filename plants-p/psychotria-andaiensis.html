Psychotria andaiensis

Psychotria andaiensis, commonly known as the Andean psychotria, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of the Andes.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Due to its habitat in high-altitude regions, it is adapted to cool, humid conditions and is often found in shaded environments.
