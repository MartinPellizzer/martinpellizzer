Panicum lagascae var. schreberianum

Panicum lagascae var. schreberianum, commonly known as the Schreber's bluestem, is a variant of the panic grass genus.

It is a perennial grass native to certain regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and slender, grass-like blades. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typical of the Poaceae family.
