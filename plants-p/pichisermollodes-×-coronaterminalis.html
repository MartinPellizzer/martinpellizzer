Pichisermollodes × coronaterminalis

Pichisermollodes × coronaterminalis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pichisermollodes and Coronaterminalis.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display a mix of colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its botanical features include elongated, narrow leaves and a complex inflorescence with multiple blooms.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
