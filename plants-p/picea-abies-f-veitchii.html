Picea abies f. veitchii

Picea abies f. veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's Norway spruce, is a horticultural form of the Norway spruce.

It is characterized by its compact, pyramidal shape and dense foliage. The foliage is typically a deep green, with a slightly bluish tinge, making it visually striking. It is a slow-growing evergreen conifer that is often used in ornamental landscaping.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
