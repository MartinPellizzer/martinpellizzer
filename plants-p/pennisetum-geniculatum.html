Pennisetum geniculatum

Pennisetum geniculatum, commonly known as purple top grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple-tinted foliage and tall, slender stalks that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive violet hue, especially along the edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum geniculatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
