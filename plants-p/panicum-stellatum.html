Panicum stellatum

Panicum stellatum, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Panicum stellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in natural landscapes.
