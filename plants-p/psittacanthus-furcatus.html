Psittacanthus furcatus

Psittacanthus furcatus, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact fronds that form a rounded clump. Psittacanthus furcatus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
