Potentilla verticillaris var. condensata

Potentilla verticillaris var. condensata, commonly known as the compact potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has wiry, thornless stems and small, ovate leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact form and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
