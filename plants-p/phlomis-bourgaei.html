Phlomis bourgaei

Phlomis bourgaei, commonly known as the Algerian plume, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces tall spikes of pale blue to purple flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fuzzy texture.

Phlomis bourgaei is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as cliffs and slopes.
