Piper obovatibracteum

Piper obovatibracteum, commonly known as the broad-bract pepper vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulture and botanical research.
