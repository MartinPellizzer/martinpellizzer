Primulina swinglei

Primulina swinglei, commonly known as the Swingle's primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a small shrub or perennial, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
