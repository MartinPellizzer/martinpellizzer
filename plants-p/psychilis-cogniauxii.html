Psychilis cogniauxii

Psychilis cogniauxii, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, colorful flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Psychilis cogniauxii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
