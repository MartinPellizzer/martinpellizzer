Pourretia rubricaulis

Pourretia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed pteridium, is a rare fern species characterized by its distinctive red-colored stems.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a pale green hue and a unique reddish stem that becomes more prominent as the plant matures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
