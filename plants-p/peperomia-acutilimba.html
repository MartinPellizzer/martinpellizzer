Peperomia acutilimba

Peperomia acutilimba, commonly known as the sharp-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia acutilimba is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
