Panicum nigrirostre

Panicum nigrirostre, commonly known as black-stemmed panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that turn a striking blackish-red color as they mature. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
