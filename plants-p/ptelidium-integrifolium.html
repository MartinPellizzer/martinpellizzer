Ptelidium integrifolium

Ptelidium integrifolium, commonly known as the smooth fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters at the base of the stems. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Ptelidium integrifolium is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
