Poa Ligulata

Poa ligulata, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a hardy perennial grass native to the northern hemisphere. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks. This plant is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species. Its delicate appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for ecological restoration projects. With its narrow leaves and tufted growth habit, Poa ligulata adds a unique aesthetic to natural landscapes.
