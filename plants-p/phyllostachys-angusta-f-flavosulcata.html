Phyllostachys angusta f. flavosulcata

Phyllostachys angusta f. flavosulcata, commonly known as the golden groove bamboo, is a compact, ornamental bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright culms that exhibit a distinctive golden-yellow stripe along the node valleys. The leaves are lance-shaped and a deep green, providing a striking contrast to the yellow markings. This bamboo thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

It is prized for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
