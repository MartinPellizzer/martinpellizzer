Phoenix hanceana

Phoenix hanceana, commonly known as the Chinese fan palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
