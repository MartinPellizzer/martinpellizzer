Phyllanthus pileostigma

Phyllanthus pileostigma, commonly known as the African sandmat, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

The fruit is a capsule that contains small, hard seeds.
