Polypodium beckleri

Polypodium beckleri, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its leathery, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizome is creeping and produces both roots and new shoots.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, rugged texture.
