Pleurothallis Verrucosa

Pleurothallis verrucosa is a fascinating orchid species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. This small, delicate plant is known for its intricate, often fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Despite its beauty, it is a rare and elusive plant, often found growing in shaded, humid environments. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss and illegal collection, it faces significant conservation challenges.
