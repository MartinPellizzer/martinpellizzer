Polystichum fukuyamae

Polystichum fukuyamae, commonly known as the Fukuyama holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sorangia on the undersides of its leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Polystichum fukuyamae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
