Phymatopteris fukienensis

Phymatopteris fukienensis, commonly known as the Fukien huperzia, is a species of clubmoss native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and short, creeping rhizomes. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate and often have a waxy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
