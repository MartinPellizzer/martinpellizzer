Pulsatilla ajanensis

Pulsatilla ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis pasqueflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to the high mountain regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Mongolia. The plant is characterized by its showy, early spring blooms, which are typically pink or white and have a distinctive, fluffy appearance. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
