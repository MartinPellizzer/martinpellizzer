Phillyrea medianifolia var. balearica

Phillyrea medianifolia var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Balearic Islands.

It features dense, dark green foliage with glossy, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for Mediterranean landscapes.
