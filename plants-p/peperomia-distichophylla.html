Peperomia distichophylla

Peperomia distichophylla, commonly known as the two-ranked peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, glossy, and often variegated leaves that are arranged in two rows along the stems, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia distichophylla is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
