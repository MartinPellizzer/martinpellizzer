Polygala canaliculata

Polygala canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color and are arranged in clusters.

Polygala canaliculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
