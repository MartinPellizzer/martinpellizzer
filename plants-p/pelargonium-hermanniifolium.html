Pelargonium hermanniifolium

Pelargonium hermanniifolium, commonly known as the Hermann's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer seasons. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
