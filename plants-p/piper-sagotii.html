Piper sagotii

Piper sagotii, commonly known as the Sagot's pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are typically ovate with a glossy surface and a strong, pungent aroma.

Piper sagotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
