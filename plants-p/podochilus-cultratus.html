Podochilus cultratus

Podochilus cultratus, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its linear, sword-shaped leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Podochilus cultratus is a terrestrial orchid that prefers well-drained soil and partial sunlight.
