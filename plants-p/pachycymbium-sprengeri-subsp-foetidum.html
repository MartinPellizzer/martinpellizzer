Pachycymbium sprengeri subsp. foetidum

Pachycymbium sprengeri subsp.

foetidum, commonly known as the stinking pachycymbium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines.

The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and small, often fragrant or strongly scented flowers. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
