Polystichum daguanense

Polystichum daguanense, commonly known as the Daguan fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, supporting its growth in rocky or soil-rich substrates.
