Potentilla saundersiana var. saundersiana

Potentilla saundersiana var. saundersiana, commonly known as the yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a low-growing shrub with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has palmate leaves divided into five lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and ornamental species suitable for garden cultivation.
