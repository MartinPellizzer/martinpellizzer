Peperomia unifoliata

Peperomia unifoliata, commonly known as the single-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features a rosette of thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for succulent and air plant collections.
