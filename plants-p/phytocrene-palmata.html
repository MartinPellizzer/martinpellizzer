Phytocrene palmata

Phytocrene palmata, commonly known as the red-flowered palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers, which are highly visible and attract a variety of pollinators. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
