Pilea anivoranensis

Pilea anivoranensis, commonly known as the Madagascar pilea, is a rare and unique species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
