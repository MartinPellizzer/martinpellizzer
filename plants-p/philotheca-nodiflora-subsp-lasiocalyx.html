Philotheca nodiflora subsp. lasiocalyx

Philotheca nodiflora subsp.

lasiocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved philotheca, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in open woodlands or heathlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
