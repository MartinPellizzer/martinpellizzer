Potentilla agrimonioides subsp. hispanica

Potentilla agrimonioides subsp.

hispanica, commonly known as Spanish cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has slender, wiry stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
