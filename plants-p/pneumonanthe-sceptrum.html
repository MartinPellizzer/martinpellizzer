Pneumonanthe sceptrum

Pneumonanthe sceptrum, commonly known as the sacred flame plant, is a unique member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
