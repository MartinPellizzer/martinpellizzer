Phyllanthus schomburgkianus var. anceps

Phyllanthus schomburgkianus var. anceps, commonly known as the Schomburgk's false crosswort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple leaves with a distinctive glandular underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in compact clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels.
