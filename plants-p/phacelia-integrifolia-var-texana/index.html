Phacelia Integrifolia Var. Texana

Phacelia integrifolia var. texana is a striking wildflower native to the southwestern united states. it is known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. this plant thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in dry, open habitats. it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife. its unique appearance and ecological value make it a noteworthy species in native plant gardens.
