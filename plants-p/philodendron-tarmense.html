Philodendron tarmense

Philodendron tarmense, commonly known as the Tarmes philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often adorned with striking silver or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are typically followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and foliage plants.
