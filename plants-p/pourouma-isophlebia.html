Pourouma isophlebia

Pourouma isophlebia, commonly known as the pink river bamboo, is a striking plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 10 meters in height. It thrives in wet, tropical environments and plays a significant role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat.
