Prunella × coutinhoi

Prunella × coutinhoi, commonly known as the hybrid selfheal, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Prunella vulgaris (common selfheal) and Prunella grandiflora (large flowered selfheal). The plant features square, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically blue to violet in color, arranged in dense whorls along the stem.

Prunella × coutinhoi is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, thriving in temperate climates.
