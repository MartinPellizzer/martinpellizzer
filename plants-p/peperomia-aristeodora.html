Peperomia aristeodora

Peperomia aristeodora, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
