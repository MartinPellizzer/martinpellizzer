Polygala aurata var. macrostachya

Polygala aurata var. macrostachya, commonly known as the golden milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, fluffy inflorescences that resemble milkweed, and it belongs to the family Polygalaceae. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
