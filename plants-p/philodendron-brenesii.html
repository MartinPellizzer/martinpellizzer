Philodendron brenesii

Philodendron brenesii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by green, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron brenesii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
