Pinellia ternata var. vulgaris

Pinellia ternata var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common pinellia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features a short, erect stem with heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a typical feature of the Araceae family.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
