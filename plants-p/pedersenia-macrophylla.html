Pedersenia macrophylla

Pedersenia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant pederseria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
