Pycreus polystachyos

Pycreus polystachyos, commonly known as the goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense, branched clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Pycreus polystachyos is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
