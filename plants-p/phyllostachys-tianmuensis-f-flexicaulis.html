Phyllostachys tianmuensis f. flexicaulis

Phyllostachys tianmuensis f. flexicaulis, commonly known as the flexible giant bamboo, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Tianmu bamboo species.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that exhibit a natural curvature, giving it a graceful appearance. This form is native to China, specifically the Zhejiang province, where it grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is highly sought after by bamboo enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
