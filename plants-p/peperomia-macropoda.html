Peperomia macropoda

Peperomia macropoda, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia macropoda is relatively low maintenance and makes a popular choice for indoor gardening.
