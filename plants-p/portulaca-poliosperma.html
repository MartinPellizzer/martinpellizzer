Portulaca poliosperma

Portulaca poliosperma, commonly known as the desert purslane, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is adapted to survive in hot, dry environments due to its thick, water-storing leaves. It produces small, rounded seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Portulaca poliosperma is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
