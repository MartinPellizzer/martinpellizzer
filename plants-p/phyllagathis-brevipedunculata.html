Phyllagathis brevipedunculata

Phyllagathis brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stalked phyllagathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. This shrub typically grows in lowland rainforests and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by dark purple berries.
