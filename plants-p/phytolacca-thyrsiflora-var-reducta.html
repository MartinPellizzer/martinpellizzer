Phytolacca thyrsiflora var. reducta

Phytolacca thyrsiflora var. reducta, commonly known as the reduced thyrse phytolacca, is a variant of the phytolacca genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences called thyrses. Its fruit is a fleshy, reddish berry that contains several seeds.

This variant is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical regions.
