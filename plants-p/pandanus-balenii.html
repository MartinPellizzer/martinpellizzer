Pandanus balenii

Pandanus balenii, commonly known as the Bornean pandanus, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by orange or red fruits. Its roots are fibrous and can be used for making ropes and mats.

The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline environments.
