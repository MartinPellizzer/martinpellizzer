Piper gracilispicum

Piper gracilispicum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into small, edible berries.

This plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
