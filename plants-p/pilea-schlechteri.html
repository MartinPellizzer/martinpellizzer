Pilea schlechteri

Pilea schlechteri, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of glossy, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green with a subtle sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by distinctive round, papery bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular indoor plant.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements make it a favored choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
