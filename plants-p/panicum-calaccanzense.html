Panicum calaccanzense

Panicum calaccanzense, commonly known as the Calaccanze Panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
