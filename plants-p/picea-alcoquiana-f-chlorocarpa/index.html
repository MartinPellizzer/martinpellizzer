Picea Alcoquiana F. Chlorocarpa

Blue spruce, scientifically known as Picea alcoquiana f. chlorocarpa, is a striking evergreen tree prized for its vibrant blue foliage. Native to high-altitude regions of the Andes, this unique variety thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its dense, conical shape and striking color make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The plant's slow growth rate ensures it maintains its form for decades, offering long-term beauty. Despite its ornamental appeal, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native environments.
