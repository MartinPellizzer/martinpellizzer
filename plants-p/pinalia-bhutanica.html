Pinalia bhutanica

Pinalia bhutanica, commonly known as the Bhutanese pinyon, is a unique shrub native to the Himalayan regions of Bhutan.

It belongs to the Pinaceae family, which includes various coniferous trees and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a rare and valuable species in Bhutan's flora.
