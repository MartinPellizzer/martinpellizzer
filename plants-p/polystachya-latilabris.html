Polystachya latilabris

Polystachya latilabris, commonly known as the broad-lipped sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a broad, flat base, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
