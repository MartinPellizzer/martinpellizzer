Pandanus asauensis

Pandanus asauensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific islands.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces fragrant, orange-colored flowers that bloom in clusters at the base of the leaves. Its roots and leaves are utilized in local cultures for various purposes, including medicinal and culinary applications.

The species thrives in coastal and lowland areas, adapting well to humid, saline environments.
