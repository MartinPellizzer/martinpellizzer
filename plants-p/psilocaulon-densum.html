Psilocaulon densum

Psilocaulon densum, commonly known as the dense psilocaulon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants such as buckwheat and smartweed. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

While not psychoactive, it shares some morphological traits with other members of the Polygonaceae family that do have medicinal or hallucinogenic properties.
