Protea alpina

Protea alpina, commonly known as the Alpine Protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its showy, cone-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive central style that resembles a brush.

Protea alpina is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
