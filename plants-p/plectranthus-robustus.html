Plectranthus robustus

Plectranthus robustus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in dense whorls and are usually violet or purple in color.

Plectranthus robustus thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
