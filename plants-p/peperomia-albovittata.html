Peperomia albovittata

Peperomia albovittata, commonly known as the White-striped Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features upright, glossy dark green leaves with striking white or light green stripes running along the midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia albovittata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
