Panicum cayennense var. quadriglume

Panicum cayennense var. quadriglume, commonly known as the Cayenne panic grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

This variety is distinguished by its four-sided glumes, which give it its specific epithet "quadriglume.".
