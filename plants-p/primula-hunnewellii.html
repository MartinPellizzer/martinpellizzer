Primula hunnewellii

Primula hunnewellii, commonly known as the Hunnewell's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Primula hunnewellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate and vibrant blooms.
