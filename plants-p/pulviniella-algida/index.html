Pulviniella Algida

Pulviniella algida is a rare and enigmatic plant found in cold, high-altitude environments. It thrives in regions where temperatures remain consistently low, showcasing unique adaptations to its harsh habitat. Its structure is delicate, with small, rounded leaves and a compact growth form that helps it conserve heat. The plant's survival depends on specific microclimatic conditions that are increasingly threatened by climate change. Studying Pulviniella algida offers valuable insights into plant resilience and ecological balance in extreme climates.
