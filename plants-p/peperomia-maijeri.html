Peperomia maijeri

Peperomia maijeri, commonly known as the Mayjer's Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia maijeri is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
