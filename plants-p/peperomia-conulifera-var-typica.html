Peperomia conulifera var. typica

Peperomia conulifera var. typica, commonly known as the lace-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive lace-like pattern of white veins, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on short, erect stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia conulifera var. typica is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
