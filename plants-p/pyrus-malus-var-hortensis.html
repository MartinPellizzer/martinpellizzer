Pyrus malus var. hortensis

Pyrus malus var. hortensis, commonly known as the European pear.

It is a variety of the common pear tree, characterized by its ornamental value and edible fruit. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree with a broad, spreading crown. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, while the flowers are white and fragrant, appearing in spring.

The fruit is usually pear-shaped, with a thin, smooth skin and a sweet, juicy flesh.
