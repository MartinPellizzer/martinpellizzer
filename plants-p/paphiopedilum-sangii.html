Paphiopedilum sangii

Paphiopedilum sangii, commonly known as the Chinese velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a pseudobulb with broad, leathery leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive, lip-like structure that is usually pink or purple in hue. It blooms in late spring to early summer, and its flowers are highly prized in horticulture for their beauty and fragrance.

Paphiopedilum sangii prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated in shaded, well-drained soils.
