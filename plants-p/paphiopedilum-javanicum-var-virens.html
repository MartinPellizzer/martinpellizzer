Paphiopedilum javanicum var. virens

Paphiopedilum javanicum var. virens, commonly known as the green-flowered lady's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flower with a greenish-yellow hue, which distinguishes it from other Paphiopedilum species. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, contributing to its lush appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
