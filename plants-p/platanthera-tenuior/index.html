Platanthera Tenuior

Platanthera tenuior is a delicate orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. This species is known for its slender, pale green stems and small, fragrant flowers. Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in many regions. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare and beautiful plant.
