Phlomis rotata

Phlomis rotata, commonly known as bordered bugloss, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fuzzy texture.

Phlomis rotata is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, sunny conditions.
