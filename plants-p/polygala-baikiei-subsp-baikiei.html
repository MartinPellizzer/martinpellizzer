Polygala baikiei subsp. baikiei

Polygala baikiei subsp.

baikiei, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are opposite and often have a hairy texture, while the flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white.
