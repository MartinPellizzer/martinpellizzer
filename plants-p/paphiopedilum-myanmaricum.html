Paphiopedilum myanmaricum

Paphiopedilum myanmaricum, commonly known as the Myanmar slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique slipper-shaped pouch, which is a distinctive feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow with a contrasting red or brown lip.

Paphiopedilum myanmaricum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and rarity.
