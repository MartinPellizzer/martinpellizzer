Periploca apiculata

Periploca apiculata, commonly known as the Cape fuchsia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has aromatic leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to a Mediterranean climate.

Periploca apiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
