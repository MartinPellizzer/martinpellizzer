Pleurospermum rotundatum

Pleurospermum rotundatum, commonly known as the roundleaf pleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features a stout, branched stem and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually rounded or oval, with a hairy surface that helps reduce water loss.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
