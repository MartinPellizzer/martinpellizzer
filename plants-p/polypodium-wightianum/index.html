Polypodium Wightianum

Polypodium wightianum is a unique fern species native to parts of Asia and the Pacific. It is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. This plant is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in tropical regions. Its fronds are typically long and narrow, with a distinctive appearance. It is valued for both its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local cultures.
