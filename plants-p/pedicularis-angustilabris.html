Pedicularis angustilabris

Pedicularis angustilabris, commonly known as the narrow-lipped monkey orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a member of the Orobanchaceae family, known for its hemiparasitic nature, meaning it partially relies on host plants for nutrients. Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
