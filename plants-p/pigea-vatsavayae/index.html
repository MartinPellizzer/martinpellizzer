Pigea Vatsavayae

Pigea vatsavayae is a rare and unique plant discovered in the remote regions of India. It is named after Vatsavayana, a prominent ancient Indian scholar, highlighting its cultural significance. This plant is known for its striking appearance and unusual growth patterns. It thrives in specific microhabitats, making it difficult to study and cultivate. Due to its rarity, it is considered a flagship species for conservation efforts in its native region.
