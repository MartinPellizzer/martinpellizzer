Polyscias kikuyuensis

Polyscias kikuyuensis, commonly known as the Kikuyu glory bush, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spreading growth habit. The plant features compound leaves with a glossy green surface and small, white flowers that appear in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Polyscias kikuyuensis is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
