Piper elmuletonum

Piper elmuletonum, commonly known as the Mexican pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in spikes and are followed by small, fleshy fruits.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of medicinal compounds.
