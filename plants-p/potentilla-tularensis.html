Potentilla tularensis

Potentilla tularensis, commonly known as the Tulare mountain cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to nine oval-shaped leaflets.

Potentilla tularensis thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
