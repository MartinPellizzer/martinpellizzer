Phebalium filifolium

Phebalium filifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf honeysuckle, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features slender, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale yellow, typically blooming in spring. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, providing ornamental value in gardens.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
