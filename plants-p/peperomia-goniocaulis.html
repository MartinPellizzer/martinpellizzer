Peperomia goniocaulis

Peperomia goniocaulis, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features distinctive, zigzagging stems that add visual interest to its overall appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are often lance-shaped and have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Peperomia goniocaulis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
