Pleioceras barteri var. zenkeri

Pleioceras barteri var. zenkeri, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, with stems that can be either upright or trailing, depending on the variety. Its ability to produce offsets makes it easy to propagate and maintain in home environments.
