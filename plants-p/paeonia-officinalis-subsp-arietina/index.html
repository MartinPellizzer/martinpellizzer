Paeonia Officinalis Subsp. Arietina

Crested peony, scientifically known as Paeonia officinalis subsp. arietina, is a rare and striking variant of the peony plant that captivates with its unique appearance. Unlike typical peonies, this subspecies features a distinctive crown of elongated, curled petals that resemble a crest, giving it a regal and otherworldly look. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, it thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soils. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, the crested peony is relatively uncommon, making it a prized addition to botanical collections and gardens.
