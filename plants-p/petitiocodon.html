Petitiocodon

Petitiocodon, commonly known as the small-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
