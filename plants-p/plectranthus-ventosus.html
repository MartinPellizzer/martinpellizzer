Plectranthus ventosus

Plectranthus ventosus, commonly known as the wind plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Plectranthus ventosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
