Piper linkii

Piper linkii, commonly known as the Java pepper or Link's pepper, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and they are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are also utilized in herbal remedies due to their aromatic properties.

Piper linkii thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires support for its climbing growth habit.
