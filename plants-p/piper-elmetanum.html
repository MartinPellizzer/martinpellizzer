Piper elmetanum

Piper elmetanum, commonly known as the Madagascar pepper or elmetanum pepper, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local remedies for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves.
