Pleurothallis decurva

Pleurothallis decurva, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. Its inflorescence is unique, with flowers arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stem.

Pleurothallis decurva is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
