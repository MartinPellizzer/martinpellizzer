Peperomia tacticana

Peperomia tacticana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia tacticana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
