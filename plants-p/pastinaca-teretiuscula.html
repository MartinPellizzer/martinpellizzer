Pastinaca teretiuscula

Pastinaca teretiuscula, commonly known as the slender burdock.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features tall, slender stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small and clustered in composite heads, typically ranging in color from yellow to orange.

The roots are often harvested for their medicinal and culinary uses.
