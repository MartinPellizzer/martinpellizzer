Peperomia samoensis var. glabrescens

Peperomia samoensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Smooth-leaved Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial native to Samoa.

It features thick, waxy, and slightly hairy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
