Persicaria laxiflora

Persicaria laxiflora, commonly known as slenderflower smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late summer.

Persicaria laxiflora is known for its ability to thrive in wet environments and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
