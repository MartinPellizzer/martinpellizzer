Pleurothallis ghiesbreghtiana

Pleurothallis ghiesbreghtiana, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Pleurothallis ghiesbreghtiana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
