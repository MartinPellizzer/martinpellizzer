Phylica amoena

Phylica amoena, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces distinctive, cone-like seed pods that turn red as they mature. Its leaves are narrow and silver-gray, giving it a striking appearance.

Phylica amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to arid environments.
