Pelargonium × dubium

Pelargonium × dubium, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inquinans. The plant is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, pink, and white. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Pelargonium × dubium thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and container planting.
