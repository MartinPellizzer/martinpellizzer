Pilea ovalis

Pilea ovalis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, shield-shaped leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It is a popular indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea ovalis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain healthy growth.
