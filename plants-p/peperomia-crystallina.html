Peperomia crystallina

Peperomia crystallina, commonly known as the glassy peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rosettes of glossy, waxy leaves that are often tinged with silver or white, giving it a crystalline appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf texture and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
