Parsonsia hienghenensis

Parsonsia hienghenensis, commonly known as the Heng Hen flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Parsonsia hienghenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
