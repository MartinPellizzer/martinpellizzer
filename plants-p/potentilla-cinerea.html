Potentilla cinerea

Potentilla cinerea, commonly known as silver cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments such as meadows and stream banks. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Potentilla cinerea is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
