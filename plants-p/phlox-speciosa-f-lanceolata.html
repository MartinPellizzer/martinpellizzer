Phlox speciosa f. lanceolata

Phlox speciosa f. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf garden phlox, is a cultivar of the garden phlox species.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches, featuring lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a variety of garden conditions.
