Pleurothallis chaetoglossa

Pleurothallis chaetoglossa, commonly known as the hairy lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, hairy sepals and petals. The flowers typically feature a prominent, hairy lip that distinguishes it from other pleurothallis species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
