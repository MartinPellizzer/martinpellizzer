Peperomia elongata

Peperomia elongata, commonly known as the elongated peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia elongata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
