Premna subscandens var. glabrescens

Premna subscandens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the silky premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine, silky hair that gives the plant its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
