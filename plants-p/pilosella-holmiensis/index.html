Pilosella Holmiensis

Pilosella holmiensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, Pilosella holmiensis is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from further decline.
