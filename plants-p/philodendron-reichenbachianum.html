Philodendron reichenbachianum

Philodendron reichenbachianum, commonly known as the velvet leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to deep purple. The plant has a robust stem that can grow several meters long, often supported by aerial roots. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist soil for optimal growth.
