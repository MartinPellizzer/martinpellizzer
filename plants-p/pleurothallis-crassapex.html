Pleurothallis crassapex

Pleurothallis crassapex, commonly known as the broad-leafed pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. It features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
