Porcellites maculata

Porcellites maculata, commonly known as the spotted porcellite, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
