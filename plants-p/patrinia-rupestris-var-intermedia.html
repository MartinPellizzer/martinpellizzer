Patrinia rupestris var. intermedia

Patrinia rupestris var. intermedia, commonly known as the rock patrinia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, upright stems that often grow in rocky or mountainous habitats. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
