Pycnostachys togoensis

Pycnostachys togoensis, commonly known as the Togo flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters.
