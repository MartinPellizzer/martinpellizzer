Primulina rongshuiensis

Primulina rongshuiensis, commonly known as the Rongshui gloxinia, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
