Piper pervulsum

Piper pervulsum, commonly known as the small pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries.

Piper pervulsum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
