Prenanthes lanceolata

Prenanthes lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf waterleaf, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive lance-shaped leaves and spadix-like inflorescence. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with a short, narrow spadix surrounded by a greenish-white spathe.

Prenanthes lanceolata is often found in wetland habitats and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
