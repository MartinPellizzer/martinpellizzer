Peperomia treleasei

Peperomia treleasei, commonly known as the Trelease's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia treleasei is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
