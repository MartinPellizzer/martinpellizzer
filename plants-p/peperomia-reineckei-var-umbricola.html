Peperomia reineckei var. umbricola

Peperomia reineckei var. umbricola, commonly known as the dark peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features broad, dark green, velvety leaves that are often slightly curled and have a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
