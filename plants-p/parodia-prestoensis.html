Parodia prestoensis

Parodia prestoensis, commonly known as the Presto's cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often adorned with spines and sometimes flowers. The plant belongs to the Cactaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments. Its growth is typically slow, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.

The plant's unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among cactus enthusiasts.
