Polygonum micranthemum

Polygonum micranthemum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red and bloom in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
