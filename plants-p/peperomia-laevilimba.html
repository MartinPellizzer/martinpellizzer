Peperomia laevilimba

Peperomia laevilimba, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are slightly wavy and have a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia laevilimba is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
