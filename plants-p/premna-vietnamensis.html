Premna vietnamensis

Premna vietnamensis, commonly known as Vietnamese nettle or Vietnamese glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its fruit is a small, spherical capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

The species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
