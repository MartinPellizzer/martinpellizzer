Peperomia aguilae

Peperomia aguilae, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Mexico.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia aguilae is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
