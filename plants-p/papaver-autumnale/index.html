Papaver Autumnale

Papaver autumnale is a striking wildflower known for its vibrant orange-red blooms. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. This plant is part of the poppy family and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. Its unique appearance makes it a popular subject for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic if ingested, requiring caution when handling.
