Polygonum nuttallii

Polygonum nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which is characterized by its swollen nodes and often triangular stems. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows, stream banks, and marshes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a bluish-green hue with prominent veins.

The small, greenish-white flowers appear in dense clusters and are accompanied by bracts that resemble leaves.
