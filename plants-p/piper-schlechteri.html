Piper schlechteri

Piper schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that twine around supports. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a glossy green surface.

The small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, three-lobed berries.
