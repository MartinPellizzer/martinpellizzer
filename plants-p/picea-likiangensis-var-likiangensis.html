Picea likiangensis var. likiangensis

Picea likiangensis var. likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spruce, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needles, and distinctive, elongated cones. The tree thrives in cool, moist climates at high elevations, often growing in subalpine forests. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing protection against harsh environmental conditions.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting diverse flora and fauna.
