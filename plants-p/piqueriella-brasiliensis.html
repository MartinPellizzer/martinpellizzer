Piqueriella brasiliensis

Piqueriella brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Brazil.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with slender, grass-like stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
