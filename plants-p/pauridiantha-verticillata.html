Pauridiantha verticillata

Pauridiantha verticillata, commonly known as the spiral-leaved pauridiantha, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
