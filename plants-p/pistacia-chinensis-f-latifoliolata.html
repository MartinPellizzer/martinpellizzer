Pistacia chinensis f. latifoliolata

Pistacia chinensis f. latifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved Chinese pistache, is a cultivar of the Chinese pistache tree.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often broader than those of the typical species. The plant is native to China and is valued for its ornamental qualities, including its vibrant autumn foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various climates.
