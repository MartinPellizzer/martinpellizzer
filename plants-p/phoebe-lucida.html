Phoebe lucida

Phoebe lucida, commonly known as the shining phoebe, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic plants such as laurel and avocado. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed.

Phoebe lucida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
