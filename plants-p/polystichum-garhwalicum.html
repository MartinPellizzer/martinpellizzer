Polystichum garhwalicum

Polystichum garhwalicum, commonly known as the Garhwal fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are bi-directionally pinnate, with a distinctive, upright growth habit.
