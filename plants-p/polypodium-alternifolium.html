Polypodium alternifolium

Polypodium alternifolium, commonly known as the alternate fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its alternate arrangement of leaflets, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually lanceolate or ovate in shape, with a serrated or toothed margin.

Polypodium alternifolium is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
