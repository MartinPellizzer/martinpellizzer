Persoonia Muelleri Subsp. Densifolia

Persoonia muelleri subsp. densifolia is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southwestern australia. it is known for its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to native plant collections. it also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecological balance.
