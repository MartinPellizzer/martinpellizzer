Prenanthes cacaliifolia

Prenanthes cacaliifolia, commonly known as the showy sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble sunflowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
