Potentilla chinensis

Potentilla chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

Potentilla chinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
