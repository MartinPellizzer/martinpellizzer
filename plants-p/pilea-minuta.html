Pilea minuta

Pilea minuta, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both homes and offices.
