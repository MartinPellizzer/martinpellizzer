Platylophus obscurus

Platylophus obscurus, commonly known as the dark-winged orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, pale greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a delicate, herbaceous stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
