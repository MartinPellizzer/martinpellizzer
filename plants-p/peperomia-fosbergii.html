Peperomia fosbergii

Peperomia fosbergii, commonly known as the Fosberg peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia fosbergii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
