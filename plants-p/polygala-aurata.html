Polygala aurata

Polygala aurata, commonly known as golden beard or golden thread, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its unique inflorescence, which resembles a beard, is a distinguishing characteristic.

Polygala aurata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
