Potentilla foersteriana var. foersteriana

Potentilla foersteriana var. foersteriana, commonly known as the Alpine cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and pinnately compound leaves with five to seven leaflets. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in cold climates.
