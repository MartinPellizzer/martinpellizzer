Philodendron vargealtense

Philodendron vargealtense, commonly known as the Vargealtense Philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy but are followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
