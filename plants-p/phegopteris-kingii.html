Phegopteris kingii

Phegopteris kingii, commonly known as the King's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fronds are bioluminescent, a unique trait that aids in photosynthesis in low-light conditions.
