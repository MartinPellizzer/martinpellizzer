Phalaenopsis × intermedia var. brymeriana

Phalaenopsis × intermedia var. brymeriana, commonly known as the hybrid phalaenopsis orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces long, pendulous flower spikes with showy, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

It is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is often used in floral arrangements and as a houseplant.
