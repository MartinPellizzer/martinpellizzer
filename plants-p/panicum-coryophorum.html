Panicum coryophorum

Panicum coryophorum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and swampy areas. The plant has slender, upright stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance.
