Pasania viridis

Pasania viridis, commonly known as the green pasania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Pasania viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
