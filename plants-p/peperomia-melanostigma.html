Peperomia melanostigma

Peperomia melanostigma, commonly known as the black-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia melanostigma is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
