Parinari scabra

Parinari scabra, commonly known as the rough parinari, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and simple, alternate leaves. Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and for reforestation projects.
