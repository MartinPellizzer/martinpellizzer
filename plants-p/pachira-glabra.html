Pachira glabra

Pachira glabra, commonly known as the smooth pachira, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
