Peperomia tricarinata

Peperomia tricarinata, commonly known as the Three-ribbed Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive three-ribbed pattern on the underside, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tricarinata is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its drought tolerance and minimal pruning requirements.
