Pleurospermum dielsianum

Pleurospermum dielsianum, commonly known as the woolly pleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of disk florets, forming a composite flower head. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
