Phaseolus coccineus var. strigillosus

Phaseolus coccineus var. strigillosus, commonly known as the hairy scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and edible, kidney-shaped beans. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and culinary uses.
