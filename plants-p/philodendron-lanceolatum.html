Philodendron lanceolatum

Philodendron lanceolatum, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by green, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Philodendron lanceolatum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
