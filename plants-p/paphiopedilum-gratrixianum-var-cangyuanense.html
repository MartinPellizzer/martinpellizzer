Paphiopedilum gratrixianum var. cangyuanense

Paphiopedilum gratrixianum var. cangyuanense, commonly known as the Cangyuan lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green to reddish-brown leaves and its unique, intricate flower structure. The flowers typically feature a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a defining trait of the Paphiopedilum genus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and conservation status.
