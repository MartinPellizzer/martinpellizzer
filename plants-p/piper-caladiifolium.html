Piper caladiifolium

Piper caladiifolium, commonly known as the Caladiifolium pepper or the Caladium pepper, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, brightly colored leaves that come in shades of green, red, and purple. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by small, round berries.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant rather than for its edible fruits.
