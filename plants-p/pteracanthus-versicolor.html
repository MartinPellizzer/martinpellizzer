Pteracanthus versicolor

Pteracanthus versicolor, commonly known as the variable fern, is a species of fern characterized by its diverse and often striking coloration.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features delicate fronds with a variety of shapes and sizes, depending on environmental conditions. Its leaves are typically bioluminescent under certain light conditions, adding to its unique appearance.

Pteracanthus versicolor thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
