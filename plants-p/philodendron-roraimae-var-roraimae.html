Philodendron roraimae var. roraimae

Philodendron roraimae var. roraimae, commonly known as the Roraima philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

This variety is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb, often reaching significant heights in its natural habitat.
