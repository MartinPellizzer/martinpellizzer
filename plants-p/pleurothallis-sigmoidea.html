Pleurothallis sigmoidea

Pleurothallis sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in the understory of humid forests. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its inflorescence is often curved, giving it the characteristic "zigzag" appearance that inspired its common name.

Pleurothallis sigmoidea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right growing conditions.
