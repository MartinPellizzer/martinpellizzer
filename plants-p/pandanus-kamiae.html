Pandanus kamiae

Pandanus kamiae, commonly known as the Kamia pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly found in parts of Micronesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, fragrant flowers that attract various pollinators.

The plant's adaptability to coastal and wetland environments makes it an important component of its native ecosystems.
