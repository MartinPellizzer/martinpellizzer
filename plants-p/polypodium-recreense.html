Polypodium recreense

Polypodium recreense, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces. The plant has a compact, dense frond structure with a dark green, leathery appearance. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Polypodium recreense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
