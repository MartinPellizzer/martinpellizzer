Persicaria rothrockii

Persicaria rothrockii, commonly known as Rothrock's smartweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its triangular stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and stream banks. It produces pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
