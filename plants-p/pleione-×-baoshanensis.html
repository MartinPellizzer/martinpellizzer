Pleione × baoshanensis

Pleione × baoshanensis, commonly known as the Baoshan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on forest floors or cliff faces. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered with a thin layer of brownish bark, and its leaves are strap-shaped and evergreen.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
