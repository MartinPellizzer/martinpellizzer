Pittosporum roimata

Pittosporum roimata, commonly known as the silver mist tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it a distinctive, mist-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Pittosporum roimata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in landscaping and conservation efforts.
