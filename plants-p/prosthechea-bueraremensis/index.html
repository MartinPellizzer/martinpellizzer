Prosthechea Bueraremensis

Prosthechea bueraremensis is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. It is known for its striking violet and green floral structure that blends seamlessly with its natural habitat. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments where it clings to tree trunks and branches. Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
