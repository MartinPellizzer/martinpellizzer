Polystachya hollandii

Polystachya hollandii, commonly known as the Holland's spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit and produces clusters of elongated, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically yellow or green with intricate patterns, and they bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
