Polymeria lanata

Polymeria lanata, commonly known as the woolly polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Polymeria lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
