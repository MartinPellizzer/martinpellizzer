Piper tuberculatum var. cangrejalense

Piper tuberculatum var. cangrejalense, commonly known as the Cangrejalense pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

Botanically, it is notable for its distinctive tuberculate (bumpy) texture on its stems and leaves.
