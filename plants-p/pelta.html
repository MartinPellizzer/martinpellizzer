Pelta

Pelta, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the wetlands of North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds.

Pelta is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to help filter water in aquatic environments.
