Pteronia acerosa var. multiflora

Pteronia acerosa var. multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered pteronia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing vibrant pink or red flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
