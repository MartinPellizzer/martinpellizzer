Protea swynnertonii

Protea swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, with leathery, evergreen leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
