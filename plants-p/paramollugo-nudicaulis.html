Paramollugo nudicaulis

Paramollugo nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender sand mat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with small, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on short pedicels. Its succulent leaves store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

Paramollugo nudicaulis is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall.
