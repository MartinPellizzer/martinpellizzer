Prunus spinosa var. dulcescens

Prunus spinosa var. dulcescens, commonly known as the sweetthorn cherry, is a variant of the common blackthorn.

It is characterized by its small, thorned branches and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. These fruits have a sweet, tangy flavor and are used in jams and culinary applications.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in hedgerows and woodland edges.
