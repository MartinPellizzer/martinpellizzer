Prangos platychlaena subsp. platychlaena

Prangos platychlaena subsp.

platychlaena, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically broad and slightly lobed, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and modest flowering display.
