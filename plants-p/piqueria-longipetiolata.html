Piqueria longipetiolata

Piqueria longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled piqueria, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in arid regions. It has small, alternate leaves with long petioles, which help it conserve water in its native environment.

Piqueria longipetiolata is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
