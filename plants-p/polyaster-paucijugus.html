Polyaster paucijugus

Polyaster paucijugus, commonly known as the few-juged starflower, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of many small florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its flowers are usually small and yellow, with a star-like appearance, giving it its common name.

Polyaster paucijugus is noted for its ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
