Peperomia ottoniana var. ecuadorensis

Peperomia ottoniana var. ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorean pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish or bronze hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often hidden beneath the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
