Peperomia bernhardiana

Peperomia bernhardiana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a silvery sheen, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia bernhardiana is valued for its ornamental appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
