Potentilla discolor

Potentilla discolor, commonly known as the variable cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves with five leaflets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural practices.
