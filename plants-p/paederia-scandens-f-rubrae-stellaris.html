Paederia scandens f. rubrae-stellaris

Paederia scandens f. rubrae-stellaris, commonly known as the starry red paederia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, which gives the plant its common name. The species belongs to the Rubiaceae family, known for its diverse and often fragrant flowers.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
