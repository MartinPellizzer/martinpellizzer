Pilea obesa

Pilea obesa, commonly known as the "Fat Plant," is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are rounded and often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and can produce small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers under the right conditions. Its leaves are typically a vibrant green, though they may develop a reddish tinge when exposed to bright light.

Pilea obesa is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and unique appearance.
