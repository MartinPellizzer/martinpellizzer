Panicum cristatellum

Panicum cristatellum, commonly known as the crowned panic grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and distinctive, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum cristatellum is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
