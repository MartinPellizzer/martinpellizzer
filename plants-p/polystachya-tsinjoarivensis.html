Polystachya tsinjoarivensis

Polystachya tsinjoarivensis, commonly known as the Madagascar spider orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive inflorescence composed of multiple racemes bearing numerous small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with intricate patterns, adapted for specific pollinators.
