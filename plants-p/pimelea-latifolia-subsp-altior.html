Pimelea latifolia subsp. altior

Pimelea latifolia subsp.

altior, commonly known as the tall broadleaf pimelea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spring. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
