Plectranthus annuus

Plectranthus annuus, commonly known as African violet, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of violet, blue, or white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Plectranthus annuus is popular as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
