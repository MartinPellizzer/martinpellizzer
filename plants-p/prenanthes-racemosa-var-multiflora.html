Prenanthes racemosa var. multiflora

Prenanthes racemosa var. multiflora, commonly known as the clustered arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and a popular choice for native plant gardens.
