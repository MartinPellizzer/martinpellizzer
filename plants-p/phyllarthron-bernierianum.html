Phyllarthron bernierianum

Phyllarthron bernierianum, commonly known as the Bernier's phyllanthus, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Phyllanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually opposite or whorled, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
