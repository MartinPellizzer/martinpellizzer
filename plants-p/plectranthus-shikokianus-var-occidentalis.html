Plectranthus shikokianus var. occidentalis

Plectranthus shikokianus var. occidentalis, commonly known as the Western Shikokian Plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct aromatic scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of violet or blue, typically arranged in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to attract pollinators.
