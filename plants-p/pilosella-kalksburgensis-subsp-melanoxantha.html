Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp. melanoxantha

Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp.

melanoxantha, commonly known as the black-hair pilosella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, hairy leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dark-colored hairs, which give it its common name. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
