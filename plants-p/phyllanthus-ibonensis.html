Phyllanthus ibonensis

Phyllanthus ibonensis, commonly known as the Philippine goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, smooth seeds.
