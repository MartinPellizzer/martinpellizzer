Polygala rosea var. occidentalis

Polygala rosea var. occidentalis, commonly known as the western rose galingale, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
