Phlomoides glabra

Phlomoides glabra, commonly known as smooth phlomoides, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, short hairs. Its small, pale blue to purple flowers are clustered in dense spikes, blooming in the summer months.

Phlomoides glabra is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
