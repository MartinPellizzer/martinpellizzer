Pimelandra disticha

Pimelandra disticha, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a silvery-green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix. It thrives in humid environments and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pimelandra disticha is also known for its ability to purify air and is relatively easy to care for.
