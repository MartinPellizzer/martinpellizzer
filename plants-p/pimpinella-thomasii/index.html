Pimpinella Thomasii

Pimpinella thomasii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its small, white or pale yellow blooms that appear in late spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
