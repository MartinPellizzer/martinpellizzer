Pyrethrum scariosum

Pyrethrum scariosum, commonly known as the scarious pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Pyrethrum scariosum is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
