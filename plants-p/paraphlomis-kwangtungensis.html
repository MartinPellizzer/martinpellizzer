Paraphlomis kwangtungensis

Paraphlomis kwangtungensis, commonly known as Kwangtung false bugloss, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. Its small, clustered flowers are typically purple or blue, blooming in the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
