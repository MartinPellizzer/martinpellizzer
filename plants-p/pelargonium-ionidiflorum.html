Pelargonium ionidiflorum

Pelargonium ionidiflorum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
