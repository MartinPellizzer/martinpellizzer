Peperomia paniculata

Peperomia paniculata, commonly known as the coral bells peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy and have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia paniculata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
