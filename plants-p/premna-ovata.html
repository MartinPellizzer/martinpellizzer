Premna ovata

Premna ovata, commonly known as the common premna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in terminal or axillary inflorescences.

Premna ovata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
