Peperomia brasiliensis

Peperomia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to reddish flowers, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia brasiliensis is known for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
