Paraceterach marantae

Paraceterach marantae, commonly known as the silver tamarind.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a metallic sheen, which give it its common name. It belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental value.

Paraceterach marantae is often cultivated for its striking foliage rather than its flowers.
