Plectronia mundiana var. pubescens

Plectronia mundiana var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf plectronia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany.
