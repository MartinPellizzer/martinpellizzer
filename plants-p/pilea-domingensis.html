Pilea domingensis

Pilea domingensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
