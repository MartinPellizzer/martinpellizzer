Pyrethrum canescens

Pyrethrum canescens, commonly known as the white pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, silver-white leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for producing pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in coastal or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a popular choice for gardens and ecological restoration projects.
