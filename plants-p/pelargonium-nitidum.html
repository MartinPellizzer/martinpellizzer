Pelargonium nitidum

Pelargonium nitidum, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pelargonium nitidum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
