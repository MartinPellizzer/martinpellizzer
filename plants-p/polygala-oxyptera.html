Polygala oxyptera

Polygala oxyptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has pinnately divided leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Polygala oxyptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
