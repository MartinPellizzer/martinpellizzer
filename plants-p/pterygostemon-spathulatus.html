Pterygostemon spathulatus

Pterygostemon spathulatus, commonly known as the broad-leaf pterygostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features lanceolate to ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, often displaying a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences, often in shades of purple or blue. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
