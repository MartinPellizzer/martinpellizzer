Peucedanum praeruptorum var. grande

Peucedanum praeruptorum var. grande, commonly known as the large-flowered angel's trumpets, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and showy flowers.

The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits called schizocarps, which split into two one-seeded units upon maturity. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with finely divided lobes that give it a delicate appearance.
