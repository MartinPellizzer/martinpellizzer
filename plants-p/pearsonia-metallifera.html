Pearsonia metallifera

Pearsonia metallifera, commonly known as the metallic pearsonia, is a rare and unique plant species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking, metallic-green foliage that reflects light in a shimmering manner. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. Pearsonia metallifera thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
