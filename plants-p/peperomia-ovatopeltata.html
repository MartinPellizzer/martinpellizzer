Peperomia ovatopeltata

Peperomia ovatopeltata, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia ovatopeltata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
