Polycampium

Polycampium, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is native to regions in Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Due to its invasive nature, it is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
