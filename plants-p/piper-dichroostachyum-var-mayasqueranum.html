Piper dichroostachyum var. mayasqueranum

Piper dichroostachyum var. mayasqueranum, commonly known as the Maya pepper, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in terminal spikes. Its leaves are typically ovate with a glossy surface and a strong, spicy aroma.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that ripens to a bright red color, often used in traditional culinary and medicinal applications.
