Piper pubipetiolum

Piper pubipetiolum, commonly known as the hairy pipevine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes.

The fruit is a three-lobed berry that contains several seeds.
