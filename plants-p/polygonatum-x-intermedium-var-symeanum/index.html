Polygonatum × Intermedium Var. Symeanum

Yellow asparagus fern, scientifically known as Polygonatum × intermedium var. symeanum, is a striking plant prized for its vibrant, golden-green foliage. Native to parts of Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments and is a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its feathery leaves and delicate appearance make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts seeking a touch of elegance. Easy to care for, it requires minimal maintenance and can flourish in a variety of indoor settings. With its unique color and graceful form, it adds a natural, soothing presence to any space.
