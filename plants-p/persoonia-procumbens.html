Persoonia procumbens

Persoonia procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate geebung, is a low-growing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features dense clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Persoonia procumbens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
