Phyllanthus vitiensis

Phyllanthus vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Phyllanthus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Fiji.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features small, clustered leaves with a waxy surface, and its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow. It produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

Phyllanthus vitiensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
