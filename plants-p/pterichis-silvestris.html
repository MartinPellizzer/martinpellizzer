Pterichis silvestris

Pterichis silvestris, commonly known as the wild pterichis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Pterichis silvestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
