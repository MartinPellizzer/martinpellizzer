Phyllanthus schaulsii

Phyllanthus schaulsii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, alternate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky substance. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

Phyllanthus schaulsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
