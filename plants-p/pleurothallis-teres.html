Pleurothallis teres

Pleurothallis teres, commonly known as the compact pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and often features dense clusters of narrow, strap-like leaves. The flowers are typically small and may be clustered in inflorescences, often in shades of white, yellow, or green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
