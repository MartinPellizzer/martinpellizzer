Potentilla serotina f. stenopetala

Potentilla serotina f. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its low-growing habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
