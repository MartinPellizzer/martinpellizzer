Parabouchetia brasiliensis

Parabouchetia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a complex root system that helps it anchor in the dense, moist soil of its natural habitat. It thrives in high humidity and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Parabouchetia brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
