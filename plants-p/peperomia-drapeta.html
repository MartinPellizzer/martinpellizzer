Peperomia drapeta

Peperomia drapeta, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a unique reddish-brown vein pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often prized for its ornamental value.
