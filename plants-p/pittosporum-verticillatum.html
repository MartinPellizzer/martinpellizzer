Pittosporum verticillatum

Pittosporum verticillatum, commonly known as the mountain pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features aromatic, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
