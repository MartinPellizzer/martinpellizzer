Podospermum willkommii

Podospermum willkommii, commonly known as the silverleaf podo, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a distinctive waxy texture.

Podospermum willkommii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
