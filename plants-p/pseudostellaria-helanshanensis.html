Pseudostellaria helanshanensis

Pseudostellaria helanshanensis, commonly known as the Helan Mountain starwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
