Peperomia reflexa f. capensis

Peperomia reflexa f. capensis, commonly known as the Cape peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are followed by round, orange berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

This cultivar is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
