Premna obtusifolia var. minor

Premna obtusifolia var. minor, commonly known as the small-leaf or narrow-leaf morning glory, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing or trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically blue to purple in color and bloom in clusters.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
