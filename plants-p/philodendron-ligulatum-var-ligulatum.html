Philodendron ligulatum var. ligulatum

Philodendron ligulatum var. ligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive narrow, elongated shape and a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by dark, round berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
