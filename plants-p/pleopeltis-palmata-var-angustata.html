Pleopeltis palmata var. angustata

Pleopeltis palmata var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on rocky or shaded forest floors. Its fronds are usually a vibrant green, with a slightly serrated margin.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
