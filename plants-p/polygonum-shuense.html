Polygonum shuense

Polygonum shuense, commonly known as Shu Polygonum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes species known for their distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous regions and forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of red or pink, adding ornamental value.

Polygonum shuense is valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity and its potential use in traditional medicine.
