Paphiopedilum chiwuanum

Paphiopedilum chiwuanum, commonly known as the Chiwanum lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a unique, pouch-like lip that is a defining characteristic of the genus Paphiopedilum. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
