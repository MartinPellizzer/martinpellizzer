Pleurothallis per-dusenii

Pleurothallis per-dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, helping it to conserve moisture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
