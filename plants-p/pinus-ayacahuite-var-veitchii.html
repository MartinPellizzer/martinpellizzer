Pinus ayacahuite var. veitchii

Pinus ayacahuite var. veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's Mexican pine, is a variant of the Ayacahuite pine native to Mexico.

It is a medium-sized evergreen conifer with needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in bundles of five. The cones of this species are woody and can reach lengths of up to 12 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
