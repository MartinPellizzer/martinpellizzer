Paullinia meliifolia f. subglabra

Paullinia meliifolia f. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved cupuaçu, is a variant of the cupuaçu plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves, which distinguish it from other forms of the species. The plant produces large, edible fruits that are similar to cacao in appearance and flavor. Its botanical features make it valuable for both ecological and agricultural purposes.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates with rich, well-drained soil.
