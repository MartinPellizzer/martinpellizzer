Pronephrium fengkaiense

Pronephrium fengkaiense, commonly known as the Fengkai spleenwort, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pterobryaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems that bear dense clusters of leafy shoots. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its reproductive structures, including capsules, are borne on short, upright stalks at the tips of the stems.
