Prunus avium var. bigarella

Prunus avium var. bigarella, commonly known as the Bigarreau cherry, is a variant of the sweet cherry tree characterized by its small, round fruits.

The berries are typically dark red to black in color, with a firm, juicy flesh that is highly flavorful. This variety is prized for its early ripening and good resistance to diseases. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and fruit production.
