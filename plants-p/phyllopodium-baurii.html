Phyllopodium baurii

Phyllopodium baurii, commonly known as the Baur's fan-leaf, is a unique plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 feet in diameter. The leaves are usually dark green and have a distinctive, waxy texture. This plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its striking appearance.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
