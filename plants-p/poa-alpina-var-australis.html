Poa alpina var. australis

Poa alpina var. australis, commonly known as the Australian alpine grass, is a variant of the alpine bentgrass native to the southern hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that thrives in cold, high-altitude environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and can withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in alpine ecosystems.
