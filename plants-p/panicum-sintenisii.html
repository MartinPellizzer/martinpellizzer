Panicum sintenisii

Panicum sintenisii, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed sites.

Due to its limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
