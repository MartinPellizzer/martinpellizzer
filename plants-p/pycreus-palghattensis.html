Pycreus palghattensis

Pycreus palghattensis, commonly known as the Palghatt pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
