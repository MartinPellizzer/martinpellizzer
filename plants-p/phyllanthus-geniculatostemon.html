Phyllanthus geniculatostemon

Phyllanthus geniculatostemon, commonly known as the spiral-stemmed phyllanthus, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves along its stems. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Phyllanthus geniculatostemon is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
