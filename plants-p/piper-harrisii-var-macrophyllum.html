Piper harrisii var. macrophyllum

Piper harrisii var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
