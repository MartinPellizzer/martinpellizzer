Platytheca galioides var. crassifolia

Platytheca galioides var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf galioides, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of broad, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a terminal spike during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
