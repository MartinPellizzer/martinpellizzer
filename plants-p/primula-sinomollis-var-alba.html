Primula sinomollis var. alba

Primula sinomollis var. alba, commonly known as the white Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a delicate and early-blooming floral display.
