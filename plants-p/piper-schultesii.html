Piper schultesii

Piper schultesii, commonly known as the Schultesi pepper or Schultes' pepper, is a climbing vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and the leaves are often used in traditional medicine. It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and has a distinctive, peppery flavor.

Due to its potential medicinal properties, it has attracted interest from botanists and ethnobotologists.
