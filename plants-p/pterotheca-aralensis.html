Pterotheca aralensis

Pterotheca aralensis, commonly known as the Aral Sea daisy, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, helping to reduce water loss.

Pterotheca aralensis plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest for its ornamental value.
