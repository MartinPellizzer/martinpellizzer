Pittosporum kunmingense

Pittosporum kunmingense, commonly known as the Yunnan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive leaves and showy blooms.

Pittosporum kunmingense is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
