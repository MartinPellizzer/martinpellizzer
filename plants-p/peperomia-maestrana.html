Peperomia maestrana

Peperomia maestrana, commonly known as the Maestrana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, often with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for container cultivation.
