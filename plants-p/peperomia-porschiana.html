Peperomia porschiana

Peperomia porschiana, commonly known as the Porschiana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a variety of colors, including shades of green, silver, and red, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia porschiana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
