Psephellus bornmuelleri

Psephellus bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves. Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
