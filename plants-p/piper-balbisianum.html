Piper balbisianum

Piper balbisianum, commonly known as Balbisianum pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into berries. Its fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
