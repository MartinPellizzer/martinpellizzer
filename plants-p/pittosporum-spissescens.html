Pittosporum spissescens

Pittosporum spissescens, commonly known as the dense-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange or red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum spissescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
