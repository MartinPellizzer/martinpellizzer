Piper nubigaudens

Piper nubigaudens, commonly known as the misty pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and dark green, glossy leaves. Its small, white or greenish flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, fleshy berries.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
