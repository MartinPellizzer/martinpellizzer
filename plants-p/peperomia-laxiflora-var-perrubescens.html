Peperomia laxiflora var. perrubescens

Peperomia laxiflora var. perrubescens, commonly known as the red-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking red or reddish veins, giving it a vibrant appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it suitable for both container and garden settings.
