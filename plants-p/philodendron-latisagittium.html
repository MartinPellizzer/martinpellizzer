Philodendron latisagittium

Philodendron latisagittium, commonly known as the broad arrowhead philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive broad arrowhead shape, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
