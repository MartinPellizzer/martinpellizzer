Pterostylis atrosanguinea

Pterostylis atrosanguinea, commonly known as the black-veined hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish floral parts, which are often veined with a contrasting color. The plant produces a single, hooded flower with a lip that is usually pale or reddish in color. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and understoreys.

The species is part of the rich and diverse Orchidaceae family, known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
