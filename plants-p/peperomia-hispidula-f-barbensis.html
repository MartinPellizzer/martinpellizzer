Peperomia hispidula f. barbensis

Peperomia hispidula f. barbensis, commonly known as the Barben's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture, giving it a unique tactile quality. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, typical of the Piperaceae family. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

Due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
