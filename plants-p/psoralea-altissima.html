Psoralea altissima

Psoralea altissima, commonly known as the tall psoralea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in grassland and rocky habitats. It produces legume pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.
