Penstemon × mirus

Penstemon × mirus, commonly known as the hybrid penstemon, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Penstemon, which includes over 250 species of flowering plants. This particular hybrid is known for its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple and pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
