Peperomia ulugurensis var. ukingensis

Peperomia ulugurensis var. ukingensis, commonly known as the Ukinge Peperomia, is a rare and unique species native to the tropical rainforests of East Africa.

The plant features small, clustered inflorescences that produce tiny, inconspicuous flowers, typical of the Piperaceae family. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its humid, shaded habitat.
