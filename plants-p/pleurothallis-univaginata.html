Pleurothallis univaginata

Pleurothallis univaginata, commonly known as the single-vessel pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically features a short, stout stem with a single, elongated inflorescence bearing several flowers. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Pleurothallis univaginata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
