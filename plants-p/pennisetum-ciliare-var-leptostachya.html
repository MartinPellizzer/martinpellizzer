Pennisetum ciliare var. leptostachya

Pennisetum ciliare var. leptostachya, commonly known as slender-stemmed fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery plumes that give it a soft, fountain-like appearance. The plant is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and blooms in the summer months.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to gardens and landscapes.
