Piper olfersianum

Piper olfersianum, commonly known as the Olfers' pepper, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fruits are used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some regions.
