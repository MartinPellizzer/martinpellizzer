Piper hymenopodum

Piper hymenopodum, commonly known as the African pepper or African black pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn dark when mature.

It is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
