Polystachya monolenis

Polystachya monolenis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flame grass, is a species of orchid native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright orange or red flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a slight sheen.

Polystachya monolenis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
