Pachysandra erecta

Pachysandra erecta, commonly known as upright pachysandra, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover in shaded areas. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Pachysandra erecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to suppress weeds in woodland gardens.
