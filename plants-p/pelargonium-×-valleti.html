Pelargonium × valleti

Pelargonium × valleti, commonly known as the Zonal Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically velvety and have distinct zonation patterns, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as potted plants.
