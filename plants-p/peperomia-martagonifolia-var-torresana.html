Peperomia martagonifolia var. torresana

Peperomia martagonifolia var. torresana, commonly known as the Torresana peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
