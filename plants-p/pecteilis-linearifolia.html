Pecteilis linearifolia

Pecteilis linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf pecteilis, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Pecteilis linearifolia is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and other moist habitats.
