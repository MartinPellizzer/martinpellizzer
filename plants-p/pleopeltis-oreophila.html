Pleopeltis oreophila

Pleopeltis oreophila, commonly known as the cloud forest huperzia, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Huperjiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rocky substrates or in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, serrated margin.

Pleopeltis oreophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
