Panicum schlechtendahlii var. monstrosum

Panicum schlechtendahlii var. monstrosum, commonly known as the giant panicgrass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 3 meters, with long, narrow leaves that are often tinged with purple or red. The plant produces feathery, silver-gray plumes in late summer, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscape design.

Its unique appearance and moderate maintenance requirements make it a standout in both residential and commercial landscapes.
