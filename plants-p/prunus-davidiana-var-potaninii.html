Prunus davidiana var. potaninii

Prunus davidiana var. potaninii, commonly known as the Chinese sand cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, edible berries. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and a grayish-barked trunk. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is also valued for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
