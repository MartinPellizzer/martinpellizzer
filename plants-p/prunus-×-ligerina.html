Prunus × ligerina

Prunus × ligerina, commonly known as the cherry laurel, is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Prunus × ligerina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
