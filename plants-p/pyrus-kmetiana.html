Pyrus kmetiana

Pyrus kmetiana, commonly known as the Kmetian pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the eastern Mediterranean, particularly in parts of Turkey and the Caucasus. Its fruit, a small pear-like apple, is typically edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

Pyrus kmetiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in traditional medicine.
