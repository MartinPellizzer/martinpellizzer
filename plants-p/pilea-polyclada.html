Pilea polyclada

Pilea polyclada, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea polyclada thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
