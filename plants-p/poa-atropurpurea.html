Poa atropurpurea

Poa atropurpurea, commonly known as purple mountain grass, is a species of grass native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its dark purple foliage, which becomes more pronounced in cooler conditions. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowering stems are usually shorter than the leaves, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Poa atropurpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique coloration.
