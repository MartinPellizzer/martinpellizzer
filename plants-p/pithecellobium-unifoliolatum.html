Pithecellobium unifoliolatum

Pithecellobium unifoliolatum, commonly known as the single leaf legume, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its unique foliage, featuring single leaflets on each stem. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in nitrogen fixation.
