Piper biauritum

Piper biauritum, commonly known as the African black pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are small berries that turn from green to black as they mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some regions.
