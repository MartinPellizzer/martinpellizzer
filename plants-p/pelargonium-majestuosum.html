Pelargonium majestuosum

Pelargonium majestuosum, commonly known as the Royal Pelargonium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, double-layered flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening due to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Pelargonium majestuosum is also known for its ability to produce fragrant blooms, adding both visual and olfactory appeal to any garden setting.
