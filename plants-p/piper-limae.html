Piper limae

Piper limae, commonly known as the lime pepper or wild lime, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, lime-scented leaves and small, peppery fruits. The plant is often cultivated for its leaves, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive fragrance and flavor.

Piper limae thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates.
