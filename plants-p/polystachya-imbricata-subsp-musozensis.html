Polystachya imbricata subsp. musozensis

Polystachya imbricata subsp.

musozensis, commonly known as the Musozense bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly in the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

This species is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and conservation efforts.
