Pittosporum leroyanum

Pittosporum leroyanum, commonly known as the Leroy's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
