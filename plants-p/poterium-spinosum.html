Poterium spinosum

Poterium spinosum, commonly known as the spiny broom.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, thorny growth and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not particularly palatable.

Poterium spinosum is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soils.
