Peramium menziesii

Peramium menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' peramium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Peramium menziesii is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its aesthetic appeal in native plant gardens.
