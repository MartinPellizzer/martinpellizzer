Piper canyazasense

Piper canyazasense, commonly known as the Canyaza pepper plant, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

These berries are often used in traditional medicine and local cuisine for their pungent flavor and medicinal properties.
