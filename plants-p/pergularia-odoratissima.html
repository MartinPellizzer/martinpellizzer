Pergularia odoratissima

Pergularia odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant pogostemon, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and India.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems.

Pergularia odoratissima is often used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
