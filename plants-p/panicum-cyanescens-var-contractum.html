Panicum cyanescens var. contractum

Panicum cyanescens var. contractum, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage, which becomes more pronounced as the plant matures. The leaves are narrow and tapering, with a dense, tufted appearance at the base. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
