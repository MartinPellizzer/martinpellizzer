Peperomia lanuginosa

Peperomia lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a soft, fuzzy texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia lanuginosa is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
