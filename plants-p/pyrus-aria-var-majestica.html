Pyrus aria var. majestica

Pyrus aria var. majestica, commonly known as the noble pear, is a rare and ornamental variant of the pear tree.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically not cultivated for commercial use. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often grown in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
