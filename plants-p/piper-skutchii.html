Piper skutchii

Piper skutchii, commonly known as Skutch's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly in countries like Guatemala and Honduras. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits.

The plant is notable for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic leaves and fruits.
