Pogostemon stellipila

Pogostemon stellipila, commonly known as the star-leaved pogostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique leaf arrangement and ornamental flowers make it a desirable plant for botanical gardens and wetland restoration projects.
