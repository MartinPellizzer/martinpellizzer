Prasophyllum correctum

Prasophyllum correctum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved greenhood, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique hood-shaped flower that is typically green or greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces a single spike with several flowers, each featuring a distinctive labellum that aids in pollination. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and heathlands.

Prasophyllum correctum is a terrestrial orchid with fibrous roots and narrow, grass-like leaves.
