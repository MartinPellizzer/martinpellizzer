Pedilonum conicum

Pedilonum conicum, commonly known as the spiral-shaped orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a visually striking appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white and green.

Botanically, it is notable for its specialized root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the forest floor.
