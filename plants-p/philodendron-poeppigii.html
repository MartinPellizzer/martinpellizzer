Philodendron poeppigii

Philodendron poeppigii, commonly known as the Poeppig's philodendron, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
