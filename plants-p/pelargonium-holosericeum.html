Pelargonium holosericeum

Pelargonium holosericeum, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-blue foliage and clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance and help reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium holosericeum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
