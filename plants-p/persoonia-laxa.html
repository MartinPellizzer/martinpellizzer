Persoonia laxa

Persoonia laxa, commonly known as the slender geebung, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, cylindrical stems and small, often clustered, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in coastal and heathland environments.

Persoonia laxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
