Philodendron ayangannense

Philodendron ayangannense, commonly known as the Ayangannense Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and unique inflorescence structure. The plant features large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
