Potentilla schugnanica

Potentilla schugnanica, commonly known as the Schugnan rose or Schugnan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 cm. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a slightly hairy texture.

Potentilla schugnanica is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and adaptability.
