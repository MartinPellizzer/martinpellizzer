Pilosella hoppeana subsp. virentisquama

Pilosella hoppeana subsp.

virentisquama, commonly known as the green-veined hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its foliage has a distinctive greenish hue, which contributes to its common name.
