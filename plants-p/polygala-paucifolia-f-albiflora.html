Polygala paucifolia f. albiflora

Polygala paucifolia f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered milkweed, is a rare and distinctive plant with unique botanical features.

The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
