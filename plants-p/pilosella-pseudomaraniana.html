Pilosella pseudomaraniana

Pilosella pseudomaraniana, commonly known as the slender pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. The plant features slender, erect stems covered with fine, white hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange in color, and they bloom in late spring to early summer.

Pilosella pseudomaraniana is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
