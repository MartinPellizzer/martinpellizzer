Pleroma obscurum

Pleroma obscurum, commonly known as the dark pleroma, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest undergrowth. Its flowers bloom during the late spring to early summer months.

Due to habitat loss, Pleroma obscurum is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
