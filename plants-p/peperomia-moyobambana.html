Peperomia moyobambana

Peperomia moyobambana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a silvery sheen, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or purple in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia moyobambana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
