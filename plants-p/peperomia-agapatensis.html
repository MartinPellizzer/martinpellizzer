Peperomia agapatensis

Peperomia agapatensis, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. Peperomia agapatensis is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
