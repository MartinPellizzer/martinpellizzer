Podostemum weddellianum

Podostemum weddellianum, commonly known as the Weddell's waterstar, is a unique aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom above the water surface, while its roots anchor in the substrate. The plant thrives in fast-flowing streams and rivers, where it forms dense mats on rocks. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, adapted to reduce drag in flowing water.

Podostemum weddellianum plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
