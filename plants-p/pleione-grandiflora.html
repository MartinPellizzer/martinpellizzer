Pleione grandiflora

Pleione grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring, featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. The plant grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity, often found in forest understories.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
