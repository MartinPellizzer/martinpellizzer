Peperomia humilis var. cumulicola

Peperomia humilis var. cumulicola, commonly known as the mountain peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for moderate humidity and its ability to thrive in smaller containers.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
