Pilea brevicornuta

Pilea brevicornuta, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are rounded and often have a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea brevicornuta is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
