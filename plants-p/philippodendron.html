Philippodendron

Philippodendron, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular houseplant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of heart-shaped leaves that give it a lush, green appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries, which are a key feature in its reproductive cycle. Philippodendron thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its ability to climb or trail makes it versatile for various indoor gardening setups.
