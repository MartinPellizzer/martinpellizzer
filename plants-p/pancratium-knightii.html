Pancratium knightii

Pancratium knightii, commonly known as the Cape daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with red centers, which bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a distinctive, short stem.

Pancratium knightii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
