Phanera excelsa var. aurora

Phanera excelsa var. aurora, commonly known as the Aurora Flame Tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface with a slightly serrated edge. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a specimen plant.
