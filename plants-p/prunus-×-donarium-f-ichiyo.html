Prunus × donarium f. ichiyo

Prunus × donarium f. ichiyo, commonly known as the Ichiyo cherry, is a hybrid ornamental shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, double pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking visual display to gardens. The plant typically grows to a height of 3–6 meters, with a rounded or spreading form. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The Ichiyo cherry is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in parks and gardens for its beauty.
