Panicum quetameense

Panicum quetameense, commonly known as Quetame grass, is a species of grass native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and pastures. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs.

Panicum quetameense is valued for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
