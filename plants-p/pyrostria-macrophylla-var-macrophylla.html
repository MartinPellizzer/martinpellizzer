Pyrostria macrophylla var. macrophylla

Pyrostria macrophylla var. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
