Panicum macrostachyum var. patens

Panicum macrostachyum var. patens, commonly known as the broad-stalked panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and panicles that are often broad and open. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
