Phaseolus coccineus var. argenteus

Phaseolus coccineus var. argenteus, commonly known as the silver bean, is a striking variant of the scarlet runner bean.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and pods that mature to a striking orange-red color. The leaves are typically large and heart-shaped, with a matte green surface. This variety is prized for its ornamental value as well as its edible beans, which have a sweet, nutty flavor.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both gardeners and farmers.
