Peperomia stenocaulis

Peperomia stenocaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture and vibrant green or variegated coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia stenocaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
