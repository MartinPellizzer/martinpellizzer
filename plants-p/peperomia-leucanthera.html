Peperomia leucanthera

Peperomia leucanthera, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or white powdery coating, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia leucanthera thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.
