Phytolacca yunnanensis

Phytolacca yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan lacca, is a shrub native to China, particularly in Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible but have a strong, unpleasant taste.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
