Panicum brownii

Panicum brownii, commonly known as brown panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are often seen in late summer. Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and a tufted growth habit.

Panicum brownii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
