Peperomia galioides var. saxicola

Peperomia galioides var. saxicola, commonly known as the rock peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
