Peperomia distachyos f. geminispica

Peperomia distachyos f. geminispica, commonly known as the gemini peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
