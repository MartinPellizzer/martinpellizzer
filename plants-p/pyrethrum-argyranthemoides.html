Pyrethrum argyranthemoides

Pyrethrum argyranthemoides, commonly known as silverleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its name derives from the Greek words "pyrethrum" and "argyranthemum," reflecting its association with pyrethrin, a natural insecticide.
