Phyllanthus nothisii

Phyllanthus nothisii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 1.5 meters tall, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid conditions.

The species is noted for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
