Prosaptia kanashiroi

Prosaptia kanashiroi, commonly known as the Japanese spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Prosaptia kanashiroi prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
