Podocarpium menglaense

Podocarpium menglaense, commonly known as Mengla pine.

It is a rare gymnosperm native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its coniferous structure, featuring needle-like leaves and cone-bearing reproductive structures. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
