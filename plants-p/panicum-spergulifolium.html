Panicum spergulifolium

Panicum spergulifolium, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a flowering plant native to the central and southern United States.

It is a warm-season grass that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairie and open woodland habitats. The plant produces dense, tufted clusters of small, silver-gray flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and bluish-gray in color, contributing to its ornamental value.

Silver bluestem is also valued for its erosion control capabilities and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
