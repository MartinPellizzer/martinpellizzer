Psathura polyantha

Psathura polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its root system is fibrous and helps it thrive in well-drained soils.

Psathura polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
