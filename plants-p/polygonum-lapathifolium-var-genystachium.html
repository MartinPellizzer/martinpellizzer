Polygonum lapathifolium var. genystachium

Polygonum lapathifolium var. genystachium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks. Its stems are often reddish in color and can become woody with age.

The species is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
