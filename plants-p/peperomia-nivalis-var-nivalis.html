Peperomia nivalis var. nivalis

Peperomia nivalis var. nivalis, commonly known as the snow peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, giving it a frost-like appearance. The leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

This variety is well-suited for indoor environments with bright, indirect light and moderate humidity.
