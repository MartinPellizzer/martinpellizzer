Peperomia nicolliae

Peperomia nicolliae, commonly known as the Nicoll's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale green. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia nicolliae is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
