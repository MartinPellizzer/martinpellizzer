Papyrus venustus

Papyrus venustus, commonly known as the beautiful papyrus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.
