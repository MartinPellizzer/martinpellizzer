Potamophila

Potamophila, commonly known as the water plantain, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Alismataceae.

These plants are typically found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of streams and ponds. Their rhizomatous growth habit allows them to spread and colonize aquatic environments effectively.
