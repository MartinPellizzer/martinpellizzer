Pyrus kleinhofiana

Pyrus kleinhofiana, commonly known as the Kleinhof's pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, slender branches and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or yellow-green when mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

Pyrus kleinhofiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and early blooming habit.
