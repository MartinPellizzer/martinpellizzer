Peperomia distachyos var. distachyoides

Peperomia distachyos var. distachyoides, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This low-maintenance plant is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
