Phalaenopsis hombronii

Phalaenopsis hombronii, commonly known as the Hombron's moth orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms once a year, with flowers lasting several weeks. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding environment.

It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
