Polybotrya fulvostrigosa

Polybotrya fulvostrigosa, commonly known as the golden-haired fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are covered with fine, golden hairs, giving it its common name.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
