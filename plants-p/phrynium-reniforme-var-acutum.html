Phrynium reniforme var. acutum

Phrynium reniforme var. acutum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
