Pyrus kusnetzovii

Pyrus kusnetzovii, commonly known as the Kusnetzov's pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tien Shan and Pamir mountains. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white to pink flowers in early spring. Its fruit, a small pear-like berry, is edible but not widely cultivated for commercial use.

Pyrus kusnetzovii is often grown in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
