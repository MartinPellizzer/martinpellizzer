Peperomia subacaulis

Peperomia subacaulis, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia subacaulis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
