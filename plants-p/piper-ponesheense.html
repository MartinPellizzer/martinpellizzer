Piper ponesheense

Piper ponesheense, commonly known as the Poneshe pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local remedies for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's botanical significance lies in its potential pharmacological applications and its role in the biodiversity of the Amazon.
