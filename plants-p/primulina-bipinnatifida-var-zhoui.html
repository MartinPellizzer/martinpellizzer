Primulina bipinnatifida var. zhoui

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with a finely divided, bipinnatifid margin. Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is a sought-after species among botanists and plant enthusiasts.
