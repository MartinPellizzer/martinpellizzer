Polygala nathadzeae

Polygala nathadzeae, commonly known as the Natal polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its unique, often intricate flower structures. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
