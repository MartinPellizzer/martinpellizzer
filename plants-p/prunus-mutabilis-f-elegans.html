Prunus mutabilis f. elegans

Prunus mutabilis f. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Cherry, is a unique variation of the Flowering Cherry.

It is characterized by its striking, variable flower colors that range from white to pink and red, depending on environmental conditions. The plant is a small deciduous tree or shrub with slender branches and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its showy blooms, which provide a vibrant display in spring.
