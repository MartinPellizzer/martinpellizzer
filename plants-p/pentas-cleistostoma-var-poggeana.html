Pentas cleistostoma var. poggeana

Pentas cleistostoma var. poggeana, commonly known as the African flame or purple flame, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, vibrant flowers. The flowers are typically tubular and come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for warm climates.
