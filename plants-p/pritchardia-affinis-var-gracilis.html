Pritchardia affinis var. gracilis

Pritchardia affinis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a rare and endangered plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, feather-like leaves that grow in a dense rosette. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Due to habitat loss and invasive species, it is critically threatened in the wild.

Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique botanical species.
