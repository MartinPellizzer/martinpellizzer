Peperomia grisarii

Peperomia grisarii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically silver-white with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia grisarii is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
