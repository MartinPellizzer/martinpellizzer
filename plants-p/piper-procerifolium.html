Piper procerifolium

Piper procerifolium, commonly known as the tall-leaf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and pharmacological studies.
