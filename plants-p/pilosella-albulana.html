Pilosella albulana

Pilosella albulana, commonly known as the white goatsbeard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are hairy and lobed, while the flowers are white with yellow centers.

Pilosella albulana is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
