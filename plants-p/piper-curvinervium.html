Piper curvinervium

Piper curvinervium, commonly known as the zigzag pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, opposite leaves with a distinctive zigzag venation pattern. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on different plants, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Piper curvinervium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
