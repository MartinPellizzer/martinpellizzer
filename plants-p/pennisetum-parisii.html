Pennisetum parisii

Pennisetum parisii, commonly known as the Paris grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often adorned with a fine layer of silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or purplish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Pennisetum parisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
