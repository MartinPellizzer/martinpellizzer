Photinia parvifolia var. chingshuiensis

Photinia parvifolia var. chingshuiensis, commonly known as the Chingshui photinia, is a compact shrub native to southern China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
