Pittosporum heckelii

Pittosporum heckelii, commonly known as the silver pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum heckelii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
