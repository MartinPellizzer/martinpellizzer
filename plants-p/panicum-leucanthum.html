Panicum leucanthum

Panicum leucanthum, commonly known as white panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, slender leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that are typically white or pale yellow, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open fields and disturbed areas.

Panicum leucanthum is a hardy perennial that is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
