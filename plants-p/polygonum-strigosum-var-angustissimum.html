Polygonum strigosum var. angustissimum

Polygonum strigosum var. angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
