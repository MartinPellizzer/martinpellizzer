Pinalia multiflora

Pinalia multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora pinalia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered inflorescences and small, delicate flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated edge.

Pinalia multiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
