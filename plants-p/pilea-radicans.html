Pilea radicans

Pilea radicans, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, trailing evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by spherical, reddish-brown fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea radicans is known for its easy care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
