Panicum molle

Panicum molle, commonly known as soft panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, soft foliage and produces fluffy, pale green to brown seed heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in disturbed soils.

Panicum molle is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
