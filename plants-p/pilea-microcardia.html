Pilea microcardia

Pilea microcardia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features rosettes of velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored houseplant worldwide.
