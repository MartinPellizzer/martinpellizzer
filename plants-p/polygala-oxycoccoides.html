Polygala oxycoccoides

Polygala oxycoccoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces pods that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
