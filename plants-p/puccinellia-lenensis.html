Puccinellia lenensis

Puccinellia lenensis, commonly known as the narrowleaf saltbush, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to arid and saline environments, particularly in Central Asia and parts of China. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in poor soils and is often found in salt marshes and coastal dunes.

Puccinellia lenensis plays a role in stabilizing soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
