Pelargonium × beadoniae

Pelargonium × beadoniae, commonly known as the beadonia geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking, bead-like red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, hairy leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × beadoniae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
