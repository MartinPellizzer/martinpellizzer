Pleurothallis sotarae

Pleurothallis sotarae, commonly known as the Sotarae orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanical researchers and orchid enthusiasts.
