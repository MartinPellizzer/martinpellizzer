Polygala muratii

Polygala muratii, commonly known as the Murat's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces pods that contain minute seeds.

Polygala muratii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
