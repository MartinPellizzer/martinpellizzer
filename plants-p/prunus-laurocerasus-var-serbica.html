Prunus laurocerasus var. serbica

Prunus laurocerasus var. serbica, commonly known as the Serbian cherry laurel, is a variant of the common cherry laurel.

It is a dense, evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Balkans. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring. Its fruit is a dark blue-black drupe, often used in jams and preserves.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in various climates.
