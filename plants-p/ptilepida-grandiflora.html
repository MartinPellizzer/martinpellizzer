Ptilepida grandiflora

Ptilepida grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered ptilepida, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or mountainous areas. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a slightly waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
