Parodia matthesiana

Parodia matthesiana, commonly known as the Matthesian Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, cylindrical stems that resemble a hedgehog. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived but striking. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Parodia matthesiana is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
