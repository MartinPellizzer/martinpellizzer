Podochilus hasseltii

Podochilus hasseltii, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces fragrant blooms that attract pollinators.

Podochilus hasseltii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in traditional medicine.
