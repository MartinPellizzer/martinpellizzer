Pilea tippenhaueri

Pilea tippenhaueri, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a compact, low-growing houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea tippenhaueri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
