Panicum petrosum var. molle

Panicum petrosum var. molle, commonly known as soft panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, exhibiting a clumping growth habit. The plant features slender, cylindrical spikes of pale green to golden brown flowers, which are arranged in panicles. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
