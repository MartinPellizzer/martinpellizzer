Psilothonna tagetes

Psilothonna tagetes, commonly known as Mexican marigold, is a flowering plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its use in traditional medicine.
