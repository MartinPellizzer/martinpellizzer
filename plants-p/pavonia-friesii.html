Pavonia friesii

Pavonia friesii, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia friesii thrives in full sun to partial shade and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
