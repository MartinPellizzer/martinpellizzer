Parachimarrhis breviloba

Parachimarrhis breviloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chimarrh.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is characterized by its drought tolerance and adaptability to harsh conditions.
