Protea stellaris

Protea stellaris, commonly known as the star protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous petal-like bracts that form a star-shaped appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in regions with a Mediterranean climate. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a central cone-like structure called a torus.

Protea stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.
