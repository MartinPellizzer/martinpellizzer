Paramochloa crispifolia

Paramochloa crispifolia, commonly known as the crinkled leaf fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are often curled or crinkled, giving it a distinctive appearance. Paramochloa crispifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
