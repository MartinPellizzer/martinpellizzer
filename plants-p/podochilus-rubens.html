Podochilus rubens

Podochilus rubens, commonly known as the red orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking red and pink floral colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are showy and have a pleasant fragrance, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
