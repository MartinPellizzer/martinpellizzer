Prangos eryngioides

Prangos eryngioides, commonly known as the silverleaf eryngo, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, spiky flower heads that range in color from white to pink, resembling those of the genus Eryngium. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Prangos eryngioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
