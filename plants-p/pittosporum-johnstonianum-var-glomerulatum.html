Pittosporum johnstonianum var. glomerulatum

Pittosporum johnstonianum var. glomerulatum, commonly known as the clustered pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

This variety is particularly noted for its adaptability to coastal conditions.
