Peperomia perrottetiana var. macrophylla

Peperomia perrottetiana var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage is often prized for its ornamental value and ease of care.
