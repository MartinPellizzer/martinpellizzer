Potentilla turicinensis

Potentilla turicinensis, commonly known as the Turicinensis cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Potentilla turicinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
