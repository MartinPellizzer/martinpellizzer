Pterocarya insignis

Pterocarya insignis, commonly known as the Chinese wingnut, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Juglandaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive winged seeds. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and produces yellowish-green flowers in early spring. Its fruits are rough, woody capsules that split open to release the seeds.

Pterocarya insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
