Poinsettia pulcherrima var. plenissima

Poinsettia pulcherrima var. plenissima, commonly known as the Christmas star poinsettia, is a popular ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts, which are modified leaves that resemble flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its short blooming period during the holiday season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is widely used in festive decorations.
