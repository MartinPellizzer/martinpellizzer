Podocarpus hookeri

Podocarpus hookeri, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or the evergreen tree, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like structures. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters in its natural habitat.

Podocarpus hookeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and landscapes.
