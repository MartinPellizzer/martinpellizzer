Phyteuma spiciforme

Phyteuma spiciforme, commonly known as the spiked phyteuma, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces elongated flower spikes that rise above the foliage. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Phyteuma spiciforme is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
