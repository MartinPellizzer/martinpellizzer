Pennisetum flexispica

Pennisetum flexispica, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, white plumes that give it a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in clumps and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum flexispica is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
