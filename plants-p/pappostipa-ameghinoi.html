Pappostipa ameghinoi

Pappostipa ameghinoi, commonly known as the Ameghino's pappus grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.
