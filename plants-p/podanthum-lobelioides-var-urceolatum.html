Podanthum lobelioides var. urceolatum

Podanthum lobelioides var. urceolatum, commonly known as the urn-shaped podanthum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of blue and purple. The leaves are typically elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and the plant produces capsule fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture to garden landscapes.
