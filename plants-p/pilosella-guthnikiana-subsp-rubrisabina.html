Pilosella guthnikiana subsp. rubrisabina

Pilosella guthnikiana subsp.

rubrisabina, commonly known as the red-stemmed goatsbeard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and small, yellowish flower heads.

The subspecies is distinguished by its reddish stems and bracts, which give it its common name. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.

This plant is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, arid environments.
