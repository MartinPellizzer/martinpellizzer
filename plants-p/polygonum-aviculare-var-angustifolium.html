Polygonum aviculare var. angustifolium

Polygonum aviculare var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a pointed tip. The small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. This species is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and thrives in moist to wet environments.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
