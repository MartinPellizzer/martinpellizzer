Pilosella paucilingua

Pilosella paucilingua, commonly known as the slender leaf goatweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, disturbed soils, and rocky areas. It has small, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Pilosella paucilingua is often found in dry, sun-exposed environments and is adapted to thrive in low-nutrient soils.
