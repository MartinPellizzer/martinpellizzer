Polygala sophiae

Polygala sophiae, commonly known as the sacred polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that come in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its small, clustered flowers are accompanied by leafless stems, giving it a unique and elegant appearance.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
