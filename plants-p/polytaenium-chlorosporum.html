Polytaenium chlorosporum

Polytaenium chlorosporum, commonly known as the green-spored threadleaf, is a unique member of the Polytaenium genus, characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked due to its foliage being more prominent.
