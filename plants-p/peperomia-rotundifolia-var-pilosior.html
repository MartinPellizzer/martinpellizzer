Peperomia rotundifolia var. pilosior

Peperomia rotundifolia var. pilosior, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves with a distinctive velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
