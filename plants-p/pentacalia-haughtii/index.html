Pentacalia Haughtii

Pentacalia haughtii is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Mexico, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare in the wild, prompting conservation efforts to protect its habitat. This plant exemplifies the diversity and adaptability of flora in challenging environments.
