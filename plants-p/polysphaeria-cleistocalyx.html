Polysphaeria cleistocalyx

Polysphaeria cleistocalyx, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the shade of dense foliage. Its unique morphology includes a slender stem and a single, broad leaf that is often covered in a waxy coating.
