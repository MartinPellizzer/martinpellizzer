Pedicularis centranthera var. exulans

Pedicularis centranthera var. exulans, commonly known as the alpine monkey orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is an herbaceous perennial that produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of red and orange, often resembling orchids in appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
